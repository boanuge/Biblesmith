1. Huan, Toupan a gensa bangin Sara a veha, Toupan a gensa bangin Sara tungah leng a hihsaka.
2. Huchiin, Sara a gaia, Pathianin a kianga agen hun seh takin Abraham a tek nungin tapa a neihsakta hi.
3. Huan, Abrahamin, a tapa neih, Sarain a neihsak, amin dingin Isaak a saa.
4. Huan, Abrahamin, Pathianin a kianga thu a piak bangin a tapa Isaak ni giat a chinin a zek a sumsaka.
5. Abraham a tapa Isaak a neih laiin kum ja a upa ahi.
6. Huan, Sarain, Pathianin honnuisakta; a ja mi chihon honnuihpih ding uh, achia.
7. Huan, amah mahin, Sarain naungekte a honpom ding chih, kuan ahia Abraham kianga genna dan thei ding? a tek nungin tapa ka neihsakta achia.
8. Huan, naungek a hongkhangliana, a nawinek a tawpsakta ua: huan, Isaak a nawinek a tawpsak ni uh Abrahamin ankuang thupi a luita hi.
9. Huan, Aiguptami Hagar tapa, Abraham a dinga a neihpan, a nuihsan lai Sarain a mua.
10. Huaijiakin Abraham kiangah, hiai sikhanu leh a tapa delhkhia in: hiai sikhanu tapa ka tapa Isaak gouluah pih ding ahi theikei ding, achia.
11. Huan, hiai thu a tapa jiakin Abraham ngaihin thil hoihlou tak ahi.
12. Himahleh Pathianin Abraham kiangah, Naupang tungtang thu leh na sikhanu tungtang thu na ngaihin thil hoihlou tak hi kei hen; Sarain na kianga thu a gen tengteng juiin; Isaak ah na suante a chi ding uh la ahi ngala.
13. Huan, sikhanu tapa ah leng, na suan a hih jiakin, chi chih tham ngial ka honbawl ding, achia.
14. Huchiin, Abraham bel takin a thoua, tanghou leh tuithawl tui a dim a laa, Hagar kiangah a piaa, a liangkolou ah a suansaka, naupang mah leng, a paisak taa; huchiin a paia, Beer-seba gamdai ah a vak a vakta hi.
15. Huan, tuithawl a tui a hong omta keia, huchiin naupang loubuk munkhat ah a koihta mai hi.
16. Huan, Naupang si ding ka en ngam kei, a chih jiakin a paia, gamla kuamah, thal kap phakna ding bangah, a majawn ah a vatuta khong khonga. Huchiin, a majawn ah a tua, ngaihtakin a kapta hi.
17. Huan, Pathianin naupang aw a jaa; huan, Pathian angelin van akipanin Hagar a sama, a kiangah, Bang na chia ahia Hagar? Lau ken: na omna naupang aw Pathianin a ja hi.
18. Thou inla, huai naupang phong inla, na khutin lenin, amah chi thupi taka bawl ding ka hi, achia.
19. Huan, Pathianin a mit a hihvak saka, tuileh a muta hi; huchiin a vapaia, tuithawl ah tui a dim dima, naupang a dawnsakta hi.
20. Huan, naupang kiangah Pathian a om jela, a hong khanglian jela; gamdai ah a om tou jela, thalkap siam a honghita.
21. Paran gamdai ah a oma; huan, anun Aigupta gam akipanin ji a guan hi.
22. Huan, hichi ahia, huailai khawngin Abimelek leh a sepaih pawl heutu Phikolin Abraham kiangah, na thil hih peuh Pathian na kiangah om jeljela:
23. Huchiin tuin hiailaiah Pathian min lou inla, kei leh ka tapa leh ka tapa tapa leng khem louin, ka honna hehpih khittaksa bangin kei leh na omna gam hehpih thuk sam dingin ka kiangah kichiam ve leh! chiin a gena.
24. Huan Abrahamin, Ka kichiam ding, achia.
25. Huan, Abimelek sikhaten tuileh a suh jiak un Abrahamin Abimelek a sala.
26. Huan, Abimelekin, hiai thil hihpen kua ahia, ka thei himhim kei: nang lah non hilh ngal keia, tuni ngallouin ka za nai himhim kei, achia.
27. Huchiin, Abrahamin belamte leh bawngtalte a pia, Abimelek kiangah a piaa; huan, a nih un thu a khungta uhi.
28. Huan, Abrahamin a belam hon laka belamnou a nu sagih a koih tuama.
29. Huchiin, Abimelekin, Abraham kiangah, Belamnou a nu sagih a tuama na koih bang achia ahia? achia.
30. Huan, aman, Hiai tuileh kei leh ahi chih na hontheihpihna dingin hiai belamnou a nu sagih ka khut akipan na lak ding ahi, achia.
31. Huaijiakin huailai mun Beer-seba ahia; huailaia a nih ua kichiam jiak un.
32. Huchiin, Beer-seba ah thu a khungta uh; huan, Abimelek a thoua, a sepaih pawl heutu Philok toh, Philistinte gamah a pai nawnta uh.
33. Huan, Abrahamin, Beer-seba ah meilah sing a suana, huaiah Toupa khantawn Pathian min a loua.Huchiin, Abraham Philistinte gamah sawtpi a om.
34. Huchiin, Abraham Philistinte gamah sawtpi a om.
