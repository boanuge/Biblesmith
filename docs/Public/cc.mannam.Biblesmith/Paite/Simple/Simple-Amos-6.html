1. Zion tanga nuam luaa omte leh samari tanga bittaka omte leh nam lianpen mi thupite, a kiang ua Israel inkote painate tung a gik hi:
2. Kalneh khua ah hoh unla, en un; huan, huai akipanin Hamath kho thupi ah hoh unla: huan, Philistia gam Gath khua ah pai suk unla: hiai gamte sangin a hoihjaw uhia? Na gam sang un a gam uh a zajaw hia?
3. Nou ni hoihlou gamlapia koih leh hiamgam tutna naihsakte aw,
4. Saiha lupnaa luma, a tutphah sau tungte ua kijiaa, belam hon laka belam noute leh bawng omte lak akipan bawng tuite nea;
5. Kaihginga thadahna lasaa, David banga amau adinga la tumna bawla;
6. Belhbu liana uain dawna, sathau hoihpena kinuhte mah; himahleh Josephte haksatna jiakin a lungkham kei uh.
7. Huchiin tua sal dia pai masate toh salin a pai ding ua, kizete nuamsak vengvung a mang ding.
8. TOUPA, sepaihte Pathianin, TOUPa PATHIAN in amah leh amah kilouin a kichiama; Jakob kisaktheihna ka kiha, a kumpipa inte ka mudaha: huaijiakin khua leh a sunga om tengteng ka mansak sin ahi.
9. Huan, hichi a honghi dinga, in khat mi sawm a om lai uleh a si ding uh.
10. Huan mi khat pangak a halpa ngeiin leng a laa, in sunga kipana a guh la khe dingin, in sungtawng pena ommi kiangah, Na kiangah kuapeuh a om lai a om uhia? a chiha, aman, Om lou, a chih leh; huan, aman, Dai sisipin! TOUPa min lah i lou ding ahi ngal keia, a chi ding a.
11. Ngai dih ua, TOUPAN lah thu a pe ngala; huan, in lian lah chim lemlum dinga, inneu leng a khang kham ial-ual ding hi.
12. Sakolte suangpi tungah a tai ding ua hia? huaiah min bawngin lei a let ding ua hia? nouten vaihawmna lah gu na suaksak ua diktatna gah singkha na suaksak uh:
13. Nou thil bangmahlou tunga kipaka, I hatnain ahi kei maw kite i lak ua? chite aw.En dih ua, Aw, Israel inkote aw, nou hondou dingin nam ka ding khesak dinga; huan Hamath khomual akipanin Arabah lui tanin nou a hon engbawl ding ua, TOUPA, sepaite Pathianin lah a chi ngala.
14. En dih ua, Aw, Israel inkote aw, nou hondou dingin nam ka ding khesak dinga; huan Hamath khomual akipanin Arabah lui tanin nou a hon engbawl ding ua, TOUPA, sepaite Pathianin lah a chi ngala.
