Titus (1/3)  →
1. Paula, Pathian sikha leh Jesu Kris sawltak, Pathian telte phatuamsak ding- ginna leh Pathian limsakna toh kithuah theihna leh
2. khantawna hinna lametna ah; huai khantawn hinna tuh Pathian zuaugen theilouhin khovel omma in achiama;
3. himahleh, amah huntakah athu ah thugenna zangin a hihlangta, huai thugenna thugenna tuh Pathian I Hondampa tupiak bangzel a kepsak ka hi.
4. Tita, vantang ginna a ka ta taktak kiangah: Pathian leh Kris Jesu I Hondampa kianga kipan hehpihna leh lemna om hen.
5. Hiai ziakin ahi huai Kret tuikulha nang kon nutsiah, thil hihzoh louhte na hihzoh theihna ding leh, thu ka hon piak banga kho tenga upate na seh theihna dingin;
6. kuapeuh gensiatbei, Ji khat nei, tate Pathian gingta, nopsak bawlluatna lam sang a gensiat loh lou, dan banglou a omlou, nei ahih peuhmah uleh.
7. Saptuam heutu tuh Pathian sum kempa ahih ziakin gensiatbei ahih ding ahi; migenhak, lunggam, uaiin khamhat, mivuakhat, punna nin a huaiham mi hiding hilou ahi;
8. mi kipahpih dan siam, hoihlam deih, lungsim diktak, midiktat, misiangthou, kidekthei mi hizaw ding ahi;
9. thuhilhna diktak lamah leng mi ahasot theihna ding leh, selhatmite siamlouh atansak theihna ding in thuhilhna bangzel thu muanhuai tuh lenkip mi ahi nawn lai ding ahi.
10. Dan banglou a om, thu genmawkmawkte leh ki khemte mi tampi a om ngal ua, zeksumte lak a mi ahi deuh tuam uhi;
11. huaite kam tuh humsak ding ahi, punna niin deih ziak a thuhilhlouh ding hilhin, mite a inkuankuan uh a tokgawp uhi.
12. Amaulaka mi amau Jawlnei khat in houh, Kret tuikulh a mitetuh zuaitheite, gamsa giloute, thadah, anhaite ahi uh, chi'n a gen hi.
13. Huai thugen tuh adik ahi. Huaziakin naktakin taihilh in, ginna a adik theihna ding un.
14. Judate tangthu khawng leh thutak nungngnatsan nak a thupiak limsakloute,
15. Misiangthoute ading in bangkim a siangthou hi; himahleh minin leh gingloute adingin siangthou himhim a omkei hi, alungsim uh a sia lehpha theihna him uh leng a nin ta hi.Pathian ithei, chi in, apang ua, himahleh, kih huaite, thumangloute, thilhoih peuhmah hihlam a Pathian deihlouh lamte ahi ua, a thilhih un a pampaih nak ngal ua.
16. Pathian ithei, chi in, apang ua, himahleh, kih huaite, thumangloute, thilhoih peuhmah hihlam a Pathian deihlouh lamte ahi ua, a thilhih un a pampaih nak ngal ua.
