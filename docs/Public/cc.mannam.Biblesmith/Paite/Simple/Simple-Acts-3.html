1. Huan, Peter leh Johan, dak kuana ah, thum hun laiin, Pathian biakin a lut dingin a hohtou ua.
2. Huan, piching kuahiam a pian tung apat khebai a honjawng ua, huai mi tuh, biakina lutte kianga khutdoh dingin, biakin kongkhak, Kilawm, a chih bul uah ni tengin a lumsak jel uhi.
3. Huaiin Peter leh Johan Pathian biakina lut ding a mu a, khut a doha.
4. Huan, Peterin amah a en gigea, Johan inleng; huan, Peterin, Hon en in, a chi a.
5. Huan, aman a lak ua banghiam muh a lamena, a en a.
6. Himahleh, Peterin, Dangka leh dangkaeng bangmah ka neikei; himahleh ka neih sun ka honpe ding, Nazaret Jesu Kris minin khein paita in, a chi a.
7. Huan, a khut taklam a lena, a kaithouta a; huan, thakhatin a khete leh a siakmit guhte a honghat pahta hi.
8. Huan, amah tuh a tawm tou a, a dinga, khein a pai pan ahi. Huan, khea paipai leh, diang diang leh, Pathian phat kawmkawmin biakin ah a lut hi.
9. Huan, khea a pai leh Pathian a phat mi tengtengin a mu ua;
10. Biakin Kongkhak Kilawm bula khutdoha tu jel pa ahi chih a thei ua; a tunga thiltung lamdang a sa mahmah ua, a buai ngial uhi.
11. Huan, aman Peter leh Johan a let laiin, mi tengtengin lamdang a sa mahmah ua, inlim Solomona a chih uah a kiang uah a hongtai khawm ua.
12. Huan, Peterin huai a muhin, mite a houpiha, Nou Israel mite aw, hiaite bangdia lamdang sa? Koumau thilhihtheihna leh Pathian ka limsak jiak ua amah ka paisak bang jen ua bangachia honen gige?
13. Abraham te, Isaak te, Jakob te Pathian, i pipute Pathianin, a Sikha Jesu a pahtawita ahi; amah na mansak ua, Pilatin khah a tupin leng a maah na ut kei uh.
14. Mi Siangthou leh Dik na deih kei ua, tualthatpa na kiang ua piak na ngen zota ua.
15. Hinna Siampa tuh na that ua; himahleh Pathianin amah misi laka pat a kaithou nawna; huai theihpihte ka hi uhi.
16. Huan, amah min gin jiakin a minin hiai mi na muh uh leh na theih uh a hihhatta uhi; ahi, amah jiaka ginna a hongomin na vek ua mitmuhin a hihdam vilvelta ahi.
17. Himahleh, unaute aw, na heutute un leng a hai jiak ua a hih bang un, na hai jiak un hiai khawng na hih uh ahi chih ka thei.
18. Himahleh, Pathianin, jawlnei tengteng kamin, a Krisin gim a thuak ding thu a theikholsak hi, huchibangin a tungsak zota ahi.
19. Huaijiakin na khelhna te uh thaimang ahih theihna dingin lungsim khek unla, kihei nawn un, huchiin Toupa kiang apat halh hun khawng a hongtung thei dinga.
20. Kris Jesu, nou adia a seh mahleng, a honsawl thei ding;
21. Ahihhangin, bangkim bawlhoih hun a hongtun masiah vanin amah a kem tadih ding ahi; huai bawlhoih ding thu tuh Pathianin leilung pian tunga kipana a jawlnei siangthou omte kama a gen khit ahi.
22. Mosi ngeiin, Toupa Pathianin keimah a honbawl bangin na unaute lakah jawlnei khat a honbawlsak ding; aman thu tengteng na kiang ua a gen peuh na ngaihkhiak sak ding uh;
23. huan, mi kuapeuh, huai jawlnei thu ngaihkhiak sak lou tuh, mi lak akipana hihmangthang ahi ding uh, a chih.
24. Ahi, Samuel om akipan jawlnei tengteng, a nung ua mite toh, thugen peuhten, tulai tanchin a genkhol sam uhi.
25. Noute jawlneite leh thukhun tate nei ve ua; Pathianin, Abraham kiangah, Na suante jiakin khovela nam chih a navak ding uh, chiin, na pipute uh kiangah achiam hi.Pathianin a Sikha a sehna, na khelhnate uh kiheisan sak dinga vualjawlsak dingin noumau kiangah a honsawl masapen ahi, a chi a.
26. Pathianin a Sikha a sehna, na khelhnate uh kiheisan sak dinga vualjawlsak dingin noumau kiangah a honsawl masapen ahi, a chi a.
