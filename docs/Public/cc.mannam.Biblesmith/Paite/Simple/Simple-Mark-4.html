1. Huan dil piau ah thu a hilh nawnta a. Huan mipi hon tampi tak a kiangah a hongkikhawm ua, huchiin, dil ah longah a tuanga, a tu a, mipi hon tengteng tuh dil piau a leitung ah a om ua.
2. Huan, gentehna thuin thil tampi a hilh a; a thuhilhna ah a kiang uah,
3. Ngai khia unla, en dih ua; buhchithehpa buhchi theh dingin a kuan a.
4. Huan, hichi a honghi a, a theh laiin a khen lampi sikah a kia a, vasaten a nezota ua.
5. Huan, a khen suangsi lei lakah a kia a; lei a sah louh jiakin a pou pah
6. Himahleh, ni a hong suahin haivuaiin a hongoma, zung a neih louh jiakin a vuai mangta mai hi.
7. Huan, akhen loulingnei lakah a kia a, loupa tuh a hongkhang sanga, a depmangta a, gah himhim a gahta kei hi.
8. Huan, a khen lei hoih ah a kia a, a pou a, a khangliana, a gahta a; a leh sawmthum khawng, a leh sawmguk khawng, aleh ja khawngin a pung chiatta uh, a chi a.
9. Huan, amahmahin, Kuapeuh jakna ding a bil neiin ja uheh, a chi a.
10. Huan, a tang om laiin a kima omten sawm leh nihte toh gentehna thu tuh a dong chian ua.
11. Huan, aman tuh akiang uah, Pathian gam thuguk jaw noute kianga piak ahi a, po lam mite a dingin bel bangkim gentehnaa gen ahi; huchiin.
12. Muh jaw a mu thei na ua, theihsiam jaw a theisiam louhna ding un; huchilou injaw a kihei nawn ding ua, ngaihdam a honghi kha ding uh, a chi a.
13. Huan, amahmahin, a kiang uah, Hiai gentehna thu na theisiam kei umaw? Huchi ahihin bangchin gentehna thu dang tengtengte na theisiam tuan ding ua?
14. Buhchithehpan thu a theh ahi.
15. Huan, lam sika thu thehnate tuh hiai bang ahi uh; thu a jak tak un Setanin a lungtang ua thu theh tuh a laksak pahpah a.
16. Huan, huchibangmahin suangsi leitunga theh tuh hiai-te ahi uh; thu a jak phet ua kipak taka pom pahte bang
17. himahleh amau ah zung himhim a neikei ua, sawt lou chik kia a om uh; huai nungin thu jiaka gimthuakna hiam sawina hiam a hongom phetin a kipukpah pah uhi.
18. Huan, mi dangte tuh loulingnei laka theh ahi uh; huaiten tuh thu a zata ua
19. himahleh khovel lungkhamnate, thil dang duhgawlnate a honglut chiangin, thu a dep a, gah louin a om nak.
20. Huan, lei hoiha tuh bel hiaite ahi uh; thu ja a, poma, a leh sawmthum khawng, a leh sawmguk khawng, a leh ja khawnga gahte ahi uh, a chi a.
21. Huan, amahmahin, a kiang uah, Khawnvak a koihnaa koih lou a, loh nuai hiam, lupna nuai hiam a koih dingin a honla ngei ua hia?
22. Thang lou dinga im bangmah a om ngei keia, langlou dinga sel bangmah a om sam kei.
23. Kuapeuhin jakna dinga bil a neih uleh ja uheh, a chi a.
24. Huan, amahmahin, a kiang uah, Na jak uh bang ahia, pilvang un; na tehna ngei mah ua piakin na om ding ua, a tam jaw bawn piakin na om ding uh.
25. Kuapeuh a nei tuh piak behlap in om ding ua, kuapeuh a neilou tuh a neih sun nangawn laksak in om ding uh, a chi a.
26. Huan, a mahmahin, Pathian gam tuh min leia chi a tuh bang ahi.
27. Jan chiangin a ihmu a, sun chiangin a thou jela; chi tuh ana pouin ana khanga, himahleh bangchi a pou a bangchi a khang ahia chih a theikei.
28. Leiin amahin gah a honsuah jela; A masapenin a kung, huan, a vui, huan, a vui ah a tang.
29. Himahleh, huai a gah a hongmin chiangin, buh lak a hun tak jiakin, kaite in atpah hi, a chi a.
30. Huan, amahmahin, Pathian gam tuh bangtoh i tehpih dia? ahihkeileh, bang gentehna thin ahia i genteh ding?
31. Ankam chi tang khat leia a tuh un, leia chi tengteng sangin neupen mahleh,
32. a tuh nung un a hongpou a, loupa tengteng sangin a honglianzota a, hiang lian taktak a honnei a, huchiin tungleng vasate tuh a lim ah a giak uhi, huchi bangmah ahi, a chi a.
33. Huan, huchi bang gentehna thu tampiin, a jak theih khomkhom un, thu a kiang uah a gen jel a.
34. Himahleh, gentehna lou in a kiang uah thu a gen ngei kei; amah nungjuite kiangah tuh a simin bangkim a hilhchian jel hi.
35. Huan, huai ni mahin, nitaklam a honghihin, aman, a kiang uah Gal khat lam i kai ding uh, a chi a.
36. Huchiin, mipite pai sanin, amah, a om ngeingei bangin, long ah a paipih pah ngal ua, Long dangte leng a kiangah a om hi.
37. Huan, huihpi thupitak a hongnung a, tuikihawtte tuh longah a hongkihawt lut juajua a, huchiin long a hong dim dek maimah a,
38. huan, amah tuh, lukham khama ihmuin, long mei lamah a oma. Huan, a phawng ua, a kiangah, Heutupa, ka manthat uh khawksa lou na hi maw? a chi ua
39. Huan, amah hongkhanglouta a, huih tuh a taia, dil kiangah leng, Dai inla, om kinken in, achia, Huan, huih tuh a tawpta a, a hong dai dide maimah.
40. Huan, aman, a kiang uah, Bangchi a hichi tela lau na hi ua oi? Ginna himhim nei nai lou na hi umaw? a chi a.Huan, lau petmahin a lau ua, Hiai mi kua ahia le, huih leh dil nangawnin a thu aman uh? a kichi khiukheu uh.
41. Huan, lau petmahin a lau ua, Hiai mi kua ahia le, huih leh dil nangawnin a thu aman uh? a kichi khiukheu uh.
