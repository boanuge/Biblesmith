1. Hiaite ahi uh huai gam kumpipa, Israel suanten a hihman ua, a gam uh Jordan lui gal, ni suahnalam gam, Arnon guam akipan Hermon tang tan, suah lampanga Araba tengteng toh a neihsak uh kumpipate:
2. Hiai Amorte kumpipa sihon, Hesbona omin Arnon guam panga om Aroer khua akipanin vai a hawma, huan, guam lai taka khopi te, Amon suante gamgi Jabok lui phain Gilead gam kimkhat tungah vai a hawma;
3. Huan, Suahlam ah Araba ah Kineroth dil tanin ahi a; huan, suahlam ah mah Beth-jeshimoth hohna lampi lamah Araba tuipi, Tuipi AI tanin ahi a; huan, simlamah Pisga tang kham nuai, phain ahia:
4. Huan, Astaroth leh Edrei khuaa om Rephaim nam omsunte lakah Basan kumpipa Ogin vai a hawma:
5. Amah Hermon tang ah te, Salka khua ah te leh Basan tengtengah Gesurte lah Maakatte gamgi pha leh Hesbon kumpipa Sihon gamgi chiang Gilead gam kimkhat phain vai a hawm hi.
6. TOUPA sikha Mosi leh Israel suanten huaite a that ua: huan, TOUPA sikha Mosiin Reuben chite, Gad chite, Manassi nam kimkhatte a gouluah ding un a hawm hi.
7. Huan, hiai gam Kumpipate ahi Josua leh Israel suanten Jordan lui gal tumlam panga a thahte uh, Lebanon guam Baal-gad akipana Seir tang sun tou Halak tang phain; huan, Josuain Israel namte a kikhen dan bang jel un huai gam a gouluah ding un a pe chiate;
8. Tang gamah te, phai gamah te, Araba ah te, tang kham gamah te, gamdai gamah te sim gamah te; huaite bel Hit mite, Amor mite, Kanan mite, Pariz mite, hiv mite, Jebus mite gamahi.
9. Hriko Kho Kumpipa, khat, Bethel kho sika Ai kho kumpipa, khat;
10. Jerusalem kho kumpipa, khat, Hebron kho kumpipa, khat;
11. Jarmuth kho kumpipa, khat, Lakis kho kumpipa, khat;
12. Eglon kho kumpipa, khat, Gezer kho kumpipa, khat;
13. Debir kho kumpipa, khat, Geder kho kumpipa, khat;
14. Horma kho kumpipa, khat, Arad kho kumpipa, khat;
15. Libna kho kumpipa, khat, Adulam kho kumpipa, khat;
16. Makeda kho kumpipa, khat, Bethel kho kumpipa, khat;
17. Tapua kho kumpipa, khat, Hepher kho kumpipa, khat;
18. Aphek kho kumpipa, khat, Lasaron kho kumpipa, khat;
19. Madon kho kumpipa, khat, Hazor kho kumpipa, khat;
20. Simron-meron kho kumpipa, khat, Aksaph kho kumpipa, khat;
21. Taanak kho kumpipa, khat, Magido kho kumpipa, khat;
22. Kedes kho kumpipa, khat, Karmel tanga Jokneam kho kumpipa, khat;
23. Dor kho zousanga Dork kho kumpipa, khat Gilgala Goimnamte kumpipa, khat.Tirza kho kumpipa, khat; kumpipa tengteng sawmthum leh khat ahi uh.
24. Tirza kho kumpipa, khat; kumpipa tengteng sawmthum leh khat ahi uh.
