1. Toupa muangte tuh Zion tang hihlin vuallouh, khantawna om gige ding bang tuh ahi.
2. Tangte Jerusalem kima a om bangin Toupa tuh a mite kimah a om jela. Tua kipana, khantawnin.
3. Gilou- salou lalchiang lah mi diktat tan tungah a kinga kei dinga; midiktatten a khut ua thulimlouhna a khoih kei uleh.
4. Toupa aw, hoih taka omte tungah hoih takin hih in, a lungtang ua diktaka omte tungah leng.A lampi kawi khawng ua pialte bel Toupan thilhihkhialte toh a pi khe ding. Israelte tungah khamuanna om hen.
5. A lampi kawi khawng ua pialte bel Toupan thilhihkhialte toh a pi khe ding. Israelte tungah khamuanna om hen.
