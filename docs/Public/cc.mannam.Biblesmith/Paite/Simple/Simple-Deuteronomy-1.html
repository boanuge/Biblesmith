Deuteronomy (1/34)  →
1. Hiai thu Jordan suah lam gamdai, Paran leh Tophel leh Laban leh Haze-roth leh Di-zahab kikal a Suph chin Arabaa Mosi in Israel mite tengteng kiang a a gen ahi.
2. Horeb tang a kipan in Kades-barnea bel Seir tang lam lampi ah ni sawm leh ni khat lam ahi.
3. Huan, kum sawmli kum, kha sawm leh kha khatna, huai kha ni khat ni in, hichi ahi a, TOUPA'N amau hilh ding a thu a piak tengteng Israel suante kiang ah Mosi in a gen a;
4. Hesbon a om Amorte kumpipa Sihon leh Edrei leh Astarot a om Basan kumpipa Og a thah nung in:
5. Jordan suah lam Moab gamah hiai dan Mosi in a genkhe panta hi.
6. TOUPA i Pathian un Horeb tang ah hon houpih a, Hiai tang ah na om sawt hunta uh:
7. Kisuan ta unla, paikhia unla, Amorte tang gamah leh a kimvel teng Araba ah khawng, tang gam te ah khawng, phai gamah te leh Lebanon ahte, luipi Euphrates tan in pai un.
8. Ngai un, gam ka hon pekhin a: na pu uh Abrahamte, Isakte, Jakobte leh amau nung a a suan sawnte uh kiang a kichiam a TOUPA'N a piak gam va luah un, chi in.
9. Huan, huai lai in na kiang uah, Kei kia in ka hon pozou kei a;
10. TOUPA na Pathian un a hon pung sak a, ngai un, tu in na tamdan uh van a aksi zah hial a hong hi ta uh.
11. TOUPA, na pi-le-pute un Pathian in tu sanga amun sang in hon pung sak henla, a hon chiam bang in hon vualzawl hen.
12. Bang chi in kei kia in na buainate uh, na puakgikte uh, na kihou theihlouhnate uh ka po zou dia?
13. Na nam lak ua mi, mi pil, theisiam mi, a dan ding theimi kitelkhia unla, na tung ua heutu ding in ka hon bawl ding, chi in a hon houpih a.
14. Huan, nou na thil gen kou a ding in a hoih mahmah, chi in no'n dawng ua.
15. Huchi in na nam heutute uh ka pom a, na nam lak ua mi, mi pil a dan ding thei mite na tung ua heutu ding in ka bawl a, sang tung a heutu, za tung a heutute, sawmnga tung a heutute, sawm tung a heutute, inntek pente toh.
16. Huan, huailai in na vaihawm mite uh, Na unaute uh kal a thu ngaihkhiak sak unla, unau leh unau kal ah leh gamdang mi, a ompih toh a kaal uah diktat tak in hawm un.
17. Vaihawmna ah deihsak tuam na neih ding uh ahi kei; mi neu leh mi lian kibang tak in na ngaihkhiak sak ding uh ahi; kuamah na lau ding uh ahi kei; vaihawmna lah Pathian a ahi ngal a: nou a ding a thu haksa luate ka kiang ah na hon tun zel ding ua, huan, huai ka ngaikhe zel ding, chi in thu ka pia.
18. Huan, huai lai in thil na hihdan ding tengteng uah thu ka hon pia hi.
19. Huan, TOUPA'N thu hon piak bang in Horeb tang a kipan in i pawt ua, Amorte tang gam lampi a gamdai zapi leh mulkimhuai tak na muh uh i kantan ua, Kadesbarnea khua I va tung ua.
20. Huan, na kiang uah, TOUPA i Pathian in a hon piak Amorte tang gam na hong tung ta ua.
21. Ngai dih ua, TOUPA na Pathian un gam a hon pekhin ta hi; hohtou unla, TOUPA na pi-le-pute uh Pathian in a na genkhitsa bang in va luahtou un; lau kei unla, lunglel sam kei un, ka hon chi a.
22. Huan, ka kiang ah na hong paivek ua, a gam khawng va enkhia in, i hohtouhna ding lampi leh i hohna ding kho tanchinte hong hilh nawn ding in i ma uah mi sawl leng, na chi ua:
23. Huai in hon kipaksak ngial a; huchi in na lak ua mi sawm leh nih, nam khat a khat zel ka tel a:
24. Huchi in a pawt ta ua, tang ah a kahtou ua, Eskol guam a va tung ua, a enkhe vek ua.
25. Huai gam a theite a lou ua, a hon puak ua, tanchin a hon tun ua; TOUPA i Pathian hon gam piak hoih petmah ahi, a chi ua.
26. Himahleh na hohtou nuam kei ua, TOUPA na Pathian uh thupiak na helsan ua:
27. Na puan innte uah na phun ua, TOUPA'N a hon huat ziak in hon hihmang ding a, Amorte khut a hon pe ding in Aigupta gam a kipan hon pikhia ahi.
28. Koi lai ah ahia i hoh touh ding? I unaute'n, mihing ei sang in a lian ua, san leng a sang zaw ua; khuate khawng a lian a, van sun in kulh a nei uh; huan, Anakte tate leng i mu a, chi in i lungtang uh a kesak ua, na chi uh.
29. Huan, ke'n na kiang uah, Amaute kihta kei unla, lau leng lau sam kei un.
30. TOUPA na Pathian na makaipa un na mitmuh ua Aigupta gam a hon hihsak tengteng bang in a hon sual pih ding a;
31. Gamdai ah leng mi'n a ta a pua TOUPA na Pathian un hiai lai mun na tun tan un lam tawn a hon puakdan na mu ding uhi, ka chi a.
32. Himahleh hiai thil ah TOUPA na Pathian uh na gingta kei uh,
33. Na puan inn na zak ding uh hon zonsak ding leh na lampi uh hon kawkmuh ding a zan a mei a, sun a meipi a na ma uh pipa.
34. Huan TOUPA'N na thugen uh a na za a, a heh a, a kichiam a,
35. Hiai suan gilou tak lak a mi khat mahmah in leng na pi-le-pute uh pe ding a ka kichiamna gam hoih petmah a mu kei ding uh,
36. Jephune tapa Kaleb kia ngallou in; aman zaw a mu ding; a va sik gam amah leh a tate ka pe ding, aman TOUPA a pomkip ziak in, chi in.
37. Noute ziak in ka tungah leng TOUPA a heh a, Nang leng huai lai ah na lut kei ding a:
38. Na nnasempa Nun tapa Josua bel a lut ding; amah hasuan in, Israelte a lutsak sin ahi.
39. Na ta neute uh, Gallak in a la mai ding uh, na chih uleh, tua asia-apha thei nailou na tate uh a lut ding ua, amau zaw ka pe ding a, a luah ding uh.
40. Nou a hihleh kihei unla, Tuipi San lam ah gamdai ah hoh un, chi in.
41. Huan, Nou TOUPA tungah i na khial ta uhi, i hohtou ding ua, TOUPA i Pathan in thu a hon piak tengteng bang in i va sual ding uh, chi in no'n dawng ua, huchi in kidouna van in na kivan chiat ua, tang a hohtouh thil neuchik ding in na ngaihtuah ua.
42. Huan, TOUPA'N ka kiang ah, Hohtou kei un, va sual sam kei un, na lak uah lah ka om ngal kei a, na melmate un a hon na hihlum kha ding uh, a kiang uah chi in, a hon chi a.
43. Huai ka hon hilh a, na pom kei ua, TOUPA thupiak na helsan zo ta ua, tang ah na hohtou teitei uh.
44. Huan, huai tang ah Amorte'n a honna sual ua, khuai mi delh bang in a hon na delh ua, Seir a kipan in Horma tan in a hon that zel ua.
45. Huan, na hong kiknawn ua, TOUPA ma ah na kap ua; himahleh TOUPA'N na aw uh a ngaikhe nuamkei a, a bil leng a hon doh nuam sam kei hi.Huchi in huai a na om sung uh ngaihtuah in Kades ah sawtpi na om uhi.
46. Huchi in huai a na om sung uh ngaihtuah in Kades ah sawtpi na om uhi.
