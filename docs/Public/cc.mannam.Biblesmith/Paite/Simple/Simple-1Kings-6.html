1. Huan, hichi ahia, Israel suante Aigupta gama kipan a pawt nung uh kum zali leh sawmgiatna kum, Israelte tunga Solomon a lal kum lina, Ziv kha, kha nihna khain Toupa in lam a pan.
2. Huan, Solomonin Toupa adinga a in lam a dunglam tong sawmguk ahia, a vailam tong sawmnih ahi, a sanlam tong sawmthum ahi.
3. Huan, huai ina mun siangthou ma lama inlim bel ain vailam tan tong sawmniha sau ahi a, a ma lamapen vailam bel tong sawm ahi.
4. Huan, in adingin tohlet neuchik vathangkalh omin a bawla.
5. Huan, a in bang kimvelin, biakin bang kimvelin leng, biakin mun bang kimvel leng huai in bang siin in chial nei a lam a;
6. Huan, a kimvel indantan a bawla: achialnuainungpen availam tong nga ahi a, a laia pen a vailam tong guk ahi a, a thumna a vailam tong sagih ahi: kalvaite bel in banga kigolh kiphamoh louin polamah a kimvelin bangah a kingakna bawl jiak ahi.
7. Huan, in, a lam lai un suang a laihna muna seksa veka bawl ahi: huchiin a lam sung un sek ging te, heipi ging te, sik vangzat ging himhim te huai in ah jak ahi kei.
8. Dantan laita pente kongpi bel huai in tklam pangah a oma: a laita ah kahna kivialin a kah tou ua, huan, a laita akipanin a thumnaah.
9. Huchiin huai in a honlama a zou a, in bel sidar inkam leh a pekin a khuh hi.
10. Huan, in chial neite a kisi vekin tong nga chiata sangin a lama: huaite in toh sidar singa peh ahi.
11. Huan, Solomon kiangah Toupa thu a hongtunga,
12. Hiai in na lam lai tak tungtang thu- ka thusehte na poma, ka vaihawmte na zuiha, ka thupiak tengteng jui dinga napom peuhmah leh, na pa David kianga ka chihsa, huai ka thu na tungah ka hihkip ding.
13. Huan, Israel suante lakah ka teng dinga, ka mi Israelte ka limsaklou kei ding, chiin.
14. Huchiin, Solomonin huai in a lama, a zouta hi.
15. In sung lam bang dingin sidar singpek a zanga; a pialkhang akipan tung lam bang phain a sung lamah sing a belh vek hi: a pialkhang dingin meilah singpek a phah beh a.
16. Huan, in phaitam lam tong sawmnih pen pialkhang akipana a bangte tanin sidar singpek vekin a bawl a: biakna mun, mun siangthoupen adingin leng a sung lamah a zang hi.
17. Huan, in biakna mun china biakin pen a dung lam tong sawmli ahi.
18. Huan, in sung lamah a pakmum leh a pak palh limgelh kilawm sidar sing a oma; a vekin sidar sing vek ahia; suang himhim a dawk kei.
19. Huan, Toupa thukhunna bawm koihna dingin huai in ah a sunga biakna lamah mun a bawl hi.
20. Huai, haui biakna mun sung bel a dung lam tong sawmnih, a vai lam tong sawmnih, a san lam tong sawmnih ahia, huai tuh dangkaeng hul ngenin a luana; huan, sidar maitam leng a luana.
21. Huchiin, Solomonin huai in sung dangkaeng hul ngenin a luana: huan, biakna mun ma lam bel dangkaeng khainiangin a daikaih; huai tuh dangkaengin a luan hi.
22. Huan, in tuh a veka a zoh phain a pumpiin dangkaeng in a luan tawmtawm hi: biakna mun maitam pumpi leng huai dangkaeng in a luan.
23. Huan, biakna munah tong sawm tuaktuaka sang, cherub nih oliv singin a bawl a.
24. Huan, cherub kha khat pen tong nga ahi a, cherub kha langkhat pen tong nga mah ahi: kha khat mong akipan a kha langkhat mong kikal tong sawm ahi.
25. Cherub khat leng tong sawm ahia: Cherub te a kikim geiha a kibang geih uh.
26. Cherub khat san lam tong sawm ahia, Cherub khat leng huai toh kikim mah ahi.
27. Huan, Cherub te in sunggil jaw ah a koih a; huan, cherubte kha a kijak a, khat kha in bang langkhat lam ava khoih a, huan, cherub khat pen kha in leng bang langkhat a khoih sama; huai in lai takah a kha u leh a kha uh a kikoih uh.
28. Huan, huai cherub te dangkaeng in a luan a.
29. Huan, in bang tengteng a kimvel in cherub te, tum sing leh pak palh limte gelh kilawm in sunglam leh polamah a bawl.
30. Huan, a in pialkhang sunglam leh polamah dangkaeng in a luan hi.
31. Huan, biakna mun lutna dingin oliv sing kongkhak a bawla: kongpi lutung leh kongpi biangte a bang seh nga suah sehkhat chia ahi.
32. Huchiin, oliv singin kongkhak nih a bawla; huaiah cherub te, tum sing leh pak palh lim te gelh kilawm in a bawla, huai tuh dangkaengin a luana; huan, cherub te leh tum sing te dangkaeng a nuh hi.
33. Huan, biakin lutna ding in a bang seh li suah seh khat chia in oliv kongpi khuam a bawl laia;
34. Meilah sing kongkhak nih toh; kongkhak khata pek nih bel khep theih ahia, kongkhak khata pek nih leng khep theih ahi.
35. Huaite ah cherub te, tum sing leh pak palh limte a gelh ua: huai gelh kilawm bel mam takin dangkaeng in a luan hi.
36. Biak in huangsung intual sung gil zopen suang sek chial thum leh, sidar singpek thuah khat in a bawl hi.
37. A kum lina, Ziv kha in toupa in suangphum lep ahi.Huan, kum sawm leh kum khatna, bul kha, kha giatna in huai in zuih ding bangbang a, bangkim mansa vek ahi hi. Huchiin kum sagih a lam.
38. Huan, kum sawm leh kum khatna, bul kha, kha giatna in huai in zuih ding bangbang a, bangkim mansa vek ahi hi. Huchiin kum sagih a lam.
