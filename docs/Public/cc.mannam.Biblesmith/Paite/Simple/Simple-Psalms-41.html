1. Gentheite ngaihtuah mi a hampha hi, mangbat niin Toupan amah a humbit ding hi.
2. Toupan amah humbitin a hihhing dinga, leiah vualjawl in a om ding; amah douten ututa a bawl dingin pe ken.
3. Toupan a gimna lupna munah a hihhat dinga; nang a chi a nat lai in a lupna na bawlsak vek jel hi.
4. Toupa aw, honhehpih inla, ka hinna hihdam in, na tungah thil ka hihkhial ngal a, ka chi a.
5. Hondouten, chikchiang in a siin, a min a mang de aw! Chiin, hon gensia uh.
6. Huan a lak ua kuahiamin a hongveh laiin, thu bangmahlou maimai a gen jela; a lungtang in thulimlouhna a lakhawm naka; a pawt khiak takin mi a hilh jel hi.
7. Honhote tengteng in hon gensiain a kihuau ua: honhihsiatna ding uh a ngaihtuah ua.
8. Natna hoihlou takin a man kipta, tu-a lum a thou nawn ta kei ding a chi ua.
9. A hi, ka lawm kithuahpih theih, ka muantak, ka tanghou nekpih ngeiin a khetul ka tungah a likta hi.
10. Hiamhleh, Toupa aw, nang honhehpih inla, amau ka thuk theihna dingin honkai thou nawnin.
11. Hiaiah ka tungah na kipak chih ka thei hi, hondou in a honzoh louh jiakin.
12. Kei jaw lungsim hoihtak ka putna ah nangman non lenchinten a, Na maah khantawn adingin non koih hi.Toupa, Israel Pathian, Khantawn akipan khantawn pha in phat in om hen, Amen, Amen.
13. Toupa, Israel Pathian, Khantawn akipan khantawn pha in phat in om hen, Amen, Amen.
