1. Huan TOUPAN Mosi kiangah, Pharo tung leh, Aigupta gam tungah gawtna khat ka tun lai ding; huai khit chiangin hiai akipanin a hon paisak ding; a honpai sak chiang in, hiaia kipanin a honsawn khe siang sipsip petmah ding hi.
2. Tuin mite bil ah thugen inla, pasal cihin a inveng lakah ngen hen, numei chih in a inveng lakah ngen hen, dangka suangmantamte leh dangkaeng suanmantamte, a chi a.
3. Huchiin TOUPAN Aiguptate mitmuhin mite deihsakna a pia. Huailouin Aigupta gamah leh, Pharo sikhate mitmuh in leh, mite mitmuhin huai mi Mosi a thupi mahmah hi
4. Huan, Mosiin, Hichibangin TOUPAN a chi, Jankim khawng chiangin Aigupta gamlaiah kapai khe ding;
5. Huchiin Aigupta gamah ta masa tengteng, a laltutphaha tu Pharo ta masapen a kipan, khawl liaha om sikhanu ta masapen tanpha hialin si ding uh; gan ta masa tengteng toh.
6. Aigupta gam tengtengah kahna thupi a om dinga, huchibang om ngei nailou, huchibang om ngei nawn lou ding.
7. Himahleh Israel suante laka kuamah siatna dingin uiin a lei a tangsak kei ding, mi hiam gan hiam siatna ding: Huchia TOUPAN Aiguptate leh Israel a khentuam dan na theih theihna ding un.
8. Huan hiai na sikhate tengteng ka kiangah a hongsuk ding ua, ka kiangah a khupboh ding ua, Pawt khia inla, nang honjui mite tengteng leng: huan huai khit chiangin ka pai khe ding, chiin, a chi a.
9. Huan TOUPAN Mosi kiangah, Ka thillamdangte Aigupta gama a khanna dingin Pharoin a honngaikhe kei ding, a chi a.Huan Mosi leh Aronin Pharo maah hiai thillamdang tengteng a bawlta ua: huchiin TOUPAN Pharo lungtang a khauhsaka, gam akipanin Israel suante a paisak nuamta kei hi.
10. Huan Mosi leh Aronin Pharo maah hiai thillamdang tengteng a bawlta ua: huchiin TOUPAN Pharo lungtang a khauhsaka, gam akipanin Israel suante a paisak nuamta kei hi.
