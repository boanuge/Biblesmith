1. Huan Bazaleel in singgi sing in bawm a bawl a: tong nih leh a kim a dung lam ahi a, huan tong khat leh a kim a vai lam ahi, tong khat leh a kim a san lam:
2. Huan dangka-eng tak in a sunglam leh a po lam a luan a, dangka-eng lal lukhu akim-avel in a bawlsak hi.
3. Huan dangka-eng sikkual li a sun sak a, a ning lite a bulh ding in: a pang khat a sikkual nih, a pang khat lam ah sikkual nih.
4. Huan singgi sing in chiang a bawl a huai dangka-eng in a luan a.
5. Huchi in chiangte bawm pang a sikkual sung ah, bawm puakna ding in a thun lut.
6. Huan dangka-eng tak in chitna tutphah a bawl: tong nih leh a kim a dung lam ahi a, tong khat leh a kim a vai lam ahi.
7. Dangka-eng in cherub khiak in huaite a bawl a, chitna tutphah mong nihte tuak ah;
8. Hiai lam pang mong ah cherub khat, huai lam pang mong ah cherub dang: chitna tutphah a kipan in a mong lang nihte ah cherubte a bawl hi.
9. Huan cherubte'n tunglam ah a khat uh a zak ua, chitna tutphah tunglam ah, a mai uh kingattuah in, a kha un a khuh ua; chitna tutphah lam ah cerubte mai a om hi.
10. Huchiin singgi sing in dohkan a bawl a: tong nih a dung lam ahi a, tong khat a vai lam, tong khat leh a kim a san lam:
11. Dangka-eng tak in a luan a, akim-avel in dangka-eng lal lukhu a bawl sak hi.
12. Huai lou in huai ah akim-avel in khutpek chia in a gei a bawl: akim-avel in huai ah a gei ding in dangka-eng lal lukhu a bawl hi.
13. Huchi in dangka-eng sikkual li a sung a, huan huai a a khe lite a om a ning lite tungah sikkualte a bulh.
14. A gei chin ah, dohkan puakna ding chiangte a ding in mun, sikkualte a om uhi.
15. Huan singgi sing in chiangte a bawl a, dohkan puakna ding in, dangka-eng in a luan hi.
16. Huan dohkan tung a om tui-um-belsuante a bawl a, a kuangte leh, a sikkeute leh, a noubemte leh, a khuhna ding a singte, dangka-eng tak in a bawl hi.
17. Huchi in khawnvak koihna dangka-eng tak in a bawl: nnasep hel in khawnvak koihna a bawl hi; akungpi, ahiang, a noubemte, a mumte, a pakte, kibang chiat in;
18. Huai a pangte a kipan in hiang guk a kakhia a; khanvak koihna hiang thum a pang khat a kipan in huan, a pang dang a kipan in khawnvak koihna hiang thum:
19. Noubem thum makha gah bang a bawl hiang khat ah, a mum leh a pak; huan noubem thum makha gah bang a bawl hiang dang ah, a mum leh a pak: huchi in hiang gukte teng ah khawnvak koihna a kipan in.
20. Huan khawnvak koihna makha gah bang a bawl noubem li a om, a mumte leh a pakte;
21. Huan kibang in hiang nihte dang ah mum khat, kibang in hiang nihte dang ah mum khat, huan kibang in hiang nihte dang ah mum khat, hiang gukte bang zel in a kakhia uhi.
22. A mumte uh leh a hingte kibang ahi ua: a vek un dangka-eng tak a nnasep hel ahi.
23. Huan a khawnvak sagihte, a meihalnate leh a meivu kuangte, dangka-eng tak in a bawl hi.
24. Dangka-eng tak talent khat in a bawl a, tui-um-belsuan teng toh.
25. Huan gimlim maitam singgi sing in a bwla: a dung lam tong khat ahi, a vai lam tong khat; kiptak a bawl ahi a; a san lam tong nih ahi; huai a a kibang vek uh.
26. Huchi in dangka-eng tak in a luan a, a tung leh akim-avel in a pangte, a kite: huai lou leng akim-avel in in dangka-eng lal lukhu a bawl sak hi.
27. Huan huai lai a lal lukhu nuai ah dangka-eng in sikkual nih a bawl sak a, a ning nihte kiang ah, a sik nihte tungah, a puakna ding chiangte omna ding in.
28. Huchi in singgi sing in chiangte a bawl a, huaite dangka-eng in a luan hi.Huchi in nilhtheih thau siangthou, gimlim namtui siangthou, damdawi mi hih bang in, a bawl.
29. Huchi in nilhtheih thau siangthou, gimlim namtui siangthou, damdawi mi hih bang in, a bawl.
