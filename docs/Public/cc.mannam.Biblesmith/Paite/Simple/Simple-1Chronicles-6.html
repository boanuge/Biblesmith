1. Levi Tapate; Gersom, Kohath, Merari.
2. Huan Kohath tapate; Amaram, Izhar, Hebron, Uzziel.
3. Huan Amram tate; Aron, Mosi, Miriam. Huan Aron tapate; Nadab leh Abihu, Eleazar leh Ithamar.
4. Eleazarin Phineha a suanga, Phinehain Abisua a suanga;
5. Huan Abisuain Bukki a suanga, huan Bukkiin Uzzi a suanga;
6. Huan Uzziin Zerahia a suanga, Zerahiain Meraioth a suanga;
7. Meraiothin Amaria a suanga, Amariain Ahitub a suanga;
8. Ahitubin Zadok a suanga, Zadokin Ahimaaz a suanga;
9. Huan Ahimaazin Azaria a suanga, Azariain Johanan a suanga;
10. Huan Johanan in Azaria a suanga, (amah Jerusalema Solomonin a lam in siampu nasep sempa ahi: )
11. Huan Azariain Amaria a suanga, Amariain Ahitub a suanga;
12. Huan Ahitubin Zadok a suanga, Zadokin Sallum a suanga;
13. Huan Sallumin Hilkia a suanga, Hilkiain Azaria a suanga;
14. Huan Azariain Serai a suanga, Seraiin Jahozadak a suanga;
15. Huan Jahozadak saltanna lamah a pai hi, TOUPAN Nebukadnezzar khut a Juda leh Jerusalem a pi mang laiin.
16. Levi tapate; Gersom, Kahath, leh Merari.
17. Huan hiaite Gersom tapate minte ahi uh; Libni leh Simei.
18. Huan Kohath tapate Amram, Ezhar, Hebron, Uzziel ahi uh.
19. Merari tapate; Mahli leh Musi. Huan hiaite a pipute uh inkote dungjuiin Levite inkuante ahi uh.
20. Gersom akipan; Libni a tapa, Jahath a tapa, Zimmah a tapa;
21. Joa a tapa, Iddo a tapa, Zera a tapa, Jeatherai a tapa.
22. Kohath tapate; Amminadab a tapa, Korah a tapa, Assir a tapa;
23. Elkan a tapa, Ebiasaph a tapa, Assir a tapa;
24. Tahath a tapa, Uriel a tapa, Uzzia a tapa, Shaul a tapa.
25. Huan Elkana tapate; Amasai, Ahimoth,
26. Elkan a hihleh: Elkan tapate; Zophai a tapa, Nahath a tapa;
27. Eliab a tapa, Jeroham a tapa, Elkan a tapa.
28. Huan Samuel tapate; ta masapen Joel, a zom Abija.
29. Merari tapate; Mahli, Libni a tapa, Simei a tapa, Uzza a tapa;
30. Sime a tapa, Haggi a tapa, Asai a tapa.
31. Huan hiaite Davidin thukhunna bawm a khol nunga, TOUPA in sunga la nasepna tunga heutua a koihte ahi uh.
32. Huan Jerusalema Solomin TOUPA in a lam khit masiah, kihoupihna puanin biakbuk maah lain na a sem uhi: huan a thupiak bang jel un a nasepna uh a hih uhi.
33. Huan hiaite a ngakte leh a tapate ahi uhi. Kohath mite tapate ahihleh: Heman lasami, Joel tapa, Samuel tapa;
34. Elkan tapa, Jeroham tapa, Eliel tapa, Toa tapa;
35. Zuph tapa, Elkan tapa, Mahath tapa, Amasai tapa;
36. Elkan tapa, Joel tapa, Azaria tapa, Zephania tapa;
37. Tahath tapa, Assir tapa, Ebiasaph tapa, Korah tapa;
38. Ezhar tapa, Kohath tapa, Levi tapa, Israel tapa.
39. Huan a unaupa Asaph, a taklam panga dingpa, Asaph Berekia tapa mahmah, Sime tapa;
40. Mikael tapa, Bassei tapa, Malkija tapa;
41. Ethni tapa, Zera tapa, Adai tapa;
42. Ethan tapa, Zimma tapa, Simei tapa,
43. Jahath tapa, Gersom tapa, Levi tapa,
44. Huan a khut veilam ah a unaute uh Merari tapate: Etham Kisi tapa, Abdi Malluk tapa;
45. Hasabia tapa, Amazia tapa, Hilkia tapa;
46. Amazi tapa, Hani tapa, Semer tapa;
47. Mahli tapa, Musi tapa, Merari tapa, Levi tapa.
48. Huan a unaute uh Levi mite Pathian in biakbuka nasepna tengteng adinga seh ahi uhi.
49. Himahleh Aron leh a tapaten halmang maitam tungah, gimlim maitam tungah, mun siangthou pena nasepna tengteng adingin a lan ua, Pathian sikha Mosiin thupiak tengteng dungjuia, Israel adingin tatna bawl dingin.
50. Huan hiaite Aron tapate ahi uh; Eleazar tapa, Phineha a tapa, Abisua a tapa;
51. Bukki a tapa, Uzzi a tapa, Zerahia a tapa;
52. Meraioth a tapa, Amaria a tapa, Ahitub a tapa:
53. Zadok a tapa, Ahimaaz a tapa.
54. Huchiin hiaite a gamgite ua a puaninkaihnate uh dungjuia a tenna uh ahi: Aron tapate kiangah, Kohath mite inkotea, amaua lah inmun masapen ahi ngala.
55. Amau kiangah Juda gamah Hebron leh, a kim a vela a huapkhakte a pia uhi;
56. Himahleh huaia khopi munte leh khotate, Jephunne tapa Kaleb kiangah a pia uh.
57. Huan Aron tapate kiangah kihumbitna khopite a pia uh, Hebron; Libna leng a huapkhakte toh, Jattir, Estemo a huapkhakte toh;
58. Huan Hilen a huapkhakte toh, Debir a huapkhakte toh;
59. Huan Asan a huapkhakte toh, Bethsemes a huapkhakte toh:
60. Huan Benjamin nam akipan; Geba a huapkhakte toh, Allemeth a huapkhakte toh, Anathoth a huapkhakte toh. A inkuante tengtenga a khopite tengteng uh khopi sawm le thum ahi hi.
61. Huan Kohath tapa omlaite kiangah aisana piak ahia, huai nam inkuan akipan, huai nam kimkhat akipan, Manassi kimkhat, khopi sawmte.
62. Huan Gersom tapate ahihleh, a inkuante uh dungjuiin, Isakar nam akipan, Aser nam akipan, Naphtali nam akipan, Basana Manassi nam akipan, khopi sawmlethum.
63. Merari tapate kiangah aisana piak ahi, a inkote uh dungjuiin, Reuben nam akipan, Gad nam akipan, Zebulun nam akipan, khopi sawm le nihte.
64. Huan Israel taten Levi mite kiangah khopite a huapkhakte uh toh a pia uhi.
65. Huan Juda tate nam akipan, Simeon tapa nam akipan, Benjamin tate nam akipan, hiai khopi minlohte, aisanin a pia uhi.
66. Huan Kohath tapa inkote laka khenkhatten Ephraim nam akipan amau gamgi khopite a nei uhi.
67. Huan amau kiangah bitna khopite a pia uh, Ephraim singtang gama Sekem a huapkhakte toh; Gezer leng a huapkhakte toh;
68. Huan Jokmeam a huapkhakte toh, Beth-horon a huapkhakte toh;
69. Huan Aijalon a huapkhakte toh, Gath-rimmon a huapkhakte toh;
70. Huan Manassi nam kimkhat akipan; Aner a huapkhakte toh; Bileam a huapkhakte toh, Kohath tapate inko laka omlaite din.
71. Gersom tapa kiangah, Manassi nam kimkhat inkuan akipana piak ahi, Basana Golan a huapkhakte toh, Astaroth a huapkhakte toh:
72. Isakar nam akipanin; Kedesh a huapkhakte toh, Daberath a huapkhakte toh;
73. Huan Ramoth, a huapkhakte toh, Anem a huapkhakte toh:
74. Huan Aser nam akipanin; Masal a huapkhakte toh, Abdon a huapkhakte toh;
75. Huan Hukok a huapkhakte toh, Rehob a huapkhakte toh:
76. Huan Naphtali nam akipanin; Galilia Kedesh a huapkhakte toh, Hammon a huapkhakte toh, Kiriathaim a huapkhakte toh.
77. Levi mite omlaite kiangah, Merari tapate, Zebulun nam akipana piak ahi, Rimmono a huapkhakte toh, Tabor a huapkhakte toh:
78. Huan Jeriko a Jordan khen, Jordan suahlam pang, Reuben nam akipana, amaute piak ahi, gamdaia Bezer a huapkhakte toh, Jahzah a huapkhakte toh,
79. Huan Kedemoth a huapkhkate toh, Mephaath a huapkhakte toh:
80. Huan Gad nam akipanin; Gilead a Ramoth a huapkhakte toh, Mahanaim a huapkhakte toh,Huan Hesbon a huapkhakte toh, Jazer a huapkhakte toh.
81. Huan Hesbon a huapkhakte toh, Jazer a huapkhakte toh.
