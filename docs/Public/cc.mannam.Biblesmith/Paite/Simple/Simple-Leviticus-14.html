1. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
2. Hiai phak siansak ni a hihdan ding ahi ding: siampu kiang a pi ding ahi:
3. Huan, siampu dainawl ah a paikhe ding a; huan, siampu in a en ding a, huan, ngai dih, phak pen a phakna hii a dam leh
4. Siampu in a kisian sak ding mi tuh vasa hing siangthou nih leh sidar sing leh khauzang san leh husop tawisak ding in thu a pe ding:
5. Huan, siampu in vasa khat pen leibel ah lui tui tungah goh ding in thu a pe ding a:
6. Vasa hing pen, sidar singte, khauzang sante, husopte, vasa hing toh a tawi ding a, luitui tung a vasa gohsa sisan ah a diah vek ding a:
7. Huan, phakna lak a kisiangsak ding mi sagih vei a theh ding a, a siangthou chih a phuang ding, huan, vasa hing pen gamlak ah a khah ding hi.
8. Huan, a kisiangsak ding mi in a puante a sawp ding a, a kimet mai vek ding a, a kisil ding ahi; huchi in a hong siangthou ding: huan, huai khit chiang in giah phual ah a hoh ding a, himahleh a puan inn po lam ah ni sagih a om ding ahi.
9. Huan, hichi ahi ding a, a ni sagih ni in a lu sam tengteng leh a khamul leh a mit khu multe a met mai ding a, a mul tengteng a met mai ding: huan, a puante a sawp ding a, a kisil ding ahi: huchi in a hong siangthou ta ding.
10. Huan, ni giat ni in belamnou a pa nih gensiatbei leh belamnou a nu khat gensiatbei, kum khat a upa leh tangbuang thillat ding in tangbuang nel mahmah epha mun sawm a khen mun thum sathau a mek leh sathau log khat a tawi ding ahi.
11. Huan, a siang sakpa siampu in kisiang sak ding mi leh huai thil kihoupihna kongpi bul ah TOUPA ma ah a koih ding:
12. Huan, siampu in belamnou pa khat pen sathau log khat toh a la ding a, tatlek thoihna ding in a lan ding a, TOUPA ma a vei thillat ding in a vei ding ahi.
13. Huan, khelh thoihna leh haltum thillat a goh zelna mun uh, mun siangthou ah belamnou pa pen a gou ding ahi: khelh thoihna siampu a tuam ahi bangmah in tatlek thoihna leng siampu a tuam ahi ngal a: thil siangthou mahmah ahi hi:
14. Huan, siampu in tatlek thoihna sisan a la ding a, kisiang sak ding mi bil tak lam mongte, a khut tak lam khutpite, a tak lam khepite siampu in a nuh ding a;
15. Huan, sathau log khat a tuh siampu in a la ding a, amah khutpek vei lam ah a sung ding a;
16. Siampu in a khut vei lam a sathau ah a khut tak lam zung a diah ding a, sathau in TOUPA ma ah a khut zung in sagih vei a theh ding
17. Huan, a khut a sathau om laitengteng in kisiang sak ding mi bil tak lam dawn bang, a khut tak lam khutpi bang, a khe tak lam khepi bang ah tatlekna kithoihna sisan tungah a nuh ding ahi:
18. Huan, siampu in a khut a om laitengteng in kisiang sak ding mi lu a nuh ding a; huan, siampu in TOUPA ma ah kilepna a bawl sak ding ahi.
19. Huan, siampu in khelh thoihna a lan ding a, a siangthou louhna ziak a kisiang sak ding mi pen kilepna a bawl sak ding a; huan, huai khit in haltum thillat a gou ding;
20. Huan, siampu in haltum thillat leh tangbuang thillat maitam ah a lan ding a, siampu in kilepna a bawl sak ding hi.
21. Huan, mi genthei a hih a, huchi bang a chitkei leh, belamnou pa khat tatlek thoihna, kilepna bawl sakna ding a vei ding leh tangbuang thillat ding a tangbuang nel mahmah epha mun sawm a khen mun khat, sathau a zut, sathau log khat toh a tawi dia;
22. Huan, vakhu kop khat hiam, vapal nou kop khat hiam, a neih zoh zaw lamlam; khat pen khelh thoihna, khat pen haltum thillat ding ahi ding.
23. Ni giat ni in a kisian sakna ding in siampu kiang ah kihoupihna puan inn kongpi bul ah TOUPA ma ah a hon tawi ding ahi.
24. Huchi in siampu tatlek thoihna belamnou leh sathau log khat a la ding a, huan, vei thillat ding in TOUPA ma ah siampu in a vei ding ahi;
25. Huan, tatlek thoihna belamnou pen a gou ding a; siampu in tatlek thoihna sisan a la ding a, kisiang sak ding mi bil tak lam dawn bang, a khut tak lam khutpi bang, a khe tak lam khepi bang a nuh ding hi:
26. Huan, siampu in sathau amah khutpek vei lam ah a sung ding a;
27. Huan, siampu in a khut vei lam a sathau khenkhat a khut tak lam in TOUPA ma ah sagih vei a theh ding;
28. Huan, siampu in a khut a sathau in kisiang sak ding mi bil tak lam dawn bang, a khut tak lam khutpi bang, a khe tak lam khepi bang ah tatlek kithoihna sisan tung tak ah a nuh ding hi:
29. Huan, siampu khut a sathau om laiteng in TOUPA ma a kilepna bawl sak ding in kisiang sak ding mi lu a nuh ding ahi.
30. Huan, vakhu hiam, vapal nou hiam, a neih zoh lamlam khat a lan ding a;
31. A neih zoh lamlam, khelh thoihna ding in khat, haltum thillat ding in khat, tangbuang thillat toh: huan, siampu in kisiang sak ding mi pen TOUPA ma ah kilemna a bawl sak ding hi.
32. Hiai bel phak hii vei, kisian sakna dan bang a chinloute a dia dan ahi, chi in.
33. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi leh Aron a houpih a,
34. Luah ding a ka hon piak Kanan gam na lut chiang un, na luah ding uh gam koi inn pen ah leng phak hii ka koih leh,
35. Huai in neitu in, Hiai inn ah hii hileh kilawm tak om leh kilawm ka sa, chi in siampu a hong hilh ding ahi:
36. Huan, siampu hii en ding a a lut ma in inn sung a thil om tengteng a buah a suah louhna ding in inn pen a hihvuak ding in siampu in thu a pe ding a; huai khit in siampu in a en ding in a lut ding hi.
37. Huan, hii a en ding a, huan, ngai dih, hii in a hing lam sim hiam a san lam sim hiam a baang a nekkuak a, baang sang a kiam a a kimuh leh,
38. Siampu pen inn a kipan kongpi ah a pawt ding a, ni sagih in a khak ding:
39. Huan, ni sagih ni in siampu in a va en nawn ding a, huan, ngai dih, hii baang a a na zamzak leh hi omna suang tak lakkhiak a,
40. Dainawl a nin paihna mun a paih ding in siampu in thu a pe ding:
41. huan, inn sung lam a kiat kimvel sak ding a, huan, suang zutbitna a kiatkhiak uh pen dainawl mun nin ah a sung ding uhi:
42. Huan, suang dang a la ding ua, huai suangte mun ah a koih ding ua; huan, suang zutbitna dang a la ding a, inn a hu ding hi.
43. Huan, suang a lakkhiak a inn a kiat a a huk khit nung a hii inn a a hong dawk nawn leh, siampu a va lut ding a, va en nawn ding hi;
44. Huan, ngai dih, hii inn a a na zamzak leh huai inn a phak a kip chi ahi a, a nin ahi
45. Huai inn a phel dia, a suang khawng, a sing khawng, a suang zutbitna tengteng leng; huan, dainawl ah mun nin ah a nawnkhiak vek ding ahi.
46. Kuapeuh huai inn khak sung himhim a lut taphot nitak lam tan in a nin lai ding uhi.
47. Huan, kuapeuh huai inn a lum taphot in a puante uh a sawp ding uh. Huan, kuapeuh huai inn a ann ne taphot in a puante uh a sawp ding uh ahi.
48. Huan, siampu a lut a, a et kimvel a, huan, ngai dih, huai in huk hoih a hih nung a hii a zamzak keileh, inn a siangthou chih siampu in a phuang ding, hii a kikhinmang tak ziak in.
49. Huan, inn sian sak na ding in vasa nih leh sidar sing leh khau zang san leh husop a tawi ding a;
50. Huan, vasa khat pen lei bel ah lui tui tungah a gou ding a;
51. Huan, Sidar sing leh husop leh khau san leh vasa hing pen a la ding a, vasa gohsa sisan ah leh lui tui ah a diah ding hi.
52. Huan, inn sagih vei a theh ding: huan, inn, vasa sisan bang, khau san bang in a siang sak ding:
53. Vasa hing pen bel khua a kipan in gamlak ah a lengkhe sak ding ahi: huchi in a hong siangthou ta ding hi.
54. Hiai pen phak hii chichih leh meimasungtum tungthu a dan ahi;
55. Puan a phak om leh inn ate a dia van,
56. Bawk bang, khem bang, pol bang a dia van,A sianthou louhna mun leh a sianthouna mun kawkmuh na ding in hiai phak tungthu a dan ahi, chi in.
57. A sianthou louhna mun leh a sianthouna mun kawkmuh na ding in hiai phak tungthu a dan ahi, chi in.
