1. Huchi in, Israelte vualzawl sak TOUPA deihlam ahi chih Balaam in a theih in ni dang a a hih bang in khimhaina lam hih ding a hoh lou in gamdai lam a zuan zo ta.
2. Huan, Balaam a vandak a, Israel mite a nam dan uh dungzui zel a om a mu a; huan, Pathian kha a tungah a hong tung hi
3. Huan, hiai thu a gen a, Beor tapa Balaam vak:
4. Pathian thute za a, Bangkim hihthei a kilakna mu a khup a puk mit hong vak ta in,
5. Jakobte aw, na puan innte uh hoih hina mah e, Israelte aw, na biakbukte uh!
6. Guam bang in a kiphah zeizoi a, lui dung a huante bang in, TOUPA suan tosaw bang in, lui dung a sidar singte bang ahi uh.
7. A baltin a kipan in tui a luangkhe ding a, a chi tui tampi ahte a om ding a, a kumpipa Agag sang in a thupi ding.
8. Aigupta a kipan Pathian in a pikhia a, gam bawngtal tha bang a nei uh, nam dang a melma uh a nekhin ua, guhte uh a hihzan vek ding uh, a thal un a kap pailet ding uh.
9. A kun ua, humpinelkai bang in a kual ua, humpinelkai bang mai in; kuan a tokthou dia? Hon vualzawl peuhmahte vualzawl in om uhenla, hon hamsiat peuhmahte hamsiat in om uhen, a chi a.
10. Huchi in Balaam tungah Balak a heh mahmah a: Balak in Balaam kiang ah Ka melmate hamsiat ding in ka hon sam a, tu in, thumvei tak amau na vualzawl ta,
11. Huaiziak in na omna ah paimang in: thupi tak a hon tawisang ding ka hoh chih na hi a; himahleh, ngai in, TOUPA'N nang kon tawisang a dal ahi, a chi a.
12. Huan, Balaam in Balak kiang ah, Na mi sawlte kiang ah leng ka gen him kei maw.
13. Balak in a inn dangka leh dangka-eng a dim in hon pe sin mahleh, a hoihlam hiam, a selam hiam keimah lungsim a suak mawng in TOUPA thu khen lam ah bangmah ka hih theikei: TOUPA'N a gen peuhmah ka gen ding, chi in?
14. Ngai in, ka mite kiang ah ka pai ta ding a, hong in, hiai mite'n tunung chiang a na mite tung a a hihdan ding ka hoh hilh ding, a chi a.
15. Huan, hiai thu a gen a. Beor tapa Balaam mit vak:
16. Pathian thute za a, Tungnungpen theihna a, Bangkim hihthei a kilakna mu, a khup a puk a mit hong vak ta in;
17. Amah ka mu a, tua a om bang hilou ka en a, nai chik a bang hilou in: Jakob a kipan in aksi a hong suakkhe ding a, Israel a kipan in lalchiang a hong dingkhe ding a, Moab gam tawn in a sun pailet ding a, buaina ta tengteng a hihniam ding hi.
18. A melma Edom leh Seirte a tang ta ding a; Israel tan a hoih sun teng.
19. Jakob a kipan in kua hiam a lal ding a, khua a omsunte a hihmang ding, a chi, a chi a.
20. Huan, Amalek lam a nga a, thu a gen a, Amalek bel nam bul pen ahi na a, a dawn lam manthatna ahi, a chi a.
21. Huan, Ken lam a nga a, thu a gen a, Na omna mun zaw a kip a, na bu suangpi a seh ahi.
22. Assurte sal a hon pimang uh tan in, a chi a.
23. Huan, thu a gen nawn a, Pathian in hichi bang a a hih in kua a hing thei dia leh?
24. Himahleh Kittim gam a kipan in longte a hong tung ding a, Assur a hihgim ding ua, Eber a hihgim ding ua, amah leng a mangthang ding, a chi a.Huchi in Balaam a thou a, a kholam ah a paita: Balak leng a pai sam hi.
25. Huchi in Balaam a thou a, a kholam ah a paita: Balak leng a pai sam hi.
