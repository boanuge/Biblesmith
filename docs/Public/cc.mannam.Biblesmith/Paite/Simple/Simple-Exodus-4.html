1. Huchiin, Mosi in a dawnga, a hina a, ngai in, a hon um kei ding ua, ka aw leng a ngai khe sam kei ding uh: Toupa na kiangah a kilak kei, a chih ding jiak un, achia.
2. Huan, Toupan a kiangah, na khut a bang ahia? Achia. Aman, chiang, achi.
3. Huan, aman, leiah khia in, achia. Huchiin, leiah a khia a, gulpi a hong suaka; huan, Mosi ama a kipan in a taita hi.
4. Huan, Toupa Mosi kiangah, na khut zan inla, gul a mei in la in, achia: (a khut a zana, gul amei in a manta a, a khut ah chiang a hong suak nawn leuleu hi):
5. Hichia apipute uh Toupa Pathian, Abraham Pathian, Isaak Pathian leh Jakob Pathian, na kiangah a kilak chih a up na ding un, achi hi.
6. Huan, Toupan a kiangah, tu in na khut na angah koih in, a chi nawn a. Huchiin, a khut a angah a koih a: a zan khiah takin, ngaiin, a khut vuk bangin a phak ta hi.
7. Huan, aman, na khut na angah koih nawnin, achia. (A khut a angah a koih nawna: a anga kipan a khah kheta, ngaiin, a khut khatlam bang a hongsuak nawn hi).
8. Hichi ahi dinga, a hon gintak kei ua, chiamtehna khatna aw leng a ngaihkhiak sam kei uleh, chiamtehna nanung aw a up ding uh ahi ding.
9. Hichi a honghi dinga, hiai chiamtehna nihte a up kei lai ua, na aw leng a ngaihkhiak sam kei uleh, lui tui akipan na la dinga, gam keu ah na sung ding: huan, lui akipana na tui lak khiak gam keu ah sisan a honghi ding hi, a chi a.
10. Huchiin, Mosiin Toupa kiangah, Aw Toupa, mi dangnal ka hi kei, chiklai mahin, na sikha kianga thu na gen nungsangin leng: mi dangawk leh pau zekai ka hi jaw hi, a chi a.
11. Huan, Toupan a kiangah, mi kam bawl kua ahia? Ahihkeileh pautheiloute, bengngongte, muhnate, mittawte kua bawl ahia? Kei Toupan ka bawl ahi kei maw?
12. Huaijiakin tuin kuan inla, na kam ka ompih ding, na gen ding ka honhilh ding hi, achia.
13. Huan, aman, Aw Toupa, sawlin, kon ngen hi, na sawldingpa khutin, a chi a.
14. Huchiin, Mosi tungah Toupa hehna a soutaa, Aron na unaupa a om kei maw? Hoihtakin thu a gen thei chih ka thei. Huailouin, ngaiin, nang kimuhpih dingin a hongpai khia: a hon muh chiangin a lungtangin a kipak ding hi.
15. A kiangah thu na gen dinga, a kamah thute na guang ding: na kam ka ompih dinga, na hih ding ka honhilh ding hi.
16. Huchiin amah mite kianga na thugenpa ahi ding: amah nang dingin kam tang ahi dinga, huan nang amaha dingin Pathian tang na hi ding hi.
17. Huan, chiamtehnate na hihna dingin, na khutin hiai chiang na tawi ding hi, a chi a.
18. Huchiin, Mosi a kisa a a sungpa Jethro kiangah a kika, a kiangah, ka hon ngen hi, hon paisak inla Aigupta gama om ka unaute kiangah ka kik ding, a dam lailai na u hiam kava thei ding, achia. Huan, Jethro in Mosi kiangah, damtak in vahoh in, achi hi.
19. Huan, Toupan Midian gam Mosi kiangah, pai inla, Aigupta gamah kik nawn in; thah hontum pasal te teng lah a sita ngal ua, achia.
20. Huchiin, Mosi in a ji leh a tate a pi a, sabengtung tungah a tuangsak a Aigupta gamah a kik nawnta hi; huan, Mosi in a khutin Pathian chiang a tawi hi.
21. Toupan Mosi kiangah, Aigupta gama kik dinga na kuan chiangin, na khuta ka koih huai thil lamdangte tengteng Pharo maah hih ngei-ngei in: a hihhangin a lungtang ka saksak dinga, huchiin mite a paisak kei ding hi, achia.
22. Huan, Pharo kiangah, hichiin Toupan, Israel ka tapa ahi, ka ta masapen pen, achi:
23. Ka honchi ahi, na honsepsak theihna dingin, ka tapa paisakin: paisak na nial leh, ngaiin, na tapa ka that ding, na ta masapen mahmah, na chi ding.
24. Huan, lampia khualbukah hichi a hong hita, Toupan amah ana tuaka, thah dingin a zongta hi.
25. Huchihlai in Zipporain suanghiam a la a, a tapa zek mukvum a atkhia a, a khe bulah a khia a, kei dingin si kai pasal na hi mahmah hi, achia.
26. Huchiin amah a paisaka: huan, zekvum jiak in, sisan kai pasal na hi, achia.
27. Huan, Toupan Aron kiangah, Mosi tuak dingin gamdai ah hoh in, achia. Huchiin, a hohta a, Pathian tangah amah a tuaka, a tawpta hi.
28. Huan, Mosiin amah sawl Toupa thu tengteng Aron a hilh a, amah a piak chiamtehna tengteng toh.
29. Huan, Mosi leh Aron a paita ua Israel suante upate tengteng a kaikhawm vek ua:
30. Mosi kianga toupan thu a gen tengteng leh, mite mitmuh achiamtehna a hih tengteng Aron in a genta hi.Huchiin miten a gingta uh: huan, Toupan Israel suante a hongveha, a gimna uh a mute chih a jaktakun, a lu uh akun ua, a beta uhi.
31. Huchiin miten a gingta uh: huan, Toupan Israel suante a hongveha, a gimna uh a mute chih a jaktakun, a lu uh akun ua, a beta uhi.
