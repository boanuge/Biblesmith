1. Huan zophar Naamanmi in a dawnga, huchiin a chi a:
2. Thu tampite dawn louh ding ahia hia? huan mi houlimnaa dim siamtansak ding hia?
3. Na kisaktheihnaten mite daisak ding hia? huan na simmoh chiangin, kuamahin nang honzahlaksak lou ding uhia?
4. Ka thuhilh a siangthoua, huan na mit uah ka siang hi, na chi ngala.
5. Himahleh Pathianin thugen maijen le jaw, nang demin a mukte hongleh;
6. Huchin pilna thugukte nang a honensak dinga, a theihna ah thuah tampi ahi chih: huaijiakin na thulimlouhna gun sanga tawmjaw na lakah Pathianin nang a honphut chih theiin.
7. Zonin Pathian na mu thei diam; hoihkimna ah Thilbangkimhihthei na mu thei diam?
8. Van bangin a sanga; bang na hih thei dia? Seol sangin a thuka; bang na thei thei dia?
9. Huaia tehna lei sangin a saujawa, tuipi sangin a zajaw hi.
10. Paisuak henla, man henla, huan vaihawmna ah sam leh, huchiin kuan amah a dal thei dia?
11. Aman lah mi bangmahloute a thei ngala: ngaihtuah kei mahleh, thu limlouhna leng a mu hi.
12. Mihing theihsiamna beiin theihsiamna a nei ding, sabengtung nou mihinga a pian chiangin.
13. Na lungtang na koih dika, amah lama na khutte na jak leh;
14. Na khuta thulimlouhna a oma, gamlapia na koihkhiaka, gitlouhna na puanina na tensak kei leh;
15. Huchiin gensiatna ding omlouin na mai na daksak taktak dinga; ahi, na kip dinga, na lau kei ding:
16. Na haksatna na mangngilh ding jiakin; tui luang mangsa bangin na theigige ding hi:
17. Huan sunlai sangin na hinna a telzo dinga; khomial om mahleh, jingsang bang ahi ding hi.
18. Huan lametna a om jiakin, na kipta ding hi; ahi, amah tanchin na zong dinga, muangin na khawlta ding hi.
19. Lup leng na lum lai dinga, huan kuamahin nang honlausak kei ding uhi; ahi, mi tampiin nang a honzong ding uhi.Himahleh gilou mitte a juausuak dinga, taina ding lampi a neikei ding ua, huan a lametna uh kha khah ahi ding hi.
20. Himahleh gilou mitte a juausuak dinga, taina ding lampi a neikei ding ua, huan a lametna uh kha khah ahi ding hi.
