1. Lungkiaa na paimang louhna ding un hiaite na kiang uah ka genta ahi.
2. Kikhopnain ahte a honnong sak ding ua; ahi, kuapeuh nou honhihlum tuh Pathian tunga thil hoih hihin a kithei ding; huchi bang hun a tung ding ahi.
3. Huai thilte a hih ding uh, Pa leh kei hontheih louh jiakun.
4. Hiai thu khawng na kiang uah ka genta hi, a hun a hongtun chia, kon hilh khinta chih na theih nawnna ding un.
5. Hiai thil khawng a tung akipan kon na hilh louh him na hi uh, na kiang ua ka omtak dih jiakin. Tuin jaw honsawlpa kiangah ka pai dinga noute kuamahin, Koia hoh ding? Chiin, non dong tuan kei uh.
6. Hiai thute na kiang ua ka gen jiakin na lungtang uh lungkhamnain a dimzo hialtaa a.
7. Himahleh, thutak ka honhilh ahi; ka pai nou dingin a hoih hi; ka pai keileh, Khamuanpa na kiang uah a hongtung kei ding; ka pai lebel na kiang uah ka honsawl ding hi.
8. Amah a hongtun chiangin, khelhna thu ahte, diktatna thu ahte, vaihawmna thu ahte khovel a siamlouh dan a theisak ding hi;
9. Khelhna thu ahte siam louh a tang ding uh, kei hon gintak louh jiak un; diktatna thu ahte siamlouh a tang ding uh;
10. Pa kiangah ka pai dinga, nou kei non muh nawn tak louh ding jiak un;
11. vaihawm thu ahte siamlouh a tang ding uh, hiai khovel lal siamlouha a puktak jiakin.
12. Na kiang ua thu gen ding tampi ka nei laia, himahleh tuin jaw na chin tadih kei ding uh.
13. Himahleh, amah, Thutak Kha a hongtun chiangin, thutak tengteng ah nou a honpi ding; amah phuahtawmin thu a gen dek ngal keia; a jak peuhmah a gen zo ding, thil hongtung dingte leng nou a hontheisak ding.
14. Aman kei honpahtawi dinga; keia a la dinga, nou kiangah a hontheisak ding ahi ngala.
15. Pan thil a neih tengteng keia ahi; huaijiakin, Keia a la dinga, na kiang uah a hontheisak ding, ka chi ahi.
16. Sawtlou chikin nou kei non mu kei ding ua; sawtlou chik mahin nou kei non mu nawn ding uh, a chi a.
17. Huan, a nungjuite khenkhatin, I kianga a thugen bang ahi, Sawtlou chikin nou kei non mu kei ding uh, sawtlou chik mahin nou kei non mu ding uh, a chih, leh, Pa kianga ka hoh dek jiakin, a chih? a kichi ua.
18. Huan, amau mahin, hiai sawtlou chik, a chih bang chihna ahia? a thugen I theisiam kei uh; a chi ua.
19. Huan, amah dot a ut uh chih Jesun a thei a, a kiang uah, Sawtlou chikin nou kei non mu kei ding uh, sawtlou chik mahin nou kei non mu nawn ding uh, chia, hiai ka thugen na kidong umaw?
20. Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin ka honhilh ahi, Nou na khitui uh a ke dinga, na kap huahhuah ding uh, khovel bel a kipak ding; nou na lungkham ding ua, himahleh na lungkham uh kipah a suak ding hi.
21. Numeiin nau a veiin a lungkham naka, a hun a tuntak jiakin; himahleh, nau a suah takin a lunggimna a thei nawnta kei hi, khovela mihing hongpiang a kipah jiakin.
22. Huan, tuin nou na lungkham uh; himahleh ka honmu nawn dinga, na lungtang uh a kipak ding, na kipahna uh kuamahin honlaksak kei ding.
23. Huai ni chiangin nou kei bangmah nonngen kei ding uh. Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin, kon hilh ahi, Pa kianga bangpeuh na nget uleh, keimah minin nou a honpe ding hi.
24. Tu tanin ka minin bangmah na ngen nai kei uh; ngen un, huchiin na mu ding uh, na kipah uh a kimna dingin.
25. Huai thu khawng a hem-in na kiang uah ka genta hi; a hun a tung dek, huai hun chiangin na kiang uah a hemin thu ka gen nawn ta kei dinga, chian takin Pa tanchin ka honhilh ding.
26. Huai ni chiangin keimah minin thil na ngen ding uh; Pa kiangh ken ka honnget sak ding ka chi kei hi.
27. Pain amah ngeiin nou a honit ngala, nou kei non it ua, Pa a kipan ka hi chih non gintak jiakun.
28. Kei Pa kianga kipana hongpawta, khovela hongpai ka hi a; khovel paisana, Pa kianga pai nawn ding ka hi, a chi a.
29. Huan, a nungjuiten, Ngai dih tuin jaw tel takin thu na gena, a hem himhimin nagen ta kei hi.
30. Nang bangkim na theia, kuamah hondot na kiphamoh kei chih tuin ka theita uh; hiai jiakin Pathian kianga kipana hongpai na hi chih ka gingta uhi, a chi ua.
31. Huan, Jesun a dawnga, a kiang uah, Tuin na gingta u maw?
32. Ngai un, a hun a tung, ahi, tun ah a tungta, huai hun chiangin noumau in chiat ah na dalh vek ding uh, keimah kiain non nuse ding uh; himahleh Pa ka kianga a om jiakin keimah kiain ka om tuan kei hi.Hiai thute na kiang uah ka genta hi, keimaha na khamuanna dingun. Khovel ah gimthuakna na neiding uh, himahleh khamuang takin om un, ken khovel ka zouta, a chi a.
33. Hiai thute na kiang uah ka genta hi, keimaha na khamuanna dingun. Khovel ah gimthuakna na neiding uh, himahleh khamuang takin om un, ken khovel ka zouta, a chi a.
