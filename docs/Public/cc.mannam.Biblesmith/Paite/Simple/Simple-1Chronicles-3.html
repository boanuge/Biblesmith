1. Huchiin hiaite Hebrona a laka piang David tapate ahi uh: ta masa, Amnon, Jezreel numei Ahinoam laka; nihna, Danial, Karmel numei Abigail laka;
2. Thumna, Absalom Gesur kumpipa Talmai tanu Maaka tapa; lina, Adonija Haggit tapa;
3. Ngana, Abitala Sephatia; gukna, A ji Ekla laka Ithream.
4. Hebron ah a lak ah guk a piang ua; huan huailaiah kum sagih leh kha guk a lal hi: huan Jerusalem ah kum sawmthum leh kum thum a lal.
5. Huan hiaite Jerusalema a laka piangte ahi uh: Simea, Sobab, Nathan, Solomon, li, Ammiel tanu Bathsua lakah:
6. Huan Ibhar, Elisama, Eliphelet;
7. Huan Noga, Nepek, Japhia;
8. Huan Elisama, Eliada, Eliphellet, kua.
9. Hiaite tengteng, meite tapate chihlouhin, David tapate ahi uh; huan Tamar bel a sanggamnu uh ahi.
10. Huan Solomon tapa Rehoboam ahi. Abija a tapa, Asa a tapa, Jehosaphat a tapa;
11. Joram a tapa, Ahazia a tapa, Ahazia a tapa, Joas a tapa;
12. Amazia a tapa, Azaria a tapa, Jotham a tapa;
13. Ahaz a tapa, Hezekia a tapa, Manasi a tapa;
14. Amon a tapa, Josia a tapa.
15. Huan Josia tapate; tamasa Johanan, nihna Jehoiakim, thumna Zedekia, lina Sallum.
16. Huan Jehoiakim tapate: Jekonia a tapa, Zedekia a tapa.
17. Huan salmatpa Jekonia suante: Sealtiel a tapa,
18. Malkiram, Pedaia, Senazzar, Jekamia, Hosama, Nedabia.
19. Huan Pedaia tapate; Zerubbabel leh simei: huan Zerubbabel tapate; Mesullam, Hanania; huan Selomit a sanggamnu ahi:
20. Huan Hasuba, Ohel, Berekia, Hasadia, Jusa-hesed, nga.
21. Huan Hanania tapate; Pelatia, Jesaia: Rephaia tapate, Arnan tapate, Obadia tapate, Sekania tapate.
22. Huan Sekania tapate, Semaia: huan Semaia tapate; Hattus, Igal, Baria, Nearia, Saphat, guk.
23. Huan Nearia tapate; Elioenai, Hizkia, Azrikam, thum.Huan Elioenai tapate; Hodavia, Eliasib, Pelaia, Akkub, Johanan, Delaia, Anani, sagih.
24. Huan Elioenai tapate; Hodavia, Eliasib, Pelaia, Akkub, Johanan, Delaia, Anani, sagih.
