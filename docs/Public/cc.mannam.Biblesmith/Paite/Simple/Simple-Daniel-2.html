1. Huan Nebukadnezzar vaihawm kum nih kum in Nebukednezzar in mang a neia; a lungsim a buai mahmaha, a ihmu theita kei hi.
2. Huan kumpipan mitkhialdawitawite leh, bummite leh, aisanmite leh Kaldai mite, kumpipa a mang hilh dinga sap dingin thu a piaa. Huchiin a hong lut ua kumpipa maah a hong ding ta uhi.
3. Huchiin kumpipan a kiang uah, Mang ka neia, mang thei dingin ka lungsim a buai mahmah hi, a chi a.
4. Huan Kaldai miten kumpipa kiangah Suria pauin, Aw kumpia, khantawnin dam in; na sikhate mang tuh hilh inla a hilhchetna ka ensak ding uh, a chi ua.
5. Kumpipan a dawnga, Kaldai mite kiangah, A thil keia kipan a paikheta; mang ka kianga na hontheih sak kei ua leh hilhchetna toh, at nenin na om ding ua, na inte uh ekvum a honghi ding.
6. Himahleh mang leh a hilhchetna na theihsak ua leh, kei a kipanin thilpiakte leh kipahmante leh nakpitaka zahtakna na mu ding uh; huaijiakin mang leh a hilhchetna hon ensak un, a chi a.
7. A nihveina dingin amau a dawng ua, Kumpipan mang tuh honhilh henla, huan kou a hilhchetna ka ensak ding uh, a chi ua.
8. Kumpipan a dawnga, Kei akipanin a thil a pai kheta chih na muh jiak un, hun lei tum na hi uh chih ka thei tel hi.
9. Ahihhangin mang tuh ka kianga na kitheih sak kei u leh, nou adingin dan khat kia a om; hun a kikhen tana, ka maa thu juau leh sia gelkhol na hih jiak un; huaijiakin mang tuh honhilh unla, huan a hilhchetna na hon ensak thei uh chih ka thei ding, a chi a.
10. Kaldai miten kumpipa maah a dawng ua, Kumpipa thu ensak thei leitungah kumah a om kei; kumpipa hiam, toupa hiam, vaihawmmi kuamahin mitkhialdawitawi kuamahmah hiam, bummi kuamahmah hiam, Kaldai mi kuamahmah ah huchibang a dot ngei louh jiak un.
11. Kumpipa thil deih vangtak ahi, pathiante a omna uh sa toh hiloute chihlouhngal, midang kuamahin kumpipa maah huai a ensak theikei ding uh, a chi ua.
12. Hiai jiakin kumpipa a heh mahmaha a thangpai tuntuna, Babulon mi pilte tengteng hihmang dingin thu a peta hi.
13. Huchiin thupiak a pai khetaa, mi pilte a thah ding ahi ta ua; huan Danial leh a lawmte thah dingin a zong uhi.
14. Huchiin Danialin kumpipa veng sepaih zaheutu Ariok kiangah thuhilhna leh thupil toh dawnna a kik saka, Babulon mi pilte that dinga kuankhe pa kiangah.
15. Ariok kumpipa sepaih zaheutu a dawnga a kiangah, Bangdia kumpipa a kipan thupiak huchitela kin ahia? a chi a. Huchiin Ariokin Danial kiangah a thil a kitheisak a.
16. Huan Danial a luta, amah adia hun sehsak dingin kumpipa a ngena, huchia a hilh chetna kumpipa a etsak dingin.
17. Huchiin Danial a in ah a paia, Hanania te, Misael te, Azaria te, a lawmte kiangah huai thil tuh a theisak ta hi;
18. Huchia amauten hiai thuguk tanchina van Pathian chitnate a nget theihna dingun; huchia Danial leh a lawmte Babulon a mi pilte lakate toh amanthat louhna ding un.
19. Huchiin jan mengmuhna ah Danial kiangah thuguk tuh taklatin a om ta hi. Huan Danialin van Pathian a phat hi.
20. Danialin a dawnga, Khantawn khantawnin Pathian min phatin om hen; Pilna leh hatna lah amaha ahi ngala;
21. Huan aman hunte leh hunbite a hihlamdanga; kumpipate a suan khia, kumpipate a tung khiaa; mi pilte kiangah pilna a piaa, theihsiamna theite kiangah theihna a pia hi.
22. Thil thukte leh gukte a taklanga, miala om bang ahia aman a thei a, a kiangah khovak a om hi.
23. Na tungah ka kipaka, ka honphat hi. Aw nang ka pipute Pathian, pilna leh hatna honpe pa, leh nang ka honnget uh tua ka kianga hontheisakpa; kumpipa thu lah ka kiang uah na hontheisak ta ngala, a chi a.
24. Huaijiakin Danial, kumpipan Babulon mi pilte hihse dia a sehpa, Ariok kiangah a hoh a; a vahoha a kiangah hichibangin a chi hi; Babulon mipilte hihmang ken; kumpipa maah honpi lut inla, huan kumpipa kiangah a hilhchetna ka ensak ding, a chi a.
25. Huchiin Ariokin kintakin kumpipa maah Danial a pi luta, a kiangah hichibangin a chi a; Juda saltangte lak ah mi khat ka mu a, aman kumpipa kiangah a hilhchetna a theisak ding, a chi a.
26. Kumpipan a dawnga Danial kiangah, a min Beltesazar hiven, Mang ka neih na hon theisak theiin, a hilhchetna na hon theisak thei maw? a chi a.
27. Danial in kumpipa maah a dawnga, Thuguk kumpipan a nget mipil hiin, aisanmite hileh, kumpipa kiangah a ensak theikei ding ua.
28. Himahleh van ah Pathian a oma aman thugukte a taklang hi; ni nanung lama bang ahia om ding kumpipa Nebukadnezzar kiangah a theisak ta hi. Na lupna tunga na lutang mengmuhna leh na mang hiaite ahi;
29. Nang na hih leh aw kumpipa na lupna tungah na lungsim sungah na ngeihtuahnate a hongom ua; tunung chianga bang ahia hongom ding thu: huan amah thugukte na kianga taklangpan bang ahia hongom ding na kiangah a theisak ta ahi.
30. Himahleh kei bel, pilna ahing himhim sanga pilna ka neih tam jawk jiaka hiai thuguk ka kianga taklat ahi keia: himahleh a hilhchetna kumpipa kianga theihsak a hih theihna ding leh nang na lungtang ngaihtuahte na theihna ding a hijaw hi.
31. Nang, Aw kumpipa, namuta; ngaiin, milim thupi tak. Hiai milim, hat tak mai, a tetna thupi mahmah, na maah a ding a; amel tuh a kihtakhuai mahmah hi.
32. Hiai milim a hih leh, a lutang dangkaeng hoih ahi a. a awm leh a bante dangka, a gilleh apheipite dal,
33. akhete sik, a khepekte sikkhenkhatleh tung khenkhat.
34. Suang khuta sek louh. huai milim a khepek sik, leh tung denga kitam nen sak dimdem tanin na mu a.
35. Huchiin sik toh, tung toh. dal toh, dangka toh, dangkaeng loh a kitam nen khawm vek ua: nipi buhphuala buhsi bang mai a hong hi ua; huchiin huihin amaute amut mang ua: huan huai milim deng suang pen mual thupi a hong suaka. lei pumpi a dimta hi.
36. Hiai mang ahi a; kumpipa maah a hilhchetna ka gen ding uh.
37. Nang. Aw kumpipa, kumpipate kumpipa, van Pathianin a kianga gam te, thilhihtheihna te. hatna te, thupina te a piak. na hi a;
38. huan koipeuha mite tate a tenna uah, gama sate leh vana vasate na khut ah a honpia; amaute tengteng tunga lal dingin nang a honbawla: dangkaeng lutang na hi.
39. Huan nang nungin nanga sanga niamjaw gam dang khat a hong ding khe ding; huchiin lal gam thumna dang, leitung tengtenga thunei ding.
40. Huan gam lina sik bangin a hat dinga: sikin a kek nen saka thil tengteng a nuaisiah bang takin: huan sikin hiaite tengteng a delh jan angin, a kek nen sakin, a delh jan ding hi.
41. Huan khepekte leh khezung na muhte, a khen belvelmi tung leh a khen sik a hih leh, gam hawmjak ahi dinga; himahleh a sungah sik hatna a om dinga, sik buannawi tung toh helkhawmna muh-bangtakin.
42. Huan khepek khezungte akhen sik leh akhen tung hi pen a hihleh. huchibangin gam hatna chiang nei, kitapna chiang neia hi ding hi.
43. Huan sik buannawi tung toh kihel na muh pen a hihleh, mite chi toh a mau a kitok hel ding uh; himahleh a kikawi kip kei ding ua. sik tung toh a kigawm lou mah bangin.
44. Huan huai kumpipate dam laiin van Pathianin gam suksiata om ngei lou ding, a thuneihna mi dang kianga nutsiata om ngei sam lou ding, a honding khe sak dinga; himahleh hiai gam tengteng a kitam nen sak dinga, a suse vek dinga, amah bel khantawnin a ding ding hi.
45. Suang mual akipan khuta sek louh hongom khiaa, sik te, dal te, tung te. dangka te leh dangkaeng te a kitam sak dimdem na muh pen a hihleh; tunung chianga bang ahia hongom ding kumpipa kiang ah Pathian thupiin a thei sakta hi: huan mang tuh theihsa ahia, a hilhchetna a kichian hi, a chi a,
46. Huaitakin Nebukadnezzarin a maiin lei a sia, Danial a bia a. thillat leh gim namtui a kianga lan dingin amau thu a pia hi.
47. Kumpipan Danial a dawnga, Chihtaktakin na Pathian pathiante Pathian leh, kumpipate TOUPA leh, thugukte taklangpa ahi; hiai thuguk taklang dinga hihtheihna na neih jiakin, a chi a.
48. Huchiin kumpipan Danial a lian saka, thilpiak tampi a piaa. Babulon bial pumpia heutuin a bawla. Babulon mi pilte tengteng tunga ukpipa pen dingin.Huan Danial in Sadrak te, Mesak te, Abed-nego te Babulon bial thulehla tunga koih dingin kumpipa kiangah a ngena; himahleh Danial jaw kumpipa kongpi ah a om hi.
49. Huan Danial in Sadrak te, Mesak te, Abed-nego te Babulon bial thulehla tunga koih dingin kumpipa kiangah a ngena; himahleh Danial jaw kumpipa kongpi ah a om hi.
