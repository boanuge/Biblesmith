1. Huan, Rehoboam bel Sekem khua ah a hoh a; Israel te tengteng Sekem khua ah kumpipa dia bawl dingin ahoh khintangal ua.
2. huan, hichi ahi a, Nebat tapa Jeroboam in huaithu ana zak in (kumpipa Solomon kiang akipan a taimang na Aigupta gama omlai ahingal a) Aigupta gam akipan Jeroboam ahong pai nawn a
3. Mi asawl ua, avasam ua; huchiin Jeroboam leh Israelte tengteng ahong pai ua. Rehoboam kiangah,
4. Na pan ka hakkol uh agik sak mahmah seka; huaijiakin napan honzat gim na leh a hakkol giktak ka tung ua a koih hon suk jang sak inla, huchiin na naa ka sem diing uh, chiin a gen ua.
5. Huan, aman akiang uah Nithum khit chiang in hong pai non jaw un, a chi a. Huchiin mipite kihem khia uhi.
6. Huan, kumpipa Rehoboam in, Hiai mipite dawnna diing thupha bang ahia non hilh diing uh? chiin, apa Solomon damlai a akianga omsek upate a dong a.
7. Huan, amau akiangah, Hiai mipite na hehpiha na kipahsaka, a kiang ua thu hoih tata nagen leh khantawnin na mite ahi diing uh, chiin agen uhi.
8. Himahleh, hoihsakpih apom kei a, amah khanvualpih tangvalte akiang a omsekte a dong zosop hi.
9. Akiang uah, Hiai mipite ka kiang a, Napan ka tung ua hakkol akoih hon hih zangsak in chia gen mite ka dawn dan diing hoih bang ahia non hilh diing uh? a chi a
10. Huan, a khanvualpih tangvalten a kiangah hiai mipite napan a hakkol uh ahih gik thei mahmah a, nang jaw hon hih zangsak jaw in na kiangah chimite kiangah hichiin chi inla, hichiin akiang uah gen in, kapa kawng sang in akhut zung a gol jaw
11. Kapan hakkol diktak a honna posak a ken ahihleh na hakkol uh behlap lai sin kaka: kapan khetna in nou ahon sawi jel a, ken ahihleh aikam a hon sawi sin kaka chiin agen ua.
12. Huchiin Jeroboam leh mipite tengteng kumpipan nithum khit chiang in hong non un, chia ahilh bang in anithum ni in Rehoboam kiangah ava hoh ua
13. Kumpipan amau gum takin a dawng a, kumpipa Rehoboamin upate phatsak apom kei a;
14. Tangvalte phatsak pih bangin, kapan na hakkol uh anahihgik a, ken huai behlap diing kahi; kapan khetnain nou a honna sawi a, ken jaw aikama nou hon sawi ding kahi, a chi a.
15. Huchiin kumpipan mite thugen a apom ta kei a huai bel Pathian jiak ahi, TOUPAN Silo mi Ahija zang a, Nibat tapa Jeroboam kiangah a tnhu gen sa akipsak theih na diing in
16. Huan, kumpipan athu uh a pom kei uh chih Israelte tengteng in atheih un mipiten, David ah tantuan bang ahia I neih uh? Jesai tapa ah gouluah ding inei sam kei uh: Israel mite aw, na puan in lam chiat uah pai un: David, nainnkuansung ngaihtuah mai in, chiin kumpipa a dong uhi. huchiin Israelte tengteng apuan in lam uah apai ta uh
17. Israel suan, Juda gam khua a omte bel atung uah Rehoboam alal a
18. Huchiin kumpipa Rehoboamin nasem dia achih teitei te heutu Hadoram asawl a; amah huai Israelte tengteng suang in ana deng ua, asita hi. huan kumpipa Rehoboam Jerusalem khua a tai tum in a kangtalai ah atuang pah a.Huchiin Israelte David inkote tungah ahong hel ta ua, tutan in.
19. Huchiin Israelte David inkote tungah ahong hel ta ua, tutan in.
