1. Huai ni chiangin khelhna leh nitna siangsak dingin, David inkote ading leh Jerusalema tengte adigin tuikhuk kihong a hongom ding.
2. Huai ni chiangin hichi ahi dinga, gam akipanin milimte minte ka sat khe dinga, theihgigein a omta kei ding uh; huan jawlneite leh kha nin gam akipanin ka pai khe sak lai ding, sepaihte TOUPAN a chi.
3. Huan hichi ahi dinga, kuapeuhmain thu a genkholh lai aleh, huchiin amah suang a nu leh a pan a kiangah Na hing kei ding: TOUPa minin juau na gen ngala, a chi ding uh; huan amah suang a nu leh a pan thu a gen kholh jiakin amah a sunpailet ding uhi.
4. Huai ni chiangin hichi ahi dinga, jawlneite thu a genkholhna uah, a mengmuh uah a zahlak chiat ding ua; khem dingin samul pha puannak lenga a silh sam kei ding.
5. Jawlnei ka hi kei, lei letmi ka hi; ka tuailai akipana sikhaa bawl ka nahih khittak jiakin, a chi zo ding hi.
6. Huan, min a kiangah, Na bante kikala liamnate bang ahia? a chi dinga. Huchiin aman, Huaite ka lawmte in sunga ka liamna ahi uh, chiin a dawng ding.
7. Aw namsau, ka belamohingmi dou ding leh, mi ka lawm hi dou dingin halin, sepaihte TOUPAN a chi; belamchingmi khenin, huan belamte a dalh ding uh; huchiin a neute tungah ka khut ka lik ding hi.
8. Huan hichi a honghi dinga, gam tengtengah, huaia seh nih sat khaikin a om dia a si ding; himahleh huaia a thumna nutsiatin a om ding, TOUPAN a chi.Huan, a seh thumna meia paisuakin ka hontawi dia, dangka hihsiangthou bangin a mau ka hihsiangthou dinga, dangka ettel bangin amau ka entel ding hi; ka min a lou ding ua, huan amau ka dawng ding; Ka mite ahi, ka chi dia, amau, TOUPA ka Pathian ahi, hongchi ding uh.
9. Huan, a seh thumna meia paisuakin ka hontawi dia, dangka hihsiangthou bangin a mau ka hihsiangthou dinga, dangka ettel bangin amau ka entel ding hi; ka min a lou ding ua, huan amau ka dawng ding; Ka mite ahi, ka chi dia, amau, TOUPA ka Pathian ahi, hongchi ding uh.
