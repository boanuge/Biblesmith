1. Hiaite Israel tapate ahi uh: Reuben, Simeon, Levi leh Juda, Issakar leh Zebulun;
2. Dan, Joseph leh Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad leh Aser.
3. Juda tapate; Er, leh Onan, leh Sela: huaite kanaannu Bath-sua akipana amah laka piang thumte ahi uh. Huan Er, Juda ta masapen, TOUPA mitmuhin a giloua; amah a that hi.
4. Huchiin Tamar a tapa jiin Pharez leh Zera a suansaka. Juda tapate tengteng nga ahi uh.
5. Pharez tapate; Hezron leh Hamul.
6. Huan Zera tapate; Zimri, leh Ethan leh Heman, leh kalkol leh Dara: a vek un nga.
7. Huan Karmi tapate; Akar thil siangthoua talekpa, Israel hihbuaipa,
8. Huan Ethan tapate; Azaria.
9. Hezron, amah laka piangte leng; Jerahmeel leh, Ram, leh Chelubai.
10. Huan Ramin Amminadab a suang; huan Amaminadabin Nahson, Juda suante lal, a suang;
11. Huan Nahsonin Salma a suang, huan Salmain Boaz a suang;
12. Huan Boazin Obed a suanga, huan Obedin Jesai
13. Huan Jesai in a ta masapen Eliab leh a nihna Abinadad leh, a thumna Sima a suanga;
14. A lina Nethanel, a ngana Raddai;
15. A gukna Ozem, a sagihna David:
16. Huan a sanggamnute uh Zeruia leh Abigail ahi uh. Huchiin Zeruia tapate; Abisai leh Joab, leh Asahel, thum.
17. Huan Abigailin Amasa a suanga: huchiin Amasa pa Ismael mi Jether ahi.
18. Huan Jezron tapa Kalebin Azuba a ji, leh Jeriot lakah tate a suanga: huchiin hiaite a tapate ahi; Jeser leh, Sobab leh Ardon.
19. Huan Azuba a sia, Kalebin amah dingin Ephrat a pia, huaiin Hur a neihsak hi.
20. Huan Hurin Uri a suanga, huan Uriin Bezalel a suang.
21. Huainungin Jezron Gilead pa Makir tanu kiangah a luta; huai jiin a pia kum sawmguka upa a hih laiin; aman Segub a suan sak hi.
22. Huan Segubin, Gilead gama khopi sawmih lah thum nei Jair, a suansaka.
23. Huan Gesur leh Aramin amau akipan Jair khuate a la ua, Kenath leh, huaia kho neite, khopi sawmguk taktoh. Hiaite tengteng Gileadpa Makir tapate ahi uh.
24. Huan huainung in Kalebephrathat ah Hezron a sia, huchiin Abija Hezron jiin Tekoa pa Asur a suansak hi.
25. Huan Hezron ta masapen Jerahmeel tapate Ram ta masapen, leh Buna, leh Oren, Ozem, Ahija ahi uh.
26. Huan Jerahmeelin ji dang a neia, a min Atara ahi; Onam nu ahi.
27. Huan Jerahmeel ta masapen Ram tapate Maaz, Jamin, Eker ahi uh.
28. Huan Onam tapate Sammai, Jada ahi uh: huan Sammai tapate; Nadab, Abisur.
29. Huan Abisur ji min Abihail ahi; huan Ahban leh Molid a suansak hi.
30. Huan Nadab tapate; Seled leh Appaim: himahleh Seled bel tate neilou in a si hi.
31. Huan Appaim tapate; Isi. Huan Isi tapate; Sesan. Huan Sesan tapate; Ahlai.
32. Huan Jada Sammai unaupa tapate; Jether, Jonathan: huan Jether tate beiin a si.
33. Huan Jonathan tapate: Peleth, Zaza. Hiaite Jerahmeel tapate ahi uh.
34. Huchiin Sesanin, tanute chihlouh, tapate a neikei. Huan Sesanin, Aigupta mi khat, sikha a neia, a min Jarha ahi.
35. Huan Sesanin a tanu a sikha Jerha kiangah ji din a piaa; huan Attai a neihsaka.
36. Huan Attaiin Nathan a suanga, Nathanin Zabad a suang;
37. Huan Zabadin Ephlal a suanga; Ephlalin Obed a suang;
38. Huan Obedin Jehu a suanga, Jehuin Azaria a suang;
39. Huan Azariain Helez a suanga, Helezin Eleasa a suang;
40. Huan Eleasain Sismai a suanga, Sismaiin Sallum a suang;
41. Huan Sallumin Jekamia a suanga, Jekamia in Elisama a suanghi.
42. Huchiin Jerahmeel unaupa Kaleb tapate Mesa a ta masapen, Ziph pa ahia; huan Hebron pa Maresah tapate.
43. Huan Hebron tapate; Kora, Tappua, Rekem, Sema.
44. Huan Semain Raham, Jorkeam pa a suanga; Rekemin Sammai a suang hi.
45. Huan Sammai tapa Maon ahi; huan Maon Bethzur pa ahi.
46. Ephah, Kaleb meiin, Haran, Moza, Gazez a suanga: huan Haranin Gazez a suang hi.
47. Huan Jahdai tapate; Regem, Jotham, Gesan, Pelet, Ephah, Saap.
48. Maaka, Kaleb meiin, Seber leh Tirhana a neia.
49. Madmana pa Saap leng, Makbena pa leh Gibea pa Seva a neia; huan Kaleb tanu Aksa ahi.
50. Hiaite Kaleb tapate ahi uh; Hur tapa, Ephrathah ta masapen, Sobal Kiriath-Jearim pa;
51. Salma Beth-lehem pa Harep Beth-gader pa.
52. Kiriath-jearim pa Sobalin tapate a neia; Haroe, Menuhoth Kimkhat.
53. Huan Kiriath-jearim inkote; Ithrit mite, Puthit mite, Sumathit mite, Misrai mite; Zorathit mite leh Estaolit mite hongkipatna.
54. Salma tapate; Beth-lehem, Netophathit mite, Atrothbeth-Joab, Manahathit mite, Zorit mite kimkhatte.Huan Jabeza teng laigelhmite inkote; Tirathit mite, Simeathit mite, Sukathit mite. Hiaite Ken mi Rekab inkote pa, Hammat akipan hongpaite ahi uh.
55. Huan Jabeza teng laigelhmite inkote; Tirathit mite, Simeathit mite, Sukathit mite. Hiaite Ken mi Rekab inkote pa, Hammat akipan hongpaite ahi uh.
