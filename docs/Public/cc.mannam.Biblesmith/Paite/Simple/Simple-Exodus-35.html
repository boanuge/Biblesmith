1. Huan Mosi in Israel suante khawmpi tengteng a khawmkhawm a, a kiang uah, Hiaite, na hih ding ua, TOUPA'N a piak thute ahi.
2. Ni guk sung in nnasep zoh ahi dia, a hihhang in ni sagih ni in ni siangthou a om ding, TOUPA a dia chiktak a khawlna khawlni, nou a dia inn om ni: huaini a kuapeuh nnasem sih hi.
3. Khawlni na omna tengteng uah mei na chih louh ding uh ahi, a chi a.
4. Huan Israel suante khawmpi tengteng kiang ah Mosi in thu a gen a, Hiai thil TOUPA thupiak ahi.
5. Na lak ua kipan in TOUPA kiang a thillat kilak un: utna lungtang nei kuapeuh in, TOUPA thillat, hon tawi hen; dangka-eng leh, dangka leh daal;
6. A dum leh, a sandup leh, a san-au leh, puan malngat hoih, leh kel mul;
7. Leh belampa mul diahsan, leh ngalvang vun leh, singgi sing;
8. Leh khawnvak a ding thau leh, thaunilh ding in gimlim leh hal namtui ding in;
9. Onik suangte, siampu puanak a siat ding suangte leh awmtuam a ding in.
10. Huan na lak ua lungtang pilte na hong ding ua, TOUPA'N thu a piak tengteng na bawl ding uh;
11. Biakbuk, a puan inn, a khuhna, a sik kawi, a singpekte, a sik tawnte, a khuamte, leh a kingakna khuak kuakte;
12. A bawm leh huai a achiangte, chitna tutphah leh khuhna puanzak;
13. Dohkan leh, achiangte leh, a tui-um-belsuante leh tanghou koih;
14. Khawnvak a ding in khawnvak koihna leng, a vante, a khawnvakte, a vakna thau;
15. Huan gimlim maitam leh, achiangte, nilh theih thau, gimlim leh, biakbuk lutna a kongkhak a dia puankhai.
16. Halmang thillatna maitam, a dal vutluah, achiangte leh a tui-um-belsuan tengteng, tuikuang leh a khe;
17. A huangsung puankhaite, a khuamte, a kingakna khuak kuakte, huangsung kongkhak a dia puankhai;
18. Biakbuk pinte, huangsung pinte leh a khaute uh;
19. Nnasepna puante, mun siangthou a nnasepna hih ding in siampu Aron puan siangthoute, a tapate puante, siampu nnasepna mun a sem ding in, chi in.
20. Huchi in Israel suante khawmpi tengteng Mosi omna a kipan in a paita uhi.
21. Huan michih a lungtang in a tohhalh peuhmah leh michih a kha in a utsakte, a hong pai ua, kihoupihna puan inn nnasepna ding in TOUPA thillat a hon tawi ua, a nnasepna tengteng a ding leh a puan siangthou a ding in.
22. Huchi in, pasalte leh numeite, a hong pai tuaktuak ua, lungtang a utna nei zahzah, taute leh, bilbahte leh, zungbuhte leh, saiha peite, dangka-eng mantamte; huchi in michih a lanten TOUPA kiang ah dangka-eng thillat a lan uhi.
23. Huchi in michih, a dum, a sandup, a san-au, puan malngat hoih, kelmul, belampa vun, ngalvang vun neiten, huaite a hon tawi hi.
24. Michih dangka leh daal lat lanten TOUPA thillat a hon tawi ua: huan michih, nnasepna hihna ding himhim singgi sing a hon tawi uhi.
25. Huchi in numei tengteng lungtang pilten a khut un khau a khek ua, huai a kheksa uh hon tawi ua, a dum, a sandup, a san-au leh puan malngat hoih.
26. Huchi in numei tengteng a lungtang uh pilna a tohthoh a omten kel mul a khek uhi:
27. Huan vaihawm miten onik suangte, suangte, siampu puan leh, awm tuam a siat dingte a hon tawi uhi:
28. Namtui, khawnvak a dia, thau nilh theih leh, gimlim toh,
29. Israel suanten TOUPA kiang ah ut tak in thillat a hon tawi ua, pasal chih leh numei chih, nnasep tuamtuam teng a ding in, TOUPA'N Mosi khut a bawl ding a thu a piakte, a lungtang ua hon tawi ding a utsak a omten.
30. Huan Mosi in Israel suante kiang ah, En un, TOUPA'N Bezaleel, Uri tapa, Hur tapa, Juda nam a mi min in a sam hi;
31. Huchi in pilna in, theihtheihna in leh, theihna in leh, nnasep siamna chiteng in amah a dimsak hi;
32. Huchi a nnasep lamdang gelkhe ding, dangka-eng a thilhih ding leh, dangka thil hih ding leh daal thil hih ding in,
33. Suangsek ahte, lepkhawm ahte, sing gelh ahte, nnasep pilhuai chiteng bangpeuh bawl ding in.
34. Huan a sinsak theihna ding in a lungtang ah a koih a, amah leh, Aholiab, Ahisamak tapa, Dan nam a mi.Nnasep chiteng hih ding, gelhmi nnasep, nnasem pilmi nnasep sepdan siam mi nnasep, a dum, a sandup, a san-au, leh puan malngat hoihte hih ding in puan ganmi nnasep, na himhim hih mite nnasep nangawn leh, nnasep pilhuai ngaihsunte nnasep.
35. Nnasep chiteng hih ding, gelhmi nnasep, nnasem pilmi nnasep sepdan siam mi nnasep, a dum, a sandup, a san-au, leh puan malngat hoihte hih ding in puan ganmi nnasep, na himhim hih mite nnasep nangawn leh, nnasep pilhuai ngaihsunte nnasep.
