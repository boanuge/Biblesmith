1. Huan, hichi ahi a, Jesu'n huai thu tengteng a gen khit in, a nungjuite kiang ah,
2. Ni nih nung chiang in Paikan Ankuangluina a hong om ding chih na thei ua, Mihing Tapa kilhden ding in a mansak ding uh, ahi, a chi a.
3. Huailai in siampu lianpen Kaiapha a chih uh huangsung ah siampu lalte leh vantang upate a kikhawm ua;
4. Jesu tuh zekhem a mat a, a hihlup theihna ding un a kikum ua.
5. Himahleh, Ankuanglui sung in jaw hikei leh, huchilou in jaw vantang lak ah buaina a hongpiang kha ding, a chi ua.
6. Huan, Jesu Bethani khua a, phak Simon in a a omlai in,
7. Numei khat alabasta bawm, thau gimlim mantampi tawi in, a kiang ah a hongpai a, an a umlai in a lu ah a sung a.
8. Himahleh, nungjuite'n huai a muh un a heh ua, Bangdia hichi bang a hihmangthang mawkmawk ahia?
9. Hiai thau man tampi zuak a, mi jawngte kiang a piak theih ahi ngal a, a chi ua.
10. Jesu'n tuh huai a theih in, a kiang uah, Numei bang dia hihbuai na hi ua? ka tungah thil hiohtak a hih ahi.
11. Mi jawngte khantawn in na kiang uah a om uh a keihiam, kei bel khantawn a na kiang ua om ka hikei.
12. Hiai thau gimlim ka sa a sun tuh, vui ding a hon bawl ding in a hih ahi.
13. Chihtaktakin ka hon hilh ahi, khovel tengteng ah koi ah leng hiai Tanchin Hoih a genna peuh uah hiai numei thil hih leng amah theihgigena ding in a gen jel ding uh, a chi a.
14. Huai nung in sawm leh nihte lak a mi khat, Juda Iskariot a chih uh, siampu liante kiang ah a hoh a.
15. Amah kon mansak ding a, bang non pe ding ua? a chi a. Huan, dangka sawmthum a buksakta ua.
16. Huai nung tuh matsakna lemtang a zongta a.
17. Huan, Tanghou Silngou hellouh ni, ni masapen in nungjuite tuh Jesu kiang ah a hong ua, Paikan Ankuanglui nekna ding in koi ah ahia hon bawlsak le ung na ut? a chi ua.
18. Huan, aman, Khopi ah kua hiam kiang ah hoh unla, a kiang ah, Sinsakpa'n, Ka hun a naita a; nungjuite toh Paikan Ankuang na in ah ka um ding u e, a chi, vachi un, a chi a.
19. Huan, Jesu'n a kiang ua a chih bang in nungjuite'n a hih ua; Paikan Ankuang tuh a bawlta ua.
20. Huan, nitaklam a honghih in, nungjui sawm leh nihte toh an a um ua.
21. Huan, a neklai un, aman, Chihtaktakin ka hon hilh ahi, Na lak ua mikhat in kei a hon mansak ding, a chi a.
22. Huan, a lungkham mahmahta ua, a kiang ah, Toupa, kei ka hi maw? a chi chiat ua.
23. Huan, aman, Kuang hon sawkpihpa ngeingei in kei a hon juausan in hon mansak ding.
24. Mihing Tapa a tanchin a gelh bang tak un a pai ding ahi; himahleh, Mihing Tapa mansak dingpa tung a gik e: huai mipa tuh piangkei leh amah a ding in a hoihzo ding, chi in, a dawng a.
25. Huan a mansak dingpa Juda in a dong a, Heutupa, keimah ka hi maw? a chi a. Aman tuh a kiang ah. Hi e, a chi a.
26. Huan, a neklai un Jesu'n tanghou a la a, vual a jawl sak a, a balkham a; huan, nungjuite kiang ah a pia a, La unla, ne un; hiai ka sa ahi, a chi a.
27. Huan, nou a la a, kipahthu a gen a, a kiang uah a pia a,
28. Na vek un dawn un, hiai ka sisan, khelhnate ngaihdamna ding a mi tampi dia pawt ding, thukhunna tuh ahi.
29. Himahleh, ka hon hilh ahi, ka Pa gam a nou toh a thak ka dawn ni a tun masiah tuban grepgui gah a ka dawn nawnta kei ding, a chi a.
30. Huan, la a sak zoh un a pawt ua, Oliv tang ah a paita uh.
31. Huan, Jesu'n a kiang uah, Tuzan chiah navek un ka tung thu a lunghilou in non taisan vek ding uh; Belam chingpa ka jep ding a, belam hon a dalh ding uh, chih gelh ahi ngal a.
32. Himahleh, ka thoh nawn nung in Galili gamah na ma uah ka pai ding, a chi a.
33. Peter in a kiang ah, A vek un na tung thu ah lunghilou in om mahle uh kei jaw lunghilou in ka omkei hial ding, chi in, a dawng a.
34. Jesu'n a kiang ah, Chihtaktakin ka hon hilh ahi, tuzan in ak khuan ma in, nang thumvei non kitheihmohbawl ding, a chi a.
35. Peter in a kiang ah, Na sihna a leng si sam ding himah leng kon kitheihmohbawl kei hial ding, a chi a. Huchi bangmah in nungjuite tengteng in a chi chiat ua.
36. Huai zoh in amaute toh mun khat Gethsemani a chih uh a tung ua: huan, aman tuh a nungjuite kiang ah, Pam a ka vathum kal in hiai ah na tu un, a chi a.
37. Huan, Peter leh Zebedai tapate nih a pi a, a lungkham in a hong mangbang mahmahta a.
38. Huan, a kiang uah, Ka kha a lungkham mahmah a, sihna khop hial ahi; hiai ah om unla, hon venpih un, a chi a.
39. Huan, neukha in malam ah a vapai zek a, khupboh in, Ka Pa aw, a hihtheih leh hiai nou in hon pel mahmah heh; a hihhang in keimah thu hilou in, nangmah thuthu hijaw heh, chi in, a thum a.
40. Huan, nungjuite kiang ah a hongpai a, amau tuh ihmu in a hon mu a, huchi in, Peter kiang ah, E, nou dakkal khat lel leng hon hahpih theilou na hi umaw?
41. Khemna a na lutlouhna ding un kiging gige in thum un; kha in jaw a ngap a, sa bel a hatkei hi, a chi a.
42. Huan nihveina ah a vapai a, a thumnawn a, Ka Pa aw, ka dawnlouh ahiai in a honpelh theihkei mahmah leh, nang mah thuthtu hi heh, a chi a.
43. Huan, a hongpai nawn a, amaute tuh ihmu in a hon mu nawn a, a ihmut uh a suak mahmah ngal a.
44. Huan, a paisan nawn a, a thumveina ah a gen ngei gen in a thum nawn a.
45. Huai khit in nungjuite kiang ah a hongpai nawn a. a kiang uah, Na ihmu lailai umaw, na tawlnga lailai umaw? Ngai dih uh, a hun a hongtungta a, Mihing Tapa mi khialte khut ah khotkhit ahita.
46. Thou unla, pai ni, en dih uh, a hon mansak ding pa a hong naita ve, a chi a.
47. Huan, a genlai mah in Juda, sawmlehnihte lak a mi khat tuh, a hongtungta a, siampu liante leh laigelhmite leh upate kiang a kipan mipi thupitak namsau leh khetbukte toh.
48. Huan, a mansak dingpa'n chiamtehna ding a hilh khol sipsip a, Ka tawp penpen tuh amah ahi ding, na man ding uh, chi in.
49. Huchi in, Jesu tuh a va juan pah a, Chibai, Heutupa, chi in, a tawp tantan a.
50. Huan, Jesu'n a kiang ah, Lawm, na hong nasan hih in, a chi a. Huaitak in, Jesu tuh a len ua, a manta uh.
51. Huan, ngaidih, Jesu ompihte lak a khat in a khut in a sawk a, a namsau a dok a, siampu lianpen sikha a ban a, a bil a ban khiaksak a.
52. Huan, Jesu'n a kiang ah, Na namsau a koihna ah koih nawn in, namsau pan peuhmah tuh namsau mah a a sih ding jiak in.
53. Ka Pa ngen leng angel sangguk mun sawm leh nih hon sawl pah ding; ngen theilou in non ngaihtuah ahia?
54. Himahleh, ngen taleng, bangchin ahia laisiangthou- hichi bang a a hong om ding- a tun theih ding? a chi a.
55. Huchih laitak in Jesu'n mipite kiang ah, Namsaute, khetbukte tawi in suamhat man ding bang hial in kei hon man ding a hong pawt na hi umaw? Pathian biakin ah ni teng in thu hilh in ka tu a, non man ngalkei ua.
56. Himahleh jawlnei laisiangthou thute a hongtangtun theihna dia hiai tengteng hongtungta ei ve, a chi a. Huchihlai in nungjuite tengteng in amah a nosia ua, a taimangta uh.
57. Huan Jesu mante'n siampu lianpen Kaiapha in a, laigelhmite leh upate omkhawmna ah amah a pita ua.
58. Himahleh, Peter in gamla tak a pat in amah a jui a, siampu lianpen huangsung tan in; huan a valut a, a tawpna thei ding sikhate lak ah a vatu sam a.
59. Huan, amah a hihlup theihna ding un siampu liante leh vaihawmte tengteng in Jesu hekna a zong ua;
60. theihpih juau tampi hong mahle uh a muthei ngalkei ua. A den chiang in a hihleh mi nih a hongpai ua.
61. Hiai mipa'n, Pathian biakin hihsiat a, ni thum sung a lam nawn thei ka hi, a chi ve, a chi ua.
62. Huchi in, siampu lianpen tuh a ding khia a, a kiang ah, Bangmah a dawnglou maw? Hiaite hon hekna bangchidan ahia? a chi a.
63. Jesu bel a dai dide a. Huan, siampu lianpen in a kiang ah, Pathian hing min in ka hon vau ahi, Kris, Pathian Tapa tuh na hi hia? hon hilh in, a chi a.
64. Jesu'n a kiang ah, Hi e. Himahleh, ka hon hilh ahi, tunung chiang in Mihing Tapa thilhihtheihna taklam a tu a, van mei tung a hongpai na mu ding uhi, a chi a.
65. Huan, siampu lianpen in a puansilh a botkek a, Pathian a genseta; theihpih dang bangdi'n i kiphamoh nawn dia? Ngai dih uh, Pathian a gensiatdan tuh tu in na ja uh. Bangchi nasa ua? a chi a.
66. Amau tuh, Si tak ahi, chi in, a dawng ua.
67. Huan, a mai ah chil a sia ua, a tum ua; khenkhat in a khutpek ua beng in,
68. Nang Kris, kua ahia hon tum? hon hilh in, a chi ua.
69. Huan, Peter tuh polam ah, huangsung ah a tu a: huan, nungak khat a kiang ah a hongpai a, Nang leng Jesu Galili gam a mi kiang a om na hi ve, a chi a.
70. Aman bel, Na thu gen ka theikei hial, chi in, a vek ua ma ah a kitheihmohbawl a.
71. Huan, inlam a vapawt tak in nungak dang in amah tuh a mu a, huailai a omte kiang ah, Hiai mi leng Nazaret Jesu kiang a ompa ahi ve, a chi a.
72. Huan, Huai mihing jaw ka theikei, chi in, kichiam in a kitheihmohbawl nawn a.
73. Huan, sawtlou nung in, a kiang a dingte'n, Peter kiang ah, Nang leng a lak ua mi na hi tawk vo oi, na pau ah a kithei ve, a chi ua.
74. Huan, aman, Huai mihing jaw ka theikei, chi in, hamsia kiloh in a kichiamta a. Huan, ak a khuangpahta ngal a.Huan, Peter in Jesu'n, Ak a khuan ma in nang thumvei non kitheihmohbawl ding, a chih thu a theithak nawnta a. Huan, a pawt a, a kap huphup hi.
75. Huan, Peter in Jesu'n, Ak a khuan ma in nang thumvei non kitheihmohbawl ding, a chih thu a theithak nawnta a. Huan, a pawt a, a kap huphup hi.
