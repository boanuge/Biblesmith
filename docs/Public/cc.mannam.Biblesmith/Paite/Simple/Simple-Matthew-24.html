1. Huan, Jesu Pathian biakin akipan a pawt khia a; a pai lai in, a nungjuite Pathian biakin khawng ensak ding in a kiang ah a hongpai ua.
2. Himahleh aman tuh a kiang uah, Hiai tengteng na mu umaw? chihtaktakin ka hon hilh ahi, hihchiplouh a suang kichiang himhim hiai ah a omkei ding, a chi a, a dawng a.
3. Huan, Oliv tang a a tutlai in nungjuite tuh amaua kia in a kiang ah, a hong ua. Huaite chik chiang in a hong om dia? na hong nawnna leh khovel tawpna ding chiamtehna bang ahi dia? hon hilh in, a chi ua.
4. Huan, Jesu'n a dawng a, a kiang uah, Kuamah in a hon khemzohlouhna ding un pilvang un.
5. Mi tampi'n, Kris ka hi, chi in, keimah min lou in ahong ding uh, mi tampi a khemzoh ding jiak un.
6. Huan, kidou thu leh kidou thuthang na za ding ua; na mangbatlouhna ding un pilvang un; huai khawng a hong om ding him ahi; himahleh tawpna jaw ahi nai tadihkei ding.
7. Nam tuam leh nam tuam a kidou ding ua, gam tuam leh gam tuam a kidou ding ua, mun tuamtuam ah kial a ke dia, jin bang a ling ding.
8. Huai tengteng tuh nau vei kipatna ahi.
9. Huai hun chiang in gimthuak ding in a hon mansak ding ua, a hon hihlum ding uh; ka min ziak in nam tengteng huat na hi ding uh.
10. Huai hun chiang in mi tampi a puk ding ua, a kiman tuah sak ding ua, a kiho tuah ding uh.
11. Jawlnei taklou tampi a hong pawt ding ua, mi tampi a khemzou ding uh.
12. Huan, hihkhelhna a pun ding jiak in mi tam jaw itna tuh a hong dai ding.
13. Himahleh, a tawp tan a thuak ngap peuhmah, huai mi ngei tuh hotdam in a om ding.
14. Huan, hiai gam tanchin hoih nam tengteng theihsakna ding in khovel tengteng ah hilh in a om ding; huai khit chiang in tawpna tuh a hong tung ding.
15. Huchi in, hihmangbangpa kihhuai, jawlnei Daniel gen, mun siangthou a ding na muh chiang un (a sim peuhmah in a omdan thei heh),
16. Judia gam a omte tangsang bang ah taimang uheh;
17. in tung a om in a in a bang hiam tuh ding in kumkei heh;
18. huan, lou a om in a puan tuh ding in kik nawnkei heh.
19. Huai nin gaite leh naunou pomte tung a gik ding!
20. Huan, na taimang hun ding uh phalbi lai hiam, Khawlni in hiam a hongtunlouhna ding in thum un;
21. huai hun chiang in gimthuakna thupi a om ding jiak in, hichi bang a thupi himhim leilung piantung a kipan tu tan in a om ngeikei a, om leng chikmah chiang in a hong om nawn samkei ding.
22. Huan, huai nite tuh hihtom hikei leh mihing himhim dam in a pawtkei ding uh; a hihhang in, telte jiak in huai nite tuh hihtom ahi ding.
23. Huai hun chiang in kuapeuh in na kiang ua, En dih, Kris hiai ah a om, hiam, Huai ah, hiam, a chih uleh, gingkei un.
24. Kris taklou leh jawlnei takloute a hong pawt ding jiak un, huaite'n tuh hihthei liai le uh telte nangawn leng a khemzoh theihna ding un chiamtehna leh thillamdang thupi takte a hih ding uh.
25. Ngai un, ka hon hilhkholta ahi.
26. Huchi in, na kiang uah, Ngai un, gamdai ah a om, a hon chih uleh, paikhekei un; dantan sunggil ah a om, a hon chih uleh, taksang samkei un.
27. Khua, suahlam ah, a hong phia a, tumlam tan in leng a kilang nak, Mihing Tapa a hongpai nawnna tuh huchi bang ahi ding ahi.
28. Sapul omna munpeuh ah musanete leng a om khawm ding uh.
29. Himahleh, huai gimthuakna nite nungzek in, ni hihmial in a om dia, kha a vak nawnta kei dia, van a kipan in aksite a hong ke ding ua, van thilhihtheihnate hihlin ahi ding;
30. huai hun chiang in Mihing Tapa chiamtehna van ah a hong lang ding. Huai hun chiang in leitung a nam tengteng in a sun ding ua, Mihing Tapa tuh thilhihtheihna leh thupina lian toh vanmei tung a hongpai a mu ding uh.
31. Huan, a angelte tuh, pengkul ging thupi toh, a sawlkhe ding, amau tuh a telte lei ning li te akipan, van tawp a kipan a lehlam tawp pha in, a khawm ding uh.
32. Huchi in, theipi kung ah a gentehna thu zil un: ahiang a hong nou a, a nah a hong sel in nipi a nai chih na thei uh;
33. Huchi bang in nou leng huai tengteng na muh chiang un, amah tuh a hongnaita a, kongkhak bul mahmah ah a om chih thei un.
34. Chihtaktakin ka hon hilh ahi, huai tengteng a tunkim masiah tulai khangthakte a mang kei ding;
35. lei leh van a mang dia, ka thu a hihleh a mang kei ding.
36. Himahleh, huai ni leh a hun thu tuh Pa kia loungal kuamah in, van a angelte'n leng, Tapa in leng, a theikei uh,
37. Huan, Noa damlai bang in Mihing Tapa hongpai nawnna tuh ahi ding.
38. Huai ni, thuchim tun ma khawng in, Noa long a a lut ni tan in, a ne un a dawn ua, zi leh pasal in a kinei ua;
39. tuichim a hongtung a, a vek ua a lak mang masiah bangmah a theikei uh; Mihing Tapa hongpai nawnna tuh huchi mahbang ahi ding.
40. Huai hun chiang in lou ah mi nih a om ding ua; khat tuh paimangpih in a om dia, khat tuh nutsiat in a om ding.
41. Numei nih buh gawinelna ah buh gawinel in a om ding ua, khat paimangpih in a om dia, khat tuh nutsiat in a om ding.
42. Huaijiak in kiging un, bangni in ahia na Toupa uh a hongpai ding na theihlouh jiak un,
43. Himahleh, hiai thei un, guta a hongpai hun ding innei in thei leh, a veng dia, a in a phen vang sak kei ding.
44. Huaijiak in nou leng kiging gige in om un, na ginlouh dak un Mihing Tapa a hongpai ding jiak in.
45. Sikha muanhuai, piltak, a pu'n a hun teng a a tan ding uh hawmsak ding a a inkuanpihte tung a heutu dia a koih, kuapen ahi dia le?
46. A pu a hongpai chiang a sikha huchi bang hih a a hon muh tuh a hampha ding.
47. Chihtaktakin ka hon hilh ahi, a neih tengteng tungah heutu di'n a koih ding.
48. Ahihhang in sikha gilou in, a lungtang in, ka pu a hong zokkei ding, chi in,
49. a sikha hihpihte na navota henla, nekham-takhamte kiang ah ne in dawnta leh:
50. huai sikha pu a gintaklouh ni in, a theihlouh lai in, a hongpai ding a,tum nih a hon suah ding a, mi lepchiahte tan bangbang a tangsak ding; huai ah tuh kah leh hagawi a om ding.
51. tum nih a hon suah ding a, mi lepchiahte tan bangbang a tangsak ding; huai ah tuh kah leh hagawi a om ding.
