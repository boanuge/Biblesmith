1. Huailouin Toupan Job a dawnga, hichiin a chi a:
2. Kuapeuh awlmoh neilou in thil bangkim hihthei a kiselpih diam? Kuapeuh Pathian kiselpih, huai tuh amah dawngsak un.
3. Huan Jobin Toupa a dawnga, hichiin a chi a.:
4. Ngaiin, Bangmah ka hi keia; bang ka hon dawng dia? Ka kam ah ka khut ka koih.
5. Khatvei ka pauta a, ka dawng kei ding; ahi nihvei, himahleh ka pau nawn kei ding.
6. Huchiin Toupan Job pingpei akipanin a dawnga, hichiin a chi a:
7. Ki pasal sakin tuin na kawng gak in: nang akipan ka honphut dia, kei kiangah na honphuang ding hi.
8. Ka vaihawmna nangawn bangmahloua koih ding na hi hia? Siam na tantheihna dia, kei honmohpaih ding na hi hia?
9. Ahihkeileh Pathian bangin ban na nei hia? Huan amah bangin awin vanpi na gingsak thei hia?
10. Tuin nang hoihkim leh zahnain kijem in; zahna leh lalnain kivan in.
11. Na hehna dimlette sungkhia in; huan mi chih kisatheite en inla, hihniam in.
12. Mi chih kisathei en inla, kainiam in; huan a dinna uah migiloute tuanchil in.
13. Leivui ah amaute selkhawm in; mun daiah a mai uh kan in.
14. Huchiin na khut taklamin nang a hondam thei chih ka honthei ding.
15. Nang kon siam banga ka siam, tuin en in chingpi; bawngtal bangin loupa a ne.
16. En in tuin, a hatna a kawngah a oma, a gilpi thate ah a hatna a om hi.
17. Sidar sing bangin a mei a tangsaka: a pheite thaguite phankhawmin a om hi.
18. A guhte dal lawng ahi uh; a guh leh tangte sik tawn bang ahi.
19. Pathian lampitea a pipen ahi: amah bawlpa kiain a namsau amah kiang lam naih dingin a bawl thei ding.
20. Chihtaktakin mualten an amah a puak ua; gamsate tengteng mawlna munah.
21. Tuilili singte nuaiah a luma, pumpeng mun dai leh gam nawng ah.
22. Tuilili singten amah a limun a khuh ua; luite singnahkaiten amah a tuam kimvel vek.
23. Ngaiin, lui a dimlet leh, a ling kei: a kam chiang pha hialin Jordan khang mahleh, a muang hi.A kiven laiin kuapeuhin amah sikkawiin a man diam, ahihkeileh thangin a nak a vut pailet diam?
24. A kiven laiin kuapeuhin amah sikkawiin a man diam, ahihkeileh thangin a nak a vut pailet diam?
