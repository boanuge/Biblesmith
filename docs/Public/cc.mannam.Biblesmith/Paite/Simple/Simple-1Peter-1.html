1. Peter, Jesu Kris sawltak in, Ponto gamte Galatia gamte, Kappadosia gamte, Asia gamte, Bithinia gamte a dalh-te,
2. thumang ding leh Jesu Kris sisan theh tang ding a Kha hihtuam, Pa Pathian theihkholh bang a telte kiang ah: Na tunguah hehpihna leh lemna hihkhanin omhen.
3. I Toupa Jesu Kris Pathian leh Pa tuh phatin om hen, Aman tuh a hehpihna thupi tak bang jelin, misi lak akipan Jesu Kris thohnawnna jiakin lametna hing mu dingin a honsuang nawna,
4. goutan sethei lou, nin bang lou, tul theilou, vana noua dinga sit muhna dingin;
5. hotdamna, hun nanunga lang dinga zohsa mu dingin, ginna jiakin Pathian thilhihtheihnaa ven na hi uhi.
6. Huaiah nakpi takin na kipak uhi, tuin (a kiphatmoh leh) khemna chiteng jiakin lungkham tadih mahle uchin;
7. na ginna uh zeetna, dangkaeng meia zeet nung nangawna mangthang jel sanga manpha jaw, Jesu Kris kilak chiangin phatna leh pahtawina leh thupina ding a honghih theihna ding in.
8. Mu ngeilou piin leng amah na it ua, tuin mu kei mahle uchin leng, amah na gingta ua, kipahna gen tuan theih louh leh thupitakin na kipak mahmah jel uhi;
9. na gintak pen uh, na kha uh hotdamna tak, muin.
10. Huai hotdamna thu tuh huai jawlneite hehpihna na kiang ua hongtung ding thu genkholten, a Kankan ua, siamnel takin a zong khia ua:
11. Kris kha amaua omin Kris thuakna ding leh, huaizoha thupina honjui dingte a theihkholh sak laiin, bang hun ahia, bangchibang hun ahia, a theihsak chih zonkhiak tum in.
12. Huai thuin amau nasep kisep louin, noumau nasep a sem jaw uh chih a kiang uah theihsak ahita, huai thute tuh van akipana sawlkhiak Kha Siangthou panpih in tanchinhoih nou honhilhten tuin a honhilh ua, huai thilte tuh angelten leng vel a ut uhi.
13. Huajiakin na lungsim puan uh tengin pilvang unla, Jesu Kris kilak chiangin hehpihna nou a honpuak ding tak lungke lou in lamen jel un;
14. ta thumangte bangin, na theihlouh lai ua na nidang lai duhgawlna uh banga omlou in;
15. nou honsampa a siangthou bangin nou leng na tatdan tengteng uah hong siangthou jaw un;
16. Noute na siangthou ding uh ahi, kei ka siangthou ngala, chih gelh ahi.
17. Huan, khentuam neiloua michih nasep bang chiata ngaihtuahpa tuh Pa banga na loh uleh, hiailaia na tam laiteng un laudansiam in om un;
18. na pipute uh piaksawn, na awmdan uh bangmah hilou khawng a kipan, thil sethei, dangka leh dangkaeng a tat na hikei ua,
19. Belamnou gensiatbei leh ninbanglou sisan bang a, Kris sisan pha a tat nahi zaw uh chih nathei ngalua;
20. Amahtuh leilung pianma in sehkhawlh in a om ngeitak a, himahleh, hun nanungte ah noumau ziakin kilak in a om hi;
21. Amahziakin misilak akipan kaithoupa leh a thuipina pepa Pathian gingtate na hi uhi, na ginna uhleh na lametna uh Pathian a aom theihna ding in.
22. Unaute it taktak dinga thutak juia na kha uh na hihsiangthou tak jiak un lungtanga phatuamngai takin kiit tuah chiat un;
23. chi sethei lakah hi louin, Pathian thu hing gige leh om gige ah, chi se theilou laka piang thak na hi zaw ngal ua.
24. Mi tengteng tuh loupa bang ahi ua, a thupina tengteng uleng loupa pak bang ahi. Loupa tuh a vuaia, a paka pulha,Toupa bel khantawnin a om gige ahi, chih ahi ngala. Huaituh ahi, tanchin hoih thu nou a honhilh uh.
25. Toupa bel khantawnin a om gige ahi, chih ahi ngala. Huaituh ahi, tanchin hoih thu nou a honhilh uh.
