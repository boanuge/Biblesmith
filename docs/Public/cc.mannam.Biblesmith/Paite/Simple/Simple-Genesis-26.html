1. Huan, huai gamah, Abraham damlaia kial ke masapen chih louh, kial a hong ke nawna. Huchiin, Isaak bel Philistinte kumpipa Abimelek kiangah Gerar khua ah a vahoh suka.
2. Huan, Toupa a kiangah a hong kilaka, Aigupta gamah hohsuk ken; kon gam hilh dingah om in.
3. Hiai gamah om tadih inla huchiin na kiangah ka om dinga, honvualjawl ding ka hi; hiai gam tengteng na kiangah leh na suante kiangah pia in, na pa Abraham kianga ka kichiamna ka hihkip ding hi;
4. Huan, van a aksi zahin na suante ka pungsak dinga, hiai gam tengteng na suante kiangah ka pe lai ding; huan, na suan ah khovela chi chih a hong navak ding uh;
5. Abrahamin ka thu a manga, ka thuhilhte Ka thupiakte, ka thusehte, ka dante leng a zuih bangin, achia.
6. Huchiin, Isaak Gerar khuaah tenna a bawlta:
7. Huan, huai khua a miten a ji tungtang a dong ua; huan aman, kasanggamnu ahi, achia: a mel a hoih jiakin, hiai khua a miten Rebek jiakin a honthat kha ding uh, chiin, ka ji ahi, chi ngam lou hi.
8. Huan, hichi ahia, huailaia sawtpi a om nungin, Philistinte kumpipa Abimelek tohleta kipanin a ena, huan, ngaiin, Isaakin a ji Rebeka a thoh lai a muta hi.
9. Huchiin, Abimelekin Isaak a sama, ngaiin, na ji a hina hi: bangdia, ka sanggamnu ahi, chi maimah na hia? achia. Huan, Isaakin a kiangah, Amah jiakin ka si kha ding, ka chih jiak ahi, achia.
10. Huan, Abimelekin, Ka tung ua na thilhih bangchidan ahia? mite laka mi kuapeuh in na ji luppih kha thei uh a kei maw, huchiin, ka tunguah siamlouhna na hontungsak ahi ding hi, achia.
11. Huan, Abimelek mahin, Kuapeuh hiai mi hiam, a ji hiam, khoih hihlup ngeingei ding ahi, chiin mi tengteng thu a pia hi.
12. Huan, Isaak huai gamah buh a ching sama, huai kumin amun ja a neia: Toupan lah a vualjawla,
13. Huchiin amah a hong thupi mahmaha, nakpitaka mi thupi hongsuak hialin a honglian huaihuai hi:
14. Belam honte leh, bawnghonte a neia, inkuan leng tampi a nei hi: huchiin Philistinten a haja uh.
15. Huan, a pa Abraham damlaia, a pa sikhaten tuileh a toh tengteng uh Philistinten ahudim ua, leiin a hihdim vekta uhi
16. Huan, Abimelekin, Isaak kiangah, ka kiang ua kipanin pai mangin, kou sangin lah na hatzo ngala, a chi hi.
17. Huchiin, Isaak huaia kipanin a pawta, Gerar guamah a puanin a kaia, huailaiah a tengta.
18. Huan, Isaakin apa Abraham damlaia a tuileh tohte uh a tou nawna; Abraham sih nungin Philistinten la a nahudim vek ngal ua: huan, a pan a min a nasak bangbangin a min a sa chiat hi.
19. Huan, Isaak sikhaten guamah a tou ua, huailaiah tuileh naknei a mu uhi.
20. Huchiin, Gerar khuaa ganputen, Kou tui ahi, chiin, Isaak ganpute toh a kisel uh: huchiin amah toh a kisel jiak un tuileh min din Esek a sata hi.
21. Huan, tuileh dang a tou nawn ua; huai leng tuh tumin a kisel nawn ua; huchiin a min din Sitna a sa nawn hi.
22. Huailaia kipanin a kisuana, tuileh dang a tou nawna; huaijaw tuh tumin a kiselta kei uh: huchiin, tun Toupan mun lianpi a honbawlsak ta a, hiai gamah chi tampi i suangta ding hi, chiin, a min din Rehoboth a sa hi.
23. Huan, huai akipanin Beer-sebaah a hohtou a.
24. Huai jan mahin Toupa a kiangah a hong kilak a, Kei na pa Abraham Pathian ka hi: lau ken, na kiangah ka om hi, kon vualjawl ding a, ka sikha Abraham jiakin na suante pungsak ding ka hi, achia.
25. Huchiin, huailaiah maitam a doh a, Toupa min a loua, huailaiah a puanin leng a kai hi: huan, Isaak sikhaten huailaiah tuileh a tou nawn uh.
26. Huan, Gerar khau akipanin Abimelek a lawm Ahuzaath toh, sepaih pawl heutu Phikol toh, a kiangah a hoh ua.
27. Huan, Isaakin, a kiangah, Non ngai kei ua, na kiangua kipanin non pai mang sak ngal ua, bangchidan in ahia ka kianga na hong uh? A chia.
28. Huan, amau, Toupa na kiangah a om chih chiantakin ka thei ua, huchiin I kikal uah, kou leh nangmah kal ngei ah, thu I khung ding;
29. Na jakdah ka hih ngeilouh bang ua, thil hoih kia loungal na tung himhim a ka hih louh ua, hoih taka ka honpai mangsak bang un, kou leng ka siatna dingin; tuin nang jaw Toupa vualjawl na hita hi, a chi ua.
30. Huan, aman amau adingin ankuang a luisak a, huchiin a ne ua a dawn uhi.
31. Huan, jingsangin beltakin a thou ua, a kichiam tuah ua: huan, Isaakin amau a paisaka, huchiin a kianga kipanin hoihtakin a pawtta uh.
32. Huan, hichi ahia, huai ni mahin Isaak sikhate a hong ua, tuileh toh thu a honghilh ua, a kiangah, Tui ka muta uh, a chi uh.
33. Huchiin, huai tuileh Seba a chi uh: Huaijiakin huai tuileh Seba a chi uh: Huaijiakin huai kho min tutanin Beer-seba ahi laia hi.
34. Huan, Esau kum sawm li a upa a hihin, Hit mi Beeri tanu Judith leh, Hit mi Elon tanu Basemath ji in a neia:Huaite Isaak leh Rebeka lungtang hih gentheite ahi uh.
35. Huaite Isaak leh Rebeka lungtang hih gentheite ahi uh.
