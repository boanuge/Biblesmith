1. Babulon kumpipa Nebukadnezzar te, a sepaih hon tengteng, amah thu nuaia leia lalna gam tengteng leh mipi tengteng in Jerusalem leh a khopi tengteng a sual lai ua TOUPA kiang akipana Jeremia kianga thu hong tung:
2. Israel Pathian, TOUPAN hichiin achi: Juda kumpipa Zedekia vahoupih in, hichiin achi: Ngaiin, hiai khopi Babulon kumpipa khut ah ka pe dinga, meiin a hal ding:
3. A khut akipan na suakta hetkei ding: mat na hiding a, a khut ah hon pe ding ua, na mit ngei in Babulon kumpipa na mu dinga, paukam ngeiin na kihoupih ding ua, Babulon gamah na hoh ding hi.
4. Ahihhangin, aw Juda kumpipa, Zedekia, TOUPA thu ngaikhe jel dih: na tungtang thu ah TOUPAN hichiin a chi hi:
5. Namsau in na si kei dinga, muang takin ahi na sih ding: na maa kumpipa masa, na pipute gimlim a halsak bang un, nang leng ahon halsak ding uh: a honsun ding ua, Ehe heh, ka pu, chiin. Thu ka khahta hi, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi, chiin.
6. Huan, jawlnei Jeremia in hiai thu tengteng Jerusalem a Juda kumpipa Zedekia kiangah a gen a;
7. Babulon kumpipa sepaih honten Jerusalem leh Juda khopi omlai tengteng, Laki leh Azeka a sual lai un: Juda khopi laka kulh neite lah hiai te kia ahita ngal ua.
8. Kumpipa Zedekia in Jerusalema om mipi tengteng kiang ah, mi chih in a sikhanu toh a sikhapa te uh, Hebru nu hiam Hebru pa hiam ahih uleh, a khah ding uh ahi:
9. A unaupa Hebru mi nasepsakin kuamah a om ding uh ahi kei, chia noplenna thu a phuana, thukhun a bawl nunga Toupa kianga kipan Jeremia kianga thu hong tung:
10. Mi chihin nasemnu hiam nasempa hiam a khah ding ahi, amau nasepsakin kuamah a om ding uh ahi kei, chih thukhun pommi heutu tengteng leh mipi tengteng in thukhun a mang ua, a khahta ngei ua;
11. Ahihhangin huai nungin a kihei ua, a nasemnu uh leh a nasempa uh a khahsate uh a kai kik ua, a thu nuai uah nasemmi dingin a koih nawn uh:
12. Huaijiakin Jeremia kiang ah TOUPA thu a hongtung nawna; Toupa Israel Pathian in hichiin a chi:
13. Aigupta gam, saltanna gam a kipan ka pi khiak niin na pipute uh kiangah thukhun ka bawla,
14. Mi chihin a unaupa, Hebru mi, a kianga juaksa, kum guk ana nasemsa a kum sagihna tawpin a khah ding ahi, chiin: na pipute un bel a honngaikhe kei ua, a bil uh a doh sam kei uh.
15. Tuin nouten, na hongkihei ua, mi chihin a insak inkhangpa kianga noplenna thu phuangin ka mitmuhin thil dik non hihta ua: Ka min tam inah ka maah thukhun na hongkibawlta ua;
16. Himahleh na kihei nawn ua, ka min na hihbuah un a ut bangtak ua na khahsa nung uh, na sikhanu leh na sikhapa uh na pi kiknawn ua, na thunuai uah nasemmi ding bang sikhanute ding bangin na koih nawn uh.
17. Huaijiakin TOUPAN hichiin achi: Michih amah unau leh amah insak inkhang kianga noplenna thu phuang ding in ka thu na ngaikhe kei ua; ngai un, ken na kiang uah noplenna thu ka phuang hi, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi; namsau te, hipi te, kial te in ahi ding; lei a lalgamte tengteng lakah vik lenlen dingin ka honbawl ding hi.
18. Huan, ka thukhun bohsiate, ka maa bawngnou at phel kikala pai suak hiala thu chiama tangtung sakloumi.
19. Juda heutute leh Jerusalem heutute, michilgehte, siampute, mipi tengteng, bawngnou at phel kikala pai te;
20. a melmate uleh a hinna zongmite khut ah ka pe dinga; huan, a luang te uh tunga leng vasate leh leia gamsate an ding lel ahi ding.
21. Huan, Juda kumpipa Zedekia leh a lawite a melma te uh leh a hinna zongmite leh Babulon kumpipa sepaih hon, nou honpaisansate khut ah ka pe ding hi.Ngai un, hiai khopia hong kik nawn ding in thu ka pe dinga, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi, huchiin a sual ding ua, a la ding ua, meiin a hal ding uh: huan, luahmi omlou in Juda khopite ka hihgam vek ding hi, chiin.
22. Ngai un, hiai khopia hong kik nawn ding in thu ka pe dinga, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi, huchiin a sual ding ua, a la ding ua, meiin a hal ding uh: huan, luahmi omlou in Juda khopite ka hihgam vek ding hi, chiin.
