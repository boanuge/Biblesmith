1. Bangchidana dangkaeng mial ahia! bangchidana dangkaeng siangthou penpen lamdangta ahia! Kongzing chiteng tungah munsiangthou suangte sunkhiak ahi.
2. Zion tapa manphate, dangkaeng hoih toh tehpih theih, bangchidana leibel, belvelmi khutnasepbanga ngaih hita ahia!
3. Sehalte nasanin a nawi uh a suah ua, ata tuailaite uh a tepsak uh: ka mite tanu jaw honggiloua, gamdai vasa mulneilou bangin.
4. Nawitep lai naupang lai dangtak jiakin a kam dangtawngah a belha: naupang tuailaiten tanghou a ngen ua, amah dingin kuamahin a balkham kei uh.
5. Nuam taka nete kongzing khawngah a mangthang uh: puansandupa khanglianten ekvut a kawi uh.
6. Ka mite tanu gitlouhna lah Sodom khelhna sangin a thupizo ngala, huai bel mitphiat kallouin a kizoua, amah tungah khut a kinga kei hi.
7. A miliante vuk sangin a siangthou jaw ua, nawitui sangin a ngou jaw uh, suangmantam sandup sangin a pumpi uah a sandup jaw ua, a tetna uh a saphir suangmantam bang ahi:
8. A mel uh suangkuangthei sangin a vom jaw a: kongzingtea theihin a om kei uh: a vun uh a guh uah a belh bikbeka; a keua, chiangkang a bang.
9. Namsaua thaha om dingte kiala thaha om dingte sangina hoihjaw uh; gama gahte duh jiakin hiaite lah a gawng gawp un, nakpiin a se khin uh.
10. Hehpihthei numei khutten amau tate a huan ua, ka mite tanu hihsiatna ah a an uh ahi.
11. TOUPAN a thangpaihna a hihpichingta, a hehna mahmah a sung khiaa; Zion ah mei a toha, huaiin huaia suangphumte a suse vek hi.
12. Leia kumpipaten a um kei ua, khovel a tengtengin leng, Jerusalem kongpi sungah gal leh melma a lut ding uh chih.
13. A jawlneite khelhna jiak leh a siampute ginatlouhna jiak ahi, huchibanga a lak taka midikte sisan suah a a om.
14. Mitto mite bangin kongzing khawngah a vakvai ua, sisana suknitin a om ua, huchiin miten a puante uh a lawng kei uh.
15. Pai mang un, Nin! pai mang un, pai mang un, lawng kei un, chiin amau a kikou khum ua: a tai mang ua, a vialvak lai un, miten namte lakah, hiai ah a om nawn kei ding uh, a chi uh.
16. TOUPA hehnain amaute a hawmjaka; amaute a limsak nawnta kei ding: siampute mina a zahtak kei ua, upate a deihsak kei uh.
17. I mitte I panpihna juau sosan lametna ah a vaikhina: I ngakna uah nam hondam theilou ding I na ngak ta ua.
18. I kongzingte ua i pai theih louhna dingin, I kalsuante a zong ua: I tawp uh a naia, I nite a kimta: I tawp uh lah a hongtungta.
19. Vana muvanlaite sangin ei hondelhte a man-gangjaw uh: mual tungahte a hon delh ua, gamdaiah a honna buak uh.
20. I nakvangte hu, TOUPA thaunilh, a kokhuk ua lakin a om ua; amah thu genin. Alim nuaia namte lakah hing ding, I chi ua.
21. Nuamsa inla kipakin Aw Edom tanu. Uz gama teng: nou lah nang phain leng a hongpai suak ding; na kham dinga vuaktangin na kibawl ding.Aw zion tanu, na thulimlouhna gawtna zoh a hita; saltanna ah nang a honpi mang nawnta kei ding: na thulimlouhna a hongveh ding, Aw Edom tanu; na khelhnate a mukhe dinghi
22. Aw zion tanu, na thulimlouhna gawtna zoh a hita; saltanna ah nang a honpi mang nawnta kei ding: na thulimlouhna a hongveh ding, Aw Edom tanu; na khelhnate a mukhe dinghi
