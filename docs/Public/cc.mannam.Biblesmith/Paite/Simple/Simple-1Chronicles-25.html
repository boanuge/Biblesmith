1. Huan Asaph te, Heman te, Jeduthun te tapa khenkhat kaihging te, pekgingte, dakkhutbette toh a la veka nasemmi dingin David leh sepaih heututen a hihtuam ua; Huan huchibang sem nasemmite tam la;
2. Asaph tapate laka mi Zakur te, Joseph te, Nethani te. Asarel te ahi ua, amau bel Saph tapate, kumpipa thupiak banga lavek jel. Asaph thuthu-a om ahi uh.
3. Jeduthun lakah; Jeduthun ta Gedali te, Zeri te, Jesai te, Simei te; Hasabi te, Matithi te, guk ahi uh; kaihging toha TOUPA kianga kipahthu gena leveka a pa uh Jeduthun thuthu-a om ahi uh.
4. Heman lakah; Heman ta Bukia te, Matania te, Uziel te, Seboelte, Jerimot te, Hanania te, Hannia te, Eliath te, Gidalti te leh Romamitiezer te, Josbekas te, Malothi te, Hothir te, Mahzioth te ahi hi;
5. Huaite tengteng Pathian lam thu-a kumpipa jawlnei Heman ta pengulkawi mutmi dingte ahi uhi. Pathianin Heman tapa sawm leh li leh tanu thum a pia.
6. Huaite tengteng TOUPA ina dakkhutbet toh, kaihging toh, pekging toh, Pathian ina nasepna dinga la toh lemmite, a pa uh thuthu-a om ahi uh; Asaph te, Jeduthun te, Heman te bel kumpipa thupiaka om ahi leuleu uh.
7. Huan a tamlam uh, a unau, TOUPA dinga lasak kisin, siam khinsa tengtengte toh za nih leh sawmgiat leh giat ahi uh.
8. Huan a neu a lian, sinsakmi leh sinmiin a tan ding uh kibangsapin ai a san uhi.
9. Huchiin aisan khatveina Asaph tapa Joseph tungah a puka; nihveina Gedali tungah; amah leh a unaute leh a tapate sawm leh nih ahi ua;
10. Thumveina bel Zakur tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih ahi uh;
11. Liveina Izri tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih ahi ua;
12. Ngaveina Nethani tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
13. Gukveina Buki tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
14. Sagihveina Jesarel tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
15. Giatveina bel Jesai tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
16. Kuaveina bel Matani tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
17. Sawmveina Simei tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
18. Sawm leh khatveina Azarel tungah, a tapa leh a a unaute sawm leh nih;
19. Sawm leh nihveina Hasabi tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
20. Sawm leh thumveina Subael tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
21. Sawm leh liveina Matithi tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
22. Sawm leh ngaveina Jeremot tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
23. Sawm leh gukveina Hanania tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
24. Sawm leh sagihveina Josbekas tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
25. Sawm leh giatveina Hanania tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
26. Sawm leh kuaveina Malothi tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
27. Sawmnihveina Eliath tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
28. Sawmnih leh khatveina Hothir tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
29. Sawmnih leh nihveina Gidalti tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;
30. Sawmnih leh thumveina Mahazioth tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih;Sawmnih leh liveina Romamti-ezer tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih.
31. Sawmnih leh liveina Romamti-ezer tungah, a tapate leh a unaute sawm leh nih.
