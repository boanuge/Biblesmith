1. Huan, Amphikoli khua leh Appolonia khua a kheng ua, Thessalonika khua a tungta uh, huaiah Judate kikhop in a om hi;
2. Huan, Paula, a dan ngeinain, a kiang uah a luta, Khawlni thumvei Laisiangthou akipanin teltaka sutpihin,
3. Kris tuh gimthuak ding him a hihdan khawng, misi lak akipana leng thou nawn ding him a hihdan khawng a hilhchiana, a hihlang jel hi. Hiai Jesu, a thu ka honhilh, amah Kris tuh ahi, chiin a gen behlap hi.
4. Huchiin, a lak ua khenkhatin a ging ua, Paula leh Sila a kithuahpihta uhi; Pathian limsak Grik mipi thupi tak leh numei makai tampi toh.
5. Himahleh ginglou Judaten a mullit ua; dawl mun a mi gilou thadah kuate hiam a kiang uah a tonpih ua, mipi a sam khawm ua, khua pumpi a tokbuai ua, Jason in a luh ua, vantang kianga pikhiak a tum uhi.
6. Huan, amau a mu kei ua, Jason leh unau ding kuate hiam kho heutute kiangah a kailut ua, a kikou ua, Khovel tokbuaite hiaiah leng a hong ua,
7. Jasonin a nazintun viala; hiaite tengteng tuh, A min Jesu, kumpipa dang a om chiin, Kaisar thu kalhin a om uhi, a chi ua.
8. Huan, mipi leh kho heututen huai thu a jak un a buaita ua.
9. Huan, Jason leh midangte kiang akipanin, a sik ua hen dingin bang hiam a la ua, amau tuh a khahta uhi.
10. Huan, unauten ajan ajanin Beria khua ah Paula leh Sila a hohsak pah uh; huailai a vatun un Judate kikhopna in ah a lut ua.
11. Huai mite tuh Thessalonika khuate sangin a na hoihjaw ua, huchiin lungsim kipaktakin thu a pom ua, a thugen uh a dik leh diklouh theihna dingin nitengin laisiangthou a zong jel uhi.
12. Huchiin, huaite mi tampiin a gingtata uhi; Grik numei zahtakhuai leh pasalte leng duaisanin a gingtata kei uhi.
13. Himahleh, Thessalonika khuaa Judanten, Paulain Beria khua ah Pathian thu a gen chih a jak tak un, huaiah leng a vahoh ua, mipite a hasuan gawp ua, a hihbuai nawnta uhi.
14. Huchiin, unauten tuipi tana hoh dingin Paula a sawlkhe pah ua, Sila leh Timothi bel huaiah a om tadih uhi.
15. Huan, Paula piten Anthen kha tannin a pi ua; huan, Sila leh Timothi tuh theihtawpa a kianga hongpai mengna ding thukhah puain a paita uhi.
16. Huan, Paulain Athen khua a amau a nangak lain, huai khua pathian lima dimin a mu a, a lungsimin a hehta a.
17. Huchiin, kikhopna in ah Judate leh Pathian limsakte a sel jela, dawl mun ah leng huaia naom peuhmahte tuh nitengin a sel jel hi.
18. Huan, Epikuria mi leh Stok mi laka mi pil kuate hiam in leng amah a hongkiselpih ua. Hiai mi tamtamin bang ahia gen a tup? a chi ua. Akhenin, Jesu thu leh Thohnawnna thu a gen jiakin gam dang pathiante thugenpa a hih tuak e, a chi ua.
19. Huan, amah a len ua, Areopaga ah a honpi ua, a kiangah, hiai thuhilhna thak na gengen non theisak thei diam?
20. Thil lamdang banghiam ka bil uah na hontun ahi; huchiin, hiai thil omdan theih ka ut uh, a chi ua.
21. Athente tengteng, a khua ua mikhual omte leng, a man chiang peuh un thu thak photphot genin hiam, a ngaikhe bekin hiamin a om uhi.
22. Huan, Areopaga laitak ah Paula a dinga, a gena, nou Athen khua a mite aw, bangkim ah sabiak limsaktak mi na hi uh chih ka thei hi.
23. Ka vak kawma, na chibai bukte uh ka et lain, maitam khat, a tunga, Theihlouh Pathian Ading, chia gelh, ka mu ahi. Huchiin, theiloua chibai na buk uh, huai thu tuh ka honhilh ahi.
24. Pathian khovel leh a sunga thil tengteng siampa, lei leh van Toupa a hihjiakin khuta lam in ah khawngpeuh a om kei hi;
25. thil tasam bangin mihing khuta nasep sakin leng om kei, amah mah tuh bangkim kianga hinna, hatna leh thil tengteng pepa ahi jaw hi.
26. Huan, leitung maikaih tengtenga om dingin bul mun khat vekin nam chih mi a siama, amau adingin hun bisehte a sehsaka, a omna gi uh leng a khunsak.
27. Pathian a zon ua, a mai a mai ua, a muh teiteina ding un; himahleh aman lah hongamlat kei chiat ngala.
28. Amah vangin i hingin i tangin, i om uh ahi ngala; Noumau laphuahsiam kuate hiamin leng, ei leng a suante i hi ngal ua, chia a gen bang un.
29. Huchiin, Pathian Suante i hih nung, Pathian tuh dangkaeng peuh, dangka peuh, suang peuh, mihing khutsiam leh suktuaha sekte bang hiam ahi, chih, i gingta ding ahi kei.
30. Huchiin, Pathianin i theihlouh laikhawng bang a kitheihmohbawla; himahleh, tunah jaw gam chiha mi tengteng kisik ding thu a peta hi, ni achiam jiakin;
31. huai ni chiangin Pathianin a mi seh zangin diktatnain khovel a vaihawm ding; huan a miseh tung thu tuh amah misi laka kipana kaithouin, mitengteng kiangah a theisakta hi, chiin.
32. Huan, misi laka kipana thohnawnna thu a jak tak un, a khenin a nuihsan ua; a khenin, hiai thu na kiangah ka ngaikhe nawn ding uh, a chi ua.
33. Huchiin, Paula tuh a lak ua kipan a pawtta hi.Himahleh, mi kuate hiam amah lamah a pang chinten ua, a gingtata ua, huai laka khenkhat tuh Areopaga mi Dionisia leh, numei khat a min Damari leh, a kiang ua midangte toh ahi uh.
34. Himahleh, mi kuate hiam amah lamah a pang chinten ua, a gingtata ua, huai laka khenkhat tuh Areopaga mi Dionisia leh, numei khat a min Damari leh, a kiang ua midangte toh ahi uh.
