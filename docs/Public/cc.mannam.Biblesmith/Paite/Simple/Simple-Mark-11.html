1. Huan, Jerusalem khua tung dinga a kisak ua, Oliv tanga Bethphage leh Bethani kho kiang a tun un, a nungjuite laka mi nih a sawla, a kiang uah,
2. Hua na majawn ua, khua ah vahoh unla; huan, na lut tung un sabengtung nou khih, kuamah tuan ngei louhna, na vamu ding ua; huai tuh phel unla, honkai un.
3. Huan, kuapeuhin, Huai bangdia hih? A honchih uleh, Toupan a deih ahi; hiai lai-ah a honkoihsak nawnpah ding, na chi ding uh, a chi a.
4. Huan, a hohta ua, kongzing ah kongkhak polama sabengtung nou khih a vamu ua; huan, huai tuh a phelta uh.
5. Huan, huaia ding kuate hiamin, a kiang uah, Sabengtung nou bangdia phel? a chi ua.
6. Huan, amau Jesu gen bang takin a kiang uah a gen ua; huchiin a phalta uh.
7. Huan, sabengtung nou tuh Jesu kiangah a honkai ua, a tungah a puante uh a phah ua; huan, a tungah a tuangta hi.
8. Huan, mi tampiin lampi ah a puan uh a phah ua; mi dangin leng gama sawlnah satin a phah ua.
9. Huan, a ma lama paite leh a nunglama honjuiten, Hosanna! Toupa mina hongpai a hampha!
10. Gam hongtung ding, i Pa David gam a hampha! Tungnungpen ah Hosanna! chiin a kikou ua.
11. Huan, Jerusalem khuaa Pathian biakin ah a valut a; huan, bangkim a et velvel khitin nitaklam leng a honghita a, a pawt a, sawm leh nihte toh Bethani ah a hohta ua.
12. Huan, a jingin Bethani akipan a pawt nungin a gil a hongkialta a.
13. Huan, theipi kung nah nei gamlapia a mu a, A gah ka muh khak leh, chiin a vajuana; huan, a vatunin a nah kia loungal bangmah a mu kei a; theipi gah hun ahi kei him a.
14. Huchiin, Jesun sing tuh a houpihta a, Tuban khantawnin nang apat kuamahin gah ne nawnta kei hen, a chi a. A nungjuiten huai tuh a ja uh.
15. Huan, Jerusalem khua a tung ua, Pathian biakin ah a valut-a, biakina banghiam juakmite leimite a delh khia-a, dangka khengmite dohkante leh vakhu juakmite tutphah khawng a sawn puk sak-a;
16. Huan, Pathian biakina kuamahin van a lut paisuak pih uh a phalta kei hi.
17. Huan, a kiang uah, Ka in nam chih adingin thumna in a chi ding uh, chih gelh ahi ka hia? Nou lah suamhatte khukin nabawl jaw ua, chiin, a hilhta.
18. Huan, siampu liante leh laigelhmiten huai a ja ua, huan amah a hihmanthat theihna ding a zongta ua; mipi tengtengin amah a lau ngal ua, a thuhilh lamdang a sak jiakun.
19. Huan, nitaklam a honghihin khopia kipanin a pawt khia uh.
20. Huan, jingsanga a pai kawm un theipi kung tuh a zung tan phaa vuai khin vekin a muta ua.
21. Huchiin, Peterin theikhiain, a kiangah, Heutupa, en dih: theipi kung hamsiat na loh a na vuai khin vekta, a chi a.
22. Huan, Jesun a kiang uah, Pathian gingta un.
23. Chihtaktakin ka honhilh ahi, kuapeuhmahin, hiai tang kiangah, Na kisuan inla, tuipi ah nakipai in, chia, a lungtanga ginglel loua a gen tuh a tung ngeingei ding chih gingta peuhmahin a gen bangbang a mu ding uh.
24. Huaijiakin ka honhilh ahi, bangkim na thum ua na nget peuhmah uh mu sain kiging unla huchiin, na mu ding uh.
25. Huan, thuma na din peuhmah chiang un kua tungah leng thupoi banghiam na neih uleh ngaidam un; na Pa uh vana omin leng na tatlekna uh a honngaihdam theihna dingin.
26. A hihhangin na ngaihdam kei uleh, na Pa uh vana omin leng na tatleknate uh honngaidam sam kei ding hi, a chi a, a dawnga.
27. Huan, Jerusalem khua a tung nawn ua; huan, Pathian biakina a vakvak laiin siampu liante, laigelhmite, upate a kiangah a hong ua,
28. A kiangah, Kua thuin ahia hiai thilte na hih? Hiai thilte hih dingin kuan ahia thu nang honpia? a chi ua.
29. Huan, Jesun a kiang uah, Ken leng thu kam khat ka hondong sam ding, hondawng le uchin, huchiin kua thuin ahia hiai thil ka hih chih ka honhilh ding.
30. Johan Baptisma vana kipan hia; mihing a kipan? hondawng un, a chi a.
31. Huan, a kingaihtuah ua, Vana kipan chi lehang, Bang achia a thu ging lou na hi ua leh? a honchi kha dia;
32. mihinga kipan, i chi dia eita maw? a ki chi ua. Himahleh mipi a kihta ua; mi tengtengin Johan lah jawlnei taktakin a bawl chiat ngal ua.Huchiin, Jesu a dawng ua, Thei kei ung, a chi ua. Huan, Jesun a kiang uah, Ken leng kua thuin ahia hiai thil ka hih, ka honhilh sam kei ding, achia.
33. Huchiin, Jesu a dawng ua, Thei kei ung, a chi ua. Huan, Jesun a kiang uah, Ken leng kua thuin ahia hiai thil ka hih, ka honhilh sam kei ding, achia.
