1. Aw Israel, na hongkik leh, ka kiangah ngei na hongkik ding ahi, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi. Na kihhuaina te ka mitmuh louha na koih manga, chih taktaka, thugel diktak zanga diktak taka;
2. TOUPA a hina a hin laiteng, chia na kichiam leh, namte amah ah nuamsa a kichi ding ua, amah a suangta ding uh;
3. Juda mite leh Jerusalem kiangah TOUPA Pathianin hichiin a chi hi: Na lei sit uh let unla, ling lakate chi theh kei un.
4. Nou Jud agama mi leh Jerusalema tengte aw, TOUPA adingin na zek uh sum unla, na lungtang zek uh sum un, huchilouin jaw na thil gilou hihte uh jiakin, ka hehna mei bangin a pawtkhe dinga, kuamah phelh theihlouhin a kang kha ding hi.
5. Juda gamah hilh unla, Jerusalem ah phuang un, Gam pumpi ah pengkul mut unla, ngaih takin kikou un. Hongkikhawm unla, kulhnei khopite ah I lut ding uh, chi un;
6. Zion lam ah puanjak khai unla, bitna zongin tai unla, zeol kei un, mallam a kipanthil hoihlou ka hon tungsak sin, nakpia siatna.
7. Gammang nuai ningkitel akipanin humpineilkai a pawt hi, namte hihse ding; a mun akipan a pawtta, a hongpai lellel hi, nagam hihse ding leh na khopite tengmi neilou khopa sungawp dingin.
8. Huaijiak takin saiip puan teng unla, kap unla, mau vongvong un; I lak ua kipanin TOUPA hehna thupi mahmah lah a kikai khe ngal kei a.
9. Huai ni chiangin hichi a honghi ding, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi: kumpipa lungtang a lungke khin dinga, heutute lungtang leng; siamputen gen ding a theikei ding ua, jawlneiten lamdang a sa mahmah ding uh.
10. Huan ken bel, Ah, Toupa Pathian, hiai mite leh Jerusalem nakpi taka khem nei ve, Muanna na nei ding uh, na chi a, namsauin hinna a sun zo ta hi, ka chi a.
11. Huai hun chiangin hiai mite leh Jerusalem kiangah, Gamdai tang giau velvul akipan huih sa, Ka tanu, ka mite japsiang ding hi lou; siangsak ding hi sam lou;
12. Huih hat lua, ka thuin a hongtung sin ahi: tuin leng a tung thu uh ngaihtuahpa kei ka hi, chih ahi ding.
13. Ngai dih, a hongpai tou ding, meipi bangin, a kangtalai pingpei bangmaite; a sakolte muvanlai sangin a hatjaw uh, I tung gik na e! Buluha om I hive ua.
14. Aw Jerusalem, thulimlouhnaa kipan na lungtang silsiang inla, hotdama na om theihna dingin; bangtan ahia na ngaihtuahna gilou na pai ding?
15. Dan gam akipan aw a hongpawt khiaa, Ephraim tang a kipanin thil hoihlou a phuang hi.
16. Namte hilh unla, Jerusalem ah phuang un, ngai dih, gam gamla pia kipan sualmite a hongtung uh, Juda khopite tungah a aw uh a suah uhi.
17. Louma vengmite bang main a kimvel in a um ua, ka tunga a hel jiak un chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
18. Na omdan leh na thilhihten, hiaite na tungah a tun ahi, hiai na siatna ding ahi, a na mahmaha na lungtang sun ei ve.
19. Ka lung a na, ka lung a na, ka lungtang mahmah ah a na, ka sungah ka lungtang a buaikhin ta, ka om maimai theikei, aw ka hinna, pengkul ging, kidouna heiha na jak jiakin.
20. Siatna tungah siatna a tungta, gam pumpi lohin a om ta, ka puaninte a hihse guih ua, ka puanjakte leng mitphiat kal louin.
21. Bangtan ahia kidouna puanjak ka muh dinga, pengkul ging ka jak ding?
22. Ka mite a hai mahmah ua, lah a thei ngal kei ua, naupang hai tak, theihna neilou bang mai ahi uah, thil gilou hih a siam ua, thil hoih hihdan bel a siam kei uh.
23. Leitung ka et leh, ngai dih, segawp leh vuakin a oma; vanten leng vakna a neikei uh.
24. Tangte ka et leh, ngaiin, a ling uh, mual tengteng leng a tang jualjual uh.
25. Ka et leh, ngai in, mihing a om kei ua, tungleng vasate tengteng leng a leng mang khinta uh.
26. Ka et leh, ngai in, louma hoihte gamdai a suak khina, khopi tengteng leng a chim khin, TOUPA leh a heh mahmahna maah.
27. TOUPAN hichiin a chi hi: Gam pumpi a segawp dinga, himahleh ka hihtawp tawntung kei ding.
28. Huaijiakin leiin a sun dinga, tunglam vante leng a mial ding: thu ka gentaa, ka tumta hi, ka kisik keia, ka lunghei sin sam kei.
29. Sakol tunga tuang leh thalpeu zangmite husa ah, khopi pumpi a taikek ua, gammang ah a lut ua, kho chih a nusia ua, luahmi khat lel leng om louin.
30. Huan, nang, loha na om hun chiangin bangchiin na hih dia? Puan san-au a kijemin, kivatna dangkaeng a kivanin, na mitte nuh za mahlechin, na kihihlawm thawn lel ahi, na tangvalten a honmusit thouthou ding ua, na hinna lak a tum uhi.Numei nauvei kikou husa bang ka jaa, ta chil neinu na thauk lai, Zion tanu nak huhu ging, a khutte jaka, Ka tung gik hina mai e! tualthatmite maah ka hinna a bah khinta, chi.
31. Numei nauvei kikou husa bang ka jaa, ta chil neinu na thauk lai, Zion tanu nak huhu ging, a khutte jaka, Ka tung gik hina mai e! tualthatmite maah ka hinna a bah khinta, chi.
