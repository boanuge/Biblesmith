1. TOUPA na Pathian un a gam uh nou a honpiak nam TOUPAN a hihmana na luah ua, a khua khawng ua, a in khawng ua na om chiang un;
2. TOUPA na Pathian un luah dinga nou a honpiak na gam uah nou adingin khaw thum na sep ding uhi.
3. Mithat taphot a tai lutna ding un, TOUPAN a honluah sak ding gam seh thum na suah ding ua, na gamgite uh na bawl ding ua, lampite na bawl ding uhi.
4. Huan, mithatte huailaia tailut a bit theite bel hiaite ahi uhi; min a insakinkhang tumalama huatna leng nei khol loua that kha peuhmahte;
5. Mi a insakinkhang toh singphuk dinga gamlaka kuana singphuk dinga a heipia sata, heipi tang tolin a insakinkhang denglum khate bang, huaite lakah khua khatah a tai dinga, a bit ding:
6. Sisan phu lamiin a hehna apai laia mithatmi a delha, lampi a sau jiaka delhphain a vasat lum kha ding hi: tumalam a huatna na nei khol lou a hih jiakin si tak lah ahi ngal kei hi.
7. Huaijiakin, Kho thum nou adingin na sep ding uhi, chiin thu ka hon pia ahi.
8. Huan, TOUPA na Pathian un na pi leh pute uh kianga a nakichiam banga na pi leh pute uh piak a nachiam gam a honpiaka;
9. TOUPA na Pathian uh it ding leh a lampi jui gige dinga a thupiak tengteng, tua ka honthupiak na pom ua, na gam uh a hihlet leh hiai kho thumte sim louin nou adingin kho thum dang na behlap nawn ding ua:
10. TOUPAN luah dinga a honpiak na gamsung uah poi khoihlou sisan suah a om louha, na tung ua sisan a om louhna dingin.
11. Himahleh min a insakinkhang a huata, a tana, a suala, si khop hiala a vuaka, huai khote laka khata a tai leh;
12. A kho upate un mi sawl uhenla, huai akipan va pi uhenla, hihlup dingin sisanphuala mi khutah pe khia uhen.
13. Na hehpih ding uh ahi keia, nou dia hoih dingin Israel gam akipan poi khoihlou sisan suahna na hihmang ding uh ahi.
14. TOUPA na Pathian un luah dinga a honpiak gam, na luah ding uh gama a maa a naphuhsa uh na insakinkhang uh gamgi na suan ding uh ahi kei.
15. Mi a thulimlouhna hiam, a khelhna himhim hiam theihpih khat a hun kei dinga; theihpih nih kam hiam, theihpih thum kam hiama thu hihkip ding ahi.
16. Theihpih diklouin mi thil diklou hih ahi chi dinga, dem dinga a kisak leh;
17. A kiselna uah a nih un TOUPA maah huai huna siampute leh vaihawmte maah a ding ding uhi;
18. Huan, vaihawmten ngentel takin thu a dong ding ua, huan, ngaiin, thu theihpihmi thutheihpih juau thei a hiha, a unau juaua ngoh a hihleh;
19. A unau tunga hih a natup bangin a tungah na hih ding uh; huchibangin na lak ua thil hoih lou na hihmang dinguh ahi.
20. A omlaiten a za ding ua, a lau ding ua, tunung chiangin huchibang thil hoih lou mawngmawng na lak uah a hih nawnta kei ding uhi. sNa hehpih ding uh ahi keia; hinna tangin hinna, mit tangin mit, ha tangin ha, khut tangin khut, khe tangin khe ahi ding.
21. Na hehpih ding uh ahi keia; hinna tangin hinna, mit tangin mit, ha tangin ha, khut tangin khut, khe tangin khe ahi ding.
