1. Huan Isakar tapate; Tola, Puah, Jasub, Simron, Li.
2. Huan Tola tapate; Uzzi, Rephai, Jeriel, Jahmai, Ibsam, Semuel, a pipute uh inkote lupente, huaibel, Tola akipan te; a khangte ua mi hat hangsante: David damlaia a zah uh sing nih leh sang nih leh za guk ahi uh.
3. Huan Uzzi tapate; Izrahia: huan Izrahia tapate; Mikael, Obadia, Joel, Issia, nga: avek un mipipente ahi uh.
4. Huan amau toh, a pipute uh inkote danin, a khangte un, kidouna dinga sepaih pawlte ahi uh, sing thum leh sang guk; ji leh tapa tampi a nei ngal ua.
5. Huan Isakar inkote tengteng laka, mi hat hangsante, a unaute uh a vek ua khangthusutnaa sutin, sang sagih leh sawmgiar ahi.
6. Benjamin tapate; Bela, Beker, Jediael, thum.
7. Huan Bela tapate; Ezbon, Uzzi, Uzziel, Jerimoth, Iri, nga; pipute inkote lupente, mi hat hangsante; huan khangthusutnaa simin sing nih leh sang nih leh sawmthum leh li ahi uh.
8. Huan Beker, tapate; Zemira, Joas, Elizer, Elioenai, Omri, Jeremoth, Abija, Anathoth, Alemeth. Hiaite tengteng Beker tapate ahi uh.
9. Huan khangthu sutnaa sim ahi ua, a khangte uh dungjuiin, a pipute uh inkote lupente, mi hat hangsante, sing nih leh za nih ahi uh.
10. Huan Jediael tapate; Bilhan: huan Bilhan tapate; Jeus, Benjamin, Ehud, Kenaan, Zethan, tarsis, Ahisahar.
11. Hiaite tengteng Jediael tapate ahi uh, a pipute uh inkote lupente dungjuiin, mi hat hangsan, sing khat leh sang sagih leh za nih ahi uh, kidouna adia sepaih pawla kuankhe thei dingte.
12. Suppim leng Huppim, Ir tapate, Husim, Aher tapate.
13. Naphtali tapate; Jahziel, Guni, Jezer, Sallum, Bilhah tapate.
14. Manassi tapate, Asriel, a jiin a suan: (a mei Aram numeiin Gilead pa Makir a suang hi;
15. Huan Makirin Huppim leh Suppim sanggamnu a min Maak jiin pi a; ) huan a nihna min Zelophehadin tanute a nei hi.
16. Huan Maak Makir jiin tapa a neia, a minin Peres a saa; huan a unaupa min Seres ahi; huan a tapate Ulam leh Rakem ahi uh.
17. Huan Ulam tapate; Bedan. Hiaite Gilead tapate ahi uh Makir tapa, Manassi tapa.
18. Huan a sanggamnu Hammoieketin Ishbod, Abiezer, Mahlah a nei a.
19. Huan Semid tapate Ahian, Sekem, Likhi, Aniam ahi uh.
20. Huan Ephraim tapate; Suthelah, Bered a tapa, Tahath a tapa, Elead a tapa, Tahath a tapa,
21. Huan Zabad a tapa, Suthel a tapa, Ezer, Elead, a gante uh la mang dinga a hongpai suk gamsunga om Gath miten a thatta uh.
22. Huan a pa uh Ephraimin ni tampi a suna, huchiin a unaute amah lungmuan dingin a hong uh.
23. Huan a ji kiangah a hoha, nau a paia, tapa a neia, a insung a tuahsiat jiakin a min dingin Beria a sata hi.
24. Huan a tanu Sira ahi a, Beth-horon nuainung leh tungtung leh Uzzen-sera lamnu.
25. Huan Repha a tapa ahi, Reseph leng; Reseph tapa Tela, Tahan a tapa;
26. Ladan a tapa, Ammihud a tapa, Elisam a tapa;
27. Nun a tapa, Josua a tapa.
28. Huan a neihte uleh a tennate uh Bethel leh huaia khuate ahi, suahlamah Naaran, tumlamah Gezer, huaia khopite toh, huaia Azzah leh khopite tanin:
29. Huan Manassi tate gamgi chinah, Beth-sean leh, a khopite, Taanak leh a khopite, Megiddo leh a khopite, Dor leh a khopite, Hiaite ah Israel tapa Joseph tate a teng uhi.
30. Aser tapate; Imna, Isva, Isvi, Beria, Sera a sanggamnu uh.
31. Huan Beria tapate, Heber, Malkiel, Birzaith pa.
32. Huan Heberin Japhlet, Somer, Hotham, Sua a sanggamnu uh a suang a.
33. Huan Japhlet tapate; Pasak, Bimhal, Asvat. Hiaite Japhlet tate ahi uh.
34. Huan Semer tapate; Ahi, Rohga, Jehubba, Aram.
35. Huan Helem a unaupa tapate; Zopha, Imna, Seles, Amal.
36. Zopha tapate; Sua, Hernephar, Sual, Beri, Imra;
37. Bezer, Hod Samma, Silsa, Ithran, Beera.
38. Haun Jether tapate; Jephunne, Pispa, Ara.
39. Huan Ulla tapate; Ara, Hanniel, Rizia.Hiaite tengteng Aser tate ahi uh, pipute inkote lupente, mihat, tel, hangsan, lalte laka lalte. Huan kidouna nasepna dinga Khangthusutnaa simin amau zah sing nih sang guk ahi uhi.
40. Hiaite tengteng Aser tate ahi uh, pipute inkote lupente, mihat, tel, hangsan, lalte laka lalte. Huan kidouna nasepna dinga Khangthusutnaa simin amau zah sing nih sang guk ahi uhi.
