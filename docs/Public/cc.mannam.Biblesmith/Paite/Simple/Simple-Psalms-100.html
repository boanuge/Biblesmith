1. Gam tengteng aw, TOUPA pahtawiin nuamsa takin kikou un.
2. Kipak kawmin TOUPA na sem unla: lasa kawmin a maah hongpai un.
3. TOUPA tuh Pathian ahi chih thei un: honbawlpa amah ahi a, amaha I hi ua; a mite leh a gantatna muna belamte I hi.
4. Kipahthu gen kawmin a kongkhak ah lut unla, phat kawmin a biakin huangsung intul ah lut un: a kiangah kipathu gen unla, a min phat un.TOUPA a hoih ngala: a chitna tuh khantawnin a om ding hi: a muanhuaidan tuh suan tengteng tanin a om ding hi.
5. TOUPA a hoih ngala: a chitna tuh khantawnin a om ding hi: a muanhuaidan tuh suan tengteng tanin a om ding hi.
