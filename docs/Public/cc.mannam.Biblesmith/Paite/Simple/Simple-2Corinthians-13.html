1. Hiaipen na kiang a ka hong a a thum veina ding ahi. Theihpih nih hiam, thum hiam kamah thu chiteng hihkipin a om ding.
2. Ka genkholtaa, a nihveinaa na kiang ua ka om laia ka gen bangin, tun ah na kiang ua om louin, tumalama thil hihkhialte kiangah leh midang tengteng kiangah ka gen khol non dek hi, ka hong nawn leh ka hawi kei ding, chih;
3. Kris keimaha thugen jelpa pen zeetna na zon jiak un, amah jaw noumau lamah a hatlou keia, na lak uah thil hihthei tak ahi jaw hi.
4. Amah jaw hatlouhna jiakin kilhdenin a om ahi a, himahleh, Pathian thilhihtheihna jiakin a hing gige ahi. Kou leng amahah ka hat kei uhi, himahleh, noumau lama Pathian thilhihtheihna jiakin a kiangah ka hing samding uh.
5. Ginna ah na omna uhiam chih noumau ngeingei ki enchian jel un. Ahihkeileh, noumau ah Jesu Kris a om chih noumau ngeingeiin na thei chian kei ua hia? deihlouh na hih kei ngal uleh.
6. Kou tuh deihlouin ka om kei uh chih na thei lai ding un ka gingta hi.
7. Huan, thil himhim na hihkhelh louhna ding un Pathian kiangah ka ngen jel uh; huai tuh koute deihhuaia ka lat theihna ding uh ahi keia, nouten a kilawm peuhmah na hih theihna ding uh ahi jaw, kou jaw deih louh bangin om mah le ung.
8. Thutak gum ding lou injaw, thutak dou dingin bangmah ka hih theikei uhi.
9. Koute ka hatlouh lai un leng noute na hat un ka kipak uhi; hiai thumnaa ka nget uh ahi: noumau hoih lama na kibawlphat uh.
10. Huaijiakin hihse ding hi lou a bawlhoih ding jaw a, Toupan thuneihna honpiak bang jelin, na kiang ua ka om chianga nakpi taka ka hontaihilh louhna dingin, na kiang ua ka om louh laiin hiai thu ka gelh ahi.
11. A tawppenin, unaute aw, kipak takin om un, Na omdan uh bawlhoih unla; konngetna limsak unla, khamuang takin om unla; lungsim munkhat pu unla, lunglualtakin om unla; huchiin itna leh lemna Pathian na kiang ua om ding ahi.
12. Tawp siangthouin chibai kibuk un.
13. Mi siangthou tengtengin chibai a honbuk uhi.Toupa Jesu Kris hehpihna, Pathian itna, Kha Siangthou kithuahpihna na vek ua kiangah om hen. Amen.
14. Toupa Jesu Kris hehpihna, Pathian itna, Kha Siangthou kithuahpihna na vek ua kiangah om hen. Amen.
