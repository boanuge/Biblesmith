1. Huan, lungke louin a thum gige ding uh ahi chih theihna dingin akiang uah gentehna a gen a:
2. Koilai khua hiamah thukhenmi khat a oma, huai min Pathian a lau keia, mihing leng a khawksa kei;
3. huan, huai khuaah meithai khat a oma; aman, Ka lang tungah phu honlaksak in, chiin, a kiangah a hoh zihzeh a.
4. Huan, amah tuh sawt khop a ut keia; himahleh, a nungin a lungsimin, Pathian lau louin mihing khawksa kei mah leng;
5. hiai meithaiin honhihbuai jiakin a phu ka lak sak mai ding, huchilouin jaw, a hong zihzeh leh a honhih gim ek kha ding, a chi a.
6. Huan, Toupa mahin, Thukhenmi diktatlou thugen ja un.
7. A tunguah dohzou tadih mahleh leng a mitel, a sun a jana amah samte phu tuh Pathianin a lak sak kei dia hia?
8. Ka honhilh ahi, a phu uh a lak sak pah ding. Himahleh Mihing Tapa a hongpai hun chiangin leitungah tuabang ginna a honmu nadiam? a chi a.
9. Huan, mi diktat hih kigingta a, mi dang tengteng musit kuate hiam kiangah hiai gentehna thu leng a gen a:
10. Mi nih Pathian biakin ah thum dingin a hoh tou ua; khat tuh Pharisai ahi a, khat tuh siahkhonmi ahi.
11. Pharisai tuh a dinga, amah kiain hichibangin a thum a: Pathian, mi dang, a negute, dikloute, angkawmte bang ka hih louh jiak leh, hiai siahkhonmi bang leng ka hih louh jiakin na tungah ka kipak hi.
12. Nipikal khat tengin nihvei an ka ngawl jel; ka neih teng sawm ah khat ka pe jela, a chi a.
13. Siahkhonmin bel gamla taka dingin, van lam leng a dak ngam keia, Pathian, kei mi khial, ka tungah zahngai in, chiin, a awm a kitum a.
14. Ka honhilh ahi, a masa sangin huai mipen tuh siam tangin a in ah a pai kheta. Kingaithupi peuh a niam ding ua, kingainiam tuh pahtawiin a om ding, a chi a.
15. Huan, a khoih dingin a kiangah a naungekte uh a honpi ua; nungjuiten bel huai a muh tak un a natai ua.
16. Himahleh, Jesun amau a sama, Naupang neute ka kiangah honpai sak unla, kham kei un, Pathian gam huchibang mite a ahi ngala.
17. Chihtaktakin ka honhilh ahi, Kuapeuh Pathian gam naupang neuin a kipahpih banga kipahpih lou tuh a sungah alut keihial ding, a chi a.
18. Huan, vaihawmmi kuahiamin, a kiangah, Sinsakpa hoih, khantawn hinna tanna dingin bang ka hih dia? chiin, a donga.
19. Huan, Jesun a kiangah, Bangdia hoih honchi? mi khat kia ngallou kuamah hoih a om kei, huai tuh Pathian ahi.
20. Thupiakte na thei vo oi, Angkawm ken, Tualthat ken, Gu ken, Hek ken, Na nu leh pa pahtawi in, chihte, a chi a.
21. Huan, aman, Huai thu tengteng ka naupan chik a kipan ka jui jel, achia.
22. Huan, Jesun huai a jakin a kiangah, Tuin leng thil khat na sam hi; na neih tengteng juak inla, tagahte kiangah hawm in, huchiin vanah gou na nei ding; huan, hongpai inla, honjui in, a chi a.
23. Aman bel, huai thute a jakin a lungkham mahmah a, a hauh mahmah jiakin.
24. Huchiin, Jesun amah tuh a ena, Sum haute Pathian gama lut haksa na e:
25. Mi sumhau Pathian gama lut sangin sangawngsau phim bila lut suak a nuam jaw ahi, a chi a.
26. Huan, huai thu zaten, Huchi a hihin kua hotdamin a om thei dia? a chi ua.
27. Aman tuh, Mihing hih vual louh Pathian hih theih ahi, a chi a.
28. Huan, Peterin, ngai in, kou ka in uh leh lou pai sanin kon jui ua, a chi a.
29. Huan, aman, a kiang uah, Chih taktakin ka honhilh ahi, Kuapeuh Pathian gam jiaka, in hiam, ji hiam, unaute hiam, nu le pa hiam, tate hiam paisan photphot,
30. tu dam sung mahmahin a sanga tam jaw mu lou ding kuamah a om kei, hun hongtung lai dinga khantawna hinna toh, a chi a.
31. Huan, sawm leh nihte a kiangah api tuama, a kiang uah, Ngai un, Jerusalem khua a hoh tou ding i hi ua; jawlneite thu gelh tengteng Mihing Tapa tungah a tung ding.
32. Jentelte kiangah piakin a om ding a, nuihzabawlna din a zang ding uh, zumhuai takin a bawl ding ua, chil a siat ding uh;
33. Huan, a vo ding ua, a hihlum ding uh; huan, ni thum niin a thou nawn ding, a chi a.
34. Huan, huai thute himhim a theisiam kei ua, a thu gen tuh a lak uah selin a oma, bang gen ahia chih leng a theikei uh.
35. Huan, hichi ahi a, Jeriko khua a tun kuanin, mittaw kuahiam lam geiah khut dohin a na tu a;
36. huai min tuh mipi pai ging a ja a, bangchi thu a hitaa chih a donga.
37. Huan amau, Nazaret Jesu a pai ahi chih a hilh ua.
38. Huchiin, aman, Jesu, David Tapa, ka tungah zahngai in, chiin, a kikou a.
39. Huan, a masaten dai dingin a tai ua, himahleh, David Tapa, honhehpih in, chiin, a kinak kou semsem a.
40. Huchiin, Jesu a dinga, a kianga honpi dingin thu a pia a;
41. huan, a kiang a hongtun in, Na tungah bangchia ka hih ahia na deih? a chi a, a na donga. Huan, aman, Toupa, muh theih ka ut, a chi a.
42. Huan, Jesun a kiangah, Mu thei ta in, na ginna na dampih ahi, a chi a.Huan, thakhatin a mu thei pahtaa, Pathian phat kawm jelin amah a juita a; huan, mi tengtengin, huai a muh un, Pathian a phat uh.
43. Huan, thakhatin a mu thei pahtaa, Pathian phat kawm jelin amah a juita a; huan, mi tengtengin, huai a muh un, Pathian a phat uh.
