1. Huchiin, Kris toh kaihthoh khawma na omtak ngal uleh, tunglama Pathian taklama tu Kris omnaa thil omte tuh zong un,
2. Tung lama thil omte ah na lungsim uh nga un, leia thil omtea nga louin.
3. Na sita ua, tuin na hinna uh Pathian ah Kris toh selin a om hi.
4. Kris i hinna uh a hongkilak chiangin nou leng amah kiangah thupiin na kilak sam ding uhi.
5. Huchiin, noumaua leilam thil deihna omte hihlum un, kingaihnate, nitnate, huknate, utna giloute, huaihamnate; huaihamna tuh milimbiakna ahi ngala.
6. Huai thilte jiakin ahi, Pathian thangpaihna tuh thumanglou tate tunga a tun nak;
7. Nou leng tumalama huai thiltea na nuntak lai un, huaite ah na om jel uhi.
8. Tun jaw, nou leng hiai tengteng, hehnate, thangpainate, huatnate, gensiatnate, na kam ua pau zahmohte koih mang un.
9. Juau kihilh tuah kei un, mihing lui tuh a thilhih toh na suahkheta ua.
10. Mihing thakin na kivanta ngal ua; huai mihing thak tuh a siampa batpih banga theihnaa siamthakin a om lellel hi.
11. Huai ah tuh Grik mi leh Juda mi leh Juda mi, zeksum leh sumlouh, melmak mite, Skithia mite, bawite, bawiloute leng a om theih kei; Kris tuh bangkim hijawin, bangkim ah a om behlap lai hi.
12. Huchiin, Pathian mi tel, misiangthou leh deih takte bang in lainatna lungtang te, migitnate, kingaihniamna te, thunuailutnate, thuakzohnatein kivan un.
13. Kua peuhin kua tungah leng thubuai nei bang hile uchin, kithuakzou chiat in, kingaidam tuah unla, Toupan a honngaihdamtak bangin, kingaidam sam un.
14. Huan, huai tengteng tungah leng itna kisilhthuah un; huaiin thil bangkim kituak takin a gakkhawm hi.
15. Huan, Kris khamuannain na lungtang uah vaihawm hen, huchibang hi dingin pumpi khata sap na hi him uhi; kipahna dan leng thei un.
16. Kris thu tuh a hauhna tengtoh noumau ah omsak kiihkeih un, pilna chitenga kisinsak leh kihilh tuaha oma kipahna lungtang toh pathian kianga phatna la bang, Pathian la bang, khalam la bang saa omin.
17. Huan, na hih peuhmah uh, thugen ah hiam, thilhih ah hiam amah jiaka Pa Pathian kianga kipahthugen kawmin Toupa Jesu minin hih vek un.
18. Jite aw, Toupa ah a kilawm hun bang jelin na pasalte uh kiang ah kingainiam un.
19. Pasalte aw, na jite uh it unla, a lak uah hanhat kei un.
20. Naupangte aw, bangkim ah na nu u leh na pate uh thumang un, huai tuh Toupa pahtak lam ahi.
21. Pate aw, na tate uh hihnuak kei un, huchilou injaw a lunglel kha ding uh.
22. Sikhate aw, saa na heutute uh thu bangkim ah mang un, mihing hihkipakte lel banga mit nasem hi louin, lungtang sakhata Toupa kihta kawmin.
23. Na hih peuhmah uh, mihing ading banga hih louin, Toupa ading banga hihin, kilawp takin hih un;
24. A itna, gouluah ding tuh, Toupa akipanin na mu ding uh chih theiin, Toupa Kris nasem na hi uhi.Thil dik lou hihpan a thil diklou hih man a mu nawn ding hi; mi khentuamna himhim a om ngal keia.
25. Thil dik lou hihpan a thil diklou hih man a mu nawn ding hi; mi khentuamna himhim a om ngal keia.
