1. Huan, Zipte bel Gibea ah Saula kiangah a hoh ua, David gamdai china Hakila tangah a bu ahi kei maw? chiin a vagen ua.
2. Huchiin Saula a thou a, Zip gamdai ah Israel mite lak akipan telkhiak chiat mi sang thum piin Zip gamdai ah David zong dingin a hoh sukta uh.
3. Huan, Saulain gamdai chin, lamkalah Hakila tangah panmun a bawla. David bel gamdai ah a om theka; huan, Saula amah zongin gamdai ah a hong chih a thei a.
4. Huaijiakin Davidin enkhemite a sawla, Saula a hong taktak chih a thei telta hi.
5. Huan, David a thou a, Saula panmun bawlna ah a hoha: huan, Davidin Saula leh a sepaih heutu, Ner tapa Abner lupna a mu a: Saula bel kangtalai umsungah a luma, a kimah miten ommun a bawl uh.
6. Huchiin Davidin Hit mi Abimelek leh Joab unau Zerui tapa Abisai kiangah, Saula panmunah kua ahia ka kianga hong ding? Chiin a gen a. Huan, Abisaiin, kei na kiangah ka hongsuk sam ding, a chi a.
7. Huchiin David leh Abisai mipite omna ah janin a vahoh ua: huan, ngaiin, Saula bel kangtalai umsungah a naluma, a lu lamah a teipi lei ah a siat tala: Abner leh mite bel a kimah a lum ua.
8. Huan, Abisaiin David kiangah, tuniin Pathianin na melma na khuta honpia ei ve; huaijiakin teipiin khatveiin leiah kon sun den suk ding aw? ka sun tuan nawn kei ding, a chi a.
9. Himahleh Davidin Abisai kiangah, hihmang thang ken, TOUPA sathau nilh tunga khut khaa mohlou kua a om theia? A chi a.
10. Huan, David mahin, TOUPA hinna louin ka gen hi. TOUPAN a tungah khut kha na ve: a hihkeileh leng a sih ni a hongtungna ding; ahihkeileh leng, kidouna ah a hoh dia, a mangthang mai ding.
11. TOUPA sathau nilh tungah khut ka kha het kei ding: a lu kiang china teipi leh tuithawl ahihleh la lechin ake, huan I pai ding, a chi a.
12. Huchiin Davidin Saula lu kiangchina teipi leh tuithawl a laa, a pai nawnta ua, kuamahin a mu kei ua, kuamahin a phawk un a khanglou sam kei uh: TOUPAN nuamtaka a ihmut sak jiakin a ihmu vek uhi.
13. Huchiin David bel gal khat lamah a hoha, gamla kuamah mualvum ah a dinga; a kikal uh gamlapi ahi.
14. Huan, Davidin mipite leh Ner tapa Abner bel, Abner, na hondawng kei dia hia? chiin a sama. Huchiin Abnerin a dawnga, Kumpipa sam vial kua na hia? a chi a.
15. Huan, Davidin Abner kiangah, mi hangsan na hi kei maw? Israelte lakah nang bang kua a oma? Bangchidan ahia kumpipa na pu na ven louh? Kumpipa na pu hihmang dingin kua hiam khat a hongpai hi.
16. Na thilhih dan a hoih kei. TOUPA hinna louin ka gen hi, TOUPA sathau nilh na pu na ven hoih louh jiakin si mai dia kilawm na hi. Kumpipa lu china teipi leh tuithawl om koia om a hitaa en him dih, a chi a.
17. Huan, Saulain David aw a theia, tua na aw hia, ka tapa David? A chi a. Huan, Davidin, he toupa, kumpipa aw, ka aw ahi a, a chi a.
18. Huan, bangjiakin ahia ka puin a sikha a delh? Bang thil ka hiha ahia? a khut ah thil gilou banga hia om?
19. Huaijiakin toupa, ka puin a sikha thu ngaikhia hen. Kei dou dinga na lungtang tokthoupa TOUPA a hihleh thillat pom teitei henla; mihing tate a hih uleh bel TOUPA ah hamse thuakin om uhen, pai inla, pathian dangte na semin, chia TOUPA gou ka luah theihlouhna dinga tunia hondelh mang lah ahi ngal ua.
20. Huaijiakin ka sisan TOUPA muh phak louhin leiah luang kei heh; vengkeng gem dinga tang khawnga mi a gamvak bang un Israel kumpipa uihik leltak zong dinga pai khia ei ve, a chi a.
21. Huchiin Saulain, ka nakhialta hi; ka tapa David, hongpai nawn in: tunia na mitmuha ka hinna a manphat mahmah jiakin bangmah himhim ka hihta kei ding hi: ngai in, thil haihuai mahmah ka nahihtaa, ka nak khelh mahmah hi, a chi a.
22. Huan, Davidin a dawnga, toupa aw, teipi en dih, tangval khat hong henla hong la hen.
23. Huan, TOUPAN mi chih a diktat man uleh a muanhuai man uh a pe sina: tuniin TOUPAN ka khut ah nang a honpe naa, TOUPA sathau nilh tungah ka khut ka lik nuam keia ahi.
24. Huan, ngaiin, tunia na hinna ka honmantamsak mahmah bangin TOUPAN ka hinna hon mantamsak henla, mangbatna tengteng akipan hon honkhe leh, a chi a.Huchiin Saulain David kiangah, ka tapa David, vualjawlin omin; thilhihtheitak in na om dinga, na zou jel ding, a chi a. Huchiin David a paia, huan, Saula a mun lamah a pai nawnta hi.
25. Huchiin Saulain David kiangah, ka tapa David, vualjawlin omin; thilhihtheitak in na om dinga, na zou jel ding, a chi a. Huchiin David a paia, huan, Saula a mun lamah a pai nawnta hi.
