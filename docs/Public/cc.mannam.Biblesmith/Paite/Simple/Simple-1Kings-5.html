1. Huan, Tura kumpipa Hiramin Solomon kiangah amite a sawla; a pa munah kumpipa dingin amah sathau a nilh uh chih lah a naja ngala; Hiram lah David it gigepa ahi hima.
2. Huan, Solomonin Hiram kiangah.
3. Ka pa David in Toupan a khepek nuaia a koih masiah a kim a vela mun chih mual chiha kidounate jiakin Toupa a Pathian min dingin in a lam theikei chih na thei thei a
4. Tuin bel Toupa ka Pathianin munchih mualchihah muanna a honna nei sakta a; melmate a om nawn kei ua, vangsiatna leng a om sam kei.
5. Huan, ngai dih, Toupan ka pa David kiangah, Na tapa, na sikha na laltutphah ka luahsak dingin ka min adin in a honlam ding, chia a na gensa bangin, Toupa ka Pathianmin dinga in lam ka tum hi.
6. Huchiin, Lebanon tanga sidar singte honphuk sak dingin thu pia inla, ka sikhate leng na sikhate lakah a hongtel tei ding uh; huan, na sikhate loh a hihleh na chih peuhmah bangin ka honpe jel ding: Zidon mite banga sing phuk siam ka lak uah kuamah a om kei uh chih na thei ngala, chiin ma a sawla.
7. Huan, hichi ahi a, Hiramin Solomon thu a jakin a kipak mahmah a, Hiai mi tampi tunga vaihawm dinga tapa pil tak David pepa Toupa tuniin phatin om hen, achia.
8. Huan, Hiramin Solomon kiangah, na hon thukhah ka naja a; sidar sing thu leh meilah sing thu na deih peuhmah kana hih ding.
9. Ka sikhaten Lebanon tang a kipanin tuipa phain a honla suk ding uh; Huan, non mun seh saka tuipia tolh dingin ka gak lom sakdinga, huan, huai laiah ka phel sak dinga, nang nana la ding hi: huan, nang leng ka inkote nek ding tumin ka deih non awnghuksak tei ding a kei deh, chiin mi a sawl hi.
10. Huchiin, Hiramin sidar sing leh meilah sing Solomon a deih peuhmah a piaa.
11. Huan, Solomonin Hiram bel a inkote nek dingin buh pota sing nih leh sathau suk pota sawmnih a pia: huai bel Solomonin kum tenga Hiram a piak sek dan ahi.
12. Huan, Toupan achiam bang takin Solomon pilna a piaa: huan, Hiram leh Solomon kalah kilemna aoma; a nih un thu a khung uh.
13. Huan, kumpipa Solomonin Israelte tengteng laka mi nasem dinga goih teitei pawl a bawla; goih teitei pawl mi sing thum ahi uh.
14. Lebanon ah kha khata kikheng jelin sing kha a sawl jela; kha khat Lebanon ah a om ua, kha nih amau inlamah: Adoniram bel nasem dia goih teiteite heutu a hi
15. Huan, Solomonin thil po mi sing sagih leh, suangsek mi sing giat tang khawngah a neia;
16. Huaite sim louin Solomonin a nasep tunga heutu tuam mawng, nasep vengmi sang thum leh za thum anei lai hi.
17. Huan, kumpipa thupiakin in suangphum suang seksaa lep dingin suang lian pipi, suangman tam pipi a hon pua uh.Huan, inlammite, Solomon mite leh Hiram mite leh Gebal miten suangte a sek ua, a in lamna ding un sing leh suangte a bawl uh..
18. Huan, inlammite, Solomon mite leh Hiram mite leh Gebal miten suangte a sek ua, a in lamna ding un sing leh suangte a bawl uh..
