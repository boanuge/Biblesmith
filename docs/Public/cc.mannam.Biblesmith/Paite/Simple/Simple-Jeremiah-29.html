1. Huan, Jawlnei Jeremiain Jerusalem akipana saltang upate, siampute, jawlneite, mi dangte, Nebukadnezzarin Jerusalem akipana Babulon a pi khit, a dam sunte kianga a laikhak khak thu hiaite ahi:
2. (Kumpipa Jekonia te, kumpinu te, michilgehte, Juda leh Jerusalem heutute, mi khutsiamte, siksekmite Jerusalem a paikhiat nung ua; )
3. Shalhan tapa, Elasa leh Hilkia tapa, Gemaria te khut ah a khak hi: (huaite bel Juda kumpipa Zedekiain Babulon gama Babulon kumpipa Nebukadnezzar kianga a sawlte ahi ih: )
4. Israel Pathian, sepaihte TOUPAN saltang tengteng, Jerusalem akipan Babulon sal dia ka pisak sate kiangah hichiin a chi:
5. Inte lam unla, huaiah teng unla, huante bawl unla, a gahte ne unla, jite nei unla, tanute tapate nei unla;
6. Na tapate uh adingin jite neihsak unla, na tanute uh pasal neisak unla, tanu tapa a nei h theihna ding un: huaiah hong pung unla, kiam lam nawt kei un:
7. Sal dia ka hontansakna khopi hoihna ding zong unla, TOUPA kiang ah ngetsak un: amah hoihna ah nou leng hoihna na mu sin ngal ua,
8. Israel Pathian, sepaihte TOUPAN hichiin a chi: Na lak ua jawlneite leh aisansiamten hon khem kei uhenla, a mang a mat uh leng ngaike sam kei un.
9. Ka min sialin juauthu na kiang ah a gen uhi: kei sawl ahi kei ua, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
10. TOUPAN hichiin a chi ngala, Babulon adingin kum sawmsagih a chih tak in ka hong veh dinga, na kiang ua ka thichiam hoihtak ka tungsak dinga, hiai munah ka honkik nawn sak ding.
11. Nou lama ka lungsim putdan lah ka kithei ngala, na hun nanung tawp ua lametna honpe dingin thil hoihlou hihlouin hoihna ngaihtuah ka hi.
12. Huan, non kan ding ua, ka kiangah nong thum ding ua, ka hon ngaikhe ding.
13. Huan, non zong ding ua, na lungtang tengteng ua na honzon hun chiang un non mu ding uh.
14. Huan, na hon muh un ka om dia, TOUPA thu pawtin a chi, na omdan uh ka hei dinga, nam tengtengte laka ka hondalhsakna mun akipan ka honpi khawm nawn ding, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi; sal dia ka hon matsakna mun ngeiah ka hontungsak nawn ding hi, chih.
15. TOUPAN Babulon gamah leng jawlnei hinbawlsaka, na chi ngal ua.
16. TOUPAN David tutphahah kumpipa tungtang thu leh hiai khawpia teng mipi tengteng, sal dia pe tel louh, na unaute uh tungtang thu ah hichiin a chi ngala:
17. Sepaihte TOUPAN hichiin a chi: Ngai un, a tunguah namsau te, kial te, hipi tein ka delhjui dinga, theipi hut, a hoihlouh mahmah jiaka nek tuak het lou bang maiin ka bawl ding.
18. Huan, namsau te, kial te, hipi tein ka delhjui dinga, leitunga lalna gam tengteng lakah thil mulkimhuai takin ka bawl dinga, amau ka dalhsakna nam tengteng lakah hamse thuak din, lamdang sak chiat din, tek khum tektek dingin gensiat hi dingin ka bawl ding.
19. Jing pia thoua ka nasem, jawlnei zanga ka thu na kiang ua ka hontut khawk a sak louh jiak un. TOUPA thu pawtin a chi: na ngaikhe nuam ngal kei ua, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
20. Huaijiakin nou Jerusalem akipana Babulona ka sawl, saltang tengteng aw, TOUPA thu ngaikhia un.
21. Israel Pathian, sepaihte TOUPAN Kolai tapa, Ahab leh Maaseia tapa, Zedekia ka min siala na kaing ua juauthu genmite tungtang thua ah hichiin a chi: Ngai un, Babulon kumpipa Nebukadnezzar khut ah ka mansak sin hi; na mit muh ngei un a that ding:
22. Amau thu ah Babulon gama Juda saltang tengteng in, Babulon kumpipan meia a em Zedekia leh Ahab bang maiin TOUPAN na tungah hih heh, chih hamse lohnain a neih lohding uh.
23. Israel gamah thil haihuai tak a hih ua, a inveng uh jite ang a kawm ua, ka min sialin ka thupiak louhin juauthu a gen jiak un; lei a theipa leh a theihpihpa ka hi, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
24. Huan, Nehelam mi, Semaia tungtang thu ah nang hicia na gen dian ahi.
25. Israel Pathian, sepaihte TOUPAN hichiin a chi a, Jerusalem mipi tengtengte kiangah nangmah min mahmahj in laikhak na khaka.
26. TOUPAN siampu Jehodia sikin siampu dian nang a honbawla, TOUPAin kemmi na hi ua, mi hai, jawlnei kichi peuhte kemte, sik kolte na butsak ding ahi.
27. Huchiin bangdia Anathot khuaa mi Jeremia, na kaing ua jawlnei kichi na taihilh louh uh?
28. Babulon gamah i kian uah, Setanna ding a sawt dinag, in khawng lam unla, hiaiah teng unla, a gahte ne un, chih thi a honkhak hi, chiin.
29. Huan, siampu Zephaniain huai laikhak jawlnei Jeremia jakin a sim a.
30. Huan, Jeremia kiangah TOUPA thu a hongtungta.
31. Saltangte tengteng kiangah, Nehelam mi Semaia tungtang thi ah TOUPAN hichiin a a chi: Ka sawl het louhin Semaia leh a suante ka gawt dinga, hiai mite lakah mi khat lel leing a neikei ding ka mite dinga thil hoih ka hih ding mu ding: TOUPA tungah helna thu pawt ahi, chiin khak in, achia.Huaiziakin TOUPAN hichiin a chi: Ngaiun, Nehelam mi Semaia leh a suante ka gawt dinga, hiai mite lakah mi khat lel leng a neikei ding, ka mite dinga thil hoih ka hih ding mu ding: TOUPA tungah helna thu a gen jiakin, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi, chiin khak in achia.
32. Huaiziakin TOUPAN hichiin a chi: Ngaiun, Nehelam mi Semaia leh a suante ka gawt dinga, hiai mite lakah mi khat lel leng a neikei ding, ka mite dinga thil hoih ka hih ding mu ding: TOUPA tungah helna thu a gen jiakin, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi, chiin khak in achia.
