1. Huan hiaite pi manga om koltanna akipana pai tou, biala tate, Babulon lama kumpipa Nebukadnezzarin a pi mang, Jerusalem leh Juda a, mi chih amah khua chiata hongkik nawnte ahi uh;
2. Zerubababel, Josua, Nehemia, Seraina, Reelaia, Mordekai, Bilsan, Mispar, Bigvai, Rehum, Baana toh hongtonte, Israel mite laka pasal zah:
3. Paros tate sang nih za khat sawmsagih leh nih.
4. Sephatia tatte, za thum sawmsagih leh nih.
5. Arah tate, za sagih sawmsagih leh nga.
6. Pahath Moab tate, Jesua leh Joab tate, sang nih za giat leh sawm leh nih.
7. Elam tate, sang khat za nih sawmnga leh li.
8. Zattu tate, za kua sawmli leh nga.
9. Zakkai tate, za sagih leh sawmguk.
10. Bani tate, za guk sawmli leh nih.
11. Bebai tate, za guk sawmnih leh thum.
12. Azgad tate, sang khat za nih sawmnih leh nih.
13. Adonikam tate, za guk sawmguk leh guk.
14. Bigvai tate, sang nih sawmnga leh guk.
15. Adin tate, za li sawmnga leh li.
16. Ater tate, Hezekia tate, sawmkua leh giat.
17. Bezai tate, za thum sawmnih leh thum.
18. Jora tate, za khat leh sawmlehnih.
19. Hasum tae, za nih sawmnih leh thum.
20. Gibbar tate, sawmkualeh nga.
21. Beth-lehem tate, za khat sawmnih leh thum.
22. Netophah mite, sawmnga leh guk.
23. Anathot mite, za khat sawmnih leh giat.
24. Azmavet tate, sawmli leh nih.
25. Kiriath-arim Kephira, Beer-oth tate, za sagih leh sawmli leh thum.
26. Rama leh Geba tate, za guk swamnih leh khat.
27. Mikma mite, za khat sawmnih leh nih.
28. Beth-el leh Ai mite, za nih sawmnih leh thum.
29. Nebo tate, sawmnga leh guk.
30. Magbis tate, za khat sawmnga leh guk.
31. Elam dang tate, sang khat za nih sawmnga leh li.
32. Harim tate, za thum leh sawmnih.
33. Lod, Hadid leh Ono tate, za sagih sawmnih leh nga.
34. Jeriko tate, za thum sawmli leh nga.
35. Sena tate, sang thum leh za guk leh sawmthum.
36. Siampute: Jedai tate, Jesua inkote, za kua sawmsagih leh thum.
37. Immer tate, sang khat sawmnga leh nih.
38. Peshur tate, sang khat za nih sawmli leh sagih.
39. Harim tate, sang khat leh sawmleh sagih.
40. Levi mite: Jesua leh Kadmiel tate, Hodavi tate, sawmsagih leh li.
41. Lasa mite: Asaph tate, za khat sawmnih leh giat.
42. Kongkhak ngak mite: Sallum tate, Ater tate, Talmon tate, Akkub tate, Hatita tate, Sobai tate, a vek un za khat sawmthum leh kua.
43. Nithunum mite: Ziha tae, Hasupha tate, Tabbaoth tate;
44. Keros tate, Siaha tate, Padon tate;
45. Lebana tate, Hagaba tate, Akkub tate;
46. Hagab tate, Samlai tate, Hanan tate;
47. Giddel tate, Gahar tate Reaia tate;
48. Rezin tate, Nekoda tate, Gazzam tate;
49. Uzza tate, Pasea tate Besai tate;
50. Asan tate, Meunim tate, Nephisim tate;
51. Bakbuk tate, Hakupha tate, Harhur tate;
52. Bazlut tate, Mehida tate, Harsa tate;
53. Barko tate, Sesera tate, Tema tate;
54. Nezia tate, Hatipha tate,
55. Solomon sikhate tate: Sotai tate, Hassopheret tate, Peruda tate;
56. Jaala tate, Darkon tate, Giddel tate;
57. Sephatia tate, Hattil tate, Pokereth-hazzebaim tate, Ami tate.
58. Nithinim mite tengteng, Solomon sikhate tate, za thum sawmkua leh nih.
59. Huchiin hiaite Tel-melah, Tel-harsa, Cherub, Addan, leh Immer akipana paitoute ahi uh: himahleh a pipute inkote, a suan uh, Israel akipan ahi uhia chih a taklang theikei uh:
60. Delai tate, Tobia tate, Nekoda tate, za guk sawmnga leh nih.
61. Huan siampute tate: Habai tate, Hakkoz tate, Barzillai Gileadmi Barzillai tanute akipana ji pipa tate, amau min juia sakin a om hi.
62. Hiai khangthusutna a sim mite laka mingelhna a zong ua, himahleh a mu kei uh: huaijiak in buaha simin a om ua siamputna akipan dang koihin a om uhi.
63. Huan Tirsathain, siampu Urim leh Thummim toh a hongdin khiak mateng, thil siangthou penpente ne lou dingin amau a chi a.
64. Omkhawmte tengteng a vek un sing li sang nih za thum leh sawmguk ahia,
65. A pasal sikhate uleh a numei sikhate uh, sang sagih za thum sawm leh sagihte sim louin: huan pasal lasami leh numei lasami sang nih a nei uh.
66. A sakolte uh za sagih sawmthum leh guk ahi ua; sakolneute, za nih sawmli leh nga;
67. Sangawngsaute, za li sawmthum leh nga; a sabentung uh, sang guk za sagih leh sawmnih.
68. Huan pipute uh inkuantea lupente laka a khen, Jerusalem a om TOUPA ina a honglai ua ut taka Pathianin a muna ding dinga thillante:
69. Nasepna sumbawm ah a hihtheih dan un dangkaeng darik sing guk leh sang khat, dangka paun sangnga leh, siampute puansilh za khat.Huchiin siampute, Levi mite, mite laka a khenkhat leh lasamite leh, kongkhak ngakmite leh, Nithinim mite, a khopite uah a teng ua, Israel a khopite uah.
70. Huchiin siampute, Levi mite, mite laka a khenkhat leh lasamite leh, kongkhak ngakmite leh, Nithinim mite, a khopite uah a teng ua, Israel a khopite uah.
