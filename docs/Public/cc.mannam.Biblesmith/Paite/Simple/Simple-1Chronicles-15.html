1. Huchiin David khopi sungah Davidin inte a kibawla; huan Pathian bawm adingin mun a bawla, puanin a kaih sakta hi.
2. Huailaiin Davidin, Levi mite chihlouhngal kuamahin Pathian bawm a po ding uh ahi kei: TOUPAN lah amau Pathian bawm po ding leh khantawna amah na sepsak dingin amaute a natelta ngala, achia.
3. Huan a mun, a bawlsaksaa TOUPA bawm honla tou dingin, Davidin Jerusalem ah Israel tengteng a kaikhawmta hi.
4. Huchiin Davidin Aron tapate leh Levi mite a khawma:
5. Kohath tapate lakah; Uriel a pipen leh a unaute ja leh sawmnih:
6. Merrari tapate lakah; Asai a pipen leh a unaute za nih sawmnih:
7. Gersom tapate lakah: Joel a pipen leh a unaute ja leh sawmthum:
8. Elizaphan tapate lakah; Semai a pipen leh a unaute za nih:
9. Hebron tapate lakah; Eliel a pipen leh a unaute sawmgiat:
10. Uzziel tapate lakah; Amminadab a pipen leh a unaute ja leh sawmlehnih.
11. Huan Davidin Zadok leh Abiathar siampute, Levi mite, Uri, Asai, Joel, Semai, Eliel, leh Amminadab a sam saka,
12. A kiang uah, Noute Levi mite pipute inkote lupente na hi ua: kihihsiang unla, nou leh na unaute uh, mun ka bawlsak saa Israel Pathian, TOUPA bawm na lak theihna ding un.
13. A masapena na puak louh jiak un, TOUPA i Pathian un i tunguah thuchiam a bawla, hihdingdan dungjuiin lah amah i zong ngal kei ua, a chi a.
14. Huchiin siampute leh Levi mite, Israel Pathian, TOUPA bawm la tou dingin a kisiangsak uhi.
15. Huan Mosiin TOUPA thu banga thu a piak bangin Levi taten a liang jang uah huaia chiang tawntein Pathian bawm a jawngta uhi.
16. Huan Davidin Levi mi pipente kiangah a unaute uh lasamite tum gingtheihte toh, pekging guisawmneite leh kaihgingte leh dakkhutbette, ngaihtaka ging leh nuamsaa aw suah ding sep dingin a hilh hi.
17. Huchiin Levi miten Joel tapa Heman a sep ua; huan a unaute lakah, Asaph Berekia tapa; huan Merari tapate a unaute uh lakah, Kusai tapa Ethan;
18. Huan amau toh a unaute uh pawl nihnnate, Zekaria, Ben, Jaaziel, Semiramot, Jehiel, Unni, Eliab, Benaia, Maasei, Mattithia, Eliphelehu, Miknei, Obed-edom, Jeiel, kongkhakngakmite.
19. Huchiin lasamite, Heman, Asaph, Ethen te sehin a omta ua, ngaihtaka tum ding, dal dakkhutbette toh;
20. Huan Zekaria, Aziel, Semiramot, Jehiel, Unni, Eliab, Maasei, Benai, pekging guisawmneite Alamoth toh kituaka bawl toh;
21. Huan Mattithia, Eliphelehu, Minei, Obed-edom, Jeiel, Azazia, kaihgingte Seminit toh bawl, makai dingin.
22. Huan Kenania, Levi mite laka a pipen, lapuin a panga: a siam jiakin, la hilhin a pang hi.
23. Huan Berekia leh Elkana bawm adia kongkhak ngakmitein a om uhi.
24. Huan Sebania, Josaphat, Nethanel, Amasai, Zekaria, Benai, Eliezer, siamputen, Pathian bawm maah pengkulte a mut ua: huan Obededom leh Jehia bawm adinga kongkhakngakmitein a om uhi.
25. Huchiin David leh, Israel upate leh, sangte tunga heutute, Obed-edom in akipan TOUPA thukhunna bawm nuamna toh a vala tou dingin a kuanta ua;
26. Huan hichi ahi a, Pathianin TOUPA bawm jawng Levi mite a panpih laiin, amau bawngtal sagih leh belamtal sagihin a kithoih uhi.
27. Huan David puanmalngat hoih puannaktualin a kijema, huan bawm jawng Levi mite tengteng leh, lasamite leh, la heutupa Kenania lasamite toh: huan Davidin puanmalngat siampulian puannak a silh hi.
28. Huchibangin Israel tengtengin TOUPA thukhunna bawm kikou kawm leh, pengkul ging binei toh, pengkulte toh, dakkhutbette toh pekging guisawmneite leh kaihgingte ngaihtaka ging toh, a honla touta uhi.Huan hichi ahi a, TOUPA thukhunna bawm David khopi a hong tunin, Saul tanu Mikal tohlet ah a dak khiaa, David lam leh mawl a mutaa; a lungtangin a musitta hi.
29. Huan hichi ahi a, TOUPA thukhunna bawm David khopi a hong tunin, Saul tanu Mikal tohlet ah a dak khiaa, David lam leh mawl a mutaa; a lungtangin a musitta hi.
