1. Huan, Ikoniam khua ah Judate kikhopna in ah a lut tuak ua, thu a gen jel uh, huchiin Juda leh Grik tampiin leng a gingta hialta uh.
2. Himahleh, gingta nuamlou Judaten Jentelte lungsim a hihthou ua, unaute a hosak uh.
3. Huaijiakin, Toupa ah hangtaka thu genin huaiah sawt sim a om ua, aman tuh chiamtehna leh thillamdangte a khut ua hihsakin, a hehpih thu a theihsak jel hi.
4. Himahleh, khua a mipite a kilangta ua, a khenin Judate a gum ua, a khenin sawltakte a gum uh.
5. Huan, Jentelte, Judate a heutute uh toh sawltakte zahhuaitaka bawl leh suanga den tuma a hih dek laiun,
6. amau a na thei ua, Likaonia gam mah ah Listra leh Darbi khua ah leh, a kim gamah a tai mang ua;
7. huaiah Tanchin Hoih a gen jel uh.
8. Huan, Listra khua ah khe jaw kuahiam a tu a, a pian tung apat khebai him ahi a, khein a pai ngeikei hi.
9. Huai min Paula thugen a ngaikhia a; huan, Paulain a en gige a, damna dingin ginna a nei chih a theia, aw ngaihtakin, Na khein ding tang in, a chi a.
10. Huan, amah a tawma, khein a paita.
11. Huan, mipiten Paula thilhih a muh tak un, Likaonia Pauin, ngaihtakin, Pathiante mihing banga i kiang ua hongsukta uh hi e! a chi ua.
12. Huan, Barnaba, Jupiter, a chi ua, Paula bel, thugenpen a hihjiakin Merkuri, a chi uh.
13. Huan, Jupiter, siampu a biakin polama om tuh, bawngpate leh pak khigui limte tuh kongkhak kiang khawnga hontawiin, mipite toh biakna dingin oh a tumta a.
14. Himahleh sawltak Barnaba leh Paulain huai a jak un, a puan uh a botkek ua, mipi lakah a dianglut ua, a kikou ua.
15. Pute aw, hiai thilte bangdia hih na hi ua? Kou leng, noumau nuntakdan bang pu mihing ka hive ua le: hiai thil ginaloute khawng na kiheisan ua, van khawng, lei khawng, tuipi khawng, a sung ua om tengteng toh bawlpa Pathian hing kianga nong kaina ding un Tanchin Hoih kon tun uh ahi.
16. Huai Pathianin nidang laiin nam chih amau lam chiat zuih a phal a,
17. Himahleh, amah theihsakna om louin a kibawl kei hi, huchiin thil a hih hoih jela, van akipanin vuahtui khawng a honpia a, na lungtang uh nekding leh kipahnain a hihdim gige a kei hiam, a chi a.
18. Huan, huai thuin mipiten amaute biakna dia gan a goh ding uh a kham zou teitei hi.
19. Himahleh, Antiok leh Ikoniam khua a mi Juda kuate hiam huaiah a hong ua, mipite a khempukta uh; huchiin suangin Paula a deng ua, sia a gintak jiak un khua akipan a kaikheta ua.
20. Himahleh, nungjuite a kima a din lai un, a hongthou a, khua ah a lut nawnta hi. Huan, a jingin Barnaba toh Darbi khua ah a hohta ua.
21. Huan, huai khuate kiangah Tanchin Hoih a hilh ua, nungjui tampi a bawl nung un, Listra khua ah, Ikoniam khua ah, Antiok khua ah, a kiknawn jel uhi.
22. Gim tampi thuakin Pathian gamah i lut ding ahi him ahi, chiin, nungjuite lungsim a hihkip ua, ginna ah om gige dingin a hasuan jel uh.
23. Huan, saptuam pawl tengah upate a sehsak ua, anngawl kawma a thum khit un, a gin uh Toupa kiangah a kemsak jeljel uhi.
24. Huan, Pisidia gam a tawnsuak ua, Pamphilia gam a tung uh.
25. Huan, Perga khua a a thu a genkhit un Attalia khua ah a hoh suk ua;
26. huai akipanin longin a pawt ua, Antiok khua a hongtung nawnta ua, huai tuh a thilhih khitsa uh hih dinga unauten Pathian hehpihna kemsaka a pawtna uh ahi.
27. Huan, a tun nung un a saptuamte a sam khawm ua, Pathianin, amau zang a thil a hih tengteng leh, Jentelte dia ginna kongkhak a honsakdan khawng a hilh uh.Huan, nungjuite kiangah sawt sima omte uhi.
28. Huan, nungjuite kiangah sawt sima omte uhi.
