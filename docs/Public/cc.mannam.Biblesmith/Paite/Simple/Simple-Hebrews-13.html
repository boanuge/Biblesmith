1. Unau kiitna om jel hen,
2. Mikhualte tungah itna suah ding mangngilh kei un; huchibang hihin khenkhat in theilou in angelte ana zintun ngal ua.
3. Hen a awmte tuh hen hisam bangin thei gige unla; gimthuakte tuh sa a awmsam nahih ziakun thei gige un.
4. Mi tengteng lak ah kitenna pahtawi in awm henla, lupna leng hihbuah louh in awm hen; kingaite leh angkawmte tuh Pathian in a ngaihtuah dek ngala.
5. Na lungsim putdan uh tuh dangka deihtakna panglou hihen; na thil neih sun tunguah lungkim un; amah mah in, bangchikmah in kawn dawn lou kei ding a, paileng ka hon paisan kei hial ding, a chi ngal a.
6. Huchiin, hangtakin Toupa tuh hon panpihpa ahi, ka lau kei ding; mihing in bang a hon chih thei dia? Ichi uhi.
7. Nou hon heu, Pathian thu honhilhte, theigige unla, a awmdan ua thil suakte ngaihtuah in a hindan uh ching un.
8. Jesu Kris tuh zan tawh, tuni tawh, chiklai peuh inleng angeingei in a awm gige ding hi.
9. Ki sinsakna tuamtuam leh lamdang mutleng in awmkei un; lungtang hehpihna a hihkip a awm tuh a hawih ngal a, nekdingte bang a hihkip a awmlou in; huchibang lam lungkhamten huaite ah phattuamna a mu tuan kei uhi.
10. Enzaw maitam khat inei; huaituh biakbuk nasemten anek theihlouhna mun uh ahi.
11. Gan, asisan Siampu Lianpenin Mun Siangthou a khelhna a dia lana a lutpih zel luangte tuh, dainawl ah a hal zel uhi.
12. Huchiin, Jesu in leng amah sisan mahmah a mite a hih siangthou theihna ding in, kulh kongkhak pawlam ah ana thuakta hi.
13. Huchiin, amah a muhsitna uh pu in dainawl ah a kianglam ah ipai khe ding uhi.
14. Hialai a zaw khaw munkip inei ngalkei a, khaw hong awm ding izong ahizaw hi.
15. Huchiin, amah vangin phatna kithawihna Pathian kiang ah ilan gige ding uh, huaituh amin gupna mukte gah ahi.
16. Himahleh, thil hihhawih leh thil phal khawng mangngilh kei un; tuabang kithawihna tungah Pathian a lunghi mahmah ngal a.
17. Nou hawn heutute thu mang unla, a thuthu un awm un, anasepte uh tanchin gen nawn ngei dinga, na khate uh ching a hon veng ahi hi, kipak takin lungkham kawm hilou in, semsak un; huchilouinzaw nou-a ding a khawk ahi zaw ding hi.
18. Hon thumsak un; bangkima diktat taka awm tuma, sia leh pha theihna siangtak nei ka hi uh chih diktak in ka kithei ngal ua.
19. Huan, kintak a nakiang ua sawlnawna ka awm theihna ding in, huai tuh hih ding in ka hon ngennawn diak ahi.
20. Huan lemna Pathian belam Chingpa thupi tak, I Toupa Jesu mah, khantawna thukhun na sisan vang a misi lak a kipan a pikhenawn pan,
21. amitmuha a kipahlam peuhmah, Jesu Kris ziakin eimah a sem in, a deihlam hih ding in thil hoih chiteng lam ah nou hawn hihkim hen: Jesu Kris kiang ah thupina khantawn in awm hen. Amen.
22. Himahleh, unaute aw, ka hawnngen hi, hasawtna thu ngai thei teitei un; na kiang uah thu tawmchik ka gelh ngal ua,
23. I unaupa Timothi apawtsak ta uh chih na nathei sam un; ahongpai ma deuh in, amah tawh nou ka hon mu ding hi.
24. Nou hawn heute tengteng leh, misiangthoute tengteng chibai hon buksak un. Italy gam a mi ten chibai a hon buk uhi.Navek ua kiangah hehpihna awm hen. Amen.
25. Navek ua kiangah hehpihna awm hen. Amen.
