1Timothy (1/6)  →
1. Paula, Pathian in Hondampa leh Khrist Jesu, I Lamet, thupiak banga Kris Jesu sawltak in:
2. Ginna a ka ta taktak Timothi kiang ah: Pathian I Pa leh, Kris Jesu I Toupa kiang a kipan, hehpihna, zahngaihna, lemna hong om hen.
3. Masidonia gama ka hoh dek laia konnget bangin Ephesa khua ah om gige in, huchia mi khenkhat thugin tuam himhim sinsak lou dinga na hilh theihna dingin.
4. Huan Pathian lama phattuamna, ginnaa ompen hiloua, kiselna leh gintakthu gen khawng omsak lel tangthu lel khang thu sui zohlouh peuh buaipih sam lou dinga na hilh theihna dingin:
5. Eiten thuhilh ding I kep pen bel lungtang siangthou leh sialehphatheihna siang leh ginnah taktak akipana pawt itna ahi zaw ngala.
6. Mi khenkhatten hiaite pialsanin thuginalou genna lamah a vakmangta ua.
7. A thu gen uh leh thil ahi-a agente uleng bang ahia chih theiloupe in, dan hilhmite hih a ut nak uhi.
8. Dan jaw a hoih chih I thei ngal ua, min a zat hoih peuhmah uleh.
9. Ahia lah, dan jaw mi dikatte adinga bawl ahi keia, dan kalhte leh dan lou a omte ading, Pathian limsakloute leh khialte mi siangthouloute leh Pathian zahloute, pate that khawng leh nute that khawng, mihing that khawng.
10. kingaihhat khawng ading, tawkohuk khawng a ding, mihing gukhat khawng a ding, juauthei khawng a ding, kichiam khemkhem khawng a ding leh
11. phat tuak Pathian tanchinhoih thupi hilhna dik ka kiang a kepsak bang kalha om thil dang himhim a dinga leng bawl ahi jaw hi.
12. A honhihhatpa Kris Jesu I Toupa kiangah kipahthu ka gen hi, muanhuai a honsak a, a nasempa dia a hon chih tak jiakin;
13. tumalamin Pathian gensepa leh sawipa leh hihsemi nahita mahleng; himahleh, ginglou pia theilou a hih ka hih jiakin ahi, zahngaihna ka namuh tak;
14. huan, I Toupa hehpihna tuh, gina leh Kris Jesua itna in nakpi takin a dimletta.
15. Kris Jesu khialte hondam dingin khovel ah a hongpai chih tuh ataksanhuai in a laktuak mahmah hi. A lak ua khialpen ka hi;
16. himaleh, hiai jiakin ahi zangaihna ka na muh: Jesu Kris in a dohzohna kim akhialpen keimaha a latsak theihna ding ahi, huai tuh tubansiah khantawna hinna muhna dinga Amah hon gingta ding-te adia etsakna hi ding in.
17. Kumpipa khantawna om, setheilou, muhtheihlouh, Pathian tak kia, khantawna zahtak leh thupiin om hen, Amen.
18. Ka tapa Timothi aw, na tungtang genkholhnate toh kituakin, hiai mohpuakna leh thuhilhna konpia, huchia huai thute-a hilha om a, kidouna hoih na dou heihna ding in,
19. ginna leh sialehpha theihna siang tak len chinten in, Mi khenkhatten huai sialehpha- theihna tuh paikhia in ginna lamah long sia bangin a kekseta uhi;huaite lakah tuh Himenai leh Aleksandar a tel ua; Pathian gensiat louh a chin theihna ding un amau tuh Setan ka peta hi.
20. huaite lakah tuh Himenai leh Aleksandar a tel ua; Pathian gensiat louh a chin theihna ding un amau tuh Setan ka peta hi.
