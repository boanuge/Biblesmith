1. Huan TOUPA thu ka kiangah a hongtunga, ka kianga,
2. Mihing tapa, Israel inkote kiangah thuhaksa bawl khia inla, gentehnain gen in,
3. TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin a chi ahi, chiin: Muvanlai lianpi, kha zapi, khaveng sau pi gial emum, mul bit tak Lebanon tangah a lenga, Sidar sing jik a tuaha.
4. A hiang jik nou pen a kitana, mi vialvakna gamah a lenpiha, sum sinna khopiah a phut a.
5. Huai gam hai chi leng a tuaha, lei hoihah a pawi pou a, tui tampi geiah a phut a, nahkai sing bangin a phut hi,
6. Huan a khanga, grep gui niam bilbial a honghi a, selte a honsuaha, ahiangte a hong dawntaa.
7. Muvanlai lianpi dang a om nawna, kha zapi leh mul bit tak a nei teia, huan, ngaiin, hiai grep kung amah lamah a zung a jama: a suanna mun akipanin amah lamah hiangte a pou khiaa, amah tuia a vak theihna dingin.
8. Hiangte a suaha, grep hoih tak a hih theihna dingin tui tampi geia lei hoiha phuh ahi.
9. TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin a chi ahi: A piching den dia hia? A vuai maimah theihna ding in a nahte satkhiain, a zung toh a bot khe sin kei dia hia? a nah sel thak tengtengin leng a vuai loh ding; a zung toh bot khe dingin thilhihtheihna thupi tak hiam, muvanlai tampi hiam a kiphamoh tuan kei dia hia?
10. A hi, ngai in, phuh hileh leng a piching den dia hia? Suahlam huihin a honnuai hun chiangin a vuai khin maimah kei dia hia? A pouna mun mahah a vuai maimah ding chiin, a chi a.
11. Huan TOUPA thu ka kiangah a hongtung nawna, ka kiangah,
12. Helhat innkote kiangah gen in, hiai thil omdan a theikei ua hia? Hilh in, ngai un, Babulon kumpipa Jerusalemah a hongpai a,
13. A kumpipa uh leh a heutu uh a mana, amah kiangah Babulonah a pia, kumpipa uh suan khat a mana, thukhun a bawl piha, thu achiam teitei saka, lalna gam a keniama, amaha a din khun len kawm kiaa a din theihna dingin.
14. A lalna gam a keniama, amaha a din khiak theih nawn louhna dingin a thukhun len kawm kiaa a din theihna dingin
15. Himahleh sakolte leh mi tampi honsawl dinga ngen dingin Aigupta gama palai sawlin a tungah a helta, A lohching ding hia? hichibang hihmi a suakta ding hia? Thukhun lah hihse ngalin a pawt khe thou ding hia?
16. A hina ka hin laiteng, chih TOUPA PATHIAN thu pawt ahi; kumpipa, kumpipa dia bawlpa tenna ngeiah a kichiamnate musit leh, a thukhunte bohsiaa, amah kiang mahmahah Babulon ah a si ding hi.
17. Mi tampi hihlup dinga mualpawngte leh kulhte a bawl hun chiang un leng Pharo sepaih hon leh pawl thupi mahmah lengbangmah a hihsak theikei ding uh.
18. Thukhun bohsiain kichiamna a musit ta ngal ua: thu a kichiam nungin hiai thil tengteng a hih nawn ngala, a suakta peuhmah kei ding hi.
19. Huaijiakin TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin a chi ahi: ka kichiamna a musita, ka thukhun a bohsiaa, a hina ka hon laiteng, amah tungah ka bung ngeingei ding.
20. Lenin ka khuh dinga, ka thang ah a awk ding, Babulonah ka honpi dinga, huchiin a bohsiatna, ka tung ngeia a bohsiatna te jiakin a mah ka sual ding.
21. Huan a pawl tengtenga taikekte tengteng namsauin a si ding ua, a om laite kawlmong chihah a dalh ding uh: huan kei TOUPAN ka genta chih a thei khe ding uh.
22. TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin a chi ahi: Sidar sang mahmah dawn ken ka la dinga, ka phut ding; a jikpi pena jik nou ka khiak dinga, tang sang kidoh takah ka phut ding.
23. Israel sana tanah k aphut dinga, hiangte a honsuak dinga, a honggah dinga, sidar thupi tak ahi ding; a nuaiah tungleng vasa chi chih a teng ding ua, ahiang limte ah a om ding uh.Huan gam sing tengtengin kei TOUPAN sing sangte ka niamsaka, sing niamte ka sangsaka, sing hingte ka keusaka, sing keute ka hingpha saka, ka chi nalai hi, a chi a.
24. Huan gam sing tengtengin kei TOUPAN sing sangte ka niamsaka, sing niamte ka sangsaka, sing hingte ka keusaka, sing keute ka hingpha saka, ka chi nalai hi, a chi a.
