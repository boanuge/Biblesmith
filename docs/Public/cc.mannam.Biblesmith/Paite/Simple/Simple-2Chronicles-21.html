1. Huan, Jehosaphat a pi leh pute kiangah a ihmua, a pute lakah a pu David khopi ah a vui uh; huan a tapa Jehoram a sikin a lalta hi.
2. Huan, aman unau, Jehosaphat tapate a neia, Azaria te, Jehiel te, Zekaria te, Azaria te, Mikael te, Sephatia tea hi uh. Hiai tengteng Israel kumpipa Jehosaphat tapate vek ahi uh.
3. Huan, a pa un thil thupi taktak a piaa, dangkasik te, dangkaeng te, thil namtam pite, Juda gama kulh nei khuate toh; himahleh gamte pen Jehoram a piaa, a ta upapen a hih jiakin.
4. Huchiin, Jehoramin a pa gam a luahin a hongkihih hattaa, a unaute tengteng namsauin a hihlum veka, Israelte laka intek tampi toh.
5. Jehoram bel kum sawmthum leh kum niha upa a hihin a lal pana: Jerusalem lem ah kum giat a lal.
6. Ahab inkote hih bangin Israel kumpipate lampi a jui sam hi: Ahab tanu jiin a nei hi: huchiin TOUPA mitmuhin thil hoih lou tak a hih seka.
7. Himahleh, TOUPAN David inkote a hihmang nuam keia, David kianga thukhun jiak leh amah leh a suante tana khawnvak neihsak thak kana chiam khit jiakin.
8. Amah damlaiin Edomte Judate laka kipan a hel ua, kumpipa a kibawl tawm ua.
9. Huchiin Jehoram a sepaih heutute leh a kangtalai tengteng toh a vakuan khia a, janin a vakuana, amau leh kangtalai tunga heutute ummi Edomte a vasual hi.
10. Huchiin Edomte tu tanin Judate laka kipan a hel lailai uhi; huan, huai lai mahin Libna khua leng a laka kipan a hel teia: TOUPA, a pi leh pute uh pathian a lehngatsan jiakin.
11. Huan, Juda gam teng ah te munsang a bawla, Jerusalem mite a angkawmsaka, Judate a pi mang hi.
12. Huan, jawlnei Elija akipanin as kiangah lai a hongtunga TOUPA, na pu David Pathianin hichiin a chi, na pa Jehosaphat lempite na tawn keia, Judate kumpipa Asa lempite leng na jui sam keia,
13. Israel kumpipate lampi na jui jawa, Judate leh Jerusalema omte Ahab inkuan bangin ang na kawmsaka, na unau, napa inkuante nang sanga hoihjaw na thah lai jiakin.
14. Ngadih, TOUPAN na mite, na tate, na jite, na gante hipiin a gawt dinga;
15. Huan, gil natna thupi mahmah na nei dinga, ni tengin natna jiaka na gil a kiakkiaka, a kiak khit vek tanin, chiin.
16. Huchiin, Ethiopia mite kiang khawnga mi philistiate leh Arabia te lungsim Jehoram sual dingin TOUPAN a tokthou:
17. Huchiin, Judate bel a vasual ua, a gam uah a lut ua, kumpipa ina thil tengteng a la vek ua, a tapate, a jite uh leng; hichiin a tapa naupangpen Jehoahaz kia loungal a pa leng a nutsiat sak kei uhi.
18. Huan, huainungin TOUPAN hihdam theilouh gil natnain a gawta.
19. Huan, hichi ahia, a kum nihna a hongbeiin a natna jiakin a gil a kiakia taa, natna mulkimhuai takin a hongsita hi. A miten a pa meipi a seh sak bang un a seh sak tei kei uhi.A lal tungin kum sawnthum leh kum nih ahi a, Jerusalem ah kum giat a lala, kuamah it louhin a sia, David khopiah a vui ua, kumpipate hanah bel ahi kei hi.
20. A lal tungin kum sawnthum leh kum nih ahi a, Jerusalem ah kum giat a lala, kuamah it louhin a sia, David khopiah a vui ua, kumpipate hanah bel ahi kei hi.
