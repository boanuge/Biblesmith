1. Huan suai bawlte hiaite ahi uh Nehemia Tirsatha, Hakalia tapa, leh Zedekia;
2. Seraia, Azaria, Jeremia;
3. Pashur, Amaria, Malkija;
4. Hattus, Sebania, Malluk;
5. Harim, Meremot, Obadia;
6. Daniel, Gennethon, Baruk;
7. Mesullam, Abija, Mijamin;
8. Maazia, Bilgai, Semaia: hiaite siampute ahi uh.
9. Huan Levi mite: huaite, Jesua Azania tapa, Binnui Henadad tapa, Kadmiel;
10. Huan a unaute uh, Sebania, Hodia, Kelita, Pelaia, Hanan;
11. Mika, Rehob, Hasabia;
12. Zakkur, Serebia, Sebania;
13. Hodia, Bani, Beninu;
14. Mite hausate: Paros, Pahath-moab, Elam, Zattu, Bani;
15. Bunni, Azgad, Bebai;
16. Adonija, Bigvai, Adin;
17. Ater, Hezekia, Azzur;
18. Hodia, Hasum, Bezai;
19. Harip, Anathot, Nebia;
20. Magpias, Mesullam, Hezir;
21. Mesezabel, Zadok, Jaddua;
22. Pelatia, Hanan, Anaia;
23. Hosia, Hanania, Hasub;
24. Hallohes, Pilha, Sobek;
25. Rehum, Hasabna, Maaseia;
26. Huan Ahia, Hanan, Anan;
27. Malluk, Harim, Baana.
28. Huan mipi laka adangte, siampute, Levi mite, kongkhakngakte, lasamite, Nithinim mite, leh gamtea mite laka kipana amau leh amau Pathian dan lama ki tuamsehte tengteng, a jite uh, a tapate uh, a tanute uh, mi chih theihna leh theihtheihna neite;
29. A unaute uh, a miliante uh lamah a belh chinten ua, Mosi Pathian sikhain a piak Pathian dana om ding leh, TOUPA i Toupa uh thupiak tengteng jui ding leh hih dinga, a vaihawmte leh a thusehte hih dingin hamsia leh kichiamna a bawl uhi;
30. Huan i tanute gama mite kianga pe lou ding leh, i tapate adia a tanute uh la lou dingin:
31. Huan gama miten khawlnia van hiam annek hiam juak ding a hontawi uleh, khawlni hiam, ni siang ni hiamin huaite i lei kei ding uh: huchiin kum sagihna lou hiate lak leh leiba chiteng sik i kidek ding uh.
32. Huailou leng i Pathian uh in nasepna dinga kum tenga sekel sih thumna kiba sak dingin, dante leng i kibawl ua;
33. Tanghou koih ading, anpiak thillat gige ading, halmang thillat gige ading, khawlnite, kha thakte, sehsa ankuangte ading, thil siangthoute ading, Israel adia lemna kithoihna bawlna dia khelhna thillatte ading, leh i Pathian in ua nasepna tengteng adingin.
34. Huan ai i san ua, siampute, Levi mite leh mite, sing thillat adingin, dana a kigelh banga, i Pathian uh TOUPA maitam tunga khul ding, kum tenga, hunte sehsa, i pipute inkote dungjuia, i Pathian in ua lak lut dingin:
35. Huan TOUPA ina, kum tenga, i lei theigah masapente, sing chitenga gah tengteng akipan gahmasapente, tawi dingin:
36. Dana kigelh bangin, i tapate laka piang masapen leh i gantate laka leng, i bawnghonte lak leh i belam honte laka nou masapente, i Pathian in ua nasem siampute kianga i Pathian in ua tawi dingin:
37. Huan i tangbuang mek masapen u leh, i vei thillatte uh leh, sing chiteng gah, uain leh thau, i Pathian uh ina dantante ah, siampute kiangah i tawi ding uh; huan i loumaa sawm-khatte Levi mite kiangah; amau, Levi miten lah, i singtang khopi tengteng uah sawm-khat a la ngal ua.
38. Huan Aron tapa Siampu, Levi miten sawma-khatte sawma-khat i Pathian in uah, dantante ah, sumbawm in ah a tawi tou ding uhi.Israel tate leh Levi taten buhlehbal vei, uain, thau, thillatmunsiang tuiumbelsuante, nasem siampute, kongkhakngakte leh lasamite omna, munsiangthoua a tawi ding uh lah ahi ngala: Huan i Pathian in uh i nuse kei ding uhi.
39. Israel tate leh Levi taten buhlehbal vei, uain, thau, thillatmunsiang tuiumbelsuante, nasem siampute, kongkhakngakte leh lasamite omna, munsiangthoua a tawi ding uh lah ahi ngala: Huan i Pathian in uh i nuse kei ding uhi.
