1. Gamdai leh phaksap gam a kipan dinga; gam keuin nuamsain pawn pak bangin a pak ding.
2. A pak lun dinga, kipak tak leh lasain nuam a sa ding; Lebanon thupi na a kiangah piak ahi ding, Karmel leh Sharon kilawmna toh; TOUPA thupina a mu ding ua, i Pathian kilawmna toh.
3. Khut hatloute hatsak unla, khuk jawte kipsak un.
4. Lungtang dawilokte kiangah, Hat takin om unla, lau kei un; ngai un, na Pathian uh phulakna toh a hongpai dinga, a honhondam ding, chi un.
5. Huai hun chiangin mittote mit hahsakin a om dinga bengngongte bil hihvakin a om ding.
6. Huai hun chiangin khebaite sakhi bangin a tawm ding ua, pautheiloute leiin nuamsa takin la a sa ding; gamdai ahte lah tuite a hongphul khe biabia sin ngala, gam keute ah luita tui a hong phulkhe buabua sin ngal ua.
7. Huchiin piaunel sa mahmah munte dil a hongsuak dinga, lei dangtak mahmah tuinak a hongsuak ding; sehalte tennaa, a lupna uah, loupa, tuilou leh sikhapat toh, a om ding.
8. Huan, huailaiah lamlian a om ding, kongpi toh, huai tuh siangthouna Lampi a chi ding uh; mi thanghuaiin lampi a tawn kei ding; ahihbangin huai mite adingin ahi zo ding; mi vialzinte, ahi, mi haite leng, huailaiah a mang kei ding uh.
9. Humpinelkai himhim huailaiah a om kei dinga, sa hing ne himhim huailaiah a pai tou sam kei ding uh, huailah muh ding leng a om kei ding; tatte bel huailaiah a pai tou ding uh;Huan TOUPA tatte a kik nawn ding ua, lasa khawmin Zion a tung ding uh, khantawna kipahna a lu tunguah a omta dinga; nuamsakna leh kipahna leh thumna a mangta ding hi.
10. Huan TOUPA tatte a kik nawn ding ua, lasa khawmin Zion a tung ding uh, khantawna kipahna a lu tunguah a omta dinga; nuamsakna leh kipahna leh thumna a mangta ding hi.
