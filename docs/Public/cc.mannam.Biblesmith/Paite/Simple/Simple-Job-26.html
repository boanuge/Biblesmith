1. Huan Jobin a dawnga, hichiin a chi a:
2. Thilhihtheihna neilou amah bangchiin na panpihta a amai; hanta neilou ban bangchiin na hondamta a amai;
3. Pilna neilou amah bangchin na thuhilha, huan nakpitaka theihna hoih na phuangkheta a;
4. Kua kiangah ahia thute na genkhiak tak? Huan kua kha ahia nang akipan hongpai khia?
5. Sisate tuite nuai leh huaia tengte nuaiah a ling uh.
6. A maah Seol vuaktangin a oma, huan Abaddon in kikhuhna a neikei hi.
7. Aman mal mun awng tungah a phalh khia a, huan bang mahlou tungah lei a khai hi.
8. A meipi sahin tuite a tuna; huan huaite nuaiah meipi a kikek kei hi.
9. Khapi mai a khuha, a tungah a meipi a phalh hi.
10. Tuite tungah, khovak leh khomial kigitna ah, kawlmong a kaikual hi.
11. A salhna ah van khuamte a ling ua, lamdang a sa uh.
12. A thilhihtheihnain tuipi a khawlsaka, huan a theihsiamna in Rahab a sat.
13. A huin vante bawlhoihin a oma; a khutin gulpi man-gang tailai a sun hi.Ngaiin, hiaite a thilhih mong lel ahi uh. Ngatchika a huau siausiau lel! A thilhihtheihna Vanging kua ahia theisiam ding?
14. Ngaiin, hiaite a thilhih mong lel ahi uh. Ngatchika a huau siausiau lel! A thilhihtheihna Vanging kua ahia theisiam ding?
