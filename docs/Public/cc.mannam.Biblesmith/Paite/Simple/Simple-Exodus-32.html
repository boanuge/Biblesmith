1. Tang akipana Mosi a hongpai suk pah louh miten a theih un, mite Aron kiangah a hongkikhawm khawm ua, a kiangah, Kisain, ka ma ua pai ding, pathiante honbawl sakin; hiai Mosi, Aigupta gam akipana honpi khepa lah, bang chita ahia ka thei ngal kei ua, a chi ua.
2. Huan Aronin a kiang uah, Na jite uh bila om, natapate uh bila om, na tanute uh bila om, dangkaeng bilbahte khong khia unla, ka kiangah huaite hontawi un, a chi a.
3. Huchiin mite tengtengin a bil ua om dangkaeng bilbahte a khong khia ua, huaite Aron kiangah a tawi uhi.
4. Huan aman a khut ua kipanin huaite a laa, bawngnou suna a bawl zohin, gelhna vanin a bawlta hi: huan Israel aw, hiaite na pathiante, Aigupta gama kipan a honlakhete hi uhen, a chi uh.
5. Aronin huai a muh takin, a maah maitam a bawla, huan Aronin thuphuanna a neia, Jingchiang TOUPA dia ankuangluina ahi, a chi a.
6. Huchiin a jingin beltakin a thou ua, halmang thillat a lan ua, lemna thillat a tawi uh: huan mite ne ding leh dawn dingin a tu ua, mawl dingin a ding uh.
7. Huan TOUPAN Mosi kiangah, Paiin, kumsukin, na mi Aigupta gama kipana na pi khiakte, a kihihsiat jiakun:
8. Amau ka lampi hilhna akipanin a pial pah manhial uhi: bawngnou sun a kibawl ua, huai a bia ua, huaia kithoihin, Israel aw, hiaite na pathiante, Aigupta gama kipana honkhete hi uhen, a chi uh, a chi a.
9. Huan TOUPAN Mosi kiangah, Hiai mite ka mutaa, ngaiin, mi genhakte ahi uh:
10. Huaijiakin tuin a tung ua ka thangpaihna a puah jaka, amaute ka hul theihna dingin honomsak maimai un: huchiin nam thupiin ka honbawl ding a chi a.
11. Huan Mosiin TOUPA a Pathian a ngena, TOUPA, thilhihtheihna thupi leh, ban thahata Aigupta gama kipana na pi khiaksa, na mite tungah bang dinga na thangpaihna puakjak ding ahia?
12. Bangdingin ahia Aiguptaten thu gena, Siatna dinga amau pi khia eive, tangtea amau that ding leh, lei tengtenga kipana amaute hul dingin? chiin, Na thangpaihna thupi la kik inla, na mite tunga hiai gilou kikhel sakin.
13. A kiang ua, Vana aksite bangin na suante ka puangsak dinga, na suante kianga a thu ka gensa hiai gam tengteng ka honpe ding, huchiin khantawnin a luah ding uh, a chi a, nangmah ngeia na kichiamna, na sikhate, Abraham, Isaak leh Jakob theigigein, a chi hi.
14. Huchiin a mite tunga gilou hih dinga a ngaihtuah TOUPAN a kikhel sakta hi.
15. Huan Mosi a kiheia, tang akipan a pai sukta, a khutah theihpihna suangpek nihte a om: suang pekte a langnih tuaka gelh ahi, khat lam leh langkhat lama gelh ahi uh.
16. Suangpekte Pathian thilhih ahi a, a gelhte leng Pathian gelh ahi, suangpekte tunga gelhte.
17. Huchia Joshuain mite akikou husa uh a jakin Mosi kiangah, Giahbuk ah kidouna husa a om, a chi a.
18. Huan a man, Tungnun kithua kikou te aw ahi keia, nuaisiaha om jiaka kapte aw leng ahi sam kei: lasate husa ahi jaw ka jak, a chi a.
19. Huan hichi ahi a, giahbuk kiang a hongtun takin, bawngnou leh lam a mutaa: huchiin Mosi hehna a sou a, a khuta kipanin suangpekte a paia, tangnuaiah a keksakta hi.
20. Huan a bawngnou bawl uh a laa, meiah a khula, a gawi nena, tui tungah a theha Israel suante a dawnsak hi.
21. Huan Mosiin Aron kiangah, Hiai miten bang ahia na kianga a hih uh, a tung ua hichitela khelhna thupi na tun sak? a chi a.
22. Huan Aronin, Ka pu hehna puak jak kei hen: mite na thei hi, siatna a zon dan uh.
23. Ka ma ua pai ding, pathiante honbawl sakin: hiai Mosi Aigupta gama kipana honpi khetoupa lah, bang suakta ahia ka thei ngal kei ua, ka kianga a chih jiak un.
24. Huchiin ken a kiang uah, Dangkaeng neipeuhin, khong khia un, ka chi a. Huchiin amau kei a honpia ua: huan ken mei ah ka khiaa, huan hiai bawngnou a hongsuakta hi, a chi a.
25. Huan, Mosi in mite vuaktang a om a muh in; Aron in lah a melmate uh lak a a zahlakna ding un, amau a vuaktang sak ngal a:
26. Huailai in giahbuk kongpi ah Mosi a ding a, TOUPA lam a pang kua ahia? ka kiang ah hong pai heh, a chi a. Huan, a kiang ah Levi tate tengteng a kikhawmkhawm ta uh.
27. Huan aman a kiang uah, Israel TOUPA Pathian in hichi in a chi, Michih in a pang ah a namsau kuah henla, giahbuk tuansuak in kongpi khat a kipan kongpi dang ah, lut in pawt henla, michih in a sanggampa that henla, michih in a lawmpa, michih in a innveng, chi in.
28. Huan Levi tate’n Mosi thu dungzui in a hih ta uh: huchi in huai ni in mite lak ah mi sangthum khawng a puk uh.
29. Mosi in lah, Tuni in TOUPA lam ah kihih siangthou un, michih a tapa tungah leh, a unaupa tungah; tuni a na tung ua vualzawlna a honpiak theihna ding in, a na chi ta ngal a.
30. Huan a zing in hichi ahi a, Mosi in mite kiang ah, Khelhna thupi na khial ta uh: huchi in tu in TOUPA kiang ah ka hohtou dia; na khelhna uh a ding in lemna ka bawl khamai ding, achi a.
31. Huan Mosi TOUPA kiang ah a kik nawn a, Aw, hiai mite'n khelhna thupi a khial uh, dangka-eng pathiante a kibawl ta uh
32. Ahi a lah tu in, a khelhna uh na ngaihdam leh; na ngaihdam kei ding leh, na gelhsa na laibu a kipan, hon siakmang in, ko'n ngen hi, achi a.
33. Huan TOUPA'N Mosi kiang ah, Kuapeuh ka tung a khial, huai ka laibu a kipan ka siakmang ding.
34. Huaiziak in tu innkuan inla, na kiang a ka genna mun ah mite pi in: ngai in, na ma ah ka Angel a pai ding: akoi-abang hileh ka veh ni chiang in a tunguah a khelhna uh ka veh ding, achi hi.Huchi in Aron bawl bawngnou a bawl ziak un, TOUPA'N mite a gawt ta.
35. Huchi in Aron bawl bawngnou a bawl ziak un, TOUPA'N mite a gawt ta.
