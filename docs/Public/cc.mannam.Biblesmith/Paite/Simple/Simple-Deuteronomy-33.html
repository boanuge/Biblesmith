1. Hiai vualjawlnain ahi, Pathian mi Mosiin a sih maa Israel suante vual a jawl.
2. Sinai akipanin TOUPA a honga, Seir akipanin a kiang uah a thou khiaa, mi siangthou sang sawm laka kipanin a hongpai; a khut taklamah amau adingin dan kuang thei a om.
3. A hi, chi chihte a ita, mi siangthou tengteng nakhutah a om uh; a khebulah a tu ua; na thu a pom chiat ding uh.
4. Mosiin dan honpia, Jakob mipite dingin gouluah ding.
5. Jesurunah kumpipa ahia, mipite intekpen a kikhawm khawm ua; Israel chi tengteng a om khawm uh.
6. Reuben dam gige leh, silouin, a mite a hihleh tawm zo le uh, a chi a.
7. Huan, hiai Judate a vualjawlna ahi; TOUPA, Juda aw ngaikhia inla, a mite kiangah pi lutin; a khut amah adingin kiching henla, a melmate sualpihpa honghiin, chih.
8. Huan, Levite tungthu ah bel, Na Thumin leh na Urim na mi siangthou kiangah a om, Masa ah na zeeta, Meribe tui ahte na sual hi;
9. Ka nu leh ka pa ka theikei, chiin; a unaute leng a thei sam kei hi, amah tate ngei leng a thei tuan kei; na thu a pom ua, na thukhun a jui uhi.
10. Jakobte na vaihawmte a sin sak ding ua, Israelte, na dan; na maah gimlim a koih ding ua, na maitam ah pumhal thillat.
11. Vualjawlin, TOUPA, sum a pai, a khut nasepte nana pom inla; amah sualte kawng sat inla, amah hote kawng toh, a tat nawn louhna ding un, a chi a.
12. Huan, Benjaminte tungthu ah jaw, TOUPA deihtak jaw a kiangah bitin a om ding; nitumin a opkhum dinga, a po jel ding, a chi a.
13. Huan, Josephte tung thu ah jaw, A gam TOUPA vualjawlin om hen, van thil mantam daituitein, tui thuk mahmah nuai lama tuitein.
14. Ni gah suah thil mantam tein, khaten thil mantama suah tein.
15. Nidanglai tang tea thil deihhuai pentein, khantawn tanga thil mantamtein.
16. Lei leh a tunga thil mantam tein, loubuka ompa deihsakna in; vualjawlna Josephte lu tungah hongom hen, a unaute akipana hihtuama ompa sipah.
17. A thupina a bawngtal piang masa thupina bang ahi a, a ki gam bawngtal ki bang ahi; huaiin nam chih a si ding, kawlmong tanin; Ephraimte sangsawm ahi ua, Manaserte sang ahi uhi, a chi a.
18. Huan, Zebulunte tung thu ah bel, Kipakin, Zebulun, na vak khiaknate ah, huan, Isakar, na puaninte ah.
19. Tangah chi chih a sam ding ua, huaiah diktatna kithoihnate a lan ding uh; tuipi buchinna a ne sin ngal ua, piaunela gou kiselte toh, a chi a.
20. Gadte tungthu ah bel, Gadte hihzapa phatin om hen, humpinelkai bangin a tenga, ban a ham keka, ahi, lujang leng.
21. Amah adingin a hoihna pen a tela, vaihawmpa tantuan lah guanbehin a om ngala, Intekpente kiangah a hoha, TOUPA vaihawmna dik a juia; Israelte kianga vaihawmnate, a chi a.
22. Dante tungthu ah bel, Dan bel humpinelkai nou ahi a, Basan akipana hongtawm khia, a chi a.
23. Naphtalite tungthu ah bel, Naphtali aw, deihsaknaa lungkim, TOUPA vualjawlnaa tai, sim leh tum luahin, a chi a.
24. Aserte tungthuah bel, Aser bel tatea vualjawlin om henla, a unaute lakah tuitum henla; thau ah a kha diah heh.
25. Na painate sik leh dal ahi ding, na damsung ni bangin na hatna leng a om ding, a chi a.
26. Pathian bang kuamahmah a om kei uh, Jesurun aw, nang honpahpih dinga van a tuang, thupi taka vana tuang, thupi taka van a tuang.
27. Khantawn Pathian na omna ahi a, nuailamah khantawn ban a om, melmate lah a honsat mang saka, Hih mangin, a chi.
28. Huan, Israel bel galmuang takin a oma, Jakob tuileh a pumut; buh leh uain gamah, ahi, a vanten daitui chim a tang sak.A hampha hi, aw, Israel, nang bang mahmah kua a oma, TOUPA hotdam mite? Nang hon honpanpihpa phaw, na thupina namsau a hi! Na melmate na kiangah a hongkipe ding ua, a mun sangte uh na chil ding hi, a chi a.
29. A hampha hi, aw, Israel, nang bang mahmah kua a oma, TOUPA hotdam mite? Nang hon honpanpihpa phaw, na thupina namsau a hi! Na melmate na kiangah a hongkipe ding ua, a mun sangte uh na chil ding hi, a chi a.
