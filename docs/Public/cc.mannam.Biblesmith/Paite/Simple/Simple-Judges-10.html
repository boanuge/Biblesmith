1. Huan, Abimelek nungin Israelte honkhe dingin Isakar mi Dodo tapa Tola a hongsuaka, Ephraim tanggam Samir khua ah a om hi.
2. Huan, kum sawmnih leh kum thum Israelte vai a hawma, huan, a sia Samir khuaah a vui uhi.
3. Huai nungin Gilead mi Jair a hongsuaka, kum sawmnih leh kum nih Israelte vai a hawma.
4. Huan, huai miin tapa sawmthum, sabengtung nou sawmthum tunga tuang sak mi a neia, huan, Gilead gamah kho sawmthum a neia, huaite tu tanin Havoth-jair a chi ua.
5. Huan, Jair bel a sia, Kamon khua ah a vui uhi.
6. Huan, Israel suanten TOUPA mitmuhin thil gilou a hih nawn ua, Baal-te leh Astoret-te, Suriate pathiante, Zidonte pathiante, Moabte pathiante, Amon suante pathiante, Philistiate pathiante na a nasemta ua; huchiin TOUPA a lehngatsan ua, a na a semta kei uhi.
7. Huchiin TOUPA Israelte tungah a hehta mahmah maia, Philistiate kiangah leh Amon suante kiangah a khawngta hi.
8. Huan, huaikumin Israel suante a hihbuai ua, a nuaisiah mahmah mai uhi: Jordan gal Gilead gama Amorte gama Israel suan om tengteng kum sawm leh kum giat sung tak a nuaisiah uh.
9. Huan, Amon suante bel Judate, Benjaminte, Ephraim suante sual dingin Jordan gal a kah ua; huchiin Israelte buai petmahin a buaita uhi.
10. Huan, Israel suanten, I Pathian uh i nalehngatsanta ngeia, Baal-te na i naseptak jiakin a tungah thil i nahihkhialta uhi, chiin TOUPA a sam ua.
11. Huan, TOUPA Israel suante kiangah, Aiguptate lak akipan, Amorte lak akipan, Amon suante lak akipan, Philistiate laka kipan noute ka honhonkhia ahi kei maw?
12. Zidonte, Amalekte, Maonten leng nou a honnuaisiah ua; huan, non sam ua, huchiin a khut ua kipanin ka honhonkhia.
13. Himahleh nouten nou lehngatsan ua, pathian dangte na na sem zota uh; huaijiakin ka honhonkhe nawn kei ding hi.
14. Na pathian telte uh sam ta unla, amau na buai hun un hon honkhe le uh ake, a chi a.
15. Huan, Israel suanten TOUPA kiangah, Thil ka nahihkhialta uh ahi: nang pha na sak dandanin ka tunguah honhih inla; hot a hihleh tuniin hehpihtakin honhonkhe teiteiin, a chi ua.
16. Huchi in a lak ua nam dang pathiante a koih mang vek ua, TOUPA na a semta uh: Huan, Israelte buaina jiakin a lungsim a dahta mahmah hi.
17. Huan, Amon suante a kikhawm khawm ua, Gilead gamah a vakikulh ua. Huan, Israel suante leng a kikhawm khawmta ua, Mizpa khua ah a kikulh uh.Huan, mipite, Gilead gama mi liante a kihou ua, Amon suante sual pan ding kua a om aleh? Gilead gama om tengteng tungah heutuin a om ding, a chi ua.
18. Huan, mipite, Gilead gama mi liante a kihou ua, Amon suante sual pan ding kua a om aleh? Gilead gama om tengteng tungah heutuin a om ding, a chi ua.
