1. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi leh Aron a houpih a,
2. Hiai TOUPA thupiak dan ahi: Israel suante bawngla san, dem bei, gensiat bei, hakkol bulhsak ngei het louh hon kai ding in hilh in:
3. Huan, siampu Eleazar na pe ding a, huan, aman dainawl ah a pikhe ding a, a maah kua hiam in a hihlup ding ahi:
4. Huan, siampu Eleazar in a khut in a sisan a la ding a, kihoupihna puan inn malam ah a sisan sagih vei a theh ding.
5. Huan, kua hiam in huai bawngla a mit muh in a hal ding; a vunte, a sate, a sisante, a ekte toh a hal ding.
6. Huan, siampu in sidar sing leh husop leh khausan a la ding a, bawngla a hal uah a pailut ding ahi.
7. Huan, siampu in puansilhte a sawp ding a, a kisil ding a, huai khit in giahphual ah a hoh ding a, nitaklam tan in siampu a siangthou kei ding.
8. Huan, a hal mi in a puansilhte a sawp ding a, a kisil dinga nitaklam tan in a siangthou kei ding.
9. Huan, mi thanghuai lou in bawngla vu a lakhawm ding a, dainawl mun siang ah a koih ding a, Israel suante mipi suante ding in kisian sakna tui dia zat ding ahi: huai bel khelh thoihna ahi
10. Huan, bawngla vu hih khawm min a puansilhte a sawp ding a, nitaklam tan in a siangthou kei ding: hiai Israel suante ding in leh nam dang a kiang ua omte ding in khantawn dan ding ahi.
11. Kua luang leng a khoihmi peuhmah ni sagih a siangthou kei ding uh;
12. Huai mi a hihleh ni thum ni in a kisiangsak ding a, ni sagih ni in a siangthou ding; himahleh, ni thum ni a a kisiansak kei leh ni sagih ni in leng a siangthou kei ding hi.
13. Kuapeuh kua luang leng a khoih a, a kisiansak kei leh TOUPA biakbuk a hihnin ahi; huai mi pen Israel mite lak a kipan a satkhiak ding ahi; kisiansakna tui a tung a thehlouh a hihziak in a siangthou kei ding; a sianthou louhna a tungah a om lai ahi.
14. Puan inn a mi a sih leh hiai a dan ding ahi: puan inn a lut peuhmah leh puan inn sunga om peuhmah te ni sagih a siangthou kei ding uh.
15. Huan, tui-um-belsuan kihong, a sitna a sit louh peuhmah a nin ahi.
16. Huan gamlak ah nam kua thah hiam, misi luang hiam, mihing guh hiam, han hiam a khoih leh ni sagih a siangthou kei ding.
17. Huan, a siangthou loute tuam ding in khelh thoihna vu a la ding ua, bel ah tuikhuk tui toh a sung ding uh:
18. Huan, mi siang in, husop a la ding a, tui ah a diah ding a, puan innte, tui-um-belsuan tengtengte, huailai a om mi tengteng, guh hiam, thah luang hiam, misi luang hiam, han hiam khoih mite a theh ding a:
19. Huan, mi siang in ni thum ni leh ni sagih ni in mi siang lou a theh ding a, ni sagih ni in a hih siangthou ding a; a puansilhte a sawp ding a, a kisil ding a, nitaklam in a hong siangthou ta ding hi.
20. Mi siangthou lou kisiangsak lou in bel TOUPA mun siangthou a hihnit ziak in huai mi pen omkhawmte lak a kipan a satkhiak ding ahi; kisiansakna tui a theh louh ahi a, a siangthou lou ahi.
21. Hiai amau a ding in khantawn dan ding ahi: kisiansakna tui a theh peuhmah in a puansilhte a sawp ding ua; huan, a kisiansakna tui khoih peuhmah nitaklam tan in a siangthou kei ding uh.Huan, mi siangthou lou in a khoih peuhmah a siangthou kei zel ding a; huan, huai mi khoih mi kuapeuh nitaklam tan in a siangthou kei ding uhi, chi in.
22. Huan, mi siangthou lou in a khoih peuhmah a siangthou kei zel ding a; huan, huai mi khoih mi kuapeuh nitaklam tan in a siangthou kei ding uhi, chi in.
