←  Luke (8/24)  →
1. Anungin Jesu a paipaha, thu hilh leh Pathian gam Tanchin Hoih tun kawmin kho lian leh kho neute a tawn vialviala; a kiangah sawm leh nihte a om ua,
2. Numei kuate hiam dawi jawl dam nawnte leh china dam nawnte toh. Huai numeite tuh Mari a sunga pat dawi sagih pawtna Magdalini a chih uh,
3. Herod sumkempa Chuza ji Jonna, Sussana leh mi dang tampite toh ahi uh; huaiten amau neihsain amah a khotuah jel uh.
4. Huan, mipi tampitak a hongkikhawm ua, kho chiha mi a kianga a hong un, gentehnain thu a gena:
5. Buhchithehpa a buh chi theh din a kuana; huan, a theh lain a khen lampi sikah a kia a, min a sik ua, tungleng vasaten a ne zouta uh.
6. Huan, a khen suang tungah a kia a; huan a pout akin a kawt louh jiakin a vuai pah a.
7. Huan, a khen loulingnei lakah a kia a, louin a pou tonpiha, a dep mangta.
8. Huan, a khen lei hoih ah a kia a, a hongpou a, a leh jain a gahta a, a chi a. Huan, huai thu a gen lain a kikou a, Kuapeuh jakna dinga bilneiin ja uheh, a chi hi.
9. Huan, a nungjuiten a kiang ah, Hiai gentehna thu bang chihna ahia? chiin, a dong ua.
10. Huan, Pathian gam thugukte nou theih dia piak ahia, mi dang kiangah bel gentehna kiaa gen ahi; mu napia a muh theihlouhna ding uleh za napi-a a theihsiam louhna ding un.
11. Huai gentehna thu hiai ahi: Buh chi tuh Pathian thu ahi.
12. Huan, lampi sika te tuh thu zasate ahi uh; huan, diabol a hongpaia, a gin louh ua, hotdam a hih theih louhna ding un a lungtang ua kipan thu tuh a lak mangsak jel.
13. Huan, suang tunga te tuh a jak tak ua thu kipak taka pom pahte ahi ua; himahleh zung himhim a neikei ua, sawtlou chik kia a ging ua, khem hunin a tawp san nak uh.
14. Huan, loulingnei laka kiate tuh thu zate ahi ua; himahleh a khosak jelna uah damsung khosak ngaihtuahnate, hauhsaknate, nuamsaknaten a depa, a gah piching kei nak uh.
15. Lei hoih ate bel thu jaa lungtang kizen leh hoih a kem kinken a, kuhkaltaka gah suahte ahi uh.
16. Kuamahin khawnvak dein belin a khuh kei uh, lupna nuaiah leng a koih kei uh; a lut peuhmahin a vak a muh theihna dingun a koihna ah a koih jaw uh.
17. Thang lou ding a im bangmah a om ngal kei a, theih louh ding leh lang lou dinga selguk bangmah a om sam kei.
18. Huchiin, bangchibangin ahia na jak uh pilvang un; kuapeuh a nei tuh piak behlap ahi ding a, kuapeuh a neilou tuh, nei a kichih sun leng lak sak ahi ding, a chi hi.
19. Huan, a nu leh a unauten Jesu lam a honnaih ua, himahleh mipi jiakin a kiang a hongtung theikei ua.
20. Huan, Na nu leh na unaute nangmah muh tumin inpua ah ding nilouh ve uh, chiin, a hilh ua.
21. Huan, aman a kiang uah, Hiai Pathian thu jaa gingtate ka nu leh ka unaute ahi uh, a chi a, a dawnga.
22. Huan, hichi ahi a, huailaiin ni khat tuh longah a tuanga, a nungjuite toh; huan, a kiang uah, Dil gal khat lamah I kai ding, a chi a. Huchiin long a phel ua, a paita ua.
23. Huan, a pai lai un Jesu a ihmutaa; huan, dilah huihpi a hongnung suka, a long uh tuiin a dim dekdeka, lauhuai piin a omta uh.
24. Huan, a kiangah a vahoh ua, a phawng ua, Heutupa, Heutupa, mangthang ding ka kei umaw, a chi ua. Huan amah a khanglou-a huih leh tuikihot a taita a; huan, a tawp ua, a daita mai uhi.
25. Huan, a kiang uah, Na ginna uh kaw om ahia? a chi a. Huan, a lau ua, lamdang a sa mahmah ua, Hiai mi kua ahia leh? huih leh tui nangawn thu a piaka, a thu a man uh? a kichi uhi.
26. Huan, Gaili gam jawn Gerasinte gam a tung ua.
27. Huan, gama a pai khiakin, khuaa mi dawi mat kuahiamin a nadawna; huai mi tuh sawtpi puan himhim silh louin a oma, in himhim ah leng a om keia, han khawng ngen ah a om jaw a.
28. Huan, Jesu a muh takin a kikou a, a maah a khupboha, aw ngaih piin, Jesu, Pathian Tungnungpen Tapa, kei leh nang bang i kizolhna a om a? honhihgim mahmah lou dingin ka honngen ahi, a chi a.
29. Jesun dawi nin tuh huai mi sunga kipana pawt dingin thu a nape khin ngala. Huai mi tuh dawiin a man gige ahi; min khainiang leh kol a bun sak ua, a ching ngitnget ua; himahleh a gakna uh a hihtata, dawiin gamkeu khawngah a delhkhe jel hi.
30. Huan, Jesun, Na min bang ahia? chiin, a donga. Huan, aman, Nuai, a chi a, a sunga dawi tampi a lut jiakin.
31. Huan, kokhuk toneiloua pai thu a kiang ua pe lou dingin Jesu kiangah a ngen uh.
32. Huan, huailaiah tangah vok hon tampi a ta ua; huan, vok sunga lut theihna thu a kiangah a ngen ua. Huan, aman thu a pia a.
33. Huan, Dawite tuh huai mi sung akipan a pawt ua, vok sungah a lut ua; huan, vok hon tuh tuigei ken ah a tai suk duamduam ua, dil ah a tai lut ua, tuihakin a sita uh.
34. Huan, a vulten huai thil omdan a mu ua, a tai ua, khuaah leh gamahte khawng a gen ua.
35. Huan, Bang a hita a? chia, en dingin a pawt chiat ua; huan, Jesu kiang a hongtun un, dawi pawtsan nung puan silha, lungsim ngeingei puin, Jesu khe bula tu a hongmu ua, huchiin a lauta ua.
36. Huan, a theiten dawi mat a hihdamdan thu a maute a na hilh ua.
37. Huan a kim Gerasinte gama mi tengtengin, a lau mahmah jiak un, amah tuh a kiang ua kipan pai mang dingin a ngen ua; huan, longah a tuanga, a kik nawnta.
38. Huan, dawi pawtsan miin Jesu kianga om den a ngena.
39. Himahleh, Jesun, Na in ah pai inla, Pathianin nang dia thil a honhihsak a thupidan vahilh in, chiin a sawl khia hi. Huchiin, a paia, khopi sung tengteng ah Jesun amah adia thil a hihsak thupidan a tangkou pihta hi.
40. Huan, Jesu a hongkik nawnin Mipiin amah a kipahpih ua; amah a nangak nilouh ngal ua.
41. Huan, ngai dih, mi khat a min Jaira kikhopna in vaihawmpa a hongpaia, Jesu khebul ah a khupboha, a ina hoh dingin a ngen ngutnguta.
42. Tanu tangkhat kum sawm leh kum nih hiam phial ding a neia, huai tuh laitatin a lum ngala. Huan Jesu a pai laiin mipi honin a boh lutlut ua.
43. Huan, numei khat kum sawm leh kum nih si pawt a china a sum neih tengteng daktorte lakah zang khintaa, kuamahin a hihdam theih tuan;
44. louh uh a nungah a hongpaia, a puan mong a khoiha; thakhatin a si pawt tuh a hongkam guiha.
45. Huan, Jesun, Honkhoih kua ahia? a chi a. Huan, Kei jaw ka hi kei, a nachi chiat ua. Peter leh a lawmten, Heutupa, mipi honin a honboh lutlut ua, honvel vengvung ve ua oi, a chi ua.
46. Himahleh, Jesun, Kua hiam in jaw honkhoih ve aw, thilhihtheihna ka sung akipan a pawt chih ka theih jiakin, a chi a.
47. Huan, numeiin a kisel theih louh a theihin, ling kawmin a hongpaia, a maah khupbohin a khoih san leh thakhata a damtak dan mi tengteng maah a genkhe ta.
48. Huan, aman, a kiangah, Tanu, na ginna dampih hi chia, khamuangtakin pai in, a chi a.
49. A pau laitakin kikhopna in vaihawmpa in akipan mi khat a hongpaia, Na tanu a sita, Heutupa hihgim nuamdahin, a chi a.
50. Jesun huai thu a jakin, Lau ken, gingta mai in, huchiin a dam nawn ding, a chi a, a dawnga.
51. Huan, in a vatunin, Peter, Johan, Jakob leh naupang nu leh pate kia chih louhngal kuamah lut a phal keia.
52. Huan, a vek un a nakap kheukhou ua, a kah vengvung ua, himahleh, aman, kap kei un, a si kei, a ihmu ahi jaw, a chi a.
53. Huan, sikhina a theih jiak un a nuihsan maimah ua.
54. Jesun bel a khut a lena, Nungak, thou ve, a chi a, a sam a.
55. Huan, a kha tuh a hongkik nawna, a thou paha.Huan, Banghiam nek ding piak ding thu a piaa. Huan, a nu leh a pan lamdang a sa mahmah ua. Himahleh, aman a thilhih tuh kuamah hilh lou dingin a chi bikbek hi.
56. Huan, Banghiam nek ding piak ding thu a piaa. Huan, a nu leh a pan lamdang a sa mahmah ua. Himahleh, aman a thilhih tuh kuamah hilh lou dingin a chi bikbek hi.
