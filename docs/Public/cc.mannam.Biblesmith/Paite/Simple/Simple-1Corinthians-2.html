1. Huan, unaute aw, kei jaw Pathian thuguk nou honhilh dinga na kiang ua ka hong laiin, thu gen thupi leh pilna thupi toh hong ka hi kei hi.
2. Jesu Kris, amah mah kilhdena om thu kia loungal na lak uah bangmah himhim theih ka tum kei a.
3. Huan, hatlou leh lauin, nakpi taka lingkawmin na lak uah ka om a.
4. Ka paukam suak leh ka thu gente thu pila khemna thuin ahi kei hi. Kha leh thilhihtheihna theihsaknain ahi jaw hi.
5. Huchia, na ginna uh mihing pilnaa kinga loua, Pathian thilhihtheihna a kinga a a om theih jawkna dingin.
6. Ahihhangin pichingte lakah ahihleh thu pil i hilh jela, himahleh hiai khovel thupil jaw ahi kei; hiai khovel vaihawmte mangthang dingte thupil leng ahi sam kei hi:
7. Pathian pilna guk leh kisel, i thupina dinga Pathianin khovel om maa a sehkholhsa pen i hilh zonak.
8. Huai tuh hiai khovel vaihawmte himhimin a theikei uh; thei le ujaw thupina Toupa kilhden lou ding hi ve un.
9. Mita muh ngei louh, bila jak ngeilouh, mihing lungtanga lut ngeilou, Pathianin mah itte adia a bawl, chih gelh bangin.
10. Pathianin Khain huaite ei a honthei sakta ahi; Khain bangkim a sui khe vek ngala; ahi, Pathian thugil leng.
11. Mihing ngaihtuahte mihing lungsim a sunga om mah louin, kuan ahia thei ding? Huchimahbangin Pathian ngaihtuahte Pathian Kha mahlouin kuamahin a theikei uhi.
12. Huan, a thawna Pathianin thil honpiakte i theihna dingin, eiten jaw khovel kha hi louin, Kha, Pathian kianga ngei, i mu jaw uhi
13. Hiai tuh mihing pilnaa sinsak thute-a hilh louin Kha sinsakin i hilh jaw ua, Kha neite kiangah khalam thutakte i hilhchian uhi.
14. Khovel min Pathian Kha thilpiakte a sang kei nak hi, amah ngaihdanin haihuai ahi ngala, kha a theihchet ahihjiakin a theithei kei himhim ahi.
15. Khamiin bel thil bangkim achian jela, amah lah kuamahin achian kei uhi.Kuan ahia Toupa lungsim theikhin a, amah thuhilh thei ding? Eiten bel Kris lungsim i pu ahi.
16. Kuan ahia Toupa lungsim theikhin a, amah thuhilh thei ding? Eiten bel Kris lungsim i pu ahi.
