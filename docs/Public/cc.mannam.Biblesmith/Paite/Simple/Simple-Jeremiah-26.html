1. Juda kumpipa Josia tapa Jehoiakim lal pattungin hiai thu TOUPA kiang akipan a hongtunga,
2. TOUPAN hichiin a chi: TOUPA in huang sungah ding inla, Juda khopi tengteng Pathian be dinga TOUPA ina hongpaite kiangah a kiang ua gen dinga thu ka honpiak tengteng khat lel leng im louin gen vek in.
3. Michihin a omdan gilou uh paisanin a ngaikhe zenzen moh ding uhia, a thilhih gilou uh jiaka a tung ua thil hoihlou hih ka tup ka hep theihna dingin.
4. Huan, nang a kiang uah, TOUPAN hichiin a chi: non ngaihkhiak louh ua, ka dan na ma ua ka koih na zuih louh ua,
5. Jing pipia thoua amau ka sawl na kiang ua ka sawl ka nasemmi jawlnei thu na limsak kei uleh;
6. Hiai in Siloh bangin ka bawl dinga, leitunga nam tengtengte hamsia thuak dingin ka bawl ding, na chi ding hi.
7. Huan, siampute, jawlneite leh mipi tengtengin TOUPA ina Jeremiain hiai thu a gen a ngaikhia uh.
8. Huan, hichi ahia, mipi tengteng kianga gen dinga TOUPAN a piak tengteng a gen khitin, siampute, jawlneite leh mipi tengtengin amah a manta ua, Si peuhmah ni teh.
9. Bangdia TOUPA min loua, Hiai in Siloh bang ahi ding, hiai khopi luahmi om louin a segawp ding, chia gen na hia? a chi ua. Huchiin TOUPA in ah mipi tengtengin Jeremia a boh lutlut uh.
10. Huan, Juda heututen hiai thu a jak un, kumpipa in a kipanin TOUPA in lutna kongpi thak ah a tu uh.
11. Huan, siampute leh jawlneiten heutute leh mipi tengteng kiangah, Hiai mi sitak ahi, khopi siat ding thu, na bil ua na jak bang un a gen khol ngala, a chi ua.
12. Huan, heutute leh mipi tengteng kiangah Jeremiain, Na thu ngaihkhiak khitsa uh tengteng bangin hiai in leh hiai khopi siat ding thu gen dingin TOUPAN kei a honsawl ahi.
13. Huaijiakin tuin na omdan uleh na gamtatdan uh bawl hoih unla, TOUPA na Pathian uh thu mang un; huchiin TOUPAN na tung ua thil hoih lou tung ding a phuankhiaksa a kikhelsak thei ding.
14. Kei ahihleh, na khut ua om keita ve. Dik leh hoih na chih bangbang un hon bawl un.
15. Na honthah uleh, noumau leh hiai khopi leh khua leh tuite tungah mi hoih sisan na tungsak ngeisin uh chih thei mai un; a dikdikin na bil ua hiai thu tengteng gen dia TOUPAN na kiang ua honsawl ka hi hi, achia.
16. Huchiin heutute leh mipiten, siampute jawlneite kiangah, Hiai mi a sih ding ahi kei, TOUPA i Pathian muna i kiang ua thu gen ahi ve, a chi ua.
17. Huan, huai gam upa kuate hiam a hongding ua, mipi omkhawmte kiangah,
18. Mika, Moraseth miin Juda kumpipa Hezekia lal laiin thu a gena, huan, Juda mi tengteng kiangah, Sepaihte TOUPAN hichiin a chi: Zion lou banga leh ahi dinga, Jerusalem thil se omkhawm ahi ding, a in mual gammang mun sang bang ahi ding, achia.
19. Juda kumpipa Hezekia leh Judate tengtengin amah a that uhia? TOUPA lauin TOUPA deihsakna a ngen ua, TOUPAN a tung ua thil hoihlou tung ding a phuan a kikhelsak ahi kei maw? En jaw i tung ua thil hoihlou tungsak ding i hi ve ua.
20. Huan, mi dang, TOUPA mina thu gen Keriath-Jenrima mi Semei tapa Uria mah leng a oma, Jeremia thu gen tengteng mahbang in hiai gam leh khopi siat ding thu a gen hi.
21. Kumpipa Jehoiakim leh a mi thupite tengteng leh heutute tengtengin a jak un kumpipan hihlup a tuma; himahleh Uriain a jakin a laua, a taimanga, Aigupta gamah a paita hi.
22. Huan, kumpipa Jehoiakimin Aigupta gamah mi sawla, Akbor tapa Elnathan leh mi dangte toh;
23. Huan, Aigupta gam akipanin Uria a pi khia ua, kumpipa Jehoiakim kiangah a honpi ua, huaiin namsauin a thata, a luang vantang han ah a pailut ua, a chi ua.Himahleh Saphan tapa Ahikamin Jeremia a guma, huchiin hihlup ding in mipite kiangah piak ahita kei.
24. Himahleh Saphan tapa Ahikamin Jeremia a guma, huchiin hihlup ding in mipite kiangah piak ahita kei.
