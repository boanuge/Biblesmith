1. Na lak uah kuapeuhin midang toh thubuai neita le uchin, mi siangthoute maa tun louin, mi diktatloute maah na tun ngam ding ua hia?
2. Ahihleh, misiangthouten khovel a vaihawm ding uh chih na thei ua hia? Huan, khovel bawn na vaihawm ding u leh, thil neupente leng ngaihtuah dia kilawmlou na hi ua hia?
3. Tulai damsung thute gen louh, angelte nangawn i vaihawm ding uh chih na theikei ua hia?
4. Hichiin, tulai damsung thute ngaihtuah ding na neih un bangdia saptuamte ngaihsanglouhpente ngaihtuah sak nahi ua?
5. Na zumna ding un ka gen ahi, A unaute kala thu hihpuk thei mi pil, khat bek na lak uah na mu kei ua a ding maw?
6. Unau in a unau vaihawmna a tut jawk nak, ginglou maah a hih behlap toh?
7. Hilou, hiai na kizen zoh louhna khat uh ahi pah hi, thubuai na kineih tuah nak uh. Bang dia diklou pia hihna nathuak zo lou? Bang achia kijang sak zo mailou oi?
8. Hilou, noumau ngeiin na unaute uh tungah leng na hih dik kei ua, na jang sak nak uhi.
9. Ahihleh, mi diktatlouten Pathian Gam a luah kei ding uh chih na theikei ua hia? Khemin om kei un, kingaihhatte hiam, milim bete hiam, angkawmte hiam, mi hukte hiam, tohukte hiam,
10. Gutate hiam, mihuaihamte hiam, zukhamhatte hiam, salhhatte hiam, lepgute hiamin Pathian Gam a luah kei ding uh.
11. Huan, noute khenkhat huchi bang na hi uhi; himahleh, Toupa Jesu Kris min ah leh i Pathian Kha ah siansak na hita ua, hihsiangthou na hita uh, siamtang na hita uhi.
12. Thil bangkim hih kei dingin a sianga, himahleh thil bangkim hih a hoih kim khol kei; thil bangkim hih kei ding in a sianga, himahleh bangmah sikha din ka kiphal kei hi.
13. Nek dingte khawng gila ahi a, gil khawng leng nek dinga ahi; himahleh Pathianin ahihmang tuaktuak ding. Huan, pumpi pen kingaihna ding ahi keia. Toupa ading ahijaw hi; Toupa leng pumpi ading ahi hi:
14. Huan, Pathianin Toupa a kaithoua, eite leng a thilhihtheihnain a honkaithou sam ding hi.
15. Na pumpi uh Kris hiangte ahi chih na theikei ua hia? Kris hiangte laksakin kijuak hiang ding peuhin ka bawl dia hia leh?
16. Bawl lou hial ding. A hihleh, kijuak zop pen, amah toh pumkhat ahi uh chih na theikei ua hia? A nih un sa pumkhat a hong hita ding uh, a chi ngala.
17. Toupa zop bel amah toh kha pumkhat ahi uhi.
18. Kingaih taisan un. Mihing thil hihkhelh chiteng pumpi po lamah ahi; mingaihhat inbel amah pumpi tungah thil a hihkhial ahi.
19. Ahihleh, na pumpi uh, na sung ua om Pathian kianga kipana na muh uh, Kha Siangthou temple ahi chih na theikei ua hia? Noumaua leng na hi kei ua, mana leisa na hita uh.Huaijiakin, na pumpi un Pathian pahtawi un.
20. Huaijiakin, na pumpi un Pathian pahtawi un.
