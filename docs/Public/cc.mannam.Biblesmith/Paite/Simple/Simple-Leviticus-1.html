Leviticus (1/27)  →
1. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a sam a, kihoupihna puan inn a kipan in a houpih a,
2. Israel suante houph inla, a kiang uah, Na lak ua kuapeuh in thillat TOUPA kiang a na lat un, gan, bawng hon leh belam hon lak ate, na lat ding uh ahi.
3. A thillat pen bawng hon lak ate, haltum thillat a hih leh a pa, gensiatbei a lat ding ahi: amah TOUPA ma a pom a hih theihna ding in kihoupihna puan inn kongpi bul a lat ding ahi
4. Huan, haltum thillat lu tungah a khut a koih ding a; huchi in amah a ding in lemna bawlna ding in pomsak ahi ding.
5. Huan, TOUPA ma ah bawngpa a gou ding a; huan, Aron tapa, siampute'n sisan a lan ding ua, kihoupihna puan inn kongpi bul a maitam sisan in a theh ding uh ahi.
6. Huan, haltum thillat a lip ding a, a sem ding ahi.
7. Huan, siampu Aron tapate'n maitam ah mei a koih ding ua, mei tungah sing achiangkhawm ding uh:
8. Huan, Aron tapa, siampute'n sabakte, a lute, a thaute maitam a mei tungah sing ah kilawmtak in a koih ding uh:
9. A sungkuate leh a khete bel tui in a sil ding: siampu in TOUPA a ding gimlim tak, mei a lat haltum thillat ding in a pum in maitam ah a hal ding ahi.
10. Huan, a thillat haltum thillat ding a gan hon lak ate, belam hiam, kel hiam a hih leh, a pa, gensiatbei a lat ding ahi.
11. Huan, maitam sik, mal lam pang ah TOUPA ma ah a gou ding a: huan, Aron tapa, siampute'n maitam sisan in a kimvel in a theh ding uh ahi.
12. Huan, a sem ding a, alu toh, a thau toh; huan, siampu in maitam tung a mei tung a sing ah a koih hoih ding:
13. A sungkuate leh a khete bel tui in a sil ding: huan, a pum in siampu in a lan ding a, maitam ah a hal ding ahi: TOUPA a ding a gimlim tak, mei a lat haltum thillat ahi.
14. Huan, TOUPA a dia thillat vasa lam haltum thillat a hihleh, a thillat ah vakhute hiam, vapal noute hiam a lat ding ahi.
15. Huan, siampu in maitam ah a hon tawi ding a, a lu a hektan ding a, maitam ah a hal ding; huan, a sisan maitam pang ah a takkang sak ding;
16. Huan, a gongkholh a mul toh a pai ding a, maitam kiang, suah lam pang a meivu koihna mun ah a pai ding:Huan, a khate a bottol ding a, a tak botzak lou in: huan, siampu in maitam ah, mei tungah a hal ding; huai bel TOUPA a ding a gimlim tak, mei a lat haltum thillat ahi.
17. Huan, a khate a bottol ding a, a tak botzak lou in: huan, siampu in maitam ah, mei tungah a hal ding; huai bel TOUPA a ding a gimlim tak, mei a lat haltum thillat ahi.
