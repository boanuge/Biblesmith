1. Huan, Ephraimte a kikai khawm ua, mallam juanin a pai ua; huan, Jephtha kiangah, Bangdia Amon suante sual dinga hongpai pheia, na kianga hoh dinga hon chial sam lou? na in ah ka hon buhal ding uh, a chi ua.
2. Huan, Jephthain a kianguah, Amon suante ka mite toh theihtawp suahin ka sual ua; ka hon thukhah laiin a khut ua kipanin lah non honkhe ngal kei ua.
3. Huchiin non honkhe nuamkei uh chih ka theih tak in, sih khawk salou hialin ka panga, Amon suante sual dingin ka kuana, hichiin TOUPAN amaute ka khut ah a honpiaa: huai jiakin bangdinga honsual dinga tunia hongkuan na hi ua? a chi a.
4. Huchiin Jephthain Gilead mi tengteng a kaikhawma, Ephraimte a sualtaa: huan, Gileadten Ephraimte a that ek ua, Nou Ephraim nam tai mangte, nou Gileadte, Ephraimte lakah leh Manasite laka teltei maimaite, a chihna jiak un.
5. Huan, Gileadten Ephraimte naih Jordan lui kainate a nala ua; Huan, hichi ahia, Ephraim taimang kuapeuhin, Ka hong galkah ding uh, a chih tengin Gileadten a kiangah, Ephraim mi na hi hia? a na chi jel ua. Aman, Hi lou e, a na chih leh,
6. A kiangah, Shiboleth, honchi dih, a na chi ua, huan, aman, Siboleth ana chi zo jela; a lou tel tei siam ngal sek keia; huchiin a man ua, Jordan lui kainate ah a na that jel uh; huchiin huai tungin Ephraimte sing li leh sang nih a that uh.
7. Huan, Jephthain Israelte tungah kum guk vai a hawma, huchiin Gilead gam a khua khat ah a vui uh.
8. Huan, a nungin Beth-lehem khuaa mi Ibzanin Israelte tungah vai a hawma.
9. Huaiin tapa sawmthum a neia, tanu sawmthum gam dangah a paisaka, huan a tapate adingin gam danga mi tanu sawmthum a pisak laia. Huan, Israelte tungah kum sagih vai a hawma.
10. Huan, Ibzan a sia, Beth-lehem khua ah a vui uh.
11. Huan, a nungin Zebulun mi Elonin Israelte tungah vai a hawma; Israelte tungah kum sawm vai a hawm.
12. Huan, Zebulun mi Elon a sia, Zebulun gama Aijalon khua ah a vui uh.
13. Huan, huainungin Pirathon mi, Hilel tapa Abdonin Israelte tungah vai a hawma.
14. Huai miin tapa sawmli leh tupa sawmthum sabengtung nou sawmsagih tunga tuang jel mi a nei a: aman Israelte tungah kum giat vai a hawma.Huan, Pirathon mi, Hilel tapa Abdon a sia, Ephraim gam, Amalek mite tanggam ah Pirathon khua ah a vuita uh.
15. Huan, Pirathon mi, Hilel tapa Abdon a sia, Ephraim gam, Amalek mite tanggam ah Pirathon khua ah a vuita uh.
