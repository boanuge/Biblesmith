1. Huan, hiai theiin, ni nanung lamahte hun haksa pipi a hong om ding hi.
2. Mi amau ki itte bang, dangka deihta te bang, kiuangsakte bang, kisatheite bang. Pathian gensete bang, nulehpa thu mangloute bang, kipahna dan theiloute bang, siangthouloute bang,
3. itna pianpih neiloute bang, buaina bawlte bang, hekte bang, kidektheiloute bang, mi huhamte bang, hoih deih hetloute bang,
4. zuautatte bang, kingaisang bang, thunung khualloute bang, Pathian itsang a amau kipahna kia itzawte bang hiding ahingal ua;
5. Pathian limsak batna anei ding ua, himahleh a thilhihtheihna a taksang kei ding uh; tuabangte nungngatsan in.
6. Huchibangte lak a mituh, inn peuh a lut a gitloh gikpua numei hai deuhte sala manman te ahingal ua, huchibang numeite tuh duhgawlna chichih a pimang,
7. zilsam napia, thutak theipha ngeiloute ahi uhi.
8. Huan, Janni leh Jambri in Mosi adou bang un, huaimiten leng thutak tuh adou gige uhi; lungsim sesa ginna lama Pathian deihlouh mi ahi uhi.
9. Himahleh huai val ahih kei ding uhi, ahaidan uh mitengteng kiang a hong kilang ding ahingala, Janni leh Jambri haidan a hong kitheih mahbang in.
10. Nangzaw ka hon thuhilhte, ka omdante, ka thiltupte, ka ginnate, ka thuakzohnate, ka itna te, ka kuhkalnate,
11. hon sawina uh, hon gimbawlna uh na thei mahmah a; Antiok khua khawng a, Ikoniam khua khawng a, Listra khua khawng bang a ka tung a thiltung leh sawina ka thuaknate khawng; huaitengteng ah tuh Toupan a hon honkheta ngal a;
12. ahi, kuapeuh Kris Jesu Pathian limsaka, om tum photphot in sawina khawng a thuak sam ding uhi.
13. Migilou leh mi khemhatte tuh, mi khem leh khema omin a gilou semsem ding uhi.
14. Himahleh nangzaw na zilsa leh kiptak a na gintaksa thutak a om gige in, huaituh kua a kipan a na zil ahia chih thei gige in.
15. Huan, Kris Jesu ginna ziak a Hotdamna mu ding a nang hon hih pil thei Laisiangthoute naupang nou a kipan theikhin na hi chih thei gige in.
16. Laisiangthou peuhmah Pathian hatkhuma piak ahi a; huaziakin, thuhilhna ding bang, taihilhna ding bang, bawlhoihna ding bang, diktatna lam a kichilna ding bang in a phatuam hi;huchia Pathian mi tuh nahoih chiteng sem ding a kimtak a kivana a kim theihna ding in.
17. huchia Pathian mi tuh nahoih chiteng sem ding a kimtak a kivana a kim theihna ding in.
