1. Huaijiakin, bila ijakte khawng phatuamngai takin I khawksa nawn diak ding uhi, huchilouin jaw I tolh mang san zenzen kha ding.
2. Angelte zanga thugen nangawn anakipa, tatlekna leh thumanlouhna chi tengtengin thukna diktathuaitak a muh jel ngal un tuh,
3. huchi tela hotdamna thupi ngeingaih kei leng, bangchin ahia isuahtak theih mahmah ding? Huai hotdamna thutuh a tunga Toupa zanga gen khit ahi a, azaten honna hihkipsak uhi;
4. Pathianin leng, chiamtehnate leh thil lamdangte leh thilhihtheihna chiteng toh, amahdeih bangjela Kha Siangthou thil hawmte khawng toh, amaute a theihpih hi.
5. Khovel hongom ding a tanchin I gen uh, angelte nuaiah jaw a koih kei hi.
6. Himahleh, mi kuahiamin koilai hiamah hiai thu tuh genin a theisak zota hi; Mihing bang atela, na theihgige? Mihing tapa leng bang a tela, na veh nak?
7. Amah tuh angelte sanga nuaiin na bawl tadiha; thupina leh pahtawina a khusaka, na khutsuak tungahte heutuin na bawl laia;
8. A khe nuaiahte khawng bangkim na koiha, chiin. Huchiin bangkim a nuaia a koih jiakin anuaia koih louh bangmah a neikei hi. Ahihhangin tun ah jaw bangkim a nuaiah omin I mu nai tadih kei uhi.
9. Himahleh, Jesu, angelte sanga nuaia a bawl tadih tuh, sihna thuak jiaka thupina leh pahtawina khuksaka omin I muta jaw ahi, Pathian hehpihna jiakin sihna tuh mi chih a chepsakna ding.
10. Amah a ding leh amah jiaka bangkim om-pan ta tampi thupinaa piluta amaute hotdamna Bawlpa pen, a thuakna jiaka, a hihbukim tuh amaha dia kilawm him ahi.
11. Hihsiangthoupa leh hihsiangthoute tuh avekun Mi khata pawt ahi ngal ua; huaijiakin amah tuh amaute, Unaute, chih a zum kei hi.
12. Ka unaute kiangah na min ka gen dia; omkhawmte lakah lasain ka honphat ding, chiin.
13. Huan, Amah ah ka ginna ka nga dia, a chi nawna. Huan, endih ua, kei leh Pathian honpiak tate, achi nawn nalaia.
14. Huchiin, naupangte tuh sa sisan tangte a hih chiat jiak un, amah ngeiin huai bangmah a tang samta a; sihnaa sihna thuneipa (huai tuh Diabol) ahihmang theihna ding leh,
15. Sih kihtak jiaka damsunga sikha a om tengtengte tuh a suahtak sak theihna dingin.
16. A awlmoha, panpih jelte tuh angelte ahi dek kei ua, Abraham suante ahi jaw uhi.
17. Huchiin, mite adinga khelhna thuphatawina bawlsak dingin, Pathian lamthilte ah Siampu Lianpen, mi lainatthei leh muanhuai taka hih theihna dingin, bangkima a unaute banga hongom tuh amah adingin a kiphamoh him ahi.Amah ngeiin khemna tuakin a na thuaktak jiakin khemna tuakte tuh a panpih thei ahi.
18. Amah ngeiin khemna tuakin a na thuaktak jiakin khemna tuakte tuh a panpih thei ahi.
