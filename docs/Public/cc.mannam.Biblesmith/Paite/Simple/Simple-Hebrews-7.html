1. Huai Melkisedek, Salemkumpipa Tungnungpen Siampu, Abraham kumpipate that hongpai na dawn a vualzawlpa,
2. Abraham in bangkim ah sawmakhat a pia. Amin khiakna genchian in amasa pen in Diktatna Kumpipa, azom nawn in Salem Kumpipa ahinawna; huaituh Lemna Kumpipa chihna ahi.
3. Amahtuh pa neilou in, nu neilou in, khangthu bei, ompatni neilou hintawpni leng neisamlou in. Pathian tapa bang a siam tangtawn a siampu hi gige ahi zaw hi.
4. Huchi in, chikhah Abraham in a galllak hoihpeuhmah laka sawmakhat apiakna pa, huaimipa thupi dan ngaihtuah dih ua.
5. Levi suante laka siampuna tang peuhmah in dan thu bangin mite lak ah sawmakhat lak ding thu amu ngei uhi, huaituh a unaute uh lak ah ahi, huaite tuh Abraham khal a pawt hisam mahle u leng.
6. Himahleh, alak ua a suante suikhawmsak sam louh pan Abraham lak ah sawmakhat ala a, thuchiamte kempa tuh vual ana zawlta hi.
7. Himahleh, sel vuallouhin a neujaw tuh thupi zaw vualzawl sak in a om hi.
8. Huan, hiai ah mihing si zel ten sawmakhat a mu ua, huai ah bel, ahing nilouh, chi a, a thu a theisak un a mu ahi.
9. Huan, Levi, sawmakhat lazelpa nangawn in, Abraham khut ah sawmakhatte a peta, chih theih ahi;
10. Melkisedek in a na dawn lai in apakhal ah a om lai- ngal a.
11. Huchiin, Levi ten siampu hihna ziaka bukimna muhtheih na hi leh (huainuai ah miten tuh dan thu ana mu ngal ua), Aaron omdan bang a bawl louh Melkisedek omdan bang a siampu dang hong om bang kiphamawh ding ahia?
12. Siampu hihna tuh ahong lamdan tak ziakin dan thu tuh hihlamdan sam a hong kiphamawh himhim ahi.
13. Hiai a thu agenpa namtuam mi ahi ngal a, huai nam laka a mi tuh kuamah maitam ah a gamtang ngeikei uhi.
14. I Toupa lah Juda nam lak a hong suak ahi chih a kichian ngal a: Huai nam thu ah tuh Mosi in siampu te lam thu bangmah a gen kei hi.
15. Huan, Melkisedek bang a siampu dang hong suah tuh nakpi takin I thugen pen a kichian nawn diak hi;
16. Amahtuh sa thupiak dan thu bang a siam bang hilou, hin tawpneilou thil hihtheihna bang zaw a, siam ahi.
17. Nang zaw, Melkisedek omdan bang a khantawn a siampu na hi chih theihsak ahi ngala.
18. Ahatlouh leh a phattuamlouhna ziakin thupiak awm masa tuh paihkhiak ahi.
19. (Dan thu in bangmah a hihkim ngal kei a). Lametna hoih zaw, Pathian I naihna zel tuh, pilutin a om zawta hi.
20. Huan, kichiamna tellou lou-in amah tuh Siampu a bawl ahita hi:
21. Amau zaw kichiamlou a siampu a siam ahi ngal ua; amah bel, nang zaw khantawna Siampu na hi, chi in, Toupa a kichiam a, ka khelkik kei ding, chi a a kiang a genpa khut ah kichiam in:
22. Huchitaka thupi in Jesu tuh thukhun hoih zaw hihkip pa a hong hita hi.
23. Huan, amau zaw sih ziaka omden theilou ahih ziakun, siampu a siam a tam ngei ua;
24. Amahbel khantawna a om gige ziakin, a Siampu hihna tuh khen theih vuallouh in a nei zaw hi.
25. Huchiin, amaha Pathian kianga hongpaite tuh a tawp dong in a hondam thei ahi, amau thumsak in khantawna a hin gige ziak in.
26. Huchibang Siampu Lianpen, misiangthou, zulhzau lou, ninbanglou, khialte laka omtuam, vante sang a tungnung zaw a, siam, tuh eia ding in a kilawm him hi.
27. Aman tuh huai siampu lianpente lat bang in, niteng in amah khelh ziak a kithoihna lan masa phawt a, huainung a mite khelhna ziaka lat a kiphamoh kei; Amah a kilat laitak in setden ding in khat vei in ana hihkhinta ngal a,Dan thu in, mihing hatlouhna neite tuh, siampu lianpen ding in a sep zel a; ki chiam thu, dan thu nung a a hong om in bel, Tapa, khantawna ding a hihkim khitsa tuh, asep zouta ahi.
28. Dan thu in, mihing hatlouhna neite tuh, siampu lianpen ding in a sep zel a; ki chiam thu, dan thu nung a a hong om in bel, Tapa, khantawna ding a hihkim khitsa tuh, asep zouta ahi.
