←  Zechariah (14/14)    
1. Ngaiin, TOUPA ni a hongtung, na gallak na laka hawma a om ding hun.
2. Jerusalem doua galdou dingin namte tengteng lah ka kaikhawm sin ngala; huan khopi tuh lakin a om dia, inte lohin leh, numeite sualin a om ding uh; huan khopi kimkhat sal ah a pai khe dinga, mi omlaite khopi akipana sat khiakin a om kei ding uhi.
3. Huaichiangin TOUPA a pai khe dia, kidou laia a dou bang in huai namte a dou ding hi.
4. Huan huai ni chiangin Oliv mual tungah a ding dinga, suahlam panga Jerusalem majawna om ah, huchiin Oliv mual bel suahlam sang tumlam sangah huai laiah a kitam dinga, huchiin guam thupi mahmah a omta ding; huan mual kimkhat mallam ah a kisuan dia a kimkhat simlamah. Huchiin ka mualte guam lamah na taikek ding uh.
5. Mualte guam lah Azal tung ding ahi ngala; ahi, Juda kumpipa Uzzia dam laia jinling ma akipan na taikek uh bangin; huchiin TOUPA ka Pathian a hongpai dia, a kianga mi siangthou te tengteng toh.
6. Huan huai ni chiangin hichi ahi dia, khovot a omkei dia, vuk a om sam kei ding.
7. Himahleh TOUPa adia theiha om ni khat ahi ding; sun hi lou, jan hi lou; himahleh hichi ahi dinga, nitaklamin khovak a om ding.
8. Huan huai ni chiangin hichi ahi dia, Jerusalem akipanin tui hingte a pawt khe dia; a lak ua kimkhat suahlam tuipiah, a lak ua kimkhat tumlam tuipi ah; nipi leh phalbiin a om den ding.
9. Huchiin leitung tengtengah TOUPA kumpipa ahi ding; huai ni chiangin TOUPa bel khat ahi dinga, a min khat
10. Jerusalem sim Geba akipan Rimmon tan, gam tengteng phaijang a suak ding; huan Jerusalem bel Benjamin kongpi a kipan kongpi khatna chiang, a ninga kongpi chiang. Hananel insang akipan kumpipa uainsukna pha akipan khen tuamin a om ding hi.
11. Huaiah mite a teng ding ua, hamsia a om nawn kei dia; himahleh Jerusalem muangtakin a teng ding hi.
12. Huan Jerusalem kidoupih mite tengteng TOUPAN a gawtna ding hi bel hiai ahi ding; a khe ua a din lai un, a sa uh a muat dia, a hawm sungah a mit uh a muat ding, a kam sung uah a lei uh a muat ding hi.
13. Huan huai ni chiangin hichi ahi dia, TOUPa akipan nakpi taka buaina a lak uah a hongom dia; mi chihin a insakinkhang khut ah a len chinten ding ua, a insakinkhang khut dou dingin a khut a kisa ding hi.
14. Huan Judain leng Jerusalem a dou dia; khokim khovela namte tengteng hauhsakna lakkhawm ahi ding, dangkaeng, dangka, puansilh a tama tamin.
15. Huchibangin huai puanintea om ding gamsa tengteng leh sakol, sakolneu, sangawnsau leh sabengtungte gawtna a om ding, hiai gawtna bangin.
16. Huan hichi ahi dinga, Jerusalem dou dia hongkuan nam tengteng laka nutsiata om mi chih a kum sikin Kumpipa, sepaihte TOUPA, be dingin a kuan tou ding ua, biakbukte ankuang luina nei dingin.
17. Huan hichi ahi dia, leia inko tengtenga, kuapeuh Jerusalema Kumpipa, sepaihte TOUPA, be dinga hoh tou lou peuhmah, a tunguah vuah a om kei ding hi.
18. Huan Aigupta inkuan a hoh touh louh ua, a hongpai kei uleh, amau tungah leng a om sam kei ding; biakbukte ankuang luina nei dia hoh tou namte gawtna ding hi tuh a om zo ding hi.
19. Hiai Aigupta gawtna leh biakbukte ankuanglui nei dia hoh tou namte tengteng gawtna ahi ding.
20. Huai ni chiangin sakolte longte tungah, TOUPA DINGA SIANGTHOU, chih a om dinga; huan TOUPa ina belte maitam maa noubemte bang ahi ding uhA hi, Jerusalem leh Judaa bel teng sepaihte TOUPA adingin siangthou ahi ding uh; huan a kithoihte a hongpai ding ua huai belte la-in kithoihna sa a huanna ding uh, huan huai ni chiangin sepaihte TOUPa in ah Kanaan mi a om nawnta kei ding hi.
21. A hi, Jerusalem leh Judaa bel teng sepaihte TOUPA adingin siangthou ahi ding uh; huan a kithoihte a hongpai ding ua huai belte la-in kithoihna sa a huanna ding uh, huan huai ni chiangin sepaihte TOUPa in ah Kanaan mi a om nawnta kei ding hi.
