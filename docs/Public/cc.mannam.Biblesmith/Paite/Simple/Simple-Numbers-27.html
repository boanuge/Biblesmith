1. Huan, Joseph tapa Manase suan Manase ta Makir, huai mi ta Gilea, huai mi ta Hepher tapa Zelophehad tanute a hong pai ua: a tanute min: Mala bang, Nova ban, Hogla bang, Milka bang, Tirza bang ahi uh.
2. Huan, kihoupihna puan inn kongkhak ah Mosi leh Siampu Eleazar leh mi liante leh omkhawmte tengteng maitam ah a va ding ua,
3. Gamdai ah ka pu uh a si a, TOUPA sual ding a kikhawm pawl, Kora pawl ah tel tei kei: Himahleh amah khelh ziak in a si a, tapa a neikei hi.
4. Tapa a neih louh ziak lel in bang ding a ka pa uh min a suante lak a kipan mang ding ahia? Ka pa unaute lak ah tantuan hon pia in, a chi ua.
5. Huan, Mosi in a thu uh TOUPA ma ah a tun a.
6. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
7. Zelophehad tanute'n a hihdik uhi: a pa uh unaute lak ah tantuan na bawl ngei ding ahi, a pa uh tantuan na piak ding ahi.
8. Huan, Israel suante'n a kiang ah, Mi, tapa neilou a a sih leh a gou a tanute na piak ding uh ahi.
9. Huan, tanu a neih kei leh a gou a unaute na piak ding uh ahi.
10. Huan, unaute a neih kei leh a gou a pa unaute na piak ding uh ahi.
11. Huan, a pa'n unaute a neih kei leh a gou a tanau na pe ding ua, aman a luah ding a: huan, TOUPA'N Mosi thu a piak bang in Israel suante ding in dan thupiak kip ahi ding, chi in gen in, chi in.
12. Huan TOUPA'N Mosi kiang ah, Abarim tang ah hohtou inla, Israel suante ka piak ding gam va en in.
13. Huan, na muh khit chiang in na unaupa Aron a chipihte kiang ah pi a hih mah bang in nang leng pi na hi ta ding hi!
14. Zin gamdai a a kikhawmte a phun lai ua a mitmuh ua tui a kei hon pahtawi ding a ka thu ka honpiak kalh a na hel ziak in, a chi a (Huaite Zin gamdai a Kadesa Mariba tuite ahi).
15. Huan, Mosi in TOUPA a houpih a,
16. Khawmpite tung a om ding mi khat, sa tengteng khate Pathian, TOUPA'N sep hen.
17. TOUPA mi khawmpite chingmi neilou belam bang a a om louhna ding un, a ma uh kai ding, amaute pikhia a pilut ding in, chi in.
18. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi kiang ah, Nun tapa mi lungsim neitak Josua pi inla, a tungah na khut nga inla:
19. Siampu Eleazar leh khawmpite ma ah ding sak inla, a mitmuh uah thuneihna pia in.
20. Huan, Israel suante omkhawm tengteng in thu a man theihna ding un a tungah na thupina nga in.
21. Huchi in amah siampu Eleazar ma ah a ding diing a, aman TOUPA ma ah Urmin thu a laksak ding a: a thu in a pawt ding ua, a thu in a lut ding ua, amah leh Israel suante tengteng, omkhawmte toh a chi a.
22. Huan, Mosi in TOUPA thupiak bang in a hih a: Josua a pi a, Eleazar leh omkhawmte ma ah a ding sak a:Huan, a tungah a khut a nga a, Mosi zang a TOUPA'N a gen bang in thuneihna a peta hi.
23. Huan, a tungah a khut a nga a, Mosi zang a TOUPA'N a gen bang in thuneihna a peta hi.
