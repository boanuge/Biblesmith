1. A diktatna uh vakna banga suaha, amah hotdamna khawnvak kuang banga a suah ma siah, Zion jiakin ka dai nilouh kei dinga, Jerusalem jiakin ka om kinken kei ding.
2. Huchiin na diktatna a mu ding ua, kumpipa tengtengin leng na thupina a mu ding uh: huan, min thak, Toupa kamin a sak dingin, a honsam ding uh.
3. Toupa kuta kilawmna lallukhu leh i Pathian khuta thupina lukhu na hi ding.
4. Lamdang, a hon chi nawnta kei ding uh; na gam leng, Hihsiat a chi nawn sam kei ding uh; Hephzibah, a hon chi zota ding ua, na gam leng Biulah, a chi ding uh: Toupa lah na tungah a kipak ngala, na gamin leng pasal a nei sin hi.
5. Tangvalin nungak ji dinga a nei jel bangin na tapaten jiin a hon nei ding uh: mou neimi mou tunga a kipah bangin, na Pathian na tungah a kipak ding hi.
6. Jerusalem aw, na kulhtungah galvilmi ka vilsaka; sun leh janin chiklaimahin in a dai kei ding uh: nou Toupa theihpih dinga omte aw, om kinken hetkei un.
7. Amah leng om kinken sak sam kei un, Jerusalem a hihkipa, leia phathuaia a bawl masiah.
8. Toupan a khut taklam leh a ban hat tak lou-a kichiamin, Na buh nang hondoumite an dingin ka pe nawn kei hial dinga: gam dang miten na sep khiak uain a dawn nawn kei ding uh.
9. A buh laten a ne zo ding ua, Toupa a phat ding uh; a loumiten ka mun siangthou huang sung intualah a dawnta ding, chiin.
10. Pawt unla, kulh kongpite ah pawt un; mite dingin lampi bawl unla; sung vum unla, lamlian sung vum un; suangte tom siang unla; mite adingin chiamtehna tak khia un.
11. Ngai un, kawlmong phain hiciin a theisak ahi, Zion tanu kiangah hichiin gen un, Enin, nangmah hotdamna a hongtung sin; enin, a kipahman a kiangah a om hi, a ditna leng a maah a om hi.Huchiin amau, Mi siangthoute, Toupa tatte, a chi ding uh; nang jaw Zon khiak, Pai san louh khopi a hon chi ding uh, chiin, a chi a.
12. Huchiin amau, Mi siangthoute, Toupa tatte, a chi ding uh; nang jaw Zon khiak, Pai san louh khopi a hon chi ding uh, chiin, a chi a.
