1. Huan TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
2. Israel suante houpih inla, a innkuan chih uh tuam ding in chiang khat chiat zel, a innkuan uh dungzui zel a a innteek pen tengteng zah, chiang sawm leh nih la lechin; huan, achiang uah a min uh gelh in.
3. Huan, Levi chiang ah Aron min gelh in: a innkuan chih uh a ding in chiang khat zel ahi ding ahi.
4. Huan, i kihouna, kihoupihna puan inn thutheihsakna ma ah na koih ding ahi.
5. Huan, hichi ahi ding a, mi ka tel ding chiang a sel ding a: huchi in Israel suante phunna, na tung a a phunna uh ka hihdai ding hi, chi in,
6. Huchi in Mosi in Israel suante a houpih a, a innteek pente tengteng un amau innkuan chih tuam ding in, innteek pen chih in chiang khat zel, chiang sawm leh nih a pia uh: Aron chiang leng achiang lak uah a tel sam hi.
7. Huchi in Mosi in thu theihsakna biakbuk ah TOUPA ma ah huai chiangte a koih hi.
8. Huan a zing in hichi ahi a, Mosi theihpihna puan inn ah a va hoh a; huan, ngai in, Levi innkuante a dia Aron chiang a sel a, pakmum a suah a, a pakpalh a, vaisuaklu min a suang hi.
9. Huan, Mosi in TOUPA ma a kipan chiang tengteng Israelte tengteng kiang ah a lakhia a; a na en ua, amau chiang khat a la uhi.
10. TOUPA'N Mosi kiang ah, Helhat tate ahi uh chih theihsak zelna ding in Aron chiang thu theihsakna bul ah koih nawn in; huchi in ka tung a a helna uh na hihdaihsak thei ding hi, a sihlouhna ding un, a chi a,
11. Huchi bang in Mosi in a hih a: TOUPA'N thu a piak bang in a hih hi.
12. Huan, Israel suante'n Mosi kiang ah, Ngai in, mangthang ka hi uhi, ka segawp ta uhi, ka segawp khin vekta uhi.TOUPA biakbuk hon naih peuhmah a si zel ua; mangthang vek ding ka hi ding uam? A chi uhi.
13. TOUPA biakbuk hon naih peuhmah a si zel ua; mangthang vek ding ka hi ding uam? A chi uhi.
