1. Belsazar kumpipan a toupate laka mi sang khat adingin ankuang thupi tak a bawla, sangkhatte maah uain a dawn hi.
2. Belsazarin, uain a chep laiin, a pa Nebukadnezzarin Jerusalema om biakin akipana a lak dangkaeng leh dangka tuiumbelsuante honla dingin thu a piaa; huchia kumpipa leh a toupate, a jite leh a meiten huaitea a dawn theihna ding un.
3. Huchiin Jerusalema om Pathian in, biakin akipana lakkhiak dangkaeng tuiumbelsuante a honla ta uhi; huan kumpipa leh a toupaten, a jite leh a meiten, huaitein a dawnta uh.
4. Uain a dawn ua, dangkaeng te, dangka, dalsik, sing, leh suang pathiante a phat uh.
5. Huai dak mahin mihinga khutzungte a hongpai khiaa, kumpipa inpi bang pek tungah khawnvakkoihna toh etdenna takah a gelhtaa; huan kumpipan laigelh khut bangtan hiam a mu ta hi.
6. Huailaiin kumpipa mel amah ah a lamdanga, a ngaihtuahnaten amah a patausakta hi; huchiin a khut a khea kipta keia, a khukte a kisat tuah nomnom uh.
7. Bummite, Kaldai mite leh aisanmite honpi un chiin nakpi takin kumpipa a kikou a. Kumpipan thu a gena, Babulon mipilte kiangah, Kuapeuhmah hiai laigelh sima, a hilhchetna honensak, puansandup silhsak ahi dinga; a ngawng kimvelin dangkaeng khainiang a ok dinga; huan gamsungah vaihawmmi thumna ahi ding, a chi a.
8. Huchiin kumpipa mi pilte tengteng a honglut ua: himahleh laigelh a sim theikei ua, a hilhchetna leng kumpipa a theisak theikei uhi.
9. Huchiin kumpipa Belsazar a patau mahmaha; amahah a mel a honglamdanga; a toupate a lungjing uhi.
10. Huchiin kumpipa leh a toupate thu jiakin kumpinu ankuangluina in sungah a hong luta; kumpinun thu a gena, Aw kumpipa, khantawnin hingin; na ngaihtuahnaten patausak kei henla, na mel leng lamdang kei heh:
11. Na gamsunga mi khat, amaha pathian siangthoute kha om, a om a; na pu dam laiin vakna leh theihtheihna leh pilna, pathiante pilna bang, amahah muhin a om; huan kumpipa Nebukadnezzar, kumpipa, na pan, ka chi, amah mitkhialdawitawite leh, bummite, Kaldaimite leh aisanmite heutuin a bawla;
12. Lungsim hoihpen, theihna, theihtheihna, mangte hilhchetna, thugen haksa etsakna, ginlelhna hihvengna kumpipan Beltesazar min a sakpa, huai Danial mahah muhin a om hi. Tuin Danial kisam sak henla, aman hilhchetna en sak leh, a chi a.
13. Huan Danial kumpipa maah a honpi ua, Kumpipan thu a gena Danial kiangah, Danial na hi hia, huai Juda saltang tate laka, ka pan Juda a kipan a pi?
14. Nangah pathian kha a oma, vakna leh theihtheihna leh pilna hoihpen nanga muhin a om chih na tanchin ka jaa.
15. Huchiin mi pilte, bummite, ka maah a honpi lut ua, hiai laigelh sima, a hilhchetna ka kianga theisak dingin; himahleh huai thil hilhchetna a ensak theikei uhi.
16. Himahleh hilhchetnate na bawl theia, ginlelhnate na bawlhoih thei chih, na tanchin ka ja: tuin laigelh na sim theiha, a hilhchetna non hilh theih aleh, puansandup kon silh sak dinga; na ngawng kimvelin dangkaeng na ok dinga; gamsungah vaihawmmi thumna na hi ding hi, a chi a.
17. Huchiin Danialin a dawnga kumpipa maah, Na thilpiakte nangmah kiangah hi jaw henla, na kipahmante midang kiangah pe jawin; ahi-alah kumpipa kiangah laigelh ka sim dinga, a hilhchetna amah ka theisak ding.
18. Aw nang kumpipa, Tungnungpen Pathianin na pa Nebukadnezzar gam leh, thupina leh letna leh lalna a piaa:
19. Huan huai amah a piak letna jiakin mite tengteng, namte, paute a maah a ling ua a lau uh: a ut a that a, a ut a hing hawi; a ut a kai thoua, a ut a sawm niam hi.
20. Himahleh a lungtang a kisaktheih laiin leh kiuangsak taka gamtaa a lungsim hihsaka a om laiin, a kumpi laltutphah akipan suanin a oma, amah akipan a thupina a la kheta uh.
21. Huan mite tapate akipana bawlkhiakin a om a; huchiin a lungtang sate banga bawlin a om a; bawngtalte banga loupa a vakin a oma, a pumpi van daituiin a kawta; Tungnungpen Pathianin mite gamah vai vai a hawma, a utut a tungah a dingkhesak chih a hontheih matan.
22. Huan nang a tapa, Aw Belsazar, na lungtang na niamkhiak nai keia, hiai tengteng na theihin leng.
23. Himahleh van TOUPA demin na kihihsanga; huan amau na maah A ina tuiumbelsuante a honla ua; huan nang leh na toupate, na jite leh na meiten, huaitein uain na dawn uh; dangka, leh dangkaeng, dal, sik, sing leh suang pathiante, mu theilou, za theilou, thei theilou-te na phata; huan Pathian a khuta na hatna a omna, na lampite tengteng amaha hi, na thupibawlta kei hi.
24. Huchiin amaha kipan huai khut banghiam tan sawlin a hongoma, hiai laigelh gelhin a omta hi.
25. Huan gelha om laigelh hiai ahi, MENE, MENE, TEKEL, UPHARSIN.
26. Huai thil hilhchetna hiai ahi: MENE: Pathianin na gam a simta, a bei sakta hi;
27. TEKEL; buknaa bukin na oma, na chingzou kei hi;
28. PERES; na gam hawmin a oma, Mid mite leh Perisia mite kiangah piak a hita hi, a chi a.
29. Huchiin Belsazarin thu a piaa, huan dangkaeng khainiang a ngawng kimvelin a oksak ua; amah tungah thu a phuang ua, gamsunga vaihawmmi a thumna hi dingin.
30. Huaijanin Kaldai kumpipa Belsazar thahin a oma.Huan Mid mi Dariain gam a tanga; kum sawmguk leh niha upa ahi.
31. Huan Mid mi Dariain gam a tanga; kum sawmguk leh niha upa ahi.
