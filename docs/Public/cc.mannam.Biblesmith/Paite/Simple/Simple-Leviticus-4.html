1. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
2. Israel suante houpih inla, a kiang uah, TOUPA'N thil hih louh ding a thu a piakte, a bangpeuh leng min na hihkhial kha in, a bangpeuh leng na hita leh,
3. Sathaunilh siampu in mipite tung a mohpaihna tungsak a a hihkhelh leh, a thilhih khelhsa khelh thoihna ding in TOUPA kiang ah bawng tuai gensiatbei, khelh thoihna ding in lan hen.
4. Bawng pen kihoupihna puan inn kongpi bul ah TOUPA ma ah hon kai ding a, bawng lu tungah a khut a koih ding a, TOUPA ma ah huai bawng a gou ding ahi.
5. Huan, sathau nilh siampu in bawng sisan bangzah hiam a la ding a, kihoupihna puan inn ah a hon tawi ding a:
6. Huan, siampu in sisan ah a khut a diah ding a, a khenkhat TOUPA ma ah, mun siangthou puanzak bul ah sagihvei a theh ding ahi.
7. Huan, siampu in TOUPA ma ah kihoupihna puan inn a gimlim lim tak maitam kite ah sisan pen bangzah hiam a tat ding: huan, bawng sisan tengteng kihoupihna puan in kongpi kiang a haltum thillat maitam bul ah a sungkhe ding ahi.
8. Huan, khelh thoihna bawng thau tengteng a la vek ding a; a sungkua thau leh a gilboh tengteng,
9. A kal nihte leh a phuk chin a om a tuam thau leh a sin tuam a lam, a kal nihte nangawn toh a suah ding a;
10. Lemna thillat kithoihna bawngpa a kipan lak bang in, huan siampu in haltum thillat maitam ah a hal ding ahi.
11. Huan bawng vunte, a sa tengtengte, a lu toh, a khete toh, a sungkuate a ekte,
12. A bawng pumpi toh dainawl lam mun siang ah, vut paihna ah a paipih ding a, sing tungah a hal ding: vut paihna ah a hal ding hi.
13. Huan, Israel mipi tengteng in a sukhelh khak ua, mipi mitmuh a sel a hih a, TOUPA'N hih louh ding a thu a piakte, a bang mahmah a hih ua,
14. Siamlouh a tan ua, a thil gilou hih uh theih khiak a hong hih leh, mipi in khelh thoihna ding in bawng tuai a lan ding ua, kihoupihna puan inn kong ah a hong kai ding uh ahi.
15. Huan, nite upaten TOUPA maah bawng lu tungah a khut uh a koih ding ua; huan bawng tuh Toupa maah goh ding ahi.
16. Huan, sathau nilh siampu in kihoupihna puan inn ah bawng sisan bangzah hiam a hon tawi ding a:
17. Huan, siampu in sisan ah a khutzung a diah ding a, puanzak bul ah TOUPA ma ah sagih vei a theh ding ahi.
18. Huan, sisan pen kihoupihna puan inn a TOUPA ma ah maitam ki ah tamlou a tat ding a, sisan tengteng kihoupihna puan inn kongpi kiang a haltum thillat maitam bul ah a sungkhe ding ahi.
19. Huan, a thau tengteng a la vek ding a, maitam ah a hal ding.
20. Huchi bang in bawng tuh a hih ding a; khelh thoihna bawng a hih bang geih in hiai leng a hih ding ahi: huan, siampu in kilepna a bawl ding a, ngaihdam ahi ding uh.
21. Huan, bawng tuh dainawl lam ah a paipih ding a, bawng masa a hal bang in a hal ding ahi: huai bel mipi a dia khelh thoihna ahi.
22. Vaihawmtu in thil a hihkhelh a, TOUPA a Pathian in hih louh ding a thu a piak tengteng a bangmah a hih khak a, siamlouh a tan a,
23. A thil gilou hih theihsak a hih leh a thillat ding in kel, a pa, gensiatbei a hon kai ding a:
24. Kel lu tungah a khut a koih ding a, TOUPA ma ah haltum thillat a goh zel na uah a gou ding ahi: huai bel khelh thoihna ahi.
25. Huan, siampu in khelh thoihna sisan a khut zung in tamlou a pik ding a, haltum thillat maitam kite ah a tat ding a, haltum thillat maitam bul ah a sisan a sungkhe ding ahi.
26. Huan, a thau tengteng lemna thillat kithoihna thau a hal bang in maitam ah a hal ding ahi: huan, a khelhna tungthu ah siampu in kilepna a bawl saang ding a, ngaihdam ahi ding hi.
27. Huan, vantang lak a mi a kuamahmah in TOUPA'N hih louh ding a thu a piakte a bangmahmah hihkhial ka in, siamlouh na tang ta leh,
28. A thil gilou hih theihsak a hihleh a thillat ding in kel, a nu gensiatbei, a thilhih khelhsa khelh thoihna ding in a honkai ding ahi.
29. Khelh thoihna lu tungah a khut a koih ding a, haltum thillat na mun ah khelh thoihna a gou ding ahi.
30. Huan, siampu in sisan a khutzung in tamlou a pik ding a, haltum thillat maitam kite ah a tat ding a, a sisan tengteng maitam bul ah a sungkhe ding ahi.
31. Huan, a thau tengteng lemna thillat kithoihna thau lak bang un a la vek ding a; huan, siampu in TOUPA a ding in gimlim ding in maitam ah a hal ding ahi; huan, siampu in kilepna a bawlsak ding a, ngaihdam ahi ding.
32. Huan, khelh thoihna ding a thillat ding belamnou a hon tawi leh, a nu, gensiatbei a hon tawi ding ahi.
33. Khelh thoihna lu tungah a khut a koih ding a, haltum thillat a gohna uah khelhna thoihna ding in a gou ding ahi.
34. Huan, siampu in khelh thoihna sisan a khutzung in tamlou a pik ding a, haltum thillat maitam kite ah a tat ding a, sisan tengteng maitam bul ah a sungkhe ding ahi:Huan, a thau tengteng, belamnou thau, lemna thillat kithoihna a lak bang in a la vek ding a; huan, siampu in TOUPA a ding a mei a thillat bang in maitam ah a hal ding ahi: huan, siampu in a thil hihkhelhsa tung thu ah a kilepna a bawlsak ding a, ngaihdam ahi ding.
35. Huan, a thau tengteng, belamnou thau, lemna thillat kithoihna a lak bang in a la vek ding a; huan, siampu in TOUPA a ding a mei a thillat bang in maitam ah a hal ding ahi: huan, siampu in a thil hihkhelhsa tung thu ah a kilepna a bawlsak ding a, ngaihdam ahi ding.
