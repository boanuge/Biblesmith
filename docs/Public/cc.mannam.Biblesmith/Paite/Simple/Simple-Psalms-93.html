1. TOUPA tuh kumpipain a tu-a a thupinain a kithuama; TOUPA tuh a kithuama, hatnain a taiah a kigakta hi: khovel leng, a lin theihlouhna dingin, hihkipin a om hi.
2. Na laltutphah tuh nidanglaia na nahihkipsa ahi a: nang tuh khantawn a omsa na hi.
3. Luipiten, TOUPA aw, a suah ua, luipiten a aw uh a suah ua, luipiten a ging thupitak uh a suah uhi.
4. Tui tampi awte sangin, tuipi kihawt hattakte sangin leng, tunglama TOUPA tuh a hatjaw ahi.Na thutheihsakte tuh a muanhuai mahmaha: TOUPA aw, khantawnin siangthouna na in toh a kilawm ahi.
5. Na thutheihsakte tuh a muanhuai mahmaha: TOUPA aw, khantawnin siangthouna na in toh a kilawm ahi.
