1. Huan David a nadam sawtin a na upa mahmah taa; huchiin a tapa Solomon Israelte kumpipain a bawlta hi.
2. Huan Israelte makai tengteng siampute leh Levite toh a sam khawma.
3. Huan, Levite kum sawmthum akipan tunglam sim ahi ua; a tamlam uh piching sing thum leh sang giat ahi uh.
4. Huaite laka sing nih leh sang li TOUPA in lamheutute heutu ding ahi ua; huan, sang gukte bel heutute leh vaihawmmite dingte ahi uh;
5. Huan sang lite kongkhakngakmite ding ahi ua, huan sang li te bel, Phatna dinga thil tumging chi ka bawl, Davidin a chihte zanga TOUPA phatmi dingte ahi uh.
6. Huan Levi ta Gerson te, Kahath te, Merari te zahin Davidin pawl a bawla.
7. Gerson mite Ladan leh Simei ahi uh.
8. Ladan tate pipente Jehiel te, Setham te, Joel te, thum ahi uh.
9. Simei tate Solomoth te, Haziel te, Haran te, thum ahi uh. Hiaite Ladan suan inkote pipente ahi uh.
10. Huan Simei tate bel Juhath te Juhath te, Zin te, Jeus te, Beria te ahi uh. Hiai lite Simei tate ahi uh.
11. Huan Jahath a upa pen ahi a, Ziza a sang ahi; Jeus leh Beriaten bel ta a hau kei ua; huaijiakin inkuan khat banga sim ahi uh.
12. Kahath tate Amram te, Izhar te, Hebron te, Uziel te, li ahi uh.
13. Amram tate Aron leh Mosi ahi uh; Aron bel amah leh a suante khantawna dinga thil siangthou takte hihsiangthou-a, khantawna TOUPA maa gimlim hala, a na sema, a mina vualjawlpa dinga hihtuam ahi uh.
14. Pathian mi Mosi bel, a tate Levi nam laka sim ahi uh.
15. Mosi tate Gersom leh Eliezer ahi uh, Gersom tate laka upa pen Sebuel ahi.
16. Huan Eliezer tate laka upapen Rehabia ahi.
17. Huan Eliezerin tapa dang himhim a neikei; Rehabia in bel tapa tampi a nei.
18. Izhar tapate; Selmoth a pipen.
19. Hebron tate laka pipen Jeria ahi a, a sang Amaria, a sang nawn Jahaziel, a sang nawn Jekameam ahi.
20. Uziel tate laka upajaw Mika ahi a, a sang Isia ahi.
21. Merari tate; Merari tate bel Mahli leh Musi ahi uh, Mahli tate, Eleazar leh Kis ahi uh.
22. Huan Eleazar a si a, tapate neilouin, tanute kia a nei a; huan Kis tapa a unaute un jiin a nei uh.
23. Musi tate Mahli te, Eder te, Jeremoth te, thum ahi uh.
24. Huaite Levi suan, a inkuan uh bang jela min gelha a sim uh inkuan pipen, TOUPA in lama kuan, kum sawmnih akipan tunglama sim tengte ahi uh.
25. Davidin, TOUPA, Israelte Pathianin a mite galmuang takin a omsaktaa, Jerusalem ah a om nilouha,
26. Leviten leng biakbuk leh a nasepna ding tuiumbelsuan tengteng a zon uh a kiphamohta kei hi, a chi ahi.
27. David thupiak nanung jiakin Levite kum sawmnih akipan tung lam tanin sim ahi ngal ua.
28. A nasep uh Pathian ina nasepa Aron tapate panpiha intual sung leh dantan sunga thil siangthou tengteng hihsiangthou-a, TOUPA ina nasep lah ahi ngala;
29. Tanghou koih ding te, tangbuang thillat dinga tangbuang nel mahmah, silngou hel louh tanghoupek hiam, bela uk hiam, kan hiam, a teh leh a letdan bang tengteng ding bawl khawng te toh;
30. Huan jingsang tenga TOUPA kianga kipahthu gen leh amah phatte toh, nitaka leng huchi mah;
31. Huan khawlni te-a, kha thak te-a ankuanglui neih te-a, a hihding seh bang jela TOUPA kianga halmang thillat tengteng TOUPA maa lat gige te toh;Huan kihoupihna puanin kep te, mun siangthou kep te, TOUPA ina nasema a unau Aron tapaten hih ding a seh peuhmah uh hih te toh.
32. Huan kihoupihna puanin kep te, mun siangthou kep te, TOUPA ina nasema a unau Aron tapaten hih ding a seh peuhmah uh hih te toh.
