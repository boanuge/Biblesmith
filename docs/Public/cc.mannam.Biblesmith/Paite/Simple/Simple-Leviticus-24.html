1. TOUPAN Mosi a houpiha,
2. Israel suante, oliv thau chim tak, det dinga suk, khawnvak kuan sak gigena dingin na kianga honla dingin thupia in.
3. Kihoupihna puanina thutheihpihna puanjak po lamah nitak lam akipan jinglam tan adingin TOUPA ma ah Aronin koih hoih hen: hiai bel na suante jel uh tana khantawn dan ding ahi.
4. TOUPA maah khawnvak koihna siangthoutak tungah khawnvakte koih hoih jel heh.
5. Huan, tangbuang nel chi la inla, huai tanghou sawm leh nihin na bawl ding ahi: tanghou khat ah epha mun sawma khena mun nih zat ding ahi.
6. TOUPA maah dohkan siangthou tungah vual nihin, vual khata guk tuaktuakin na chiang ding ahi.
7. Tanghou tunga, a vual nih ah begaw chim tak na koih dia, theihgigena, TOUPA adia meia thillat bang a honghihtheihna dingin.
8. Khawlni tengin TOUPA maah a koih hoih jel ding ahi: khantawn thukhun, Israel suante a ding ahi.
9. Huan, Aron leh a tapate atuam ahi dinga, mun siangthou ah a ne ding uhi: khantawn dan dia TOUPA adinga meia thillatte laka, amah adia thil siangthou tak ahi, chiin.
10. Israel numei tapa, a pa Aigupta mi, mi khat Israel suante laka a vahoha: huai Israel numei tapa leh Israel mi, mi khat giahmun ah a kisual ua:
11. Israel numei tapain hamsiatin Pathian min a gensiaa; huchiin Mosi kiangah a honpi uh. A nu min bel Selomit ahi a, Dan chi Dibri tanu ahi.
12. TOUPA kam akipana thu pawt ngakna dingin suangkulh ah a khum uhi.
13. Huan, TOUPAN Mosi a houpiha,
14. Hamsiat loupa dainawl ah pi unla, a theihpihte tengtengin a lu ah a khut uh koih uhenla, mi tengtengin suangin deng uhen.
15. Huan, Israel suante houpih inla, a kianga uah, Kuapeuh a Pathian hamsiat loh peuhmahin a khelh moh a po ding.
16. Huan, TOUPA min gense peuhmah hihlup ngeingei ding ahi: mi tengtengin suanga a den ngeingei ding uh: TOUPA min a gensiat u leh gam dang mi leng, hiai gama mi him leng hihlup ding ahi.
17. Si khopa mi tunga khut kha peuhmah mi jaw hihlup ngeingei ding ahi;
18. Si khopa ganta tunga khut kha peuhmahin a dit ding ahi: hin sikin hin.
19. Huan, min a insakinkhang a melsiatsak leh a hih bang geih a tunga hih sam ding ahi;
20. Guh kitan sikin guh kitan, mit sikin mit, ha sikin ha; mi a hihmelsiat bangin a tungahleng hih sam ding ahi.
21. Ganta hihlum peuhmain a dit ding ahi; mi hihlum peuhmah hihlup sam ding ahi.
22. Gam dang mite ading leh hiai gama mi himte adingin dan kibang khat na neih ding uh ahi: Kei TOUPA na Pathian ka hi hi, na chi ding ahi, chiin.Huchiin Mosiin Israel suante a houpiha, hamse loupa pen dainawl ah a pi khia ua, suangin a deng uh, Israel suanten TOUPAN Mosi thu a piak bangin a hihta uh.
23. Huchiin Mosiin Israel suante a houpiha, hamse loupa pen dainawl ah a pi khia ua, suangin a deng uh, Israel suanten TOUPAN Mosi thu a piak bangin a hihta uh.
