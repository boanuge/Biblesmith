1. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
2. Israel suante thu pia inla, a kiang uah, Ka ann kei a dia gimlim mei a thillat, ka kiang ah thillat pen a hun geih zel chiah na lan ding uhi, chi in.
3. Huan, a kiang uah, Hiai mei a thillat TOUPA kiang a na lat ding uh ahi; belamnou a pa, kum khat a upa, gensiatbei, ni teng in nih, halmang thillat lan gige ding in.
4. Belamnou khat zingsang in na lan ding ua, khat nitaklam in na lan ding uh:
5. Tangbuang thillat ding in tangbuang nelte epha mun sawm a khen mun khat sathau suk hin mun li a khen mun khat a hel toh.
6. TOUPA ding a mai a thillat, gimlim ding a sinai tang a sehsa halmang a thillat lat gige ding chih ahi.
7. Huan, belamnou khat latna a dawn ding thillat, hin mun li a khen mun khat ahi ding ahi: TOUPA ding a uain, dawn ding thillat mun siangthou ah na bungkhe ding ahi.
8. Huan, belamnou khat nitaklam in na lan ding hi: zingsang lam in tangbuang thillat bang leh huai a dawn ding thillat bangbang TOUPA ding a gimlim di'n mei a thillat na lat ding ahi.
9. Huan, khawlni in belamnou, a pa kum khat a upa gensiatbei nih leh tangbuang thillat ding in tangbuang nelte epha mun sawm a khen a mun nih sathau a helkhawm leh huai a dawn ding thillat toh na lat ding ahi;
10. Hiai khawlni teng a halmang a thillat lat gige leh huai a dawn ding thillat sim louh a halmanga thillat lat ding ahi.
11. Huan, na kha thak teng un TOUPA ding halmang thil na lat ding uh ahi; bawngtal tung nih leh belamtal khat leh belamnou a pa kum khat a upa gensiatbei sagih;
12. Huan, bawngtal chih ah tangbuang thillat ding tangbuang nelte sathau a helkhawm epha mun sawm a khen a mun thum; huan, belam khat ah tangbuang thillat ah tangbuang nelte, sathau a helkhawm mun sawm a khen a mun nih;
13. Huan, belamnou chih ah tangbuang thillat ah tangbuang nelte, sathau a helkhawm mun sawm a khen a mun khat: hiai halmang thillat gimlim TOUPA a dia mei a thillat ahi.
14. Huan, a dawn ding thillat uh bawngtal khat ah uain hin kimkhat ahi ding a belamtal khat ah hin mun thum a khen a mun khat, belamnou khat ah hin mun li a khen a mun khat: hiai kumtawn a kha teng halmang thillat ahi.
15. Huan, TOUPA kiang a khelh thoihna dia keltal khat na lat ding uh ahi; halmang a thillat lat gige leh huai a dawn ding thillat sim lou in.
16. Huan, kha khat na, ni sawm leh ni li ni TOUPA paikan ahi.
17. Huan, huai kha ni sawm leh ni nga ni annkuanglui ni ahi ding; ni sagih tanghou silngou soh louh nek ding ahi.
18. Ni khat ni in khawmpi siangthou a om ding a: bang nna mahmah na sep ding uh ahi kei;
19. Mei a thillat bel TOUPA kiang a na lat ding uh ahi; bawngtal tung nih leh belamtal khat leh belamnou a pa kum khat a upa sagih: na ngaihtuah ua gensiatbei ahi ding ahi:
20. Tangbuang nelte, sathau helkhawm, a tangbuang thillat utoh: bawngtal khat ah mun sawm a khen a mun thum belamtal khat ah mun sawm a khen a mun nih:
21. Belamnou sagih bel belamnou khat ah mun sawm a khen a mun khat ahi ding ahi;
22. Huan nou a ding a kilemna bawlna ding in khelh thoihna ding in keltal khat.
23. Hiaite zingsang a halmang thillat, halmang thillat lat gige leh huai a dawn ding thillat sim lou in
24. Hichibangin Toupa' dinga gimlim, mei a thillat nek ding ni tengin, ni sagih na lat ding uh ahi: halmang thilat lat gige leh huai dawn ding thillat sim lou in.
25. Huan, a ni sagih ni in khawmpi siangthou na nei ding ua, bang nna mahmah na sem ding uh hilou ahi.
26. Huan, na nipikal annkuang luina na buh thak tung annkuang luina TOUPA ding a thil thak tangbuang thillat na lat ni un leng khawmpi siangthou na nei ding ua; bang nna mahmah na sem ding uh ahi kei;
27. TOUPA ding a gimlim halmang thillat bel na lat ding uh ahi; bawngtal tung nih, belamtal khat, belamnou a pa kum khat a upa sagih;
28. A tangbuang thillat uh, tangbuang nelte sathau a helkhawm, bawngtal khat ah mun sawm a khen a mun thum.
29. Belamtal khat ah mun sawm a khen a mun nih, belamnou sagih, belamnou khat chiat ah mun sawm a khen a mun khat;
30. Nou a dia kilemna bawlna ding keltal khat toh.Halmang thillat gige leh huai a tangbuang thillat, huaite na lat ding uh ahi (na ngaihtuah ua gensiatbei ahi ding ahi), dawn ding thillat toh.
31. Halmang thillat gige leh huai a tangbuang thillat, huaite na lat ding uh ahi (na ngaihtuah ua gensiatbei ahi ding ahi), dawn ding thillat toh.
