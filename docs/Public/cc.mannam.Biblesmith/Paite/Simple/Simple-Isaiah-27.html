1. Huai ni chiangin TOUPAN, a namsau hoihtaka hiam, lianpi, muanhuaitakin, leviathan gulpi hattak leh leviathan gulpi kivial ngeingoi a gawt dinga, tuipi-a dragon om leng a that ding.
2. Huai ni chiangin: Uain grep huan khat, huai pahtawiin lasa un.
3. Kei TOUPA lah a vengpa ka hi a; mitphiatkal tengin tui ka buak dinga, kuamahin a khoih louh ding un sun leh janin ka veng ding.
4. Keiah hehna a om kei hi: loulingnei leh khaulingneiten hondouin honsual him le u aw! amau sual din ka kuan dinga, mun khatah ka hal lunglung ding.
5. A hih kei lelah, hon kileppihna dingin ka hatna len hen: ahi, honkilep pih hen.
6. Hun hontung dingah Jakobten zung a kha ding ua; Israelten zung a kha ding ua; Israelte a mum un a pak ding uh: huchiin leitung gahin adimsak ding uhi.
7. Amah vote a vuak bang letin amah a vo sam ahia? a hih kei leh, amah thahte thahna bang letin amah thahin a om hia?
8. Amah na pai mangsak laiin a hun lelin amah na sela: suahlam huih nun niin a hu thupitakin amah a mutlengta hi.
9. Huaijiakin hiaiin Jakobte thulimlouhna silsiang ahi dinga, hiai a khelhna uh pai mangpihna gah ngen ahi: Asherim hiam, gimlim maitamte hiam ding nawn lou ding khopin maitam suang tengteng suang pak den khap zeizoi bangin a bawl uhi.
10. Khua kulhpi nei lah a lungjuanga, omna nutsiat leh paisan, gamdai bang ahi: huailaiah bawngnou a ta dinga, huailai mahah a kual dinga, a selte a ne ding.
11. A hiangte lah a vuai chiang un khiahin a om dinga; numeite a hongpai ding ua a hal ding uh: amau lah theihtheihna himhim neilou mi ahi ngal ua: huaijiakin amau bawlpan amaute a hehpih kei dinga, amaute sutuahpan amaute deihsakna himhim a neikei ding hi.
12. Huan, huai niin hichi ahi dinga, TOUPAN, Lui a kuang dima luang zoizoi akipanin, Aigupta luita phain, a gah a sat khe dinga, huchiin, Israel suante aw, khatkhata simin na om ding uhi.Huan, huai niin hichi ahi dinga, pengkul lianpi a mut ding ua: huchiin Assuria gama mangthang dekdekte leh, Aigupta gama delhkhiaka omte a hongpai ding ua: Jerusalem khuaa tang siangthouah TOUPA a be ding uhi.
13. Huan, huai niin hichi ahi dinga, pengkul lianpi a mut ding ua: huchiin Assuria gama mangthang dekdekte leh, Aigupta gama delhkhiaka omte a hongpai ding ua: Jerusalem khuaa tang siangthouah TOUPA a be ding uhi.
