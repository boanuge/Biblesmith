1. Mi gilou-salou tatlekna in ka lungtang sungah thu a gena, A mitmuh ah Pathian kihtakna himhim a omkei, chi in.
2. A thulimlouhna a mukhe kei ding ua, a pai tuan kei ding uh, chiin. Amah mitmuhin amah mah a kiphat tawm ngala.
3. a A kama thupawt te thulimlouhna leh khemna thu gen ahi naka: pil leh thilhoih hih a tawpta ahi.
4. A lupna ah thil thulimlou a gel gige a; lampi hoihlou ah a pai teitei a: gilou tuh a kih kei hi.
5. Toupa aw, na chitna tung van khawng ah a om a; na ginomna in tung van dumte a su hi.
6. Na diktatana Pathian tangte bang ahi; na vaihawmte tuh tui thuk talkhauh bang ahi; Toupa aw, mihing leh gamsa na humbit jela.
7. Pathian aw, na chitna tuh manpha hi na e! Mihing tate na kha nuaiah a tu jel uhi.
8. Amau tuh na in sung thil hoih in a tai mahmah ding ua; na kipahna luia na dawn sak ding hi.
9. Hinna tuikhuk na kiangah a om ngala: na vakah vak ka mu ding uhi.
10. Aw, nangmah theite tungah na chitna kitheisak zel inla; lungtang diktak pute tungah leng na diktatna langsak jelin.
11. Kisathei khe tuh honsual dingin kei lam ah kalsuan dah henla, mi gilou salou khut in leng hon delh mang kei hen.Huailaiah thulimlouhna hihte a puk ua; sawnpuk ahi ua, a thou thei nawn kei ding uhi.
12. Huailaiah thulimlouhna hihte a puk ua; sawnpuk ahi ua, a thou thei nawn kei ding uhi.
