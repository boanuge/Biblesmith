1. Huan huai thil nungin hichi ahia, Jareel mi Nabothin grep huan a nei a, huai bel Jezreel ah Samari kumpipa Ahab in kiang chin a om ahi uh.
2. Huan Ahabin Naboth bel ka in kiang chin ahihziakin an-teh louhing huan ding in na grephuan hon pia in, a sik ding in grephuan hoihzaw ka hon pe ding; ahih kei leh na deihzawk ngial le-houh a dangka in aman hun ka hon pe ding chi in a houpih a.
3. Huan Naboth in Ahab kiangah, ka pute gou nang ka hon piak ding a-lawm in aki lawm kei achi a.
4. Huan Jezreel mi Nabothin huchibang thu a kianga a gen jiakin Ahab bel ngui leh lung hilou takin a in ah a lut a, ka pute gou ka hon pe theikei ding, achi bikbek ngal a. Huchi in a lupna ah a lum a, khat lam a nga a, an himhim a ne nuam ta kei hi.
5. Huchi in a ji Jezebel a va hoh a, a kiangah, "bang chi dan ahia na lungsim huchi taka a lungkhama, an himhim na neklouh? " a chi a.
6. Huan aman a kiangah, "na grephuan hon leisak in, ahihkeileh na deih zawk leh a sik ding in grephuan dang ka honpe ding" kachi a; huan aman, "ka grephuan zaw ka hon pekei ding", chi in ahon dawng maimah a ahi, achi a.
7. Huan, a ji Jezebel in a kiangah, "Israel gam kumpipa na hi kei ahia? thou in la, an ne in la, lungsim nuamsa takin om ve, Jezreel mi Naboth grephuan ken ka hon pe ding" achi a.
8. Huchi in Ahab min in lai a gelh a, a sawn nain a sawn a, huan huai laia a omna khua a Naboth insak-inkhang upate leh mi hoihte kiangah a thon a.
9. Huan, lai ah, an-ngawl thu na naphuang unla, mipi lak ah Naboth pen makai sak unla;
10. Mi nih Belial tate a maah tusak unla, Pathian leh kumpipa na gensia hi, chi a mi amah hek ding na bawl un. Huan, pikhia unla, ana deng lum un, achi a.
11. Huan a om na khua a mi, a om na khua a upate leh mi hoihte'n Jezebelin akiang ua a khak lai a agelh bang in ana hih ua.
12. An-ngawl thu a phuang ua, mipi lakah Naboth a makai sak uhi.
13. Huan, mi nih Belial tate a ma ah a va tu ua; huan, Belial mite'n mipite ma ah, "Naboth in Pathian leh kumpipa a gensia" chi in amah a hek uhi. Huchi in khua a kipan a pi khia ua, suangin a deng ua, a si ta hi.
14. Huchi in Jezebel kiangah, Naboth suangin a deng ua, a si ta, chi in mi a sawl uhi.
15. Huan, Jezebelin Naboth suangin a deng ua, a si ta chih a jakin hichi ahi a, Jezebelin Ahab kiangah, "thou in la, Jezreel mi Naboth grephuan, a hon leisak phallouh nei ta oleh: Naboth a dam non takei, a si ta" achi a.
16. Huan, Ahabin Naboth a si ta chih a jakin hichi ahia a, Jezreel mi Naboth grephuan nei dinga kuan suk dingin Ahab a thou ta.
17. Huan Toupa thu Tisbe mi Elija kiangah a hong tunga:
18. Thou inla, Samari khua a om Israel kumpipa houpih ding in hoh sukin: ngai in, Naboth grephuan ah a om a, neih tum in huai ah a hoh suk hi.
19. A kiangah, TOUPA hichi in achi, "na that a, na neihsak behlap lai maw? " chi in, chi inla, a kiangah, TOUPA'N hichin achi, "ui in Naboth sisan a liah na mun mahmah uah nang sisan leng a liak sin uhi" chi in na chi ding, chi in.
20. Huan Ahabin Elija kiangah, "Na hong mu ta maw, ka melma pa? " achi a. Huan, aman a dawnga, "hon mu ta kei ve, Toupa mitmuh a thil hoihlou hih dinga kizuak hive chin".
21. "Ngai in na tungah thil hoihlou ka hon tungsak dinga, ka hon hih mang ding, Ahab akipanin ta pasal taphot Israel gama mi etzui leh etzui louh leng ka hihmang dinga,
22. Nang non hehsak jiakin leh Israelte thil na hih khelh sak jiakin Nebat tapa Jeroboam inkote leh Ahija tapa Baaz inkote bang mai in na inkote ka bawl ding" achi a.
23. Huan Jezebel tungtang thu leng Toupa'n a gen a, Jezebel kho-kulh chin-ah ui in Jezebel a ne ding uh;
24. Ahab inkote laka mi khopi a si peuhmah te ui in a ne ding uh; gama misi peuhmahte tunga leng vasate'n a ne ding uh, chi in.
25. (A ji Jezebel hasotna Toupa mitmuha thil hoihlou hih dinga kijuak, Ahab bang mahmah a om kei.
26. Toupa'n Israel suante ma a adelhkhiak Amorte thilhih chi teng bang, milimte bia in thil kih huai takin ahih sek).
27. Huan, Ahab in huai thu a jak in hichi ahi a, a puan abot kek a, sai-ip puan a silha, an a ngawl a, sai-ip puan ah a lum a, dawm takin a gamtangta.
28. Huan, Toupa thu Tisbe mi Eliza kiangah a hong tunga, Ahab ka ma a aki niamkhiak dan na mu hia?Ka ma a aki niamkhiak jiakin a damsung in thil hoihlou ka tungsak kei dinga, a tapa damsung in bel a inkote tungah thil hoih lou ka tungsak ding, chi in.
29. Ka ma a aki niamkhiak jiakin a damsung in thil hoihlou ka tungsak kei dinga, a tapa damsung in bel a inkote tungah thil hoih lou ka tungsak ding, chi in.
