1. Himahleh ni nanung chiangin hichi ahi dinga, TOUPA na omna tang uh tang lian tungtea hihkipin a om dinga, tang neute tungah tawisangin a om lai ding; huaiah tawisangin a om lai ding; huaiah chichihte a pai zungzung ding uh.
2. Huan, nam tampite a pai ding ua, Hong unla, TOUPA tangah, Jakob Pathian in ah. i hoh tou ding uh; huchiin aman a lampi tanchinte honsinsak dinga, a lampiahte i pai ding ua, a chi ding uh; Zion akipan in dan thu a hongpawt sin ngala. TOUPa thu bel Jerusalem a kipanin hong pawt sin ahi.
3. Huan, aman chi tampi kalah vai a hawm dinga, gamla pia nam hat tak leng a sal ding; huan, a namsaute uh leilehna muk zum dingin a sek sing ua, a teipite uh thei kung attanna temkawi dingin a sek dung uh; nam dangin nam dang tungah namsaw a lik ta kei dinga, kidou dan leng a sinsakta kei ding uh.
4. Mi chihte a grep gui nuai uah leh a theipi kung nuai uah a tu zo ta ding ua; kuamahin a lausak kei ding uh: sepaihte TOUPA kamin a ganta ngal a.
5. Chi chih amau pathian min chiatin a om ding ua, ei leng TOUPA i Pathian minin khantawnin i om ding hi.
6. TOUPAN hichiin a chi: Huai ni chiangin khebai ka kaikhawm dinga, delhmanga om leh ka sukgima om ka kaikhawm ding.
7. Khebai bel a si valin ka bawl dinga, gamla pia delh mang bel nam hat takin na bawl ding: tuban siah Zion tangah khantawnin TOUPAN a tunguah vai a hawm ding.
8. Aw nang, gan hon kalh, Zion tanu omna tang, na kiangah a hongtung ding; ahi, lalna masa pen a hongtung ding, Jeusalem tanu lalna tak.
9. Bang dia hichi nak kah na hia? Na sungah kumpipa a om ka hia, nang hon gelsakpa a mangthangta ahia, numei nauvei banga natnain nang a honmat?
10. Aw Zion tanu, numei nauvei suak ding bangin natna thuak inla veiin: tuin khopi akipanin na pawt khe dinga, gamah na teng dinga, Babulon tanphain leng a pai sin ngala, huaiah hotkhiakin na om dinga, huaiah na melmate khut akipan TOUPAN nang a hon honkhe ding hi.
11. Tuin tampi nang hondouin a ki khom ua, Hihbuai in om henla, i mit in zion tung a lunggulh pen mu hen, a chi ua.
12. himahleh toupa ngaihtuahnate a theikei ua, a phatsakna leng a thei siam sam kei uh: phual a buhphal koih khom bangin amau lah a kaikhawmta ngala.Aw Zion tanu, thou inla chilin: na ki sik bang in ka bawl dinga, na khepek bomte dal bangin ka bawl ding ahi ngala; huan, chi tampite na sat ding: a thil lep uh TOUPA a dia na tuam koih ding ahi, a sum uh lei kumpi TOUPA adin ah tuam koih ding.
13. Aw Zion tanu, thou inla chilin: na ki sik bang in ka bawl dinga, na khepek bomte dal bangin ka bawl ding ahi ngala; huan, chi tampite na sat ding: a thil lep uh TOUPA a dia na tuam koih ding ahi, a sum uh lei kumpi TOUPA adin ah tuam koih ding.
