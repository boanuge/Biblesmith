1. Huan, Pathian biakina kipan a pai khiak laiin, a nungjuite laka mi khatin a kiangah, Sinsakpa, en dih, a suang leh a in khawng thupi mahmah hi a, a chi a.
2. Huan, Jesun, a kiangah, Hiai in thupi tak na mu maw? hihchip louha, suang kichiang himhim hiaiah a om kei ding, a chi a.
3. Huan, Oliv tanga Pathian biakin jawna a tut laiin, Peter, Jakob, Johan leh Andru ten a kiangah,
4. Huaite chikchiangin a hongom dia? huai tengteng a tunding chiangin chiamtehna bang a om dia: honhilh in, chiin a sim dot ua.
5. Huan, Jesun a kiang uah, Kuamahin honkhem louhna ding un, pilvang un.
6. Mi tampi, Kris tuh ka hi, chiin, keimah min louin a hong ding uh, mi tampi a khem ding uh.
7. Huan, kidou thu leh kidou thuthang khawng na jak hun chiang un mangbang kei un; huaite hongtung ding him ahi uh; himahleh, tawpna jaw ahi nai ta-dih kei ding.
8. Nam tuam leh nam tuam a kidou ding ua, gam tuam leh gam tuam a kidou ding ua; mun tuamtuam ah jin a ling dia; kial a ke dia. Huai tuh nauvei kipatna ahi.
9. Himahleh, pilvang un; vaihawmmite a honmansak ding ua; kikhopnain khawngah a honvo ding ua; keimah jiakin gam ukte leh kumpipate ma ah theihpihna pe ding na hongding ding uh.
10. Huan, Tanchin Hoih tuh nam tengteng kianga hilh masak ding ahi.
11. Huan, a honpi ua, a honmatsak hun chiang un, Bang ka gen de aw? chiin, ngaihtuah khol kei un; a hun taka theihsaka naom peuh uh, huai ngeingei gen jaw un; a genpen jaw noumau na hi kei ding uhi, Kha Siangthou ahi zo ding.
12. Huan, hihlup dingin unaupa leh unaupa a kimansak ding ua, pain leng tapa a mansak ding; taten leng a nu leh pate uh a dou ding uh, a hihlum sak ding uh.
13. Keimah min jiakin mi tengteng huat na hi ding uh; himahleh, a tawp phaa thuakpa, huai mi mah tuh hotdamin a om ding.
14. Himahleh, hihmangbangpa kihhuai a din louhna dinga ding na muh chiang un, (a sim peuhin a omdan thei heh) huai hun chiangin Judia gama omte tangsang ah taimang uheh;
15. huan, in tunga om kum kei heh; a ina banghiam om tuh dingin zong lut sam kei heh;
16. huan, lou-a om a puan tuh dingin kik nawn kei heh.
17. Huai niin gaite leh naunou pomte tung a gik ding.
18. Huan, huaite phalbia a hongtun louhna dingin thum un.
19. Huai nite banga gimthuakna thupi himhim Pathian bawl leilungpian tunga kipan tutanin a om ngei nai keia, chikchiangmahin a hongom ngei nawn kei ding hi.
20. Huan, Toupan huai nite hihtom keileh mi hing himhim hingin a pawt kei ding uh; himahleh, mi tel, a sehte jiakin nite tuh a hih tom a.
21. Huan, huai hun chiangin, kuapeuhin na kiang ua, En dih uh: Kris tuh hiailai ah a om, a hihkeileh, En dih uh: hua laiah, hiam a chih uh leh, um kei un;
22. Kris takloute leh jawlnei takloute a hongpawt ding uh ahi ngala, huaiten, chiamtehna leh thillamdang a hih ding uh, hithei lehouh, telte nangawn a khem theihna ding un.
23. Nou jaw pilvang un, ngai dih uh, bangkim ka honhilh khol khin ahi.
24. Himahleh, huai nitein, huai gimthuakna khitin, ni hihmialin a om dinga, kha a vak nawnta kei dia.
25. Van akipanin aksite a hongke ding ua, vana thilhihtheihna omte hihlinin a om ding uh.
26. Huai hun chiangin Mihing Tapa Thilhihtheihna leh thupina lianpi toh mei laka hongpai a mu ding uh.
27. Huai hun chiangin angelte a sawl khe dia, a telte lei ningli a pat-lei tawp akipana van tawp pha in a khawm khawm ding uh.
28. Huchiin, theipi kungah a gentehna thu zil un: ahiang a hongnou a, a nah a hong selin nipi a nai chih na thei uh.
29. Huchi bangmahin, nou leng, huaite a hongtung na muh hun chiang un, amah a hongnaita a, kongkhak bul mahmah ah a om chih, thei un.
30. Chihtaktakin ka honhilh ahi, huai tengteng a tunkim masiah tulai khangthakte a mang kei ding uh.
31. Lei leh van a mang dia, ka thu a hihleh a mang kei ding.
32. Himahleh, huai ni leh a dak thu Pa kia loungal kuamahin, vana angelten leng, Tapain leng a theikei uh.
33. Pilvang unla, kiging gigea omin thum un; a hun lah na thei ngal kei ua.
34. Mi, a in pai sana, a sikhate thu nei saka, a nasep ding uh pe chiata, kongkhakveng leng kigin ding thu pe phota, gam danga vatam bang ahi.
35. Huaijiakin kiging gigein om un, inneipa hong ding hun, nitak lam hia, jan ah hia, akkhuanin hia, jingin hia hi ding chih na thei ngal kei ua;
36. Huchilou injaw, hongpai guih khaleh ihmuin a honmu khading.Huan, na kiang ua ka gen tuh mi tengteng kianga ka gen ahi, Kiging gigein om un, a chita a.
37. Huan, na kiang ua ka gen tuh mi tengteng kianga ka gen ahi, Kiging gigein om un, a chita a.
