1. Huailouin Elihuin a dawnga, hichiin a chi a:
2. Hiai na dikna hiin na ngaihtuah hia, ahihkeileh, keia mawng ahi, Pathian mai ah, na chi hia,
3. Kei adingin bang phattuamna a om dia? huan. Ka khelh nak sal aleh, bang phattuamna ka neia? na chih?
4. Nang, leh na kianga na lawmte ka hondawng ding.
5. Vante lam en inla, mu in; huan vante, nang sanga sangjaw, en in.
6. Na hihkhelh leh, amah dema bang hih na hia? Huan na tatleknate punsak hileh, amah tunga bang hih na hia?
7. Daktat hi lechin, amah bang na pia a? Ahihkeileh na khut akipan bang a mu a?
8. Na gitlouhnain mi bang bangin a hihna thei a; huan na diktatnain mihing tapa a hih hampha thei hi.
9. Nuaisiahna tam mahmah jiakin a kikou khia ua; mi hat ban jiakin panpih deihin a kikou uh.
10. Himahleh kuamahin, Jan chia late pia, Pathian kei Bawlpa koia om ahia;
11. Leia gamsate sanga honsinsak tamjaw leh, vana vasate sanga hon pilsakjaw? A chi kei uh.
12. Huailaiah a kikou ua, himahleh, mi gilou kisaktheihna jiakin, kuamahin dawnna a pe kei uh.
13. Chihtaktakin Pathianin bangmahlou aza kei dia, Thil bangkim hihtheiin leng a limsak sak kei ding hi.
14. Nang ngial houh: Amah ka mu kei, a thu maah a om, amah ka ngak hi, na chih vial.
15. Himahleh tuin, heha a veh louha, kiotsakna leng a limsaklouin jiakin;Huaijiakin Jobin bangmahlouin a kam a ka ahi; theihna beiin thu a tamsak ahi.
16. Huaijiakin Jobin bangmahlouin a kam a ka ahi; theihna beiin thu a tamsak ahi.
