1. Huan, Antiok khuaa saptuam omte lakah jawlnei kuate hiam leh, thuhilhmi kuate hiam a om ua; huaite tuh Barnaba bang, Sumeon Niger a chih bang uh, Kurini khuaa mi Lusia bang, kumpipa Herod unau ding Manaen bang, Saula bang ahi uh.
2. Huan, Toupa na a sem ua, an a ngawl lai un, Kha Siangthouin, Barnaba leh Saula amaute ka sehna hih dingin honhih khensak un, a chi a.
3. Huchiin, an a ngawl ua, a thum khit un a tunguah a khut uh a koih ua, a paisakta uh.
4. Huchiin, Kha Siangthou sawlkhiak in a pai ua, Seliusia khua ah a hoh suk ua, huai akipanin Kupra tuikulh ah longin a pai uh.
5. Huan, Salami khuaa a om lai un Judate kikhopna in ah Pathian thu a gen ua; Johan leng a nek uh bawl dingin a pi ua.
6. Huan, tuikulh tuh Papho khua phain a nawk suak ua, bumsiam, jawlnei taklou, Juda mi khat a mu ua, a min tuh Bar-Jesu ahi.
7. Huai mi tuh gamukpa Serjia Paulus -mi piltak kianga om ahi. Huai gamukpa Barnaba leh Saula a sama, Pathian thu jak a tum a.
8. Himahleha, bumsiam Elima, (Bar-Jesu min omdan ahi a); huaiin gamukpa ginlouhsak tumin, amau tuh a na khak hi.
9. Huchiin, Saula, Paula a chih mah uh; Kha Siangthouin a dima, amah tuh a ensala.
10. Nang mi khem leh huathuai chitenga dimpa, diabol tapa aw, nang diktatna teng teng melmapa aw, Toupa lam diktakte hihsiat na tawp kei ding maw?
11. Theiin, tuin Toupa khut na tungah a tu ahi, na mita hongto dinga, khovak na mu tadih kei ding, a chi a. Huchiin mei leh mialin amah a boh pah ngala, khuta kai ding zongin a vak a vakta hi.
12. Huan, gamukpan huai thilhih tuh a mu a, Toupa thuhilh tuh lamdang a sa mahmah hi, a gingtata hi.
13. Huan, Paula tuh a tonpihte toh Papho khua akipanin longin a pai ua, Pamphilia gama Perga khua a tung ua; huan, Johanin amau a paisana, Jerusalem khua ah a kik nawnta hi.
14. Himahleh, amau Perga khua akipan a pai jel ua, Pisidia gama Antiok khua a tung ua; huan, Khawlniin kikhopna in ah a lut ua, a tu ua.
15. Huan, Dan laibu leh jawlneite laibu a sim khit un, kikhopna in upaten a kiang uah mi a sawl ua, Unaute aw, mite hasotna thu banghiam na neih uh leh gen un, a chi ua.
16. Huan, Paula a dinga, khutin a japa, a gen a; Israel mite leh Pathian laudansiamte aw, ngai un.
17. Hiai Israel chite Pathianin I pipute a tela, Aigupta gama a tam lai un a hihthupi a, tungnung khutin huaia kipanin a pikheta.
18. Huan, kum sawmli khawng gamdai ah a thilhihte uh a thuak theitheia.
19. Huan, Kanan gama nam sagih a hihsiat khitin, a gam uh luah dingin a pe jela, kum za li leh kum sawmnga sung teng.
20. Huai nungin vaihawmmite a pe jela, jawlnei Samuel tannin.
21. Huan, huai nungin kumpipa a ngen ua; huchiin Pathianin, Saula, Kish tapa, Benjamin chia, amau a pia a, kum sawmli sung dingin.
22. Huan, amah a suan mang nungin a kumpipa ding ua om dingin David a bawlta a; Pathianin huai thu theihsakin, David, Jesai tapa, ka lungtang bang pu mi ka mukheta, aman ka deihlam peuh a hih ding, a chi a.
23. Huai suante lakah Pathianin, thuchiam bang jelin, Israelte kiangah Hondampa Jesu a hontuna;
24. A hongpai main Johanin kisikna baptisma thu Israel mi tengteng kiang ah a na hilh msask hi.
25. Huan, Johanin a lam a tot khit kuan lain, Nouten kua dingin non sep ua? Amah jaw ka hi kei. Himahleh, ngai un, ka nungah mi kuahiam khat hongpai dek ahi, kei jaw a khepeka khetuam phel tham leng ka hi kei, a chi a.
26. Unaute aw, Abraham suana tate aw, na lak ua Pathian laudansiamte tengteng aw, eimahte kiangah hiai hotdamna thu a honkhakkhia uhi.
27. Jerusalem khuaa omten a makaite utoh, amah tuh a theih louh jiak uleh, jawlneite thu, Khawlni peuhmahai a sim jel uh, a theih louh jiak un, amah tuh siamlouh tangsakin, huai thu ana hihtangtungta uh ahi.
28. A sihna ding khop a tungah bangmah a muh louh un leng, Pilat kiangah hihlup dingin a ngen ua.
29. Huan, a thu jawlneiten a gelh kholh tengteng uh a hihtangtun khit un, singa kipanin a lakhia ua, han ah a sialta uhi.
30. Himahleh, Pathianin misi lak akipan a kaithou nawnta a;
31. huan, Galili gam akipan Jerusalem khuaa amah toh hohtoute kiangah ni sawtpi tak a kilak ngitngeta; huai mite tun ah mi kianga a thu theihpihte ahi uh.
32. Kou leng pipute kianga chiamsa huai thuchiam a tanchin kipahhuai ka honhilh uhi;
33. huchia Pathianin Jesu a kaihthoh, I tate kiangah huai tuh a hihtangtung ahi; Sam nihna ah, Ka tapa na knahi a, tuniin kon suang, chih gelh bangin.
34. Muatna muna pai nawn lou dingin Pathianin amah misi akipan a kaihthoh dingdan thu sehsa bangin a gen, David kianga hehpihna ka chiamte nangmah ka honpe ding, chiin.
35. Huaijiakin Sam dangah, Na Mi Siangthou muatmang na phal kei ding, a chi behlap hi.
36. David jaw, Pathian vaihawm sak bangin a hinpihte na a sep khitin, a ihmu a, a pipute kiangah a sial ua, a muat mangta ahi;
37. himahleh Pathian kaihthohin jaw muatna a mu kei hi.
38. Huchiin, unaute aw, thei un; amah jiakin khelhna ngaihdamna thu na kiang ua gen ahi;
39. kuapeuh a gingta tuh bangkim ah amah siamtan sakin a om, huaiah tuh Mosi danin siamtansakin na om theikei uh.
40. Huaijiakin pilvang un, huchilou in jaw jawlneite laibu a a gen uh na tunguah a hongtung ding hi,
41. Musitte aw, en un, lamdang sa unla, mangthang un; na damsung un na khat ka sem hi, huai na tuh, min nou honhilh mahle uh, na gingta kei hial ding uhi, chih, a chi a.
42. Huan, a pawtun, Khawlni nawn chianga huai thute gen nawn dingin a ngen uh.
43. Huan, kikhawmte tuh a kikhen tak un, Judate leh phungkaite Pathian limsak laka mi tampiin Paula leh Barnaba a jui jel ua; amau a na houpih ua, Pathian hehpihna ah om gige dingin a hasuan uh.
44. Huan, Khawlni nawnin, a khojang phialin Pathian thu ngaikhe dingin a hongkikhawm ua.
45. Himahleh, Judaten mipite a muh tak un a haza mahmah ua; Paula thugen a kalh ua, a gensia uhi.
46. Huan, Paula leh Barnabain hangtakin a genkhia ua, Pathian thu jaw noumaute kiangah gen masak ding him ahi; himahleh, na nial ua, khantawna hing tak loua na kisehkhit nung un, thei un, Jentelte lam ka ngata uh.
47. Huchibangin Toupan thu a honpia hi, Jentelte hihvakpa din ka honsep a, kawlmong pha a Hondampa dia na om theihna dingin, chih gelh bangin, a chi a.
48. Huan, Jentelten huai thu a jak un, a kipak ua, Pathian thu a pahtawi ua, huan, khantawna hing dinga seh peuhmahin a gingta uhi.
49. Huan, Toupa thu tuh, huai gam khokim khovel ah a dalhta a.
50. Himahleh, Judaten numei zahtakhuai Pathian limsakte leh khua upahonte a hasuan ua, Paula leh Barnaba a sawisak ua, a gam ua kipan a delhkhia uh.
51. Huan, amau a tunguah a khepek ua leivui a singkhia ua, Ikoniam khua a tung uh.Huan, nungjuite kipahna leh Kha Siangthouin a dim chiat uh.
52. Huan, nungjuite kipahna leh Kha Siangthouin a dim chiat uh.
