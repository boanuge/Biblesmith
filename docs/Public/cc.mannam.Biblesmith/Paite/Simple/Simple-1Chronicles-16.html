1. Huchiin Pathian bawm a honla lut ua, puanin Davidin a bawlsak, laizang ah a koihta uhi: huan Pathian maah halmang thillatte leh lemna thillatte a lan uhi.
2. Huan Davidin halmang thillat leh lemna thillatte a lat khitin TOUPA minin mite a vualjawl hi.
3. Huan Israel mi chih kiangah, pasal leh numei mi chih kiangah tanghou beu khat, sa bak khat, leh grepmingaw beu khat a hawma.
4. Huchiin TOUPA bawm maia nasem ding leh TOUPA, Israel Pathian kipahpih ding leh kipahthugen ding leh phat dingin Levi mite laka kuate hiam a septa hi:
5. Asaph a pipen, amah zomin Zekaria, Semiramot, Jeiel, Mattithia, Eliab, Benai, Obed-edom, Jeiel, Pekging guisawmneite leh kaihgingte toh; huan Asaph dakkhutbette, ngaihtaka ging toh;
6. Huan Benai leh Jahaziel siampute Pathian thukhunna bawm maa, om nilouhlouhte toh.
7. Huchiin huai niin Davidin, Asaph leh a unaute khutin, TOUPA kianga kipahthute gen ding a hihkip masapenpen hi.
8. Aw TOUPA kiangah kipahthute gen unla, a min lou un; mite lakah a thilhihte theisak un.
9. Amah lasaksak unla, phatnalate saksak un; a nasep lamdangte tengteng houlim un.
10. A min siangthou suang unla: TOUPA zongte lungtang kipak hen.
11. TOUPA leh a hatna zong unla; a mel zong nilouh un.
12. A hihkhitsa a thilhih lamdangte theigige unla; a lamdannate leh, a kama vaihawmte;
13. Aw nou a sikha Israel chi, nou a mitelte, Jakob tate.
14. Amah TOUPA i Pathian ahi: a vaihawmte lei tengtengah a om hi.
15. Khantawnin a thukhun theigige unla, suan sang khatte kianga a piak a thu;
16. Thukhun Abraham toh a bawl, huan Isaak kianga a thuchiam;
17. Huan huaimah thuseh dingin Jakob kiangah a hihkipa, Israel kiangah khantawn thkhun dingin:
18. Na kiangah Kanaan gam, na goutan tuan, konpe ding, chiin:
19. Hihzaha mi tawmchik phet na hih lai un; ahi, tawm chik mahmah, a sunga khualzinmite;
20. Nam dangdang kiangah a ban hoh ua, gam dangdang akipanin mi tuamtuamte kiangah.
21. Kuamah nuaisiah a phal keia; ahi, amaute jiakin kumpipate leng a sal hi;
22. Khoih kei unla, ka jawlneite poi himhim leng khoih kei un, chiin.
23. Leitung luahte tengteng aw, TOUPA pahtawiin lasa unla, ni tengin a hotdamna thu theisak un.
24. Nam chih lakah a thupidan gen unla, mi chihte lakah a thillamdang hihte gen un.
25. TOUPA lah a thupi-a, nakpi mahmaha phat ding a hih jiakin; pathian tengteng sanga kihtak ding ahi.
26. Mi chih pathiante lah milim ahi ngal ua; TOUPAN bel vante a bawl hi.
27. Pahtawina leh thupina a maah a oma; hatna leh kilawmna a mun siangthou-ah a om hi.
28. Mi chiha chi chihte aw, TOUPA kiangah pia unla, TOUPA thupina leh hatna pia un.
29. TOUPA a min thupina pia unla, thillat ding tawi unla, a maah hongpai un; siangthou kilawmnain TOUPA chibaibuk un.
30. Lei tengteng aw, a maah ling un; khovel a lin theih louhna dingin hihkipin a om hi.
31. Vante kipak takin om uhenla, lei leng nuamsa takin om hen; nam chih lakah, TOUPAN vai a hawm, chi uheh.
32. Tuipi leh a sunga thil om tengteng ging hon henla; gam leh a sunga om tengteng kipak takin om hen;
33. Huchih hun chiangin gamnuaia sing tengtengin TOUPA maah nuamsain la a sa ding ua, lei vaihawm dingin lah a hong pai sin ngala.
34. Aw TOUPA kiangah kipahthu gen un, amah lah a hoih ngala; a chitna khantawna om ding ahi ngala.
35. Aw, ka hotdamna uh Pathian, honhondam inla, na min siangthou phat ding leh, na phatna suang dingin, nam chih laka kipan honpi khawm inla, honhumbitin, chi un.
36. Israelte Pathian TOUPA phatin om hen, Khantawn akipan khantawn dongin. Huan, mipi tengtengin, Amen, a chi ua, TOUPA a phatta uhi.
37. Huchiin huaia TOUPA thukhunna bawm maa ni teng a kuldan bang jela nasem dingin Asaph leh a unaute a koihtaa;
38. Obed-edom leh Hosa, a unau uh sawmguk leh giat te toh; Jeduthun tapa Obed-edom leh Hosa te kongkhakngakmi dingin ahi ua;
39. Huan Zadok siampu leh, a unaute siampute, Gibeona om mun sanga TOUPA biakbuk maa,
40. Jingsang leh nitaka halmangthillat maitam tunga TOUPA adia halmang thillatte lan gige dingin, Israel kiangah thupiaka piak, TOUPA dan sunga kigelh dungjuiin;
41. A chitna khantawna a om jiakin TOUPA kianga kipahthugen ding Heman leh Jeduthun leh mi dang min loha telkhiakte toh;
42. Heman leh Jeduthunin Pathian lasaklna dia vanzatte, pengkulte leh dakkhutbette, ngaihtaka ginsak dingte a nei ua; kongkhakngakmi ding Jeduthun tapate toh.Huchiin mipi tengteng amau in lam chiat ah a paita ua; huan David leng a inkote vualjawl dingin a pai sam hi.
43. Huchiin mipi tengteng amau in lam chiat ah a paita ua; huan David leng a inkote vualjawl dingin a pai sam hi.
