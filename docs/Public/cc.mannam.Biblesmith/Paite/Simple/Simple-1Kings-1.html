1Kings (1/22)  →
1. Huan, David a hong upa a, a hong tek mahmah ta; huchiin puanin a khuh jel uh, himahleh lum asa thei tuan hetkei.
2. Huchiin, a sikhaten a kiangah, ka pu uh kumpipa adingin nungak zong le ungla: Kumpipa kiang chinah om gige henla, kem leh; huan, na ang sungah hong lum henla, ka pu uh Kumpipan lum a hon sathei ding hi, a chi ua.
3. Huchiin, Israel gam tengteng ah nungak melhoih a zong ua, Shunam mi Abisag a vamu ua, Kumpipa kiangah a pi uh.
4. Huai nungak a mel a hoih mahmah; Kumpipa a kema, a nektak a bawl sak sek hi; himahleh Kumpipan a kithuahpih kei.
5. Huchiin, Hagith tapa Adonija in, kei Kumpipa a pang ding keive, chiin a kingai thupi mahmaha: kangtalai leh sakol tungtuang mite, mi sawmnga ama a tai dingte toh a kisa saka.
6. A pan lah chiklaiin leng, bang dia hichibanga hih na hia? Chiin a lungkhamsak ngeingal keia; huan, amah lah mel hoih mahmah ahi ngala, Absalom sang ahi.
7. Aman Zerui tapa Joab leh siampu Abiathar a thu a donga: amau Adonija lamah a pang ua, amah a panpih jel uh.
8. Siampu Zadok te, Jehoiada tapa Benai te, jawlnei Nathan te, Simei te, Rei te leh David pawla mi hangsante bel Adonija lamah a pang kei uh.
9. Huan, Adonija in belamte, bawngtalte, gan vak thaute Zoholet suangah a gou a, huai bel En-rogel kiang china om ahi; huan, kumpipa tapa, a unaute tengteng leh, Juda gama Kumpipa pipente tengteng achial a.
10. Jawlnei Nathan te, Benai te, mi hangsante leh a unaupa Solomon bel achial kei hi.
11. Huchiin, Nathan in Solomon nu Batseba kiangah, Hagith tapa Adonija I pu David theihlouh in vaihawmin a om chih za lou na hi maw?
12. Huchiin nang bitna ding leh na tapa Solomon bitna ding kon na hilh ding.
13. Kumpipa David kiangah valut inla, a kiangah, Aw Toupa, ka pu, na sikhanu kiangah na tapa Solomon, ka nungah a hong lal ngeingei sina, amah ka laltutphah ah a tu ngei sin hi, chiin na kichiam ahi kei maw? Bang achia Adonija in vaihawm ahia? Va chi dih.
14. Ngaiin, Kumpipa na vahoupih laitakin ken leng ka honjui lut tei dinga, na thugen ka honhihkip pih ding chiin a gen a.
15. Huan, Batseba Kumpipa kiangah indan ah a valuta; Kumpipa bel a upa mahmah taa, Shunam mi Abisag in Kumpipa nektak ana bawla.
16. Huan, Batseba in Kumpipa kunin chibai a buka. Huan, Kumpipan, bang na deih a? ana chi a.
17. Huan, aman, Pu aw, Toupa na Pathian min louin na sikhanu kiangah, na tapa Solomon ka nungin a hong lal ngeingei ding, amah ka laltutphah ah hong tu ding, chiin na na kichiam khina.
18. Ngaiin, tuin Adonija lah a lal a; nang ka pu toupan lah na theikei a;
19. Bawngtalte, gan vak thaute, belamte a gou liapluapa, kumpipa tapate tengteng leh, siampu Abiathar leh, sepaih heutu Joab te achiala, na sikha Solomon bel achial kei.
20. Huchiin, pu aw, Toupa nunga a laltutphaha kua hongtu ding ahia chih na gen chiangin, Israelte tengteng mit nang ka pu, toupa, na tungah a tu chiat ahi.
21. Huchilouin jaw hichi ahi ding, pu aw, toupa apute kianga a ihmut sam hun chiangin, kei leh ka tapa Solomon thil hihkhialin a honsep mai sin uh ahi, a chi a.
22. Huan, ngaiin, huchia kumpipa a houpih lai takin, jawlnei Nathan a valut teia.
23. Endih, jawlnei Nathan a hong chiin kumpipa a hilh ua. Huan, kumpipa kiang a vatunin, kumpipa maah a maia lei siin a khupboha.
24. Huan, Nathan in, Toupa aw, pu aw, ka nungah Adonija a lal dinga, ka laltutphah ah a tu ding na chi maw?
25. Tuniin a kuan suka, bawngtalte, gan vak thaute, belamte a gou zengzunga, kumpipa tapa tengteng, sepaih heutute, siampu Abiathar te achial a; huan, ngaiin, a ne un a dawn khawm ua, Pathianin kumpipa Adonija humbit hen, a chi uhi.
26. Himahleh kei na sikha ngei leh, siampu Zadok leh, Jehoiada tapa Benai leh na sikha Solomon te jaw hon chial kei hi.
27. Hiai toupa ka pu thu a hih a hih ngal leh toupa ka puin a nunga laltutphaha tu dingpa a kua pen ahia chih na sikhate na na hilh nai lou na hi ding maw? a chi a.
28. Huchiin kumpipa David in a dawnga, Bat-seba honsap sak dih ua, a chi a. Huan, kumpipa kiangah a vahoh a, kumpipa maah ava dinga.
29. Huan, kumpipa a kichiama, lungjinna tengtenga ka hinna humbitpa Toupa hinna louin,
30. Na kianga Toupa Israel te Pathian min loua, na tapa Solomon ka nungin a hong lal ngeingei ding, ka sikin ka laltutphah ah a hongtu sin hi, chia ka kichiam bangbangin, tuniin leng ka hih ding, achia.
31. Huchiin, Bat-seba bel a maia lei siin a khupboha, kumpipa chibai a buka, ka pu, kumpipa David khantawnin dam thek hen, a chi a.
32. Huan, kumpipa David in, siampu Zadok leh, jawlnei Nathan leh Jehoiada tapa Benai hon vasap sak dih ua, a chi a, Huan, kumpipa ma lamah a vapai ua.
33. Huan, kumpipan a kiang uah, na pu uh sikhate pi unla, ka tapa Solomon keimah sabengtung mahmah ah tuangsak unla, Gihon mun ah va pi suk un:
34. Huan, siampu Zadok leh jawlnei Nathan in Israelte kumpipa dingin sathau vanilh heh: Huan, pengkul mut unla, Pathianin kumpipa Solomon humbit hen, chi un.
35. Huchiin amah na jui tou ding ua, ka laltutphah ah a hongtu ding hi; amah lah ka sika lal ding ahi ngala: huan, amah Israel gam leh Juda gam heutu dinga ka seh ahi, a chi a.
36. Huan, Jehoiada tapa Benai in kumpipa a dawnga, Amen; Toupa, ka toupa kumpipa Pathianin leng pha sa hen.
37. Toupa ka pu, toupa kianga a om sak bangin, Solomon kiangah leng om sam jel henla ka pu kumpipa David laltutphah sangin a laltutphah hih thupi jaw hen, a chi a.
38. Huchiin, siampu Zadok te, jawlnei Nathan te, Jehoiada tapa Benai te, Keret mite, Pelet mite a hoh suk ua, Solomon David sabengtung tungah a tuang sak ua, Gihon mun ah a pi uh.
39. Huan, siampu Zadok in puanin akipan sathau koihna a laa, Solomon a nilh hi. Huan, pengkul a mut ua, huchiin mi tengtengin, Pathianin kumpipa Solomon humbit hen, a chi ua.
40. Huan, mipite tengtengin amah a jui tou ua, mipiten tamngai te a tum ua, a kipak mahmah ua, a husa ging un lei a kheng khama.
41. Huan, Adonija leh chialte a kianga omte tengtengin ankuang a lui khit un a ja ua. Huan, Joab in pengkul mut ging a jakin, khopi sunga husa ging juajua bang chi dan ahia leh? a chi a.
42. Huchia a chih lai takin, ngaiin, siampu Abiathar tapa Jonathan a vahoh a: Adonija in, Hongpai dih ve, nang jaw mi kizen na hi a, tanchin hoih na hontun mahmah ding, a na chi a.
43. Huan, Jonathan in a dawnga, Adonija kiangah, tun keng e, pu aw, kumpipa David in Solomon kumpipa dinga bawlkhin hi;
44. Amah toh siampu Zadok te, jawlnei Nathan te, Jehoiada tapa Benai te, Keret mite, Pelet mite kumpipan a sawla, kumpipa sabengtung tungah a tuang sak uh:
45. Huan, siampu Zadok leh jawlnei Nathan in Gihon munah kumpipa dingin sathau a nilh ua: huai akipanin nuamsa takin a pai tou ua, huaiin khopi sung a thawn juajua hi. Na husa jak uh huai ahi,
46. Huan, Solomon bel gam laltutphah ah a tuta hi.
47. Huan, kumpipa sikhate, Na Pathianin na min sangin Solomon min hihhoih jaw henla, na laltutphah sangin a laltutphah hih thupi jaw heh, chia ka pu uh kumpipa David kipahpih dingin a hoh ua; huan, kumpipa lupna siin a kuna.
48. Huan, kumpipan leng hichiin a chi, Toupa Israelte Pathian, tuni mahmaha ka laltutphaha tu ding hon seppa phatin om hen, ka mit mahmahin a muta hi, achia, achia.
49. Huan, Adonija mi chialte tengtengin a lauta ua, a thou ua, amau lampi pi uah a pai vek uh.
50. Huan, Adonija in Solomon a kihtaa, a thou a, maitam ki a valen a.
51. Huan, ngaiin, Adonija in kumpipa Solomon a kihta a; kumpipa Solomon in a sikha namsau a hihlum lou dingin ka kiangah kichiam phot heh chiin maitam ki a len hi, chiin Solomon a hilh ua.
52. Huan, Solomon in, mi ginom tak a hih a suklat peuhmah leh a sam zang khat leng leiah a ke kei ding; thulimlouhna a nei chih theihna om leh a si ding, achia.Huchiin kumpipa Solomon in mi a sawla, maitam akipan a vapi suk uh. Huan, a vahoha, kumpipa Solomon chibai a buka: huan, Solomon in a kiangah, Na inah paita in, ana chi hi
53. Huchiin kumpipa Solomon in mi a sawla, maitam akipan a vapi suk uh. Huan, a vahoha, kumpipa Solomon chibai a buka: huan, Solomon in a kiangah, Na inah paita in, ana chi hi
