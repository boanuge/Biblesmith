1. Huchiin Benjaminin a ta masa Bela, a zom Asbel, a thumna Ahar a suanga;
2. Nohah a lina, Rapha a ngana.
3. Huan Belain tapate a neia, Addar, Gera, Abihud;
4. Huan Abisua, Naaman, Ahoa;
5. Huan Gera, Sephuphan, Huram.
6. Huan hiaite Ehud tapate ahi uh: hiaite Gebaa tengte pipute inkote lupente ahi uh, huchiin amaute Manahath ah salin a pi uhi:
7. Huan Naaman, Ahija, Gera, amaute salin a pia; huan Uzza leh Ahihud a suang hi.
8. Huan Saharaimin amaute a sawl mang nungin, Moab mun ah tate a suanga; Husim leh Baar a jite ahi uh.
9. Huan a ji Hodes akipan, Jobaba, Zibia, Messa, Malkam a suang hi;
10. Huan Jeuz, Sakia, Mirma. Hiaite pipute inkote lupente, a tapate ahi uhi.
11. Huan Husim lakah Abitub leh Elpaal a suang hi.
12. Huan Elpaal tapate, Eber, Misam, Semed, Ono leh Lod, huaia khopite toh lampa:
13. Huan Beria, Sema, Aijalona Gatha tengteng delhjakte tengtengte pipute inkote lupente ahi uh;
14. Huan Ahio, Saksak, Jeremoth;
15. Huan Zebadia, Arad, Edar,
16. Huan Mikael, Ispah, Joha Beria tapate;
17. Huan Zebadia, Mosullam, Hizki, Heber;
18. Huan Ismerai, Izlia, Jobab, Elpaal tapate;
19. Huan Jakim, Zikri, Zabdi;
20. Huan Elienai, Zillethai, Eliel;
21. Huan Adai, Berai, Simrath, Simei tapate;
22. Huan Ispan, Eber, Eliel;
23. Huan Abdon, Zikri, Hanan;
24. Huan Hanania, Elam, Anthothija;
25. Huan Ipdei, Penuel, Sasak tapate;
26. Huan Samserai, Seharia, Athalia;
27. Huan Jaaresia, Elija, Zikri, Jeroham tapate.
28. Hiaite a khangte ua pipute ankote lupente ahi uh, mi pipente: hiaite Jerusalem ah a teng uhi.
29. Huan Gibeon ah Gibeon pa a tenga, Jeiel, a ji min Maaka:
30. Huan a tapa masapen Abdon, Zur, Kis, Baal, Nadab;
31. Huan Gedor, Ahio, Zeker.
32. Huan Mikloin Simea a suang hi. Huan a naute uh toh Jerusalem ah, a unaute uh jawnah a teng lai uhi.
33. Huan Nerin Kis a suanga; huan Kisin Saul a suang; huan Saulin Jonathan, Malki-sua, Abinadab, Esbaal a suang.
34. Huan Jonathan tapa Merib-baal ahi a; huan Merib-baalin Mika a suang.
35. Huan Mika tapate; Pithon, Melek, Tarea, Ahaz.
36. Huan Ahazin Jehoaddahin Alemeth, Azmavet, Zimri a suanga; huan Zimriin Moza a suang;
37. Huan Mozain Binea a suanga; Rapha a tapa ahi, Elesa a tapa, Azel a tapa:
38. Huan Azelin tapa guk a neia, a min uh hiaite ahi; Azrikam, Bokeru, Ishmael, Searia, Obadia, Hanan. Hiaite tengteng Azel tapate ahi uh.
39. Huan a unaupa Esek tapate; Ulam a ta masa, Jeus a zom, Eliphelet a thumna.Huan Ulam tapate mi hat hangsan, thalkapmite ahi uh, tapate, tapate tapate tampi, za khat leh sawmnga a nei uhi. Hiaite tengteng Benjamin tapate ahi uh.
40. Huan Ulam tapate mi hat hangsan, thalkapmite ahi uh, tapate, tapate tapate tampi, za khat leh sawmnga a nei uhi. Hiaite tengteng Benjamin tapate ahi uh.
