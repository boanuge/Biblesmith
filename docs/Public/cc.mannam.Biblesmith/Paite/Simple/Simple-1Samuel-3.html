1. Huan, Naupang Samuelin Eli maah Toupa na a sema, Huai laiin Toupa thu lah a manpha mahmaha; alanga kilaknate a om kei.
2. Huan, hichi ahi a, huai laiin, Eli amah mun ah a lup laiin, (a mit a mial deuhta a, khua a gina muh thei nawn ngel kei, )
3. Pathian khawnvak leng a mih main, Pathian bawm omna Toupa biakin ah Samuel a luma;
4. TOUPAN Samuel a sama; aman, Hiaiah ka om, a chi a.
5. Huan, Eli kiangah a vataia, a kiangah, Hiaiah ka om, non sama, a chi a. Huan, aman, Kon sam kei; valum nawnin, a chi a. Huchiin a paia, a valum nawnta hi.
6. Huan, TOUPAN Samuel a sam nawna. Huan, Samuel a thoua, Eli kiangah a hoha, Hiaiah ka om; non sama, a chi a. Huan, aman a dawnga, ka honsam kei, ka tapa; va lum nawnin, a chi a.
7. Huailaiin Samuelin TOUPA a thei nai keia, TOUPA thu lah a kianga pulak leng ahi nai keia.
8. Huan, TOUPAN Samuel a sam nawna, huai bel a thumveina ahi. Huan, a thoua, Eli kiangah a hoha, Hiaiah ka om; non sama, a chi a. Huchiin Eliin naupang TOUPAN sam ahi chih a theitaa.
9. Huaijiakin Eliin Samuel kiangah, Pai inla, va lum nawn inla, a honsap nawn leh, TOUPA, thugenin, na sikhain a ngaikhia hi, na chi ding hi, a chi a. Huchiin Samuel a paita a, a munah a valum nawn hi.
10. Huan, TOUPA a hongpaia, a hong dinga, a maa a sap bangin, Samuel, Samuel, chiin a sam nawna, huchiin Samuelin, Thu genin, na sikhain a ngaikhia hi, a chi a.
11. Huan, TOUPAN Samuel kiangah, Ngaiin, Israel gamah a za peuhmahte bil a lang nihniha aphuai takin thil ka hih sin ahi.
12. Huai niin a inkuan tungtang thu ka gen tengteng a bul akipanin a tawp tanin Eli tungah ka hih sin ahi.
13. A taten Pathian a gensiatna khelhna uh theigigena pia a khamlouh jiakin a inkuan khantawna dingin ka ngaihtuah sin ahi chih ka hilhkhinta ngala.
14. Huaijiakin Eli inkuan kiangah Eli inkuan bohsiatna kithoihnain hiam, thillatin hiam, chikchiangin leng siamsak theih ahi kei ding chiin ka kichiam khinta hi, a chi a.
15. Huan, Samuel jingsang tanin a luma, huan, TOUPA in kongkhakte a honga, Huchiin Samuelin huai kilakna Eli theihsak a lauthawng hi.
16. Huchiin Eliin Samuel a sama, Samuel, ka tapa, a chi a. Huan, aman, Hiaiah ka om, a chi a.
17. Huan, aman, Bang thil ahia TOUPAN na kianga a gen? ka kiangah bangmah im ken aw; na kianga a gen tengteng ka kiangah im na neih leh na tungah Pathianin a hihhihin hih henla, huai sanga thupijaw leng, a chi a.
18. Huan, Samuelin bangteng a hilh sipsipa, a kiangah bangmah im a neikei. Huchiin aman, TOUPA ahi mah ve, hoih a sak bangbangin hih hen, a chi a.
19. Huan, Samuel a hongkhang lian deuhdeuha, a kiangah TOUPA a om jela, a thu himhim leitungah a luang mangsak kei hi.
20. Huchiin Daan akipan Beer-seba tan hialin Israel mite tengtengin Samuel hi TOUPA jawlnei dinga hihkip ahi chih a na theita uhi.Huan, TOUPA bel Silo ah a kilak nawna: TOUPA bel thuin Samuel kiangah Silo ah a kilak lah ahi ngala.
21. Huan, TOUPA bel Silo ah a kilak nawna: TOUPA bel thuin Samuel kiangah Silo ah a kilak lah ahi ngala.
