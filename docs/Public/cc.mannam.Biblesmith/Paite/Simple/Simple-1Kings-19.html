1. Huan, Ahab in Elija thilhih tengteng, namsau a jawlnei tengteng a thah thute toh Jezebel a hilh a.
2. Huchiin Elija kiangah, huai mite laka mi khat hinna bangin jingchianga hichi hun ma a na hinna ka hon bawlsak sam kei leh Pathian ten a lohloh uh honloh mai u henla, huai sanga thupi jaw inleng, chiin mi a sawla.
3. Huchiin a laua, a thou a, a tai a, Juda gama Beer-seba khua a vatunga, huaiah a sikha a nusia hi.
4. Amah bel gamdai ah ni khat a phak ah a hoh a, juniper sing nuaiah a tu a; huan, sih a ngenta hi, hun ta hi; Toupa aw, tuin ka hinna la maiin, ka pute sang inlah ka hoih tuan ngal keia, achia.
5. Huchiin juniper sing nuaiah a luma, a ihmuta hi; huan, ngaiin, angel in a hongkhoih a, akiangah, thou inla ne in, achia.
6. Huan, a en vialviala, ngaiin, a lu chinah tanghou pei, suang sa a hai minsa leh, tuithawl ana om a. Huchiin a ne a, a dawn a, a lum nawn hi.
7. Huan, Toupa angel a hongpai kik nawn a, a hong khoiha, thou inla, ne in, na paina ding nanga din a thupi lua hi, achia.
8. Huan, a thou a, a nein a dawn a, huchiin ann jiak in Pathian tang Horeb tan ni sawm li leh jan sawm li a pai a.
9. Huan, huai a kokhuk khat ava tunga, huaiah a omta a; huan, ngaiin, Toupa thu a kiangah a hongtunga, a kiangah, hiaiah bang na hih a, Elija? Achia.
10. Aman, Toupa, sepaih te Pathian a dingin phatuam kangai mahmah jela, Israel suanten lah na thukhun a leh ngatsan ua, na maitam te a chimsak ua, na jawlnei te namsau in a satlum ngal ua; a omlai lah kei kia ka hia, ka hinna honlak sak a tum uh hi, achia.
11. Huan, aman, va pawtkhia inla, hua tangah Toupa maah va dingin, achia. Huan, ngaiin, Toupa a hongpai a, huihpi hat mahmah in tangte Toupa maah amut khanga, suangpi a kitam phiakphuak ua; himahleh huaiah Toupa a om kei: huan, huih nun khitin jin a hongling a; himahleh jinling ah Toupa a om kei;
12. Huan, jinlin khitin mei a hongpawt a; himahleh nei ah Toupa a om kei; huan, mei nung in aw ngat ngeingei a hong pawt.
13. Huan, hichi ahia, Elija in huai a jakin a puanin a mai a khuh a, ava pawtkhia a, kokhuk kongpi ah ava ding a. Huan, ngaiin, aw in a kiangah, hiaiah bang na hih Elija? A hongchi a.
14. Huan, aman, Toupa, sepaihte Pathian a dingin phatuam ka nei mahmah jela, Israel suante lah na thukhun a lehngatsan ua, na maitam te a chimsak ua, na jawlnei te namsau in a satlum ngal ua; a omlai kei ka hia, ka hinna honlak sak a tum uh, achia.
15. Huan, Toupa a kiangah, Pai nawnta inla, gamdai lamah Damaska khua ah paiin: na tun chiangin Suria kumpipa dingin Hazael sathau na nilh dinga;
16. Huan, Israel kumpipa dingin Nimsi tapa Jehu sathau na nilh ding; huan, na sika jawlnei hi dingin Abelmehol khua a mi Saphat tapa Elisa leng sathau na nilh ding.
17. Huan, hichi ahi dinga, Hazael namsau pel peuhmah Jehu in a that dinga; huan, Jehu namsau pel peuhmah Elisa in a that ding.
18. Himahleh Israel gamah Baal ma a khukdin ngei nailou leh, tawp ngei nailou mi sangsagih kei a din ka hum ding hi, achia.
19. Huchiin huai a kipan a pawtkhia a, Saphat tapa Elisa ava mukhia a, aman lei ana leta, a maah bawng tuak sawm leh nih a om a, a sawm leh nihna amah mah in ana hawla; huan, Elija in ava naih a, a puan a liangbat saka.
20. Huan, bawng te a paisan a, Elija a delh a, ka nu leh ka pa kava tawp tadih phot ding, huchiin ka honjui ding, achia. Huan, aman a kiangah, va pai nawn lechin ake, bang kon chih a eita? Achia.Huan, amah jui lai a kika, bawng tuak khat a hawl a gou a, asa bawng vante in a huan a, a mite a hawm a, a ne uh. Huchiin a thou a, Elija a delh a, ana a sepsak ta hi.
21. Huan, amah jui lai a kika, bawng tuak khat a hawl a gou a, asa bawng vante in a huan a, a mite a hawm a, a ne uh. Huchiin a thou a, Elija a delh a, ana a sepsak ta hi.
