1. Huchi in, Israelte Sittim ah a om ua, huan, Moabte tanute a kithuahpih chiat ta ua:
2. A pathiante uh kiang a kithoihnate uah achial ua; huchi in a ne ua, a pathiante uh chibai a buk tei ta uh.
3. Huan, Israelte'n Baalpeor mah a pom tei ta ua; huchi in Israelte tungah TOUPA a heh mahmah hi.
4. Huchi in TOUPA'N Mosi kiang ah, Mipite heutu tengteng pi inla, TOUPA hehna kihtakhuai tak mai Israelte tung a kipan a a kikhinmang theihna ding in sun khovak ah TOUPA mah ah awklum sak in, a chi a.
5. Huan, Mosi in Israelte vaihawm mite kiang ah, Baal-peor pom peuhmahte hihlum vek un, a chi a.
6. Huan, ngai in, Israel lak a mi khat in kihoupihna puan inn kongkhak a a kah laitak un Mosi mitmuh in leh Israelte omkhawm mitmuh in Midia numei khat a unaute kiang ah a hon pi a
7. Huan, siampu Aron ta Eleazar tapa Phinehasa a muh leh omkhawmte a kipan a thou a, teipi a la ta hi;
8. Huan, Israel mi puan inn sung ah a delh lut a, Israel mi toh a nih un a sun pailet a, numei pen a gil ah. Huch in Israel suante tung a gawtna a dai ta hi.
9. Huai mi gawtna a site sing nih leh sang li ahi uh.
10. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
11. Siampu Aron ta Eleazar tapa Phinehasa in ka thik bang a a lak ua a mullitna in ka hehna Israel suante lak a kipan a kikhinkhe sak ta a, huchi ka mullitna in Israel suante ka hihmangthang kei ding.
12. Huaiziak in, Ngai in, ka kilemna thukhun a tungah ka khung ding a;
13. Huan, amah leh a nung a a suante ding in khantawn siamputna thukhun ahi ding a; a Pathian a dia mullit, Israel suante ding a kilemna a bawl ziak in, chi in, chi in.
14. Israel mi a thah uh, Midia numei toh a thah uh min Zimri, Simeonte lak a a innkuan inntek pen Salu tapa ahi.
15. Huan, Midia numei a thah uh min Kozbi, Zur tanu ahi: amah Midiate innkuan lak a heutu ahi.
16. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
17. Midiate hih lungzing unla, hih niam un:Amau leng Peor tungtang thu ah leh Peor tungtang thu a gawt ni a a sanggamnu uh, Midiate mi lian tanu a thah uh, Kozbi tungtang thu ah git louh pil a a honkhem uh bang in a hon khem lailai ngal ua, chi in.
18. Amau leng Peor tungtang thu ah leh Peor tungtang thu a gawt ni a a sanggamnu uh, Midiate mi lian tanu a thah uh, Kozbi tungtang thu ah git louh pil a a honkhem uh bang in a hon khem lailai ngal ua, chi in.
