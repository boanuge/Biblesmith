1. Huan, huaite nungin hichi ahia, Joseph kiangah, ngaiin, na pa a chi ana, achi ua. Huchiin, a tapa nihte Manassi leh Ephraim a kiangah a tonpiha,
2. Huan, Jakob kingah, ngaiin, na tapa Joseph na kiangah a hong hi, chiin. A hilh ua: huchiin, Israel a kihalhsaka, lupna ah a tuta a.
3. Huan, Jakob in, Joseph kiangah, Pathian bangkim hihthei Kanan gama Luz khua ah ka kiangah a kilaka, a honvualjawla,
4. Ka kiangah, chi tampi ka honsuangsak dinga, ka honhihpung lai ding, chi tuamtuam omkhawm in ka honbawl ding; huan, hiai gam na nunga na suante kiangah khantawn gou dingin ka pe ding, honchi a.
5. Huan, na tapa nihte, Aigupta gama na neihte, keia ahi uh; Ephraim leh Manassi, Reuben leh Simeon bangmah in, keia ahi ding uh.
6. Huan, hiai mite nunga ta ne neihte nanga ahi ding uh, a goutan uah a ute uh min a kikoppih mai ding uh.
7. Huan, kei tungthu ahihleh, Paddan gam a kipana ka hong laiin Rakil pen Kanan gamah lamkalah, Ephtath khua a gamla kuam ahihlaiin, ka kiangah a si a: huailaiah Ephrath kho lampi ah ka vuita, (huaibel Bethlehem khua ahi), achia.
8. Huan, Israel in Joseph tapate a ena, hiaite kuate ahi ua? achia.
9. Huan, Joseph in, apa kiangah, hiaite ka tapa, Pathian in hiai laia honpiak te ahi uh, achia. Huan, aman, hehpihtakin ka kiangah honpiin, amau kana vualjawl ding, achia.
10. Israel mit bel a upat jiakin a chim nawn kei a, huan, aman ana tawp a, ana pom a.
11. Huan, Israel in, Joseph kiangah, na mel muh ka lamen nawn keia, himahleh, ngaiin, Pathian in, na suante mel nangawn a honmusakta hi, achia.
12. Huan, Joseph in amau apa pheikal akipan a kai khia a, a maia lei siin chibai a buka.
13. Huan, Joseph in a nih un a kai a, Ephraim pen a khut taklamin Israel khut veilam pangah a kai a, Manassi pen a khut veilamin Israel khut taklam pangah a kai a, honpi nai a.
14. Huan, Israel in a sawk kalh teitei a, a khut taklam in a sawka, Ephraim a naupang jaw siipah a nga a; Manassi lah ta masa ahi ngala.
15. Huan, Joseph a vualjawla, honsuangpa Abraham leh Isaak ama a a om jelna Pathian, tuni tana ka damsung tengtenga honvakpa Pathian,
16. Gilou tengteng a kipan hon tanpa angel in naupang te vualjawl hen; huan, ka min leh honsuangpa Abraham leh Isaak min amau minin pu uhen; huan, khovel laizang ah mipa thupitak hi dingin hongpung uhen, achia.
17. Huan, Joseph in apan akhut taklam Ephraim sipa a ngak a muhin a lungkim takei a; huchiin apa khut Ephraim sipa kipan Manassi sipa suan dingin a domkanga.
18. Huan, Joseph in, apa kiangah, pa, hichibang ding ahi kei: hiai ahi ta masa, na khut taklam a siipah nga jaw in, achia.
19. Himahleh, apa a ut keia, ka thei hi, ka tapa aw, ka thei hi: amah leh nam a gen thamin a om sam dinga, amah leng a thupi sam ding: Ahihhangin a naupa amah sangin a thupi zo dinga, a suante leng chi tuamtuam tamtak a hong hi ding uh, achia.
20. Huchiin, Jakob in, Israelten, Ephraim leh Manassi bangin Pathianin nang honomsak hen, chiin, noumau min louin ahon vualjawl jel ding uh, chiin huai niin, amau a vualjawl a: huchiin Ephraim Manassi maah a omsakta hi.
21. Huan, Israel mahin, Joseph kiangah, ngaiin, si ding ka hita hi: himahleh Pathian jaw na kiang uah a om ding a, nou hon suangte gamah a honpi lut nawn ding.Huailou leng, na unaute tantuan sangin tan khat in ka honpe tuan hi, ka namsau leh ka thalpeu a Amor te khut akipan ka laksak pen, achia.
22. Huailou leng, na unaute tantuan sangin tan khat in ka honpe tuan hi, ka namsau leh ka thalpeu a Amor te khut akipan ka laksak pen, achia.
