1. Huan, huai niin Jerusalem khua a saptuam omte khawng, min nak pitakin a sawi panta ua; huchiin, sawltakte kia loungal Judia leh Samari gam kho tengah a dalhjakta vek uhi.
2. Huan, Pathian limsak kuate hiamin Stephen a vui ua, nakpi takin a kah uh.
3. Saulain bel saptuam pawlte a sawia, in tengah a luta, numei pasal a kaikhia a, suangkulh ah a khum hi.
4. Huan, a dalhjakte kho teng ah a paita ua, thu tuh a gen jel uh.
5. Huan, Samari khua ah Philip a hohsuk a, a kiang uah Kris thu a tangkoupih hi.
6. Huan, a thillamdang hihte a jak ua, a muh un, mipiten lungsim mun khatin, Philip thugente a limsakta uhi.
7. Banghanghiam i chih leh, dawininte mi tampi a matte sung ua kipanin aw ngaihtaka kikou in a pawt ua; jaw leh khebai tampi hihdamin a om uhi.
8. Huchiin tua khopi ah nakpitaka kipahna a om hi.
9. Huan, mi khat a min Simon a oma; aman, Mi lian ka hi, chiin, tumalamin huai khua ah mitkhialdawiin Samari khuaa mite lamdang sa takin a bawl nilouhlouha.
10. Mi tengtengin, tagahpen akipan lalpen tanin, Hiai mi Pathian thilhihtheihna thupi a chih uh, ahi tham, chiin, a thu a limsak chiat ua.
11. A mitkhialdawiin sawtpi lamdang sa taka a bawl nak jiakin a thu a limsak uhi.
12. Himahleh, amau tuh Pathian gam tanchin hoih leh Jesu Kris min tanchin hoih Philip gen a gintak un, anumei apasalin baptisma a tangta ua.
13. Huan, Simon nangawnin leng a gingtata a, huan, baptisma a tan zohin Philip kiangah a omgige a, chiamtehna leh thillamdang thuipite a mu a, lamdang a sa thei mahmah.
14. Huan, sawltak Jerusalem khuaa omten Samari khuaa miten Pathian Thu a gingta ta uh chih a jak un, a kiang uah Peter leh Johan a sawl ua;
15. huai mite tuh a vatun suk uleh Kha Siangthou a muh theihna ding un a thumsak ua.
16. Huai main a lak uah kuamah tungah a tung nai kei; Toupa minin baptisma a tang phet uh ahi.
17. Huan, sawltakten a tunguah a khut uh a koih ua, Kha Siangthou a muta uhi.
18. Huan, sawltakte khut koih jiaka Kha Siangthou piak ahi chih Simonin a muhin.
19. Hichibang thuneihna honpe sam un, kua tung peuh aleng khut ka koihnain Kha Siangthou a muh samna dingin, chiin, a kiang uah dangka piak a tum hi.
20. Himahleh Peterin a kiangah, Na dangka nang toh mang heh, Pathian thilpiak dangka suma lei na tup jiakin.
21. Hiai thu ah nang jaw na pang kei, tan leng na neikei hi; na lungtang Pathian ngaihin a dikkei ahi.
22. Huaijiakin, hiai na gitlouhna tawp san inla, Toupa kiangah thumin, huchiin na lungtanga na ngaihtuah a honngaidam kha moh ding hiam.
23. Thil khakna sinkha ah leh gitlouh gaklopna ah na om chih ka thei hi, a chi a.
24. Huan, Simonin, Na thil gen uh bangmah ka tunga hongtun louhna dingin noumauin Toupa kiangah honthumsak un, a chi a, a dawnga.
25. Huchiin, amau Toupa thu a theihsak ua, a gen zoh nung un. Samari gama mite kho tampi kianga Tanchin Hoih a gen jel in, Jerusalem khua ah a pai nawn uh hi.
26. Huan, Toupa angel khatin Philip a houpiha, Thou inla, simlama Jerusalem khua akipana Gaza kho sunsuk lampi huai gamdai ah hoh in, a chi a.
27. Huchiin, a thou a, a paita. Huan, ngaiin, Ethiopia gama mi khat, Ethiopia kumpipa Kanda nuaia mi chilgeh laltak, a gou tengteng kempa, Jerusalem khua ah Pathian be dingin a hoh tou a,
28. a pai lamin a kangtalaia tu kawmin jawlnei Isai laibu a simsim hi.
29. Huan, Khain, Philip kiangah, Huai kangtalai vanaih inla, vakithuahpih in, a chi a.
30. Huan, Philip tuh mi chilgeh kiangah a vataia, jawlnei Isai laibu a sim a ja a, Na sim a omdan na thei hia? a chi a.
31. Huan, aman, Kuamah honhilhchet louhin bangchin ka thei dia? a chi a. Huan, a kianga tu paisuak dingin Philip tuh achialta a.
32. Huan, laisiangthou a sim lai tuh hiai ahi: Gohding belam bangin amah a pi ua; Belamnou a mulmetpa ma a a daidinden bangin aman leng a kam a ka kei;
33. A vuallelh laiin a tungah thu gen a phal kei uh; a suante tanchin kuan a hongen ding ua? khovel akipanin a hinna a laksak ngal ua, chiin.
34. Huan, mi chilgehin, Philip a houpiha, a kiangah, Jawlneiin hiai thu khawng kua thu a genna ahia, amah thu hia, midang thu? ka hondong ahi, a chi a.
35. Huan, Philipin a gena, huai laisiangthou thu abul ah panin, a kiangah Jesu thu a hilh a.
36. Huan, lampia a pai kawm un tui banghiam a tung ua; huan, mi chilgeh in, Endih, hiaiah tui a om, baptisma tanna ding bangin a hondal khola ahia? a chi a.
37. Huan, Philipin, Na lungtang tengtenga na gintak leh, na hi thei, a chi a. Huan, amah a dawnga, Jesu Kris tuh Pathian Tapa ahi chih ka gingta, a chi a.
38. Huan, kangtalai a khawlsak ua, a nih un, Philip leh mi chilgeh, tui ah a hoh khawm suk ua; huan, Philipin amah a baptista hi.
39. Huan, tui apat a pai touh tak un Toupa Khain Philip tuh a la mangta hi; huan, mi chilgehin amah a mu nawnta kei, nuamsa takin a paita jel hi.Himahleh, Philip tuh Azota khua ah muhin a om nawnta hi; huan, a pai suak a, Kaisaria khua a tun masiah kho tengah Tanchin Hoih a gen jeljel hi.
40. Himahleh, Philip tuh Azota khua ah muhin a om nawnta hi; huan, a pai suak a, Kaisaria khua a tun masiah kho tengah Tanchin Hoih a gen jeljel hi.
