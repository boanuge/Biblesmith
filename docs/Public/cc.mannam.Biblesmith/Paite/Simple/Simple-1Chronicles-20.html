1. Huan hichi ahi a, kum a hongbeia kumpipate kidou dinga a kuankhiak sek lai un, Joabin sepaih ahatahat a pi khiaa. Amon suante gam a suhsaka, Raba khua a um uh. David bel Jerusalem ah a om. Huan Joabin Raba khua a suala, a lata hi.
2. Huan Davidin a kumpipa uh lallukhu khuk lai a laksaka, a gikdan dangkaeng talent khat ahi, suangmantamte leng a tela; huan, David a khusak ua; huan, khopi-a om gallak sum tampi a la hi.
3. Huan huailaia mipite bel a pi khiaa, singatnate, lei lehna sik zumte, heipitein na a semsaka. Huchibangin Amon tate khopi tengtengte Davidin a hih hi, Huan David leh mi tengteng Jerusalem ah a pai nawnta uhi.
4. Huan hichi ahi a, huainungin, Philistia mite toh Gezer ah kidouna a oma; huchiin Husat mi Sibekaiin milian suante laka mi Sippai a thata; huan, a vuallelta uhi.
5. Huan Philistia mite toh kidouna a om nawna; huan Jair tapa Elhanin Git mi Goliath unaupa Lahmi a thata, a tei kung chiampek bang ahi.
6. Huan Gath khua ah kidouna a om nawna, huaiah mi sang mahmah, a khut khatlam khatlama khutzung guk, a khe khatlam khatlama khezung guk, a veka sawmnih leh li nei a oma; amah leng milian laka piang ahi.
7. Huan aman Israel mite a simmoha, David unau Sime tapa Jonathanin a nathat hi.Huaite Gath khuaa milian laka piang ahi uh; huaite David leh a mite khut ah a si uhi.
8. Huaite Gath khuaa milian laka piang ahi uh; huaite David leh a mite khut ah a si uhi.
