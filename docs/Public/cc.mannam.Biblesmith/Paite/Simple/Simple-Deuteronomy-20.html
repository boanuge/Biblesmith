1. Na Melmate uh dou dinga na kuan ua, sakol, kangtalai, mipi nou sanga tam na muh un, amau na kihta ding uh ahi kei; Aigupta gam akipan nou hinpi khepa TOUPA na Pathian uh lah na kiang uah a om ngala.
2. Huan, na kidou kuan un siampuin mipi vanaih henla, houpih henla, a kiang uah,
3. Israelte aw, j aun, tuin na melmate uh dou ding na hi ua, lau kei unla. Kihta kei unla, ling sam kei un,. amaute kihta kei un:
4. Nou honhondam dinga na melmate uh honsual sak dia na kiang ua om jelpa lah TOUPA na Pathian uh ahi ngala, chi heh.
5. Huan, heututen mipite kiangah, In thak lama lan nai lou na om uhia? A in ah pai nawn hen, kidounaah a vasi kha dinga, mi dangin a lan kha ding hi.
6. Huan, grep huan bawla, a gah ne nai lou na om uhia? A in lam ah pai nawn heh, kidounaah a si kha dinga, mi dangin a ne kha ding uhi.
7. Huan, ji khama kiteng nai lou na om uhia? A in lam ah pai nawn heh, kidounaah a sikha dinga, mi dangin a kitenpih kha ding uhi, chiin gen heh
8. Huan, heututen mipite kiangah, Lau leh kihta na om uhia. A in lamuah pai uhen, a unaute uh lungtang amah lungtang bangin azul tei kha ding hi, chi lailai uheh.
9. Huan, hichi ahi dinga, heututen mipite kianga thu a gen khit un mipite makai ding sepaih heutute a sep ding uhi.
10. Sual dinga khua na vanaih hun chiang un kituahna thu gen phot unla.
11. Huan, hichi ahi dinga, a hon kituahpih ua, a nakipiak uleh hichi ahi dinga, huaia mi om tengteng nasem dinga na goih ding uh ahi dinga, na na uh a sem ding uh.
12. Huan, a honna kituahpih nop louh ua, a honna dou sin uleh na um ding ua:
13. Huan, TOUPA na Pathian un na khut ua a honpiak hun chiangin huaia pasal tengteng namsauin na hihlum vek ding uhi:
14. Numei leh a ta neu chikchikte uh, gante leh khuaa thil om himhimte jaw huaia gallak thil tengteng toh na tuam ding ua na lak ding uh ahi; huan TOUPA na Pathian un a honpiak na melmate uh laka gallak thil na ne un na ta ding uhi.
15. Hiai namte khua hi lou, na omna ua kipana kho gamla tengteng huchibangin na hih jel ding uhi.
16. TOUPA na Pathian un luah dinga a honpiak hiai nam khuate jaw TOUPA na Pathian un thu a honpiak bangin a hing peuhmah bangmah na hawi ding uh ahi keia:
17. Hitte, Amorte, Kanante, Perizte, Hivte, Jebuste na hihmang vek ding uhi:
18. A pathiante uh tunga a hih sek uh thil kihhuai tengteng a honsin sak louhna ding un, huchilouinjaw TOUPA na Pathian tunguah thil na hihkhial kha ding uhi.
19. Lak tuma na sual uh a khua sawtpi na um un leng huaia singte jaw heipiin na phuk ding uh ahi keia, na nek ding uh ahi a, na phuk ding uh ahi kei: huai sing mihing ahi ua hia na um uh?A gah nek louh chi sing ahi chih na theihte ubel na hihsia un na phuk ding ua, a hondoute khua a siat masiah kulhin na umkhum ding uhi.
20. A gah nek louh chi sing ahi chih na theihte ubel na hihsia un na phuk ding ua, a hondoute khua a siat masiah kulhin na umkhum ding uhi.
