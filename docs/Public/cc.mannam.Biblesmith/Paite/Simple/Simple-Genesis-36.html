1. Huan, hiaite Esau suante ahi uh (Esau Edom ahi).
2. Esauin a jite Kanan gama nungakte laka a neih ahi; Hit mi Elon tanu Ada leh, Hiv mi Zibeon tanu, Ana Tanu Oholibamte ahi uh;
3. Ismael tanu, Nebaioth sanggamnu Basemath toh.
4. Huan, Ada in Eliphaz Esau a neihsaka; Basemath in Reuel a neia;
5. Oholibam in Jeus leh Jalam leh Korah a neia; huaite Esau tapa, Kanan gama a neihte ahi uh.
6. Huan, Esauin a jite leh, a tapate leh, a tanute leh, a in a mi tengteng leh, a gante leh, a sa tengteng leh, a sum tengteng, Kanan gama a muhte a paipih, a nau Jakob kianga kipan gamdang ah a pai mangta hi.
7. A thilneihte uh a thupi lua a, a om khawm theikei uhi; a gante jiakin mikhual a a omna gam un lah a chinta kei hi.
8. Huchiin, Esau tangah a omta: Esau tuh Edom ahi.
9. Huan, hiaite Seir tang a Edom te suangpa Esau suante ahi uh:
10. Hiaite Esau tapate minte ahi: Eliphaz Esau ji Ada tapa Reuel Esau ji Basemath ta.
11. Huan, Eliphaz tapate Tamante Omarte, Zephote, Gatamte, Kenazte ahi uh.
12. Huan, Timna tuh Esau tapa Eliphaz mei ahia; aman Eliphaz Amalek a neihsaka: Huaite Esau ji Ada suante ahi uh.
13. Huan, hiaite Reuel tapate ahi uh; Nahath leh Zerahte, Samma leh Mizzate: huaite Esau ji Basemath suante ahi uh.
14. Huan, hiaite Zibeon tanu Ana tanu Esau ji Oholibam tapate ahi uh: aman Esau Jeus leh Jalam leh Koran a neihsak hi.
15. Hiaite Esau tapate laka heutute ahi uh: Esau ta chil Eliphaz tapate; heutu Temante, heutu Omarte, heutu Zephote, heutu Kenazte,
16. Heutu Korah te, heutu Gatamte, heutu Amalek te: huaite Edom gam a Eliphaz akipana pawt heutute ahi uh; huaite Ada suante ahi uh.
17. Huan, hiaite Esau tapa Reuel suante ahi uh; heutu Nahath te, heutu Zerahte, heutu Samma te, Heutu Mizzate: huaite Edom gam a Reuel akipana pawt heutute ahi uh; huaite Esau ji Basemath suante ahi uhi.
18. Huan, hiaite Esau ji Oholibam suante ahi uh; heutu Jeus te, heutu Jalam te, heutu Korah te: huaite heutu Ana tanu Esau ji Oholibam suante ahi uh.
19. Huaite Esau tapate ahi uh, huaite a lak ua heutute ahi uh: Esau tuh Edom ahi.
20. Hiaite Hor mi Seir tapa huai gama omte ahi uh; Lotan te, Sobal te, Zibeon te, Ana te, Dison te, Ezer te, Disan te,
21. Huaite Edom gama Seir ta Horta laka kipana pawt heutute ahi uh.
22. Huan, Lotan tate Hor leh Hemam ahi uh. Lotan sanggamnu Timna ahi.
23. Huan, hiaite Sobal tate ahi uh; Alvan te, Manahath te, Ebalt e, Sepho te, Onam te.
24. Huan, hiaite Zibeon tate ahi uh; Aia leh Ana: hiai Ana ahi, apa Zibeon sabengtungte a khah laia gamdaia tui sa khuk te mukhepa.
25. Huan, hiaite Dison tate ahi uh; Dison leh Ana tanu Oholibam.
26. Hiaite Disan tate ahi uh; Uz leh Aran.
27. Hiaite Horte laka kipana pawt heutute ahi uh; heutu Lotan te, heutu Sobal te, heutu Zibeon te, heutu Ana te,
28. Heutu Dison te, heutu Ezer te, heutu Disan te: huaite Seir gama a heutu uh dan banga Horte laka kipana pawt a heutute ahi uh.
29. Hiaite, kumpipa kuamah in Israel suante tunga vai a hawm ma ua, Edom gama vaihawm kumpipate ahi uhi.
30. Huan. Beor tapa Velin Edom gama vai a hawma; a khopi min Dinhaba ahi.
31. Huan, Vel a si a, Bozra khua a mi Zerah tapa Jobab in a sikin vai a hawm ta.
32. Huan, Jobab a si a, Timante gam a mi Husam-in a sikin vai a hawm ta a.
33. Huan, Husam a si a, Bedad tapa Hadad, tapa Hadad, Moab pawn a Midiante that pan, a sikin vai a hawma: a khopi min Avith ahi.
34. Huan, Hadad a si a, Masrek mi Samla in a sikin vai a hawm.
35. Huan, Samla a si a, Luipi gei a Rebototh khua a mi Saula in a sikin vai a hawm hi.
36. Huan, Saula, a si a, Akbor tapa Baal-hanana in a sikin vai a hawm ta hi.
37. Huan, Akbor tapa Baal-hanana a si a, Hada in a sikin vai a hawma: a khopi min Pau ahi; a ji min Hehetabel ahia, amah Mesahaba ta Matred tanu ahi.
38. Huan, hiaite a inkuan uh bang jel leh, a mun bang jel u leh a min bang jel Esau suan heutute min ahi; heutu Timna te, heutu Alva te, heutu Jetheth te;
39. Heutu Oholibamte, heutu Elate, heutu Pinonte;
40. Heutu Kenazte, heutu Temante, heutu Mibzarte;
41. Heutu Magdiel te, heutu Iram te; huaite a gam neih uah a omna uh bang jelin Edom suan heutute ahi uh: huai Edom Edomte suangpa Esau ahi.
42. Heutu Kenazte, heutu Temante, heutu Mibzarte;Heutu Magdiel te, heutu Iram te; huaite a gam neih uah a omna uh bang jelin Edom suan heutute ahi uh: huai Edom Edomte suangpa Esau ahi.
43. Heutu Magdiel te, heutu Iram te; huaite a gam neih uah a omna uh bang jelin Edom suan heutute ahi uh: huai Edom Edomte suangpa Esau ahi.
