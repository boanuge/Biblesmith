1. Huchiin hiaite vaihawmna a ma ua na ngak dingte ahi uh.
2. Hebru sikha na lei leh, kum guk na a sep ding: a kum sagihnaah bangmah louin nuamlenin a pawt khe ding.
3. Amah tuma honglut leh, amahtumin a pawt khe ding: ji a nei a hihleh, huchiin a jiin amah a jui khe ding.
4. A puin ji apiaka, tapate hiam tanute hiam a neihsak leh, ji leh a tate a pua ahi ding ua, amah tumin a pawt khe ding.
5. Sikhain teltaka, Ka pu ka it, ka ji, leh ka tate ka it; nuamlenin ka pawt khe kei ding, a chileh:
6. Huchiin a puin vaihawmte kiangah amah a pi dia; kongkhak chiangah hiam kongkhak khuam chiangah hiam a pi ding; huan a puin vuhnain a bil a vut pailet ding; huchiin aman a pu na a sem tawntung ding.
7. Huan pasalin a tanu sikhanu dingin juak leh, sikhapate bangin a pai khe keiding.
8. Amah khampa a pu a lungkimsak keileh, a tansak ding hi: a lakah kheltat takin a hihta chih theiin, melmak nam kianga juak dingin thuneihna a neikei.
9. Huan a tapa dinga a kham leh, tanute bawl dandanin amah a bawl ding hi.
10. Zi dang a neihsak leh; a annek, a puansilh, leh a kitenna man a sukniam louh ding.
11. Huan a tunga hiai thumte a hih keileh, sum leh pai louin amah nuamlenin, a pawt khe ding hi.
12. A sihna dinga, mi vua peuhmah, sih sak ngeingei ding ahi.
13. Huchiin min buk kei henla, Pathianin amah a khut ah tankhe zota leh, huchi a hihin a taina ding mun ka honseh sak ding hi.
14. Ahihhangin zekhema amah hihlum dingin, mi a insakinkhang tungah maingal takin honggamta leh; a sihna dingin, amah ka maitam akipanin na la ding hi.
15. Huan kuapeuhmahin a pa hiam, a nu hiam vua leh, sihsak ngeingei ding ahi.
16. Huan kuapeuhin mi guin, juak leh, a hihkeileh a khuta muh khiakin om leh, amah sihsak ngeingei ding ahi.
17. Huan kuapeuhin a pa hiam, a nu hiam hamsiatlohta leh sihsak ngeingei ding ahi.
18. Huan mite kisual uhenla, khatin khat suangin deng hiam, a khuttum hiamin tum henla, si louin, lupna ngak ta leh;
19. Thou nawnin, chiangkhun khunin kholak vak leh, a vawpa a hul dia; a hun sukbei man a pe dinga, amah a dam siang sak sipsip ding hi.
20. Min a sikhapa hiam, a sikhanu hiam chiangin vua henla, a khut lumin sileh: amah gawt ngeingei ding ahi.
21. Ahihhang in nikhat hiam ni nih hiam apaisuak leh, gawt louh ding ahi: amah sum leh pai ahih jiakin
22. Mite kisualin, numei naunei hihnain, amaha kipanin a gah paihkhiain, a hihhangin siatnain jui kei leh: numeinu pasalin a tunga a chihbangbanga amah gawt ngeingei ding; vaihawmten a lep bang ua a liau ding ahi.
23. Huan siatna mawngmawngin juita leh, huchiin hin tanga hin a piak ding ahi.
24. Mit tanga mit, ha tanga ha, khut tanga khut, khe tanga khe,
25. Hal tanga, hal, liam tanga liam, pawn tanga pawn.
26. Min a sikha mit hiam, a sikha nu mit hiam suk sakin, seta leh: a mit jiakin nuamlenin a khah ding ahi.
27. Huan a sikhapa ha hiam, a sikhanu ha hiam sukhe taleh; a ha jiakin nuamlenin amah a khah ding ahi.
28. Bawngtalin pasal hiam numei hiam siin, si leh uh: huchiin bawngtal denlup ngeingei ding ahi, huan a sa nek louh hial ding: ahihhangin a bawngtal neipen a hul ding.
29. Ahihhangin huai bawngtal a malamin a kia phut hat na hitaleh, a neipen kianga hilhsa hiin, amah khumta keileh, himahleh pasal hiam numei hiam na thatta leh; bawngtal den lup ding ahia, a neipen leng sihsak ding ahi.
30. A tungah dangka sum kikoih leh, huan a tunga kikoih peuhpeuh a hinna tatna man din a pe ding.
31. Tapa hiam, tanu hiam na nasita leh, hiai vaihawmna bangin a tunga hih ding ahi.
32. Bawngtalin sikhapa hiam sikhanu hiam phu leh; a pu uh kiangah dangka sekel sawmthum a piak dinga, bawngtal a denlup ding uh ahi.
33. Pasalin kokhuk bawl henla, a hih keileh pasalin kokhuk tou henla, sin kei henla, huaiah bawngtal hiam sabengtung hiam ke leh,
34. Kokhuk neipenin a bawl hoiha, amau neipa kianga dangka sum a piak ding; huchiin a gan si amaha ahi ding.
35. Mi khat bawngtalin midanga hihna henla, si leh; a bawngtal hinglai pen a juak ding ua, a sum a kihawm ding uh; huan a bawngtal sipen leng a kihawm ding uhi.Ahihkeileh a malamin huai bawngtal phut hat ahi chih a kitheiha a neiin a khum kei chih a kitheih leh; bawngtal tanga bawngtal a piak ngeingei ding; huchiin a gan si amah tuam a hih ding ahi.
36. Ahihkeileh a malamin huai bawngtal phut hat ahi chih a kitheiha a neiin a khum kei chih a kitheih leh; bawngtal tanga bawngtal a piak ngeingei ding; huchiin a gan si amah tuam a hih ding ahi.
