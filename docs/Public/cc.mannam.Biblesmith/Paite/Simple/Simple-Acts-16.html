1. Huan, Darbi, khua leh Listra khua ah leng a hoh a; huaiah nungjui khat a min Timothi a oma; amah tuh gingta sam Juda numei tapa ahi, a pa bel Grik ahi a;
2. Listra leh Ikoniam khua a unau omten amah a phat mahmah uhi.
3. Paula in amah tuh a kianga hoh sam dingin a deiha; huchiin huailama Juda omte jiak in a zek a sumsaka, a pa Grik ahi chih a thei vek ngal ua.
4. Huan, kho tenga a paikawm un Jerusalem khua a sawltak leh upa omte vaihawm jui dingin unaute kiangah a pe jel uhi.
5. Huchiin, ginna ah saptuam pawlte a kipta ua, nitengin a khang deuhdeuh uhi.
6. Huan, Kha Siangthouin Asia gama thugen a kham jiakin Phrigia leh Galatia gam a tawn suakta ua.
7. Huan, Missia gam kiang a tun un Bithinia gama lut a tum ua; himahleh Jesu Khain a phal kei hi.
8. Huchiin, Missia gam a kheng ua, Troasi khua ah a hoh sukta uhi.
9. Huan, janin Paulain mengmu bangin Masidonia gama mi khat dinga, Masidonia gamah hongkai inla, honghuh in, chia thumthumin a mu a.
10. Huan, huai mengmuhna a neih khit takin Masidonia gamah hoh ka tum pah ua, a kiang ua Tanchin Hoih gen dingin Pathianin a honsam ahi chih kichian taka theiin.
11. Huan, Troasi khua akipan longin ka pawt ua, tang takin Samothrasi khua ah ka pai ua, a jingin Neapoli khua ah ka pai nawn ua;
12. huan, huai akipanin Phillippi khua ah ka pai ua, huai khua tuh Masidonia gam a khua khat Rome khua a mi pemte khua ahia, huai uk a kho poimoh pen ahi. Huai khua ni tam sim ka tamta ua.
13. Huan, Khawlniin kulh kongkhak polam ah luigei ah, thumna mun hia ka gintak uah ka hoh ua; huan, ka tu ua, numei hongpai khawmte kiangah thu ka gen uhi.
14. Huan, a min Lidia, puansan dup juak, Thiatira khua te, Pathian bia numei khatin ka thu a ja a; huan, Paula thugen jui dingin Toupan a lungtang a hihvaksakta hi.
15. Huan, amah leh a inkuanpihten baptisma a tan khit un, Toupa a ginoma non seh uleh, ka in ah hongtung un, chiin, a honngen ngen a, a honkhouta a.
16. Huan, thumna muna ka hoh lai un, nungak kuahiam dawi aisansiam jawlin a honkituahpih a, aman tuh a aisan jiakin a pute sum tampi a muhsak jela.
17. Huai miin Paula leh kou a honjuia, hiai mite, hotdamna lampi nou honhilhte, Tungnungpen Pathian sikha ahi uh, chiin, a kikou a.
18. Huchi bangin ni tampi a hih a. Huchiin, Paula a lungkimta keia, a kiheia, dawi kiangah, Jesu Kris minin a sunga kipan pawt thu ka honpia, a chi a. Huan, huchih laitakin, a pawt pah a.
19. Huan, a puten a summuhna uh a mang chih a theih tak un, Paula leh Sila a man ua, heutute maah dawl munah a kailut uhi.
20. Huchin, vaihawmte kiangah a pi ua, hiai mite Judate ahi uh, ka khua uh a hihbuai mahmah ua;
21. Dan bang hiam ka kipahpih louh uh leh zuih siangloute a gengen uh, Rome khote ka hi ngal ua, a chi ua.
22. Huchiin, mipite leng sawltakte sual dingin a hongkisa sam ua, vaihawmten a puan khawng uh a sahkeksak ua, chianga sat dingin thu a pia uhi.
23. Huan, tamvei pi a sat khit un suangkulh ah a khum ua, a vengpa kiangah bittaka ven ding a chi bikbek uhi.
24. Huchitela thu a piak jiak un aman tuh tanna sungnungjaw ah a khuma, a khe uh singkol a bunsak hi.
25. Himahleh, jan lain, Pathian kiangah Paula leh Sila a thum ua, la a sa ua, hentaten a ngaikhia ua;
26. huan, thakhat in jin nakpitakin a honglinga, huchiin, suangkulh suangphumte tannin a ling hi; huan, kongkhak tengteng a kihong pah a, mi chih kolbutte a hongkiphel vekta hi.
27. Huan, a vengpa ihmu a honghalha, suangkulh kongkhakte tuh kihongin a mu a, hentate tuh taimang uh a sa a, a temsau a doka, a kihihlum dek tak hi.
28. Himahleh Paula aw ngaihtakin a kikou a, bangmah kichih ken, ka vek un hiaiah ka om lai ve ua oi, a chi a.
29. Huan, a vengpan a khawnvakte a kana, a tawmluta, laua ling kawmin Paula leh Sila maah a khupboh a;
30. huan, amau a pi khia a, Pute aw, hotdama om dingin bang ahia ka hih ding? a chi a.
31. Huan amau, Toupa Jesu ging inla, huchiin hotdamin na om ding, nang leh na inkuanpihte toh, a chi ua.
32. Huan, a kiangah leh a ina om tengtengte kiangah Toupa thu a gen uhi.
33. Huan, huai janin, huailai tak mahmahin a pia, a satna golhte uh a sil sianga; huan, amah leh a inkuanpihte tengtengin baptisma a tang pah ngal uhi.
34. Huan, a in ah a pitou a nekding a luia, huan, Pathian a gintak jiak un, amah, a inkuanpihte tengteng toh, a kipak mahmah uhi.
35. Huan, ni a suahin, vaihawmten, huai mite khahin, chiin, mi manmite a sawl ua.
36. Huan, suangkulh vengpan, Paula kiangah a thu uh a hilha, Vaihawmten, nou khah dingin, mi a honsawl uhi; huaijiakin tunah hongpawt unla, khamuangtakin pai un, a chi a.
37. Himahleh, Paulain, a kiang uah, Rome khote ka hi ngal ua, ka siamna uleh siamlouhna uh theilouin vantang mitmuhin non sat ua, suangkulh ah na honkhum ua; tunah aguka hondelhkhe dek na hi vial u maw? ahi kei hial ding; amau mahmah hong uhenla hongpikhia uheh, a chi a.
38. Huchiin mi manmiten huai thute tuh vaihawmte kiangah a hilh ua. Huan, Rome khua a mi ahi uh chih a jak un a lauta uhi;
39. huan, a vahoh ua, a khemkhem ua, a pi khia ua, khua akipan paimang dingin a ngen uhi.Huan, suangkulh akipan a pawt ua, Lidia in ah a lut ua, a khamuan ua, a pawtta uhi.
40. Huan, suangkulh akipan a pawt ua, Lidia in ah a lut ua, a khamuan ua, a pawtta uhi.
