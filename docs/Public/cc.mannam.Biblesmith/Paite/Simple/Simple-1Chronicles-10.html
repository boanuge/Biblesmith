1. Huan Philistia miten Israel a dou ua; huchiin Israel mite Philistia mite maah a taikek ua, Gilboa mual ah thah pukin a om uhi.
2. Huan Philistia miten Saul leh a tapate a delh vengveng ua; huan Philistia miten Jonathan, Abinadab leh Malki-sua, Saul tapate a that uhi.
3. Huan kidouna Saul adingin a haksa mahmahtaa, thalkapmiten amah a phata uhi; huan thalkapmite jiakin a lungkham mahmah hi.
4. Huchihlaiin Saulin galvanpopa kiangah, Na namsau dok khia inla, huaiin hondawt pailetin; huchilouinjaw hiai zeksumlouhte hongpaiin bawl louhlouhin a hon bawl kha ding uh, a chi a. Himahleh a galvanpopa a ut kei; a lau mahmah ngala. Huajiakin Saulin a namsau a laa, kisatnan a neita hi.
5. Huan a galvanpopan Saul a sita chih a theihin, huaibangmahin aman leng a namsau kisatnain a zanga, a sita hi.
6. Huchiin Saul a sitaa, a tapa thumte; huan a inkote tengteng leng a si khawmvek uhi.
7. Huan guama om Israel mite tengtengin Sepaihte a taikek uh leh Saul leh a tapate a si uh chih a muh un a khopite uh a nusia ua, a taikekta uhi; huan Philistia mite a hong ua huaite ah a tengta uhi.
8. Huan a jingsangin hichi ahi a, thahsate suah dinga Philistia mite a hongpai lai un, Gilboa mual ah Saul leh a tapate a puk uh chih a mu uhi.
9. Huan amah a sual ua, a lutang a la ua, a galvan leng, a milimte uh kiang leh mite kiangah tanchin puak dingin Philistia mite gamsungah a khak kimvel vek uhi.
10. Huchiin a galvan a pathiante uh in ah a koih ua, Dagon in ah a lutang a tak uhi.
11. Huan Jabesh-gilead tengtengin Saul tunga Philistia mite thilhih tengteng a jak un,
12. Mi hangsante tengteng a thou ua, Saul luang leh, a tapate luang a vala mang ua, Jabesh ah a honpua ua, Jabesha toso sing nuaiah a guhte uh a vui ua, ni sagih an a ngawl uhi.
13. Huchiin TOUPA tunga tatlekna a hiha, TOUPA thu a zuih louh jiakin, Saul a sitaa; dawi-jawl aisansiam thu a dotjiakin,Huaia thukana, TOUPA akipan thu a kan louh jiakin: huaijiakin amah a thata, gam tuh Jesai tapa David lamah a peta hi.
14. Huaia thukana, TOUPA akipan thu a kan louh jiakin: huaijiakin amah a thata, gam tuh Jesai tapa David lamah a peta hi.
