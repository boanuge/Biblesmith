1. Huan, Jakob bel a pai jela, huan, Pathian angelten a na dawn uh.
2. Huan, Jakobin amau a muhin, hiaite Pathian pawlte ahi uh, achia: huchiin huai mun minin Mahanaim a chi hi.
3. Huan, Jakob in, Seir gam a, Edom pawn lamah, a u Esau kiangah a maah thangkoute a paisaka.
4. A kianguah, ka toupa Esau a kiangah hichiin na gen ding uh, na sikha Jakobin hichiin a chi hi: Laban kiangah mikhual ka hia, tu tanin ka om nilouha:
5. Bawngtalte, sabengtung leh belamte, sikhapa leh sikhanute khawng ka neia; hichiin na hehpihna ka muh theihna dingin ka toupa hilh dingin mi ka honsawl ahi, a chi a, chiin, amau thu a pia hi.
6. Huan, thangkoute Jakob kiangah a hong kik nawn ua, Na u Esau kiang ka va tung ua, huan, mi za li honpiin hongdawn dingin a hongpai hi, chiin, a gen ua.
7. Huchiin, Jakobin a lau mahmah a, a lungkhamta a: huchiin a kianga mi omte, belam honte, bawng honte, sangawngsaute pawl nihin a khen a;
8. Esau pawl khat a hongpaia, a thah leh, pawl khat om lai a tai mang thei ding, a
9. Huan, Jakob mahin, aw Toupa, na gam lamah leh na tanute kiangah pai nawnta in, huchiin ka honhih hoih ding, honchipa:
10. Na sikha tunga hehpihna tengteng na muhsak leh thutak tengteng na muhsak laka, a neupen leng mu tuak ka hi kei hi; hiai Jordan lui ka chiang kia toh ka kana; tuin jaw pawl nih hial ka honghita hi.
11. Hehpihtakin ka u khut akipan, Esau khut akipan, hon humbit in; numei naupang takna honthah lah ka lau ngala.
12. Nang, ka hon hihhoih ngeingei dinga, na suante tuipi a piaunel sim zohlouh tan hiala tam bangin ka bawl ding na chi a, a chi a.
13. Huan, huai jan in huailaiah a giakta a; huan, a neih laka kipan a u Esau adingin kipahman a lakhia;
14. Kelpi zanih, keltal sawmnih, belampi zanih leh belampa sawmnih,
15. Sangawngsau zatlai sawmthum a noute utoh, bawngpi sawmli leh bawngtal sawm, sabengtungpi sawm nih leh sabengtung nou sawm.
16. Huan, huaite pawl chih a tuam chiatin a sikhate khut ah a kemsaka; huan, a sikhate kiangah, ka maah pai galkah unla, pawl chih kal nei sak jel un, a chi a.
17. Huan, a makai pa kiangah, ka u Esauin hontuaha, na kianga, kua na hia? kaw hoh dek na hia? na maa omte kuaa ahi ua? chia a hondot leh,
18. Na sikha Jakob a ahi uh; ka pu Esau, nanga dia kipahman dinga honkhak ahi: ngaiin, amah hua ka nung uah a om, chiin, thu a pia hi.
19. Huan, a zom leh a zom nawn leh, gan hon hawlte tengteng kiangah leng, Esau na muh hun chiang un huchibang mahin na gen ding uh ahi.
20. Huan, ngaiin, na sikha hua ka nung uah a om, chi chiat un, chiin thu a pe chiat hi. Kipahman ka maah pai leh a lung ka damsak ding a, huai nungin a mel ka mu ding; huchiin, a honna kipahpih kha ding, a chi ahi.
21. Huchiin, kipahman a maah a pai galkah saka; amah bel, huai janin a pawlte lakah a giak hi.
22. Huan, janin a thoua, a ji nih leh, a sikhanu nihte leh a ta sawm leh khat te a pi a, Jabbok lui ngahna ah a pita.
23. Huchiin, a mau a pi a, lui a kan ua, a neih tengteng leng a kansak vek hi.
24. Huan, Jakob amah kia in a omta; huan, mi khat toh phalvak tan a kibuan buan
25. Huan, a zou kei chih a kitheihin a pheipi guhtuah a khoihsak a, huchiin a kibuan lai un Jakob pheipi guhtuah a kilawita hi.
26. Huan, aman, hon khah in, phalvak ding a hita, a chi a. Huan, aman, na hon vualjawl keileh ka hon khah kei ding, achia.
27. Huan, aman, a kiangah, na min bang ahia? Achia. Huan, aman, Jakob a chi a
28. Huan, aman, na min Jakob ahi nawn kei ding, Israel a honchi zota ding uh; Pathian leh mihing te na suala, na zouta ngala, achia.
29. Huan, Jakobin, amah a dawnga, hehpihtakin na min honhilh in, a chi a. Huan, aman, bangjiakin ahia ka min non dot? Achia. Huan, huailaiah a vualjawlta.
30. Huan, Jakobin huailai mun ding in Peniel a chi a: Pathian a ma chin mahmah ah ka mu a, ka hinna hon hawisak jiakin a chi a.
31. Huan, Peniel a khen laiin a ni a hongsuak a, amah a hon suna, a pheipi jiakin a bai ta hi.Huaijiakin tutanin Israel suanten pheipi guhtuah a sadual om a ne ngei kei uh: Jakob pheipi guhtuah a sadual lai tak a khoih jiakin.
32. Huaijiakin tutanin Israel suanten pheipi guhtuah a sadual om a ne ngei kei uh: Jakob pheipi guhtuah a sadual lai tak a khoih jiakin.
