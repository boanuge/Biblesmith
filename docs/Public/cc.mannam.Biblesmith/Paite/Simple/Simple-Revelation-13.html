1. Huan tuipa piaunel tunga dingin, tuipa akipan gamsa khat, ki sawm leh lu sagih neia, a a kite tunga lallukhu sawm nei, a lu tungtea Pathian gensiatna om, hong paikhe tou tuh ka muta hi.
2. Huan ka gamsa muh tuh gialbem a banga, a khe tuh vompi khe a banga, huan a kam tuh humpinelkai kam a banga: huan dragonin a thuneihna thupi leh a laltutphah leh a thilhihtheihna amah a peta hi.
3. Huan a lute laka khat tuh sihpih khop hial dia liam hileh kilawm in ka mu a; huan sihpih khop hial dia liam tuh hihdamin a omta a; huan leitung pumpiin huai gamsa tuh lamdang a sa ua, a jui uhi;
4. huan dragon tuh a beta ua, gamsa kianga a thuneihna a piak jiakin; huchiin gamsa tuh a beta ua. Gamsa toh kibang kua ahia? Huan amah kidou pih thei kua ahia? chiin.
5. Huan amah kiangah tuh thil thupite leh Pathian gensiatnate genna ding kam piakin a omta a; huan amah kiangah thuneihna piakin a oma, kha sawmli leh kha nih gamtang jel dingin.
6. Pathian gensiatna ding, a min gense ding leh a omna, huai vana tengte ngei gensiain akamkata hi.
7. Huan misiangthoute kidou pih ding leh amau zou dingin amah kiangah tuh phalna piakin a omta a: huan chi tengteng leh mite leh paute leh namte tunga thuneihna a kiangah piakin a omta hi.
8. Huan leitunga teng tengtengin amah a be ding ua, khovel om tunga kipata belamnou thahsa hinna laibua a min ua gelh louha om tengtengin.
9. Kuapeuh bil neiin, ja hen:
10. Kuapeuh sal pi tuh sala piin a om ding; kuapeuh namsaua that tuh namsaua thahin a om ding. Hiaiah misiangthoute kuhkalna leh ginna neidinga chialna a om hi.
11. Huan gamsa ding leitunga kipata hongpawt khe tou ka muta a; huan belamnou bangin ki nih a neia, dragon bangin thu a gen hi.
12. Huan gamsa masapen thuneihna tengteng a mitmuhin a nei a. Huan leitung leh a sunga teng tengteng tuh gamsa masapen si khop hial dia liama hihdama om tuh be dingin a bawl hi.
13. Huan mite mitmuhin van akipanin leitung mei bang ke suk thei khop dingin thillamdang pipi a hiha.
14. Huan gamsa mitmuha thillamdang hihtheihna piaka a omnain aman tuh leitunga tengte a khemta a; huai gamsa temsaua sat liam leh dam nawn lim bawl dingin amau, huai leia tengte a hilhta hi.
15. Huan gamsa lim tuh a pau theihna ding leh, gamsa lim be nuam lou tengteng leng a thah sak theina dingin gamsa lim kiangah tuh nakna piak ahi.
16. Huan mi tengteng, a lian a neu, a jawng a pha, sikha leh sikha lou, a khut taklam uah hiam, a tal uah hiam chiamtehna tuh piakin a om sak chiata;
17. Huchia kuamahin huai chiamtehna hiam, gamsa min ngei hiam, a hih keileh a nambat hiam a neih keileh lei theilou, juak theilou a a omna dingin.Hiai ah pilna a kilang, kuapeuh theihsiamna neiin, gamsa nambat sim hen; mihing nambat ahi ngala; huan a nambat tuh zaguk leh sawmguk leh guk ahi.
18. Hiai ah pilna a kilang, kuapeuh theihsiamna neiin, gamsa nambat sim hen; mihing nambat ahi ngala; huan a nambat tuh zaguk leh sawmguk leh guk ahi.
