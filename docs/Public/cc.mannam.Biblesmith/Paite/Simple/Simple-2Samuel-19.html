1. Huchi in Joab a hilh ua, Ngai in, kumpipa'n Absalom a sun ve, a chi ua.
2. Huchi in huai ni a a zohna uh mi tengteng a ding in sunna a hong hi zota hi: Kumpipa a tapa ziak in a lungkham mahmah, a chih uh huai ni in mipite'n lah a na zata ngal ua.
3. Huan, mipite bel kidouna a mi taimang a zum ziak ua a guk a paimang bang in huai ni in a guk in khopi ah a lut uhi.
4. Huan, kumpipa'n a mai a hum a, Aw ka tapa Absalom, aw Absalom, Ka tapa, ka tapa! Chi in kumpipa a kap huphup a.
5. Huan, Joab in ah kumpipa kiang ah a lut a, Na hinna te, na tapate leh na tanute hinna leh na zite leh na meite hinna humbitmite tuni in zum tak in na bawlta ve;
6. Nang hon hawte na it a, nang hon itte na mudah a. Heutute leh sepaihte nang a ding a bangmah hilou bang in tuni in na hihlangta a: Absalom pen dam in, kou dang tengteng tuni in si zo vek le ung, huai tuh na kipahlam tak hi leh kilawm in tuni in ka thei ahi.
7. Huaiziak in thou inla, pawt khe mai in, na mite lungkimsak lam sang in thu gen in: na pawt khiak kei leh TOUPA lou in ka kichiam ahi, mi khat lel leng tu zan in na kiang a om a omkei ding uh: huan, huai lah nang a ding in na tuailai a kipan tutan a na tung a thil hoihlou hongtung tengteng sang tham in a hoihkei zo ding, a chi a.
8. Huchi in kumpipa a thou a, kulh kongpi ah a tu a. Ngai un, kumpipa kulh kongpi ah a tu hi, chi in mipi tengteng a hilh ua: huan, mipi tengteng kumpipa ma ah a vahoh uh. Israelte bel amau puan in chiat uah a taikek uh.
9. Kumpipa'n i melmate uh khut a kipan hon honkhia a, Philistiate khut a kipan hon hondamta; huan, tu in hiai gam a kipan in Absalom a taikhiaksan.
10. Huan, Absalom i kumpipa ding a sathau i nilhpa kidouna ah a sita ngal a, Bang achia ahia kumpipa kamkhat bek sap vai na hawmlouh uh? Chi in Israel mite tengteng a kisel tualtual uh.
11. Huchi in kumpipa'n siampu Zadok leh Abiathar kiang ah, Juda upate kaing ah, Bangdia kumpipa a in lam a pikik thu a a zekaipen a pang na hi ua? Israel mite'n kumpipa pikik thu a gen ulah kumpipa kiang a tungta ngal a.
12. Ka unaute na hi ua, ka guh leh ka sa lah na hi ngal ua, bang achia a zekaipen a pang peuhmah na hi ua? Chi in va gen un.
13. Huan, Amasa kiang ah, Ka guh leh ka sa na hi ka hia? Joab sik a ka ma a sepaihte heutu dia ka hon bawllouh a ka hon bawlkei leh, Pathian in a loh loh hon loh mai henla, huai sang a thupi zaw in leng, a chi hi, va chi in, chi in mi a sawl a.
14. Huchi in Juda mite tengteng lungsim mi khat lungsim bang mai in a awnsakta a; huchi in kumpipa kiang a, Na mite tengteng toh hongkik nawn un, chi ding in mi a sawl uh.
15. Huchi in kumpipa a pai nawn a, Jordan a vatung a. Huan, Judate kumpipa na dawn a, kumpipa Jordan a na pi galkah ding in Gilgal ah a hoh uh.
16. Huan, Hahurim khua a mi Benjamin mi Ger tapa Simei leng kimtak in kumpipa dawn ding in Judate mah toh kiton in a pawt khia.
17. Huan, a kiang ah Benjamin mi sangkhat toh, Saula inkote sikha Sib, a tapa sawm leh nga leh a sikha sawmnih toh a tel ua: huan, kumpipa mitmuh in Jordan a galkahta uhi.
18. Huan, kumpipa inkote pi galkahna ding leh hoih a sak bangbang a a hih ding in long a paipih ua. Huan, Jordan lui a galkah leh Ger tapa Simei kumpipa ma ah a khupboh.
19. Huan, kumpipa kiang ah, Ka pu in thulimlouhna nei in hon sepkei teitei leh, toupa ka pu in Jerusalem a pawtsan ni a na sikha in diklou tak a a na hihte leng thei gige samkei leh, kumpipa'n lungsim ah paikei leh.
20. Na sikha in thil ka hihkhial chih ka kithei a; huaiziak in, ngai in, Joseph inkote tengteng lak a ka pu toupa dawn ding a tuni a hong pawt masapen leng ka hi hi, a chi a.
21. Himahleh Zerui tapa Abisai in a dawng a, TOUPA sathau nilhpa a hamsiat ziak in Simei pen hihlup ding ahi kei dia hia? a chi a.
22. Huan, David in, Nou Zerui tapate aw, noute hon na theihsak ding uh bang ahia om? Tuni in kei hon doute na hi kei umaw? Tuni a Israel lak a mi mawngmawng hihlup ding ahi hia? Tuni in Israel mite tung a kumpipa ka hih a theilou ka hi maw? a chi a.
23. Huan, kumpipa'n Simei kiang ah, Na si hetkei ding, a chi a. Kumpipa a kiang ah a kichiamta hi.
24. Huan, Saula tapa Mephiboseth kumpipa dawn ding in a hong khia a; kumpipa a pawt khiak ni a kipan a galmuang a hong pai nawn ni tan in a khete a zunkei a, a khamul a sumkei a, a puan leng a sawp samkei hi.
25. Huan, hichi ahi a, kumpipa dawn ding a Jerusalem a hoh kumpipa'n a kiang ah, Mephiboseth, Bangdia ka kiang a hong sam lou? A chi a.
26. Huan, aman a dawng a, Aw toupa, ka pu, ka sikha in hon khem a: na sikha in, Kumpipa kiang a hoh ding in ka tuanna ding in sabengtung ka van ding, ka chi a; na sikha lah a khe a bai ngal a.
27. Huan, kumpipa ka pu kiang ah na sikha a hek a; himahleh toupa ka pu Pathian angel bang ahi a; huaiziak in hoih na sak bangbang in hih in.
28. Ka pa inkote tengteng toupa ka pu ma a hihlum vek dia kilawm ahi ngut ua; himahleh na sikha na an umpihte lak ah na pangsak a. Huaiziak in kumpipa kiang ah thil dang phut ngam lai dia kilawm ka hi maw? a chi a.
29. Huan, kumpipa'n a kiang ah, Na tungtang thu bang dia gen nawn nak na hia? Nang leh Zib gam kihawm un ka chi hi, a chi a.
30. Huan, Mephiboseth in kumpipa kiang ah, Eh, aman tang vek heh, toupa ka pu lah amah in ah galmuang in a hongta ngal a, a chi a.
31. Huan, Gilead mi Berzilai Rogelim a kipan in a hong suk a; kumpipa Jordan a pi galkah ding in a kiang ah Jordan a kan hi.
32. Barzilai bel upa pi ahi a, kum sawmgiat a upa ahi a: Mahanaim a kumpipa om sung in nek ding a na tuah sak a; mi neizou tak ahi.
33. Huan, kumpipa'n Berzilai kiang ah, ka kiang ah hong inla, ka kiang ah Jerusalem ah ka hon vak ding, a chi a.
34. Huan, Barzilai in kumpipa kiang ah, Ni bangzah kum bangzah damlai ding ka hi a, Jerusalem a kumpipa kiang a ka hoh touh tei na ding?
35. Tu in kum sawmgiat ka hia; a sia leh a pha leng khen ka hia hia? Na sikha in ka thil nek leh dawnte a lim leh limlou leng thei thei ka hi nawn ahia? Pasal leh numeite lasak ging leng ka za thei nawn ahia? Huaiziak in na sikha bang ding in toupa ka pu ding a puakgik ka hih maizen ding?
36. Na sikha in kumpipa kiang a Jordan galkah tei lel ka tup ahi; bang ding in kumpipa'n huchi bang in hoih man a hon thuk chitchiat dia?
37. Hehpihtak in na sikha zaw hon pai nawnsak mai in ka hon ngen hi, ka nu leh ka pa han omna koumau khua ngei ah ka si ding. Ahihhang in en in, na sikha Chimham; TOUPA ka pu kiang ah galkah henla, a tungah hoih na sak bangbang in hih in, a chi a.
38. Huan, kumpipa'n a dawng a, Chimham ka kiang ah a galkah sam ding a, hoih na sak bang zel in a tungah ka hih ding: huan, thil bang peuh na hon nget peuhmah ka hon hihsak ding, a chi a.
39. Huan, mipi tengteng Jordan gal a kah ua, huan, kumpipa leng a galkah a; huan, kumpipa'n Barzilai a tawp a, vual a zawl a: huan, amah mun lam ah a pai nawnta hi.
40. Huchi in kumpipa Gilgal ah a galkah a, Chimham a kiang ah a galkah sam hi. Huan, Juda mi tengteng in kumpipa a pi galkah ua, Israel mi leng a kimkhat a pang uh.
41. Huan, ngai in, Israel mi tengteng kumpipa kiang ah a hong ua, kumpipa kiang ah, Bang achia ka unau uh Juda mite'n nang hon gukhia a, kumpipa leh a inkote leh David mi tengteng leng Jordan gal a a pi khawm vek mawk uh? A chi ua.
42. Huan, Judami tengteng in Israel mite a dawng ua, Kumpipa ka kinaihpih uh a hih ziak ahi: bang dia hiai thu a heh mawk? Kumpipa neih muang a om ka hi mahmah ua maw? thil piak bang hiam te leng a hon nei ahia? a chi ua.Huan, Israel mite'n Juda mite a dawng ua, Kumpipa ah tan sawm ka nei ua, David ah noute sang in anglaitanna ka neizaw uh; bang dia hon ngaineu maw, kumpipa pi kik nawn sawm masate ka hikei uh maw? a chi ua. Huchi in Juda mite thu Israel mite thu sang in a gum zaw hi.
43. Huan, Israel mite'n Juda mite a dawng ua, Kumpipa ah tan sawm ka nei ua, David ah noute sang in anglaitanna ka neizaw uh; bang dia hon ngaineu maw, kumpipa pi kik nawn sawm masate ka hikei uh maw? a chi ua. Huchi in Juda mite thu Israel mite thu sang in a gum zaw hi.
