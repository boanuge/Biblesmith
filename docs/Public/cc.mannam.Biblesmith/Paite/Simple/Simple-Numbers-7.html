1. Huchia Mosiin biakbuk a lam khiata, thau a nilh khita, ahihsiangthou khita, a van tengteng leh a maitam leh a tuiumbelsuan tengteng thau nilha, ahihsiangthou niin hichi ahi a;
2. Israelte intekpen, a inkuan ua intekpenten thillat a lan ua, huaite bela nam ua intekpente, a simte uh tunga omte ahi uh:
3. A thillatte uh kangtalai tungnei guk leh bawngtal sawm leh nih, intekpen nihin kangtalai khat jel, khatin bawngtal khat jel TOUPA maah a hontawi ua, biakbuk bulah a honglan uhi.
4. Huan, TOUPAN Mosi houpiha,
5. Biakbuk nasep dingin a phatuam kha ding, nana la chiat un; Levite, a nasep uh dungjui bangjelin na pe chiat ding hi, chiin
6. Huchiin Mosiin kangtalaite leh bawngtalte a laa, Levite a pia,
7. Kangtalaite nih leh bawngtalte li Gerson tapate a nasep uh dungjui jelin a pia:
8. Huan, kangtalai li leh bawngtal giat, siampu Aron tapa Ithamar te Merari tapate a nasep uh dungjui jelin a pia,
9. Kohath tapate bel a pe tei kei; mun siangthou nasep amau tuam ahia, a zona zong sek ahi uh.
10. Intekpenten thaunilh niin maitam latna dinga a thillatte uh maitam bulah a lan uh.
11. Huan, TOUPAN Mosi kiangah, Intekpen chihin maitam latna dinga thillat, a thillat uh amau tan hi jel dingin lan ding uhi, a chi.
12. Ni khat nia a thillat a lan ding mi Juda chi Aminadab tapa Nason ahi:
13. A thillat dangkasik kuang khat, a gikdan sekel ja leh sawmthum, mun siangthoua sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dangkasik belhbu khat, tangbuang thillat dingin thau helsa tangbuang nel mahmaha dim tuaktuak;
14. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;
15. Halmang thillat dingin bawngtal tuai khat, belamtal khat, belamnou apa kum khata up khat;
16. Khelh thoihna dingin keltal khat;
17. Huan, lemna kithoih thillat dingin bawngtal nih, belamtal nih, belamtal nga, keltal nga, belamnou apa kum khata upa nga: hiaite Aminadab tap Nason thillatte ahi uh.
18. Ni nih niin Isakarte intekpenten Zuar tapa Nathanelin a lana:
19. A thillat dingin dangkasik kuang khat a gikdan sekel ja leh sawmthum, mun siangthoua sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dankasik belhbu khat, tangbuang thillat ding thau toh hel tangbuang nel mahmah dim tuaktuak;
20. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;
21. Halmang thillat dingin bawngtal tuai khat belamtal khat, belamnou apa kum khata upa khat;
22. Khelh thoihna dingin keltal khat;
23. Huan, lemna kithoihna thillat dingin bawngtal nih, belamtal nga, keltal nga, belamnou apa kum khata upa nga a lana. Hiai Zuar tapa Nathanel thillatte ahi.
24. Ni thum niin Zebulun tate intekpen Helon tapa Eliabin a lana:
25. A thillat dangkasik kuang khat, a gikdan sekel ja leh sawmthum, mun siangthoua sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dangkasik belhbu khat; tangbang thillat ding thau toh jel tangbuang nel mahmaha dim tuaktuak;
26. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;
27. Halmang thillat ding bawngtal tuai khat, belamtal khat, belamnou apa kum khata upa khat;
28. Khelh thoihna dingin keltal khat;
29. Huan, lemna kithoih thillat dingin bawngtal nih, belamtal nga, keltal nga, belamnou apa kum khata upa nga ahi. Hiaite Helon tapa Eliab thillatte ahi uh.
30. Ni li niin Reuben tate intekpen Sedeur tapa Elizurin a lana:
31. A thillat dangkasik kuang khat a gikdan sekel ja leh sawmthum, mun siangthoua sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dankasik belhbu khat, tangbuang thillat dinga thau hel tangbuang nel mahmaha dim tuaktuak;
32. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;
33. Halmang thillat ding bawngtal tuai khat, belamtal khat, belamnou apa kum khata up khat;
34. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat;
35. Huan, lemna kihthoihna thillat dingin bawngtal nih, belamtal nga, keltal nga, belamnou apa kum khata upa nga ahi. Hiaite Sedeur tapa Elizur thillatte ahi.
36. Ni nga niin Simeon tate intekpen Zurisadai tapa Selumielin a lana:
37. A thillat dangkasik kuang khat, a gikdan sekel ja leh sawmthum; mun siangthou sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dangkasik belhbu khat; tangbuang thillat dingin thau toh hel tangbuang nel mahmaha dim tuaktuak;
38. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;
39. Halmang thillat lat dingin bawngtal tuai khat, belamtal khat, belamnou apa kum khata upa khat;
40. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat;
41. Huan, lemna kithoih thillat ding bawngtal nih, belamtal nga, keltal nga, belamnou apa kum khata upa nga ahi; hiai Zurisadai tapa Selumiel thillatte ahi.
42. Ni guk niin Gad tate intekpen Deuel tapa Eliasaphin a lana:
43. A thillat dangkasik kuang khat, a gikdan sekel ja leh sawmthum, mun siangthoua sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dangkasik belhbu khat, tangbuang thillat ding thau toh hel tangbuang nel mahmah dim tuaktuak;
44. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;
45. Halmang thillat ding bawngtal tuai khat, belamtal khat, belamnou apa kum khata upa, khat;
46. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat;
47. Huan, lemna kithoih thillat ding bawngtal nih, belamtal nga, keltal nga, belamnou apa kum khata upa nga ahi. Hiaite Deuel tapa Eliasaph thillatte ahi.
48. Ni sagih niin Ephraim tate intekpen Amihud tapa Eliasamin a lana:
49. A thillat dangkasik kuang khat, a gikdan sekel ja leh sawmthum, khat, mun siangthoua sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dangkasik belhbu khat; halmang thillat ding thau toh hel tangbuang nel mahmah dim tuaktuak;
50. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;
51. Halmang thillat ding bawngtal tuai khat, belamtal khat, belamnou apa kum khata upa, khat;
52. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat;
53. Huan, lemna kithoih thillat ding bawngtal nih, belamtal nga, keltal nga, belamnou apa kum khata upa nga ahi: hiaite Amihud tapa Eliasam thillatte ahi.
54. Ni giat niin Manase tate intekpen Pendazur tapa Gamalielin a lana:
55. A thillat dangkasik kuang khat, a gikdan sekel ja leh sawnthum, mun siangthoua sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dangkasik belhbu khat; tangbuang thillat ding thau toh hel, tangbuang nel mahmah a dim tuaktuak;
56. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;
57. Halmang thillat ding bawngtal tuai khat, belamtal khat, belamnou apa kum khata upa khat;
58. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat;
59. Huan, lemna kihthoih thillat ding bawngtal nih, belamtal nga, keltal nga, belamnou apa kum khata upa nga ahi. Hiaite Pendazur tapa Gameliel thillatte ahi.
60. Ni kua niin Benjamin tate intekpen Gideoni tapa Abidanin a lana;
61. A thillat dangkasik kuang khat, a gikdan sekel ja leh sawmthum, mun siangthoua sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dangkasik belhbu khat, tangbuang thillat ding thau toh hel tangbuang nel mahmaha dim tuaktuak.
62. Sekel sawm toh kima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;
63. Halmang thillat ding bawngtal tuai khat, belamtal khat, belam nou a pa kum khat a upa;
64. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat;
65. Huan, lemna kithoihna thillat ding bawngtal nih, belamtal nga, keltal nga, belamnou apa kum khata upa nga ahi: hiaite Gideoni tapa Abidan thillat ahi.
66. Ni sawm niin Dan tate intekpen Amisadai tapa Ahiezerin a lana:

68. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;

72. Ni sawm leh ni khat niin Aser tate intekpen Okran tapa Pagielin a lana:
73. A thillat dangkasik kuang khat, a gikdan sekel sawmthum, khat, mun siangthoua sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dangkasik belhbu khat, tangbuang thillat ding thau toh hel tangbuang nel mahmaha dim tuaktuak;
74. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangka keu khat;
75. Halmang thillat ding bawngtal tuai khat, belamtal khat, belamnou apa kum khata upa khat;
76. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat;

78. Ni sawm leh ni nih niin Naphtali tate intekpen Enan tapa Ahirin a lana;
79. A thillat dangkasik kuang khat, a gikdan sekel ja leh sawmthum, khat, mun siangthoua sekel sawmsagih toh kikima gik dangkasik belhbu khat, tangbuang thillat ding thau toh hel tangbuang toh hel tangbuang nel mahmaha dim tuaktuak;
80. Sekel sawm toh kikima gik gimlima dim dangkaeng keu khat;
81. Halmang thillat ding bawngtal tuai khat, belamtal khat, belamnou apa kum khata upa khat;
82. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat;
83. Huan, lemna kithoih thillat ding bawngtal nih, belamtal nga, keltal nga, belamnou apa kum khata upa nga ahi: hiaite Enan tapa Ahir thillatte ahi.
84. Huan, maitam thau nilh nia Israel intekpente thillatte dangkasik kuang sawmlenih, dangkasik belhbu sawmlenih, dangkaeng keu sawmlenih ahi.
85. Dangkasik kuang sekel ja leh sawmthum toh kikima gik chiat ahia, belhbu sawmsagih chiat ahi: dangkasik tuiumbelsuan tengteng mun siangthoua sekel sang nih leh za li toh kikima gik ahi;
86. Gimlima dim dangkaeng keu sawm leh nih mun siangthoua sekel sawm toh kikima gik chiat ahi: dangkaeng keu tengteng sekel ja leh sawmnih toh kikima gik ahi:
87. Halmang thillat ding bawngtal tengteng sawm leh nih ahia, belamtal sawmlenih, belamnou apa kum khata upa sawm leh nih, a tangbuang thillatte uh toh; huan, khelhna thoihna ding keltal sawm leh nih:
88. Huan, lemna kithoihna thillat ding bel bawngtal sawmnih leh li ahia, belamtal bel sawmguk, keltal bel sawmguk, belamnou apa kum khata upa bel sawmguk ahi. Hiai thau nilh khita maitam latna dinga thilpiakte ahi.Huchiin biakbuka amah be dinga Mosi a lutin thukunna bawm tunga chitna tutphah tunglama cherub nihte kikal akipanin amah honhoupihin aw a ja a: huan a beta hi.
