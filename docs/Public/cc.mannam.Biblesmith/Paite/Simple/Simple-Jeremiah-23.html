1. I Gam tatna muna belam hihmangthanga dalhjaksak belamchingmite a tung uh a gik hi, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
2. Huaijiakin TOUPA Israelte Pathianin ka mite chingpa belamchingmite tungtang thu ah hichiin a chi: Ka gan honte na dalhsak ua, na delh mang ua, na veh kei uh, ngai un, na thilhih uh gilou jiakin nou ka hongveh sin ahi, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
3. Huan, ka gan hon om sunte ka delhjakna gam tengtenga kipan ka pi khawm nawn dinga, a huangsung uah ka hawl khawm dinga, gah nuam mahmah ahi ding ua, a pung ding uh.
4. Amau limsak ding amaua dingin belamchingmi ka koih dinga, a lau nawnta kei ding ua, a kuamahmah mang a om nawn sam kei ding uh, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
5. Ngaidih ua, David adin Hiang diktat ka suahsak hun ding a hong tung sina, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi; amah kumpipa in a tu dinga, pil takin a gamta dinga, gam pumpi ah dikna leh diktatna a tangtungsak ding.
6. A hun sungin Juda hotdam ahi dinga, Israel bit takin a om ding. Huai a min dinga a sak ding uh hiai ahi, TOUPA i diktatna uh ahi, chih.
7. Huchiin, ngaidih ua, a hun a tung sin ta, TOUPA thu pawtin a chi. Israel mite Aigupta gama kipana pikhepa TOUPA a hina a hin sung, chi nawn louin.
8. Mal lam gam leh a delhjakna gam tengteng akipan Israel inkote suante pikhiaa, pi kik nawnpa TOUPA a hina a hin sung, a chi zota ding uh. Huan, amau gam ngei ah a teng ding uh.
9. Jawlneite tungtang thu pen, ka sungah ka lungtang a puakkham hi, ka guh tengteng a linga, zu kham bang mai ka hi, juin a zelh zoh mi bang maiin, TOUPA jiak leh a thu siangthou jiakin.
10. Gam angkawm honin a dima, hamsia jiakin gamin a sun-a, gamdai lou hing munte a keu khinta; a lampi uh a giloua, a hatna uh a dik kei hi.
11. Jawlneite leh siamputen Pathian khawk a sa kei ua, ka in sung mahmah ah leng a khelhna uh ka mu.
12. Huaijiakin amau adingin a paina lampi uh, jan khomial lampi nal bang ahi dinga, huailaiah delhin a om ding ua a tolhpuk ding ua, a tunguah thilhoih lah ka tungsaksin ngala, amaute gawtna hun, TOUPA thu pawtin a chi.
13. Samari jawlneite tungah kihhuai ka mu, Baal louin thu a gen ua, Israelte a pi mang ua.
14. Jerusalem jawlneihon tungah leng thil mulkimhuai kamu, ang a kawm ua, juauin a gamta ua, thilgilou hihte khut a hatsak ua, kuamahin a gitlouh uh kisik a tup louh loh uhi; kei dingin Sodom khuaa mite bang a honghi vek ua, a kho sunga mite tengteng leng Gomorrah khua a mite bang hial.
15. Huaijiakin jawlneihon tungtang thu ah sepaihte TOUPAN hichiin a chi: Ngaidih ua, gamsaiin ka vak dinga, thil kha ka dawnsak dinga; Jerusalem a jawlneite kiang akipanin gam pumpi ah Pathian poisaklouhna lah a pawt ta ngala.
16. Sepaihte TOUPAN hichiin a chi: Jawlnei nakiang ua thugente thu ngaikhe kei un; bangmahlou a honsinsak ua. TOUPA kama kipan hilou amau lungsim geltawmte a gen sek uh.
17. TOUPA thu musitmite kiangah, Nang dingin a hoih ding, a chi vial ua; huan, amau deihlam engtata hih teiteimite kiangah, Thil hoihlou bangmah na tungah a hongtung kei ding, a chi uh.
18. TOUPA thugelna ah kua apang ngeia, a thu sanga thei ding kua a oma? ahihleh a thu pen kuan ahia limsaka ngaikhia om?
19. Ngaidih ua, TOUPA huihpi, hehna a pawtta, ahi, pingpei huihpi; mi giloute lutang a nuai ding.
20. TOUPA hehna a mawk kik kei ding, a lungsima a tup chinten a tangtungsaka, a hih khit phot masiah; ni nanung lamahte non theichian ding uh.
21. Jawlneite bel ka sawl louhpiin a tai ua, a kiang ua thu ka gen louhin leng thu a gen uh.
22. Ka thugelnaa om tei hi le ujaw, ka thu ka mite kianga phuang ding hi ve un, a thilhih diklou ua kipan lehngatsak ding hi ve un.
23. Gamla pia om Pathian hi louin, Pathian nai gige ka hi hia? chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
24. Mun kiselah ka muh theihlouh dia bu theimi a om sin ua hia? chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi. Lei leh vanah ka dim ahi kei maw? chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
25. Mang ka nei, mang ka nei, chia ka mina juau gen jawlneite thugen ka zata hi.
26. Jawlnei juau gen sek, amau lungtang kikhem tawma thu sekmite lungtang ah hiai bangtan ahia a om sin?
27. Amau bel a invengte uh kiangah a mang uh a hilh ua ka mite ka min mangngilh sak a tum ua, a pate un Baal jiaka ka min a mangngilh khit bang un,
28. Jawlnei mang neiin a mang gen hen, ka thu neimiin ka thu tak dik takin gen hin. Buhpawl leh buhtangin kibatna bang a nei ua? chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
29. A thu bel mei bang, sek suang seknenna bang ahi ka hia? chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
30. Huaijiakin, ngaiun, jawlnei hon, ka thu kiguksak tuah sekte ka dou ahi, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
31. Ngaidih ua, Jawlnei amah dangnal saka, TOUPAN a chi, chi sekte ka dou ahi, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
32. Ngaidih ua, mang juau leltak gena, hilha, a juau thute uh leh a kiotsakna thu ginaloute uh jiaka ka mite pimang jawlneite ka dou ahi, TOUPA thu pawtin a chi; ka sawl ngal keia, thu lah ka pe keia, hiai mipite a lohtak sak himhim kei ding uh, chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
33. Hiai mipite laka mi kuain hiam, jawlnei kua hiamin, siampu kuahiamin, TOUPA puakgik bang ahia? chia a hondot leh hichiin na dawng ding hi, Noumau mah puakgik pen na hi uh, ka honpai mang sin; chih TOUPA thu pawt ahi.
34. Jawlnei hiam, siampu hiam, mi kua hiam, TOUPA puakgik, chi a om uleh, huai mi leh a inkote ka gawt ding.
35. Huchiin mi kuapeuhin a insakinkhangpa kiangah leh kuapeuhin a unaupa kiangah, TOUPAN bangchiin a dawnga. TOUPAN bang a gena? chiin na kidong ding uh.
36. TOUPA puakgik na loukha nawn kei ding ua, mi kuapeuh amah mah thu gen lah puakgik ahi din ngala. Pathian hing, sepaihte TOUPA, i Pathian thu u lah na lehbulh khinta ngal ua.
37. Jawlnei kiangah, TOUPAN bangchiin nang a hondawnga? TOUPAN bang ahia a gen? na chi ding.
38. Huan, TOUPA puakgik, na chih peuhmah uleh, TOUPAN hichiin a chi: TOUPA puakgik na chi ding uh ahi kei, chia ka honsawla, TOUPA puakgik chia thu na gen jiakin;
39. Huchiin, ngaiun, ka honmangngilh keuhkeuh dinga, ka honpai mang dinga, noute leh hiai khopi na kiang uleh na pate uh kianga ka na piaksa toh.Huan, khantawn demsiatna leh zahlak nilouhna mangngilh vuallouh na tunguah ka hontun ding.
40. Huan, khantawn demsiatna leh zahlak nilouhna mangngilh vuallouh na tunguah ka hontun ding.
