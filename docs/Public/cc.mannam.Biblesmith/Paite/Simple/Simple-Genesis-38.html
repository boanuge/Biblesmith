1. Huchih laiin, hichi ahia, Juda bel a unaute kianga kipanin a pai suka, Adullam mi khat a min Hira a vebelta hi:
2. Huan, Juda in huailaiah Kanan mi khat tanu a min Suah a mua; huan, amah a kitenpih a, a kithuahpih ta.
3. Huchiin a gaia, tapa a neia; huan; Juda in amin dingin Er asa hi.
4. Huan, a gai nawna, tapa a nei nawna; huan, a nuin a min dingin Onan asa hi.
5. Huan, tapa a nei nawn jela, a min ding Sela asa a; amah a neihlaiin Juda Chezib khua ah a om hi.
6. Huan, Juda in a ta masa Er a dingin ji api saka, a min Tamar ahi.
7. Huan, Juda ta masa Er Toupa mitmuh in gilou ahia; huchiin, Toupan amah a si saka.
8. Huan, Juda in, Onan kiangah, na u ji kithuahpih inla kitenpih inla, na u a dingin chi khahsakin achia.
9. Huan, Onan in chi suah ding pen amah suan ahikei ding chih athei a, huchiin hichi ahia, a u ji a kithuahpih tengin a u a dinga chi a suahsak louh na dingin leiah a kesak jel hi.
10. Huan, a thil hihdan Toupa mitmuh in giloutak ahi: huchiin amah leng a sisak ta.
11. Huan, Juda in amou nu Tamar kiangah, ka tapa Sela a pichin mateng na pa in lamah pasal neilou in na om tadih in, achia. Huchilou in jaw amah leng a ute bangin a si kha ding hi, achi ahi. Huchiin, Tamar apa in lamah ava omte hi.
12. Huan, a nungin Suah tanu Juda ji a si a; huan, Juda in a ngaih a daita a, Timna khua ah a belam mulmet mi te kiang lamah amah leh a lawm Adullam mi Hira a hoh tou uh.
13. Huan, mi in, Tamar, na pu a belam mulmet dingin Timna khua ah a hohtou dek, chiin, a hilh ua.
14. Huchiin, aman a pasal sunna puansilh te a suah a, kikhuh na puanin a kikhuh a, a kituam bikbek a, Timna kho lampi ah Enaim kulh kongpi ah ana tu a; Sela lah a piching ta ngala, apan aji dingin amah api sak kei chih a thei hi.
15. Huan, Juda in amah amuh in kijuak hiinteh achia; amai lah akikhuh bikbek ngala.
16. Huchiin, lampi gei ah huai numei lam ava juan a, kisa in hehpihtakin I lumkhawm ding, achia: amou mah ahi chih lah athei ngal kei a. Huan, aman, hon luppih bang non pe dia? Achia.
17. Huan, aman, ganhon laka kipan kelnou ka hon khak ding, achia. Huan, aman huai na hon khak ma a kana let ding banghiam bek non pe diam? Achia.
18. Huan, aman, let ding bang ka hon pe dia? Achia. Huan, aman, na min sawnna leh na tau, na chiang tawi toh, achia. Huchiin, apia a, a luppih ta, huan, amah a kipan apaita hi.
19. Huan, a thou a, a kikhuhna puan a suah a, a meithaina puansilh te a silh non ta hi.
20. Huan, a thillet numei khut a kipana la dingin a lawmpa Adullam mi khut ah kelnou a khak a: himahleh aman ava muzou takei hi.
21. Huchiin, numei omna mun a mite kiangah, lampi gei a Enaima kijuak nu koi a om ahita a? Chiin a dong a. Huan, amau, kijuak mawngmawng hiai laiah a om kei hi, achi ua.
22. Huchiin, Juda kiangah a kik nawn a, amah ka muzou kei; amun a miten leng, kijuak mawngmawng hiai laiah a om kei, ana chih behlap uh, achia.
23. Huan, Juda in, neiden maileh huchilouin in jaw I zahlak kha ding; ngaiin, hiai kelnou khak hi inga, amah lah mu zoulou chin na, achia.
24. Huan, kha thum hiam tak nungin, min Juda, na mounu Tamar in tangval a ngai a; huailou leng, ngai in sawn a pai hi, chiin, ahilh ua. Huan, Juda in pi khia unla, hal lup in om heh, achia.
25. Huan, a pi khiak uleh, Tamar in, hiai thil te neipa lakah ahi nau ka pai, chiin, a pu kiangah mi a sawl ta a; amah mah in, hehpihtakin hiai min sawn na te, tau te, chiang te, kua a ahia, en chian dih ve, achia.
26. Huan, Juda in huai thilte amah a ahi chih ana thei a. Kei sang amah diktat jaw ahi; ka tapa Sela lah amah toh kana kiteng sak ta kei a, achia. Huan, Juda in amah a kithuahpih nawn hetkei.
27. Huan, nau a vei laiin hichi ahia, ngai in, a sul ah phik ana om ua.
28. Huan, hichi ahia, nau avei laiin khat in a khut a hon golh khia a: huchiin nau dom mi in, hiai a pawt masa jaw ahi, chiin ana len a, a khut ah khau san a bunsak hi.
29. Huan, huchi ahia, khut a laklut nawn laiin, ngaiin, a unau a hongpawt ta a: huchiin naudom miin, bang dia pawtna kibawl na hia? Achia, huaijiakin a min dingin Perez a sa uh.Huan, huai nungin a unau, huai nungin a unau, huai a khut a khau san bunpen, a hongpawt nawna: a min dingin Zera a sa uhi.
30. Huan, huai nungin a unau, huai nungin a unau, huai a khut a khau san bunpen, a hongpawt nawna: a min dingin Zera a sa uhi.
