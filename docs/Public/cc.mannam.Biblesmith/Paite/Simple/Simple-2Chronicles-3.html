1. Huchiin, Solomon in Jerusalemah, Moriah tang, apa David kiang a TOUPA kilakna, Jebus mi Ornan phualjang, David naseh saah TOUPA in alam panta hi,
2. Alal kumli kum, khanihna ni nihni in alam panta hi.
3. Pathian in lamdan ding Solomon in a ngaihtuah dan hichi bang ahi. Adung lambel tong tehdan ngeina in tong sawmguk ahi a, a vai lam bel tong sawmnih ahi.
4. Amalam ah inlim dunglam pen in vailam tan mah tong sawmnih ahi a, asan lam bel ja leh sawmnih ahi a, a sung bel dangka eng chi keleu ngen in a luan hi,
5. Huan dantan lianjaw pen bangah meilah sing a thuap a, huai tuh dangkaeng chikeleu ngen in a luan a, huaiah tumsing leh khainiang lim a tuang sak hi.
6. Huan, a kilawmsak dingin in bel suangmantam in a jem a, dangkaeng bel Paravaim dangkaeng ahi.
7. In bel, kalkhang te, bangte, apialkhang te, kongkhakte dangkaeng in a luana; bangte ah cherub lim te a tuang sak ahi.
8. Huan, munsiangthoupen a bawla adunglam in vailam tanmah, tongsawmnih ahi a, a vailam bel tongsawmnih ahi a, dangkaeng chikeleu ngen in a luan a, talent zaguk a pha hi.
9. Huan, a kilhna gikdan dangkaeng sekel sawmnga ahi. Huan, dantan tungnung te dangkaeng in a luan a.
10. Huan, insiangthou pen ah cherub lim nih a bawla, huaite dangkaeng in a luan hi.
11. Huan cherub kha ten tong sawmnih a; khak a cherub khat kha tongnga ahi a, bang ava pha a, huan, a kha langkhat leng tong nga mah ahi a, cherub khat kha ava pha hi.
12. Huan, cherub khat kha leng tong nga ahi a, bang ava pha a; huan, akha lang khat tong nga mah ahi a, cherub khat kha ava pha hi.
13. Huan cherub ten tong sawmnih khak in akha uh a jak ua, a ding ua, innsung lam nga in a ding uh.
14. Huan puanjak ding a dumbuangte, asan dupte, a sanaute, khauzang ngat mahmahte in a bawl a, huai ah cherub limte a tuang sak hi.
15. Huan, amailam ah tong sawmthum leh tong ngaa sang khuam nih a phuta, huaite dawn tong nga tuaktuak ahi.
16. Huailaiah khainiang a bawla, khuamdawnte ah a viala; huan, pomegranet ja a bawla, khainiang ah a khai hi,Huan, biakin mailam ah khuamte a phuta, khat taklam ah, khat veilam ah, huan, taklam a pen Jakin a chi a, veilam a pen boaz achi hi.
17. Huan, biakin mailam ah khuamte a phuta, khat taklam ah, khat veilam ah, huan, taklam a pen Jakin a chi a, veilam a pen boaz achi hi.
