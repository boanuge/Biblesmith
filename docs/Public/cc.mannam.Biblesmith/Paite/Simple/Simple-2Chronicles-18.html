1. Huchiin Jehosaphat bel a honghauin hongthupita mahmaha, huan Ahab toh kizopna a bawla.
2. Huan, kum bangzah hiam tak nungin Samari ah Ahab kiangah a hoh suka, Huan Ahabin amah leh a heutu belam leh bawngtalte tampi a nagoha, Ramoth-gilead khuaa hoh touhpih dingin achiala.
3. Huan, Israel kumpipa ahabin Juda kumpipa Jehosaphet kiangah, Ramoth-gilead khuaa I hoh tou khawm kei dia hia? achia. Huan, aman a dawnga, kei leh nang, ka mite na mite pumkhat I hi uh aka, gal I sual khawm kei ding ua, a chi a.
4. Huan, Jehosaphatin Israel kumpipa kiangah, TOUPA thu hungaih phot lechin lah, a chi a.
5. Huchiin Israel kumpipan, jawlneite mi za li a sam khawma, a kiang uah, Ramoth gilead khua sual dingin ka vakuan tou ding uam tou lou ding? a chi a. Huan amau va kuan tou in, pathianin kumpipa khuta pe sin ahi, a chi ua.
6. Himahleh jehosaphatin, TOUPA jawlnei kuate hiam I dot ding a om kei ua eita aw? a chi a.
7. Huan, Israel kumpipa Jehosaphat kiangah, TOUPA hondot sak ding mi khat om ve e; himahleh amah bel ka muhdan lam tak ahi; ka tungtang thu a hoih lou lam kia loungal a hoih lam himhim a gen ngei ngal keia; huai mi bel Imla tapa Mikai ahi, a chi a.
8. Huchiin Israel kumpipan heutu khat a sama, Imla tapa Mikai honpi meng dih, a chi a.
9. Huan, Israel kumpipa leh Juda kumpipa Samari kulh kongpi lutna chin mun awng ah a lal puan ua kijemin a laltutphah tuaktuak uah a tu ua; huan jawlnei tengtengin a ma uah thtu a gen uh.
10. Huan, Kenaan tapa Zedakia in sik kite a bawla, TOUPAN hichiin a chi, Hiaiten Suriate mangthang sipsip khopin na si ding, achi, achi a.
11. Huan, huchimahbangin jawlnei dangten leng, Ramoth-gilead ah hoh tou in la. valamzangin; TOUPAN kumpipa khutah a pe sin ahi, chiin a gen ua.
12. Huan, Mikai sam dinga a sawlpa un, ngai dih, jawlneiten kithutuakin a hoih lamin kumpipa kiangah thu a gen chiat ua; huaijiakin nangleng a chihsa bang un a hoihlamin vagen sam dih ve, chiin a gen a.
13. Huan, Mikaiin, TOUPA henna louin ka chi hi, ka pathianin a chih bang geihin ahi ka gen sin, a na chi a.
14. Huan, kumpipa kiang a tun touhin kumpipan a kiangah, Mikai Ramoth-gilead khua sual dingin ka kuan tou ding ua kuan tou lou ding? achi a huan, aman, kuan tou unla, va lamzang un, na khuta piak ahi sin hi, a chi a.
15. Huan kumpipan a kiangah, thu tak ke leu leu lou ka kiangah TOUPA mina gen lou dingin bangzahvei ahia nang kon kichiam sak ding? a chi a.
16. Huan, aman Belam chingmi neilou bangin Israelte tengteng tangah dalh jakin ka mu a; huan, TOUPAN, hiaiten heutu a nei te kei ua, amau in lamah lungmuang takin paidiam mai uhen, a chi ahi, a chi a.
17. Huan, Israel kumpipan Jehosaphat kiangah, ka tungtang thu jaw a se lam kia loungal a hoih lam himhim ahihleh a gen sam keiding chih ka hohilh ahi kei maw? a chi a.
18. Huan, Mikaiin, khai le, TOUPA thu ngaikhe dih ua leh, TOUPA bel a laltutphah a tua, van mipite tengteng a taklam leh a vei lama dingin ka mua.
19. Huan, TOUPAN Israel kumpipa Ahab Ramoth-gileada hoh toua, vasi dingin kua ahia khem sin? a chih hun chiangin khatin. Huchi khachiin, a chi a, mi dangin, huchibang khachibangin a chi hi.
20. Huan, khakhat a hongpawt a, TOUPA maah a dinga, ken kakhem ding, a chi a, huan TOUPAN a kiangah, bangin eita? a chi a.
21. Huan, aman, ka vahoh dinga, jawlneite tengteng kamah kha juautheiin ka va suak ding, a chi a: Huan aman, na vakhem ngei dinga, loh leng nava lohching sam ding, va kuan inla, huchibangin vahihin, a chi a.
22. Huchiin ngai dih, TOUPAN hiai na jawlneite kamah kha juauthei a koiha; na tungtangthu TOUPAN a hoih lou lamin a genta ahi, a chi a.
23. Huai takin Kenaan tapa Zedakiain a vanaiha, mikai biangah a vabenga, na kianga thug en dingin TOUPA khain koi lama honpawtsan ahia? a chi a.
24. Huan Mikaiin, ngai dih, dantan sungilla bu dinga nalut ni chiangin na honthei ding, a chi a.
25. Huchiin, Israel kumpipan, Mikai pi unla, kho ukpa Amaon leh kumpipa tapa kiangah paipih nawn un,
26. Huan, kumpipan hichiin achia, hiai mi suangkulh ah khum unla, lungmuang taka ka hong nawn ma siah haksa an leh haksa tui nana pia un, a chi a na chi ding uh, a chi a.
27. Huan, Mikaiin, lungmuang taka na hongpai nawn peuhmah leh TOUPAN kei hongzangin thu a gen ka hi ve leh, a chi a huan amah mahin, nou mipi tengtengin leng na naja a un, a chi a.
28. Huan, Israel kumpipa leh Juda kumpipa Jehosaphet bel Ramoth gilead khuaah va hoh tou ua.
29. Huan, Israel kumpipan Jehosophet kiangah, ka kibawl lamdang dinga, kisualnaah ka hoh sam ding; nang jaw na kumpipa puan kisilhin, a chi a. Huchiin Israel kumpipa bel a kibawl lamdanga, kisualnaah a hoh tou a.
30. Huan, Suria kumpipan a kangtalaite tunga heutute kiangah, Israel kumpipa loungal mi neu leng milian leng na sual ding uh ahi kei, chiin thu apia.
31. Huan, hichi ahi a, kangtalai tunga heututen Jehosaphet a muh uleh Israel kumpipa eive, a chi ua. Huchiin Sual tumin a umta uh; himahleh Jehosaphet a vakikou a, huan, TOUPAN a panpihtaa, huchiin Pathianin amau amah a nusesak hi.
32. Huan, hichi ahi a, kangtalai tunga heututen Israel kumpipa ahi kei chih a theih un delh louin a kiksan nawnta uhi.
33. Huan, mi khatin tup tuan nei tuanlou in a thalpeu a laia, Israel kumpipa a awmphaw leh a awmphaw kikalah a kap a, huchiin a kangtalai hawlpa kiangah, leh hawl inla, mipi laka kipan honpaikhialk pih dih ve, liamna na tak ka tuak hi, a chi a.Huan, huai niin kisualna a thupi mahmaha; huan Israel kumpipa bel Suriate lam ngain a kangtalaiah nitak lam tanpha in a dinga; huan ni tum kuan ding khawngin a sita hi.
34. Huan, huai niin kisualna a thupi mahmaha; huan Israel kumpipa bel Suriate lam ngain a kangtalaiah nitak lam tanpha in a dinga; huan ni tum kuan ding khawngin a sita hi.
