1. Huan, gentehna thuin a kiang uah a genta a, Kuahiamin grephuan a bawla, a kim huangin a um a, grep sukna ding khuk a tou a, in sang a lama, loukhoumite kiangah khohloh dingin a pia a, gam dangah a hohta-a.
2. Huan, a loh hunin huan-a a gah tuh a tansik amau kianga kipana la dingin loukhohlohmite kiangah sikha khat a sawla.
3. Huan, huai mi tuh a na man ua, a vua ua, khut vuakin a pai sak nawnta ua.
4. Huan, sikha dang a kiang uah a sawl nawna; huai mi leng a lu ah a na hihliam ua, zumhuaipiin a na bawl ua.
5. Huan, sikha dang a sawl nawn jela; huai mi tuh a na hihlum ua. Sikha dang tampi leng-khenkhat a na vua ua, khenkhat a na hihlum ua.
6. Huan, khat, tapa deihtak, a nei lai a; Ka tapa jaw a zah ding uh, chiin, amah a nanungpenin a kiang uah a sawla.
7. Himahleh, huai loukhohlohmiten, Hiai mi gouluahdingpa ahi; i that ding uh aw: huchiin a gou luahding eia ahi mai ding, a kichi ua.
8. Huchiin, amah a na man ua, a hihlum ua, grep huan apat a lawn kheta ua.
9. Huaijiakin, grephuan neipan bangchin a honhih dia? Loukhohloh mite a honhih mangthang dia; huan tuh midangte a pe ding.
10. Hiai laisiangthou na sim kei ua hia? In lam mite suang deihlouhpen, huai mahmah a ninga suang phatuampen a honghita hi.
11. Huai tuh Toupa hih ahi, i mit muh uah a lamdang hi, chih, a chi a.
12. Huan, amah mat a tumta ua; huai gentehna thu lah amau dema a gen ahi chih a thei ngal ua. Huchiin amah tuh a nusia ua, a paimangta uh.
13. Huan, a thu gena mattheihna dingin Pharisaite leh Herod pawla mi kuate hiam a kiangah a sawl ua.
14. Huchiin, amau a hongtun un, a kiangah, Sinsakpa, mi diktat na hi a, deihsak tuan kuamah na neikei chih ka thei uh, kuamah khentuam neilouin, chihtak ngialin Pathian lam thu na hilh jel ahi: Kaisar kianga leiman liau a siang hia siang lou?
15. Pe ding ka hi ua, pe lou ding? a chi ua. Aman bel a lepchiahdan uh theiin, a kiang uah, Bangdia hon zeet na hi ua? Makhai hontawi un, ka na en ding, a chi a.
16. A hontawi ua. Huan aman a kiang uah, Hiai kua lim leh kua min ahia? a chi a.
17. Huan, amau, Kaisar-a, a chi ua. Huan, Jesun a kiang uah, Kaisar thilte Kaisar kiangah pia unla, Pathian thilte Pathian kiangah peta unla, a chi a. Huchiin, lamdang a sa mahmah uh.
18. Huan, Saddukaite, thohnawnna a om kei chite, a kiangah a hongua;
19. A kiangah, Sinsakpa, Mosiin, kuapeuh a unaupan ta neilou in a ji sihsan taleh, a unaupan a ji luahin a unaupa dingin chi a suahsak ding, chiin, a hongelh sak ahi.
20. A hi, sal sagih a om ua; a upa penin ji a neia, chi suah louin a si a.
21. Huan, a nihnain a luah a, chi suah louin a si nawn jela; a thumna in huchibang mahin.
22. Huchiin, sal sagihte tuh chi himhim suah louin a si jel uh. A nanungpenin numei leng a sita hi.
23. Thohnawn hunin a kuapen ji ahi dia le? sagih ten jiin a nei chiat ngal ua, chin a dong ua.
24. Jesun a kiang uah, Hiai jiak hilou hia, na theihkhelh nak uh, Laisiangthou leh Pathian thilhihtheihna na theih louh jiak un?
25. Misi lak apat a thoh nawnun ji leh pasala kinei lou ding ahi ua, vana angelte bang hizota ding uh ahi ngalua.
26. Huam, misite kaihthoha a om ding thu u lah Mosi laibua, Loubuk ah, Pathianin, Kei Abraham Pathian, Isaak Pathian, Jakob Pathian ka hi, chia, amah a houpihdan na sim kei ua hia?
27. Amah jaw misi Pathian ahi keia, mihing Pathian ahi jaw; na thei khial tel u e, a chi a.
28. Huan, laigelhmite laka mi khat a honga, a kisel uh a na ngaikhia a, Jesun amaute thutakin a dawng chih a theia, a kiang ah, Thupiak tengteng ah koi ahia thupipen? chiin, a donga.
29. Jesun, A thupipen tuh hiai ahi: Israel aw, ngai dih uh, Toupa i Pathian uh Toupa khat kia ahi a;
30. Toupa na Pathian tuh na lungtang tengtengin, na kha tengtengin, na lungsim tengtengin leh na hatna tengtengin na it ding ahi, chih.
31. A zom tuh hiai ahi, Nangmah na kiit bangin na vengte leng na it ding ahi, chih. Hiai khawng sanga thupiak thupijaw himhim a om kei, chiin, a dawnga.
32. Huan, laigelhmin a kiangah, Hi-na ngei e, Sinsakpa amah jaw khatkia ahi a, amah lou jaw kua dangmah a om kei uh,
33. Amah tuh lungtang tengtenga, theihsiamna tengtenga, hatna tengtenga ita, eimah i kiit banga vengte it tuh pum hal thilpiak leh kithoihna tengteng sangin a hoihzo petmah chih, na gendik hilhial ngei, a chi a.
34. Huan, piltakin a dawng chih Jesun a theihin, a kiang ah, Pathian gam na gamlat kei ve, a chi a. Huai nungsang tuh kuamahin thu himhim a dong ngamta kei uh.
35. Huan, Jesun Pathian biakina thu a hilh laiin, amaute a houpiha, Kris tuh David tapa ahi chih laigelhmiten bangchidana gen uh ahia?
36. David ngeiin, Kha Siangthouin, Toupan, ka Toupa kiangah, Na melmate na khepek ngaknaa ka bawl masiah ka taklamah tuin, a chi a, a chi a.
37. David ngeiin amah, Toupa, a chi ngala, koi akipanin ahia amah a tapa a hih? a chi a. Huan, mivantangin kipak takin a thu a ngai khia ua.
38. Huan, a thuhilhna ah, laigelhmite lakah pilvang un; amau lah puannak tual silha vak a ut ua, dawl muna chibai buk bang,
39. Kikhopna ina tutphah hoih pen bang, ankuangluina-a mun hoihpen bang, a nuamtuam nak uh;
40. meithai in a ne gai ua, tangpi khemna dingin sawtpipi a thum uh; huaiten siamlouhtanna lianjaw a tang ding uh hi, a chi a.
41. Huan, sum kholna jawnah a tu a; sumkholnaa mipi sum khiakdan a en a; mi hau tampi takin tampipi a khia ua.
42. Huan, meithai jawng tak a honga, dal dangka muja nih a khia a, huai tuh paihsa khat hu ahi.
43. Huan, Jesun a nungjuite a sama, a kiang uah, Chihtaktakin ka honhilh ahi, Sumkholna a khe tengteng lakah hiai meithai jawng takin a khe tam pen hi.Hiaite tengtengin a neihlet ua a khia ua; aman jaw a hawmthoh laitak, a neih sun, a nuntakna ding tengteng a khe vek ahi, a chi a.
44. Hiaite tengtengin a neihlet ua a khia ua; aman jaw a hawmthoh laitak, a neih sun, a nuntakna ding tengteng a khe vek ahi, a chi a.
