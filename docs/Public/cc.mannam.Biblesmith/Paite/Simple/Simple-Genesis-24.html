1. Huan, Abraham a hong upataa, a hong tek mahmah taa: huan, Toupan Abraham thil bangkimah a vualjawl hi.
2. Huan, Abrahamin, a sikha, a inkuanpihte laka upapen, a neih tengteng tunga thuneipa kiangah, Hehpihtakin na khut ka pheipi nuiah golhin:
3. Huchiin, Toupa, van Pathian leh lei Pathian louin, ka omna Kanan mite nungak laka mi ka tapa ji dinga neihsak lou dingin, ka hon kichiamsak ding ahi:
4. Ka gam lama, ka tanaute kianga hoha, ka tapa Isaak ji na neihsak zo ding ahi, achia.
5. Huan, sikhain, a kiangah, Numeiin hiai gam lamah honjui nuam khol kei dia: na tapa na hong pawtna gam lamah ka pi kik nawn ding ahi diam? A chia.
6. Huan, Abrahamin, a kiangah, ka tapa huailaia pi kik nawnlou dingin pilvangin.
7. Toupa van Pathian, ka pa in akipan leh ka pianna gam a kipan hon pikhepa leh hon houpihpa leh, hiai gam na suante kiangah ka pe ding, chia, ka kianga kichiampan, amah ngeiin a angel na maah a paisak dinga, huai akipanin ka tapa ji na hon neisak ding hi.
8. Huan, numeiin a hon zuih nop kei leh, ka kianga na kichiamna thu ah na siang mai ding; a bang a bang hitaleh, ka tapa huailaiah na pi kik nawn ding ahi kei, achia.
9. Huchiin, sikhain a khut a pu Abraham pheipi nuaiah a golha, huai thu ah a kiangah a kichiamta hi.
10. Huan, sikhain a pu sangawngsau laka sangawngsau sawm a kaia, a pu thil hoih chiteng tawiin, a pawttaa: huchiin a thoua, Mesopotamia gam a, Nahorte khua aha hoha.
11. Huan, nitaklama, numeite tuitawi dinga a kuan hun lai tak un, dainawla tuileh kiangah a sangawngsaute akhukdinsak a.
12. Huan, Aw Toupa, ka pu Abraham Pathian, hehpihtakin tuniin hon lohchingsak in, ka pu Abraham tungah hehpihna langsakin.
13. Enin, tuikhuk kiangah ka dinga, khua a mite tanute leng tuitawi dingin a hongkuan uh:
14. Huchiin, hichi hihen, nungak kuapeuh kianga, hehpihtakin ka dawn dingin na tui bel niamsak dih ve, ka chiha, huan, aman, Dawnin, na sangawngsaute leng dawn ding ka pe ding, chi peuhmah, huai mi ngei na sikha Isaak a dinga na mi seh hihen; huan huaiah ka pu tungah hehpihna na langsak chih ka thei ding hi, a chi hi.
15. Huan, hichi ahia, a gen khit main, ngaiin, Abraham nau Nahor ji Milka tapa Bethuel tanu Rebeka tui bel a liangjang a puain a hongkuana.
16. Huai nungak a mel a hoih mahmaha, nungak siangthou ahi a, pasal himhimin a kithuahpih ngei hetkei uh. Huan, tuikhuk ah a pai suka, a tuibel a dimsaka, a pai tou nawna.
17. Huan, sikha amah tuak dingin a taia, Hehpihtakin na tuibel a tui tawm chik hondan sak ve, achia.
18. Huan, aman, dawn ve, ka toupa, achia; huan, kintakin a tuibel a khut in a dom khe suka, a dawnsak hi.
19. Huan, a dawn nungin, na sangawngsaute dingin leng a dawn kham masiah uh ka tawi jel ding, achia.
20. Huan, kintakin a tuibel a gan kuangah a teia, tui tawi dingin tuileh lamah a tai nawna, a sangawngsau tengteng a dingin leng a tawitawi hi.
21. Huan, sikhain a lohchingsak leh saklouh theihna dingin a dai didea.
22. Huan, hichi ahia, sangawngsauten a dawn kham takun sikhain dangkaeng bilbah makhai buk leh, a khut a dinga tau nih dangkaeng dangka sawm buk a suaha;
23. Huan, a kiangah, kua tanu na hia? Hehpihtakin hin hilh in. Na pa in ah ka giahna ding uh mun om ding hia? achia.
24. Huan, aman, a kiangah, Bethuel, Milka tapa, Nahor adinga neihsaknu tanu ka hi, achia.
25. Huan, amah mahin, a kiangah, buhpawl leh gan an ka hau tuaktuak ua, giahna mun leng ka nei uh, achia.
26. Huchiin, sikhain a lu a kunsaka, Toupa chibai a buk hi.
27. Huan, ka pu tunga a hehpihna leh a takna tawp tuanlou, toupa, ka pu Abraham Pathian, phatin om hen: kei leng, Toupan ka pu unaute in lamah hon na pi eive, achia.
28. Huan, nungak a taia, huai thute a nu inkuanpihte a vahilh hi.
29. Rebekain sanggam pasal a neia, a min Laban ahia: huchiin, Laban a pawta, tuikhuk lamah, mi kiangah a vatai taa.
30. Hichi ahia, bilbah leh a sanggamnu khut a taute a mua, min huchi khachiin honchi, chia, a sanggamnu Rebeka thu gente a jak takin, huai mi kiangah a hohta a; huan, ngaiin, amah tuikhuka sangawngsaute kiangah a na ding gige a.
31. Huan, Labanin, nang Toupa vualjawl pa, hong inla, ka in ah lutin; bangdia inpua a ding nilouh? in leh sangawngsaute adingin mun ka bawl khinta, achia.
32. Huan, huai in ah a luta, huan Labanin sangawngsau van a phela; huan, sangawngsaute adingin buhpawl leh gan an apia a, akhe leh a lawmte khe silna dingin tui a pia hi.
33. Huan, Abraham sikha maah nek ding a lui ua; himahleh, aman, ka hong na san thu kagen mateng ka ne kei ding, achia. Huan, aman, gen ve, achia.
34. Huan, aman, Abraham sikha ka hi.
35. Huan, Toupan ka pu nakpiin a vualjawla, mi liantak a honghi a: Toupan a kiangah belam honte, bawng honte, dangkate, dangkaengte, sikhapate, sikhanute, sangawngsaute, sabengtung te leng a pia.
36. Ku pu ji Sarain tek ningin ka pu adingin tapa a neia: amah kiangah a neih tengteng a pe vek hi.
37. Huan, ka puin, ka omna gama Kanante nungak laka ka tapa ji dinga na neihsak ding ahi kei:
38. Ka pa in lamah, ka tanaute kiangah hohin, ka tapa dingin ji na neihsak zo ding ahi, chiin, hon kichiamsaka.
39. Huan, ken ka pu kiangah, Numeiin honjui nuam kei kha ding, ka chia.
40. Huan, aman, ka kiangah, Toupa a maa ka om jelnain a angel na kiangah honsawl dinga; na hohna dingah hon lohchingsak ding; huchiin, ka tapa dingin ka tanaute leh ka pa inkuanpihte laka ji dingin na neihsak ding hi:
41. Ka tanaute kiang ngeia na tun chiangin ka kianga na kichiamna apat na siang ding; nungak a honpiak kei uleh ka kianga na kichiamna akipan na siang ding, achia.
42. Huchiin, tuniin tuikhuk ka hongtunga, Aw, Toupa, ka pu Abraham Pathian, ka paina ah na hon lohchinsak ding leh, enin, tuikhuk kiangah ka ding hi:
43. Huan, hichi hi hen, nungak tui tawi dinga hongkuan, kuapeuh kianga, hehpihtakin dawn dingin na tuibel a tui tawm chik honpe ve, ka chih a;
44. Huan, aman, ka kianga, dawnin, sa sangawngsaute ading leng ka tawi ding, chi peuhmah, huai mi ngeingei ka pu tapa adinga Toupa numei seh hihen, ka chia.
45. Huan, ka lungsim a ka gen khit main, ngaiin, Rebeka a tuibel puain a hongkuana; huan, tuikhuk ah a paisuka, tui a tawia; huan, a kiangah, hehpihtakin tui hondawnsak ve, ka chia.
46. Huan, aman kintakin a liangjang akipan tuibel a dom khiaa, dawnin, na sangawngsaute leng dawn ding ka pe ding, achia: huchiin ka dawna, sangawngsaute leng a dawnsaka.
47. Huan, ken, kua tanu na hia? chiin, ka donga. Huan, aman, Bethuel, Nohar tapa, Milkhain a neihsakpa tanu, achia: huan bilbah abil ah ka batsaka, taute leng a khut ah ka bunsak.
48. Huan, ka lu ka kunsaka, Toupa chibai ka buka, Toupa, ka pu Abraham Pathian, ka pu unaupa tanu a tapa adinga ji dia neisak dinga lampi diktaka honpipa, ka phat hi.
49. Huchiin, tuin ka pu na hehpih ua, a tunga chihtak peta na hih ding uleh honhilh un; na hih kei ding uleh leng honhilh un; huchiin taklam hiam, veilam hiam, ka nga thei ding hi, achia.
50. Huan, Laban leh Bethuel in a dawng ua, hiai thil Toupa kianga kipana pawt ahi: a sia a pha leng na kiangah ka gen theikei uh.
51. Enin, Rebeka na maah a om hi, pi inla, pain, Toupan a gensa bangin na pu tapa ji dingin om hen, a chi ua.
52. Huan, hichi ahia, Abraham sikhain a thu uh a jak takin lei siin Toupa chibai a buk hi.
53. Huan, Abraham sikhain dangka suang kizepnate leh, dangkaeng kizepnate leh, silh leh ten a lakhia a, Rebeka kiangah a pia a: a sanggampa kiangah leh a nu kiangah leng thil manpha pipi a pia hi.
54. Huan, amah leh a lawmten a ne ua a dawn ua, a giakta ua; huan, jingsangin a thou ua ka pu kiangah honpaisakta un, achia.
55. Huan, a sanggampa leh a nuin, naunu ni sawtlou chik ni sawm lel bek, ka kiang uah om tadih hen; huai khit chiangin a kuan nading, a chi ua.
56. Huan, aman, a kiang uah, hon zekai sak kei dih ua, Toupan leh ka hohna ah hon lohching sakta ngala; ka pu kianga ka pai theihna dingin, honsawl zomai ve ua, achia.
57. Huan, amau, naunu ka sam ding ua, amah kam mahmah kihoupih leh, a chi ua.
58. Huchiin, Rebeka a sam ua a kiangah, hiai mipa kiangah na pai nuam maw a chi ua, huan, aman, pai nuam ve ning e, achia.
59. Huchiin, a sanggamnu uh Rebeka leh, a donnu leh, Abraham sikha leh a lawite a paita uhi.
60. Huan, Rebeka a vualjawl ua, a kiangah, ka sanggamnu uh aw, nang sing ngenta sang tampi nu honghi inla, na suanten amau hote kulh kongkhak neihsak uhen, a chi ua.
61. Huchiin, Rebeka a thoua, a sikhanute toh, sangawngsaute tungah a tuang ua, mi a juita uh; huchiin Abraham sikhain Rebeka a pi a, apaita.
62. Huan, Isaak Beer-lahai-roi lam akipan a honga, Sim gama om ahi ngala,
63. Huan, Isaak nitaklam pawnah kingaihtuah dingin a pawtkhia a: huan a daka, a ena, huan ngaiin, sangawngsau hongpaite a mu hi.
64. Huan, Rebeka a daka, Isaak a muhtakin sangawngsau tunga kipan a kuma.
65. Huan, Abraham sikha kiangah, pawna hondawn dinga hongpai kua ahia? Achia. Huan, sikhain, ka pu eive, achia: huchiin a kikhuhna puan a laa, a kikhuhta hi.
66. Huan, sikhain a thilhih tengteng Isaak a hilha.Huan, Isaakin Rebeka a nu Sara puan in ah a pi luta, huan, Rebeka a la a, a ji a honghita a; huan amah a it mahmah a: huchiin Isaak a nu sih nungin leng a hong lungmuang theita hi.
67. Huan, Isaakin Rebeka a nu Sara puan in ah a pi luta, huan, Rebeka a la a, a ji a honghita a; huan amah a it mahmah a: huchiin Isaak a nu sih nungin leng a hong lungmuang theita hi.
