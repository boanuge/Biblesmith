1. Huan, Sarai Abram jiin ta himhim a neihsak keia; huan, Saraiin Aiguptami sikhanu khat a neia, huai mi min Hagar ahi.
2. Huan, Saraiin, Abram kiangah, ngai dihve, Toupan lah ta ka neih honphal loua; hehpihtakin ka sikhanu kithuahpih in; amah laka kipana tate ka neih khak leh, achia. Huchiin, Abramin Sarai thu a juita.
3. Huchiin, Abram ji Saraiin Abram Kanan gama kum sawm a om nungin, a sikhanu Aiguptami a pia, a pasal Abram kiangah a ji kia om dingin a pia hi.
4. Huan, Hagar a kithuahpiha; huan amah a gaita: huan, a gai chih a kitheih takin a pi a simmoh maimaha.
5. Huchiin, Saraiin, Abram kiangah, ka thil thuak nang a mohpua in; ka sikhanu na kawi dingin kon piaa; huan, a gai chih a kitheih takin a hon simmoh maimah hi: Toupan nang leh kei thu ngaihtuah hen, a chi a.
6. Abramin bel, Sarai kiangah, Ngaiin, na sikhanu na khuta om ahi; nang pha na saksak a tungah hihin, a chi a. Huchiin Saraiin a hihgenthei mahmaha, Huaijiakin a taimangsanta hi
7. Huan, Toupa angelin gamdai tui khuk kiangah, Sur zotna lampi a tui khuk kiangah, a na mu a.
8. Huan, a kiangah, Hagar, Sarai sikhanu aw, koia kipana hongpai na hia, koia hoh dia kisa na hia? a chi a. Huan, aman, ka pi Sarai kianga kipana taimang ka hi, a chi a.
9. Huan, Toupa angelin, a kiangah, na pi kiangah kik nawn inla, a thuthuin omin, a chi a.
10. Huan, Toupa angelin, a kiangah, Na suante simsenglouh khop hiala tamin ka hihpung ding, a chi nawna.
11. Huan, Toupa angel mahin, a kiangah, Ngaiin, nau na paita, tapa na nei dinga; Toupan na gimthuakna a jak jiakin a min dingin Ismael na sak ding ahi.
12. Amah mi laka gamsabengtung bang ahi ding; aman mi chih a dou dinga, mi chihin leng amah a dou ding uh; a unau tengteng kiang chinah a om ding, a chi nawn laia.
13. Huan, Hagarin amah houpiha Toupa min din, Nang muthei Pathian na hi, chih a saa, aman lah, Pathian mua hing nalai ka hi maw? A chi ngala.
14. Huaijiakin tuileh Beerlahai-roi sak ahita; ngaiin, Kades leh Bered kho kikalah a om.
15. Huan, Hagarin Abram tapa a neih saka; huan, Abramin a tapa Hagar neih a min dingin Ismael a sata hi.Huan, Hagarin Abram dinga Ismael a neih laiin Abram kum sawm giat leh kum guk a upa ahi.
16. Huan, Hagarin Abram dinga Ismael a neih laiin Abram kum sawm giat leh kum guk a upa ahi.
