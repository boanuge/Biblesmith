1. Huan, kha sagihna, a ni khat ni in khawmpi siangthou na nei ding ua; bang nna mahmah na sem ding uh ahi kei: nou a ding in pengkul mut ni ahi ding ahi.
2. TOUPA a ding in gimlim di'n halmang thillat na lat ding uh ahi, bawngtal tung khat, belamtal khat, belamnou a pa kum khat a upa gensiatbei sagih:
3. A tangbuang thillat uh, tangbuang nelte sathau a helkhawm, bawngtal khat ah mun sawm a khen a mun thum, belamtal khat ah mun sawma khen a mun nih,
4. Belamnou sagih, khat chiat ah mun sawm a khen a mun khat:
5. Nou a dia kilemna bawlna di'n khelh thoihna keltal khat toh.
6. Kha thak halmang thillat leh huai a tangbuang thillatte, halmang thillat lat gige leh huai a tangbuang thillatte, dawn ding thillatte, a hihdan ngeina uh TOUPA ding a gimlim dia mei a thillatte sim lou in.
7. Huan, hiai kha sagihna, ni sawm ni in khawmpi siangthou na nei ding ua: na hihna uh na hihgim ding ua, bang nna mahmah na sep ding uh ahi kei;
8. TOUPA ding a gimlim dia halmang thillat bel na lan ding uhi; bawngtal khat, belamtal khat, belamnou a pa kum khat a upa sagih; na ngaihtuah ua gensiatbei ahi ding ahi;
9. A tangbuang thillat uh, tangbuang nelte sathau a helkhawm, bawngtal khat ah mun sawm a khen a mun thum, belamtal khat ah mun sawm a khen a mun nih.
10. Belamnou sagih, belamnou khat chiat ah mun sawm a khen a mun khat:
11. Khelh thoihna di'n keltal khat toh; kilemna ding a khelh thoihnate, halmang thillat lat gigete, huai a tangbuang thillat leh a dawn ding thillatte uh sim lou in.
12. Huan, kha sagihna, ni sawm leh ni nga ni in kikhawmpi siangthou na nei ding ua; bang nna mahmah na sem ding uh ahi kei, TOUPA ding in ni sagih annkuang na lui ding uhi;
13. Huan, TOUPA ding a gimlim dia mei a thillat, halmang thillat na lat ding uh ahi: bawngtal tung sawm leh thum, belamtal nih, belamnou a pa kum khat a upa sawm leh li: gensiatbei chiat a hih ding ahi:
14. A tangbuang thillat uh sathau toh helkhawm bawngtal sawm leh thum, bawngtal khat ah mun sawm a khen mun thum, belamtal nih, belamtal khat ah mun sawm a khen a mun nih;
15. Belamnou sawm leh li, belamnou khat ah mun sawm a khen a mun khat:
16. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat toh; halmang thillat lat gige, huai a tangbuang thillatte, dawn ding thillatte sim lou in.
17. Huan, a ni nih ni in bawngtal tung sawm leh nih, belamtal nih, belamnou a pa kum khat a upa gensiatbei sawm leh li na lat ding uh ahi;
18. A tangbuang thillat uh leh dawn ding thillat a dan bang tak in a tamdan bang zel un bawngtal a ding bang, belamtal a ding bang, belamnou a ding bang;
19. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat toh: halmang thillat gige bang huai a tangbuang thillatte, dawn ding thillatte sim lou in.
20. Huan, a ni thum ni in bawngtal sawm leh khat, belamtal nih, belamnou kum khat a upa gensiatbei sawm leh li,
21. A tangbuang thillat uh leh dawn ding thillat a dan bang tak in a tamdan bang zel un bawngtal a ding bang, belamtal a ding bang, belamnou a ding bang:
22. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat toh; halmang thillat lat gige leh huaia tangbuang thillat leh huai a dawn ding thillat sim lou in.
23. Huan, a ni li ni in bawngtal sawm, belamtal nih, belamnou a pa kum khat a upa gensiatbei sawm leh li;
24. A tangbuang thillat uh leh dawn ding thillat, a dan bang tak in a tamdan bang zel un bawngtal a ding bang, belamtal a ding bang, belamnou a ding bang:
25. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat toh: halmang thillat gigete, huai a tangbuang thillat leh dawn ding thillatte sim lou in.
26. Huan, a ni nga ni in bawngtal kua, belamtal nih, belamnou a pa kum khat a upa gensiatbei sawm leh li;
27. A tangbuang thillat uh leh dawn ding thillat uh, a dan bang tak in a tam dan bang zel tangbuang thillat uh leh dawn ding thillat uh, a dan bang tak in a tamdan bang zel un, bawngtal a ding bang, belamtal a ding bang, belamnou a ding bang:
28. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat toh; halmang thillat lat gigete, huai tangbuang thillat leh huai dawn ding thillatte sim lou in.
29. Huan a ni guk ni in bawngtal giat, belamtal nih, belamnou a pa kum khat a upa gensiatbei sawm leh li:
30. A tangbuang thillat uh leh dawn ding thillat a dan bang tak in a tamdan bangzel un bawngtal a ding bang:
31. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat toh; halmang thillat lat gigete, huai a tangbuang thillat leh huai a dawn ding thillatte sim lou in.
32. Huan, a ni sagih ni in bawngtal sagih, belamtal nih, belamnou a pa kum khat a upa gensiatbei sawm leh li:
33. A tangbuang thillat uh leh dawn ding thillat, a dan bang tak in a tamdan bangzel un, bawngtal a ding bang, belamtal a ding bang, belamnou a ding bang:
34. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat toh; halmang thillat lat gigete, huai a tangbuang thillat leh huai a dawn ding thillatte sim lou in.
35. Huan, a ni giat ni in khawmpi thupi tak na nei ding ua: bang nna mahmah na sem ding uh ahi kei:
36. TOUPA a dia gimlim ding in mai a halmang thillat bel na lat ding uh ahi: bawngtal khat, belamtal khat, belamnou a pa kum khat a upa gensiatbei sagih:
37. A tangbuang thillat uh leh dawn ding thillat uh, bawngtal a ding bang, belamtal a ding bang, belamnou a ding bang, a dan bangtak in a tamdan bang zel un:
38. Khelh thoihna ding keltal khat toh; halmang thillat lat gige leh huai a tangbuang thillat leh huai a dawn ding thillat sim lou in.
39. Na thuchiamte uh, utthu a thillatte, na halmang thillatte uh, na tangbuang thillatte uh, na dawn ding thillatte uh, na lemna thillatte uh sim lou in hiaite annkuangluina hunbi nei a na neihte uh ni in TOUPA kiang ah na lan ding uhi, chi in, chi in.Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi thu a piak tengteng Israel suante Mosi in a hilh hi.
40. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi thu a piak tengteng Israel suante Mosi in a hilh hi.
