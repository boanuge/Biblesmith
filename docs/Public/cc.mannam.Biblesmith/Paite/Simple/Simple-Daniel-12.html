1. Huchiin huai hun chiangin Mikael, lal thupi na mite tapa adia pangpa, a ding khe ding hi; huan buai hun, nam a om kipan huai hun mah tan hiala leng huchibang om ngei lou, a om ding, huai hun chiangin na mite hutkhiakin a om ding ua, laibu sunga gelha oma amuh khiak ding mi chiteng.
2. Huan a lak ua mi tampi leia leivuia ihmute a khanglou ding ua, a khen tangtawn hinna tang dingin, a khen zumna leh tangtawna simmohna tang dingin.
3. Huan a pilte vansin vakna bangin a tang ding ua; huan mi tampi diktatna lama heite tangtawn tangtawn in akisite bangin.
4. Himahleh nang, Aw Danial, thute khakkhum bikbek inla, tawp hun tan ading ngeiin, laibu tuh bilhin; mi tampi a vial tai ding ua, theihna a pung ding hi.
5. Huailaiin kei Danialin ka ena, huan ngaiin, mi nih dang, khat lui gei hiai lam pangah, huan khat lui gei hua lam pang ah a ding ua.
6. Huan khatin puan malngat a silha, lui tuite tunga ompa kiangah, Hiai thillamdangte tawpna ding bangchi sawt ahi ding? a chi a.
7. Huan puanmalngat silha, lui tuite tunga dingpan van lama a khut taklam leh a khut veilam zan toua, hun khat, hunte leh hun kimkhat ahi ding chia amah khantawna hingpaa a kichiam ka zataa; huan mi siangthoute thilhihtheihna a suk nen dimdem khit chiang un, hiai thil tengteng zoh ahi ding hi, chiin.
8. Huan ka jaa, himahleh ka theisiamn keia; huan ken, Aw ka TOUPA, hiai thilte tawp bang ahi ding? ka chi a.
9. Huan aman, Pai in, Danial: thute lah khakkhum bikbek ahi ua tap hun matana bilh bikbek ahi ngala.
10. Mi tampi a kihihsiang ding ua, a kingousak ding ua, a kibawlhoih ding uh; himahleh gilouten giloutakin a hih ding ua; huan giloute laka kuamahin a theisiam kei ding uh; himahleh a pilten a theisiam ding uhi.
11. Huchiin halmang thillat gige lak manga oma, hihgawptu kihhuai tun khiaka hong om ding hun akipan, ni sang khat zanih leh sawmkua a om ding hi.
12. Ni sangkhat za thum leh sawmthum leh nga ngaka, huai a hong-a paiten nuam a sa uhi.Himahleh a tawp matan nang jaw pai in; na khawl dinga, nang adia piak na muh ah nite beina ah ding ding na hi ngala.
13. Himahleh a tawp matan nang jaw pai in; na khawl dinga, nang adia piak na muh ah nite beina ah ding ding na hi ngala.
