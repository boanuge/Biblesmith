1. Huan, hichi ahi a, Solomon in TOUPA, in leh amah in alam sung kum sawmnih ahong bei in,
2. Huramin Solomon khua a piakte Solomon in a bawl a, Israel suante a omsak hi.
3. Huan, Solomonin Hamath-Zoba a sual a, a zou hi,
4. Huan, gamdai ah, Tadmor a bawl a, Hamatha van koihna khua te toh.
5. Huan, Beth- heron saknung jaw leh khangnung jaw leng, kulhte, kongkhakte, a kalhna te nei in a bawl beh a.
6. Huan, Baalath khuate, avan koihna khua tengteng te, a kangtalai koihna khua te, a sakol tungtuang mite omna khua te, Jerusalem leh Lebanon leh a gamsung tengteng ah Solomon in a deih bangbang a bawl hi.
7. Huan. Israel suante hilou, Hit te, Amor te, Periz te, Hiv te, Jebus te suan a omlai mite tengteng;
8. Israel suanten ahihman louhte uh, huai gam a amaute nung a nutsiat a om a suante uh, tutan a, Solomon in sikha nasem dinga a goih teitei pawl din a bawl hi.
9. Israel suante bel Solomon in a nasem mi sikha ding in abawl tei keia, kidou mite a sepaih heutu te inntekte akangtalai leh a Sakol tungtuang mi heutu ahi jaw uh.
10. Huaite mipite heusek mi, Solomon inntek pente ahi ua, zanih leh sawmnga ahi uh.
11. Huan Solomon in Pharo tanu David khopi a kipan atuam a in alam sak ah a pitou a, ka ji Israel kumpipa David in a om ding ahi kei, TOUPA bawm hong tunna munsiangthou ahi, achi ahi.
12. Huchiin, Solomon in in lim mailam a abawl TOUPA maitam ah TOUPA ding in halmang thillat te alan a;
13. Mosi thupiak, niteng a, dinga bikhiah zah bangbang in khawlni ah te, khathak ni ah te, annkuang luihun nei, kumkhata thumvei, tanghou silngou sohlouh annkuang luina a te, nipi kal annkuang luia ah te, bawkte annkuang lui ah te,
14. Huan, apa David nanaseh bang in siampu pawlte leh Levi te atantuam chiat ua, niteng a sep kul dungzui jel a, phat ding te, siampute maa nasem ding khawng inasep a; huan, kongkhak tengah a pawlpawl a vengmite a sep laia; huchibangin Pathian mi David a nasep ahi.
15. Kumpipan siampu te, Levi te thu a piak bang chimah in sumthu a te leng a limsak lou hetkei uh.
16. Huan, TOUPA in a bawlkhiak tung ua, kipana a zohtan un, Solomon in ana suktuah sa bang geih ahi. Huchiin TOUPA in pichiin a hita hi.
17. Huchiin Solomon bel Edom gama luipi gei a Ezion-Geber ah leh Eloth ah a hoha,Huan, Huramin a sikha tuipi a gamtang ngei mi, long te toh a kuansak a: Solomon sikha te toh Ophir ah a hoh ua, huailaiah dangkaeng talent za li leh sawm nga a hon tun ua, kumpipa Solomon kiang ah hon tun uhi.
18. Huan, Huramin a sikha tuipi a gamtang ngei mi, long te toh a kuansak a: Solomon sikha te toh Ophir ah a hoh ua, huailaiah dangkaeng talent za li leh sawm nga a hon tun ua, kumpipa Solomon kiang ah hon tun uhi.
