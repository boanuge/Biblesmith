1. Huan, Sinar kho kumpipa Amraphelte, Ellasar kho kumpipa Ariokte, Elam kho kumpipa Kedorlamomerte, Goiim kho kumpipa Tidalte dam laiin hichi ahia.
2. Amau bel Sodom kho kumpipa Bera a sim uh. Gomorra kho kumpipa Birsate, Admah kho kumpipa Sinabte, Zeboiim kho kumpipate Smeberte, Bela kho kumpipate leng a sim ua, (huai tuh Zoar ahi).
3. Huan hiai tengteng Siddim guam ah a pang ua, (huai guam Tuipi Al ahita).
4. Kum sawm leh kum nih singin Kedorlaomer nuaiah a om ua, a kum sawm leh kum thumna ah a helta uh.
5. Huchiin, a kum sawm leh kum lina ah Kedorlaomer leh a kianga kumpipa omte a hong ua, Asteroth-karnaim khua ah Rephaimte a that ua, ham khua ah Zusimte a that ua, Kariathaim jangah Emimte a that ua,
6. A omna Seir tangah Elparan phain, Horte a that ua, (El-paran gamdei juala om ahi).
7. Huan, a kik nawn ua, Enmispet a tungua (huai Kades ahita), Amelekte gam a mi teng leh, Amorte, Hazazontamar khua a omte leng, a that nawn uh.
8. Huchiin, Sodom kho kumpipate, Gomorra kho kumpipate, Adma kho kumpipate, Zeboiim kho kumpipate, Bela kho kumpipate a pawt ua, (Bela khua Zoar khua ahita); huan, Siddim guam ah amau sual dingin a pangta uhi.
9. Elam kho kumpipa Kedorlaomerte, Goiim kho kumpipa Tidalte, Sinar kho kumpipate Amraphelte, Ellasa kho kumpipa Ariokte sual dingin; kumpipa li in kumpipate nga a sual uh.
10. Huan, Siddim guam longnai khukin a dim a; huan, Sodom leh Gomorra kho kumpipate a taikek ua, huai laiah a kia ua, ke valte tang ah a tai touta uhi.
11. Huchiin, Sodom leh Gomorra mite sum tengteng leh a nek ding tengteng uh a la ua, a paita uh.
12. Lota, Abram unau tapa, /Sodom khua a om, a sumte toh a la ua, a paita uhi.
13. Huan, mi khat taikhia a hongpaia, Hebru mi Abram a hilha; amah bel Amor mi Mamre tosaw kiang khawngah a om. Mamre Eskol unau leh Aner unau ahia; huai mite Abramte toh pangkhawm ahi uh.
14. Huan, Abramin a ta salin a pi uh chih a jak takin, a inna piang, amah mi, chilsiamsa, za thum leh sawm leh giat a pi khia a, Dan kho phain a delhta.
15. Huan, jan in amau sual dingin amah leh a sikhate a kihawmjak ua, (Hoba Damaska kho mallamah a om).
16. Huan, sum tengteng a honla kik nawn vek a, a ta Lota leh a sum tengteng a honla kik nawn veka, numeite leng a honpi nawna mi dang tengteng toh
17. Huan, Kedorlaomer leh a kianga kumpipa omte that a pai nungin Sodom kumpipa Save guamah Abram dawnin a hoh suka, (Save guam kumpipa guam a hita).
18. Huan, Salem kho kumpipa Melkisedekin tanghou leh uain a honla a; amah bel Pathian Tungnungpen siampu ahi.:
19. Huan, aman Abram a vualjawla. Lei leh van neipa, Pathian Tungnungpen Abram vualjawlin omhen:
20. Na melmate na khuta honpepa, Pathian Tungnungpen, phatin om hen, achia. Huan, Abramin amah bangkim sawm a khat a pia hi.
21. Huan, Sodom kho kumpipan Abram kiangah, mite honpia inla, sumte jaw nang kineih maiin, a chi.
22. Huan, Abramin, Sodom kumpipa kiangah, lei leh van neipa, Toupa, Pathian Tungnungpen lamah ka khut ka jakta hi.
23. Khauzang khat kia leng, khedap khau khat kia leng, na neih himhim kala kei ding, huchilouinjaw, Abram jaw kei hauhsak ahi, na chi kha ding:Tangvalte nek mang leh, ka mi paipihte tantuan kia loujaw kala kei ding; Anerte, Eskolte, Mamreten a tantuan uh la uhen, a chi hi.
24. Tangvalte nek mang leh, ka mi paipihte tantuan kia loujaw kala kei ding; Anerte, Eskolte, Mamreten a tantuan uh la uhen, a chi hi.
