1. Huan, Joseph Aigupta gamah a pisuk ua; huan, Pharo nuaia heutu khat, vengtu pawl heutu, Aigupta mi, Potiphar in, huai ahon pisuk Ismael te khut a kipan a leita hi.
2. Huan, Toupa Joseph kiangah a om a, huchiin, amah mi lamzang tak ahia; huan, a pu Aigupta mi ah a om hi.
3. Huan, a puin, Toupa a kiangah a om a, a thilhih peuhmah Toupan a khut ah a lohchingsak chih a thei a.
4. Huchiin, Joseph in a pu hehpihna a mu a, a na a sem jela: huan, aman a inkuante tungah heutu in a bawla, a neih tengteng a khut ah a koih vek hi.
5. Huan, hichi ahia, a in ah heutu in a bawla, a neih tengteng tunga heutu a abawl nungsiah Joseph jiakin Toupan Aigupta inkuanpihte a vualjawlta hi; in lam leh gam lama a neih tengteng Toupa vual a jawl jel hi.
6. Huchiin a neih tengteng Joseph khut ah a koiha, a kianga thil om himhim a theihpih hetkei hi, a annek kia loungal a theikei hi. Huan, Joseph lah mi hoih mahmah ahia, a mel mah leng a hoih hi.
7. Huan, huai nungin hichi ahia, a pu ji in Joseph a ngaih eta; huan, hon luppih in, achia.
8. Himahleh a ut keia, a pu ji kiangah, ka pu in hiai inn ah ka kiangah bangbang a om a chih lah a thei ngal keia, a neih tengteng ka khut ah a koih veka;
9. Hiai in ah kei sanga lian jaw kuamah a om kei uh; a ji na hihjiakin amah kia loungal ka lakah bangmah it a neikei hi; huchiin bangchin hiai khelhna liantak hihin Pathian tungah thil ka hihkhial thei dia? Achia.
10. Huan, hichi ahia, ni tengin Joseph houpih jel mah leh, amah luppih lamthu hiam, amah kianga om lamthu hiam a limsak nuam kei hi.
11. Huan, huchilai khawngin hichi ahia, ana sem dingin in sungah a va luta; huan, huai laiah in a mi himhim kuamah a om kei uhi.
12. Huchiin, aman honluppih in, chiin, a puansilh in ana mana: huan, aman a puansilh a khut ah a taisan a, a taikheta hi.
13. Huan hichi ahia, a puansilh a khuta nusia in a taikhia chih a theih takin,
14. A ina mite a sama, a kiang uah, en un, hon zahlak sak dingin Hebru mi khat I kiangua honpi eita ve; hon luppih tumin ka kiangah a honglut a, huchiin ngaihtakin ka kikou ta hi:
15. Huan, hichi ahia, aw suaha ngaihtaka ka kikou a jak takin hiai ka kiangah a puansilh a taisana, a taikheta hi, achia.
16. Huan, Joseph pu a hongtun masiah a puansilh a kiangah ana koih gige hi.
17. Huan, a kiangah hichibang thu ana gena, Hebru sikha I kianga na honpi, hon zah laksak tumin ka kiangah a hong luta:
18. Huan, hichi ahia, aw suaha ka kikou takin hiai a puansilh ka kiangah nusiain a taikheta hi, achia.
19. Huan, hichi ahia, Joseph puin, a jiin a kianga, na sikha in hichiin a honbawl, chia, a thugen a jakin, a heh guiha.
20. Huchiin, Joseph puin amah a pia, suangkulh ah, lal suangkulh tang hena a omna uah a khumta a: huchiin huaiah suangkulh ah a omta hi.
21. Ahihhangin, Toupa bel Joseph kiangah a om jela, a tungah a chinga, suangkulh vengmi mah leng a hehpihsak a.
22. Huchiin, suangkulh vengmiten suangkulha tang omte tengteng Joseph khut ah a kemsak tavek ua; huchiin, huailaia a hihpeuh uh Joseph bel a hihpa ahi jela.Toupa Joseph kiangah a oma, a thilhih peuhmah Toupan a lohchinsak nak jiakin suangkulh vengmi in a khut a thil om himhim a en nuam takei hi.
23. Toupa Joseph kiangah a oma, a thilhih peuhmah Toupan a lohchinsak nak jiakin suangkulh vengmi in a khut a thil om himhim a en nuam takei hi.
