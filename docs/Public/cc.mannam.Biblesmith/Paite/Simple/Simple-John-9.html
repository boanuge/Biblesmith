1. Huan, Jesun a pai kawmin a pian tunga kipan mittaw khat a mu a.
2. Huan, a nungjuiten a kiangah, Rabbi, mitto saa a pian kuan ahia thil hihkhial, hia mi hia, a nu leh a pan? a chi ua, a dong ua.
3. Jesun, Hiai min a hih khial kei, a nu leh a pan leng a hihkhail kei uh; Pathian nasepte a tung a latna ding ahi jaw.
4. Sun a hihlaiin honsawlpa na I sep ding uhi; jan hi dekdekta ahi, huai hun chiangin kuamahin na a sem theikei ding uh.
5. Khovela ka om sungin khovel vak ka hi, a chi a, a dawnga.
6. Huchibanga a gen khitin, leiah chil a sia a, chilin tungman a bawla, huan tungman tuh a mit ah a nuha, huan, a kiangah, Siloam dil ah vasil in, a chi a. (Siloam tuh, Sawl, chihna a hi)
7. Huan, avapaia, a sila, mitvakin a hongkik nawnta hi.
8. Huchiin, a vengte leh tumaa khutdoha mumuten, Hiai mi tu kawma khutdoh dohpa ahi ka hia? a chi ua.
9. Midangin, Amah ahi vo oi, a chi ua. Midangin, A hi kei, a kibatpih ahi jaw, a chi ua. Aman, Huai amah ka hi, a chi a.
10. Huan, amau a kiangah, Na mit bangchin ahia a hong vak? a chi ua.
11. Aman, Jesu a chih un tungman a bawla, ka mitah a nuih a, Siloam dil ah vahoh in silin, a honchi a, huchiin, ka vahoha, ka sil a, mitvakna ka muta ahi, a chi.
12. Huan, amau a kiangah, Amah kaw om ahia? a chi ua. Aman, Ka theikei, a chi a.
13. Huai maa mittopa tuh Pharisaite kiangah a honpi ua.
14. Jesun tungman a bawla, a mit a hihvak ni Khawlni ahi a.
15. Huchiin, Pharisaiten a mit a hongvak takdan a dong nawn ua. Aman a kiang uah, Ka mit ah tungman a koiha, ka sil, ka mu theita maimah hi, a chi a.
16. Huan, Pharisai khenkhatin, Huai mi tuh Khawlni a tan louh jiakin, Pathiana kipan ahi kei, a chi ua. Khenkhatin bel, Mi khialin bangchin hichi bang thillamdang a hih thei dia? a chi ua. Huan, a kikhen ua, a kilangta uh.
17. Huan, mittopa kiangah, Na mit a hihvak, nang bangchi bangin amah na ngaihtuah? a chi nawn ua. Huan, aman, Jawlnei ahi, a chi a.
18. Himahleh, Judaten a mit hongvak ta nu leh pa a sap masiah uh, mittaw a nahi hima, a mit a hongvakta chih, a tungtang thu a taksang kei uh.
19. Huan, a nu leh a pa kiangah, Hiai mi, na tapa uh mitto saa piang ahi na chih uh ahi hia? Bangchin ahia tua a muh theih tak? Chiin a dong ua.
20. Huan, a nu leh a pan. Hiai tuh ka tapa uh ahi chih ka thei uh, mitto sain a piang chih leng ka thei uh;
21. himahleh, bangchin ahia tua honmuh theih tak chih ka theikei uh, a mit hihvakpa leng ka thei sam kei uh; amah dong un; piching a hina; amah thu aman gen leh ake, a chi ua, a dawng uh.
22. Judate a kihtak jiak un a nu leh a pan tuh huai thute a gen uhi; Judaten, huai ma himin leng, Amah jaw Kris ahi, chia gum a om leh, kikhopna in a nong ding chih a lemsa chit ngal ua.
23. Huai jiakin ahi, a nu leh a pan, Piching ahi, amah dong un, a chih uh.
24. Huchiin, mittopa a sam nawn ua, a kiangah, Pathian phatin hiai mi jaw mi khial ahi chih ka thei uh, a chi ua.
25. Huan, aman, Mi khial ahi hia hi lou ka theikei, thu khat ka thei, tumain ka mit a taw a, tuin ka mu theita hi, a chi a, a dawng a.
26. Huan, amau a kiang ah, Na tungah bangchin ahia a hih? Na mit bangchin ahia a hihvak? a chi ua.
27. Huan, aman a kiang uah, Denin kon hilhta a ka, na thei nuam ngal kei ua; bang achia thei nuam nawn na hi ua? Nou leng a nungjuite hihsam na ut ua hia? Chiin, a dawng a.
28. Huan, amau atai ua, Nang amah nungjui na hi, kou jaw Mosi nungjuite ka hi uh.
29. Pathianin Mosi a houpih chih ka thei uh, hiai mi phiangsan koia hongkipan ahia ka theihsak kei uh, a chi ua.
30. Huan, huai min a kiang uah, Huaipen tak ahi, a lamdanna, koia hongkipan ahia na theih louh uh, kamit lah a hihvak ngei ngala.
31. Pathianin mi khial thu a ngaikhe kei nak chih I thei uh; himahleh, kuapeuhin Pathian a limsaka, a deihlam a hih leh, Pathianin amah a ngaikhe nak hi.
32. Khovel om tunga kipan kuaman mitto saa piang mit a hihvak nawn chih a kithei ngei kei.
33. Hiai mi Pathian kianga pawt a hih keileh, bangmah a hih theikei ding, a chi a, a dawnga.
34. Huan, amau a kiangah, Nang khial lumleta piang naka, nang non hilh vial maw? a chi ua, a dawng ua. Huchiin, amah tuh a delhkhe ta uh.
35. Huan, a delh khia uh chih Jesun a naja a; huan, a zong khia a, Pathian Tapa na gingta hia? a chi a.
36. Huan, aman, Pu, amah kua ahia, ka gintak theihna din? a chi a, a dawnga.
37. Jesun a kiangah, Amah na mu khinta a, nang honhoupihpa ahi, a chi a.
38. Huan, aman, Toupa, ka gingta hi, a chi a, amah a bia a.
39. Huan, Jesun, Thukhen dingin hiai khovel ah hong ka hi, mittawte a mit uh a hong vakna ding leh, mitvakte a mit uh a hongtawtna dingin, a chi a.
40. Huan, a kianga Pharisai omten, huai thu a jak un, a kiangah, Kou leng mittaw ka hi sam u maw? a chi ua.Huan, Jesun a kiang uah, Mittaw anahi tale uchiin khelhna na neikei ding uh, himahleh, Ka mu thei uh, tuin na chih jiakun na khelhna uh a om gige hi.
41. Huan, Jesun a kiang uah, Mittaw anahi tale uchiin khelhna na neikei ding uh, himahleh, Ka mu thei uh, tuin na chih jiakun na khelhna uh a om gige hi.
