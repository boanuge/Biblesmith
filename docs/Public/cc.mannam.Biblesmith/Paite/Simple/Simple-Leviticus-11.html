1. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi leh Aron a houpih a,
2. Israel suante houpih unla, Leitung a gamsa tengteng lak a na nek ding uh thilhingte hiai ahi.
3. Gamsa lak a chin ka, chin ka paisuak, eknel hai chi peuhmah na ne thei ding uh.
4. Himahleh eknel hai hiam, chin ka paisuak hiam lak ate hichibangte zaw na nek ding uh ahi kei; sangawngsau, eknel hai leleng, a chin a kak louh ziak in; nou ding in a nin ahi.
5. Huan, zupi, eknel a hai na a, a hihhang in a chin a kat louh ziak in; nou ding in a nin ahi.
6. Huan, zusapi, eknel a hai na a, a hihhang in a chin a kat louh ziak in, nou ding in a nin ahi.
7. Huan, vok, a chin a ka a, a ka paisuak na a, a hihhang in eknel a hai louh ziak in; nou ding in a nin ahi.
8. A sa uh na ne ding uh ahi kei, a luang uh leng na khoih ding uh ahi kei, nou ding in a nin ahi.
9. Tui sung a teng tengteng ah hiaite na ne thei ding uh: tuipite, luite, tui a om khate leh lipte nei photphot na ne thei ding uh.
10. Tui sung a teng tengteng leh tui a thil hing om tengteng lak ah tuipi ate, lui ate, khate leh lipte neilou photphot zaw, nou ding in thil kihhuai ahi a,
11. Nou ding in thil kihhuai ahi ding ua; a sa uh na ne ding uh ahi kei, a luangte uh thil kihhuai a na neih ding uh ahi.
12. Tui a khate leh lipte neilou photphot zaw nou ding in thil kihhuai ahi.
13. Huan, vasa lak ah hiaite thil kihhuai a na neih ding uh ahi; nek ding ahi kei hi, thil kihhuai ahi uh: muvanlai, musane leh tuivapi,
14. Muvompi, musi a chi himhimte;
15. Va-ak chiteng a chi himhimte;
16. Simbu, vabak, tuipi vasate, mu chi himhim;
17. Simbutanglip, ngagal, simbupi,
18. Tuivapi, vabukngak, musanepi,
19. Inntungva, dil vasa a chi himhim, huihtun leh bakte.
20. Kha nei khe li a pai chi peuhmah nou ding in thil kihhuai ahi.
21. Himahleh kha nei, khe li a pai chi leng leitung a tawmna ding a a khezung tung lam ua khe nei tengteng lak ah hiaite na ne thei ding uh;
22. Hiaite leng na ne thei ding uh: khau a chi himhim, ekkhingne khaupe a chi himhim, khuangbai a chi himhim leh khaupe a chi himhimte.
23. Himahleh kha nei, khe li nei, a pai a pai photphot nou ding in thil kihhuai ahi vek hi.
24. Huan, hiaite ziak in na buah ding uh: kuapeuh, sa luang khoih peuhmah nitak lam tan in a buah ding ua;
25. Huan, kuapeuh huai luang lami peuhmah in a puante uh a sawp ding uh ahi, nitak lam tan in a buah ding uh.
26. Gamsa chin ka a, ka paisuaklou, eknel leng hailou chi tengteng nou a ding in thil nin ahi a; kuapeuh huaite khoih a nin ding uh.
27. Huan, bok a pai tengteng lak a khebom a pai peuhmahte nou ding in thil nin ahi a; a luang uh khoih peuhmah nitaklam tan in a nin ding uh.
28. Huan, a luang lami peuhmah in a puante uh a sawp ding uh ahi, nitaklam tan in a nin ding ua; nou ding in thil nin ahi.
29. Huan, lei a a vak a vakthoh chi lak a nou ding a thil nin hiaite ahi: buite, chikpite, thankikpi, a chi himhim,
30. Innhik, tangteuh, thankik dokdawn leh zusa.
31. Hiaite a vak a vak thoh chi tengteng lak a nou ding a thil ninte ahi uh: kuapeuh in a sisa hiaite a khoih uleh nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh.
32. Huan, a sih ua bangpeuh a delhkhum uh, a nin zel ding; vanzat photphot sing a bawl tui-um-belsuan hiam, puan hiam, savun hiam, sai-ip hiam leng hi hen aw, tui a diah ding ahi, huan, nitak lam tan in a nin ding; huai khit chiang in a siangthou ta ding hi.
33. Leibel, a bang mahmah a kiakna peuhpeuh na hihtam zel ding uh ahi, a sung a om tengteng a nin ahi.
34. Huai bel a nek chi om tengteng leng huai a om tui a diahsa himhim, thil nin ahi ding; huan, dawn chi, dawn theih chi tengteng leng, huai bel a himhim zaw thil nin ahi ding.
35. Huan, thil bangpeuh a luang up a banglai peuh in a delhkhum sa photphot thil nin ahi zel ding; tanghou kanna hiam, kan lumna bel hiam leng hihtap vek ding ahi, a nin khin a, nou a ding in thil nin ahi ding.
36. Himahleh tuikhuk hiam, tuibem, tui chin khawm hiam a nin kei ding a, a luang uh sikkhak peuhmah a nin ding.
37. Huan, haichi, chin ding himhim zaw a luang uh a banglai peuh in na delhkhum ta leh, a nin kei.
38. Himahleh haichi, tui a diahsa a luang un a koilai peuh in delh leh, nou ding in thil nin ahi.
39. Gamsa na nek theih chi himhim u leng si leh, a luang khoih mi peuhmah nitak lam tan in a nin ding.
40. A sa ne peuhmah in a puante uh sawp ding uh ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding: huan, a luang lami in leng a puante a sawp ding ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding.
41. Huan, ganhing leitung a a bok a vak chi tengteng thil kihhuai ahi ua, nek ding ahi kei.
42. A tholh a kitholh chi leh khe li a pai chi hiam, khe tampi nei chi hiam, leitung a bok a vak chi himhim na ne ding uh ahi kei, thil kihhuai ahi ngal ua.
43. A bok a vak chi himhim in na kihihthanghuai ding uh ahi kei, na kihihbuah louhna ding un, huaite in na kihihnin ding uh ahi kei.
44. Kei zaw TOUPA na Pathian uh ka hi ngal a; huaiziak in kisiangthou sak unla, siangthou in om un; kei lah ka siangthou ngal a, leitung a teng, a bok a vak, a bang chi mah in kihihbuah kei un.
45. N Pathian uh hi ding a Aigupta gam a kipan a nou hon pikhia TOUPA lah ka hi ngal a: kei ka siangthou ngal a, huaiziak in nou leng na siangthou ding uh ahi.
46. Hiai pen gamsa leh vasate leh tui a teng chi thil hing chiteng leh lei a a bok a vak chi chitengte tungthu a dan ding ahi.Siangthou leh siangthoulou khenna ding leh, gamsa nek theih chi leh gamsa nek theihlouh chi khenna ding in, chi in, chi in.
47. Siangthou leh siangthoulou khenna ding leh, gamsa nek theih chi leh gamsa nek theihlouh chi khenna ding in, chi in, chi in.
