1. Kikai khawm un, ahi, kikai khawm un, zumna neilou nam aw;
2. Thupiak a kuan khiak main, buhvai banga ni a bei main, na tung ua TOUPa hehna kihtakhuai a hongtun main, na tung ua TOUPa hehna ni a hongtun main.
3. A vaihomna hih, nou leia mi migite tengteng, TOUPA zong un; diktatna zong un, migitna zong un; TOUPA hehna ni ah selin na om kha thei uhi.
4. Gaza lah nutsiatin a om dia, Askelon hihgawpin; suntang laiin Asdod a hawl khe ding ua, Ekron a kizung kalh ding.
5. Tuipi piaua tengte tung a gik hi, Kret namte; TOUPa thu noute demna ahi, Kanaan aw, Philistiate gam; a teng om loudingin ka honhihgawp ding.
6. Huchiin tuipi, piaute louhingnouna ahi dinga, belamchingte a dia inte leh belamhonte a dia huangte toh.
7. Huan huai piau bel Juda inko omlaite ading ahi ding; huai laiah a belamhonte uh a vak ding uh; nitak lamin Askelon inte ah a kual ding uh; TOUPa a Pathian un amaute a vehin, sala a tang uh pi khe nawn ding a hih jiakin/
8. A kigitpihte uh tunga kiliansaka, ka mite a kouna uh, Moab kouna leh, Ammon tate gensiatna ka zata hi.
9. Huaijiakin sepaihte TOUPa, Israel Pathian ka hin bangin, Chihtaktakin Moab Sodom bang ahi dinga, Ammon tate Gomorra bang, loubineite leh chikhukte tan leh segigea omna; ka mite laka omlaiten a lok ding ua, ka nama omlaiten a maute a luah ding uh.
10. Sepaihte Toupa mite tunga a kiletsak ua, a kou jiak un, a kisaktheihna jiakun hiai a nei ding uhi.
11. Amau tungah TOUPa a lauhuai ding a; lei pathiante tengteng kialin a kiamsak ding; miten amah a be ding ua, mi chih amau akipanin, namte tuikulh gam tengteng na ngawn.
12. Nou Ethiopiate leng, ka namsaua thaha om ding na hi uhi.
13. Huan aman mal douin a khut a lik khe dinga, Assuria a hihse ding; huan Ninevi a kisegawp sak ding, huchiin gamdai bangin a keu ding.
14. Huan belamte a lai ah a kual ding ua, namte ganta tengteng; vangawngsau leh sakuh achialtungnungahte khawng a giak ding ua; tohletah a aw in la a sa ding uh; sangkil-ahte siatgopna a om ding; sidar singa nasepte a hihsiat jiakin.Hiai khopi nuamsa pilvangloutaka tenga, a lungtanga, Kei ka hi, kei lou kuamah dang a om kei; chi ahi; bangchidana hongsegawpa, gan tate adia kualna ding hi ahia; kuapeuh a kianga paiin a luisanin, a khut a lehkhum ding hi.
15. Hiai khopi nuamsa pilvangloutaka tenga, a lungtanga, Kei ka hi, kei lou kuamah dang a om kei; chi ahi; bangchidana hongsegawpa, gan tate adia kualna ding hi ahia; kuapeuh a kianga paiin a luisanin, a khut a lehkhum ding hi.
