1. Huailouin leng Israel heutute tungtangah sunna la phuah ahi:
2. Na nu bang ahia a hih? humpinelkaipi ahi; humpinelkaite lakah a kuala, humpinelkai lunlaite lakah a noute a vaklian a.
3. Huan a noute lakah khat a vakteia; humpinelkai lunlai a honghi: huan samat a siama, mite a gai.
4. Namten leng tanchin a ja ua: a kokhuk uah matin aom: huan Aigupta gamah sikkawiin amah a pi ua.
5. Huchiin a ngaka, a lametna a vuaksuak chih a theihin, a noute lakah a dang khat a la a, humpinelkai lunlaiin a bawl hi.
6. Huan humpinelkai lakah a pai touin a pai suk a, humpinelkai lunlai a honghi: huan samat a siama, mite a gai hi.
7. Huan a innpite uh a theia, a khopite uh a hihgawpa; huan gam tuh a segawpa, a humham husa jiakin, huaia tengteng.
8. Huchiin bialte akipanin ning chiha namten amah a dou ua: huan a tungah len uh a jak ua; a kokhuk uah matin a omta hi.
9. Huan sikkawitein bawm sungah amah a koih ua; Babulon kumpipa kiangah a pi ua; kulh muanhuaite sungah a pi lut ua, huchia Israel tangsangte tunga a aw a kijak nawn louhna dingin.
10. Na nu grepgui bang a ahi, na sisana, tuite kianga suan: tui tampite jiakin gahthei leh hiangtein a dim.
11. Huan vaihawmna pote lalchiang sikchiang khauh a nein, huan nahbawk pha lakah a sandan uh hihsangin a om hi, huchiin ahiang tampite u toh sangpia muhin a om uhi.
12. Himahleh thangpaihna a bohkhiakin a oma, leia paihin a oma, suahlam huihin a gah a mut keu: a kung khaukte a kitana, a keu; meiin a maute a kang beita.
13. Huan tuin gamdai ah, gam keu leh tui om lou ah phuh in a om,Huchiin ahianga sikchiangtea kipan mei a pai khia, a gah a hulbeia, huchiin amahah vaihawm dingin lalchiang ding sikchiang khauh a om kei hi. Hiai kahla ahi, kahla ading ahi ding hi.
14. Huchiin ahianga sikchiangtea kipan mei a pai khia, a gah a hulbeia, huchiin amahah vaihawm dingin lalchiang ding sikchiang khauh a om kei hi. Hiai kahla ahi, kahla ading ahi ding hi.
