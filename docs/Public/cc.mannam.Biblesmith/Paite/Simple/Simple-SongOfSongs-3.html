1. Janin ka lupna tungah amah ka khain a it ka zonga: ka zong a, himahleh amah ka mu kei.
2. Ka thou dia, khopi kimah ka pai dinga, kongzingte leh lam liante ah, amah ka khain a it ka zong ding, ka chi a ka zonga, himahleh amah ka mu kei.
3. Khopia vialpai vengmiten a honmu ua: akiang uah, Amah ka khain a it na mu uhia? ka chi a.
4. Amaute ka paisan sawt lou chik ahi, huan amah ka khain a it ka muta: ka pai sak kei, ka nu in sunga, kei honpai nu dantansunga amah ka pi lut ma siah.
5. Zuktal leh gama zukpitein ka hon ngen hi, Aw Jerusalem tanute, ka it hihbuai kei unla, phawng kei un, a lungkim matan.
6. Gamdaia kipana meikhu kitung banga hongpai hiai kua ahia, murra leh begaw kinuha, sumsinmi nuhngou vui toh?
7. Ngaiin, Solomon lupna ahi; a kimah mi hat sawmguk, Israel mi hatte a kipan a om uh.
8. Namsau a tawi chiat ua, kidounaa kivan ahi uh: Mi chihin a pheipi tngah a namsau a tawi chiat ua, jana launa jiakin.
9. Kumpipa Solomonin Lebanon singin kangtalai a kibawla.
10. Huaia khuamte dangkain a bawla, huai a nuai dangkaeng, a tutphah a sandup, huai a laizang, Jerusalem tanute akipana itnain phah ahi.Aw nou Zion tanute, pawt khia unla, kumpipa Solomon en un, a zineih ni leh a lungtang kipahna nia, a nun amah lallukhu a khuksak toh.
11. Aw nou Zion tanute, pawt khia unla, kumpipa Solomon en un, a zineih ni leh a lungtang kipahna nia, a nun amah lallukhu a khuksak toh.
