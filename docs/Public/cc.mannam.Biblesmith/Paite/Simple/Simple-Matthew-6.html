1. Na thil hoih hihte uh mi muh dingin, mihing mitmuha hihlou dingin pilvang un; huchilouin jaw na Pa uh vana om kiangah kipahman na neikei ding uh.
2. Huchinin, mi kianga thilpiak na piakin, mi lepchiahten mi phat a loh theihna ding ua. kikhopna in khawng, kongzing khawnga a hih nak bang un na ma ah pengkul mut ken. Chihtaktakin ka honhilh ahi. A kipahman uh a mu ta uh.
3. Nang jaw mi kianga thilpiak napiakin, na khut taklamin a hih na khut veilamin thei dah heh.
4. Na thilpiak tuh a guka a om theihna din: huchiin na Pa a guka mupan tuh a hondin ding hi.
5. Huan, na thum chiangun, mi lepchiah te bangin na hih ding uh ahi kei; min a muh theihna ding un, kikhopnain bangah kongzing kathuam laitak bangah ding kawmin thum a uttuan sese uh. Chihtaktakin ka honhilh ahi, a kipahman uh a muta uh.
6. Nang jaw na thumin, na dantan sunggil ah lut inla, na kong khak inla, na Pa a guka om kiang ah thumin huchiin napa a guka honmuin a hondin ding.
7. Huan, na thum un, Jentelte bangin gentuan mawkmawk kei un: a pau tam jiak ua jak a om ding a kisa uh.
8. Huaijiakin, amau bangin om kei un, amah na nget ma ua na Pa un bangbang ahia na khawkna uh a thei gige ngala.
9. Huchuun nou jaw hichibangin thum un: Ka Pa uh vana om. na min ki zahtak hen.
10. Na gam hongtung hen. Na deihlam vana a kihih bangin, lei ah leng kihih hen.
11. Tunin ka nek khop uh an honpia in.
12. Kabatte uh honngaidam in.. kouleng ka leibate uh ka ngaihdamtak bangun.
13. Khemn ah honpilut kenla, gilou lakah honhu jaw in. chiin.
14. Mi a tatleknate uh na ngaihdam uleh, na Pa uh vana omin leng a hongaidam sam ding.
15. Himahleh, mite a tatlekna uh ngaihdam kei ngal un, na Pa un na tatlekna teuh a ngaidam sam kei ding.
16. An na ngolh un len, mi lepchiahte bang in ngui mel po kei un: amau jaw mi mit muha an ngawla kilat theihna ding un, amai uh a gum sak nak uh ahi. chihtaktakin ka honhilh ahi, a kipahman uh a muta uh.
17. Nou jaw, an na ngolh chiangun na lu uh sathau nilh unla, na mai uh phiat unla;
18. huchiin, na Pa uh aguka om mitmuh ah loungal mihing mitmuh ah an ngawl na bang kei ding ua; huchiin, na Pa uh aguka mupan tuh a hondin ding.
19. Leia, leikha leh tuiekin a nek siatna ah, gutaten a phen vang ua gukna uah gou kikhol kei un;
20. van ah, leikha leh tuiekin a nek siat louhna ah, gutaten a phen ua a guk sam louhna uah, gou kikol jaw un.
21. Na gou omna peuh uah na lungtang u leng a om ding.
22. Sapum khawnvak tuh mit ahi; huchia namit a chim leh na sapum a pumin a tan suak ding.
23. Na mit a siat le tuh na sapum a pumin a mial khin vek ding. Huchiin na sunga vak tuh mial hileh, mial hina tel inchia.
24. Khuamahin heutu nih na a sem kop theikei; khat huain khat a it dinga; a hihkeileh, khat gumin khat a simmoh ding. pathian leh sum na na sem kop theikei ding uh.
25. Huaijiakin, ka honhilh ahi. Bang ahia ka nek ding, bang ahia ka dawn ding, chiin, na hinna thu uleh. Bang ahia ka silh ding, Bang ahia ka ten ding, chiin, na sapum thu uh lunghimoh kei un. Nek ding sang in hinna a thupi zo ka hia? Silh leh ten sangin sapum leng a thupi zo ka hia?
26. Tungleng vasate en un; buh a tuh kei uh, a at kei uh, buh in ah leng a sek lut sam kei uh; himahleh, na Pa uh vana omin amau a vak hi. Noute amau sangaa nakpitaka manpha jaw hilou na hi umaw?
27. Huan, noute lakah lunghihmoh jiaka kuan ahia amah dung lam tong khata hihsang tuan thei?
28. Huan; silh leh ten thu bang dinga lunghimoh na hi ua? Gamlaklili pak khandan hoihtakin ngaihtuah un; na a sem kei ua, khau leng a khek kei uh;
29. himahleh, ka honhilh ahi, Solomon hilhial leng athupina tengteng a akizepin hia lili pak khat phain leng zepin a om kei.
30. huchiin, gamlak loupa, tuni banga hinga, a jinga thuka khul mai ding bawn Pathianin huchi lawmlawma a zep in tuh, aw nou gintawnte, silh leh ten honpe zo tham lou ding hia?
31. Huchiin. Bang ahia i nek ding, ahikeileh. Bang ahia i dawn ding? ahihkei leh. Banga ahia i dawn ding? a hihkeileh, Bangsilin ahia i om dingm hiam chiin lunghimoh ken un.
32. Jentelten huai tenteng a zong uhi. Huai tengteng na khawk uh chih na Pa uh van a omin a thei gige hi.
33. A gam leh a diktatna tuh zong masa jaw un, huchiin huai teng tuh a honpiak behlap ding hi.Huchiin, a jiinga ding lunghimoh keiun, a jingin amah a ding a kilunghimoh tawm ding jiakin. Nikhatading in ni khat gimthuak a hun hi.
34. Huchiin, a jiinga ding lunghimoh keiun, a jingin amah a ding a kilunghimoh tawm ding jiakin. Nikhatading in ni khat gimthuak a hun hi.
