2Chronicles (1/36)  →
1. Huan, David tapa Solomon bel a gamsungah hatsakin a oma; TOUPA a Pathian a kiangah a oma, nakpi mahmah in a thupi sak hi
2. Huan, Solomonin Israelte tengteng, sepaih sang heutute, za heutute, vaihawm mite, Israel gam tengteng a milian, inkuan a intekpen tengteng a houpiha.
3. Huchiin, Solomon leh kikhawmte tengteng Gibeon khuaa mun sangah a hoh ua; huailaiah TOUPA sikha Mosiin gamdaia a bawl Pathian biakna buk a om hi.
4. Pathian bawm bel Davidin a nabawl khitsa munah Kiriathjearim a kipan in Davidin ana latou khina; Jerusalemah a koihna ding puanin anajak khitsa him ahi.
5. Huan, Hur ta Uri tapa Bazalel bawl dal maitam TOUPA biakna buk chinah a om behap a; huan, Solomon leh mipiten a vajuan ua.
6. Huan, Solomon bel kihoupihna puanin china dal maitam omna TOUPA maah a hoh tou a, halmang thillat sang khat a lana.
7. Huai janin Solomon kiangah Pathian akilaka, a kiangah, Bang ahia ka honpiak ding hon ngenin, a chi a.
8. Huan, Solomonin Pathian kiangah, Ka pa David kiangah hehpihna thupi tak nalangsaka, a sikin kumpipan non nabawltaa
9. Huchiin aw TOUPA Pathian, ka pa David kianga na thuchiam hihpichingin, nang leivui zah takmipite kumpipain non na bawlta a.
10. Huchiin hiai mipite maa lut leh pawta ka om theihna dingin pilna leh theihna hon piain; na mi hiai zahzah kua ahia vaihawm sak thei ding? A chi a.
11. Huan, Pathianin Solomon kiangah, hichibang lungsim na puta, sum leh pai te, hausakna te, pahtawina te, nang honmudah te hinna te ngen loua, dam sawtna te leng ngen loua, a tung ua kumpipaa ka hon bawl na mite vaina hawm sak theihna dinga pilna leh theihna te na hon nget jawk jiakin, pilna leh theihna piak na hita hi:
12. Huan, na maa kumpipaten leng a naneih ngeingei louh uh, nang nunga leng a neih het louh ding uh sum leh pai te, hausakna te, pahtawina te ka hon pe lai ding, a chi a.
13. Huchiin Solomon mun sang china biakbuk omna Gibeon khuaa a zin Jerusalem ah apaia, Israel tungah vai a hawmta hi.
14. Huanm Solomonin kangtalaite leh sakol tung tuang mite a kaikhawm eka; kangtalai sang khat leh za li leh sakol tung tuang mi sing khat leh sang nih a neia, huaite kangtalai koihna khua ah te, Jerusalem khuaa kumpipa omna ah te a koih a.
15. Huan, kumpipan Jerusalem khua ah dangkaeng leh dangkasikte suang bang mai chih khopin a koih eka, sidar singte leng phaijanga theipi sing bang lela chih khopin a koih lai hi.
16. Huan, Solomon neih sakolte Aigupta gam akipana lak khiak ahi; kumpipa sumsinmiten huaite a honhonin a nakhak jel ua, a hona lei jel ahi.Aigupte gama kangtalaite bel dangkasik sekel za guk jelin a tungsak jel ua; huan, sakolte dangkasik sekel ja leh sawmnga jelin; huchibang mahin Hitte kumpipate tengteng leh Suria kumpipate leng a tutsak sek uhi.
17. Aigupte gama kangtalaite bel dangkasik sekel za guk jelin a tungsak jel ua; huan, sakolte dangkasik sekel ja leh sawmnga jelin; huchibang mahin Hitte kumpipate tengteng leh Suria kumpipate leng a tutsak sek uhi.
