1Corinthians (1/16)  →
1. Paula, Pathian deihlama Jesu Kris sawltaka sap leh i unaupa Sostheni,
2. Korinth khua a Pathian saptuam omte, Kris Jesu a sehtuam, gam chiha I Toupa, amau leh eite Toupa, Jesu Kris min lou tengteng toh misiangthou hi dinga sapte kiangah:
3. Pathian I Pa leh Toupa Jesu Kris kianga kipan hehpihna leh lemna na kiang uah om hen.
4. Kris Jesu a Pathian hehpihna na kiang ua hongkipia jiakin na tung thu uah Pathian kiangah kipahthu ka gen gige hi;
5. Bangkimah thugen peuhmah ah leh theihna chi tengah, amaha hihhauhin na om uh.
6. Huchibangin Kris thu ka theihsak pen noumau ah hihkipin a om hi;
7. Huan, i Toupa Jesu Kris kilakna ngak kawmin, khalam thilpiak himhim na tasam kei uhi;
8. Aman, i Toupa Jesu Kris ni chia gensiat beia na omna ding un, a tawp phain a honhihkip behlap ding hi.
9. Pathian, a Tapa Jesu Kris i Toupa kithuahpihnaa nou honsam lutpa, a muanhuai hi.
10. Huan, unaute aw, na vek un thu kibang na gen ua, na lak ua kikhenna himhim a om louhna dingin lungsim munkhat leh ngaihtuah munkhata diktaka na kizop chinten jawkna ding un Toupa Jesu Kris minin kon ngen ahi.
11. Ka unaute aw, na tungtangthu uh, na lak uah kiselna bang a om chih Kloi inkuanpihten honhilh ua.
12. Hiai ka chihna ahi, mi tengtengin, Kei Paula pawl ka hi, Kei jaw Apolla pawl ka hi, Kei jaw Kifa pawl ka hi, Kei jaw Kris pawl ka hi, na chih vengvung uh.
13. Kris khenin a om ahia? Paula na sik un kilhdenin a om ahia? Ahihkeileh Paula mina baptisin na om ua hia?
14. Krispa leh Gaia loungal na lak uah mi kuamah kon baptis louh jiakin Pathian kiangah kipahthu ka gen jel ahi;
15. Huchi hikei leh jaw min keimah mina baptis na hi uh a chi kha ding uhi.
16. Stephan inkuanpihte bel ka baptis ngeia, tuate loujaw mi dang ka baptis hiam ka theikei.
17. Kris in baptis dingin honsawl ngal keia, Tanchin Hoih gen dingin honsawl jaw ahi; thu pila gen louin, huchilou injaw Kris kros thilhihtheihna bangmah lou a suaksak kha ding uhi.
18. Kros thu jaw mangthang lelte adin haina ahi ngala; eite hotdam lelte adin bel Pathian thilhihtheihna ahi jaw hi.
19. Mi pil pilna ka hihmang dinga, lemthei lemtheihna leng ka paikhe ding, chih gelh ahi ngal a.
20. Mipil koiah ahia? Laigelhmi koiah ahia? Hiai khovela selhatmi koiah ahia? Pathianin khovel pilna haihuai ahi chih a theisak kei ahia?
21. Pathian pilna ah bel, khovelin a pilna zanga Pathian a theihtheih louh nungin thugen haihuai zanga a gingtate hotdam tuh a kipahlam ahi.
22. Judaten chiamtehnate a phut nak ua, Grikten pilna a zong nak uhi.
23. Eiten bel Kris kilhden thu i gen jel ua; huai tuh Judate adin pukna, Jentelte adin haina ahi a;
24. Himahleh Judate leng hi hen aw, Grikte leng hi heh, sapte adin jaw Kris, Pathian thilhihtheihna leh Pathian pilna pen ahi.
25. Pathian haina mihing sangin a piljaw a, Pathian hatlouhna leng huai mihing sangin a hat jaw nak nak hi.
26. Unaute aw, a honsapna ngaihtuah dih ua, salama mi pil tampite, mi hat tampite, mi hoih pipite, sap a hih louhdan uh:
27. Himahleh, Pathian in khovel thil haite a tel jaw a, mi pilte a zahlaksak theihna dingin; huan Pathianin khovel thil hatloute a tela, a hatte a zahlak sak theihna dingin;
28. Khovel thil niam leh thil siammohhuaite, thil omloute toh tanin Pathian in a tela, thil omte bangmah loua a bawl theihna dingin;
29. Huchiin, Pathian maah mi kuamah a kiuangsak theikei ding uh.
30. Amah tuh Kris-a noute hinna nak ahi; huan Pathianin Kris tuh i pilna bang, I diktatna bang, i sianthouna bang, i tatna bang hi dingin a bawlta;Huaijiakin, gelh ahih bangin, a suang peuhmahin Toupa suang hen.
31. Huaijiakin, gelh ahih bangin, a suang peuhmahin Toupa suang hen.
