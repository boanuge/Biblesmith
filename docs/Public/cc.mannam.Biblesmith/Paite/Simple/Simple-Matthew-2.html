1. Huan, kumpipa Herod a lal laia, Judia gama, Bethlehem khuaa Jesu a hongpianin, ngaiin, suahlam a kipan mi pilte Jerusalem khua ah a hongpai ua.
2. Koiah ahia Judate Kumpipa hongpian? suahlam ah a aksi ka mu ua, amah be dia hongpai ka hi uh, a chi ua.
3. Huan kumpipa Herodin huai a jak phetin a lungjing a, Jerusalemte tengteng leng a lung uh a jing sam ua.
4. Huchiin, siampu liante leh mite laigelhmite tengteng a sam khawm a, Kris pianna ding tuh a dinga.
5. Huan, amau tuh a kiangah Judia gama Bethlehem khua ah; hichiin jawlneiin a gelh a kei hiam.
6. Nang, Judia gama Bethlehem aw, Judia khopite lakah na neupen kei hial, Na sung akipana ukpa hongsuak ding ahi ngala, ka mi Israelte vengpa dingin, chih, a chi ua
7. Huai nungin Herodin a gukin mi pilte tuh a sama, ngentel takin aksi kilat lai a dong kheta a.
8. Huan, amau tuh Bethlehem khua ah a paisaka, Pai ta unla, naungek thu ngentel takin dong khia un, huan ken leng amah ka hongbiak theih samna dingin, na muh tak un, honghilh un, a chi a.
9. Huan, kumpipa thu a jak un; a paita ua; huan ngaiin suahlama aksi a muh uh naunek omna tung jawn a tuna a khawl masiah a ma uah a paizel a.
10. Huan, aksi tuh amuh un, a kipak lo mahmah ua
11. Huan, in sungah a valut ua, a nu Mari kianga naungek tuh a mu ua, a bok ua, a bia ua; huan a gou bawm khawng uh a hong ua, a kiangah kipahna dangkaengte, begawte, murrate a lan ua.
12. Huan, Herod kianga kik nawnlou dingin a mang ua vauhilha a om jiak un, amau kho lamah lam tuam ah a paita uh.
13. Huan, a pai nung un, ngaiin, Toupa angel Joseph kiangah a mangin a kilaka, Thou inla; naungek leh a nu pi inla, Aigupta gamah taimang in, kon hilh nawn masiah huailaiah om gige in; Herodin naungek hihmang dia zong ding a hih jiakin, a chi a.
14. Huchiin, a thou a, a jan ajanin naungek leh a nu a pi a, Aigupta gamah a paita a.
15. Huan, Herod sih mateng hualai ah a om gige a; Toupan jawlnei kama, Ka tapa tuh Aigupta gam apat ka sam khia, chia a gen tuh a hongtun theihna dingin.
16. Huchihlaiin Herodin mi pilte khem a hih a kitheih tak in a heh mahmah a, mi a sawla, a hun lai tak thu ngentel taka mi pilte a dot khiak bangin, Bethlehem khua leh a kim tengtenga pasal naupang kum nih chiang nuai lam tuh a vek un a that a.
17. Huchihlaiin jawlnei Jeremiain
18. Rama khua ah husa a ja uh, kah khawng, misi ngaia mau vengvung khawng, Rakelin a tate a kah a, a om nawn louh jiak un a khamuan theih kei, chia a gen tuh a hongtung ta.
19. Himahleh Herod a sih nungin, ngaiin, Toupa angel Aigupta gamah Joseph kiangah a mangin a kilaka.
20. Thou inla, naungek leh a nu pi inla, Israel gamah pai in; naungek thah tumte a sihtak jiak un, a chi a.
21. Huchiin a thou a, naungek leh a nu tuh a pi a, Israel gamah a lut taa.
22. Himahleh, Arkelausi a pa Herod sikin Judia gamah a lal chih a jakin, huai lama pai a lauta a; huan a manga vauhilha a om jiakin Galili lamah a paita a;Nazaret kichi khua khatah a va omta a; Amah jaw Nazaret mi a chi ding uh, chia jawlneite nagen a hongtun theihna dingin.
23. Nazaret kichi khua khatah a va omta a; Amah jaw Nazaret mi a chi ding uh, chia jawlneite nagen a hongtun theihna dingin.
