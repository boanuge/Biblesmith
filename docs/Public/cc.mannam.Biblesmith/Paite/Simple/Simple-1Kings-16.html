1. Huan, Baasa tungtang thu ah Hanani tapa Jehu kiangah Toupa thu a hongtunga:
2. Leivui a kipan ka hondom sanga, ka mite Israel te tungah heutu in ka hon bawla; huan, nang Jeroboam lampi tot na tawn teia, a khelhnate ua a hon hehsakna ding un ka mi Israel te thil na hihkhial saka;
3. Ngaiin, Baasa leh a inkote ka hihmang sin hi; huchiin Nebat Jeroboam inkote bangmai in na inkote ka bawl ding.
4. Baasa inkote laka mi khopi a si peuhmah te ui in a ne ding ua; huan a inko laka mi gam laka si peuhmah te tungleng vasa ten a ne ding uh, chiin.
5. Baasa tanchin dangte, a thilhihte a thilhih theihdan te Israel kumpipate lal lai thu gelhna bu ah atuang kei maw?
6. Huan, Baasa apute kiangah a ihmu a, Tirza khua ah a vui uh; huan, a tapa Ela a sikin a lal ta a.
7. Huan, Baasa leh a inkote tungtang thu ah khutsuak a Toupa hehsak na dia Jeroboam inkote batna nei a Toupa mitmuh a thil hoihlou mahmah a hih jiak leh amah a thah jiak in Hanani tapa jawlnei Jehu zanga a gen Toupa thu a hongtung hi.
8. Juda kumpipa Asa lal kum sawmnih leh kum guk na ah Baasa tapa Ela Tirza khua ah Israel te tungah a lal pana, kum nih a lal hi.
9. Huan, a sikha Zimri, a kangtalai kimkhat tunga heutu in amah a guk sawma: huchihlai in Tirza khua ah, Tirza khuaa a insung lam heutu Arza inn ah ana kidawn khama:
10. Huan, Juda kumpipa Asa lal kum sawmnih leh kum sagih kum in Zimri ava luta, a suala, a that a, a sikin a lal ta.
11. Huan, hichi ahia, a hong lal pana, a laltutphaha a hong tut takin, Baasa inkote tengteng a that veka: a tanaute leh a lawm te laka mi pasal ta photphot khat leng a hawi kei.
12. Huchiin, jawlnei Jehu zanga Baasa dema a gen, toupa thu bangin, Zimri in Baasa inkote tengteng a hih gawp vek hi.
13. Toupa, Israel te Pathian, a ginnat louh na ua hehsak dinga, a thohthoh ua, a hihkhelh uh, Baasa khelhnate leh Ela a tapa khelhna, huaia Israel te a hihkhelhsak na jiak un.
14. Ela tanchin dangte leh a thilhih tengteng Israel kumpipate lal lai thu gelhna buah a tuang ka hia?
15. Juda kumpipa Asa lal kumsawm nih leh kum sagihna ah Zimri, Tirza khua ah ni sagih a lal a. Philistiate tan, Gibbethon khua miten a um uhi,
16. Huan, a um miten Zimri in a guk sawma, kumpipa a that chih a gen uh ana ja ua; hichiin Israel te tengteng sepaih heutu, Omri, huai ni in a giahmun uah Israel te tunga kumpipa in a bawl uh.
17. Huan, Omri bel Israel te tengteng toh Gibbethon khua a kipan in ava hih tou ua, Tirza khua ava um uh.
18. Huan, hichi ahia, Zimri in khua ala uh chih a theih in, kumpipa ina kulh ah a tailuta, kumpipa innah a kibuhal a, a si ta.
19. Toupa mitmuh a thil hoihlou mahmah hiha a khelh nate jiak leh Jeroboam lampi tot tawn teia Israel te thil hihkhial saka a khelhna jiak in.
20. Zimri tanchin dang te leh, a sawm guk na thu te Israel te kumpipa te lal lai thu gelhna bu ah a tuang ahi kei maw?
21. Huchiin Israel mite pawl nihin a hongkikhenta uh: mipi kimkhatin Ginath tapa Tibni kumpipa dinga bawl tumin a jui ua; huan, a kimkhatin Omri a jui uh.
22. Himahleh Omri juiten Ginath tapa Tibni juite a zou ua: huchiin Tibni a sia, Omri a lalta hi.
23. Juda kumpipa Asa lal kum sawmthum leh kum khatnaah Omri Israelte tungah a lal pana, kum sawm leh nih a lala: Tirza khuaah kum guk a lala.
24. Huan, Semer ta Samari tang dangka talent nihin a leia; huan, tang ah khua a bawla, a kho bawl min dingin huai neipa Semer min tamin Samari a pu sak.
25. Omriin TOUPA mitmuhin thil hoih lou mahmah a hih seka, amaa mite tengteng sangin thil a hihkhial thupi tuamse hi.
26. Nebat tapa Jeroboam dan tengteng alaa, a ginnatlouhna jiak ua Toupa, Israelte Pathian hehsakna dinga Israelte thil a hihkhelhna bangin a khial teita ngala.
27. Omri thilhih tanchin dangte leh a thilhihtheihdan a hihlat thute Israel kumpipate lal lai thu gelhna bu ah a tuang ka hia?
28. Huchiin Omri a pute kiangah a ihmua, Samari khua ah a vuita uhi; huan, a tapa Ahab a sikin a lal.
29. Juda kumpipa Asa lal kum sawmthum leh kum giatnaah Omri tapa Ahab Israelte tungah a lal pana: huan, Omri tapa Ahab Samari khua ah kum sawmnih leh kum nih Israelte tungah a lal.
30. Huan, Omri tapa Ahabin a maa mite tengteng sangin Toupa mitmuhin thil hoihlou mahmah a hih nak.
31. Huan, hichi ahi a, Nebat tapa Jeroboam khelhna bang hih sam khawng bangmah loua ngai hileh kilawm takin, Zidonte kumpipa Eth-bal tanu Jezebel jiin a neia, Baal na te semin a be tei ta mawk a.
32. Huan, Samari khuaa Baal in a lamah Baal adingin maitam a bawla.
33. Huan, Ahabin Asehr a bawla; a maa Israel kumpipate tengteng sangin Ahabin Toupa, Israelte Pathian hehsakna thil a nak hih tungtuan hi.A lal laiin Bethel mi Hielin Jeriko khua a bawla; a tachil Abiram tanin a suangphum aphuma; huan, a nautum Segup tanin a kulh kongkhakte a dingkhe saka, TOUPAN Nun tapa Josua zanga a nagensa bangin.
34. A lal laiin Bethel mi Hielin Jeriko khua a bawla; a tachil Abiram tanin a suangphum aphuma; huan, a nautum Segup tanin a kulh kongkhakte a dingkhe saka, TOUPAN Nun tapa Josua zanga a nagensa bangin.
