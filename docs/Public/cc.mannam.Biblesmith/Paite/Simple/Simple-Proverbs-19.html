1. Ginoma om mi genthei tuh mihau a muktea kilawm gen leh mi hai sangin a hoihjaw hi
2. Huailouin, kha theihna beia a om ding a hoih kei; Huan kuapeuh a khea man-gang a khial nak hi.
3. Mihing hainain a lampi a hihsiaa; huan a lungtangin TOUPA a hek.
4. Hausaknain lawmte tampi a behlapa; himahleh mi genthei a lawm akipan khemin a om.
5. Theihpih taklou gawt louhin a om kei ding; huan kuapeuh juau gen a suak ta kei ding.
6. Mi tampiin mi siam deihsakna a ngen ding ua; huan mi chih thilpiakte pia adingin lawm ahi.
7. Mi genthei unaute tengtengin amah a hua ua; amah akipan bangchia gamlain ahia a lawmte a paiuh! Thutein amau a delha, himahleh a pai khin uh.
8. Kuapeuh pilna muin a kha a ita; kuapeuh theihsiamna kemin hoih a mu ding hi.
9. Theihpih taklou gawt louhin a om kei dinga; kuapeuh juau gen a mangthang ding.
10. Nuamtat mi hai adingin a kilawm keia; sikha lalte tunga vaihawm a kilawm kei diak.
11. Mihing ngentelnain amah a hehhak saka; huan a tatlekna paikan a thupina ahi.
12. Kumpipa thangpaihna humpinelkai humham bang ahi; himahleh a deihsakna loupa tunga daitui bang ahi.
13. Tapa hai a pa tuahsiatna ahi; huan ji hanhat a intung keh gige ahi.
14. In leh hauhsaknate pate akipan gouluah ahi: himahleh ji pil TOUPA akipan ahi.
15. Thadahna nakpia ihmutna ah a paia; huan mi thadahin gilkial a thuak ding.
16. Kuapeuh thupiak juiin a kha a kem; himahleh kuapeuh a lampitea pilvang lou a si ding.
17. Kuapeuh genthei hehpihna neiin TOUPA adingin a leitawi saka, huan a thil hoih hih amah adin nawn ding.
18. Lametna a om laiin na tapa sawi in; huan a siatna dingin na lungtang koih kip hen.
19. Nakpitaka thangpai min gawtna a thuak ding: amah na hut khiak leh, huaituh na hih kik nawn kul ding ahi ngala.
20. Thupha ja inla, hilhna sangin, na tawpna nanunga na pil theihna dingin.
21. Mihing lungtang ah thugel tampi a oma; himahleh TOUPA thupha, huaimah a ding ding hi.
22. Mihing deih thusam a migitna tehna ahi; huan mi genthei mijuauthei sangin a hoihjaw hi.
23. TOUPA kihtak hinna lam zotna ahi; huan kuapeuh huai nei lungkimin a om gige ding; giloua vehin a om kei ding hi.
24. Mi thadahin kuangsung ah a khut a phuma, huchiin a kam ah huai tuh a hontun nawn ngap kei hi.
25. Simmohhat sat in, mi mawlin pilvangdana sin ding; huan theih siamna nei salin, huan theihna a theisiam ding.
26. Kuapeuh a pa tunga hiamgama, a nu delh mang, zumna omsak leh simmohna tun tapa ahi.
27. Theihna thute akipana lammang lel din, ka tapa, hilhna jak tawp in.
28. Theihpihmi phattuamna neilouin vaihawmna a simmoh: Huan mi gilou kamin thulimlouhna a nawmvalh hi.Muhsithatmi adingin vaihawmnate bawlkholhsa ahi, mihai nungjang adingin vuaknate.
29. Muhsithatmi adingin vaihawmnate bawlkholhsa ahi, mihai nungjang adingin vuaknate.
