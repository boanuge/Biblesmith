1. Huchi in Bezaleel leh Aholiab in a hih ua, michih lungtang pil, amau a TOUPA'N mun siangthou a nnasepna dan chiteng bangchi bang a sep ding ahia chih thei dia pilna leh theihna a koihnate, TOUPA'N thu a piak tengteng bang in.
2. Huchi in Mosi in Bezaleel leh Aholiab leh, michih lungtang pil nei, a lungtang ua TOUPA'N pilna a koihnate, michih a lung ua nnasepna hong kuan a hih ding a tohthoh a omte ngei a sam ta hi:
3. Huan Mosi a kipan in thillat tengteng, Israel suanten mun siangthou a nnasepna ding a a hon tawi uh, huai nnasepna ding a la ua. Huan zingsang teng in utthu a piak thillatte a kiang ah a hon tawi nalai uhi.
4. Huan mun siangthou a nnasep tengteng hih, mi pil tengteng, michih a thilhih a bawl ua kipan a hong pai ua;
5. Mosi kiang a thugen in, Miten nnasepna a sepna hunzah sang a tamzaw a hon tawi uh, chi in.
6. Huan Mosi in thupiak a pia a, giahbuk teng ah a tangkou pih ua, pasal hileh numei hileh mun siangthou a thillat ding in thilhih bawl neikei un, chi in. Huchi in, mite hon tawi lou ding a kham in a om ta uhi.
7. Mun siangthou bawl ding a nnasepna khempeuh a ding in a van neihsa uh lah a kiching in, a tam lo ta ngal a.
8. A lak ua lungtang pil michih biakbuk nnasep hihten puan malngat koptah hoih puanzak sawm, a dum, a sandup, a san-au a bawl uh: khutsiam a hih cherubte toh huaite a bawl uh.
9. Puanzak khat dung lam tong sawmnih leh giat ahi, puanzak khat vai lam tong li; puanzak tengteng let lam kibang chiat ahi uh.
10. Huan puanzak ngate a kithuah in a khep hi. Huan puanzak a dang ngate leng a khep a.
11. Huchi in a kithuahna ah mong khepna a kipan in puanzak khat gei ah a dum in bate a bawl; huchimah in a nihna khepna ah, puanzak dang gei chin ah a bawl hi.
12. Puanzak khat ah ba sawmnga a bawl a, a nihna kikhepna a puanzak gei ah ba sawmnga a bawl: bate a kizawn geihgeih in a om uhi.
13. Huan dangka-eng in kilhna sawmnga a bawl a, kilhnate in puanzakte a gawmkhawm: huchi in biakbuk khat a hong hi hi.
14. Huan puan inn biakbuk tung a ding in kel mul in puanzak a bawl a: huan sawm leh khat a bawl hi.
15. Puanzak khat dung lam tong sawmthuam ahi a, puanzak khat vai lam tong li ahi: puanzak sawm leh khatte a leh lam kibang vek uh ahi.
16. Huchi in puanzak ngate amau in a khep tuam a, puanzak gukte amau in khep tuam hi.
17. Huchi in a kikhepna ah puanzak mong chin ah ba sawmnga a bawl a, puanzak a nihna thuahna pen mong ah ba sawmnga a bawl hi
18. Khat a hih theihna ding in puan in kaihkhawmna ding in dal in kilhna sawmnga a bawl hi.
19. Puan in khuhna ding in belampa vun diahsan in a bawl a, hual tungah ngalvang vun in khuhna a bawl.
20. Huchi in singgi sing in biakbuk singpek ding a bawl a, tun thoh in.
21. Singpek sau lam tong sawm ahi, singpek zat lam tong khat leh a kim.
22. Singpek khat in atgek nih a nei, a kigamlatdan uh kikim in: huchi bang in biakbuk singpek tengteng a ding in a bawl.
23. Huchi in biakbuk a ding in singpek a bawl; sim lam sang a sim lam pang a ding in singpek sawmnih:
24. Singpek sawmnih nuai ah dangka-eng in kingakna khuakkuak sawmli a bawl; a atgek nihte a ding in singpek khat dang ah kingakna khuakkuak nih, a atgek nih a ding in singpek dang ah kingakna khuakkuak nih.
25. Huan biakbuk pang khat lam a ding in, a mal lam ning a om a ding in, singpek sawmnih a bawl,
26. A dangka-eng kingakna khuakkuak sawmlite toh; singpek khat dang ah kingakna khuakkuak nih, huan singpek dang khat dang ah kingakna khuakkuak nih.
27. Huchi in tum lam biakbuk pang ate ding in singpek nih a bawl.
28. A pang nihte ah biakbuk ningte a ding in singpek nih a bawl.
29. Huchi in a nuai ah a kithuah ua, huai a a tungah, sikkual khat ah a kikaikhawm: huchi bang in a ning tuaktuak ah a hih tuaktuak hi.
30. Huan singpek giat a om; a kingakna khuakkuak uh dangka bawl kingakna khuakkuak sawm leh guk ahi ua, singpek chih dang ah kingakna khuakkuak nih.
31. Huan singgi sing chiangtawn a bawl; biakbuk pang khat a singpekte a ding in nga,
32. Biakbuk pang dang a singpekte a ding in chiang tawn nga, tum lam pang a biakbuk singpekte a ding in chiangtawn nga.
33. Huchi in chiangtawn laita pen singpekte khat a kipan a dang kap paisuak in a bawl hi.
34. Huchi in singpekte dangka-eng in a luan a, a dangka-eng sikkualte uh chiangtawn omna ding in a bawl a, chiangtawnte dangka-eng in a luan hi.
35. Huan a dum, a sandup, a san-au leh puan malngat koptah hoih in puankhai a bawl a: khutsiam tak in huai cherubte a bawl hi.
36. Huchi in huai ah singgi sing in khuam li a bawl, dangka-eng in a luan: a bannate uh dangka-eng a bawl ahi: huan huaite a ding in dangka in kingakna khuakkuak li a sung hi.
37. Huchi in biakbuk kongkhak a ding in a dum in, a sandup in, a san-au in leh puan malngat hoih koptah, phim a hih in puankhai a bawl a:Huan a khuam ngate a banna utoh: huchi in, a zingte uleh a puanduangte dangka-eng in a luan a: himahleh a kingakna khuakkuak ngate uh dal ahi.
38. Huan a khuam ngate a banna utoh: huchi in, a zingte uleh a puanduangte dangka-eng in a luan a: himahleh a kingakna khuakkuak ngate uh dal ahi.
