1. Huan, unaute aw, khalam thilpiak thute jaw na theih louh uh ka ut kei hi.
2. Jentelte na hih lai un bangchidan peuha honpi lai un leng, huai milim pautheiloute lamah pi mangin na om uh chih na thei uhi.
3. Huaijiakin, kuamahin, Pathian Khaa thugenin, Jesu jaw hamsiatin om hen, a chi ngei kei uh; huan, kuamahin, Kha Siangthou kia loungalin, Jesu jaw Toupa ahi, a chi ngei kei uh chih kon theisak ahi.
4. Huan, thilpiak tuamtuam a oma, Kha bel kikhat ahi.
5. Nasepna tuamtuam leng a om, himahleh Toupa bel khat ahi.
6. Nasepsakna tuamtuam leng a om, ahihhangin Pathian bel khat ahi – mi tengtenga bangkim sempa tuh.
7. Himahleh phattuam chiatna dingin Kha theihsakna michih kianga piak chiat ahi.
8. Mi khat kiangah, khain pilna thu a pia hi; midang kiangah, huai kha mahin theihna thu a pia hi;
9. Huan, midang kianga, huai kha mah-in, ginna a pia hi; midang kiangah hihdam theihna a pia hi;
10. Midang kiangah thillamdang hihtheihna, midang kiangah tugen theihna, midang kiangah khate theihkak theihna, midang kiangah pau chi tuamtuamte, midang kiangah paute hilhchetna.
11. Himahleh, hiaite tengteng Kha khat leh akikhat mahin, mi chih kiangah a deih bang jela hawm chiatin a sem jel hi.
12. Pumpi i chih khat ahia, hiang a neia, pumpi hiangte tengteng, tam mahle uh, pumpi khat ahi ngala; Kris tuh huchibangmah ahi hi.
13. I vek un pumpi khata om dingin Kha khata baptisin i omta ua – Judate hiam, Griskte hiam, sikhate hiam sikhalou hiam- huan i vek un Kha khat akipan dawnsakin i omta ngal ua.
14. Pumpi i chih hiang khat kia ahi ngal keia, hiang tampi ahi jaw.
15. Khepekin, khut ka hih louh jiakin pumpia pang ka hi kei, chileh, huai thu jiakin pumpia panglou ahi tuan kei.
16. Huan, bilin, mit ka hih louh jiakin pumpia pang ka hi kei, chileh, huai thu jiakin pumpia panglou ahi tuan kei.
17. Pumpi tengteng mit hi vek leh, ging jakna pen koiah a om dia? A vekin ging jakna hileh, gim jakna pen koiah a om dia?
18. Himahleh Pathianin hiang khempeuh pumpi ah a utna peuh ah a omsak chiatta hi.
19. Huchiin, huai tengteng hiang khat kia hileh, pumpi pen koi ah a om dia?
20. Ahihhangin hiang tampi ahi jaw uh, himahleh, pumpi khat kia ahi ngal ua.
21. Huan, mitin, khut kiangah, Bangmahin kon deih kei, a chi theikei; luin leng khepekte kiangah, Bangmahin kon deih kei, a chi thei sam kei hi.
22. Ahi, huchih naksangin, pumpi hiang hat dia kilawm loupente a kiphamoh zo mahmah uhi;
23. Huchiin, pumpi hiang pahtawi tham louh jawa i sehte, huaite ngei tuh i pahtawi jaw uhi; huan, i hiang kilawm louten kilawmna thupijaw a mu jel uhi;
24. I hiang kilawm ten bel huchibang a hih a kiphamoh kei uhi. Pathianin, kilawmna neilou hiang tuh pahtawi zopenin, pumpi tuh a lemkhawm zo ngala,
25. Huchia pumpia kikhenna himhim omloua, ahiangten kibang taka a kibuaipih tuah theih jawkna ding un.
26. Huchiin, hiang khatin a thuak leh, hiang tengtengin a thuak pih jela, hiang khat pahtawia a om leh, hiang tengtengin a kipahpih jel hi.
27. Huchiin noute Kris pumpi pen na hi ua, ahiangte na hi chiat lai uhi.
28. Huan, Pathianin khenkhat saptuam lakah a honkoiha – a khatna ah sawltakte, a nihna ah thugenmite, a thumna ah heutute a koiha; huai nungin thillamdang hihte a oma; huai nungin hihdam theihna thilpiakte, panpih theihna thilpiakte, vaihawm siamna thilpiakte, pautheihlouh chi tuamtuama thugen theihna thilpiakte a om nawn hi.
29. A vek un sawltak ahi vek uhia? A vek un thugenmi ahi vek uhia? A vek un heutu ahi vek uhia?
30. A vek un thillamdang hihmi ahi vek uhia? A vek un hihdim theihna thilpiakte a nei vek uhia? A vek un pautheihlouhtein thu a gen vek uhia?A vek un thuhilhchianmi ahi vek u hia? Thilpiak thupipen te lunggulh mahmah jaw un. Huan, lampi hoih sem ka honensak ding hi.
31. A vek un thuhilhchianmi ahi vek u hia? Thilpiak thupipen te lunggulh mahmah jaw un. Huan, lampi hoih sem ka honensak ding hi.
