1. Huan, Israel suan mipite tengteng Silo khua ah a kikhawm dudup ua, huailaiah kihoupihna puanin a kai ua: gam tuh a thunuai uah a o mta hi.
2. Huan, Israel suante lakah a tantuam uh hawm nailouh nam sagih a om laia.
3. Huchiin Josuain Israel suante kiangah, TOUPA na pipute uh Pathianin nou a honna piaksa gam pen bangtan ahia luah loua na awltat lai sin uh?
4. Nam chiha mi thum jel kitel khia unla: amau a kuan ding ua, gam a va-en vialvial ding ua, a tantuam hun ding uh bang chiat jelin a vagelh ding ua; huan ka kiangah a hong nawn ding uh.
5. Huan, mun sagihin a khen phot ding ua: juda pawl sim lamah a tantuam uah a om dia, huan, Joseph inkuante mal lamah a tantuam uah a om ding uh.
6. Huan, gam pen mun sagihin vakhen unla, huai gelhna hongpia un: huchiin hiaiah TOUPA I Pathian maah ai ka honna sansak ding hi.
7. Levite tengin na lak uah tantuam a neikei ding ua: TOUPA siampu na a tantuam uh a hih jiakin: huan Gad pawl te, Reuben pawl te leh Manassi nam kimkhat te bel Jordan gal suahlamah amau tantuam TOUPAsikha Mosiin a pik a natangta ua, a chi a.
8. Huchiin huai mite a pawt kheta ua, huan, gam tanchiin vagelh khiak dingte Josuain, Gam va-en vialvial unla, hongelh khia unla, ka kiangah hontun nawn unla, hiaiah Silo khua ah TOUPA maah ai ka honna sansak ding, chiin thu a pia hi.
9. Huan, mite a kuan ua, gam a va-en vialvial ua, mun sagihin a khen ua, laibu ah a khuakhuain a gelh khia ua, giahmun Silo ah Josua kiangah a hoh nawn uh.
10. Huan, Josuain Silo ah TOUPA maah ai a nasan saka; huaiah Josuain Israel suante a kikhen dan bang jel un gam a hawmta hi.
11. Huan, aisan tuh a inkuan uh dungjui jelin Benjamin suante tungah a keta a: huan, a gamgi chiang uh Juda suante leh Joseph suante kikal ahi.
12. Huan, mal lampanga a gamgi chiang uh Jordan akipanin ahi: huan, gamgi Jeriko sikah mal lampangah a pai tou toua, huan, tumlam pangah tang gamah a pai tou jela; gamgi tawpna Beth-aven gamdai ah ahi.
13. Huan, gamgi hiai akipanin Luz lamah, Luz (huai bel Bethel a hi) sikah, simlam pangah a pai jela; huan, gamgi Beth-horon khangnung simlam panga tang julah Atarothadarah a pai suk.
14. Huan, gamgi a pai jela, Beth-horon simlam panga tang chin akipanin tumlamah simlam pangah a kual pheia; huai bel Kiriath-baal (huai tuh Kiriathjearim a hi) Juda suante khopi ah a tawpa: huai bel tumlam pang gamahi,
15. Huan, simlam panga gam Kiriath-jearim tawp akipanin ahi, huan, gamgi tum lamah a pawt, nephtoa lui tuite ah a pai jela:
16. Huan, gamgi Rephiam mallam pang guama Hinom tapa guam china tang mongah a pai suka; huchiin Jebus-te sikah, simlam panga Hinom guam ah a pai suka, huan, En-rogelah a pai suk;
17. Huan, mallam juanin a paia, En-semesah a paia, Adumim kah touhna chin Gelilothah a paia; huan, Reuben tapa Bohan suang ah a pai suka;
18. Huan, Araba sik mallam pangah a pai jela, Araba ah a pai suka:
19. Huan, gamgi Beth-hogla sik, mallam pangah a pai jela: huan, gamgi tawpna bel Jordan sim tawp, Tuipi Al mal tuipimeng ah ahi: huai bel simlam gamgi ahi.
20. Jordan bel suahlam panga gamgi chiang ahi. Huai ahi Benjamin suante a inkuan uh dungjui jela a tantuam gam un a kual sung.
21. Huchiin Benjamin suante a inkuan uh dungjui jela a khopite uh Jeriko te, Beth-hogla te, Emek-keziz te,
22. Betharaba te, Zemariam te, Bethel te;
23. Avim te, Para te, Ophra te;
24. Kephar-amoni te, Ophani te, Geba te; khopi sawm leh nih a khopelte toh:
25. Gibeon te, Ram ate, Beeroth te;
26. Mizpe te, Kephira te, Moza te;
27. Rekem te, Irpeel te, Tarala te;Zela te, Eleph te, Jebus te (huai bel Jerusalem ahi), Sibeath te ahi; khopisawm leh li a khopelte toh. Huaite ahi Benjamin suante a inkuan uh dungjui jela a tantuam ding ua seh.
28. Zela te, Eleph te, Jebus te (huai bel Jerusalem ahi), Sibeath te ahi; khopisawm leh li a khopelte toh. Huaite ahi Benjamin suante a inkuan uh dungjui jela a tantuam ding ua seh.
