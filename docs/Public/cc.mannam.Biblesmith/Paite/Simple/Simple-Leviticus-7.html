1. Huan, Hiai tatleek thoihdan ding ahi: thil siangthou tak ahi.
2. Haltum thillat a goh zelna ua tatleek kithoihna leng goh ding ahi: huan, a sisan a maitam theh kimvel ding ahi.
3. Huan, a thau tengteng a lat ding ahi: a mei thau takte, a sungkua thaute,
4. A kal nihte leh a boh thau, a phuk kiang a thau leh a sin tuam lap, a kalte nangawn in a suahkhe ding a:
5. Huan, siampu in TOUPA ding a mei a thillat ding in maitam tungah a hal ding ahi: huaibel tatleek thoihna ahi.
6. Pasal siampu tengteng in a ne ding ua: mun siangthou a nek ding ahi: huaibel thil siangthou tak ahi.
7. Tatleek thoihna pen khelh thoihna bangmah ahi a, dan khat ahi: huai a kilepna bawltu siampu in, huai a tan ding ahi.
8. Siampu, kuapeuh haltum thillat latsaktu siampu ngei in haltum thillat dia a lat vun a pumtan ding ahi.
9. Huan, a kanna a kan tangbuang thillat peuhmah, sikbel a kan leh kuang a kan tengteng a lantu siampu a tuam ding ahi.
10. Tangbuang thillat tengteng sathau a mek leng, a gaw leng a dang bang mah in Aron tapate tengteng in a kihawm dialdial ding uh ahi.
11. Huan, hiai pen mi'n TOUPA kiang a a lat ding, lepna thillat kithoih dan ding ahi.
12. Kipahthu genna dia a lat leh kipahthu genna kithoihna ding in sathau a zut silngou hel louh tanghou leh sathau a zut silngou hel louh tanghoupa didekte leh sathau a mek tangbuang nel mahmah kidiah suak a lat tel ding ahi.
13. Kipahthu genna ding a a lemna thillat kithoihna ah tanghou silngou hel a lat tel ding ahi.
14. Huan, huai a thillat teng a TOUPA a dia vei thillat a ding in khat chiat a lan ding a; lemna thillat sisan a thehtu siampu a tuam ding ahi.
15. Huan, kipahthu genna dia a lepna thillat kithoihna sa pen thillat a lat ni mah a nekgaih ding ahi a; a zingchiang tan a bangmah sit ding ahi kei hi.
16. Ahihhang in a thillat kithoihna pen thuchiam hiam, amah ut thu a thillat hiam ahih leh a kithoihna a lat ni mah a nek ding ahi: huan, nek bang a zingchia nek ding ahi:
17. Ahihhang in kithoihna sa a ni thum ni tan a om lailai bel hal ding ahi.
18. A lenna thillat kithoihna sa ni thum ni tan a a nek uleh, pomsak ahi kei ding a, a lantu leng a lan a sim ahi sam kei ding: thil kihhuai ahi ding a, kuapeuh a ne in a thulimlouhna moh uh a po ding uh.
19. Huan, sa, thil siangthoulou himhim toh kisukha nek ding ahi kei a, hal ding ahi.
20. Huan, sa a siangthou peuhmahte'n a ne ding ua, lemna thillat kithoihna sa, TOUPA a tuam, siangthoulou pi a netu bel, huai mi zaw a chipihte lak a kipan tuamkoih ding ahi
21. Huan, kuapeuh in thil siangthoulou himhim, mi siangthoulou hiam, gan siangthoulou hiam, thil kihhuai siangthoulou hiam himhim a khoih a, lemna thillat kithoihna sa, TOUPA a tuam a nek leh huai mi a chipihte lak a kipan tuamkoih a om ding ahi, chi in Aron leh a tapate houpih in, chi in.
22. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
23. Bawng hiam, belam hiam, kel hiam thau na ne ding uh ahi kei.
24. Amah sih a si thau leh sa keih thau thil dang bang ding hiam a zat theih ahi ding: nek bel na ne himhim ding uh ahi kei.
25. Kuapeuh, TOUPA a dia min mei a thillat a lat uh sathau ne zaw, huai mi a netu a chipihte lak a kipan a tuamkoih ding ahi hi.
26. Na omna peuh uah sisan himhim, vasa hiam, gamsa hiam sisan na ne ding uh ahi kei.
27. Kuapeuh hita leh, sisan ne himhim zaw, huai mi zaw a chipihte lak a kipan a tuamkoih ding ahi, chi in Israel suante houpih in, chi in.
28. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
29. TOUPA kiang a lemna thillat kithoihna lan peuhmah in, TOUPA a ding a a thillat a lemna thillat kithoihna a kipan a hong lat ding ahi.
30. TOUPA a ding a mei a thillat amah khut mahmah in a hon tawi ding a; a thau a awm toh a hon la ding ahi, a awm pen TOUPA ma a vei thillat ding a vei a hih theihna ding in.
31. Huan, siampu in maitam ah a thau a hal ding a: a awm bel Aron leh a tapate a tuam ding ahi.
32. A liang tak lam pen kilepna thillat kithoihna a kipan vei thillat ding in siampu na piak ding uh ahi.
33. Aron tapate lak a lemna thillat sisan leh thau lantu kuapeuh in a tantuan ding in a liang tak lam a tan ding ahi.
34. A awm leh a liang a vei zel uh pen Israel suante a lemna thillat kithoihna a kipan ka la tuam a, Israel suante khantawn silbawl dan ding in siampu Aron leh a tapate ka pia ahi, chi in Israel suante kiang ah gen in chi in.
35. Hiai ahi siampu nna sem a TOUPA nnasem ding a a lat ni nungsiah a TOUPA ding a mei a thillat a kipan Aron tantuan leh a tapate tantuan ding;
36. Amau sathau a nilh ni a Israel suante'n a piak ding ua TOUPA'N thu a piak. Hiai hi khantawn a a suante zel tan a a hih dan ding uh ahi.
37. Hiai hi haltum thillat, tangbuang thillat, khelh thoihna, tatleek kithoihna dan;Sinai gamdai a Israel suante'n thillat TOUPA kiang a lat ding a thu a piak ni a Sinai tang a TOUPA'N Mosi thu a piak khum.
38. Sinai gamdai a Israel suante'n thillat TOUPA kiang a lat ding a thu a piak ni a Sinai tang a TOUPA'N Mosi thu a piak khum.
