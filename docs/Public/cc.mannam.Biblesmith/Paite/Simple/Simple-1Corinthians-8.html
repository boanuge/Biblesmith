1. Huan, milimte laka kithoihna tungtang jaw: I vekin theihna i nei chiat uh chih i kithei hi. Theihnain mi a kisathei saka itna inbel mi a bawlhoih nak hi.
2. Kuapeuhin thil bang ahi hiam theia a kigintak leh, a theih ding thei nailou hi.
3. Ahihhangin, kuapeuhin Pathian a it leh, huai mi mah amah theih ahi
4. Huchiin, milimte laka kithoihna thil nek thu jaw, khovel ah milim himhim bangmah ahih louhdan uleh, khat kia loungal Pathian dang himhim a om louhdan i thei hi.
5. Van ah hiam, lei ah hiam, Pathian min pute om mahle uh, -huchi bang Pathian tampi leh toupa tampi a om ngal ua-
6. Eite adingin bel Pathian khat kia, Pa, a oma, amah kiang akipanin bangkim a pawta, eite leng amah adia om i hi; Toupa khat kia, Jesu Kris, a om hi, amah jiakin bangkim a om hi, eite leng amah jiak maha om i hi uhi.
7. Ahihhangin, huai theihna mi tengteng ah a om kei hi; huchiin khenkhatin, tutanin milim be sasa a hihjiak un, milim laka kithoihna thil bang taka theiin a ne nak uhi; huchiin, a sia leh pha theihna uh a hat louh jiak a thanghuai nak uhi.
8. Himahleh, nek dingin Pathian mit hontung sak tuan kei ding ne kei mahlehang i se tuan keia. Ne mah lehang leng i hoih tuan sam kei hi.
9. Himahleh, pilvang un, huchilou injaw na noplenna uh bang chichi hiamin mi hatloute adin pukna a hong hi kha ding.
10. Nang, theihna nei mi, kuapeuhin milimbiakna inn a ankuang uma a honmuh ngal un, amau mi hatlou na hita le uh, milim laka kithoihna thil ne dingin a sia leh pha theihna hong maingal lou ding hia?
11. Huchiin na theihna jiakin mi hatlou, unau a sih sika Kris in a sih sakpa, a mangthang ding ahi.
12. Unaute tuanga huchi bang thil na hihkhelh ua, a sia leh pha theihna uh, a hatlouh naklaia, na liamsak behlap un, Kris tuangah thil na hihkhial uhi.Huaijiak takin nekdingin ka unau a hihpuk leh, ka unau ka hihpuk louhna dingin kumtawnin sa himhim ka neta kei ding.
13. Huaijiak takin nekdingin ka unau a hihpuk leh, ka unau ka hihpuk louhna dingin kumtawnin sa himhim ka neta kei ding.
