1. Huan, hichi ahi a a nam pumpiin Jordan lui a galkah siang nung un TOUPAN Josua kiangah,
2. Mipite laka mi sawm leh nih, nam chiha mi khat jel tel khia inla,
3. A kiang uah, Jordan lui lai, siampute dinna laitak ate suang sawm leh nih la unla, po phei unla, giahmun, tujana na giahna ding uah koih un, chiin thu piain, chiin a gen a.
4. Huchiin josuain Israel suante laka mi sawm leh nih, nam chiha mi khat jel a tel khiakte a sama:
5. Huan, Josuain a kiang uah, Toupa na pathian uh bawm maah Jordan lui laiah galkah unla, Israel suante a nam zah jein, mi chihin suang khat chiat pua un,
6. Huchiin hiaite na lak uah chiamtehna a honghi dinga, tunung chiangin na tate un, hiai suangte bang na chihna uh ahia oi? chia ahon dot hun chiang un,
7. Nou a kiang uah, TOUPA thukhun bawm maa Jordan tuite a hong kan tak jiakin ahi; Jordan a kan laiin, Jordan tuite a hong kitata ahi: huchiinhiaisuangte isreal suanten khantawna a theihgigena ding uh ahi, na chi ding uhi, a chi a.
8. Huan, Israel suanten Josuain thu a piak bangin a hih ua, Israel suanten a nam zah jelin, Jordan lui laia suang sawm leh nih a la ua; huan, a giahna mun uah, a po phei ua, huailaiah a koih uhi.
9. Huan, thukhun bawm jawng siampute dinna lai tak Jordan lui laiah Josuain huai suang sawm leh nihte a phuta; huaite tu tanin a om lai uhi.
10. Mosiin Josua thu a napiak tengteng banga TOUPAN mipite kianga hih dinga Josua thu a piak a hih zoh vek mateng uh siampu bawm jawngte Jordan lui laiah a ding nilouh ua: huchiin mipite a kinoh ua, a galkahta uhi.
11. Huan, hichi ahi a, mipi tengteng a galkah siang vek nung un TOUPA bawm leh siampute mipi mitmuhin a galkah sam ta uhi.
12. Huan, Reuben suante, Gad suante leh Manassi nam kimkhatte Mosiin a kiang ua a nagen khitsa bangin Israel suante maah kigalvanin a galkah uh:
13. Galvana kivan diamdiam mi sing li khawng kidou dingin Jeriko phai khawngahte TOUPA maah a galkah uh.
14. Huai niin TOUPAN Josua tuh Israel-te tengteng mitmuhin a pahtawia; Mosi a kihtak bangmah un a damsung teng a kihta uhi.
15. Huan, TOUPAN Josua kiangah thu a gen a,
16. Siampute, thutheisakna bawm jawngte Jordan lui akipan hongpawt dingin thu piain, chiin.
17. Huchiin Josuain siampute, Jordan lui akipan hongpawt ta un, chiin thu a piaa.
18. Huan, hichi ahi a, siampute, TOUPA thukhun bawm jawngte Jordan lui lai akipan a hongpawt ua, siampute kheten lei hul a sik takin, Jordan tuite a luan ngeina un a hongluang nawna, a ma mah bangin a kuang tengteng ah a luang let nawn juajua hi.
19. Huan, mipite kha khatna, ni sawm niin Jordan lui akipan a pawt ua, Jeriko suahlam pang Gilgal ah ommun a bawlta uh.
20. Huan, huai suang sawm leh nih, Jordan lui akipan a puakkhiak uh JosuainGilgal ah a phuta,
21. Huan, Israel suante kiangah, Tunung chianga na suante un a pate uh, Hiai suangte bangchihna ahia oi? chi a hondot chiang un,
22. Israelten Jordan lui lei hul ah a galkah ua ahi, chiin na suante uh na hontheisak ding uhi.
23. TOUPA na Pathian un nidanglaia Tuipi san I galkah masiah ua i ma ua a na kang tatsak mahbangin TOUPA na Pathian un Jordan tuite na galkah masiah uh na ma uah a kangtat saka ahi:Leia nam chitengin TOUPA khut a nahat ngei chih a theih theih ua, TOUPA na Pathian uh khantawna a kihtak theihna ding un, chiin a gen.
24. Leia nam chitengin TOUPA khut a nahat ngei chih a theih theih ua, TOUPA na Pathian uh khantawna a kihtak theihna ding un, chiin a gen.
