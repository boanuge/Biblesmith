1. Huan Bildad Sua miin a dawnga, hichiin a chi a:
2. Lalna leh launa Pathian kiangah a om; aman a mun sangte ah muanna a bawl hi.
3. A sepaihte hihzah himhim a om ahia? huan a vakna suahlouhna kua a om a?
4. Huchi a hih chiangin Pathian lakah bangchiin mihing a dik thei dia? ahihkeileh amah numei suan bangchiin a siang thei dia?
5. Ngaiin, khapi nangawnin tetna a neikeia, a mitmuhin aksite a siangthou kei hi.Mihing, tangtel, bangchiphetin a siangkei zo de aw; huan mihing ta, tangtel;
6. Mihing, tangtel, bangchiphetin a siangkei zo de aw; huan mihing ta, tangtel;
