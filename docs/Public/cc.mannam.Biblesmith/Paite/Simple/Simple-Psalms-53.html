1. Mi haiin a lungtangin, Pathian a om kei, a chi naknak. A se khin ua, thil thulimlou kihhuaitak a hih uh; kuamah thilhoih hih himhim a om kei uh.
2. Theisiama Pathian zong himhim a om uhiam chih thei dingin Pathianin van akipan in mihing tatea vaen suka.
3. A lehngat chiat ua; nin takin a na om vekta ua; kuamah thil hoih hih himhim a om kei ua, khat lel leng a om kei uh.
4. Thilhihkhialten theih himhim a neikei ua hia! Tanghou a nek bang un ka mite a ne khin ua, Pathian lah a ngeingaih sam kei uhi.
5. Launa om louhna ah a lau petpet ua: Pathianin nang hon umpa guhte a dalhsak jiakin: Pathianin amau a muhsit jiakin, amau na zahlak sak.Israelte hotdamna tuh Zion akipanin hongpawt him le aw! Pathianin a mite sala a om uh a pi kik nawn chiangin, huai hun chiangin Jakobte a nuam ding ua, Israelte a kipak ding uh.
6. Israelte hotdamna tuh Zion akipanin hongpawt him le aw! Pathianin a mite sala a om uh a pi kik nawn chiangin, huai hun chiangin Jakobte a nuam ding ua, Israelte a kipak ding uh.
