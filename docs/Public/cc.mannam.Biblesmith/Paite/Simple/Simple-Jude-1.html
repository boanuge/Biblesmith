Jude (1/1)    
1. Juda, Jesu Kris sikha leh Jakob unauin, Pa Pathiana deihtak, Jesu Kris adinga sit, sapte kiangah:
2. Hehpihnate, lemnate, itnate na tunguah hihkhanin om hen.
3. Deihtakte aw, i kikop hotdamna thu na kiang ua gelh ka tup teitei lain, misiangthoute kianga ginna khatvei kepsak lamah phatuam ngai taka pang dinga honhasuan dingin na kiang ua lai gelh a kiphamoh chih kana theia.
4. Mi kuatehiam a guka hong lut a om ngal ua, nidang laia pan hiai siamlouhtanna dia sehte; huaite tuh Pathian hehpihna akaikawiin, i Heutupa leh i Toupa neih sun, Jesu Kris tuh a kitheihmohbawl behlap uhi.
5. Huan, bangkim khatveiin thei vek mahle uchin, Toupan Aigupta gama kipana mite a suahtaksaka, huai nung gingtaloute tuh a hihmanthat dan nou hontheihsak nawn ka ut ahi.
6. Huan, angel te amau sawltak hihna jui loua, a omna ngeingei uh paisan zote tuh, ni thupi vaihawmnaa dinga mial nuaia khantawn henna hen kawmin a sit ahi.
7. Sodom leh Gomorra, a kiang kim ua khuate toh, huchibangmaha kingaihnaa kipumpiaka, gitlouhna leklua a delh sam tak jiak un, khantawna meia gawtna thuaka, theihsakna dinga suankhiaka a om bang un.
8. Himahleh, huaiten leng, huchi bang mahin, a deihthusam bang un sa tuh a hihthanghuai ua, vaihawm sakna a musit ua, thupinate a gense nak uhi.
9. Himahleh, Mikael angel tungunngpenin bawn, diabol toh a kisel ua, Mosi luang thua a kinak lai un a tungthu gen sakin amah a gense ngam kei hi, Toupan hontai jaw hen, a chi zota hi.
10. Huai miten bel thil a theih louh peuhmah uh a gense jel ua; huan ngaihtuahna neilou ganhingte banga theihsiamna pianpih a neihte un, huai lampang mahmah ah a kihehse naknak uhi.
11. A tung ua a gik hi! Kaina lampi a jui ua, punna delhin Balaam mohnaah hahtaiin a tai ua, Kora thukalhna ah a mangthangta uhi.
12. Hiaite tuh ahi uh, na itna-ankuangluina ua hihmelhemtu leh lauhuai kiselte- amau mah kikem, angsung ngaihtuaha, maingaltaka tathangte; mei tuipai lou, huih mut lengte ahi ua; phavanga sing, gah lou, nihvei sisa, a zung toh bohkhiakte ahi ua;
13. Tuipi kihawt vonvon, amau zahlakna thankhia ahi ua; aksi vialvak, amau dia khantawna mial bingbikbek sit sakte ahi uhi.
14. Huan, Enok, Adam akipana sagihnain hiaite tungtang nagenkholin, Ngai un, Toupa tuh a mi siangthou singkhatte toh a hongpai ahi, chiin,
15. mi tengteng tunga vaihawm ding leh, Pathian limsak loute tengteng Pathian limsak louhna na Pathian limsak loutunga a sep tengteng uah leh, Pathian limsak loumikhialten a tunga thukhauh tak a gen tengteng ua siamlouh tangsak dingin.
16. Huaite tuh phunhatte leh ngohhatte ahi ua, amau utna bengjel in a om ua, a kamte khawng un leng kiotsakna thu a gen gawpgawp a, punna deih manin i a deih uhi.
17. Himahleh, deihtakte aw, noujaw i Toupa Jesu Kris sawltakten thu a genkholhte uh theigige un;
18. hunnanungah te Pathian limsak loulam amau utna bang jela omin, nuihsanhatte a hongom ding uhi, chih na kiang ua a gendan khawng uh.
19. Huai mite tuh sa mi, Kha neilou, pawl tuamte bawlte tuh ahi uhi.
20. Himahleh, deihtakte, nou jaw na ginna siangthou uh tunga noumau leh noumau kilem touin, Kha Siangthoua thumin,
21. khantawn hinna neihsakna khop i Toupa Jesu Kris hehpihna tuh ngak kawmin, Pathian itna a kikem hoih un.
22. Khenkhatte, ginglaha omte tuh hehpih unla;
23. Khenkhatte, meia kipanin la khe zok unla, hondam un; khenkhatte, a sa thanghuai banna uh puansilh lam leng kihin, lau kawmin hehpih un.
24. Tuin, na puk louhna ding ua honveng thei leh, a thupina ma taka nakpi taka nuamsaa nin bangloua nou hon koih theipa,Hondampa Pathian tak kia kiangah, i Toups Jesu Kris jiakin, hun himhim om ma leh tu leh khantawn dongin, pahtawinate thupinate, vaihawmate, thuneihnate om hen. Amen.
25. Hondampa Pathian tak kia kiangah, i Toups Jesu Kris jiakin, hun himhim om ma leh tu leh khantawn dongin, pahtawinate thupinate, vaihawmate, thuneihnate om hen. Amen.
      Jude (1/1)
