←  Nehemiah (7/13)  →
1. Huan hichi ahi a, kulhbang lamkhit a hih a, kongkhakte ka tun khita, kongkhakngakmite leh lasamite leh Levi mite seh a hih khit nung un,
2. Ka unau Hanani, leh Hanania kulh ukpa kiangah Jerusalem kepna ka piaa: mi ginom ahi a, tampi sanga Pathian laudansiam a hih jiakin.
3. Huan a kiangah, Ni a sat mateng Jerusalem kongpite hon hi kei hen; huan galvila a din lai un, kong khakte khak uhenla, amaute kalhkhum un: huan Jerusalema tengte vengmi sep inla, mi chih amah dinmunah, mi chih amah in jawn chiat ah.
4. Huan khopi a jain a liana: himahleh huaiah mite a tawm ua, inte lah lam ahi kei uh.
5. Huchiin ka Pathianin ka lungtang miliante, vaihawmmite, mipite, kaikhawm dingin a koiha, huchia khangsutnaa sim a hih theihna ding un. Huan a masapena honglang amaute khangsutna laibu ka mutaa, huaiah gelha om ka mu:
6. Hiaite bial naupangte ahi ua, Nebukadnezzar Babulon kumpipan a pimangte, huai pimanga omte laka saltanna kipana piakhe toute, Jerusalem leh Judaa kik nawn, mi chih amah khopia kik;
7. Zerubbabel, Jesua, Nehemia, Azaria, Raamia, Nahamani, Mordekai, Bilsan, Mispereth, Bigvai, Nehum, Baana toh hong paite.
8. Israel mite laka pasal zah: Paros tate, sangnih za khat leh sawmsagih leh nih.
9. Sephatia tate, za thum sawmsagih leh nih.
10. Ara tate, za guk sawmnga leh nih.
11. Pahath-moab, tate, Jesua leh Joab tate, sang nih leh za giat leh sawmlehgiat.
12. Elam tate, sang khat za nih sawmnga leh li.
13. Zattu tate, za giat sawmli leh nga.
14. Zakkai tate, za sagih leh sawmguk.
15. Binnui tate, za guk sawmli leh giat.
16. Bebai tate, za guk sawmnih leh giat.
17. Azgad tate, sang nih za thum sawmnih leh nih.
18. Adonikam tate, za guk sawmguk leh sagih.
19. Bigvai tate, sang nih sawmguk leh sagih.
20. Adin tate, za guk sawmnga leh nga.
21. Ater, Hezekia tate, sawmkua leh giat.
22. Hasum tate, za thum sawmnih leh giat.
23. Bezai tate, za thum sawmnih leh li.
24. Harip tate, za khat leh sawmlehnih.
25. Gibeon tate, sawmkua leh nga.
26. Beth-lehem leh Netophah tate, za khat sawmgiat leh giat.
27. Anathot mite, za khat sawmnih leh giat.
28. Beth-azmaveth, mite sawmli leh nih.
29. Kiriathjerim, Kephir, Beer-oth tate, za sagih sawmli leh thum.
30. Rama leh Geba mite, za guk sawmnih leh khat.
31. Mikma tate, za khat leh sawmnih leh nih.
32. Beth-el leh Ai mite, za khat sawmnih leh thum.
33. Nebo dang mite, sawmnga leh nih.
34. Elam dang tate, sang khat za nih sawmnga leh li.
35. Harim tate, za thum leh sawmnih.
36. Jeriko tate, za thum sawmli leh nga.
37. Lod, Hadid leh Ono tate, za sagih sawmnih leh khat.
38. Sena tate, sang thum za kua leh sawmthum.
39. Siampute: Jedaia tate, Jesua inkuan akipan, za kua sawmsagih leh thum.
40. Imer tate, sang khat sawmnga leh nih.
41. Pasur tate, sang khat za nih sawmli leh sagih.
42. Harim tate, sang khat leh sawmlehsagih.
43. Levi mite: Jesua, Kadmiel tate, Hodeva tate, sawmsagih leh li.
44. Lesamite: Asaph tate, za khat sawmli leh giat.
45. Kongkhakngakte: Sallum tate, Ater tate, Talmon tate, Akkub tate, Hatita tate, Sobai tate, za khat sawmthum leh giat.
46. Nithinim mite: Ziha tate, Hasupha tate, Tabbaoth tate;
47. Keros tate, Sia tate, Padon tate;
48. Lebana tate, Hagaba tate, Salmai tate;
49. Hanan tate, Giddel tate, Gahar tate;
50. Reaia tate, Rezin tate, Nekoda tate;
51. Gazzam tate, Uzza tate, Pasea tate;
52. Besai tate, Me-unim tate, Nephusesim tate;
53. Bakbuk tate, Hakupha tate, Harhur tate;
54. Bazlit tate, Mehida tate, Harsa tate;
55. Barko tate, Sisera tate, Tema tate;
56. Nezia tate, Hatipha tate.
57. Solomon sikhate tate: Sotai tate, Sophereth tate, Perida tate;
58. Jaala tate, Darkon tate, Giddel tate;
59. Sephatia tate, Hattil tate, Pakereth-hazzebaim tate, Amon tate.
60. Nithinim mite tengteng leh Solomon sikhate tate, za thum leh sawmkua leh nih.
61. Huan hiaite Tel-melah, Tel-harsa, Kerub, Addon leh Immer akipan pai toute ahi uh: himahleh a pipute uh inkote, hiam a chi uh hiam, Israel akipan ahi hia chih a ensak theikei uhi:
62. Delaia tate, Tobia tate, Nekoda tate, za guk sawmli leh nih.
63. Siampute akipan: Hobaia tate, Hakkoz tate, Barzillai tate, Gilead mi Barzillai tanute laka zi neipa, a min uh tama minsak.
64. Hiaiten khangthusuinaa sima omte lakah a mingelhna uh a zong uh, himahleh muhin a om kei: huaijiakin nin a sim ahi ua siamputna akipan koihkhiak.
65. Huan ukpipain a kiang uah, siampu khat Urim leh Thummim toh a hongsuah mateng thil siangthou penpente laka a nek louh ding uh ahi chih a gen a.
66. Omkhawmte tengteng a vek un sing li leh sang nih leh za thum leh sawmguk,
67. A pasal sikhate uh leh a nungak sikhate uh, sang sagih za thum sawmthum leh sagihte, sim louhin: huan pasal lasa leh numei lasa za nih sawmli leh nga a nei uhi.
68. A sakolte uh za sagih sawmthum leh guk; a sakolneute uh, za nih sawmli leh nga;
69. A sangawngsaute uh, za li sawmthum leh nga; a sabengtungte uh sang guk za sagih leh sawmnih.
70. Huan pipute inkote laka lupente lak akipan khenkhatte nasem dingin a kipia uh. Ukpipain sumbawm ah dangkaeng darik sang khat, maiphiatkuang sawmnga, siampute puan za nga leh sawmthum a pia hi.
71. Huan pupute inkote laka lupente laka khenkhatten dangkaeng darik sang sawmnih leh, dangka puan sang nih leh za nih a pia uhi.
72. Huan mipitelaka adangdangte piak dangkaeng darik sang sawmnih leh dangka puan sang sawmnih leh, siampute puan sawmguk leh sagih ahi.Huchiin siampute leh, Levi mite leh, kongkhakngakte leh lasamite leh, mite laka a khente leh, Nethinim mite leh Israel tengteng a khopite uah a tengta uhi. Huan a kha sagihna a hongtunin, Israel tate a khopite uah a om uhi.
73. Huchiin siampute leh, Levi mite leh, kongkhakngakte leh lasamite leh, mite laka a khente leh, Nethinim mite leh Israel tengteng a khopite uah a tengta uhi. Huan a kha sagihna a hongtunin, Israel tate a khopite uah a om uhi.
