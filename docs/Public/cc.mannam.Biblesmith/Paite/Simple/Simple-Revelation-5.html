1. Huan, amah huai laltutphah tunga tupa khut taklam ah laibu a sunglam leh a polama kigelh chiamtehna sagiha chiamteh ka mutaa.
2. Huan angel hat khatin aw ngaihtaka, Kua ahia laibu tuh honga, huai achiamteha phel tham? chia genkhai ka muta a.
3. Huan vantung hiin, leitung hiam, a hihkeileh lei nuaiah leng huai laibu hong thei hiam, ahihkeileh a tung en thei kuamah a om kei a.
4. Huan huai laibu hongtham hiam, a hihkeileh huai a tung en tham, kuamah a om louh jiakin kei tuh ka kap kapa:
5. Huan upate laka khatin ka kiangah, Kap ken, ngai in, himpinelkai, Juda nam a, David Zungin, laibu th hong thei ding leh huai achiamtehna sagihte leng phel thei dingin a vual zouta, a chi a.
6. Huan, huai laltutphah leh huai gan hing lite leh huai upate lai ah thahsa hileh kilawm, Belamnou, ki sagih leh mit sagih nei, ding ka mu a, huan huai mit sagihte tuh Pathian kha agih, leitung tengtenga sawlkhiakte ahi uhi.
7. Huan, amah a hongpaia, huan laltutphah tunga tupa khut takalama kipan laibu tuh a la a.
8. Huan huai laibu a lak takin, gan hing lite leh upa sawmnih leh lite, gosem khat chiat leh dangkaeng belhbute gim namtuia dim chiat tawiin, Belamnou maah a khupboh ua, huai gim namtiu tuh misiangthoute thumna ahi.
9. Huan la thak khat a sa ua, Laibu tuh la a, huaia achiamtehna te phel tham na hi: thahin na omta a, chi chih leh pau chih leh mi chih leh nam chiha mite Pathian adinga na sisana na leitak jiakin.
10. Huan ka Pathiankiang uah amau lalgam leh siampute hi dingin na bawla; huan amau tuh leitung ah vai a hawm ding uhi, chiin
11. Huan ka et leh, huan laltutphah leh, gan hingte lah upate kimvel ah, angel tampi aw ka zata a; huan a tam dan uh sang sawm mun sang sawm leh a sanga sang tampi ahi ua;
12. thaha om, Belamnou-in hatna khawng, hauhsakna khawng, thilhihtheihna khawng zahtakhuaina khawng, thupina khawng, vualjawlna khawng mu tham ahi, chiin nakpiin a kikou ua.
13. Huan thil siam khempeuh, vana om leh leitunga om leh lei nuaia om leh tuipi tunga om leh amaua thil omte tengtengin, laltutphah tunga tupa kiangah leh, Belamnou kiangah, vualjawlna leh zahtakhuaina leh thupina leh vaihawmna, khantawn khantawnin om hen, a chih uh ka ja a.Huan ganging liten, Amen, a chi ua. Huan upate tuh a khupboh ua, a beta uhi.
14. Huan ganging liten, Amen, a chi ua. Huan upate tuh a khupboh ua, a beta uhi.
