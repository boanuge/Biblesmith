1. Huchiin Israel tengteng khangthu-a simin a om ua; huan, ngaiin, Israel kumpipate laibu ah gelhin a om uhi: huan a tatlekna jiak un Juda bel Babulon lamah sala pi mangin a om hi.
2. Huan amau tantea a khopite ua teng masapente bel, Israel, siampute, Levi mite, leh Nethinim mite ahi uh.
3. Huan Jerusalem ah Juda tate, Benjamin tate, Ephraim leh Manasi tate a teng uhi;
4. Uthai Ammihud tapa, Omri tapa, Imri tapa, Bani tapa, Perez tate ah Juda tapa.
5. Huan Silon mite; Asai piang masapen, a tapate.
6. Huan Zera tapate ah; Jeuel, a unaute, za guk leh sawm kua.
7. Huan Benjamin tapate ah; Sallu Mesullam tapa, Hodavia tapa, Hassenua tapa;
8. Huan Ibnei Jeroham tapa, Ela Uzzi tapa, Mikri tapa, Sallum Sephatia tapa, Reuel tapa, Ibnija tapa;
9. Huan a unaute uh, a khangte uh dungjuiin, za kua leh sawmnga leh guk. Hiai mite tengteng a pipute uh inkotea pipute inkote lupente ahi uhi.
10. Huan siampute ah; Jedai, Jehoiarib; Jachin;
11. Huan Azaria Hilkia tapa, Mesullam tapa, Ahitub tapa, Pathian in a vaihawmpa;
12. Huan Adai Jeroham tapa, Pashur tapa, Malkijab tapa, Maasai Adiel tapa, Jahzera tapa, Mesullam tapa, Mesillemit tapa, Immer tapa;
13. Huan a unaute uh, a pipute uh lupente, sang khat leh za sagih leh sawmguk; Pathian ina nasepna thilhihna dinga mi hithei mahmahte.
14. Huan Levi mite ah; Hashub tapa, Azrikam tapa, Hasabia tapa, Morari tapate ah;
15. Huan Bakbakkar, Heres, Galal, Mattania Mika tapa, Zikri tapa, Asaph tapa;
16. Huan Obadia Semai tapa, Galal tapa, Jeduthun tapa, Berekia Asa tapa, Elkan tapa, Netophath mite khuatea tengte.
17. Huan kongkhak ngakmite; Sallum, Akkub, Talmon, Ahiman, a unaute uh:
18. Sallum bel a pipen ahi; kumpipa kongpi suahlam panga na ngak sek: Levi tate puanin adia kongkhakngakmite ahi uhi.
19. Huan Sallum Kore tapa, Ebiasaph tapa, Korah tapa, a unaute, a pa inkotea, Korah mite, nasepna thilhihnaa heutu ahi ua, biakbuk kongpite ngakmite: huan a pipute uh TOUPA puanin tunga heutu a na hi ua, lutna ngakmite;
20. Huan Phineha Eleazer tapa hun paisaah amaute tunga vaihawmpa ahi a, huan a kiangah TOUPA a om hi.
21. Zekeria Meselemia tapa kihoupihna puanin kongkhak ngakpa ahi a.
22. Hiaite tengteng kongpite kongkhakngakmite hi dia telte za nih leh sawmleh nih ahi uh. Hiaite a khuate ua khangthu-a sim ahi ua, a sepding uh bawlsaa David leh jawlnei Samuelin a nahihkipsa uh.
23. Huchiin amau leh a tate un TOUPA in kongpite a kem ua, a dan danin, biakbuk in nasanin.
24. A pang lite ah, suahlam, tumlam, mallam, simlam ah kongkhakngakmite a om uhi.
25. Huan a unaute uh, a kallaklakin ni sagihnite tengin amau kianga om dingin a hongpai jel ding uh ahi:
26. Kongkhakngak pipen lite lah, Levi mite, sepding sehsaa om ahi ua, Pathian in sunga dantante leh sumkoihnate tungah heutu ahi uhi.
27. Huan huailaia thupiak amau tunga a om jiakin, Pathian in kimvelah a giak ua, huan jingsang tenga huai hon ding thu a mau nasep ahi hi.
28. Huan a lak ua kuate hiamin nasepna tuiumbel suante a kem ua; kikou-a lak lutin a om ua, kikou-a lak khiakin lah a om ngal ua.
29. A lak ua khenkhatte vante, mun siangthoupen tuium-belsuante, tangbuang nelte, uainte, sathaute begaw leh gimnamtuite kem ding a seh ahi uhi.
30. Huan siampute tapate laka khenkhatten gimnamtuite tohhel a bawl uh.
31. Huan Mattithia, Levi mite, Korah mi Sallum ta masapen, laka khatin belliangneite kan thilte tunga nasepna sehsa a nei hi.
32. Huan a unaute uh laka a khen, Kohath mite tapaten tanghou a kem ua, khawlni chiha bawl dingin.
33. Huan hiaite lasamite ahi ua, Levi mite inkote pipute inkote lupente, dantante sunga tenga, nasep dang a kipan awlte ahi uh: sun leh janin a nasepna ua zatin lah a om ngal ua.
34. A khang pumpite uah, hiaite Levi mite pipute inkote lupente ahi uhi, mi pipente: hiaite Jerusalem ah a teng uh.
35. Huan Gibeon ah Gibeon pa a tenga, Jeiel, ji min Maaka:
36. Huan a tapa masapen Abdon, Zur, Kis, Baal, Ner, Nadab;
37. Huan Gedor, Ahie, Zekaria, Miklot.
38. Huan Miklotin Simeam a suanga, Huan amau leng a unaute utoh Jerusalem ah, a unaute uh jawnah a teng uh.
39. Huan Nerin Kis a suanga; huan Kisin Saul a suang; huan Saulin Jonathan, huan Malki-sua, Abinadab, Esbaal.
40. Huan Jonathan tapa bel Meribbaal ahi; huan Merib-baalin Mika a suang hi.
41. Huan Mika tapate; Pithon, Melek Tahrea, Ahaz.
42. Huan Ahazin Jara a suanga; huan Jarain Alamet, Azmavet, Zimri a suang hi;
43. Huan Zimriin Moza a suanga: huan Mozain Binea a suang; huan Rephai a tapa, Eleasa a tapa, Azel a tapa:Huan Azelin tapa guk a neia, a min uh hiaite ahi; Azrikam, Bokeru, Ismael, Seriah, Obadia, Hanan: hiaite Azel tapate ahi uh.
44. Huan Azelin tapa guk a neia, a min uh hiaite ahi; Azrikam, Bokeru, Ismael, Seriah, Obadia, Hanan: hiaite Azel tapate ahi uh.
