1. Huan, angel nihin nitaklamin Sodom khua a va pha suk ua; Lota Sodom kho kulh kongkhak ah a na tua: huan, Lotain amau a na mua, kipahpih dingin a dinga; huan, a mai a leisiin chibai a buka;
2. Huan, Ngai un, ka toupate aw, hehpihtakin na sikha uh in ah lut phot unla, giak unla, na khete uh sil un; huchiin na thou baih ding ua, na pai nawn na ding uh, achia. Himahleh amau, hi lo e, kholak ah ka giak zo ding uh, a chi ua.
3. Ahihhangin aman a jawl jawla; huchiin a kiangah a pi ua, a in ah a lut ua; huan, aman meh a bawl a, tanghou silngou sohlouh a kanga, huan, a neta uh.
4. Himahleh, a lup ma un khua a mi, Sodom khuaa mite ngeiin veng tenga mi tengteng a tek akhangin, a in a um kimvelta ua;
5. Huan, Lota kiangah, Tujana na in a mi honglutte koia om uh ahia? amau ka kituahpih theihna ding un ka kiang uah honpi khiain, a chi ua.
6. Huan, Lota kongkhak bul ah a kiang uah a vapawta, kongkhak a khak nawna.
7. Huan, Ka unaute aw, hehpihtakin hichitelin gilou dah ve ua.
8. Ngai un, pasal kithuahpih ngei nailou tanu nih ka nei ahi; honhehpih unla, amau na kiang uah kon pikhe ding, hoih na sak bangun a tunguah na hih nading uh; hiai mite tungah jaw bangmah hih mahmah kei un, Huaijiakin ahi, ka in sunga a honglut uh, achia.
9. Huan, amau, honhon maiin, a chi ua, Huan, amau mahin, hiai mi soplai hiaia om dingin a hong pem luta, vaihawmpa a om a tum viala: tunjaw amau tunga hih sangin na tungah a hih hoih semsem ding uh, a chi ua. Huan, mi (Lota) a bohta ua, kongkhak suse dingin a honnaih ua.
10. Himahleh miten a khut uh a golh khia ua, Lota a kiang uah in sungah a kailut ua, kongkhak a khak bikbek uh.
11. Huan, kongkhak bula mite, a lian a neuin, a mit uh a tosak ua, huchiin kongkhak a muzou kei uhi.
12. Huan, miten, Lota kiangah, hiai mite lou hiaiah inkuanpihte dang na nei hia? na makpapa leh, na tapate leh, na tanute leh, kho sunga na neih peuhmahte khua akipanin pi khiain:
13. Toupa maa a kikou husa a nathupi jiakin hiai khua hihmang ding ka hi uh; hihmang dingin Toupan lah a honsawl ngala, a chi ua.
14. Huchiin Lota a pai khiaa, a tanute kitenpih, a makpate kiangah a vagena, thou unla hiai khua akipan pawt un, Toupan khua hihmang sin ahi, achia. Himahleh a makpate ngaihin chiamnuih thugen bang lel ahi.
15. Huan, phalvakin, angelten, Thou inla, na ji leh hiaia na tanu nih omte piin; huchilouin jaw hiai kho gawtna ah na mang tel kha ding uh, chiin, Lota a nohta ua.
16. Himahleh a awltat mahmah lai ngala; huchiin, Toupan amah a hehpih jiakin miten a khut leh, a ji khut leh a tanu nihte khut a len ua: huchiin a pikhia ua, kho polamah a koihta uh.
17. Huan, hichi ahi a, polamah a pikhiak khit un, Na hinna dingin taikhiain; na nunglam nga kenla, Phaijang himhim ah leng khawl ken; hua tang sang ah suahtak sanin, huchilouinjaw na mang tek kha ding hi, achia.
18. Himahleh Lotain a kiang uah, ka Toupa, huchijaw hi kei hen:
19. Ngaiin, na sikhain na hehpihna a mua, nang na chitna na liansaka, huai chitna ka hinna humbitin ka tungah na langsak ahi; hua tang sang ah jaw ka tai theikei ding, thil hoihlou takin a honphain ka si kha ding:
20. Ngaiin, hua khua taina dingin a nai pena, kho neu chilik leng ahi ngala: aw, huailaiah (kho neu chilik ahi ka hia?) hontai sak jawin, huchiin ka hing ding hi, achia.
21. Huan, aman, a kiangah, Ngaiin, huai thil tungtangah leng kon mang hi, na kho gen ka hihmang dah ding.
22. Kin inla, huailaiah taiin; huailai na tun mateng lah bangmah ka hih thei sin ngal keia, achia. Huaijiakin huai kho min dingin Zoar a sata uh.
23. Lota Zoar khua a tunin leitungah ni a hongsuak khinta.
24. Huan, Toupan Sodom leh Gomorra kho tungah van a Toupa kianga kipanin kat leh mei a zu saka;
25. Huchiin, huai khuate leh, Phaijang tengteng leh, khua a mite tengteng leh, leitunga thil poute a hihmang vek hi.
26. A ji bel Lota nung lama kipanin a lehngata, chi khuam a hongsuakta hi.
27. Huan, Abraham bel takin Toupa maa a dinna munah a hohtou a:
28. Huan, Sodom leh Gomorra kho lam leh Phaijang gam tengteng lam a ena, a en gigea; huan, ngaiin, huai gam a meikhu halzulna meikhu bang maiin khu tou ngoingoi hi.
29. Huan, hichi ahia, Pathianin Phaijang khuate a hihman laia, Lota manthatna laka kipanin a sawlkhia hi.
30. Huan, Lota Zoar khua akipan a pai toua, tangah a oma, a tanu nihte leng a kiangah a om uh. Zoar khua ah lah a om ngam ngal kei ua; huan, amah leh a tanu nihte kohawm ah a omta uhi.
31. Huan, a upa jawin, a naupang jaw kiangah, I pa a upataa, khovel tengteng dan banga honkithuahpih dingin lei ah lah pasal khat lel leng a om nawn ngal keia:
32. Kisain, I pa uain I dawnsak dia, huchiin I pa chi suan bei loua I bawl theihna dingin a kiangah I lum ding, achia.
33. Huchiin, huai janin a pa uh uain a dawnsakta ua: huan, a upa jaw a luta, a pa kiangah a valuma; huan, aman a lup lam a phawk keia, a thoh nawn lam leng a phawk sam kei hi.
34. Huan, hichi ahia, a jingin a upa jawin a naupang jaw kiangah, ngaiin, jan janin ka pa kiangah ka luma; tu janin leng uain I dawn sak nawn dia; huan, nang lut inla, I pa chi suan bei loua I bawl theihna dingin a kiangah valum samin, achia.
35. Huchiin, huai janin leng a pa uh uain a dawn sak nawn ua: huan, a naupang jaw a thou a, a kiangah a valumtaa; huan, aman a lup lama phawk keia, a thoh nawn lam leng a phawk sam kei.
36. Huchibangin Lota tanu nihten a pa uh lakah nau a pai tuaktuak uhi.
37. Huan, a upa jawin tapa a nei a, a min din Moab a sa: huai mi tulaia Moabte suangpa ahi.Huan, a naupang jawin leng tapa a neia, amin dingin Ben-Ammi a saa: huai mi tulaia Ammon suante suangpa ahi hi.
38. Huan, a naupang jawin leng tapa a neia, amin dingin Ben-Ammi a saa: huai mi tulaia Ammon suante suangpa ahi hi.
