1. Huan, Solomonin Aigupta kumpia Pharo toh kizolhna a bawla, Pharo tanu a kitenpiha, amah in leh Toupa in a lam ma leh, Jerusalem khua kulha a um suak ma siah David khopi ah a om sak hi.
2. Mipite lah mun sangte khawngah a kithoih mai nak ua, huai main Toupa min dinga in lam a om nai tak dih louh jiakin.
3. Solomonin Toupa a ita, a pa David thusehte a jui seka; mun sangtea kithoihin, gimlimte a nahal sek mawk naa,
4. Huan, kumpipa kithoih dingin Gibeon khuaah a hoha; huai lah mun sang poimoh tak ahi ngala: Solomonin huai maitam tungah halmanga thillat sangkhat tal alan hi.
5. Huan, Gibeon khuaah Toupa janin Solomon kiangah amangin a hongkihila: huan, Pathianin, ngen ve, bang ahia ka honpiak ding? a chi a.
6. Huan, Solomonin, na sikha ka pa David na mitmuha thutaka leh diktatna leh, lungtang dik tak puaa a om jel bangin nang a kiangah hehpihna thupi mahmah nalangsaka; nang hiai hehpihna thupi mahmah amah adingin na deihguana, nang a laltutphaha tu ding tapa na piaa, tunia a hongom petmah bangin.
7. Huchiin, aw Toupa ka Pathian, nang na sikha, ka pa David sikin kumpipa dingin na nabawltaa: kei jaw naupang lel ka hia; thu tung lam leh kuan lam leng ka theikei hi.
8. Na sikha na mite na telte, mi tampi, a tam jiak ua sim seng vual louh leh, a zah theih seng vuallouhte lakah a om hi.
9. Huchiin, na mite vaihawmsakna dingin na sikha theihna lungtang piain, a sia leh a pha ka khen theihna dingin; na mi hiai zahzah kuana hia vaihawm sak thei ding? a chi a.
10. Huan, Solomonin huai a nget jiakin huai thu genin Toupa a kipaksak mahmah mai a.
11. Huan, Pathianin a kiangah, nang dam sawtna kingetsak loua, huchibang na nget jiakin leh, hausaknate leng kingetsak loua, na melmate sihna leng ngen loua, vaihawm siamna na kingetsak jawk jiakin,
12. Ngaiin, na nget bangin ka hihta: Ngaiin, pilna leh theihna lungtang ka honpia; huchiin, na maah nang bang mahmah kuamah a om kei ua, na nungchiangin leng nang bang mahmah kuamah a hongom sam kei ding uh.
13. Huan, na ngetlouh, hausakna leh thupina leng ka honpe lai hi; huchiin na dam sungin kumpipate lakah nang bang mahmah a om kei ding uh.
14. Na pa Davidin a zuih banga ka thusehte leh ka thupiak te pom dinga ka lampite na zuih leh ka hondam sawt sak lai ding, achia.
15. Huan, Solomon a khanglou a, ngai in, mang ana hi: huan, Jerusalem ah a hoh a, Toupa thukhun na bawm maah a dinga, halmanga thillat leh lemna thillatte a lana, a sikhate tengteng a dingin ankuang a lui.
16. Huchiin numei nih, kijuak, kumpipa kiangah a hoh ua, a maah ava ding uh.
17. Huan, numei khat in, aw ka pu, hiai numei toh in khata teng khawm ka hi ua; huai inna ka om tei laiin nau ka nei a.
18. Huan, hichi ahia, ka nau neih nung ni thum nungin hiai numeiin leng a nei sama; om khawm ka hi ua; huai ina om kou tegel lou, huai in ah kuamah mikhual om a om kei uh.
19. Huan, hiai numei ta janin a nasi a; a delh lup ahi.
20. Huchiin, jan laiin a thou a, na sikhanu a ihmut kalin, ka sika ka tapa a la a, a poma, ata sipen ka angah ana sial.
21. A jingsanga ka ta nawi pe dinga ka thoh leh, ngaiin, a si ana hi nilouh mawk hi: himahleh jingsanga ka etchet leh ngaiin, ka tapa, ka suanpen ana hi hetkei hial hi, achia.
22. Huan, numei dangin, Hi lou e, a hingpen ka tapa ahi jaw, a sipen na tapa ahi, a chi tei a. Khatin, Hi lou, a sipen na tapa ahi, a hing pen ka tapa ahi jaw, a chi chiltel sam a. Huchibangin kumpipa maah a gentuaktuak uh.
23. Huchiin, kumpipan, Khatin, ahingpen ka tapa ahi: a sipen na tapa ahi, a chi a: huan, khatin, Hilou, a sipen na tapa ahi, a hingpen ka tapa ahi jaw, a chi jel ngala, achia.
24. Huan, kumpipan, namsau honva lak dih uh, a chi a, huchiin namsau kumpipa maah a honla ua.
25. Huan, kumpipan, naungek hingpen tum nih suah unla, a kimkhat khat pia unla, a kimkhat khat pia un, a chi a.
26. Huchiin, naungek hingpen nuin a ta a lainat mahmah mai ngala, kumpipa kiangah, Aw ka pu, naungek hingpen amah pe maiin, a thah a hihleh that mahmah ken, a chi a. Khatpenin bel, keia lah hi tuan kenteh, nanga lah hi tuan ken teh: tumtan maiin, a chi a.
27. Huchiin, kumpipan a dawnga, naungek hingpen amah pia un, that het kei un: amah mah a nu ahi, a chi a.Huan, Israelte tengtengin huai thua kumpipa vaihawm dan ana ja ua; kumpipa akihtata mahmah mai uh: vaihawmna dingin Pathian pil na amah ah a om chi a thei ngal ua.
28. Huan, Israelte tengtengin huai thua kumpipa vaihawm dan ana ja ua; kumpipa akihtata mahmah mai uh: vaihawmna dingin Pathian pil na amah ah a om chi a thei ngal ua.
