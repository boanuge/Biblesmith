1. Huan, Mosi in Israel suante namte inntek pente a houpih a, Hiai ahi, TOUPA'N thu a piaktak.
2. Mi'n TOUPA kiang a thu achiam hiam, a lungsim thuchiam a kigak ding a a kichiam hiam a thu a bohse ding ahi kei; kam a pawt bangbang a a hih ding ahi.
3. Numei in leng a nungak lai a a pa inn a a om lai in TOUPA kiang a thu achiam a, thuchiam a a kigak a;
4. A thuchiam leh thuchiam a a lungsim a gak a pa'n a zak a, a pa'n a daihsan maimah leh a thuchiam tengteng a piching ding: Huan, thuchiam chiteng a a lungsim a gaknate a om gige ding.
5. Himahleh a pa'n a zak ni a a phal kei leh a thuchiamte a kigakna thuchiamte a koi mahmah a piching kei ding: a pa'n a phallouh a hihziak in TOUPA'N amah a ngaidam ding.
6. Thuchiam in hiam, thu khual lou a a lungsim gakna in hiam a delh lai a pasal a neih a;
7. A pasal in a zak a, a zak ni a a daih dide leh a thuchiam a om gige ding a, a lungsim gakna khual lou a a thuchiam leng a om gige ding.
8. Himahleh a pasal in a zak ni a a phal kei leh a tung a thuchiam leh a lungsim gakna khual lou a a thuchiam a hihmang ding; TOUPA'N a ngaidam ding.
9. Himahleh huai meithai hiam, maak a omnu hiam thuchiam zaw, a lungsim a gakna tengteng in a delh gige ding.
10. Huan, inn a thu achiam a, kichiam a a lungsim gak ding a thu a khun a:
11. A pasal in a zak a leng a daih dide a, a phallouh kei leh a thuchiam tengteng a om gige ding a, a lungsim gak ding a a thuchiam chiteng leng a om gige ding.
12. Himahleh a pasal in a zak ni a bangmah lou a bawl a, a thihmang leh a thuchiam hiam, a lungsim gakna hiam thu a a kam a thu pawt peuhmah a om nawn kei ding: a pasal in a hihmang khin ahi: huan, TOUPA'N amah a ngaidam ding.
13. Thuchiam tengteng leh hinna hihgim ding a gakna kichiamna tengteng a pasal in a kipsak thei in a hihmang thei hi.
14. Himahleh a pasal in a zak ni a kipan a a deih den leh a thuchiam chiam tengteng leh a tung a gakna om tengteng a kipsak ahi a: a zak ni a kipan a a daihsan ziak in a kipsak ahi.
15. Himahleh a zak khit nung a bangmah lou a bawl a a hihman leh a thulimlouhna moh a puak ding ahi, chi in.Hiaite, pasal leh a zi kal leh a pa inn a om gige lai a kikal ua dan ding a TOUPA'N Mosi thu a piakte ahi.
16. Hiaite, pasal leh a zi kal leh a pa inn a om gige lai a kikal ua dan ding a TOUPA'N Mosi thu a piakte ahi.
