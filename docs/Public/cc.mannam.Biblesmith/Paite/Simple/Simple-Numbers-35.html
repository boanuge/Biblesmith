1. Huan, Jeriko zawn Jordan gei, Moab phai ah TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
2. Israel suante thupia inla, a gam uah Levite omna ding khuate bawlsak uhen; huan, a gante uh tatna ding in kho dainawl kim khawng Levite na piak ding ahi.
3. Khuate a omna ding uh ahi ding a, a kimte khawng bel bawng leh a gante uh, a gan vulh tengteng uh a ding ahi ding.
4. Huan, dainawl Levite na piak ding kho kulh a kipan tong sangkhat a sau ahi ding ahi.
5. Huan, kulh polam a kipan in na teh ding a, suah lam pang ah tong sangnih, mal lam pang ah tong sangnih, khua lai tak a om in. Hiai amau dainawl tuam ahi ding.
6. Huan, Levite na piak ding khuate tualthatte taina ding kihumbitna khopi guk ahi ding a; huaite lou kho sawmli leh kho nih na piak lai ding ahi.
7. Levite na piak ding khua tengteng kho sawmli leh kho giat ahi ding: dainawl toh na piak ding ahi.
8. Israel suante luah khua na piakdan ding, a tam deuh a kipan in pe tam ding a, a tawm deuh a kipan in na pe tawm ding a, michih in a luah uh gam tamdan dungzui a khua Levite a piak ding ahi, chi in
9. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
10. Israel suante houpih inla, a kiang uah,
11. Jordan lui na galkah ua Kanan gam na lut chiang un kihumbitna khua ding khua na seh ding uh ahi, tualthat kha mi taina ding in.
12. Huai khuate phula mi a kipan na kihumbitna khua uh ahi ding; omkhawmte ma a vaihawm sak ding a a din ma a tualthatmi a sih louhna ding in.
13. Huchi in, nou a ding a kihumbitna ding na sehte uh khopi guk ahi ding.
14. Jordan galah khopi thum na sep ding ua, Kanan gamah khopi thum na sep ding ua, kihumbitna khopite ahi ding uh.
15. Israel suante leh nam dang leh a kiang ua tengte di'n hiai khopi guk kihumbitna ahi ding; huai lai ah kuapeuh mi that kha a tai ding uhi.
16. Himahleh sik zang a mi a sual a, a sih leh tualthat ahi a: tualthat bel hihlup ngeingei ding ahi.
17. Huan, si khop a suang a mi a den a a sih leh tualthat ahi a, tualthat bel hihlup ngeingei ding ahi.
18. Huan, si khop a mi sing a a sat a, a sih leh tualthat ahi a, tualthat bel hihlup ngeingei ding ahi.
19. Sisan phula mi'n tualthat mi a that pah mai ding: a muh peuh chiang in a hihlum ding ahi.
20. Huan, mi a huat ziak a a sawm ek hiam, na guk tan a a den hiam a a sih in hiam,
21. Kiselna ziak a tuplup hiam a hihleh, a hihlum mi pen hihlup ngei ding ahi.
22. A hihhang in kisel ziak hi khol lou a sawm guih hiam, huan, tang hi khol lou a, mi a om uh chih leng theilou a,
23. A melma leng hi lou a, a poikhoih tum leng hi tuan lou a suang hiam a den a, mi a na sih a, a hih leh:
24. Omkhawmte'n hiai vaihawmna dan bang in a hihlum mi leh sisan phula mi vai a hawm sak ding uh ahi:
25. Huan, omkhawmte'n tualthat mi sisan phula mi lak a kipan a honkhe ding ua, omkhawmte'n a taina, kihumbitna khua ah a omsak nawn ding ua, sathau siangthou nilh siampu lian a sih mateng huailai ah a om ding hi.
26. Himahleh chiklai peuh in tualthat mi a tai na, a kihumbitna kho gamgi po lam a pai a;
27. Sisan phula mi in a kihumbitna kho po lam a a muh a sisan phula mi in a thah leh sisan ah moh a tang kei ding;
28. Siampu lian a sih mateng a kihumbitna khua om gige ding a hihziak in; siampu a sih nung in bel tualthat mi a omna gam a pai nawn ding ahi.
29. Hiai thilte na suante uh tan a na omna peuh ua na vaihawm dan ding uh ahi.
30. Kuapeuh in mi a thah leh tualthat mi pen theihpihte kam a kipan thupawt a hihlup ding ahi: himahleh theihpih khat zaw mi hihlupna ding in a hun kei.
31. Huan, tualthat mi zaw na tat ding uh ahi kei, sihna khop dia moh ahi a, hihlup ngei ding ahi zaw.
32. Huan, siampu sih ma in, a kihumbitna khua a tai, a gam a om nawn ding a tatna na lak ding uh ahi kei.
33. Huchi in na omna gam uh na hih thanghuai kei ding ua, sisan in gam a hih thanghuai leh sisan suahna gam hih thanghuai silna di'n a suahmi sisan kia lou lah zat theih ahi ngal kei a.Na omna gam uh, ka omna na hih thanghuai ding uh ahi kei; kei TOUPA lah Israel suante lak ah ka om ngal a, chi in, chi in.
34. Na omna gam uh, ka omna na hih thanghuai ding uh ahi kei; kei TOUPA lah Israel suante lak ah ka om ngal a, chi in, chi in.
