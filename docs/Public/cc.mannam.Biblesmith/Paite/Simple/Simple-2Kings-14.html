1. Israel kumpipa Jehoahaz tapa Jehoas kum nih a lal kumin Juda kumpipa Joas tapa Amazia a lal pan.
2. A lal pat tungin kum sawmnih leh kum ngaa upa ahi a, Jerusalem ah kum sawmnih leh kum kua a lal; a nu min Jehoadan, Jerusalem mi ahi.
3. Huan, Toupa mitmuhin thil diktat tak ahih seka, a pu David jaw a pha kei; a pa Joas thilhih bang ahih teisam jel hi.
4. Himahleh mun sangte jaw hihman ahi tuankei; mun sangte ah mite a kithoih lailai un, gimlim a hal gige uh.
5. Huan, a gam a hong din kiptakin hichi ahi a, a mite, apa, kumpipa thatmite a that thuk sam hi;
6. Himahleh huai tualthatmite tate jaw a that kei; Mosi dan bu a, tate jiakin pate hihlup ding ahikeia, pate jiakin tate hihlup ding ahi samkei, michih amau khelhna jiakin a si ding uh ahi, chi a Toupa thupiak dan gelh bangin
7. Edomte chi Guam ah singkhat a that a, Sela khua a suala, alaa, a min dingin Joktheel a saa, tutanin a salai uhi.
8. Huchiin Amaziain Israel kumpipa Jehu tapa Jehoahaz ta Jehoas kiangah, Hongkuanin, kimatuahin I ki-en peuhmah ding, chiin mi a sawla.
9. Huan, Israel kumpipa Jehoasin Juda kumpipa Amazia kiangah, Lebanon tanga lingin Lebanon tanga sidar sing kiangah, Na tanu ka tapa ji dingin neisak dih ve, chiin a thukhaka; huan, Lebanon tanga gamsa a hongpaia, huai ling a hongtuan puk gawpa.
10. Nang Edomte na zou ngeia, na lungsim hong kisathei eita ve; na kisaktheihna ompih maimaiin; nang leh Juda nangawn tana siatna, nang adia hoih lou ding thil bangdinga khoih khe ding na hia? Chiin a dawng.
11. Himahleh Amaziain a na ngaikhe nuam keia. Huchiin Israel kumpipa Jehoas a vahoh tou a; Juda kumpipa Amazia toh Juda gam Beth-semes khuaah kimatuahin a ki-en ta ngei ua.
12. Huan, Juda bel Israelte lakah a zahlaktaa; mi chih a mau in lam chiat ah a pai chiat uhi.
13. Huan, Israel kumpipa Jehoasin Juda kumpipa Ahazia tapa Joas ta Amazia bel Bath-semes ah a mana, Jerusalem khuaah a hoha, Jerusalem kulh Ephraim kongpi a kipana kulh ning kongpi pha in tong zali tak a hihchim.
14. Huan, Toupa ina leh kumpipa ina gou koih khawmnaa, dangkaeng leh dangka leh tui um belsuan tengteng a omom a laa, thuchiam tangtunna dia henmite toh, huchiin, Samari kho lamah a pai nawntaa.
15. Huchiin Jehoas thilhih tanchindangte, a thilhihtheihdante, Juda kumpipa Amazia a sual thute Israel kumpipate lal lai thu gelhna bu ah a tuang ahi ka hia?
16. Huan, Jehoas a pi leh pute kiangah a ihmu-a, Samari khuaah Israel kumpipate lakah a vui uh; huan, a tapa Jeroboam a sikin a lalta hi.
17. Huan, Juda kumpipa Joas tapa Amazia bel Israelte kumpipa Jehoahaz tapa Jehoas sih nungin kum sawm leh kum nga a dam lai.
18. Huchiin Amazia tanchin dangte bel Juda kumpipate lal lai thu gelhna bu ah a tuang ahi kei maw?
19. Huan, Jerusalem khuaah amah a gukin a sawm ua; huchiin Lakis khuaah mi a delhsak ua, huailaiah a that uhi.
20. Huan, a luang sakolin a paipih ua, a pi leh pute lakah David khopi Jerusalem ah a vui uh.
21. Huan, Juda mi tengtengin kum sawm leh kum guka upa, Azaria a pi ua, a pa Amazia sikin kumpipain a bawl uhi.
22. Aman Elath khua a bawlhoih nawna, Juda gamah a pang sak nawn, huainungin kumpipa a pi leh pute kiangah a ihmuta.
23. Juda kumpipa Joas tapa Amazia kum sawm leh kum nga a lal kumin Israel kumpipa Jehoas tapa Jeroboam bel Samari khua ah a lal pana, kum sawmli leh kum khat a lal.
24. Huan, Toupa mitmuhin thil hoih lou pipi a hihseka; Israelte thil hihkhialsaka a hih Nebat tapa Jeroboam khelhna tengteng paisan himhim a neikei,
25. Toupa, Israelte Pathianin a sikha, Gath-hepher mi Amittai tapa jawlnei Jona zanga a thugen bangin, Hamath lutna akipana Araba tuipi phain Israel gamah a pangsak nawn hi.
26. Toupan Israelte haksatna a thupi mahmah chih lah a thei ngala; nehchihleh chih loute leng lah a om ngal kei ua, Israelte panpih ding kuamah lah om sam kei uh.
27. Toupan Israel min van nuaiah a chimitsak zenzen sin chih a hihleh a gen kei hima; Jehoas tapa Jeroboam zangin a hon dam zota ahi.
28. Huchiin Jeroboam tanchin dangte, a thilhih tengtengte, a thilhihtheihdante, a gal sual thu te, tuma lama Juda gam tan hi gige Damaska leh Hamath khua Israelte a dingin a nalak saknawn thute Israel kumpipate lal lai thu gelhna bu ah a tuang ahi kei maw?Huan, Jeroboam bel a pi leh pu, Israel kumpipate kiangah a ihmu teitaa; huchiin a tapa Zekaria a sikin a lalta hi.
29. Huan, Jeroboam bel a pi leh pu, Israel kumpipate kiangah a ihmu teitaa; huchiin a tapa Zekaria a sikin a lalta hi.
