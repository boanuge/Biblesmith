1. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi leh Aron a houpih a,
2. Israel suante houpih unla, a kiang uah, Mi kua leng sa a thil a pawt leh a thil pawt ziak in a nin ahi.
3. A thil pawt sung teng a nin ahi: a sa ah a pawt gige hiam, pawt tawpta hiam zong hileh, a nin himhim ahi.
4. Thil pawt mi lupna a lupna peuhmah a nin ding; huan, a tutna peuhmah leng a nin lai ding a.
5. Huan, kuapeuh a lupna khoih peuhmahte'n a puante uh a sawp ding ua, a kisil ding uh ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh.
6. Thil pawt mi tutna a tu peuhmahte'n a puante uh a sawp ding ua, a kisil ding uh ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh.
7. Kuapeuh thil pawt mi sa khoih peuhmah in a puante uh a sawp ding ua, a kisil ding uh ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh.
8. Huan, thil pawt mi in mi ninlou chil a siat leh a puante a sawp ding a, a ksil ding ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding.
9. Tuan na bangpeuh a leng thil pawtmi a tuan leh a nin ding.
10. Kuapeuh a kingaihna sa nung thil khoih peuhmahte nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh; huan, huai thil la suan mi peuhmah in a puante uh a sawp ding uh, a kisil ding uh ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding uhi.
11. Huan, kuapeuh thil pawt mi in a khut sillou a a khoih peuhmah in a puante uh a sawp ding ua, a kisil ding uh ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh.
12. Huan, thil pawt mi leibel khoihsa zaw suktap ding ahi; huan, sing vanzat tengteng silsiang vek ding ahi.
13. Huan, thil pawt mi bel, a thil pawt hih siangthou a hih leh, a kisian sakna ding in ni sagih a kipe ding a, a puante a sawp ding a; huan, luitui ah a kisil ding a; huchi in a hong siangthou ta ding hi.
14. Huan, a ni giat ni in vakhu kop khat hiam, palva noukop khat hiam tawi in TOUPA ma ah kihoupihna puan inn kongpi bul ah a hoh ding a, siampu a pe ding ahi:
15. Huan, siampu in khelh thoihna ding in khat, haltum thillat ding in khat a len ding hi; huan, siampu in a thil pawt thu ah TOUPA ma ah kilepna a bawl sak ding.
16. Kuapeuh kithuahna chi amah a kipan a pawt leh a pumpi in a kisil vek dia, nitak lam tan in a nin ding.
17. Huan, puansilh khawng, vun khawng kithuahna chi omna peuhmah sawp siang a silsiang vek ding ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding
18. Huan, numei a luppih leleng a nih ua a kisil tuaktuak ding uh ahi, huan, nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh.
19. Numei in thil pawt a neih a, a sa a thil pawt bel si a hihleh ni sagih sung a nin ding; huan, kuapeuh amah khoih taphot nitak lam tan in a nin ding.
20. Huan, a nit sung a lupna peuhmah a nin ding a, a tutna peuhmah leng a nin lai ding hi.
21. Lupna khoih peuhte'n a puante uh a sawp ding ua, a kisil ding uh ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh.
22. Kuapuah a tutna khoih peuhmahte'n a puante uh a sawp ding ua, a kisil ding uh ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh.
23. Lupna a thil om hiam, a tutna a thil om hiam khoih peuhmahte nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh.
24. Kuapeuh in a luppih ua, a nitna a ban uleh ni sagih a nin ding ua; a lupna peuh uh lupna a nin ding hi.
25. Huan, numei a nit hun ngeina hilou a ni sawtkuam tak a si a pawt hiam, a nitdan ngeina sang a sawt a si a pawt hiam a hihleh, a nitna dan ngeina sung a a om zel bang in si pawtmi a nit sung teng in a om tei ding hi; a nin ahi.
26. A si pawt sung teng a lupna a lupna taphot a nitdan ngeina hun lai a a lupna bang ahi ding a; a tutna peuhmah a nitdan ngeina hun lai a a nit bang in a nin ding.
27. Kuapeuh huai thil khoih peuhmahte a nin ding ua, a puante uh a sawp ding ua, kisil ding uh ahi, nitak lam tan in a nin ding uh.
28. Himahleh a si pawt pen hih siangthou a hihleh, ni sagih a kipe ding a, huai nung in a hong siangthou ta ding.
29. Huan, a ni giat ni in vakhu kop khat hiam, vapal nou kop khat hiam a tawi ding a, kihoupihna puan inn kongpi bul ah siampu a hong pe ding ahi.
30. Huan, siampu in khelh thoihna ding in khat, haltum thillat ding in khat a lan ding ahi; huchi in siampu in si pawt mi a nitna thu ah TOUPA ma ah kilepna a bawl sak ding ahi
31. Hichi bang in Israel suante a nitna ua kipan na hih tuam ding uh ahi, a lak ua ka biakbuk a hih buah khak chiang ua leng a nitna ziak ua a sih louhna ding un.
32. Hiai bel thil pawt mite leh nit loh a kithuahna chi pawt mite a dia dan ahi;A nitna ziak a china nu bang, thil pawt nei mipa bang, numei-pasal, numei a nit lai ua luppihte a ding leng, chi in.
33. A nitna ziak a china nu bang, thil pawt nei mipa bang, numei-pasal, numei a nit lai ua luppihte a ding leng, chi in.
