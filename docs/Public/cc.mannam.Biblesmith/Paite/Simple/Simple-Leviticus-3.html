1. Huan, a thillat lemna thillat kithoihna a hih a, bawng hon lak a a lat leh, TOUPA ma ah gensiatbei a lan ding ahi.
2. A thillat lu tungah a khut a koih ding a, kihoupihna puan inn kongpibul ah a gou ding: huan, Aron tapa, siampute'n maitam sisan in a kimvel in a theh ding uh ahi.
3. Huan, lemna thillat kithoihna a TOUPA a dia mei a thillat a lan ding a: a sungkua thau leh a gilboh tengteng leh,
4. A kal nihte leh a tuam thau a phuk chin a om leh a sin tuam lam, a kalte toh kigawm in a suah ding ahi.
5. Huan, Aron tapate'n maitam ah, mei a sing om tung a haltum thillat tungah a hal ding uhi: huai bel TOUPA a ding a gimlim tak, mei a thillat ahi.
6. Huan, lemna thillat kithoihna a TOUPA a ding a a thillat belam hon lak ate a hihleh, a nu leng hita hen aw, a pa zong hita leh, gensiatbei a lat ding ahi.
7. A thillat a belamnou a lat leh TOUPA ma ah a lan ding a:
8. A thillat lu tungah a khut a koih ding a, kihoupihna puan inn kongpi ah a gou ding: huan, Aron tapate'n maitam sisan in a kimvel in a theh ding uhi.
9. Huchi in lemna thillat kithoihna ate, TOUPA ding a mei a thillat a lan ding a: a thaute, a mei thau mahmah a tawn in, a kawngguh tan in a la vek ding a; a sungkua thaute, a gil boh tengtengte,
10. A kal nihte leh a phuk chin a om a tuam thau leh a sin tuam a lap, a kalte nangawn toh a suah ding ahi
11. Huan, siampu in huai maitam ah a hal ding ahi: huai bel TOUPA a dia mei a thillat nek ding annsiah ahi.
12. A thillat kel a hih leh TOUPA ma ah a lan ding a:
13. Huan, a lu tungah a khut a koih ding a, kihoupihna puan in kong ah a gou ding a; huan, Aron tapate'n maitam sisan in a kimvel in a theh ding uh ahi.
14. Huan, huai a a thillat pen a lan ding a, TOUPA a dia mei a thillat tak; a sungkua thau leh a gilboh tengteng leh,
15. A kal nihte leh a phuk chin a om a tuam thau leh a sin tuam lam, a kalte toh nangawn in a suah ding ahi.
16. Huan, huai pen siampu in maitam ah a hal ding ahi: gimlim ding a mei a thillat nek ding annsiah ahi a; a thau tengteng TOUPA a tuam ahi.Sathau hiam, sisan hiam na ne ding uh ahi kei chih na suante uh ding a banban in na omna phot uah khantawn a thuseh ahi ding, chi in, chi in.
17. Sathau hiam, sisan hiam na ne ding uh ahi kei chih na suante uh ding a banban in na omna phot uah khantawn a thuseh ahi ding, chi in, chi in.
