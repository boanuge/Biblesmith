1. Huan TOUPA thu ka kiangah a hongtung nawna,
2. Mihing tapa, Israel jawlnei, mah lungtang kia akipana gen chingte tungah thu gen inla, TOUPA thu ngai un, TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin a chi ahi.
3. Jawlnei hai, amau ngaihdan juia, bangmah lah mu ngal loute a tung uh a gik hi:
4. Aw Israel, na jawlneite kho Tulsa sehal bang lel a hita uh.
5. TOUPA nia kidounaa ding zou dingin a vana tak na phu ngam kei ua, Israel inkote human ah lah n aphatuam sam kei uh.
6. TOUPA thu pawt ahi chimiten bangmah lou lel leh gen kholhna lim lel a tang ua, TOUPA sawl ahi kei himhim uhi; a thu uh hi ngei ding bangin mite a gingta sak ua.
7. Kilakna hongvuaksuak lel na mu un, thu gen kholh dik lou na gen uh ahi kei maw? TOUPA thupawt ahi na chi nawn ngal ua, kenlah ka gen het kei a.
8. Huaijiakin TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin a chi ahi: Bangmah lou phet na gen ua juau lel na muh jiak un, ngai un, ka hon nang ahi, chih TOUpa thu pawt ahi.
9. Huan ka khutin bangmahlou mute leh juau gen kholmite a nang dinga, ka mite kidot khawmna ah a tel kei ding ua, Israel innkuante min gelhna ah gelhin a om sam kei ding; huchiin TOUPA PATHIANIN ka hi chih na thei ding uh.
10. Galmuan om lou pia, Muan huai e, chia mite a pi mang jiak un leh mi bang bawla, sunu nuhmiten,
11. A ke nawn mai ding, vuahpi a hongzu dinga, nou gial lian pite aw, na hongke ding ua, huih thupiin a hihse ding, chiin.
12. Huan, Ngai un, bang a chip khit chiangin, Na sunu nuh uh koiah, a honchi kei ding ua hia?
13. Huaijiakin TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin a chi ahi: Ka hehin huihpi thupiin ka hihsevek ding, ka hehin gial thupi tak hihmang dingin a hongke ding.
14. Huchiin sunu na nuh ngou bang uh ka chim sak dinga, lei phain a chim dia, huchiin a suangphumte a dawk hial ding hi: huan a chim dinga, bang chim ah hihmangin na om ding uh; huchiin TOUPA ka hi chih na thei ding uh.
15. Hichibangin bang tungah leh sunu nuhmite tungah ka hehna ka lungdam sak ding; huan, Bang a om nawn keia, a belmite leng a om nawn ta kei uh, ka honchi ding hi.
16. Huai bel Israel jawlnei Jerusalem tung thu gena, galmuanna om lou pia galmuanna musakmite ngei, a om nawn kei uh, chih TOUPA PATHIAN thu pawt ahi.
17. Huan, nang mihing tapa, na mite tanute, a lungtang lel ua genmite masuan inla, amau kalhin thu genin, TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin a chi ahi:
18. Numei, mite bana sik bunsak lehmi hinnate mat belhna dinga mi lian mite lu khuhna khuimite a tung uh a gik hi: Nou hinna dingin ka mite hinna na man ding uh maw?
19. Buhman tawmchik leh tanghou them mu dinga si lou ding sisak ding leh hing lou ding hingsak dingin na juau gen uh ka miten a up jiak un ka mite lakah ka min a thanghuai ahi.
20. Huaijiakin TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin a chi a: Ngai un, mi hinna, vasa banga man dinga na thil but sakte uh k amudah ahi; na bante uh ka bot dinga, na mi mat dingte uh hinna ka pawt sak vek ding; vasa banga na mat dingte uh hinna.
21. Na lukhuhnate uh leng ka botkek dinga, ka mite na khut ua kipan ka suakta sak ding, na mat theih ding un na khut uah a omta kei ding uh; huchiin TOUPA ka hi chih na thei ding uh.
22. Kei lungkham sak hi loua, mi diktatte lungtang juaua na hihnat jiak un leh mi gilou hingdama a omna dinga a khelhna lampia kipan kilehhei louhna dingin mi gilou khutte na hihhat ua,Huaijiakin bangmahlou lel, thu genkhawl diklou hiam jaw na mu nawn himhim kei ding uh; huan ka mite na khut ua kipan ka hun khe dinga, TOUPA ka hi chih na thei ding uh, a chi, chiin gen in, achi a.
23. Huaijiakin bangmahlou lel, thu genkhawl diklou hiam jaw na mu nawn himhim kei ding uh; huan ka mite na khut ua kipan ka hun khe dinga, TOUPA ka hi chih na thei ding uh, a chi, chiin gen in, achi a.
