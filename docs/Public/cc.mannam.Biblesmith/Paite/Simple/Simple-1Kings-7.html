1. Huan, Solomon in a in kum sawm leh kum thum a lama, a in a mansak vek a.
2. Lebanon Gam mang In, khuam vual li, khuam tunga sidar singpek tungah a lama; a dunglam tong ja ahia, a vailam tong sawmnga ahia, a sanlam tong sawmthum ahi.
3. Huan, huai bel khuama kinga dantan sawmli leh nga tung lamah sidar singphela khuh ahi; vual khat ah sawm leh nga jel.
4. Huan, vual thum in tohlet a om a, vak leh vak te bel vual thumin a kigalsai vek uh.
5. Huan, kongkhak leh khuam tengteng singpek in ning lia bawl ahia, vak leh vakte vual thum in a kigalsai vek uh.
6. Huan, Inlim khuam nei a bawla; a dunglam tong sawmnga ahi a, a vailam tong sawmthum ahi a, huan, huaite maah inlim a bawla; khuamte leh a maa lutna tungkah toh.
7. Huan, a vaihawmna ding laltutphah inlim, vaihawma inlim, a vaihawmna ding a bawla: huai bel pialkhang langkhat akipan langkhat phaa sidar singa khuh ahi.
8. Huan, a omna ding in, inlim nunga, huangsung intual dangnei om pen huchibang maha bawl ahi. Huan, huai inlim mahbangin Pharo tanu Solomonin jia a neih in a lam sak lai hi.
9. Huaite tengteng suang man pha pipi, a hun geihgeiha sek jel suang, atnaa ata lam ahi, sunglam leng, polam leng a suangphum akipana a lek phain, huan, huchimahbangin polamah huangsung intual lianpi tan phain.
10. Huan, suangphum bel suang man pha pipi, suang lian pipi, suang tong sawm te, suang tong giat tea lep ahi a.
11. Huai tung lamah suang man pha pipi, suang a hun geihgeih jela sek, leh sidar sing ahi.
12. Huan, a kimvelin huang sung intual lian pi suang sek chial thum leh sidar singpek thuah khata um ahi: Toupa in huangsung intual sunggil jaw leh, in inlim mahbangin.
13. Huan, kumpipa Solomonin mi a sawla. Tura khuaa kipan Hiram a sama.
14. Huai mi bel Naphtali nam meithai ta ahi, a pa Tura khuaa mi, dal sek mi ahi; amah bel dal sek lam himhim ah a pilin a theihsiamin a khutsiam petmah a. Kumpipa Solomon kiangah a hoha, a na tengteng asep sak vek hi.
15. Huan, tong sawm leh tong giata sang dal khuam nih a bawla; a langkhat tong sawm leh tong nih ahi tuaktuak.
16. Khuam lu a koih ding dal sun bawk nih a bawla; bawk khat sanlam tong nga ahi a, huan, bawk khat sanlam leng tong nga ahi.
17. Khuam lu a bawkte dingin gial imiam leh kihek nginngiante a oma; bawk adingin sagih, huan, bawk khat adingin leng sagih mah.
18. Huchiin khuamte a bawla; huai khuam lua bawkte tuamna dingin gial imiam khat tungah a kimvel vual nih a oma: huan, huchimah bangin khat tungah leng a om lai.
19. Huan, inlima khuam dawna bawk ah lili lim a tuanga, tong li.
20. Huan, khuam nih dawna bawkte ah a gei ah, gial imiam chin ah pomgranet te a om a: huan, pomgranet bel huai bawkte ah zanih tuaktuaka volhin a om hi.
21. Huan, Biakin inlim ah khuamte a dingsaka: taklam khuam a dingsaka, a min dingin Jakin a sa a: huan, veilam khuam a dingsaka. Amin dingin Boaz a sa hi.
22. Huan, khuam dawnah lili lim a tuanga: huchiin khuam lam nasep mansak ahi ta.
23. Huan, dal tuibem lianpi, tong sawm kia, a sanlam tong nga a sunga: a gei tong sawmthum kimkot ahi.
24. Huan, a gei nuaiah pakmum limin a kimvel veka, tong sawm ahi a, tuibem kimvelin a om: huai pakmumte bel vual nihin a om a, sun khawm pah ahi.
25. Huai bawngtal sawm leh nih tungah a kingaa, thum mal lam a nga ua, thum tum lam a nga ua, thum sim lam a nga ua, thum suah lam a nga uh. Huan, tuibem a tunguah a kitunga, a mei lam un sunglam a doha.
26. Huchiin khutpek boka sah ahi; a gei lili pak bang, nou gei banga bawl ahi: bath sang nih takna ahi.
27. Huan, tun sawmna sawm dalin a bawla; tunsawnna khat saulam tong li ahi a, a vailam tong li mah ahi a, a sanlam tong thum ahi.
28. Huan, tunsawnna bawl dan bel hichibanga hi: huai in kuak a neia, a kuakte a geite kikalah a oma:
29. Huan, a geite kikal kuakte ah humpinelkaite, bawngtal te, cherub te a tuanga; a gei tungte ah tunsawnna a om a: huan, humpinelkai leh bawngtalte nuai lamah kilawmsakna te khawng a om dindian a.
30. Huan, tunsawnnate ah dal kangtalai li leh dal tungchih a om chiata: a ning li te ah kingakna a om chiat a: kisilna bel nuai lamah a kingakna sun a oma, a sik chiatah kilawmsakna a om chiat;
31. Huan, valh gong sung tong khat kia ahi: a kam a kimvelin valh banga bem ahi a, tong khat leh a kim ahi: huan, kam gei kimvel gial kilawm ahi a, a kuakte a hihleh ning li ahi a, a bem kei.
32. Huan, kangtalai lite a kuak nuai lamah a om a; huan, kangtalai tungchihte bel tunsawnna ah a om: kangtalai san dan tong khat leh a kim ahi.
33. Huan, huai kangtalaite bawl dan bel sakol kangtalai khe bawl dan bang ahi: a tungchih te, a valh te, a sutkalhna te, a lai bawkte a vek ua sun ahi.
34. Huan, tunsawnna ning li chiatah kingakna li a oma: huai kingaknate tunsawnna toh pumkhat vek ahi.
35. Huan, tunsawnna tungah valh, tong kimkhata sang a om a: tunsawnna tunga a tunna khete leh a kuakte pumkhat vek ahi.
36. Huan, a tunna khe pekte ah leh, a kuak ah te, a zat bang-bangin cherubte, humpinelkaite, tum sing te a tuang saka, a kimvelin kilawmsakna a om dindian hi.
37. Huchibang vekin tunsawnna sawmte a bawla; a vekin pumkhata sun ahi chiat a, kikim chiat, bawl dan kibang geih ahi chiat uh.
38. Huan, kisilna bel sawm dalin a bawla: kisilna bel khatin bath sawmli a donga: kisilna bel tong li chiat ahi: tunsawnna sawm tungah kisilna bel khat a kitung chiat hi.
39. Huan, tunsawnnate in taklam pangah nga a tunga, in veilam pang ah nga: huan, tuibem bel in taklam pangah suah lamah sim lam ngain a tung.
40. Huan, Hiramin kisilna belte, vutluahte, maiphiatkuangte a bawla, huchiin, Hiramin Toupa in dingia Solomon a bawl sak tengteng a man vekta a;
41. khuam nih te, khuam dawna bel banga bawk nih te khuam dawna bel banga bawk nih tuamna gial imiam nihte,
42. huai tuamna gial imiam nih adinga pomgranet za li te, khuam dawna bel banga bawk nih tuamna ding gial imiam tuaktuak dingin pomgranet vual nih te;
43. huan, tunsawnna sawm leh tunsawnna tunga kisilna bel sawm te;
44. tuibem khat leh tuibem nuaia bawngtal sawm leh nihte,
45. belte leh, vutluahte leh maiphiatkuang te toh: huan, tuiumbelsuante tengteng Toupa in adinga Hiramin kumpipa Solomon a bawlsak dal nawtteta bawl vel ahi.
46. Jordan phaijangah Sukkoth leh Zarethan kikal bellei omnaah ahi kumpipan a sun.
47. Huan, huai tuiumbelsuante tengteng a tam mahmah jiak un Solomonin buk khe louin a koih mawk hi: dal buk zah pen theih khiak vual ahi kei.
48. Huan, Solomonin Toupa ina tuiumbelsuan om tengteng a bawla; dangkaeng maitam leh tanghou koih omna dohkan dangkaengin;
49. Huan, mun siangthoupen maa khawnvak koihnate, taklama nga leh veilama nga bel dangkaeng siangin; pak te, khawnvak te leh taitehte bel dangkaengin;
50. Noute, khawnvak puan tehnate, maiphiatkuangte, keute, meilakna te dangkaeng siangin; in sung gil jaw, mun siangthou pen kongkhakte leh in (biakin) kongkhak bulhnate bel dangkaengin.Huchiin, kumpipa Solomonin Toupa in adinga a bawl tengteng a naman vek ta hi. Huchiin Solomonin a pa Davidin a na lat khitsa-dangkachi te, dangkaeng te, tuiumbelsuante a la luta, Toupa ina goute lakah a koihta hi.
51. Huchiin, kumpipa Solomonin Toupa in adinga a bawl tengteng a naman vek ta hi. Huchiin Solomonin a pa Davidin a na lat khitsa-dangkachi te, dangkaeng te, tuiumbelsuante a la luta, Toupa ina goute lakah a koihta hi.
