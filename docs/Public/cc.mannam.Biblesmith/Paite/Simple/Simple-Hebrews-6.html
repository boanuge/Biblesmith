1. Huchiin, Kris thubulte gen nawn louin pichinlam I manoh ding uh; thilhih sisa kisiknate, Pathian lama ginnate,
2. baptisma leh, khutngak leh misi thohnawnna leh, khantawn vaihawmnalam thu hilhnate suang khawng phum nawnta louin.
3. Pathianin pha a sak leh huai bang I hih ngei ding uhi.
4. Khatvei hihvaka omkhina, van thilpiak chiamkhina, Kha Siangthou dongkhina,
5. Pathian thu hoihna leh hun hongom dinga thil hihtheihnate chiamkhin hinapia,
6. ginna a kiheimangsan uleh bel, huchibang mite jaw a kisik nading ua bawlthak theih ahi kei uhi; Pathian Tapa amau adinga kilhlum tuan nawna selphou a hih jiakun.
7. Lou, a tunga vuahtui ke gige na dawnkhina, huai khohsaka ompa adia nek theih antehlouhing honsuah penin Pathian vualjawlna a tang hi.
8. A hihhangin lou lingnei leh khaulingnei khawng a suahnak inbel, a phatuam keia; hamsiat loh phialin a oma; a tawpna tuh hal himai ding ahi.
9. Himahleh, deihtakte aw, huchibang thu gen mahleng zong, nou jaw huchibang thil sanga thil hoihjaw, hotdamna jui thilte, nein a hih uh ka um uhi.
10. Na nasep u leh a min jiaka itna na latsak uh, mi siangthoute na nasepsak uh, tutana leng na seplai uh, mangngilh dingin Pathian mitak lou ahi ngal kei ahi.
11. Himahleh, atawp tana lametna theihchetna kim mu dingin na thanop ngeingei bang un thanop suah gige chiat le uchin ka chi mahmah uhi.
12. Huchiin, na kithadahsak kei ding ua, ginna leh kuhkalna jiaka thuchiamte gouluah khin khawngte entawnte na hi zo ding uhi.
13. Pathianin Abraham kianga thu achiam laiin, amah sanga thupi jaw loua a kichiam theihlouh jiakin, amah leh amah kilouin,
14. Taktakin vual ka honjawl ngeingei dinga, hih leng ka honhih pung ngeingei ding, chiin, a kichiamta a.
15. Huchiin, kuhkal taka a thuak khiak nungin, thilchiam tuh a muta ngei hi.
16. Mihingte jaw a sang ua thupi jaw louin a kichiam ua; a kiselna chiteng uah leng hihkipna dingin kichiam tuh thutawp ahi.
17. Huchiin, Pathianin thuchiam gouluah dingte kiangah a thiltup lamdan tuan theihlouhdan nakpi mahmaha theihsak utin, kichiamna tuh a palaisaka;
18. Pathian juau vual ahihlouhna thil lamdang theilou nih jiakin, ei I kihumbitna delhten I maa lametna koih tuh la dinga hasotna thupi tak i neih theihna dingin.
19. Huai lametna tuh hinna sikkipin I neia, muanhuai leh kiptak ahi, huai lametna tuh puanjak sung ah leng a lutta hi;huaiah tuh Jesu, Melkisedek omdan banga khantawn a Siampu Lianpen nahitain ei adingin makaipain a na lutta hi.
20. huaiah tuh Jesu, Melkisedek omdan banga khantawn a Siampu Lianpen nahitain ei adingin makaipain a na lutta hi.
