1. Reuben leh Gad tate'n gan a hau mahmah ua: huan, Jazer gam leh Gilead gam a muh un, a mun gan a dia mun hoih tak hi in a thei ua;
2. Huchi in Gad tate leh Reuben tate'n Mosi leh siampu Eleazar leh omkhawm mipite a va houpih ua,
3. Israel omkhawmte ma a TOUPA'N a daisak gam Atarotte, Dibonte, Jazerte, Nimrate, Hesbonte,
4. Elealete, Sebamte, Nebote, Beonte gan ding a hoih gamahi a, huan, na sikhate'n gan ka nei ua,
5. Hoih na sak lam a hihleh hiai gam ka luah ding un hon pia inla, Jordan lui hon pi galkah dah mai ve, a chi ua.
6. Huan, Mosi in Gad tate leh Reuben tate kiang ah, Na unaute uh kidouna ah a hoh ding ua, nou bel hiai lai ah na na om maimah ding uh maw?
7. Bang ding a TOUPA'N a piak gam a lut lou ding a Israel suante lungtang lunglel sak na hi ua?
8. Kades-barnea a kipan a gam va enkhe ding a ka sawl in hichi mah bang in na pute un a hih ua.
9. Eskol guam a tuntouh un gam a va mu ua, TOUPA'N gam a piak a lut nop louhna ding un Israel suante lungtang a lunglel sak uh.
10. Huchi in huai ni in TOUPA a heh mahmah a, a kichiam a,
11. Aigupta gam a kipan a hong pawt, kum sawmnih chiang tunglam mi himhim in Abrahamte, Isaakte, Jakobte, kiang a kichiam a ka piak gam kuamah in a mu kei ding uhi; a hon zuih taktak louh ziak un;
12. Keniz mi Jephune tapa Kaleb leh Nun tapa Joshuate kia lou ngal, amau TOUPA a zuih taktak ziak un, chi in.
13. Huan, Israelte tungah TOUPA a heh mahmah a, TOUPA mitmuh a thil hoihlou hih mi suan tengteng a man vek masiah uh gamdai ah kum sawmli a vak saksak a.
14. Huan, ngai un, Israelte tung a TOUPA hehna kihtakhuai tak behlap in nou gilou in na pute uh sik in na ding ta uhi.
15. TOUPA zui lou a na om himhim uleh aman amaute gamdai ah a omsak nawn mai sin ahi. Hiai mi tengteng na hihmang mai kei ding umaw? a chi a.
16. Huan, a va naih ua, a kiang ah, Hiai lai ah ka gan ua ding in belam huangte leh ka tate uh a ding khua peuh ka bawl ding ua:
17. Kou bel a mun ding ka tun masiah uh Israel suante ma a pai zel ding in ka kigalvan ding uhi: hiai gam a mite ziak in ka tate uh kulh nei khuate ah a om ding uh.
18. Israel suante mi chih in a mun luah ding uh a luah chiat ma un ka inn lam uah ka pai kei ding uh.
19. Ka luah ding uh bel hiai lam gal Jordan suahlam a hih ziak in Jordan gal khat lam leh a khen lam zaw ka luahpih tei sin kei uhi, a chi ua.
20. Huan, Mosi in a kiang uah, Huchi bang a na chih ua, kidouna a TOUPA ma a hoh ding a na kigalvat ua, a ma a kipat melmate a delhkhiak a,
21. TOUPA ma a gam zoh a hih masiah na lak ua kigalvan tengteng TOUPA ma a Jordan na galkah vek uleh,
22. Denchiang in na pai ding ua, TOUPA tungah leh Israelte tungah na moh kei ding ua; huchi in hiai gam TOUPA ma a na luah ding uh ahi ding a.
23. Himahleh huchi bang a na hih kei uleh ngai un, TOUPA tungah na khial uh ahi ding a, na khelhna un a hon mukhe ngeingei ding chih thei un.
24. Na tate uh ding khuate bawl unla, na belamte uh ding in leng huante bawl unla, na gen bangbang un hih un, a chi a.
25. Huan, Gad tate leh Reuben tate'n Mosi kiang ah, Na sikhate'n ka pu uh thupiak bang in ka hih ding uh.
26. Ka zi, ka tate uh, ka belam honte uh, ka gan hon tengteng uh Gilead gam khuate ah a na om ding ua:
27. Himahleh na sikha, kidou ding a kigalvan tengteng in ka pu uh gen bang in TOUPA ma ah ka va galkah ding uhi, chi in, a gen uh.
28. Huchi in Mosi in, a tungtang thu uah siampu Eleazarte, Nun tapa Josuate, Israel nam innkuan inntek pente thu a pia a.
29. Huan, Mosi in a kiang uah, Gad tate, Reuben tate, kidou ding a kigalvan tengteng in TOUPA ma a Jordan lui a hong galkah pih ua, huai gam na zoh uleh Gilead gam pen a luah ding ua na piak ding uh ahi:
30. Himahleh kigalvan a hong galkah pih kei uleh Kanan gamah na lak uah a tel tei mai ding uh, a chi a.
31. Huan, Gad tate leh Reuben tate'n, Na sikhate kiang a TOUPA'N a gen bang in ka hih ding uh,
32. Kigalvan a TOUPA ma a galkah in Kanan gamah ka lut ding uh, huchi in ka gam luah ding uh Jordan lui khenlam ah ahi lai ding, chi in a dawng ua.
33. Huan, Mosi in, Gad tate leh Reuben tate leh Joseph ta Manase suan nam kim khatte Amor kumpipa Sihon gamte, Basan Kumpipa Og gam, huai gamte a khua om tengteng toh, a kim gam khuate nangawn toh a pia a.
34. Huan, Gad tate'n Dibonte, Atarote, Aroerte;
35. Atrot-sophante, Jazerte, Jogbehte;
36. Betnimrate, Betharante a lam ua, khopi kulh neite, belam huangte toh.
37. Huan, Reuben tate'n Hesbonte, Elealete, Kiriathaimte, Nebote, Beal-meonte,
38. (A minte hihlamdan khit ahi) Sibmate a lam ua; a kho bawlte uh min dang a phuak uh.
39. Huan, Manase tapa Makir tate Gilead gamah a hoh ua, a la ua, Amorte huailai a omte a suhsak uh.
40. Huan, Mosi in Gilead gam Manase tapa Makir a pia; huai ah a om.
41. Huan, Manase tapa Jairin huai a khuate a vala a, Havot-jiar a sa hi.Huan, Nob in Kenat a vala a, khopelte toh, amah min tam in Nob a sa hi.
42. Huan, Nob in Kenat a vala a, khopelte toh, amah min tam in Nob a sa hi.
