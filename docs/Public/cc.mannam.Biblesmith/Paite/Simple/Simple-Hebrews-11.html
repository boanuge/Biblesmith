1. Huan, ginna tuh thil lamette om ngeia theihna, thil muhlouhte theihchetna ahi.
2. Huaiah tuh nidanglai miten phat aloh ngal ua.
3. Ginnain khovelte tuh Pathian thu a suktuah a hita chih I thei ua, huchiin thil muh theihte leng thil langtheitea siam ahi kei hi.
4. Ginnain Abelin Kaina sangin kithoihna hoihjaw Pathian kiangah a lana, huai jiakin mi dik hihna a mu chih, Pathianin a thilpiakte a pomna ah a theisaka; huan huai ginna jiakin aman tuh sih khit nungin thu a gen nalai ahi.
5. Ginnain Enok leng sihna chiamlou dinga lakmangin ana oma; huan, Pathianin alakmang jiakin muhtheih ahita keia; lakmanga aom main Pathian pahtak lam mi ahi, chih theihsakna amukhinta him hi.
6. Himahleh, ginna louin amah pahtak lammi hihvual ahi kei; Pathian kianga hongpaiin, amah tuh a om chih leh, a zong mite lohchingsakpa ahi chih, gin ding ahi.
7. Ginnain Noa tuh thil muh nailouhte lam thu ah Pathian hilha naomin, Pathian laudan siamin, a inkuansung hotdamna dingin longa bawla; huaiin khovel siamlouh a tangsakta a, ginna jiaka om diktatna gouluahpa ahong hi hi.
8. Ginnain Abraham tuh sapa a om laiin a gouluah ding muna lut dingin pai khiakna ding thu a muta a; huan, a paina lam ding leng theilouin a pai kheta a.
9. Ginnain gam chiam ah mi gama tam bang lelin a natamta nilouha, thuchiam kikhat gouluahpih ding Isak leh Jacobte toh puanin ahte a om ua;
10. Khua suangphumte nei, Pathian lam leh, bawl tuh, a ngak nilouh a.
11. Ginnain Sara ngeiin leng, chiampa tuh muanhuai asak jiakin, teknungin leng chi pai theihna amu hi.
12. Huchiin, pasal khat, amah lah misi bang mai, akipan vana aksite banga tam, tuipiaua piaunel banga sim zohlouh suante a hongsuakta uhi.
13. Hiai mi tengtengten thilchiamte tangkhin hilou-in, himahleh huaite gamlapia galmuha nakipahpih hi jawin, leh lei ah jaw khualzin leh mikhual lel ahi uh chih kithei gigein, amaute tuh ginna neiin ana sita uh hi.
14. Huchibanga thu na genten amau gam ding a zong uh chih ahih lang mahmah uhi.
15. A pawtsan gam uh lung gulh hile uh bel kikna hun lem muta mahmah ding ahi ngal ua.
16. Himahleh, tun bel gam hoihjaw sem deih ana hi ua, huai tuh vana om ahi; huchin Pathian, a Pathian uh chiha om tuh a zum kei hi; amau dingin khopi khat anangaihtuahsakta ngala.
17. Ginnain Abraham tuh zeta omin, Isak a lana; huan thuchiamte kipak taka sangpan tuh a tapa neihsun alan hi, huai lah
18. Isak ah na suante aki minloh ding, a chihpen ahia.
19. Pathianin misi lak akipan nangawna kaithou theiin a sepa; huchiin gentehnadana genin, amah tuh misi laka kipan mu nawn ahi ngei nguta.
20. Ginnain Isakin thil hongtung dingte thu ah leng Jacob leh Esau vual a jawl a.
21. Ginnain Jacobin, a sih ding kuanin, Joseph tate vual a jawl tuaktuaka; achiangkhun dawna kingaiin Pathian a bia hi.
22. Ginnain Josephin, a sih ding kuanin, Isreal suante pawt ding thu a gena; a guhte vui ding tungtang ah thu a pia hi.
23. Ginnain Mosi, a pian laiin, ngaungek mel hoihtak ahi a chih jiak un, kha thum sung a nu leh a pa selin a oma; kumpipa thupiak leng a lau kei uhi.
24. Ginna in, Mosi in a hopichin nung in, Pharo tanu, tapa a lawh a utkei a;
25. sawtlou kal a dia khelhna nawpna kipahpih sangin Pathian mite tawh sukgentheihna thuak a tel zawta hi;
26. Kris a muhsitna uh tuh Aigupta gam goute sang a sum thupizaw in angai a; kipahman piak ding tuh a et ziakin.
27. Ginna in kimpipa thangpaihna tuh laulou in Aigupta gam a paisan a, muhtheihlouhpa a muh ziak in a thuak teitei hi.
28. Ginna in Paikan Ankuangluina leh sisan thehna a bawl a, ta masa hihmangthangpan amaute a khoih louhna ding in.
29. Ginna in gam hula pai bangin Tuipi San a kai a; Aigupata ten tua bang a hih a tm sam ua, valh zawh in a omta uhi.
30. Ginna in Jeriko khawkulh ni sagih a um nung un a chimta hi.
31. Ginna in Rahab kizuaknu gingloute lakah a mangthang khawm ta kei, enkhete im neilou a a pahtak ziakin.
32. Huan, bang kagen nalai dia? Gideon thute, Barak thute, Samson thute, Jeptha thute leh, David thute, Samuel thute, Jawlneite thute gen ni chile hang, hun in hon chinlou ding ahi ngala;
33. Huaiten tuh ginna in gamte ala ua, thudik a vaihawm a zawng ua, thuchiamte atang ua, humpinelkai kamte a chihsak ua;
34. mei thil hihtheihna a hihmit ua, namsau hiam nangawn a pel ua, hatlouhna a kipan hattak a bawl in a om ua, kidouna bang ah mihat minthang ana hi ua, namdang kidou pawlte bang a taikek sak uhi.
35. Numeiten a misite uh thounawn a mu ua; khenkhatte gim thuaksak in a om ua, thawhnawnna hoih zawsem a tan theihna ding un khahkhiakna a deih kei ua;
36. midangten leng nuihza bawlna leh vuak bang a thuak ua, ahi, huailou leng, kawlbut leh hentakna bang a tuak ua;
37. suang a den in a awm ua, singatna a attan in a awm ua, khemna a tuak ua, namsau a satlup in a awm ua; Belam vun leh kel vun silh in a vakvai ua; genthei takin, gimthuakin, sawi in a awm ua-
38. Khawvel a mau tenna tak hilou mite- gamdai ah bang, tang a bang, guam ah bang, leikua ah bang, a vak avak uhi.
39. Ahia, hiaitetengteng in, a ginnaziakun phat nalawh khin mahleuhleng thil chiam pen ana tang kei uhi;aziakbel, Pathianin eia ding a thil hawihza muhkhawlh a neih ziak a, ei tellou-a amaute hih bukima a awmlouh ding uh ahihziak ahi.
40. aziakbel, Pathianin eia ding a thil hawihza muhkhawlh a neih ziak a, ei tellou-a amaute hih bukima a awmlouh ding uh ahihziak ahi.
