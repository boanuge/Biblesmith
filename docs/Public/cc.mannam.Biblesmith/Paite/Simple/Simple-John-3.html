1. Huan, pharisaite laka mi, a min Nikodem, judate vaihawmpa khat a oma,
2. Huai mi tuh janin Jesu kiangah a honga, a kiangah, Rabbi, Pathiana kipana hongpai sinsakpa na hi chih ka thei uhi. Pathian a kianga a om keiin hiai thil lamdang na hih bang kuamahin a hih theikei ding, a chi a.
3. Huan, Jesun a dawnga, a kiangah, Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin ka honhilh ahi, Mi kuapeuh a pian thak kei aleh Pathian gam a mu theikei, a chi a.
4. Nikodemin a kiangah, Mi a tek nungin bang chin a piang thei dia? A nu gila nihvei lutin a piang thei dia hia? a chi a.
5. Jesun a dawnga, Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin kon hilh ahi, kuapeuhmah tuia leh Khaa piang ahih kei leh Pathian gam a lut theikei.
6. Sa suan peuhmah sa ahi a, Kha suan peuhmah kha ahi.
7. Na pianthak ngeingei ding ahi konchih lamdang sa ken.
8. Huih a utnana ah a nunga, a ging na ja hi, himahleh koia kipana hongpai ahia, koiah ahia a hoh ding chihleng na thei ngal keia; kuapeuh Khaa piang tuh huchibang mahin a om hi, a chi a.
9. Huan, Nikodemin a dawnga, a kiangah, Hiai bangchin a om thei dia? a chi a.
10. Jesun a dawnga, a kiangah, Israelte sinsakpa na hi ngala, huaite na theikei maw?
11. Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin ka honhilh ahi, kou ka theih uh ka gen ua, ka muh uh ka theisak uh; himahleh, ka thu theihsak uh na sang ngal kei uhi.
12. Khovela thil ka honhilh bawn ging louten, vana thil honhilh leng bang chin na taksang ding ua?
13. Van ah kuamah a kah ngei kei a, van akipana hongsukpa kia loungal, Mihing Tapa vana ompa tuh.
14. Huan, Mosiin gamdaia gul a khaikang bangin, Mihing Tapa khai kangin a om ding ahi;
15. kuapeuh a gingtain amah jiaka khantawna hinna a neihna dingin.
16. Banghanghiam chih leh, Pathianin khovel a it mahmah a, huchin a Tapa tang neihsun a pia hi, huchia kuapeuh amah gingta tuh a man that louh a, khantawna hinna a neih jawkna dingin.
17. Khovel siam louh tangsak dingin Pathianin Tapa khovel ah a sawl kei, khovel amah vanga a hin theihna ding in a sawl ahi jaw hi.
18. Kuapeuh amah gingta tuh siam louhin a om kei ding a, kuapeuh a gingta lou tuh, tu a patin siam louhin a om pah ngal hi, Pathian Tapa tang neih sun min a gintak louh jiakin.
19. Hiai tuh siam louhna ahi, vak khovel ah a honga, himahleh min vak sangin mial a it jaw uh, a thilhihte uh a gitlouh jiakin.
20. Kuapeuh thil gilou hih in vak a ho nak, vakah leng a hongpai kei hi; huchi lou in jaw a thilhihte a lang kha ding.
21. Ahihhangin, kuapeuh a dik hih tuh a thilhihte Pathian jiaka hih a ahi chih a latna dingin, vakah a hong jel hi, a chi a.
22. Huai khitin Jesu a nungjuite toh Judia gamah a hoh ua, huaiah Jesu a kiang uah a om nilouh a, a baptis jel hi.
23. Johan inleng Ainon ah, Salim kho kiangah a baptis sama, huaia tui a tam jiakin; huan, mi a hong ua, baptisma a tang duamduam uhi.
24. Huchihlaiin Johan suangkulh ah a khum nai ngal kei ua.
25. Huan, Johan nungjuite leh Juda khat, silsiang thu ah a na kisel ua.
26. Huan, Johan kiangah a hongpai ua, a kiangah, Rabbi, Jordan gala na kianga om, a thu mi na theihsakpan, ngai dih, huai min mi a baptis a, mi tengteng a kiangah a hongpai uhi, a chi ua.
27. Johanin a dawng a, Mihingin bangmah a nei theikei uh, vana mi piak a hih kei leh.
28. Kei Kris ka hi kei hi, a maa mi sawl ka hi, ka chih, noumau mahmah hontheihpihte na hi uhi.
29. Mou neipa tuh mou pasal ahi; himahleh, mouneipa lawm dinga a thu ja tuh, mouneipa aw jiakin a kipak mahmah a; en un ka kipahna a tungta ahi.
30. Amah a lian semsem dinga, kei bel ka kiam semsem ding hi.
31. Tung lama kipana hongpai jaw mi tengteng sanga tungnungpen ahi; leia kipan bel leia ahi a, lei thu a gen nak, vana kipana hongpaipa jaw mi tengteng sanga tungnungpen ahi.
32. A muh leh a jak peuh a theisak jel ahi; kuamahin a thu theihsak a taksang kei uh.
33. A thu theihsak taksangin, Pathian mi diktat ahi, chih a kipsak hi.
34. Pathian sawlin Pathian thu a gen hi; aman jaw teh chitchiatin Kha a pe ngal keia.
35. Pan Tapa a it a, a khutah bangkim a vekin a peta hi.Kuapeuh Tapa gingtain khantawna hinna a nei; himahleh, Tapa thu manglouin hinna a neikei ding a, Pathian hehna a tungah a om jaw hi, a chi a.
36. Kuapeuh Tapa gingtain khantawna hinna a nei; himahleh, Tapa thu manglouin hinna a neikei ding a, Pathian hehna a tungah a om jaw hi, a chi a.
