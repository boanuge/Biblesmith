1. Dan thuin thil hoih, hongom dingte a taktak lohkhiakna neilouin, a limkia aneih jiakin, kumtenga kithoihna ngeingei alat jelte un, a honnaih mite bangchik mahin a bukimsak thei ngei kei hi.
2. Huchi hita kei leh, lat tawp thamta lou ding uhia? Abemite khatvei a hihsiangthou pahngal hile uh, khelhna kitheihna lah nei nawnlou ding hi ua.
3. Himahleh, huai kithoihna ah kum tengin khelhnate theihnawnna a hongom nak hi.
4. Bawngpate leh kelte sisanin khelhnate lamang thei vual ahingal keia.
5. Huchiin, khovela ahonglut laiin, Kithoihna leh thillat na deih keia, keia dingin pumpi non bawlsak jawa:
6. Halmanga pumpiaknate leh, khelhna dinga kithoihnate khawngah kipahna himhim na neikei hi.
7. Huaitakin, ken, Pathian aw, ngaiin, Laibu jiala ka tungthu gelh ahih bangin, Na deihlam hih dingin ka hongpai, ka chia, achi a.
8. A tungah, Kithoihna te, thillatte, halmanga pumpiaknate, khelhna dinga kithoihnate na deih keia, huaiahte kipahna na nei sam kei, a chi a, (huaite tuh danthu banga lat ahi a),
9. huaitakin, Ngaiin, na deihlam hih dingin ka hongpai, a na chita a, A nihna a tunding theihna dingin a masapen a lamangta ahi.
10. Huai deihlamin, Jesu Kris pumpi khatvei thu a lat pah jiakin, hihsiangthouin I omta hi.
11. Huan, siampu chitengin kawla nisuak sima na semin, khelhnate bangchik mah a hihmang theilou kithoihna ngeingei lan gige in, a ding uhi.
12. Amah bel, khantawna dinga khelhnate adia kithoihna khat kia alat nungin, Pathian taklam ah a vatuta jaw hi,
13. huai nungsiah tuh, a melmate a khepek ngakna asiama hih masiah uh a ngak nilouh a.
14. Thillat khat kiain hihsiangthou sate khantawna dingin hihkim ta ngala.
15. Huan, Kha Siangthouin leng a hoh theisak sam a:
16. Huai ni khit chiangin a kiang ua thu ka khun ding tuh hiaite ahi, Toupan achi: a lungtang uah ka thupiakte ka koih dinga, a lungsim uah ka gelhna lai ding, chia a gen khitin,
17. A khelhnate uh leh a tatleknate uh ka theigige ta kei ding, a chi ngala.
18. Huchiin, huchibangte ngaihdamna omna peuh ah khelhna adingin thillat a om nawn kei ahi.
19. Huchiin, unaute aw, Jesu sisan jiaka Mun Siangthoua lutngamna neiin,
20. Lam a honbawlsak, lam thak leh hing ah, puanjak (huai tuh a pumpi chihna ahi) kantan in,
21. Pathian inkuante tunga Siampu thupi tak ineih jiakin,
22. I lungtanga sia leh pha theihna sianglou thehsiansaka oma, I pumpi leng tuichima sila omin, ginna a theih chetna kimneiin lungtang tak mah puin I naih ding uhi.
23. I lamet thugin, ling-lou in I pomkip ding uh, thuchiampa lah a muanhuai ngala;
24. huan, itna leh thil hoih hiha kitokthou dingin ikingaih tuah tek ding uhi;
25. khenkhat hih dan banga I kikhopnate uh tawpsan louin, kihasuan tuah jaw ni; nituh hongnai hulhula na thei un kihasuan tuah semsem un.
26. Thutak theihna kim itan nunga thil I hihkhelh teitei ngal injaw, khelhnate adia kithoihna a om tak het louh jiakin,
27. vaihawmna lauhuai mulkimhuai tak bang hiam leh, doumite kangmang ding thangpaihna mei maban neiin I om zota ding hi.
28. Mosi dan thu khawksa lou mi tuh theihpih mi nihtou thumtou hiam thum lainat omlouin asi jel uhi.
29. Pathian Tapa sikdena, thukhun sisan amah hihsiangthouna pen thil namaia bawla, hehpihna Kha hihvui mi tuh, huaimi sanga bangchi pheta gawtna thupijaw ahia, thuak taka naseh uh?
30. Phulakna himhim keimah lak ding ahi, keimahin ka thuk ding, chih leh, Toupan a mite a vaihawmsak ding, chi nawnpa lah I thei ngal ua.
31. Pathian hing khuta puk tuh thil mulkimhuai taka hi.
32. Himahleh, tuma lama hihvaka na om nung ua thuakna buaina thupi tak na thuak lai nite uh thei nawn un;
33. Kong khatah jaw muhsit leh haksatna bang leng thuakin etnopa bawlin na om ua, kong khat ah jaw huchibanga bawla omte tuh thuakpihte na honghi uhi
34. Kolbunte tuh na lainat ua, gou hoihjaw, omden ding hi behlap, noumauin na neih uh kitheiin na van honlaksakte uh leng kipak takin na nathuakzouta behlap uhi.
35. Huchiin, na ginna uh, kipahman piak thupi tak nei ding ngei, paimang kei un.
36. Pathian deihlam na hih nungun thilchiam tuh na muh theihna ding un kuhkalna na kiphamoh uhi.
37. Sawtlou nung chiangin hongpai dingpa hongpai ding ahi, sawt a halta kei ding.
38. Himahleh, ka mi diktat tuh ginnain a hing ding; a nungtolh le bel, ka khain amah ah kipahna himhim a neikei ding, chih.Himahleh, ei jaw mangthang dinga nungtolh pawla pang I hi kei ua; hinna humbit dinga ginna nei pawla pang I hi jaw uhi.
39. Himahleh, ei jaw mangthang dinga nungtolh pawla pang I hi kei ua; hinna humbit dinga ginna nei pawla pang I hi jaw uhi.
