1. Huan, hichi ahi a, Samuel a upata a, a tapate Israel mite tunga vaihawmmiin a bawl hi.
2. A ta masapen min Joel ahi; huan, a zom min Abija ahi; Beer-sebaa vaihawmmi ahi uh.
3. Himahleh a taten a omdan a la kei ua, sum leh pai lam a delh ua, golhna a la ua, diklou takin vai a hawm uh.
4. Huchiin Israel mite upate tengteng a kikhawm ua, Rama khua ah Samuel kiangah a hoh ua;
5. A kiangah, Ngaiin, na upa mahmahta a, na tapaten lah na omdan a la kei hial ua; nam dangte tengteng banga ka tung ua vai hawm dingin kumpipa hon bawlsakin, a chi ua.
6. Himahleh a kianga, vai hon hawmsak ding kumpipa honpia in, a chih un Samuel akipaksak kei hiala. Huchiin Samuel TOUPA kiangah a thuma.
7. Huan, TOUPAN Samuel kiangah, mipiten na kianga a gen tengteng uh ngaikhia in; nang hon deihlou a hikei ua, a tung ua kumpipa dinga kei hon deihlou uh ahi zaw.
8. Aigupta gam akipan ka pi khiak ni akipan tu tana a thilhih dan tengteng uh, hon lehngatsana pathian dang na a hon sepsan jel uh mahbangin nang tungah a hih uh ahi.
9. Huaijiakin a thu uh ngaikhia in: ahihhangin chihtak peta thu kamkhumin a tung ua vaihawmmi kumpipa tatdan ding kawkmuh chitchiat in, a chi a.
10. Huan Samuelin TOUPA thu tengteng kumpipa ngen mipite a hilh vek a.
11. Na tung ua vaihawmmi ding kumpipa tatdan hichibang ahi ding: na tapate uh a sakol leh a kangtalai tuanpih dingin a sep dinga, a kangtalai maa taita a om ding ua:
12. Huan, a sepaih sang heutu ding, sawmnga heutu ding a sep dinga; huan, khenkhatte a lei let ding, a buh la ding, a kidouna van bawlmi ding, a kangtalai van bawlmi dingte dingin a zang ding.
13. Huan, na tanute uh gimlim bawl dng, anhuan ding, tanghou bawl dingin a zang ding.
14. Huan, na loute, na grep huante, olive huante a hoih tata a honsut dinga, a sikhate a pe ding.
15. Huan, na haichi leh na grep huana sawma khat a honlaksak dinga: a sikhate leh heutute a pe ding.
16. Huan, na sikhapate, na sikhanute, na tangval hoihhoihte, na bawngte a honsut dinga, a nasem dingin a zang ding hi.
17. Na belam hon laka sawma khat a honlaksak dinga; huan, a sikhate na hi ding uh.
18. Huan, huai hun chiangin na tel ding uh kumpipa jiakin na thum khe ding ua; himahleh huai hun chiangin TOUPAN a hondawng kei ding, a chi a.
19. Himahleh mipiten Samuel thu a jui nuam kei ua, ahi kei, kumpipa I neih ngeingei ding ahi;
20. Huchiin nam dangte tengteng bang I honghi dinga, huan, I kumpipan vai a hon hawmsak dinga, I namte piin galte a hondou pih ding, a chi ua.
21. Huan, Samuelin mipite thugen tengteng a ja a, TOUPA bilah a thun sawm a.Huan, TOUPAN Samuel kiangah, a thu uh la inla, kumpipa bawlsakin, a chi a. Huan, Samuelin Israel mite kiangah, mi chih noumau khua ah pai chiat un, a chi a.
22. Huan, TOUPAN Samuel kiangah, a thu uh la inla, kumpipa bawlsakin, a chi a. Huan, Samuelin Israel mite kiangah, mi chih noumau khua ah pai chiat un, a chi a.
