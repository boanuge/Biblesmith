1. Huchia Juda leh Benjamin melmaten saltangte taten TOUPA, Israel Pathian adingin biakin a lam uh chih a jak un;
2. Huai laiin Zerubbabel leh, pipute inkote lupente kiangah a hongpia nai ua, a kiang uah, Honlam pih le ung ake: noute bangin, na Pathian ulah ka zong ngal ua; Assuria kumpipa Esarhaddon hiailaia honpipa damlai nite akipan amah kiangah ka kithoih uhi, a chi ua.
3. Himahleh Zerubbabel leh, Jesua leh Israel pipute inkote lupenten a kiang uah, Ka Pathian uh adia in non lampihna ding uh bangmah na neikei uh; himahleh Persia kumpipa Kurain kou thu a honpiak bangin, Israel Pathian, TOUPA adingin kouten ka lam khawm ding uhi, a chi ua.
4. Huchiin gama miten Judaa mite khut a hatsak ta kei ua, inlamnaah amau a subuaita ua.
5. Persia kumpia Kura damlai tengteng, Persia kumpipa Daria lal lai tanpha hialin, a thiltup uh kalhse ding, amaute siatna dingin thuhilhmite a guai uhi.
6. Huan Ahasura vaihawm laiin, a vaihawm pattung laiin, Juda leh Jerusalema tengte hekna a gelh uh.
7. Huan Artaksarsi damlaiin Bislam te, Mithredath te, Tabeel te leh, a kithuahpih ten, Persia kumpipa Artaksarsis kiangah lai a gelh ua; huan laikhak gelhdan Suria laimala gelha, Suria paua bawlkhiak ahi.
8. Dansiam Rehum leh laigelhmi Simsaiin Jerusalem siatna dingin kumpipa Artaksarsi kiangah hichibangin laikhak a gelhta uh:
9. Dansiam Rehum leh, laigelhmi Simsai leh, a kithuahpihte un huchiin a gelhta ua; Dinai mite leh, Aphratchi mite, Tarpe mite, Aphar mite, Arki mite, Babulon mite, Susanchi mite, Deha mite, Elam mite leh
10. Sanppar lian leh thupiin a honpi khiaka, Samaria khopi leh luipi khena gam dang leh a dangdanga a koih namte.
11. Kumpipa Artaksarsis kianga a laikhak uh kopi tuh hiai ahi; Luipi khenlam leh, a dangdanga na sikhate.
12. Na kiang akipana hong tou Judate Jerusalem ah kou kiangah ahong uh chih kumpipa kiangah theihin om hen: khopi helhat leh hoihlou a lam lellel ua, a kulhbangte a zouta ua, a suangphumte a puahkhinta uh.
13. Kumpipan thei hen, hiai khopi lama a oma, a kulh bangte zoha a om lejaw, vanleiman te, sumsinnaleiman te hiam lamlianleiman te khawng a pe kei ding ua, a tawptawpin kumpipa siatna ahi ding.
14. Tuin inpia chi ne ka hih jiak un, kumpipa zahlakna muh kou adingin a kilawm keia, huaijiakin kumpipa tuh ka khak un, ahi ngeingei ka chita uhi;
15. Na pipute chiamtehna laibu sungah kizong hen: huchiin chiamtehna laibu sungah na mukhe dinga, hiai khopi khopi helhat, kumpipate leh bialte adia poikhoihhat chih leh, nidang lai mahin leng huai sung mahah helna a nabawlta uh na thei ding hi: huaijiaka hiai khopi kihihse gawp lah ahi ngala.
16. Hiai khopi a kilama, a kulhbangte hichibanga a kihihkhit lejaw, luipi khenlamah tan na neikei ding chih kumpipa ka hontheitel sak chinten uhi, chiin.
17. Huchiin dansiam Rehum leh, laigelhmi Simsai leh, Samaria leh, luipi khena gama teng a kithuahpihte dante tengteng kiangah kumpipan dawnna a khaka, Chibai, huchibang jelin.
18. Laikhak ka kiang ua na honkhak uh ka maah tel taka sim a hita.
19. Huan thu ka piaa, a kizong khina, nidang laiin hiai khopiin kumpipate tunga helna a bawla, huailaiah helpina leh helna a nakibawlta chih muhkhiakin a om hi.
20. Huailouin kumpipa thilhihthei taktak, luipi khen gam tengteng tunga vaihawm, Jerusalem tunga vaihawmte a naom ua; vanleimate, sumsinnaleiman te leh, lamlianleiman amau kianga piak ahi hi.
21. Huchiin keimahin thupiak ka bawl masiah, huai mite khawl ding leh, hiai khopi lam lou dingin, thupiak bawl un.
22. Huan hiaia zeol lou dingin pilvang un: bangdingin kumpipate siatna dingin hoihlouhna a khang dia? chiin.
23. Huchia kumpipa Artaksarsi laikhak kopi Rehum leh, laigelhmi Simsai leh a kithuahpihte uh maa a sim khit un, kintakin Jerusalem Judate kiangah ahoh ua, kihatsakna leh thilhihtheihna zangin amau thu a khawlsakta uhi.Huchiin Jerusalema Pathian in nasep a khawlta: Persia kumpipa Daria vaihawm kum nihna tanin a khawl hi.
24. Huchiin Jerusalema Pathian in nasep a khawlta: Persia kumpipa Daria vaihawm kum nihna tanin a khawl hi.
