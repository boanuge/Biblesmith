1. Huan, Jesu'n, amaute a houpih a, a kiang uah gentehna thu a gen nawn a,
2. Vangam tuh kumpipa kua hiam a tapa a dia kitenna ankuang lui toh teh ahi.
3. Huan, kitenna ankuang um dia achialte tuh sam ding in a sikhate a sawl a, himahleh, a hong nuamkei uh.
4. Huan, aman, Ka chialte kiang ah, Ngai dih ua, ka ankuang ka manta; ka bawngtalte keh ka gan vak thaute ka gou a, bangteng a manta hi; kitenna ankuang um ding in hongpaita un, chi un, chi in, a sikha dangte a sawl nawn a.
5. Himahleh, a na limsakkei ua, a pai mang ua, khat tuh a lou ah a kuan a, khat tuh a sumsinna ah;
6. mi dangte'n a sikhate a man ua, zumhuai tak in a bawl ua, a hihlumta mai ua.
7. Huchi in, kumpipa tuh a thangpaita a; a sepaih hon a sawl a, huai tualthatte a hihmang a, a khua uh leng a halsakta.
8. Huan, a sikhate kiang ah, Kitenna ankuang man a hita, ka chialte lah ne tak a hikei uh.
9. Huaiziak in, lamkathuamte khawng ah pai unla, mi na muh peuhpeuh uh kitenna ankuang um ding in chial un, a chi a.
10. Huchi in, huai sikhate a pawt ua, lamkathuam khawng a vapai in, a muh peuhmah uh, migilou leh mihoihte leng, a pikhawmvek ua: huchi in, kitenna dantan tuh mi chialte in a hong dimta a.
11. Himahleh, kumpipa tuh mi chialte en ding a a hong lut leh, kitenna puan silhlou mi khat huai ah a mu a;
12. Huchi in, a kiang ah, Lawm, bangchidan ahia, kitenna puan silhlou ahiai lai a nong lut? a chi a. Huan, amah tuh a dai dide a.
13. Huan, kumpipa'n a sikhate kiang ah, A khut leh a khe gak unla, polam mial ah paikhia un; huailai ah kah leh hagawi a om ding, a chi.
14. Chialte tuh tampi ahi ua, telte jaw tawmchik ahi uh, a chi a.
15. Huailai in Pharisaite a thugen a mat theihna dingdan a vakikum ua.
16. Huan, amau tuh a nungjuite uleh Herod pawlte a kiang ah a sawl ua, huai mite'n tuh, Sinsakpa, mi diktak na hi a, diktak in Pathian lam thu na hilh a, poisak tuan kuamah na neikei chih ka thei uh; kuamah khentuam na neikei a.
17. Huaijiak in, bangchi na sa a? hon hilh di aw? Kaisar kiang a siah tawi a siang hia sianglou? A chi ua.
18. Himahleh, Jesu'n a gitlouhdan uh a nathei a, Lepchiahte aw, bangdia hon ze-et na hi ua?
19. Siahsum tuh honla un, a chi a. Huan, a kiang ah makhai a honla ua.
20. Huan, aman a kiang uah, Hiai kua lim leh kua min ahia? A chi a.
21. Amau tuh a kiang ah, Kaisar a, a chi ua. Huan, aman a kiang uah, A hih nung, Kaisar thilte Kaisar kiang ah pia unla, Pathian thilte Pathian kiang ah peta unla, a chi a.
22. Huai a jak un lamdang a sa mahmah ua, amah a paisan ua, a paimangta uh.
23. Huai ni mah in, Saddukaite, thohnawnna a omkei chite, a kiang ah a hong ua;
24. a kiang ah, Sinsakpa, Mosi in, Mi ta neilou in si leh, a unaupa'n a ji luah in a unaupa a di'n chi a khah sak ding ahi, a chi a.
25. A hi, ka lak uah sal sagih a om ua; a upapen in ji a nei a, a si a, huan, chi a khahlouh jiak in a ji tuh a unaupa luah ding in a nutsiat a;
26. huchimah bang in nihna in a luah a, thumna in a luah lai a, sagihna tan in a luah chiat ua.
27. Huan, a vek ua a sih nung un numei mah leng a sita a.
28. Huchi in, thohnawnna ah amau sagihte lak ah kua pen ji ahi dia? a vek un lah a nei chiat ngal ua, chi in, a dong ua.
29. Jesu'n a kiang uah, Laisiangthou leh Pathian thilhihtheihna na theihlouh jiak un na ngai khial nak uhi.
30. Thohnawnna ah ji leh pasal a kitenna om nawnkei a, van a angelte bang ahi zota ding uh.
31. Ahihhang in, misi thohnawnna thu Pathian in na kiang ua a gen na simkei ua hia?
32. Kei Abraham Pathian, Isaak Pathian, Jakob Pathian ka hi, chi in, Pathian jaw misi Pathian a hikei a, mihing Pathian ahi jaw, a chi a, a dawng a.
33. Huan, mipite'n huai a jak un a thuhilh tuh lamdang a sa mahmah ua.
34. Pharisaite'n bel, Jesu'n Saddukaite a selkhia chih a zak un a kikhawmta ua.
35. Huan, a lak ua dansiammi khat in, amah ze-et in thu a dong a,
36. Sinsakpa, dan thu ah bang thupiak pen ahia thupipen? chi in,
37. Huan, aman a kiang ah, Toupa na Pathian tuh na lungtang tengteng in, na kha tengteng in, na lungsim tengteng in na it ding ahi, chih.
38. Huai tuh thupiak thupipen leh poimoh pen ahi.
39. Huan, a zom in, a kibatpih tuh hiai ahi, Nangmah na ki-it bang in na vengte leng na it ding ahi, chih.
40. Huai thupiak nih ah dan thu leh jawlneite thu tengteng a kikhaikhawm vek ahi, a chi a.
41. Huan, Pharisaite a kikhoplai un. Jesu'n amaute thu a dong a.
42. Kris tuh bangchi na sa ua? kua tapa ahia? A chi a. Amau tuh a kiang ah, David tapa, a chi ua.
43. Aman tuh a kiang uah, Bangchidan in ahia David in Kha a amah Toupa, a chih a.
44. Toupa'n, ka Toupa kiang ah, Na melmate na khepek nuai a ka koih ka taklam ah tu in, chia, a chih?
45. David in amah, Toupa, a chih ngal leh, bangchin a tapa ahi dia? a chi a.Huan, kuamah in kam khat in leng a mah a dawng theikei ua, huai ni a kipan tuh kuamah in thu dang himhim a dong ngam nawnta kei uh.
46. Huan, kuamah in kam khat in leng a mah a dawng theikei ua, huai ni a kipan tuh kuamah in thu dang himhim a dong ngam nawnta kei uh.
