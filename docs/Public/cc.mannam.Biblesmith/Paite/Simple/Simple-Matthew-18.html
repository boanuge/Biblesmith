1. Huai laitakin nungjuite Jesu kiangah a hongpai ua, Vangamah kua a thupipen dia le? A chi ua.
2. Huan, aman tuh naupang neu chik a sama; a lak uah a ding saka.
3. Chihtaktakin ka honchi ahi, Na lamdan ua, naupang neuchik bang na honghih kei uleh, vangam ah na lut kei hial ding uh.
4. Huchi in, kuapeuh hiai naupang neu chik banga kingainiam, huai mi tuh vangam ah a thupipen ding.
5. Huan kuapeuh ka min jiaka hichi bang naupang neu chik khat kipahpih tuh keimah honkipahpih ahi ding.
6. Himahleh, hiai hongingta neu chikte laka khar leng hihpuk tuh, a ngawng uah buh gawijanna suang lianpi ok sakin tupi thuk lai ah khe mang le uh amau a dingin a phatuam zo ding.
7. Puknate jiak in khovel tung a gik hi; puknate bel hongom ding him ahi a; himahleh pukna omsakpa tung a gik.
8. Huan, na khutin hiam, na khein hiam, a honhihpuk leh, tan inla, pai mang in; khut nih hiam khe nih hiam neia khantawn meia paih a om sang in, piangsualin hiam khebaiin hiam hinna a lut nanga ding in a hoihjaw.
9. Huan, mitin a honhihpuk leh khel khia inla, pai mang in; mit lang nih neia Gehenna meia paiha om sangin, mit lang khat neia hinna a lut nanga ding in a hoihjaw.
10. Hiai mineute lak a khat leng simmoh lou ding in pilvang un, a angelte un van ah ka Pa van a om mel a mu gige uh chih ka hon hilh ahi.
11. Mihing Tapa tuh mangthangte zong ding a hongpai ahi ngal a.
12. Bangchi na sa ua? Mi kuapeuh in belam za nei henla, a lak ua khat vak mang taleh, sawmkua leh kuate tuh nusia in, tang a pai in, a mangpen a zongkei dia hia?
13. Chihtaktak in ka hon hilh ahi, a muh nawn zenzen in tuh, sawmkua leh kua vak mang loute tungah sang in, a tungah a kipak zo ding.
14. Huchibang mah in hiai mineute lak a khat manthat leng na Pa uh van a om deihlam a hikei hi.
15. Huan, na unau in na tungah thil ahihkhelh leh, pai inla, amah leh nang kia theih in a khelhna ah hilh in; a hon ngaih khiak leh na unau tuh na zou ahi ding.
16. A hon ngaihkhiak kei leh mi dang khat hiam nih hiam na kiang ah na pi dia, huchi in thu chiteng theihpih mi nih hiam thum hiam kam a hihkip a a om theihna ding in.
17. Huan amau leng a ngaihkhiak nopkei a leh, saptuamte kiang ah tun in; huan, saptuamte leng a ngaihkhiak nopkei leh, amah tuh nang ding in Jentel leh siahkhonmi bang hi heh.
18. Chihtaktakin ka hon hilh ahi, leitung ah bangpeuh na hen uh tuh, van ah hen ahi ding. Huan leitung a na phel peuhmah uh, van ah leng phel ahi ding.
19. Chihtaktak in ka hon hilh nawn ahi. Leitung ah na lak ua mi nih a thil ngetlam ua a kithutuah uleh, ka Pa van a om hihsak in a om ding uh.
20. Mi nih hiam thum hiam ka min a a kikhopna peuh uah a lak uah ka omzel ngal a, a chi a.
21. Huailai in Peter a hongpai a, a kiang ah, Toupa, ka unau in bangzahvei ahia ka tungah khial leh ka ngaihdam ding? sagihvei tan in ahi diam? A chi a.
22. Jesu'n a kiang ah, Sagihvei tan in ka hon chikei, Sagihvei mun sawmsagih tan in, ka honchi jaw ahi.
23. Huaijiak in, vangam tuh kumpipa kua hiam a sikhate kiang a a sum thu hihchet tum toh tehpih ahi.
24. Huchia a hihchet pat tak in, mi khat talent singkhat ba a kiang ah a honpi uh.
25. Huan piak ding a neihlouh jiak in, a pu in amah toh, a ji toh, a tate toh, a neih tengteng toh zuak a dit ding thu a pia a.
26. Huchi in, sikha in tuh, Toupa, ka tungah zahngai tadih inla, a vek in ka hon pe ding, chi in khupboh in chibai a buk a.
27. Huan, huai sikhapa pun a hehpihta mahmah a, a khah a, a bat a ngaidamta.
28. Himahleh, huai sikha tuh a paikhia a, a sikha hihpihte lak a khat a makhai za ba a vamu a; huan, amah a man a, Na bat hon pia in, chi in a gawl a mek a.
29. Huchi in a sikha hihpihpan tuh khupboh in, Ka tungah zahngai tadih inla, ka hon pe ding, chi in a ngen ngutngut a.
30. Himahleh a utkei a; a bat a piak zoh masiah suangkulh ah a vakhumta a.
31. Huchi in, a sikha hihpihte'n a thil hih tuh a muh un, a lungkham mahmah ua. A thil hih tengteng a Toupa uh a vahilhta uh.
32. Huaitak in a Toupa'n amah tuh a sam a, a kiang ah, Sikha gilou, non nget ngutngut jiak a, na hon bat tengteng hon ngaidam hi ing a;
33. ka hon hehpih bang in nang leng na sikha hihpih na hehpih sam ding hilou hia? A chi a.
34. Huan, a Toupa tuh a thangpaita a, a bat tengteng a piak zoh masiah migawtte kiang ah a peta a.Mi chin in na lungtang tak ua na unaute uh na ngaihdam kei uleh ka Pa van a om in leng huchi bangmah in na tunguah a hih sam ding, a chi a.
35. Mi chin in na lungtang tak ua na unaute uh na ngaihdam kei uleh ka Pa van a om in leng huchi bangmah in na tunguah a hih sam ding, a chi a.
