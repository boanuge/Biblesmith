1. Sakhi tal, luita tui duha nak hawkhawk bangin, Pathian aw, ka hinna nangmah duhin a nak hawkhawk ahi.
2. Ka hinna Pathian duh in, Pathian hing duhin, a dangtak ahi; chikchiang in ahia Pathian ma ka tun dinga, A mai ka muh ding.
3. Ka khitui sun leh jan in ka an ahia, Na Pathian koia om ahia leh! Honchi nilouhlouh ua.
4. Hiai thil ka thei gige a, ka hinna ka sungah ka sungbua a, mipi laka ka paiin, nuamna leh phatna ging toh, mi tampi khawlni-a Pathian biakin juana a ma uh ka kaih jel dante.
5. Ka hinna aw, bangachia kun nilouh? Bangachia ka sunga buai? Pathian lamen in; a melma damna jiakin amah ka honphat ding ahi ngala, hon panpihpa leh ka Pathian.
6. Ka Pathian aw, ka hinna ka sungah a kun nilouh; huaijiakin Jordan leh Hermon gam akipan, Mizar tang akipanin kon thei gige ahi.
7. Na tui luang ging juajua khawngah tuithuk leh tuithuk a kisam ua: na tui kihawt leh na tui kisep tengteng in honvuk tumta hi.
8. Himahleh, Toupan a chitna tuh sunin thu a pe ding a, a lain jan chiah honompih ding hi, ka hinna Pathian kiang a thumna in.
9. Pathian ka suangpi kiang ah, Bangdia honmangngilh na hia! Bangdinga melma nuaisiahna jiaka lungkhama ka mau vialvial nak! ka chi ding hi.
10. Ka guh a namsau om bang maiin hondouten hon gense gige ua; Na Pathian koia om ahia leh! honchi nilouhlouh ua.Ka hinna aw, Bangachia kun nilouh? Bangachia ka sung a buai? Pathian lamen in: amah ka honphat ding ahi ngala, hon panpihpa leh ka Pathian ahi.
11. Ka hinna aw, Bangachia kun nilouh? Bangachia ka sung a buai? Pathian lamen in: amah ka honphat ding ahi ngala, hon panpihpa leh ka Pathian ahi.
