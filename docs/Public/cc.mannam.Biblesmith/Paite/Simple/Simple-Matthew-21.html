1. Huan, Jerusalem khua a tun ding un, Bethphage khua, Oliv tang tuh, a tun un Jesu'n nungjuite nih a sawl a, a kiang uah,
2. Na ma jawn khua uah vahohta unla, sabengtung khih, a kiang a a nou toh, na mu ding ua; phel unla, ka kiang ah hon kai un.
3. Huan, kuapeuh in na kiang ua banghiam a gen uleh, Toupa'n a deih a ahi, na chi ding uh; huan, aman tuh a hon sawl pah mai ding, a chi a.
4. Hiai tengteng tuh jawlnei kam a gen a hong tun theihna ding in a hong omta hi.
5. En dih, na Kumpipa, thunuailut tak in, sabengtung tungah- sabengtung nou tung ngei a tuang in na kiang ah a hong dek, chi in, Zion tanu tuh hilh in, a chih.
6. Huchi in, nungjuite tuh a vapai ua, Jesu'n a kiang ua thu a piak bang ngei in a hih ua,
7. sabengtung leh a nou tuh a honkai ua, a tunguah a puante uh a phah ua, huai ah a tuangta a.
8. Huan, mipi lak a tamjaw in lampi ah a puante uh a phah ua, midang in sing sawl a sat ua, lampi ah a phah ua.
9. Huan, mipi a malam a paite leh a juite'n, David Tapa kiang ah Hosanna: Toupa min a hongpai tuh a hampha hi: tungnungpen ah Hosanna: chi in, a kikou ua.
10. Huan, Jerusalem khua a honglut in, a khua in, Hiai mi kua ahia? Chi in, a ling souhsouh ua.
11. Huan, mipite'n, hiai mi jawlnei Jesu, Galili gam a Nazaret khua a ahi, a chi ua.
12. Huan, Jesu Pathian biak in ah a valut a, biakin a juakmite leh leimite tengteng a delhkhia a, dangka khengte dohkante leh vakhu juakte tutphahte leng a sawnpaih sak a;
13. a kiang uah, Ka in thumna in a chi ding uh, chih gelh ahi; nou bel suamhatte khuk in na bawljaw uh, a chi a.
14. Huan, Pathian biak in ah mittawte leh khebaite a kiang ah a hong ua; amau a na hihdam.
15. Himahleh, siampu lalte leh laigelhmite'n a thil lamdang hihte leh biakin a, David Tapa Hosanna: chi a naupang kikoute a muh un, a lungkimkei mahmah ua,
16. a kiang ah, Hiaite thugen na ja hia? a chi ua. Huan, Jesu'n a kiang uah, Ja e; Naungekte leh nawi nelaite kam a pawt in pahtawina na hihkimta, chih na sim ngeikei ua hia? A chi a.
17. Huan, amau tuh a paisan a, khopi apat a pawt a, Bethani ah a pai a; huai ah a giakta.
18. Huan, jing a khopi lam a akik nawnlai in a gil a hong kialta a.
19. Huan, lamsik ah theipi kung a mu a, a va naih a, a nah kia loungal bangmah a mukei a. Huan, a kiang ah, Khantawn in na gah nawnta ken a chi a.
20. Huan, theipi tuh a vul pah ngal a. Huan, nungjuite'n huai amuh un, Bangchidan ahia theipi a vulpah tel? chi in lamdang a sa mahmah ua.
21. Huan, Jesu'n a kiang uah, Chihtaktakin, ka hon hilh ahi, ginna na neih ua, na gin lah kei uleh, theipi tung a hih bang phet leng na hihkei ding ua, hiai tang kiang ah leng, Na kisuan inla, tuipi ah na kipai in, chi le uchin, na chih bang un, a hong om ding.
22. Huan, bang peuh gingta a thum a na nget uh na mu ding uh, a chi a, a dawng a.
23. Huan, Pathian biakin a lut nung a, thu a hilhlai in, siampu lalte leh vantang upate a kiang ah a hong ua, a kiang ah, Kua thu in ahia hiai thil na hih? Kuan thu a hon pai a? A chi ua.
24. Huan, Jesu'n a kiang uah, Ken leng thu kam khat kon dong sam ding, huai tuh na hon hilh uleh, kua thu in ahia hiai thil ka hih, ka hon hilh sam ding.
25. Johan baptisma, hiai kaw kipan ahia? Van a kipan hia, a hihkeileh mihing a kipan? A chi a, a dong a. Huan, a kingaihtuah ua. Van a kipan, chi lehang, Bang dia amah ginglou na hi ua leh? A hon chi kha dia.
26. Huan, Mihing a kipan, chi lehang, mipi i kihta ngal a; mi tengteng in lah Johan jawlnei in a sim ngal ua, a chi ua.
27. Huchi in, Jesu a dawng ua, Theikei ung, a chi ua. Aman leng a kiang uah, Ken leng kua thu in ahia hiai thil ka hih ka hon hilh samkei ding, a chi a.
28. Himahleh, nou bang chi na sa ua? Mi kuahiam in tapa nih a nei a, Khatpa kiang ah a vahoh a, Ta aw, tuni'n grep huan ah vasem ve, a chi a.
29. Aman, Ka utkei, a chi a, a dawng a; himahleh a denchiang in a lungsim a kheng a, a kuanta a.
30. Huan, khatpa kiang ah leng a vahoh a, tua bangmah in a vachi a. Huan, aman, Pu, ka kuan ding, a chi a, a dawng a; himahleh a kuanta kei.
31. Huai mi nihte a kua jaw in, a pa uh deihlam a hih aleh a chi a. Amau tuh, A masapen in, a chi ua. Jesu'n a kiang uah, Chihtaktakin ka hon hilh ahi, siahkhonte leh kijuakte'n Pathian gamah nou a hon lut khalh ding uh.
32. Johan tuh diktatna lam tawn in na kiang uah a hong a, amah lah na gingta ngalkei ua: siahkhonmite leh kijuakte'n bel amah a gingta ua; noute'n huai na muh nung un leng amah na gintak theihna ding un na kisik nuam tuankei uh.
33. Gentehna thu dang ngaikhia un: Mi kuahiam, in nei a om a, huai min tuh grephuan a bawl a, gawl in a um suak a, grep sukna khuk a tou a, in sang a lam a; huan, lou kemte kiang ah khoh loh ding in a pia a, gam dang ah a paita a.
34. Huan, a gah loh a hun ding kuan in a gah la ding in lou kemte kiang ah a sikhate a sawl a.
35. Huan, lou kemte'n tuh a sikhate a naman ua, khat a vua ua, khat a that ua, khat suang in a deng ua.
36. Huan, sikha dang, a masa pawl sang a tamjaw a sawl nawn a; amau tungah leng a ma ua mite a hih bangmah un a na hih nawn ua.
37. Huai nung in tuh, Ka tapa a zahtak ding uh, chi in, a tapa tuh a kiang uah a sawl a.
38. Himahleh lou kemte'n a tapa a na muh tak un, Hiai mipa gouluahdingpa ahi; that ni e: a gou luah ding tansak ni, a kichi ua.
39. Huchi in, amah tuh a man ua, grephuan a kipan a pai khia ua, a thatta ua.
40. Huchi in grep huan neipa a hongpai chiang in huai lou kemte tungah bang chin a hon hih dia le? A chi a.
41. Amau tuh a kiang ah, Huai migiloute tuh haksa tak in a hihmangthang ding a, grep huan lah lou kemmi dang a loh hun teng a a gahte a kiang a pe jel dingte kiang ah, khoh loh ding in a pe ding, a chi ua.
42. Jesu'n a kiang uah, Laisiangthoute a, In lammite suang deihlouhpen, huai ngeingei a ning a suang phatuampen a hong hita. Huai tuh Toupa hih ahi, i mit muh in a lamdang ahi, chih, na sim ngeikei ua hia?
43. Huaijiak in, Pathian gam tuh na kiang ua kipan laksak ahi dia, a gah suah ding nam dang kiang ah piak ahi ding, chih, ka hon hilh ahi.
44. Huan, huai suang tung a puk tuh a kitam gawp dia; a delh khakte houh a gawijan ding, a chi a.
45. Huan, siampu lalte leh Pharisaite'n a gentehna thute a jak un amau tung thu a gen chih a theita ua.Huan, amah tuh mat a tum ua; himahleh mipite a kihta ngal ua, jawlnei a a sim jiak un.
46. Huan, amah tuh mat a tum ua; himahleh mipite a kihta ngal ua, jawlnei a a sim jiak un.
