1. Huan, Paikan Ankuangluina ding ni guk a om nalai a, Jesun misi laka kipana a kaihthoh Lazar omna Bethani khua a hongtung a.
2. Huan, huaiah amaha din nitakan a bawl ua, Marthain nek a bawl hi, Lazar bel a ankuang umpih khat ahi a.
3. Huan, Mariin buk kimkhat spiknardi that namtui manpha tak a hontawia, Jesu khete a nuih a, a samin a khete a nula, huchiin huai thau gimin in tuh a dimta hi.
4. Himahleh, Iskariot, Juda, a nungjui khat, a mansak dingpan,
5. Hiai thau namtui bangdia makhai zathuma juaka mi gentheite kianga pe zolou uh ahia? a chi a.
6. Mi gentheite a khotuah jiaka huai theu gen ahi kei; guta ahi a, dangka ip a kem a, a sunga koih a lak jel jiak ahi jaw.
7. Huan, Jesun, Hih hen aw. Ka kivui ni-a dinga a sit a zang ahi, sen hen aw.
8. Mi gentheite na kiang ua om gige ahi ua, kei bel na kiang ua om gige ding ka hi kei, a chi a.
9. Huan, Jesu huaiah a om chih Juda mipi vantangin a na theita ua; huan, a hong ua, Jesu jiak kia hi louin, misi laka kipan a kaihthoh, Lazar mah leng a muh nop jiak uh ahi.
10. Huan, siampu liante Lazar leng hihlum dingin a kikou ua;
11. amah jiaka Judate tampiin a langkai san ua, Jesun a gintak jiakun.
12. A jingin, ankuangluinaa hong mipi tampiin, Jesu Jerusalem khua ah a hong ding chih a jak un,
13. tum nahte a tawi ua, a dawn dingin a vapawt ua, Hosanna, Toupa mina Israelte Kumpipa hongpai a hampha e, chiin, a kikou ua.
14. Huan, Jesun sabengtung nou a mu a, a tungah a tuang a.
15. Lau ken, Zion tanu; en in, na Kumpipa, sabengtung nou tunga tuangin, a hongding, chih gelh bangin.
16. Huai thute a tungin a nungjuiten a theisiam keu ua; himahleh, Jesu pahtawia a om nungin, a tanchin ah huai thu gelh ahi chih leh, a tungah huai thilte a na hih ngei uh chih a theita ua.
17. Huan, Lazar hana kipana a sapkhiakpa, misi laka kipan a kaihthoh laia a kianga mipi omten, a thu a theihpih uhi.
18. Huaijiakin ahi, mipiten leng amah a vadawn uh, huai thillamdang a hih chih a thei ngal ua.
19. Huchiin, Pharisaiten, Thei un, bangmah na hih theih louh uh, en un, khovelin amah a juita hi, a kichi ua.
20. Huan, ankuangluinaa Pathian be dinga hohtoute lakah Grik kuate hiam a om ua;
21. huan huaite Galili gama Bethsaida khuaa mi Philip kiangah a hong ua, a kiangah, Pu, Jesu mu nuam ung, a chi ua, a thum un.
22. Philipin Andru a honghilha; huan, Andru leh Philipin Jesu a honghilh uh.
23. Huan, Jesun a dawnga, a kiang uah, Mihing Tapa pahtawia a omna ding hun a tungta.
24. Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin kon hilh ahi, Buh tang leia a kiak a, a sih kei leh, amah kiain a om tawntung hi; a sihin bel, tampiin a gah nak hi.
25. Kuapeuh a hinna itin a magsak a; kuapeuh hiai khovela a hinna ngaineuin khantawna hinna ah a vom gige ahi.
26. Kuapeuhin ka na a sep leh honjui hen; ka omna ah ka nasempa leng a om ding hi; kuapeuhin ka na a sep leh Pain amah a pahtawi ding hi.
27. Tun ah ka kha a mangbang hi; bang ka gen dia? Pa, hiai hun lakah honhondam in, ka chi dia hia? Chi lou e. Hiai dinga tu tana na om hiing a.
28. Pa na min pahtawi in, a chi a. Huchiin vana kipanin aw hongsuaka, Ka pahtawita, ka pahtawi lai ding achia a.
29. Huan, mipi a kianga dingten huai a ja ua, Vanpi ging ahi, a chi ua; midangten, Angelin amah a houpih ahi, a chi ua.
30. Jesun a dawnga, Huai aw keia dia hongsuak ahi kei, noumaua ding ahi jaw.
31. Tu tuh hiai khovel vaihawmsakna hun ahi; tuin hiai khovel lal paih khiak ahi ding.
32. Huan, leia kipana khai kanga ka om leh, mi tengteng kei mah kiangah ka hip ding, a chi a.
33. Huai thu tuh a sih dingdan theihsakna dia a gen ahi.
34. Huan, mipin a kiangah, Kris khantawnin a om ding chih dan bu ah ka thei uh, Nang, Mihing Tapa khaikanga om ding ahi, bangchi chiha chi na hia? Huai Mihing Tapa tuh kua ahia? a chi ua, adawng ua.
35. Huan, Jesun a kianguah, Vak na lak uah sawt lou a omtadih hi. Vak na neih lai un vak ah khosa un, huchilouin jaw mialin honlap ding hi. Kuapeuh miala khosain a paina a theikei.
36. Vak na neih lai un vak gingta un, vak tate na honghih theihna dingun, a chi a. Huai thute Jesun a gena; huan, apaia, amuh louh dingun a buta.
37. A ma uah huchi tela tam thillamdang hih mahleh, amah a gingta tuan kei ua:
38. Toupa, ka thu gen uh kuan ahia gingta? Toupa khut leng kua kiangah ahia latsaka a om? chih jawlnei Isai thugen a tun na dingin.
39. Hiai jiakin ahi a gintak theih louh uh, Isaiin.
40. Aman a mit uh a hihtaw a, a lung tang uh a hihkhauh a; huchilou injaw a mit un a mu khading ua, a lungtang un a thei kha ding uh, huan, hongkihei nawn khading ua, ken amau ka hihdam khading hi, chih, a gen nawn jiakin.
41. Isaiin a thupina a muh leh a tanchin thu agen jiakin huai thu a gen hi.
42. Himahleh, heutute laka nangawn tampiin Jesu a gingta ua; a hihhangin Pharisaite jiakin a kipulak nuam kei uh, huchilou injaw kikhopna in ah a nong kha ding uh:
43. Pathian pahtawina sangin mihing pahtawina a deih zo ngal ua.
44. Huan, Jesu a kikou a, Kuapeuh hon gingta tuh kei gingta ahi kei, honsawlpa gingta ahi jaw.
45. Kuapeuh kei hon mu tuh honsawlpa mu ahi.
46. Vak dia khovela hong ka hi a, kuapeuh kei hon gingta tuh miala a om louhna ding in.
47. Kuapeuh ka thu gen jain, gingta kei mahleh, ken siam louh ka tangsak kei: khovel siam louh tangsak dinga hong ka hi kei a, khovel humbit dinga hong ka hi zo ngala.
48. Kuapeuh kei hondeih lou a, ka thu gente pom louin, siamlouh tangsak khat a nei ahi, ka thu gen, huai thu mahmahin, ni tawpni chiah amah siamlouh a tang sak ding hi.
49. Keimah phuahtawmin ka gen ngal keia; Pa honsawlpan ka gen ding leh ka hih ding thu a honpe jaw ahi.Amah thupiak khantawna hinna ahi chih ka thei; huaijiakin thu ka gen, Pa honhilh bang jelin ka gen ahi, a chi a.
50. Amah thupiak khantawna hinna ahi chih ka thei; huaijiakin thu ka gen, Pa honhilh bang jelin ka gen ahi, a chi a.
