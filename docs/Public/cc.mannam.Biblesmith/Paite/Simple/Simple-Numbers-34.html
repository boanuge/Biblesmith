1. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
2. Israel suante thu pia inla, a kiang uah, Kanan gam na tun chiang un (Kanan gam, a gamgi dan dungzui zel a na luah ding gam uh),
3. Sim lam a na luah ding chiang uh Zin gamdai a kipan Edom gam zulzul ahi ding a, sim lam gamgi Tuipi Al tawp a kipan suah lam pang ahi ding.
4. Huan, na gamgi uh Akrabim tungkahna sim lam pang ah a kawi ding a, huan, a hong pawtna Kades-barnea sim lam ah ahi ding: huan, Hazar-adar ah a pai ding a, Azmon ah a pai zel ding:
5. Huan, gamgi Azmon a kipan in Aigupta lui tan in a kawi ding a, a tunna tuipi ah ahi ding.
6. Huan, tumlam pang ah tuipi zaa a gamgi ding in na tang ding a, hiai tum lam a na gamgi uh ahi ding.
7. Huan, hiai mal lam a na gamgi uh ahi ding: tuipi zaa a kipan in Hor tang huap in na bawl ding a:
8. Hor tang a kipan Hamat lutna tan in na bawl ding a, gamgi tunna bel Zedah ah ahi ding a;
9. Huan, gamgi Ziphron ah pai zel ding a, huan, a tunna Hazar-anah ah ahi ding; hiai mal lam a na gamgi uh ahi ding.
10. Huan, suah lam a na gamgi uh Hazar-enan a kipan a Sepham tan in na bawl ding a:
11. Huan, gamgi Sepham a kipan in Rebla tan in Ain suah lam pang ah a sukpai ding a; huchi in, gamgi a paisuksuk dia, Kineret tuipi suah lam pang a sun ding:
12. Huan, gamgi Jordan ah a paisuk ding a, huan, a tunna Tuipi Al ah ahi ding: hiai a kimvel a gamgi paidan bang in na gam uh ahi ding, chi in, chi in.
13. Huchi in Mosi in Israel suante thu a pia a, Hiai TOUPA'N nam kua leh a kimkhatte piak ding a thu a piak aisan a na luah ding gam uh ahi:
14. Reuben tate nam in a innkuan uh dungzui zel leh Gad tate nam in a innkuan uh dungzui zel leh Manase nam kimkhat in leng a gam luah ding uh a tang ta ngal ua:
15. Nam nih leh nam kimkhatte'n a gam luah ding uh ni suahlam Jeriko zawn Jordan gal suah lam ah a tangta uh, chi in.
16. Huan, TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
17. Luah ding a gam hon khennen pihte min hiaite ahi: siampu Eleazar leh Nun tapa Josuate.
18. Huan, gam luah ding khen mi a ding in namchih lak a inntek pen khat chiat na tel ding ahi, chi in.
19. Huai mite min bel hiaite ahi: Juda nam a mi Jephune tapa Kaleb.
20. Huan, Simeon tate nam a mi Amihud tapa Semuel.
21. Huan, Benzamin nam a mi Kislon tapa Elidad.
22. Dan tate nam lak a inntek pen Jogli tapa Buki.
23. Joseph tate lak a mi, Manase tate nam inntek pen Ephod tapa Haniel:
24. Epharim tate nam lak a inntek pen Sitan tapa Kemuel.
25. Huan, Zebulun tate nam lak a inntek pen Parnak tapa Elizaphan.
26. Isakar tate nam lak a inntek pen Azan tapa Paltiel.
27. Aser tate nam lak a inntek pen Selomi tapa Ahihud.
28. Naphtali tate nam lak a inntek pen Amidhud tapa Padahel.Hiaite Kanan gam a Israel suante gam luah ding hawm ding a TOUPA'N thu a piakte ahi uhi.
29. Hiaite Kanan gam a Israel suante gam luah ding hawm ding a TOUPA'N thu a piakte ahi uhi.
