1. Paikan Ankuanglui kipat main, Jesun, hiai khovel nusiaa Pa kianga a pai hun hongtungta chih a theia; hiai khovel amah-ate na itkhinin a tawp phain a it hi.
2. Huan, nitak annek laiin, diabolin Juda Iskariot, Simon tapa lungtanga Jesu juausana-matsak lungsim a koihsak khitnungin,
3. Jesun, Pa-in a khut ah thil bangkim a peta chih leh Pathian akipana hongpai ahia, Pathian kianga pai ding ahi chih theigigein,
4. A nitakan um tuh a dinga, a puante a koiha, kinulna puan a laa, tenga.
5. Huan, maiphiatkuangah tui a sunga, nungjuite khe a silta a, kinulna puan a tenin a nul jel hi.
6. Huchiin, Simon Peter kiangah a hong a. Aman, Toupa, nang telin ka khe na sil dia hia? a chi a.
7. Jesun a kiangah, Ka thilhih tunah na theikei: himahleh tunung chiangin na thei ding, a chi a, a dawnga.
8. Peterin a kiangah, Ka khe na sil kei hial ding, a chi a. Jesun a kiangah, Kon sil kei leh ka kiangah tan na neikei ding, a chi a.
9. Simon Peterin a kiangah, Toupa, ka khe kia hilouin, ka khut leh ka lutang leng aw, a chi a,
10. Jesun a kiangah, Kisil khinsa a khe lou ngal sil a kiphamoh kei, a pumpiin a siang; nou na siang uh, na vek un jaw ahi kei, a chi a.
11. A mansakpa ding a thei ngala; huaijiakin, Na vek un na siang kei uh, a chi ahi.
12. Huchiin, a khe tuh a sila, a puante a laka, a tut nawn in a kiang uah, Na tung ua ka thil hih bang chhihna ahia?
13. Nou Sinsakpa leh Toupa non chi ua; na chi dik uhi, ka hi ngei tak a.
14. Kei Toupa leh Sinsakpan, na khe uh ka sil leh, nou leng na khe uh na kisil sak ding uh ahi.
15. Na tung ua ka hih bangin, nou leng na hih samna ding un, hihdingdan honhilh ka hi ngala.
16. Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin kon hilh ahi, Sikha a pu sangin a thupizo kei; sawltak leng a sawlpa sangin a thupizo sam kei hi.
17. Huai thilte na theih ua, na hih uleh hampha nahi uh.
18. Na vek ua thu ka gen kei a; ka telte ka thei; himahleh, huai tuh, Ka tanghou nepan ka tungah a khetul a likta, chih, laisiangthou a hongtun theihna ding ahi.
19. Tuin a hongtun main kon hilh ahi, a hongtun hun chia kei amah ka hi chih na gintak theihna ding un.
20. Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin, kon hilh ahi, Kuapeuh ka sawl kipahpih tuh, keimah honkipahpih ahi; kuapeuh kei honkipahpih tuh, honsawlpa kipahpih ahi, a chi a.
21. Jesun huchi banga a genkhitin, a lungsim a mangbang a, Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin kon hilh ahi, na lak ua mi khatin kei honmansak ding, chiin a theisakta a.
22. Huan, nungjuiten a chihtuampen a theikei ua, a kien chiat ua.
23. Huan, a nungjuite laka khat, Jesun a itpa, dohkan sikah Jesu angsung ah kingaiin a om a.
24. Huan, Simon Peterin huai mi kiangah, Kua thu pen ahia a gen, honhilh dih, chiin a mitmei a.
25. Huan, Jesu awm ngai mahin a awn pheia, a kiangah, Toupa, kuapen ahia? a chi a.
26. Huan, Jesun, Tanghou them ka diah saka, ka piakpa ahi ding, a chi a, a dawnga. Huchiin, tanghou them a diah nungin, Iskariot, Simon tapa Juda kiangah a pai a.
27. Huan, tanghou them a piak nungin Setan a sungah a lutta a. Huchiin, Jesun a kiangah, Na hih ding hih meng in, a chi a.
28. Huan, dohkan sika miten kuamahin bang jiaka chi ahi chih a theikei uh.
29. A khenin, Judain dangka ip a vom jiakin, Jesun, Ankuangluinaa dingin i thil kiphamoh peuh lei in, a chi hiam, mi gentheite kiangah banghiam piak ding, a chi hiam a sa uh.
30. Huan, aman tanghou them a la a, a pawt khe pah a; huai tuh jan lai ahi.
31. Huchiin a pawt nungin, Jesun a gen a, Tuin Mihing Tapa pahtawiin a om, huna, amah ah Pathian leng pahtawiin a om ahi.
32. Amaha Pathian pahtawia a om leh amah leng Pathianin amah ah a pahtwi ding, a pahtawi pah ngal ding hi.
33. Naute aw, sawt lou chik na kiang uah ka omlai tadih a. Nou non zong ding uh; huan, Judate kianga, Ka paina dingah nou na hongpai theikei ding uh, chih ka gen bangin, tuin huchi mahbangin na kiang uah ka gen ahi.
34. Thuchiam thak kon pia ahi, kiit un; ken nou kon it bangin, nou leng kiit un.
35. Na kiit uleh, mi tengtengin ka nungjuite nahi uh chih huai ah a thei ding uh, a chi a.
36. Simon Peterin a kiang ah, Toupa, koia hoh ding na hia? a chi a, Jesun, Ka hohna dingah nang tuin non jui theikei ding; tunung chiah jaw nonjui ding, a chi a, a dawng a.
37. Peterin a kiangah, Toupa, bangdingin ahia tua leng kon zuih theih louh ding? Nangmah jiakin ka hinna ka pai ding, a chi a.Jesun a dawnga, Keimah jiakin na hinna na pai ding maw? Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin kon hilh ahi, nang thum vei non kitheihmohbawl main, ak a khuang kei ding.
38. Jesun a dawnga, Keimah jiakin na hinna na pai ding maw? Chihtaktakin, chihtaktakin kon hilh ahi, nang thum vei non kitheihmohbawl main, ak a khuang kei ding.
