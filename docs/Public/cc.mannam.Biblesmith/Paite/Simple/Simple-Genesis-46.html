1. Huan, Israel a neih tengteng toh a pawta, Beer-seba a tunga, a pa Isaak Pathian kiangah kithoihnate alan.
2. Huan, Pathianin jana a mangin Israel a houpih a, Jakob, Jakob, achia.
3. Huan, aman, hiaiah ka om, achia. Huan, aman, kei Pathian, na Pathian, ka hi: Aigupta gama hoh suk lau ken; huailaiah chi thupi takin ka honbawl ding hi:
4. Aigupta gamah na kiangah ka hong dinga; huan, ka honpi tou nawn ngeingei ding: huan, Joseph in na sih hun chiangin na mit a honbawl sak ding hi, achia.
5. Huchiin, Jakob Beer-seba akipanin a pawta: Israel taten a pa uh Jakob leh a ta neu chikchik te uhleh a jite uh tuanna dinga Pharo in kangtalai a khak te ah a tuang sak uhi.
6. Huchiin, Jakob leh a suante tengteng in kanan gama a lamkhiak uh, a gante u leh a vante uh, a tawi ua, Aigupta gama tungta uh:
7. A tapa te, a tupa te, a tanu te, a tunu te, a suante tengteng, Aigupta gamah a lut pihta hi.
8. Huan, hiaite Israel suan Aigupta gama lut, Jakob leh a tapate min ahi: Reuben Jakob ta masa.
9. Huan, Reuben tapate Hanok te, Pallu te, Hezron te, Karmi te ahi uhi.
10. Huan, Simeon tapate Jemuel te, Jamin te, Ohad te, Jakin te, Johat te, Kanan numei laka a tapa Saula te ahi uhi.
11. Huan, Levi tapa te Gerson te, Kohat te, Merari te, ahi uhi.
12. Huan, Juda tapate Er te, Onan te, Sela te, Perez te, Zerah te ahi ua: Er leh Onan bel Kanan gamah a sita ua. Perez tapate Hezrun leh Hamul ahi uhi.
13. Huan, Issakar tapate Tola te, Puva te, Ioba te, Simron te ahi uhi.
14. Huan, Zebulun tapate Sered te, Elon te, Jhaleel te ahi uhi.
15. Huaite tengteng Lea tapa, a tanu Dina toh, Padanaram gama Jakob a neihsakte ahi uhi: a tapate leh a tanute tengteng sawm thum leh thum ahi uhi.
16. Huan, Gad tapate Ziphion te, Haggi te, Suni te, Ezbon te, Eri te, Arobdi te, Areli te ahi uh.
17. Huan, Aser tapate Imna te, Isva te, Isvi te, Beria te ahi uh, a sanggamnu uh Sera toh: huan, Beria tapate Beber leh Malkiel ahi uhi.
18. Huaite Zilpai Laban in a tanu Lea kianga a piakpa tapa te ahi ua, huaite Jakob a neihsaka, mi sawm leh gukte.
19. Huan, Jakob ji Rakil tapate Joseph leh Benjamin ahi uhi.
20. Huan, Joseph in Aigupta gamah tapa Manassi leh Ephraim a neia, huai mite On kho siampu Potipher tanu Asenathin amah a neihsak ahi.
21. Huan, Benjamin in tapate Bel te, Bekor te, Asbel te, Ger te, Naaman te, Ehi te, Ros te, Muppim te, Huppin te, Ardi te ahi uhi.
22. Huaite Rakil tapa Jakob a neihsakte ahi uh: a vek un sawm leh li ahi uh.
23. Huan, Dan tapa Husim ahi.
24. Huan, Naphtali tapate Jahzeel te, guni te, Jezer te, Sillem te ahi uh.
25. Huaite Bilhai Laban in a tanu Rakil kianga a piakpa tapate ahi uh, huaite Jakob a neihsaka: a vek un sagih ahi uh.
26. Jakob tapate jite simlouh in Jakob kianga Aigupta gama lut a khala pawt tengteng a vek un sawm guk leh guk ahi uhi;
27. Huan, Joseph in Aigupta gama tapa a neihte nih ahi ua: Jakob inkuanpihte tengteng, Aigupta gam a lutte, sawm sagih ahi uh.
28. Huan, Jakob in, Gosen gam lam lampi na nakawk muh dingin, Juda bel Joseph kiangah a hoh masa saka;
29. Huchiin, Gosen gamah lutta uh. Huan, Joseph in a kangtalai a bawlkhola, Gosen gamah a pa dawn dingin a hih tou hi; huan, a kiangah a kigena, a ngawnga kunin a kap nilouh hi.
30. Huan, Israel in, Joseph kiangah, dam dama na oma, na mel ka muh nawn jiak in tun jaw ut leh sita leng le, achia.
31. Huan, Joseph in, a unaute leh apa inkuanpihte kiangah kava hoh tou dia, Pharo ka hilh dinga, a kiangah, ka unaute leh ka pa inkuanpihte, huai Kanan gama omte, ka kiangah a hongtung ta uh;
32. Amaute ganvulmi ahihjiakun belam chingmite ahi uh; a belam honte u leh a bawng honte uh leh a neih tengteng uh ahonpo vek hi, chiin, kava gen ding.
33. Huan, hichi ahi dinga, Pharo in nou a honsapa, bang nasem na hi ua? a honchih hun chiangin,Nou, na sikha te, honsuangte akipan, naupang chik ka hihlai ua kipan in tu tanin gan vulmi ka hi uh, na chi ding uh: huchiin, Gosen gamah na om thei ding uhi, achia. Belam chingmite himhim lah Aigupta mite kih lamtak ahi ngal ua.
34. Nou, na sikha te, honsuangte akipan, naupang chik ka hihlai ua kipan in tu tanin gan vulmi ka hi uh, na chi ding uh: huchiin, Gosen gamah na om thei ding uhi, achia. Belam chingmite himhim lah Aigupta mite kih lamtak ahi ngal ua.
