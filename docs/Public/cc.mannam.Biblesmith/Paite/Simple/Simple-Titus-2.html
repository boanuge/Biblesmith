1. Nang zaw thuhilhna hoih toh kituak thu gen in.
2. Putekte tuh kidek thei, zahtakhuai, lungsim hoihtak, ginna leh itna leh kuhkalna a diktak hi ding in hasuan in.
3. pitekte leng huchimahbang in, siangthouna toh kimeh, khoheia zahdan siam ding leh, hekhatlou, uaiin hai lou, thilhoihte zilsakte hizaw dingin hasuan in.
4. Nungakte tuh, apasalte uh itding leh, atate uh itding leh, lungsim hoihtaka a om ding a a hilh theihan ding un,
5. Nungakte tuh, a pasal uh it ding leh, a tate uh it ding leh, lungsim hoihtaka om dinga ahilh theihna ding uh.
6. Huchibangmah in, tangvante tuh lungsim hohtak pu ding in hasuan in.
7. Thil bangkim a thil hoih hih lam a zuih ding a ki hihlang in: thuginpi a siatna omlou, hoihna tak dan hihlang in.
8. Thugen dik dan gensiat vuallouh tekhawng lehlam a pangte lak a mi na tungthu a thusia gen ding neilou a a zum theihna dingin.
9. Sikhate, sel loua, apute uh thuthu a om ding leh bangkim a a kipahna ding ua om ding in hasuan in:
10. Pathian I hondampa thuhilhna thu bangkim a kilawmsak theihna ding un, khut ommawh louin, muanhuai a na hoih tengteng hihlang zaw ding in.
11. Mitengteng kiang ah hotdamna hongtung in Pathian hehpihna a hong tungta ngal a;
12. Pathian limsaklouhna leh khovel duhgawlna khawng tawpsan in, tulai khovel ah kidek leh diktattak leh Pathian limsak in I om ding chih theihsak in;
13. lametna kipahhuai- i Pathian thupi tak Hondampa Jesu Kris thupina kilakna tuh ngak in;
14. Aman tuh tatlekna tengteng lak a kipan in a hontat theihna dingin leh, chi tel tuam, thilhoih hih a phatuam ngai, amaha dinga a hih siangthou theihna dingin, I sik in a kipeta hi.Thunei takin huaithute tuh hilh inla, hasuan inla, taihilh in. Kuamah in nang hon musit kei uhen.
15. Thunei takin huaithute tuh hilh inla, hasuan inla, taihilh in. Kuamah in nang hon musit kei uhen.
