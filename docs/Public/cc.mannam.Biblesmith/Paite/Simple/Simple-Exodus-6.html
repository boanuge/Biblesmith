1. Huchiin TOUPA'n Mosi kiangah, Tuin Pharo kianga bang ahia kahih ding na mu ding: khut thahat in amaute a paisak dinga, khut thahat in amaute a gama kipanin a hawlkhia ding ahi ngala, a chi a.
2. Huan Pathianin Mosi kianga thu genin, a kiangah, TOUPA ka hi:
3. Thilbangkimhih thei Pathian minin Abraham kiangah, Isaak kiangah, Jakob kiangah, ka kilakta, a hihhangin ka min JEHOVAHIN a lak ua theih ka hi kei hi.
4. Amaute Kanan gam, mikhuala a omna gam uh, a khualzinna gam uh, pe ding in amau toh ka thukhun leng ka hihkiptaa.
5. Huan Aiguptaten koltannaa a tansak un, Israel suante mauna te leng ka zata; huchiin ka thukhun ka theigige.
6. Huaijiakin Israel suante kiangah, TOUPA ka hi, chih gen inla, Aiguptate puakgikna nuai a kipan ka hon pi khe dinga, a koltanna ua kipan in ka hon suaktasak ding, ban jakin ka hon tan ding, vaihawmna thupi te toh.
7. Mipite dingin kong kilak ding a, nou dingin Pathian ka hi ding: huchiin Aigupta te puakgikte nuai a kipan a hon lakhe pa TOUPA na Pathian uh ka hi chih na thei ding uh.
8. Gam a tungtangthu Abraham kiang, Isaak kiang leh Jakob kianga pe ding aka chiam, gamsungah ka hontun ding: huchiin, gou luah ding inka hon pe ding: TOUPA ka hi chiin.
9. Huchiin Mosiin Israel suante kiang ah huchibangin thu a genta: ahihhang inlungsim dahna ziak leh koltanna haksatna ziakin Mosi a ngaikheta kei uh.
10. Huan TOUPAN Mosi kiangah thugenin,
11. Lutin, a gam akipan Israel suante a pai khiak sak na ding in Pharo kiangah thu genin, chiin.
12. Huchiin Mosiin TOUPA ma ah thu a genta a, ngaiin, Israel suanten hon ngaikhe kei uh; kei, muk sumlouh, bangchiin Pharo in a hon ngaikhe dia? chiin.
13. Huchiin TOUPAN Mosi kiang leh Aron kiang ah thua gena, Aigupta gam a kipan Israel suante pi khe ding in Israel suante lam leh Aigupta kumpipa lamah amau thu a peta hi.
14. Hiaite a pate innkote lupente ahi ding uh: Reuben Israel ta masa pen; Hanok, leh Pallu, Hazron, leh Karmi: hiaite Reuben inkote ahi uh.
15. Huan, Simeon tapate; Jemuel, leh Jamin, Ohad, Jakin, Zohar, Shaul Kanan numei tapa: hiaite Simeon inkote ahi uh.
16. Huchiin a khang uh dungzuiin hiaite Levi tapate a min tea hi uh; Gerson, leh Merari: huan Levi damsung kumte kum za leh sawmthum leh sagih.
17. Gerson tapate; a inko dungzui un, Libni leh Simi.
18. Huan Kohath tapate; Amran, Izhar, Hebron, Uzziel: huan Kohath damsung kumte kum za leh sawmthum leh thum ahi.
19. Huan Merari tapate; Mahali leh Musi: a khang uh dungzui in hiaite Levi inkote ahi uh.
20. Huchiin Amramin a pa sanggamnu Jodebed jiin a neia; Aron leh Miso a neihsaka: huan Amram damsung kumte kum za leh sawmthum leh sagih ahi.
21. Huan Izhar tapate; Kora, Nepheg, leh Ziri
22. Huan Uzziel tapate; Mikael, Elzaphan, leh Ziri.
23. Huchiin Aron in Amminadab tanu, Nason sanggamnu, Eliseba jiin a neia, Nadab, Abihu, Eleazar, leh Ithamar a neihsak hi.
24. Huan Kora tapate; Assir, Elkana, Abiasaph; hiaite Kora innkote ahi uh.
25. Huchiin Eleazar Aron tapa in Putiel tanute laka khat jiin a neia; Phineha a neihsak: a inko dungzui un hiaite Levite pate ahi uhi.
26. Hiaite TOUPAN Aron leh Mosi kianga A sepaihte uh dungzuiin Aigupta gama kipanin Israel suante honpi khia un, a chih te uh ahi uh.
27. Hiaite amau Aigupta kumpipa Pharo kianga Aigupta akipana Israel suante pi khe dinga: thugente ahi uh: hiaite huai Mosi leh Aron te ahi uh.
28. Huchiin hichi ahia Aigupta gama Mosi kianga TOUPAN thu gena,
29. Kei TOUPA ka hi: na kianga ka chih tengteng Aigupta kumpipa Pharo kiangah genin, chiin.Huan, Mosiin TOUPA maah. Ngaiin, muk sumlouh ka hia, bangchiin Pharoin a honngaikhe dia? a chi a.
30. Huan, Mosiin TOUPA maah. Ngaiin, muk sumlouh ka hia, bangchiin Pharoin a honngaikhe dia? a chi a.
