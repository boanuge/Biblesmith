1. Mosi leh Aron makaih a Aigupta gam a kipan a a pawlpawl a a pawt ua, a pai zeldan uh hichi bang ahi.
2. TOUPA thupiak in a paikhia ua, a pai zel nate uh Mosi in a gelh a: a paikhak ua a pai nate uh hiaite ahi:
3. Kha khatna, kha khatna ni sawm leh ni nga ni in Ramase a kipan in a pawt ua; paikan annkuang lui zing in,
4. Aigupta mite mitmuh in, a ta masa pen teng uh TOUPA'N a hihlup sak a vui lai un Israel suante innbul tak in a pawt ua: TOUPA'N a pathiante uh leng a gawt,
5. Huchi in Israel suante Ramase a kipan in a pawt ua, Sukot ah giahbuk a sat ua.
6. Huan, Sukot a kipan in a pawt ua, gamdai phak tung Etham ah giahbuk a sat uh.
7. Huan, Etham a kipan in a pawt ua, Baal-z-ephon bul a Pi-hahirot lam ah a kinungkhin ua, Migdol chin ah giahbuk a sat uh.
8. Huan, Hahirot chin a kipan a pawt ua, tuipi a pal tan ua, gamdai ah a lut uh: huan, Etham gamdai ah ni thum lam a pai ua, Mara ah giahbuk a sat uh.
9. Huan, Mara a kipan in a pawt ua, Elim a tung uh: Elim ah tuinak nei sawm leh nih leh tum kung sawmsagih a om a; huai ah giahbuk a sat uh.
10. Huan, Elim a kipan n a pawt ua, Tupi San ah giahbuk a sat uh.
11. Huan, Tuipi San a kipan in a pawt ua, Sin gamdai ah giahbuk a sat uh.
12. Sin Gamdai a kipan a pawt ua, Dopka ah giahbuk a sat uh.
13. Huan, Dopka a kipan in a pawt ua, Alus ah giahbuk a sat uh.
14. Alus a kipan in a pawt ua, Rephidim ah giahbuk a sat ua, huai ah mipite dawn ding tui a om kei hi.
15. Huan, Rephidim a kipan a pawt ua, Sinai gamdai ah giahbuk a sat ua.
16. Sinai gamdai a kipan in a pawt ua, Kibrot-hattava ah giahbuk a sat uh.
17. Huan, Kibrot-hattava a kipan in a pawt ua, Heze-rot ah giahbuk a sat uh.
18. Haze-rot a kipan a pawt ua, Ritma ah giahbuk a sat uh.
19. Huan, Ritma a kipan in a pawt ua, Rimon-perez ah giahbuk a sat uh.
20. Rimon-perez a kipan in a pawt ua, Lebna ah giahbuk a sat uh.
21. Huan, Libna a kipan a pawt ua, Risa ah giahbuk a sat uh.
22. Huan, Risa a kipan a pawt ua, Kehelatha ah giahbuk a sat uh.
23. Huan, Kehelatha a kipan a pawt ua, Sepher tang ah giahbuk a sat uh.
24. Huan, Sepher tang a kipan a pawt ua, Harada ah giahbuk a sat uh.
25. Huan, Harada a kipan in a pawt ua, Makhe-lot ah giahbuk a sat uh.
26. Huan, Makhelot a kipan a pawt ua, Tahat ah giahbuk a sat uh.
27. Huan, Tahat a kipan a pawt ua, Tera ah giahbuk a sat uh.
28. Huan, Tera a kipan a pawt ua, Mitka ah giahbuk a sat uh;
29. Huan, Mitka a kipan a pawt ua, Hasmona ah giahbuk a sat uh.
30. Hasmona a kipan in a pawt ua, Moserot ah giahbuk a sat uh.
31. Huan, Moserot a kipan in a pawt ua, Bene-jaakan ah giahbuk a sat uh.
32. Huan, Bene-jaakan a kipan in a pawt ua, Hor hagidgad ah giahbuk a sat uh.
33. Huan, Hor hagidgad a kipan in a pawt ua, Jotbatha ah giahbuk a sat uh.
34. Huan, Jotbatha a kipan in a pawt ua, Abrona ah giahbuk a sat uh.
35. Huan, Abrona a kipan in a pawt ua, Ezion-geber ah giahbuk a sat uh.
36. Huan, Ezion-geber a kipan in a pawt ua, Zin gamdai ah giahbuk a sat uh (huai bel Kades leng a chi uh).
37. Huan, Kades a kipan in a pawt ua, Edom gam chin Jor tang ah giahbuk a sat uh.
38. Huan, siampu Aron TOUPA thupiak in Hor tang ah a hohtou a, Israel suante Aigupta gam a kipan a a hong pawt uh kum sawmli kum, kha nga na, ni khat ni in huai lai ah a sita hi.
39. Huchi in Aron bel Hor tang a a sih in kum za leh kum sawmnih leh kum thum ahi.
40. Huan, Kanan mi, Kanan gam a Simlam a om Arad kumpipa'n Israel suante a hong uh a za a.
41. Huan, Hor tang a kipan a pawt ua, Zalmon ah giahbuk a sat uh.
42. Huan, Zalmon a kipan a pawt ua, Punon ah giahbuk a sat ua.
43. Huan, Punon a kipan in a pawt ua, Obot ah giahbuk a sat uh.
44. Obot a kipan a pawt ua, Moab gam naih Iym-abarim ah giahbuk a sat uh.
45. Huan, Iym a kipan a pawt ua, Dibongad ah giahbuk a sat ua.
46. Huan, Dibongad a kipan a pawt ua, Almon-diblathaim ah giahbuk a sat uh.
47. Huan, Almon-diblathaim a kipan in a pawt ua, Nebo chin a Abarim tang khawng ah giahbuk a sat uh.
48. Huan, Abarim tang a kipan in a pawt ua, Jeriko zawn, Jordan gei Moab phai ah giahbuk a sat uh.
49. Jordan gei Bet-jesimot a kipan Abel-sitim pha in Moab phai ah giahbuk a sat uh.
50. Huan, Jeriko zawn Jordan gei Moab phai ah TOUPA'N Mosi a houpih a,
51. Israel tate houpih inla, a kiang uah Jordan lui na kan ua, Kanan gam na lut chiang un,
52. Huai gam a om tengteng na delhkhe vek ding ua, a suang milim tengteng uh na hihse vek ding ua, a milim sunte uh na hihse vek ding ua, a mun sangte uh na hihse vek ding uhi:
53. Huan, gam na luah ding ua, huai ah na om ding uh: na luah ding ua gam ka hon piak sa na hi uhi.
54. Huan, a chi dungzui bangzel in aisan in gam na luah ding ua, a innbul deuhte gam za deuh na pe ding ua, a innbul lou deuhte gam neu deuh na pe ding uh: aisan na a pukna peuhmah a tuam uh ahi ding a; na pi-le-pute uh chi paidan bangzel in na luah ding uhi.
55. Himahleh huai gam a omte na delhkhiak kei uleh na omsakte uh na mit ua ding in sutna zum leh na chi ua ding in ling bang ahi ding ua, na omna gam uah hon hih nuammoh ding uhi.Huan, hichi ahi ding a, a tung a hih ka tup bang na tunguah ka hih ding, chi in, chi in.
56. Huan, hichi ahi ding a, a tung a hih ka tup bang na tunguah ka hih ding, chi in, chi in.
