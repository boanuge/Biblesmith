Ruth (1/4)  →
1. Vaihawmmiten Vai a hawm lai un hichi ahi a, huai gamah kial a kia a. Huan, Bethlehem-juda a mi khat Moab gama om dingin amah leh a ji leh a tapate nih toh a hoh ua,
2. Huai mi min Elimelek ahi, a ji min Naomi ahi, a tapate nih min Malon leh Kilon, Bethlehem-juda a Ephrat mi ahi uh. Moab gamah a hoh ua, huai ah a om nilouh uh.
3. Huan, Elimelek, Naomi pasal a si a, amah leh a tapate nih kia a hita ua.
4. Huan, Moab gam numei jiin a nei ua, a khatpen min Orpa ahi a, a khatpen min Ruthi ahi: huaiah kum sawm hiam tak a om uhi.
5. Huan, Malon leh Kilon a si tuaktuak ua; huchiin huai numei tuh a tate nih leh a pasalin a sihsanta uhi.
6. Huchiin a moute toh Moab gam a kipan kiknawn dingin a kisa ua, TOUPAN nek ding piain a mite a vehdan khawng Moab gama kipan a najak jiakin.
7. Huchiin a omna akipan a moute nih toh a pawt ua; Juda gam a kiknawn dingin a paita uh.
8. Huan Naomi-in a moute nih kiangah, Na nu uh inlam tuak tuaka pai nawn dingin paita un; misi tung leh ka tunga na hih bang un TOUPAN na tunguah siamtak in hih hen.
9. TOUPAN na nih un pasalte in a omna na muh theihna ding un hon hehpih hen, a chi a. Huchiin a tawpa, huan, aw suah hialin a kapta uhi.
10. Huan, a kiangah, Paikei nung, na chite kianga na pai naah ka teltei zo ding uh, a chi uh.
11. Huan, Naomiin, Ka tanute aw, pai nawn jaw un; bangjiaka ka kianga hongpai tei mahmah ding na hi ua? Na pasal ding uh tapa ka pai nawn thei sin ahia?
12. Ka tanute aw, kisa unla pai un; pasal nei dingin lah ka upa lota ngala. Huan, lametna ka nei chi mahleng, tujan mahmahin leng pasal neiin tapate leng nei mahleng,
13. A pichin tan ua om ding na hi ding uam? Pasal louin na om nilouh sin ua hia? Hi tel lou e, ka tanute aw; nou jiakin ka lungkhamna a uang semsem hi, Toupa khut ka tung a tu eita ve, a chi a.
14. Huan, a aw uh suahin a kap nawn leuleu ua; huan Orpain a pi a tawpa; Ruthiin bel a len chinten hi.
15. Huan, aman, Ngaiin, na moupihnu a chite kiang leh a pathian kiang lamah a kik nawnta; nang leng na moupihnu delh inla, pai nawnta in aw, achia a.
16. Huan, Ruthiin, Nang khen ding leh nang jui loua kik nawn dingin hon khem kei teitei in; na paina peuhah ka pai dinga; na giahna peuhah ka giak dinga; na chite ka chite ahi ding ua, na Pathian ka Pathian ahi ding.
17. Na sihna peuhah ka si dinga, huaiah non vui sam ding ahi; nang leh kei zaw sihna kia lou thil dangin a honkhen sin leh TOUPAN a hihhihin honhih mai henla, huai sanga thupi zaw in leng, a chi a.
18. Huchiin a kianga pai a tum chiltel chih a theih in a daih santa hi.
19. Huchiin Bethlehem tan a pai khawmta uh. Huan, hichi ahia, Bethlehem a tun un a khuain a ling ua, numeiten, Hiai tua Naomi ahi maw? a chi ua.
20. Huan aman a kianguah, Naomi honchi kei un. Mara hon chi jaw un. Bangkimhihtheipan lah khasiathuai takin a honhihta ngala.
21. Buching takin ka pawta, TOUPAN a vuakin a honpi nawn hi: TOUPAN ka tungah a lungkimlouhna a hihlang a, Bangkimhihtheipan a hon hih gentheih dan thei gige napia bang dia Naomi honchi lailai na hi ua? A chi a.Huchiin Naomi a hongkika, a mounu Moab mi Ruthi toh Moab gam akipanin a hongkik uh ahi: barli-buh lak pat tung laiin Bethlehem a tung uhi.
22. Huchiin Naomi a hongkika, a mounu Moab mi Ruthi toh Moab gam akipanin a hongkik uh ahi: barli-buh lak pat tung laiin Bethlehem a tung uhi.
