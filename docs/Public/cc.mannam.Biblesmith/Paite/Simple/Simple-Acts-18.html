1. Huai thil zohin Athen khua akipan Paula a pawta, Korinth khua ah a hohta a.
2. Huan, a min Akuila, Juda khat a mu a, huai mi tuh Ponta gama piang ahi a, Klaudiain Juda tengteng Rome khua akipan pawt thu a piak jiakin a ji Priscilla toh Itali gama kipan hongtung phet ahi ua. Huan, Paula tuh huai mite kiangah a tungta hi,
3. huan, a nasep bang uh sem ahih jiakin amau a kikoppih a, a semkhawm uhi; a nasep uh tuh puanin bawl ahi.
4. Huan, Khawlni peuhin kikhopna in ah chitaktakin thu a gen jela, Judate leh Grikte thuzoh a tum jel hi.
5. Huan, Masidonia gam akipan Sila leh Timothi a hongsuk un, Paula tuh thugen kiain a omta hi, tua Jesu Kris tuh ahi chih Judate kiangah a hilhchian jel hi.
6. Himahleh, a kalh un a gensia ua, Paulain a puan a singa, a kiang uah, na sihna moh uh noumau pua un; kei jaw ka hula hi; tuban Jentelte kiangah ka pai ding, a chi a.
7. Huan, huai akipan a paia, a min Tita Justa mi khat in ah a lut a, huai min Pathian a be jel hi, a in leng kikhopna in toh a kizom ahi.
8. Huan, Krispa, kikhopna in heutuin a inkuanpihte tengteng toh Toupa a gingta a, Korinth khua a mi tampi inleng jain a gingta ua, baptisma a tangta uhi.
9. Huan, janin Toupan Paula kiangah mengmu bangin. Lau kenla, dai louin thu gen jel in;
10. Kei na kiangah ka om a kei hiam, sihna dingin kuamahin na tungah khut a kha kei ding uhi; hiai khua ah mi tampi ka nein gala, a chi a.
11. Huan, Paula huaiah a lak uah Pathian thu hilhin kum khat leh kha guk a omta hi.
12. Huan, Gallio tuh Akaia gamukpa a hih lain Judaten kituakin Paula a sual ua, vaihawmna tutphah maah a pi ua;
13. Huai mi phiangsanin dan lou bangin a Pathian be dingin mi a khempuk ahi, a chi ua.
14. Huan, Paulain a dawn dek leh, Gallioin, Judate kiangah, Judate aw, hiai khawng thil diklou thu hiam, thil hihkhelh thu hiam bek hileh, pel louin kon awlmoh dinga;
15. himahleh, thute, minte, noumau dante tungthu lel ahihleh jaw moumau kingaihtuah jaw un; ken jaw huchibang thu ngaihtuah ka ut kei, a chi a.
16. Huan, vaihawm tutphah akipanin amau a delhkheta hi.
17. Huan, Grik mi tengtengin kikhopnain heutu Sosotheini a man ua, vaihawm tutphah maah a vua ua. Gallioin huai a thilhih uh khawk a sa kei hi.
18. Huan, huai zohin Paula ni sawttak a tam nilouha; huai zohin unaute kamphain a khena, huai akipan Suria gam lam ah longin a paita, a kiangah Priscilla leh Akuilla leng a pai sam uhi, Paulain thuchiam a neih jiakin Kenkriai khua ah sam a met phot a.
19. Huan, Ephesa a tung ua, huaiah amau a nuseta; amahbel kikhopna in ah a lut a, Judate kiangah chitaktakin thu a gen tadih hi.
20. Huan, sawt deuh tam dingin a khou ua, himahleh a kiawi kei,
21. Pathianin a phal leh na kiang uah ka hong nawn ding, chiin, kampha in a khena, Ephesa khua akipan longin a paita.
22. Huan, Kaisaria khua ah a hongkaia, Jerusalem khua ah a hohtou a, saptuamte chibai a buka; huai khitin Antiok khua ah a hohsukta hi.
23. Huan, huaia sawt ngial a om nungin a pai nawna, nungjuite tengteng hihkipin Galatia gam leh Phrigia gam a tawnsuak vek hi.
24. Huan, Juda khat, a min Apollo, Aleksandria khua a piang, mi piltak, Ephesa khua ah a hohsuka; aman laisiangthou a nak theih mahmah a.
25. Huai mi tuh Toupa Lampi thu zilsa ahia, lungsima phatuamngaitak a hih jiakin Jesu a hilh jel hi, Johan baptisma thu kia a thei lel a.
26. Huan, kikhopna in ah hangtakin thu a gen panta a. Huchiin Priskilla leh Akuilain a thugen a jak un, a in uah a kiangah Pathian Lampi thu kichian semin a hilhchianta uhi.
27. Huan, aman Akai agama paikai a tup takin unauten a hasuan ua, amah a nakipahpihna ding un nungjuite kiangah lai a gelhsak uhi; huan, a tunin, Pathian hehpihna jiaka gingtate nakpitakin a panpih a;vantang theihin Judate nakpitakin a seldonga, laisiangthou zangin tua Jesu Kris tuh ahi chih a theisak hi.
28. vantang theihin Judate nakpitakin a seldonga, laisiangthou zangin tua Jesu Kris tuh ahi chih a theisak hi.
