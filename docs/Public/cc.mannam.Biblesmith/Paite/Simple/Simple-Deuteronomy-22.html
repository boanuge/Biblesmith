1. Na unaute uh bawngtal hiam, a belam uh hiam mang na muh un na daihsan maimah ding uh ahi keia, na unaute inah na pi sak nawn ngeingei ding uh ahi.
2. Huan, na unaute uh in kiang naia om a hih kei ua, a neimi na theih louh uh hiam a hihleh, na in lam uah na pi tou ding ua, na kiang uah a om nilouh dinga, na unau un a zon hunhunin na nape nawn ding uhi.
3. A sabengtung uh leng huchimahbangin na hih ding uhi, huchimahbangin a puansilh te uh leng na hih ding uhi, huchimahbangin na unau thil mansak na muhpeuhmah uh na hih ding uh ahi, na daih beisan mawkmawk ding uh ahi kei.
4. Na unaute uh sabengtung hiam, bawngtal hiam, lampia puk na muh un na daihsan maimah ding uh ahi keia, na phot pih ngeingei ding uh ahi.
5. Numeiin pasal van a silh ding ahi keia, pasalin numei puansilh a silh ding leng ahi sam sam kei; kuapeuh huchibang hih jaw TOUPA na Pathian uh theihin mi kihhuai ahi.
6. Lampia vasa bu, singah hiam, leiah hiam, a nou hiam, a tui hiam om, a nou hiam a tui hiam, a piin a op lai na muh uleh a nou a pitoh na lak ding uh ahi keia:
7. A pi khah ngeingei ding ahi, a nou bel na la thei ding uh: huchiin nou adingin a hoih dinga, na damsawt ding uhi.
8. In khat na lam ua huaia mi a kiak ua na in uh sisan suahna in a hih louhna dingin na intung uh na um ding uh ahi.
9. Huan, na grep huan ua thil chi nih na chin ding uh ahi kei; huchilouinjaw na chi tuh uh leh na grep huan ua thil tung tengteng TOUPA tuam ahi mai ding hi.
10. Bawngtal leh sabengtung lei na let khawm sak ding uh ahi kei.
11. Samul leh pat gat khawm puan na silh ding ahi kei.
12. Na kituamna puan uh a gei li ah mongkhih na bawl ding ahi.
13. Min ji a neiha, a kithuahpiha, a muhdah nawn thepthupa,
14. Hiai numei ka kitenpiha, ka kithuahpiha, nungak siangthou a hihna ka mu keia, chia zahlak taka a bawla, a min a hihsiat leh,
15. Nungak nu leh pan kulhkongpiah huai kho upate kiangah nungak siangthou a hihna a hohhihlang ding uh;
16. Huan, nungak pan upate kiangah, Hiai mi ka tanu ka nei saka, a ho nawn thepthup ngala;
17. Huan, ngai un, Na tanu nungak singthou a hihna ka mu kei chiin zahlak takin a bawla, himahleh hiaite ka tanu nungak siangthou a hihna ahi hi, a chi ding hi. Huchiin huai kho upate maah puan a phah ding uh.
18. Huan, huai kho upaten mi a gawt ding ua;
19. Israel nungak siangthou a suk minsiat jiakin dangka sekel ja a liau sak ding ua, nungak pa a pe ding uhi, amah ji ahi dinga, a damsungin a khen theikei ding.
20. Himahleh huai mi thu a na dika, nungak nungak siangthou a hihna a lat keileh:
21. Nungak a pain kongpiah a pi khe ding ua a omna khuaa miten suangin a denglum ding uh, a pa ina kijuaka Israelte laka thil gilou haihuai mahmah a hih jiakin; huchibangin na lak ua kipan, huai thil hoih lou na hihmang ding uhi.
22. Min pasal nei a luppih a muh uh leh a nih un a sih ding uh ahi, a lup pih pasal leh numei; huchibangin Israel laka kipan, huai thil hoihlou na hihmang ding uhi.
23. Nungak siangthou, mi zikham, min khuaa a muh ua, a luppih leh;
24. A nih un kho kulh kongpiah na pi khe ding ua, suangin na denglum ding uhi; numei pen, khuaa om ngala, a kikou louh jiakin; huan pasal pen, a insakinkhang ji ahihminsiat jiakin; huchibangin na lak ua kipan, huai thil hoihlou na hihmang ding uh ahi.
25. Himahleh min gamnuaiah nungak, mi kham a muha, a chih teiteia a luppih leh a luppih pasal kia hihlup ding ahi:
26. A nungak pen bangmah a chih ding uh ahi kei, nungak tungah jaw sihna khop khelhna a om kei; hichibang, mi a insakinkhang suala, that bang ahia:
27. Gamlakah a vatuak khaa, mi kham nungak a kikou a, panpih ding mi lah a om ngal kei ua.
28. Min nungak siangthou kham louh a muha, a mata, a luppih a muh uleh;
29. A luppih pasalin nungak pa dangka sekel sawmnga a pe ding ahi, huan, a mah ji ahi ding, a hihminsiat tak jiakin; a damsungin a khen theikei ding hi.Min a pa ji a kitenpih ding ahi keia, a pa nik a lem sak ding ahi kei hi.
30. Min a pa ji a kitenpih ding ahi keia, a pa nik a lem sak ding ahi kei hi.
