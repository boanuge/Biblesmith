1. Huan, huai nung in hichi ahi a David in Philistia mite a sual a, a zou a: huchi in David in Philistia mite thu neihna bulpi pen a laksak hi.
2. Huan, Moab mite a sual a, lei ah a lumsak a, tehna khau in a teh a; vual nih bel hihlup ding in a teh a, vual khat bel a tawn a hing a hawi ding in a teh a. Huchi in Moab mite bel David sikhate a hong hita ua, siah a pia uhi.
3. David in Lui a a gam la nawn ding a a hoh kawm in Zoba kumpipa Rehob tapa Hadadezer leng a sual a.
4. Huchi in David in a sakol tung tuangmi sangkhat leh za sagih leh sepaih singnih a matsak a: huchi in kangtalai kai sakol tengteng a tha uh a tah sak a, kangtalai za kai ding bel a hawi hi.
5. Huan, Damaska a Suria mite Zoba kumpipa Hadadezer hon ding a kuante David in Suria mite lak a mi singnih leh sangnih a that a.
6. Huchi in David in Suria gam a Damaska ah sepaih a koih a; huan Suria mite David sikhate a honghita ua, siah a liau uhi. Huan, TOUPAN David a hohna peuh a zousak zel hi.
7. Huan, David in Hadadezer pawlte dangka-eng lumte a laksak a, Jerusalem ah a paipih a.
8. Huan, Hadadezer khua Beta a kipan, Berothai a kipan in David in dak tampi a la hi.
9. Huan, Hamath kumpipa Toi in Hadadezer sepaih tengteng David in a zou chih a zak in,
10. Hadadezer a sual a a zoh ziak a chibai buk ding leh vualzawl ding in Toi in a tapa Joram David kiang ah a sawl a: Hadadezer in lah Toi a nakidoupih khin ngal a. Huchi in Joram in dangka belte, dangka-eng belte leh dal belte a hon la hi:
11. Huaite gam dang a zoh tengteng a dangka chi leh dangka-eng a latte toh kumpipa David in TOUPA kiang ah a lan lai hi:
12. Suriate a bang, Moabte a bang, Amon suante a bang, Philistiate a bang, Amalekte a bang, Zoba kumpipa Rehob tapa Hadadezer a kipan a a gallakte.
13. Huan, David Chi guam ah, Suria mi singkhat leh sanggiat va sual pailam a min a na thang taa.
14. Huan, Edom ah sepaih a koih a; Edom gam mun chih ah sepaih a koih ngennguan a, huchi in Edom mi tengteng David sikha a hong hita uh. Huchi in TOUPAN David bel a paina peuh ah a zousak zel hi.
15. Huchi in David in Israel mi tengteng tungah vai a hawm a; huan, David in a mite tengteng tungah diktat tak in vai a hawm hi.
16. Huan, Zerui tapa Joab sepaih heutu pen ahi a; huan, Ahilud tapa Jehosaphat bel tanchin gelhpa ahi a;
17. Huan, Ahitub tapa Zadok leh Abiathar tapa Ahimelek bel siampu ahi uh; huan, Serai bel laigelhmi ahi;Huan, Jehoiada tapa Benai bel Keret mi leh Pelet mite heutu ahi; huan, David tapate siampu ahi uh.
18. Huan, Jehoiada tapa Benai bel Keret mi leh Pelet mite heutu ahi; huan, David tapate siampu ahi uh.
