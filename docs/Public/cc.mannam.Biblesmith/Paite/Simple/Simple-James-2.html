1. Ka unaute aw, I Toupa Jesu Kris, Thupina Toupa gingta kawmin, mi khentuam neikei un,
2. Na kikhopna in uah mi dangkaeng zungbuh bun, puan hoih tak silh honglut leh, huan, mi genthei puan se tak silh honglut samta leh;
3. huchiin, puan hoih tak silhmi tuh na limsak ua, a kiangah, Nang hiai mun hoih ah tuin, na chi ua, mi genthei kiang abel, Nang jaw huaiah ding inla, a hih keileh, ka khe ngakna nuaiah tuin, na chih uleh,
4. na lungsim uah khentuam neia ngaihtuahna gilou pu, gense mite na hong hi kei ding ua hia?
5. Ka unau deihtakte aw, Pathianin hiai khovela gentheite ginnaa hau ding leh, amah itte kianga achiam gam luah ding khawng bangin a chi kei ahia?
6. Nou bel migentheite bang na zahlaksak zota ua. Mihaute hilou uhia nou honnuaisiah? Mihaute hilou uhia vaihawmna ina nou honkailut?
7. Amaute hilou uhia min manpha na put uh gensete?
8. Himahleh laisiangthouin, Nangmah na kiit bangin na vengte na it ding ahi, chih kumpi dan na zop uleh, na hih hoih uh;
9. Himahleh mi khentuam na neih uleh, na hih khial uhi, danin talekte na hih uh a honthei sak ngala.
10. Kuapeuhin dan jui kim mahleh, munkhata a hihkhelh ngalin tuh, a vekah siamlouhtangin a om khinta ahi.
11. Angkawm ken, chipan, Tualthat ken, a chi laingala. Huchiin angkawm keimah lechin tual na thah ngalin tuh, dana talekpa na hita himhim ahi.
12. Huaijiakin noplenna dana vaihawm sakdingte bangin thugen unla, thil hih belap un.
13. Mi tunga chitna neilou tuh chitna pang louin vaihawm a zou nak ahi.
14. Ka unauate aw, min ginna ka nei chimahleh, thil hih aneihkei leh, bang a phatuam dia? Huchibang ginna in amah a hondam thei ding hia?
15. Unaupa hiam, unau nu hiam, vuaktanga om in a niteng annek tasam le uh,
16. Huchiin nalak ua kuahiam in a kiang ah, khamuang takin pai unla, lum tak leh vahtak in awm un chi unla, asa a ding a thil kiphamawh pe tuankei le uchin bang a phatuam dia?
17. Huchibangmahin ginna leng thilhih neiloua amah kiaa a om leh a si ahi.
18. Ahi, mi kuahiamin, Nang ginna na neia, ken thilhih ka nei ahi; na ginna tuh na thilhih panglouin hon ensak in huan ken ka thilhihin ka ginna ka hon ensak ding,
19. Pathian tuh khat ahi, na gingta maw? Na hih hoih e: dawiten leng a gingta ua, a ling zel uhi, a chi ding.
20. Mi thulimlou aw, thilhih panglou a ginna lel bangmah ahi kei chih na thei nuam hia?
21. I pu Abrahamin a tapa Isaak maitam tung a alat laiin thilhih ziaka siamtang ahika hia?
22. Ginna in a thilhih toh a sem khawm khawma, huan, thilhih ziakin ginna uh akimta chih I thei hi.
23. Huan, Abrahamin Pathian a gingtaa, huai ginna tuh a diktatna a sim ahi, chih Laisiangthou hihkim in a om a; huchiin mah tuh Pathian lawm chih in a om hi.
24. Ginna ziak maimah hilou in, thilhih ziak zaw in, min siam a tang jel uh chih natheita uhi.
25. Huchibangmah in, Rahab kizuaknun leng sawltakte a nakipahpih a lam tuama a pai sak laiin, thilhih ziakin siam a tang ahi kei maw?Kha panglou in sa tuh si ahi, huchibangmahin thilhihte panglou in ginna leng si ahi.
26. Kha panglou in sa tuh si ahi, huchibangmahin thilhihte panglou in ginna leng si ahi.
