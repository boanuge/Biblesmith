1. Huan, Hichi ahi a, a ni giat ni in Mosi in Aron leh a tapate leh Israel upate a sam a:
2. Huan, Aron kiang ah, Khelh thoihna ding in bawngnou a pa leh a haltum thillat ding in belampa gensiatbei tuaktuak kai inla, TOUPA ma ah lan in.
3. Huan, Israel suante kiang ah, Khelh thoihna ding in kelpa, huan haltum thillat ding in bawngnou leh belamnou kum khat a upa tuaktuak gensiatbei chiat.
4. Leh bawngpa leh belampa TOUPA ma a lepna thillat a lat ding in hon kai unla; huan, sathau a mek tangbuang thillat toh: tuni in na kiang uah TOUPA a hong lang sin ahi, chi in hilh in, a chi a.
5. Huan, Mosi in thu a piak bang in kihoupihna puan inn kong ah a hon kai ua; huan, mi tengteng in a va naih ua, TOUPA ma ah a va ding uh.
6. Huan, Mosi in, Hiai ahi na hih ding ua TOUPA'N thu a hon piak: huchi in TOUPA thupina na lak uah a hong lang ding, a chi a.
7. Huan, Mosi in Aron kiang ah, Maitam hon naih inla, na khelh thoihnate, na haltum thillatte lan inla, nang a ding leh mipite a ding in kilemna bawl in: huan, mipite thillat pen lan inla, amau a ding in kilepna bawl in, TOUPA thupiak bang in, a chi a.
8. Huan, Aron in maitam a va naih a, amah a tuam a khelh thoihna bawngnou a gou a.
9. Huan, Aron tapate'n sisan a kiang ah a lan ua, sisan ah a khutzung a diah a, maitam kite ah a tat a, sisan pen maitam bul ah a sung a:
10. Khelh thoihna thaute, a kalte, a sin tuam pen lapte bel maitam tungah a hal a, TOUPA'N Mosi thu a piak bang in.
11. Huan, a sa leh a vun dainawl ah a hal.
12. Huan, haltum a thillat pen a gou a; Aron tapate'n sisan a pua ua, aman maitam kimvel in a theh.
13. Haltum thillat sabak khatkhat in a lu toh a pia ua, aman maitam ah a na hal a.
14. Huan, a sungkuate, a khete a sil a, maitam ah haltum thillat tungah a hal a.
15. Huan, mipite thillat pen a lan a, mipite khelh thoihna kel a kai a, a gou a, a ma a mah bang in khelh thoihna di'n a lan hi.
16. Huan, haltum thillat a lan a, a dan bangbang in a lan hi.
17. Huan, tangbuang thillat a lan a, huailai a a pham khat a la a, zingsang lam a haltum thillat chih louh leng maitam ah a hal lai hi.
18. Huan, mipte a tuam dia lepna thillat kithoihna bawngpa leh elampa leng a gou a; huan, Aron tapate'n a sisan a pia ua, maitam a kimvel in a theh hi;
19. Huan, bawngpa thau leh belampa thaute, a mei thau takte, a gilbohte, a kalte, a sintuam pen a lapte leng a pia ua:
20. A thau pen a awm tungah a koih ua, huan, aman a thau pen maitam ah a hal hi.
21. Huan, a awm leh a liang tak lam pen Aron in TOUPA ma ah vei thillat ding in a vei a; Mosi thupiak bang in.
22. Huan, Aron in mipi lam ah a ban a zak a, a vek un vual a zawl a; huan, khelh thoihna leh haltum thillat leh, lemna thillat a lat khit in a hon pai suk.
23. Huan, Mosi leh Aron kihoupihna puan inn ah a va lut ua, huan, a hong pawtkhe nawn ua, mipite a vualzawl ua; huan, TOUPA thupina mi tengteng ah a hong lang hi.Huan, TOUPA ma a kipan mei a hong pawtkhia a, haltum thillat leh thaute maitam ah a kangman ta a: huan, mipi tengteng in huai a muh un a kikou zuazua ua, a khupboh khekhup uhi.
24. Huan, TOUPA ma a kipan mei a hong pawtkhia a, haltum thillat leh thaute maitam ah a kangman ta a: huan, mipi tengteng in huai a muh un a kikou zuazua ua, a khupboh khekhup uhi.
