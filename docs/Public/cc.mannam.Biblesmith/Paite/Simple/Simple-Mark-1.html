Mark (1/16)  →
1. Pathian Tapa Jesu Kris Tanchin Hoih tun tungna
2. Jawlnei Isai laibu ah, Ngaidih, ka sawltak na maah ka sawla, aman na lampi a bawl ding.
3. Gamdaia mi kikou aw in, Toupa lamlian sial unla, a lampite hihtang un, a chi, chia gelh bangin.
4. Johan, gamdaia baptispa, thilhihkhelh ngaihdamna dinga kisikna baptis thu genin, a hongpawt a,
5. Huan, Judia gama mi tengteng leh Jerusalem khua a mi tengteng a pawt ua, a kiang lamah a pai ua, a khelhnate uh thupha tawiin Jordan luiah, a kiangah baptisma a tang chiat ua.
6. Huan, Johan tuh sangawngsau mul puanin a kivana, a kawng savun kawnggakin a gaka a an tuh khaukhup leh khuaiju ahi.
7. Huan, Ka nungah kei sanga thupijaw a hong lel ahi; kuna a khedap khau phel tak leng ka hi kei.
8. Ken tuiin nou ka honbaptis jel a; aman jaw Kha Siangthouin a honbaptis ding, chiin a gen a.
9. Huan, huai nite khawngin hichi a honghi a, Jesu Galili gama Nazaret khuaa kipanin a hongpaia, Jordan lui ah Johan baptisma tansakin a om.
10. Huaitak in; tui apat a pawtin, van kihonga, Kha vakhu banga a tunga hongkum a mu a,
11. Huan, Nang ka Tapa deihtak na hi a, na tungah ka kipak mahmah ahi, chiin, van a kipanin aw a hongpawt a.
12. Huan, Khain amah tuh gamdai ah a sawlpah ngala.
13. Huchiin, Setan khem kawmin gamdai ah ni sawmli a om a, gamsa lakah bang a om a; angelten a na a sep sak uh.
14. Huan, Johan mat a om nungin, Jesu, Pathian Tanchin Hoih genin, Galili gamah a honga.
15. A hun a hongtungta a, Pathian gam leng a hongnaita; kisik unla Tanchin Hoih gingta un, chiin.
16. Huan, Galili dil gei a nawk laiin Simon leh Simon unaupa Andru dil ah len paiin a mu a; nga manmi ahi him ua.
17. Huan, Jesun, a kiang uah, Honjui un, huchiin mihing man din ka honbawl ding, a chi a.
18. Huan, len khawng paisanin a jui pah ua.
19. Huan, saulou a pai leh Zebedai tapa Jakob leh a unau pa Johan a mu a, huaite tuh len bawl phain long mahah a om ua.
20. Huan, aman amau a sam pah a; huchiin, a pa uh Zebedai tuh kilohmite a goihte utoh longah a nusia ua, a juita uh.
21. Huan, Kapernaum khua ah a valut ua; huan, Khawlniin kikhopna in ah a lut a, thu a hilh a.
22. A thuhilh tuh lamdang a sa mahmah ua; laigelhmite banglou a, theihna nei hileh kilawm taka amau thu a hilh jiakin.
23. Huan, a kikhopna in uah dawi nin paimi khat ana om a; aman tuh,
24. Nazaret Jesu, kou leh nang banga hia i kizoolhna om? Kou honhimang dia hong na hi maw? Nang ka honthei a, Pathian Mi Siangthou na hi, chiin, a kikou khe pah ngala.
25. Huan, Jesun a taia, Dai inla, a sunga kipanin hongpawt in, a chi a.
26. Huan, dawi ninin amah engbawla ngaihtaka kikouin a sunga kipan a hongpawt ta a.
27. Huan, a vek un lamdang a sa mahmah ua, huchiin, Hiai bang ahia le? thuhilhna thak ahi ve! Dawi ninte nangawn thunei takin thu a pia a, a thu a mang jel uh, chiin, a kidong uh.
28. Huan, Galili gam kim tengtengah gam chih ah a tanchin a thang pah ta a.
29. Huan, kikhopna in akipan a pawt un, Jakob leh Johan toh Simon leh Andru in ah a lut pah ua.
30. Huan, Simon ji nu khosikin a chi a na a, a na lum a; huchiin, a tanchin tuh Jesu a hilh pah ua.
31. Huan, aman a khut a valen a, a phongta a; huchiin a khosik a damta a, a na uh a sepsakta.
32. Huan, nitaklama ni a tum nungin, damlou tengteng leh dawi matte a kinagah a honpi ua.
33. A khojang un kongkhak ah a hongom khawm kheukhou ua.
34. Huan, mi tampi natna chitenga nate a hihdama, dawi tampi a delhkhia a; dawiten amah a theih jiak un a pau uh a phal kei.
35. Huan, jingsangin, khovak ma tham in a thoua, a pawta, gamdai mun ah a vahoha, huaiah a thum a.
36. Huan, Simon leh a lawmten a nungah a jui ta ua.
37. huan, amah a vamuh un a kiangah, Mi tengtengin hongzong hi ve un, a chi ua.
38. Huan aman tuh a kiang uah, Mun dangah kho naite khawngah hohni, huailai khawng banga leng thu ka gen theihna dingin; huaimah lah ka hongpawt san ahi ngala, a chi a.
39. Huchiin thu hilha dawite delhkhe kawm jelin Galili gam tengteng a a kikhopnain uah te a lut jel hi.
40. Huan, a kiangah phak a hongpaia, Na ut leh non hihsiangthou thei, chiin, ama ah khukdinin, a ngen ngutngut a.
41. Huan, aman tuh a na hehpih mahmaha, a sawka, a khoiha, Ka ut hi, na siangthouta in, a chi a.
42. Huchiin, phaknain tuh amah a taisan pah ngala, hong siangthouta hi.
43. Huan, Jesun amah a kham bikbeka, a sawlkhe paha.
44. Kuamah kianga bangmah gen lou dingin pilvang in; a hihhangin siampi kiangah vakilak inla, na kisiansakna dingin Mosi in thil a sehte lan in, a theihchetna ding un, a chi a.Himahleh, amah tuh a pawta, huai thu nakpi taka tangkoupihin a than jakta a; huchiin Jesu tuh a langtangin khua ah a lut theita kei a, gamdai mun khawngah a om zota; huan, mun teng apatin a kiangah mite a hong jel uh.
45. Himahleh, amah tuh a pawta, huai thu nakpi taka tangkoupihin a than jakta a; huchiin Jesu tuh a langtangin khua ah a lut theita kei a, gamdai mun khawngah a om zota; huan, mun teng apatin a kiangah mite a hong jel uh.
