1. Huchiin pingpei akipanin TOUPAN job a dawnga, hichiin a chi a:
2. Theihna bei thutea thuhilh hihmial hiai kua ahia?
3. Kipasalsakin tuin na kawng gakin; Hondong ding ka hi, ka kianga na gen ding ahi.
4. Lei kingaknate ka koih laiin nang koiah na oma? Phuangin, theihsiamna nei na hih leh.
5. Huaia tehnate kua bawl ahi, thei na hih leh! ahihkeileh a tunga tehna khau kua zan ahia?
6. A nuai suangphumte kua phum ahia, ahihkeileh huai a ninga suang kua koih ahia;
7. Jingsang aksiten la a sak khawm ua, Pathian tapate tengteng kipah jiaka a kikou lai un?
8. Ahihkeileh kuan kongkhaktein tuipite a khak binga, gil akipan hongpawt khia banga, a hongpuahkhiak laiin.
9. Huaia puansilh meipi leh, khomial bikbek, huaia dinga tuamna puan ka bawla,
10. Huaia dinga ka thupiak bawla, kalhnate leh kongkhakte koiha,
11. Hiaitan na hongpai ding, himahleh a ban hilouin, ka chiha; huan hiailaiah a kiuangsak na tuikihotte lettangin a om ding uh, ka chih laiin?
12. Na nite a kipat nungsiah jingsang thu na piain, phalvak a mun thei dingin na bawlta hia;
13. Huchia lei tawpnate a mat theiha, huai akipana gilou sinkhiak a hihtheihna dingin?
14. Chiamtehna nuaia tungmanlei bangin mel a kikhenga; huan puansilh bangin thil tengteng ze neiin a dingkhia hi:
15. Huan gilou akipana a khovak uh lettang sak ahia, a ban liksang uh a kitan hi.
16. Tuipi nakte sungah na lut ta hia? Ahihkeileh tuithuk tawpna ah na paita hia?
17. Sihna kongpite na kiangah taklatin a omta hia? Ahihkeileh sihna limliap kongpite na muta hia?
18. Lei zatdan na thei siamte hia? Phuang in, huai na theih vek leh.
19. Khovak tenna zotna lampi koiah a oma, huan khomial omna mun koisan ahia;
20. Huchia huai a gamgi tana na tonpi theiha, huchia huai a in tana lampite na theihna dingin?
21. Thei mah niteh maw, huailaia piangta lah hi ding china? Hhuan na nite tamdan lah thupi mahmah hi dia!
22. Vuk koihkhawmna ah na lut ngeita hia, ahihkeileh gial koihkawm na muta hia.
23. Buaina hun adia ka sit, kidou leh kisat nia dia?
24. Bang lampi ah ahia khovak a kikhen a, ahihkeileh leitunga suahlam huih a dalhjak?
25. Kuan ahia tui tampi luanna dinga tuilam khuak, ahihkeileh kekkia adia lampi;
26. Mihing om louhna mun gam tunga vuah zusak dingin; mihing om louhna, gamdai tungah;
27. Lei kisia leh segawpsa lungkimsak ding; loupa hing nou khangkhesak ding?
28. Vuahin pa a nei hia? Ahihkeileh daitui takte kua suan ahia?
29. Kua gil akipana tuikhal hongpai ahia? Huan van daituikhal ngou, kua suahsak ahia?
30. Suang bangin tuite a taka, tuithuk maite a khal hi.
31. Siguk khaute na khihkhawm thei hia, ahihkeileh zuheisuktun khaute na phel thei hia?
32. Amah hunbi ah Aksilamvaka chiamtehnate na pikhe thei hia? Ahihkeileh Jangkhua leh tate na pi thei hia?
33. Van thusehte na thei hia? Huai lalna lei ah na dingkhesak thei diam?
34. Nang tui tampiin a khuh theihna dingin meipite kiangah na aw na suahkhe thei diam?
35. A pai theihna ding un khophiate na sawl khe thei hia?
36. Sunggil lamah pilna kuan a koiha? Ahihkeileh lungsim kiangah kuan theihsiamna a pia a?
37. Pilnain kuan meipite a sim thei dia? Ahihkeileh kuan van tuithawlte a bo khe thei dia.
38. A lompia leivui a luana, a sakte a kimat khawm ngiungeu lai un?
39. Humpinelkai adingin samat na betsak ding hia? Ahihkeileh humpinelkai noute gilkial na hihdam ding hia?
40. A kua ua a boh ua, buk dinga a bukna mun ua a om nilouh lai un?Vaak ading a an kuan a pia a? A noute Pathian kianga kikou a ann duh mana a vakvak lai un?
41. Vaak ading a an kuan a pia a? A noute Pathian kianga kikou a ann duh mana a vakvak lai un?
