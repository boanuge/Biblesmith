1. Saulain bel Toupa nungjuite a vau thu leh thah a tup thu a se khe nalailai hi.
2. Siampu Lianpen kiangah a hoha, Damaska khua a kikhopna in tengtenga mite kianga piak dingin lai a ngena, Lama mi kuapeuh a muh leh, numei leng, pasal leng, Jerusalem khuaa hensa a a honpi theihna dingin.
3. Huan, a pai lain, Damaska khua a tun kuanin, thakhatin van akipan vak a kimah a hongvak puapa.
4. Huan, lei ah a puka a kiangah, Saula, Saula, Bangachia nang honsawi na hia? Chiin, aw a ja a.
5. Huan, aman, Toupa, kua na hia? a chi a. Huan, Toupan, Jesu, na sawi ka hi;
6. himahleh, thou inla, khopi ah lut in, huchiin na hihding hilhin na om ding hi, a chi a.
7. Huan, a kianga paite pau theihloin a ding ua, aw jaw a zana ua, himahleh kuamah a mu kei uhi.
8. Huan, lei akipanin Saula a thou a; huan, a honghaka bangmah a mu theita keia; huchiin a khutin a kai ua, Damaska khua ah apilut uh hi.
9. Huan, ni thum kho mu louin a oma, bangmah a dawnin a ne kei hi.
10. Huan, Damaska khua ah a min Anania nungjui khat a om. Huan, Toupan mengmu bangin a kiangah, Anania, a chi a.
11. Huan, aman, En in, Toupa, hiaiah ka om, a chi a. Huan, Toupan a kiangah, Thou inla, kongzing Tang a chih uah hoh inla, Juda in ah, Tarsa khuaa mi, a min Suala, kankhia in;
12. ngaiin, a thum laitak ahi; a min Anania mi khat luta, a mit hihvak dingin a tungah khut koihin a muta hi, a chi a.
13. Huan, Ananiain, Toupa huai mi Jerusalem khua a na mi siangthoute tunga a tatsiat thu mi tampi kam ah ka ja a.
14. Huan, hiaiah leng na min lou peuhmah henna dingin siampu lalte kianga kipanin thu a mu a, a chi a, a dawng a.
15. Himahleh, Toupan a kiangah, Hoh mai in; Jentelte, kumpipate, Israel suante kianga ka min puaklutna dia ka bel-she ahi;
16. Ka min jiakin gimna bangchia thupi ahia a thuak ding a kiangah ka theisak ding ahi, a chi a.
17. Huan, Anania tuh avahoha, in ah a luta, huan, a tungah a khut a koiha, Unau Saula, na mit vakna ding leh, Kha Siangthou dima na om theihna dingin, Toupa, na hongna lampia na kianga kilak Jesuin, a honsawl ahi, a chi a.
18. Huan, thakhatin a mit akipan lip bang a keta a, a mit a vak nawnta hi; huan, a thou a, baptisma a tangta hi.
19. Huan, an a ne a, a halhta hi. Huan, Damaska khuaa nungjui omte kiangah ni bangjah hiam khawng a oma.
20. Kikhopna in khawngah Jesu tuh Pathian Tapa a hihdan thu a tangkoupih pah ngala.
21. Huan, a thu zate tengtengin lamdang a sa mahmah ua, Hiai mi Jerusalem khua ahiai min loute sawi jelpa ahi ka hia? Huai mite siampu lalte kianga hensa a pi, a hongna pen ahi, a chi ua.
22. Saula bel a honghat diaka, huai mi tuh Kris ahi taktak chih a hilhchiana, Damaska khuaa Juda omte khawng gending beiin a koih hilhial hi.
23. Huan, ni sawttak nungin Saula hihlum dingin Judate a kihou ua;
24. Himahleh, aman a thutup uh a na theia. Huan, hihlum dingin kulh kong tengah a sun a janin a tang uh.
25. Himahleh, a nungzuiten amah a pi ua, janin kulh tohlet ah bawmin a khaikheta uhi.
26. Huan, Saula Jerusalem khua a tun takin nungjuite kithuahpih a tuma; amau bel avek un amah a kihta uhi, nungjui a hih sam a ging kei uh.
27. Barnabain amah a pi a, sawltakte kiangah a honpaipiha, lampia Toupa a muha, a houpihdan bang, Damaska khua a Jesu mina hangtaka thu a hilh dan bang leng a kiang uah a genta a.
28. Huchiin, Saula tuh Jerusalem khua ah a kiang uah lut jel pawt jelin a omta hi, Toupa minin hangtakin thu a gen jel a.
29. Huan Grik-Judate kiangah thu a gena, a sela; amau bel amah hihlup a tum uhi.
30. Huan, unauten huai a theih tak un, Kaisari khua ah a pi suk ua, Tarsa khua ah a sawl mangta uh.
31. Huchiin, saptuamte tuh Judia gam kimah, Galili gam kimah, Samari gam kimah a kip deuhdeuh ua, muangtakin a omta uhi; huan, Toupa laudan siamin, Kha Siangthou khamuanin, a om ua, a hongpung deuhdeuh uhi.
32. Huan, Peterin khokim a tot vialvial kawmin, Lidda khua a mi siangthou omte kiangah leng a hoh suk a.
33. Huaiah mi khat a min Ainisi a jawt jiaka kum giat lupna a lum nilouh a mu a.
34. Huan Peterin a kiangah, Ainisi aw, Jesu Krisin a honhihdam, thou inla, na awngphah jial in, a chi a. Huchiin, a thou pahta ngala.
35. Huan, Lidda khua leh Saron phaia omte tengtengin Ainisi a mu ua, Toupa lam a nga duam uhi.
36. Huan, Joppa khua ah a min Tabitha nungjui khat a oma, (Tabitha omdan tuh Dorka ahi); huai numeiin thilhih hoih leh thilpiak a hau mahmah hi.
37. Huan, huai lain, a chi a na a, a sita a; huan, a silsiang ua, indan tungnung ah a lumsak ua.
38. Huan, Lidda khua tuh Joppa khokiang ahihjiakin, nungjuiten Peter huaiah a om chih a ja ua, sawt hal louin ka kiang uah hongpai in, chia vathum dingin a kiangah mi nih a sawl ua.
39. Huan, Peter a thou a, a kiang uah a paita hi. Huan, a tun takin indan tungnung ah a pi ua; meithai tengteng dingin Dorka a kiang ua a om laia a puannak bawlte a puan bawlte lak kawmin a kap chiat ua.
40. Himahleh, Peterin a vek un a pawtsaka, a khukdina, a thumta hi; huan, luang lam a nga a, Tabitha, thou in, a chi a. Huchiin, a mit a honghaka, Peter a muh takin a tu touta a.
41. Huan, Peterin a khut a pia a, a kaithouta a; huan, mi siangthoute leh meithaite a sama, damin a peta hi.
42. Huan, huai tuh Joppa khua tengtengin a thei vek ua; mi tampiin Toupa a gingta uh.Huan, Joppa khuaah savun bawlpa Simon in ah ni tampi tak Peter a tam nilouh a.
43. Huan, Joppa khuaah savun bawlpa Simon in ah ni tampi tak Peter a tam nilouh a.
