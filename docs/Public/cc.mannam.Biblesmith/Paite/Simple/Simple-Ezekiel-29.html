1. Kum sawmna, kha sawmna, huai kha ni sawmnihna ah TOUPA thu ka kiang ah a hongtungta,
2. Mihing tapa, Aigupta kumpipa Pharo masuan inla, a tungah leh aigupta mitengteng tungah thu genin.
3. Gen inla, TOUPA PATHIANIN huchiin a chi ahi: Ngaiin, Aigupta kumpipa, Pharo luipite laiah olepi thupi tak, Ka luipi keia ahi, keimah ngei a dia kabawl, chipa ka hondou ahi.
4. Na haizekte ah ngakuaiin ka honman dinga; luipi a ngasate na lip dingin ka belsak dinga; luipi lai akipan nalip ah ngasate tengteng toh ka honbot khe ding.
5. Huan gamdai ah ka hong pai dinga, nang leh na luipia ngasa tengteng leng; gamlak ah na puk dia; na luang lawm a hikei dinga, vui na hi sam kei ding; gam sahangte lehtungleng vasate kiangah an dia honpe ding ka hi.
6. Israel inkote adia sialluang chiang lel na honghih jiakin Aigupta gamah teng tengtengin kei TOUPA ka hi chih a thei ding uh.
7. Khuta honlet ua, a liangzang uh na tansaka, na satkhia a; hon ngaiua, a kawngte uh kitana tanglouin na omsaka.
8. Huaijiakin TOUPA PATHIANIN hichin in achi: Ngaiin, natungah namsau kahontun dinga, na laka kipan mi leh sa ka phuk mang ding.
9. Huan Aigupta gam, gam segawp leh kho sia ahi mai dinga, kei TOUPA kahi chih a thei dinguh. Luipi keia ahi a, ka bawl ahi a, na chih jiakin.
10. Huaijiakin ka hon dou ahi, na luipi leng: Seveneh kulh akipanin Ethiopia gamgi phain Aigupta gam bel gam segawp leh kho siain ka bawl ding.
11. Mihing khe in asik ngei kei dinga, sate leng a pai ngei sam kei ding ua; kum sawmli sung in luahin a om sam kei ding hi.
12. Huan Aigupta gam segawpte lakah khatin ka bawl dinga, a khopite ka khopi siate lakah Kum sawmli a kigawp ding: Aigupa mite nam te lakah ka dalh sak dinga, gam chih a ka dalh sak ding.
13. TOUPA PATHIANIN in hichiin a chi ngala; Kum sawmli nungin a dalhna mite ua kipan Aigupta mite ka lakhawm nawn ding:
14. Aigupta mite omdan ka hei dinga, a pianna gam uh, Pathros gamah ka kiksak ding; huan huailaiah vuallel takin a lal ding uh.
15. Lalna gam vuallel pen ahi ding, namte lakah leng a kilalsak nawn kei dinga; Namdang tunga thu a neih nawn louhna dingun amau ka neusak sin ahi.
16. Amau lam nga peuhmah in a thulimlouhna uh a theikhiak pih sek jiakin Israelte muanpi ahi nawn ta kei ding uh; huan TOUPA PATHIAN kahi chih a thei ding uh, a chi, chi in, chiin.
17. Huan, huchi ahi a, kum sawmnihleh kum sagihna, kha khatna, huai kha nikhat niin TOUPA thu ka kiangah a hongtunga,
18. Mihing tapa, Babulon kumpipa Nebukadnezzerin Tura tungah na thupitak tak sem ding in a sepaih hon achia, michih lu a giau loh a, mi chih liangzangin a dulhloh lai a; himahleh amah leh a sepaih honten a na sepna uah Tura akipan punna bangmah a mu tuan kei uh.
19. Huaijiakin TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin chi ahi: ngaiin, Aigupta gam Babulon kumpipa Nebukadnezzer kiangah ka pe sina, a thil hauh ziazuate a la mang dinga, alok dinga, a gallak sak ding; huai tuh a sepaih honte loh ahi mai ding.
20. Kana a sep jiakun Aigupta gama loh ding uh ka pia ahi, TOUPA PATHIANIN a chi ahi.Huai ni chiang in Israel inkote dingin ki ka mengkeh sak dinga; alak uah na kam ka ka sak ding; huchiin TOUPA kahi chih thei ding uh, chiin.
21. Huai ni chiang in Israel inkote dingin ki ka mengkeh sak dinga; alak uah na kam ka ka sak ding; huchiin TOUPA kahi chih thei ding uh, chiin.
