1. Huchiin, unau siangthou, van sapna tangsamte aw, I thugina Sawltak leh Siampu Lian, Jesu tuh, ngaihtuah un;
2. Pathian inkuante tengteng lakah Mosi tuh a muanhuai bangmahin, amah tuh a seppa lakah amuanhuai ahi.
3. Insunga papen tuh ainkuante sanga apahtawi jawk bang ngei un, amah tuh Mosi sanga thupi jawkna sem mu dia kilawma seh a hita ngala.
4. Inkuan chihin papen kuahiam bek a nei chiat ngal ua; bangkim papen bel Pathian ahi.
5. Huan, huai nunga thugen nalai dingte theihsakna dingin, Mosi tuh Pathian inkuante tengteng laka nasempa injaw a muanhuai ngei hi;
6. Kris bel Pathian inkuante tunga Tapa-in a muanhuai ahi. A inkuante tuh eite I hi uhi, atawp tana I hansanna leh I lametna suanna kiptaka I vom hoih photphot leh.
7. Huchiin, Kha Siangthou gen bangin, Tuni a a aw na jak chiangun,
8. Gamdaia zeet na nia hel bang un, na lungtang uh khauh sak kei un;
9. Huailai ah tuh na pi leh pute un honjeet in hon khem ua, kum sawmli sunga ka thil hihte a mu ua;
10. Huchiin huai suan te tungah ka lung hita keia, a lungtangun a ngaihtuah khial gige ua, Kalampite leng a theikei uhi, ka chi a,
11. Ka thangpaihna aka kichiam bangin ka khawlna ah alut kei ding uh, chiin.
12. Unaute aw, pilvang un, huchi lou in jaw, Pathian hing tawpsan ding in na lak uah kuapeuh sungah ginlouhna lungtang gilou ahong om kha ding hi.
13. Tuniin achih lai un niteng in ki hasuan tuah jaw un, huchia nalak ua mi kuamah khelhna khemhat jiaka hihkhawla na om louhna ding un,
14. Kris neite ina hita ngal ua, i ginna pen kipat na tuh a tawptan a kiptaka I vom hoih phot leh,
15. Tuniin a aw nazak chiangun a hel lai bang un, na lungtang uh khauhsak kei un. a kichih laiteng.
16. Ahihleh, kuate ahi ua theinapi a helte? Aigupta akipana Mosi pi pawt tengteng hi lou uhia?
17. Huan, kuate tungah ahia, kum sawmli sung a alunghihlouh? Hihkhialte a luang uh gamdaia puk tate tungah hilou hia?
18. Huan, thu mangloute kiangah kia loungal kua kiangah ahia a khawlna a lut lou dinga a kichiam?Huchiin, ginlahna jiakin a lut theita kei uh chih I thei uhi.
19. Huchiin, ginlahna jiakin a lut theita kei uh chih I thei uhi.
