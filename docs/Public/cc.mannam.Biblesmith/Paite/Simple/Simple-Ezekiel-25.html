1. Huan TOUPA thu ka kiangah a hong tunga, ka kiangah,
2. Mihing tapa, Ammon tate ma suan inla, atungtang thu uh gen in.
3. Huan Ammon tate kiangah, TOUPA PATHIAN thu ngai khia unla, TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin achi ahi: ka mun siangthou hihnita a oma, Israel gam hih gawpa a oma, zuda inkote sala pi a omlai ua, Awi ing e: Numei: na na chihjiak in,
4. Ngai in, suahlam mite kiang aha hong tan ding unka hon pe sin ahi; nang ah buk akoih ding ua, nang ah tenna a bawl ding uh; na theigahte a ne ding u, na nawi tui a dawn ding uh.
5. Rabba khua sangawngsau in dingin ka bawl dia, Ammon tate mun ganhon kualna mun ding in ka bawl ding; huchiin TOUPA kahi chih na thei ding uh.
6. TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin achi ngala: Israel gam tunga nakhut na bet a, na khe na peka, lungsim teng tenga a siatna tunga na kipah jiakin.
7. Ngai in, na tungah ka khut ka lik dinga, namte kiangah gallak ding in ka honman sak sin ahi; mite a kipan kahon phuk dinga, huai gam a kipan in ka hon hih mang vengveng ding, ka honhihse dinga, huchiin TOUPA kahi chih na thei ding, chiin.
8. TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin achi ahi: Moab leh Seirin, Ngai in, Zuda inkote nam dang bang ahi uh, achih jiak un.
9. Moab pang khat, khopite lampang, gamgi zul a khopi, a gamthupi na pente, Beth-Zeshimit te, Baalmeon te, Kiriathaim te kahong dinga,
10. Suahlam tate tan dingin Ammon tate kape dinga, Ammon tate namte laka theih gige ahih nawnlouhna ding un.
11. Moab gam tungah thugel ka tangtung sak dinga, huchiin TOUPA kahi chih a thei ding uh, chiin.
12. TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin achi a; Edom in Zuda inkote tunga phu la in nakpitak in thil a nahihkhial ahi, amah thu kiaa a tung ua phu alak jiakin.
13. Huaijiakin TOUPA PATHIANIN hi chiin achi hi: Edom tungah ka khut kalik dinga, a lak ua kipan mi leh sate ka phuk mang vek ding; Teman a kipanin ka hih gawp dinga; Dedan phain namsau in asi ding uh.
14. Huan ka mi Israel te khut in ka phu lakna ka tungsak dinga, Edom tungah ka hehna bangzel leh ka thangpaihna bang zelin ahih ding ua, ka phulakna a na thei ding uh, chih TOUPA PATHIAN thupawt ahi.
15. TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin in achi ahi: Philistia miten phulaa, khantawn kimelmaknaa, mi hihman utna lungsim sia puaa phua lak jiak un.
16. TOUPA PATHIANIN hichiin achi ahi, ngai in, Philistia mite tungah ka khut kalik dinga, Kareth kho mite ka phuk vek dinga, tui gei a mi omsunte ka hihmang vek ding.Huan, heh mahmah in sal in a tunguah phu ka la dinga, huchia a tung ua ka phulakna ka tun sak hun chiang in TOUPA kahi chih a thei ta ding uh, achi a.
17. Huan, heh mahmah in sal in a tunguah phu ka la dinga, huchia a tung ua ka phulakna ka tun sak hun chiang in TOUPA kahi chih a thei ta ding uh, achi a.
