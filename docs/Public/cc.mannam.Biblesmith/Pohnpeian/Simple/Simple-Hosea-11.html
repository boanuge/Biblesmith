1. KAUN-O ketin mahsanih, “Ni ahnsou me Israel wia seri men, iei ahnsou me I poakohng; oh I likwerihodo sang nan Isip oh kahdaneki nei pwutak.
2. Ahpw uwen ei wia sahm men ong ih, iei uwen eh sohpeiweisangieier. Nei aramas akan meirongohng Paal; re pil isikohng dikedik en eni kan warpwohmwhu.
3. Ahpw ngehi me kaiahnehkihda mehn Israel kan alu. I tupwengeirailda, ahpw re sohte pohnese ei kin apwahpwalih irail.
4. I kaiahnehkiniraildohng rehi kadek oh limpoak. I pwekirailda oh pwoaleiraildi; I pil keipwekedi oh kamwenge irail.
5. “Ahpw re kahng sohpeidohng ie, ihme re pahn pwurkilahng Isip, oh Asiria pahn kaunirailda.
6. Mahwen laud pahn lelohng ar kahnimw kan oh kauwehla ar ewen kehl kan. Nei aramas akan ahpw pahn mwomwkihla arail idawehnla pein nsenarail kan.
7. Re nannantihongete ar sohpeisang ie. Re ahpw pahn sengiseng pwehki mehn wisik toutou me mi pohrail, sohte emen me pahn pwekadahsang pohrail.
8. “Mehn Israel kan, ia duwen ei pahn kak keseikumwailla? Ia duwen ei pahn kak likidmelieikumwailla? Ia duwe, I pahn kak kamwomwkumwailla, duwehte me I wiahiong Adma, de wiahiong kumwail soahng me I wiahiong Sepoim? Mohngiongiet sohte pahn mweidohng I en wia soahng wet! Ei poakohng kumwail me inenen laud.
9. I sohte pahn kalokei kumwail ni ei engieng, I sohte pahn pwurehng kamwomwala Israel. Pwe ngehi me Koht, kaidehn aramas, ngehi, Koht me sarawi, kin ieiang kumwail. I sohte pahn ketido rehmwail nan ei engieng.
10. “Nei aramas akan pahn idawehniehdo ni ei pahn ngiringir rasehng laion men ong ar imwintihti kan. Re pahn mwadangodo rehi sang palikapi.
11. Re pahn kohdo sang Isip, marahra duwehte menpihr, oh sang Asiria, marahra duwehte mwuroi. I pahn kapwureiraildo nan uhdakarail. Ngehi, KAUN-O, me mahmahsen.”
12. KAUN-O ketin mahsanih, “Mehn Israel kan kapilkiniepenehr likamw oh widing, oh mehn Suda nannantihlahte ar kahngohdiong ie, ar Koht loalopwoat oh sarawi.
