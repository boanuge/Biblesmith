1. Kapinga KAUN-O! Meid pai aramas me kin wauneki KAUN-O, me kin alehki eh peren sang ni eh kin kapwaiada sapwellime kosonned akan.
2. Nein aramas mwahu seri kan pahn ahneki manaman nan sahpwo; kadaudoke kan pahn ale kupwuramwahu.
3. Eh peneinei pahn paiamwahu oh kepwehpwe, oh e pahn paiamwahu kohkohlahte.
4. Marain kin dakerada rotorot ong aramas mwahu kan, ong irail kan me kalahngan, kadek, oh pwung.
5. Meid pai aramas me kin kadek ni eh kin kihwei lohn, me kin wia eh doadoahk en netinet ni mehlel.
6. Aramas mwahu sohte pahn sapwungala; e sohte pahn manoknokla kohkohlahte.
7. E sohte kin masak rong rohng suwed akan; eh pwoson me kehlail, oh e kin likih KAUN-O.
8. E sohte kin pwunod de masepwehk; e koapworpworki me e pahn kilang eh imwintihti kan ar pahn lohdi.
9. E kin sapan ong me paisuwed akan, oh eh kadek kin duweduwehte; e pahn ahneki manaman oh wahun.
10. Aramas suwed kan re kilang met oh lingeringerkihda; re kangkakilkin irail ar kailok oh irail kohkohla; arail koapworopwor sohralahr kohkohlahte.
