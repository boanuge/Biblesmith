1. LALPA'n in a sak loh chuan, A satute chuan an thawk rim thlawn mai a ni: LALPA'n khawpui a vawn that loh chuan, A vengtu chu a meng thlawn mai a ni.
2. Zing takah in tho a, tlai takah in chawl a, Thawhrimna chaw in ei thin hi, in tan a thlawn mai a ni: Hetiang hian a mi duh tak chu a muthilhtir thin a ni.
3. Ngai teh, fanaute hi LALPA laka kan rochan an ni a: Rila rah hi a lawmman mi pek a ni.
4. Vanglai fate chu Mi chak kuta thlal awmte ang hi an ni.A thal bawm faa khat nei mi chu an nihlawh e: Kulh kawngkaa an dote an biak lai phawin An mualpho lo ang.
5. A thal bawm faa khat nei mi chu an nihlawh e: Kulh kawngkaa an dote an biak lai phawin An mualpho lo ang.
