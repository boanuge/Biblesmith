1. Tin, Davida chuan Israel mite zinga mi thlan thiau singthum a khawm leh a.
2. Tin, Davida chu a tho a, Judai rama Baale khua atangin, cherubte chunga chuang sipaihote LALPA hming an puttir Pathian bawm chu la cho turin a hnena mi awm zawng zawngte nen chuan an thawk ta a.
3. Tin, Pathian bawm chu tawlailir tharah an dah a, tlanga Abinadaba in ata an la chhuak a: Abinadaba fapa Uza le Ahioa-te chuan tawlailir thar chu an khalh a.
4. tin, tlanga Abinadaba in atan chu Pathian bawm chawpin an la chhuak a; Ahioa chu chu Pathian bawm hma lamah chuan a kal a.
5. Tin, Davida leh Israel mi zawng zawngte chuan LALPA hmaah chuan thil ri thei chi hrang hrang far thing sim leh tingtang te, perhkhuang te, khuangte te, tawtawrawt bi nei te, darbenthek te n tum a.
6. Tin, Nakona hui zawl an thlen chuan Uza chuan Pathian bawm chu a han ban a, a chelh a; sebawng chu a pai a pai avangin.
7. Tin, LALPA thin chu Uza chungah chuan a ur ta thut a, a thiltih dik loh avang chuan Pathianin chutah chuan a tihlum a, Pathian bawm kiangah chuan a thi ta a.
8. Tin, LALPA chu Uza chnga a tuan tak avangin Davida chu a thinur a, chu mi hmun chu Perez-uza a vuah a, tun thleng hian a la ni ta reng a.
9. Tin, chu mi ni chuan Davida chuan LALPA chu ahlau a; "Engtin nge LALPA bawm chu k a hnen a lo thlen theih tak ang?" a ti a.
10. Chutichuan Davida chuan LALPA bawm chu Davida khuaa ama hnenah chuan a suan du ta lo a; Davida chuan Git mi Obed-edoma in lamah a dah ta zawk a.
11. Tin, LALPA bawm chu Git mi Obed-edoma inah chuan thla thum a a awm a; tichuan Obed-edoma leh a chhungte zawng zawng chu LALPA'n mal a sawm a.
12. Tin, lal Davida chu, "Pathian bawm avangin Obed-edoma chhungkua leh a in leh lo zawng zawng LALPA'n mal a sawm a ni," tiin an hrilh a. Tichuan Davida chuan Pathian bawm chu Obed-edoma in ata, Davida kuaah chuan lawm takin a zuk la ta a.
13. Tin, heti hi ani a, LALPA bawm zawntute chu pen ruk an kal veleh sebawngpa leh ran chawm thauin an inthawi a.
14. Tin, Davida chu a theihtawpin LALPA hmaa a lam a, Davida chuan puan zai sin puithiam kawr a ha a.
15. Chutichuan Davida leh Israel mi zawng zawngte chuan au dur dur chung leh tawtawrawt ham ruai ruai chungin LALPA bawm chu an la chho ta a.
16. Tin, heti hi ani a, LALPA bawm chu Davida khuaa a luh lai chuan Saula fanu Mikali chuan tukverh atngin a lo thlir a, LALPA hmaa Davida zuang leh lam chu a han hmu a, a hmusit rilru ta a.
17. Tichuan LALPA bawm chu an la lut a, a dahna tura Davidana zar, puan in chhunga a hmun tura chuan an dah ta a: tin, Davida chuan halral thilhlan te leh remna thilhlante LALPA hmaah chuan a hlan a.
18. TinDavidan halral thilhlan leh remna thilhlante chu a hlan zawh chuan sipaihote LALPA hmingin mipuite chu mal a sawm a.
19. Tin, mipui zawng zawng, Israel pum puite chu ahmei apain mi tin, chhang hlawm khat leh sa hrang khat leh grep rah pho ro bawr khat a sem theuh a.
20. Chutichuan mi zawng zawng mahni in lam theuhvah an haw ta a. Chutichuan Davida chua ac hhungte malsawm turin a haw leh ta a. Tin, Saula fanu Mikali chu Davida hmuak turin a lo chhuak a, vawiina Israel lal chu a va ropui ve le, mi ho tak zah nachang hre lo saruaka awm ang maiin vawiinah a chhiahhlawh nulate mithmuha saruakin a awm kha le!" a lo ti a.
21. Tin, Davida chuan Mikali hnenah chuan, "I pa leh a chhungte zwng zwng aiai mi thleng a, LALPA mi, Israelte chunga lala mi ruattu LALPA hmaah a sin; LALPA hmaah chuan lam awm reng ka ni alawm,
22. Heng ai mah hian ka la zahpuiawm ang, keimah pawh ka la insit cheu ang; amaherwhchu chug chhiahhlaw nula i sawite chawimawiin ka awm ang," a ti a.Chutichuan Saula fanu Mikali chuan a thih thlengin fa a nei lo a.
23. Chutichuan Saula fanu Mikali chuan a thih thlengin fa a nei lo a.
