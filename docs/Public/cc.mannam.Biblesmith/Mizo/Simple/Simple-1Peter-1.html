1. Petera, Isua Krista tirhkoh chuan, Ponto ram te, Galatia ram te, Kappadokia ram te, Asia ram te, Bithunia ram tea Darhho awm,
2. awih tur leh Isua Krista thisen theh dawng tura Thlarau tihhran a, Pa Pathian hriat lawk anga thlante hnenah chuan: In chungah khawngaihna leh remna tihpunin awm rawh se.
3. Kan Lalpa Isua Krista Pathian leh Pa chu fakin awm rawh se. Ani chuan a khawngaihna nasa tak ang zelin, mitthi zing ata Isua Krista thawhlehna avangin beiseina nung hmu turin min hring leh a,
4. rochan chhe thei lo, bawlhhlawh kai lo, chuai thei lo, vana in tana khek chu hmuhna turin;
5. chhandamna, hun hnuhnunga lang tura peih sa chu hmu turin, rinna avangin Pathian thiltihtheihnaa ven chu in ni.
6. Chu miah chuan nasa takin in hlim a ni, tunah (a tul chuan) thlemna tinreng avangin lungngai rih mah u la;
7. in rinna fiahna, rangkachak meia fiah hnu pawha boral thin aia hlu zawk chu, Isua Krista inlar hunah chuan fakna leh chawimawina leh ropuina tur a lo nih theihna'n.
8. Amah chu hmu ngai lo pawhin in hmangaih a, amah chu tunah hmu lo mah u la in ring a, lawm sawi hleih theih loh leh ropui takin in hlim em em thin;
9. in rin chhan - in thlarau chhandamna chu - hmuin.
10. In chunga khawngaihna lo thleng tur thu hril lawktu zawlneiten, Krista Thlarau anmahnia awm chuan Krista tuarna turte leh chu mi hnua ropuina awm turte chu a hriat lawktir laite khan,
11. eng hun nge ni, eng ang hun nge ni a hriattir thin tih chhui chhuah tumin, chu chhandamna chanchin chu an zawng a, ngun takin an chhui thin.
12. Chung thu chuan mahni rawng inbawl loin, nangmahni rawng an bawl zawk tih an hnenah hriattir a ni tawh, chung thu chu van atanga tirh chhuah Thlarau Thianghlim tanpuiin Chanchin Tha hrilhtu che u khan tunah an hrilh che u a; chung thil chu vantirhkohten bih an duh thin a ni.
13. Chuvangin, in rilru puan vengin ngaihven u la, Isua Krista inlar hunah chuan khawngaihna a rawn kensak tur che u chu beidawng loin beisei zel rawh u;
14. fa tihawihte angin, in hriat loh laia in hman lai chaknate anga awm loin;
15. a kotu che u a thianghlim angin nangni pawh in chetzia zawng zawngah lo thianghlim zawk rawh u;
16. "Nangni in thianghlim tur a ni, kei ka thianghlim si a," tih ziak a nih kha.
17. Tin, thlei bik nei loa mi tin hnathawh ang theuha ngaihtuah thintu chu Pa anga in lam chuan heta in cham chhung zawng tih dekin awm rawh u;
18. in thlahtute pek chhawn, in awm dan engkhaw ni lawilo lak ata chu, thil chhe thei, tangka leh rangkachaka tlan in ni lo a,
19. beram no hmelhem lo leh bawlhhlawh kai lo thisen anga Krista thisen hlu chuan tlan in ni zawk tih in hre si a;
20. ani chu leilung pian hmain ruat lawkin a awm ngei mai, nimahsela hun hnuhnungahte hian nangmahni avangin inlarin a awm a;
21. amah avangin mitthi zing ata a kai thotu leh a ropuina petu Pathian ringtute in ni a, in rinna leh in beiseina chu Pathianah a awm theihna'n.
22. Unaute hmangaih tak tak turin thutak zawma in thlarau in tihthianghlim tawh avangin thinlunga thahnem ngai takin inhmangaih tawn rawh u,
23. chi chhe thei lakah ni loin, Pathian thu nung reng leh awm rengah chuan chi chhe thei lo laka piang thar in ni zawk si a.
24. "Mi zawng zawng hlobet ang an ni a, an ropuina zawng zawng pawh hlobet par ang a ni. Hlobet chu a vuai a,Lalpa thu erawh chu chatuanin a awm reng a ni," tih a ni si a. Chu chu a ni Chanchin Tha thu an hrilh che u kha.
25. Lalpa thu erawh chu chatuanin a awm reng a ni," tih a ni si a. Chu chu a ni Chanchin Tha thu an hrilh che u kha.
