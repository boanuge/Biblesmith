1. Tin, Amphipoli khua leh Apollonia khua an kal pel a, Thessalonika khua an thleng ta a, chutah chuan Judate inkhawmna in a awm a;
2. tin, Paula a dan pangngaiin an hnenah a lut a, Chawlhni vawi thum Pathian Lehkha Thu atangin khun taka chhutpuiin,
3. Krista chu hrehawm tuar tur reng a nihzia te, mitthi zing ata pawh tho leh tur reng a nihzia te chu a hrilhfiah a, a tilang thin a; "He Isua, a thu ka hrilh che u hi, amah hi Krista chu a ni," a ti bawk thin a.
4. Tichuan an zinga thenkhat chu thlemthlukin an awm a,Paula leh Sila chu an pawl ta a; Pathian ngaihsak Grik mipui tam tak leh hmeichhe puipa tam takte nen.
5. Nimahsela Judaten an huphurh a; dawr hmuna mi sual thatchhia tute emaw an hnenah an hruai a, mipui an ko khawm a, khua zawng zawng chu an chawk buai a; Jasona in an rawn thawng a, vantlang hnena hruai chhuah tumin.
6. Tin, anni chu an hmu lo a, Jasona leh unau dang tute emaw khaw hotute hnenah an hnuk lut a, an au a, "Khawvel chawk buaitute kha hetah pawh an lo kal a,
7. Jasona hian a lo thleng vei a; hengho zawng zawng hi, 'A hming Isua, lal dang a awm,' tiin Kaisara thu kalhin an awm thin a ni," an ti a.
8. Tin, mipui leh khaw hotute chuan chung thu chu an han hriat chuan an buai ta a;
9. tin, Jasona leh mi dangte hnen ata chu an aia hren tur englo an la a, an chhuah ta a.
10. Tin, unaute chuan azan azanin Paula leh Sila chu Beroia khuaah an kaltir ta nghal a; chutah an va thlen chuan Judate inkhawmna inah an lut a.
11. Chuta mite chu Thessalonika khuaa mi ai chuan an lo tha zawk a, chutichuan rilru lawm takin thu chu an pawm a, an thusawi chu a dik leh dik loh hriat nan ni tin Pathian Lehkhaah an zawng thin a;
12. Chutichuan chungho chuan mi tam takin an ring ta a; Grik hmeichhe zahawm te leh mipate pawh duaiin an ring ta lo bawk a.
13. Nimahsela Thessalonika khuaa Judahote chuan Paulan Beroia khuaah Pathian thu a hril tih an hriat veleh chutah pawh chuan an va kal a, mipuite an fuih pawrh a, an tibuai leh ta a.
14. Chutichuan unauten tuifinriat thlenga kal turin Paula chu an tir chhuak ta nghal a. Sila leh Timothea erawh chu chutah chuan an la awm reng a.
15. Tin, Paula hruaitute chuan Atheni khaw thlengin an hruai a; tin, Sila leh Timothea chu theihtawpa a hnena kal thuaina tur thuchah sinin an haw ta a.
16. Tin, Paulan Atheni khuaah anni chu a lo nghah lai chuan, khua chu Pathian lema khatin a hmu a, a rilruin a helh ta a.
17. Chutichuan inkhawmna inah Judate leh Pathian ngaihsakte a hnial thin a, dawr hmunah pawh chuta lo awm apiangte chu ni tin a hnial bawk thin.
18. Tin, Epikuria mi leh Stoik mi zinga mi fing tute emaw pawhin ani chu an rawn hnial a. A thenin, "He mi kamtam hian eng nge sawi a tum?" an ti a. A thenin, (Isua thu leh Thawhlehna thu a hril avangin), "Ram dang pathiante thu hriltu a ni awm e," an ti a.
19. Tin, ani chu an chelh a, Areopagi-ah chuan an rawn hruai a, a hnenah, "He zirtirna thar i sawi thin hi min hriattir thei ang em?
20. Thil mak eng emaw kan bengahte i rawn thlen a ni; chutichuan he'ng thil awmzia hi hriat kan duh e," an ti a.
21. Atheni khuaa mi zawng zawng chu, an khuaa mikhual awmte pawh, an hman chang apiangin thu thar tawh phawt chu sawiin emaw, a ngaithla talin emaw an awm chawt thin reng a ni.
22. Tin, Paula chu, Areopagi lai takah chuan a ding a, a sawi a. "Nangni Atheni khuaa mite u, engkimah sakhaw ngaihsak tak mi ni ni tih ka hria e.
23. Ka len paha in chibai bukate ka en laiin, maicham pakhat, a chungah, 'Hriat Loh Pathian tan' tia ziak ka hmu a ni. Chutichuan hre loa chibai in buk thina kha, chu mia thu chu ka hrilh che u a nih hi.
24. Pathian, khawvel leh a chhunga thil zawng zawng siamtu chu lei leh van lal a nih avangin kuta sak inahte a awm lo a;
25. thil tlachham angin mihring kuta rawngbawlsakin a awm hek lo, amah ngei chu engkim hnena nunnate, thawknate leh thil zawng zawngte petu a ni zawk e.
26. Tin, lei chung zawng zawnga awm turin bul hmun khat vekin hnam tin mi a siam a, an tan hun bite a ruat a, an awmna rite pawh a khamsak a,
27. Pathian an zawn a, an dap an dap a, an hmuh mial theihna'n; nimahsela ani chuan min hlat lo theuh si a.
28. Amah avangin kan nung, kan che, kan awm a ni si a; nangmahni hlaphuahthiam tute emaw pawhin, 'Keini pawh a thlahte kan ni si,' tia an sawi ang khan.
29. Chutichuan Pathian thlahte kan nih ahnuin, Pathian pangti chu rangkachak te, tangka te, lung te mihring themthiam leh remruata chherte ang hi emaw tih, kan rin tur a ni lo e.
30. Chutichuan Pathianin kan hriat loh laite a haider thin a; nimahsela tunah hian ram tina mi zawng zawng sim tur thu a pe ta a ni, ni a tiam avangin;
31. chu mi niah chuan a mi ruata chu, leia mite fel takin a ngaihtuahtir ang; chu chu Pathianin amah chu mitthi zing ata kai thoin, mi zawng zawng hnenah a hriattir tawh a ni," tiin.
32. Tin, mitthi zing ata thawhlehna thu chu an hriat veleh a thenin an nuihsan a; a thenin, "He thu hi i hnenah kan la ngaithla leh ang e," an ti a.
33. Chutichuan Paula chu an zing ata a chhuak ta a.Nimahsela mi tute emaw a hnenah an tel ta tlat a, an ring ta a; chung zinga thenkhat chu Areopagi-a mi Dionusia leh hmeichhe pakhat a hming Damari leh an hnena mi dangte nen chuan an ni.
34. Nimahsela mi tute emaw a hnenah an tel ta tlat a, an ring ta a; chung zinga thenkhat chu Areopagi-a mi Dionusia leh hmeichhe pakhat a hming Damari leh an hnena mi dangte nen chuan an ni.
