1. Tin, chu mi hnuah chuan heti ani a, Davida chuan Pilistia mite chu a bei a, a hneh a: tichuan Davida chuan Philistia mite thu neihna bulpui chu a chhuhsak a,
2. Tin, Moab mite a bei leh a, leiah a muttir a, tehna hruiin a teh a; tlar hnihchu tihhlum turin a teh a, tlar khat chu a tluana nunga zuh turin a teh a. Tichuan Moab mite chu Davida chhiah khua an lo ni ta a, hhiahte an pe thin a.
3. Davida chuan Luia a ram la leh tura a kal pahin Zoba lal Rehoba fapa Hadadezera pawh a bei a.
4. Tichuan Davida chuan a sakawr chung chuang mi 1,700 leh sipai 20,000 a mansak a: tichuan Davida chuan tawlailir hnuk mi sakawr zawng zawng chu an tha a chhahsak a, tawlailir za hnuktu tur erawh chu a zuah a.
5. Tin, Damasaka-a Suria mite Zoba lal Hadadezera chan tura kalte chu Davida'n Suria mite zinga mi 22,000 a that a.
6. chutichuanDavida chuan Suria rama Damaska-ah chuan sipai a da a; tichuan Suria mite hu Davida chua Suria rama Damaska-ah chuan sipai a dah a; tichuan Suria mite chu Davida chhiah khua an lo ni ta a, chiahte an pe thin a. Tin, LALPA'n Davida chu a kalna apiangah a hnehtir zel a.
7. Tin, Davida chuan Hadadezera hote rangkachak phawte a laksak a, Jerusalemah a hawn a.
8. Tin, Hadadezera khua Beta atangte, Berothai atang tein Davida chuan dar tam tak a la a.
9. Tin, Hamath lal Toia chuan hadadezera sipai zawng zawng Davida'n a hneh tih a lo hria a.
10. Hadadezera chu a beia a hneh avanga chibai buk tur leh lawmpui turin Toia chuan a fapa Jorama chu Davida hnenah a tir a: Dadadezera chuan Toia hi do thin a lo ni si a. Tin, Jorama chuan tangkaraw bel te, rangkachak bel te, dr bel te a rawn keng a;
11. chung chu ram dang a ngam zawng zawnga mi tangkarua leh rangkachak a hlante nen chuan lal Davida'n LALPA hnenah a hlan bawk a:
12. Suria-ho ta te, Moab ho ta te, Amona thlahho ta te, Philistia ho ta te, Amlek-ho ta te, Zoba lal Rehoba fapa Hadadezer laka a rallakte chu.
13. Tin, Davida chu chi Ruamah, Suria mi mi 18,000 a bei haw chu a hming a lo thang ta a.
14. Tin awng chu Davida chhiah khua an lo ni ta a. Tichuan LALPA'n Davida chu a kalna apiangah ahnehtir zel a.
15. Chutichuan Davida chuan Israel mi zawng zawn chungah ro a rel ; tin, Davida chuan a mi zawn zawngte chungah chuan fel takin ro a rel a.
16. Tin, Zeruia fapa Joaba chu sipai hotu ber a ni a; tin, Ahiluda fapa Jehosaphata chu chanchin ziaktu a ni a;
17. tin, Ahituba fapa Zadoka leh Abiathara fapa Ahimelek chu puithiam an ni a; tin, Seraia chu ziaktu ani a;tin, Jehoia da fapa Benaia chu Keret mi leh Pelet mite hotu ai a; tin, Davida fapate chu puithiam an ni.
18. tin, Jehoia da fapa Benaia chu Keret mi leh Pelet mite hotu ai a; tin, Davida fapate chu puithiam an ni.
