1. Heng hnamte hi a ni LALPA'n Israelte Kanaan rama ral la bei ve ngai hauh lo fiahna tura a awmtirte chu,
2. chung Israela thlah chhuanho, tun hmaa engmah la hre hauh lote chuan an lo hriat nan leh ral beih dan zirtir tur atan chauh awmtir ani;
3. chungte chu Philistia-ho lal panga te, Kanaan mi zawng zawng te, Zindon-ho te, Lebanon tlanga awm Hiv-hote an ni, Baal-hermon tlang atanga hamath khaw luhna thlengin.
4. LALPA'n Mosia hmanga an thlahtute thu a lo pekte kha an pawm dawn leh dawn loh hriatna'n chungho chu Israelte fiahna tur an ni.
5. Tin, Israela thlahte chu Kanaan-ho te, Hit-ho te, Amor-ho te, Periz-ho te, Hiv-ho te, Jebus-hote zingah chuan an awm a;
6. tin, an fanute chu nupuiah an nei a, tin, an fanute chu an fapate an neihtir thung a, an pathiante rawng an bawl a.
7. Tin, Israela thlahte chuan LALPA mithmuhin thil sual an lo ti ta a, LALPA an Pathian chu an theihnghilh a, Baal-ho leh Astoreti rawng an bawl ta a.
8. Chutichuan LALPA thin chu Israelte chungah chuan a ur ta a, Mesopotamia ram lal Kusan-risathaima hnenah a hralh ta a; tichuan Israela thlahte chu Kusan-risathaima thu hnuaah kum riat
9. Tin, Israela thlahte chuan LALPA an auh veleh, LALPA'n Israela thlahte chhandamtu a rawn siamsak a, chu mi chuan a rawn chhandam ta a, chu chu Kaleba nau Kenaza fapa Othniela a ni.
10. Tin, LALPA thlarau chu a chungah a lo thleng a, tichuan Israelte ro a relsak ta a; ral bei turin a va thawk a, tin, LALPA'n Mesopotamia ram lal Kusan-risathaima chu a pe a: Kusan-risa
11. Tin, ram chu kum sawmli ralmuangin a awm a. Tin, Kenaza fapa Othniela chu a thi ta a.
12. Tin, Israela thlahte chu LALPA mithmuhin thil sual an lo ti leh ta a: chutichuan LALPA mithmuha thil an lo tihsual avangin LALPA'n Moab lal Eglona chu Israelte laka chakin a siam a.
13. Tin, ani chu Amona thlahte leh Amalek-hote a pun khawm a; tin, Israel-hote chu a va bei a, tum khawpui chu a luah ta a.
14. Tichuan Israela thlahte chu Moab lal Eglona thu hnuaiah kum sawm leh kum riat an awm a.
15. Nimahsela Israela thlahte chuan LALPA an han auh veleh LALPA'n Gera fapa Ehuda, Benjamin mi, mi veikhawr, chhandamtu a rawn siamsak a: tin Israela thlahte chuan Moab lal Eglona pek tur
16. Tin, Ehuda chuan khandaih hriam tawn, tawng chena seiin a chher a; chu chu a malpui ding lam puan hnuaiah a taivawn a.
17. Tin, Moab lal Eglona chu lawmman chu a han pe a: Eglona chu mi thau tak a ni a.
18. Tin, lawmman a han pek zawh veleh chuan, lawmman kengtute chu a kaltir leh ta a.
19. Ani erawh chu Gilgal khaw kianga lung chherna hmun atangin a let leh a, "I e lalpa, thuruk hrilh tur che ka nei e," a ti a. Tin, ani chuan,"Ngawi thiap rawh u," a ti a. Tichuan a bula dingho zawng zawngte chuan an chhuahsan ta vek a.
20. Tin, Ehuda chu a hnenah a va kal a; ani chu a thlan daihna pindanah a mah chauhin a lo thu a. Tin, Ehuda chuan, "Pathianin i hnena sawi tur thu mi chah e," a ti a. Tin, a thutphah ata chu a tho a.
21. Tin, Ehuda chuan a kut vei lamin a malpui dinglama khandaih chu a han phawrh a, a pumah a chiu ta vak mai a:
22. a fawng pawh chu a lut pil hmawk a; tin, a khandaih chu a thauin a hnawh ping ta tlat a, a puma khandaih chu a phawi si lo a; a hnungah a tlang a ni.
23. Chutichuan Ehuda chu hulhliapah a chhuak a, pindan kawngkharte chuan a kharhung a, a kalh ta tlat a.
24. Tin, chutia a chhuah tak hnu chuan, a chhiahhlawhte chu an lo kal a; tin, an han hmuh chuan ngai teh, pindan kawngkate chu a lo inkalh tlat mai a; "A thlandaihna pindanah a va inthiar fihlim rih a ni ang chu maw," an ti a.
25. Tin, zahthlak khawpin an nghak ta reng mai a: tin, ngai teh, pindan kawngkate chu a hawng ta hauh lo mai a; chutichuan chabi an la a, an han hawng a: tin ngai teh, an lalpa chu chhuatah chuan, thiin a lo lum der mai a.
26. Tin, Ehuda lah chu khatia an nghah reng chhung khan a lo tlan bo tawh a, lung chherna hmun kha a pel a,
27. Seira-ah a tlan a. Tin, heti hi a ni a, chu mi hmun a thlen chuan Ephraim tlang ramah chuan tawtawrawt a ham ta, tin, Israela thlahte chu tlang ram ata a hnenah an chhuk a, ani chuan an hma a hruai ta a.
28. Tin, ani chuan an hnenah, "Mi zui rawh u khai: LALPA'n in hmelma Moab-hote hi a pe tawh che u a ni," a ti a. Tichuan an zui thla a, Moab ram dep Jordan lui kaite chu an man a, tumah an kaitir phal lo.
29. Chu mi tum chuan Moab-ho singkhat lai, mi awmkhauh thiau leh mi chak huaisen tha thiau an that a; pakhat mah tlan chhuak an awm lo.
30. Chutichuan chu mi ni chuan Moab-hote chu Israel hote lakah an lo tawm ta a. Tin, ram chu kum sawmriat chhung ralmuangin a awm a.Tin, a hnuah chuan Anatha fapa Samgara a lo lang ve a, chu mi chuan Philistia-ho mi zaruk sebawng chhunna tiangin a chhun hlum a: amah bawk chuan Israel-hote chu a chhandam a ni.
31. Tin, a hnuah chuan Anatha fapa Samgara a lo lang ve a, chu mi chuan Philistia-ho mi zaruk sebawng chhunna tiangin a chhun hlum a: amah bawk chuan Israel-hote chu a chhandam a ni.
