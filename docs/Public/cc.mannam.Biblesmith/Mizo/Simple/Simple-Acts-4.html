1. Tin, Petera leh Johanan mite hnena thu an sawi lai chuan, puithiamho te, biak in hotu te, 1Saddukaiho te chu an hnenah an lo thleng phut a,
2. mi an zirtir leh Isua avnga mitthi zing ata thawhleh thu an hrilh avngin an lungni lo em em a.
3. Tin, an man a, tlai lam a nih avangin atuk thlengin tan inah an khung ta a.
4. Nimahsela thu ngaithlahote zinga tam takin an awih a; an tam lam pawh patling hlir pawh sangnga lai an tling a.
5. Tin, atukah chuan an hotute, upate, lehkhaziaktute,
6. Puithiam lalber Ana te, Kaiapha te, Johana te, Aleksandra te, Puithiam Lalber chi zawng zawngte pawh Jerusalem khuaah an awm kham a.
7. Tin, Petera leh Johana chu a laiah an dintir a, "Eng tihtheihnain nge, tu hmingin nge he'ng hi in tih?" an ti a, an zawt a.
8. Tichuan Petera chu Thlarau Thianghlimin a khat a, an hnenah, "Mi zawng zawng hotute u, upate u,
9. mi chak lo tihthat thu-ah, 'He mi hi eng vangin nge a lo dam tak?' tiin vawiinah hian min chhuahchhal chuan,
10. nangni zawng zawngin hre rawh u, Israel mi zawng zawng pawhin hre rawh se, Nazareth Isua Krista, kraws-a in khenbeh, Pathianin mitthi zing ata a kaihthawh leh kha, amaha hming ngeiin he mi hi hetah in hmaah damin a ding ta a nih hi.
11. Nangni in satute u, in lung paih, a kila lung tangkai ber lo ni ta kha amah hi a ni e.
12. Mi dang tuma hnenah chhandamna a awm lo; van hnuaia mihring sak zingah, min chhandam tur hming dang reng a awm lo," a ti a.
13. Tin, Petera leh Johana huaizia chu an hmuhin, lehkha thiam lo, mi mawl tak an ni tih an hriatin mak an ti hle a; Isua hnenah an awm tawh tih an hre ta a.
14. Tin, mi an tihdama chu an hnena ding an hmu a, engmah an sawisel thei si lo.
15. Chutichuan an rorelna ata an kal hrantir hnuin an inrawn a,
16. "Heng mite hi engtin nge kan tih ang? thilmak thang tak an ti tih Jerusalem khuaa awm zawng zawngin fiah takin an hriat tak hi; keini pawhin kan hnial teh si lo.
17. Nimahsela mi hnena a puan lehzual theih lohna'n, tun achinah tu hnenah mah he hminga thu an sawi lohna turin i vau ang u," an ti a.
18. Tichuan anni chu an ko a, Isua hminga thu sawi tawp loh tur leh zirtir tawp loh tur an ti tlat a.
19. Nimahsela Petera leh Johanan an hnenah, "Pathian mithmuhin Pathian thu zawm aiin nangni thu zawm a dik zawk emaw chu ngaihtuah rawh u.
20. Keini zawng thil kan hmuh leh kan hriatte kha sawi loin kan awm thei lo," an ti a.
21. Tin, anni chuan an vau lehzual a, an chhuah ta a, mipui avangin hremna remchang an hmu lo; an thiltih avang khan mi zawng zawngin Pathian an chawimawi si a.
22. Thilmaka an tihdama chu kum sawmli aia tam zawk mah a ni si a.
23. Tin, Petera leh Johana chu an chhuahin, anmahni pawlte hnenah an va kal a, puithiam lalte leh upaten an hnena an sawi apiang chu an hrilh vek a.
24. Chutichuan a ngaithlatute chuan arualin aw ring takin Pathian hnenah, "Lalpa, nang van te, lei te, tuifinriat te, a chhunga awmte zawng zawng Siamtu i ni;
25. nangin Thlarau Thianghlimin, min thlahtu i chhiahhlawh Davida kain, he'ng thu hi i sawi a ni: 'Jentailte engati nge an thinur kha? Hnam tinin engati nge thil sawt lo an ngaihtuah?
26. Khawvel lalten an ep huai huai a, hotute pawh an pungkhawm a, Lalpa leh a Krista chungah chuan,' tiin.
27. "He khuaah hian i Chhiahhlawh thianghlim i hriak thih Isua chungah khan, Heroda leh Pontia Pilata nen, Jentailte leh Israel mite nen an pungkhawm tak zet a ni,
28. i kut leh i rilruin lo thleng tura a ruat lawk apiang kha ti turin.
29. Tichuan, tunah Lalpa an mi vauziate hi ngai teh; i bawihte hi huai takin i thu hriltir la,
30. mite tidam turin nangin i kut phar ang che; i chhiahhlawh thianghlim Isua hming avangin chhinchhiahna leh thilmak takte tihtir bawk ang che," an ti a.
31. Tin, an tawngtai zawhin an awmkhawmna chu a lo nghing a, an zain Thlarau Thianghlimin an lo khat a, huai takin Pathian thu an hril ta a.
32. Tin, a ringtu mipuite chu rilru hmun khat leh ngaihtuah hmun khatin an awm a: an zinga pakhat mahin an sum neih, "Ka ta bik a ni," an ti lo; thil zawng zawng an intawm zawk thin.
33. Tin, tirhkohte chuan awihawm takin Lal Isua thawh leh thu an hriattir thin a; an zaa chungah khawngaihna nasa tak a awm a.
34. Tin, an zingah pawh tlachham tumah an awm lo; ramte, inte nei apiangin an hralh a, an thil hralhte chu a man an rawn keng a,
35. tirhkohte ke bulah an dah thin a; an chakkhai daih tawk theuhin an sem theuh thin a.
36. Tin, Josefa, tirhkohten Barnaba an tiha chu, (chu mi hming awmzia chu, "Thlamuanna Fa," tihna a ni a,) Levia chi, Kupra thliarkara mi a ni a,a lo neih chu hralhin a man a rawn keng a, tirhkohte ke bulah a dah ta a.
37. a lo neih chu hralhin a man a rawn keng a, tirhkohte ke bulah a dah ta a.
