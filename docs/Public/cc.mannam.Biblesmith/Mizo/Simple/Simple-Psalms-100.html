1. Ram zawng zawngte u, LALPA chawimawiin hlim takin au rawh u.
2. Lawm chungin LALPA rawng bawl u la: Hla sa chungin a hmaah lo kal rawh u.
3. LALPA chu Pathian ani tih hre rawh u: Min siamtu chu amah chu ani a, ama ta kan ni. A mit leh a ran tlatna hmuna beramhote chu kan ni.
4. Lawm thu sawi chungin a kawng kharah chuan lut u la, Fak chungin a biak in hung chhng kawtlaiah chuan lut rawh u: A hnenah lawm thu saiw u la, a hming chu fak rawh u.LALPA chu a tha si a; angilneihna chu kumkhuain a awm ang a; A rinawmna chu chhuan zawng zawng thlengin a awm ang.
5. LALPA chu a tha si a; angilneihna chu kumkhuain a awm ang a; A rinawmna chu chhuan zawng zawng thlengin a awm ang.
