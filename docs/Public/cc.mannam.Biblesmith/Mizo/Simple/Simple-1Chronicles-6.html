1. Levia fapate chu Gersona te, Lohatha te, Meraria te an ni.
2. Tin, Kohatha fapate chu Amrama te, Izhara te, Hebrona te, Uziela te an ni.
3. Tin, Amrama thlahte chu Arona te, Mosia te, Miriami te an ni a. Tin, Arona fapate chu Nadaba te, Abihua te, Eleazara te, Ithamara te an ni.
4. Elazara chuan Phinehasa a hring a, Phinehasan Abisua a hring a;
5. tin, Abisuan Bukia a hring a, tin, Bukian Uzia a hring a;
6. tin, Uzian Zerahia a hring a, tin, Zerahian Meraotha a hring a;
7. Meraiothan Amarai a hring a, tin, Amarian Ahituba a hring a;
8. tin, Ahituban Zadoka a hring a, tin, Zadokan Ahimaaza a hring a;
9. tin, Ahimaazan Azaria a hring a, tin, Azarian Johanana ah ring a,
10. tin, Johananan Azaria a hring a, (chu mi chuan a ni, Jerusalema Solomona sak biak ina puithiam hna thawk ni:)
11. tin, Azarian Amaria a hring a, tin, Amarian Ahituba a hring a,
12. tin, Ahituban Zadoka a hring a, tin, Zadokan Saluma a hring a;
13. tin, Saluman Hilkia a hring a, tin, Hilkian Azaria a hring a;
14. tin, Azarian Seraia a hring a, tin, Seraian Jehozadaka a hring a;
15. tin, LALPA'n Judai ram leh Jerusalem khua Nebukdanezzara kuta a pek lai khan Jehozadaka chu salah a tang ve a.
16. Levia fapate chu Gersoma te, Kohatha te, Meraria te an ni.
17. Tin, he'ng hi Gersoma fapate hming chu a ni: Libnia leh Simeia.
18. Tin, Kohatha fapate chu Amrama te, Izhara te, Hebrona te, Uziela te an ni.
19. Meraria fapate chu Mahlia leh Musia an ni. Chunge chu an chhungkaw kal dan ang zela Levi-ho hnamte chu an ni.
20. Gersoma fapa chu Libnia a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Jahatha a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Zima a ni a;
21. chu mi fapa chu Joa a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Idova a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Zera a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Jeatheraia a ni.
22. Kohatha fapae chu Aminadaba a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Kora a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Asira a ni a;
23. chu mi fapa chu Elkana a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Ebiasapha a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Asira a ni a;
24. chu mi fapa chu Tahatha a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Uriela a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Uzia ani a, tin, chu mi fapa chu Saula a ni.
25. Tin, Elkana fapate chu Amasaia leh Ahimotha an ni.
26. Elkana thlahte chu: Elkana fapa chu Zophaia a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Nahatha a ni a;
27. chu mi fapa chu Eliaba a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Jerohama a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Elkana a ni.
28. Tin, Samuela fapate chu a upa zawk chu Joela a nia , a dawttu chu Abija a ni.
29. Meraria fapa chu Mahlia a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Libnia a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Simeia a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Uza a ni a;
30. chu mi fapa chu Simea a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Jagia a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Asaia a ni.
31. Chungte chu a ni, bawm dah hnua LALPA ina hla hruaitu atan Davida'n a ruatte chu.
32. Tichuan Solomonan Jerusalem-a LALPA in a sak hma loh chu, inbiakna puan inah chuan hlain rawng an bawl thin a; a pawl pawlin rawngbawl hna an inchhawk kual thin a.
33. Tin, henge hi chung hnathawktute chu a ni, an fapate nen, Kohath-ho thlahte chu hla satu Hemana a ni a, chu mi pa chu Joela a ni a, chu mi pa chu Samuela a ni a;
34. chu mi pa chu Elkana a ni a, chu mi pa chu Jerohama a ni a, chu mi pa chu Eliela a ni a, chu mi pa chu Tova a ni a;
35. chu mi pa chu Zupha a ni a, chu mi pa chu Elkana a ni a, chu mi pa chu Mahatha a ni a, chu mi pa chu Amasaia a ni a,
36. chumi pa chu Elkana a ni a, chu mi pa chu Joela a ni a, chu mi pa chu Azaria a ni a, chu mi pa chu Zephani a ani a,
37. chu mi pa chu Tahatha a ni a, chu mi pa chu Asira a ni a, chu mi pa chu Asira a ni a, chu mi pa chu Ebiasapha a ni a, chu mi pa chu Kora a ni a;
38. chu mi pa chu Izhara a ni a, chu mi pa chu Kohatha a ni a, chu mi pa chu Levia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Israela a ni a.
39. Tin, a unaupa Asapha, a ding lama che thin a ni a, Asapha pa chu Berekia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Simea a ni a;
40. chu mi pa chu Mikaela a ni a, chu mi pa chu Baaseia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Malkija a ni a;
41. chu mi pa chu Ethnia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Zera a ni a, chu mi pa chu Adaia a ni a;
42. chu mi pa chu Ethana a ni a, chu mi pa chu Zima a ni a, chu mi pa chu Simeia a ni a;
43. chu mi pa chu Jahatha a ni a, chu mi pa chu Gersoma a ni a, chu mi pa chu Levia a ni a.
44. Tin, vei lama che thin an unau, Meraria fapa Ethana a ni a, chu mi pa chu Kisia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Abdia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Maluka a ni a;
45. chu mi pa chu Hasabia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Amazia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Hilkia a ni a;
46. chu mi pa chu Amzia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Bania a ni a, chu mi pa chu Semera a ni a;
47. chu mi pa chu Mahlia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Musia a ni a, chu mi pa chu Meraria a ni a, chu mi pa chu Levia a ni a.
48. Tin, an unau Levi-hote chu Pathian biakbuka rawngbawl hna zawng zawng thawk tura ruat an ni.
49. Arona leh a thlahte erawh chuan Pathian chhiahhlawh Mosia'n thu a lo pek tawh zawng zawng ang khan, hmun thianghlim ber hna zawng zawng chu changin, Israelte tana remna siamna'n halral thilhlanna maichamah leh rimtui hlanna maichamah an hlan thin a.
50. Tin, hengte hi Arona fapate chu an ni: Eleazara chu a fapa a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Phinehasa a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Abisua a ni a;
51. chu mi fapa chu Bukia a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Uzia ani a, chu mi fapa chu Zerahia a ni a;
52. chu mi fapa chu Meraiotha a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Amaria a ni a,chu mi fapa chu Ahituba a ni a;
53. chu mi fapa chu Zadoka a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Ahimaaza a ni.
54. Tin, hengte hi an ram chhunga an awmhmun khuar dan ang zela na awmhmunte chu a ni: Arona fa, Kohath mi hnamte chu (thumvawr chu an chunga tlu hmasa ber a ni reng a).
55. Judai ram a Hebron leh a daivelte chu an pe a,
56. khawpui daiv0elte leh a khawperte erawh chu Jephunea fapa Kaleba an pe a.
57. Tin, Arona fapate chu inhumhimna khawpui Hebron te, Libna khua a daivelte nen, Jatir leh Estomoa khua a daivelte nen;
58. tin, Hilen khua a daivelte nen, Debir khua a daivelte nen;
59. tin, Asan khua a daivelte nen, tin, Beth-semes khua a daivelte nen;
60. tin, Benjamin hmate zing ata Geba khua a daivelte nen, Anathoth khua a daivelte nen an pe a. An hnam zinga khawpui pek zawng zawng chu khaw sawm leh khaw thum a ni.
61. Tin, Kohatha fapa dangte chu hnam zatve, Manasea thlahte hnam zatve chanpuala mite khawpui sawm, thumvawrin an pe a.
62. Tin, Gersoma fapate chu an hnam kal dan ang zelin, Isakar hnam chanpuala mi te, Aser hnam chanpuala mi te, Naphtali hnam chanpuala mi te, Basan rama Manase hnam chanpuala mi te, khawpui sawm leh pathum an pe a.
63. Meraria fapate chu an hnam kal dan ang zelin Reuben hnam chanpuala mi te, Gad hnam chanpuala mi te, Zebulun hnam chanpuala mi te, thumvawrin khaw sawm leh pahnih an pe a.
64. Tin, Israela thlahte chuan Levi-ho chite chu khawpuite leh a daivelte an pe a.
65. Tichuan Juda hnam chanpuala mi te, Simeon hnam chanpuala mi te, Benjamin hnam chanpuala mi te khawpui hming sawi takte chu thumvawrin an pe a ni.
66. Tin, Kohatha thlahte hnam thenkhat chuan Ephraim-hote chanpuala mi khawpuite an chang a.
67. Tin, Ephraim tlang rama inhumhimna khawpui Sekem chu a daivelte nen an pe a;
68. Gezer a daivelte nen; Jokmeam a daivelte nen Beth-horon a daivelte nen;
69. Tin, Aijalon a daivelte nen, Gath-rimon a daivelte nen;
70. Tin, Manase hnam zatvete chanpuala mi chu - Kohatha thlahte hnam la awmte tan chuan - Aner a daivelte nen, Bileam a daivelte nen an pe a.
71. Manase hnam zatvete chanpuala mi Gersoma fapate an pek chu Basan rama Golan a daivelte nen, Astaroth a daivelte nen a ni,
72. Isakar hnam chanpuala mi Kedes a daivelte nen, Daberoth a daivelte nen,
73. Ramoth a daivelte nen, Anem a daivelte nen,
74. Aser hnam chanpuala mi Masal a daivelte nen, Abdon a daivelte nen,
75. Hukok a daivelte nen, Rehob a daivelte nen,
76. Naphtali hnam chanpuala mi Galili rama Kedes a daivelte nen, Hamon a daivelte nen, Kiriathaim a daivelte nen a ni.
77. Tin, Meraria thlah Levi-ho dangte an pek chu Zebulun hnam chanpuala mi Rimono khua a daivelte nen, Tabor khua a daivelte nen,
78. Jeriko zawn Jordan ral, Jordan chhak lampangah Reuben hnam chanpuala mi thlalera Bezer khua a daivelte nen, Jaza khua a daivelte nen,
79. Kedemoth khua a daivelte nen, Mephaath khua a daivelte nen,
80. Gad hnam chanpuala mi Gilead rama Ramoth khua a daivelte nen, Mahanaim khua a daivelte nen,Hesbon khua a daivelte nen,Jazer khua a daivelte nen a ni.
81. Hesbon khua a daivelte nen,Jazer khua a daivelte nen a ni.
