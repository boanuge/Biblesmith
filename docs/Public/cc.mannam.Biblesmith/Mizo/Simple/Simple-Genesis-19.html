1. Tin, vantirhkoh pahnih chu tlai lamah Sodom khua an zuk thleng a; Lota chu Sodom khaw kulh kawngkharah chuan a lo thu a: tin, Lotan anmahni a lo hmu a, lawm turin a ding ta a; tin, a hmaia lei siin chibai a buk a;
2. tin, "Ngai teh u, ka pute u, khawngaih takin in chhiahhlawh inah hian lut rih ula, riak u la, in kete sil rawh u, tichuan zing takah in tho ang a, in kal leh dawn nia," a ti a. Nimahsela anni chuan, "Ni lo e, khawlaiah kan riak zawk ang," an ti a.
3. Nimahsela ani chuan a thlem chiam a; tichuan a hnenah an kal a, a inah chuan an lut ta a, tin, ani chuan chawhmeh a siam a, chhang dawidim telh loh a kang a, tin, an ei ta a.
4. Nimahsela an mut hmain khuaa mi, Sodom khuaa mite ngei chuan, veng tina mi zawng zawngin, apui apangin, in chu an hual vel hlawm a;
5. tin, Lota chu an ko a, a hnenah, "Zanina i hnena mi lo lutte kha khawnge an awm? Anmahni kan pawl theihna'n kan hnenah rawn hruai chhuak rawh," an ti a.
6. Tin, Lota chu kawngka bulah chuan an hnenah a va chhuak a, kawng a khar leh a.
7. Tin, "Ka unaute u, khawngaih takin chuti em chuan sual ma ta che u.
8. Ngai teh u, mipa la pawl ngai loh fanu pahnih ka nei a ni; mi khawngaih u la, anni chu in hnenah ka rawn hruai chhuak ang e, tha in tih ang apiangin an chungah ti nang che u: he mite chungah erawh hi chuan engmah ti lul suh ang che u; chu mi avang chuan a sin ka in chhungah hian an lo luh ni," a ti a.
9. Tin, anni chuan, "Kiang mai rawh," an ti a. Tin, anmahni vek chuan, "He mi hrep hi heta awm tur hian a lo pem lut a, roreltuah awm a tum vei a: tunah zawng anni chunga tih ai mahin i chungah kan titha lo lehzual ang," an ti a. Tin, mi (Lota) chu an bawh chiam a, kawngkhar chu tichhe turin an rawn hnaih a.
10. Nimahsela mite chuan an kut an zen chhuak a, Lota chu an hnenah in chhungah chuan an hnuk lut a, kawng an khar ta tlat a.
11. Tin, kawngka bula mite chu alian atein, an mit an tihdelsak a: chutichuan kawngkhar chu an zawng hmu zo ta lo a.
12. Tin, mite chuan Lota hnenah, "Heng mite lo hi hetah hian chhungte dang i nei em? I makpa leh i fapate leh i fanute leh khaw chhunga i neih apiangte chu khua ata hi hruai chhuak rawh:
13. LALPA hmaah chuan an au thawm chu a lo nasat tak avangin he khua hi kan tiboral dawn a ni: tiboral turin LALPA chuan min tir bawk si a," an ti a.
14. Tichuan Lota chu a kal chhuak a, a fanute neitu, a makpate hnenah chuan a va sawi a, "Tho u la, he mi khua ata hi chhuak rawh u; LALPA'n khua hi a tiboral dawn a ni," a ti a. Nimahsela a makpate ngaih chuan fiamthu sawi ang lek a ni a.
15. Tin, khawfing a chah veleh chuan vantirhkohte chuan, "Tho la, i nupui leh heta i fanu pahnih awmte hi hruai rawh; chutilochuan he khaw hremnaah hian in boral ve dah ang e," tiin Lota chu an tur ta sek a.
16. Nimahsela a la muang hle si a; tichuan LALPA'n amah a khawngaih avangin mite chuan a kutah leh a nupui kutah leh a fanute pahnih kutah chuan an chelh a, tichuan an hruai chhuak a, khaw pawnah chuan an dah ta a.
17. Tin, heti hi a ni a, pawna an hruai chhuah hnu chuan, "I nunna turin tlan chhuak rawh; i hnung lam hawi suh la, Phaizawl reng rengah hian ding hek suh; tlangah sawn tlan rawh, chutilochuan i boral dah ang e," a ti a.
18. Nimahsela Lota chuan a hnenah, "Ka pu, chung ang chu ni suh se:
19. ngai teh, i chhiahhlawh hian i mit a tlung a, nangin i zahngaihna chu i tilian a, chu zahngaihna chu ka nun humhimin ka chungah i lantir a ni; tlangah saw zawng ka tlan thei lo ang, thil tha lo tak hian mi rawn nangin ka thi dah ang e:
20. ngai teh, saw khua saw tlanna'n a hnai ber a, khaw te tak te a ni bawk si a: aw, chutah chuan (khaw te tak te a ni lo'm ni?) mi tlantir zawk rawh, tichuan ka nung dawn nia," a ti a.
21. Tin, ani chuan a hnenah, ""Ngai teh, chu thil kawngah pawh chuan ka zawm che a ni, khua i sawi chu ka tiboral lo mai ang e.
22. Hmanhmawh la, chutah chuan tlan rawh; chu mi i thlen hma loh chuan engmah ka ti thei dawn si lo a," a ti a. Chuvangin chu khaw hmingah chuan Zoar an vuah ta a.
23. Lota chuan Zoar khua chu a thlen chuan lei chungah chuan ni a lo chhuak tawh a.
24. Tin, LALPA chuan Sodom leh Gomorra khaw chungah chuan vana LALPA hnen atang chuan kat leh mei a surtira;
25. tichuan chung khuate leh Phaizawl zawng zawng leh khuaa mi zawng zawngte leh leia thil tote chu a tiboral ta vek a.
26. A nupui erawh chuan Lota hnung atang chuan a lehhawi a, chi khawn dingah a lo chang ta a.
27. Tin, Abrahama chu zing takah LALPA hmaa a dinna hmunah khan a han chho a;
28. tin, Sodom leh Gomorra khaw lam leh Phaizawl ram zawng zawng lam chu a thlir a, a en reng a; tin, Ngai teh, chu mi rama meikhu chu rawhtuina meikhu ang maiin a khu chho ngut ngut a.
29. Tin, heti hi a ni a, Pathianin Phaizawl khuate chu a tihboral laia Lota awmna khuate chu a tihboral lai chuan, Pathianin Abrahama chu a hre reng a, Lota chu boralna zing ata chu a tir chhuak a.
30. Tin, Lota chu Zoar khua ata chu a chho a, tlangah chuan a awm a, a fanute pahnih chu a hnenah an awm bawk a; Zoar khuaah a awm ngam si loa: tin, amah leh a fanute pahnih chu pukah an awm ta a.
31. Tin, a upa zawk chuan a naupang zawk hnenah chuan, "Kan pa hi a upa tawh a, khawvel zawng zawng dan anga min pawl turin lei chungah hian mipa pakhat mah an awm tawh si loa:
32. hawh, kan pa hi uain i intir ang khai, tichuan kan pa chithlah mang loa kan siam theihna'n a hnenah kan mu dawn nia," a ti a.
33. Tichuan chu mi zan chuan an pa chu uain an intir ta a: tin, a upa zawk chu a lut a, a pa hnenah chuan a va mu a; tin, ani chuan a lo mut lai chu a hre lo a, a thawh leh lai pawh chu a hre hek lo.
34. Tin, heti hi a ni a, atukah chuan a upa zawk chuan a naupang zawk hnenah chuan "Ngai teh, nizanah khan ka pa hnenah ka mu a: zaninah pawh hian uain i intir leh ang khai; tin, nang lut la, kan pa chithlah mang loa kan siam theihna'n a hnenah va mu ve rawh," a ti a.
35. Tichuan chu mi zan pawh chuan an pa chu uain an intir leh a: tin, a naupang zawk chu a tho a, a hnenah a va mu ta a; tin, ani chuan a lo mut lai chu a hre lo a, a thawh leh lai pawh chu a hre hek lo.
36. Chutiang chuan Lota fanute pahnih chuan an pa lakah chuan nau an lo pai ta ve ve a.
37. Tin, a upa zawk chuan fapa a hringa, a hmingah chuan Moaba a sa a: chu mi chu tun laia Moab-ho thlahtu hi a ni a.Tin, a naupang zawk pawh chuan fapa a hring ve a, a hmingah chuan Ben-ammia a sa a: chu mi chu tun laia Ammona thlahte thlahtu hi a ni a.
38. Tin, a naupang zawk pawh chuan fapa a hring ve a, a hmingah chuan Ben-ammia a sa a: chu mi chu tun laia Ammona thlahte thlahtu hi a ni a.
