1. Moab ram phur tir chu, Zan khatah Moab rama Ar khua chu tihchhiatin a awm a, engmah loa siamin a awm ta si a; zan khatah Moab ram Kir khua chu tihchhiatin a awm a, engmah loa siamin a awm ta si a.
2. Bajith-ah leh Dobon-ah, hmun sangahte chuan, tap turin a chho ta a: Moab-ho chuan Nebo leh Medeba chu an tah khum vak vak a, an lu zawng zawng a kawlh vek a, khabe hmul tinreng pawh mehin a awm ta vek a.
3. An kawtthlerahte chuan saiip puan an veng a: an in chungahte leh an mual zau laiahte chuan mi tin chu nasa taka tapin an chiau vak vak a.
4. Tin, Hesbon-ho pawh an au a. Elealeh-ho nen; an aw chu Jahaz in Moab-ho ralthuama inthuamte chu ring takin an au a; an nuna chu an kaw chhungah chuan a khur a.
5. Moab-ho avangin ka thinlung chu a au a; an m thate chu Zoar khuaah leh Eglath-selisi-jah-ah an tlan a: Luhith chhovah chuan tap chungin an kal chho a ni; Horonaim kawngah chuan chhiatna auin an au chhuak a ni.
6. Nimrim tuite chu tihchhiatin a awm dawn si a: hlobet chu a vuai zo a, hlobet chawrno chu a rawp a, thil hring reng reng ch a awm ta si lo.
7. Chuvangin an sum la neih chhunte leh an dah thatte chu tuipuisuthlah Lui ralah chuan an kal bopui ang.
8. Au thawm chu Moab ramri velahte chuan a thang si a; a chiau vak vak thawm chu Eglaim khaw lamah a thang a, a chiau vak vak thawm chu Beer-elim khaw lamah a thang a ni.Dimon tuite chu thisenin a bual zo vek si a: Dimon chungah chuan thil nasalehzual ka thlentir dawn si a, Moab-ho zinga tlan chhuakte chungah leh, chu ram ala awm chhunte chungah chuan sakeibaknei ka thlentir ang.
9. Dimon tuite chu thisenin a bual zo vek si a: Dimon chungah chuan thil nasalehzual ka thlentir dawn si a, Moab-ho zinga tlan chhuakte chungah leh, chu ram ala awm chhunte chungah chuan sakeibaknei ka thlentir ang.
