1. Tin, Kaisari khuaah a hming Kornelia mi pakhat a awm a, Itali pawl an tih, chu mi pawla sipai za hotu a ni a,
2. Pathian ngaihsak mi a ni a, a chhungte zawng zawng nen Pathian an tih thin a, mite hnenah thil tam tak a pe thin a, Pathian hnenah pawh a tawngtai fo a.
3. Chu mi chuan behliang mun hun lai velah mumang angin fiah takin Pathian vantirhkoh pakhat a hnenah luta, "Korneli," tiin a hmu a.
4. Tin, Kornelia chuan ani chu a lo en reng a, a hlau a, "Lalpa, eng nge ni?" a ti a. Tin, ani chuan a hnenah, "I tawngtainate leh i thilpek thute kha hriat reng turin Pathian hmaah a thleng chho ta a.
5. Tunah Joppa khuaah mite tir la mi pakhat Simona, Petera an tiha chu, han hruaitir rawh;
6. savun siamtu Simona hnenah a thleng a ni, a in chu tuifinriat kamah a awm," a ti a.
7. Tin, vantirhkoh a betu chu a kal veleh, Kornelia chuan a chhiahhlawhte zinga mi pahnih leh a rawngbawl fote zinga Pathian ngaihsak sipai pakhat a ko a;
8. tin, thu zawng zawng chu an hnenah a hrilh zawhin Joppa khuaah chuan a tir ta a.
9. Tin, atukah an kal laia khaw kiang an thlen dawnin, Petera chu tawngtai turin chawfak hun laiin, in chungah a lawn chho a;
10. tin, a ril a lo tam a, chaw ei a duh ta a; nimahsela ei tur an siam lai chuan engloin a zel ta a.
11. Tin, van inhawngin englo bawm, puan lian tak ang, a kil lia hlina leia uai thlain a hmu a;
12. chu mi chhungah chuan fuliafa leh rannung leh chungleng sava tinrengte an awm a.
13. Tin, a hnenah awin, "Peter, tho rawh; talh la, ei rawh," a rawn ti a.
14. Nimahsela Petera chuan, "Lalpa, ni lo e; thiang lo leh bawlhhlawh engmah reng ka ei ngai lo e," a ti a.
15. Tin, aw chuan a vawi hnihnaah, "Pathianin a tihthiaghlim tawh chu nangin thiang lo ti suh," a ti a.
16. Chutiang hlirin vawi thum a lo awm a: tin, bawm chu vana khai chhohin a awm leh ta nghal a.
17. Tin, Petera chuan mumang anga a hmuh chu a ngaihna hre loa a mangan laiin Kornelia mi tirhte chuan Simona in chu an lo zawt chhuak tawh a, kawngkhar bulah an lo ding a,
18. an au a, Simona, Petera an tiha chu, chutah chuan a thlen leh thlen loh an zawt a.
19. Tin, Petera chuan a mumang ang thu chu a ngaihtuah laiin Thlarauin a hnenah, "Ngai teh, mi pathumin an zawng a che.
20. Chutichuan, tho la, chhuk la, engmah ringhlel loin anmahni zui rawh; anni chu `ka tirh an ni e," a ti a.
21. Chutichuan, Petera chu, chu mite hnenah chuan a chhuk a, an hnenah, "In zawn hi ka ni e; eng nge in rawn kal chhan?" a ti a.
22. Tin, anni chuan, "Vantirhkoh thianghlim pakhatin, Kornelia, sipai za hotu, mi fel tak, Pathian tih, Juda hnam zawng zawng zinga mi hmingtha hnenah chuan i thusawi a hriatna'n, a inah nangmah ko tur chein a hrilh a ni," an ti a.
23. Chutichuan mite chu a ko lut a, a riahtir ta a. Tin, atukah chuan Petera chu a tho a, an hnenah a kal a, Joppa khuaa mi unau tute emaw pawh a hnenah an kal ve a,
24. Tin, atuk lehah Kaisari khuaah chuan an lut ta a. Tin, Kornelia chuan a laichinte leh a thian duh berte ko khawmin, anni chu a lo nghak reng a.
25. Tin, Petera chu a luh dawn lai takin Kornelia chuan a lo hmuak a, a ke bulah a bawkkhup a, Pathian chibai buk angin a buk a.
26. Nimahsela Petera chuan ani chu a kai tho a, "Ding rawh, kei pawh mihring ka ni alawm," a ti a.
27. Tin, a hnenah thusawi chungin a lut a, mi tam tak awm khawm chu a va hmu a.
28. Tin, an hnenah, "Juda miin hnam dang pawl leh an hnena kal a thian lohzia nangnin in hria a ni; nimahsela tumah thiang lo leh bawlhhlawh ka tih tur a ni lo tih Pathianin mi hriattir a ni.
29. Chuvangin mi rawn koh veleh khan hnial loin ka lo kal ta a nih hi. Tichuan ka zawt a che u, eng nge mi rawn koh chhan le?" a ti a.
30. Tin, Kornelia chuan, "Ni li a ni ta, tun hun ang thleng hian ka inah, behliang mun hun tawngtainaah ka tawngtai a; tin, ngai teh, mi pakhat puan eng tak sinin ka hmaah a lo ding a,
31. 'Korneli, i tawngtaina kha hriatin a awm ta, mi hnena i thilpekte pawh kha Pathianin a hre reng a.
32. Chuvangin Joppa khuaah mi tir la, Simona,Petera an tiha chu,i hnenah ko rawh; ani chu tuifinriat kamah savun siamtu Simona inah a thleng a ni,' a ti a.
33. Chuvangin i hnenah mi ka rawn tir ta nghal a ni; i lo kal hi a tha e. Chutichuan, Lalpa'n thu a pek apiang che hre turin tunah Pathian mithmuhin kan zain hetah kan awm a nih hi," a ti a.
34. Tin, Petera chuan a sawi a: "Pathianin mi duhsak bik a nei lo tih dik takin ka hria e;
35. nimahsela hnam tin zingah tupawh amah tiha fel taka ti apiang a lawmzawng mi an ni thin.
36. Ani chuan thlamuanna Chanchin Tha Isua Krista kaa hrilin, Israela thlahte hnenah thu a thawn a, (Isua chu mi zawng zawng Lal a ni a)
37. Johanan a tlangaupui thin baptisma zawhah khan, thu, Galili ram atanga lo thang tana Judai ram zawng zawnga thang chhuak kha, nangnin in hre bawk alawm;
38. Nazaret Isua chungchang thu kha, khatia Pathianin ani chu Thlarau Thianghlim leh thiltihtheihnaa a vurzia kha; ani chuan mi chunga titha leh diabola tihduhdah zawng zawng tidamin a fang vel thin a, Pathian a hnenah a awm avangin.
39. Judate ramah leh Jerusalem khuaa a thiltih zawng zawng hretute kan ni e; ani chu thinglerah khaiin an tihlum ta a.
40. Pathianin ani chu ni thum niin a kai tho leh a, fiah takin a entir a,
41. amaherawhchu, mi zawng zawng hnenah a ni lo, Pathianin hretua a ruat lawkate hnenah chauh a ni, - keimahni, mitthi zing ata a thawh leh hnua a hnena eia barte hnenah hian.
42. Ani chu mitthite leh mi nungte chunga Roreltua Pathian ruat chu a ni tih, mi hnena tlangaupui leh hriattir turin Pathianin thu min pe a ni.
43. Tupawh amah chu ring apiang a hming avangin sualte ngaihhnathiamin an awm ang tih, he'ng a chanchin hi zawlnei zawng zawngin an hriattir thin kha," a ti a.
44. Tin, Petera'n chung thu chu a sawi lai tak chuan thu hretute zawng zawng chungah chuan Thlarau Thianghlim a lo thleng ta a.
45. Tin, Serhtanho zinga ringtute Petera rawn zui apiang chuan Thlarau Thianghlim pek chu Jentailte chungah pawh leih a nih avangin mak an ti hle a;
46. tawng hriat lohtea thu sawiin Pathian fakin an hre si a. Tin, Petera'n a bia a,
47. "Heng mite, keimahni anga Thlarau Thianghlim hmu ve hi baptisma an chan lohna'n miin tui hman an khap thei em ni?" a ti a.Tichuan isua Krista hminga baptisma chan tur thu a pe a. Chung zawhah chuan ni engzat emaw chamah an ngen a.
48. Tichuan isua Krista hminga baptisma chan tur thu a pe a. Chung zawhah chuan ni engzat emaw chamah an ngen a.
