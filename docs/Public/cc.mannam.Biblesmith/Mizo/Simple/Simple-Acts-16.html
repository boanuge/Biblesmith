1. Tin, Derbe khuaah leh Lustra khuaah chuan a kal bawk a; tin, chutah chuan a hming Timothea zirtir pakhat a awm; ani chu ring ve Juda hmeichhe fapa a ni a, a pa erawh chu Grik a ni;
2. Lustra leh Ikonion khuaa unau awmte chuan ani chu an fak thin a.
3. Paulan ani chu an hnena kal veah a duh a; tichuan chu mi lama Juda awmte avang chuan a serh a tansak a, a pa chu Grik a ni tih an hre vek si a.
4. Tin, khaw tinah an kal pahin Jerusalem khuaa tirhkoh leh upa awmte thu rel kha zawm atan unaute hnenah an pe zel a.
5. Chutichuan kohhran pawlte chu rinnaah chuan an nghet ta a, ni tin an pung ta deuh deuh a.
6. Tin, Thlarau Thianghlimin Asia rama thu hril a khap avangin Phrugia leh Galatia ram chu an fang chhuak ta a;
7. tin, Musia ram kiang an thlenin Bethunia rama luh an tum a, nimahsela Isua thlarauin a phal si lo a.
8. Chutichuan Musia ram chu an pel a, Troas khuaah an chhuk ta a.
9. Tin, zanah Paulan mumang angin Makedonia rama mi mi pakhat dingin,"Makedonia ramah lo kai la, min pui rawh," tia ngen chiamin a hmu a.
10. Tin, chu mang chu a man zawh veleh Makedonia ramah chuan kal kan tum ta nghal a, an hnena Chanchin Tha hrilah Pathianin min ko tih kan hria a.
11. Tin, Troas khua ata lawngin kan chhuak a, tluang takin Samathraki khuaah kan kal a, atukah chuan Neapoli khuaah kan kal leh a;
12. tin, chuta tang chuan Philippi khuaah kan kal a, chu mi khua chu Makedonia rama khaw pakhat a ni a, chu mi bialah chuan khaw pawimawh ber a ni a, Rom khuaa mi pemte khua a ni. Chu mi khuaah chuan ni tam deuh kan cham a.
13. Tin, Chawlhniin kulh kawngkhar pawn lamah lui kamah tawngtaina hmun nia kanrinah chuankan kal a; tin, kan thu a, hmeichhe lo kal khawmhote hnenah chuan thu kan sawi a.
14. Tin, a hming Ludi, puan sen duk zuartu, Thuatira khua a mi, Pathian betu hmeichhe pakhatin kan thu a hria a, tin, Paula thusawi zawm turin Lalpa chuan a thinlung a tivar ta a.
15. Tin, amah leh a chhungten baptisma an chan zawhin, "Lalpa ring tak zeta mi ruat chuan ka inah lo thleng rawh u," tiin min ngen chiam a, min chelh ta a.
16. Tin, tawngtaina hmunah chuan kan kal laiin, nula tu emaw ramhuai aienthiam zawlin min tawk a, ani chuan a ai en avangin a pute chu sum tam tak a hmuhtir thin a.
17. Chu mi chuan Paula leh keini min zui a, "Heng mi chhandamna kawng hrilhtu che u hi, Chungnungber Pathian bawih an nih hi," tiin a au a.
18. Chutiang chuan ni rei tak a ti thin a. Chutichuan Paula chu a lungni ta lo a,a hawi a,ramhuai hnenah chuan,"Isua Krista hmingin a kawchhung ata chhuah thu ka pe a che," a ti a. Tin, chutih lai tak chuan a chhuak ta nghal a.
19. Tin, a pute chuan an sum hmuhna a bo tih an hriatin, Paula leh Sila chu an man a, hotute hmaah dawr hmunah an hnuk lut a.
20. Tichuan thu reltute hnenah an hruai a, "Heng mite hi Judate an ni a, kan khua an tibuai em em a,
21. dan eng emaw kan lawm leh zawm thiang lote hi an hril thin a, Rom mi kan ni si a," an ti a.
22. Chutichuan mipuite pawh chu tirhkohte bei turin an lo thawk ve a, thu reltute chuan an puante an phih hlihsak a, tianga vuak thu an pe a.
23. Tin, vawi tam tak an vuak zawhin tan inah an khung a, a vengtu hnenah him taka ven tur an ti tlat a;
24. chuti kauva thu an pek avangin ani chuan rip chhungnungah a khung a, an keahte chuan thing kawl a buntir tlat a.
25. Nimahsela zan laiah Paula leh Sila chuan Pathian hnenah an tawngtai a, hla an sa a, mi tangten an ngaithla a;
26. tin,thawklehkhatah lir nasa takin a lo nghing a,chutichuan tan in lungphumte lam chu a nghing a; tin, kawngkhar zawng zawng a inhawng nghal a, mi tin kawl bunte chu a lo inphelh ta vek a.
27. Tin, a vengtu chu a muhil a lo harh a, tan in kawngkharte chu inhawngin a hmu a, mi tangte chu an tlan bo emaw a ti a, a khandaih a phawi a, a intihhlum dawn a.
28. Nimahsela Paula chu aw ring takin a au a, "Engmah intih suh; kan zain hetah kan la awm vek alawm," a ti a.
29. Tin, a vengtu chuan khawnvarte a lam a, a zuang lut a, hlauva khur chungin Paula leh Sila hmaah a bawkkhup a;
30. tin, anni chu a hruai chhuak a, "Pute u, chhandama awm turin eng nge ka tih tur?" a ti a.
31. Tin, anni chuan, "Lalpa Isua ring la, tichuan chhandamin i awm ang, nang leh i chhungte nen," an ti a.
32. Tin, a hnenah leh a ina awm zawng zawngte hnenah chuan Lalpa thu an sawi a.
33. Tin,chu mi zanah,chu mi lai la la chuan a hruai a,an vuakna vualte chu a silfai a;tin,anileh a chhungte zawng zawng chuan baptisma an chang ta nghal a.
34. Tin, a inah a hruai chho a, ei tur a hlui a, tin, Pathian an rin avangin, ani chu a chhungte zawng zawng nen an hlim ta em em a.
35. Tin, ni a chhuahin, thu reltute chuan, "Kha mite kha chhuah rawh," tiin mi mantute an tir a.
36. Tin, tan in vengtu chuan Paula hnenah an thu a hrilh a, "Thu reltute chuan chhuah tur che uin mi an rawn tir a ni; chuvangin tunah lo chhuak u la, thlamuang takin kal rawh u," a ti a.
37. Nimahsela Paula chuan an hnenah, "Rom mi kan ni si a, kan thiam leh thiam loh hre loin vantlang mithmuhin min vua a, tan inah min khung a; tunah hian arukin min hnawt chhuak dawn vei a ni maw? a ni tawp lo ang; anmahni ngei lo kal se la min rawn hruai chhuak rawh se," a ti a.
38. Chutichuan mi mantute chuan chung thu chu thu reltute hnenah chuan an hrilh a. Tin, Rom mi an ni tih an hriat chuan an hlau ta a;
39. tin, an va kal a, an thlem chiam a, an hruai chhuak a, khua ata chu kal bovah an ngen a,Tin, tan in ata chu an chhuak a, Ludi inah chuan an lut a; tin, unaute chu an hmu a, an thlamuan a, an chhuak ta a.
40. Tin, tan in ata chu an chhuak a, Ludi inah chuan an lut a; tin, unaute chu an hmu a, an thlamuan a, an chhuak ta a.
