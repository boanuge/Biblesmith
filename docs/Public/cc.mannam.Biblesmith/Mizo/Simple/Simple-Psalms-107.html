1. Aw, LALPA hnenah lawm thu hril rawh u; ani chu a tha si a: A ngilneihna pawh kumkhuain a awm dawn si a.
2. LALPA mi tlante chuan chutiang chu sawi rawh se, Dotu kut ata a tlan,
3. Ram tin, ata a hruai khawm, Chhak leh thlang, chhim leh hmar atate chuan.
4. Thlalera ram ro kawngah chuan an vak an vak a; Mihring awmna khua reng reng an hmu lo a.
5. Riltam leh tuihalin An nunna hchu an kawchhungah a chau ta a.
6. Chu veleh an buainaah chuan LALPA chu an au a, Ani chuan an manganna atate chu a chhanhim a.
7. Mihring awmna khuaa na kal theihna'n Kawng ngil takah a hruai ta a.
8. Aw, mihringte hian LALPA chu a ngilneihna avang leh, Mihring fate chunga a thilmak tihte avang hian fak teh se la aw!
9. Ani chuan nuna thil chak em em chu a titlain, Nunna riltam chu thatnain a tipuar si thin a.
10. Thim hnuaiah leh thihna hlima thua, Hrehawmna leh thira phuarte chu;
11. Pathian thu chungahte an hel a, Chungnungbera remruat chu an ngaihnepa vnagin:
12. Ani chuan an thinlung chu thawhrimnain ahmin ta a; An tlu a, tanpuitu tur reng reng an awm si lo a,
13. Chu veleh an buainaah chuan LALPA chu an au a, Ani chuan an manganna atate chu a chhandam a.
14. Thim hnuai ata leh thihna hli ata chu a hruai chhuak a, An phuarnate chu a tichat ta a.
15. Aw, mihringte hian LALPA chu a ngilneihna avang leh, Mihring fate chunga a thilmak tihte avang hian fak teh se la aw!
16. Ani chuan dar kawngkharte chu a tichhia a, A kalhna thir tlawnte chu a tibung ta si a.
17. Mi ate chu an bawhchhiatna avang leh, An khawlohnate avangin an intihreawm thin a.
18. An nunna chuan ei tur tinreng hi a ten a; Thihna kulh kawngkharte chu an hnaih thin a.
19. Chu veleh an buainaah chuan LALPA chu an au a, Ani chuan an manganna atate chu a chhandam thin.
20. A thu chu a tir chhuak a, anni chu a tidam thin a, An boralna tur atate chu a chhandam thin.
21. Aw, mihringte hian LALPA chu a ngilneihna avang leh, Mihring fate chunga a t hilmak tihte avang hian fak teh se la aw!
22. Anni chu lawm thu sawi inthawinate chu hlan se la, A thiltihte chu hla sain hril rawh se.
23. Tuifinriatah lawng liana zuk kal a, Tui zau tak chungte hnathawk thinte chuan;
24. Chungte chuan LALPA thiltihte leh, Tui thuk taka a thilmak tihte chu an hmu thin.
25. Ani chuan thu a pe a, thlipui a tlehtir a, Chu chuan a tuifawnte ch a tisang thin.
26. Vanah an chho tual tual a, thuk takah an chhuk leh tual tual thin a: Manganna avangin an nunna chu a tuiral thin.
27. Zu rui angin an thle an thle a, an pei thlawk thlawk a, Awm ngaihna reng reng an hre thin lo.
28. Chu veleh an buainaah chuan LALPA chu an au a, Ani chuan an manganna atate chu a hruai chhuak thin.
29. Thlipui chu a tibang thuap a, Chutichuan a tuifawnte a reh thup thin.
30. Tin, a reh tak avangin an lawm thin a; Ani chuan an ngaih tak thli upah chuan a hruai thleng thin.
31. Aw, mihringte hian LALPA chu a ngilneihna avang leh, Mihirng fate chunga a t hilmak tihte avang hian fak teh se la aw;
32. Anni chuan mite inkhawmnaah amah chu fak se la, Upaho thutnaah chuan fak rawh se.
33. Ani chuan luipuite chu thlalerah a chantir thin a, Tuihnate chu lei rovah a chantir bawk thin.
34. Ram tha chu ram ro alah a chantir thin, A chhunga awmho suahsual avangin.
35. Thlaler chu dilah a chantir thin a, Ram ro chu tuihnaahte a chantir bawk thin.
36. Chutah chuan riltamte chu a awmtir thin, Awmna tur khua an buatsaih a,
37. Lote an neih a, grep huante an siam a, A rah lo pungte chu an hmuh theihna turin.
38. Anni chu mal a samw thin a, chutichuan nasa taka tihpunin an awm a; An rante chu a hmuh a phal thin lo a.
39. Tin, hnehchhiahna te, hrehawmna te lungngaihna te avangin, An hmuih ta telh telh a, an kun ta tlawk tlawk thung a.
40. Ani chuan mi liante chungah hmusitna a leihbaw thin a, Thlaler, kawng reng reng awm lohnaah chuan a vah a vahtir a.
41. Nimahsela retheite chu hrehawmna atangin hmun sangah a hlang thin a, Ran rual angin chhungte a siamsak thin.
42. Mi dik takte chuan chu chu an hmu ang a, an lawm ang; Khawlohna zawng zawng chuan a ka a hup ang.Tupawh fing apiangin chungte chu an vawng tha ang a, LALPA ngilneihnate an ngaihtuah ang.
43. Tupawh fing apiangin chungte chu an vawng tha ang a, LALPA ngilneihnate an ngaihtuah ang.
