1. Zanah laikhum chungah ka dawn thin, ka hmangaih chu, ka zawng ruai e, ka tawng si lo e.
2. Ka tho ang a, khawpui daikawn ka vel ang, kawtthlerah leh mual laiahte chuan; ka zawng zel dawn e, ka hmangaih chu. Ka zawng ruai e, ka tawng si lo e.
3. Khawpui venga kal hualtuten min hmu a; ka hmangaih chu in hmu emaw? Ka ti a.
4. Anmahni ka pelh hnu lawkah chuan, ka hmu ta e, ka hmangaih chu; ka vuan ta a, ka kaltir leh tawh rua lo, ka nu inah, mi paitu pindan chhungah, amah ka hruai luh hma loh zawng.
5. Ka ngen tlat che u, aw Jerusalem fanute, saza leh ram zukpui chhalin, hmangaihna chu chawk thoa tiharh lo turin; amahin a duh hma chuan.
6. Eng nge ni hei, meikhu ding ang thlaler ata lo kal hi, murra leh beraw rimtui nam rum rum sumdawngtu paudar tinreng rim chenin?
7. Ngai rawh, Solomona zawnna tawlai chu, ralhrat sawmrukin an din hual e, Israel ralhrat zinga mite chuan.
8. An zain khandaih an vawng, do thiam an ni: Mi tinin khandaih an pai, an khel chungah, zantianga hlauhawm vang hian.
9. Lal Solomona'n zawn bawm a tan a insiam, Lebanon tlang thing hmanrua ngeiin;
10. A bante a siam a, tangkaruain, a nghenchhan chu rangkachakin, a thutthleng chu puan sendukin; a chhung lam chu an rem, duhawm takin, Jerusalem fanute chuan.Kal chhuak u la, aw Zion fanute, Lal Solomona chu en r'u; a khumtir kha, a nu'n lallukhum, a nupui neih ni, a thinlung lawmna niah chuan.
11. Kal chhuak u la, aw Zion fanute, Lal Solomona chu en r'u; a khumtir kha, a nu'n lallukhum, a nupui neih ni, a thinlung lawmna niah chuan.
