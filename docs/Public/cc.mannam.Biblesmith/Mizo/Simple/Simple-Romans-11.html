1. A nih leh, Pathianin a mite chu a paih thla em ni? Hnai lo e. Kei pawh Israel mi, Abrahama thlah, Benjamina hnam ka ni si a.
2. Pathianin a mi hriat lawkte chu a paih thla lo e. Pathian Lehkha Thuin Elija thu a sawi hi in hre lo em ni? Ani chuan Israel mite chung thu Pathian hnenah a thlen a,
3. "Lalpa, i zawlneite an tihlum a; i maichamte an tichim a; keimah chauh ka la awm a, kei pawh mi tihhlum an tum a ni" a tih hi.
4. Nimahsela Pathianin engtin nge a chhan? "Baala hmaa thingthi ngai lo mi sangsarih keimah atan ka la zuah reng a ni," tiin a chhang a.
5. Chutiang bawkin tun lai pawh hian khawngaiha thlan ang zia ziain paih thlak bak chu an la awm.
6. Khawngaihna avang a nih chuan thiltih avang a ni tawh lo ang; chuti a nih loh chuan khawngaihna hi khawngaihna a ni tawh lo ang.
7. Engtin nge ni ta ang le?Israel mite chuan an zawn chu an hmu lo a, Thlante erawh chuan an hmu a, a dang zawng zawngte chu an chawlawl zo tawh a.
8. "Pathianin vawiin thlengin an hnenah mutthlukna thlarau a pe a, hmuh theih lohna mitte, hriat theih lohna bengte nen," tih ziak ang khan.
9. Tin, Davida chuan, "An dawhkan chu an tan thang leh fal leh,Tlukna leh thungrulhna lo ni rawh se;
10. An hmuh theih lohna'n an mitte lo thim rawh se, nangin an hnungzang kuntir fo ang che,"a ti a.
11. Anih leh, an petek chu an tluk hlenna tur em ni? Hnai lo e. An tluk avangin Jentailte hnenah chuan chhandamna a lo thleng ta zawk a ni, Israel mite thiktirna'n.
12. Tichuan an tlukna mah khawvel tihhausakna a nih a, an kiamna mah Jentailte hausakna a nih bawk chuan an famkimna chu an hausakna a va ni lehzual dawn em!
13. Chutichuan nangni Jentailte hnenah ka sawi a ni. Kei, Jentailte tirhkoh ka nih avangin ka chipuite ka thiktir theih hramna tur leh,
14. an zinga thenkhatte ka chhandam theihna turin ka rawngbawlna hi ka chawimawi a ni.
15. Anmahni paih thlakna mah khawvel thian lehna a nih chuan anmahni lawmlehna tur chu thihna ata nun lehna lo chu eng nge ni ang?
16. Tin, chhang hlawm sawh thlak hmasak chu a thianghlim chuan a hlawm pum chu chutiang bawk a ni; tin, a zung chu a thianghlim chuan a zarte chu chutiang bawk a ni.
17. Tin, a zar thenkhatte chu tihtliaha a awmin, nang, oliv suak ni a, anmahni zinga zawmin i awm a, oliv thinga thatna zung tawm ve tawh i nih chuan,
18. zar dangte lakah chuan inchhuang suh. Inchhuang mah la, nang a zung chawmtu i ni lo a, a zung chu a chawmtu che a ni zawk e.
19. Tin, nangin, "Zawma ka awm theihna'n zarte tihtliahin an awm tawh alawm," i ti ang le.
20. A ni, an rin lohna avangin tihtliahin an awm a, nang i rinna avangin i ding a ni. Chapo suh la, hlau zawk rawh;
21. Pathianin zar takte chu a zuah si loh chuan nang pawh a zuah bik lo ang che.
22. Chutichuan Pathian thatzia leh a tihbaizia chu ngaihtuah rawh; a tlute tan chuan a tihbai a ni; nang atan erawh chuan a tha a ni, a thatnaa i awm reng chuan; chutilochuan nang pawh sah thlakin i awm ve ang.
23. Tin, anni pawh chu ring loa an awm reng loh chuan zawmin an awm ang; Pathianin anmahni a zawm leh thei si a.
24. Nang oliv suak reng ata sah chum mah, piandan lo anga oliv thing thaah zawma i awm chuan chung a zar taktakte chu, anmahni oliv thing pangngaiah chuan zawm an va ni dawn nasa em!
25. Unaute u, he thuruk hi in hriat loh ka duh si lo, chutilochuan in intifing ang; Jentailte famkimna a lo thlen hma chuan Israel mi thenkhatte chungah chawlawlna a lo thleng rih a ni, tih hi;
26. chutichuan, Israel mi zawng zawng chhandamin an awm ang; "Zion atangin Chhandamtu chu a lo chhuak ang a, Jakoba hnen ata Pathian ngaihsak lohna a tibo ang,"
27. "Chu chu an hnena ka thuthlun tur chu a ni, an sualte ka lak bo hunah chuan," tih ziak ang khan.
28. Chanchin Tha lam ngaihtuah chuan nangmahni avangin hmelma an ni a; Thlan thu lam ngaihtuah erawh chuan thlahtute avang khan duh takte an ni.
29. Pathianin a thilthlawnpekah leh a kohnaah chuan a rilru a lamlet ngai si lo a.
30. Hmanah chuan Pathian thu in awih lo a, nimahsela tunah chuan an awih loh avangin khawngaihna in hmu si a,
31. chutiang bawkin, tunah chuan Pathianin a khawngaih che u avang hian, khawngaihna an hmuh ve theihna turin anni pawh chuan an awih ta lo a nih hi.
32. Pathianin a khawngaih vek theihna'n mi zawng zawng awih lohnaah a khung ta vek si a.
33. Pathian finna leh hriatna ngahzia chu a va thuk em! A rorelnate chu hriat theih rual leh, a kawngte chu chhui theih rual a va ni lo em!
34. Lalpa rilru chu tuin nge hria? Tunge a remruatsaktuah pawh tang tawh?
35. A rulh leh tur pawhin a hnenah tuin nge thil pe hmasa?Amah atang leh, amah avang leh amah atan thil zawng zawng a lo awm a ni si a; ama hnenah chuan chatuanin ropuina chu awm rawh se. Amen.
36. Amah atang leh, amah avang leh amah atan thil zawng zawng a lo awm a ni si a; ama hnenah chuan chatuanin ropuina chu awm rawh se. Amen.
