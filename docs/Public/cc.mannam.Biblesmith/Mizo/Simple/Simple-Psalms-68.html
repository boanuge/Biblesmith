1. Pathian chu tho se la, a dote chu tihdarhin awm se la; Amah hawtute pawh chu a hmaah tlanchhe rawh se.
2. Mei khu hi len bova a awm angin, anni chu len bo ang che: Khawihnun hi mei luma a tuiral angin, Mi suaksualte chu Pathian hmaah boral rawh se.
3. Mi felte erawh chu lawm takin awm se la; Pathian hmaah hlim rawh se: A ni, lawm takin hlim rawh se.
4. Pathian chawimawiin hla sa u la, a hming fakin zai rawh u; Tawlailira thlalerte kal tlang tura tan chuan lamlian tur chhung pawng u la; A hming chu JAA a ni; a hmaah chuan hlim rawh u.
5. Pathian a awmna thianghlima awm chu. Pa nei lote pa leh hmeithaite ngaihtuahtu a ni.
6. Pathian chuan mi malte chu chhungkaw zingahte a teltir thin a: Mi tangho chu a hruai chhuak a, vanneihnaah a hruai lut thin: Helhmangte erawh chu ram rovah an awm thin.
7. Aw Pathian, i mite hma hruaia i lo chhuah a, Thlalera i kal tlang lai khan, (Selah)
8. Lei hi a nghing dur dur a, vante pawh khi Pathian hmaah chuan a far a: Sinai tlang meuh pawh saw Pathian hmaah, Israel-ho Pathian hmaah chuan, a nghin dur dur kha,
9. Aw Pathian, nang chuan ruah tam tak i surtir a, I rochan chu, a chauh lai khan, i tinghet a.
10. I hote chuan chutah chuan awmhmun an khuar a: Aw Pathian, nang chuan i thatnain retheite tan i buatsaih a.
11. LALPA'n thu a pe a, Chanchin hriltu hmeichhe tam takin an hril darh a,
12. Sipaiho lalte chu an tlanchhia a, an tlachhia a: In nghaktunu chuan rallak sum chu a sem a.
13. Thuro thla tangkaruaa tuam a, A thla hmawr pawh rangkachak eng taka tuamangin Beram huang karahte i mu dawn em ni?
14. Engkimtitheian chutah chuan lalte a tihdarh lai chu, Zalmon tlanga vu sur lai ang a ni.
15. Basan tlang chu Pathian tlang a ni a; Basan tlang chu tlang sang tak a ni.
16. Tlang sang takte u, Pathianin a awmna tura a tlang duh chu Engah nge itsika in melh ni? A ni, LALPA chu chutah chuan kumkhuain a awm reng dawn a sin.
17. Pathian tawlailir chu a sing a sing, a sang a sang tam tak a ni: LALPA chu, Sinai tlanga a awm ang khan, chung zingah chuan hmun thianghlimah a awm ta.
18. Nang chu chung lamah i chho a, i mante chu salah i hruai a; Mihringte zingah thilpekte i dawng a, A ni, helhmangte zingah pawh; LALPA Pathianc hu an hnena a awm theihna'n.
19. Lalpa, kan phur ni tina min phurhsaktu, Min chhandamtu Pathian chu fakin awm rawh se. (Selah)
20. Pathian chu kan tan chhanhimtu Pathian a ni; Thihna ata chhuahnate chu Lalpa JEHOVA ta a ni bawk.
21. Nimahsela Pathianin amah dotute lu chu a sat phel phawk ang, A thiam lohnaa la kal zela lu vun sam buk tak chu.
22. Lalpa'n, "Basan ram atangin ka hruai leh ang a, Tuifinriat mawng thuk tak ata ch ka hruai leh ang,
23. I ke chu thisena i hnim theih a, I ui leite chuan i dote laka chan a neih theih nan," a ti a.
24. Aw Pathian, i kal laite an hmu a, Ka Pathian ka Lalber hmun thianghlima kal laite chu.
25. Nula khuangte bengho laiah chuan, Hla satute an kal hmasa a, englo tumtute chuan an zui a.
26. Inkhawmnaahte hian Pathian fak u la, Israela tuihnaa chhuakte u, LALPA chu fak rawh u.
27. Fa tlum Benjamin-hoin hma hruaiin Juda-ho mi liante leh an upahote, Jebulun-ho mi liante, Naphtali-ho mi lianten an zui a.
28. I Pathian chuan i thiltihtheihna chu thu a pe a: Aw Pathian, kan tan thil min tihsak chu tichak ang che.
29. Jerusalem-a i biak in avangin Lalhoin lawmmante an la rawn kensak ang che.
30. Sakawlh phairuang zinga cheng chu zilh la, Hnamdang, bawngchal leh bawng no rualte chu zilh la, Kuna an tangkate i hmaa na hlan hma chu; Indo chak mite chu tidarh rawh.
31. Aigupta ram atangin mi liante an lo chhuak ang a; Ethiopia-ho chu an kut Pathian lama phar turin an hmanhmawh ang.
32. Leilung ramte u, Pathian chawimawiin hla sa u la; Aw, LALPA fakin zai rawh u; (Selah)
33. Vante van hmasanga awmte chunga chuanga chu; Ngai teh, a aw a chhuah e, aw nasa tak mai chu.
34. Pathian hnenah chuan chakna pe rawh u: A ropuina chu Israel-ho chungah a awm a, A chakna chu van dumahte khian a awmAw Pathian i hmun thianghlim atangte chuan i hlauhawm a ni: Amah Israel-ho Pathian ngei chuan a mite hnenah chakna leh thiltihtheihna a pe thin a ni. Pathian chu fakin awm rawh se.
35. Aw Pathian i hmun thianghlim atangte chuan i hlauhawm a ni: Amah Israel-ho Pathian ngei chuan a mite hnenah chakna leh thiltihtheihna a pe thin a ni. Pathian chu fakin awm rawh se.
