1. Tin, luang, tiang ang hi mi a pe a, tichuan tuin emaw, "Tho la, Pathian biak in, maicham nen a chhunga betute nen teh rawh.
2. Biak in pawn lama hung chhung kawtlai erawh chu teh tel suh, chu chu Jentailte hnena pek a ni tawh si a; anni chuan Khaw Thianghlim chu thla sawmli leh thla hnih an rapbet ang:
3. Tin, ka thu hretu pahnihte hnenah chuan thu ka pe ang a, tichuan saiip puan sinin ni 1,260 chhung zawng thu an hril ang," a ti a.
4. Chung chu oliv thing pahnih leh khawnvar dahna pahnih khawvel Lal hmaa dingte chu an ni.
5. Tin, anni chu tupawhin tihnat an tum chuan anka atangin mei a lo chhuak a, an hmelmate chu a kangral thin. Tin, tupawhin anni chu tihnat tum ang se la, chutiang bawka tihhlumin an awm tur a ni.
6. Chungte chuan an thu hrilh chhung zawng ruah sur lo tura van tihpin theihna an nei; an duh hun apianga thisena chantir turin tuite chungah thu a nei a, khawvel hi hremna tinrenga hrem turin thu an nei bawk a.
7. Tin, an hriattirna thu chu an hrilh zawh huna leilawt atanga sakawlh lo chhuak chho tur chuan anmahni a do ang a, a hneh ang a, a tihlum ang.
8. Tin, an ruang chu khaw ropui kawtthlerah chuan a awm reng ang a; chu khaw ropui chu thlarau lam thu-ah chuan Sodom leh Aigupta an ti a ni, chutah bawk chuan an Lalpa chu khenbehin a awm kha.
9. Tin, mite zinga mi te, chite zinga mi te, tawngte zinga mi te, hnamte zinga miten ni thum leh a chanve an ruangte chu an en ang a, an ruangte chu thlana phum an phal lo ang.
10. Tin, an thih avangin leia awmte chu an lawm ang a, hlimna an siam ang a, lawmnate an inthawn bawk ang; zawlnei pahnihte chuan leia awmte chu an tihhrehawm thin avangin.
11. Tin, ni thum leh a chanve hnuah chuan Pathian hnen ata nunna thaw chu an chhungah a lo lut a, an kein an ding ta a; tichuan a hmutute chuan an hlau ta em em a.
12. Tin, an hnenah, "Hetah hian lo chho rawh u," tia van atanga aw ring tak an hria a. Tichuan, chhum zingah chuan vanah an chho ta a; an hmelmate chuan an hmu reng a.
13. Tin, chu mi lai tak chuan lir nasa takin a lo nghing a, khua chu hmun sawma thena hmun khat a chim ta a, lirnghingah chuan mi 7,000 an thi a; tin, a bakte chuan an hlau a, van Pathian chu an chawimawi ta a.
14. Chungpikna pahnihna chu a reh tawh a, ngai teh, Chungpikna pathumna chu a lo awm lawk dawn e.
15. Tin, vantirhkoh pasarihna chuan a han ham a, tichuan, "Khawvel ram chu kan Lalpa leh a Krista ram a lo ni ta, ani chuan chatuanin ro a rel ang," tiin, vanah chuan aw ring takte a lo chhuak ta nghal a.
16. Tichuan, Pathian hmaa an lalthutphaha upa sawmhnih leh pali thute chu an bawkkhup a,
17. "Aw Lalpa Pathian, Engkimtithei, awm mek leh awm tawh, i thiltihtheihna ropui tak chu laa ro i rel avangin lawmthu kan hrilh a che.
18. Hnamte chu an thinur a, tin, i thinur chu a lo thleng ve thung a, mitthi ro relsak hun leh i bawih zawlneite hnenah te an lawmman pek hun leh lei tichhetute tihchhiat ve thung hun chu a lo thleng bawk a," tiin Pathian chibai an buk a.Tin, vana Pathian biak in chu an hawng a, tichuan a biak inah chuan a thuthlunna bawm chu a lo lang ta a; tin, kawlphe te, aw te, khawpui ri te, lirnghing te, rial nasa tak te a lo awm ta nghal a.
19. Tin, vana Pathian biak in chu an hawng a, tichuan a biak inah chuan a thuthlunna bawm chu a lo lang ta a; tin, kawlphe te, aw te, khawpui ri te, lirnghing te, rial nasa tak te a lo awm ta nghal a.
