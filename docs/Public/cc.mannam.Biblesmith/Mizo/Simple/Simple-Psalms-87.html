1. A lungphum chu tlang thianghlimahte chuana awm.
2. LALPA chuan Zion kulh kawngkharte chu Jakob-ho awmna zawng zawng aiin a ngaina zakw a ni.
3. Aw Pathian khawpui, I chungchang thu ropui takin an sawi thin a ni. (Selah)
4. Mi hretute zingah an tel ve tih, Rahab leh Babulon chungchang thu ka sawi ang: Philistia leh tura hi, Ethiopia nen hian, en teh u; He mi hi chuta piang chu a ni.
5. A ni, "He mi leh saw mi saw amaha piang an ni a; Chungnungbera ngei chuan amah chu a tinghet ang," tih Zion chungchang thu chu an sawi ang.
6. LALPA'n, mi tin, hming a ziak hunah chuan, He mi hi chuta piang chu a ni," tiin a chhiar ang. (Selah)Hla satuten, a lamate nen chuan, "Ka tuihna zawng zawng chu nangmahah a ni, a awm ni," an ti ang.
7. Hla satuten, a lamate nen chuan, "Ka tuihna zawng zawng chu nangmahah a ni, a awm ni," an ti ang.
