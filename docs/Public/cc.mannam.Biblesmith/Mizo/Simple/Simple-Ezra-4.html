1. Juda mi leh Benjamina mite hmelma te chuan sal chhuakhoin LALPA, Israelte Pathian biak in an saksak tih an lo hriat chuan,
2. Zerubabela leh an chhungkaw puipate hnen lamah an va kal a, an hnenah, "Kan sakpui ve ang che u; keini pawhin in zawn ang hian in Pathian chu kan zawng ve a; heta min hruai chhotu Assuria lal Essar-haddona a lal lai atang khan a hnena kan inthawi thin a sin," an ti a.
3. Nimahsela Zerubabela leh Jesua leh Israelte chhungkaw puipa dangte chuan an hnenah, "kan Pathian in saksaknaa hian in tel vena kawng reng reng a awm lo e; Persia lal Kura thu min pek angin keimahniho ngawtin LALPA, Israelte Pathian kan saksak dawn a ni," an ti a.
4. Chutichuan chu mi rama mite chuan Juda mite kut chu an tihchauhsak a, an in sa chu chu an tibuai a.
5. Persia lal Kura lal chhung zawng, Persia lal Daria lal thleng hialin an thiltih tumte engmahlova siam turin sawipuitute an ruai a.
6. Chutichuan Ahasuera lal lai, a lal tantirh laiin Judai ram leh Jerusalem-a awmte hekna lehkha an phuah a.
7. Tin, Artazerzia lal lai chuan Bislama te, Mithredatha te, Tabeela te leh an thian dangte nen chuan Pesia lal Artazerzia chu lehkha an thawn a; an lehkha ziak chu Aramik tawngin Aramik hawrawpa ziak a ni.
8. Hotu Rehuma leh lehkha ziaktu Simsaia chuan Jerusalem-a mite hekin Persia lal Artazerzia hetiang lehkha hi an thawn a:
9. Lehkha thawntute chu: Hotu Rehuma leh lehkhaziaktu Simsaia leha thiante - roreltute leh, ram awptu te, Persia mi te, Elam rama awm thin Erek mi te, Babulon mi te leh Susan mi te;
10. Mi ropui Asurpanipalan sala a hruai Somorasia khuaah leh Euphrates lui thlang lama a awmtir takte an ni.
11. Lal Artezerzia chu hetiangin lehkha an thawn a: "Lalber Artezerzia chi i chiahhlawh Euphrates lui khawthlang lam ram miten chibai kan buk e.
12. I hnen atanga Juda mi lo chote chu kan hnenah Jerusalem-ah an lo kal a; khaw hel hmang leh sual chu an din tha a, a kulh chu an peih tawh a, a lungphum chu an siam tha tawh tih lal chuan lo hre rawh se.
13. He mi khua hi an din a, a kulhte an siam zawh chuan, chiah te, thil neih man te leh leiman te an chawi duh lo ang a, a tawpah chuan lalte tan chhiatna a lo ni mai ang tih lalin lo hre rawh se.
14. Lal ina chi liak kan nih avangin lal mawi lohna hmuh hi kan tan a fel lo hle si a;
15. chuvangin i pi leh pute thil chhinchhiah ziakna buah i zawn a, chu mi ziakna buah chuan i hmuh a, he mi khua hi hel hmang khua a nih leh lalte leh an ramte tana thil hlauhawm tak a nih a, an hel kumkhua a, chu mi avangin he khua hi a lo chia a ni tih i hriatna'n lehkha kan thawn a, lal kan hriattir a ni.
16. He mi khua hi an din a, a kulhte an peih vek chuan lui ral lama reng reng chuan chan i neih loh phah ang tih lal kan hriattir a ni," an ti a.
17. Chutichuan Lalber chuan chhanna a thawn a, "Hotu Rehuma te, lehkha ziaktu Simsaia te leh an thian dang Samaria ram leh Euphrates lui thlang lama awmte chibai ka buk a che u.
18. Kan hnena in lehkha rawn thawn kha ka hmaah fiah takin an chhiar a.
19. Tin, thu ka pe a, an zawng a, he khua hi hman atangin lal chunga an hel thin a, chutah chuan helna leh buaina a awm thin tih chu kan hmu chhuak a ni.
20. Jerusalem-ah khan lal thiltithei tak tak lui ral lehlam ram thlinga rorolte pawh an lo awm tawh a; chhiah te, thil neih man te, leiman te an la thin a.
21. Khang mite kha chawlhtir u la, thu ka pek hma loh chuan khua chu din su se.
22. Muang hauh suh u: engah tehlul nge lalte tana chhiatna thil pawi tak a lo zualkai ang?" a ti a.
23. Chutichuan lal Artazerzia lehkha chu Rehuma leh lehkhaziaktu Simsaia leh an thiante hmaa an chhiar zawh chuan hmanhmawh takin Jerusalem-a Judate hnenah an kal a, thuneihnain an ban luihtir a.Chutichuan Jerusalem-a Pathian in an bei chu an chawlhsan ta a; Persia lal Daria lal kum hnihna thlengin chawlhsan a ni ta a.
24. Chutichuan Jerusalem-a Pathian in an bei chu an chawlhsan ta a; Persia lal Daria lal kum hnihna thlengin chawlhsan a ni ta a.
