1. Tin, zing a lo nih veleh puithiamte chu upate leh lehkhaziaktute leh roreltuho zawng zawng nen an inrawn a, Isua chu an phuar a, an hruai a, Pilata hnenah an pe ta nghal a.
2. Tin, Pilata chuan a hnenah, "Judate lal i ni em?" tiin a zawt a. Tin, ani chuan a hnenah, "Ni e," tiin a chhang a.
3. Tin, puithiamte chuan kawng tam takin an hek a.
4. Tichuan Pilatan a zawt leh a, a hnenah, "Engmahin i chhang lo maw? Ngai rawh, an hekna che zozai hi!" a ti a.
5. Isua erawh chuan engmahin a chhang ta lo a; chutichuan Pilatan mak a ti hle a.
6. Tin, Pilatan kut niah chuan mi tang pakhat an ngen apianga chu a chhuahsak thin a.
7. Tin, mi pakhat Barabba an tih chu, helte zingah phuarin a lum reng a, chung chuan helnaah chuan tual an that a ni.
8. Tin, mipuiho an han chho a, an tan a tihsak thin anga ti turin an ngen ta a.
9. Tin, Pilatan a chhang a, an hnenah, "Judate lal hi chhuahsak che u i la in duh em?" a ti a.
10. (Puithiam lalten itsikna avangin an rawn hruai tih a hre si a)
11. Nimahsela puithiam lalte chuan mipuiho chu Barabba chhuahsak zawk dil turin an fuih pawrh a.
12. Pilata chuan an hnenah, "Judate lal in tiha hi engtin nge ka tih tak ang le?" tiin a chhang leh a.
13. Tin, anniho chu, "Krawsah khengbet rawh," tiin an au leh a.
14. Tin, Pilata chuan an hnenah, "Enga maw? Engnge a tihsual ni?" a ti a. Nimahsela anniho chu, "Krawsah khengbet rawh," tiin an au chiam a.
15. Tichuan Pilatan mipuiho tihlungawi a duh a, Barabba chu a chhuahsak ta a; tin, Isua chu a vuak zawhin kraws-a khenbeh turin a pe ta a.
16. Tin, sipaite chuan in hual chhung kawtlai, Praitorion an tihah chuan amah chu an hruai lut a, an pawla mi zawng zawng an ko khawm a.
17. Tichuan puan sen duk an sintir a, hling lukhum an phiar a, an khumtir a.
18. Tin, "Chibai, Judate lal!" tiin chibai an buk ta a.
19. Tin, a lu-ah chuan luangin an vua a, chil an chhak a, thingthiin chibai an buk a.
20. Tin, nuihza siamna'n an hman zawhin puan sen duk chu an hlip a, ama puan an sintir leh a, kraws-a khenbeh turin an hruai chhuak ta a.
21. Tin, mi veivak pakhat, ram lam ata lo kal, a kraws pu turin an man a - Simona, Kurini khuaa mi, Aleksandra leh Rufa pa chu.
22. Tin, Isua chu Golgotha hmunah an hruai a, (chu chu "Lu ruh hmun," tihna a ni.)
23. Tin, uain, murra nena pawlh an pe a, nimahsela a la duh lo a.
24. Tin, kraws-ah an khengbet a, an chan theuh tur hriatna'n thum vawrin a silhfente chu an insem a.
25. Tin, Isua an khenbeh chu fehrehsan lai a ni a.
26. Tin, a hekna lehkha a chung lamah an ziak a, "JUDATE LAL" tiin.
27. Tin, ama rual chuan suamhmang pahnih an khengbet ve a; a ding lamah pakhat, a vei lamah pakhat.
28. Tichuan, 'Bawhchhetute zinga chhiar telin a awm a,' tih Pathian Lehkha Thu hi a lo thleng ta.
29. Tin, mi vei apiangte chuan an lu thingin "Ehei! nang Pathian biak in thiata ni thuma sa lehtu,
30. intidam la, kraws ata kha lo chhuk ta che," tiin an tiel a.
31. Chutiang bawkin puithiam lalte chuan lehkhaziaktute nen anmahni zingah tielin, "Mi dang mah a tidam thin a, mahni a intidam thei si lo.
32. Kan hmuha kan rin theihna'n, Krista, Israel lal chu, tunah hian kraws ata chu lo chhuk ang hmiang," an ti a. Tin, ama ruala khenbeh vete chuan ani chu an hau a.
33. Tin, chawfak hun a lo nih chuan behliang mun hun thlengin khawvel a lo thim ta vek a.
34. Tin, behliang mun hun lai chuan Isua chu aw ring takin, "Eloi, Eloi, lama sabakthani?" tiin a au chhuak a, (chu chu "Ka Pathian, ka Pathian, engah nge mi kalsan?" tihna a ni.)
35. Tin, a kianga ding thenkhatte chuan chu chu an hriatin, "Ngai teh u, Elija a ko a ni," an ti a.
36. Tin, mi pakhat a tlan a, spanj uain thura chiah khatin, tiangah a tar a, a han intir a."Awm rawh se, amah la thla turin Elija a lo kal emaw i en ang u," a ti a.
37. Tin, Isua chu aw ring takin a au a, a thlarau a thlah ta a.
38. Tin, Pathian biak in puanzar chu a chung lam atangin a hnuai lam thlengin a lo thler chhuak ta a.
39. Tin, a hma zawna sipai za hotu ding chuan a thlarau chutianga a thlah a hmuh chuan, "He mi hi Pathian Fapa a ni ngei mai," a ti a.
40. Tin, hmeichhiate pawh hla taka thlirin an awm a; chung zingah chuan Mari Magdalini te, Mari, Jakoba te leh Josea nu te, Salomi te an tel a,
41. (chung chu Galili rama a awm lai a, amah zuia a rawng bawlsak thinte chu an ni), hmeichhe dang tam tak, Jerusalem khuaa ama ruala chho vete chu an tel bawk a.
42. Tin, khua a lo tlai lam chuan Inrinni a nih avangin (chu chu Chawlhni hmaa ni hi a ni),
43. Josefa, Arimathai khuaa mi, roreltu zahawm, Pathian ram beisei ve mi bawk, a lo kal a, huai takin Pilata hnenah a lut a, Isua ruang chu a dil ta a.
44. Tichuan Pilatan chutia a thi ta thuai chu mak a ti hle a, tichuan sipai za hotu chu koin, a thihna a rei tawh emaw tih a zawt a.
45. Tin, sipai za hotu kaa a hriat chuan, a ruang chu Josefa hnenah a pe a.
46. Tin, ani chuan puan zai sin a lei a, amah chu a la thla a, puan zai sin chuan a tuam a, thlan, lung kerkuakah a zalh a, thlan kawngkaah chuan lung a lum ta a.Tin, Mari Magdalini leh Josea nu Mari chuan an zalhna chu an hmu reng a.
47. Tin, Mari Magdalini leh Josea nu Mari chuan an zalhna chu an hmu reng a.
