1. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia a bia a,
2. "Arona leh a fapate hnena sawi la, Israel thlahte thil thianghlim, ka tana an hlan lakahte chuan inthiarfihlim rawh se, ka hming an tihhliau dah ang e. Kei hi LALPA chu ka ni a.
3. An hnenah, 'In chhuante zel thlenga in thlahte zawng zawng zinga mi, tupawh bawlhhlawh chunga Israela thlahten LALPA tana an hlan thil thianghlim rawn hnaih apiang chu ka lak ata tuithlarin a awm tur a ni. Kei hi LALPA chu ka ni.
4. Arona thlah tupawh phar emaw, thil puut emaw chuana thianghlim hma loh chuan thil thianghlimte zinga mi chu a ei tur a ni lo. Tin, tupawh, mitthi tihbawlhhlawh emaw, chi hhuaka emaw khawih apiang te,
5. tupawh amah tibawlhhlawh thei, a bawha bawkvak reng reng emaw, bawlhhlawh kai thei nei mi emaw khawih apiangte;
6. chutiang khawihtu chu tlai lam thlengin a bawlhhlawh ang a, tin, a inbual loh chuan thil thianghlimte zinga mi chu a ei tur a ni lo.
7. Tin, ni a tlak chuan a thianghlimt awh ang; chu mi hnuah chuan thil thianghlimte zinga mi chu a ei thei tawh ang, a ei tur a ni avangin,
8. Intibawlhhlawh turin amaha thi emaw, asa she emaw a ei tur a ni lo. Kei hi LALPA chu ka ni.
9. Chuvangin ka thupe chu a pawm tur a ni ; chuti lo zawng an tihbawrhban chuan a sual mawh an phur ang a, an thih phah ang. Kei hi LALPA, anamahni tithianghlimtu ka ni si a.
10. Ram dang miin thil thianghlim chu an ei tur a ni lo; puithiam hnena khawsa emaw, inhlawhfa emaw chuan thil thianghlim chu an ei tur a ni lo.
11. Amaherawhchu puithiamin tu emaw ama suma a lei a lei chuan, chu mi chuan a ei thei ang; tin, a ina piangte chuan a ei tur chu an ei ve ang.
12. Puithiam fanuin ram dang mi a neih chuan then thilhlan, thil thianghlimte zinga mi chu a ei tur a ni lo.
13. Amaherawchu puithiam fanu chu fa nei loin hmeithaiin emaw, makin emaw a lo awm a, a pa in lama a kir leh chuan a nulat lai ang bawk khan a pa ei tur chu a ei ve leh ang; nimahsela ram dang mi chuan an ei tur a ni lo.
14. Miin hre loa thil thianghlim chu a ei chuan a hmun ngaa thena hmun khat chu a dah belh ang a, thil thianghlim chu puithiam a pe tur a ni.
15. An thil thianghlimte ei chuan thiam lohna thlensaktu, khawlohna mawh chu inphurhtir turin Israela thlahte thil thianghlim LALPA hnena an hlante chu an tibawrhbang tur a ni lo a.
16. Kei chu LALPA, anmahni tithianghlimtu chu ka ni si a;' ti rawh," tiin.
17. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia a bia a.
18. "Arona leh a fapate leh Israela thlahte zawng zawng bia la, an hnenah, Israela chhungkaw zinga mi emaw, israelte zinga awm, ram dang mi emaw, tupawh an thilhlan, halral thilhlana LALPA hnena an hlan, an thutiamna emaw, mahni duh thua thilhlan ema hlan apiang chuan thua thilhlan ema hlan apiangte chuan
19. chhan tlak in nih theihna turin bawng emaw, beram emaw, kel emaw, a pa sawiselbo in hlan tur a ni.
20. Sawisel kai reng reng chu in hlan tur a ni lo; pawmsak tlak cheu a ni bawk si lo a.
21. Tupawh thutiam tikimchang turin emaw, mahni duh thua thilhlanah emaw remna thilhlan inthawina, a ran rual zinga mi emaw, a beram rual zinga mi emaw LALPA hnena hlan apiang chuan, pawm tlak a nih theihna'n sawiselbo a hlan tur a ni a; sawisel kaina reng reng a awm tur a ni lo.
22. A mitdel te, a ruh tliak te, a silawng te, pan nei te, a tangseh te, a phar te chu LALPA hnenah a hlan tur a ni lo a, meia thilhlanah LALPA hnenah maichamah in hlan tur a ni lo.
23. mahni duh thua thilhlanah LALPA hnenah maichamah in hlan tur a ni lo.
24. Mahni duh thua thilhlanah chuan bawngpa emaw, beram no emaw fuke sei lua emaw, tawi lua emaw pawh in hlan thei ang a; thutiamna'n erawh chu pawmsak a ni lo ang," tiin.
25. Ramdang mi a tanga lain chung zinga mi reng reng chu in Pathian ei tur chiahah in hlan tur a ni lo, chunga chhiatna leh sawisel kaina a awm avang chuan; pawmsak theih che u a ni lo ang,' ti rawh,"
26. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia a bia a,
27. "Bawngpa emaw, beram emaw, kel emaw, a lo pianin a pui hnenah ni sarih a awm tur a ni; ni riat ni atanga chu mi hnu lamah chuan LALPA tana meia thilhlanah pawm theih a ni ang.
28. Bawngpui emaw, berampui emaw, a ni nen ni khatah in talh tur a ni lo.
29. Tin, lawmthu sawi inthawia LALPA hnena in inthawi hunin chhan tlak in nih theihna turin in inthawi tur a ni.
30. Chu mi ni chuan ei zawh tur a ni a, a tuk thlengin engmah in khek tur a ni lo. Kei hi LALPA chu ka ni.
31. Chuvangin ka thupekte hi in pawmin in zawm tur a ni. Kei hi LALPA chu ka ni.
32. Tin, ka hming thianghlim hi in tihliau tur a ni lo a, Israela thlahte zingah chawimawin ka awm tur a ni. Kei hi nangmahni chawimawitu,in Pathian ni tura Aigupta ram ata hruai chhuaktu che u ka ni. Kei hi LALPA chu ka ni," tiin.
33. in Pathian ni tura Aigupta ram ata hruai chhuaktu che u ka ni. Kei hi LALPA chu ka ni," tiin.
