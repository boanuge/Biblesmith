1. "LALPA inah i kal ang u," Ka hnena an tih lai khan ka lawm a,
2. Aw Jerusalem, i kulh kawngkhar chhungah Kan kete chu a din tak hi;
3. Jerusalem, khawpui din thar Rinawm taka rem chu:
4. LALPA hming fak turin Chutah chuan hnamte chu an chho thin, LALPA hnamte chu, Israel-ho hnena thuhriattirna atan.
5. Chutah chuan rorelna'n lalthutphahte hun a ni si a Davida chhungte lalthutphahte chu,
6. Jerusalem thlamuanna tur dilsak rawh u: Nang ngainatute chu an thawveng ang.
7. I kulh chhungahte chuan thlamuanna awm se la, I lal inahte chuan thawvenna awm rawh se.
8. Ka unaute leh ka thiante avangin "I chhungah thlamuanna awm rawh se," ka ti ang.LALPA kan Pathian in avang chuan I thatna tur ka zawng ang.
9. LALPA kan Pathian in avang chuan I thatna tur ka zawng ang.
