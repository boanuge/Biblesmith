1. Hengte hi a ni chung ram lal, Israela thlahten an tihboral a, an ram Jordan lui ral, ni chhuahna lal ram, Arnon chhak lampanga Araba zawng zawng nena an neihsak lalte chu:
2. he'ng Amor-ho lal Shihona, Hebona awm chuan Arnon ruam sira awm Aroer khua atangin ro a rel a ni a. tin, ruam lai taka khawpui te, Amona thlahte ramri Jabok lui thlengin Gilead ram zatve chungah chuana rel bawk a;
3. tin, chhak lamah chuan Araba-ah Kineroth dil thlengin a ni a; tin, chhak lamah vek Beth-jesimoth kalna kawng lamah Araba tuifinriat, Tuifinriat Al thlengin a ni a; tin, chhim lamah Pisga awih pangper hnuai thlengin a ni a a;
4. tin, Astaroth leh Edrei khuaa awm Repahim hnam la awm chhunte zingah chuan Basan lal Oga a lal a:
5. ani chuan Hermon tlangah te, Salka khuaah te leh Basan ram zawng zawngah Gesurbon lal Sihona ramri chin Gilead ram zatve thlengin ro a rel a.
6. LALPA chhihhlawh Mosia leh Israela thlahte chuan chunghote chu an that a: tin, LALPA chhiahhlawh Mosia chuan Reuben chi e, Gad chi te, manase hnam zatvete chu an rochanah a sem a.
7. Tin, he'ng ram lalho hi a ni Josua leh Israela thlahten Jordan lui ral thlang lampanga an thlahte chu, Lebanon ruam Baal-gad atanga Seir tlang han sutu Halak tlang thlengin; tin, Josua chuan Israel hnamte an inthen dan ang zelin chu ram chu an rochan atan a pe hlawm a;
8. tlang ramah te, phai ramah te, Araba-ah te, tlang pangper amah te, thlaler ramah te, Chhim ramah te, chung chu Hit mi te, Amor mi te, Kanaan mi te, Periz m te, Hiv mi te, Jebus mi te ram kha a ni.
9. Jeriko khaw lal, pakhat, Bethel khaw depa Ai khaw lal, pakhat,
10. Jerusalem khaw lal, pakhat, Hebron khaw lal, pakhat,
11. Jarmuth khaw lal, pakhat, Lakis khaw lal, pakhat,
12. Eglon khaw lal, pakhat, Gezer khaw lal, pakhat,
13. Debir khaw lal, pakhat, Geder khaw lal, pakhat,
14. Horma khaw lal, pakhat, Arad khaw lal, pakhat,
15. Libna khaw lal, pakhat, Adulam khaw lal, pakhat,
16. Makeda khaw lal, pakhat, Bethel khaw lal, pakhat,
17. Tapua khaw lal, pakhat, Hepher khaw lal, pakhat,
18. Aphek khaw lal, pakhat, Lasaron khaw lal, pakhat,
19. Madon khaw lal, pakhat, Hazor khaw lal, pakhat,
20. Simronmeron khaw lal, pakhat, Aksaph khaw lal, pakhat,
21. Taanak khaw lal, pakhat, Megido khaw lal, pakhat,
22. Kedes khaw lal, pakhat, Karmel tlanga Joneam khaw lal, pakhat,
23. Dor khaw zosanga Dor khaw lal, pakhat; Gilgal-a Goim hnamho lal, pakhat,Tirza khaw lal, pakhat; lal zawng zawng chu sawmthum leh pakhat an ni.
24. Tirza khaw lal, pakhat; lal zawng zawng chu sawmthum leh pakhat an ni.
