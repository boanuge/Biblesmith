1. Tin, Davida chuan htou zawng zawng, sang hotute leh za hotute chu a rawn hlawm a.
2. Tin, Davida'n Israel pung khawmho zawng zawng hnenah chuan, "Tha in tihzawng a nih a, LALPA kan Pathian remtihzawng ani bawk chuan, Israel ram zawng zawng akan unau la awmate chu, dai vel nei khuaa awm puithiamte leh Levi-hote nen kan hnena rawn inkhawm ve tur hian i sawm vel ang u hmiang.
3. Tin, kan Pathian bawm hi i la leh teh ang u Sauala lal lai chan kan ngaihven hau lo mai a," a ti a.
4. Tichuan pungkhawmho zawng zawng chuan, chutianga tih chu an duh a, mi zawng zawng ngaiha thil dik a ni reng a.
5. Chutichan Davida'n Aigupta lui Sihor atanga Hamath khaw luhna thlenga Israel mi zawng zawngte chu la turin a khawm vek a.
6. Chutichuan Davida leh mi zawng zawng te chuJudai rama Baala Kiriath-jearim an tihah chuan Cherub-ho chunga thu LALPA hmiong chuanna thuthlung bawm chu la turin an han chho a.
7. Tin, Pathian bawm chu Abinadaba in ata tawlailir tharin an kalpui a, Uza leh Ahiovan tawlailir chu an khal a.
8. Tin, Davida leh Israel mi zawng zawngte chuan hal sain, tingtang te, perhkhuang te, khuang te, darbenthek te, tawtawrawt te nen, pathian hmaah an lam bung bung a.
9. Tin, Kidron hruih an han thlen chuan, Uzan bawm chu chelh tumin a han ban a, bawngte chu an pai an pai avangin.
10. Tin, LALPA thin chu Uza chngah a thawk ta thut mai a, bawm a chelh avang chuan a tihlum ta a; chutah chuan Pathian hmaah a thi ta a.
11. Tin, LALPA chuan Uza chunga a kut a thlak avangin, Davida chu a thinur a; chu mi hmun chu Perez-uza a vuah a; tun thleng hian a la ni ta reng a.
12. Tin, Davida chuan chu mi ni chuan Pathian chu a hlau a, Engtin nge Pathian bawm chu ka hawn theih tak ang?" a tia.
13. Chutichuan Davida chuan bawm chu Davida khuaah ama hnenah chuan a suan ta lo a, Git mi Obed-edoma inah a da ta zawk a.Tin, Pathian bawm chu Git mi Obed-edoma inah, a chhungte hnenah chuan thla thum a awm a; tichuan Obed-edoma chhungte leh aneih zawng zawngte chu LALPA'n mal a sawm a.
14. Tin, Pathian bawm chu Git mi Obed-edoma inah, a chhungte hnenah chuan thla thum a awm a; tichuan Obed-edoma chhungte leh aneih zawng zawngte chu LALPA'n mal a sawm a.
