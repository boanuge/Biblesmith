1. Tin, ka han en a, tin ngai teh, Beram No Zion Tlanga ding leh an chala a hming leh a Pa hming ziak, mi 1,44,000 a hnenah ka hmu a.
2. Tin,van atanga englo ri,tui tam tak ri ang leh khawpui ri nasa tak ri ang ka hria a; tin, ka ri hriat chu tingtang kaituten an tingtang kaih ri ang a ni a;
3. lalthutphah hmaah leh Thilnung pali leh upate hmaah chuan hla thar ni awm tak hi an sa a; tin, lei ata tlan tawh mi 1,44,000 te chauh lo kha chuan tuma'n chu hla chu an zir thei lo.
4. Chung chu hmeichhe laka intibawlhhlawh lote chu an ni; nula thianghlim ang an ni si a. Chung chu Beram No a kalna apianga zuitute chu an ni. Chung chu Pathian tan leh Beram No tan rah hmasa ber ni turin mihring zing ata tlan an ni.
5. An kaah dawt a chhuah reng miin an hre ngai lo; sawiselbo an ni si a.
6. Tin, leia awmte hnenah leh hnam tinreng te, chi tinreng te, tawng tinreng te, mi tinreng te hnena tlangaupui tura chatuana Chanchin Tha nei vantirhkoh dang vanlaizawla thlawk ka hmu a;
7. tin, ani chuan aw ring takin, "A rorel hun chu a lo thlen tak avangin Pathian chu hlau u la, chawimawi rawh u; van te, lei te, tuifinriat te, tuihna te siamtu chu chibai buk rawh u," a ti a.
8. Tin, vantirhkoh dang a pahnihna chuan, "Babulon khaw ropui, a mi ngaih chin vanga thinurna uain hnam zawng zawng intirtu chu a tlawm ta, a tlawm ta," tiin a rawn zui a.
9. Tin, vantirhkoh dang a pathumna chuan aw ring takin, "Tupawhin Sakawlh leh a lem chibai bukin an chalah emaw an kutah emaw chhinchhiahna an neih chuan,
10. chu mite ngei chuan Pathian thinurna uain, a thinurna nova engmah pawlh loa siam chu an in ang a, vantirhkoh thianghlimte mithmuh leh Beram No mithmuhah chuan mei leh kata nghaisakin an awm bawk ang.
11. Tin, chung mite nghaisakna meikhu chu chatuanin a chho ang a; sakawlh leh a lem chibai buktute, a hming chhinchhiahna nei apiangte pawh chuan achhun azanin chawlh reng an nei lo ang," tiin anni chu a rawn zui a.
12. Mi thianghlim, Pathian thupek awihtute leh Isua zuitu rinawmte tan hetah hian chhel a tulzia a lang.
13. Tin, van atangin awin, "Heti hian ziak rawh:Tun hnuah chuan mitthi, Lalpaa thite chu an eng a thawl ang; Thlarauin, a ni, an thawhrimnate chu an chawlhsan tawh ang:an thiltihte chuan a rawn zui ve dawn si a, a ti e," a tih ka hria.
14. Tin, ka han en a, tin, ngai teh, chhum var ka hmu a; tin, chhum chungah chuan mihring fapa ang tak hi rangkachak lallukhum khuma favah hriam tak kenga thu ka hmu bawk a.
15. Tin, vantirhkoh dang, Pathian biak in atangin a lo chhuak a, chhum chunga thu hnenah chuan aw ring takin, "I favah kha phawrh la, at rawh; ah a hun tawh si a; lei buh khu a ngui tawh khu," a ti a.
16. Tichuan, chhum chunga thu chuan a favah chu leiah a thlak a, lei buh chu an at ta a.
17. Tin, vantirhkoh dang vana Pathian biak in atangin a lo chhuak leh a, ani pawh chuan favah hriam tak a keng bawk a.
18. Tin, vantirhkoh dang, mei chunga thuneitu chu maicham atangin a lo chhuak leh a; tin, ani chuan aw ring takin, "I favah hriam tak kha phawrh la, leia grep hrui rah bawrte khu zuk at rawh; grep rahte khu a hmin tuah tawh khu," tiin favah hriam tak kengtu chu a au a.
19. Tichuan, vantirhkoh chuan a favah chu leiah a thlak a, leia grep rah chu a at a, grep sawrna khur zau tak, Pathian thinurna grep sawrna khurah chuan a theh lut a.Tin, khaw pawn lama grep sawrna khurah chuan an rap sawr a, tichuan, grep sawrna khur atang chuan thisen a lo chhuak a, fing 1,600 chen, nghawng thlenga thuk a ni.
20. Tin, khaw pawn lama grep sawrna khurah chuan an rap sawr a, tichuan, grep sawrna khur atang chuan thisen a lo chhuak a, fing 1,600 chen, nghawng thlenga thuk a ni.
