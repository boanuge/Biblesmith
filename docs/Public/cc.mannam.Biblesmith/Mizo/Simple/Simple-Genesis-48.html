1. Tin, chung hnuah chuan heti hi a ni a, Josefa hnenah, "Ngai teh, i pa a dam lo ve," an ti a. Tichuan a fapate pahnih Manasea leh Ephraima chu a hnenah a hruai ve a.
2. Tin, Jakoba hnenah "Ngai teh, i fapa Josefa i hnenah a lo kal e," tiin an hrilh a: tichuan Israela chu a intiharh a, khumah chuan a thu ta a.
3. Tin, Jakoba'n Josefa hnenah, "Pathian Engkimtitheia chu Kanaan rama Luz khuaah chuan ka hnenah a inlar a, mal mi sawm a,
4. ka hnenah, 'Chi tam tak ka thlahtir ang chia, ka tipung bawk ang chia, chi hrang hrang awm khawmah ka siam ang che; tin, he ram hi i hnua i thlahte hnenah chuan kumkhaw ro atan ka pe ang,' mi ti a.
5. Tin, i fapate pahnih, Aigupta rama i hnena ka lo kal hmaa Aigupta rama i neihte hi ka ta an ni; Ephraima leh Mansea hi, Reubena leh Simeona ang bawkin ka ta an ni ang.
6. Tin, he mite hnua fa i neihte chu i ta an ni ang, an rochanah chuan an ute hming chu an tawm mai ang.
7. Tin, keia thu zawng, Paddan ram atanga ka lo kal lai khan Rakili chu Kanaan ramah kawng lakah, Ephrath khua chu a la hlat fe laiin ka bulah a thi a: chuta Ephrath khaw kawngah chuan ka phum ta a (chu chu Bethlehem khua a ni)," a ti a.
8. Tin, Israelan Josefa fapate chu a en a, "Heng hi tute nge ni?" a ti a.
9. Tin, Josefa chuan a pa hnenah, "Heng hi ka fapa, Pathianin heta mi pekate chu an ni," a ti a. Tin, ani chuan, "Khawngaih takin ka hnenah hian han hruai rawh, tichuan anmahni mal ka lo sawmsak ang e," a ti a.
10. Israela mit chu a upat avangin a fiah lo a, a hmu thei lo a ni. Tin, a han hruai hnai a, tin, ani chuan a lo fawp a, a lo pawm a.
11. Tin, Israelan Josefa hnenah, "I hmel hmuh hi ka beisei tawh lo a, nimahsela ngai teh, Pathianin i thlahte hmel lam pawh mi hmuhtir ta a nih hi," a ti a.
12. Tin, Josefa chuan anni chu a pa kapkar ata chu a kai chhuak a, a hmaia lei siin chibai a buk a.
13. Tin, Josefan an pahnih chuan a kai a, Epraima chu a kut ding lamin Israela kut vei lamin Israela kut ding lam pangah a kai a, a rawn hruai hnai a.
14. Tin, Israela chuan a ban kawkalh lui a, a kut ding lam chuan a ban a, Ephraima, a naupang zawk chhipah a nghat a; Manasea chu fa tir a ni si a.
15. Tin, Josefa chu mal a sawmsak a, "Mi thlahtu Abrahama leh Isaaka, a hmaa an awm thinna Pathian, vawiin thlenga ka damchhung zawng zawnga mi chawmtu Pathian,
16. sual zawng zawng laka mi tlantu Vantirhkoh chuan naupangte hi malsawm rawh se, tin, ka hming leh mi thlahtu Abrahama leh Isaaka hming chu anmahni hmingah pu rawh se; tin, khawvel lailiah mipui tam tak ni turin lo pung rawh se," a ti a.
17. Tin, Josefan a pain a kut ding lam Ephraima chhipa a nghat chu a hmuhin a lung a ni ta lo a; tichuan a pa kut Ephraima chhip ata chu Manasea chhipa sawn turin a chawi kang a.
18. Tin, Josefan a pa hnenah, "Ka pa, hetiang tur a ni lo: hei hi a sin fa tir chu ni, i kut dinglam hi a chhipah nghat zawk rawh," a ti a.
19. Nimahsela a pa chuan a duh lo a, "Ka hria e, ka fapa, ka hria e: ani pawh hnama sawi thamin a awm bawk ang a, ani pawh a ropui bawk ang: amaherawhchu a nau hi a aiin a ropui zawk ang a, a thlahte pawh chi hrang hrang tam tak mai an lo la ni ang," a ti a.
20. Tichuan Jakoba'n, "Israel-ho chuan Ephraima leh Manasea angin Pathianin awmtir che rawh se,' tiin nangmahni hming lamin mal an sawmsak thin ang," tiin chu mi ni chuan anni chu mal a sawmsak a: tichuan Ephraima chu Manasea hmaah a awmtir ta a.
21. Tin, Israela vek chuan Josefa hnenah, "Ngai teh, ka thi dawn ta e: nimahsela Pathian chu in hnenah a awm ang a, in thlahtute ramah chuan a hruai leh ang che u.Chu mi lo pawh chu, i unaute chanvo aiin chan khatin ka pe chuang a che, ka khandaih leh ka thalngula Amor-ho kut ata ka laksaka kha," a ti a.
22. Chu mi lo pawh chu, i unaute chanvo aiin chan khatin ka pe chuang a che, ka khandaih leh ka thalngula Amor-ho kut ata ka laksaka kha," a ti a.
