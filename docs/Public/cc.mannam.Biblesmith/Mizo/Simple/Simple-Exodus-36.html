1. Tin, Bezalela leh Oholiaba mi leh themthiam tupawh LALPA'n thu a pek ang zela hmun thianghlima rawngbawlna hna tinreng thawk thei tura finna leh hriatthiamna a pek apiangte chuan an bei tur a ni," a ti a.
2. Tin, Mosia'n Bezalela leh Oliaba leh mi themthiamho, LALPA'n an thinlunga finna a pek a, chu mi hna bei peih tura thinlung taka phur sarh apiang chu a ko va:
3. tin, Israela thlahten hmun thianghlima rawngbawl nana thil siam tura an rawn thawh zawng zawng chu Mosia hnen atangin an la zel a. Tin, tuk tin, duh thua thilhlante chu a hnenah an rawn hlen zel bawk a.
4. Tin, mi themthiamho zawng zawng, hmun thianghlim atana hna tinreng thawkte chuan an hnathawh lai chu antlansan theuh a,
5. Mosia hnenah, "LALPA'n siam tura thu a pek siamna'n mite chuan a chuang aliamin thil an rawn la e," tiin an thlen a.
6. Tin, Mosia chuan thu a lo pe a, tichuan, "Hmun thianghlim atana thil pek tur chu a hmei apa, tumahin rawn buatsaih tawh suh se," tiin an riahhmunah c chuan an tlangautir ta a. Chuti chuan mite chuan an rawn lak phal a ni ta lo a.
7. An neih sa chu siam tul apiang siamna tur chuan a tawk hle mai a, a tam lutuk mah a ni.
8. Tin, an zinga mi themthiam hna thawkho zawng zawngte chuan biak buk chu puanzar sawm neiin an siam a; chu puanzarte chu la zai sin tak kawp te, dum pawl te, sen duk te, sen lar te hmangin cherub lemte mi thiam tak siam chuantirin a siam a.
9. Puanzar hu a sei lam tawng sawmhnih leh tawng riat theuh a ni a, puanzar chu a hlai lam tawng li theuh a ni: puanzar zawng zawng chu a inchen vek a.
10. Tin, puanzar pangate chu a zawm vek a; tin, puanzar panga dangte pawh chu a zawm bawk a.
11. Tin, puanzar zawm khat hmawrtwng ber tlangah chuan a beng a dum pawlin a siam a: chutiang bawkin puanzar zawm lehlam hmawrtawng ber tlangah chuan a siam bawk a.
12. Puanzar pakhatah chuan beng sawmnga a siam a, tin, zawm khat puan zar tlangah pawh chuan beng sawmnga a siam bawk a: chung bengte chu a inep thliah a.
13. Tin, rangkachak chilhbehna sawmnga a siam a, a chilhbehnate chuan puanzarte chu a chilh bet a: tichuan biak buk chu pindan pakhat a lo ni t a.
14. Tin, biak buk chu khuhna'n kel hmun puanzarte a siam a: puanzar sawm leh pakhat a siam a.
15. Puanzar chu a sei lam tawng sawmthum theuh a ni a, tin, puanzar chu a hlai lam tawng li theuh a ni: puanzar sawm leh pakhatte chu a inchen vek a.
16. Tin, puanzar pangate chu athui zawm vek a, tin, puanzar paruk pawh chu a thui zawm vek a.
17. Tin, puanzar zawm khat hmawrtawng ber tlangah chuan beng sawmnga a siam a, tin, puanzar zawm lehlam tlangah chuan beng sawmnga a siam bawk a.
18. Tin, puan in chu pindan pakhat a lo ni theihna'n a chilh zawna turin dar chilhbehna sawmnga a siam a.
19. Tin, berampa vun chhum senin puan in chu khuhna te, savun tha miin a chua khu lehnate a siam a.
20. Tin, biak buk ban rem khawm turte chu ngil taka dingin thingri thingin a siam a.
21. ban rem khawm sei lam chu tawng sawm a ni a, tin, a vang lam chu tawng khat leh a chanve theuh a ni a.
22. Ban rem khawm tinah chuan ke pahnih inzawmin a zawm a: chutiang chuan biak buk ban rem khawm zawng zawng chu a siam a.
23. Tin, biak buk atan chuan ban rem khawmte a siam a; chhim chhawng chhim lampang atan chuan ban rem khawm sawmhnih a siam a:
24. tin, chu mi ban rem khawm sawmhnih nghah chhan atan chuan tangkaraw tlang kerkuak sawmli a siam a; a ke pahnih atan ban rem khawm pakhat nghah chhan kerkuak pahnih leh a ke pahnih atan ban rem khawm dang pakhat nghah chhan kerkuak pahnih a siam a.
25. Tin, biak buk sir lehlam hmar chhawng lam atan chuan ban rem khawm sawmhnih a siam a, tin, chhung atan chuan ban rem khawmsawmhnih a siam a.
26. tin, chu atan chuan tangkaraw tlang kekuak sawmli nen, ban rem khawm pakhat atan nghahchhan kerkuak pahnih nen, ban rem khawm dang pakhat atan nghah chhan kerkuak pahnih a siam a.
27. Tin, biak buk kawmchar khawthlang chhawng lam atan chuan ban rem khawm paruk a siam a,
28. Tin, biak buk kawmchar kil ve ve atan chuan chuan ban remkhawm pahnih a siam a.
29. Tin, a hnuai lamah chuan thlawp a ni a, chutiang bawkin a chhip lam kang hmasa ber thlengin thlawp a ni a, a kil pahnihah chuan chutiangin a ti ve ve a.
30. Tin, ban rem khawm pariat, a nghah chhan tur tangkaraw tlang kerkuak sawm leh paruk nen a awm a, ban remkhawm tin, innghahna'n kerkuak pahnih zel a awm a ni.
31. Tin, thingri thingin bangrel, biak bung ban nglehlam ban rem khawm atan panga,
32. biak buk bang leh lam ban rem khawm atan panga, tin, biak buk kawmchar khawthlang chhawng lam ban rem khawm atan pawh panga a siam a,
33. Tin, bangrel laita, ban rem khawm lai taka mi chu ban rem khawm h mawrtawng ber atangin a lehlam hmawrtawng ber atangin a lehlam hmawrtawng ber thlenga thil tlang turin a siam a.
34. Tin, ban rem khawmte chu rangkachak tiin a luan a, tin, bangrelte thilna'n rang kachak khangte a siam a, tin, chung bangrelte chu rangkachak tiin a luan a.
35. Tin, puanzar tur chu la zai sin tak kawp te, a dum pawl te, a sen duk te, sen lar te hmangin cherub lemte mi thiam tak siam chuantirin a siam a.
36. Tin, chu mi atan chuan thingri ban pali a siam a, chu chu rangkachak tiin a luan a: a bannate chu rangkachak a ni: tin, chu nghah chhan atan chuan tangkaraw tlang kerkuak pali a chu a.
37. Tin, Puan in kawngkaa zar tur la zai sin tak kawp te, a dum pawl te, a sen duk te, a sen larte hmangin, puan zeh mawi thiam tak zeh nen a siam a:tin, chu mi zarna tur ban panga a bannate nen a siam a: tin, ban lubawkte leh a zarfungte chu rangkachak tiin a luan a: tin, chu nghahchhan kerkuak pangate chu dar a ni a.
38. tin, chu mi zarna tur ban panga a bannate nen a siam a: tin, ban lubawkte leh a zarfungte chu rangkachak tiin a luan a: tin, chu nghahchhan kerkuak pangate chu dar a ni a.
