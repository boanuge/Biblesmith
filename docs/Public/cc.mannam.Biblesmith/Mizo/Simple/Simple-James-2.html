1. Ka unaute u, kan Lalpa Isua Krista, Ropuina Lalpa chu ring chungin, mi thlei bik nei suh u.
2. In inkhawmna inah mi rangkachak zungbun bun, puan mawi tak sin lo lut se la, tin, mi rethei puan chhe tak sin lo lut bawk se la;
3. tin, puan mawi tak sin chu in ngaihsak a, a hnenah chuan, "Nang he hmun thaah hian thu rawh," in tih a, tin, mi rethei hnenah chuan, "Nang chu sawtah sawn ding la, a nih loh leh ka ke nghahchhan hrulah hian thu rawh," in tih chuan,
4. in rilruah thlei bik neiin ngaihtuahna sual pua sawiseltute in lo ni dawn lo'm ni?
5. Ka unau duh takte u, ngai teh u, Pathianin he khawvel thila retheite hi rinnaa hausa tur leh, amah hmangaihtute hnena a tiam ram chu luahtu turahte a ruat lo em ni?
6. Nangni zawngin mi rethei chu in timualpho ta zawk a ni. Mi hausate chu a tiduhdahtu che u leh rorelna ina hnuk lut thintu che u chu an ni lo'm ni?
7. Hming zahawm tak in put hi sawichhetute chu an ni lo'm ni?
8. Nimahsela Pathian Lehkha Thu, "Nangmah i inhmangaih angin i vengte i hmangaih tur a ni," tih lal dan hi in zawm chuan in ti tha e;
9. amaherawhchu, mi thlei bik in neih chuan in tisual a ni, Danin bawhchhetu-ah a chantir si che u a.
10. Tupawhin Dan zawm kim mah se la, kawng khata a tihsual si chuan a zaah chuan thiam loh changin a awm tawh a ni.
11. "Uire suh," titu chuan, "Tual that suh," a ti bawk si a. Chutichuan uire lo mah la, tual i thah si chuan Dan bawhchhetu i ni reng reng tawh a ni.
12. Chuvangin,zalenna dana rorelsak turate angin thu sawi u la, thil ti bawk rawh u.
13. Mi chunga khawngaihna nei lo chu khawngaihna tel loin rorelsakin a awm ang; khawngaihna hi rorelna aiin a chungnung zawk a ni.
14. Ka unau te u, miin, "Rinna ka nei," ti mah se la, thiltih a neih si loh chuan eng nge sawt ang? Chutiang rinna chuan amah a chhandam thei ang em ni?
15. Unaupa emaw, unaunu emaw, saruaka awmin an ni tin chaw tlachham se la,
16. tin, in zinga tuin emaw an hnenah, "Thlamuang takin kal u la, lum tak leh puar takin awm rawh u," ti u la, taksa tana thil chakkhai chu in pek si loh chuan eng nge sawt ang?
17. Chutiang bawkin rinna chu thiltih nei loin amah chauhin a awm chuan thi a ni.
18. A ni, mi tuin emaw, "Nangin rinna i nei a, keiin thiltih ka nei a ni; i rinna chu i thiltih tel loin mi entir rawh, tin, keiin ka thiltihin ka rinna ka entir ang che.
19. Pathian chu pakhat a ni tih i ring a ni maw? I ti tha e; ramhuaite pawhin an ring a, an khur thin a sin," a ti ang.
20. Aw mi lawilopa, thiltih tel loa rinna mai chu engmah a ni lo tih i hre duh em le?
21. Min thlahtu Abrahama chuan a fapa Isaaka maicham chunga a hlan lai khan thiltih avangin thiam a chang a ni lo'm ni?
22. Rinna chuan a thiltih nen a thawk dun a, tin, thiltih avangin a rinna chu a famkim ta tih i hria e.
23. Pathian Lehkha Thu, "Abrahama chuan Pathian a ring a, chu rinna chu a felnaah ruat a ni," tih thu kha a lo hlawhthling a, tichuan Pathian Thianah vuahin a awm ta a.
24. Rinna avang ngawt ni loin, thiltih avang zawkin miin thiam a chang thin tih in hre ta.
25. Chutiang bawkin Rahabi nawhchizuar pawh khan tirhkohte kha a lo lawm a, kawng danga a hawntir lai khan thiltih avangin thiam a chang a ni lo'm ni?Thlarau tel loin taksa chu thi a ni, chutiang bawkin thiltihte tel loin rinna pawh chu thi a ni.
26. Thlarau tel loin taksa chu thi a ni, chutiang bawkin thiltihte tel loin rinna pawh chu thi a ni.
