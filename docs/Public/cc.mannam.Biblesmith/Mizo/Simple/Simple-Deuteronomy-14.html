1. LALPA in Pathian fate in ni a; mitthi avangin in inzai tur a ni lo. In mit hmul in met kawng tur a ni hek lo.
2. LALPA in Pathian tan hnam thianghlim in ni si a, leilunga hnam zawng zawng zinga a pual bik ni tura LALPA'n a tlan che u in ni a.
3. Thil tenawm reng reng in ei tur a ni lo.
4. Heng sate hi a ni, in ei theih tur chu; bawngpa te, beram te, kel te,
5. sakhi te, sangai te, sakhipa te, sathar te, ram kel te, ram lawi te, ram beram te hi.
6. Tin, sa tinreng, tin, kaka tin, kak chhuak, thialret thial chi tawh phawt zinga mi chu in ei thei ang.
7. Amaheawhchu he'ng thialret pawh thial si, an tin, pawh kak site hi chu, in ei tur a ni lo; sanghawngsei te, zupi te, szupui te hi, thialret chu an thial na a, an tin, a kak loh avangin in tan a bawlhhlawh a ni.
8. Tin, vawk hi a tin, chu a kak na a, thialret a thial loh avangin in tan a bawlhhlawh a ni; a sa in ei tur ani lo a, a ruang pawh in khawi tur a ni lo.
9. Tuia cheng zawng zawnga hengte hi in ei thei ang: pangparh leh phuhlip nei tawh phawt chu in ei thei ang a,
10. pangparh leh phuhlip nei lo apiangte chu in ei thei lo ang, in tan a bawlhhlawh a ni.
11. Sava thianghlim zawng zawng chu in ei thei ang a,
12. Hengte erawh hi chu in ei tur a ni lo: mipui te, lungdup te, muarla te,
13. mulukawlh te, ngawihup e, mute chi reng reng te;
14. choak chi reng reng e;
15. sava sang te, vabak te, tuifinriat sava te, mu chi reng reng te,
16. hrangkir te, chhimbuk te, chingpirinu te, chhimbuksialki te;
17. tuivalawng te, mulukawlh te, lui dung mu te;
18. kanghlai te, dil sava chi reng reng e, chhuangtuar leh bak te.
19. Thla nei kea kal chi zawng zawng te chu in tan thil bawlhhlawh a ni a,
20. Sava thianghlim zawng zawngte chu in ei thei ang.
21. Amaha thi chu engma in ei tur ani lo; in awmpui ram dang mite ei atan in pe thei ang a; hnam dang mite hnenah in hral thei bawk ang; nangni zawng LALPA in Pathian tana hnam thianghlim in ni si a. Kel note chu a pi hnutein in chum tur a ni lo.
22. Kum tin in loa thlai haw zawng zawng sawma pakhat in tihrang ngei ngei tur a ni.
23. LALPA in Pathian tih nachang in lo hriat fo theihna'n sawma pakhat, in buh te, in uain te, in hriak te leh in bawng rual leh in beram rual zinga piang hmasa berte chu LALPA in Pathian hmaah, a hming awmna tura a la ruat turah chuan in eit ur a ni.
24. LALPA in Pathianin mal sawmhun che u a, LALPA in Pathianin a hming awmna tur a la ruat tur hu a hlat lutuk avanga in tnan kawng a thui luattuka in ken theih loh chuan,
25. ah in chantir ang a, pawisa chu in keng ang a, LALPA in Pathian hmun ruatah chuan in kal tur a ni;
26. tin, pawisa chu in rilrua in duh ber, bawngpa emaw, beram emaw, uain emaw, zupui emaw, in rilrua in chak zawng ang apiang atan in hmang ang a; chuta chuan LALPA in Pathian hmaah in ei ang a, hlim takin in awm tur a ni, nangni leh inchhungte nen;
27. Levi-ho in awmnaa awmte chu thlauthla hauh suh ang che u; in zingah chanvo pawh, rochan pawh an nei ve si lo a.
28. Kum thum a vei kum apiangin chu mi kuma in thil hawng zawng zawnga sawma pakhat chu in la khawm vek ang a, in awmnaah in da tur a ni;tin, in awmnaa awm Levi-ho pawh, in zinga chanvo, rochan pawh nei ve lote leh ram dang mite leh pa nei lote leh hmeithaite chuan an ei ang a, an lo kahwsa ve thei ang; tichuan LALPA in Pathianin in kutkawih zawng zawngah chuan mal a sawm dawn che u nia.
29. tin, in awmnaa awm Levi-ho pawh, in zinga chanvo, rochan pawh nei ve lote leh ram dang mite leh pa nei lote leh hmeithaite chuan an ei ang a, an lo kahwsa ve thei ang; tichuan LALPA in Pathianin in kutkawih zawng zawngah chuan mal a sawm dawn che u nia.
