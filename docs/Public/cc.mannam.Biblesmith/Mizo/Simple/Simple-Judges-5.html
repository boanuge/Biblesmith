1. Chutichuan Debori leh Abinoama fapa Baraka chuan chu mi ni chuan,
2. "Hotute chuan Israel zingah zai an zap a. Mipuite chu chak takin an lo inpek tak hi, LALPA chu fak rawh u.
3. Aw, nangni lalte u, lo ngaithla u la; Aw, nangni hotute u, beng lo chhi rawh u; Keimah ngeiin LALPA chawimawiin hla ka sa ang a, LALPA, Israelte Pathian fakin hla ka sa ang.
4. LALPA, Seir ram ata i lo kal chhuah a, Edom phul ata i lo kal chhuah khan, Lei chu a nghing dur dur a, vante pawh a lo far a, A ni, chhungte chuan ruah a surtir a.
5. Tlangte chu LALPA hmaah a lo khur a, Sinai tlang lam pawh saw - LALPA Israelte Pathian hmaah chuan.
6. Anatha fapa Samgara dam laite leh, Jaeli dam laite khan, lamliante chu a thing thup a. Zin veivakte chu kawng mai maiahte an kal thin a.
7. Israel rama khuate chu luah lohin a ram zo a, a ram zo a. Kei Debori ka chet a, Israel ram nu hi ka chet hma loh kha chuan.
8. Pathian tharte a vuan a; Tichuan kulh kawngkhar bulahte khan indona a lo awm a: Israel rama singli zet zingah Phaw leh fei kha awm reng reng a awm em ni?
9. Ka rilru chu Israel-hote roreltu, Mipuite hnena chak taka inpete lamah chuan a awm: LALPA chu fak rawh u.
10. Nangni sabengtung var chunga chuangte u, Nangni puan phah chhah taka thute u; Nangni mi mirte u, chung thute chu puang rawh u.
11. Thal kaptute thawm reng reng hriat phak loh, Tui chawina hmunah chuan LALPA thil fel tihte chu an sawi an sawi ang, Israel ram chunga a thil fel tihte ngei chu. Chutichuan LALPA mipuite chu kulh kawngkhar lamahte chuan an zu chhuk a.
12. Harh la, harh la, Debor; Harh la, harh la, hla sa rawh.
13. Tichuan mi lian la awm chhungte leh mipuite chu an lo chhuk a; Mi chak tak mi beihpui turin LALPA chu a lo chhuk ta.
14. Ameleka zungzamte chu Ephraim atangin an lo chhuk a; Benjamin, i mite zinga miten an rawn zui che, Makir atangin ram roreltute an lo chhuk. Tin, Zebulun atangin ziaktu tiang kengtute chu an lo chhuk.
15. Isakar mi liante chu Debori hnenah an tel a; Isakara angin, Baraka pawh: Ruam lamah chuan amah nen an tlan chhuk chiam a. Reuben-ho tui luannahte chuan An tum a ruh hle mai.
16. Eng atan nge beram huang karahte i thut reng, Beram rual kohna phenglawng ngaithla tur maiin? Reuben-ho tui luannaahte chuan Ngun taka i ngaihtuah chhuahna a awm a,
17. Gilead-ho chu Jordan ralah an awm reng a: Tin, Dan-ho chu engah nge lawngah te an awm reng? Aser-ho chu tuifinriat kamah an thu hle hle a, An litengsawlahte chuan an awm reng.
18. Zebulun-ho leh Napthali-ho hi Phul hmun sanga thih huam reng mi hi.
19. Lalten an rawn bei a, Tichuan Kanaan lalte chuan Megido tui kiang, Taanak khuaha an bei a: Sum lak reng an nei lo
20. Van atangin an rawn bei a; Arsiten an hmun atangin Sisera chu an rawn bei a.
21. Kison luiin anni chu a hnawl vek a Hmasang lui, Kison lui chuan. Aw ka thlarau, chak takin kal zel rawh.
22. Tichuan sakawrte chu an per ta tlut tlut a, Sakawr chakte chu an zuan an zuan avangin.
23. 'Meroz chu anchhia lawh r'u,' LALPA vanrihkohin a ti, Chuta awmte chu nasa takin anchhia lawh rawh u; LALPA tanpuiin, LALPA'n mi chak tak a beinaa tanpui tura an lo kal loh avangin,'
24. Hmeichhia azawngah Jaeli, Ken mi Hebera nupui hi engthawl ber a ni ang, Hmeichhia puan ina chengho azawngah chuan a engthawl ber a ni ang.
25. Ani chuan tui a rawn dil a, hnute tui a lo pe a. Mi tha noin hnutetui khal a lo pe a.
26. Kilhna a keng a, A ding lamin hnathawk mi tuboh; tin, tuboh chuan Sisera chu a kheng a, a luah a kheng tlang a, A ni, a bekbawrah chuan a kheng a, a kheng bur ta ngur a.
27. A ke bulah a awp rawk a, a tlu a, a bawk ta der a: A ke bulah a awp rawk a, a tlu a, a tlu ta a: A awp rawkna hmunah chuan a thi ta der mai.
28. Sisera nu chu tukverah a han dak a, Zawngdaikalh awngah a au a, 'Engati nge a tawlailir a lo thlen har? Engati nge a tawlailir a muan? A ti.
29. A hmeichhe fingten an lo chhang a, A ni, amah a inchhang chawp leh a,
30. 'An hmu zo ta lo em ni? rallak an insem ta lo em ni ;e? Mi tinin nula pahnih khat emaw theuh; Sisera chan tur chu rallak puan ze hrang hrang, Rallak puan zehmawi, ze hrang hrang, Rallak nghawnga bah tur puan a khing hniha zehmawi, ze hrang hrang,' tiin.Chutiang chuan aw LALPA, I hmelmate zawng zawng chu boral vek se la: Amah hmangaihtute erawh chu chak taka kal, ni ang hi ni rawh se," tiin hla an sa a. Tin, ram chu kum sawmli chhung ralmuangin a awm a.
31. Chutiang chuan aw LALPA, I hmelmate zawng zawng chu boral vek se la: Amah hmangaihtute erawh chu chak taka kal, ni ang hi ni rawh se," tiin hla an sa a. Tin, ram chu kum sawmli chhung ralmuangin a awm a.
