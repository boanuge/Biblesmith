1. Juda lal Josia fapa Jehoiakima lal tantirhin he thu hi LALPA hnen ata a lo thleng a,
2. LALPA chuan heti hian a ti: LALPA in hung chhungah ding la, Juda khawpui zawng zawng Pathian be tura LALPA ina lo kalte hnenah chuan an hnena sawi tur thu ka pek zawng zawng chu pakhat mah zep loin sawi vk rawh.
3. Mi tinin an awm dan sual kalsanin an ngaithla mial mahna, an thil tih sual avanga an chunga thil tha lo tih ka tum chu ka lamlet theihna'n.
4. Tin, nangin an hnenah, "LALPA chuan heti a ti: mi ngaihthlak lo a, ka dan in hmaa ka dah in zawm lo a, zing taka thova tirin in hnena ka tirh ka rawngbawltu zawlnei thu in bengkhawn loh chuan (in lo bengkhawn tawh si lo va),
5. he in hi Siloh angin ka siam ang a, lei chunga hnam zawng zawngte anchhia dawng turin ka siam ang," ti ang che.
6. Tin, puithiam te, zawlneite leh mipui zawng zawngte chuan LALPA ina Jeremian he'ng thu a sawi hi an ngaithla a.
7. Tin, heti hi a ni a, mipui zawng zawngte hnen sawi tura LALPA'n thu a pek zawng zawng chu a sawi zawhin, puithiam te, zawlneite leh mipui zawng zawng chuan amah an man ta a. "I thi mawlh ang.
8. Engah nge LALPA hming chhala, 'He in hi Siloh ang a ni ang a, he khawpui hi luahtu awm loin a tiau vang,' tia i sawi ni?" an ti a. Tichuan LALPA inah chuan mipui zawng zawng chuan Jeremia chu an bawr ta huai huai a.
9. Tin, Juda hotute chuan he'ng thu hi an hriatin, lal in atangin LALPA in luhna Kawngka tharah an thu a.
10. Ti puithiamte leh zawlneite chuan hotute leh mipui zawng zawngte hnenah chuan, "He mi hi thi tlak a ni, khawpui chhiat tur thu, in benga in hriat angin a sawi lawk si a," an ti a.
11. Ti hotue leh mipui zawng zawngte hnenah chuan Jeremia chuan, "I thu ngaihthlak tak zawng zawng ang khan he in leh he khawpui chhiat tur thu sawi turin LALPA-in mi tir a ni.
12. Chuvangin tunah hian a awm dan leh in chet dan siam tha u la, LALPA in Pathian thu chu awih rawh u; tichuan LALPA chuan in chunga thil tha lo thleng tur a puan tawh chu a lamlet thei ang.
13. Kei rawh chu hei, in kuta ka awm hi. Dik leh tha in tih ang angin mi ti rawh u.
14. Mi tihhlum chuan, nangmahni leh he khawpui leh khua leh tuite chungah mi thiam thisen in thlen ngei dawn a ni tih hre mai rawh u; dik takin in benga he'ng thu zawng zawng hi sawi turin LALPA'n in hnenah mi tir zuk nia," a ti a.
15. Tichuan hotute leh mipuite chuan, puithiamte leh zawlneite hnenah chuan, "He mi hi thi tur a ni lo e, LALPA kan Pathian hminga kan hnena thu sawi a nih hi," an ti a.
16. Tin, chumi rama upa tue emaw an lo ding a, mipui awm khawmte hnenah chuan,
17. "Mika, Monaseth mi chuan Juda lal Jezekia lal lai khan thu a sawi a, tin, Juda mi zawng zawngte hnenah chuan,'Sipaihote LALPA chuan heti hian a sawi:Zion chu lo anga leh a ni ang a, Jerusalem chu thil chhe awmkhawm a ni ang, A in tlang chu ram ngaw hmun sang ang a ni ang,' a ti a.
18. Juda lal Hezekia leh Judate zawng zawng khan amah an tihlum em ni? LALPA hlauin LALPA duhsakna chu an ngen a, LALPA chuan an chunga thil tha lo thleng tur a puan chu a lamlet ta a ni lo em ni? Keini zawngin kan chungah thi tha lo kan thlen dan a ni hi.
19. Tin, mi dang, LALPA hminga thu sawi Keriath-jearima mi Semeia fapa Uria kha a awm bawk a, Jeremia thu sawi zawng zawng ang bawkin he ram leh khawpui chhiat tur thu a sawi a.
20. Lal Jehoiakima leh a mi ropuite zawng zawng leh hotute zawng zawngin an hriatin lal chuan tihhlum a tum a; mahse Urian a hriatin a hlau a, a tlan bo a, Aigupta ramah a kal ta a.
21. Tin, lal Jehoiakima chuan Aiupta ramah mi a tir a, Akbora fapa Elnathana leh mi dangte nen;
22. tin, Aigupta ram atangin Uria an hruai chhuak a, lal Jehoiakima hnenah an rawn hruai a, chu chuan khandaihin a tha a, a ruang chu vantlang thlanah an paih lut a," an ti a.Mahse Saphana fapa Ahitkama chuan mipui hnena tihhlum tura an pek lohna'n Jeremia chu atan a.
23. Mahse Saphana fapa Ahitkama chuan mipui hnena tihhlum tura an pek lohna'n Jeremia chu atan a.
