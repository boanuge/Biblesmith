1. Tin, Mosia'n Israela thlah pung khawmho zawng zawngte chu a ko khawm a, an hnenah, "Heng hi a ni hi LALPA'n in tih tura thu a pek che u chu.
2. Ni ruk chhung hna thawhna'n hman tur a ni a, a ni sarih ni erawh chu in tan ni thianghlim, LALPA tana chawlh derna chawlhni a ni tur a ni: tupawh chu mi nia hna thawk apiang chu tihhlum tur ani ang.
3. Chawlhni chuan in chenna hmun reng rengahte chuan mei in chhem tur a ni lo," a ti a.
4. Tin, Mosia'n Israela thlahte pung khawmho zawng zawng hnenah chuan, "Hei hi a ni hi LALPA'n thu apek che u chu,
5. in zing ata LALPA tna thilhlan tur rawn la rawh u: tupawhin a thinlunga pek chak apiang chuan LALPA tana hlan tur chu rawn la rawh se; rangkachak te, tangka te, dar te,
6. la dum pawl te, sen duk te, sen lar te, la zai sin te, kel hmul te nen;
7. tin, berampa vun chhum sen te, savun tha mi te, thingri thingte nen;
8. tin, khawnvar atan hriak te, hriak thih mi atan leh hal tur rimtui tak atan englo rimtui nen;
9. tin, puithiam kawr leh kawr awma zeh turin onukion lungte leh lunghlute nen.
10. Tin, in zinga mi themthiam apiang chu lo kal se la, LALPA'n siam tura thu a pek apiang chu siam rawh se;
11. biak buk te, a puan in te, a khuhna te, a chilhbehna te, a ban rem khawm te, a bangrel te, a ban te, a nghahchhan kerkuak te nen;
12. bawm leh a zawnfung te, zahngaihna thutphah te, a daidanna tur puanzar nen;
13. dawhkan leh a zawnfungte leh a hmanrua zawng zawng te, chhang dah te nen;
14. tin, eng atan khawnvar dahna te leh a hmanrua zawng zawng te, a khawnvar te, chhit en atan hriak te nen;
15. tin, rimtui halna maicham leh a zawnfung te, hriak thih mi te, hal tur rimtui tak te, tin, biak buk kawngkaa zar tur kawngka puanzar te nen;
16. halrala thilhlanna maicham, a dar len leh a zawnfungte leh a hmanrua zawng zawng te, maihun leh a hun chhante nen;
17. biak buk hung chhung kawtlaia puan zar te, a ban te, a ban nghahchhan kekuar te leh a hung chhung kawngkaa puanzar te nen;
18. biak buk kil behna te, a hung chhung kawtlai kilh behna te, a hrui te nen:
19. hmun thianghlima rawngbawlna tura silhfen mawi taka tah te, puithiam rawng a bawl huna hak tur puithiam Arona silhfen thianghlim leh a fapate silhfen te,' tiin," a ti a.
20. Tichuan Israela thlah pung khawmho zawng zawngte chu Mosia hnen ata an tin ta vek a.
21. Tin, mi tin, thinlung taka phur sarh apiang leh rilru taka phal apiang chu an lo kal a, inbiakna puan ina hman tur leh rawngbawlna lam atana thil hman tul apiang leh silhfen thianghlim tur chu LALPA tan an rawn thawh hlawm a.
22. Tin, a hmei apain pek chak apiangte chu an lo kal a, banbun te, bengbeh te, hming nemna zungbun te, thi te, rangkachak incheina tinrengte an rawn la a; tin, mi tin rangkachak hlan tur nei apiangin LALPA tan an hlan theuh a.
23. Tin, mi tin la dum pawl te, sen duk te, sen lar te, la zai sin te, kel hmun te , berampa vun chhum sen te, savun tha mi te nei apiangin an rawn keng hlawm a.
24. Mi tin, tangka leh dar te thawh apiangin LALPA tana thilhlan tur an rawn keng a: tin, mi tin, thingri thing, hmanraw tinreng atana hman tlak nei apiangin an rawn la a.
25. Tin, hmeichhe themthiamho zawng zawngte chuan la an kai a, tin, chu an la kaih dum pawl te, sen duk te, sen lar te, la zai sinte chu an rawn keng hlawm a.
26. Tin, hmeiche themthiam phur sarhho zawng zawngte pawhin kel hmun an kai a.
27. Tin, puithiam kawr leh kawr awma zel turin roreltuten onukion lungte leh lunghlute an rawn keng a;
28. tin, rimtui te, hriak te nen; chhit en atan te, hriak thih mi atan te, hal tur rimtui tak atan te pawh.
29. Israela thlahte chuan LALPA hnenah duh thua thilhlan an rawn hlan a: mi tin, a hmei apa, LALPA'n Mosia kuta thil siam tura thu apek zawng zawngte siamna tura thinlunga chak taka kengtue chuan.
30. Tin, Mosia'n Israela thlahte hnenah chuan, "Ngai teh u, LALPA'n Juda hnama mi Hura fapa Uria, chu mi fapa Bezalela chu a thlang tawh e.
31. Chu mi chu finna te, hriatthiamna te, hriatna te, kawng tinrenga themthiamna te nei turin PATHIAN thlarauin a tikhat tawh a,
32. thiltih tur fing taka suangtuah thiamna te, rangkachak leh tangkarua leh dar chher thiamna te,
33. lunghlute zeh tura phel thiamna te, thing zai mawi dan thiamna te, kawng tinrenga themthiamna te nei turin.
34. Tin, amah nen, Dana hnama mi Ahisamaka fapa Oholiaba nen mite zirtir theihna finna a pe ve ve a.Anni chu kawng tinrenga themthiamna te, englo ziak thiamna te, themthiamna te, la dum pawl te, sen duk te, sen lar te, la zai sin te zehmawi thiamna te, puan tah thiamna te, hnathawh tinreng thiamna te, thiltih tur fing taka suangtuah thiamna te nei turin thinlung finnain a tikhat tawh e.
35. Anni chu kawng tinrenga themthiamna te, englo ziak thiamna te, themthiamna te, la dum pawl te, sen duk te, sen lar te, la zai sin te zehmawi thiamna te, puan tah thiamna te, hnathawh tinreng thiamna te, thiltih tur fing taka suangtuah thiamna te nei turin thinlung finnain a tikhat tawh e.
