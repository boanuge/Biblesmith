1. Unaute u, ka thinlung duhzawng leh anni tana Pathian hnena ka tawngtaina chhan ber hi chhandama an lo awm theihna tur a ni.
2. Pathian lamah thahnem an ngai tih ka hriatpui a ni, hre famkim ang erawh chuan an ngai lo.
3. Pathian felna hre loin, anmahni felna tihngheh tumin, Pathian felnaah an intulut si lo.
4. Krista chu a ring apiangte tan felna an hmuh theihna turin Dan tibangtu a ni si a.
5. Mosia chuan, "Dana felna zawm chu, chu felna avang chuan a nung ang," tiin a ziak a.
6. Rinnaa felna erawh chuan heti hian a sawi: "In rilruin, 'Tunge vanah lawn ang?' emaw (chu chu chung lam ata Krista hruai chhuk tur tihna a ni);
7. 'Tunge leilawtah chhuk ang?' emaw (chu chu mitthi zing ata Krista hruai chhoh leh tur tihna a ni) ti suh u," tiin.
8. A nih leh,eng nge a sawi? "Thu chuanin a hnaih a che,i ka leh i thinlungah pawh a awm," tiin a sawi a ni; chu chu rinna thu kan hrilh thin hi a ni;
9. "I kain Isua chu Lalpa a ni tih i tan a, Mitthi zing ata Pathianin a kai tho tih i thinlunga i rin chuan chhandamin i awm ang," tih hi.
10. Miten thinlungin an ring a, felna an hmu thin a; kain an tan a, chhandamna an hmu bawk thin a ni.
11. Pathian Lehkha Thuin,"Tupawh amah ring apiang an mualpho lo ang,"a ti a ni.
12. Juda leh Grik mite chu engah mah an danglam chuang si lo a, Lalpa pakhat an zaa chunga Lalpa a ni a, a lamtu apiangte chungah chuan thatna a ngah si thin a.
13. "Tupawh Lalpa hming lam apiangte chu chhandamin an awm ang," tih a ni a.
14. A nih leh, an rin loha chu engtin nge an lam ang? A thu an hriat loha chu engtin nge an rin ang? Thu hriltu awm loin engtin nge an hriat ang?
15. Tirh an nih loh chuan engtin nge thu an hril ang? "Thil tha chanchin lawmawm thlentute ke chu a va mawi em!" tih ziak ang khan.
16. Nimahsela Chanchin Tha chu an za chuan an awih vek lo. Isaian, "Lalpa, kan thusawi tuin nge ring?" a ti si a.
17. Chutichuan rinna chu hriatna avangin a lo awm thin a, tin, hriatna chu Krista thu avangin a lo awm.
18. A nih leh, an bengin an hre lo em ni? Hria e le, "An thawm chu ram tinah a thang a, an thu chu kawlkil thlengin a thang chhuak ta."
19. A nih leh, Israel miten an hre lo em ni? "Chia sawi tham lohte chu hmangin ka thiktir ang che u a; chi fing lote chu hmangin ka tithinur ang che u," tiin, Mosia'n a sawi hmasa ber.
20. Tin, Isaian huai takin, "Mi zawng lote hmuhin ka awm a, mi lam lote hnenah hriattirin ka awm," a ti a.Amaherawhchu, Israel mite thu-ah chuan, "Mi tihmawh leh hnialhratte tan nilengin ka bante ka phar thin kha," a ti a.
21. Amaherawhchu, Israel mite thu-ah chuan, "Mi tihmawh leh hnialhratte tan nilengin ka bante ka phar thin kha," a ti a.
