1. Juda lal Josia fapa Jehoiakima lal laia LALPA hnen ta Jeremia hnena thu lo thleng chuan;
2. "Rebeka chhungte hnenah kalin va bia la, LALPA ina pindan pakhatah hruai lut la, uain in tur pe rawh," a ti a.
3. Tin, Habazinia fapa, Jeremia fapa, Jaazania leh a unaute, a fapa zawng zawngte, Rekaba chhungte zawng zawng nen ka hruai a;
4. tin, Pathian mi Igdalia fapa Hanana fate pindan, LALPA inah chuan ka hruai lut a; chu pindan chu hotute pindan bul kawngka vengtu Salluma fapa Maaseia pindan chunga awm a ni:
5. tin, Rekaba thlahte paho hmaah chuan berhbute, note uaina khat ka hung a, an hnenah, "Uain hi in rawh u" ka ti a.
6. Anni erawh chuan, "Uain chu ka in tawp lo ang; kan thlatu Rabaka fapa Jonadaba chuan 'Nangmahni leh in thlahte pawhin kumkhuain uain in in tur a ni lo;
7. inte in sain thlai chi in tuhin, grep huante in siamin in nei tur a ni hek lo: in dam chhungniahte chuan puaninahte in cheng zawk tur a ni, in awmna ramah chuan in dam rei theihna'n' tiin thu min pe a ni.
8. Tichuan kan thlahtu Rekaba fapa Jonadaban awkaa thu min chah zawng zawng, kan dam chhunga kei mahni leh kan nupuite, kan fapate leh kan fanutein uain in loh tur te,
9. kan chenna in sak lo tur te, grep huan emaw lo emaw thlai chi emaw neih loh tur chenin kan zawm a ni:
10. puaninahte chuan kan cheng zawk a, kan thlahtu Jonadaba thupek zawng zawng chu awihin kan zawm vek a ni.
11. Tin, heti hi a lo ni a, Babulon lal Nebukadnezzaran ram a rawn beih lai khan keini chuan, 'Hawh u, Kaldai sipai rualte leh Suria sipai rualte hlau avangin Jerusalemah i kal ang u,' kan ti a, tichuan Jerusalemah hian kan lo cheng ta mai a ni," an ti a.
12. Tin, LALPA thu chu Jeremia hnenah a lo thleng leh a:
13. "Israel Pathian, Sipaihote LALPA chuan heti hian a ti: Juda rama mite leh Jerusalema mi chengte hnenah va sawi la, ka thute ngaithla turin zirtirna chu an dawngsawng lo ang maw? Tih hi LALPA thu chhuak a ni.
14. Rebeka fapa Jonadaba thu, a fapa hnena a pek, uain in loh tur chu an zawm a, vawiin thlengin engmah an in lo a, an thlahtu thupek chu an zawm em em a ni: kei erawh chuan zing tak ata thoin in hnenah thu sawi a, mi ngai thla lo a.
15. Ka rawngbawltu zawlneite chu zing tak taka thoin in hnenah ka tir a, mi tin, in awmdan sual atangin lo kir vek u la, inthiltihte chu siam tha u la, pathhian dangte chu an rawngbawl turin zui suh u la; tin, nangni leh in thlahtute hnena ram ka pekah chuan in cheng ang tiin; mahse in beng in dawh lo a, mi ngaithla hek lo.
16. Rekaba fapa Jonadaba thlate chuan an thlahtu thupek an zawm a, he mite erawh hi chuan mi ngai thla si lo a;
17. Chuvangin LALPA Israel Pathian, Sipaihote Pathian chuan heti hian a ti: Ngai rawh u, Juda leh Jerusalema cheng zawng zawngte chungah chuan thil tha lo an chunga lo thleng tur ka sawi chhuah tawhte kha ka rawn thlen ang: an hnena thu ka sawi a, an ngaithla si lo a, anmahni ka auh leh mi chhang hek lo" tiin.Tin, Jeremia chuan Rekaba thlahte hnenah, "Siapihote LALPA, Israel Pathian chuan heti hian a ti: In thlahtu Jonadaba thupek in zawm a, a thuchhuak zawng zawng in zawm a, a thupek zawng zawng ang zela in tih avangin Rekaba fapa Jonadaba chuan kumkhuain ka hmaa ding tur mi a hmai lo ang," tiin Sipaihote LALPA, Israel Pathian chuan a ti.
18. Tin, Jeremia chuan Rekaba thlahte hnenah, "Siapihote LALPA, Israel Pathian chuan heti hian a ti: In thlahtu Jonadaba thupek in zawm a, a thuchhuak zawng zawng in zawm a, a thupek zawng zawng ang zela in tih avangin Rekaba fapa Jonadaba chuan kumkhuain ka hmaa ding tur mi a hmai lo ang," tiin Sipaihote LALPA, Israel Pathian chuan a ti.
