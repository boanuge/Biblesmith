1. LALPA chu fak rawh u. Mi dik takte inrawn khawmnaah leh inkhawmnaah chuan Ka thinlung zawng zawngin LALPA hnenah lawm thu ka hrilh ang.
2. LALPA thiltihte chu a ropui a, A chnga lawm apiangten zawn chhuah a ni thin.
3. A hnathawh chu a zahawmin a ropui a ni; A felna chu kumkhua aawm tur a ni a.
4. Ani chan a thilmak tihte chu a hriat rengtir a: LALPA ch mi khawngaih thei tak, lainatnaa khat a ni.
5. Amah tihtute chu ei tur a pe a: A thuthlun chu kumkhuain a hre reng ang.
6. A thiltihte thiltihtheihzia ch amite hnenah alantir a, Hnam tin rochan chu anmahni pein
7. A kut thiltithe chu thutak leh rorelna fel ani a; A thununna thu zawng zawngte ch a rinawm a ni.
8. Chung chu kumkhaw tlaitluan atan tihnghehin a awm a, Thutak leh dik taka tih a ni hlawm a.
9. Ani chuan amite hnenah tlanna a rawn tir a; A thuthlun chu kumkhua atan a ruat a: A hming chu a thianghlimin a zahawm a a ni.LALPA tihna chu finna bul a ni a; Chutianga ti apiangte chuan hriat thiamna tha an nei a: Amah fakna chu kumkhuaa awm tur a ni.
10. LALPA tihna chu finna bul a ni a; Chutianga ti apiangte chuan hriat thiamna tha an nei a: Amah fakna chu kumkhuaa awm tur a ni.
