1. Tin, van thar leh lei thar ka hmu a; van hmasa leh lei hmasa chu an boral tawh a, tuifinriat pawh a awm tawh lo a ni.
2. Tin, khaw thianghlim Jerusalem thar chu, mo a pasal ela a inchei anga peih diamin Pathian hnena mi van ata lo chhuk ka hmu bawk a.
3. Tin, lalthutphah atangin aw ring takin, "Ngai teh, Pathian bawkte chu mihring zingah a lo awm ta, ani chu an hnenah a awm ang a, anni chu a mite an ni ang a, Pathian ngei chu an hnenah a awm ang a, an Pathian a ni bawk ang;
4. An mit ata mittui zawng zawng a hrufai ang a; thihna a awm leh thei tawh lo vang a, lungngaih te, tah te, nat te pawh a awm leh tawh hek lo ang; thil hmasate chu a ral ta," a tih ka hria.
5. Tin,lalthutphaha thu chuan, "Ngai teh, thil engkim ka siam thar e,"a ti a. Tin, "Ziak rawh, he'ng thu hi a rinawmin a dik si a," a ti a.
6. Tin, ka hnenah, "A lo thleng ta. Kei Alpha leh Omega, a Bul leh a Tawp ka ni. Tuihal apiang hnenah chuan Nunna Tuikhura mi athlawnin ka pe ang.
7. Hnehtu apiang chuan chung chu an luah ang a, kei an Pathian ka ni ang a, anni chu ka fate an ni ang.
8. Amaherawhchu, mi dawih te, ring lo te, tenawm te, tualthat te, inngaih ching te, dawithiam te, milem bia te, dawthei zawng zawngte chantur chu mei leh kata kang dilah chuan a awm ang; chu chu thih hnihna chu a ni," a ti a.
9. Tin, berhbu pasarih keng, hremna hnuhnung ber pasarih sin vantirhkoh pasarih zinga pakhat chu a lo kal a; ani chuan, "Hawh teh, mo, Beram No nupui chu kan entir ang che," tiin mi bia a.
10. Tin, ani chuan Thlarauin tlang lian pui sang takah mi kalpui a, Jerusalem khaw thianghlim chu Pathian ropuina neiin, Pathian hnena mi van ata lo chhuk mi entir a.
11. A enzia chu lung man tam ber ang, jaspi lung ang mai hi a ni a, krustal lung angin a tle a ni;
12. kulh nasa tak sang tak a nei a; kawngkhar sawm leh pahnih a nei a, kawngkhar bulahte chuan vantirhkoh sawm leh pahnih an awm a; chung kawngkharah chuan hming ziak a awm bawk a, chung chu Israela thlahte hnam sawm leh hnam hnih hmingte chu a ni a;
13. chhak lam pangah kawngkhar pathum, hmar lam pangah kawngkhar pathum, chhim lam pangah kawngkhar pathum, thlang lam pangah kawngkhar pathum a awm a.
14. Tin, chu khaw kulh chuan lungphum sawm leh pahnih a nei a, chungah chuan Beram No tirhkoh sawm leh pahnihte hming a chuang a.
15. Tin, mi betu chuan khua leh a kawngkharte leh a kulh chu tehna turin rangkachak luang a keng a.
16. Khua chu inbial velin a awm a, a dung lam chu a vang lam chen a ni; khua chu luang chuan a teh a, fing 12,000 a ni. A dung lam leh a vang lam leh a san lam chu a inang a ni.
17. Tin, a kulh chu a teh leh a, mihring teh dan pangngaiin (chu chu vantirhkoh teh dan pangngai chu a ni) tawng 144 a ni.
18. Tin, a kulh chu jaspi lunga siam a ni; khua chu darthlalang thianghlim ang maia rangkachak thianghlima siam a ni.
19. Chu khaw kulh lung phumte chu lung man tam chi tinrenga cheimawi a ni. Lungmawi hmasa ber chu jaspi lung a ni a; a pahnihna chu sapphir lung a ni a; a pathumna chu kalkidon lung a ni a; a palina chu smaragdin lung a ni a;
20. a pangana chu sardonuk lung a ni a; a parukna chu sardion lung a ni a; a pasarihna chu kruso lung a ni a; a pariatna chu birul lung a ni a; a pakuana chu topazi lung a ni a; a sawmna chu krusopra lung a ni a; a sawm leh pakhatna chu huakintho lung a ni a; a sawm leh pahnihna chu amethusto lung a ni a.
21. Tin, kawngkhar sawm leh pahnih chu tuikeplung sawm leh pahnih a ni; kawngkhar zawng zawng chu tuikeplung pakhat theuha siam a ni; tin, chu khaw kawtlai chu rangkachak thianghlim a ni, darthlalang entlang theih ang mai hi.
22. Tin, a chhungah chuan biak in reng ka hmu lo a; Lalpa Pathian Engkimtitheia leh Beram No chu an biak in an ni si a.
23. Tin, chu khua chuan a en turin ni a ngai lo a, thla pawh a ngai hek lo; Pathian ropuina chuan a en a, an khawnvar chu Beram No a ni si a.
24. Tin, hnam tin a engah chuan an awm ang a; leia lalte chuan an ropuina an la lut ang.
25. Tin, a kawngkate chu chhunah an khar tawp lo ang, chutah chuan zan reng a awm dawn si lo a.
26. Tin, hnam tin ropuina leh chawimawina an la lut ang a.Tin, thil bawlhhlawh reng a lut hauh lo ang a, thil tenawm tite leh dawt hmangte pawh an lut hek lo ang; Beram No Nunna Bua hming an ziakte chauh an lut ang.
27. Tin, thil bawlhhlawh reng a lut hauh lo ang a, thil tenawm tite leh dawt hmangte pawh an lut hek lo ang; Beram No Nunna Bua hming an ziakte chauh an lut ang.
