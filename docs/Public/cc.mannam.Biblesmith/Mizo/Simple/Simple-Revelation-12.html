1. Tin, thilmak ropui tak vanah a lo lang ta a; hmeichhia nia inthum, a ke hnuaia thla awm, arsi sawm leh pahniha siam lallukhum khum a lo awm a;
2. ani chuan nau a pai a; nau vei nain a sahram a.
3. Tin, thilmak dang vanah a lo lang leh a; ngai teh, drakon sen lian pui, lu sarih leh ki sawm nei, lallukhum pasarih khum a lo awm a.
4. A mei chuan van arsi hmun thuma thena hmun khat chu a hnuk a, leiah a paih thla ta a; tin, a hrin huna a nau chu a lem theihna'n drakon chu hmeichhe nau hring tur hmaah chuan a ding reng a.
5. Tin, fapa, hnam zawng zawng chunga thir tianga rorel tur a hring a; tin, a fa chu Pathian hnenah leh a lalthutphahah chuan lak chhohin a awm ta a.
6. Tin, hmeichhia chu thlalera Pathian buatsaih hmun a neihnaah chuan a tlan bo a, chutah chuan ni 1,260 an chawm theihna turin.
7. Tin, vanah chuan indona a lo awm a; Mikaela leh a vantirhkohte chuan drakon chu an do a; drakon leh a tirhkohte pawh chuan an do ve a;
8. nimahsela, an hneh ta lo a, an hmun chu vanah a awm ta bawk hek lo.
9. Tin, drakon lian pui, rulpui tar Diabola leh Setana an tih, khawvel zawng zawng bumtu chu an paih thla ta a; leiah an paih a, a tirhkohte pawh an paih thla tel ve a.
10. Tin, vanah chuan aw ring takin, "Tunah chuan kan Pathian chhandamna leh thiltihtheihna leh ram chu a lo thleng ta,a Krista thuneihna nen;kan unaute hektu, kan Pathian hmaa achhun azana hek thintu chu an paih thla ta si a.
11. Nimahsela, Beram No thisen avang leh an hriattirna thu avangin ani chu an hneh a ni, thih ngam khawpin an nunna an ngainep bawk a.
12. Chuvangin, aw, vante leh a chhunga awmte u, lawm rawh u! Lei leh tuifinriat chung a pik e! Diabola chuan hun tlem te chauh a nei tih a inhriat avanga thinur em ema in hnena a lo chhuk tak avangin," a tih ka hria.
13. Tin, drakon chuan leia paih thlak a ni tih a inhriat veleh fapa hringtu hmeichhia chu a tiduhdah ta a.
14. Tichuan, thlalera a hmunah chuan a thlawh luh theihna'n hmeichhia chu mupui lian tak thla khing hnih an pe a, chutah chuan rulpui hmuh phak lohvah hun khat leh a chanve chhung zawng an chawm ang.
15. Tin, rulpui chuan tuiin a len botir theihna'n hmeichhe hnungah chuan lui ang mai hian a ka ata tui a pik chhuak a.
16. Nimahsela, leiin hmeichhia chu a tanpui a, lei chuan a ka a ang a, drakon-in a ka ata a pik chhuah lui chu a lem zo ta a.Tichuan, drakon chu hmeichhe chungah chuan a lo thinur a, a thlah la awm Pathian thupekte zawma Isua hriattirna thu pawmte chu do turin a kal ta a.
17. Tichuan, drakon chu hmeichhe chungah chuan a lo thinur a, a thlah la awm Pathian thupekte zawma Isua hriattirna thu pawmte chu do turin a kal ta a.
