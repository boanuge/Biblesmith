1. Tin, heti hi a ni a, chun ghnuah chuan Amoa thlahte lal chu a thi a, a fapa Hanuna chu a ai awhin a lal a.
2. Tin, Davida chuan, "A pa chuan mi lo khawngaih angin kei pawhin Nahasa fapa Hanuna chu ka khawngiah ve ang," a ti a. Chutichuan Davida chuan a pa chungchang thua thlamuan turin ama mite a tir a. Tin, Davia mite chu Amona thlahte rama n va thelng a.
3. Nimahsela Amona thlahte puipaho chuan an pu Hanuna hnenah chuan, "Davida hian i pa a zah avangin i hnenah thlamuantu a rawn tir niin i ring em ni? Davida hian amite hi khuate rawn tiche tura thlitlai leh enthla turin i hnenah a rawn tira nai lo'm ni?" an ti a.
4. Chutichuan, Hanuna chuan Davida mite chu a man a, an khabe hmul khingkhat a mehsak a, tin, an silhfente chu alai takah an mawng thleng pha chauin a tansak a, akaltir ta a.
5. Tin, Davida an han hrilhc uan, a hmuak turin mi a tir a; anni chu an zak hle mai si a. Tin, lal chuan, "In khabe hmul a chawr ser hma chu Jeriko ah awm rih u la, chu mi hnua chuan in lo haw a ni ang chu," at ia .
6. Tin, Amona thlahten Davia huat zawng an lo ni ta tih an lo inhirat chuan Amona thlahte chuan Beth-rehob-a mi Suria-ho leh Zoba -a mi Suria-ho, sipai singhnih leh, lal Maaka a mi sangkhat nen, Topa mite singkaht leh sanghnih nen an chah a, an ruai a.
7. Tin, Davida'n chu chu a hriatin Joaba leh sipai huaisen zawng zawngte a tir a.
8. Tin, Amona thlahte chu an lo chhuak a, khaw luhna kawngkhar lama chuan tanhmun an siam a: tin, Zoba-a mi leh Reoba-a mi Suria hote leh Toba leh Maaka-hote erawh chu a hrangin phula an awm a.
9. Chutichuan an hma lam leh an hnung lamah an rawn bei dawn tih Joaban a hriatin Israel mite zinga mi atha tha thlang chhuak a, Suria mite bei turin thanhmun a lo siam a:
10. tin, mi dang zawng zawng chu aunaupa Abisaia ktuah adah vek a, Amona thlahte bei turin tanhmun a siam bawk a.
11. "Suria mite hi ka chelh dawn loh huan nangin min pi ang a; tin, nangin Amona thlahte i chelh dawn loh chuan ka rawn pi ang che.
12. Huai takin awm u la, kan mite le kan Pathian khaw chhanin i intipachang ang u; tin, LALPA chua ntha a tih ang zelin ti rawh se," a tia .
13. Chutichuan Joaba leh a hnea mite chuan Suria mite bei turin indona hmun lam chu an va pan hnai a; tin, a hmaah chuan an tlanchia a.
14. Tin, Amona thlahten Suria mite an tlanchhia tih an hriat chuan anni pawh chu Abisaia hmaa chuan a tlanche ve ta a, kuaah chuan an lut a. Tin, Joaba chuan Amona thlahte chu a kirsan a, Jerusalemah a haw ta a.
15. Tin, Suria miten Israelte hmaah a chan a lo chhe ta tih an lo hriat chuan an inpun khawm a.
16. Tin, Hadarezera sipai hotu Sobaka chuan hma a hruai a: Helam-ah an kal a.
17. Tin, chu chu Davida an hrilh a; tin, ani chuan Israel mi zawng zawng ako khawm a, Jordan lui an kai a, Helam-ah chuan an kal a. Tim Suria mite chuan Daivda do tur chuan tanhmun an lo siam a, an lo bei a.
18. Tin, Suria mite hchu Israelte hmaa chuan an tlanche ta a; tin, Davida chuan Suria mi tawlailir zasarih chunga hchuan leh sakawr chung chuang mi singli a that a, tin, an sipai hotu Sobaka pawh a that a, chutah chuan a thi ta a.chutichuan Hadarezera fatea awm lal zawng zawnge Israelte hmaah an chan a chhe ta tih an lo hriat chuan, Israelte nena inremna an siam a, an fateah an awm ta a. chutichuan Suria mite chuan Amona thlahte chu an pui leh ngam ta lo a.
19. chutichuan Hadarezera fatea awm lal zawng zawnge Israelte hmaah an chan a chhe ta tih an lo hriat chuan, Israelte nena inremna an siam a, an fateah an awm ta a. chutichuan Suria mite chuan Amona thlahte chu an pui leh ngam ta lo a.
