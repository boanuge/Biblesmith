1. LALPA in pi leh pute Pathianin a pek che u ram in luah a, leilunga in awm chunga fimkhur taka in zawm tur dan te leh rorel dante chu hengte hi a ni.
2. In va luah tur ram hnamte tlang lian te, tlang te te, thing hring tin, hnuai tea an pathiante rawng an bawlna hmun zawng zawngte chu in tichhe vek tur a ni;
3. an maichamte in tichim ang a, an lungphunte in tisawm vek ang a, an Aser te in hal tur a ni; an pathian lem siam chawpte chu in kihsak ang a, chuta an hming chu in thai bo tur a ni.
4. chutiang chuan LALPA in Pathian chungah in ti tur a ni lo.
5. LALPA in Pathianin in hnam zawng zawng zinga a hming awmna tur hmun a la ruat tur chu in zawng zawk ang a, chuta chuan in kal tur a ni,
6. chutah chuan in halral thilhlan te, inthawina te, in sawma pakhat te, in duh thua thilhlan te, in ran rual leh in beram rual zinga piang hmasa berte in rawn la tur a ni;
7. tin, chutah chuan LALPA in Pathian hmaah in ei ang a, in kutkawih LALPA in Pathianin mal a sawmna che uah chuan hlim takin in awm tur ani , in chhungkuain.
8. Mi tinin mahni tha tihzawng ang maia tuna kan tih ang hian in ti tur a ni lo.
9. Jordan lui in kai a, LALPA in Pathianin a luahtir tur che ua in awm a, him taka in awm theihna tura in vela hmelma zawng zawngte laka a chawlhtir hunah che u erawh chuan heti hi a ni ang a,
10. LALPA in Pathianin a hming awmtirna tur a ruat hmuna rawn lak tura ka tih zawng zawngte chu chutah chuan in rawn la tur nai, in halral thilhlan te, in inthawina te, in sawma pakhat e, in kut then thilhlan te, LALPA hnena in tiam tha thate chu;
11. tin, LALPA in Pathian hmaah chuan hlim takin in awm ang, nangmahni leh in fapate nen, in ina awm, Levi-hote nen, anni chuan in zingah chanvo leh ram nei ve si hek lo.
12. Hmun in hmuhna apiangah in halral thilhlante hlan ma lo turin fimkhur rawh u khai:
13. in hnamte zinga LALPA'n a la ruat tur hmunah chuan in halral thilhlante chu in hlan zawk ang a, chutah chuan thu ka pek zawng zawng che u chu in ti tur a ni.
14. Amaherawhchu LALPA in Pathianin malsawmna a dawntir che u ang zelin in nunnain a hchak ang ang chu in In theuhvah in talh ang a, a sa in ei thei ang; thianghlim lote leh thianghlimte pawhin sangai te, sakhite ang hi chu an ei thei ang.
15. A thisen erawh chu in ei tur ani lo; tui angin leiah in leihbaw tur a ni.
16. In inah chuan sawma pakhat, in bu te, in uain te, in hriak te leh in ran rual leh beram rual zinga piang hmasa ber te, in thutiama mi te, in duhthua thilhlan te, in kut then thilhlante chu in ei thei lo ang a;
17. LALPA in Pathian in hmun a la ruat turah, LALPA in Pathian hmaah in ei zawk tur a ni, nangmahni leh in fapa te leh in fanu te, in bawihpa te, in bawihnu te, in khuaa awm Levi-hote nen; LALPA in Pathianhmaah in kutkawih apiangah him takin in awm tur a ni.
18. In rama in awm chhung zawng Levi-ho chu thlauthla lo turin fimkhur rawh u.
19. LALPA in Pathianin a tiam che u anga in ram a tihzau a, in nunnain sa ei a chak avanga , 'Sa ka ei ang,' in tih hunah chuan, in nunnain a chakzawng apiang sa chu in ei thei ang.
20. LALPA in Pathianin ahming awmna a la ruat tur chu in hlat luattuk chuan, thu ka pek che u ang khan LALPA'n a pek che u in ran rual leh in beram rual zinga mi in talh ang a, in nunnain a chak angzawng chu in inah in ei tur a ni.
21. Sangai sa te, sakhi sa te in ei ang maiin in ei ang a, thianghlim lote leh thianghlimte pawhin angkhat rengin in ei ang.
22. Thisen eraw chu ei lo turin fimkhur rawh u; thisen chu nunna a ni si a; nunna chawpin sa chu in ei tur a ni lo.
23. In ei hauh lo tur a ni; tui angin leiah in leihbaw mai tur a ni.
24. In ei tur ani lo; tichuan LALPA mithmuha thil dik inti hian nangmahni leh in hnua in fate tan a la tha dawn nia.
25. In thil thianghlimte leh in thutiama mite chauh keng u la, LALPA'n hmun a la ruat turah chuan kal ang che u:
26. tin, LALPA in Pathian maichamah in halral thilhlan chu, a sa leh a thisen in hlan ang a; tin, in inthawina thisente chu LALPA in Pathian maichamah chuan in chhung chhuak ang a, a sa chu in ei ang.
27. Heng thu ka pek zawng zawng che u hi ngun takin ngaichang rawh u, tichuan LALPA in Pathian mithmuha thi tha leh thil dik in ti hian in tan leh in hnua in fate tan kumkhuain a la tha dawn nia.
28. LALPA in Pathianin in va hneh tur hnamte a tihbosak che u a, an ram in luah a, in khawsak hunah chuan,
29. tiborala an awmna angah khan in aw ve lohna turin fimkhur u la, 'Heng hnamte hian engtin nge an pathian rawng an bawl? An tih angin keini pawhin I ti ve ang u,' tiin an pathiante chungchang thute zawt vel suh uu.
30. chutiang hchuan LALPA in Pathian chungah in ti tur ani lo; an pathian chungah chuan LALPA ngaiha thil tenawm tinreng, a huat tak maite chu an ti thin a; an fapa te, an fanute ngei pawh an pathiante tan an hla thin a ni.Tih tura thu ka pek che u apiang chu in ti tur a ni; in belhchhah tur a ni lo a, in tikiam tur a ni hek lo.
31. Tih tura thu ka pek che u apiang chu in ti tur a ni; in belhchhah tur a ni lo a, in tikiam tur a ni hek lo.
