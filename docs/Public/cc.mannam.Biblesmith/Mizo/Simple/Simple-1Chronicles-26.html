1. Kawngkhar vengtu pawl then dan chu: Kora mi chu Asapha fapate zinga Korea fapa Meselmia chuan fapate a nei a; a fa tir chu Zekaria a ni a, a dawttu chu Jediaela, a dawt lehtu chu Zebadia, a dawt lehtu chu Jathniela;
2. a dawt lehtu chu Elama, a dawt lehtu chu Jehohanana, a dawt lehtu chu Eliehoenaia a ni.
3. Tin, Obed-edoman fapa tea nei a; a fa tir chu Semaia a ni a, a dawttu chu Jehozabada, a dawt lehtu chu Joa, a dawt lehtu chu Sakara, tin, a a dawt lehtu chu Nethanela;
4. a dawt lehtu chu Amiela, a dawt lehtu chu Isakara, a dawt lehtu chu Peulethaia a ni a, ani chu Pathianin mal a sawma ni.
5. A fapa Semaia lakah chuan fapa an chhungkaw puipate an piang bawk a; mi chak huaisen an ni hlawm a ni.
6. Semaia fapate chu Othnia te, Rephaela te, Obeda te, Elzabada te a ni a, an unau Elihua leh Semakia chu mi chak tak an ni.
7. Chung zawng zawng chu Oed-edoma thlahte an ni: anmahni leh an fapate leh a unaute chu rawngbawlna kawnga mi pitling an ni a; Oed-edoma laka mite chu sawmruk leh pahnih an ni.
8. Meselemia chuan fapa lehunau mi chak tak tak sawm leh pariat anei.
9. Tin, Meraria thlahte zinga Hosa chuan fapate a nei bawk a; Simria chu puipa ber a ni a, (fa tir a ni lo na a, a pain puipa berah a siam a ni);
10. a dawttu chu Hilkia a ni a, a dawt lehtu chu Tebalia, a dawt lehtu chu Zekaria a ni. Hosa fapate leh a unaute chu an zain sawm leh pathum an ni.
11. Chung puipaho zinga mi chu an unaute anga LALPA ina rawngbawl hmaa chanvo neia kawngkhar vengtu pawla an thente chu an ni.
12. Alian atein, an chhungkaw zir ang zelin kawngka tina awm tur atan thum an vawr a.
13. Tin, chhak lam atana thumvawr chu Selemia chungah a tlu a. Tin, roreltu fing tak, a fapa Zekaria tan thum an vawr a, hmar lam chu a pualah a tlu a.
14. Chhim lam chu Obed-edoma pualah; tin, thil dahkhawmna in chu a fapate pualah.
15. Thlang lam chu a chhohan kawnga Saleket kawngkhar vena nen, Supima leh Hosa pualah.
16. Chak lamah Levi-ho paruk an awm a, ni tin, hmar lamah pali, ni tin, chhim lama pali, tin, thil dah khawmna inah pahnih ve ve.
17. Thlang lam in hualchhung atan chuan a luhnaah pali; in hualchungah chuan pahnih.
18. Chungte chu an ni, Kora mi fapate leh Merari mi fapate kawngkhar vengtu pawla an thente chu.
19. ,21 Tin, an unau Levi-ho Pathian sum dah khawmna in leh thil thianghlim dah khawmna in vawngtute chu Ladana chhungkaw pipa, Ladana laka Gerson mi thlah,
20. ,20 Tin, an unau Levi-ho Pathian sum dah khawmna in leh thil thianghlim dah khawmna in vawngtute chu Ladana chhungkaw pipa, Ladana laka Gerson mi thlah,
21. Jehiela leh a unau Zethama leh Joela te an ni; chungte chu LALPA sum dah khawmna in vawngtute an ni.
22. Amram-ho te, Izhar-ho te, Hebron-ho te, Uziel-ho te zinga mi.
23. Mosia fa Gersoma fapa Sebuela hu Sum dah khawmna in vawngtu a ni.
24. Tin, a unaute chu Eliezera fapa Rehabia te, Jesaia te, Jorama te, Zikria te, Selomotha te an ni.
25. Selemotha leh a unaute chu lal Davida leh an chhungkaw puipa sipai sang otute le za hotute leh sipai pawl hotuten an tihthianghlimt awh, thil thianghlim dah khawmna in zawng zawng vawngtu an ni.
26. Indonaa trallak thilte chu LALPA in siam thatna'n an dah a ni.
27. Zawlnei Samuela te, Kisa fapa Saula e, Nere fapa Abnera te, Zeruia fapa Joaba tena an tihthianghlim zawng zawng, tupawhinthil an tihthianghlimt awh phawt chu Selomotha leh a unaute vawn vek a ni.
28. Izhar mi Kenania leh a fapate ch Israel ram pawn lam chungchang thua hottue leh roreltute an ni.
29. Hebron mi Hasabia le a unau mi huaisen sangkhat leh zasarih te chu Jordan ral thalng lama Israel-ho awptu an ni; LALPA hna zawng zawng leh lal rawngbawlna lam hnaat.
30. Hebron thlahte hruaitu ber chu Jeria a ni a. Davida lal kum sawmpali kum khan Hebron thlahte chhuichhuahna a awm a, Gilead rama Jazer-ah chuanan zingah mi chakhuaisen an lo awm tuh mai a,Davida chuan Jerija chhungte zinga mi huaisente chu mi 2,700 a thlang chhuak a, chung mite chu Jordan lui khawchhak lam Reuben-ho te, Gad hote leh chhak lam Manasea hote rama Israel-te sakhaw thu leh tualchhung thu rel turin a ruat a.
31. Davida chuan Jerija chhungte zinga mi huaisente chu mi 2,700 a thlang chhuak a, chung mite chu Jordan lui khawchhak lam Reuben-ho te, Gad hote leh chhak lam Manasea hote rama Israel-te sakhaw thu leh tualchhung thu rel turin a ruat a.
