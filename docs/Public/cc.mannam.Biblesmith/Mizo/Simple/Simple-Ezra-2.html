69. an pek theih tawkin in sakna thawhlawmah chuan rangkachak darik 61,000, tangka maneh 5,000, puithiam silfen za an pe a.Chutichuan puithiam te, Levi chi te, vantlang thenkhat te, hla satute, kawngkhar vengtute leh Nethin-hote chu, an khuaahte an awm ta a, Israel mi zawng zawngte chu anmahni khuaah.
70. Chutichuan puithiam te, Levi chi te, vantlang thenkhat te, hla satute, kawngkhar vengtute leh Nethin-hote chu, an khuaahte an awm ta a, Israel mi zawng zawngte chu anmahni khuaah.
