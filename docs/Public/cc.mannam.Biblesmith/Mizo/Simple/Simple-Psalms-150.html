1. LALPA chu fak rawh u. Pathian chu a hmun thianghlimah chuan fak u la:
2. A thiltihtheihna boruak zau takah khian amah chu fak rawh u. A thiltih ropui takte avangin amah chu fak u la:
3. A ropui nasat em avangin amah chu fak rawh u. Tawtawrawt riin amah chu fak u la:
4. Perhkhuang leh tingtangin amah chu fak rawh u. Khuangte leh lamin amah chu fak ula: Tingtang chi hrang hrang leh phenglawngin amah chu fak rawh u.
5. Darbenthek ri thum miin amah chu fak u la: Darbenthek ri fiak miin amah chu fak rawh u.Engkim, thaw thei tawh phawt chuan LALPA chu fak rawh se. LALPA chu fak rawh u.
6. Engkim, thaw thei tawh phawt chuan LALPA chu fak rawh se. LALPA chu fak rawh u.
