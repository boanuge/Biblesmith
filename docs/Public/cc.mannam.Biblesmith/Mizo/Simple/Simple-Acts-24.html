1. Tin, ni nga chinah puithiam Lalber Anania a lo thleng chhuk a, upa tute emaw nen, a hming Tertulla thusawi thiam pakhat nen; tin, Paula chunga thupawi chu Ram awptu hnenah an sawi a.
2. Tin, Paula chu an koh chhuah hnu chuan Tertulla chuan amah a hek tan a: "Felik tha ber, nangmaha zarah thlamuang em emin kan awm avang leh, i enkawl avanga he chite tan hian sualte khap thatin a awm thin avangin,
3. kumkhuain khawiah pawh lungawi takin kan lawm thin.
4. Amaherawhchu i tan ninawm lutuk nih ka duh lo a, i nunnemnain thu kamkhat erawh chu min ngaihthlaksak turin ka ngen a che.
5. He mi hi mi huatthlala tak, khawvela Judateho zawng zawng chawk buaitu leh Nazaret mite pawla an tupa ber pakhat a nih kan hria a;
6. Pathian biak in tihbawlhhlawh a tum bawk a; chuvangin kan man a,
7. kan dan anga a chungthu relsak kan tum a ni a; mahse, sipai hotu lal ber Lusia a lo kal a, kan kuta ta min lak luihsak a, amah hektute i hmaa lo kal turin thu min pe ta a ni,
8. nangmah ngeiin chhuahchhalin kan hekna thil zawng zawng hi ama lakah i hre thei ang," a ti a.
9. Tin, Judate pawh chuan,"Chung thu chu chutiang chu a ni," tiin an tanpui ve a.
10. Tin, Ram Awptu chuan thu sawi tura Paula chu kuta a zah veleh Paula chuan, "Nang kum tam tak he chite rorelsaktu hi i nih ka hriat avangin lawm takin ka thiamna thu ka sawi dawn hi.
11. Jerusalem khuaah Pathian be turin ka han kal achin chu ni sawm leh ni hnih lo liam a la ni lo ang tih I la hre thei ang;
12. Pathian biak inah pawh, inkhawmna inahte pawh, khuaah pawh tumah ka hnial leh mipui ka fuihpawrh annin mi hmu hek lo;
13. tunah pawh an mi hekna thu hi i hriatah a bul an chhuichhuak thei hek lo ang.
14. Nimahsela he thu hi i hnenah ka inpuang ang, 'pawl hrang' an tih Kawng dan angin min thlahtute Pathian ka be thin. Dan leh Zawlneite Lehkhabua ziak zawng zawng ringin;
15. tin, mi felte leh fel lote thawhlehna a awm ang tih Pathian lamah ka beisei a, henghote pawh hian chung chu an awih ve bawk si a;
16. chu mi avang chuan Pathian hnen lamah leh mihring hnenah pawh rilru sual lo mawlh hi vawn fo ka tum thin.
17. Tin, kum tam tak hnuah hian ka chipuite hnenah thilpek tur leh sa talh tur rawn kengin ka lo kal a;
18. tin, chung thil ka pek lai chuan mipui tel loin, bengchheng loin, Pathian biak inah thianghlimin mi hmu a.
19. Nimahsela chutah chuan Asia rama mi Juda tute emaw an awm kha, chungte chuan ka chungah thu an neih chuan hetah hian i hmaa awmin mi hek tur a ni.
20. A nih loh leh, he'ng mite ngei hian roreltute hmaa ka din lai khan, ka tihsual eng nge an hmuh sawi rawh se,
21. he thu kamkhat avang hian a nih nghal loh chuan, an zinga dingin, 'Mitthi thawhlehna thu-ah ka awm a nih hi,' ka tih kha," a ti a, a chhang a.
22. Tin, Felika chuan Kawng thu chu a lo hre hle tawh a, "Sipai hotu lal ber Lusia chu a lo thlen chhuk hunah in thu ka la rem ang," tiin, thu chu a dah ta rih a.
23. Tin, Paula chu a duat deuh a, a thiante rawngbawlsak khap lo a, ventir turin sipai za hotu hnenah chuan thu a pe a.
24. Tin, ni eng zat emaw chinan Felika chu a lo kal leh a, a nupui Drusilli, Juda hmeichhia nen, Paula chu a kohtir a, Krista Isua rinna thu chu a ngaithla a.
25. Tin, Paula chuan felna thu te, insum thute, rorelna lo la awm tur thute khun taka a hrilh laiin Felika chuan hlauhawm a ti em a, "Kal leh ta rih tawh, hun remchang ka neih hunah ka ko ang che," a ti a.
26. Paulan tangka a pek a beisei bawk a, chuvangin a ko zing deuh a, a be thin a ni.Nimahsela kum hnih a vei hnuin Porkia Festan Felika chu a thlak ta a; tin, Felika chuan Judate zingah hmingthat a duh a, Paula chu phuar sain a hnutchhiah ta a.
27. Nimahsela kum hnih a vei hnuin Porkia Festan Felika chu a thlak ta a; tin, Felika chuan Judate zingah hmingthat a duh a, Paula chu phuar sain a hnutchhiah ta a.
