Ezra (1/10)  →
1. Persia lal Kura lal tirh kumin zawlnei Jeremia hmanga a lo sawi tawh chu LALPA'n a thlen kimtir ta. He thupek pe tur leh a ram zawng zawnga chhiar vek tura thawn chhuak turin LALPA chuan Kura thinlung chu a chawk tho a.
2. "Persia lal Kura chuan heti hian a ti, Khawvel ram zawng zawng chu LALPA, van Pathian chuan min pe ta a; tin, Judai rama Jerusalem-ah chuan a in saksak turin thu min pe a ni.
3. In zinga ama mite tawh phawt hnenah chuan a Pathian chu awm rawh se; tichuan Judai rama Jerusalem-ah chuan han chho se la, LALPA, Israel-hote Pathian in, Jerusalem-a awm chu han sa rawh se; amah chu Pathian a ni.
4. Tin, tupawh khawii hmunah pawh, an awmnaa la chambang apiangte chu, an awmna hmuna mite chuan Jerusalem-a awm Pathian in atan anmahni duh thua an thawh tiam lohin, tangkarua te, rangkachak te, bungrua te, ran tein lo tanpui rawh se," tiin.
5. Chutichuan Juda leh Benjamin chia chhungkaw puipa deuh deuhte, puithiam te, Levi-ho te, Jerusalem-a LALPA in han sa tura Pathianin an thinlung a chawh thawh zawng zawngte chu an thawk ta a.
6. Tin, anmahni duhthua thawh thiam loin an vela mi zawng anwgte chuan tangkaruaa siam bungbel te, rangkachak te, bungrua te, ran te, thil hlu tak takte an pein, an tanpui a.
7. Lal Kura pawhin Nebukadnezaran LALPA ina bungbel Jerusalem atanga a lak chhuah a,a pathiante ina a dahte chu a la chhuak leh a;
8. chung ngei chu Persia lal Kura chuan a vawngtu Mithredatha a lak chhuahtir a, a chhiar a, Juda puipa Sesbazara a hlan a.
9. Tin, he'ng hi a zat chin chu a ni: rangkachak maihun sawmthum, tangkaraw maihun sang, chemte sawmhnih leh pakua;
10. rangkachak no sawmthum, tangkaraw no chi dang zali leh sawm, bungbel dang sang te nen.Rangkachak leh tangkaraw bungbel zawng zawng chu sangnga leh zali a ni. Salte chu Babulon ata Jerusalem-a an hruai chhoh lai khan Sesbazaran he'ng zawng zawng hi a keng chho a.
11. Rangkachak leh tangkaraw bungbel zawng zawng chu sangnga leh zali a ni. Salte chu Babulon ata Jerusalem-a an hruai chhoh lai khan Sesbazaran he'ng zawng zawng hi a keng chho a.
