1. Tin, heti hi a ni a, a hnam pum pui chuan Jordan lui an kai fai vek hnuin LALPA'n Josua hnenah chuan,
2. "Mipuite zinga mi hi mi sawm leh pahnih, hnam tina mi pakhat zel thlang chhuak langin
3. an hnenah, 'Jordan lui lai, puithiamhote dina lai taka mi saw lung sawm leh pahnih la u la, pu phei u la, riahhmun, zanina in riahna turah dah ang che u,' tiin thu pe ang che."
4. Chutichuan Josua chuan Israela thlahte zinga mi sawm leh pahnih, hnam tina mi a thlan chhuahte chu a ko va:
5. tin, Josua chuan an hnenah, "LALPA in Pathian bawm hmaah khan Jordan lui laiah kai u la, Israela thlahte a hnam zat zelin, mi tinin lung pakhat theuh pu ang che u.
6. tichuan he'ng hi in zingah chhinchhiahna a la ni dawn nia, nakinah in faten, 'he'ng, 'Heng lungte hi eng in tihna nge ni le?' tia an la rawn zawh hunah chuan,
7. nangnin an hnenah, 'LALPA thuthlung bawm hmaah Jordan tuite a lo chah tak avang a ni; Jordan akan laiin, Jordan tuite chu a lo chat ta a ni: tichuan he'ng lungte hi Israela thlahte kumkhuaa an hriatrengna tur a nih hi,' in la ti dawn nia," a ti a.
8. Tin, Israela thlahte chuan Josua'n thu a pek ang chuan an ti a, Israel thlahte a hnam zat zelin, Jordan lui laia mi lung sawm leh pahnih an la a; tin, an riahna hmunah an pu phei a, chutah chuan an dah a.
9. Tin, thuthlung bawm zawntu puithiamho dinna lai tak, Jordan lui laiah chuan Josua'n lung sawm leh pahnih a phun a: chung chu tunt hlengin a la awm reng a ni.
10. Mosia'n Josua thu a lo pek tak zawng zawng anga LALPA'n mipuite hnena ti tura Josua thu a pek chu an tih zawh vek hma loh chuan puithiam bawm zawntute chu Jordan lui laiah chuan an ding reng mai a: tichuan mipuite chu an intihmanhmawh a, an kai ta a.
11. Tin, heti hi a ni a, mipui zawng zawng chu an kai fai vek hnu chuan LALPA bawm leh puithiamte chu mipuite mithmuhah chuan an kai ve ta a.
12. Tin, Reubena thlah te, Gada thlahte leh Manse hnam zatvete chu Mosia'nan hnena a lo sawi tawh ang khan Israel thlahte hmaah chuan inralthuamin an kai a:
13. Ralthuama inthuam diam mi singli lai indo turin Jeriko phaiahte chuan LALPA hmaah an kai a ni.
14. Chu mi ni chuan LALPA'n Josua chu Israel-ho zawng zawng mit hmuhah a chawimawi a; Mosia an tih ang bawk khan a dam chhung zawng chuan an tih ta a.
15. ,16 Tin, LALPA'n Josua hnenah chuan, "Puithiamho, thuhriattirna bawm zawntute kha Jordan lui ata lo chhuah thu pe rawh," tiin a sawi a.
16. ,15 Tin, LALPA'n Josua hnenah chuan, "Puithiamho, thuhriattirna bawm zawntute kha Jordan lui ata lo chhuah thu pe rawh," tiin a sawi a.
17. Chuti chuan Josua'n puithiamhote chu, "Jordan lui ata kha lo chhuak tawh rawh u," tiin thu a pe a.
18. Tin, heti hi a ni a, puithiamho, LALPA thuthlung bawm zawntute chu Jordan lui lai ata an lo chhuah a, puithiam kete chuan khawmual a rah veleh, Jordan tuite chu a luan pangngaiin a lo luang leh a, a hma ang bawk khan a kuang zawng zawngah chuan aluang liam leh ta dul dul a.
19. Tin, mipuite chu thla khatna, ni sawm nin Jordan lui ata chu an chhuak a, Jeriko chhak lampang Gilgal-ah awmhmun an khuar ta a.
20. Tin, chung lung sawm leh pahnih, Jordan lui ata an put chhuah chu Josua'n Gilgal-ah chuan a phun a.
21. Tin, Israel thlahte hnenah chuan, "Nakin hnua in thlahten an pate, 'Heng lungte hi eng tihna nge ni le?' tia an la zrawn zawh hunah chuan,
22. "Israel-hote chuan Jordan lui hi lei charah an kal kai a ni,' tiin in thlahte chu in la hriattir dawn zuk nia.
23. LALPA in Pathianin hmanah Tuifinriat Sen chu kan kai hma khan, LALPA in Pathianin Jordan tuite hi in kai hma loh chuan in hmaah a tikang chat ta anih hi:leilunga hnam tinrengin LALPA kut hi a lo chak ani tih an hriat theih a, LALPA in Pathian chu kumkhuaa an tih theihna'n," tiin a sawi a.
24. leilunga hnam tinrengin LALPA kut hi a lo chak ani tih an hriat theih a, LALPA in Pathian chu kumkhuaa an tih theihna'n," tiin a sawi a.
