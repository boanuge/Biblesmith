1. LALPA chu fak rawh u; Kan Pathian faka zai hi a tha si a;
2. Chu chu a nuamin, fak hi a awm reng a ni. LALPA chuan Jerusalem chu a din leh a; Israel hnawh chhuahhote chu a hruai khawm leh a.
3. Lungchhiate chu a tidam a, An hliamnate chu a tuam thin a.
4. Arsi tam chin a chhiar a; An hming a vuah theuh hlawm thin a.
5. Kan Lalpa chu a ropui a, thiltihtheih lamah pawh chak tak ani a; A finna chuan tawpintai a nei lo.
6. LALPA chuan thuhnuairawlhte chu a chelh reng a: Mi suaksualte chu leiah a titlawm thin.
7. Lawm thu sawia LALPA chawimawiin hla sa u la; tingtang nen kan Pathian fakin zai rawh u.
8. Ani chuan van khi chhum a zam hliahtir a, Leilung atan ruah a buatsaih a, Tlangahte chuan hlobet a totir thin a.
9. Ani chuan sa hnenah a ei tur a pe thin, Choak no hram hramte hnenah nen.
10. Ani chu sakawr chakna chungah chuan a lawm lo va: Mihring ke chungahte pawh lawmna reng reng a nei thin lo.
11. LALPA chuan amah tihtute chungah chuan lawmna a nei thin, A ngilneihna beiseitute chungah chuan.
12. Aw Jerusalem, LALPA chu fak rawh; Aw Zion, i Pathian chu fak rawh.
13. Ani chuan i kulh kawngkharte kalhna thing tlawnte a tikhauh a; I chhunga i fanau awmte chu mal a sawm si a.
14. I ramri chhungah chuan remna a siam a; Buh tha ber chiin a tikhat thin che a ni.
15. A thupek chu leiah hian a tir chhuak a; A thu chu chak takin a tlan thin.
16. Vur sur chu beram hmul angin a pe a; Vur tla chu vut angin a theh darh thin.
17. A tui khal chu chhang them nawi angin a paih chhuak thin a: Ama vawhna hmaah chuan tu nge ding thei ang?
18. A thu chu a tir chhuak a, chung chu a titui a: A thli a tlehtir a, chutichuan tuite chu a lo luang thin.
19. A thu chu Jakob-ho hnenah a lantir a, A thuruatte leh a rorelte pawh chu Israel-ho hnenah a lantir thin.Ani chuan eng hnam chungah mah chutiang chuan a ti ngai lo: A rorel lah chu an hre hek lo. LALPA chu fak rawh u.
20. Ani chuan eng hnam chungah mah chutiang chuan a ti ngai lo: A rorel lah chu an hre hek lo. LALPA chu fak rawh u.
