1. Manasea chu a lal tantirhin kum sawm leh kum hnih mi a ni a; Jerusalem ah kum sawmnga leh kum nga a lal a: a nu hming chu Jephzibi a ni.
2. Tin, LALPA'n Israela thlahte hmaa a hnawchhuah tak hnamten thil tenawm tak an tih thin ang, LALPA mithmuha thil tha lo takte chu a ti thin a.
3. A pa Jezekian a tihchiat tawh hmun sangte chu a din leh a; Israel lal Ahaba tih angin Baala maichamte a din a, Aseri te a siam a, vana awm zawng zawng khia a bi aa, an rawng a bawl a,
4. LALPA'n, "Jerusalem-a ka hming ka awmtir ang," a tihna LALPA inah chuan maichamte a siam a.
5. LALPA in hung chung kawtlai pahnihah chuan vana awm zawng zawng tan maicham a siam a.
6. Tin, a fapa ch meia a lutir a, aiente a ching a, dawite a hmang a, ramhuai aienthiam zawlte leh dawithiamte a ruai thin a: LALPA mithmuha thil tha lo nasa tak a tithinturin a ti thin a.
7. ,8 Tin, LALPA'n Davida leh a fapa Solomona hnenah, "He in leh Jerusalem khua, Israel chi zawng zawng zinga ka thlanah hian ka hming chu kumkhuain ka awmtir ang a; thu ka pek ang zawng zawng leh ka chhiahhlawh Mosia'n dan a pek ang zawng zawng khaan pawm phawt chuan an pi leh pute ka pek tawh ram ata chu Israelte kete pawh hi ka pen chauhtir tawh bawk hek lo ang" a lo tih tawh inahc haun Aseri lem a siam chu a dah a.
8. ,7 Tin, LALPA'n Davida leh a fapa Solomona hnenah, "He in leh Jerusalem khua, Israel chi zawng zawng zinga ka thlanah hian ka hming chu kumkhuain ka awmtir ang a; thu ka pek ang zawng zawng leh ka chhiahhlawh Mosia'n dan a pek ang zawng zawng khaan pawm phawt chuan an pi leh pute ka pek tawh ram ata chu Israelte kete pawh hi ka pen chauhtir tawh bawk hek lo ang" a lo tih tawh inahc haun Aseri lem a siam chu a dah a.
9. Nimahsela an pawm duh lo a; LALPA'n Israela thlahteho hmaa a hnawchauh tak hnamte tih ai maha nasa zawk thil tisual turin Manasea chuan mite a thlem thin a.
10. Tin, LALPA'n a chiahhlawh zawlneite hmangin thu a sawi a:
11. "Juda lal Manasea hian he'ng thil tenawm takte hi a ti a, a hmaa Amor-hote ai mahina tisual nasa a, Juda-ho hia milemtein til a tihsualtir avangin:
12. LALPA, Israelte Pathianin heti hian a ti: 'Ngai teh u, Jerusalem leh Juda chnga hian a hria apiangte beng khing hnih thawng khawp thil tha lo ka rawn thlentir dawn e.
13. Samari tehna hrui leh Ahaba chhung darmumkhai chu Jerusalem chungah ka zam ang a: tin, miin thleng a hru a, a tilinglet ang maiin Jerusalem chu ka hru ang.
14. ,15 Tin, ka rochan la awm chunte chu ka paih thla ang a, an hmelmate kutah a pe ang: tichuan an pi leh pute Aigupta rama an lo chauh ata vawiin thleng hian ka mithmuha thil tha lo tak an ti a, mi tihthinrim avang hian an hmelma zawng zawngte man tur leh rallak tur an ni ang,' tiin," a ti a.
15. ,14 Tin, ka rochan la awm chunte chu ka paih thla ang a, an hmelmate kutah a pe ang: tichuan an pi leh pute Aigupta rama an lo chauh ata vawiin thleng hian ka mithmuha thil tha lo tak an ti a, mi tihthinrim avang hian an hmelma zawng zawngte man tur leh rallak tur an ni ang,' tiin," a ti a.
16. LALPA mithmuha thil tha lo tak tia Judate thil a tihsualtira sualna thiam loin Mnasea chuan pawikhawih lo thisen nasa takin a chhuah bawk a, Jerusalem dung leh vang a khat a.
17. Tichuan Mansea chanchin dang te, a thiltih zawng zawng te, a thil tihsual te chu Juda lalte lal lai thu ziakna buah a chuang a ni lo'm ni?
18. Tin, Manasea chu a pi leh pute hnenah chuan a muhil a, Uza huana ama in bul huana an phum ta a; tichuan a fapa Amona chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
19. Amona chu alal tantirhin kum sawmhnih leh kum hnih a ni a; Jerusalem-ah kum hnih a lal a: a nu hming chu Mesulemethi, Jotba khuaa Jaruza fanu a ni a.
20. Tin, a pa Manasea tih ang bawk LALPA mithmuha thil tha lo tak a ti ve thin a.
21. A pa kawng zawh zawng zawng chu a zawh vek a, apan milem rawng a bawlte chu a bawl a, chibai a buk ve a:
22. tichuan LALPA, a pi leh pue Pathian chu a kalsan a. LALPA kawng chu a zawh lo a.
23. Tin, Amona miten amah an phiar ru a, lal chu ama ina an tihlum a.
24. Nimahsela chu mi rama mite chuan lal Amona phira rutu zawng zawngte chu an tihlum ta vek a; tichuan chu mi rama mite chuan a fapa Josia chu a aiawhin lalah an siam ta a,
25. Tichuan Amona thiltih dangte chu Juda lalte lal lai thu ziakna buah a chuang a ni lo'm ni?Tin, Uza huana ama thlana an phum a: tin, a fapa Josia chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
26. Tin, Uza huana ama thlana an phum a: tin, a fapa Josia chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
