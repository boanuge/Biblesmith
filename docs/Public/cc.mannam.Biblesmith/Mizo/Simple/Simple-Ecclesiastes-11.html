1. I chhang paih la tui chungah chuan, i hmu leh dawn si, nei rei hnuah.
2. Chan tur pe la, mi pasarih, pariat hnenah, i hre si lo, lei chungah hian thil tha lo, lo tla tur chu.
3. Tui a pai chuan chhumin, a intiruak thin, lei chungah; thing a tluk chuan chhim hawi emaw, hmar hawi emaw, a awm ngei ang thing chu, a tluk hmunah.
4. Thli pawisatu'n chi a tuh lo'ng, chhum enthlatu'n a seng hek lo ang.
5. Thli kalkawng chu i hre lo a, hmeichhe pum chhunga nau ruh than dan pawh i hre hek lo, chutiang bawkin Pathian engkm titu thiltih chu i hre lo.
6. Zingah i chi tuh la, tlaiah i kut sum suh; i hre si lo, a thang tha tur khawi nge, hei hi nge, kha kha nge, an tha ve ve dawn.
7. A nuam ngei mai tak e, eng chu, mi tan pawh ni hmuh a tha e,
8. A ni, mi kum tam tak a dam chuanin he'ng zawngah hian lawm rawh se; amaherawh, hre reng se la thimna nite, an tam hle mai danw si a; thil lo thleng zawng zawng hi engmah lo mai a lo ni.
9. Thalaite u, in that lai chen u la, i la naupang viau lai hian hlim rawh u, amaherawhchu Pathianin in chungah ro a rel dawn tih hre reng ang che u.Engmah engto nei suh u la, in pang tinatu awm hek suh se, in that lai hun a rei dawn lo.
10. Engmah engto nei suh u la, in pang tinatu awm hek suh se, in that lai hun a rei dawn lo.
