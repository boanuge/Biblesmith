1. Tin, Josua chu a lo upa a, a lo tar ta hle mai a; tin, LALPA chuan a hnenah, " I lo upain i lo tar ta hle a, ram lah chu lak tur a la tam mai si a.
2. Heng ramte hi a ni la lak loh chu: Philistia ram zawng zawng leh Gesur-ho ram zawng zawng chu:
3. Aigupta ram bula awm Sihor atanga hmar lampanga Ekron ramri thlengin (Chu chu Kanaan mite rama ngaih a ni); Philistia-ho lal panga, Gaza nitem Asdod mite, Askelon mite, Git mite leh Ekron mite chu: chim lama Avim ram pawh:
4. Kanaan mite ram zawng zawng leh Ziedon mite rama Meara, Amor-ho ramri Aphek khaw thlengin:
5. tin, Gebal mite ram leh ni chauhna lam Lebanon ram zawng zawng, Hermon tlang hnuai Baal-gad atanga Hamath khaw luhna thlengin:
6. Lebanon atanga Misrephoth-maim hmun thlengi, tlang rama cheng zawng zawng, Zidon mi zawng zawngte chu; anni chu Israela thlahte hmaah hian ka hnawtchhuak ang a: thu ka pek che ang khan Israel-hote rochanah siamsak phawt mai ang che.
7. Chutichuan he ram hi hnam kuate leh Manase hnam zatvete rochanah semsak ang che," a ti a.
8. Amah nen Reuben chite leh Gad hote chuan Jordan chhak lampangah khan an chanpual tur Mosia'n a pek chu an lo dawng tawh a, LALPA chhiahhlawh Mosia ngeiin a lo pek tawh ang khan:
9. Arnon ruam sira awm Aroer khua atang leh ruam lai taka khawpui leh, Medeba phai ram zawng zawng, Dibon thlengin;
10. tin, Amor-ho lal Sihona, Hesbon khuaa lala thua khawpui zawng zawng, Amona thlahte ramri thlengin;
11. tin, Gilead ram leh Gesur-ho leh Maakat-ho ramri chin leh Hermon tlang zawng zawng leh Basan ram zawng zawng, Salka khaw thlengin;
12. Astaroth leh Edrei khuaa lala thu Basan ram lal Oga ram zawng zawng nen (chung chu Rephaim hnam la awm chhunte an ni); chungho chu Mosia'n a lo bei a, a lo hnawtchuak tawh a ni.
13. Amaherawhchu Gesur-hote leh Maakat-hote chu Israela thlahte chuan an hnawtchhuak lo: Gesur-ho leh maakat-ho zawng tun thleng hian Israel-hote zingah an la cheng ta reng zawk a ni.
14. Levi hnamte chauh a ni rochan tur a pek ve loh ni: a hnena a lo sawi tawh ang khan LALPA, Israelte Pathian tana meia thilhlante chu an rochan a ni zawk si a.
15. Tin, Rebena thlahte hnam chu an chhungkaw zir zelin Mosia'n ram a lo pe tawh a.
16. Tin, an ramri chu Arnon ruam sira awm Aroer khua atanga ruam lai taka khawpui leh Medeba phai ram zawng zawng:
17. Hesbon leh chu mi phai rama khawpui zawng zawng: Debon te, Bamothbaal te Bethbaal-meon te:
18. Jahz khua te, Kedemoth te, Mephaath te;
19. Kiriathaim te, Sibma te, ruam tlanga Zereth-sahar te;
20. Bethpeor te, Pisga awih pangper te, Beth-jesimoth te;
21. phai ram khawpui zawng zawng te, Hesbona rorel, Amor-ho lal Sihona mi lian ho , Midian-hote lala awm Evia te, Rekema te, Zura te, Hura te, Reba te an awmna ram chawpa Mosia'n a lo tiboral takte nen khan a ni.
22. Beora fapa Balaama, aienthiam pawh Israela thlahten mi dang an thlahte zingah chuan khandaihin an that zel a.
23. Tin, Reubena thlahte ram chin chu Jordan leh a kam zawng chu a ni. Heng ram leh khawpuite leh khuate hi a ni Reubena thlahte tana an chhungkaw zir zela rochana an ruat chu.
24. Tin, Mosia'n Gad chi, Gada thlahte chu an chhungkaw zir zelin ram a pe a.
25. Tin, an ramri chu Jazer khua leh Gilead ram khawpui zawng zawng leh Amona thlahte ram zatve, Raba khaw depa awm Aroer thlengin;
26. Hesbon atanga Ramathmizpe leh Betonim thlengin; Mahanaim atanga Debir ramri thlengin;
27. tin, ruamah chuan Beth-haram te, Beth-nimra te, Sukoth te, Zaphon te, Hezbon leh Sihona ram dang te, Hezbon leh Sihona ram dang e, Jordan leh a kam te, Jordan ral chak lampanga Kinereth dil tawp rak thlengin a ni.
28. Hengte hi a ni Gada thlahte tana an chhungkaw zir zela an rochana ruat chu, a khawpuite leh khuate chu.
29. Tin, Mosia'n Manase hnam zatvete chu ram a pe a, Manasea chi zatvete tan a ni an chhungkaw zir zela a pek ni.
30. Tin, an ramri chu Mahanaim atangin Basan ram zawng zawng, Basan ram lal Oga ram zawng zawng leh basan rama Jair khawpui zawng zawngte nen, chu chu khaw sawmruk a ni:
31. tin, Gelead ram te, Edrei khuate nen: chung chu Manasea fa Makira thlah, Makira thlah zatvete tan, an chhungkaw zir zela ruat a ni.
32. Heng Moab phai, Jeriko zawn, Jordan ral, chhak lampang te hi a ni Mosia'n an rochan tura a lo sem chu.Levi chite erawh chu Mosia'n rochan tur a pe ve lo: an hnena a lo sawi tawh ang khan LALPA, Israelte Pathian chu an rochan a ni zawk.
33. Levi chite erawh chu Mosia'n rochan tur a pe ve lo: an hnena a lo sawi tawh ang khan LALPA, Israelte Pathian chu an rochan a ni zawk.
