1. Juda lal Ahazia fapa Joasa kum sawmhih leh kum thum a lal kum huan Jehua fapa Jeoabaza chu Israelte chungah Samari khuaan a lal tan a, kum sawm leh kum sarih a lal a.
2. Tin, LALPA mithmuha thil tha lo tak a ti thin a, Israel-hote thil tihsualtira a tih Nebata fapa Jeroboama sualnate ka a ching ve a, a kalsan leh ta lo.
3. Tin, LALPA thin chu Israelte chungah chuan a alh a, tichuan Suria lal Hazaela kutah leh Jazaela fapa Benhadada kutah chuan a pe ta zel a.
4. Tin, Jehoahaza chuan LALPA hnenah a ngen chiam a, tin, LALPA'n a thu chu a lo ngaihthlaksak a; Israelte hnehchhiaha an awm a, Suria lalin anmahni a hnehchhiahzia chu a hmu reng si a.
5. Tichuan LALPA'n Israel-hote chu hhandamtu a pe a, chutichuan Suria-hote hnuaia an awm chu anc hhuak a, Israela thlahte chu tun hma ang bawk khan anmahni in lamah an awm leh ta hle hle a.
6. (Amaherawhchu Israelte thil tihsualtira a tih Jeroboama chhungteho sualnate kha an kalsan lo a, an la ching ta reng zawk a; tin, Samari khuaah chuan Aseri pawh chu a la awm reng ngeh nghe a.)
7. Jehoahaza chu a mite zinga sakawr chung chuang mi sawmnga leh, tawlailir sawm leh, sipai singkhat tih loh chu neih a palsak si lo a; Suria lal chuan a tiboral vek a, vaivut chil dip ang maiin a siam a ni.
8. Tichuan Jehoahaza chanchin dangte, a thiltih zawng zawng te, a thiltihtheihzia te chu Israel lalte lal lai thu ziakna bua a chuanga ni lo'm ni?
9. Tin, Jehoahaza chu a pi leh pute hnenah a muhil ta a; tin, Samari khuaah an phum a; tin, a fapa Joasa chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
10. Juda lal Joasa kum sawmthum leh kum sarih a lal kum chuan Jeoahaza fapa Jehoasa chu Israelte chungah samari khuaan a lal tan a, kum sawm leh kum ruk a lal a.
11. Tin, LALPA mithmuha a thil tha lo tak a ti thin a; Israelte thil tihsualtira a tih Nebata fapa Jeroboama sualna zawng zawngte kha a kalsan lo a, a la ching ta reng zawk a.
12. Tichuan Joasa chanchin dang te,a thiltih zawng zawng te, Juda lal Amazia a bei laia a thiltihtheihziate chu Israel lalte lal lai tu ziakna buah a chuang a ni lo'm ni?
13. Tin, Joasa chu api leh pute hnenah a muhil ta a; tin, Jeroboama chu a lalthutthlengah a thu a, tin, Joasa chu samari khuaah Israel lalte zingah an phum a.
14. Tin, Elisa chu a thihpuina tur natnain a lo na tawh a; tichuan Israel lal Joasa chu a hnenah a zuk kal a, a tah a, "Ka pa, ka pa, Israelte tawlailir leh sakawr chung chuang mite hentu," a ti a.
15. Tin, Elisan a hnenah, "Thalngul leh thalte han la teh," a ti a: tin, thalngul leh thalte a han la a.
16. Tin, Israelte lal hnenah chuan, "Thalngul kha han chelh teh," a ti a; tichuan a han chelh a. Tin, Elisa chuan lal kut chungah a kut a nghat a.
17. Tin, ani chuan, "Chhak lam tukverh han hawng teh," a ti a; tin, a han hawng a. Tin, Elisan, "Kap rawh," tin, a han kap a. Tin, ani chuan, "LALPA hnehna thal, Suria-ho hnehna thal a nih kha; Aphek khuaa chuan Suria-hote chu boral der kawpin i hneh dawn nia," a ti a.
18. Tin, 'Thalte kha han la teh," a ti leh a; tichuan a han la teh," a ti a; tichuan a han la a, Tin, Israel lal hnenah chuan, "Lei kha han chun te," a ti a; tichuan vawithum a chun a, a bang ta mai a.
19. Tichan Pathian mi chu a chungah a thinrim ta a, "Vawi nga vawi ruk lai i chun tur a sin ni le; tichuan Suria-hote chu boral der khawpin i hneh tur a ni a. Amaherawhchu Sria-ho chu vawi thum chauh i hneh dawn anih tak hi," a ti a.
20. Tin, Elisa chu a thi a, an phum ta a. Tin, kum alo veihian, Moab-ho pawlte chuan an ram chu an rawn run thin a.
21. Tin, mi pakhat an phum dawn lai takin an han hmu a; tichuan chu mi chu Elisa thlanah chuan an thlak ta mai a; tin, a ruang chu Elisa ruhte ch a zuk deh veleh, a lo nung leh a, a ding ta mai a.
22. Tin, Suria lal Hazaela chuan Jeoahaza chhanah chan Israelte chu a hnehchiah reng a.
23. Nimahsela LALPA'n Abrahama hnenah te, Isaaka hnenah te, Jakoba hnenah te thu a thlun avangin anmahni chu a khawngaih a, a lainat a, angaihsak a, anmahni chu a tiboral duh lo a, a hnen ata a la paih thla duh lo cheu a.
24. Tin, Suria lal Jazaela chu a thi a; a fapa Benhadada chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.Tin, Jehoahaza fapa Jehoasa chuan Jazael fapa Benhadadan a pa Jehoahaza a run a, khua a laksakte kha a chhuhsak leh a, Joasa chuan vawithum a hneh a, Israel ram khuate chu a la leh a.
25. Tin, Jehoahaza fapa Jehoasa chuan Jazael fapa Benhadadan a pa Jehoahaza a run a, khua a laksakte kha a chhuhsak leh a, Joasa chuan vawithum a hneh a, Israel ram khuate chu a la leh a.
