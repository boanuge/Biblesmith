1. Ngai teh u, Pain min hmangaihna chu a va nasa em! "Pathian faahte" min vuah tak hi; chutiang chu kan ni reng e. He mi avang hian khawvelin min hre lo a ni, amah chu an hriat loh avangin.
2. Duh takte u, tunah hian Pathian fate kan ni a, engtin nge kan la awm dawn tih erawh chu a la lang rih lo e; amah a lo inlar chuan amah angin kan awm dawn tih zawng kan hria, amah chu a awm pangngai ang takin kan hmu dawn si a.
3. Tupawh ama chunga chu mi beiseina chu nei apiangin amah a thianghlim angin a intithianghlim thin.
4. Tupawh thil tisual apiangin dan pawh a bawhchhe thin; sual chu dan bawhchhiatna a ni si a.
5. Amah chu sualte la bo turin a lo lang tawh tih in hria e; amahah chuan sual engmah a awm si lo a.
6. Tupawh amaha awm reng chuan thil a tisual lo; tupawh thil tisual chuan amah chu a hmu lo a, hriat pawh a hre hek lo.
7. Naute u, tuma bumin awm suh u; felna titu chu a fel a ni, amah a fel ang bawkin.
8. Thil tisualtu chu diabolaa chhuak a ni; diabola chuan atir ata thil a tisual si a, Pathian Fapa lo lan chhan ber chu diabola hnathawhte a tihboral theih nan a ni.
9. Tupawh Pathian hrin chuan Pathian chi amahah a awm reng avangin thil a tisual thin lo; Pathian hrin a nih avangin thil a tisual thei hek lo.
10. He miah hian Pathian fate leh diabola fate a hriat a ni; tupawh felna ti lo chu Pathiana chhuak a ni lo, a unau hmanaih loa pawh chu Pathiana chhuak a ni hek lo.
11. Hei hi atir ata thuchah in hriat kha a ni si a, kan inhmangaih tawn theuh tur a ni, tih hi;
12. Kaina, mi sual laka mi, mahni unau thattu ang khan awm loin. Eng vangin nge a thah tak le? Ama thiltihte a sual a, a unau thiltihte a fel avangin.
13. Unaute u, khawvelin an huat che u chuan mak ti suh u.
14. Unaute kan hmangih avangin thihna ata kan chhuak a, nunnaah kan lut tawh tih kan hria e. Tupawh hmangaihna nei lo apiang thihnaah a la awm reng a ni.
15. Tupawh a unau hua chu tualthat a ni; tualthattu tumah chatuana nunna pai reng reng an awm lo tih in hre si a.
16. He miah hian hmangaihna eng nge a nih kan hria, amah chuan kan tan a nunna a pek tak avangin; keini pawhin unaute tan kan nunna kan pe ve tur a ni.
17. Nimahsela, tupawhin khawvel sum neiin a unau tlachhamin hmu se la, ani chu lainat si suh se la, Pathian hmangaihna chu amahah engtin nge a awm ang?
18. Naute u, thu chauhin i hmangaih suh ang u khai, lei chauhin i hmangaih hek suh ang u, thiltih leh tih tak zetin ni zawk rawh se.
19. Chu miah chuan thutaka mi kan ni tih kan inhria ang a, kan rilruin thiam loh min chantirna kawngah chuan ama mithmuh ngeiah kan rilru kan thlem dai bawk ang;
20. Pathian chu kan rilru aiin a ropui zawk a, engkim a hre si a.
21. Duh takte u, kan rilruin thiam loh min chantir loh chuan Pathian lamah kan huai a ni;
22. tichuan, a thupekte kan zawm a, a mithmuha thil lawmawm kan tih thin avangin, kan dil apiang a hnen ata kan hmu zel thin a ni.
23. Tin, a thupek chu hei hi a ni, a Fapa Isua Krista hming ring i la, aman thu min pek angin inhmangaih tawn bawk i la.A thupekte zawm chu amahah a awm reng a, amah pawh chu amahah a awm reng bawk a ni. Tin, he miah hian amah chu keimahniah a awm reng tih kan hria, Thlarau min pekah hian.
24. A thupekte zawm chu amahah a awm reng a, amah pawh chu amahah a awm reng bawk a ni. Tin, he miah hian amah chu keimahniah a awm reng tih kan hria, Thlarau min pekah hian.
