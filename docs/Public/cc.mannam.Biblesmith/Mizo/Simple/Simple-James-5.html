1. Mi hausate u, ngai teh u khai! In chunga hrehawm lo thleng dawn mek avang chuan tap u la, chiau vak vak rawh u.
2. In rote a chhe ta a, in puante sulrulin a ei zo ta.
3. In rangkachak leh in tangkate a tuiek zo ta a; a tuiek chu in thiam lohzia hriattirna a ni ang a, in tisa chu mei kan angin a ei ang. Ni hnuhnungah chuan ro in khawl khawm a nih hi.
4. Ngai teh u, in loa buh ata inhlawhfate hlawh, tihdera in pek loh kha; a au a, a attute mangang au thawm pawh chu Mipuite Lalpa bengah a lo lut ta a.
5. Khawvelah hian nuamsa takin in awm a, in inkhawhral mai mai a; inthah niin in thinlung in titlai a.
6. Mi fel chu thiam loh in chantir a,in that ta a; ani chuan a dodal lo a che u.
7. Chutichuan, unaute u, Lalpa lo kal hma loh zawng chhel takin awm rawh u. Ngai teh u, lo neitu chuan ruah tui tla hmasa leh hnuhnung chu a hmuh hma loh chuan chhel takin lei rah hlu tak chu a nghak thin.
8. Nangni pawh chhel takin awm rawh u; in thinlung tinghet rawh u; Lalpa lo kalna chu a hnai tawh si a.
9. Unaute u, thiam loh changa in awm lohna'n, inphun suh u; ngai teh u, Roreltu chu kawngka bulah a ding reng a ni.
10. Unaute u, Lalpa hming thusawitu zawlneite chu, tuar dan leh chhel dan kawngah chuan entawn atan hmang rawh u.
11. Ngai teh u, tuartute chu engthawl kan ti a; Joba chhelzia chu in hria a, Lalpa'n a tawpa a tihzia pawh kha in hmu bawk a, Lalpa chu khawngaihnain a khahzia leh zah a ngaih theihzia pawh kha.
12. Nimahsela, ka unaute u, engpawh ti mah u la chhia chham suh u; van chhalin emaw, lei chhalin emaw, thil dang reng reng chhalin emaw chhia chham suh u; "Ni" in tih chu "Ni" ni se la, "Ni lo" in tih chu "Ni lo" ni rawh se; chutilochuan thiam loh changin in awm ang.
13. In zingah thil tha lo tuar an awm em? Tawngtai rawh se. Hlim an awm em? Fakna hla sa rawh se.
14. In zingah damlo an awm em? Kohhran upa kohtir se la, anin Lalpa hminga hriak hnawihin a chungah tawngtai rawh se;
15. tin, rinnaa tawngtaina chuan damlo chu a chhandam ang a, Lalpa chuan ani chu a kai tho ang; tin, thil a lo tihsual tawh chuan ngaihdamsak a ni ang.
16. Chutichuan, tihdama in awm theihna'n in sualte thupha chawi tawn u la, intawngtaisak tawn thin rawh u. Mi fel tawngtaina chuan thil nasa tak a ti thin.
17. Elija chu keimahni nungchang ang pu mihring bawk a ni a, ani chuan ruah sur lohna'n thahnemngai takin a tawngtai a; tin, kum thum leh thla ruk chhung zawng lei chungah ruah a sur ta lo a.
18. Tin, a tawngtai leh a, vanin ruah a surtir a, leiin a rahte a rawn chhuahtir ta a.
19. Ka unaute u, in zinga mi tupawh thutak atang chuan bo se la, tin, tuin emaw hawi kirtir se la,mi sual a kawng bona ata hawi kirtirtu chuan thihna ata a thlarau a chhandam ang a, sual tam tak a khuhsak bawk ang tih inhre rawh se.
20. mi sual a kawng bona ata hawi kirtirtu chuan thihna ata a thlarau a chhandam ang a, sual tam tak a khuhsak bawk ang tih inhre rawh se.
