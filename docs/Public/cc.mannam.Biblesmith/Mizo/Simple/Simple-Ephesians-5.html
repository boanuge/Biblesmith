1. Chutichuan, a fa duh takte angin Pathian awm dan latute ni rawh u.
2. Tin, Kristan a hmangaih che u a, rimtui atan Pathian hnena thilhlanah leh inthawinaa kan aia a inpek ang bawk khan hmangaihnaah awm rawh u.
3. Nimahsela, mi thianghlimte tiha mawi awm rengin, inngaih leh bawlhhlawh zawng zawng, duhamna nen in zingah lam pawh lamin awm suh se;
4. zahmawh te, thu lawi lo sawi te, fiamthu te pawh, chung chu a mawi lo a ni; chung tih ai chuan lawmthu hrilh zawk rawh u.
5. Inngaih hmang leh mi bawlhhlawh leh mi duham reng reng chuan (mi duham chu milem betu a ni a) Krista leh Pathian ramah chuan chan reng an nei lo a ni tih in hre hle si a.
6. Tuma'n thu lawi lo maiin bum che u suh se; chung thil avang chuan Pathian thinur chu awih lo fate chungah a thleng si thin a.
7. Chuvangin, anmahni tawmpui suh ang che u.
8. Hmanah khan thim in ni a, tunah erawh chuan Lalpaah chuan eng in lo nih tak hi;
9. Lalpa lawmzawng eng nge ni tih fiahin, eng fate angin awm rawh u,
10. eng rah chu thatna leh, felna leh, thutak zawng zawngah a lang si a.
11. Tin, thim thiltih rah nei ngai loahte chuan telna reng nei suh u la, tel ahnekin a thiam lohzia hriattir zawk rawh u;
12. a ruka an thil tih thinte chu a sawi pawh a zahthlak si a.
13. Amaherawhchu thil engkim a thiam lohzia hriattira awm hi eng tihlan a ni thin: tihlana awm apiang chu eng a ni si a.
14. Chuvangin, "Nang muhil! meng la, mitthi zing ata tho rawh, tichuan Krista chuan a chhun eng ang che," a tih kha.
15. Chutichuan, mi fing lote anga awm loin mi fingte anga awm zawkin, in awm danah fimkhur rawh u;
16. nite hi a that loh avangin remchannate lei zel rawh u.
17. Chu mi avang chuan a duh suh u la, Lalpa duhzawng eng nge ni tih lo hre zawk rawh u.
18. Tin, uaiin ruiin awm suh u, chu miah chuan insum lohna a awm si a; Thlarauva khatin awm zawk rawh u.
19. Fakna hlate, Pathian hlate, thlarau lam hlatea inbe tawnin, Lalpa chu in thinlungah hla saksakin, tingtang kaihsak rawh u.
20. Kan Lalpa Isua Krista hmingin, Pathian, Pa ngei hnenah chuan engkim chungah lawmthu hril fo rawh u.
21. Krista tih dekin intulut tawn theuh rawh u.
22. Nupuite u, Lalpa hnena in intulut ang hian mahni pasalte hnenah intulut rawh u.
23. Krista chu amah ngei taksa chhandamtu nia kohhranho lu a ni ang bawk hian, pasal chu nupui lu a ni si a.
24. Chutichuan, kohhranho Krista hnena an intulut ang bawk hian nupuite chu engkimah an pasalte hnenah intulut rawh se.
25. Pasalte u, in nupuite hmangaih rawh u, Kristan kohhranho a hmangaih a, an aia mahni a inpe ang khan,
26. anni chu tuia sila thu chuan tifaiin a tihthianghlim theihna'n;
27. chutichuan kohhranho chu bawlhhlawh kai lo leh zur lo leh, chutiang thil reng pu loin, thianghlim leh hmelhem loa awm zawkin, amah leh amah kohhranho ropui takah a inpe chawp thei ang.
28. Chutiang bawkin pasalte pawhin mahni taksate angin mahni nupuite chu an hmangaih tur a ni. Mahni nupui hmangaihtu chu mahni inhmangaih a ni.
29. Tuma'n mahni tisa an haw ngai si lo a, an chawmin an duat zawk thin a ni. Kristan kohhranho a chawmin a duat ang hian;
30. a taksa pengte kan ni si a.
31. Chu mi avang chuan mipain a nu leh pa kalsanin a nupui a vuan ang a, tin, an pahnih chuan tisa pumkhat an lo ni tawh ang.
32. Hei hi thu ril thuk tak a ni; Krista leh a kohhran thu kei chuan a sawina niin ka hria.Engpawh ni se, tupawhin mahni a inhmangaih angin mahni nupui hmangaih theuh se la, nupui pawhin a pasal hlau thiam rawh se.
33. Engpawh ni se, tupawhin mahni a inhmangaih angin mahni nupui hmangaih theuh se la, nupui pawhin a pasal hlau thiam rawh se.
