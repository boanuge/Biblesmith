1. Hengte hi Israela fapate chu an ni; Reubena, Simeona, Levia, Juda, Isakara, Zebuluna,
2. Dana, Josefa, Benjamina, naphtalia, Gada, Asera.
3. Juda fapate chu Era, Onana, Sela: chu mite pathum chu Kanaan mi Bathsuii laka piang an ni. Tin, Juda fa tir Era chu LALPA mithmuhin mi sual a ni a; tichuan a tihlum ta a.
4. Tin, a monu Tamari chuan Pereza leh Zera a hrinsak a. Juda fapate zawng zawng chu panga an ni.
5. Pereza fapate chu Jezrona leh Hamula an ni.
6. Tin, Zera fapate chu Zimria te, Ethana te, Hemana te, Kalkola te, Dara te an ni: an vaiin panga an ni.
7. Tin, Karmia fapa chu Akara, Israelte tibuaitu, thilhlan bawhchhetupa kha a ni.
8. Tin, Ethana fapa chu Azaria a ni.
9. Hezrona fapa te, a laka piangte chu Jerameela te, Rama te, Kelubaia te an ni.
10. Tin, Raman Aminadaba ahring a; tin, Aminadaban Juda thlah mi lian Nasona a hring a;
11. tin, Nasonan Salma a hring a, tin, Salman Boaza a hring a;
12. tin, Boazan Obeda a hring a, tin, Obedan Jesaia a hring a;
13. tin, Jesaian a fa tirah Eliaba a hring a, a apahnihna chu Abinadaba, a pathumna chu Simea,
14. a palina chu Nethanela, a pangana chu Radaia,
15. a parukna chu Ozema, a pasarihna chu Davida a ni:
16. tin, an farnute chu Zeruii leh Abigaili an ni. Tin, Zeruii fapate chu Abisaia te, Joaba te, Asahela te an ni a, pathum an ni.
17. Tin, Abigailin Amasa a hring a: tin, Amasa pa chu Ismael mi Jethera a ni.
18. Tin, Hezrona fapa Kaleban a nupui Azubi, Jeriotha fanu lakah chuan fate a hring a: tin, hengte hi a fapate chu an ni: Jesera, Sobaba, Ardona,
19. Tin, Azubi chu a thi a, Kaleba chuan Ephrathi a nei a, ani chuan Hura a hrinsak a.
20. Tin, Hura chuan Uria a hring a, Urian Bezalela a hring a.
21. Tin, chu mi hnuah chuan Hezrona chuan Gilead pa Makira fanu a pawl a; kum sawmruk a nihin chu mi chu a nei a; ani chuan Seguba a hrinsak a.
22. Tin, Seguba chuan Jaira a hring a, ani chuan Gilead ramah khawpui sawmhnih leh pathum a nei a.
23. Tin, Gesura leh Arama chuan jaira khawpui te, Kenath khua leh a khawperte nen, khaw sawmruk alaksak a. Chung zawng zawng chu Gileada pa Makira fapate chu an ni.
24. Tin, Keleb-ephrath-a khua a Jezrona a thih hnu chuan Hezrona nupui Aibiji chuan Tekoa pa Asura a hrinsak a.
25. Tin, Hezrona fa tir Jerameela fapa upa ber chu Rama ani a, tin, Buna te, Orena te, Ozema te, Ahija te an ni.
26. Tin, Jerameela chuan nupui dang a nei a, a hming chu Atari a ni a, Onama nu a ni.
27. Jerameela fapa upa ber Rama fapate chu maaza te, Jamina te, Ekera-te an ni.
28. Tin, Onama fapate chu Samaia leh Jada an ni a; tin, Samaia fapa te chu nadaba leh Abisura an ni.
29. Tin, Abisura nupuia hming chu Abihaili a ni; tin, ani chuan Abana leh Molida a hrinsak a.
30. Tin, Nadaba fapate chu Seleda leh Apaima an ni; Seleda erawh chu fa nei loin a thi a.
31. Tin, Apaima fapa chu Isia a ni a. Tin, Isia fapa chu Sesana a ni a. Tin, Sesana fapa chu Alaia a ni.
32. Tin, Samaia unaupa Jada fapate chu Jethera leh Jonathana an ni: tin, Jethera chu fa nei loin a thi a.
33. Tin, Jonathana fapate chu Peletha leh Zaza an ni. Chungte chu Jerameela thlahte an ni.
34. Sesana chuan fanute tih loh chu fa a nei lo a. Tin, Sesana chuan Aigupta mi chhiahhlawh anei a, a hming chu Jara a ni.
35. Tin, Sesana chuan a fanu chu a chhiahhlawh Jara chu nupuiah a neihtir a; tin, Ataia a hrinsak a.
36. Tin, Ataia chuan nathana a hring a, tin, Nathana chuan Zabada a hring a,
37. tin, Zabada chuan Ephlala a hring a, tin, Ephlala chuan Obeda ahring a,
38. tin, Obeda chuan Jehua a hring a, tin, Jehua chuan Azaria ahing a.
39. Tin, Azaria chuan Heleza a hring a, tin, Heleza chuan Eleasa a hring a,
40. tin, Eleasa chuan Sismaia a hring a, tin, Sismaia chuan Saluma a hring a;
41. tin, Saluma chuan Jekamia a hring a, tin, Jekamia chuan Elisama a hring a.
42. Tin, Jerameela unaupa Keleba fa tir chu Zip-ho thlahtu Mesaka a ni:
43. Tin, Hebrona fapate chu Kora te, Tapua te, Rekema te, Sema te an ni.
44. Tin, Seman Jorkeama pa Rahama a hring a; tin, Rekeman Samaia a hring a.
45. Tin, Samaia fapa chu Maona a ni a; tin, Maona chu Beth-zura pa a ni.
46. Tin, Kaleba hmei Ephi chuan Harana te, Moza te, Gazeza te a hring a tin, harana chuan Gazeza a hring a.
47. Tin, Judaii fapate chu Regema te, Jethama te, Gesana te, Peleta te, Epha te, Saapha te an ni.
48. Kaleba hmei Maakin Sebera leh Tirhana a hring a.
49. Madmana pa Saapha leh Makbena leh Gibea pa Seva a hring bawk a; tin, Kaleba fanu chu Aksi a ni.
50. Hengte hi Kaleba thlahte an ni: Hura fa, Kiriath-jeraim-ho thlahtu, Ephrathi fa tir Sobala,
51. tin, Beth-lehem-ho thlahtu Salma, tin, Beth-gader-ho thlahtu Harepha te hi.
52. Tin, Kiriath-jearim-ho thlahtu Sobala chuan Horoe-ho leh Menuhot-ho zatve a thlah a.
53. Tin, Kiriath-jearim hnamte chu Ithra-ho te, Put-ho te, Sumat-ho te, Misra-ho te an ni; chungho atang chuan Zorat-ho te leh Estaol-ho te an lo chhuak a.
54. Salma thlahte chu Beth-lehem-ho te, Netophat-ho te, Atrot-beth-joab-ho te, Manahat-ho zatve te, Zor-ho te an ni.Tin, Jabez-a awm lehkhaziaktu hnamte chu Tirat-ho te, Simeat-ho te, Sukat-ho te an ni. Chungte chu Rekaba thlahte zinga mi Hamatha thlah Ken-hote chu an ni.
55. Tin, Jabez-a awm lehkhaziaktu hnamte chu Tirat-ho te, Simeat-ho te, Sukat-ho te an ni. Chungte chu Rekaba thlahte zinga mi Hamatha thlah Ken-hote chu an ni.
