1. Tin, unaute u, kei zawng Pathian thuruk hrilh che uin in hnena ka lo kal lai khan, thusawi ropui leh finna ropui nena lo kal ka ni lo.
2. Isua Krista, amah ngei khenbeha a awm thu chauh lo chu in zingah engmah reng hriat ka tum loh kha.
3. Tin, chak lo leh hlauin, nasa taka khur chungin in zingah ka awm a.
4. Tin, in rinna chu mihring finna innghat loin, Pathian thiltihtheihnaa innghata a awm theih zawkna turin,
5. ka tawngka chhuak leh ka thu hrilhte chu thu finga thlemna thuin a ni lo a, Thlarau leh thiltihtheihna hriattirin a nih zawk kha.
6. Amaherawhchu puitlingte zingah chuan thu fing zawng ka sawi thin, nimahsela he khawvel thu fing hi a ni lo, he khawvel roreltu boral turte thu fing hi a ni hek lo;
7. Pathian thu fing thuruk chu kan sawi zawk thin a ni, thu fing inthup, kan ropuina tura Pathianin khawvel awm hmaa a ruat lawk tawh chu.
8. Chu chu he khawvel roreltute reng reng hian an hre lo; hria se la chuan ropuina Lalpa chu an khengbet lo ang.
9. "Mita hmuh ngai loh, benga hriat ngai loh, mihring rilrua lut ngai lo, Patianin a hmangaihtute tan a buatsaih apiangte chu," tih ziak ang zawk khan.
10. Keini erawh chu chung chu Pathianin Thlarauin min hriattir ta a ni; Thlarau chuan engkim a chhui chhuak si thin; a ni. Pathian thu ril pawh.
11. Mihring thute chu mihring rilru, a chhunga awm vek lo chuan mihring zingah tuin nge hria ang? Chutiang bawkin Pathian thute chu Pathian Thlarau vek lo chuan tuma'n an hre lo.
12. Chutichuan athlawna Pathianin thil min pekte chu kan hriat theihna turin, keini zawngin khawvel thlarau ni loin, Thlarau, Pathian hnena mi chu kan hmu zawk a ni.
13. Mihring finna zirtir thua sawi loin, Thlarau zirtir thua sawi zawkin, Thlarau lam thute chu thlarau lam mite hnena hrilhfiahin, chung thil min pek thute pawh chu kan sawi thin.
14. Khawvel mi chuan Pathian Thlarau lam thilte chu a lawm thin lo, ani ngaih chuan atthlak a ni si a; Thlarauva hriat fiah a nih avangin a hre thei lo reng reng bawk a ni.
15. Thlarau mi erawh chuan thil engkim a hre fiah thin a, ani lah chu tuma'n an hre fiah si lo.Tuin nge Lalpa rilru hre tawha amah chu zirtir thei ang? Keini erawh chuan Krista rilru kan pu a ni.
16. Tuin nge Lalpa rilru hre tawha amah chu zirtir thei ang? Keini erawh chuan Krista rilru kan pu a ni.
