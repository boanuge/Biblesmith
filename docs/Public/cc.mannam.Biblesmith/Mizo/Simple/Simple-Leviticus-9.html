1. Tin, heti hi a ni a, a ni riat ni chuan Mosia'n Arona leh a fapate leh Israel upahote chu a ko a;
2. tin, Arona hnenah chuan, "Sual thawina'n bawng no a pa leh halral thilhlanah berampa sawisebo ve ve kai la, LALPA hmaah hlan rawh.
3. Tin, Israela thlahte hnenah chuan, 'Sual thawina'n kelpa, tin, halral thilhlanah bawng no leh beram no kum khat mi ve ve sawiselbo thiau
4. leh bawngpa leh berampa LALPA hmaa remna thilhlana hlanah rawn kai u la: tin, hriaka hmeh chhangphut thilhlan nen: vawiinah hian in hnenah LALPA a lo lang dawn e,' tiin hrilh rawh," a ti a.
5. Tin, Mosia'n thu a pek ang chuan inbiakna puan in kawtah an rawn kai a; tin, mi zawng zawng chuan an va hnaih a, LALPA hmaah chuan an va ding a.
6. Tin, Mosia chuan, "Hei hi a ni in tih tura LALPA'n thu apek che u kha: tichuan LALPA ropuina chu in zingah a lo lang ang." a ti a.
7. Tin, Mosia'n Arona hnenah, "Maicham chu rawn hnaih la, I sual thawina te, I halral thilhlan te hlan la, nangma tan leh mipuite tan inremna siam rawh: tin, mipuite thilhlan chu hlan la, an tan inremna siam rawh, LALPA thupek angin," a ti a.
8. Tin, Arona chuan maicham chu a va hnaih a, ama puala sual thawina bawng no chu a talh a.
9. Tin, Arona fapate chuan thisen chu a hnenah an hlan a, thisenah chuan a kut zungtang a chiah a, maicham kiahte chuan a tat a, thisen chu maicham bulah a leih a:
10. sual thawina thau te, a kal te, a thin tuamtu rang te erawh chu maicham chungah a hal a, LALPA'n Mosia thu a pek ang kha.
11. Tin, a sa leh a vun chu daipawnah a hal a.
12. Tin, halral thilhlan chu a talh a; Arona fapate chuan thisen chu an pe a, anin maicham chu a theh vel a.
13. Halral thilhlan sa hrang chu a mal malin a lu nen an pe a, ani chuan maichamah a lo hal a.
14. Tin, a kawchhung te, a kete a sil a, maichamah halral thilhlan chungah chuan a hal a.
15. Tin, mipuite thilhlan chu a hlan a, mipuite sual thawina kel chu a kai a, a talh a, a hmaa mi ang bawk khan sual thawinaah a hlan a.
16. Tin, halral thilhlan chu a hlan a, a dan ang zelin a hlan a ni.
17. Tin, chhangphut thilhlan chu a hlan a, chuta mi chu a vei khatin a la a, zing lam halral thilhlan tih loh pawh chu maichamah huan a hal bawk a.
18. tin, mipuite puala remna thilhlan inthawina bawngpa leh berampa chu a talh bawk a; tin, Arona fapaten a thisen chu an pe a, maicham chu a theh vel a;
19. tin, bawng pa thau leh berampa thau te, a mei thau tak te, a rilbawh te, a kal te, a thituamtu rang te an pe bawk a;
20. a thau chu a ir chungah te chuan an dah a, tin, a ni chuan a thau chu maichamah a lo hal a.
21. Tin, a ir leh bawp dinglam chu Aronan LALPA hmaah then thilhlanah a then a; Mosia thupek ang khan.
22. Tin, Arona chuan mipuite lamah chuan a ban a phar a, mal a sawm hlawm a; tin, sual thawina leh halral thilhlan leh remna thilhlan a hlan zo chu a lo chhuk a.
23. Tin, Mosia leh Arona chu inbiakna puan inah an va lut a, tin, an lo chhuak leh a, mipuite chu mal an sawm a: tin, LALPA ropuina chu mi zawng zawng chungah chuan a lo lang a.Tin, LALPA hma atang chuan mei a lo chhuak a, halral thilhlan leh thaute chu maichamah chuan a kangral ta a: tin, chu chu mipui zawng zawngin an hmuh chuan an au dur dur a, an bawkkhup ta thuap a.
24. Tin, LALPA hma atang chuan mei a lo chhuak a, halral thilhlan leh thaute chu maichamah chuan a kangral ta a: tin, chu chu mipui zawng zawngin an hmuh chuan an au dur dur a, an bawkkhup ta thuap a.
