1. "Kan lama tang chu LALPA ni suh se la chuan," Israel-hoin ti rawh se;
2. "Miten mi bei lai khan Kan lama tang chu LALPA ni suh se la chuan:
3. Kha mi lai ngei khan anni chuan kan nung chungin min lem tawh ang, Kan chunga an thin a thawh lai khan:
4. Kha mi lai ngei khan tuite chuan min chim pil tawh ang a, Lui chuan kan nuna hi a vur pil tawh ang: Kha mi lai ngei khan tui chapote chuan kan nuna hi a vur pil tawh ang.
5. Sa seh anga seh tura an haa min pe lotu LALPA chu, fakin awm rawh se.
6. Thangkamtute thang ata sava a chhuak angin kan nunna hi a chuak ta: Thang chu a chhe ta a, keini hi kan chhuak ta hlauh a ni.Min tanpuina tur chu. LALPA, lei leh van siamtu hmingah a awm," tiin.
7. Min tanpuina tur chu. LALPA, lei leh van siamtu hmingah a awm," tiin.
