1. Tin, Isua'n mipuite leh a zirtirte hnenah thu a sawi a.
2. Lehkhaziaktute leh Pharisaite chu Mosia thutphahah chuan an thu thin a.
3. Chuvangin an hrilh apiang che u chu ti u la, zawm zel ang che u; an thiltih ang erawh chu ti ve suh ang che u, sawi zawng an sawi thin a, an ti si thin lo a ni.
4. Phur rit hrehawm takte an siam a, mite an bel thin a, anmahni erawh chuan an kut zungtang pawhin an tisawn duh lo.
5. An thiltih zawng zawng chu mihring hmuha tan a ni, an tih zawk thin ni, an thil bun leh khimte chu an tihlai a, an puan hmawr fem an tibuk thin a.
6. Ruaithehnaah hmun tha ber te, inkhawmna inah thutphah tha ber te,
7. dawr hmuna chibai buk te, miten 'Rabbi' an tih te an ngaina si thin a.
8. Nangni erawh chu 'Rabbi' tihin awm suh u; in zirtirtu chu pakhat chauh a ni a, nangni zawng zawng chu unau in ni si a.
9. Tin, lei chungah hian tumah 'Ka pa' ti suh u; in Pa chu pakhat chauh a ni, vana mi khi.
10. 'Hruaitute' tihin awm hek suh u, in hruaitu chu pakhat chauh a ni, Krista chu.
11. In zinga ropui ber chu in rawngbawltuah a awm zawk tur a ni.
12. Tupawh inngaihlu apiang an tlawm ang a, tupawh inngaitlawm apiang chawimawiin an awm ang.
13. Nimahsela nangni lehkhaziaktu leh Pharisai vervekte u, in chung a pik e! Van ram kawng chu mite in kharsak si thin.
14. Nangni lah in lut si lo a, mi lut turte pawh luh in phal thin hek lo.
15. Nangni lehkhaziaktu leh Pharisai vervekte u, in chung a pik e! Saphun pakhat pawh siam turin tuifinriat leh khawmual in kal hual si a, tin, chutianga a lo awm veleh ani chu nangmahni leh hnihin Gehena fapaah in siam thin.
16. Nangni, hruaitu mitdel, 'Tupawh Pathian biak in chhala chhechham apiang, chu chu engmah a ni lo; tupawh Pathian biak ina rangkachak chhala chhechham apiang erawh chuan hlen tur a ni,' tite u, in chung a pik e!
17. Nangni mi a, mitdelte u! A eng nge ropui zawk, rangkachak nge, Pathian biak in, rangkachak tithianghlimtu chu?
18. Tin, 'Tupawh maicham chhala chhechham apiang, engmah a ni lo; a chunga thilpek awm chhala chhechham apiang erawh chuan hlen tur a ni' in ti.
19. Nangni mitdelte u! A eng nge ropui zawk, thilpek nge, maicham, thilpek tithianghlimtu chu?
20. Chutichuan, tupawh maicham chhala chhechham apiang chuan maicham leh a chunga awm zawng zawng chhalin chhia a chham thin.
21. Tupawh Pathian biak in chhala chhechham apiang chuan, Pathian biak in leh a chhunga cheng chu chhalin chhia a chham thin.
22. Tupawh van chhala chhechham apiang chuan, Pathian thutphah leh a chunga thu chhalin chhia a chham thin.
23. Nangni lehkhaziaktu leh Pharisai vervekte u, in chung a pik e! Pudina te, mohuri te, zira te chu sawmah pakhat in pe thin a, dan thu pawimawh zawk - felna te, khawngaihna te, rinawmna te chu - in ngaihthah si thin; nimahsela, chung chu a sin in tih tur ni, a dangte chu tih thulh chuang loin.
24. Nangni hruaitu mitdel, thosi thlifaia sanghawngsei lem si thinte u!
25. Lehkhaziaktu leh Pharisai vervekte u, in chung a pik e! No leh thleng pawn lam in silfai a, a chhung erawh chu hleprukna leh insum lohnain a khat si a.
26. Nang Pharisai mitdel, no leh thleng chhung lam chu silfai hmasa rawh, a pawn lam pawh a lo fai theihna turin.
27. Lehkhaziaktu leh Pharisai vervekte u, in chung a pik e! Thlan hnawih var, pawn lam mawi taka lang, a chhung lam erawh chu mitthi ruhte leh bawlhhlawh tinrenga khat angte chu in ni a sin.
28. Nangni pawh chutiang takin pawnlamah chuan mihring mithmuhin fel takin in lang thin a, in chhung lamah erawh chuan vervekna leh sualnain in khat si a.
29. Nangni lehkhaziaktu leh Pharisai vervekte u, in chung a pik e! Zawlneiho thlan inte in sa a, mi fel thlante pawh in cheimawi a.
30. Kan pute dam lai khan lo awm ve mah i la, zawlneite thisen an chhuahnaah chuan kan tel ve lo ang,' in ti thin.
31. Chutichuan, zawlneiho thattute fa in ni tih in inhriattir a ni.
32. In pute tehkhawng chu tikhat nghal mai rawh u.
33. Rulte u, rul thlahte u, Gehenna rorelna chu engtin nge in pumpelh theih ang?
34. Ngai teh u, chu mi avang chuan in hnenah zawlneite, mi fingte, lehkhaziaktute ka rawn tir dawn; thenkhat in tihlum ang a, kraws-ah in khengbet ang, thenkhat in inkhawmna inahte savunhruiin in vua ang a, khaw tinah in tiduhdah zui zel ang.
35. Chutichuan, Abela mi fel tak thisen atangin, Pathian biak in leh maicham inkara Barakia fapa Zakaria in thaha thisen thleng khan, lei chunga mi fel thisen an chhuah zawng zawng chu nangmahni chungah a lo la thleng thei ang.
36. Tih tak meuhin ka hrilh a che u, chung zawng zawng chu tun lai chhuante chung hian a lo la thleng ang.
37. Aw Jerusalem, Jerusalem! Zawlneiho thattu, i hnena an rawn tirhte lunga lo denghlumtu, arpuiin a note a thla hnuaia a awp khawm angin i fate vawi engzat nge awp khawm ka tum kha! Nimahsela in duh si lo.
38. En teh u, in in chu a ruaka hnutchhiah che u in a awm ta.Ka hrilh a che u, 'Lalpa hminga lo kal chu fakin awm rawh se,' in tih hma loh chuan, tun achinah mi hmu leh dawn tawh lo a nih hi, a ti a.
39. Ka hrilh a che u, 'Lalpa hminga lo kal chu fakin awm rawh se,' in tih hma loh chuan, tun achinah mi hmu leh dawn tawh lo a nih hi, a ti a.
