1. Chutichuan unaute u, Pathian khawngaihna avang hian, inthawina nung leh thianghlim leh Pathian lawm tlak ni turin in taksa chu inhlanah ka ngen a che u; chu chu in rawngbawlna awm reng a ni.
2. He khawvel dan ang hian awm suh u la; Pathian duhzawng, a tha leh lawm tlak leh, that famkim chu in hriatfiah theihna'n, in rilru athara awmin lo danglam zawk tawh rawh u.
3. Pathianin mi pek khawngaihna avang hian in zingah tumah in inngaihhlut tawk aliama inngaihlu loin, Pathianin mi tin hnena rinna tawk a pek ang zelin fel taka ngaihtuah zawk turin ka ti theuh a che u.
4. Taksa pumkhatah peng tam tak kan nei a, peng zawng zawng thiltih erawh chu a inang lo a,
5. chutiang bawkin keini tam tak ni mah i la, Kristaah chuan taksa pumkhat kan ni a: a mal malin inpeng tawn theuh kan ni.
6. Khawngaihna min pek angzia zelin thilthlawnpek hrang hrang kan nei theuh a, thu hrilh tur a nih chuan kan rin tawk ang zelin I hril ang u;
7. rawng bawl tur a nih chuan rawng I bawl tlat ang u; zirtirtu pawhin zirtir tlat rawh se;
8. zilhtu pawhin zilh tlat rawh se; thil petu chuan ui loin pe rawh se; roreltuin taima takin rel rawh se; mi khawngaihtuin lawm takin khawngaih rawh se.
9. Hmangaihna chu vervekna tel loin ni rawh se. Sual lam chu hua u la, tha lam chu pawm tlat rawh u.
10. Unaute hmangaih kawngah chuan hmangaih takin inlainat tawn theuh rawh u; chawimawi kawngah chuan induhsak tawn theuh u la;
11. taihmak kawngah chuan thatchhe suh u la; thlarau kawngah chuan thahnemngai ula; Lalpa rawng bawl u la;
12. beiseina kawngah chuan lawm u la; hrehawm tuarna kawngah chuan chhel u la tawngtaina kawngah chuan tawngtai fan fan u la;
13. mi thianghlimte tlakchhamnaah chhawmdawl u la; mikhualte lawmin awm fo u la.
14. A tiduhdahtu che u chu malsawmsak u la; malsawm u la, anchhia chu lawh suh u.
15. Lawmte chu lawmpui u la, tapte chu tahpui rawh u.
16. Ngaihtuah hmunkhatin awm tawn theuh rawh u. Inngaihtuah sang lutuk suh u la, mi tete pawl zawk rawh u. Intifing suh u.
17. Tu sual pawh sualin thungrul suh u. Mi zawng zawng ngaiha thil mawi ngaihtuah lawk rawh u.
18. A theih phawt chuan nangmahni lam tal, mi zawng zawng nen inremin awm rawh u.
19. Duh takte u, in phuba la suh u la, Pathian thinurna kian zawk rawh u, "Phuba reng reng keima lak tur a ni, keima'n ka thungrul ang,' Lalpain a ti," tih ziak a ni si.
20. Chuvangin i hmelma a riltam chuan ei tur pe rawh, a tuihal chuan in tur pe rawh: chutiang i tih chuan a lu chungah meiling i chhek khawm a ni ang.Sual ngam chein awm suh la, thatnain sual chu ngam zawk rawh.
21. Sual ngam chein awm suh la, thatnain sual chu ngam zawk rawh.
