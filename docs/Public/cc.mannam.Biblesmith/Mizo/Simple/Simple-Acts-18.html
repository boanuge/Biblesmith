1. Chung thil hnuah chuan Paula chu Atheni khua ata chu a chhuak a, Korinth khuaah a kal ta a.
2. Tin, a hming Akuilia Juda pakhat a hmu a, chu mi chu Ponto rama piang a ni a, Klaudian Juda zawng zawng Rom khua ata chhuah thu a pek avangin a nupui Priskili nen Itali ram ata lo kal hlim an ni a.
3. Tin, Paula chu, chu mite hnenah chuan a thleng ta a, tin, an hnathawh ang thawk a nih avangin anmahni a hrawn a, an hnenah a thawk ve thin a; an hnathawh chu puan in siam a ni a.
4. Tin, Chawlhni apiangin inkhawmna inah khun takin thu a hril thin a, Judate leh Grikte chu thlem thluk a tum thin a.
5. Tin, Sila leh Timothea chu Makedonia ram ata chu an lo chhukin, Paula chu thu hril chauhin a awm ta a, Isua kha Krista chu a ni tih Judate hnenah a hrilhfiah thin a.
6. Nimahsela an dodala an sawtin Paulan a puan a thing a, an hnenah, "In thihna mawh chu nangmahniin phur ang che u; kei zawng ka hul a ni e; tun achinah Jentailte hnenah ka kal ang," a ti a.
7. Tin, chuta ta chu a kal a, a hming Tita Justa mi pakhat inah a lut a, chu mi chuan Pathian a be thin a; a in pawh chu inkhawmna in nen a inzawm a ni.
8. Tin, Krispa, inkhawmna in hotu chuan a chhungte zawng zawng nen Lalpa an ring a, Korinth khuaa mitam tak pawhin an hria a, an ring ve a, baptisma an chang ta a.
9. Tin, zanah Lalpa'n Paula hnenah chuan mumang angin, "Hlau suh la, ngawi loin thu sawi zel rawh;
10. kei I hnenah ka awm alawm, tina tur chein tuma'n I chungah kut an thlak lo vang; he khuaah hian mi tam tak ka nei si a," a ti a.
11. Tin, Paula chu chutah chuan an zingah Pathian thu zirtirin kum khat leh thla ruk a awm ta a.
12. Tin, Galliova Akaia rama lal aiawha a awm laiin Judaten rual takin Paula an bei a, rorelna thutphah hmaah an hruai a,
13. "He mi hrep hian Dan lo anga Pathian be turin mi a thlemthlu thin a ni," an ti a.
14. Tin, Paulan a chhan dawnin, Galliovan Judate hnenah chuan "Judate u, he'ng hi thil dik lo thu emaw thiltih sual emaw tal ni se la, mathei loin ka ngaihsak ang che u a;
15. nimahsela thute, hmingte, in dan chung thute mai a nih chuan nangmahni'n rel zawk rawh u; kei zawngin chutiang thu chu ngaihtuah ka duh lo e," a ti a.
16. Tin, rorel thutphah atang chuan anni chu a hnawt bo ta a.
17. Tin, Grik mi zawng zawngin inkhawmna in hotu Sosthena an man a, rorel thutphah hmaah chuan an vua a, Galliova chuan chung an thiltih chu engah mah a ngai lo a.
18. Tin, chu mi hnuah chuan Paula chu ni rei tak a la cham reng a; chu mi zawh chuan unaute chu tawng thain a then a, chuta ta chu Suria ram lamah lawngin a kal ta a, a hnenah Priskili leh Akuila chu an kal ve bawk a; Paulan thutiam a neih avangin Kenkriai khuaah a lu a met zet a.
19. Tin, Ephesi khua chu an thleng a,chutah chuan anni chu a hnutchhiah ta a;ani erawh chu inkhawmna inah a lut a, Judate hnenah khun takin thu a sawi rih a.
20. Tin, rei deuh chamah an ngen a, nimahsela a remti lo a,
21. "Pathianin a phal chuan in hnenah ka lo kal leh ang," tiin tawng thain a then a, Ephesi khua ata chu lawngin a kal ta a.
22. Tin, lawng ata chu Kaisari khuaah a chhuak a, Jerusalem khuaah a han kal a, kohhranhote chu chibai a buk a; tin, chu mi zawhah chuan Antiokei khuaah a zuk kal ta a.
23. Tin, chutah chuan rei ngial a awm hnuin a kal leh a, zirtirho zawng zawng tinghetin Galatia ram leh Phrugia ram chu a fang chhuak vek a.
24. Tin, Juda pakhat, a hming Appollova, Aleksandria khuaa piang, mi fing tak, Ephesi khuaah chuan a zuk kal a; ani chuan Pathian Lehkha Thute a hre nasa em em a.
25. Chu mi chu Lalpa Kawng thu zir tawh a ni a, rilrua thahnemngai tak a nih avangin Isua chanchin chu ngun takin a hril a, a zirtir thin a, Johana baptisma thu chauh a hre si a.
26. Tin, inkhawmna inah huai takin thu a sawi tan ta a. Chutichuan Priskili leh Akuila chuan a thusawi an hriatin an inah an hruai a, a hnenah Pathian Kawng thu fel lehzualin an hrilhfiah ta a.
27. Tin, ani chuan Akaia rama kal kai a tum veleh unauten an fuih a, amah an lo lawmna turin zirtirhote hnenah lehkha an ziaksak a; tin, a thlen chuanin Pathian khawngaihna avanga ringtute chu, nasa takin a pui a;vantlang hriatin Judate chu nasa takin a tidang a, Pathian Lehkha Thu hmangin Isua kha Krista chu a ni tih a hriattir thin a.
28. vantlang hriatin Judate chu nasa takin a tidang a, Pathian Lehkha Thu hmangin Isua kha Krista chu a ni tih a hriattir thin a.
