1. LALPA chu fak rawh u. Aw ka nunna, LALPA chu fak rawh.
2. Ka dam chhung zawngin LALPA chu ka fak ang a: Ka nun chhung zawngin ka Pathian fakin ka zai ang.
3. Mi liante chu ring suh u la, Mihring fapa, tanpuina reng reng awm lohna chu, ring hek suh u.
4. A thawin a chauhsan a, ani chu a leilungah a kir leh a; Chu mi ni ngei chuan a ngaihtuahnate chu a bo nghal a ni.
5. Jakoba Pathian tanpuitua nei chu, lawm takin a awm thin, LALPA Pathiana beiseina neitu chu:
6. Ani chu lei te, van te, Tuifinriatte leh an chhunga awm zawng zawng siamtu a ni; Ani chuan thutak kumkhua atan a vawng tha thin a;
7. Ani chuan hnehchhiahna tuarte tan ro a rel thin a; Ani chuan riltamte hnenah chaw a pe thin: LALPA chuan mi tangte a chhuah thin a;
8. LALPA chuan mitdelte mit a tizvar thin a; LALPA chuan kun ngawih ngawihte chu a kai tho thin a; LALPA chuan mi felte chu a hmangaih thin a;
9. LALPA chuan mikhualte chu a vawng tha thin a; Pa nei lo leh hmeithai chu a chelh reng thin a; Mi suaksualte kawng erawh chu a tiletling thin.
10. LALPA chu kumkhuain lalah a thu ang, Aw Zion, i Pathian chu chuan zawng zawng thlengin lalah a thu ang.LALPA chu fak rawh u.
