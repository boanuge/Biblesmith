1. Isakara fapate chu Tola te, Pua te, Jasuba te, Simrona te an ni a, pali an ni.
2. Tin, Tola fapate chu Uzia te, Rephaia te, Jeriela te, Jamaia te, Ibsama te, Samuela te an ni a, Tola thlahte chhungkaw puipa an ni: anmahni chhuana mi chak huaisen an ni a; Davida dam lai chuan singhnih leh sanghnih leh zaruk an ni.
3. Tin, Uzia fapa chu Izrahia a ni a; tin, Izrahia fapate chu Mikaela te, Obadia te, Joela te, Isia te an ni a, panga an ni, an vaiin Puipa an ni.
4. Tin, an chhungkaw chhiarnaa chhiar telte sipai 36,000 an awm bawk; nupui leh fapa tam tak nei an ni a.
5. Tin, Isakar hnam zawng zawng chhiarnaa chhiar tel an unaute chu mi chak huaisen 87,000 an ni.
6. Benjamina fapate chu Bela te, Bekera te, Jediaela te an ni a, pathum an ni.
7. Tin, Bela fapate chu Ezbona te, Uzia te, Uziela te, Jerimotha te, Iria te an ni a, panga an ni; chhungkaw puipa, mi chak huaisen an ni; thlahte chhiarnaa chhiarte chu 22,034 an ni.
8. Tin, Bekera fapate chu Zemira te, Joasa te, Eliezera te, Elioenaia te, Omria te, Jeremotha te, Abija te, Anathotha te, Alemetha te an ni. Chung zawng zawng chu Bekera fapate an ni.
9. Tin, anni chu thlahte chhiarnaa ziak dan chuan chhungkaw puipa, mi chak huaisen 20,200 an ni.
10. Tin, Jediaela fapa chu Bilhana a ni a; tin, Bilhana fapate chu Jeusa te, Benjamina te, Ehuda te, Kenaana te, Zethana te, Tarsisa te, Ahisahara te an ni.
11. Jediaela thlah zawng zawngte chu chhungkaw puipa mi chak huaisen, 17,200 an ni.
12. Ira fapa Supima leh Hupima te, Ahera fapa Husima-te nen.
13. Naphtalia fapate chu Jaziela te, Gunia te, Jezera te, Saluma te an ni a, Bilhai fate an ni.
14. Manasea fapate chu, a nupuiin Asriela a hring a, (a hmeinu Aram miin Gileada pa Makira a hring a;
15. tin, Makira chuan Hupim leh Supim mi nupuiah anei a; tin, a farnu hming chu Maaki a ni:) a dawttu hming chu Zelophehada a ni; tin, Zelophehada chuan fanute a nei a.
16. Tin, Makira nupui Maaki chuan fapa a hring a, a hmingah Peresa a sa a, tin, a unaupa hming chu Seresa a ni a; a fapate chu Ulama leh Rakema an ni.
17. Tin, Ulama fapa chu Bedana a ni. Chungte chu Manasea thlah Makira fapa Gileada fapate chu an ni.
18. Tin, a farnu Hamoleketin Isoda te, Abiezera te, mala te a hring a.
19. Tin, Semida fapate chu Ahiana te, Sekema te, Likhia te, Aniama te an ni.
20. Tin, Ephraima fapa chu Suthela a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Bereda a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Tahatha a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Eleada a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Tahatha a ni a,
21. chu mi fapa chu Zabada a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Suthela a ni a, Ezera leh Eleada, chu mi rama cheng Gath khuaa mite ran rual zuk lak an tum avanga an lo thah takte nen.
22. An pa Ephraima chuan ni rei tak a sun a, tin, a unaute chu amah hnem turin an va kal a.
23. Tin, a nupui chu a pawl a, tin, a rai a, fapa a hring a, an in chhung vanduainain a tlakbuak avangin a hmingah chuan Beria a sa a.
24. Tin, a fanu chu Seeri a ni a, chu mi chuan Beth-horon thlangta zawk leh chhakta zawk leh Uzenseer a siam a.
25. A fapate chu Repha a ni a, Resepha nen, chu mi fapa chu Tela a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Tahana a ni a,
26. chu mi fapa chu Ladana a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Amihuda a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Elisama a ni a;
27. Nuna a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Josua a ni.
28. Tin, an chanpual leh an chennate chu Bethel leh a khawperte, chhak lamah Naaran, tin, thlanglamah Gezer a khawperte nen; Sekem pawh a khawperte nen, Azar a khawperte thlengin;
29. Tin, Manasea thlahte ramri lamah chuan Betsian leh a khaw per te, taanak leh a khaw perte, megido leh a khawperte, dor leh a khawperte nen ani. Chungah chuan Israel a fa Josefa thlahte chu an awm ani.
30. Asera fate chu Imna te, Isfa te, Isvia te, Beria te, an farnu Seraiite an ni.
31. Tin, Beria fapate chu Hebera leh Berozaitha pa Malkiana an ni.
32. Tin, Hebera chuan Jafleta te, Somera te, Hothama te, an farnu Suai te an hring a.
33. Tin, Joleta fapate chu Pasaka te, Bimhala te, Asvatha te an ni.
34. Tin, Semera fapate chu Ahaia te, Roga te, Jehuba te, Arama te an ni.
35. Tin, a unaupa Helema fapa te chu Jophate, Imnate, Selesa te, Amala te an ni.7
36. Tin, Jopha fapate chu Sua te, Harnephera te, Suala te, Beria te, Imra te,
37. Gezera te, Hoda te, Sama te, Silsa te, Itharana te, Beera te an ni.
38. Tin, Jethera fapate chu Jephunia te, Pista te, Ara te an ni.
39. Tin, Ula fapate chu Ara te, Haniela te, Rizia te an ni.Chung zawng zawngte chu Asera thlah, chhungkhaw puipa, mi chak huaisen, hotuber te chu an ni. Tichuan thlahte chhiarnaah ralkaia chhiarte chu 26,000 an ni.
40. Chung zawng zawngte chu Asera thlah, chhungkhaw puipa, mi chak huaisen, hotuber te chu an ni. Tichuan thlahte chhiarnaah ralkaia chhiarte chu 26,000 an ni.
