1. Tin, chu mi ni chuan kohhran, Jerusalem khuaa awmhote chu nasa takin an tiduhdah tan ta a; tichuan tirhkohte chauh lo chu Judai leh Samari ram khaw tinah an darh ta vek a.
2. Tin, Pathian ngaihsak tuten emaw Stephana chu an phum a, nasa takin an tah a.
3. Saula erawh chuan kohhranhote a tiduhdah em em a, in tinah a lut a, a hmei a pain a hnuk chhuak a, tan inah a khung thin.
4. Tin, a darhte chu khaw tinah an kal a, thu chu an hril zel a.
5. Tin, Philipa chu Samari khuaah a zuk kal a, an hnenah Krista thu a tlangaupui a.
6. Tin, ramhuai bawlhhlawhte chu mi tam tak an manate kawchhung ata aw ring taka auva an chhuah avang leh zeng leh kebai tam takte pawh an lo dam tak avangin,
7. mipuiten a thu an hriata a thilmak tihte an hmuhin, Philipa thusawi chu rilru hmunkhatin an ngaihsak a,
8. Tin, chu mi khuaah chuan nasa takin an lawm ta a.
9. Tin, a hming Simona, mi pakhat a awm a; ani chuan, "Mi lian ka ni," tiin hmanah chu mi khuaah chuan dawi hmangin Samari khuaa mite mak ti takin a siam thin a;
10. mi zawng zawng chuan mi chhe ber atangin lal ber thlengin, "He mi hi Pathian thiltihtheihna, Ropui an tih kha, a nih hi," tiin a thu an ngaihsak hlawm thin a.
11. A dawi hmangin rei tak mak ti taka a siam thin avangin a thu an ngaihsak thin a ni.
12. Nimahsela anni chuan Pathian ram chanchin tha leh Isua Krista hming chanchin tha Philipa hrilh chu an awih veleh ahmei apain baptisma an chang ta a.
13. Tin, Simona meuh pawhin a awih ve ta a; tin, baptisma a chan zawhin Philipa hnenah a awm reng a, chhinchhiahna leh thilmak nasa tak tihte a hmu a, mak a ti hle mai thin a.
14. Tin, tirhkoh Jerusalem khuaa awmte khan Samari khuaa mitten Pathian thu an awih tih an hriatin, an hnenah Petera leh Johana chu an tir a;
15. chu mite chu an zuk thlen chuan Thlarau Thianghlim an hmuh theihna'n an tawngtaisak a.
16. Chu mi hma chuan an zingah tuma chungah a la thleng lo; Lal Isua hmingin baptisma an chang chauh a la ni.
17. Tin, tirhkohte chuan an chungah an kut an nghat a, Thlarau Thianghlim an hmu ta a.
18. Tin, tirhkohte kut nghah avanga Thlarau Thianghlim pek a ni tih Simona'n a hmuhin an hnenah tangka pek a tum ta a.
19. "Hetiang thuneihna hi mi pe ve rawh u, tu chungah pawh kut ka nghahna apiangin Thlarau Thianghlim an hmuh vena'n," a ti a.
20. Nimahsela, Petera'n a hnenah, "I tangka hi i boral rualin boral ve rawh se, Pathian thilpek tangka suma lei i tum avangin.
21. Heng thu-ah hian i tel ve lo e, chan pawh i nei hek lo; i rilru Pathian ngaihin a fel lo e.
22. Chuvangin, i sual hi sim la, Lalpa hnenah tawngtai rawh, chutichuan i rilrua i ngaihtuah hi a ngaidam mahna che.
23. Awhna sual nasa tak phuarin i awm tih ka hria e," a ti a.
24. Tin, Simonan, "In thil sawi hi engmah ka chunga a thlen lohna'n nangnin Lalpa hnenah mi tawngtaisak rawh u," a ti a, a chhang a.
25. Chutichuan anni chuan Lalpa thu chu an hriattira an sawi zawhin, Jerusalem khuaah an haw leh a; Samari rama mite khaw tam tak hnenah Chanchin Tha chu an hril zel a.
26. Tin, Lalpa vantirhkoh pakhatin Philipa chu a bia a, "Tho la, chhim lama Jerusalem khua atanga Gaza khaw zuk chhun kawngah khan kal rawh; chu mi kawng chu thlalerah a kal tlang a ni," a ti a.
27. Tichuan, a tho a, a kal ta a. Tin, ngai teh, Ethiopia rama mi pakhat, Ethiopia lalnu Kandaki hnuaia mi tilreh lal tak, a ro zawng zawng enkawltu, Jerusalem khuaah Pathian be turin a han kal a;
28. a haw lam chuan a tawlailira thu chungin zawlnei Isaia lehkhabu a chhiar zel a.
29. Tin, Thlarau chuan Philipa hnenah, "Saw tawlailir saw va hnaih la, va pawl rawh," a ti a.
30. Tin, Philipa chu mi tilreh hnenah chuan a va tlan a, zawlnei Isaia lehkhabu a chhiar a hria a, "I chhiar kha a awmzia i hria em?" a ti a.
31. Tin, ani chuan, "Tuma mi hrilhfiah lohin engtin nge ka hriat thei ang?" a ti a. Tin, a hnena thu chhuak turin Philipa chu a sawm ta a.
32. Tin, Pathian Lehkha a chhiar lai chu hei hi a ni: "Talh turin beram angin ani chu an hruai a; beram no a hmul mettu hmaa a ngawi reng angin Ani pawhin a ka a ang lo;
33. A tlawm laiin a chungah thu rel an phal lo; A thlahte chanchin tuin nge la hril ang? Khawvel atangin a nun an laksak si a," tih hi.
34. Tin, mi tilreh chuan Philipa a bia a, a hnenah, "Zawlneiin he'ng thu hi tu thu nge a sawi, ama thu nge mi dang thu? ka zawt a che," a ti a.
35. Tin, Philipa chuan a sawi a, chu Pathian Lehkha thu chu a bulah a tan a, a hnenah Isua thu a hrilh a.
36. Tin, kalkawnga an kal laiin tui eng emaw an thleng a; tin, mi tilreh chuan, "En teh, heta hi tui a awm, baptisma channa tur engin nge mi dal kher?" a ti a.
37. Tin, Philipa chuan, "I thinlung zawng zawnga i rin chuan, i chang thei e," a ti a. Tin, ani chuan, "Isua Krista chu Pathian Fapa a nih ka ring," tiin a chang a.
38. Tin, tawlailir chu a dintir a, an pahnihin, Philipa leh mi tilreh chu tuiah chuan an zuk kal dun a; tin, Philipa chuan ani chu a baptis ta a.
39. Tin, tui ata an chhuah chhoh veleh Lalpa Thlarau chuan Philipa chu a la bo ta a; tin, mi tilreh chuan ani chu a hmu leh ta lo, hlim taka a kal tak zel avangin.Nimahsela Philipa chu miin Azota khuaah an hmu leh ta a; tin, a kal tlang a, Kaisari khaw thlengin khaw tinah Chanchin Tha chu a hril zel a.
40. Nimahsela Philipa chu miin Azota khuaah an hmu leh ta a; tin, a kal tlang a, Kaisari khaw thlengin khaw tinah Chanchin Tha chu a hril zel a.
