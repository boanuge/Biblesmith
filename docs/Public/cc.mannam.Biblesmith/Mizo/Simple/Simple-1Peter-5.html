1. Chutichuan, in zinga kohhran upate chu, kei, in upatpui, Krista tuarnate hretu, ropuina lo lang tur chang vetu tur hian ka fuih a che u;
2. In zinga Pathian pawlho awm chu enkawl rawh u; tih tur a nih vang ni loin, Pathian thu anga chak chung zawkin, hlepna bawlhhlawh duh vang ni hek loin, rilrua duh tak zawkin,
3. in chante chungah intilal hek loin, pawlho entawnan insiam zawkin ho rawh u.
4. Tin, Beram Vengtu Lalber chu a lo lan hunin, ropuina lallukhum vuai thei lo chu in hmu ang.
5. Chutiang bawkin nangni tleirawl deuhte u, upa hnenahte intulut rawh u. A ni, in zain rawng inbawl tawn theuh turin thuhnuairawlhnain inthuam rawh u; Pathianin chapote a dodal a, inngaitlawmte erawh chu khawngaihna a pe si thin.
6. Chuvangin, a hun teah chuan a chawimawi theihna tur che uin Pathian kut chak tak hnuaiah chuan intihnuai u la,
7. anin a ngaihsak thin che u avangin in manganna zawng zawng ama chungah nghat vek rawh u.
8. Ngaihven u la, fimkhur rawh u; in khingpui Diabola chu sakeibaknei rum thin angin a ei theih tur zawngin a phi ruai thin;
9. in unau khawvela awmhote chungah chuan chutiang tuarna chu a thleng ve a ni tih hriain, ring nghet taka awmin amah chu do rawh u.
10. Tin, khawngaihna zawng zawng Pathian, a chatuan ropuina chang tura Kristaa kotu ngei che u chuan rei lo te in tuar hnuan a siam tha famkimin, a tinghetin, a tichak ang che u.
11. Ama hnenah chuan rorelna chu chatuanin awm rawh se. Amen.
12. Ka unaupa rinawm taka ka ruat Silvana kutah hian nangmahni fuih leh, chu chu Pathian khawngaihna taktak a ni tih hriattirin tawi tein inhnenah ka ziak a ni; chungah chuan ding nghet rawh u.
13. Babulon khuaa awm nangmahni anga thlan nu hian chibai a buk a che u; chutiang bawkin ka fapa Marka pawhin chibai a buk a che u.Hmangaihna fawha fawpin chibai inbuk rawh u. In zinga Kristaa awm apiangte hnenah chuan remna awm rawh se.
14. Hmangaihna fawha fawpin chibai inbuk rawh u. In zinga Kristaa awm apiangte hnenah chuan remna awm rawh se.
