1. Tin, Pathian bawm chu an la lut a, a dahna tura Davida'n a zar, puanin chungah chuan an dah ta a; tin, halral thilhlante leh remna thilhlante Pathian hmaah chan an hlan a.
2. Tin, Davida'n halral thilhlan leh remna thilhlante a hlan zawh chuan, LALPA hmingin mipite chu mal as awm a.
3. Tin, Israel mi zawng zawngte chu ahmei apain chhang hlawm khat leh sa hrang khat leh grep rah pho ro bawr khat a pe theuh a.
4. Tin, lalpabawm hmaa rawngbawl tur leh LALPA, Israelte Pathian chu hre renga amah fak tur leh a hnena lawmthu sawi tur Levi-ho thenkhatte a ruat a;
5. a hotu bera chuan Asapha, tin, a dawttu perhkhuang leh tingtang tum mi Zekaria te, Jeiela te, Semiramotha te, Jehiela te, Matithia te, Eliaba te, Benaia te, Obed-edoma te, Jeiela te nen; Asapha chuan darbenthek ringakin a beng a;
6. tin, puithiam Benaia leh Jahaziela chuan Pathian thuthlunna bawm hmaah tawtawrawtte an ham reng a.
7. Tichuan chu mi ni chuan Davida'n LALPA hnena lawm thu hril turin Asapha leh a unaute chu a hmang tan ta a.
8. Aw, LALPA nenah lawm thu hril u la, A hming lam rawh u; Hnamte zingah chuan a thiltihte chu hriattir rawh u.
9. Amah chawimawiin hla sa u la, amah fakin zai rawh u; A thilmak tih zawng zawng thu chu chui rawh u.
10. A hming thianghlim chu chhuang rawh u; LALPA zawngtute thinlung chu hlim rawh se.
11. LALPA leh a chakna chu zawng u la; A hmel chu zawng fo rawh u.
12. A thil ropui tak tihte hre reng u la; A thiltihte leh a kaa a rorelte chu;
13. Aw, a chhiahhlawh Israela thlahho, Jakoba thlah, a thlanate u.
14. Ani chu LALPA kan Pathianc hu a ni a; A thupekte chu khawvel pum huap a ni.
15. A thuthlun chu kumkhuain hre reng u la, Chhuan sang thlenga a thu lo pek,
16. Abramhama hnena a thuthlun leh, Isaaka hnena a chhechham chu;
17. Chu vek chu Jakoba hnenah thuruatah, Israela hnena kumkhaw thuthlung atan a tinghet a,
18. "Kanaan ram hi ka pe ang che, I ro chanpual tur ram chu," tiin;
19. Mi tlemte chauh in nih a, A ni, tlem te, khuazin ang mai in nih lai kha;
20. Hnam hrang hrang hnenah an kal lawr a, Ram hrang hrang atanginmi hrang hrangte hnenah.
21. Tuma hnehchiah a phal lo a; A ni, anmahni avangin lalte pawh a zilhhau val
22. "Ka hriakthihate chu tawk suh u la, ka zawlneite pawi reng reng khawih suh u," tiin.
23. Leilung luah zawng zawngte u, LALPA chawimawiin hla sa u la, Ni tin, a chhandamna thu chu hriatir rawh u.
24. Hnam tin, zingah a ropuizia hril u la, Mi tinrengte zingah a thilmak tihte chu hril rawh u.
25. LALPA chu a ropui a, nasa taka fak tur a ni si a. Pathian zawng zawng aia tih tur a ni.
26. Hnamte pathian chu milem a ni si a. LALPA erawh chuan vante khi a siam a ni.
27. Chawimawina leh ropuina a hmaah a awm a; Chakna leh mawina a hmun thianghlima a awm.
28. Mi tina chi tinrengte u, LALPA chu bel u la, LALPA chu ropina leh chakna bel rawh u.
29. LALPA chu a hming ropuina bel u la, Thlhlan tur keng u la, a hmaah lo kal rawh u; Thianghlim mawina inbelin LALPA chu chibai buk rawh u.
30. Leilung zawng zawngte u, a hmaah khur rawh u; Khawvel hi a nghin thieh lohna turin tihnghehin a awm a.
31. Vante khi lawm takin awm se la, lei pawh hi hlim takin awm rawh se; Hnam tin, zingah chuan, "LALPA chuan ro a rel," ti rawh se.
32. Tuifinriatleh a chhunga thil awm zawng zawng hi ri hum hum se la; Lo leh a chhunga awm zawng zawng hi lawm takin awm rawh se;
33. Chutih hunah chuan ramhnuaia thing zawng zawng hi LALPA hmaah chuan hlimin an zai ang,Lei ro rel turin a lo kal dawn si a.
34. Aw, LALPA hnenah lawm thu hril rawh u, ani chu a tha si a; A ngilneihna pawh kumkhuain a awm dawn si a,
35. "Aw kan chandamna Pathian, min chhandam la, I hming thianglim fak tur leh, I fakawmna chhuang turin, Hnam tin, zing ata min hruai khawm la, min chhanhim ang che," ti rawh u.
36. Israelte Pathian LALPA chu fakin awm rawh se, Chatuan ata chatuanthleng pawhin. Tin, mipi zawng zawng chuan, "Amen," an ti a. LALPA chu an fak a.
37. Chutichuan chuta LALPA thuthlunna bawm hmaa ni tin, a tul dan ang anga rawngbawl turin Asapha leha unaute a dah ta a;
38. Obed-edoma leh Hosa, an unau sawmruk leh pariatten an pui tur a ni; Jeduthuna fapa Obed-edoma leh Hosa te chu kawngkhar vengtu tur an ni;
39. LALPA'n Israel-hote hu a pek, LALPA dana ziak zawng anwg anga halral thilhlan maicham chunga tuk leh zana Gibeon hmun sanga LALPA biak buk hmaa halral thilhlante hlan reng tur atan erawh chuan puithiam Zadoka leh
40. a unau puithiamhote ruat an ni a;
41. an zinga tel a, a khawngaihna chu kumkhua atan a i avangin, LALPA hnena lawm thu sawi tur Hemana leh Jeduthuna leh mi dang an hming lama thlan chhuahte chu ruat an ni a.
42. Hemana leh Jeduthuna te chu tawtawrawt te, darbenthek te leh thil tum chi dangte ruala tum tur kawltu an ni a. Jeduthuan leh a fate chu kawngkhar vengtu tur an ni.Tichuan mipuiz awng zawng chu mahni in lam theuvah an haw ta a; tin, Davida pawh chu a chhungte malsawm turin a haw ve ta a.
43. Tichuan mipuiz awng zawng chu mahni in lam theuvah an haw ta a; tin, Davida pawh chu a chhungte malsawm turin a haw ve ta a.
