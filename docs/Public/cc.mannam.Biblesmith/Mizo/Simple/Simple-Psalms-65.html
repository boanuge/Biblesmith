1. Aw Pathian, Zion-ah chuan faknain a nghak reng a che: Thutiam chu i lakah hlen a ni ang.
2. Aw tawngtaina ngaithlatu, I hnenah tisa zawng zawng chu an lo kal ang]
3. Khawlohnate chuan mi hneh thin na a: Kan bawhchhiatnate chuan mi hneh thin na a:
4. Kan bawhchhiatnate zawng nangin i tleng fai ang chu. Mi, i biak in hung chhung kawtlaiahte awm thei tura I thlan a, i kiang i pantir chu, a eng a thawl e: I in thatna chu kan tlai ang, I biak in hmun thianghlim thatna chuan,
5. Aw min chhandamtu Pathian, Thil rapthlaktein fel takin min chhang ang: Nang kawlkila mi zawng zawng leh, Tuifinriat chung hla taka awmte rinna chu i ni:
6. Thiltihtheihnaa taia inhrenga, Mahni chaknaa tlangte tinghet thintu chu i ni;
7. Tuifinriat ri hum hum, a fawn ri hum humte leh, Mi tin sa mur mur tireh thintu chu a ni.
8. Kawlkil tawpa awmte pawh chuan i hriattirnate chu an hlau thin a: Zing lehtlai chhuahnate chu hlimin i autir thin.
9. Lei hi i kan a, tuiin i chawm a, Nasa takin i titha thin: Pathian lui chu tuiin a khat a: Lei hi chutianga i buatsaih hnu chuan an tan buh i siamsak thin.
10. Lei hi a leileh rin khawhna thlurte chu tui tam takin i chawm a; A vung thlurte chu i timuka; Ruahtuiin i tinem a; A thil tote chu mal i samw thin a.
11. Kum chu i thatna i khumti ra; I kalna kawngte chu thil thain a far thin.
12. Thlalera ran tlatna hmunahte chuan a far a: Tlangte pawh chu hlimnaa phuar velin an awm a.Ran tlatna hmunte chu ran rualin a dap in a dap a; Ruamte pawh chu buhin a tlim hmur a; Hlimin an au a, hla an sa bawk a.
13. Ran tlatna hmunte chu ran rualin a dap in a dap a; Ruamte pawh chu buhin a tlim hmur a; Hlimin an au a, hla an sa bawk a.
