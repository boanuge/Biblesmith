1. (Jeriko khua chu Israel-te an luh theih lohna'n an khar tlat a: tumah an chhuak lo a, tumah an lut hek lo.)
2. Tin, LALPA'n Josua hnenah, "En teh, Jeriko leh an lal leh mi chak huaisente hi i kutah ka pe tawh e.
3. Tin, ral kap mi zawng zawngte hian khawpui hi velin vawi khat chu in han vel chhuak pawht ang a. chutiang chuan ni ruk in ti tur a ni.
4. Tin, puithiam pasarihin bawm hma lamah berampa ki tawtawrawt pasarih an keng ang a, tin, a ni sarih ni chuan khawpui chu vawi sarih in vel chhuak ang a, tin, puithiamten tawtawrawtte chu an ham ang.
5. Tin, heti hi a ni ang a, berampa ki an ham rik ruai ruai hun a, tawtawrawt ri in hriat chuan, mipui zawng zawng chu ring takin au dur dur rawh se; tichuan khawpui kulh chu a chim rup ang a, mipui chu mahni hma zawn theuhvah chuan an chho ang," a tia .
6. Tin, Nuna fapa Josua chuan puithiamhote chu a ko a, an hnenah, "Thuthlung bawm hi zawn u la, puithiam pasarihin LALPA bawm hma lamah chuan berampa ki tawtawrawt pasarih keng rawh se," a tia .
7. Tin, ani chuan mipuihote hnenah chuan, "Kal zel u la, khawpui chu vel chhuak rawh u, tin, inrlathuamho kha LALPA bawm hmalamah chuankal zel rawh se," a ti a.
8. Tin, chutiang chu a lo ni ta a, Josua'n mipuihote hnena thu a sawi zawh chuan LALPA hmaa berampa ki tawtawrawt pasarih keng puithiam pasarihte chu an kal zel a, tawtawrawt chu an ham a: tin, LALPA thuthlung bawm chuan a zui a.
9. Tin, inralthuamho chu tawtawrawt ham ham puithiamte hmaah chuan an kal a, tin, hnudaltute chuan bawm chu an chhawm a, puithiamho chuan kal pahin tawtawrawt an ham zel a.
10. Tin, Josua'n mipuite chu, "Au suh u la, in aw pawh hriat ni suh se la, au tura thu ka pek ni che u a thlen hma loh chuan in ka atangin thu reng reng chhuak hek suh se: chumi hunah chuan au vang che u," tiin thu a pe a.
11. Chutichuan LALPA bawm chu khawpui vawikhat velin a han vel chhuahtir a; tin, riahhmunah chuan an kal a, riahhmunah chuan an riak leh ta a.
12. Tin, Josua chu zing takah a tho a, puithiamte chuan LALPA bawm chu an zawn a.
13. Tin, LALPA bawm hmaa berampa ki tawtawrawt pasarih keng puithiam pasarihte kha an kal zel a, tawtawrawt an ham a:
14. Tin, a ni hnih ni chuan khawpui chu vawi khat an han vel chhuak elh a, riahhmunah chuan an kir leh a: chutiang chuan ni ruk an ti a.
15. Tin, heti hi a ni a, a ni sarih ni chuan zing tak vartianah an tho a, an tih dan pangngaiin vawi sarih an vel chhuak a: chu mi ni chauh chuan khawpui chu vawi sarih an vel chhuak a.
16. Tin, heti hi a ni a, a vawi sarihnaa puithiamhoten tawtawrawt an han ham chuan Josua'n mipuiho hnenah, "Au rawh u khai: LALPA'n khawpui hi a pe tawh che u a sin.
17. Khawpui hi a chhunga thil awm zawm zawng chawp hian LALPA tana hluihlawn vek tur a ni: Rahabi nawhchizuar chauh, amah leh in chhunga a hnena awm zawng zawngte erawh chu kan mi tirhte kha a lo thuhruk avangin zuah tur a ni.
18. nangni chu chu thil hluihlawn lakah chuan inthiarfihlim hlauh rawh u, chutilochuan in hluihlawn hnu chuan thil hluihlawn chu in la dah ang e, Israel-ho ruahhmun hi thil hluihlawnah in siam mai ang a, manganna in thlen dah ang e.
19. Tangkarua te, rangkachak te, dar leh thir bungbelte erawh chu LALPA pual a ni vek a: LALPA sum ina dah tur a ni ang," a ti a.
20. Chutichuan mipuite chu an au a, puithiamten tawtawrawt an ham a: tin, heti hi a ni a, mipuiten tawtawrawt an ham a: tin, heti hi a ni a, mipuiten tawtawrawt ri an han hrat veleh chuan mipuiho chu an au ta dur dur a, kulh chu a chim ta rup a, tichuan mipuite chu mahni hma zawn theuhvah chuan an chho a, khawpuiah chuan an lut a, khawpui chu an la ta a.
21. Tin, khawpui chhunga mi zawng zawng - a hmei apa, a pui apangin - bawngpa te, beram te, sabengtungte nen khandaihin an tihlum fai vek a.
22. Tin, Josua'n mi pahnih ram va enthlatute hnenah khan, "Nawhchizuar inah sawn va lut u la, a hnenaa chhia in lo chham tawh ang khan chu hmeichhia chu a neih zawng zawng nen va hruai chhuak rawh u," a ti a.
23. Tin, enthlatu tlangvalte chu an va lut a, Rahabi nen, a nu nen, a nutate nen, a neih zawng zawngte nen c huan an va hruai chhuak a, a laichun unaute zawng zawng pawh an va hruai chhuak a; Israel ho riahhmun pawn lamah an awmtir ta a.
24. Tin, khawpui chu a chhunga thil awm zawng zawng nen meiin an hal vek a: tangkarua te, rangkachak te, dar leh thir bungbelte chauh chu LALPA sum inah an dah a.
25. Amaherawhchu Rahabi nawhchizuar leh aneih zawng zawng leh a pa chhungte chu Josua'n a zuah vek a; tichuan Israel-hote zingah chuan tun tlengin a la tel ta reng a ni; Josua'n Jeriko khua enthla tura a tirhte khaa lo thuhruk avangin.
26. Tin, chu mi lai chuan Josua'n, "He Jeriko khawpui hi rawn din apiang chu LALPA hmaah anchhe dawngin awm rawh se: a fa tir chanin a lungphum chu a phum ang a, a fa tlum ber chanin a kulh kawngkate chu a din ang," tiin anni chu a vau a.Chutichuan LALPA chu Josua hnenah a awm a; tin, a ram pum puiah chuan a hming a lo thang ta a.
27. Chutichuan LALPA chu Josua hnenah a awm a; tin, a ram pum puiah chuan a hming a lo thang ta a.
