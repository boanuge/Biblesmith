1. Tho thiin a tichhia, rimtui siamtu hriak rim; chutiangin finna leh zahawmna pawh atna tlemtein a chilh bo.
2. Mi fing thinlung a hawi, a ding lamah, mi a thinlung erawh a vei lama'n.
3. A ni tak e, mi a chu tual a len laiin a hriatnain a tlin lo a, mi tin, hnenah, a a a ni, a ti zel thin.
4. Roreltu thin thawk mah se la, nangma chungah, i hmun kalsan suh ang che; lungnih lohnate a tireh, zawldawhnain.
5. Ni hnuaia ka hmuh thin thil tha lo a awm, roreltua lo chhuak thiltih dik lo ang:
6. Mi a hmun sangahte dah a, mi hausa hmun hniama an thute hi.
7. Chhiahhlawh ka hmu, sakawr chungah, hotu a kal kein, chhiahhlawh angin.
8. Hum laitu chu a hum laihah a tla ang; hungna tawn tlangtu a chu ang, rulin.
9. Lung lai chhuaktu a inhliam ang; thing ektu tan hlauhthawn a awm.
10. Thir a bil a, miin a tah hrima loh chuan tha rum a ngai lehzual ang; mahse a sawt ber mai finna, hlawhtlinna'n.
11. A chuk mai chuan rulin, dawi at hmain, dawi at thiam sawtna a awm hlei nem.
12. Mi fing kamchhuak thu zawng a hlu. Mi a hmui erawh chuan mahni a indawlh zo ang.
13. A kamchhuak thu bul chu atna a ni sa a, a kamchhuak tawpna chu atna pawikhawih chi a ni.
14. Thu a tuitam thin, mi a chuan, miin a hre lo, thil lo awm tur chu, tu nge hrilh thei, mi hnu lama thil lo awm tur?
15. Thawhrimnain a intichau thin, mi a chu, khawpui kalkawng pawh a hre lo.
16. I chung a pik e, nang ram, lal naupang leh hotute zinga ruaitheh thin neitu!
17. I nihlawh e, nang ram, lal chu lal chi fapa leh hotute ruihna'n ni lo, chakna tura hun dik taka ei thin neitu!
18. Zawmthawt avangin a sawp, in chung. Kut dawngdah vangin a far,
19. Ruai siam a ni, hlimhlawpa tan, uain chu nun tihnawmna'n, tangkain engkim a si thlu.Lalber chu lawh suh la, anchhia, i rilru pawhin; anchhia lawh suh, mi hausa chu, i khum laizawl hmunah; chungleng savate'n i awm chu an pu darh ang a, thla neite'n chung chu an hril mai ang.
20. Lalber chu lawh suh la, anchhia, i rilru pawhin; anchhia lawh suh, mi hausa chu, i khum laizawl hmunah; chungleng savate'n i awm chu an pu darh ang a, thla neite'n chung chu an hril mai ang.
