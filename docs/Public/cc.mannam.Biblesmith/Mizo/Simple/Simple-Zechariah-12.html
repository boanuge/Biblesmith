1. Israel chung thua LALPA thu phurrit chu. LALPA van tipharhtu, leilung nghahchhan phumtu, Mihring chhunga thlarau siamtu thu chu heti hi a ni:
2. Ngai rawh u, Jerusalem chu a vela mipui zawng zawngte tan Ruihna novah ka siam ang a, Jerusalem hualnaah chuan juda tan pawh a ni ve ang.
3. Chumi niah chuan heti hi a lo ni ang a, Jerusalem chu mipui zawng zawng tan Lung rit takah ka siam ang: Chumi inphurhtirtu zawng zawngte chu na takin an hliam ang: Tin, leia hnam zawng zawngte chu amah bel turin an pung khawm ang.
4. Chumi niah chuan sakawr tin mai chu ka thawng ang a, a chunga chuangte chu atnain ka hrem ang, tih chu LALPA thu chhuak a ni: Tin, Juda chhungte chungah chuan ka mit ka meng ang a, mipuite sakawrte chu mitdelnain ka hrem ang.
5. Tin, Juda hotute chuan, Jerusalem luahtute chu Sipaihote LALPA an pathian ah chuan Ka chakna an ni, an ti rilru ang.
6. Chumi niah chuan Juda hotute chu Thing zinga meivam angin ka siam ang a, buhphal zinga meichher angin ka siam ang: Tin, a vela mipuite chu a kang fai ang-dinglam leh vei lamah; Tin, Jerusalem chu ama hmun ngei, Jerusalem-ah chuan a ding leh ang.
7. Davida chhungte ropuina leh Jerusalem luahtute ropuina chu Juda khuma tihlena a awm lohna'n LALPA chuan Juda puaninte chu a chhanchhuak hmasa ber bawk ang.
8. Chumi niah chuan LALPA chuan Jerusalem luahtute chu a hum ang a; Chumi niah chuan an zinga mi chhiathate pawh chu Davida ang chu an lo ni ang; Tin, Davida chhungte chu Pathian ang an lo ni ang a, An hmaa LALPA vantirhkoh ang chu an lo ni ang.
9. Tin, chumi niah chuan heti hi a lo ni ang a, Jerusalem beitu hnamte chu tihboralna ka zawng ang.
10. Tin, Davida chhungte chungah leh Jerusalem luahtute chungah chuan khawngaihna leh dilna thlarau ka leih ang a, Tin, an chhuna chu min rawn en ang; Tin, miin a fapa neih chhun a sun angin a sun ang a, Miin a fatir chunga a tuar ang in an tuar ang.
11. Chumi niah chuan Jerusalem-ah sunna nasa tak a awm ang a, Megido ruama Hadadrimmon hmuna sunna ang mai kha.
12. Tin, ram chuan a sun ang; chhung kua kuain a hrangin; Davida thlah chhungkua pawh a hrangin, an nupuite ahrangin,
13. Levia thlah chhungkua ahrangin, an nupuite ahrangin; Simeia chhungte a hrangin, an nupuite ahrangin;Chhungkaw dang zawng zawng pawh, Chhungkaw tinreng ahrangin, an nupuite a hrang theuhin.
14. Chhungkaw dang zawng zawng pawh, Chhungkaw tinreng ahrangin, an nupuite a hrang theuhin.
