1. Simon Petera, Isua Krista bawih leh tirhkoh chuan Isua Krista kan Pathian leh chhandamtu felna avanga rinna hlu tak kan chan ang chang vete hnenah:
2. Pathian leh kan Lalpa Isua hriatnaah chuan in chungah khawngaihna leh remna tihpunin awm rawh se.
3. Ama ropuina leh thatna zara min kotu, a chanchin hriat avangin a Pathian thiltihtheihnain nunna leh Pathian ngaihsakna lam kawngah chuan thil zawng zawng min pe si a.
4. Chung zarah chuan a thutiam hlu tak leh ropui nasa takte chu min pe a ni; chutichuan chakna avanga khawvela chhiatna lo awm hi tlan chhuahsan tawhin, chung thutiam hlu tak leh ropui nasa takte avang chuan Pathian nungchang in tawmpui ve thei ang.
5. A ni, chu mi avang tak chuan taihmak reng reng chhuahin, in rinnaah chuan thatna telh u la, in thatnaah chuan hriatna telh u la,
6. in hriatnaah chuan insumna telh u la, in insumnaah chuan chhelna telh u la, in chhelnaah chuan Pathian ngaihsakna telh u la,
7. In Pathian ngaihsaknaah chuan unaute hmangaihna telh u la, in unaute hmangaihnaah chuan hmangaihna telh rawh u.
8. Chung chu in ta a nih a, a pun zel si chuan kan Lalpa Isua Krista hriatna kawngah hian thatchhe lo leh rah lo loin a siam thin che u a ni.
9. Tupawh chung thil nei lo chu mitdel a ni a, hnai tea mi chauh a hmu thei a, a hman lai sualte tlenfaisakna chu a theihnghilh ta si a.
10. Chuvangin, unaute u, a kohna leh a thlanna che u chu tinghet turin taihmak chhuah lehzual rawh u; chung chu in tih zawngin engtikah mah in tlu lo ang;
11. chutichuan Isua Krista kan Lalpa leh Chhandamtu chatuan rama luh theihna chu an pe ngei dawn si che u a.
12. Chuvangin, chung thil chu hriain thutak in hnena awmah chuan in nghet na a, chung thil chu hriattir che u ka inpeih fo ang.
13. Tin, he bawktea ka awm chhung zawng hian nangmahni hriatpuiin chawh harh fo che u tha ka ti a ni;
14. kan Lalpa Isua Kristan mi hriattir ang khan ka bawkte dah bo hun chu a thleng thuai ang tih ka hre si a.
15. A ni, ka kal chhuah hnuin chung thil chu in hriat reng fo theihna'n ka bei ang.
16. Kan Lalpa Isua Krista thiltihtheihzia leh a lo kal lehna thu chu ka hriattir lai che u khan, thawnthu fing taka ngaihtuah chhuah maite zawm kan ni lo a, a ropuina hmutute kan ni zawk a sin.
17. Ani chuan Pa Pathian hnen ata chawimawina leh ropuina a hmu a, chu mi lai chuan ropuina nasa tak atang chuan hetiang aw hi a hnenah a lo thleng a: "Hei hi ka Fapa duh tak, ka lawm em ema chu a ni," tiin.
18. Tin, tlang thianghlimah chuan a hnena kan awm lai khan chu aw chu van atangin a lo chhuak keimahni ngeiin kan hria a ni.
19. Tin, hril lawkna thu nghet lehzual chu kan nei a ni, khua a lo var a, varparh arsi chu in thinlunga a lo chhuah hma loh zawng chu chu khawnvar hmun thima eng an ngaihsak anga in ngaihsak chuan in titha ang.
20. Pathian Lehkhaa thu hril lawk reng reng hi mahniin hrilhfiah tur a ni lo tih hre hmasa phawt u la.Thu hrilh lawk reng reng hi mihring thua chhuak a ni ngai si lo a, miten Thlarau Thianghlim tirhin Pathian hnen ata an sawi zawk thin a ni.
21. Thu hrilh lawk reng reng hi mihring thua chhuak a ni ngai si lo a, miten Thlarau Thianghlim tirhin Pathian hnen ata an sawi zawk thin a ni.
