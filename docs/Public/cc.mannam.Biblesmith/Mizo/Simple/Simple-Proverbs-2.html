1. Ka fapa, ka thute i dawn a, ka thupekte i khawl khawm a,
2. Finna lama i beng i chhit a, i thinlung hriatna lam atan i pek chuan;
3. A ni, hriatna i auh a, hriatthiam theihna pawh i auh chuan,
4. Tangkarua anga i zawn a, ro phum ruk anga i zawn chuan,
5. LALPA tih nachang i lo hria ang a, Pathian hriatna chu i chhar chhuak ang.
6. LALPA chuan finna a pe thin a, a kaa tang chuan finna leh hriatthiamna a lo chhuak thin a ni:
7. Mi tluangte tan finna dik a chhek khawm thin a, rinawm taka awm thinte tan phaw a ni;
8. Rorelna kawngte a ven a, a mi thianghlimte kawng a vawn that theihna'n.
9. Tichuan felna leh rorelna chu i lo hre thiam ang a, hleih neih lohna nen, a ni, kawng tha tinreng pawh.
10. I thinlungah finna chu a lut ang a, i nunna atan hriatna chu tui tak a n ang;
11. Remhriatnain a veng ang chia, hriatthiamnain a enkawl ang che;
12. Sual kawng ata chhan chuak tur che leh thil awihawm lo sawi mite laka chhanchhuak tur chein;
13. Chu'ngte chuan kawng thima kal turin kawng dik chu an kalsan a;
14. Thil tha lo tih chu an lawmzawng a ni a, thil sual bengtla lo mai maiahte an lawm thin;
15. An kawngte chu a kawi a, an rintlak loh.
16. Hmeichhe sual lakah chuan a chhan ang che, kam thluma thlemtu che lakah;
17. A tleirawl lai thian kalsan a, a Pathian thuthlung theihnghilhtu lak ata chu.
18. A in chuan thihna lam a hawi a, a kawngte chuan mitthi lam.
19. A hnena kal reng reng chu an kir leh tawh ngia lo a, nunna kawng an pawh ngai hek lo;
20. Mi tha kawng i zawh a, mi fel kawng chu i zawh zel theihna'n.
21. Mi tluangte chu leilungah hian an cheng ang a, tha famkimte chu chutah chuan an awm reng a;Mi suaksualte erawh chu leilung ata tihboral an ni ang a, vervek taka titute chu tihboral phiar an ni ang.
22. Mi suaksualte erawh chu leilung ata tihboral an ni ang a, vervek taka titute chu tihboral phiar an ni ang.
