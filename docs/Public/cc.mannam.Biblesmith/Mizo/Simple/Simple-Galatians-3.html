1. Aw Galatia rama mi a te u, tuin nge dawi che u? in mithmuh ngeiah Isua Krista chu khenbehin chiang taka lantir a nih tehlul nen.
2. Hei chauh hi in hnen ata lo hriat ka duh. Dan thiltihte avangin nge Thlarau chu inhmuh, in ngaihthlaka in rin avangin?
3. Heti emin in a maw? Thlarauva tan tawh siin tisain in zo dawn em ni?
4. Thil chuti zozai chu sawtpui loin in tuar em ni? Sawtpui lo ni tak tak ta ang se la.
5. Tin, in hnena Thlarau petu che u leh in zinga thilmak titu chuan Dan thiltihte avangin nge a tih, in ngaihthlaka in rin avangin?
6. Abrahama'n Pathian a ring a, tin,chu rinna chu a felnaa ruat a ni ang khan.
7. Chutichuan, hre rawh u, rinnaa awmte, chung nge chu Abrahama fate an ni.
8. Tin, Pathian Lehkha Thu chuan, Pathianin rinna avangin Jentailte thiam a chantir dawn tih hre lawkin, "Nangmahah chuan hnam tin malsawmsakin an awm ang," tiin Abrahama hnenah Chanchin Tha chu a hrilh lawk kha.
9. Chutichuan, rinnaa awmte chu Abrahama rinawma nen chuan malsawmsakin an awm ve thin a ni.
10. Dan thiltiha awm apiangte chu anchhe hnuaiah an awm si a; "Dan lehkhabua thu ziak zawng zawng anga ti tura awm reng lo apiang chu anchhe dawngin an awm thin," tiin ziak a ni si a.
11. Tin, Pathian mithmuhin tumah reng Dan avanga thiam chang an awm lo tih a lang reng a ni. "Mi felte chu rinnain an nung ang," tih a ni si a;
12. tin, Dan chu rinnaa mi a ni lo, "Chung anga titu chu chungah chuan a nung ang," tih a ni zawk e.
13. Krista chu kan aia anchhe dawng lo niin, Dan anchhe lak ata min tlan chhuak ta; ( "Thinga khaikan apiang chu anchhe dawng an ni," tih ziak a ni si a);
14. chutichuan Abrahama chunga malsawmna chu Krista Isuaah chuan Jentailte chungah a lo awm thei ang a, tichuan rinna avangin Thlarau tiam chu kan hmu thei ang.
15. Unaute u, mihring angin ka sawi hi, mihring thuthlung pawh ni mah se la, tihngheh tawh hnuin an phelh ngai lo a,belh pawh an belhchhah ngai hek lo.
16. Tin, Abrahama hnenah a ni, thu chu tiamin a awm ni, a chi thlah hnenah pawh. Mi tam tak chung thu sawi angin, "Chi thlahte hnenah pawh," a ti lo a;mi pakhat chung thu sawi angin, "I chi thlah hnenah pawh," a ti a ni zawk e, chu chu Krista chu a ni.
17. Tin, heti hian ka ti a ni: Thuthlung, Pathian lo tihngheh tawh chu, Dan kum zali leh kum sawmthum hnua lo awm tawh khan, thutiam chu engmah loa siamin a tibo lo e.
18. Roluah tur chu Dan avang a nih chuan thutiam avang a ni tawh si lo ang; nimahsela Pathianin thutiam avangin a ni, Abrahama hnenah a pek ni.
19. A nih loh leh, engtiziaa Dan chu awm nge ni le? Bawhchhiatna hi sual a nih tilang fiah tura thutiam belhchhahna a ni a, Thutiamin Chi Thlah a tih lo kal hmaa hman tur, vantirhkohte hmanga palai kuta pek a ni.
20. Tin, palai reng reng mi pakhat lakah palai a ni ngai lo e, Pathian erawh chu pakhat a ni.
21. A nih loh leh, Dan chuan Pathian thutiamte chu a dodal em ni le? Hnai lo e, Dan mi tinung thei reng pekin a awm tawh si chuan, felna chu Dan avangin a awm ngei tur a ni.
22. Nimahsela, Isua Krista rin avanga thiltiam chu a ringtute hnena pek a nih theihna'n Pathian Lehkha thu chuan sual hnuaiah engkim a kharkhip ta vek a.
23. Nimahsela, rinna a lo thlen hma khan, rinna la lang tur atan kharkhipa, Dan hnuaia ven kan nih kha.
24. Chutichuan, rinna avanga thiam kan chan theihna turin Dan chu Krista hnen lama min kaihruaitu a lo ni a.
25. Nimahsela, tunah rinna a lo thlen hnu hian, kaihruaitu hnuaiah kan awm tawh lo e.
26. Krista Isua rin avangin Pathian fate in ni vek si a;
27. in zinga Kristaa baptisma chang apiangte kha Krista chuan in inthuam tawh si a.
28. Chutah chuan Juda mi a awm theih loh a, Grik mi pawh a awm theih hek loh, chutah chuan bawih a awm theih lo a, bawih lo pawh a awm theih hek loh, chutah chuan mipa leh hmeichhia pawh a awm theih loh; Krista Isuaah chuan in zain pumkhat in ni si a.Tin, Krista ta in nih chuanin Abrahama thlah, thutiam anga roluahtu turte in ni.
29. Tin, Krista ta in nih chuanin Abrahama thlah, thutiam anga roluahtu turte in ni.
