1. Tin, lal Hezekia chuan chu chu a hriatin heti hi a ni a, a silhfente chu a pawt thler a, saiip puana inbel a, LALPA ina chuan a lut ta a.
2. Tin, in chhung lam hotu Eliakima leh, lehkhaziaktu Sebna leh, puithiam upate chu saiip puan inbelin Amoza fapa zawlnei Isaia hnena a ti ra.
3. Tin, anni chuan a hnenah, "Hezekian heti ian a ti, 'Vawiin ni hi manganna leh zilhhauna leh hmingchhiatna ni a ni: naute chu an pian a hun tawh si a, hrin gturin chakna a awm taw si lo.
4. A pu Assuria lalin Pathian nung sawichhe tura a tirh Rabsake thu sawi zawng zawngte chu, LALPA i Pathian chuan a hria ang a, a thu sawi, LALPA i Pathianin a thu hriata chu a la zilhhau mahna: chu mi avang chuan a la awm chunte chu tawngtaisak rawh,' a ti e," an ti a.
5. Chutichuan lal Hezekia mite ch Isaia hnenah an kal a.
6. Tin, Isaia chuanan hnenah, "In pu hnenah heti hian sawi ang che u, 'LALPA chuan heti hian a ti, I thu hriat, Assuria lal miten mi sawi chiatnate kha hlau suh.
7. Ngai teh, a kawchhunga chuan thlarau ka awmtir ang a, thuthang a hria ang a, ama ramah a haw leh ang; tichuan ama ram ngeiah chuan khandaiha tihtlukin ka awmtir ang, a tie,' tiin," a ti a.
8. Chutichuan Rabsake chu a kir leh a, Assuria lal chuan Libna khua a lo bei lai a va hmu a; Lakis khua ata chu a chuak tih a hre tawh reng a ni.
9. Tin, "Ngai teh, nangmah bei turin a chhuak ta," tia Tirhaka, Ethiopia ram lal chungchang thu mi sawi a lo hriat chuan Hezekia hnenah mite a tir a,
10. "Heti hian Juda lal Jezekia hnena sawi ang che u, ' I pathian i rina chuan Jerusalem khua Assuria lal kuta pek a ni lo ang, tiin bum che suh se.
11. Ngai teh, Assuria lalten ram zawng zawng tiche vekaa chunga an thiltihte kha i hria a; nang hi chhanhim i ni dawn bik em ni?
12. Hnam tin, pathiante chuan ka pi leh puten an tihboralte kha an chanhim em ni? Gozan te, Haran te, Rezeph te, Eden fa Telasar khuaa awm te kha.
13. Hamath lal te, Arpad lal te, Sepharvaim khawpi lal te, Hena lal te, Iva lal te, kha khawiah nge an awm tak ?' tiin," a ti a.
14. Tin, Hezekia chuan a mi tirhte kut ata lehkha hu a hmu a, a han chhiar a: tichuan Hezekia chu LALPA inah chuan a han lut a, LALPA hmaah chuan a pharha a.
15. Tin, Hezekia chuan LALPA hmaah a tawngtai a, "Aw LALPA, cherubte chunga thu, Israela Pathian, nang, nang chauh hi leilung ram zawng zawng Pathian chu i ni; lei leh van hi nangma siam a ni.
16. Aw LALPA, i beng lo chhi la, ngaithla teh; aw LALPA, i mit meng la, en teh; Pathian nung sawichhe tura Sanakeriban thu a rawn sawi hi ngaithla teh.
17. LALPA, Assuria lalten hnamte leh an ramte chu an tichhe thin tak a,
18. an pathiante chu meiah an paih bawk thin a: chungte chu pathian an ni lo reng a, mihring kuta siam, thing leh lung an ni zawk a ni: chuvangin an tiboral a ni.
19. chuvangin aw LALPA kan pathian, nang, nangmah chauh hi LALPA Pathian i ni tih leilung luah ram zawng zawng hian an hriat teihna'n a lakah hian min chhandam ngei ngeia ng che," tiin.
20. Chu mi lai chuan Amoza fapa Isaia chuan Jezekia hnenah, "LALPA Israela Pathianc haun heti hian a ti a ni, 'Assuria lal Senakeriba chhiatna tur lam ka hnena i tawngtai kha ka lo hria e,' tin,.
21. Hei hi a chungchang thu LALPA'n a sawi chu a ni: 'Zion fanu nula chuan a hmusit chia, a nuihzatbur che; Jerusalem fanu chuan a lu a thin khum che.
22. Tunge i tihhmingchhiat a, i sawi chhiat? Tu chungah nge i aw ti tihpawr a, i mit i lek san? Israelho Mi Thianghlima chungah alawm.
23. I mi tirhte hmangin LALPA chu i tihmingchhia a, ka tawlailir tam tak nen Lebanon ram ril tlang sangte ka lo thleng chho ta a; Chuta Sidar thing sang takte leh far thing tha thate chu ka kit ang a: a lei tha hmun ramngaw a chenna hmun ril berah chuan ka lut ang.
24. Ram dang tui ka lai chhuak a, ak tlan thin a, ka kephah hnuai hian Aigupta lui zawng zawng chu ka tikang vek ang, ti ti a.
25. Keima'n hman lai ata chung chu ka tih a nihzia te, hmasang ata chung chu ka tih a nihzia te, hmasang ata chung chu ka siam anihzia te i hre lo em ni? Khaw kulh neite chu in chim khawm mai anga tichetu atan ka ruat ta che anih hi.
26. Chuvang chuan a chhunga awmte chu chakna n nei lo a, an zam a, an lo mualpho tak kha; phul hlobet ang leh, hnim hring ang leh, inchunga hlobet ang leh, buh len hmaa thli em vuai ang an nih kha.
27. Kei chuan i thut te, i luh leh i chhuah te, ka chunga i thinur te hi ka hria a sin.
28. ka chunga i thinur avang leh , i tawng chaltlai chu ka bengah a lo luh choh tak avang hian ka hnar kvuah chu i hnarah ka vuah ang a, ka thirbarh chu i hmuiah, i lo kalna kawng ngeiah khan ka kirtir leh ang che,' tiin.
29. Tin, hei hi i tan chhinchhiahna nai ang: kuminah bu hli in ring ang a; nakkumah a hli bawk: tin, a kum thumnaah chuan buhte tuh u la, at u la, grep huante siam ula, a rahte ei rawh u.
30. Tichuan Juda chungte zinga la dam chhunte chuan a chhuk zawngin zung an kai ang a, a cho zawngin an rah leh ang.
31. Jerusalem atangin a la awm chunte Zion tlang atangin a la damte chu an chhuak dawn a ni: LALPA thahnemngaihna chuan chung chu a ti ang.
32. Chuvangin LALPA chuan Assuria lal chungchang thu chu heti hian a sawi, ;He khawpui hi a lo thleng lo ang, phaw nen a kiangah a lo kal hek lo ang a, a khaw hrulah lei an tiang pawng hek lo ang.
33. A lo kalna kawngah, ch miah ngei chuan a let leh ang a, he khawpui hia lo thleng lo ang.
34. Keimah avang leh ka chhiahhlawh Davida avangin he khawpui hi chanhim turin ka veng dawn ni,' LALPA'n a ti," tiin, mi a tir a.
35. Tichuan chu mi zan chuan heti hi a ni a, LALPA vantirhkoh chu a tahwk chuak a, Assuria-ho ruahhmuna chuan mi nuai khat leh singriat leh sangnga a tihlum a: tin, zing taka an han thawh chuan, ngai teh, mitthi ruang hlir a lo ni ta mai a.
36. Chutichuan Assuria lal Senakeriba chu a chuak a, alet leh a, a kal leh ta zel a, Ninevi khuah chuan a awm ta a.Tin, a pathian Nisroka ina chuan amah a biak laiin heti hia ni a, Adrammeleka leh Sarezera te chuan khandaihin an tihlum a: tichuan Arara rama chuan an lan lut ta a. Tin, a fapa Esar-haddona chu a aiawhina lal ta a.
37. Tin, a pathian Nisroka ina chuan amah a biak laiin heti hia ni a, Adrammeleka leh Sarezera te chuan khandaihin an tihlum a: tichuan Arara rama chuan an lan lut ta a. Tin, a fapa Esar-haddona chu a aiawhina lal ta a.
