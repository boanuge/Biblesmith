1. Tin, Naoami chuan a pasala laichin, Elimeleka thlah, mi neinung tak laichin a nei a; a hming chu Boaza a ni.
2. Tin, Moab mi Ruthi chuan Naomi hnenah, "Lo lamah ka kal ang a, mi lo khawngaihtu apiang buhto ka va zawng ang e aw," a ti a. Tin, a hnenah, "Kal ta che, ka fanu," a ti a.
3. Tichuan, a kal ta a, buh sengtute seng hmaih chu a va zawng ta a: tichuan Elimeleka thlah Boaza loah ngei chuan a va kal fu hlauh mai a.
4. Tichuan, ngai teh, Bethlehem atangin Boaza chu a va kal a, buh sengtute hnenah chuan,"In hnenah LALPA awm rawh se," a ti a. Tin, anni chuan, "LALPA'n malsawm che rawh se," tiin an chhang a.
5. Chutichuan Boaza chuan a chhiahhlawh, buhsengtute chunga hotu hnenah chuan, "Hei khawi lam nula nge ni le?" a ti a.
6. Tin, a buh sengtute chunga hotu a chiahhlawh chuan, "Moab nula, Moab ram ata Naomi hnena lo kal ve a ni:
7. Chutichuan Boaza chuan Ruthi hnenah chuan, "Ka fanu, ngai teh aw; lo dangah chuan buhto zawngin kal suh la, heta ta hi kal sawn suh, ka chhiahhlawh nulate hnenah hian awm reng rawh.
8. An buh seng hi en reng la, an hnu chhui zel rawh; tlangvalte chu engmah tih loh tur chein thu ka pe tawh diam e; tin, i tui a hal apiangin tui bela tlangvalte ti chawi sa va in thin ang che," a ti a.
9. Chutichuan a kun a, lei siin a bawkkhup a, a hnenah, "Hnam dang ka ni si a, nangin hetianga min hriat duha min lo khawngaih hi eng tizia nge ni?" a ti a.
10. Tin, Boaza huan a chhang a, a hnenah, "I pasal thih achina i pi chunga i thiltih dan zawng zawngte miin mi hril vek a; tin, i nu leh i pa leh i pianna ram kalsana tun hma lama i mi hriat ngai lohte hnena i lo kal dante pawh hi.
11. LALPA chuan i thiltih chu rul se la, LALPA, Israelte Pathian, inhumhimna'na a thlahnuai i rawn chuha chuan i that man pe teuh che u rawh se," a ti a.
12. Tin, ani chuan, "Ka pu, min khawngaih ang che; nangin mithlamuan a, i chhiahhlawh u late zinga mi ni mah suh i la, i chhiahhlawhnu hi tha takin mi be ta si a," a ti a.
13. Tin, chaw ei hun chuan Boazan a hnenah, "Lo haw teh khai, chhangte hi han ei ve la, chhang themte chu uain thurah chiah ve rawh," a ti a. Chutichuan buh sengtue kiangah chuan a thu a; tin, chhangper chu a pe a, a ei a, a kham hnu pawhin a la ei bang ta cheu a.
14. Tin, buhto zawng tura a thawh leh chuan, Boaza chuan a tlangvalte hnenah, "Buhphal zinga mite pawh hi char zel se la, hau suh ang che u.
15. Tin, a tela mite pawh, pawh chauhsak u la, hnutchiah zel u la, chhar se la, hau suh u," tiin thu a pe a.
16. Chutichuan a zawng a; tin, a zawn sa chu a vua a, barli epha khat emaw lai a ni.
17. Tin, a phur a, khuaah chuan a haw ta a: tin, a pi chuan a buto zawng chu alo hmu a; tin, ani chuan a kham hnuah a ei bang chu a phawrh a, a pe a.
18. Tin, api chuan a hnenah, "Vawiinah hian khawi lama buhto nge i zawn? A lo hre duhtu che chu malsawmin awm rawh se," a ti a. Tin, ani chuan, tu hnenah nge a va thawh tih a pi chu a hrilh a. "Vawiina ka va thawhnpa chu a ming Boaza a ni," a ti a.
19. Tin, Naomi chuan a monu hnenah chuan, "LALPA, mi nung leh mitthi chunga a khawngaihna bang chuang ngai loa chuan amah chu malsawm rawh se," a ti a. Tin, Naomi chuan a hnenah, "chu mi chu, kan lamhnai, kan laichin bul a ni a sin," a ti a.
20. Tin, Moab mi Ruthi chuan, "A nih tak chu, ka hnenah pawh, 'Ka buh hi a seng zawh vek hma loh chuan ka tlangvalho hnenah hian tel reng ang che,' mi ti nghe nghe a sin," a ti a.
21. Tin, Naomi chuan a monu Ruthi hnenah chuan, "Ka fanu, lo danga an hmuh hauh lohna tur chein a chhiahhlawh nulaho zingah hlir chuan kal la a tha ang," a ti a.Chutichuan barli leh huit seng zawh thlengin Boaza chhiahhlawhnulaho zingah hlir chuan buhto zawngin a kal a; tin, a pi hnenah chuan a awm a.
22. Chutichuan barli leh huit seng zawh thlengin Boaza chhiahhlawhnulaho zingah hlir chuan buhto zawngin a kal a; tin, a pi hnenah chuan a awm a.
