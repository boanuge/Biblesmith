1. Aw ka nuna, LALPA chu fak la; Ka kawchhunga awm zawng zawngte u, a hming thianghlim chu fak rawh u.
2. Aw ka nunna, LALPA chu fak la, A thiltih that zawng zawng chu theihnghilh suh u.
3. Ani chuan i khawlohna zawng zawng a ngaidam thin a; I natna zawng zawng a tidam thin a;
4. Boralna lak ata i nun chu a tlan thin a; Ngilneihna leh lainatnate a khumtir thn chia;
5. I ka chu thil thain a titlai thin a; Chutichuan i tleirawlna chu mupui anga siam tharin a awm leh thin.
6. LALPA chuan, hnehchhiahna tuar zawng zawngte tan, Thil felte tihsakin, rote a relsak thin.
7. A kawngte chu Mosia a hriattir a, A thiltihte chu Israela thlahte a hriattir bawk a.
8. LALPA chu lainatnaa khat, mi khawngaih thei tak, Thinnel tak, ngilneihna ngah tak a ni.
9. Ani chuan a hau lo fo ang a; A thinur chu a pai kumkhua hek lo ang.
10. Kan sualnate angin kan chungah a ti lo a, Kan khawlohnate angin min thungrul hek lo.
11. Van khi lei chung sang taka awm angin, Amah tihtute chunga a ngilneihna chu a nasa a ni.
12. Chhak leh thlang a inhlat ang hian, Kan bawhchhiatnate ch kan hnen ata a dah sanw ta diah a.
13. Pain a fate a khawngaih ang hian, LALPA chuan mah tihtue chu a khawngaih thin.
14. Kan taksa siam dan hi a hriat a, Vaivut mai kan ni tih a hriat reng avangin.
15. Mihring zawng, a dam chhung nite chu hlobet ang a ni a; Phul pangpar ang hian a vul thin a.
16. Thliin a chhem a, a bo thin a ni; A awmna chuan a hre leh tawh lo ang.
17. LALPA ngilneihna erawh chu amah tihtute chungah chuan chatuan ata chatuan thlengin a awm ang a, A felna pawh an tuchhuan thlengin a awm ang;
18. A thuthlun zawm apiangte leh, A thuhrilhte awih tuma hre reng apiangte chungah chuan.
19. LALPA chuan alalthutphah chu vanahte khian a tinghet a; A ram chuan engkim chungah ro a rel a.
20. A vantirhkohte u, A thu ngaithlaa, a thu zawm thin,
21. LALPA chu fak rawh u: A sipaihote u, a duhzawng ti thin, a rawngbawltute u, LALPA chu fak rawh u.A rorelna hmun zawng zawnga a thiltih zawng zawngte u, LALPA chu fak rawh u: Aw ka nunna, LALPA chu fak rawh.
22. A rorelna hmun zawng zawnga a thiltih zawng zawngte u, LALPA chu fak rawh u: Aw ka nunna, LALPA chu fak rawh.
