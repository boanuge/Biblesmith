Daniel (1/12)  →
1. Juda lal Jehoiakima rorel kum thumnaah chuan Babulon lal Nebukadnekzara Jerusalemah a lo kal a, a huala a.
2. Tin, Lalpa chuan Juda lal Jehoiakima chu a kutah a pe a, Pathianin bungbel thenkhatte nen; tin, ani chuan Sinar rama a pathian inah a kal pui ta a; tin, bungbelte chu a pathian sum inah a rawn thlen a.
3. ,4 Tin, lal chuan Israel fate zinga mi, lal thlahte leh mi tha fate, tleirawl demna awm lo, hmeltha tak, finna tinrenga remhria, hriatna lam a bengvar, thilbul chhut thiam, lal ina chetla thiam rawn hruailut tur leh Kaldai tawng leh thil thiam zirtir turin, a mitilreh hotu Aspenaza chu a hrilh a.
4. ,3 Tin, lal chuan Israel fate zinga mi, lal thlahte leh mi tha fate, tleirawl demna awm lo, hmeltha tak, finna tinrenga remhria, hriatna lam a bengvar, thilbul chhut thiam, lal ina chetla thiam rawn hruailut tur leh Kaldai tawng leh thil thiam zirtir turin, a mitilreh hotu Aspenaza chu a hrilh a.
5. Tin, lal chuan lal chaw tuihmel ata leh a uaiin in thin ata chu a nitin chan tur a ruatsak a, chutianga kum thum chawm a, chumi tawpa lal hmaa an din theihna'n.
6. Tin, he'ng zingah hian Juda fatea mi, Daniela te, Hanania te, Misaela te, Azaria te an tel a.
7. Tin, tilreh te hotu chuan an hming a sa hlawm a: Daniela chu Beltesazzara a vuah a, Hanania chu Sadraka, Misaela chu Mesaka, Azaria chu Abednagoa.
8. Mahse Daniela chuan a rilruin lal chaw leh a uain in thin te chuan intihbawlhhlawh loh a tum tlat a; chutichuan a intihbawlhhlawh lohna'n tilrehte hotu chu a dil a.
9. Tichuan, Pathian in Daniela chu tilrehte hotu laka duhsakna keh khawngaihna chu a dawntir a.
10. Tin, tilreh hotute chuan Daniela hnenah, In eitur leh in tur ruattu ka lalpa, lalber chu ka hlau a ni; in hmel hi tleirawl, in rualpuite aia chhe zawkin an rawn hmu ang a sin! Chuti a nih chuan lalber lakah ka lu hi hlauhpui awmin in siam dawn a ni a lawm,''a ti a.
11. Tin, tilreh te hotu in Daniela te, Hanania te, Misaela te,Azaria te enkawl tua a siam hnenah chuan Danielan,
12. Ka ngen a che, i chhiahhlawhte hi ni sawm min fiah rawh, eitur thlai leh in tur tuisik min pe mai rawhse.
13. Tin i hmaa kan lan dante leh lal chaw tuihmel ei tleirawlte landan chu tehkhin nisela, tin, i hmuh dan angin i chhiahhlawhte chungah hian ti ang che,'' a it a.
14. Tichuan, he thu hi a remtihsak a, anni chu ni sawm a fiah ta a.
15. Tin, ni sawm tawpah chuan lal chaw tuihmel eitu tleirawlte zawng zawng ai chuan an hmel a lang mawi zawkin an lo thau zawk mah a.
16. Tichuan, enkawltu chuan an eitur leh an uain intur chu a la bo a, thlai a pe ta zawk a.
17. Tichuan, he'ng tleirawl palite hi chu thiamna leh finna zawng zawngah hriatna leh bengvarna Pathianin a pe a, Daniela chuan inlarna leh mumang zawng zawng a hrethiam ta a.
18. Tin, lalin anmahni rawnhruai luhna tura a ni ruat tawpah chuan tilrehte hotu chuan an ni ho chu Neukadnezzara hmaah chuan a rawn hruailut a.
19. Tin, lal chuan a lo be hlawm a, anniho zingah chuan Daniela te, Hanania te, Misaela te, Azaria te ang reng reng an awm lo a, chuvangin lal hmaah chuan an ding ta a.
20. Tin, finna leh hriatthiamna thu tinreng lalin a han zawh chuan a ram pumpuia dawivaih thiam leh aikaihthiam zawng zawng te ai chuan alet sawmin an tha zawk tih a hre ta a.Tin, Daniela chuan lal Kura kumkhatna thlengin a thawk ta zel a.
21. Tin, Daniela chuan lal Kura kumkhatna thlengin a thawk ta zel a.
