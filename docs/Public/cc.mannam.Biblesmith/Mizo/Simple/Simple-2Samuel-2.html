1. Tin, heti hi ani a, chu mi hnua chuan Davida chuan, "Judai rama khawiah emaw ka han kal ang em?" tiin LALPA a rawn a. Tin, LALPA chuan a hnenah, "Han kal rawh khai," a ti a. Tin, Davida chuan, "Khawiah nge ka han kal ang?" a ti a. Tin, ani chuan, "Hebron-ah," a ti a.
2. Chutichuan Davida chu chutah chuan a han kal ta a, anupuite pahnih Jezreel mi Ahinoami leh Karmel mi Nabala nupui Abigaili te nen.
3. Tin, Davida chuan a hnena mi zawm zawng zawng chu a hruai chho a, mi tin, an chhungkuate theuh nen, Hebron khuaahte chuan an awm ta a.
4. Tin, Judai rama mite an va kal a, Juda mite chunga lal atan hriak an thih a. Chutichuan, "Saula phumtute chu Jabes-gilead mite an ni," tiin Davida chu an hrilh a.
5. Tichuan Jabes-gilead mite hnenah chuan Davida chuan mi a tir a, an hnenah, "In pu, in pu Saula chu in khawngaihzia tilangin in lo phum avangin LALPA chuan malsawm che u rawh se.
6. Tichuan LALPA chuan in chungah ngilneihna leh thutak lantir se la: he'ng thil in tih avang hian kei pawhin in ngilneih man chu ka rul ang che u.
7. Chutichuan in kutte chu lo chak se la, huai takin awm rawh u: in pu Saula chu a ti ta si a, tin, Juda chite chuan an chunga lal atan hriak min thih ta bawk a," a ti a.
8. Tin, Nera fapa Abnera, Saual sipaite hotu chuan, saual fapa Isbosetha chu Mahanai-ah a hruai a;
9. Gilead chungah te, Asur chungah te, Jezreel chungah te, Ephraim chungah te, Israel zawng zawng chungah te lalah a siam ta a.
10. (Saula fapa Is-bosetha chu Israel chunga ro a rel tan lai chuankum sawmli mi ani a, kum hnih ro a rel a.) Nimahsela Juda mite chuan Davida chu an pawm zawk a.
11. Davida'n Hebron-a Juda chite chunga ro a lo relna chu kum sarih le thla ruk ani tawh a.
12. Tin, Nera fapa Abnera leh Saula fapa Is-bosetha chhiahhlawhte hchu Mahanaim ata an chuak a, Gibeo-ah an kal a.
13. Tin, Zeruia fapa Joaba leh Davida chhiahhlawhte chu an kal chhuak a, Gibeon dilah chuan an intawk a; tichuan pawl khat chu dil ral lehlamah an thu a. pawl khat chu dil ral lehlamah.
14. Tin, Abnera chuan Joaba hnenah, "Tlangvalte chu lo thawk se la, kan hmaah han inbei teh reng ang hmiang," a ti a. Tin, Joaba chuan, "Lo thawk ang hmiang," a ti a.
15. Chutichuan an thawk a, a zat chin ruat chiahin an kai phei a, Benjamin chi, Saula fapa Is-bosetha lama tang swm leh pahnih, tin, Davida chhiahlawh sawm leh pahnih.
16. An kingpuite chu an lu theuhvah an inman a, mahni bitumate chu khandaihin an nakah an inchhun theuh a; tichuan an tlu za t vek mi a: chuvanin chu mi hmun chu Helkath-hzurim an ti ta a, chu chu Gibeon-a awm a ni.
17. Tichuan chu mi nia an ibei chu a nasa ta hle mai a; tin, Abnera leh Israel mite chu Davida chiahhlawhte lakah chuan an tlawm ta a.
18. Tin, chutah chuan Zeruia fapate pathum, Joaba te, Abisaia te, Asahela te an awm a: Asahela chu zukpui ang maia ngalchak a ni.
19. Chutichuan Asahela chuan Abnera chu a um a; Abnera a um chu ding lam leh vei lama peng hauh loin a um ngat ngat a.
20. Chutichuan Abnera chu ahan lehhawi a, "Nang i ni maw, Asahel?" a han ti a, Tin, ani chuan a chhang a, "Keimah ni e," a ti a.
21. Tin, Abnera chuan a hnenah, "I dung lamah emaw, i vei lamah emaw khan peng la, tlangval pakhat kha man la, a inralthuamna laksak rawh," a ti a. Nimahsela Asahela chuan amah a um chu a pensan duh chuang lo a.
22. Tin, Abnera chuan Asahela hnenah, "Min pensan rawh; engah nge ka chhun thluk ang che? I unaupa Joaba hmel ka hmu ngam dawn em ni?" a ti leh a.
23. Nimahsea a peng duh chuang lo va: chuvangin Abnera chuan a fei chhawr lamin a pumah a chhun a, fei chu a hnung lamaha tlang a, chutah chuan a tlu ta tawp a, chu mi hmunah chuan a thi ta a; tin, heti hi ani a, Asahela a tluka a thihna hmun lo thleng paiang chu chutah chuan an ding chawt zel a.
24. Mahse Joaba leh Abisaia chuan Abnera chu an um zel a; tin, Gibeon thlaler kawnga Gia dep Ama tlang an thlen dawn chuan ni a tla a.
25. Tin, Benjamina thlahte chu Abnera hnenah an pung khawm a, pawl khatah an insiam a, tlang chhipah an va ding t .
26. Chutichuan Abnera chuan Joaba a au a, "Khandaihin a sam reng dawn em ni ang? A tawp lamah chuan a la lungngaihthlak hle danw tih i hre lo em ni? Eng chen nge mipuite chu an unaute um kir tura hrilh loin i awm dawn le?" a ti a.
27. Tin, Joaba chuan, "Pathian nunna chhalin ka sawi e, engmah han sawi lo la chu naktuk zing thlengin ka miten an um dawn che u a sin le," a ti a.
28. Chutichuan Joaba chuan a tawtawrawt chu a han ham a, mipuite chu an ding ta thup mai a, Israel mite chu an um ta lo a, an bei leh ta lo.
29. Tichuan Abnera leh a mite chu Araba kal tlangin chu mi zan chuan an kal zankhua a; tin, Jordan an kan a, Bithron an kal tlang zak a, Mahanaim an thleng a.
30. Tin, Joaba chu Anera a um a kir leh ta a; tin, mipui zawng zawngte a han khawm vek chuan Davida hote sawm leh pakua leh Asahela an lokim ta lo a.
31. Davida hote erawh chuan Benjamin mite leh Abnera mite chu zathum leh sawmruk an tihlum a.Chutichuan Asahela ruang chu an la a, Bethlehem-a a pa thlanah an phum a. Tin, Joaba leh a mite chu zankhuain an kal a, Hebron an thlenin khua a var a.
32. Chutichuan Asahela ruang chu an la a, Bethlehem-a a pa thlanah an phum a. Tin, Joaba leh a mite chu zankhuain an kal a, Hebron an thlenin khua a var a.
