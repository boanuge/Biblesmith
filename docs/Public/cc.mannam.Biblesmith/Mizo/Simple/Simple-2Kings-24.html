1. A lal lai chuan Babulon lal Nebukadenezzara a lo lian a, Jehoiakim achu hhiah khuaah kum thum a awm a; chu mi hnuah chuan a hawisan a, a chungah a hel ta a.
2. Tin, LALPA'n Kaldai pawl te te, Suria pawl te te, Moab pawl te te, Amona thlah pawl te te a mah bei turin a tir chhuak a, a chiahhlawh zawlneite hmanga a sawi LALPA thu angin Judai ram tihchhiattir turin a tir a.
3. ,4 Mansea thiltih zawng zawn, a thil tihsual leh pawikhawih lo thisen a chhuah a, Jerusalem chu pawikhawih lo thisena a tihkhah avangin a hmuh phak loa suan bo tur thu Juda chungah LALPA thuin a lo thleng ta ngei a, LALPA'n a ngaidam duh lo a ni.
4. ,3 Mansea thiltih zawng zawn, a thil tihsual leh pawikhawih lo thisen a chhuah a, Jerusalem chu pawikhawih lo thisena a tihkhah avangin a hmuh phak loa suan bo tur thu Juda chungah LALPA thuin a lo thleng ta ngei a, LALPA'n a ngaidam duh lo a ni.
5. Tichuan Jehoiakima chanchin dang leh a thiltih zawng zawng chu Juda lalte lal lai chanchin ziakna buah a chuang a ni lo'm ni?
6. Chutichuan Jeoiakima chu a i leh pute hnenah a muhil a; tin, a fapa Jeoiakina chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
7. Tin, Aigupta lal chu a ram ata a lo chhuak leh ta lo va: Babulon lal chuan Aigupa ram lite atanga Euphrates lui thlenga Aigpta lal ram huam zawng zawng a la tawh si a.
8. Jehoiakima chu a la tantirhin kum sawm leh kum riat a ni a; Jerusalem-ah thla thum a lal a: a nu hming chu Nehusti, Jerusalem khuaa Elnathana fanu a ni.
9. Tin, a pa tih dan ang zawng zawng tiin, LALPA mithhmua thil tha lo tak a ti thin a.
10. Chutih lai chuan Babulon lal Nebukadnezzara mite hu Jerusalem-ah an han cho a, khua chu an han hual ta a.
11. Tin, chutia a miten an han hual lai chuan Babulon lal Nebukadnezzara chukhuaah a han kal a;
12. tichuan Juda lal Jehoiakina chu a nu te, a mite, a mi lian te, a puipate nen chuan babulon lala hnenah an va hhuak a: tin, a lal kum riat kumin Babulon lal chuan a man a.
13. Tin, LALPA sawi ang khan LALPA ina ro zawng zawng leh lal ina ro zawng zawngte chu a la chuak a, LALPA biak ina Israelte lal Solomonan rangkachak bungbel a siam zawng zawngte chu a sat sawm vek a.
14. Tin, Jerusalem-a mi zawng zawng, mi lian zawng zawng te, mi chak huaisen tha zawng zawng te, singkhat zet, sal themthiam leh thirdeng zawng zawng a ruai vek a; chu mi rama mi a rethei theite tih loh chu tumah an awm tawh lo.
15. Tin, Jehoiakina chu Babulon-ah a hruaia ; tin, lal nu te, lal nupi te, a puipa te, chu mi rama mic hakte chu Jerusalem atangin Babulon-ah salah a hruai a.
16. Tin, Babyulon lal chuan pasaltha zawng zawng sangsarih zet leh, themthiam leh thirdeng sangkhat, mi chak tha zawng indo thei mi thiau hu babuylon-ah salah a hruai vek a.
17. Tin, a pa unau Mattania chu Babulon lal chuan a aiah alaltir a, a hming hu Zedekia tiin a thlaksak a.
18. Zedekia chu a lal tantirhin kum sawmhnih leh kum khat a ni a; Jerusalem-ah kum sawm leh kum khat a lal a; a nu hming chu Hamutali, Libna khuaa Jeremia fanu a ni.
19. Tin, Jehoiakima tih dan ang zawng zawng tiin, LALPA mithmuah thil tha lo tak a ti thin a.LALPA thinurna avangin a hmu phak loa a thehthang bo thlengin Jerusalem leh Juda chungah thil a lo thleng zel a ni; Zedekia chu Babulon lal chungah chuan a hel ta a.
20. LALPA thinurna avangin a hmu phak loa a thehthang bo thlengin Jerusalem leh Juda chungah thil a lo thleng zel a ni; Zedekia chu Babulon lal chungah chuan a hel ta a.
