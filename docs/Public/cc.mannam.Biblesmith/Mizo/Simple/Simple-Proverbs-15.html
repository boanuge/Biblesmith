1. Chhanna nemin thinur a tikiang thin a, tawng huatthlala erawh chuan thinur a chawk tho thin.
2. Mi fing lei chuan fina thu dik a chhakchhuak thin a, mi a ka erawh chuan thu atthlak a chhakchhuak thin.
3. LALPA mit chu hmun tinah a awm a, thil tha lo leh tha a enthla reng thin.
4. Lei dik tak chu nunna thing a ni a, luhlulna awmna erawh chu thinlung lungchhiatna a ni.
5. Mi a chuan a pa thununa a ngainep thin a, zilhhauna ngaisang chuan hmang hre takin a ti thin.
6. Mi fel inah chuan sum tam tak a awm a, mi suaksual hlawkna erawh chu buaina a ni.
7. Mi fing hmui chuan hriatna a tidarh a, mi a thinlung erawh chuan chutiang chu a ti lo.
8. Mi suaksual inthawina chu LALPA tana tenawm a ni; mi tha tawngtaina erawh chu a lawmzawng a ni.
9. Mi suaksual kawng chu LALPA tnaa tenawm ani a, felna kawng zawhtu erawh chu a hmangaih thin.
10. Kawng pensantu thununa chu a na, zilhhau hawtu chu a boral ang.
11. Seol leh Abadon LALPA a hmu tlang a, mihring thinlung chu a va hmu chiang dawn em!
12. Hmusit ching mi chuan zilhhauva awm a ngaina lo a, mi fing hnenah chuan a kal duh lo ang.
13. Thinlung hlimin hmel hlim a siam a, thinlung lungngaihna erawh chuan rilru a tilungchhe thin.
14. Hriathiamna thinlung neitu chuan hriatna a zawng a, mi a ka erawh chu dawtin a inhrai thin.
15. Hrehawm tuartu tan ni zawng zawng mai hi a tha lo vek a, thinlung hlim mite rawh chuan ruai a theh reng mai a ni.
16. LALPA hlauhna nen tlemte neih chu tam tak neiha buaina neih ai chuan a tha zawk.
17. Hmangaihna awmnaa an hnah chawa rin chu huatna awmnaa bawngpa chawm thau ei aiin a tha zawk.
18. Mi thinchhia chuan inhauna a chawk tho a, ,mi thluak nem erawh chuan inhauna a tireh.
19. Mi thatchhe kawng chu hlingpal ang a ni a, mi tluang kawng chu lamliana siam a ni.
20. Fapa fing chuan pa hlim a siam a, mi a erawh chuan a nu a ngainep thin.
21. Finna nei lote chuan atna hi an hlimna a ni a, mi thil hre thiam mi erawh chuan a kawng a tingil thin.
22. Remruat lawk loa thil tih a hlawhtling duh lo. Remhria an tam chuan a hlawhtling thin;
23. A hun taka chhanna dik hirat hi a va lawmawm em!
24. Mi fing tan chuan nunna kawng hi a chung lamah a kal a, nnuai lama Seol chu a kalsan theihna'n.
25. LALPA'n mi chapo in chu a kar phawng ang a, hmeithai hmun erawh chu a tinghet ang.
26. Remruatna tha lo chu LALPA tana thil tenawm ani a, mi thianghlim thu erawh chu benglut a ni.
27. Mi duham chuan ama chhungkua a tibuai thin a, tham hawtu erawh chu a hmuingil ang.
28. Mi fel thinlung chau ngun takin a chahng a, mi suaksual ka erawh chuan thil tha lo a chhak chhuak thin.
29. LALPA chu mi suaksual lakah chuan a hla a, mi fel tawngtai erawh chu a ngaithla thin.
30. Hlim hmelin mi a tilawm a, chanchin lawmawmin mi a tiharh.
31. Nunna zilhhauna pawm thin chu mi fing zingah a tel ang.
32. Thununna duh lo chuan a thlarau a ngainep a ni, zilhhauna pawm erawh chuan hriat thiamna a chang ang.LALPA tihna chu fina zirtirtu ani a; inngaihtlawmna chu chawimawia awmna a ni.
33. LALPA tihna chu fina zirtirtu ani a; inngaihtlawmna chu chawimawia awmna a ni.
