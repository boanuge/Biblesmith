1. Solomona chuan Pharaoa fanu chang lo pawh hnam dang hmeichha tam tak Moab te, Amon te, Edom te, Zidon te, Hit-ho hmeichhiate a hmangaih a;
2. chung hnamho chungchang thu chu LALPA'n Israela thlahte hnenah, "Anmahni pawl suh ula, anni pawhin pawl che u suh se: an pathiante lamah in thinlung an hruai kawi ngei dawn si a," tiin alo sawi tawh reng a; chungho chu Solomona chuan hmangaihnain a vuan tlat a.
3. Tichuan nupui zasarih, lal fanu a nei a, hmei zathum nen: tin, a nupuite chuan a thinlung an hruai kawisak ta a.
4. Tin, heti hi a lo ni ta a, Solomona chu a lo upa hnu chuan, a nupuite chuan pathian dangte lamah a thinlung an hruai kawisak a: tichuan a pa Davida thinlung angin LALPA a Pathian lamah chuan a thinlung chu alo tha famkim ta lo a.
5. Solomona chuan Zidon-ho pathiannu Astorethi leh Amon-ho pathian tenawm tak Milkoma chu a lo zui ve ta mai a.
6. Tin, Solomona chuan LALPA mithmuhin thil sual alo ti ta a, a pa Davida angin LALPA chu a zui tlat ve ta lo a.
7. Chutichuan Solomona chuan Jerusalem bula tlangah Moab-ho pathian tenawm tak Kemosa tan leh, Amona thlahte pahtian tenawm tak Moleka tan hmun sang a lo siam ta a.
8. Chutiang bawkin hnam dang a nupui zawng zawng an pathiante hnena inthawia rimtuite hal thinte tan pawh a siamsak a.
9. ,10 Chutichuan LALPA Israelte Pathian, a hnena vawi hnih lai inlar a, he thu pathian dangte a zui tur ani lo tih thu lo petu chu a thinlungin a hawisan tak avangin Solomona chungah chuan LALPA thin a lo uur ta a: LALPA'n thu apek chu pawm si hek lo.
10. ,9 Chutichuan LALPA Israelte Pathian, a hnena vawi hnih lai inlar a, he thu pathian dangte a zui tur ani lo tih thu lo petu chu a thinlungin a hawisan tak avangin Solomona chungah chuan LALPA thin a lo uur ta a: LALPA'n thu apek chu pawm si hek lo.
11. Chuvangin LALPA'n Solomona hnenah chuan, "Hetiang hian i lo awm ta a, ka thuthlungte leh ka thuruatte pawm tura thu ka pek che hi i lo pawmta si lo a, ram hi ka chhuhsak ngei ngei ang chia, i chhiahhlawh ka pe ang.
12. Amaherawhchu i pa Davida avangin i dam chhung em chuan ka ti rih lo ang a: nimahsela i fapa zawng ka chhuhsak dawn a ni.
13. Ram zawng zawng erawh chu k achhuhsak vek lo ang a; nimahsela ka chhiahhlawh Davida avang leh ka thlan Jerusalem avang hian hna khat chu i fapa ka pe ang," a ti a.
14. Tin, LALPA'n Edom mi, Hadada chu Solomona hmelmaah a rawn din chhuak ta a: amah chu Edom rama lal thlah a ni.
15. Tin, heti hi a ni a, Davfida Edom rama a awm laia, an mi thahte phum tura sipai hotu Joaba a han kal a, Edom mi mipa fa tawh phawt chu a han thah vek hnu khan,
16. (Joaba leh Israel mi zawng zawngte chu thla ruk zet, Edom rama mipa zawng zawng a tihhlum vek hma loh chuan an awm a ni a;)
17. Hadada chu a pa chhiahhlawhte zinga Edom mi thenkhatte nen Aigupta rama kal turin an tlan a; Hadada chu naupangte a la ni a.
18. Midian ramata an chhuak a, Paran-ah an kal a: tin, Pran atangin mite an hruai a, Aigupta ram, Aigupta ram leh Pharaoa hnen an zuk thleng a; ani chuan in te a lo pe a, ei turte a lo ngaihtuahsak a, ramte a lo pe bawk a.
19. Hadada chuan Pharaoa mit a ltung hle mai a, tichuan a nupui ngeia laizawn, lalnu Tahpenesi laizawn chu nupuiah a neihtir a.
20. Tin, Tahpenesi laizawn chuan fapa Genubatha a hrinsak a, Tahpenesiin Pharaoa inah a hnute hnek banna a siamsak a: tin, Genubatha chu Pharaoa inah Pharaoa fate zingah chuan a awm ta a.
21. Tin, Hadadan Aigupta ram atanga Davida chu a puteho hnenah amuhil ta tih le, sipai hotu Joaba pawh a thi tawh tih a lo hriatin, Hadada chuan Pharaoa hnenah, "Kan rama ka haw theihna'n mi chhuahtir ta che," a ti a.
22. Chutichuan Pharaoan a hneah, "Ka hnena i awm hian tlakchham eng nge i neih ni le, ngai teh, chutia in ram lama haw leh i tum tak mai?" a ti a. Tin, ani chuan, "Engmah phei chu nei lo e: nimahsela mi kaltir reng reng rawh khai," a ti a.
23. Tin, Pathianin a hmelmaah Eliada fapa Rezona, a pu Hadadezera, Zoba lal hnen ata tlan bo tawh kha a rawn din chhuak leh a:
24. Davida'n Zoba khuaa mite a thah khan ani chuan a hnenah mite a kai khawm a, sipai hotuah a lo tang ta a: tin, Damaska khuaah an kal a, chutah chuan an awm a, Damaska khuaha chuan lalah a thu ta a.
25. Hadada tihhreawmna belhchhahin Solomona dam chhung zawng, Israelte hmelma a ni fo thin reng a. Israelte a haw hle mai a, Suria ram chungah ro a rel a.
26. Tin, Bebata fapa Jeroboama, Zereda khuaa Phraim mi, Solomona chhiahhlawh pawh chuan, (a nu hming chu Zeruii a ni a, hmeithai ani a,_ chu mi pawh chuan lal chungah a kut a lek ve a.
27. Lal chunga akut a rawn lek chhan chu hetiang hi a ni: Solomonan Milo a siam a, a pa Davida khua chu a chhe lai te a siam tha a.
28. Chu Jeroboama chu mi chak huaisen a ni a: tichuan Solomonan chu tlangval chu mi taima tak nia a hriat avangin Josefa chhungte hnathawh zawng zawng chunga hotuah a siam a.
29. Tin, chung lai chuan heti hi a ni a, Jeroboama Jerusalem khua ata a kal chhuak chu Silo mi zawlnei Ahijan kawng lakah a lo hmu a; Ahija chuan puan thar a sin a; an pahnih chauh chuan phulah an awmdun a.
30. Tin, Ahija chuan a puan thar sin chu a la a, phel sawm leh phel hnihah apawtthler a,
31. Jeroboama hnenah chuan, "Phel sawm hi la rawh khai: LALPA, Israelte Pathianin heti hian a ti a ni: "Ngai teh, ram hi Solomona ka chhuhsak ang a, hnam sawm chu ka pe dawn che a ni:
32. (amaherawhchu ka chhiahhlawh Davida avang leh Israel hnam zawng zawng zinga ka thlan khawpui, Jerusalem avang hian hnam khat zawng anei ang chu:)
33. anni chuan mi kalsan a, Zidon-ho pathiannu Astarothi te, Moab-ho pathian Kemosa te, Amon-ho thlahte pathian Milkomate an biak avang leh ka mithmuha thil tha ti tur leh ka thuruatte leh ka rorelte hi pawm tura a pa Davida anga ka kawngte an zawh loh avangin.
34. Ram pumpui zawng ka chhuhsak lo ang: amaherawhchu ka thuupekte leh ka thuruatte pawmtu, ka thlan, ka chhiahhlawh Davida avangin a dam chhung hchuan lalah ka la awmtir ang:
35. nimahsela ram hi a fapa ka chhuhsak ang a, nangmah hi ka pe dawn che a ni, hnam sawmte chu.
36. Tin, ka chhiahhlawh Davida'n ka hming awmtirna'na ka thlan khawpui Jerusalem-ah hian ka hmaa khawnvar aneih reng theihna turin hnam khat chu a fapa ka pe ang.
37. Nang hi ka ruat ang chia, i duh ang angin ro i rel ang a, Israelte lalah i awm ang.
38. Tin, heti hi ani ang a, thu ka pek apiang che i pawm a, ka kawngte i zawh a, ka chhiahhlawh Davida anga ka thuruatte leh ka thupekte zawm tura ka mithmuha thil tha i tih chuan i hnenah ka awm zel ang a, Davida ka din nghehsak ang khan chhungkaw nghet ka dinsak ang chia, Israelte hi ka pe ang che.
39. Chuvang chuan Davida thlah chu ka tihreawm dawn ai, amaherawhchu kumkhua zawngin a ni lo ang,' a ti e," tiin.
40. Chutichuan Solomona chuan Jeroboama chu thah a tum ta a: nimahsela Jeroboama chu a tho a, Aigupta ramah, Aigupta ram lal Sisaka hnenah a tlan a, Solomona thih hma loh chuan Aigupta ramah a awm ta a.
41. Solomona chanchin dangte leh, a thiltih zawng zawng leh a finnate chu Solomona thiltih ziakna buah a chuang a ni lo'm ni?
42. Solomonan Israel-ho zawng zawng chunga Jerusalem khuaa ro a rel chhung chu kum sawmli a ni.Tichuan Solomona chu a pute hnenah chuan amuhil a, apa Davida khawpuiah chuan an phum ta a: tin, a fapa Rehoboama chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
43. Tichuan Solomona chu a pute hnenah chuan amuhil a, apa Davida khawpuiah chuan an phum ta a: tin, a fapa Rehoboama chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
