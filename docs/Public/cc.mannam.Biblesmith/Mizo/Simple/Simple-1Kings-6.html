1. Tin, heti hi ani a, Israela thlahte Aigupta ram ata an chhuah achin kum zali leh sawmriatna kum, Israel-hote chunga Solomona a lal kum lina, Ziv thla, thla hnihna thla chuan LALPA in sak chu a tan a.
2. Tin, Solomonan LALPA tana in a sak chu a dung lam tawng sawmruk ani a, a vang lam chu tawng sawmhnih a ni a, a san lam chu tawng sawmthum a ni.
3. Tin, chu mi ina hmung thianghlim hma lama hulhliap chu a in vang lam che, tawng sawmhniha sei a ni a, a hma lama mi vang lam chu tawng sawm a ni.
4. Tin, in atan chuan tukverh zimte thil kawkalh awmin a siam a.
5. Tin, a in bang velin, biak in bang velah pawh, biakna hmung bang velah pawh chu mi in bang si chuan in chhawng nei a sa a: tin, a vel chuan pindan a siam a:
6. a chhawng hnuaihnung ber chu a vang lam tawng nga a ni a, a laita chu a vang lam tawng ruk ani a, a pahthumna chu a vang lam tawng sarih a ni: kalkhangte chu in banga inrawlh ngai loin pawn lamah chuan a velin in bangah chuan a nghahna a siam vang a ni.
7. Tin, in chu, an sak lai chuan lung laihna hmuna chher sa veka sak a ni: tichuan an sak chhung zawng chu tuhoh ri te, hreipui ri te, thir hriamhrei ri reng rengte chu mi inah chuan hriat ani lo.
8. Pindan laita berte kawngka chu chu mi in ding lampangah chuan a awm a: a laitaah chuan rahka vialah an lawn chho a, tin, a laita atang chuan a pathumnaah.
9. Chutichuan in chu a han sa a, a zo ta ; in chu sidar khanchhuk leh a phekin a khuh a.
10. Tin, in chhawng neite chu a insi vekin tawng nga theuha sangin a sa a: chungte chu in nen chuan sidar thinga zawm a ni.
11. Tin, LALPA thu chu Solomona hnenah a lo thleng a,
12. "He in i sak lai mek chungchang thu hi - ka thuruatte i pawm a, ka rorelte i zawm a, ka thupek zawng zawngte zawm tura i pawm phawt zawngin i pa Davida hnena ka lo sawi tawh, ka thu kha i chungah hian ka tinghet ang.
13. Tin, Israela thlahte zingah hian ka cheng ang a, ka mi Israel-hote hi ka thlahthlam lo ang," tiin.
14. Tichuan Solomona chuan in chu a sa a, a zo ta a.
15. In chhung lam bangah chuan sidar thingphel a hmang a; a chhuat atanga chung lam bang thlengin a chhung lamah chuan thing a bel vek a: a chhuatah chuan thingphel a phah belh a.
16. Tin, in kawmchar lam tawng sawmhnih chu a chhuat atanga a bangte chenin sidar thingphel vekin a siam a: biakna hmun, hmun thianghlim ber atan pawh a chhung lamah chuan ahmang a.
17. Tin, in, biakna hmun bula biak in chu a dung lam tawng sawmli a ni.
18. Tin, in chhung lamah chuan a kuhmum leh a par parh lema ker mawi sidar thing a awm a: a vai chuan sidar thing vek ani a; lung reng reng a lang lo.
19. Tin, LALPA thuthluna bawm dahna turin chu mi inah a chhunga biakna lamah chuan hmun a buatsaih a.
20. Tin, chu mi biakna hmun chhung chua dung lam tawng sawmhnih, a vang lam tawng sawmhnih, a san lam tawng sawmhnih a ni a; chu chu rangkachak hlir hlak tiin a luan a: tin, sidar maicham chu a luan bawk a.
21. Chutichuan Solomona chuan chu mi in chhung chu rangkachak hlir hlak tiin a luan a:
22. tin, in chu a zaa a peih thlengin a pumin rangkachak tiin a luan zel a: b iakna hmunamaicham pum pui pawh chu rangkachak tiin a luan a.
23. Tin, biakna hmunah chuan tawng sawm ve vea sang, cherub pahnih olive thingin a siam a.
24. Tin, cherub thla pakhat chu tawng nga ani a, cherub thle lehlam chu tawng nga bawk a ni: thla pakhat hmawr atang a thla lehlam hmawr inkar chu tawng sawm a ni.
25. Cherub pakhat pawh chu tawng sawm a ni a: Cherubte chu intiat chiah leh pian dan inang ve ve chiah a ni.
26. Cherub pakhat sanzawng chu tawng sawm ani a, cherub pakhat pawh chu chu mi chen bawk chu a ni.
27. Tin, cherubte chu in chhungril zawkah chuan a dah a; tin, cherubte thla chu a inzar a, pakhat thla chuan bang lehlam a va dek a, tin, cherub pakhat a thla pawh bang leh lam chu a dek bawk a; chu mi in lai takah chuan an thla leh an thla chu a insi a.
28. Tin, cherub-te chu rangkachak tiin aluan a.
29. Tin, in bang zawng zawngte chu a velin cherub te, tum thing leh pangpar parh lemte ker mawiin chhung lam leh pawn lamah chuan a siam a.
30. Tin, a in chhuat chu chhung lam leh pawn lamah rangkachak tiin a luan a.
31. Tin, biakna hmun luhna atan olivthing kawngkhar a siam a: kawngka lu chung leh kawngka biangte chu a bang hmun ngaa then a hmun khat tiat a ni.
32. tin, oliv thingin kawngkhar pahnih a siam a; chu miah chuan cherubte, tum thin gleh pangpar parh lem te ker mawiin a siam a. chung chu rangkachak tiin a luan a; tin, cherubte leh tum thingte chu rangkachak a hnawih a.
33. Tin, biak in luhna atan a bang hmun lia thena hmun khat tiatin oliv thing kawngka ban a siam bawk a:
34. far thing kawngkhar pahnih nen; kawngkhar pakhata phek hnih chu thlep thei a ni a, kawngkhar pakhata phek hnih pawh chu thlep thei a ni.
35. chungahte chuan cherub te, tum thing leh pangpar parh lemte a ker a: cchung ker mawi chu mam takin rangkachak tiin a luan a.
36. Biak in hung chhung kawtlai chhungril zawk chu lung chher chhawng thum leh, sidar thingphel thuah khatin a siam a.A kum lina, Ziv thlaah chuan LALPA in lungphum chu rem a ni a. Tin, kum leh kum khatna, bul thla, thla riatnaah chuan in chu ruahman ang chiahin, a engkim chu peih vek a ni a. Chutichuan kum sarih a sa a ni.
37. A kum lina, Ziv thlaah chuan LALPA in lungphum chu rem a ni a. Tin, kum leh kum khatna, bul thla, thla riatnaah chuan in chu ruahman ang chiahin, a engkim chu peih vek a ni a. Chutichuan kum sarih a sa a ni.
