1. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia a bia a,
2. "Israela thlahte hnenah hian thu sawi rawh khai, kir leh se la, Migdol hmun leh tuipui inkar Pi-hahiroth hmun bul, Baal-Zephon hmun bulah chuan riak rawh se: chu mi dep tuipui kamah chuan riak rawh u.
3. Tichuan Pharaoan Israela thlahte chungchang thu-ah, 'Ram lakah an mangang ta zet le, thlalerah an tang ta,' a ti ang a.
4. Tin, keiin Pharaoa thinlung chu ka tihkhauhsak ang a, tichuan anmahni chu a um ang a; tin, keiin Pharaoa lakah leh a sipaiho zawng zawng lakah chuan chawimawina chu ka hlawh ang; tichuan Aigupta miten kei hi LALPA chu ka ni tih an hria ang," tiin. Tin, chutiang chuang an ti a.
5. Tin, mite chu an tlan bo zo ta tih Aigupta lal chu an hrilh a: tin, Pharaoa thinlung leh a chhiahhlawhte thinlung chu a lamlet leh ta a, tichuan, "Israel mite kan rawngbawl lai kan chhuahtir ta ngawt mai hi engtiziaa hetianga ti ta nge maw kan nih chu le?" an ti ta a.
6. Tin, an tawlailir an buatsaih a, tin, mite chu a hnenah a hruai a:
7. tin, tawlailir zaruk tha thiau leh Aigupta rama tawlailir zawng zawng, an zaa chunga hotute nen a hruai ta a.
8. Tin, LALPA'n Aigupta ram lal Pharaoa thinlung chu a tihkhauhsak a, tin, Israela thlahte chu a um ta a: Israel thlahte lah chu induh takin an chhuak a ni si a.
9. Tin, Aigupta mite chuan sakawr zawng zawng leh Pharaoa tawlailir leh a sakawr chung chuang mite leh a sipaihote nen chuan an um a, tin, Pi-hahiroth hmun bul, Baal-Zephon hmun depa tuipui kama an riak chu an va umpha ta a.
10. Tin, Pharaoan a va pan hnaih chuan Israela thlahte chu an han dak a, tin, ngai teh, Aigupta mite chuan an lam chu an pan phei ta a: tichuan an lo hlau ta em em mai a: tin, Israela thlahte chuan LALPA chu an au ta a.
11. Tin, Mosia hnenah, "Aigupta ramah khan thlan a awm loh avangin em ni thlalerah hian thi tura min hruai chhuah ni? eng vangin nge ni hetianga min ti tura Aigupta tam ata min hruai chhuah ni?
12. Aigupta ramah pawh khan, 'Aigupta mite rawngbawl hian kan awm mai ang,' tiin i hnenah khan kan sawi reng lo em ni? thlalera thih tlaw ngawt mai te ai chuan Aigupta mite rawng kha lo bawl ta i la a that zawk dawn hi," an ti a.
13. Tin, Mosia'n mipuite hnenah chuan, "Hlau suh u, awm hle hle mai u la, LALPA chhandamna, vawiina a thawhsak tur che u hi en ngawt mai rawh u, vawiina Aigupta mi in hmuhate hi kumkhuain in hmu leh dawn tawh lo e.
14. LALPA'n a dosak ang che u a, nangni chu in awm hle hle mai ang," a ti a.
15. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia hnenah chuan, "eng vangin nge maw mi auh le? Israela thlahte hi kal zel turin hrilh ta che.
16. Tin, nang pawhin i tiang kha han la la, tin, tuipui chungah khan lek la, tui chu lo inthen rawh se: tichuan tuipui chhung lei charah Israela thlahte chu an kal ang.
17. Tin, keiin, ngai teh, Aigupta mite thinlung chu ka tihkhauhsak ang a, tichuan an um zel ang: tin, Pharaoa lakah leh a sipaiteho zawng zawng lakah leh a tawlailirte lakah a sakawr chung chuang mite lakah chuan chawimawina chu ka hlawh ang.
18. Tin, Pharaoa lakah leh, a tawlailirte lakah leh a sakawr chung chuang mite laka chawimawina ka hlawh hunah chuan Aigupta miten kei hi LALPA chu ka ni tih an hria ang," a ti a.
19. Tin, Pathian vantirhkoh, Israela mite hma hruai thin kha an hnung lamah a insawn ta a; tin, chhum ding kha an hma lam ata a insawn a, an hnung lamah a ding ta a:
20. tin, Aigupta mite leh Israel mite inkarah a lo tla ta a, tichuan chhum chu a lo awm a, a thim bawk a, amaherawhchu chhum chuan zan chu a tieng a: tin, zankhuain a tu lam mah mah an inpan lo a.
21. Tin, Mosia'n tuipui chungah a kut a han phar a: tin, LALPA'n zankhuain khawchhak thli na takin a rawn thlehtir a, tuipui chu a nem kirtir a, tin, tuipui chu lei charah a chantir a, tin, tuite chu a lo inthen ta a.
22. Tin, Israela thlahte chu tuipui chhung lei charah chuan an kal ta a: tin, tuite chu an ding lampangah leh an vei lampangah chuan an tan bang a lo ni ta mai a.
23. Tin, Aigupta mite chuan an um a, Pharaoa sakawr zawng zawngte leh a tawlailirte leh a sakawr chung chuang mite chuan tuipui chhungah chuan an um lut ta a.
24. Tin, heti hi a ni a, khawfing chah veleh, LALPA'n Aigupta mite chu meialh ding leh chhum dingah chuan a zuk timangang ta a.
25. An tawlailir kete chu a thlawnsak a, an khalh chak thei ta l o a; chutichuan Aigupta mite chuan, "Israel-hote hi i tlansan ang u; LALPA'n an lama tangin he mite hi Aigupta mite a dopui a nih hi," an ti ta a.
26. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia hnenah, "Aigupta mite, an tawlailirte, an sakawr chung chuang mite chu tuiin a rawn chim leh theihna turin i kut kha tuipui chungah han phar rawh khai," a ti a.
27. Tin, Mosia'n tuipui chungah chuan a kut chu a han phar a, tin, khua a var veleh chuan tuipui chu a luan pangngaiin a luang leh ta a; tin, Aigupta mite chu an tlan kir leh a; tin, LALPA'n Aigupta mite chu tuipui chhungah chuan a paih thla ta a.
28. Tin, tuite chu a luang leh ta a, tichuan tawlailirte leh sakawr chung chuang mite, tuipuia um luttu, Pharaoa sipaiho zawng zawngte lam chuan tuiin a chim pil ta vek a; pakhat mah chhuak reng reng an awm hauh lo mai a.
29. Israela thlahte erawh chu tuipui chhung lei charah chuan an kal vek a; tin, tuite chu an ding lampangah leh an vei lampangah chuan an tan bang a lo ni ta mai a.
30. Chutiang chuan a ni, LALPA'n Israel-hote chu, chu mi ni chuan Aigupta mite kut ata a chhanchhuah ni; tin, Israel-hote chuan Aigupta mi thite chu tuipui kamah chuan an hmu a.Tin, Israel-hote chuan LALPA'n Aigupta mite chunga thil ropui tak a tih chu an hmu a, tin, mipuite chuan LALPA chu an tih a: tichuan LALPA leh a chhiahhlawh Mosia chu an ring ta a.
31. Tin, Israel-hote chuan LALPA'n Aigupta mite chunga thil ropui tak a tih chu an hmu a, tin, mipuite chuan LALPA chu an tih a: tichuan LALPA leh a chhiahhlawh Mosia chu an ring ta a.
