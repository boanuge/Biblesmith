1. LALPA chu fak rawh u. Aw LALPA chhiahhlawhte u, fak u la, LALPA hming chu fak rawh u.
2. LALPA hming chu fakin awm rawh se. Tun atanga, kumkhuain.
3. Ni chhuahna atanga a tlakna thlengin LALPA hming chu fak tur a ni.
4. LALPA chu hnam tinrengte chungah chuan chungnung daih a ni a, A ropuina chuan vante khi a khum daih a ni.
5. ,6 LALPA kan Pathian, Chung lama thutphah neia, Vana thil awmte leh leia thil awmnte en tura Intihnuai thina ang hi, tu nge awm?
6. ,5 LALPA kan Pathian, Chung lama thutphah neia, Vana thil awmte leh leia thil awmnte en tura Intihnuai thina ang hi, tu nge awm?
7. Ani chuan rethei chu vaivut ata a kai tho thin a, Pachhia chu ekdur ata a chawi kang thin;
8. Mi liante hnena a awmtir theihna turin, A mite zinga mi liante hnen ngeiah chuan.Ani chan hmeichhe ching chu in lo a vawntir a, Fanau nei nu hlim takah a siam thin. LALPA chu fak rawh u.
9. Ani chan hmeichhe ching chu in lo a vawntir a, Fanau nei nu hlim takah a siam thin. LALPA chu fak rawh u.
