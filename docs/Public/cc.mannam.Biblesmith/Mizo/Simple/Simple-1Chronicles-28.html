1. Tin, Davida'n Israel hotu zawng zawng - hnam puipa te, lal rawngbawla intlak thin pawl hotu te, sipai sang hotu te, za hotu te, lal leh a fapate sum le thil neih zawng zawng chunga hotu te, mi lian leh mi chak, mi chak huaisen zawng zawngte nen- Jerusalemah a ko khawm a.
2. Tichuan lal Davida chu a ding a, "Ka unaute leh ka mite u, ka thu hi lo ngaithla teh u: kei chuan LALPA thuthlunna bawm dahna leh kan Pathian ke nghahchhan tur in sa i la tih ka lo rilru thin a; tin, sakna tur pawh ka ngah tawh diam a.
3. Nimahsela Pathianin ka hnenah, 'Nang zawng indo mi, thisen chhuah mi i niha vangin ka hming atan i sa lo ang,' a ti a.
4. Amaherawhchu LALPA, Israelte Pathianin ka pa chhungte zawng zawng zing ata Israelte lala kumkhuaa awm turin mi lo thlang ta a; Juda hnam chu puipa atan a lo thleng ta a; tin, Juda hnamte zingah ka pa chhung te; tin, ka pa fapate zing ata Israel zawng zawng lalah mi lo thlang chhuak ta bik a;
5. tin, ka fapa zawng zawngte zingah chuan (LALPA'n fapa tam tak mi pe a) Israaelte lal atan LALPA ram lalthutthlenga thu turin ka fapa Solomona hi a lo thlang ta a.
6. Tichuan ka hnenah, 'I fapa Solomonan ka in lehka kawtlaite chu a din ang; ka fapa atan ka thlang tawh a, tichuan a pa ka ni ang.
7. ka thupekte leh ka rorelte a pawm reng chuan - tunah in tih reng ang hian - a ram chu kumkhua atan ka tinghet ang,' a ti a.
8. chutichuan LALPA mipui, Israel-ho zawng zawng mithmuhah leh ka Pathian hriata hian LALPA in Pathian thupek zawng zawngte hi pawm tur leh ngaihven turin ka fuih a che u; tichuan he ram tha tak hi in lua ang a, in hnua in thlahte kum khua atan in rochun chhawntir zel ang.
9. Tin, nang ka fapa Solomon, i pa Pathian hi hria la, thinlung tha famkim leh rilru phur takin a rawng bawl ang che; LALPA chuan thinlung a en thin a, rilrua suangtuah zawng zawngte hi a hre vek a ni; amah i zawn chuan i hmu ang a, i hawisan erawh chuan kumkhuain a thlauhthla ang che.
10. Fimkhur rawh khai; hmun thianghlim atana in sa turin LALPA'n a lo ruat tak che hi; hak takin awm la, bei rawh, a ti a.
11. Chutichuan Davida chuan a fapa Solomona ch biak in hulhliap lem te, a in lem te, sum dah khawmna in lem te, a pindan chungnung lem te, a chhung pindam lem te, zahngaihna thutphah lem te,
12. a rilrua a suangtua ang zawng zawng, LALPA in hungchhung kawtlai lem te, a vel pindan zawng zawng lem te, Pathian ina sum dah khawmna in lem te, thil serh dah khawmna in lem te chu a pe a;
13. puithiamte le Levi-hote a pawl pawla an awmna tur lem te, LALPA ina rawngbawlna lam tur zawng zawng lem te, LALPA ina rawngbawlna tur bungbel zawng zawng lem te pawh;
14. rawngbawlna lam atana bungbel chi tin, rangkachak bungbel tur, rangkachak hman zat tur te, rawngbawlna lam atana tangkaraw bungbel tur tangkaraw hman zat tur te,
15. Rangkachak khawnvar dahna leh a khawnvar tur rangkachak hman zat tur te, khawnvar dahna leh a khawnvar tur zel atana hman zat tur te, khawnvar dahna hman tulna theu ang zela tangkaraw khawnvvar dahna tur zel atana hman zat turte nen;
16. chhang chiahna dawhkan tur, pakhat zela tana rangkachak hmanzat tur te, tangkaraw dawhkan atan tangkaraw hman zat tur te.
17. Rangkachak hlir hlak sa thurna thir kawm te, maihun te, note nen; rangkachak berhbu tur, berhbu pakhat zel atana hman zat tur te, tangkaraw berhbu tur, berhbu pakhat zel atana hman zat tur te;
18. Rangkachak thianghlima siam, rimtui halna maichamtur te; cherub-te an thla zara LALPA thuthlunna bawm tuam chuanna lem ang siamna tur rangkachak te nen.
19. Davida chuan, "Hengte hi tih dan tur rem a ruat sa tichiang tura LALPA'n ama kut ngeia a ziak ang kha a ni," a ti a.
20. Tin, Davida'n a fapa Solomona hnenah chuan, "Chak tak leh huai takin awm la, bei rawh; hlau suh la, zam hek suh; LALPA Pathian, ka Pathian hi i hnenah a awm e; LALPA in lam hna zawng zawng hi peih vek a nih hma loh chuan a kalsanin a thlahthlam lo ang che.Tin, ngai teh, Pathian in hna tinreng thawktu pawl puithiamte leh Levi-hote na awm a, eng hna pawh kawng tinrenga hna thiam, chak tak theuha tanpuitu tur che i hnenah an awm zel ang; a hotute leh mipui zawng zawng pawhin i thupek apiang chu an zawm zel bawk ang," a ti a.
21. Tin, ngai teh, Pathian in hna tinreng thawktu pawl puithiamte leh Levi-hote na awm a, eng hna pawh kawng tinrenga hna thiam, chak tak theuha tanpuitu tur che i hnenah an awm zel ang; a hotute leh mipui zawng zawng pawhin i thupek apiang chu an zawm zel bawk ang," a ti a.
