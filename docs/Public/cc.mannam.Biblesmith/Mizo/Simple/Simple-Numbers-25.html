1. Tichuan Israel-ho chu Sittim-ah an awm a, tin, Moab-ho fanute chu an pawl ta hlawm a:
2. an pathiante hnenah an inthawinate chu an sawm a; tichuan an ei a, an pathiante chu chibai a buk ve a.
3. Tin, Israel-ho chuan Peor hmuna Baal chu an pawm ve ta a; tichuan Israelho chungah LALPA thin chu a alh ta a.
4. Chutichuan LALPA'n Mosia hennah, "mipuite hotu zawng zawng hi hruai la, LALPA thinurna tihbaiawm tak mai chu Israel-ho chung ata a kian theihna'n chhun engah LALPA hmaah awhhlumtir rawh," a ti a.
5. Tin, Mosia'n Israel-ho roreltute hnenah chuan,"Peor hmuna Baal pawm apiangte chu tihlum vek rawh u," a ti a.
6. Tin, ngai teh, Israela thlahte zinga mi pakhatin inbiakna puan in kawngkaa a tah lai takin Mosia mit hmuhah leh Israel-ho pungkhawmte mithmuhah Media hmeiche pakhat a chhungte hnenah a rawn hruai a.
7. Tin, puithiam Arona fa Eleazara fapa Phinehasan a han hmuh chuan pungkhawmho zing ata chu a tho a, fei a chuh a;
8. tin, Israel mi chu puan in chhungah chuan a um lut a, Israel mi nen an pahnih chuan a chhun tlang , hmeichia chu a pumah. Chuti chuan Israel thlahte chung hremna chu a reh ta .
9. Chumi hremnaa thite chu 24,000 an ni.
10. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia a bia a,
11. "puithiam Arona fa Eleazara fapa Phinehasa hian ka thin anga an zinga a thikna chuan ka thinurna chu Israela thlahte lak ata a tikiang ta a, tichuan ka thikna chuan Israela thlahte chu ka tiboral lo ang .
12. Chuvangin 'Ngai teh, ka inremna thuthlung chu a chungah ka thlung ang a;
13. tin, amah leh a hnua a thlahte tan kumkhaw puithiam nihna thuthlung ani ang a; a Pathian tan a thik a, Israela thlahte tan inremna a siam avangin,' ti rawh," tiin.
14. Israel mi an thah, Midia hmeichhia nena an thah tak hming chu Zimria, Simeon-ho zinga an chhungkaw puipa Salua fapa a ni a.
15. Tin, Midia hmeichia an thah taka hming chu Kozbi, Zura fanu a ni: ani chu Midia ho chhungkaw zinga an hotu a ni.
16. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia a bia a,
17. Mida-ho chu bei u la, tilawm rawh u:Peor hmunah an bum che u a, hrem tak Midia-ho milian fanu Kozbi an thah tak chungchang thuah in chungah thil dik lo tak an tih aeleazara hian," tiin.
18. Peor hmunah an bum che u a, hrem tak Midia-ho milian fanu Kozbi an thah tak chungchang thuah in chungah thil dik lo tak an tih aeleazara hian," tiin.
