1. Israel-ho ant am chin chu- chhungkaw puipa te, sipai sang hotu te, za hotute leh lal rawngbawltu zawng zawng, kum khat chhunga thla tina inthlak zel thin, pawl tina mi chu singhnih leh sang li an ni.
2. Pawl khatah thla khatnaa hotu ch Jebdiela fapa Jasobeama a ni a, a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
3. Ani chuan Pereza thlaha mi, thlakhatnaa sipai hotu zawng zawng hotu ber a ni.
4. Tin, thla nihnaa pawl hotu chu Aho mi Dodaia a ni a, a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
5. Thla linaa sipai hotu chu Joaba unaupa Asahela a ni a, a fpa Zebadian a ai a awh a ni; tin, a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
6. Thla nganaa sipai hotu chu Izra mi Samhutha a ni a; tin, apawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
7. Thla ruknaa sipai hotu chu Teko mi,Ikesa fapa Ira a ni a; tin, a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
8. Thla sarihnaa sipai hotu chu Ephraima thlah Pelon mi Heleza a ni a, a pawla chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
9. Thla riatnaa sipai hotu chu Zera thlah, Husat mi Sibekaia a ni a; tin, a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm..
10. Thla sarihnaa sipai hotu chu Ephraima thla Pelon mi Heleza a ni a, a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
11. Thla riatnaa sipai hotu chu Zera thlah, Husat mi Sibekaia a ni a, a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
12. Thla kuanaa sipai hotu chu Benjamina thlah Anathot mi Abiezera a ni a; a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
13. Thla sawmnaa sipai hotu chu Zera thlah Netophat mi Maharaia a ni a; tin, a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
14. Thla sawm leh thla khatnaa sipai hotu chu Ephraima thlah Pirathon mi Benaia a ni a; tin, a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
15. Thla sawm leh thla hnihnaa sipai hotu chu Othniela thlah Netophat mi Heldaia a ni a; tin, a pawlah chuan singhnih leh sangli an awm.
16. Chung lo pawhchu, Israel hnamo chunga Reuben-hote zingah chuan Zikria fapa Eliezera roreltu a ni a; Simeon-ho zingah Maaka fapa Sephatia;
17. Levi-ho zingah Kemuela fapa Hasabia; Arnon-ho zingah Zadoka;
18. Juda-ho zingah Davida unaute zinga mi Elihua; Isakar ho zingah Mikaela fapa Omria;
19. Zebulun-ho zingah Obadia fapaIsmaia; Naphtali-ho zingah Ariela fapa Jeremotha;
20. Ephraima thlahte zingah Azazaia fapa Hosea; Manasea hnam zatvete zingah Pedaia fapa Joela;
21. Gilead rama Manasea hnam zatvete zingah Zekaria fapa Idova; Benjamin-ho zingah Abnera fapa Jaasiela;
22. Dan-ho zingah Jerohama fapa Azarela. Chungte chu Israel hnam hotute an ni.
23. Davida chuan kum sawmhnih achin hnuai lam chu a chhiar lo a; Israel-o te chu vana arsi zat khiang a la tipung ang ti LALPA'n a lo sawi tawh avangin.
24. Zeruii fapa Joaba chuan a han chhiar tan a, nimahsela a chiar zo lo a, Israelte chungah pawh thinurnaa lo thleng ta a; lal Davida lal lai chanchin ziakna buah a zat ziak a ni bawk hek lo.
25. Tin, lal sum enkawltu chu Adiela fapa Azmavetha a ni a; tin, lo lamah te, khawpiah te, khawperah te, in sangtea sum enkawltu chu Uzia fapa Jonathana a ni a;
26. tin, loa leleh hnathawk ho enkawltu chu Keluba fapa Ezria a ni a;
27. tin, grep huan enkawtu chu Ramat mi Simeia a ni a; tin, grep huan a mi uain sawr da khawmna enkawltu chu Siphmot mi Zabdia a ni a;
28. tin, phai ruama Olive thing leh theipui kiungte enkalwtu c Geder mi baal-hanana a ni a; tin, hriak da khawmna enkawltu chu Joasa a ni a;
29. tin, Saron-a tla ran rual enklawtu chu Saron mi Straia a ni a; tin, ruama ran rual enkawltu chu laia fapa Saphata a ni a;
30. tin, sanghawngsei enkawltu chu Ismael mi Obila a ni a; tin, sabengtun genkawltu chu Meronot mi Jehdeia a ni a;
31. tin, beram rual enkawltu chu Hagar mi Jaziza a ni a. Chung zawng zawngte chu lal Davida sum enkawltute an ni.
32. Tin, Davida pami, mi fing, lehkha thiam tak Jonathana chu remruattu a ni a; tin, Hakmonia fapa Jehiela chu lal fapate zirtirtu a ni a;
33. tin, Ahithophela chu lal remruatpuitu a ni a; tin, Ark mi Husaia chu lal thian a ni a;tin, Ahithophela dawtah chuan Benaia fapa Jehoiada leh Abiathara an ni a; tin, lal sipaio hotu ch Joaba a ni.
34. tin, Ahithophela dawtah chuan Benaia fapa Jehoiada leh Abiathara an ni a; tin, lal sipaio hotu ch Joaba a ni.
