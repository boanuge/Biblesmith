1. Tin, lal Heroda a lal laia Judai rama Bethlehem khuaa Isua a lo pianin khaw chhak lam ata mi fingte Jerusalem khuaah an lo kal a.
2. Khawiah nge Judate lal lo piang chu? Khaw chhak lamah a arsi kan hmu a, amah chibai buk turin kan lo kal a ni, an ti a.
3. Tin, lal Heroda chuan chu chu a hriat veleh a mangang ta a, Jerusalem-a mi zawng zawng pawh an mangang ve a.
4. Tichuan, puithiam lalte leh lehkha ziaktute zawng zawng a ko khawm a. Krista pianna tur chu a zawt a.
5. Tin, anni chuan a hnenah Judai rama Bethlehem khuaah heti hian zawlneiin a ziak kha an ti a.
6. Nang, Bethlehem, Judai rama mi, Judai khawpuite zingah i te ber tawp lo! I chhung atangin awptu a lo chhuak dawn si a, ka mi Israelhote vengtu tur chu, tih hi.
7. Chu mi hnuah chuan Heroda'n a rukin mi fingte chu a ko a, dilchhut takin arsi lan lai chu a zawt chhuak a.
8. Tin, anni chu Bethlehem khuaah a kaltir a. Kal ta u la, naute thu chu dilchhut takin zawt chhuak rawh u; tin, kei pawhin amah chu chibai ka rawn buk ve theihna'n in hmuh veleh mi rawn hrilh ang che u, a ti a.
9. Tin, lal thu chu an hria a, an kal ta a; tin, ngai teh, khaw chhak lama arsi an hmuh kha, naute awmna chung zawn a thlena a din hma loh zawng, an hmaah a kal zel a.
10. Tin, arsi chu an hmuhin an hlim nasa em em a.
11. Tin, in chhungah an va lut a, a nu Mari hnena naute chu an hmu a, an bawkkhup a, chibai an buk a; tin, an ro bawmte an hawng a, a hnenah chuan lawman - rangkachak te, beraw te, murra te - an pe a.
12. Tin, Heroda hnenah kir leh lo turin an manga hrilha an awma vangin, anmahni khaw lamah kawng dangah an haw ta a.
13. Tin, an hawn hnuin ngai teh, Lalpa vantirhkoh Josefa hnenah a mangah a inlar a. Tho la, naute leh a nu hruai la, Aigupta ramah tlan bo rawh, ka hrilh leh hma loh che zawng chutah chuan awm reng ang che; Heroda'n naute tiboral turin a zawng dawn a ni, a ti a.
14. Tichuan, a tho a, a zan a zanin naute leh a nu chu a hruai a, Aigupta ramah a kal ta a.
15. Heroda thih hma loh zawng chutah chuan a awm reng a; Lalpa'n zawlnei kaah, ka fapa chu Aigupta ram atang ka ko chhuak a, tia a sawi kha a lo thlen theihna'n.
16. Chutih lai chuan Heroda'n, mi fingte tihder a nih a inhriat veleh a thinur em em a, mi a tir a, a hun lai tak thu dilchhut taka mi fingte a zawh chhuah ang khan Bethlehem khua leh a vel zawng zawnga mipa naupang kum hnih chin hnuai lam chu an zain a that ta vek a.
17. Chutih lai chuan zawlnei Jeremia sawi kha a lo thleng ta.
18. Rama khuaah thawm an hria, tap te, mitthi ngaia chiau vawng vawng te; Rakili chuan a fate a tah a, an awm tawh loha vangin a thlamuan theih loh.
19. Tichuan, Heroda thih hnuin Lalpa vantirhkoh chu Aigupta rama awm Josefa mangah a inlar a.
20. Tho la, naute leh a nu hruai la, Israel ramah haw rawh: naute thah tumte kha an thi tawh e, a ti a.
21. Tichuan, a tho a, naute leh a nu chu a hruai a, Israel ramah a lut ta a.
22. Nimahsela, Arkeulasa a pa Heroda ai awhin Judai ramah a lal tih a hriatin chuta kal a hlau ta a; tin, a manga hrilha a awm angin Galili lamah a kal ta daih a.Khaw pakhat Nazaret an tihah chuan a va awm ta a: Ani chu 'Nazaret mi' an ti ang, tia, zawlneite sawi kha a lo thlen theihna'n.
23. Khaw pakhat Nazaret an tihah chuan a va awm ta a: Ani chu 'Nazaret mi' an ti ang, tia, zawlneite sawi kha a lo thlen theihna'n.
