1. Tin, heti hi a ni a, kulhte kan siam a kawngkharte ka rel a, kawngkhar vengtute, hla satute, Levi-hote ruat an nih zawh chuan,
2. ka unau Hanania leh kulhbing hotu Hanania chu Jerusalem ka awptir a: mi rinawm tak, mi ahluana Pathian tih mi a ni si a.
3. Jerusalem kulh kawngkharte hi ni a tlangsan fe hma chu hawng suh u, tlaiah, vengtute an tin, hmain, khar se la, kalh vek rawh se. Tin, Jerusalema awmte zinga mi, vengtute ruat u la, an mawna tur ruatah awm se la, athen erawh chuan mahni in bul vel an veng theuh tur a ni.
4. Khua chu a zauin a lian hle a; nimahsela a mi awmte an tlem hle mai a, inte pawh la sak loh a ni si a.
5. Tin, ka Pathianin ka rilruah, mi thate, rorletute, mipuite ko khawm vek a, an thlahtute ang zela chhiar turin mi hriattir a. Tin, atir taka kal chhote thlahtute hming ziakna bu chu ka hmu chhuak a, chutah chuan hetianga ziak ka hmu a:
6. Sala hruai, Babulon lal Nebukadnezaran a hruaite zinga, sala tang chhuak a, Jerusalem leh Judai rama mahni khua theuha haw leh a, chu mi rama awm tate chu hengte hi an ni:

62. Delaia thlah te, Tobia thlah te, Nekoba thlahte 642
63. Tin, puithiamte zinga mi; Hobaia thlahte, Hakoza thlah te, Gilead mi barzilaia fanu zinga mi nupuia nei a, an hming pu ta nghal Barzilaia thlahte chu:
64. chungho chuan an thlahtute ang zela chhiarte zingah chuan anhming ziakna an zawng a, nimahsela an hmu lo a; chuvangin bawlhhlawha ngaihin an awm a, an puithiam nihna chu an ban a.
65. Tin, ram awptu chuan anni chu Urim leh Thumim inbela puithiam a inbela puithiam a din hma loh chuan thil serhte chu an ei tur ani lo tih a hrilh a.
66. Mipui pung kahwm zawng zawng chu 42,360 an ni a,
67. an chhiahhlawh hmeichhia leh mipa 7,337 tiam loin: tin, hla satu hmeichhia leh mipa 245 an ni a.
68. An sakawr chu 736 a ni a; an sabengtung lian chi chu 245 ani a;
69. an sanghawngsei chu 435 ani a, an sabengtung chu 6,720 a ni.
70. Tin, chhungkaw puipa thenkhatten chu mi hna atan chuan thil an thawh a. Ram awptu chuan thawhlawmah rangkachak darik sangkhat, maihun sawmnga, puithiam silhfen zanga leh sawmthum a thawh a.
71. Tin, chhungkaw puipa thenkhahtten in sakna thawhlawmah rangkachak darik singhnih leh tangkarua buk sanghnih leh zahnih an thawh a.
72. Tin, midang zawng zawng pek chu rangkachak darik singhnih leh tangkarua buk sanghnih leh puithiam silhfen sawmruk leh pasarih a ni.Tichuan puithiamte, Levi-hote, kawngkhar vengtute, hla satute leh mi thenkhatte leh Nethin-ho leh Israel mi zawngte chu mahni khua khuaah an awm ta a. Chutichuan thla sarihna a lo thlen chuan Israela thlahte chu an khuaahte chuan an awm a.
73. Tichuan puithiamte, Levi-hote, kawngkhar vengtute, hla satute leh mi thenkhatte leh Nethin-ho leh Israel mi zawngte chu mahni khua khuaah an awm ta a. Chutichuan thla sarihna a lo thlen chuan Israela thlahte chu an khuaahte chuan an awm a.
