1. Tichuan, Joba chuan a chhang a, heti hia a ti a:
2. Chakna nei lo mi chu i va han pui ve le! Thahrui nei lo ban chu i va han chhan ve le!
3. Finna nei lo chu i han zilh fing a, Hriatna tha tak i sawi hnem teh e!
4. Tu hnenah nge thu i chhah chhuak a, Tu thlarau nge i hne ata lo chhuak ni!
5. Thi tawhte an khur a, Tuite leh a chhunga chengte hnuaiah chuan
6. A hmaah chuan Seol chu saruak ani a, Abadon chuan khuhna anei lo.
7. Hmun ruakah chuan hmar a tipharh a, Lei chu engmah loah a khai kang a.
8. A chhum chah tak chuan tuite a fun khawm a, An hnuaia chhum chu a keh lo a.
9. A lalthutthleng hma chu a khuh a, A chunah chhum a zintir a.
10. Tui ramri a siam a, Thim leh eng thlen chinte nen.
11. Van dona bante chu a nghing dur dur a, A zilhhauna chu mak an ti a.
12. A thiltihtheihnain tuipui a tingwai a A hriatthiamnain Rahab chu a tan chhum a.
13. A thawin van chu a tithiang a, A kutin rulpui tlan chak chu a thil tlang a.Ngai teh, he'ng hi a kawng pawn lam lek a ni, A chanchin kan hriat hi a va beithem em! A thiltihtheihna ri thawm chu tu nge hre zo thei ang?
14. Ngai teh, he'ng hi a kawng pawn lam lek a ni, A chanchin kan hriat hi a va beithem em! A thiltihtheihna ri thawm chu tu nge hre zo thei ang?
