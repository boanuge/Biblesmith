1. Putar deuhte hau suh la, pa angin thlem zawk rawh; rawlthar deuhte chu unau angin;
2. pitar deuhte chu nu angin; nula deuhte chu thianghlim takin farnu angin.
3. Hmeithai, hmeithai tak takte chu chawimawi rawh.
4. Nimahsela, hmeithai tupawhin fate emaw, tute emaw nei se la, chungte chuan anmahni in chhung lamah Pathian ngaihsaka awm leh anmahni hringtute chungah that ve thung zir zet rawh se; chu chu Pathian lawmzawng a ni si a.
5. Hmeithai taktak mahni chauha awm chuan Pathian chungah beiseina a nei tlat a, a chhun a zanin dil leh tawngtaiin a awm fo thin a.
6. Amaherawhchu,hmeithai mahni nawmna ngawta inpe chu a dam lai pawhin a thi a ni.
7. Sawiselbova an awm theihna'n chung thu pawh chu pe rawh.
8. Tupawh mahni chhungkhat lainate ngaihsak lo a, mahni chenpui chhungte ngei pawh ngaihsak lo chuan rinna an phatsan a ni, ring lo mi aiin an sual zawk.
9. Hmeithai chu kum sawmruk achin hnuai lama mi ni lo a, mi pakhat nupui ni tawha thil tha tih kawnga mi faka a awm chuan hmeithaia ziak theih a ni -
10. fate lo chawmtlei tawha mikhualte lo thleng tawha mi thianghlimte ke lo sil tawha mi mangangte lo chhawmdawl tawha thil tha tinreng taima taka a lo tih tawh phawt chuan.
11. Hmeithai la nula deuhte erawh chu ziak duh su; An chakna avanga Krista lamah an lo ngheh lohin pasal neih an duh leh mai thin.
12. An thu tiam an bawhchhia a, an thiam lo a ni.
13. Tin, chu lo pawh chu in tinah an leng lawr a, thatchhiat pawh an ching thin a; thatchhiat chang pawh ni loin, thu sawi loh tur sawiin kamtam leh mite thiltih relsak an ching bawk thin a ni.
14. Chutichuan, hmeithai la nula deuhte chuan pasal nei se la, fate nei se la, in chhung khawsak rel se la, sawiselna tur remchang reng hmelma chu hmuhtir suh sela ka duh a ni.
15. Tunah pawh mi thenkhat chuan Setana lam panin an pensan ta si a.
16. Ringtu hmeichhiain hmeithai awmpui nei se la, anni chu amah chuan chhawmdawl mai rawh se, kohhranho chu tihbuaiin awm suh se, hmeithai, belh tur nei lote chu an chhawmdawl theihna'n.
17. Kohhran upa rorel thate chu a leh hniha chawimawi tlaka ngaih ni rawh se, thu hril leh zirtirnaa thawk rimte chu chawimawi tlaka ngaih lehzual ni rawh se.
18. Pathian Lehkha Thu chuan, "Sebawngpain buh a chil laiin a hmui phuar suh ang che," a ti a. "Hnathawktu chu a hlawh hmu tlak a ni," a ti bawk a.
19. Kohhran upa an hekna chu hretu pahnih emaw pathum emaw kaa mi lo chu pawm suh.
20. Thil tisualtute chu mi zawng zawng hmaah zilhhau rawh, mi dangte pawhin an hlauh ve theihna'n.
21. Heng thu hi thleithlak bik nei loin, thlei bik neia engmah ti loin, Pathian leh Krista Isua leh vantirhkoh thlante mithmuhin zawm turin thahnemngai takin ka hriattir a che.
22. Tuma chungah kut nghat thut suh la, mi dang sualnaahte chuan tel hek suh; intithianghlim reng rawh.
23. Tui chauh in mi ni tawh suh la, i pum avang leh i dam loh fo avangin uain tlem te hmang thin ang che.
24. Mi thenkhat sual chu a langsar a, an tuar nghal bawk thin, thenkhat erawh chu a lang lawk lem lo a.Chutiang bawkin thil tha tih lang nghal a awm a, lang nghal rih lo mah se thup hlen theih a ni lo.
25. Chutiang bawkin thil tha tih lang nghal a awm a, lang nghal rih lo mah se thup hlen theih a ni lo.
