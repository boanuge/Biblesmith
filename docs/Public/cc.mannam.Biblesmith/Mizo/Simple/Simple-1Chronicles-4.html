1. Juda fapate chu Pereza te, Hezrona te, Karmia te, Hura te, Sobala te an ni.
2. Tin, Sobala fapa Reaian Jahatha a hring a; tin, Jahathan Ahumaia leh Lahada a hring a, chung chu Zorat-ho hnamte an ni.
3. Tin, Etama pa fapate chu Jezreela te, Isma te, Idbasa te an ni; tin, an farnu hming chu Hazelelponi a ni;
4. tin, Gedora pa Penuela leh Husa pa Ezera an ni. Chungte chu Bethlehema pa, Ephrathi fatir, Hura thlahte an ni.
5. Tin, Tekoa pa Asuran nupui pahnih Heli leh Nari a nei a.
6. Tin, Nari chuan Ahuzama te, Hephera te, Temenia te, Hahastaria te a hrinsak a. Chungte chu Nari fapate an ni.
7. Tin, Heli fapate chu Zeretha te, Izhara te, Ethnana te an ni.
8. Tin, Hakozan Anuba te, Zobeba te, Haruma fapa Aharhel-ho thlahte a hring a.
9. Tin, Jabeza chu a unaute aia mi zahawm a ni. A nu chuan, "Lungngai chunga ka hrin a ni a," tiin a hmingah Jabeza a ti a ni.
10. Tin, Jabeza chuan, "Aw, mal mi sawm ngei ngei la, ka ramri hi mi zauh la, i kut chuan mi awmpuiin lungngaihna ka chungah a thlen lohna'n sual lak ata mi thiar fihlim ang che," tiin Israelte Pathian chu a au a. Tichuan Pathianin a thil dil chu a hlawhtlintir a.
11. Tin, Suha unaupa Keluban Mehira a hring a, chu mi chu Estona pa a ni.
12. Tin, Estona chuan Beth-rapha te, Pasea te, Ir-nahasa pa Tehina te a hring a. Chungte chu Reka-hote an ni.
13. Tin, Kenaza fapate chu Othniela leh Seraia an ni a: tin, Othniela fapa chu Hathatha a ni.
14. Tin, Meonothaian Ophra a hring a; tin, Seraian Geharasim-ho thlahtu Joaba a hring a: chu mite chu kut themthiam mi an ni.
15. Tin, Jephunea fapa Kaleba fapate chu Irua te, Ela te, Naama te an ni a: tin, Ela fapa chu Kenaza a ni.
16. Tin, Jehalela fapate chu Zipa te, Zipha te, Tiria te, Asarela te an ni.
17. Tin, Ezra fapate chu Jethera te, Mereda te, Ephera te, Jalona te an ni; tin, Pharaoa fanu Bithiaii Mereda nupui fapate chu Miriama te, Samaia te, Estemoa pa Isba te an ni.
18. Tin, a nupui Juda hmeichhiain Gedora pa Jereda te, Sokoa pa Hebera te, Zanoa pa Jekuthiela te a hring a.
19. Tin, Nahama farnu Hodia nupuia fapate chu Garm-ho thlahtu Keila pa leh Maakat-ho thlahtu Estemoa an ni.
20. Tin, Simona fapate chu Amnona te, Rina te, Benhanana te, Tilona te an ni. Tin, Isia fapate chu Zohetha leh Ben-zohetha an ni.
21. Juda fapa Sela fapate chu Leka pa Era te, Maresa pa Laada te, Asbea-ho puan zai sin siamtuhote an ni a;
22. tin, Jokima te, Kozeba-ho te, Joasa te, Sarapha, Moab rama rorela awma, chu mi hnua Jasubi-lehem-a awm taa te kha an ni.
23. Chung thu ziak chu hmasanga mi a ni. Chungho chu bel vawtu, Netaim leh Gedera khuaa awm an ni a: lal hnenah a hna thawkin an awm a ni.
24. Simeona fapate chu Nemuela te, Jamina te, Jariba te, Zera te, Saula te an ni;
25. Saula fapa chu Saluma a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Mibsama a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Misma a ni.
26. Tin, Misma fapa chu Hamuela a ni a; chu mi fapa chu Zakura a ni a, chu mi fapa chu Simeia a ni.
27. Tin, Simeia chuan fapa sawm leh paruk leh fanu paruk a nei a; a unaute erawh chuan fa an ngah ve lo a, Juda thlahte angin an thlahte pawh an pung ve lo.
28. Beer-seba-ah te, Molada-ah te, Hazar-sual-ah te an awm a,
29. Bilha-ah te, Ezem-ah te, Tolad-ah te,
30. Bethuel-ah te, Horma-ah te, Ziklag-ah te,
31. Bethmarkaboth-ah te, Hazar-susim-ah te, Beth-biri-ah te, Saraim-ah te pawh. Chungte chu Davida lal thlenga an khawpuite chu a ni.
32. Tin, an khua chu khaw nga, Etam te, Ain te, Rimon te, Token te, Asan te a ni:
33. tin, chu mi vela khaw te zawng zawng baal thlengin a ni. Chungte chu an chenna a ni a, an thlahtute thu ziakna an kawl bawk.
34. Tin, Mesobaba te, Jamleka te, Amazia fapa Josa te,
35. Joela te, Josibia fapa lohua te, (chu mite pa chu Adiela fapa Seraia a ni a)
36. Elioenaia te, Jakoba te, Jesohaia te, Asaia te, Adiela te, Jesi-miela te, Benaia te,
37. Siphia fapa Ziza te, (Chu mite pa chu Allona a ni a, chu mi chu Jedaia fapa ani a, chu mi chu Simria fapa a ni a, chu mi chu Semaia fapa a ni a.)
38. Chung an hming an lamte chu an chhungkaw puipate an ni a; an chhungkua chu nasa takin an lo pung ta a.
39. Tin, Gedor luhnaah, ruam chhak lampang thlengin an ran rualte tlatna tur zawngin an kal a.
40. Tin, ran tlatna hnim tui tak tak leh tha tak tak an hmu a, a ram chu a zauin a reh a, ralmuanawm tak a ni; a hmaa lo awmte chu Hama thalhte an ni a.
41. Tichuan chung an hming an ziakate chu Judai ram lal Hezekia lal lai khan an lut a, an puan inte leh chutih laia Meunim-ho lo awm te chu an tiboral vek a, tun thlengin; an hmun chu an luahsak a; an ran rual tan tlatna a awm avangin.
42. Tin, thenkhat Simeona fapateho, mi zanga lai Seir tlangah an kal a, an hotu atan Isia fapa Pelatia te, Nearia te, Rephaia te, Uziela te an hmang a.Tichuan Amalek-ho hnam tlan bo la awmte kha an hneh a, tun thlengin chutah chuan an awm ta a.
43. Tichuan Amalek-ho hnam tlan bo la awmte kha an hneh a, tun thlengin chutah chuan an awm ta a.
