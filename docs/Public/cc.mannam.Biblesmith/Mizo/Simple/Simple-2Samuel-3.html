1. Saula chhungte leh Davida chhungte chu rei tak an indo a; Davida chu a lo chak tial tial a, Saula chhungte erawh chu an chak lo tial tial a.
2. Tin, Davida chuan Hebron khuaah chuan fapate anei a: a fa hmasa ber chu Amnona, Jezreel mi Ahinoami laka mi ani a;
3. a dawttu chu Kileaba, Karmel mi Nabala nupi Abigaili laka mi a ni; tin, a pathumna chu Absaloma, Gesur lal Tamaia fanu Maaki fapa a ni a;
4. tin, a palina chu Adonija, Jagithi fapa a ni a; a pangana chu Sephatia, Aitali fapa a ni a;
5. tin, a parukna chu Ithreama, Davida nupui Ekli laka mi a ni a. Hengte hi Davida fa Hebron-a piangte chu an ni.
6. Tin, heti hi a ni a, Saula chhungte leh Davida chhungte an indo chhung chuan Abnera chu Saula chhungte zingah chuan a intichak deuh deu a.
7. Saula chuan hmei a nei a, a hming chu Rizpi a ni a, Aii fanu a ni. Tin, Is-bosetha chuan Abnera hnenah, "Engati nge ka pa hmeinu hnenah i ka;?" a ti a.
8. Chutichuan Is-bosetha thusawi chuan Abnera chu a tithinur ta hle mai a, "Juda-ho uicho lu mai em ka nih ni? Vawiin thlengin i pa Saula chhungte leh a unaute leh a thiante chungah ngilneihna ka tilang a, Davida kutah ka hlan lo chia; nimahsela nangin he hmeichhe chunga sualah hian mi puh ta mai a.
9. , 10 Saula chhungte lak ata ram chu laksaka Israel leh Judate chungah Dan atanga Beer-seba thlenga Davida lalthutthleng tungding tura LALPA'n Davida hnena chhia a chham anga ka tih loh chuan Pathianin Abnera chu a tih tihin ti se la, chu aia nasa pawhin," a ti a.
10. , 9 Saula chhungte lak ata ram chu laksaka Israel leh Judate chungah Dan atanga Beer-seba thlenga Davida lalthutthleng tungding tura LALPA'n Davida hnena chhia a chham anga ka tih loh chuan Pathianin Abnera chu a tih tihin ti se la, chu aia nasa pawhin," a ti a.
11. Tichuan Abnera chu a hlauh avangin a chhan reng reng a chhang ngam ta lo a.
12. Tin, Abnera chuan a aiah Davida hnenah, "He ram hi tu ta nge ni?" tiin mi a tir a; tin, "Ka hnenah thu thlung rawh, tichuan ngai teh, Israel mi zawng zawngte i hnen lam tang turin ka kut chu i lamah a tang ang," tiin a sawi bawk a.
13. Tin, ani chuan, "Aw le; i hnenah chuan thu ka thlung ang e; amaherawhchu i lakah thil pakhat ka phut dawn a ni, chu chu hei hi a ni, mi hmu tura i lo kal hunah Saula fanu Mikali i rawn hruai phawt loh chuan ka hmel i hmu thei lo ang," a ti a.
14. Tin, Davida chuan Saula fapa Is-bosetha hnenah chuan, "Philistia mite serh vun zakhata ka lei ka nupui Mikali chu mi rawn pe rawh," tiin mi a tir a.
15. Tin, Is-bosetha chuan mi a tir a, a pasal Laisa fapa Paltiela ngei hnen ata chu an hruai ta a.
16. Tin, a pasal chu ahnenah a kal a, tap chung zelin a kal a, Bahurim thlengin a zui a. Chutichuan Abneran a hnenah, "Kal rawh, kir leh rawh," a ti a; tin, a kir leh ta a.
17. Chutichuan Abnera chuan Israel mite upaho chu a bia a, "Tun hma khan Davida chu in lakah in duh thin a;
18. tunah hian siam rawh u khai; LALPA'n Davida chungchang thua chuan, "Ka chhiahhlawh Davida kutin ka mi Israelte chu Philistia mite kut ata leh an hmelmate zawng zawng kut ata ka chhanchhuak ang,' tiin a sawi e," tiin.
19. tin, Abnera chuan Benjamin mite beng hriatah a sawi bawk a; tin, Abnera chu Israel mite leh Benjamin chhung zawng zawngten tha an tih ang apiang Hebron-a Davida benghriatah sawi turin a kal bawk a.
20. Chutichuan Abnera chu Hebron-a Davida hnenah mi sawmhnih nen an kal a. Tin, Davida chuan Abnera leh a hnena mi sawmhnihte tan ruai a theh a.
21. Abnera chuan Davida hnenah, "Ka tho ang a, i hnena thu an thlun theihna'n leh i duh thusam ang taka ro i rel theihna'n Israel mite zawng zawng ka lalpa hnena kaikhawm turin ka kal tang e," a ti a.
22. Tin, ngai teh, Davida mite leh Joaba rammu chu an lo haw a, rallak sum tam tak an rawn hawn a; nimahsela Abnera chu Hebron-a Davida hnena chuan a lo awm tawh lo; a lo kaltir tawh si a, ani pawh thlamuang takin a kal ta a ni.
23. Joaba leh a hnena mipui zawng zawng an lo thlen chuan, "Nera fapa Abnera chu lal hnenah a lo kal a, ani chuan a kaltir leh ta a, thlamuang takin a kal ta," tiin Joaba chu an hrilh a.
24. Chutichuan Joaba chu lal hnena a kal a, "I thil lo tih hi engtizia nge ni lle? Ngai teh, Abnera chu i hnenah a lo kal a, engati nge i kal botir a, a kal tawh daih mai le?
25. Nera fapa Abnera chu i hria a, nangmah tihder turin, i chhuak lam leh i lut lam leh i thiltih apiang hriat tumin a lo kal a ni," a ti a.
26. Tin, Davida hnen ata Joaba a lo chhuah chuan Abnera um turin mi a ti ra, Sira tuichunchhuah atangin an rawn hruai kir a; nimahsela Davida chuan a hre lo.
27. Tin, Abneran Hebron a lo thlen chuan Joaba chuan kulh kawngkharah biak ruk turin a hruai hrang a, a unau Asahela thisen chuak thungrulhna'n a puma a vit hlum ta a.
28. Tin, nakina Davida'n a han hriat chan, "Nera fapa Abnera thisen chhuah thuah hian kei leh ka ram chu LALPA mithmuhin kan thiang kumkhua ang a:
29. Joaba lu chunga tla se la, a pa chhungte zawng zawng chungah nen; Joaba chhungte zingah chuan thi pur emaw, phar emaw, tiang rinchhana awm mi emaw, khandaiha thi emaw, tam emaw lo awm loin awm suh se," a ti a.
30. Chutichuan an unaupa Asahela chu Gibeon-a indonaah an thah avangin Joaba leha unau Abisaia chuan Abnera chu an that ta a.
31. in, Davida chuan Joaba le a hnena mipui zawng zawngte hnenah chuan, "In puante chu pawtthler u la, saiip puan sin u la, Abnera chu sun rawh u," a ti a. Tin, Davida chuan hlang chu a zui a.
32. TinAbnera chu Hebron-ah chuan an phum a: lal chuan a awm a chhuah a, Abnera thlanah chuan a tap ta a; mipui zawng zawng pawh an tap a.
33. Tin, lal chuan Abnera chu a tah a, "Abnera chu mi a thihin a thi dawn em ni?
34. I kut chu phuar ani lo a, i ke chu kawl bun ani hek lo, Mi suaksual fate hmaa mi a tlu anga i tluk tak mai le?" a ti a. Tin, mi zawng zawng chuan an tah leh a.
35. Tichuan chhun a la nih laiin Davida chu chaw eitir turin mi zawng zawng chu an lo kal a; nimahsela Davida chuan, "Ni tlak hmaa chaw emaw, thil dang emaw pawh a eia ka ei hlek chuan Pathianin a tih tihin mi ti rawh se: chu aia nasa pawhin" tiin chhia a chham a.
36. Chu chu mi zawng zawngin an hriatin an lawm em em a: lal thiltih apiang chuan mipui zawng zawngte chu a tilawm zel a.
37. Chutichuan mipui zawng zawng leh Israel mi zawng zawng chuan chu mi ni chuan Nera fapa Abnera thah chu lal thu ani lo tih an lo hre ta a.
38. Tin, lal chuan a mite hnenah chuan, "Vawiin hian Israel mi lian, mi ropui tak a thi tih in hre na nge?Lal atana hriak thih ni mah i la, vawiinah hian ka chak lo a ni; tin, he'ng mi, Zeruia fapate hi ka tan tuar an har lutuk a ni: LALPA'n thil tha lo titua chu a sualzia ang zelin thungrul rawh se," a ti a .
39. Lal atana hriak thih ni mah i la, vawiinah hian ka chak lo a ni; tin, he'ng mi, Zeruia fapate hi ka tan tuar an har lutuk a ni: LALPA'n thil tha lo titua chu a sualzia ang zelin thungrul rawh se," a ti a .
