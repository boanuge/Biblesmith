1. Tin, Davida chu a dam reiin a lo upa ta hle a; tichuan a fapa Solomona chu Israelte lalah a siam a.
2. Tin, Israel-ho puipa zawng zawngte chu puithiamte leh Levi-hote nen a ko khawm a.
3. Tin, Levi-hote chu kum sawmthum achin chng lam chiar an ni a; an tam lam chu patling singthum leh sangriat an ni.
4. Chung zinga sing hnih leh sangli chu LALPA in satuho hotu tur an ni a; tin, sangrukte chu hotute leh roreltu turte an ni a;
5. tin, sangli te chu kawngkhar vengtu turte an ni a, tin, sanglite chu, "Fakna tura thil tumrik chi ka siam," Davida'n a tihte chu hmanga LALPA faktu turte an ni.
6. Tin, Levia fa Gersona te, Kohatha te, Meraria te zatin Davida'n pawl a siam a,
7. Gerson mite chu Ladana leh Simeia an ni.
8. Laldana fate chu puipa ber Jehiela te, Zethama te, Joela te, pathuam an ni.
9. Simeia fate chu Selomotha te, Haziela te Harana te, pathum an ni. Hengte hi Ladana thlah chhungkaw puipate chu an ni.
10. Tin, Simeia fate chu Jahatha te, Zina te, Jeusa te, Beria te an ni . Heng palite hi Simeia fate chu an ni.
11. Tin, Jahatha chu a upa ber a ni a, Ziza chu a chhang a ni; Jeusa leh Beria erawh chuan fa an ngah lo a; chuvangin chhung khat anga chhiar an ni.
12. Kohatha fate chu Amrama te, Izhara te, Hebrona te, Uziela te, pali an ni.
13. Amrama fate chu Arona leh Mosia an ni; Arona chu amah leh athlahte chu kumkhua atana thil thianghlim takte tithianghlim a, kumkhuaa LALPA hmaa rimtui hal a, a rawng bawl a, a hming malsawmtu tura tihhran an ni.
14. Pathian mi Mosia erawh chu , a fate chu Levi hnam zinga chhiar an ni.
15. Mosia fate chu Gersoma leh Eliezera an ni.
16. Gersoma fatea upa ber chu Sebuela a ni.
17. Tin, Eliezera fatea upa ber chu Rehabia a ni. Tin, Eliezera chua nfapa dang reng reng anei lo ; Rehabia erawh chuan fapa tam tak anei.
18. Izhara fatea upa ber chu Selomitha a ni.
19. Hebrona fa tea upa ber chu Jeria a ni a, a chhang chu Amaria, a chhang leh chu Jahaziela, a chhang leh chu Jekameama a ni.
20. Uziela fatea pa zawk chu Mika a ni a, a chhang chu Isia a ni.
21. Meraria fate chu Mahlia leh Musia an ni. Mahlia fate chu Elazara leh Kisa an ni.
22. Tin, Elazara chu a thi a, fapate nei loin fanute chau a nei a; tin, Kisa fapa an unaute nupiah an nei a.
23. Musia fate chu Mahlia te, Edera te, Jeremotha te, pathuam an ni.
24. Chungte chu Levia thlah, an chhungkua an gzela hming ziaka an chhiar chhngkaw puipa, LALPA in saka tawhk, kums awmhnih achin chung lama chhiar zawngte chu an ni.
25. Davida'n, "LALPA, Israelte Pathianin a mite chu ralmuan takin a awmtir ta a, Jerusalem -ah hian a awm ta reng a,
26. Levi-hote pawh hian biak buk leh a rawngbawlna'n bungbel zawng zawngte hi an zawn a ngai tawh lo e," a ti a ni.
27. Davida thupek hnuhnugn avangin Levi-hote hchu kum sawm hnih achin chunglam thlengin chhiar an ni si a.
28. A hnathawh chu Pathian ina rawngbawla Arona fapate tanpuia kawt hung hchung leh pindan chhunga thil thianghlim zawng zawng tihthianghlim a, LALPA ina rawngbawl a ni si a;
29. chhang chiah tur te, changphut thilhlan atana changphut dip ta, dawi dim telh loh chhangper pan emaw, bela ur emaw, kan emaw, a teh leh a lenzia ang zawng anwg tur buatsaih velte nen;
30. tin, tuk tina LALPA hnena lawm thu sawileh amah fakte nen, tlaiah pawh chuti bawk;
31. tin, chawlhniah te, thla tharah te, kut bi neiah te, a tih tura ruat ang zela LALPA hnena halral thilhlan zawng zawng LALPA hmaa hlan rengte nen;tin, inbiakna puan in enkawl te, hmun thianghlim enkawl te, LALPA in arawngbawl a an unau Arona fapaten tih tur an ruat apiang tihte nen.
32. tin, inbiakna puan in enkawl te, hmun thianghlim enkawl te, LALPA in arawngbawl a an unau Arona fapaten tih tur an ruat apiang tihte nen.
