1. Israel lal Joahaza fapa Joasa kum hnih a lal kum chan Juda lal Joasa fapa Amazia a lal tan a.
2. A lal tantirh chuan kum sawmhnih leh kum nga mi a ni a, Jerusalem-ah kum kum sawmhnih leh kum kuaa lal a; anu hming chu Jehoadini, Jerusalem mi a ni.
3. Tin, LALPA mithmuha thil fel tak a ti thin a.
4. Amaherawhchu hmun sangte zawng thehthang bo ani chuang lo; hmun sangahte chuan mite chu an la inthawiin, rimti an la hal reng thin.
5. Tin, a ram chu a han din ngheh veleh heti hi a ni a, ami te, a pa, lal thattute chu a that ve thung a;
6. amaherawchu chung tualthattu fate chu a that lo; Mosia dan bua, "Fate avangin pate tihhlum tur a ni lo a, pate avangin fate tihhlum tur a ni hek lo, mi tin, mahni sual avangin an thi tur a ni," tia LALPA thupek dan ziak ang khan.
7. Edom-ho chu Chi ruamah singkhat a that a, Sela khua a bei a, a la a, a hmingah chuan Joktheel a ti a, tun thlengin an la ti a ni.
8. Tichuan Amazian Israel lal Jehua fapa Jeoahaza fa Jehoasa hnenah, "Hawh teh, hmai chhanah i lo inen teh ang," tiin mi a tir a.
9. Tin, Israel lal Jehoasa chuan Juda lal Amazia hnenah, "Lebanon tlanga hlingin Lebanon tlanga sidar hnenah, 'I fanu kha ka fapa nupuiah neihtir te khai,' tiin a chah a; tin, Lebanon tlanga ramsa a lo kal a, hling chu a rawn chil thlu ta der mai a.
10. Nang hian Edom-ho chu i hneh ngei tak a, i rilru a lo chapo ta a nih h; i chapo kha tawngpui hle hle mai rawh; nang leh Juda ram chawp chawp achhiatna, i tana tha lo tur thl eng atan gne i cho chhuah dawn ni?" tiin mi a tir ve a.
11. Nimahsela Amazia chuan a lo ngaithla duh lo a. Chutichuan Israel lal Jeoasa hua han chho a; Juda lal Amazia nen chuan Juda ram Beth-semes khuaah hmai chhanah an inen ta ngei a.
12. Tin, Juda chu Israelte lakah a lo tlawm ta a; mi tin, mahni in lamah an tlan haw ta theuh a.
13. Tin, Israel lal Jehoasa chuan Juda lal Ahazia fapa Jeoasa fa Amazia chu Beth-semes-ah chuan a man a, Jerusalem khuaah a kal a, Jerusalem kulh chu Ephraim kulh kawngka atanga kulh kil kawngka thlengin tawng zali zet a tichim a.
14. Tin, LALPA ina mi leh lal in a ro dah khawmnaa mi, rangkachak leh tangka leh bungbel zawng zawng, a awm apiang a la a, hrenga tang turte nen, tin, Samari khaw lamah a haw leh ta a.
15. Tichuan Jehoasa thiltih chanchin dang te, a thiltihtheihzia te, Juda lal Amazia a bei thut te chu Israel lalte lal lai thu ziakna bua a chuang a ni lo'm ni?
16. Tin, Jehoasa chu a pi leh pute hnenah a muhil a, Samari khuaan Israel lalte zingah an phum a; tin, a fapa Jeroboama chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
17. Tin, Juda lal Joasa fapa Amazia chu Israelte lal Jeoahaza fapa Jeoasa thih hnuin kum sawm leh kum nga a la dam a.
18. Tichuan Amazia chanchin dangte chu Juda a ni lo'm ni?
19. Tin, Jerusalem khuaah amah an phiar ru a; tihuan Lakis khuaah a tlan a; nimahsela Lakis khuaah chuan mi an umtir a, hutah chuan an that ta a,
20. Tin, a ruang chu sakawrin an kalpui a, a pi leh pute zingan Davida khaw pui Jerusalem-ah an phum ta a.
21. Tin, Juda mi zawng zawngten kum sawm leh kum ruk mi, Azaria an hruai a, a pa Amazia aiah lalah an siam ta a.
22. Ani huan Elath khua hu a siam tha leh a, Juda ramah a telh leh a, chumi hnuah chuan lal chu api leh pute hnenah a muhil ta a.
23. Juda lal Joasa fapa Amazia kum sawm leh kum nga a lal kum chuan Israel lal Joasa fapa Jeroboama chu Samari khuaan a lal ta a, ku sawli leh kum khat a lal a.
24. Tin, LALPA mithmuha thil tha lo tak a ti thin a; Israelte thil tihsualtirtu Nebata fapa Jeroboama sualna zawng zawngte kha kalsan reng a nei lo a.
25. LALPA, Israelte Pathianin a chhiahhlawh, Gath-hepher mi Amittaia fapa zawlnei Jona hmnaga a thu sawi ang khan, Hamath luhna atanga Araba tuifinriat thelngin Israel ramah a telh leh a.
26. Israelte hreawmzia chu a nasa em em mai tih a hre si a; khuahkhirh leh khirh lohte pawh an awm si lo a, Israelte tanpuitu tur tumah an awm hek lo.
27. LALPA'n Israelte hming hi van hnuaiah hian a tichimit hlauh dawn tih zawng a sawi lo reng a; Joasa fapa Jeroboama hmngin a chhandam ta zawk a ni.
28. Tichuan Jeroboama chanchin dang te, a thiltih zawng zawng te, a thiltihtheihzia te, a ral bei thu te, tun hmaa Juda ram chan ni thin Damaska leh Hamath khua Israelte tan alo laksak leh tak thute chu Israel lalte lal lai thu ziakna buah a chuang a ni lo'm ni?Tin, Jeroboama chu api leh pu, Israel lalte hnenah chuan a muhil ve a; tichuan a fapa Zekaria chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
29. Tin, Jeroboama chu api leh pu, Israel lalte hnenah chuan a muhil ve a; tichuan a fapa Zekaria chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
