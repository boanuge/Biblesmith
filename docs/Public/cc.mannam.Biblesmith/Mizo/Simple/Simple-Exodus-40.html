1. ,2 Tin, LALPA'n Mosia hnenah, "a thla khatna ni khat niah chuan inbiakna puan in biak buk chu i din tur a ni.
2. ,1 Tin, LALPA'n Mosia hnenah, "a thla khatna ni khat niah chuan inbiakna puan in biak buk chu i din tur a ni.
3. Tin, a chauh chuan thuhriattirna bawm i dah tur a ni a, tin, bawm chu puanzarin i daidan tur a ni.
4. Tin, dawhkan kha la lutin, a chua thil awmte chu i dah fel tur a ni; khawnvar dahna kha la lutin, a khawnvarte chu i dah tur a ni.
5. Tin, hal tur rimtui halna rangkachak maicham kha thuhriattirna bawm hmaa dahin, bia buk kawngka puanzar kha i zar tur a ni.
6. Tin, halrala thilhlanna maicham kha inbiakna puan in biak buk kawngka bulah i chhung tur a ni.
7. Tin, inbiakna puan in leh maicham inkarah chuan maihun chhungin, chutah chuan tui i dah tur a ni.
8. Tin, biak buk vel chuan a hung chhung kawtlai puanzar chu i zar tur a ni.
9. Tin, hriak thih mi lain, biak buk leh a chhunga awm zawng zawngte chu hnawihin, i tithianghlim tur a ni, a bungbel zawng zawngte nen: tichuan a lo thianghlim ang.
10. Tin, halrala thilhlanna maicham leh a hmanrua zawng zawngte chu hriak hnawihin, maicham chu i tithianghlim tur a ni: tichuan maicham chu thianghlim tak a lo ni ang.
11. Tin, maihun leh a hun chhan chu hriak hnawihin i tithianghlim tur a ni.
12. Tin, Arona leh a fapate chu inbiakna puan in kawngka bula rawn hruaiin tuiin i sil tur a ni.
13. Arona chu silhfen thianghlimte chu i sintir tur a ni. Tichuan puithiam hna thawka ka tan rawng a bawl theina tura hriak thihin i tithianghlim tur a ni.
14. Tin, a fapate pawh rawn hruaiin kawr i haktir tur a ni:
15. tichuan puithiam hna thawka ka tan rawng an bawl theihna turin an pa hriak i thih ang khan anmahni chu hriak i thih tur a ni: hriak thiha an awm hi an thlahte thleng pawha kumkhuaa puithiam tura ruat an ni reng tawh ang tih an tan chhinchhiahna ani ang," tiin a sawi a.
16. Chutiang chan Mosia'n a ti a: LALPA'n tih tura thu a pek ang zawng zawng chuan a ti vek a.
17. Tin, heti hi a ni a, a kum hnihna thla tir ber, chu mi thla ni khat ni tak chuan biak buk chu an lo din ta a.
18. Tin, Mosia chuan biak buk chu a han din a, a nghah chhan kerkuakte a han rem a, a ban remkhawmte chu a han din a, a nghahchhan kerkuakte a han rem a, a ban remkhawmte chu a han din a, a bangrelte chu a han rawlh a, a bante chu a han tung a.
19. Tin, biak buk chauh chuan puan in a zar a, chu mi puan in chu chu a khuhnain a khu leh a; LALPA'n Mosia thu a pek ang khan.
20. Tin, thuhriattirna chu alaa, bawm chhungah a dah a, bawmah chuan a zawnfungte a rawlh a, bawm chhunga chuan zahngaihna thuphah a dah a:
21. tin, bawm chu biak buk chhungah chuan a dah a, a daidanna puanzar chu a zar a, thuhriattirna bawm chu a daidang ta a; LALPA'n Mosia thu a pek ang khan.
22. Tin, dawhkan chu inbiakna puan in chu, biak buk hmar lampang, puan zar pawn lamah chuan a chhung a,
23. Tin, dawhkanah chuan LALPA hmaah chhang chu a dah fel diam a; LALPA'n Mosia thu a pek ang khan.
24. Tin, khawnvar dahna chu inbiakna puan in chu biak buk chim lampang dawhkan epah chuan a dah a.
25. Tin, khawnvarte chu LALPA hmaah chuan a dah a; LALPA'n Mosia thu apek ang khan.
26. Tin, rangkachak maicham chu inbiakna puan in chu puanzar hmaa chuan a dah a:
27. tin, a chhungah chuan englo rimtui tak hal tur rimtui a hal a; LALPA'n Mosia thu a pek ang khan.
28. Tin, biak buk kawngka puanzar a zar a.
29. Tin, inbiakna puan in biak buk kawngka bulah chuan halrala thilhlanna maicham a chhung a, a chhungah chuan halral thilhlan leh ei tur thilhlan a hlan a; LALPA'n Mosia thu a pek ang khan.
30. Tin, maihun chu inbiakna puan in leh maicham inkarah chuan a chhung a, insilna turin tui a chhung a.
31. Tin, Mosia leh Arona leha fapate chuan chutah chuan an kut leh an kete an sil a,
32. inbiakna puan ina an luh dawn leh, maicham bula an kal dawnin an insil a ni: LALPA'n Mosia thu a pek ang khan
33. Tin, biak buk leh maicham vel chuan hung chhung kawtlai chu a siam a, a hung chhung kawtlai kawngka puanzar chu a zar a. Chutichuan Mosia'n hna chu a zo ta a.
34. Tichuan inbiakna puan in chu chhumin a rawn tuam ta mup a, biak buk chu LALPA ropuinain a lo khat ta a.
35. Tin, chutia chhum a lo zin tak mup a, LALPA ropuinaa biak buk chu a lo khah tak avangin Mosia chu inbiakna puan in chhungah chuan a lut thei ta lo a.
36. Tin, chhum chuan biak buk chu a kian chhohsan veleh Israela thlahte chu an insawn zel a, an kalna tur lama an kal chhung zawng chuan
37. chhum chuan a kian chhohsan loh erawh chuan a kian chhohsan hma loh zawng insawn loin an awm reng thin.Chhunah chuan LALPA chhum chu biak buk chungah a zing a, chutah chuan zanah mei a lo awm thin a. Israel-ho zawng zawngte chuan an kalna tur lama an kal chung zawng chuan chu chu an hmu zel thin a ni.
38. Chhunah chuan LALPA chhum chu biak buk chungah a zing a, chutah chuan zanah mei a lo awm thin a. Israel-ho zawng zawngte chuan an kalna tur lama an kal chung zawng chuan chu chu an hmu zel thin a ni.
