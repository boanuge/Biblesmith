1. Aw LALPA hnenah lawm thi hril u la, a hming lam rawh u; Mi tin, zingah chuan a thiltihte chu hriattir rawh u.
2. Amah chawimawiin hla sa u la, amah fakin zai rawh u; A thilmak tih zawng zawng thu chu titi rawh u.
3. A hming thianghlim chu chuang rawh u. LALPA zawngtute thinlung chi hlim rawh se.
4. LALPA leh a chakna chu zawng u la; A hmel chu zawng fo rawh u.
5. ,6 Aw, a chhiahhlawh Abrahama thlahte u, Jakoba thlah, a thlante u, A thilmak tihte hre reng rawh u; A thilmakte leh a kaa a rorel chuakte chu.
6. ,5 Aw, a chhiahhlawh Abrahama thlahte u, Jakoba thlah, a thlante u, A thilmak tihte hre reng rawh u; A thilmakte leh a kaa a rorel chuakte chu.
7. Ani chu LALPA kan Pathian chu a ni a: A rorelte chu lei chung zawng zawngah hian a awm.
8. A thuthlun chu kumkhuain a hre thin a, Chhuan sangkhat thlenga a thu lo pek chu;
9. Abrahama hnena a thuthlun leh, Isaaka hnena a chhechham chu;
10. Chu vek chu thuriat atan Jakoba hnenah a tinghet a, Israela hnena kumkhaw thungthlung atan:
11. "Kanaan ram hi ka pe ang che, I ro chanpual tur chu," tiin:
12. Mi tlemte chauh an nih lai kha; A ni, tlemte mai, chu mi ramah chuan mikhual an ni nghal si a:
13. Hnam hrang hrang hnenah an kal lawr an kal lawr a. Ram hrang hang atangin mi hrang hrangte hnenah
14. Tuma hnehchhiah a phal lo va: A ni, anmahni avangin lalte pawh a zilhhau a;
15. "Ka hriak thihate chu tawk suh u la, Ka zawlneite pawi reng reng pawh khawih suh u," tiin.
16. Chu mi rama tla turin tam a ko a; Ei tur reng reng a awm lohtir ta a.
17. An hmaah mi a tir a; Josefa chu chhiahhlawh atan hralhin a awm a:
18. A kete chu kawlin a tina a; Thir khaidiat bunin a awm ta a:
19. A thu a thlen h ma loh chuan; LALPA thuin amah chu a fiah thin a.
20. Lalber chuan mi a tir a, a phelh a, A chauh ta a, mi tin, roreltu ngei chuan.
21. A chhungte chunga hotuah leh, A sum zawng zawng chunga roreltuah a siam a:
22. A mi liante chu a duh duha phur tur leh, A upaho tifing turin.
23. Israela pawh chu Aiguipta ramah a kal a; Jakoba chu Hama ramah chuan a awm ta rih a.
24. A mite chu nasa takin a tipung a, Anmahni dotute aiin a tichak ta zawk a.
25. A mite haw turin an thinlung a lamlettir a, A chhiahhlawhte chunga vervek taka ti turin.
26. A chhiahhlawh Mosia chu a tir a, A mi thlan Arona nen.
27. Anni chuan a chhinchhiahnate chu an zingah an entir a, Hama ramah chuan thilmakte an entir a.
28. Ani chuan thim a tir a, a tithim ta a; Tichuan a thute chu an hnial ta lo a.
29. An tuite chu thisenah a chantir a, An sanghate chu a tihlum ta a.
30. An ram chu chunguin a khat ta a, An lalte pindanah lam pawh.
31. Ani chuan thu a sawi a, tichuan tho tam tak a lo kal a, Hrik pawh an ram chhung zawng zawngah a lo kal bawk a.
32. An hnenah ruah aiah rial a pe a, An ramah chuan mei alh zawk zawk a pe ta bawk a.
33. An grep hruite leh an theipui kungte a tithi a; An rama thingte chu a titliak ta bawk a.
34. Ani chuan thu a sawi a, tichuan khaukhuapte an lo kal a, Pangangte nen, chhiar sen rual loh mai hian.
35. Chunge chuan an rama thlai tinrenget chu an ei zoa, An lei rahte pawh an ei zo ta bawk a.
36. An rama fa tir awm zawng zawng a tihlum a, An chakna zawng zawng rah hmasa berte chu.
37. Tichuan, tangka leh rangkachak nen a hruai chhuak ta a: Anma hnamte zingah chuan mi chak lo pakhat mah an awm lo.
38. An chhuah lai chuan Aigupta-ho chu an lawm a: Anni chu an lo hlauh tak avangin.
39. Ani chuan hliahtu atan chhum a zamtir a, Zana en turin mei a pe bawk a.
40. An dil a, tichuan vahmim a rawn hruai a, Van chawin anmahni a titlai ta a.
41. Lungpui chu a hawng a, tichuan tui a lo chhuak dur dur a, Hmun rovahte chuan lui ang mai hian a luang ta a.
42. A thu thianghlim kha a hre reng si a, A chhiahhlawh Abrahama nen khan.
43. Tichuan, a mite chu hlim takin a hruai chhuak ta a, A mi thlante chu hla sa chung zelin.
44. Tin, hnam tin ramte chu a peksak a; Anni chuan mi tin, thawh chhuak sa chu an luah ta a;A thuruatte chu an zawm theih a, A dante chu an vawn that theihna'n. LALPA chu fak rawh u.
45. A thuruatte chu an zawm theih a, A dante chu an vawn that theihna'n. LALPA chu fak rawh u.
