1. Tin, heti hi a lo ni a, chumi kum vek, Juda lal Zedekia lal tantirh lam, a kum ina, thla nganaah chuan zawlnei Hanania, Azura fapa, Gibeon mi chuan puithiamte leh mipui zawng zawng hnenah, LALPA inah chuan, mi bia a, ka hnenah,
2. Sipaihote LALPA, Israel Pathian chuan heti hian a ti: Babulon lal nghawngkawl chu ka titliak ta.
3. Kum hnih pumhlum chhungin LALPA in bungbel Babulon lal Nebukadnezzaran hemi hmun ata a laka, Babulon thlenga a kalpui zawng zawng kha, he hmun ngeiah hian ka rawn thlen leh vek ang.
4. He hmunah hian Juda lal Jehoiakima fapa, Jekonioa leh Babulona kal Juda sal zawng zawng chu ka hruai kir leh ang; Babulon lal nghawngkawl ka titliak dawn si a, tih hi LALPA thu chhuak a ni,' tiin.
5. Tin, zawlnei Jeremia chuan puithiamte leh LALPA ina ding mipui zawng zawng hmaah chuan zawlnei Hanania hnenah,
6. zawlnei Jeremia ngei chuan, "Amen, LALPA chuan chutiangin ti tak rawh se; LALPA ina bungbelte leh sal tannaa mi zawng zawng babulon ata hemi hmuna rawn thlen tura i sawi chu LALPA chuan ti ngei rawh se.
7. Amaherawhchu mipui zawng zawng leh nangma benga ka thu sawi hi ngaithla rawh khai.
8. Keimah leh nangma hmaah hmasang zawlneite chuan ram tam takte chungah leh lalna ram ropui takte chungah indona te, thil tha lo te, leh hripui thute an sawi thin a.
9. zawlnei, muanna thu sawitu thu sawi chu a lo thlen hunah, chu zawlnei chu LALPA chuan a tir ngei a ni tih a hriat mai ang chu," a ti a.
10. Tin, zawlnei Hanania chuan nghawngkawl, zawlnei Jeremian a bah chu a la a, a titliak a.
11. Tin, mipui zawng zawng hmaah Hanania chuan, "Heti hian LALPA chuan a ti: Hetiang ngei hian Babulon lal Nebukadnezzara nghawngkawl chu kum hnih pumhlum chhung ngeiin hnamte zawng zawng nghawng ata ka titliak ang," a ti a. Tin, zawlnei Jeremia chuan a kalsan ta a.
12. Tin, zawlnei Hanania chuan zawlnei Jeremia nghawng ata nghawngkawl a tihtliah hnuah chuan Jeremia hnenah LALPA thu a lo thleng a,
13. "Kal la Heti hian LALPA chuan a ti. Thing nghawngkawl chu i titliak a, a aiah thir nghawngkawl i siamsak zawk a nih hi.
14. Heti hian Israel Pathian, Sipaihote LALPA chuan a ti si a: Babulon lal Nebukadnezzara rawng an bawl theihna'n he'ng hnam zawng zawng hi thir nghawngkawl bahtir a ni: ama rawng an bawl ang: ramhnuaia ramsate pawh amah ka pe bawk a ni, tiin.
15. Tin, zawlnei Jeremia chuan zawlnei Hanania hnenah, "Ngai rawh, Hana ni, LALPA'n a tir awzawng lo che: Heng mite hi dawt i rintir mai a nih zawk hi.
16. chuvangin heti hian LALPA chuan a ti: Ngai teh, lei chung ata hi ka tibo daih ang che: LALPA chunga helna thu i sawi avangin kumin ngeiah hian i thi ang," a ti a.Tichuan chumi ku vek, thla sarihnaah chuan zawlnei Hanania chu a thi ta a.
17. Tichuan chumi ku vek, thla sarihnaah chuan zawlnei Hanania chu a thi ta a.
