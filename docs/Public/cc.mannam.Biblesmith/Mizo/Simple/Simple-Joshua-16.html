1. Tin, Josefa thlahte chanpual chu, Jeriko zawn Jordan atangin thlaler kiang, chhak lampanga Jeriko tuiahte chuan an chhuak a, Jeriko atang chuan tlang ramah Bethel thlengin a chho zel a;
2. tin, Bethel atangin Luz-ah a chhuak a, Ark-ho ramria Ataroth thlengin a kal zel a;
3. tin, thlang lampangah Japhlet-ho ramri thlengin a chhuk zel a: chu chu tuifinriatah a chhuak a.
4. Tichuan Josefa thlahte manase leh Ephraim chite chuan chanpualah a nei ta a.
5. Tin, an chhungkaw zir zela Ephraima thlahte ramri chu hetiang a ni: chhak lampanga an chanpual ramri chu Ataroth-adar a ni a, Beth-horon chhakta thlengin;
6. tin, ramri chu Mikmethath hmara thlang lampangah a chhuak a; tin, ramri chu chhak lampangah Taanath-silo thlengin a let leh a, Janoa chhak lamah a kal zel a;
7. tin, Janoa atang chuan Ataroth-ah te, Naara-ah te a chhuk zel a, Jeriko a tleng a, Jordan-aha chuak a.
8. Tapua atangin ramri chu thlang lampangah Kana luite thlengin akal a; chu chu tuifinriatah a chhuak a. Chung chu a ni Ephraima thlahte hnam an chhhungkaw zir zela an chanpual chu.
9. Manasea thlahte chanpual zinga Ephraima thlahte tana khawpui ruat hransakte nen, a khawpui zawng zawngte leh a khawper pawh.Tin, Gezer-a awm Kanaan hote chu a hnawtchuak lo va Kanaan mite chu Ephraim- hote zingah tun thleng hian an la awm a, hnathawka rawih atan chhiahhlawhah an awm ta zawk a ni.
10. Tin, Gezer-a awm Kanaan hote chu a hnawtchuak lo va Kanaan mite chu Ephraim- hote zingah tun thleng hian an la awm a, hnathawka rawih atan chhiahhlawhah an awm ta zawk a ni.
