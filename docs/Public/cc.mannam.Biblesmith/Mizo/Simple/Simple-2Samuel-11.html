1. Tin, hetih a ni a, kum a lo vei a, lalte indo tura an chauh thin lai hun chuan, Davida'n Joaba chu a tir a, amite leh Israel mi zawng zawngte nen; tin, Amona thlahte chu an tiboral a, Raba khua an hual a, Davida erawh chuJerusalem-ah a awm a.
2. Tin, heti hi a ni a, tlai nemah chuan a khum ata Davida chu a tho a, lal in chungah a han kal a: tin, in chung atang chuan hmeichhe pakhat inbual lai hi a han hmu a; chu hmeichhia chu hmel tha tak a ni.
3. Tin, Davida chuan mi a tir a, chu hmeichhe chanchin chu a zawh fiahtir a. Tin, "eliama fanu, Bath-sebi, Hit mi Uria nupui a ni lo'm ni?" an ti a.
4. Tin, Davida chuan mi a tir a, an hruai ta a; tin, a hnen chu a va thleng a, a lo mutpui ta a, (a fel loh lai tak ani a), tin, a lamah a haw leh ta a.
5. Tichuan hmeichhia chu a lo rai ta a; tin, "nau a pai ta," tiin Davida chu a hrilhtir a.
6. Tin, Davida chuan Joaba hnenah, "Hit mi Uria hu ka hnena han kaltir rawh," tiin a chah a. Tin, Joaba chuan Uria chu Davida hnena a kaltir a.
7. Tin, Urian ahnen a han thlen chuan Joaba leh mite chu engne an an a, indonaahte engne an tih a zawt a.
8. Tin, davia chuan Uria hnenah, "I in lamah haw la, i kete zuk sil rawh," a ti a. Chutichuan Uria chu lal in ata chu a chhuak a,tin, lal chu ei turtein a umzui a.
9. Nimahsela Uria hu a pu chhiahhlawhhote zingah lal in kawngkhar bulah chuan a mu ta mai a, a in lamah chuan a haw ta lo a.
10. Tin, Davida chu, "Uria chu a in lamah a haw lo," tiin an han hrilh veleh Davida'n Uria hnenah, "Khawilo ni hawng i ni lo'm ni? Engati nge i in lamah i hawn loh?" a ti a.
11. Tin, Uria chuan Davida hnena, "Pathian bawm te, Israelte leh Judate hu bukah an awm a; tin, ka pu Joaba leh ka pu mite ram lakah an inkulh a; kei ka inah ka hawngin ka eiin ka in ang a, ka nupi hnenah ka mu bik ang maw? I nunna leh i thlarau nuna chhalin ka sawi ka sawi e, chu chu ka ti lo tawp ang," a ti a.
12. Tin, Davida chuan Uria hnenah, "Vawiinah pawh hetah awm la, naktukah pawh i kal dawn nia," a ti a. Chutichuan Uria chu Jerusalem-ah chuan chu mi ni leh a tukah pawh chuan a awm ta a.
13. Tin, Davida chuan a ko a, a hmaah chuan a eiin a in a; a tirui ta der mai a; tin, tlai lamah chuan a in lamah haw loin a pu chhiahhlawhte zingah mu tur chuan a chhuak ta a.
14. Tin, heti hi a ni a, a tuk zingah chuan Davida chuan Joaba chu lehka a thawn a, Uria chu akentir a.
15. Tin, lehkhaah chuan, "Uria chu ral zualna lai berah hmatawntir u la, an thaha a thih hlauhna'n thlahthlam rawh u," tiin a ziak a.
16. Tin, heti hi a ni a, Joaban khua ch a hual vel lai chuan mi huai huai awmna nia a hriatah chuan Uria chu a tantir a.
17. Tin, khuaa mite chu an chhuak a, Joaba chu an bei a, mi thenkhat, Davida mite zinga mi ngei chu an thi a; tin, Hit mi Uria pawh a thi a.
18. Chutichuan Joaa chuan mi a tir a, indo chungchang thu lam zawng zawng chu Davida a hrilh a;
19. tin, a mi tirh chu,"Lal hnena indo chanchin zawng zawng a sawi zawh a,
20. a thin a ur thut mai mai a, 'Engati nge bei tura chutianga khaw bul lawka in kal mai:? Kulh atangin an kap mai ang che u tih in hre lo'm ni?:
21. Jerubesetha fapa Abimeleka kha tuin nge that kha? Bu rawtsawmna lun gchungnung sawk hmeichhiain kulh atanign a chunga a zuk thlak a. Thebez-ah khan a thi a ni lo'm ni? Engati nge chutianga kulh bul lawka in kal ni?" a tih chuan, 'I chhiahhlawh Hit mi Uria paw kha a thi a sin,' ti ang che," tiin a hrilh a.
22. Chutichuan ami tir chu akal ta a, a va thleng a, Joaban a tirh chhan zawng zawng hu Davida a hrilh a.
23. Tin, a mi tirh chuan Davida hnenah, "Mite chuan min rawn nawr a, phulah min rawn bei a, tin, keininan khaw luhna kawng khar thleng rawkin kan nawr ta a.
24. tin, kaptute chuan kulh atangin i chhiahhlawhte chu an kap a, tichuan lal chhiahhlawh thenkhatte an thi a, tin, i chhiahhlawh Hit mi Uria pawh kha a thi a sin," a ti a.
25. Chutichuan Davida chuan a mi tirh hnenah chuan, "Joaba hnena chan heti hian va sawi rawh, "he'ng hian i rilru tilawm lo chuang suh se, khandaih chuan atuamah a thlei si lo, nasa lehzualin khua chu bei u la tiche rawh u,' tiin fuih rawh," a ti a.
26. Tin, Uria nupi chuan a pasal Uria a thi tih a lo hriat chuan a pasal chu a sun a.Tin, a sun hun bi a pelh hnu chuan Davida chuan a in lamah a hruaitir a, a nupuiah a nei ta a, fapa a hrinsak a. Nimahsela, Davida thiltih chuan LALPA a tilungni lo a.
27. Tin, a sun hun bi a pelh hnu chuan Davida chuan a in lamah a hruaitir a, a nupuiah a nei ta a, fapa a hrinsak a. Nimahsela, Davida thiltih chuan LALPA a tilungni lo a.
