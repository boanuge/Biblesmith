1. Tin, ngai teh, Judai ram atangin Pathian mi, LALPA thuin Bethel-ah a lo kal a: tin, Jeroboama chu maicham bulah rimtui hal turin a lo ding a.
2. Tin, LALPA thuin maicham chu a au khum a, "Aw maicham, maicham, LALPA'n heti hian a ni, 'Ngai teh, Davida chhungte zingah fapa hming Josia a lo piang ang a, i chunga rimtui hal thintu, hmun sang puithiamte hi i chungah hian inthawina'n a la rawn hlan ang a, i chungah hian mihring ruhte an la rawn hal ang,' tiin," a ti a.
3. Tin, chu mi ni vek chuan chhinchhiahna a pe a, "Hei hi LALPA'n chhinchhiahna tur a tih chu a ni: 'Ngai teh u, maicham hi ala khi phawk ang a, a chunga meivapte hi a la bawk vek ang,' tih hi," tiin.
4. Tin, heti hi a ni a, Pathian miin Bethel-a maicham a han au khum thu lalin a hriat chuan, Jeroboama chuan, "man rawh u," tiin maicham atang chuan a kutin a han ban a. Tin, man tuma a banna kut chu alo zeng ta a, tichuan a kut chu a la leh thei ta lo a.
5. Pathian miin LALPA thua chhinchhiahna tura a sawi ang khan maicham pawh chu a khi bawk a, maichama meivapte chu a baw ta vek a.
6. Tin, lal chuan a chhang a Pathian mi hnenah chuan, "Ka kut hi alo lem leh theihna turin LALPA i Pathian khawngaihna ngen la, min tawngtaisak teh khai," a ti a. Tin, Pathian mi chuan LALPA hnenah a ngen a, tin, lal kut chu a lo dam leh a, a hma ang bawkin a lo awm leh ta a.
7. Tichuan lal chuan Pathian mi hnenah, "Ka in lamah ka hnenah lo kal la, va chawl hahdam rih tawh khai, tin, lawmman ka pe dawn che nia," a ti a.
8. Tin, Pathian mi chuan lal hnenah, "I in lo zatve pawh mi pe dawn mah la, i hnenah zawng ka lo kal chuang lo ang, he mi hmunah hian chhang ka eiin tui reng reng pawh ka in hek lo ang:
9. Chhang va eiin tui reng reng pawh va in suh la, i va kalna kawngah pawh haw leh tawh suh,' tia LALPA thua tirh ka ni a sin," a ti a.
10. chutichuan kawng dangah akal a, Bethel-a a lo kalna kawngah chuan a haw leh ta lo a.
11. Bethel khuaah chuan zawlnei putar pakhat hi a awm a; afapa pakhat chuan chu mi nia Bethel-a Pathaim mi thiltih zawng zawng chu a hrilh vek a: lal hnena, a thu sawite pawh chu an pa an hrilh bawk a.
12. Tin, an pa chuan an hnenah, "Khawi lam kawngah nge a kal tak?" a ti a. A fapate chuan Judai ram atanga lo kal Pathian mi haw lehna kawng chu a kawhhmuh a.
13. Tichuan a fapate hnenah, "Sabengtung mi han siam teh u," a ti a. Chutichuan sabengtung chu an siamsak a: tichuan a chuang ta a:
14. Tin, pathian mi chu a um ta a, sasqaw thing hnuaia a thu hi a va hmu a: a hnenah, "Nangmah hi em ni Judai ram atanga lo kal Pathian mi chu?" a ti a. Tin, ani chuan, "Ni e," a ti a.
15. Tichuan ani chuan a hnenah, "Ka in lamah ka hnenah lo kal la, chhang va ei rawh khai," a ti a.
16. Tin, ani chuan, "I ruala kir leh thei ka ni lo a, i hnena lo kal thei pawh ka ni hek lo: he mi hmunah hian i hnenah chhang ka eiin tui reng reng pawh ka in hek lo ang:
17. Chhang va eiin, tui reng reng pawh va in suh la, i va kalna kawngah pawh lo kir leh tawh suh,' tia LALPA thua hrilh ka ni si ," a ti a.
18. Tin, ani chuan a henah, "Kei pawh nangmah ang bawka zawlnei ka ni alawm: vantirhkohin LALPA thuin, 'Chhangte eia tuite in turin i inlamah hruai kir leh rawh khai,' tiin mi bia a sin," a ti a. Nimahsela a hnenah dawt a sawi a ni.
19. Chutichuan a hnenah chuan a kir leh ve a, a inah chuan chhangte a eiin tui te a in ta a.
20. Tin, heti hi a ni a, dawhkan an kil lai chuan a hruai kirtu zawlnei hnenah chuan LALPA thu chu a lo thleng a:
21. Judai ram atanga lo kal Pathian mi hnenah chuan, "LALPA'n heti hian a ti, LALPA thu sawi i awih loh a, LALPA i Pathianin thu a pek che i pawm loh avang leh,
22. i lo kir leh a, chhang va eiin tui reng pawh va in suh ang che, a tihna hmun ngeiah chhang i ei a, tuite i in avangin i ruang hi i pute thlana dah ani ve lo vang,' tiin, "tiin a sawi chhuak ta a.
23. Tin, heti hi a ni a, chhangte a ei a, tuite a in zawh chuan, zawlnei a hruai kir leha chu sabengtung a siamsak a.
24. Tin, a kal ta chu kawng lakah sakeibaknei nen an intawk a, a lo she ta a: tin, a ruang chu kawngah chuan thehthal derin a awm a, sabengtung chu a bulah chuan a ding a, sakeibaknei pawh chu ruang bulah chuan a ding ve reng a.
25. Tin, ngai teh, mi an va kal a, kawnga ruang thehthal dera awm leh ruang buala sakeibaknei ding reng chu an va hmu a: zawlnei putar pui awmna khuaah chuan an va sawi a.
26. Tin, kawng lak ata va hruai kir lehtu zawlnei chuan chu mi thu a lo hriat veleh chuan, "Pathian mi, LALPA thu sawi awih duh lopa kha anih chuL chuti chuan LALPA'n a hnena a sawi, a thu ang ngeiin LALPA'n sakeibaknei hnenah a pe a, a lo ham a, a sehhlum ta a nih chu," a ti a.
27. Tin, a fapate hnenah chuan, "Sabengtung mi siamsak leh u," tiin a sawi a. Tin, an siamsak ta a.
28. Tin, a kal ta a, a ruang kawnga thehthal dera lo awm leh sabengtung leh sakeibaknei ruang buala lo ding reng chu a va hmu a: sakeibaknei chuan ruang chu a lo la ei lo a, sabengtung pawh chu a lo la tipem lo a.
29. Tin, zawlnei chuan chu Pathian mi ruang chu a hlawm a, sabengtun gchungah chuan a hlang a, a kirpui ta a: tichuan zawlnei putar awmna khuaah chuan sun tur leh vui turin a lut ta a.
30. Tin, a ruang chu ama thlanah a zalh a; tichuan, "A va pawi tehluul em, ka unaupa! " tiin a tah a.
31. Tin, heti hi a ni a, a vui hnu chuan a fapate hnenah, "Ka thih hunah Pathian mi phumna thlanah hian mi rawn phum ve ang che u: a ruh bulah ka ruhte hi rawh dah ang che u.
32. Pathian thua Bethel-a maicham leh, Samari ram khawpuitea hmuns ang in zawng zawng a au khumna thu kha lo thleng ngei tur a ni si a," tiin a sawi a.
33. Chung thil hnuah pawh Jeroboama chuan a awm dan dik lo chu a la ban chuang lo a, vantlang zinga mi hmun sang puithiam atan a la siam ta cheu va: hmun sang puithiamte an awm theihna turin a duh apiang a tihrang thin a.Chu thil chu Jeroboama chhungkaw sualna anmahni tichimittu leh khawvel ata tiboral hlauhtu a lo ni ta .
34. Chu thil chu Jeroboama chhungkaw sualna anmahni tichimittu leh khawvel ata tiboral hlauhtu a lo ni ta .
