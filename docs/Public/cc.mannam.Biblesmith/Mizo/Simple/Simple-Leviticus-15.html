1. Tin, LALPA chuan Mosia leh Arona a bia a,
2. "Israela thlahte chu bia u la, an hnenah, 'Mipa tupawh a tisaah thil a puh chuan a thil put avang chuan a bawlhhlawh a ni.
3. A thil put chhung zawng chu a bawlhhlawh a ni: a tisaah chuan a put reng emaw, a put chu dan emaw pawh ni se la, a bawlhhlawh tho a ni.
4. Thil puta mutna khum tawh phawt hu a bawlhhlawh ang; tin, a thutna tawh phawt chu a bawlhhlawh bawk ang.
5. Tin, tupawh a khum khawih apiangte chuan an puante an su ang a, an inbual tur a ni a, tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlaw ang.
6. Thil puta thutnaa thu apiangte chuan an puante an su ang a, an inbual tur a ni a, tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlawh ang.
7. Tupawh thil puta tisa chu khawih apiangte chuan an puante an su ang a, an inbual tur a ni a, tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlawh ang.
8. Tin, thil puta chuan mi bawlhhlawh lo chil a chhak chuan a puante a su ang a, a inbual tur a ni a, tlai lam thlengin a bawlhhlawh ang.
9. Chuanna engpawhah thil puta chu a chuan chuan a bawlhhlawh ang.
10. Tupawh a innghahna hnu thil khawih apiangte chu tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlawh ang; tin, chung thil chawisawntu apiangte chuan an puante an su ang a, an inbual tur ani a, tlai lam thlengin a bawlhlawh ang.
11. Tin, tupawh thil puta chuan, a kut tleuh loa a khawih apiangte chuan an puante an su ang a, an inbual tur a ni a, tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlawh ang.
12. Tin, thil puta hlumbel khawih hnu chu tihkeh tur a ni; tin, thing bungbel zawng zawn chu tleuh fai vek tur a ni.
13. Tin, thil puta chu, a thil put tihthianghlim a nih chuan a inthenna'n ni sarih a intuk ang a, a puante a su ang a; tin, lui tuiah a inbual ang a; tichuan a lo thiang- hlim tawh ang.
14. Tin, a ni riat ni chuan thuro tuak khat emaw, parva no tuak khat emaw kengin LALPA hmaah inbiakna puan in kawngka bulah a kal ang a, puithiam a pe tur a ni:
15. Tin, puithiam chuan sual thawina'n pakhat, halral thilhlanah pakhat a hlan tur a ni; tin, puithiam chuan a thil put thuah LALPA hmaah inremna a siamsak ang.
16. Tupawh inpawlna chi amah atanga a chhuah chuan a pumin a inbual vek tur a ni a, tlai lam thlengin a bawlhhlawh ang.
17. Tin, silhfen te, vunte inpawlna chi awmna tawh phawt chu suk faia sil fai vek tur a ni a, tlai lam thlengin a a bawlhhlawh ang.
18. Tin, hmeichhia a mutpui pawhin an pahnihin an inbual ve ve tur a ni a, tin, tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlawh ang.
19. Tin, hmeichhiain thil put a neih a, a tisaa thil put chu thi a nih chuan ni sarih chhung chu a bawlhhlawh ang; tin, tupawh amah khawih apiangte chu tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlawh ang.
20. Tin, a bawlhhlawh chhunga a mutna tawh phawt chu a bawlhhlawh ang a, a thutna tawh phawt pawh a bawlhhlawh bawk ang.
21. Tupawh a khum khawih apiangte chuan an puante an su ang a, an inbual tur a ni a, tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlawh ang.
22. Tupawh a thutna khawih apiangte chuan an puante an su ang a, an inbual tur a ni a, tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlawh ang.
23. Khuma thil awm emaw, a thutnaa thil awm emaw khawih apiangte chu tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlawh ang.
24. Tupawhin an mutpuia a bawlhhlawhna chu an kai chuan ni sarih an bawlhhlawh ang a: an mutna khum apiangte chu a bawlhhlawh ang.
25. Tin, hmeichhia a bawlhhlawh hun pangngai ni loa ni rei fe a thi a puh emaw, a bawlhhlawh dan pangngai aia rei a thi a puh emaw chuan, a bawlhhlawhna dan pangngai chhunga a awm thin angin thi puta a bawlhhlawh chhung zawng chuan a awm bawk tur a ni; a bawlhhlawh a ni.
26. A thi puh chhung zawnga a mutna khum tawh phawt chu a bawlhhlawh dan pangngai hun laia a khum ang a ni ang a; a thutna apiang chu a bawlhhlawh dan pangngai hun laia a bawlhhlawh angin a bawlhhlawh ang.
27. Tupawh chung thil khawih apiangte chu an bawlhhlawh ang a, an puante an su ang a, an inbual tur a ni a, tlai lam thlengin an bawlhhlawh ang.
28. Amaherawhchu a thi put chu tihthianghlim a nih chuan ni sarih a intuk ang a, chu mi hnuah chuan a lo thianghlim tawh ang.
29. Tin, a ni riat niin thuro tuak khat emaw, parva no tuak khat emaw a keng ang a, inbiakna puan in kawngka bulah puithiam a rawn pe tur a ni.
30. Tin, puithiamin sual thawina'n pakhat, halral thilhlanah pakhat a hian tur a ni; tichuan puithiam chuan thi puta a bawlhhlawhna thuah LALPA hmaah inremna a siamsak ang.
31. Hetiang hian Israela thlahte chuan bawlhhlawhna ata chu in tihrang tur a ni, an zinga ka biakbuk an tihbawrhban chang pawha an bawlhhlawhna avanga an thih loh nan.
32. Hei hi thil putate leh bawlhhlawh phaha inpawlna chi chhuakate tana dan chu a ni;an bawlhhlawhna avanga peih sam lo te, ahmei apa, thil putho te, hmeichhia, an bawlhhlawh laia mutpuitute tan pawh," tiin.
33. an bawlhhlawhna avanga peih sam lo te, ahmei apa, thil putho te, hmeichhia, an bawlhhlawh laia mutpuitute tan pawh," tiin.
