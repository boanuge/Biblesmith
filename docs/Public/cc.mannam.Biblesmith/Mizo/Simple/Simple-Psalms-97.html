1. LALPA chu lalah a thu a; lei hi hlim takin awm se la; Thliarkar tam tak takte chu lawm takin awm rawh se.
2. Chhumte leh thim chhah mup chu a velah a awm a: Felna leh rorelna fel chu a lalthutthleng nghahchhan a ni.
3. A hma lamah chuan mei akal a, A vela mah dotute chu a kangral thin.
4. A kawlphete chuan khawvel hi a en zawk zawk a: lei hian a hmu a, a khur a,
5. Tlangte chu khawihnun ang hian LALPA hmaah a tuiral ta a, Khawvel zawng zawng LALPA hmaah chuan.
6. Vante khian a felna chu atlangaupui thin a, Mi tinreng chuan a ropuizia an hmu ta a.
7. Milem siam chawpte rawngbawltu zawng anwg chu mualphoin awm rawh se, Milemho chhuangtue chu: Nangni pathian zawng zawngte u, amah chibai buk rawh u.
8. Zion tlang chuan a hria a, lawm takin a awm a, Juda fanute chu hlim takin an awm bawk a;
9. Aw, LALPA i rorelnate avangin. LALPA nang hchu leilung zawng anwg chungah hian chungnung ber i ni si a: Nang chu pathian zawng zawng chung daihah chawimawiin i awm a
10. Aw LALPA hmangaihtute u, sual haw rawh u. Ani chuan ami thianghlimte nunna chu a vang tha thin a; Anni chu mi suaksualte kut ata a chhanhim thin.
11. Mi felte tan khua alo eng a, Thinlung dikte tan lawmna alo thleng.Mi felte u, LALPA-ah lawm takin awm u la; A hming thianghlim chu fak rawh u.
12. Mi felte u, LALPA-ah lawm takin awm u la; A hming thianghlim chu fak rawh u.
