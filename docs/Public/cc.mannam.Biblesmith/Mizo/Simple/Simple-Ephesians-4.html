1. Chutichuan, inngaihtlawmna leh thuhnuairawlhna zawng zawng nei a,
2. hmangaihnaa indawh tawn chunga dawhtheihna neia remna phuarnaah chuan Thlarau inpumkhatna chu vawng tura bei a,
3. a kohna che u phua awm turin, kei, Lalpaa mi tang hian ka ngen a che u.
4. A koh chhan che u beiseina hmunkhata koh in ni ang bawk khan taksa pakhat leh Thlarau pakhat chauh a awm a;
5. Lalpa pakhat, rinna hmunkhat, baptisma hmunkhat,
6. engkim Pathian leh Pa pakhat chauh a awm a; ani chu engkim chungah a awm a, engkim fang chhuakin a awm a, engkim chhungah a awm bawk a ni.
7. Nimahsela, athlawna Krista thilpek tehna ang zelin khawngaihna chu kan hnenah theuh pek a ni.
8. Chuvangin, "Chung lama a han chhoh lai khan mante chu salah a hruai a, mihringte hnenah pawh thilpekte a pe a," a tih chu.
9. Tichuan, "A han chho," tih hi, lei hmun hnuai berahte a lo chhuk hmasak zet loh chuan engtin nge ni ang le?
10. Lo chhuka chu engkim a tihkhah theihna'na van zawng zawng chung daiha han chhova vek bawk kha a ni.
11. Tin, rinna leh Pathian Fapa hriatna kawngah chuan thu khat vua a, puitling a,
12. Krista famkimna lenzia tluka kan zaa kan lo awm hma loh zawng, rawngbawl hna thawk tura mi thianghlimte tihfamkimna'n Krista taksa siam tha turin thenkhatte chu tirhkoh atan te,
13. thenkhatte chu zawlnei atan te, thenkhatte chu Chanchin Tha hriltu atan te, thenkhatte chu kohhran chawmtu leh zirtirtu atan te a pe a;
14. chutichuan dik lohna ngamthlemna angin, fing verveka mihring bum chawpin, zirtirna thli tinrenga lehlam lehlama vawrh leh len len naupang kan ni tawh lo ang a,
15. hmangaiha thu dik tak sawiin, engkimah amahah kan thang lian thei zawk ang: amah chu Lu a ni, Krista chu.
16. Amah azarah taksa pumhlum chuan inzawmna tinreng thawh avanga rem taka inchuktuah leh inzawm khawmin, peng tin thawhzia ang theuhin hmangaihnaa insiam tha turin taksa chu a lo tipung thin a ni.
17. Chutichuan, hei hi ka sawiin Lalpaah chuan ka hriattir che u a ni: Jentailte chu rilru lawi lo pua an awm angin awm tawh suh u la,
18. hriat lohna anmahniah a awm, chu mi hriat lohna avang chuan Pathian nunna lakah thena lo awmin, an hriatnaah chuan an lo thim a ni, an thinlung a chawlawl vangin.
19. Chungte chuan inhriat chhuah theihna reng reng an neih tawh loh avangin, thil bawlhhlawh tinreng duham taka ti turin hurnaah an inpumpek ta a.
20. Nangni erawh chuan chutiangin Krista chu in zir lo e;
21. a aw ngaithlaa amahah zirtira awm in nih tawh phawt chuan, Isuaa thutak awm ang hi:
22. "In hman lai awm dan kawnga mihring hlui, bumna chakna anga chhe deuh deuh kha hlip thla u la,
23. in rilru thlarauvah chuan atharin lo awm u la;
24. mihring thar, thutak felna leh thianghlimnaa Pathian anga siam khan inthuam bawk u la," tih thu hi.
25. Chutichuan, dawt sawi banin, mahni thenawm hnenah thu dik tak sawi theuh rawh u; inpeng tawn theuh kan ni si a.
26. Thinrim u la, sual erawh chu sual suh u; in thinur chu niin tlakpui suh se;
27. Diabola chu hmun kian hek suh u.
28. Ruk hmang chuan ru tawh suh se la; tlachham hnenah chuan pek tur a neih theihna turin a kuta thil tha thawkin rim zawk rawh se.
29. Thu tha lo reng in kaan chhuak suh se, a tulzia ang zela siam tha turin thu tha apiang chhuak zawk rawh se, a ngaithlatute tan khawngaihna a lo nih theihna'n.
30. Tin, Pathian Thlarau Thianghlim chu tilungngai suh u, tlanna ni atan chuan amahah chhinchhiaha in awm kha.
31. Khakna te, thinurna te, tauhna te, anna te, sawichhiatna te zawng zawng chu bansanin awm rawh se, sualna zawng zawng nen.Tin, inkhawngaih tawna lainatna neiin awm u la, Pathianin Kristaa a ngaidam che u ang bawkin inngaidam tawn rawh u.
32. Tin, inkhawngaih tawna lainatna neiin awm u la, Pathianin Kristaa a ngaidam che u ang bawkin inngaidam tawn rawh u.
