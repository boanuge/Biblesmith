1. Assuria lal Sargona tithin Tartana Asdod khuaa a lo kal a, Asdod khua a bei a, a lak kum khan;
2. chumi lai chuan LALPA'n Amozaa fapa Isaia ka chuan, "Kal la, i kawnga saiip puan kha phel la, i kea i pheikhawk kha dah rawh," tin, a sawi a. Tichuan chutiang chuan a ti a, saruak leh pheikhawk bun loin a kal a kal ta a.
3. Tin, LALPA chuan, "Ka chhiahhlawh Isaia hi Aigupta-ho chungah leh Thiopia-ho chungah chhinchhiahna leh thilmak atan kum thum chhunga saruak leh pheikhawk bun loa a kal ang hian,
4. Assuria lal chuan Aigupta-ho mante leh Ethiopia-ho salte chu, apui apangin, saruak leh pheikhawk bun lo leh mawng langin Aigupta-ho mualphona turin a hruai bo ang.
5. Tichuan an beisei ber Ethiopia-ho leh an ropuina ber Aigupta-ho avang chuan mangang tak leh mualpho takin an awm tawh ang.Tichuan, chu mi niah chuan he tuifinriat kama mi hian, 'Ngai teh, kan beisei ber, Assuria lal laka min chhanhim tura min puitu atana kan tlanna chu, heti a ni ta si a: keini hi engtin nge ka tlan chauh ang?' an la ti ang," a ti.
6. Tichuan, chu mi niah chuan he tuifinriat kama mi hian, 'Ngai teh, kan beisei ber, Assuria lal laka min chhanhim tura min puitu atana kan tlanna chu, heti a ni ta si a: keini hi engtin nge ka tlan chauh ang?' an la ti ang," a ti.
