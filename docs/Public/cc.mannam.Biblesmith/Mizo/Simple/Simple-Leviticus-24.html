1. Pathianin Mosia a bia a,
2. "Israela thlahte chu oliv hriak fim tak, chhit tura sawr, khawnvar tihalh reng nan I hnena rawn la turin thu pe rawh.
3. Inbiakna puan ina thuhriattirna puanzar pawn lamah chuan tlai lam atanga zing lam thleng atan LALPA hmaah Aronan dah fel rawh se: hei hi in thlahte zel thlenga kumkhaw dan tur a ni.
4. LALPA hmaah chuan khawnvarte chu dah fel thin rawh se.
5. Tin, chhangphut dip mi la la, chu chu chang sawm leh pahnihah I awm tur a ni: chhang pakhatah chuan epha hmun sawma thena hmun hnih hman tura ni.
6. LALPA hmaah dawhkan thianghlim chungah tlar hnihin, tlar khata paruk ve vein I tiang tur a ni.
7. Chhang chungah, a tlar hnihah chuan beraw fim tak I dah tur a ni, hriatrengna, LALPA tana meia thilhlan anga nih theihna'n.
8. Chawlhni apiangin LALPA hmaah a dah fel thin tur a ni: kumkhaw thuthlunna, Israela thlahte tana ni.
9. Tin, Arona leh a fapate pual a ni ang a, hmun thianghlimah an ei tur a ni: kumkhaw dana LALPA tana meia thilhlante zing a mi, a tana thil thianghlim tak a ni," tiin.
10. Israel hmeichhe fapa, a pa Aigupta mi, mi pakhat chu Israela thlahte zingah a va kal a: chu Israel hmeichhe fapa leh Israel mi, mi pakhat chu riah hmunah an insual a;
11. Israel hmeichhe fapa chuan anche lawhin pathin hming a sawichhia a; tichuan Mosia hnenah an rawn hruai a. A nu hming chu Selomithi a ni lo, Dan chi Difiria fanu a ni.
12. LALPA ka atanga thu chhuak nghahna'n tan inah an khung a.
13. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia a bia a,
14. "Anchhe lawhtu chu daipawnah hruai u la, a hretu zawng zawng chuan a luah an kut nghat se la, mi zawng zawng chuan lungin deng rawh se.
15. Tin, Israela thlahte chu bia la, an hneha, 'Tupawh a Pathian anchhia lawh apiang chuan a sual mawh a phur ang a.
16. Tin, LALPA hming sawichhia apiang chu tihhlum ngei tur a ni; mi zawng zawngin lungin an deng ngei ngei tur a ni: LALPA hming an sawichhiat chuan ram dang mi pawh, he rama mi reng pawh tihhlum tur a ni.
17. Thi khawpa mi chunga kutthlak apiang chu tihhlum ngei tur a ni a;
18. thi khawpa ran chunga kut thlak apiang chuan a rul tur a ni: nun aiah nun.
19. tin, miin a thenawm a tihhmelhem chuan a tih ang tak chuan a chungah tih ve tur a ni;
20. ruh tliak aiah ruh tliak, mit aiah mit, ha aiah ha; mi a tihhmelhem angin a chungah pawh tih ve tur a ni.
21. Ran tihhlum apiang chuan a rul tur a n a; mi tihlum apiang chu tihhlum ve tur a ni.
22. Ram dang mite tan leh he rama mi reng te tan dan hmun khat in nei tur a ni. Kei hi LALPA in Pathian ka nih hi,' I ti tur a ni," tiin.Tichuan Mosia chuan Israela thlahte chu a bia a, anchhe lawhtu chu daipawn an hruai chhuak a, lungin an deng a. Israela thlahte chuan LALPA'n Mosia thu apek ang chuan an ti ta a.
23. Tichuan Mosia chuan Israela thlahte chu a bia a, anchhe lawhtu chu daipawn an hruai chhuak a, lungin an deng a. Israela thlahte chuan LALPA'n Mosia thu apek ang chuan an ti ta a.
