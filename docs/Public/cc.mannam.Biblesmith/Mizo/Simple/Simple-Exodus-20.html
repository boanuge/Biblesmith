1. Pathianin he'ng thu zawng zawng hi a hrilh a,
2. "Kei, LALPA i Pathian, Aigupta ram ata, bawih ata hruai chhuak tu che u ka ni.
3. Keimah lo chu Pathian dang reng reng i nei tur a ni lo.
4. Milem siam chawp leh thil, a anpui reng reng - chung lam vana mi emaw, hnuai lam leia mi emaw, lei hnuai lama tuia mi emaw - i tan i siam tur a ni lo:
5. chung chu chibai i buk tur a ni lo a, an rawng i bawl tur a ni hek lo; kei, LALPA i Pathian hi, Pathian thikthuchhia, pate khawlohna avanga fate hrem thin, mi hawtute chu chhuan thumna leh chhuan lina thlenga hrem thin leh,
6. mi hmangaia ka thupekte pawmtu sang tam takte chunga khawngaihna lantir thin ka nih hi.
7. LALPA i Pathian hming chu i lam mai mai tur a ni lo; LALPA chuan a hming lam mai mai chu thiam loh chantir lohin a chhuah dawn si lo a.
8. Chawlhni hi thianghlim taka serh tur a ni tih hre reng rawh.
9. Ni ruk chhungin i thawk tur a ni a, i hna zawng zawng chu i thawk tur a ni:
10. a ni sarih ni erawh chu LALPA i Pathian tan chawlhni a ni e: chu mi ni chuan hna reng reng i thawk tur a ni lo, nang pawhin, i fapa pawhin, i fanu pawhin, i bawihpa pawhin, i bawihnu pawhin, i ran pawhin, i ina hnam dang pawhin:
11. ni ruk chhungin LALPA'n lei leh van te, tuifinriat leh a chhunga awm zawng zawngte chu a siam a, tin, a ni sarih ni chuan a lo chawl ta si a: chuvangin LALPA'n chawlhni chu mal a sawm a, a tithianghlim ta a ni.
12. I nu leh i pa chawimawi rawh; tichuan LALPA i Pathianin ram a pekah che hian i dam rei dawn nia.
13. Tual i that tur a ni lo
14. I uire tur a ni lo.
15. I ru tur a ni lo.
16. I vengte chungah hretu derah i insiam tur a ni lo.
17. I vengte chhungte chu i awt tur a ni lo a, i vengte nupui chu i awt tur a ni lo, a bawihpa emaw, a bawihnu emaw, a bawngpa emaw, a sabengtung emaw, i vengte ta reng reng chu engmah i awt tur a ni lo," tiin.
18. Tin, mipui zawng zawngte chuan khawpuirite, kawlphe te, tawtawrawt rite, tlang khute chu an hmu a: tin, mipuiin an han hmuh chuan an khur a, hla takah an ding ta a.
19. Tichuan Mosia hnenah, "Nangma'n kan hnenah hian thu sawi teh zawk khai, kan lo la ang e: amaherawhchu Pathian zawngin kan hnenah thu sawi teh tawh suh se, chutilozawng kan thi mai dah ang e," an ti a.
20. Tin, Mosia chuan mipuite hnenah chuan, "Hlau suh u: Pathianin a rawn fiah che u a nih hi, in mithmuhah tihbaia a awmna hi, in sual lohna turin a ni e," a ti a.
21. Tin, mipuite chu hla takah an la ding reng a, Mosia chuan Pathian awmna thim chhah mup kiang chu a va pan a.
22. Chutichuan LALPA'n Mosia hnenah, "Israela thlahte hnenah hian hetiangin va sawi rawh; 'Nangmahni ngeiin hei van atanga ka rawn biakzia che u in hre tawh a.
23. Pathian dang reng reng chu ka kiangah chuan in rawn nei tur a ni lo; tangkaraw pathiante emaw, rangrachak pathiante emaw in tan in siam tur a ni lo.
24. ka tan lei maicham i siam tur a ni, chutah chuan i beramte leh i sebawngte chu i halral thilhlanahte leh i remna inthawi nante i hlan thin tur a ni: hmun tinah ka hming ka hriattirna apiangah chuan i hnenah ka lo kal thin ang a, mal ka rawn sawm thin ang che.
25. Tin, lung maicham mi siamsak dawn chuan lung chherin i siam tur a ni lo e: i hriamhrei chu chu i hman hlauh zawngin i tibawlhhlawh a ni dawn si a.Ka maicham lawn chhohna rahkaahte chuan lawn pawh i lawn chho tur a ni lo e saruak a i awm a lan lohna turin.
26. Ka maicham lawn chhohna rahkaahte chuan lawn pawh i lawn chho tur a ni lo e saruak a i awm a lan lohna turin.
