1. Tin, i vanglai hian i siamtu hre reng rawh - Ni tha lote a lo thlen hma leh lawmna reng reng ka nei lo e, i tihna tur kumte a lo hnaih hma hian.
2. Ni te, eng te, thla te, arsite a thim hma leh ruah hnua chhum a lo kir leh hma hian.
3. In vengtu chu a lo khurh a, mi chakte chu an kaikun a, an tlem vanga rawtsawmtu nute an ban a, tukverha dak nute chu an thim a.
4. Kawtthlera kawngte a inkhar ni chuan, rawtsawmna ri a lo chuai a, sava thawmah mi a thawh a, zai nu zawng zawngte an lo zuai lai hian;
5. A ni, a sang chu an hlau a, kangah hlauhawm a awm a, vaiumkhal thing a par a, khau chu hnawksak a ni a, chakna a bo a, mi chu a chatuan in lamah a hawngin, lusunte chu kawtthlerah an vak vel si a.
6. Tangkaraw zai chu a chah a, rangkachak berhbu chu a keh a, hlumbel chu tuikhurah a keh a, tawlailir chu tuikhuah bulah a tliah hma hian
7. Vaivut chu a awm angin leiah a chang leh a, thlarau chu a petu Pathian hnenah a kir leh a ni.
8. Thuhriltu chuan, engmah ni lo mai mai, thil zawng zawng hi engmah ni lo mai a lo ni, a ti a.
9. Tin, a fing chauh pawh ni loin, Thuhriltu chuan mite hriatna a zirtir a; a ni, thufing tawi tam tak a ngaihtuah a, a zawng chhuak a, a rem fel a.
10. Thuhriltu chuan thu pawm tlak hmu turin a zawng a, tlang taka ziak, thutak thute chu.
11. Mi fing thu chu beramputen an khalhna'na an hman tiang zum ang a ni a, thufing lawrkhawm chu thir Kilhna khen bur tlat anga nghet a ni.
12. Chu chauh pawh ni loin, hengah hian, ka fapa, zilhin awm ta che. Lehkhabu tam tak siam hi tawpintai a awm lo e, nasa taka zir pawh hi tisa chauhna mai a ni.
13. Heng thu zawng zawng khaikhawmna hi i ngaithla teh ang: Pathian tih la, a thupekte vawng rawh; hei hi mihring zawng zawng tih tur chu a ni mai.Pathian chuan thiltih zawng zawng chungah ro a la rel dawn si a, thil inthup engkim, a chhia emaw, a tha emawte pawh, tih hi.
14. Pathian chuan thiltih zawng zawng chungah ro a la rel dawn si a, thil inthup engkim, a chhia emaw, a tha emawte pawh, tih hi.
