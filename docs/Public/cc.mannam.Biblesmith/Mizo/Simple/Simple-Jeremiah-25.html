1. Judate lal, Jehoiakima, Josia fapa kum linaa Juda mite zawng zawng chungchanga Jeremia hnena thu lo thleng chu: chumi hun chu Babulon lal Nebukadnezzara kum khatna a ni a,
2. chu thu chu zawlnei Jeremian Juda mi zawng zawng leh Jerusalema awm zawng zawngte hnena a sawi chu a ni.
3. kum sawmhnih leh kum thum chhung ngawt, Judate lal Josia, Amona fapa kum sawm leh kum thumna atanga tun thlengin LALPA thu chu a lo thleng thin a, zing tak taka thoin in hnenah ka sawi ka sawi a, mahse in ngaithla lo a.
4. Tin, LALPA'n in hnenah a rawngbawltu zawlneite zawng zawng a ti ra, zing tak taka thoin a tir a, mahse in ngaithla lo a, hre turin in beng in dawh hek lo;
5. chung zawlneite chuan, mi tin, mahni sual kawng ata lo kir u la, LALPA'n a hnenah leh in pate hnena hman lai atanga kumkhua atana a pek che a ramah hian cheng rawh u:
6. Pathian dangte hnena an rawng bawl tur leh chibai buk tur leh in kut hnathawha ka thin tiur turin kal suh u; tichuan ka tina lo ang che u, an ti thin a.
7. Mahse in tana tha lo tura in kut hnathawktu mi tithinur turin mi ngaithla lo, LALPA'n a ti.
8. Chuvangin Sipaihote LALPA chuan heti hian a ti: Ka thu in ngaithlak loh avangin,
9. ngai teh u, hmar lam hnam zawng zawng chu ka chah ang, LALPA thu chhuak chuan a ti. Ka rawngbawltu Babulon lal Nebukadnezzara pawh ka chah ang a, anni chu he ram leh a chhunga awmte leh he'ng vela hnam awm zawng anwgte bei turin ka rawn hruai ang a; ka tiboral der ang a, mi tihah leh chek khumah leh tiau reng atan ka siam ang.
10. Tin, an hnen ata hlimna thawm leh lawmna thawm te, moneitu aw leh mo aw te, buh rawt thawm ri leh khawnvar engte ka la bo ang.
11. He ram pum pui hi a chhia ang a, a ram ang; tin, he'ng hnamte hian Babulon lal rawng kum sawmsarih an bawl ang.
12. Ti kum sawmsarih a ral hunah Babulon lal leh chu hnam chu, Kaldaiho ram chu, an sualna avangin ka hrem ang a; kumkhuain ka titua ang. LALPA thu chhuak chuan a ti.
13. Chu ram chungah chuan ka thu an chunga ka sawi zawng zawng, Jeremia hnam zawng zawng chunga a sawi, he lehkhabua ziak zawng zawng hi ka thlentir ang.
14. Hnam hrang hrangte leh lal ropui tak takte chuan an zinga an mi duh an thing dawn si a; an thiltih ang zel leh an kut hnathawh ang zelin ka thungrul dawn a ni.
15. LALPA Israelte Pathai chuan ka hnenah heti hian a ti: Ka kuta he thinurna uain no hi la la, ka tirhna tur che hnam zawng chu intir rawh.
16. An in ang a, an pai thlawk thlawk ang a, an a ang a, an hnena ka tirh tur khandaih avang chuan, tiin.
17. Tin, LALPA kuta no chu ka la a, LALPA'n mi tirhna hnam zawng chu ka intir a:
18. chung chu hengte hi an ni: Jerusalem leh Judai khawpui te, chuta lalte leh hotute chu, tiauna te, mak tihah te, chek khumah te, anchhiaahte chang turin, tuna an awm dan ang hian:
19. Aigupta lal Pharaoa leh a chhiahhlawh te, a hotute leh ami zawng zawng te;
20. hnam dang pawlhtu zawng zawng te; Uz rama lal zawng te; Philistia rama lal zawng zawng, Askelon leh Gaza leh Ekron leh Asdoda la awm chhun lalte chu;
21. Edom leh Moab leh Amon thlah te;
22. Tura lal zawng zawngte leh Zidon lal zawng zawng leh tuipui kam lalte;
23. Dedan leh Tema leh Buz leh an bak sam met zawng zawngte;
24. Arabia lal zawng zawng te; thlalera cheng hna inchawhpawlhho lal zawng zawngte;
25. Zimri lal zawng zawng te; Elam lal zawng zawng te; Media lal zawng zawng te;
26. hmar lam lal zawng zawng te, a hla leh a hnai ami a indawt zelin; lei chunga awm khawvel lal ram zawng zawngte chu; tin, an in hnuah chuan Sesak lalin a in ang.
27. Tin, an hnenah Sipaihote LALPA, Israelte Pathian chuan heti hian a ti: In u la, rui u la, lua u la, tlu u la, tho leh suh u, khandaih in zinga ka tirh tur avang chuan, ti ang che.
28. Tin, i kuta no chu in tura an lak duh loh chuan an hnenah heti hian ti ang che: Sipaihote LALPA chuan heti hian a ti: In in ngei ang;
29. hei, ka hming vuah khawpuiah thil tha lo ka ti tan a, nangni hrem loh tawpin in awm ang maw? Hrem lohin in awm lo ang: leilunga cheng zang zangte chungah khandaih ka lek dan si a, tih hi Sipaihote LALPA thu chhuak a ni.
30. Chuvangin an chungah he'ng hi sawi la, an hnenah heti hian ti rawh: LALPA chu hmun sang atangin a rum ang, A chenna thianghlim atangin a rawl a chhuah ang, A huang chungah nasa takin a rum ang, Grep sawrtute angin a au vang, Leilung luah zawng zawngte chungah chuan.
31. Leilung tawp thlengin thawm a thang khawk ang a, LALPA chuan hnamte a khing a, tisa zawng zawng chungah ro a rel dawn, Mi suaksual chu khandaih hnenah a pe ang, LALPA thu chhuak chuan a ti.
32. Sipaihote LALPA chuan heti hian a ti: Ngai teh, thil tha lo chuan hnam tin, a rawn hrut dawn e, Thlipui na tak mai lei hmun tawp atangin a lo thawk dawn.
33. Chumi nia LALPA thahte chuan lei tawp atangin lei tawp thlengin an dap ang a; sun loh leh khawm loh leh phum loin an awm ang a;lei chunga ekdur mai an ni ang.
34. Berampute u, chiau vak vak u la, tap rawh u, Nangni beram rual zinga puipate u, tal tawp tawp rawh u, Thaha in awm ni leh in darhsarh nite a lo thleng takzet a nih hi. Bel duhawm angin in tlu ang.
35. Berampute chuan tlan bona kawng an nei lo ang a, beram rual zinga puipate pawhin pumpelhna kawng an nei lo ang.
36. Berampute tap thawm chu, beram rual zinga puipate chiau ri nen, LALPA'n an tlat hmun a tihram mek avangin.
37. Huang muan awmte chu tihreh thiapin an awm ta e, LALPA thinrimna nasa tak avangin.A tawmna a chhuahsan ta, sakeibaknei ang maiin. An ram chu a chhe zo ta si a, Hnehchiahna rawng tak avang Leh a thinrimna nasa tak avangin.
38. A tawmna a chhuahsan ta, sakeibaknei ang maiin. An ram chu a chhe zo ta si a, Hnehchiahna rawng tak avang Leh a thinrimna nasa tak avangin.
