1. Tin, Paula chuan khaw chhak bial a fang chhuak zet a, Apollova Korinth khuaa a awm hlan khan Ephesi khua chu a thleng a, zirtirho tute emaw a hmu a;
2. tin, ani chuan an hnenah, "In rin tirh lai khan Thlarau Thianghlim in chang em?" a ti a. Tin, anni chuan a hnenah, "Chang lo, Thlarau Thianghlim a awm tih reng hriat pawh kan hre lo," an ti a.
3. Tin, ani chuan, "Eng baptismaah nge in chan le?" a ti a. Anni chuan, "Johana baptismaah," an ti a.
4. Tin, Paula chuan, "Johana kha chuan mi hnenah, 'Mi rawn zuitu tur (Isua) chu in la ring tur a ni,' tiin simna baptismain a baptis thin a nih kha," a ti a.
5. Tin,chu thu chu an hriat veleh Lalpa Isua hmingin baptisma an chang ta a.
6. Tin, Paulan an chungah a kut a nghat a, Thlarau Thianghlim chu a chungah a lo thleng ta a; tichuan tawng hriat lohtein thu an sawi a, Pathian thu pawh an hril ta a.
7. An za chuan patling sawm leh pahnih lai an ni.
8. Tin, Paula chu inkhawmna inah a lut thin a, thla thum lai huai takin thu a sawi a, Pathian ram kawng thu-ah chuan mi a hnial a, mi a thlemthlu thin a.
9. Nimahsela a then an tihmawh a, an awih duh loh a, mipui hmaah Kawng chu an sawichhiat veleh chuan, ani chuan anmahni a kalsan a, zirtirhote chu a kai hrang a, ni tinTuranna zirtirna inah mi a hnial thin a.
10. Chutiang hlirin kum hnih lai a ti a; chutichuan Asia rama awm zawng zawng Judate leh Grikte pawhin Lalpa thu chu an hre ta hial a.
11. Tin, Pathianin Paula kutin thilmak namen a tihtir lo a;
12. chutichuan a taksa ata inhrukna puante emaw, fenthuahte emaw damlote hnenah an rawn keng a, an nate a reh thin a, an kawchhung ata ramhuaite pawh an chhuak thin a.
13. Tin, thiam zuara vak vak Juda mi tuten emaw, "Paulan a thu a tlangaupui thin Isua hmingin kan vau a che," tiin ramhuai paite chungah Lalpa Isua hming an lam vei thin a.
14. Tin, Juda mi puithiam lal pakhat, Skeva chuan chutiang ti ve thin fapa sarih a nei a.
15. Nimahsela ramhuaiin an hnenah, "Isua ka awih e, Paula pawh ka hria e, nangni erawh chu tu nge maw in nih le?" a ti a, a chhang a.
16. Tin, ramhuai pai chuan a rawn zuan a, an za chuan a hneh a, a ngam ta zawk a, chutichuan chu mi in ata chu saruak leh inhliamin an tlan chhuak ta hial a.
17. Chutichuan Ephesi khuaa awm zawng zawng, Judate leh Grikte hnenah pawh, chu mi thu chu a thang ta a; tichuan an zain an hlau a, Lalpa Isua hming a lian ta a.
18. Tin, ringhote pawh chu a then tam tak an lo kal a, an thiltihte thupha an chawi a, an inpuang ta a.
19. Dawihmang a then tam tak pawhin an lehkhabute an rawn la khawm a, mi zawng zawng mithmuhah an hal ta a,tin, a man an chhut a,cheng singnga niin an hria.
20. Chuti kauva nasa chuan Lalpa thu chu a darh a, a hneh ta zel a.
21. Tin, chung zawhah chuan Paulan, Makedonia ram leh Akaia ram chu fan chhuah riha Jerusalem khuaa kal a rilruk a, "Chuta ka kal zawh chuan Rom khua ka tlawh bawk tur a ni," a ti a.
22. Chutichuan a rawngbawltu pahnih, Timothea leh Erasta chu Makedonia ramah a kaltir zet a, ani erawh chu Asia ramah a awm rih a.
23. Tin, chung lai vel chuan Kawng avang chuan mi buai namen lohin an buai ta a.
24. Mi pakhat, a hming Demetria, tangkarua chhera tangkaruaa Diani lem siamtu chuan, a siam thiamte hnenah inhlawhfakna tam tak a pe thin a;
25. chu mi chuan anni chu a ko khawm a, an hnathawh ang thawktute nen; tin, an hnenah, "Pute u, he hna hi kan hausakna a ni tih in hria e.
26. Hetah Ephesi khua chauhvah hian ni loin, Asia ram khawvel zawng zawng thawah pawh he Paula hian,'Kuta siam pathiante hi pathian an ni lo,'tiin mi tam tak a thlemthlu a, a pakaitir zo tawh a ni tih nangni ngei pawhin in hmuin in hre bawk a:
27. chutichuan kan hnathawh a lan ang tih a hlauhawm chauh hi a ni lo a, pathiannu ropui tak Diani biak in pawh hi an rawn ngai tlawm ang a, Asia rama mi zawng zawng leh khawvela miin an biak thin hi, a ropui tehlul nen, a tlawm ang tih a hlauhawm bawk a ni," a ti a.
28. Tin, chung thu chu an hriat veleh an thinur ta em em a, "Ephesi khuaa mi Diani hi ropui tak a ni!" tiin an au a.
29. Tichuan akhuain an buai ta vek a, Gaia leh Aristarka, Makedonia rama mi, Paula hnungzuitute chu an man a, arualin ennawm inah an tlanpui chiam a.
30. Tin, Paulan mipui zinga luh a tum chuan zirtirhoten an phal lo a.
31. Tin, Asia ram hotu berte zinga thenkhat tute emaw Paula thiante an ni a, a hnenah mi an tir a, ennawm inah chuan lut lui lo turin an thlem chiam a.
32. Tin, awmkhawmhote chu an buai a, mi tam zawkin an inkhawm chhan pawh an hre lo a, chuvangin thenkhat thu pakhatin an au a, thenkhat thu dangin an au a.
33. Tin, mipui zinga mi thenkhatin Aleksandra chu an thiamna thu sawi dan tur chu an hrilh a, Judaten ani chu mi hma lamah an nam phei a. Tin, Aleksandra chuan a kutin a zap a, mi zawng zawng hnenah chuan an thiamna thu sawi a tum a.
34. Nimahsela ani chu Juda mi a ni tih an hriat veleh, darkar hnih lai aw rual takin, "Ephesi khuaa mi Diani hi ropui tak a ni!" ti hlirin an au an au a.
35. Tin, khaw lehkhaziaktuin mipui chu a uidaih zawh chuan, "Ephesi khuaa mite u,Diani ropui tak biak in leh van atanga milem tla kha, Ephesi khuaa mite hi a vengtu in ni tih tu nge hre lo awm ni?
36. Chutichuan he'ng thu hi hnial rual a nih loh ahnuin, in ngawi reng tur a ni, thu dawn loin engmah in ti tur a ni lo.
37. Heng mi, heta in rawn hruaite hi, biak in suamhmangte an ni hek lo, kan pathiannu sawichhetu lah an ni hek lo.
38. Chutichuan Demetria leh a thian themthiamte pawhin tu lakah pawh thupawi an neih chuan, thupawi sawina inte chu hawn sa a ni alawm, lal aiawhtute pawh an awm a; inchhal rawh se.
39. Thil dang pawi nei reng in nih chuan inkhawm pangngaiah an rem ang.
40. Vawiinah bengchhen avang hian chhal hlauhawmin kan awm tak hi a bul awm si loin; chuti chu a ni si a he inkhawm chhan hi engmah kan sawi theih dawn loh hi," a ti a.Tin, chutiang thu a sawi zawh chuan awmkhawmhote chu an tintir ta a.
41. Tin, chutiang thu a sawi zawh chuan awmkhawmhote chu an tintir ta a.
