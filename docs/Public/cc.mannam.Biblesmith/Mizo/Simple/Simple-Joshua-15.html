1. Tin, Juda thlahte hnam an chhungkaw zir zela an chanpuala ruat chu Edon ramri chin chhim lampang, Zin thlaer thleng hialin, chhim tawp thleng rakin a ni.
2. Tin, chhim lampanga an ramri chin chu Tuirinriat Al tawp rak, chim chawng litengsawl atangin a ni;
3. tin, Akrabim chhohna chhim lam panin a kal zel a, Zin a va thleng a, tin, Kadesbarnea chhim lamah a chho a, Hezron a va thleng a, tin, Adar-ah a chho a, Karka a kualkhung a:
4. tin, Azmon-ah a kal zel a, Aigupta luiah a chhuak a; tichuan tuifinriat chu ramri tawp chin chu a ni: chu chu chhim lampangah in ramri chin tur chu a ni ang.
5. Tin, chhak lampang ramri chu tui - Tuifinriat Al, Jordan chhuah tak chu a ni. Tin, Hmar lampang ramri chu tuifinriat lingtengsawl, Jordan chauh atangin a ni a:
6. tichuan ramri ch Beth-hogla-ah a chho a. Beth-araba hmarah a kal zel a: tin, ramri chu Rebena fapa Bohana lungah a chho zel a:
7. tin, ramri chu Akor ruam atangin Debir-ah a cho zel a, tin, lui chhim lama awm Adumim-a chhohna depa Gilgal lam panin hmar lamah a kal zel a: tin, ramri chu Ensemes tuiahte chuan a kal zel a, tichuan ramri chu En-rogel-aha chhuak a ni.
8. tin, ramri chu Honoma fapa ruamah Jebus-ho ram sir chhim lam (chu chu Jerusalem a ni) thlengin a chho a; tin, ramri chu Hinom thlang lampang ruam bula awm tlang chhipah a chho a, chu chu Rephaim hmar lampang ruam tawp rak a ni:
9. tin, ramri chu tlang chhip atangin Nephtoa lui tuite thlengin a kaltir a, Ephron tlanga khawpuiahte a kal zel a, tin, ramri chu Baala-ah (chu chu Kiriath-hearim ani_ an kaltir:
10. tin, ramri chu Baala atangin thlang lampang ah Seir tlang thlengin a insawh kual thla leh a, hmar lampangah Jearim (chu chu Kesalon a ni) tlang hrulah akal zela , Beth-semes-ah a chhuk a. Timna lamah a kal zel a:
11. tin, ramri chu hmar lampang Ekron hrulah a kal chuak a, tin, ramri chu Sikron-ah an kaltir a, tin, Baala tlangah a kal zel a, Jabneel-ah a chhuak a; tichuan ramri chu tuifinriatah a va tawp a ni.
12. Tin, thlang lampang ramri chu tuifinriat zau tak leh a kam chin zawng chu a ni. Chung ramri chin zawng chu ani Juda thlahte tana an chhungkaw zir zela ruat chu.
13. Tin, Jephunia fapa Kelaba chu LALPA'n Josua thu apek angin Juda thlahte zingah chuan chanpual a siamsak ve a, chu chu Kiriath-arba a ni a, Arba chu Anaka pa a ni (chu chu Hebron an ti bawk).
14. Tin, Kaleba chuan chu khua ata chu Anaka fapate pathum Sesaia te, Ahimana te, Talmaia te, Anaka fate chu a hnawt chhuak a.
15. Tin, chu tang chuan Debir khuaa mite a han run a; a hma chuan Debir khaw hming chu Kiriath-sepher a ni.
16. Tin, Kaleban, "Tupawh Kiriath-sepher han bei a, han la thei apiang chu ka fanu Aksai nupuiah ka neihtir ang." a ti a.
17. Tin, Kaleba unau Kenaza fapa Othnielan a han la a: tichuan a fanu Akai chu nupuiah a neihtir ta a.
18. Tin, a pasal hnen a va thlen chuan a pa ram dil turin a lo fuih a. tin, sabengtung ata a chuk a; Kaleba chuan a hnenah, "Enge i duh le," a lo ti a.
19. Ani chuan, "Eng emaw dil che ka duh a ni: chhim lam ram ro takah mi lo awmtir ta si a, tuikhur mi pe tel bawk turin ka ngen a che," a ti a. Tichuan tuikhur chhakta leh thlangta chu a pe ta a.
20. Chung ch ani Juda thlahte hnam an chhungkaw zir zela an rochan chu.
