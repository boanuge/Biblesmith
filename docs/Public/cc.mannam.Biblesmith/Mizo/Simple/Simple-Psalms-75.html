1. Aw Pathian, lawm thu kan hrilh a che; Lawm thu kan hrilha che, i hming a hnai si a: Miten i thilmak tih thute chu an hril thin.
2. A hun ka ruatah chuan Dik takin ro ka rel ang.
3. Lei leh a chunga awm zawng zawngte chu an tuiral zo ta: Keiin a bante chu ka phun a ni. (Selah)
4. Mi uangthuangte hnenah chuan, "Uangthuang takin awm suh u," ka ti a: Mi suaksualte hnenah, "In ki tisang suh u," ka ti bawk a.
5. In ki chu chung lamah tisang suh u la; Ring khawng chungin tawng suh u.
6. Tihsanna chu chhak lam ata a ni hek lo, thlang lam ata a ni hek lo, Chhim lam ata lah a ni hek lo.
7. Pathian chu roreltu a ni zawk: Anic haun pakhat a tilawm a, pakhat a tisang thin.
8. LALPA kutah chuan no a awm si a, a uain chu a phul a: Thil dang nena chawhpawlhin akhat a, chuta mi chu a bun chhuak a: Matheiloin a nawite lam chu leia mi suaksual zawng zawngte chuan an in fai kelh ang.
9. Nimahsela kei chuan kumkhuain ka hriattir ang a, Jakoba Pathian fakin ka zai ang.Mi suaksualte ki zawng zawng pawh ka tansak ang a; Mi felte ki erawh chu tihsanin a awm ang.
10. Mi suaksualte ki zawng zawng pawh ka tansak ang a; Mi felte ki erawh chu tihsanin a awm ang.
