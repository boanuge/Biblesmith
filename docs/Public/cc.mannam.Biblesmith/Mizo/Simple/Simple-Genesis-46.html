1. Tin, Israela chu a neih zawng zawng nen a chhuak a, Beerseba chu a thleng a, a pa Isaaka Pathian hnenah chuan inthawinate a hlan a.
2. Tin, Pathianin zana a mumangahte chuan Israela chu a bia a. "Jakob, Jakob," a ti a.Tin, ani chuan, "Heta hi ka awm," a ti a.
3. Tin, ani chuan, "Kei Pathian, i pa Pathian chu ka ni: Aigupta rama zuk kal chu hlau suh; chutah chuan chi ropui takah ka siam dawn che a ni:
4. Aigupta ramah chuan i hnenah ka zuk kal ve ang a; tin, ka hruai chho leh ngei ngei bawk ang che: tin, Josefa chuan i thih hunah i mit a siamsak dawn che nia," a ti a.
5. Tichuan Jakoba chu Beer-seba atang chuan a chhuak a: Israela fapate chuan an pa Jakoba leh an fanau te tete leh a nupuite chu a chuan nana Pharaoa tawlailir thawnahte khan an chuantir a.
6. Tichuan Jakoba leh a thlahte zawng zawng chuan Kanaan rama an dehchhuah an rante leh an bungruate chu an chhawm a, Aigupta ram chu an thleng ta a:
7. a fapate, a tupate, a fanute, a tunute, a thlahte zawng zaqwng chu Aigupta ramah chuan a luhpui ta hlawm a.
8. Tin, he'ng hi Israela thlah Aigupta rama lut Jakoba leh a fapate hming chu a ni: Reubena Jakoba fa tir chu.
9. Tin, Reubena fapate chu Hanoka te, Pallua te, Hezrona te, Karmia te an ni a.
10. Tin, Simeona fapate chu Jemuela te, Jamina te, Ohada te, Jakina te, Zohara te, Kanaan hmeichhe laka a fapa Saula te an ni a.
11. Tin, Levia fapate hu Gersona te, Kohatha te, Meraria te an ni a.
12. Tin, Juda fapate chu Era te, Onana te, Sela te, Pereza te, Zera te an ni a: Era leh Onana erawh chu Kanaan ramah chuan an thi ta a ni. Pereza fapate chu Hezrona leh Hamula an ni a.
13. Tin, Isakara fapate chu Tola te, Ouva te, Joba te, Simrona te an ni a.
14. Tin, Zebuluna fapate chu Sereda te, Elona te, Jahleela te an ni a.
15. Chungho zawng zawng chu Leaii fapa, a fanu Dinaii nen, Paddan-aram rama Jakoba a hrinsakate chu an ni a: a fapate leh a fanute zawng zawng chu sawmthum leh pathum an ni a.
16. Tin, Gada fapate chu Ziphiona te, Hagia te, Sunia te, Ezbona te, Eria te, Arodia te, Arelia te an ni a.
17. Tin, Asera fapate chu Imna te, Isvaa te, Isvia te, Beria te an ni, an farnu Seri nen: tin, Beria fapate chu Hebera leh Malkiela hi an ni a.
18. Chungho chu Zilpaii Labanan a fanu Leaii hnena a peka fapate chu an ni, chungho chu Jakoba a hrinsak a, mi sawm leh parukte chu.
19. Tin, Jakoba nupui Rakili fapate chu Josefa leh Benjamina an ni a.
20. Tin, Josefan Aigupta ramah fapa Manasea leh Ephriama chu a nei a, chu mite chu On khaw puithiam Potiphera fanu Asenathi chuan amah a hrinsak a ni.
21. Tin, Benjamina fapate chu Bela te, Bekera te, Asbela te, Gera te, Naamana te, Ehia te, Rosa te, Mupima te, Hupima te, Arda te an ni a.
22. Chungho chu Rakili fapa Jakoba a hrinsakte chu an ni: an za chuan sawm leh pali an ni.
23. Tin, Dana fapa chu Husima a ni a.
24. Tin, Naphtalia fapate hu Jazeela te, Gunia te, Jezera te, Sillema te an ni a.
25. Chungho chu Bilhaii Labanan a fanu Rakiliu hnena a peka fapate chu an ni, chungho chu Jakoba a hrinsak a: an za chuan pasarih an ni.
26. Jakoba fapate nupuiho chhiar loin Jakoba hnena Aigupta rama lut ve, a thalbea chhuak zawng zawngte chu an za chuan sawmruk leh paruk an ni a;
27. tin, Josefan Aigupta rama fapa a neihte chu pahnih an ni. Jakoba chhungte zawng zawng Aigupta rama lutte chu sawmsarih an ni.
28. Tin, Jakoba'n Gosen ram lam kawng lo kawhhmuh turin Juda chu Josefa hnenah a kal hmasaktir a: tichuan Gosen ramah chuan an lut ta a.
29. Tin, Josefa chuan a tawlailir chu a buatsaih a, Gosen ramah chuan a pa hmuak turin a han kal ta a; tin, a hnenah a inhriattir a, a ir a chuktuah a, rei fe a ir chuktuahin a tap a.
30. Tin, Israela chuan Josefa hnenah, "Mi nungdama i lo la awm a, i hmel ka hmuh leh tak avang hian tunah chuan duh leh thi tawh rawh i," a ti a.
31. Tin, Josefa chuan a unaute leh a pa chhungte hnenah, "Ka han kal ang a, Pharaoa ka hrilh ang a, a hnenah, 'Ka unaute leh ka pa chhungte, Kanaan rama awmte kha, ka hnenah an lo thleng ta;
32. anni chu ran vulhtute an nih avangin Beram vengtute an ni a; an Beram rualte leh an Bawng rualte leh an neih zawng zawng an rawn chhawm vek a ni,' tiin ka han sawi ang.
33. Tin, heti hi a ni ang a, Pharaoan a koh che u a, 'Eng hna nge in thawh thin?' a tih hunah chuan,nangnin 'I chhiahhlawhte hi kan thlahtute chenin, kan nuapan tet ata tun thlengin ran vulhtute kan ni,' ti ang che u: chutichuan Gosen ramah in awm thei dawn nia," a ti a. Beram vengtute reng reng chu Aigupta mite tenzawng tak an ni si a.
34. nangnin 'I chhiahhlawhte hi kan thlahtute chenin, kan nuapan tet ata tun thlengin ran vulhtute kan ni,' ti ang che u: chutichuan Gosen ramah in awm thei dawn nia," a ti a. Beram vengtute reng reng chu Aigupta mite tenzawng tak an ni si a.
