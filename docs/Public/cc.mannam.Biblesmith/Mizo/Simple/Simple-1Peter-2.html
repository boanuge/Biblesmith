1. Chutichuan, sualna zawng zawng te, ngamthlemna zawng zawng te, vervekna te, itsikna te, sawichiatna zawng zawng te bansan u la,
2. nausen piang tharte angin thlarau lam hnute, engmah pawlh loh chu chak rawh u, chu miah chuan chhandamna tling khawpa in than len theihna'n;
3. Lalpa chu mi khawngaih thei tak a ni tih in hriat tawh phawt chuan.
4. Amah chu lung nung, mihringte duh loh ngei, Pathian ngaihah erawh chuan thlan leh hlu tak a ni, ama hnenah chuan lo kalin,
5. nangni pawh lung nungte angin thlarau lam ina rem chhohin in awm, Isua Krista zara Pathian lawmzawng thlarau lam inthawina hlan turin puithiam thianghlimho in lo nihna'n.
6. Chuvangin, Pathian Lehkha Thuah chuan, "Ngai teh u, Zionah chuan a kila lung pawimawh ber, thlan, hlu tak ka rem a ni; Amah ringtu chu a mualpho lo ang," tih a chuang a ni.
7. Chutichuan,nangni a awihte tan chuan hlu tak a ni,a awih lote tan erawh chuan, "In satuten lung an duh loh kha,chu ngei chu a kila lung tangkai ber a lo ni ta,
8. Tlukna lung leh dalna lungpui" nen. An awih lo a, thu-ah chuan an tlu si thin a; chutianga ti turin ruat an ni reng bawk a.
9. Nimahsela, thim ata chhuaka, a eng mak taka lut tura kotu che u thatna chu in entir theihna'n nangni zawng chi thlan, lal puithiamho, hnam thianghlim, Pathian mite ngei in ni si a;
10. hman lai khan mi reng in ni lo a, tunah erwh chuan Pathian mi in ni ta a; hman lai khan zahngaihna in hmu lo a,tunah erawh chuan zahngaihna in hmu ta a.
11. Duh takte u, mikhual leh khualzin in nih avangin, thlarau do thintu tisa chaknate bansan turin ka ngen a che u;
12. Jentailte zinga in chetzia chu mawi tak ni rawh se,chutichuan thil tisualtute anga an sawichhiat che u kawngah chuan in thil tihthat an hmuhte avangin fanin an awm hunah chuan Pathian an chawimawi thei ang.
13. Lalpa avang chuan mihring ruat apiangte hnenah intulut rawh u, chungnunga awm lalber hnenah emaw,
14. thiltisualtute chunga phuba la tur leh, thil tithatute fak tura ama tirh chhawn Ram Awptu hnenah pawh.
15. Hei hi Pathian duhzawng a ni si, mi ate thusawi fing lo chu thil tha tia in tihdan hi;
16. zalen in ni a, nimahsela in zalenna chu sual khuhna'n hmang loin, Pathian bawiha awmna'n hmang zawk rawh u.
17. Mi zawng zawng chawimawi rawh u. Unauhote chu hmangaih rawh u. Pathian tih dek rawh u. Lalber chu chawimawi rawh u.
18. Chhiahhlawhte u, ti dek hlirin in hotute thu thuin awm rawh u; mi tha leh mi nunnemte lakah chauh ni loin, mi nunrawngte lakah pawh.
19. Miin Pathian hriatna avangin tuar loh tur tuarin lungngaihna a tuar hram hram chuan chu chu Pathian lawmzawng a ni si.
20. In sual a, kut thlak in tuarin chhel takin tuar mah u la, fakna eng nge in hlawh thin? Amaherawhchu thil in tihthat a, in tuarin chhel taka in tuar chuan chu chu Pathian lawmzawng a ni.
21. Krista pawh khan a tuarsak che u avangin hetiang ti turin koh in ni a sin; a hniaka in zuina turin entawn tur a hnutchhiah che u kha.
22. Ani chuan thil reng a tisual lo a, a kaah ngamthlemna a awm hek lo;
23. mi hauha a awm laiin a hau ve thung lo a, a tuar laiin a vau lo a; fel taka ngaihtuah thintua hnenah chuan a inpe zawk a.
24. Sual lamahte thi tawhin felna lamah kannun theihna'n amah ngeiin thingah khan a taksain kan sualte a phur a; amah vuakna vualte chuan tihdamin in awm ta a.Beram bo anga in bo kha, tunah erawh chuan in thlarau vengtu leh Hotu hnenah chuan in kir leh tak hi.
25. Beram bo anga in bo kha, tunah erawh chuan in thlarau vengtu leh Hotu hnenah chuan in kir leh tak hi.
