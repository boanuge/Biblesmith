1. Tin, Rehoboama chuan Jerusalem a han thlen chuan, Israel-hote do a, Rehoboama ram la leh turin Juda leh Benjamina chhung te, mi huaisen thiau thlan chhuah, nuaikhat leh singriat zet a khawm a.
2. Nimahsela Pathian mi Semaia hnenah LALPA thu a lo thleng a,
3. "Judai ram lal Solomona fapa Rehoboama hnenah leh Judai ram leh Benjamin rama Israel mi zawng zawngte hnenah,
4. LALPA'n heti hian a ti, In han kal tur a ni lo a, in unaute chu han do hek suh u: mahni in lamah haw leh vek rawh u; hei hi keimah tih a ni e,' tiin sawi rawh," tiin. Chutichuan LALPA thu chu an awi a, Jeroboama do tura an kal tur chu an kir leh ta a.
5. Tin, Rehoboama chu Jerusalem khuaah a awm a, Judai ramah invenna'n khuate a siam a,
6. Bethlehem khua te, Etam te, Tekao te,
7. Bethzur te, Soko te, Adulam te,
8. Gat te, Maresa te, Zip te,
9. Adoraim te, Lakis te, Azeka te,
10. Zora te, Aijalon te, Hebron khuate; chung chu Judai leh Benjamin rama kulh nei khuate a ni.
11. Tin, chung kulhte chu a tinghet a, hotute a dah a, ei tur te, hriak te, uain te a chek khawm a.
12. Tin, khawtinah chuan phaw te, fei te a dah a, khaw nghet takah a siam hlawm a. Tin, Judai leh Benjamin ram chu a ta a ni.
13. Tin, Israel ram zawng zawnga puithiamte leh Levi-hote chu an ram atang theuhin a hnenah an kal a.
14. Levi-hote chuan an ram leh an awmnate an chauhsan a, Judai ram leh Jerusalem-ah an kal a: Jeroboama leh a fapaten LALPA tana puithiam hna thawk lo turin a bantir a,
15. a hmun sang atan le kelpa leh bawng no lem a siam atana puithiamte a ruat tak zawk avangin.
16. Tin, Israel hnam, LALPA Israelte Pathian lama riltu thle zawng zawngte chuan an zui a, LALPA an pi leh pute Pathian hnena inthawi turin Jerusalem-ah an kal a.
17. Chutichuan Judai ram chu an tinghet a, kum thuum chung chu Solomona fapa Rehoboama chu an tinghet a: kum thum chhung chu Davida leh Solomona kawng chu an zawh a ni.
18. Tin, Rehoboama chuan Davida fapa Jerimotha fanu Mahalathi nupuiah a nei a, chu chu Jesea fapa Eliaba fanu Abihaili fanu a ni:
19. fapate a hrinsak a; Jeusa te, Semaria te, Zahama te.
20. Ani hnuah chuan Absaloma fanu Maakai a nei a; ani chuan Abija te, Ataia te, Ziza te, Selomitha te a rinsak a.
21. Tin, Rehoboama chuan Absaloma fanu Maakai chu a nupuite leh a hmeite zawng zawngah chuan a hmangai bik a; (nupui sawm leh pariat leh hmei sawmruk a nei a, fapa sawmhnih leh pariat leh fanu sawmruk a nei a.)
22. Tin, Rehoboama chuan maakai fapa Abija chu a unaute zinga chungnung berah a dah a; lala siam a rilruk nghe nghe a ni a.Fing takin rem a ruat a, a fapate zawng zawng chu Judai ram leh Benjamin ram zawng zawnga kulh nei khuaahte a awm darhtir vek a: ei tur tam tak a pe a. Nupui tam tak a zawn sak hlawm a.
23. Fing takin rem a ruat a, a fapate zawng zawng chu Judai ram leh Benjamin ram zawng zawnga kulh nei khuaahte a awm darhtir vek a: ei tur tam tak a pe a. Nupui tam tak a zawn sak hlawm a.
