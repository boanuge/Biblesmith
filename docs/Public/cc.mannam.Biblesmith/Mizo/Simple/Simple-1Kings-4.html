1. Tin, lal Solomona chu Israel-ho zawng zawng lalah a awm ta a.
2. Hengte hi a mi lianho neihte chu an ni: Zadoka fapa Azaria chu puithiam a ni a;
3. Sisa fapa Elihorepha leh Ahijate chu lehkha ziaktu an ni a; Ahiluda fapa Jehosaphat chu chanchin ziaktu ani a;
4. Jehoiada fapa Benaia chu sipai hotu a ni a Zadoka leh Abiathara chu puithiam an ni a:
5. Nathana fapa Azaria chu hotute chunga mi ani a; Nathana fapa Zabuda chu puithiam leh lal thian a ni fawm a;
6. Ahisara chu in chhung lam hotu ani a; Abda fapa Adonirama chu hnathawka rawih luihte hotu a ni.
7. Tin, Solomona chuan Israel-ho zawng zawng chungah chuan hotu sawm leh pahnih a nei a, chungho chuan lal tan leh a chhungkuate tan chuan ei tur an ngaihtuah thin a: mi pakhatin kum khatah thla khat zel ei tura ngaihtua tur a ni.
8. Hengte hi an hmingte chu a ni: Ephraim tlang ramah Ben-hura:
9. Makaz khuaah te, Saalbim khuaah te, Beth-semes khuaah te, Elon-bethhanan khuaah te Ben-dekera;
10. Aruboth ramah Ben-heseda; ani chuan Sok leh Hepher ram zawng zawng a chang tel a:
11. Dor khaw zosang ram zawng zawngah Ben-abinadaba; ani chuan Solomona fanu Taphathi nupuiah a nei a:
12. Taanak khua leh Meggido khuaah te, Jezreel khaw hnuai, Zarethan khaw bula awm Beth-sean bial zawng zawngah te, Beth-sean atanga Abelmehola-ah te, Jokmeam ram dep thlengin Ahiluda fapa Baana:
13. Ramoth-gilead khuaah Ben-gebera; ani chuan Mansea fapa Jaira khua, Basan rama Argob bial, khaw ropui tak tak khaw sawmruk, kulh leh a kalhna dar tlawn neite pawh a chang tel a:
14. Mahanaim khuaah Idoa fapa Ahinadaba:
15. Naphtali ramah Ahimaaza; ani pawh chuan Solomona fanu Basemathi nupuiah anei a:
16. Aser leh Bealoth khuaahte Husaia fapa Baana:
17. Isakar ramah Parua fapa Jehosaphata:
18. Benjamin raman Ela fapa Simeia:
19. Amor-ho lal Sihona leh Basan lal Oga ram, Gilead ramah Uria fapa Gebera; chu ramah chuan htou pakhat a awm.
20. Judate leh Israelte chu tuifinriat kama tiau vaivut zat zetin an tam a, an eiin an in a, hlim takin an awm thin a.
21. Tichuan Solomona chuan Lui atangin Philistia-ho ram leh Aigupta ramri thlengin ram zawng zawng chungah chuan ro a rel a: lawmmante an rawn keng thin a, Solomona dam chhung zawng chuan a rawng an bawl thin a.
22. Solomona ni khat ei tawk chu chhngphut dip tak tehkhawng sawmthum leh chhangphut tehkhawng sawmruk ani a;
23. bawngpa thau tak sawm te, bawngpa tla mi sawmhnih te, beram za te nen; chung lo pawh chu sakhi te, sangai te, sakhipa te, ar chawm thaute pawh.
24. Luiral piah lam ram zawng zawng -Tipsa atanga Gaza thlengin, Lui ral piah lama lalho zawng zawng chungah chuan a lal a: a chhehvela mi zawng zawngte nen chuan ralmuangin an awm hlawm a.
25. Tin, Solomona dam chhung zawng chuan Judaho leh Israel-ho chu Dan atanga Beer seba khaw thlengii mi tin, mahni grep hrui bulah leh mahni theipui bulah hchuan thlamuang takin an awm theuh a.
26. Tin, Solomona chuan a tawlailir hnuktu sakawr in singli leh sakawr chung chuang mi singkhat leh sanghnih a nei a.
27. chung hotute chuan mahni chan thla zelah Solomona leh lal Solomona dawhkana rawn kil vetu zawng zawngte ei tur chu an ngaihtuah thin a; pek kim loh an nei ngai lo.
28. Mi tinin an tih tura ruat ang zelin a awmna tur hmunah chuan sakawrte leh ran tlan chak takt tan barli leh buhpawlte an rawn dah bawk thin a.
29. Tin, Pathianin Solomona chu finna leh hriatna nasa tak leh, rilru zauna tuifinriat kama tiau vaivut ang mai a pe a.
30. Solomona finna chuan khawchhak fate zawng zawng fina leh Aigupta mite finna zawng zawng chu akhum vek a.
31. Mi zawng zawng aiin a fing a ni: Mahola fa te, Ezra mi Ethana te, Hemana te, Kalkola te, Darda te ai pawhin: tichuan a vel hnam zawng zawngte zingah chuan a hming a thang ta a.
32. Thufing sangthum a sawi a: tin, a hlate chu sangkhat leh panga a ni.
33. Thing chanchinte pawh Lebanon tlang sidar thing atanga kulha lo chawr chhuak hussop chenin a sawi thin a: sa chanchin te, bawkvaka kal chi chanchin e, sangha chanchin te pawh a sawi thin a.Tichuan Solomona finna chanchin hre tawh phawt chu khawvel lalho zawng zawng atangin chi tina mi a finna ngaithla tur an lo kal thin a.
34. Tichuan Solomona finna chanchin hre tawh phawt chu khawvel lalho zawng zawng atangin chi tina mi a finna ngaithla tur an lo kal thin a.
