1. Tin, mi tte emaw Judai ram ata an lo chhuk a, "Mosia dan anga serh in tantir loh chuan chhandam in ni thei lo ang," tiin unaute chu an zirtir a.
2. Chutichuan Paula leh Barnaba chu anmahni nen inhnial namena an inhnial loh a, an inkhin tlat avangin, Paula leh Barnaba chu an zinga tute emaw nen, chu thubuai chungah chuan Jerusalem khuaa tirhkohte leh upate hnenah chho turin an ruat a.
3. Chutichuan kohhrante chuan an han thlah a, anni chuan Jentailte hawi kir lehzia thu chu hril zelin, Phoiniki ram leh Samari ram an fang chhuak ta a; tichuan unaute zawng zawng an tilawm em em a.
4. Tin, Jerusalem khua chu an han thlen chuan kohhranhote, tirhkohte, upate chuan an lo lawm a, tin, Pathianin anmahni hmanga thil a tih zawng zawngte chu an hrilh a.
5. Nimahsela Pharisai pawla mi ring ve tute emaw an tho a, "Jentailte chu an serh tan tur a ni, Mosia dan awih pawh an hnenah tiam kher tur a ni," an ti a.
6. Tin, tirhkohte leh upate chu, chu mi thu chu ngaihtuah turin an inkhawm a.
7. Tin, an inhnial chiam hnuin Petera a tho a, an hnenah : "Unaute u, Jentailten keima kaa Chanchin Tha thu hriaa awih tura Pathianin hmana in zing ata mi ruatzia kha in hria e.
8. Tin, rilru hretu Pathianin keini min pek ang bawk khan Thlarau Thianghlim an hnenah pe vein an chungah a lawm tih a hriattir a;
9. tin, keini leh anni thlei bik loin rinin an rilru a tithianghlim a.
10. Chutichuan min thlahtu leh keini zawh tawh loh nghawngkawl kha tunah zirtirhote chunga nghah tumin engati nge Pathian fin in hnial kalh le?
11. Anmahni rin ang bawk hian keini pawhin Lalpa Isua khawngaihna avanga chhandam tur niin kan inring si a," a ti a.
12. Tichuan mipui zawng zawng chu an ngawi reng a, Barnaba leh Paulan Pathianin anmahni hmanga Jentailte zinga chhinchhiahna leh thilmak a tih thu kha an hrilh chhung zawng an ngaithla a.
13. Tin, an sawi zawhin Jakoba'n a sawi ve thung a: "Unaute u, ka thu ngaithla rawh u,
14. Pathianin ama hming pu atan an zing ata mite la chhuak turin Jentailte a kan tan tirh dan thu chu Sumeona hian a sawi ta a.
15. Tin, chung thu leh zawlneite thu hi a inhmeh a ni:
16. 'Chung zawhah chuan ka kir leh ang a, Davida bawkte chim kha ka siam leh ang a, a chhe lai apiang pawh ka rem chho leh ang;
17. Mi dang, Jentail zawng zawng ka hming mi tawmhote chuan, Lalpa an zawnna'n chu bawkte chu ka tungding ang' tiin.
18. Khawvel siam tirh ata he'ng thu hriattir thintu Lalpa chuan a ti e, tih ziak hi.
19. "Chuvangin heti hian ka ruat a ni, Jentailte zinga Pathian lam hawite chu tihrehawm lo i la,
20. milem biakna thil bawlhhlawh ei te, inngaih te, sa rehhlum leh thisen eite banna turin an hnenah lehkha thawn zawk i la ka ti ber a ni.
21. Mosia'n hman lai ata tawh khaw tinah a thu tlangaupuitu a nei thin si a, inkhawmna inahte Chawlhni apiangin a thu chu chhiar a nih thin kha," a ti a.
22. Tin, tirhkohte, upate, kohhran zawng zawngte nen chuan, an pawla mi thlana Paula leh Barnaba hnenah Antiokei khuaa kaltir tha an ti a; an thlante hming chu Juda (Barsaba an tih) leh Sila an ni a, chu mite chu unaute zinga hotu deuh an ni a.
23. Tin, hetiangin an ziak a, an kutah an thawn a. "Tirhkohte leh unaute zinga upaten Antiokei khua te, Suria ram te leh Kilikia rama Jentail unau awmte chibai an buk e.
24. "Kan pawl zinga mi tuten emaw thu sawiin an tibuai che u a, in rilru an timangang tih kan hria e, an hnenah thu engmah kan pe si lo a.
25. Chuvangin thu khat vuaa kan inkhawmin, kan duh tak Barnaba leh Paula,
26. Lalpa Isua Krista hming avanga thih dawn lote kal rual hian mi thlante in hnena rawn kaltir ve tha kan ti a.
27. Chutichuan Juda leh Sila kan rawn tir a nih hi, anni pawhin he'ng thu bawk hi tawngkain an hrilh ang che u.
28. Thlarau Thianghlim leh keinin, he'ng thu pawimawh lo hi chu, phurrit zual in chungah nghat lo i la a tha kan ti;
29. milem biakna sa ei te, sa thisen ei te, sa rehhlum ei te, inngaih te bang u la kan ti; he'ng hi in ban chuan in tan a tha ang. Dam takin awm rawh u," tiin.
30. Chutichuan an tirhin an kal a, Antiokei khua an thleng chhuk a; tin, mipui chu an ko khawm a, lehkha chu an pe a;
31. tin, an chhiar zawhin thlamuanna thu avang chuan an lo lawm ta hle a.
32. Tin, Juda leh Sila chu zawlnei an ni bawk a, thu tam takin unaute chu an thlamuan a, an tinghet a.
33. Tin, chutah chuan rei deuh an awm hnuin, anmahni tirtute hnena hawng leh turin unaute chuan thlamuang takin anchhuahtir leh ta a.
34. Nimahsela, Sila chuan chutah la awm rih chu thain a hria a.
35. Paula leh Barnaba erawh chu Antiokei khuaah chuan Lalpa thu zirtir leh hrilin an awm reng a, mi dang tam takte nen.
36. Tin, ni eng emaw zat hnuah chuan Paulan Barnaba hnenah, "Tunah kal leh i la, khaw tina Lalpa thu kan hrilhna apianga unau awmte kha kan zel i la, an awm dan i en ang," a ti a.
37. Tin, Barnaban Johana (Marka an tih chu) an hnenah hruai a tum a,
38. Paula erawh chuan ani chu an hnena hruai tha a ti lo, Pamphulia ramah anmahni a kirsan a, hnathawhnaa an hnena a kal ve tak loh avang khan.
39. Tichuan nasa takin an lo inhnial ta a, chutichuan an inthen a, Barnaban Marka chu a hnenah a hruai a, Kupra thliarkar lamah lawngin a kal ta a.
40. Paula erawh chuan Sila a thlang a, unauten Lalpa khawngaihnaa an kawltir hnuin a kal chhuak ta a.Tin, kohhran pawlte chu tinghet zelin Suria leh Kilikia ram a fang chhuak ta.
41. Tin, kohhran pawlte chu tinghet zelin Suria leh Kilikia ram a fang chhuak ta.
