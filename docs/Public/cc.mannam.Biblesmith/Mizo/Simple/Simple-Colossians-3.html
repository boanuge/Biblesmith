1. Chutichuan, Krista hnenah kaihthawhin in awm ve tawh chuan chung lam, Pathian ding lama thu Krista awmnaa thil awmte chu zawng rawh u.
2. Chung lama thil awmahte chuan in rilru nghat tlat rawh u, leia thil awmahte chuan nghat loin.
3. In thi tawh a, in nunna chu Pathianah chuan Krista hnenah thuhrukin a awm si a.
4. Krista kan nunna chu a lo inlar hunah chuan nangni pawh ama hnenah ropui zingah in inlar ve ang.
5. Chutichuan, in taksa peng leia awmte hi tihlum rawh u, inngaihna te, bawlhhlawhnate, hurna te, chakna sual te, duhamna te hi (duhamna chu milem biakna a ni si a);
6. chung thil avang chuan a ni, Pathian thinur chu awih lo fate chunga a thlen thin ni;
7. nangni pawh hmanah chung thilahte chuan in nun lai khan, chungahte chuan in awm thin kha.
8. Tunah erawh chuan nangni pawhin he'ng zawng zawng, tauhna te, thinurna te, huatna te, sawichhiatna te, in kaa chhuak tawng zahmawh te hi tibo rawh u.
9. Dawt inhrilh tawn suh u; mihring hlui chu a thiltihte chawpin in hlip tawh a,
10. mihring thar chuan in inthuam tawh si a, chu mihring thar chu hriat famkimna nei turin, a siamtu anpui anga siam tharin a awm mek a ni;
11. chu miah chuan Grik mi leh Juda mi, serhtan leh tan loh, awze mite, Skuthia mi te, bawih te, bawih lo te pawh a awm theih lo; Krista chu engkim ni zawkin, engkimah a awm bawk a ni.
12. Chutichuan, Pathian mi thlan, mi thianghlim leh duh takte angin lainatna thinlung te, ngilneihna te, inngaihtlawmna te, thuhnuairawlhna te, dawhtheihna te chuan inthuam rawh u,
13. tupawhin tu chungah pawh thupawi nei ang u la indawh tawnin inngaidam tawn u la; Lalpa chuan a ngaidam che u ang tak khan ngaidam ve rawh u.
14. Tin, chung zawng zawng chungah chuan hmangaihna inbel rawh u, chu chu phuar famkimna a ni.
15. Tin, Krista thlamuanna chuan in thinlungah ro rel rawh se, chutiang ni turin taksa pumkhata koh in ni reng a sin; lawm nachang hre bawk rawh u.
16. Krista thu chu finna tinrengin nangmahniah awm teuh rawh se; fakna hla te, Pathian hla te, thlarau lam hla tea inzirtirin inzilh taWn u la, thinlunah neiin Pathian chu hla saksak rawh u.
17. Tin, in tih apiang chu thusawiah emaw, thil tihah emaw ama zarah Pa Pathian hnena lawmthu hril chungin Lalpa Isua hmingin ti vek rawh u.
18. Nupuite u, Lalpaa a awm tawk ang zelin in pasalte hnenah intulut rawh u.
19. Pasalte u, in nupuite hmangih u la, an lakah in suh u.
20. Naupangte u, engkimah in nu leh in pate thu zawm rawh u, chu chu Lalpaah chuan a lawmam si a.
21. Pate u, in fate tinuar suh u, chutilochuan an bei a dawng dah ang e.
22. Bawihte u, tisaa in hotute thu hi engkimah zawm rawh u, mihring tilawmtute mai anga mit rawng bawl loin, thinlung sakhat vuaa Lalpa tih dek chungin zawm zawk rawh u.
23. A rulhna roluah tur chu Lalpa hnen ata in hmu dawn tih hriain, in tih apiang chu mihring tan anga ti loin, Lalpa tan anga ti zawkin, chak takin ti rawh u;
24. Lalpa Krista rawng in bawl a nih hi.Thil dik lo titu chuan a thil dik lo tih man chu a hmu leh ang; mi thlei bikna reng a awm si lo a.
25. Thil dik lo titu chuan a thil dik lo tih man chu a hmu leh ang; mi thlei bikna reng a awm si lo a.
