1. Chung lai chuan Jeroboama fapa Abija a dam lo a.
2. Tin, Jeroboama chuan a nupuia hnenah, "Tho la, Jeroboama nupui ani tih an hriat lohna'n che intidanglam la, Silo-ah va kal teh khai; ngai teh, chutah chuan zawlnei Ahija, he miteho chunga lala ka la awm tur ka chungchang thu sawitu kha a awm e.
3. Tin, chhangper sawm leh chhang hlawm te te leh khawizu um khat keng langin, a hnenah va kal teh: naupang awm dan tur chu a lo hrilh ang che," a ti a.
4. Tin, Jeroboama nupui chuan chutiang chuan a ti a, a tho a, Silo-ah chuan a kal a, Ahija inah chuan a va kal ta a. Ahija chuan khua a hmu thei tawh lo va: a upat avangin a mit a thim tawh si a.
5. Tin, LALPA'n Ahija hnenah, "Ngai teh, Jeroboama nupui a fapa chungchanga rawn tur chein a lo kal e; a dam lo a ni a: hetiang hian lo hrilh ang che: heti hi ani ang a, a lo luh hunah chuan hmeichhe dang ni awm takin a rawn insiam dawn a ni, " a ti a.
6. Tin, heti hi a ni a, Ajijan chutia kawngkaa a lo luh laia a ke rah ri a han hriat veleh chuan, "Nang, Jeroboama nupui, lo lut rawh khai; eng tiziaa mi danga insiam nge i nih? Chanchin tha lo tak hrilh tur chea tirh ka ni a sin.
7. Kal leh ta la, Jeroboama hnenah, 'LALPA, Israelte Pathianin heti hian a ti, Mipuite zing ata hi ka chawisang chia, ka mi Israelte chungah hian hotuah ka siam chia,
8. Davida chhungte ram ka chhuhsak a, chu chu ka pe chia: nimahsela nang zawng ka chhiahhlawh Davida angin i awm ve lo a, ani zawngin ka thupekte a pawm a, ka mithmuha thil tha chauh tih tumin a thinlung zawng zawngin mi zui thin a;
9. nang erawh chuan i hmaa mite zawng zawng aiin thil i tisual nasa bik a, mi tihthinrimna'n pathian dangte leh milem chhunte i siam a, mi hnuchhawnsan ta a:
10. chuvangin, ngai teh, Jeroboama chhungkaw chungah hian thil tha lo ka rawn thlentir ang a, Jeroboama atang hian mipa fa tawh phawt chu Israel rama mi khuahkhirh leh khirh loh pawh ka tiboral ang a, miin ek a phait fai hliau ang mai hian Jeroboama chhungkua hi a mang vek hma loh chuan ka phiat bo hlauh dawn a ni.
11. Jeroboama chhungkaw zinga mi, khawpuai thi apiangte chu uiin an ei ang a; tin, rama thi apiangte chu chungleng savaten an ei ang: chu chu LALPA'n a sawi tawh a ni, ti ang che.'
12. Chutichuan tho la, i in lamah haw leh mai tawh: khawpui chhung i rah veleh naupang chu a hti dawn a ni.
13. Tichuan Israel-ho zawng zawngin an sun ang a, an vui ang; Jeroboama chhungkaw zingah chuan amah chauh chu thlana dah tur a ni dawn si a: Jeroboama chhungkaw zingah hian amah chauhvah hian LALPA Israelte Pathian chunga thtna eng emaw a lan avangin.
14. Tin, LALPA'n tun la laa Jeroboama chhungkua rawn tiboraltu tur Israelte chunga lal a insiam ang; tunah pawh hian engtin nge ni ta ang le?
15. LALPA chuan tuia rairuang nghing nging ang maiin Israelte chu a rawn vaw nghing ang a; tichuan LALPA thin tirima an Aser-te chu a siam avangin an pute a lo pek tawh, he ram tha tak ata hi Israel-hotek chu a la pawt chhuak fai vek ang a, Lui piah lamah chuan a la tidarh vek ang.
16. Tin, Israel-hote htil tihsualtira a tih Jeroboama sualnate avangin Israel-hote chu a hawisan ang," a ti a.
17. Tin, Jeroboama nupui chu a tho a, a chhuak ta a, Tirza khua chu a va thleng a: tin, in kawngka bul a va thlen veleh chuan naupang chu a thi ta a.
18. Tin, LALPA'n a chhiahhlawh zawlnei Ahija hmanga thu a lo sawi tawh ang ngei khan Israel-ho zawng zawngte chuan an vui a, an sun ta a.
19. Jeroboama chanchin dang-a ral bei thu te, a rorel thu te chu - ngai teh, Israel lalte lal lai thu ziakna buah chuan a chuang a.
20. Tichuan Jeroboama rorel chhung chu kum 22 a ni: tin, a pute hnenah a muhil a, a fapa Nadaba chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
21. Tin, Solomona fapa Rehoboama chu Judai ramah a lal a. A lal tantirh lai chuan Rehoboama chu kum sawmli leh kum khat mi ani a, LALPA'n a hming awmtirna'na Israel hnam zawng zawngte zinga a thlan khawpui Jerusalemah chuan kum 17 a lal a: a nu hming chu Naami, Amon mi a ni.
22. Juda-ho chuan LALPA mithmuhin thil sual an ti a; an pute thiltih zawng zawng khuma an sualnate chuan a thikthu a chawhthawhsak a.
23. Tlang sang tinah leh thing hring tin, hnuaiah hian lungphunte phunin hmun sangte leh Aser-te lam hi an siam bawk si a;
24. Tin, chu ramah chuan mipa inzuar te pawh an awm a: Israel thlahte hmaa LALPA'n a hnawhchhuah tak hnamte thiltih tenawm tinreng ang chu an ti ve a.
25. Tin, lal Rehoboama lal kum nga kum chuan heti hi a ni a, Aigupta lal Sisakan Jerusalem khua chu a han bei a:
26. LALPA ina rote leh lal ina rote chu a la a, a vai chuan a la ta vek a: tin, Solomona rangkachak phaw siam zawng awngte chu ala tel vek a.
27. Tin, lal Rehoboama chuan chung aiah chuan lal Rehoboama chuan chung aiah chuan dar phawte a siam a, lal in kawngka vengtu hotute a kawltir a.
28. Tin, heti hi ani a, lal chu LALPA ina a luh apiangin vengtute chuan an keng a, tin, vengtute pindanah an dah leh zel thin a.
29. Rehoboama chanchin dangte leh a thiltih zawng zawngte chu Juda lalte lal lai thu ziakna buah chuana chuang a ni lo'm ni?
30. Rehoboama leh Jeroboama chu indo reng rengin an awm thin a.Tin, Rehoboama chu a pute hnenah chuan a muhil a, Davida khawpuiah a pute zingah chuan an phum ta a: a nu hming chu Naami, Amon mi a ni. Tin, a fapa Abijama chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
31. Tin, Rehoboama chu a pute hnenah chuan a muhil a, Davida khawpuiah a pute zingah chuan an phum ta a: a nu hming chu Naami, Amon mi a ni. Tin, a fapa Abijama chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
