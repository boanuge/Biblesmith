1. "Tupawh Kohhran hotua tan duh chuan hnathawh tha a duh a ni," tih hi a dik a ni.
2. Chutichuan, Kohhran hotu chu sawiselbo, nupui pakhat nei, insum thei, rilru dik tak, mi fel, mi lawm dan thiam, zirtir thiam,
3. uain ruih hmang lo, mi velh hmang lo, mi nunnem ni zawk tur, kawhmawhbawl lo, tangka sum ngaina lo,
4. zahawm taka fate kilkawia mahni inchhung khawsak rel fel thiam mi ni tur a ni;
5. miin ama in chhung khawsak mah a rel fel thiam si loh chuan Pathian Kohhranho engtin nge a enkawl theih ang?
6. Ring thar mi ni tur a ni lo, chutilochuan chapoin a awm ang a, diabola thiam loh channa chu a chang ve dah ang e.
7. Chung lo pawh chu pawn lama mite fakin a awm bawk tur a ni, chutilochuan sawichhiata awmin diabola thangah a awk dah ang e.
8. Chutiang bawkin Kohhranho rawngbawltute chu mi zahawm an ni tur a ni; kamtam te, uain heh te, hlepna bawlhhlawha duham mi te an ni tur a ni lo;
9. chhia leh tha hriatna thiangin rinna thuruk chu pawm mi an ni zawk tur a ni.
10. Tin, chungte pawh chu fiahin awm hmasa rawh se, chu mi hnuah sawiselbo an lo nih chuan Kohhranho rawngbawl lam hna thawk rawh se.
11. Chutiang bawkin hmeichhiate pawh mi zahawm an ni tur a ni, hektute ni loin, insum thei leh engkima rinawm an ni tur a ni.
12. Kohhranho rawngbawltute chu an fate leh anmahni in chhung khawsak rel felin nupui pakhat nei ni rawh se.
13. Kohhranho rawngbawl lam hna thawk tha tawhte chuan dinna tha tak leh Krista Isuaa rinna kawngah chuan huaina nasa tak an insiam si thin a.
14. Rei lo te chinah i hnenah lo kal beiseinain he'ng thu hi i hnenah ka ziak a ni;
15. nimahsela ka la awm rei tak dahin Pathian chhungte zinga mi engtia awm tur nge an nih i hriat theihna'n ka ziak a ni; Pathian chhungte chu Pathian nung Kohhranho, thutak ban leh innghahna chu an ni.Tin, Pathian ngaihsakna thuruk chu hnial rual lohin a ropui a ni: "Tisaa lo lang a, Thlaraua thiam chang a, Vantirhkohte hmuha Jentailte zinga tlangaupui a, Khawvelah hian mite rin a, Ropuinaa hruai chhoh taka chu."
16. Tin, Pathian ngaihsakna thuruk chu hnial rual lohin a ropui a ni: "Tisaa lo lang a, Thlaraua thiam chang a, Vantirhkohte hmuha Jentailte zinga tlangaupui a, Khawvelah hian mite rin a, Ropuinaa hruai chhoh taka chu."
