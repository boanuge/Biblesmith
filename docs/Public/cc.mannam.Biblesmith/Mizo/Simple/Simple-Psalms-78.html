1. Aw ka mite u, ka dan thu-ah hian beng lo chhi ua: Ka kaa thu-ahte hian in beng rawn dawh rawh u.
2. Tehkhin thuin ka ka ka ang ang a; Hmasanga thu ril sawite kha ka sawi chhuak ang:
3. Chung chu ka bengin kan hria a, kan rilruin kan hre bawk a, Min thlahtute pawhin min hrilh kha.
4. Chung chu an fate lakah kan zep lo ang a, Thangthar lo la awm tur hnenah LALPA fakna thu leh, A chakzia leh a thilmak tak tihziate chu kan hril zawk ang.
5. Jakob-ho zingah dan a ruat a, Chung chu thlahtute hnenah thupekin a pe a Anni chuan chung thu chu an fate hnenah an hriattir turin:
6. Thangthar lo la awm tur, naupang lo la piang turte chuan, an hriat theihna'n; Chungte chu an thawh a, an fate an la hrilh chhawn zelna turin:
7. An beiseina chu Pathianah an nghah theih a, Pathian thiltihte chu an theihnghilh loh a, A thupekte chu an zawm zawkna'n:
8. Leh, an thlahtute anga, Chhuan luhlul leh helhmang; Chhuan thinlung siam dik lo, Pathian laka rilru rinawm lo pua an awm lohna'n.
9. Ephraima thlahte chu ralthuama inthuam a, thal ngul kengin, Inbei niah chuan an tlanchhia a.
10. Pathian thuthlung chu an zawm lo a. A danah awm an duh lo bawk a,
11. A thiltihte chu an theihnghilh a, A thilmak tih a hmuhtirte nen khan.
12. An thlahtute mithmuhah thilmak takte a ti thin, Aigupta ramah, Zoanphulahchuan.
13. Tuifinriat chu a tithen phawka, anni chu a kaltir a; Tuite chu englo dah khawm thur angin a dintir luah a.
14. Chhunah chuan chhumin a hruai a. Zankhuain mei engin a hruai bawk thin a.
15. Thlalerah chuan lungpuite a tiphel a, Tui thuk tak ata ang mai hnianghnara in tur a pe a.
16. Lungpui atang chuan luite a tichhuak a, Tuite chu luipuite angin a luan thlaktir a.
17. Chuti chung pawhin a chungah thil an lo tisual ta cheu a, Ram rovah chuan Chungnungbera chungah an hel a.
18. An chakna tihrenna'na ei tur dilin, An thinlungin Pathian an fiah a.
19. A ni, Pathian an sawi hnawm a; "Thlalerah hian Pathianin dawhkan a buatsaih thei emaw chu?
20. Ngai teh, lungpui chu a vua a, tuite kha a lo khawh chhuakin, Luite chu a kuang a liam dul dul ta tak a; Chaw pawh a pe thei nghal emaw chu? A mite tan hian sa a buatsaih ang emaw chu?" an ti a.
21. Chutichuan LALPA chuan a lo hria a, a thin a rim ta a: Jakob-ho chunga mei a lo alh chhuak a, Israel-ho chungah thinurna a lo chho ta a;
22. Pathian an rin loh a, A chhandamna pawh an rin loh avangin.
23. Nimahsela chung lam van dumte khi thu a pe a, Van kawngkharte khi a hawnsak a;
24. An ei atan manna kha an chungah a zuk surtir a, Van buh chu a pe a ni.
25. Mihringten mi chake chhang chu an ei a: Puarna khawpin chaw a rawn pe a.
26. Khaw chhak thli chu vanah a tlehtir a: A thiltihtheihnain chhim thli chu a hruai a.
27. Sa chu vaivut zat laiin an chungah sa surtir a, Sava thla nei chu tuifinriat tiau vaivut zat laiin:
28. An riahhmun laiah a tlaktir a, An awmna buk kiiang velahte chuan.
29. Chutichuan an ei a, an puar hle a; An chaka chu a pe ta a ni.
30. An chak a la reh lo a. An chaw chu an kaah a la awm a,
31. Chu mi lai tak chuan Pathian thinurna chu an chungah a chho a, An mi nalh nalhte thenkhat a that a, Israel tlangvalte chu an vawthlu hlawm a.
32. Chuti chung pawhin a chungah thil an lo tisual ta cheu a, A thilmak tihte chu an ring chang lo.
33. Chuvangin an dam chhung nite chu engmah lo ang maiin a tihralsak a, An dam chhung kumte chu hlauhnain a tihralsak bawk a.
34. Anmahni a thah apiangin amah chu an zawng thin a. An kir leh a, zing takah Pathian an zawng thin a.
35. Pathian chu an lungpui a ni tih leh Pathian Chungnungbera chu an tlantu a ni tih an hre chhuak thin.
36. Nimahsela amah chu an kain an fak der a, An leiin an hnenah dawt thu an sawi thin.
37. An thinlung chu a lakah a dik si lo a, An thuthlungah chuan a rinawm hek lo.
38. Nimahsela, ani chu lainatnaa a khah avangin an khawlohna chu ngaidam a, anni chu a tiboral lo: A ni vawi tam tak a thinurna chu a la kir a, A thinrimna chu azain a chawk tho duh thin lo.
39. Tisa chauh an ni tih a hre reng a; Thli, let leh lo tura tleh liam thin ang hi.
40. Thlalerah khana chungah an va hei zingin, Ram rovah khan amah chu an va tilungngai zing em!
41. An hawi kir lehin Pathian an fiah a, Israel-ho Mi Thianghlim chu an ti vui thin.
42. A kut chu an hre reng lo a, Dotu laka a chhanhim ni chu an hre reng thin hek lo.
43. Khatia Aigupta rama a chinchhiahnate a dah a, Zoan phulah a thilmakte a dah bawk a;
44. An luipuite chu thisenah a chantir a, An luite chu thisenah a chantir bawk kha, tichuan an in thei ta lo a.
45. An zingah tho tam tak a tir a, chung chuan anmahni a ei zo a; Chungute a tir bawk a, chung chuan anmahni a tiboral ta a.
46. An thil tote pawh chu pangang a pe a, An thawh rim tah chu khaukhuap a pe bawk a.
47. An grep hruite chu rialin a tithi a, An theipui kungte pawh chu vurin a tithi bawk a.
48. An rante pawh chu rial a pe a, An ran rualte chu tek sa tak a pe bawk a.
49. A thinur nasa tak chu an chungah a tlak a, Thinrimna te, lungnih loh em emna te, manganna te hi, thil tha lo tak thlentu tirhkoh pawl chu.
50. A thinurna atan chuan kawng a siamsak a, A nunna chu thihna lakah a hmum lo a, An nun chu hri a pe ta zawk a;
51. Aigupta rama fa tir awm zawng zawng a tihlum a, Hama puan intea an chakna rah hmasa berte chu:
52. Nimahsela ama mite chu beram angin a hruai chhuak a, Ran rual angin thlalerah khan a hruai a.
53. Him takin a hruai a, chutichuan engmah an hlau lo va: Nimahsela tuifinriat chuan an hmelmate chu a khuh zo ta a.
54. Tin, a hmun thianghlim ramri chu a hruai thleng a, He tlang, a kut ding lama a leia hi.
55. Hnam tin chu an hmaah a hnawt chhuak a, Anni chu hruia tehin rochan atan a sem a, An puan inahte chuan Israel hnamte chu a awmtir ta a.
56. Nimahsela, Pathian Chungnungbera chuu an fiah a, a chungah an hel a, A thuhriattirte chu an zawm si lo a;
57. Hawi kirin, an thlahtute angin vervek takin an ti ta zawk a: Thal ngul rinawm lo angin an kal kawi a.
58. An hmun sangtein amah chu an tithinur si a, An milem siamtein an thiktir bawk a.
59. Pathian chu chu a lo hriatin a thin a rim a, Israel-ho chu a ten ta em em a:
60. Chutichu a Silo biak buk, Mihring zinga a puan in dah chu, a kalsan a;
61. A chakna chu salah a mantir a, A ropuina chu dotu kutah a mantir ta bawk a.
62. A mite chu khandaih hnenah a pe a; A rochan chungah chuan a thin a rim ta a.
63. Meiin an tlangvalte chu a kangral a, An nulate chuan inneihna hla reng reng an nei ta lo.
64. An puithiamte chu khandaiha tihtlukin an awm a; An hmeithaite chu tah reng reng an tap lo.
65. Chutih lai chuan LALPA chu, mi an muhil an harh angin, a harh ta a, Mi chak uain vanga au ang hian.
66. Amah dotute chu a vaw kir a, Kumkhaw hming chhiatna a hlawhtir ta a.
67. Chu lo pawh chu Josefa puan in chu a duh lo a, Ephraim hnam chu a thlang lo bawk a;
68. Juda hnam chu a thlang ta zawk a, A hmangaih Zion tlang chu.
69. A in thianghlim chu van sangte ang khian a sa a, Kumkhua atana a tihngheh leilung ang hian.
70. A chhiahhlawh Davida chu a thlang a, beram huang atate chu ala chhuak a:
71. berampui no hruaite zuite atangin a hruai chhuak a, A mite Jakob-ho leh a rochan Israel-ho chawm turin.Chutichuan ani chuan a thinlung thatna ang zelin anniho chu a chawm a; A kut thiamnain a hruaitaa.
72. Chutichuan ani chuan a thinlung thatna ang zelin anniho chu a chawm a; A kut thiamnain a hruaitaa.
