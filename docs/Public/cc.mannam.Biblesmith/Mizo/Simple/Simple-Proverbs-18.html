1. Mi inkulh hrang mi chuan mahni duhzawng a zawng thin a. Finna dik zawng zawng a tai thin.
2. Mi a chuan hriatnaah lawmna a nei lo, ama rilruin a tihlan chauh lo chu.
3. Suahsualna a lo awmin hmustina pawh a lo awm thin, mualphona a lo awmin zilhhauna.
4. Mi a kaah thu chu tui thuk tak ang, finna lui, lui luang ang a ni a.
5. Mi suaksual duhsak chu a tha lo a, rorelnaa mi fel hnawl chu a tha bawk hek lo.
6. Mi a hmui chuan inhauna a thlen a, a ka chuan vuakna a ko.
7. Mi a ka chu a boralna a ni a, a hmui chu a nunna thang a ni.
8. Sesea thu sawi hmang mi thu chu chaw tui tak ang a ni a, pum chhungril berah a lut thin.
9. A hnathawha mi dawngdah pawh tiboraltua unaupa a ni.
10. LALPA hming chu kulpui a ni a, mi fel chu a tlan lut thin a, a him thin.
11. Mi hausa chuan a sum chu a kulhpui a ni a, a suangtuah danah chuan kulh sang tak a ni.
12. Mihring rilru chapona chu boralna hmaah a kal a, inngaihtlawmna chu chawimawina hmaah.
13. Ngaihthlaka hmaa chhan chu a tan atthlak nihna leh mualphona a ni.
14. Mi rilru nei chuan a chak lohna chu a tuar zo ang a, mahse rilru hliam chu tu nge tuar thei ang?
15. Remhre thinlung chuan hriatna a dawng thin a, mi fing beng chuan hriatna a zawng thin.
16. Mi a thilpekin kawng a tihkuaksak a, mi ropuite hmaah a hruai lut thin.
17. A thiamna thu sawi hmasatu chu felin a lang thin a, mahse a thenawmte an lo kal a, an rawn tilang thin.
18. Thumvawrin inhauna a tibang a, mi chakte a tithen thin.
19. I unau pui la, kulhpui angin a hum ang che; in inhau erawh chuan, a inkhar hnan ang che.
20. Miina pumpui chu a ka rahin a tikhat ang a, a kaa thil hawngin a titlai ang.
21. Lei thiltihtheihnaah chuan thihna leh nunna a awm a, a ngainatute chuan chu mia rah chu an ei ang.
22. Nupui hmu chuan thil tha a hmu a ni, LALPA duhsakna a chang thin.
23. Mi rethei chuan a thlemin an thlem a, mi hausa erawh chu rura takin an tawng thin.Thian tam tak siam chuan mahni intihchhiatna a siam a ni a, amaherawhchu unau aia nghet zawk thian an awm thin.
24. Thian tam tak siam chuan mahni intihchhiatna a siam a ni a, amaherawhchu unau aia nghet zawk thian an awm thin.
