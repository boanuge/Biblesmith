1. Aw mi thiltithei tak fate u, LALPA hnenah pe u la; LALPA hnenah chuan ropuina leh chakna pe rawh u.
2. LALPA hnenah a hming ropuina chu pe u la; Thianghlim mawina inbelin LALPA chu chibai buk rawh u.
3. LALPA aw chu tui chungahte chuan a ri a: Ropuina Pathian chu a lo ri dur dur a, Lalpa ngei chu tui tam tak chungahte a lo ri a.
4. LALPA aw chu thiltithei tak a ni a; LALPA aw chu ropui ta a ni.
5. LALPA aw chuan sidar thingte chu a titliak ang a; A ni, LALPA chuan Lebanon tlanga sidar thingte chu a titliak seng sung thin.
6. Bawng no tualchai angin a chettir thin a; Lebanon leh Sirion tlange chu sele no ang maiin.
7. LALPA aw chuan meialhte a phel thin.
8. LALPA aw chuan thlaler a nghawr nghing a; LALPA chuan Kadesh thlaler chu a tinghing thin.
9. LALPA aw chuan zukpuite chu no a chhiattir a, Ngawpui thin ghnahte chu a nuai kawlh vek thin a: A biak inah chuan thlengkimin, "A ropui e," an ti theuh thin.
10. LALPA chu Tuilet lai khan lalah a thu a, A ni, LALPA chu kumkhua atan lalah a thu a ni.LALPA chuan a mite hnenah chakna a pe ang a, LALPA chuan a mite chu thlamuannain mal a sawm ang.
11. LALPA chuan a mite hnenah chakna a pe ang a, LALPA chuan a mite chu thlamuannain mal a sawm ang.
