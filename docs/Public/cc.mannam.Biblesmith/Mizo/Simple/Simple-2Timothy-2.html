1. Chutichuan, nang ka fa, Krista Isuaa khawngaihna awmah hian thiltihtheihna neiin awm ang che.
2. Tin, hretu tam takte zinga ka kaa thu i hriatte kha, chung chu mi rinawm, mi dangte zirtir chhawng thei tur angte hnenah chuan kawltir rawh.
3. Krista Isua sipai tha tak angin hrehawm mi tuarpui rawh.
4. Sipaia awm tura thlangtu chu a tihlawm theihna'n, sipai tumah indo laiin tun damchhung khawsaknate hian a intihnawk ngai lo a.
5. Tin, intihsiaknaahte pawh chuan miin bei mah se la, dan angin a beih loh chuan khumtirin a awm ngai lo a.
6. Lo neitu thawk rim chu a rah chang hmasatu a ni tur a ni.
7. Ka thusawi hi ngaihtuah rawh; Lalpa chuan kawng engkimah hriat theihna a pe dawn si chia.
8. Isua Krista, Davida thlaha mi, ka Chanchin Tha sawi anga mitthi zing ata tho leha kha hre reng rawh;
9. chu Chanchin Tha avang chuan thil tisualtu angin phuar khawp hialin hrehawmte ka tuar thin a ni; nimahsela Pathian thu chu phuarin a awm si lo.
10. Chuvangin, Krista Isuaa awm chhandamna chu chatuana ropuina nen an hmuh ve theihna turin, Thlante avang chuan engkim ka tuar thin.
11. He thu hi a rinawm a ni: "A thihnaah kan thih tawh si chuan, a nunnah kan nung bawk ang;
12. Kan tuar chuan a rorelnaah ro kan rel ang; Amah kan phatsan chuan Ani pawhin min phatsan ang;
13. Keini rinawm loin awm mah i la, Ani chu rinawmin a awm reng a ni; Amah leh amah a inphat thei si lo a," tih hi.
14. Chung thu chu hriatpui la, thu mai mai chunga an inhnial lohna'n Lalpa mithmuhah thahnemngai takin hriattir rawh, chu chu engmah a sawt lo a, a ngaithlatute tan boralna a ni zawk thin.
15. Hnathawktu, thutak thu fel taka hmang chu zak tur a ni lo angin, Pathian ngaiha thaa inentir turin thahnem ngai rawh.
16. Amaherawhchu, Pathian zah lo lam thu sawi mai mai chu hawisan thin ang che; chutiang mite chu Pathianngaihsak lohna lamah an nasa lehzual ang a,
17. an thu pawh chuan pan chhe ei angin a ei zel ang; an zingah chuan Humenaia leh Phileta an tel a ni;
18. chung mite chuan, "Thawhlehna chu tunah pawh a ni tawh daih," tiin thutak kawng chu an kal kawisan a, mi thenkhatte rinna an tibo thin a.
19. Nimahsela, Pathian lungphum nghet chu a la ding reng a, he chhinchhiahna hi a nei a: "Lalpa chuan ama tate chu a hria," tih leh, "Lalpa hming lam apiang chuan sual kalsan rawh se," tih hi.
20. Nimahsela, in ropuiah hian bungbel, rangkachak leh tangkaruaa siam mi chauh a awm lo a, thing leh belleia siam mite pawh a awm thin a; thenkhatte ngaihhlut tur a awm bawk a, thenkhatte chu ngaihhlut loh tur a awm bawk a.
21. Chuvangin, chung thil laka mi a inthiarfihlim chuan bungbel thianghlim, Lalpa hman tlak, hnathawh tha tinrenga inpeih sa, ngaihhlut tur mi a ni ang.
22. Nimahsela, tleirawl chaknate chu tlansan la, thinlung thianghlima Lalpa lam apiangte zingah chuan felna te, rinna te, hmangaihna te, remna te um ve rawh.
23. Amaherawhchu, indonate a hring thin tih hriain, inhnialna atthlak leh fing lovahte chuan tel duh suh.
24. Lalpa bawih chuan do tur a ni si lo a, mi zawng zawng chungah zaidamin, zirtir thiamin, sual chungah dawhtheiin,
25. do tura insiamte chu thuhnuairawlh takin a zirtir zawk tur a ni; chutichuan Pathianin thutak chu an hriat famkimna'n a simtir hlauhin,Pathian duhzawng ti atan Lalpa bawihin a manin, Diabola thang ata an chhuak thei mahna.
26. Pathian duhzawng ti atan Lalpa bawihin a manin, Diabola thang ata an chhuak thei mahna.
