1. Tin, mi thianghlimte tana thawhlawm thu kha zawng, Galatia rama kohhranho pawl hnenahte thu ka pek angin nangni pawhin lo ti ve rawh u.
2. Ni sarih ni hmasa berah chuan mi tinin, malsawmna in hmuh angzia zelin dah khawl theuh u la; ka lo kal huna thawhlawm khawn reng reng a awm lohna'n.
3. Tin, in khawngaih thilpek chu Jerusalem khuaa kalpui turin ka lo thlen hunin tupawh tha in tih apiang lehkhate kengin ka tir ang.
4. Tin, ka kal vena awm a nih phei chuan ka hnenah an kal ang.
5. Tin, Makedonia ram ka fan chhuah hnuah chuan in hnenah ka lo kal ang; Makedonia ram chu ka fang reng reng dawn si a.
6. Nangni hnenah erawh chuan ka cham reng a ni thei e, thlasik chhung zawng pawh a ni mahna, khawiah pawh ka kalna tur apiangah nangnin mi thlah theihna turin.
7. Tuna kal pah maia hmuh che u ka duh lo a, Lalpa'n rem a tih chuan in hnenah rei deuh cham ka beisei si a.
8. Nimahsela, Pentikost thlengin Ephesi khuaah hian ka cham reng ang,
9. kawngka zau tak leh sawt tak ka tan a inhawng si a,dotute tam tak an awm bawk.
10. Tin, Timothea kha a lo thlen chuan ti loa in hnena a awmna'n fimkhur rawh u; keiin ka thawh ang bawk hian Lalpa hna chu a thawk si a.
11. Chuvangin, tuma'n hmusit suh se; ka hnena a lo kal theihna'n thlamuang takin thlah zawk ang che u; unaute rualin amah lo hmuh ka beisei si a.
12. Kan unau Apollova thu kha chu, unaute ruala in hnena lo kal turin ka ngen chiam a, nimahsela tunah zawng lo kal a duh hauh lo a; a remchan hunah erawh chun a lo kal ang.
13. Inveng u la, rinnaah chuan ding nghet u la, intihuai u la, chak takin awm rawh u.
14. In tih apiang chu hmangaiha tih ni vek rawh se.
15. Unaute u, Stephana chhungte chu Akaia rama awih hmahruaitute an ni a, mi thianghlimte rawngbawl turin an inpe tih in hria e;
16. chutiang mite hnenah leh, hnathawha tanpui leh, thawk rim apiangte hnenah chuan intulut turin ka ngen a che u.
17. Tin, Stephana te, Phortunata te, Akaika te an lo kal hi ka lawm a ni, in awm loh pawh lang loin mi siam avangin.
18. Anni hian ka rilru leh in rilru an tiharh si a, chutichuan hetiang mite hi lawm rawh u.
19. Asia ram kohhranho pawlte hian chibai an buk a che u. Akuila leh Priskilli, an ina kohhran inkhawmho nen hian Lalpaah chuan chibai tam takin an buk a che u.
20. Unau zawng zawng hian chibai an buk a che u. Fawh thianghlimin chibai inbuk theuh rawh u.
21. Kei Paulan keima kut ngeia chibai ka bukna che u a nih hi.
22. Tupawhin Lalpa a hmangaih loh chuan anchhe dawngin awm rawh se. Kan Lalpa chu a lo kal e.
23. Lalpa Isua Krista khawngaihna chu in hnenah awm rawh se.Krista Isuaa ka hmangaihna hi inzaa hnenah awm rawh se. Amen.
24. Krista Isuaa ka hmangaihna hi inzaa hnenah awm rawh se. Amen.
