1. Mipui hotute chu Jerusalem-ah an awm a; sawm zinga pakhat zel khawpui thianghlim Jerusalem-a awmtir tur leh sawm zinga pakua, khaw danga awm turte hriatna'n a bak mipuite pawh chuan thum an vawr a.
2. Lungni taka Jerusalem-a awm tura inpe zawng zawngte chuan mipuite fak an hlawh a.
3. Hengte hi chu mi ram hotu Jerusalem-a awmte chu an ni: amaherawhchu Judai ram khuaahte chuan, an khuaa mahni chan tur theuhvah an awm a; chungte chu Israel-ho te, puithiamho te, Levi-ho te, Nethin-ho leh Solomona chhiahhlawhte thlah an ni.
4. Jerusalem-ah chuan Juda thlaha mi leh Benjamina thlaha mite an awm. Juda thlahte zinga mi: Pereza thlahte zinga mi, Mahalalela fapa Sephatia, chu mi fapa Amaria, chu mi fapa Sekaria, chu mi fapa Athaia, chu mi fapa Uzia leh
5. Silo mi fapa Zekaria, chu mi fapa Joiariba, chu mi fapa Adaia, chu mi fapa Hazaia, chu mi fapa Kolhozea, chu mi fapa Baruka, chu mi fapa Maseia.
6. Pereza thlah, Jerusalem-a awm zawng zawngte chu mi huaisen 468 an ni.
7. Tin, Benjamin thlahte zinga mi chu hengte hi an ni: Jesaia fapa Ithiela, chu mi fapa Maseia, chu mi fapa Kolaia, chu mi fapa Pedaia, chu mi fapa Joeda, chu mi fapa Mesulama, chu mi fapa Salua.
8. A dawt lehah chuan Gebaia te, Salaia te, an zain Benjamina thlah Jerusalem-a awmte chu mi 928 an ni.
9. Tin, Zikria fapa Joela chu an hotu a ni; Hasenua fapa, Juda chu khaw hotu ber dawttu a ni.
10. Puithiamte zinga mi; Joiariba fapa Jedaia te, Jakina te,
11. Pathian in hotu Ahituba fapa Meraiotha, chu mi fapa Zadoka, chu mi fapa Mesulama, chu mi fapa Hilkia, chu mi fapa Seraia te;
12. an unau, ina rawngbawltute zawng zawng nen 822 an ni a: tin, Malkija fapa Pasura, chu mi fapa Zekaria, chu mi fapa Amzia, chu mi fapa Pelalia, chu mi fapa Jerohama, chu mi fapa Adaia leh
13. a unaute, an chhungkaw puipate nen 242 an ni a: tin, Imera fapa Mesilemotha, chu mi fapa Azarela, chu mi fapa Amasaia leh
14. an unau mi chak huaisente nen 128 an ni; an hotu chu Hegedolima fapa Zabdiela a ni a.
15. Tin, Levi-hote zinga mi; Bunia fapa Hasabia, Hasuba, chu mi fapa Semaia te;
16. Levi-ho puipate zinga mi, Pathianin pawn lam hna enkawltu Sabethaia leh Jozabada te;
17. Asapah fapa Zabdia, chu mi fapa Mika, chu mi fapa, tawngtaia lawm thu sawi tantu ber, Matania e; a unaute zinga a dawttu Bakbukia te; Jeduthuna fapa Galala, chu mi fapa Samua, chu mi fapa Abda e,
18. Khawpui thianghlima Levi-ho awm zawng zawng chu 284 an ni.
19. Chung lote pawh chu kawngkhar vengtu Akuba te, Talmona te leh an unaute kulh kawngka vengtute, 172 an ni a.
20. A bak Israelte leh puithiamte leh Levi-hote chu Judai ram khaw tinah mahni ram theuhvah an awm a.
21. Nethin-ho erawh chu Ophel-ah an awm a: Ziha leh Gispa chu Nethin-ho hotu an ni a.
22. Jerusalem khuaa Levi-hote hotu chu Asapha fa, hla satute zinga mi Mika, chu mi fapa Matania, chu mi fapa Hasabia, chu mi fapa Bania, chu mi fapa Uzia a nia, Pathian in lam hna ngaihtuahtu a ni.
23. An chungchang thuah lal thupek a awm a, hla satute chu ni tina an tla tawk ruat tur thu a awm bawk si a.
24. Juda fapa Zera thlaha mi Mesezabela fapa Pethahia chu mipuite chungchang thu-ah engkima lal aiawh a ni a.
25. Tin, an khua leh an ram thuah chuan, Juda thlah thenkhatte chu Kiriat-arba leh a khawperah te, Dibon leh khawperah te, Zekabzela leh a khawperah e;
26. Jesua-ah leh Molada-ah leh Betpelet-ah te;
27. tin, Hazarsual-ah te, Beer-seba leh a khawperah te,
28. Ziklag-ah te, Mekona leh a khawperah te,
29. Enrimon-ah te, Adulam-ah leh an khawperah te, Lakis leh a ramah te, Azeka leh a khawperah te an awm a. Chutichuan Beer-seba atangin Hinom ruam thlengin awm hmun an khuar a.
30. Tin, Benjamina thlahte chu Geba piah lam Mikmas-ah te, Aija leh Bethel leh a khawperah te;
31. Anathot-ah te, Nob-ah te, Anania-ah te:
32. Hazor-ah te, Rama-ah te, Gitaim-ah te;
33. Hadid-ah te, Zeboim-ah te, Nebalat-ah te;
34. Lod-ah leh themthiamte ruam rama awm thin Levi-ho pawl thenkhatte chu Benjamin ramah an tel ta a.
35. Lod-ah leh themthiamte ruam Ono-ah te an awm a.Tin, Judai rama awm thin Levi ho pawl thenkhatte chu Benjamin ramah an tel a.
36. Tin, Judai rama awm thin Levi ho pawl thenkhatte chu Benjamin ramah an tel a.
