1. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia a bia a,
2. "Israela thlahte hnenah, 'Hmeichhiain nau a paia fapa a hrin chuan ni sarih a bawlhhlawh ang a; hmeichhe dan pangngai hun laia a bawlhhlawh ang chuan a bawlhhlawh ang.
3. Tin, a ni riat niin naute serh hmawr vun tan tur a ni.
4. Thisen avanga inthianin ni sawmthum leh ni thum a la awm cheu tur a ni; a inthen ni a kim hma loh chuan thil thianghlim reng reng a khawih tur a ni lo a, hmun thianghlimah pawh a lut tur a ni hek lo.
5. Hmeichhia a hrin erawh chuan hmeichhe dan pangngai hun laia a bawlhhlawh angin chawlhkar hnih a bawlhhlawh ang; tin, thisen avanga inthianin ni sawmruk leh ni ruk a la awm cheu tur a ni.
6. Tin, fapa emaw fanu emaw avanga a inthenna ni a kim veleh halral thilhlanah beram no kum khat mi leh sual thawina'n parva no emaw, thuro emaw inbiakna puan in kawngka bulah puithiam hnenah a rawn keng tur a ni:
7. Tin, anin LALPA hmaah a hlan ang a, inremna a siamsak ang a, tichuan a thisen chhuahna ata tihthianghlim a ni ang. Hei hi mipa emaw hmeichhia emaw hringtu tana dan a ni.Tin, a neihin beram no a tlin loh chuan thuro tuak khat emaw, parva no tuak khat emaw a keng tur a ni: pakhat chu halral thilhlanah, pakhat chu sual thawina'n: tin, puithiamin inremna a siamsak ang a, tichuan a thianghlim tawh ang,' tiin sawi rawh," tiin.
8. Tin, a neihin beram no a tlin loh chuan thuro tuak khat emaw, parva no tuak khat emaw a keng tur a ni: pakhat chu halral thilhlanah, pakhat chu sual thawina'n: tin, puithiamin inremna a siamsak ang a, tichuan a thianghlim tawh ang,' tiin sawi rawh," tiin.
