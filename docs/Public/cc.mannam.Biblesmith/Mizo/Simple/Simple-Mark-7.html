1. Tin, Pharisaite leh lehkhaziaktute zinga mi tute emaw, Jerusalem khua atanga lo kalte chu a hnenah an rawn inkhawm a.
2. Chunghote chuan a zirtir thenkhatte kut bawlhhlawha chhang an ei an lo hmu tawh a. ('Bawlhhlawh' tih hi 'sil loh' tihna a ni.).
3. (Pharisaite leh Judaho zawng zawng chuan upate thurochhiah chu an pawm tlat avangin an kut ngun taka an sil loh chuan an ei ngai si lo a.
4. Tin, dawr hmun atanga an hawn pawhin an inbual loh chuan an ei ngai lo. Tin, thu dang tam tak pawm tlat tura an dawn chhawnte chu a awm, no te, bel te, dar bel te sil angte hi.)
5. Chutichuan Pharisaite leh lehkhaziaktute chuan, "Engati nge i zirtirte upa thurochhiah ang zela awm lo a, kut bawlhhlawhin chhang an ei thin ni?" tiin an zawt a.
6. Tin, ani chuan an hnenah, "Isaia hian nangni mi vervekte thu hi a lo hril lawk dik zet mai. 'He miteho hian an kain mi chawimawi a, An thinlung erawh chuan mi hlat si a.
7. Engpawhni se la, an zirtirnaa mihring thupekte an zirtir avangin an mi biak thin hi a thlawn mai mai a ni," tih ziak ang hian.
8. Pathian thupekte chu thlahthlamin mihring thurochhiah chu in vuan tlat a nih hi," a ti a.
9. Tin, amah vekin an hnenah, "Nangmahni thurochhiahte chu in pawm theihna turin Pathian thupekte in duh lo hi rem in hre zet mai e!
10. Mosia'n, "I nu leh i pa chawimawi rawh,' 'Tupawh a nu emaw, a pa emaw sawichhia apiang chu thi mawlh rawh se,' a ti si a;
11. nangni erawh chuan heti hian in ti thin, 'Miin, a nu emaw, a pa emaw hnenah, I mi chhawrna tur hi Korban a ni tawh, a tih chuan,' (chu chu 'Pathian hnena pek,' tihna a ni a)
12. a nu tan emaw, a pa tan emaw engmah tihsak in phal tawh thin lo;
13. mi hnena in thurochhiah pek chhawn hianin Pathian thu hi in bawh bo thin a ni; chutiang thil chu tam tak in ti thin," a ti a.
14. Tin, mipuite chu a ko leh a, an hnenah, "In zain ka thu hi ngaithla theuh u la, lo ngai thiam rawh u:
15. mihring pawn lam ata a kawchhunga luta amah tibawlhhlawh thei engmah a awm lo; mihring kawchhung ata lo chhuakte chu mihring tibawlhhlawhtu a ni zawk e," a ti a.
16. Tu pawhin hriatna tura beng an neih chuan hre rawh se
17. Tin, mipuihote chu ina a luhsan hnuin, azirtirte chuan tehkhin thu chu amah an zawt fiah ta a.
18. Tin, ani chuan an hnenah, "Nangni pawh chuti ema hriatna nei lo in ni maw?
19. Engpawh pawn lam ata mihring kawchhunga lut apiang, a thinlung chhunga lut loin, a pum chhunga luta dailenaa a chhuah zawk avangin amah chu a tibawlhhlawh thei lo tih in hre lo'm ni?" a ti a. (Chu chu ei tur tinreng ei a thiang a tihna a ni.)
20. Tin, amah vekin, "Mihring kawchhung ata lo chhuak chuan, chu ngei chuan mihring a tibawlhhlawh thin a ni.
21. Kawchhunga mi, mihring thinlung atangte chuan ngaihtuahna sualte a lo chhuak si thin; inngaihna te, rukrukna te, tualthahna te,
22. uirena te, awhna te, sualna te, bumna te, hurna te, itsikna te, sawichiatna te, chapona te, atna te hi;
23. chung thil sual zawng zawngte chu kawchhung atangin a lo chhuak a, mihring chu a tibawlhhlawh thin," a ti a.
24. Tin, a tho a, chuta tang chuan Tura leh Sidon bialah a kal ta a; tin, inah a lut a, tuma hriat a duh lo a; nimahsela a inthup thei si lo.
25. Chutichuan hmeichhe pakhat, fanu naupang te, ramhuai bawlhhlawh pai nei chuan a chanchin a lo hria a, a ke bulah a va bawkkhup ta nghal a.
26. Hmeichhia chu Grik mi a ni a, pianna lamah chuan Suro-Phoiniki mi a ni a. Ani chuan a fanu kawchhung ata ramhuai hnawt chhuak turin a ngen a.
27. Tin, ani chuan a hnenah, "Fate chu lo puar hmasa rawh se; fate chhang laksaka uite hnena paih chu a mawi lo e," a ti a.
28. Ani chuan a hnenah, "Lalpa, nih zawng a ni a, nimahsela dawhkan hnuaia uite pawhin fate chhang nawite chu an ei thin alawm," tiin a chhang a.
29. Tin, ani chuan a hnenah, "I thu sawi avang chuan kal tawh le; ramhuai chu i fanu kawchhung ata a chhuak tawh e," a ti a.
30. Tin, a inah chuan a va hawng a, a fa chu ramhuai lo chhuahsan tawh a, khuma mu a va hmu ta a.
31. Tin, Tura ram atangin a chhuak leh a, Sidon khua tlawhin Dekapoli ram kal tlang zelin Galili dil chu a thleng ta a.
32. Tin, mi pakhat bengngawng, tawng pawh tawng fiah thei lo, a hnenah an rawn hruai a, a chunga a kut nghat turin an ngen a.
33. Tin, ani chuan mipui zing ata amah chauh chu a hruai hrang a, a kut zungtangte chuan a beng kuate a khawih a, tin chil a chhak a, a lei a dehsak a;
34. tin, van lam enin a thaw huai a, a hnenah, "Ephatha," a ti a. (Chu chu "Lo inhawng rawh," tihna a ni.)
35. Tichuan a bengte chu a lo inhawng a, a lei phuarna pawh chu a lo inphelh a, fiah takin a tawng thei ta a.
36. Tin, ani chuan an hnenah, tumah hrilh loh tur a ti tlat a; nimahsela khap nasat veleh an tlangaupui zual zawk mah thin a."Ani chuan engkim tha takin a ti a; bengngawngte a tihvarsak a, tawng thei lote pawh a titawng thin," an ti a, mak an ti nasa em em a.
37. "Ani chuan engkim tha takin a ti a; bengngawngte a tihvarsak a, tawng thei lote pawh a titawng thin," an ti a, mak an ti nasa em em a.
