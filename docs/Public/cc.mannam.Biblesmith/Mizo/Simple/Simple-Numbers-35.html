1. Tin, Jeriko zawn Jordan kam, Moab phaiah chuan LALPA'n Mosia a bia a,
2. "Israela thlahte chu thu pe la, an ramahte chuan Levi-ho awmna tur khuae siamsak rawh se; tin, ran tlatna turin khaw daikiang velte Levi-ho in pe tur a ni.
3. Kuate chu an awmna tur ani ang a, a velte chu an bawng leh an ran, an thil vulh zawng zawng tan a ni ang.
4. Tin, daikiang Levi-ho in pek tur chu khaw kulh atanga tawng 1,000-a thui ani tur a ni.
5. Tin, kulh pawn lam atangin in teh ang a, chhak lampangah tawng 2,000 chhim lampangah tawn 2,000, thlang lampangah tawng 2,000, hmar lampangah tawng 2,000, khua a lai taka awmin. Hei hi an daikiang pual ani ang.
6. Tin, Lev-ho in pek tur khuate chu tualthatte tlanna'n tual tlanna khaw paruk a ni ang a; chung lo chu khaw sawmli leh khaw hnih in pe bawk tur a ni.
7. Levi-ho in pek tur khua zawng zawng chu khaw sawmli leh khaw riat a ni ang: daikiang nen in pe tur a ni.
8. Israela thlahte luah khua in pek dan tur chu, a tam deuh atangin in pe tam ang a, a tlem deuh atangin in pe tlem ang a, mi tinin an luah ram tam dan azirin an khua chu Levi-ho an pe tur a ni," tiin.
9. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia a bia a,
10. "Israela thlahte bia la, an hnenah, 'Jordan lui in kaia Kanaan ram in luh hun chuan
11. tualtlanna khua tur khua in inruat tur a ni, tualthat palhtu tlanna'n.
12. Chung khuate chu phubalatu laka in tualtlanna ani ang; ;ungkhawm hmaa rorelsak tura a din hmaa tualthattu chu a thih lohna'n.
13. Tichuan, in tan tualtlanna tur in ruatte chu khaw paruk a ni ang.
14. Jordan ralah khaw thum in ruat ang a, Kanaan ramah khaw thum in ruat ang a, tualtlana khuate ani ang.
15. Israela thlahte leh hnam dang leh an hnena chengte tan he khaw paruk hi tualtlana a ni ang: chutah chuan tupawh mi that apiang an tlan dawn nia.
16. Amaherawhchu thir hmanga mi a bei a, a thih chuan tualthat a ni a: tualthat chu tihhlum ngei tur a ni.
17. Tin, thi khawpa lunga mi a chhuta a thih chuan tualthat ani a, tualthat chu tihhlum ngei tur a ni.
18. Tin, thi khawpa mi thinga a vuak a, a thih chuan tualthat ani a, tualthat chu tihhlum ngei tur a ni.
19. Thisen phuba latu chuan tualthattu chu a that nghal mai ang: a hmuh hun apiangin a tihlum tur a ni.
20. Tin, mi ahuat vanga anam vak emaw, lo chang rua a vawm emaw a thihin emaw
21. intaina avanga chum hhlum emaw chuan, a tihlumtu chu tihhlum ngei tur a ni; tualthata ni : thisen phuba latu chuan a hmuh hun apiangin a tihlum tur a ni.
22. Amaherawhchu intai vang ni lem loa nam thut emaw,
23. tin, chang ni kher loa mi an awm tih pawh hre lo a, a hmelma pawh ni lo a, a pawi khawih pawh tum ni chuang loa lung emawa a den a, mi a lo thi ta a nih chuan
24. pungkhawmhoin he'ng rorelna dan ang hian a tihlumtu chu thisen phuba latu ro chu an relsak tur a ni.
25. Tin, pungkhawmho chuan tualthattu chu thisen phuba latu lak ata chu a hum ang a, pungkhawmho chuan atlanna, a tualtllan khuaah chuan an awmtir leh ang a, hriak thianghlima thih puithiam lal tanglai thih hma chu chutah chuan a awm tur a ni.
26. Amaherawhchu englai pawhin tualthattu chuan a tlanna, a tualtlanna kahw ramri pawn lama a kal a,
27. thisen phuba latuin a tualtlanna khaw panw a hmuha thisen phuba latuin a thah chuan thisenah chuan thiam oloh a chang lo ang,
28. puithiam lal a thih hma loh chu a tualtlanna khuaa awm reng tur anih avangn; puithiam a thih hnu erawh chuan tualthattu chu a awmna ramah chuan a haw leh tur a ni.
29. Heng thil hi in thlahte thlenga in awmna apiang in rorel dan tur ani ang.
30. Tupawhin mi a thah chuan tualthhattu chu hretute ka atanga thu chhuakin tihhlum tur a ni: amaherawhchu hretu pakhat chu mi tihhlumna tur chuan a tak lo.
31. Tin, tualthattu chu in tlan tur ani lo, thihna khawpa thiam looh chang a ni a, tihhlum ngei tur ani zawk.
32. tin, tualtlnna khuaa tlan chu chutih laia puithiam lal thih hma chuan a awmna khaw hluia kir leh tur tlan phal a ni lo ang.
33. Chutichuan in awmna ram chu in tibawlhhlawh lo ang a: thisen chuan ram chu a tibawlhhlawh a, thisen chhuahna ram tihbawlhlawh silna'n a chhuahtu thisen ngawt lo chu hman theih a ni si lo.In awmna ram, ka awmna hi in tibawlhlawh tur ani lo, kei LALPA chu Israela thlahte zingah ka awm si a,' ti rawh" tiin.
34. In awmna ram, ka awmna hi in tibawlhlawh tur ani lo, kei LALPA chu Israela thlahte zingah ka awm si a,' ti rawh" tiin.
