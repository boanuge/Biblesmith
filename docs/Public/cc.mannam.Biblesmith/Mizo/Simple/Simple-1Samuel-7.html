1. Tichuan Kiriath-jearim khuaa mite chu an zu kal a, LALPA bawm chu an la chho a, tlanga Abinadaba ina chuan a la lut a, a fapa Elazara chu LALPA bawm enkawltu atan an tihrang a.
2. Tin, heti hi ani a, Kiriath-jearim-a bawm a awm chung chu rei tak ani a. kum sawmhnih ze a ni: tin, Israel chungte zawng zawngin LALPA chu an lunglen leh ta a.
3. Tin, Samuela chuan Israel chungte zawng zawng hnenah chuan, in thinlung takzeta LALPA lam lo hawi in nih tak tak chuan, ram dang pathiante leh Asterothi-te chu in zing ata dah bo daih ta che u, in thinlung chu LALPA tan buatsaih u la, ama rawng chau bawl rawh u tichuan Philistia mite kut ata hi a chhanchhuak dawn che u nia," tiin a sawi a.
4. Tin, Israel mite chuan Baala-te leh Asterothi-te chu an dah bo ta a, LALPA rawng chauh chu an bawl ta a.
5. Tin, Samuela chuan, "Israel mi zawng zawng chu Mizpe-ah ko khawm vek u la, LALPA hnenah ka tawngtaisak ang che u," a ti a.
6. Tichuan Mizpe-ah an kal khawm ta a, tin, tui an chawi a, LALPA hmaah chuan an leihbua a, chu mi ni chuan chaw an nghei a, chutah chuan, "LALPA chunga chuan kan lo sual taa ni," an ti a. Tin, Samuela chuan Mizpe-ah chuan Israel mite ro a relsak a.
7. Tin, Philistia miten Israel mite Mizpe-ah chuan an awm khawm tih an riat chuan Israel mite bei turin Philistia lalhoe chu an thawk chho a, Chu chu Israel miten an lo hriat chuan Philistia mite chu an hlau a.
8. Tichuan Israel mite chuan Samuela hnenah, "Philistia mite kut ata min chhandam tura LALPA kan Pathian min auhsak chu bang suh khai," an ti a.
9. Tin, Samuela chuan beram no hnute hne lai a la a, pum hal atan LALPA hnenah ahlan a: Israel mite tan Samuela chuan LALPA chu a au va: tin, LALPA chuan a chhang a.
10. tin, Samuelan halral thilhlan a hlan lai tak chuan Philistia mite chuan Israel mite bei tur chuan an rawn hnaih a; nimahsela chu mi ni chuan LALPA'n Philistia mite chunga khawpui nasa tak maiin a riktir ta dur dur mai a, a tibuai ta nuai nuai a, Israel mite hmaah chuan an tlawm ta der mai a.
11. Tin, Israel mite chu Mizpe atang chuan an chuak a, Philistia mite chu an uma, Bethkar hnuai an thlen thlengin an that zel a.
12. Chutichuan Samuela chuan lung a la a, Mizpe leh Sen inkarah chuan a phun a, "Hei lehchenhi LALPA'n min pui a ni," tiin a hmingah chuan Eben-ezer a vuah a.
13. Chutihuan Philistia mite chu an tlawm ta a, Israelte ramah chuan an kal leh ta lo a; tin, LALPA kut chan Philistia mite chu Samuela dam chhung zawng chuan a tai ta reng a,
14. Tin, Philistia miten Israel mite khua an lakte chu Ekron atanga Gath thlengin Israel mite an pe kir leh a, a ram chu Israel miten Philisita mite kut ata an chhan chhuak a. Tin, Israel mite leh Amor mite chu inremin an awm a.
15. Tin, Samuela chuan a dam chungin Israel mite ro a rel a.
16. Tin, kum tinin Bethel leh Gilgal leh Mizpe hi a kan vel thin a; chung hnuahte chuan Israel mite ro a rel thin a.Tin, a in awmna a ni avangin Rama khuaa chuan a haw thin a; chutah chuan Israel mite ro a rel a, chuta chuan LALPA tan maicham a siam a.
17. Tin, a in awmna a ni avangin Rama khuaa chuan a haw thin a; chutah chuan Israel mite ro a rel a, chuta chuan LALPA tan maicham a siam a.
