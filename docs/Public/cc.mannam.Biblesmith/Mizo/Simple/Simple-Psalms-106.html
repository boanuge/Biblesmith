1. LALPA chu fak rawh u. Aw, LALPA hnenah lawm thu hril rawh u; ani chu a tha si a: A ngilneihna pawh kumkhuain a awm dawn si a.
2. Tunge LALPA thiltih ropui takte chu sawi thei a, A fakna zawng zawng hriattir vek thei le?
3. rorelna vawng thate chu an eng a thawl e, Englai pawha fel taka titu nen.
4. Aw LALPA, i mite i duhsaknain mi hre reng la, Aw, i chhandamna nen mi kan ang che:
5. I mi thlante nihlawhna ka hmuh theih a, I rochanho nena ka chhuan theihna'n.
6. Min thlahtute nen khan thil kan tisual a, Thil sual kan ti a, sual takin kan ti ta a.
7. Min thlahtute khan Aigupta rama i thilmak tihte kha an hre thiam lo a; I ngilneihna nasa tak kha an hre reng ta lo va: Tuifinriatah an hel ta zawk a, Tuifinriat Senah khan.
8. Chuti chung pawhin ama hming avangin anni chu a chhandam ta a, A thiltihtheihna ropui tak chu a hriattir theihna turin.
9. Tuifinriat Sen chu a zilhhau a, tichuan a kang ta a: Chutichuan anni chu thlalerah hruai tlang ang maiin tui thuk takahte chuan a hruai tlang ta a.
10. Anmahni hawtu kut ata chu a chhandam a, Hmelma kut ata chu a tlan ta a.
11. Tuite chuan anmahni dotute chu a khuh zo a, Pakhat mah dama chhuak an awm ta lo a.
12. Chu mi lai chuan a thute chu an ring a; A fakna hla an sa a.
13. Nimahsela a thiltihte ch an theihnghilh ta thuai a; A remruat chu an nghak peih lo va:
14. Thlalerah chuan thil an chak em em a, Ram rovah chuan Pathian an fiah ta zawk a.
15. An thil dil chu a pe a; Nimahsela an nunnaah chan cherna aluhtir ta a.
16. An riahhmunah chuan Mosia chu an itsik a, LALPA mi thianghlim Arona nen.
17. Lei chu a ang a, Dathana chu a lem a, Abirama pawlte chu a khuh zo ta a.
18. An pawl zingah chuan mei tihalhin a lo awm a; Meialh chuan mi suaksualte chu a kang ral ta.
19. Horeb tlangah chuan bawng no lem an sima a, A lem chhun chu an be ta a.
20. Chutiang chuan an ropuina chu Bawngpa hlobet ei mi ang lekah an chantir ta a.
21. Pathian an chhandamtu an theihnghilh ta a, Aiguta rama thil ropui tak tak
22. (Hana ramah thilmak takte, Tuifinriat Sen kamah thil rapthlak takte) titu chu.
23. Chuvangin, a tiboral dah ang e, ti-a, a thinrim tikir turin, A mi thlan Mosia ch aluhna turah chuan a hmaah ding lo phei se la chuan, A tiboral ang tih a sawi a.
24. A ni, ram duhawm tak chu an ngainep a, A thu chu an ring duh lo a;
25. An puan inahte chuan an phunnawi ta zawk a, LALPA aw chu an ngaithla duh lo a.
26. Chuvangin an lamah chuan a kut a phar a, Anniho chu thlaleraa tihtlukna tur leh,
27. An chi thlah turte chu hnam tin zinga a tihtluk a, Ram tina a tihdarhna turin.
28. Baal-peora lamah chuan an bet a, Mitthi hnena thilhlante chu an ei ve ta a. Tichuan hri chu an chungah alo leng ta a.
29. Chutiang chuan an thiltihtein ani chu an tithinur a, Tichuan hri chu an chungah a lo leng ta a.
30. Chu mi lai chuan Phinehasa chu a ding a, ro a rel a: Tichuan hri chu tihrehin a awm ta a.
31. Chu chu a felnaa ruat a ni, Kumkhuain chhuan zawng zawng thlengin.
32. Meriba tuiahte pawh chuan ani chu an tithinur a, Chutichuan anmahni avangin Mosia chungah thil tha lo a thleng ta hial a:
33. A thlarau chunga an hel a, A hmuia tha lo taka a sawi avangin.
34. LALPA'n an hnena tihboral thu a pek angin Mipuihote ch an tiboral lo a.
35. Hnam tin chu an pawlh a, An thiltihte chu an zir zawk a.
36. An milem rawngte an bawl a; Chung chu an tant hang a lo ni ta a.
37. A ni, ramhuai hnenahte chan an fapate an fanutein an inthawi a,
38. Thisen sawiselbo, an fapate leh an fanute thisen ngei chu an chauh ta a. Chungho chu Kanaan ram milemte hnenah an inthawi a; Chu mi ram chu thisenin a lo bawlhhlawh ta a.
39. Chutiang chuan an hnathawhtea tihbawrhbanin an awm a, An thiltihahte chuan an lo uire ta a.
40. Chuvangin LALPA thin chu a mite chungah a thawk a, A rochan chu a ten ta a.
41. Tichuan anni chu hnam tin kutah a pe a; Anmahni hawtute chuan an chungah ro an rel ta a.
42. Anmahni dotuten an hnehchhiah a, An kut hnuaiah chuan tihtlawmin an awm ta a.
43. Vawi tam tak anni chu a chhanhim a; Nimahsela a remruatnaah chuan an hel thin a, An khawlohnaah chuan tihhniamin an awm ta a.
44. Chuti chung pawhin ani chuan, an au thawm a hriat chuan, An mangang chu a ngaihsak a:
45. A thuthlun chu anmahni a hriatsak reng a, A ngilneihna nasat avangin a rilru a lamlet ta a.
46. Sala hruaitu zawng zawngte chu Anmahni a khawngaihtir a.
47. Aw LALPA kan Pathian, min chhandam la, Hnam tin zing ata min hruai khawm ang che, I hming thianghlim chu fak tur leh I fakna chhuang turin.LALPA, Israel-ho Pathian chu, fakin awm rawh se, Chatuan ata chatuan thleng pawhin. Mi zawng zawng chuan, "Amen," ti rawh se. LALPA chu fak rawh u.
48. LALPA, Israel-ho Pathian chu, fakin awm rawh se, Chatuan ata chatuan thleng pawhin. Mi zawng zawng chuan, "Amen," ti rawh se. LALPA chu fak rawh u.
