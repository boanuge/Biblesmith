1. Ka thinlung hi thil thain a liam zawih zawih a; Lalber chungchang thu a ka thil phuah chu ka sawi dawn e: Ka lei hi lehkha ziak thiam tak kawlawm a ni.
2. Mihring fate ai hian i hmel a tha zawk a; Khawngaihna hi i hmuiah leih a ni: Chuvangin Pathianin kumkhua atan mal a sawm che a ni.
3. Aw, mi chak, i khandaih i kawngah vawng rawh, I ropuina leh i mawina nen.
4. Thutak leh thuhnuairawlhna leh felna avangin I mawina inbelin hmuingil takin skawr chunga chuangin kal zel ang che. Tichuan i kut ding lamin thil rapthlak takte a zirtir dawn che nia.
5. I thal chu a zum a: Lalber dotute thinlungah chuan a bur reng a: Mi tin, chu i hnuaiah an tlu thin a.
6. Aw Pathian, i lalthuphah chu chatuanin a awm tur a ni: Thlei bik neih lohna laltiang chu i ram laltiang chu a ni.
7. Felna chu i ngaina a, suahsualna chu i huat kha; Chuvangin, Pathian, i Pathian chuan, i thiante aia nasain Hlimna hriak a thih tak che hi.
8. I silhfen zawng zawng chu murra rim te, aloe rim e, thakthing rimte a nam rum rum a; Sainghova siam lal intea tingtang chi hrang hrang ir lo chhuakte chuan a tihlim che anih hi.
9. I hmeichhe thate zingah chuan lalho fanute an tel ve a; I ding lamah chuan Ophir rangkachaka incheiin lalnu chu a ding a.
10. Aw, ka fanu, ngaithla la, ngaithuah la, beng chhi rawh; I mite leh i pa ina mite chu theihnghilh rawh;
11. Chutichuan lalberin i hmel tha tak hi a duh dawn nia: Ani chu i Lalpa a ni si a; tichuan amah chu chibai buk thin ang che.
12. Tura fanu chu thilpek kengin chutah chuan a awm ang a; Mi zinga hausate ngei chuan i khawngaihna an ngen ang.
13. Lal in chhunga lal fanu chu a ropui vel vek a: A silhfen chu rangkachaka zeh mawi a ni.
14. Puan thui mawi sinin lalber hnenah an hruai ang a: Amah zuitu, a thian nulahote chu, I hnenah an hruai ang.
15. Lawm tak leh hlim takin an hruai ang a: Lalber inah chuan an lut ang.
16. I pate aiah i fate an awm ang a, Anni chu lei chung zawng zawngah hian mi lianah i siam ang.I hming hi chhuan zawng anwgah hian hriat reng turin ka siam ang: Chuvangin mi tinin kumkhaw tlaitluanin lawm thu an hrilh ang che.
17. I hming hi chhuan zawng anwgah hian hriat reng turin ka siam ang: Chuvangin mi tinin kumkhaw tlaitluanin lawm thu an hrilh ang che.
