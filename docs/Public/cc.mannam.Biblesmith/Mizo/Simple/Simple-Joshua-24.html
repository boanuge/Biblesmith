1. Tin, Josua'n Israel hnam zawng zawngte chu Sekem-ah a ko khawm a, Israel-ho upa te, an puipa te, an roreltu te, an hotute a kohtir a; tin, Pathian hmaah an rawn inlan hlawm a.
2. Tin, Josua'n mi zawng zawng hnenah chuan, "LALPA, Israelte Pathianin heti hian a ti a ni: 'In thlahtute kha hman laiin Lui piah lamah khan a awm thin a, Abrahama pa leh Nahora pa Tera meuh pawh kha: anni chuan pathian dangte rawng an bawl thin a.
3. Tin, in thlahtu Abrahama kha Lui ral ata ka hruai chhuak a, Kanaan ram chhung zawng zawngah khan ka hruai a, a thlahte chu ka tipung a, Isaaka kha ka pe a.
4. Tin, Isaaka chu Jakoba leh Esauva ka pe a: tin, Esauva chu a chanpual atan Seir tlang ka pe a; tin, Jakoba leh a fate kha Aigupta-ah an zuk chhuk a.
5. Tin, Mosia lehArona ka tir a, Aigupta ram chu an zingah thilmak tiin ka hrem a; tin, chu mi zawhah chuan ka hruai chhuak che u a.
6. Tin, inthlahtute chu Aigupta ram ata ka hruai chhuak a: tin, tuipui in va thleng a; tichuan Aigupta mite chuan in thlahtute chu tawlailirte leh sakawr chung chuang mite nen Tuifinriat Sen thlengin an um a.
7. Tin, anni chuan LALPA an han au a, Aigupta mite nena an inkar chu a rawn tithim ta muip a, tin, tuipui a rawn tuamtir a, a chimpil ta a; tichuan Aigupta rama ka thiltih kha in mit ngeiin a hmu a; tin, thlalerah khan rei tak in awm a.
8. Tin, Jordan lui rala awm Amor ho ramah chuan ka hruai che u a; tin, an lo do che u a: tichuan in kutah ka pe a, an ram chu in luah ta a; tin, anni chu ka tihboralsak ta che u a.
9. Tin, Moab leh Zipora fapa Balaka kha a lo thawk a, Israel-hote a lo do a; tin, Beora fapa Balaama kha an chhiahhlawh tur che uin a kohtir a:
10. a sawm ta zawk che u a: chutichuan a kut ata chu ka chhanchhuak ta che u a ni.
11. Tin, Jordan lui in kai a, Jeriko khua in han thleng a; tin, Jeriko khua amiten an lo do che u a, Amor-ho te, Periz ho te, Kanaan ho te, Hit ho te, Girgas-ho te, Hiv ho te, Jebus-ho te nen kha; tichuan anni chu in kutah ka pe ta a.
12. Tin, in hmaah khawinghla ka tir a, chu chuan in hmaah anmahni chu a um a, Amor-ho lalte pahni ngei kha; in khandaihin emaw, in thalngulin emaw ani hek lo.
13. Tin, in thawhchhuah hauh loh ram leh nangmahni siam hauh loh khawpuite ka pe che u a, chungah chuan in awm a; in phun loh grep huan leh oliv huana mite chu in ei a,' tiin.
14. Chuvangin LALPA chu tih u la, tih takzet leh dik takin a rawng chu bawl u la: inthlahtuten Lui ral leh Aigupta rama a rawng an bawl thin pathiante kha paih vek u la; LALPA rawng bawl ang che u.
15. Tin, LALPA rawngbawl hi a tha lo awm anga in hriat chuan vawiinah hian tu rawng nge in bawl dawn thlang mai rawh u; in thlahtuten Lui rala a rawng an bawl thin pathiante kha nge, in luah ram Amor-hote pathiante kha? Kei leh ka chhungte erawh hi zawngin LALPA rawng ania kan bawl dawn ni," a ti a.
16. Tin, mipuite chuan, "LALPA Pathian chu kalsanin pathian dangte rawng chu kan bawl loh turzia mai chu;
17. amah LALPA kan Pathian ngeiin alawm kan thlahtute nen Aigupta ram bawih ata min hruai chhuak a, kan mithmuha chhinchhiahna ropui tak takte ti a, kan kalna apiang leh, mipui zawng zawng kan tawn tlangna pawha min veng him zeltu chu.
18. tichuan LALPA'n kan hma ngeiah hnam tin, chung rama awm Amor-hote ngei chu a hnawt chhuak vek a: chuvangin keini pawhin LALPA rawng a ni kan bawl dawn ni: amah chu kan Pathian a ni si a," an ti a, an chhang a.
19. Tin, Josua'n mipuite hnenah chuan, "LALPA rawng chu in bawl thei lo ang; ani zawng Pathian thianghlim ani si a; Pathian thikthuchhia a ni a; in bawhchhiatnate leh in sualnate chu a ngaidam lo ang.
20. LALPA chu in kalsan a, hnam dang pathiante rawng in bawl zawngin, in chunga a tha chung hian in chungah thil tha loin a tuan ve ang a, a tiboral mai tur che u a nia," a ti a.
21. Tin, mipuite chuan Josua'n hnenah, "Ni lo e, LALPA rawng kan bawl tlat zawk dawn a ni," an ti a.
22. Tin, Josua'n mipuite hnenah,"A rawngbawl tumin LALPA chu in thlang ta ngei mai tih thu hretute chu nangmahni ngei hi in ni ta e," a ti a. Tin, anni chuan, "thu hretute chu kan ni ta alawm." an ti a.
23. Tin, amah vek chuan, "Chuti anih chuan in zinga hnam dang pathian awmte chu dah bo daih u la, in thinlunge chu LALPA, Israelte Pathian lamah chuan thlektir rawh u," a ti a.
24. Tin, mipuite chuan Josua hnenah," LALPA kan Pathian rawng chu kan bawl ang a, a thu kan zawm zel ang," an ti a.
25. Chutichuan Josua'n chu mi ni chuan mipuite hnenah thu a thlung a, Sekem khuaah chuan dan leh rorelna a ruatsak a.
26. Tichuan hosuan chung thu chu Pathian dan buah a ziak a; tin, lung lianpui a la a, LALPA hmun thianghlim bula sasaw hnuaiah a phun a.
27. Tin, Josua'n mipuiho zawng zawng hnenah chuan, "Ngai teh u, he lung hi kan thu hretu ni rawh se," LALPA'n kan hnena thu a sawi zawng zawng chu a hriat tak vek hi, chuvangin in thu hretu ani dawn a ni, chutilozawng in Pathian hi in phatsan mai dah ang e," a ti a.
28. chutichuan Josua'n mipuite chu anmahni chanpual ram theuhvah a hawntir ta a.
29. Tin, heti hi a ni a, chung thil hnuah chuan Nuna fapa Josua LALPA chhiahhlawh chu kum za leh kum sawm miin a thi a.
30. Tin, Gas tlang hmar lampang, Ephraim tlang rama ama chanpual ramri chhung Timnath-sera-ah chuan an phum ta a.
31. Tin, Josua dam chhung leh, upaho Josua khuma dam te, LALPA'n Israel-hote tana thil a tih zawng zawng hretute dam chhung hchuan Israel-hoten LALPA rawng an bawl thin a.
32. Tin, Israela thlahten Aigupta ram ata Josefa ruh an chhawm chhoh kha Sekem khuaa hmun, Sekema pa Hamora fate laka Jakoba'n tangkaa zaa a leiah khan an phum a: tichuan chung chu Josefa thlahte pualah a lo awm ta a.Tin, Arona fapa Eleazara chu a thi a; tin, Ephraim tlang rama a puala an pek, a fapa Phinehasa tlangah chuan an phum ta a.
33. Tin, Arona fapa Eleazara chu a thi a; tin, Ephraim tlang rama a puala an pek, a fapa Phinehasa tlangah chuan an phum ta a.
