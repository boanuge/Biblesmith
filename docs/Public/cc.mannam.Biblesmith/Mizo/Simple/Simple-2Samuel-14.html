1. Chutichuan Zeruia fapa Joaba chuan lal rilru chu Absaloma chungah a cheng tih a lo hria a.
2. TichuanJoaba chuan Tekoa khuaah mi a ti ra, chuta meiche fing pakhat chu a va hruaitir a, a hnenah, "Lusun angah insiam la, lusun puan sin la, sahriak pawh inthih loin hmeichhia rei tak mitthi sun angin awm rawh:
3. tin, lal hnenah kal la a hnenah hetiang hian thu va sawi ang che aw," a ti a. Chutichuan Joaba chuan thu a fah a.
4. Tin, Tekoa khuaa hmeichhia chuan lal hnena thu a sawi dawn chuan hmaiaa lei siin abawkkhup a, chibaia buk a, "Aw lalpa, min pui rawh," a ti a.
5. Tin, lal chuan a hnenah, "Engtizia nge ni le?" a ti a. Tin, ani chuan a chhang a, "Hmeithai ka ni tak tak a, ka pasal hu a thi tawh a.
6. Tin, i bawihnu hian fapa pahni anei a, an pahni chuan loah an insual a, tumah thelhtu an awm si lo a, a pakhat chuan pakhat hu avua a, atihlum ta a.
7. Tin, ngai teh, an chhungkua zawng zawng chuan i bawihnu hi an do rawn a,'A unaupa thattu chu a unaupa a thah avanga kan thah ve theihna'n min pe raw khai, tichuanro luahtu tur pawh kan tiboral ang,' an ti a: chutiang chuan ka meiling la awma chu an timit ang a, ka pasal chu hming nei lo leh lei chunag thlah reng reng nei loin a awm mai dawn a ni," a ti a.
8. Tin, lal chuan hmeiche hnenah chuan, "I inah haw ta la, i chungchang thuah chuanthu ka pe mai ang," a ti a.
9. Tin, Tekoa khuaa hmeichhia chuan lal hnenah chuan, "Aw lal pa, ka pu, bawhchhiatna mawh chu ka chungah leh ka pa chhungte chungah tla mai rawh se: tin, lal leh lalthutthleng chuan mawh phur su se," a ti a.
10. Tin, lal chuan, "Tupawh i chungah eng emaw sawi apiang chu ka hnenah rawn hruai la, a tawk leh tawh hlek lo ang che," a ti a.
11. Tin, ani chuan, "Thisen chhuahtute chungah phuba latuten tiboral tawh lo turin lal chuan LALPA i Pathian chu hre reng rawh se aw, chutilozawng ka fapa hi an tioral dah ang e," a ti a. Tin, ani chuan, "LALPA nunna chhalin ka sawi e, i fapaa samzai khat pawh leah hiana tla lo ang," a ti a.
12. Chutichuan hmeichhia chuan, "I bawihnu hian LALPA ka pu hnenah thu kam khat chauh ha sawi leh teh se aw," a ti a. Tin, ani chuan, "Sawi ta che," a ti a.
13. Tin, hmeichhia chuan, "A nih le eng vangin nge Pathian mite dodala hetianga tih i tum? Heng thu atang hian lal chu thiam loh chang angin a lang a ni, lal chuan ami, tlan bova chu a hruai hawn si loh avangin.
14. Kan thi ngei ngei tur a ni a, tin, tuisik leia baw hnu ruh theih tawh loh ang hi kan ni a: Pathianin nunna hi a la bo a ni lo a, tlan boa chu a laka mi hrang a nih lohna'n rem a ruat a ni zawk a ni.
15. Chutichuan lalpa ka pu hnena he thu sawi tura ka lo kal pawh hi mi ka hlauh vang a ni a: tin, i bawihnu chuan, lal hnenah ka sawi phawt ang a, lal chuan abawihnu dilna ch a hlawhtlintir mahna.
16. Lal chuan kei leh ka fapa hi Pathian roluah lo tura tihboral tumtu laka i bawihnu hi chan chuak turin a lo ngaihthlaksak ang chu ka ti a.
17. Chutichuan i bawihnu tan hian, LALPA ka pu thu chu thlamuan tak ni se la: achhia a tha lo thlei turin LALPA ka pu chu Pathian vantirhkoh ang mai ani si a: tin, LALPA Pathian chu i hnenah awm rawh se ka ti a ni," a ti a.
18. Tin, lal chuan a chhang a, hmeichhe hnenah chuan, "Ka zawh apiang che chu ka lakah engmah zep suh ang che aw," a ti a. Tin, hmeichhia chuan, "LALPA ka pu chuan sawi ang hmiang," a ti a.
19. Tin, lal chuan, "Heng thuah reng reng hian Joaban telna a nei em?" a ti a. Tin, hmeichhia chuan a chhang a, "LALPA ka pu, i thlarau nunna chhlalin ka sawi e, LALPA ka puin a sawi tawh chu tumahin dinglamah emaw, vei lamah emaw an pumpelh thei lo ang a: i chhiahhlawh Joaba chuan min tir a, he'ng thu zawng zawng hi i bawihnu a fah vek a ni:
20. thil awmdan tidanglam turinheng thil hi i chhiahlawhJoaban chuan a tih a ni: tin, ka pu chu khawvela thil awm zawng zawng hre theiin Pathian vantirhkoh ang maiin a fing a ni," a ti a.
21. Tin, lal chuan Joaba hnenah, "Ngai teh, ka ti ve ta e, chuvangin kal la, tlangval Absaloma chu va hruai leh rawh le," a ti a.
22. Tin, Joaba chu hmaia lei siin a bawkkhup a, chibai abuk a, lal chu mal a sawm a: tin, Joaba chuan, "Aw LALPA ka pu, LALPA chuan a chhiahhlawh dilna chu ahlawhtlintir avangin vawiinah hian i chhiahlawh hian i mit ka tlung tih ka lo hre ta," a ti a.
23. chutichuan Joaba chu atho a, Gesur-ah chuan a kal a, Absaloma chu Jerusalem-aha hruai ta a.
24. Tin, lal chuan, "Ama in lamah kal se la, k ahmel chu rawn hmu suh se," a ti a. Chutichuan Absoloma chu ama in lamah chuan a kal a, lal hmel chu a hmu ta lo a.
25. Israel zawng zawng zinga Absoloma ang renga hmel thata fak kai tuma an awm lo va: a ke ler atangin a chip thlengin sawiselna reng reng a awm lo a.
26. A lu a meh chuan (kum vei apiangin a met thin a, asam chu a rih em thin avangin a met thin a ni) a sam chu abuk thin a, lal buk dana sekel hnih a tling thin a ni.
27. Tin, Absaloma chuan fapa pathum leh fanu pakhat a nei a, a hming chu Tamari a ni a, hmel tha tak a ni.
28. Chutichuan Absaloma chu kum hnih pumhlum Jerusalem-ah a awm a, nimahsela lal hmel reng reng chu ahmu lo a.
29. Chutichuan Absaloma chuan lal hnena tirh atan Joaba chu a chah a; nimahsela a kal duh lo a; tin, a chah nawn leh a, nimahsela a kal duh chuang lo a.
30. Chutichuan a chhiahhlawhte hnenah, "I e u, Joaba lo chu ka lo kiang a ni a, barli anei a, va hal rawh u," a ti a. Tichuan Absaloma chhiahhlawhte chuan lo chu an va hal ta a.
31. Chutichuan Joaba chu a tho a, Absaloma hnena a inah a kal a, a hnenah, "Engvangin nge Gesur atangin ka lo kal? Chutah chuan awm reng i la ka tan a tha zawk tur,' tia lal hnena tirh atan ka chah chia; chuvangin lal hmel chu min hmuhtir rawh khai: tin, bawhchhiatna nei ka lo ni chuan min tihlum mai anghmiang," a ti a.Chutichuan Joaba chu lal hnenah chuan a kal a, ahrilh a; tin, Absaloma chu a ko a, lal hnenah a kal a, tin, lal hmaah chuan hmaia lei siin a bawkkhup a: tin, lal chuan Absaloma chu a fawp a.
32. Chutichuan Joaba chu lal hnenah chuan a kal a, ahrilh a; tin, Absaloma chu a ko a, lal hnenah a kal a, tin, lal hmaah chuan hmaia lei siin a bawkkhup a: tin, lal chuan Absaloma chu a fawp a.
