1. Paula, Pathian bawih, chatuana nunna beiseia Pathian thlante rinna kawngah leh Pathian ngaihsak lam thutak hriatna kawnga Isua Krista tirhkoh bawk chuan:
2. Chu chatuana nunna chu Pathian, dawt sawi thei loa chuan khawvel awm hmain a tiam a;
3. nimahsela ama hun takahte chuan a thu chu thuchahah chuan a tilang ta, chu thuchah chu Pathian kan Chhandamtu thupek ang zela kawltir ka ni.
4. Tita, kan rin thuhmun vanga ka fa taktak hnenah: Pa, Pathian leh Krista Isua min Chhandamtu hnen ata khawngaihna leh remna awm rawh se.
5. He mi avang hian Kret thliarkarah khan ka hnutchhiah che a ni, thil tihfel bakte chu i tihfel zelna tur leh, thu ka pek che ang khan khaw tinah upate i ruatna turin;
6. tupawh sawiselbo, nupui pakhat nei, fate Pathian thu ring, nawmsak bawl lutuk kawnga sawisel hlawh lo, dan ang loa awm lo nei ang nih tawh phawt chuan.
7. Kohhran hotu chu Pathian sum enkawltu a nih avangin sawiselbo ni tur a ni; mi tihmawh, thin ram, uain ruih hmang, mi velh hmang, hlepna bawlhhlawha duham mi ni tur a ni lo a;
8. mi lawm dan thiam, tha lam duh, rilru dik tak, mi fel, mi thianghlim, insum thei mi ni zawk tur a ni a;
9. zirtirna dik tak kawngah chuan mi a fuih theihna tur leh, hnialtute chu thiam loh a chantir theihna turin zirtirna ang zel thu rinawm chu pawm tlat mi ni bawk tur a ni.
10. Dan ang loa awm, thu sawi mai maite leh tihdertute mi tam tak an awm si a, serhtanho zinga mi an ni deuh ber;
11. chungho ka chu huhsak tur a ni, hlepna bawlhhlawh duh vanga thu zirtir loh tur zirtirin, mite a chhung chhungin an chawk khawlo si thin a.
12. Anmahni zinga mi, anmahni zawlnei zawlnei pakhat ngeiin, "Kret thliarkara mite chu dawthei te, sakawlh sual te, thatchhe chaw hehte an ni fo mai," tiin a sawi kha.
13. Chu thu hriattir chu a dik a ni.
14. Chuvang chuan Judaho thawnthute leh thutak hawisan thinte thupekte chu ngaihsak loin, rinnaah chuan an dik theihna turin na takin zilhhau rawh.
15. Mi thianghlimte tan chuan engkim a thianghlim thin; nimahsela mi bawlhhlawh leh ring lote tan chuan engmah thianghlim reng a awm thin lo, an rilru leh an chhia leh tha hriatna reng pawh a bawlhhlawh tawh a.Pathian hria an inti a, tenawm te, thu awih lo te, thil tha tinreng tih kawnga Pathian duh lohzawngte an ni, (an thiltihin an phatsan si).
16. Pathian hria an inti a, tenawm te, thu awih lo te, thil tha tinreng tih kawnga Pathian duh lohzawngte an ni, (an thiltihin an phatsan si).
