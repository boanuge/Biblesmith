1. Tin, LALPA thinurna chu Israelte chungah a lo thawk leh ta thut a, "Kal la, Israel mi leh Juda mite chhiar vek rawh," tiin Davida chuan an pawi khawih turin a chawk tho a.
2. Tin, lal chuan a hnena awm sipai hotu Joaba hnenah chuan, "Israel hnam zawng zawng zingah hian Dan atanga Beer-seba thlengin vak vel la, mi engzat nge an nih ka hriat theihna'n chhiar vek rawh u," a ti a.
3. Tin, Joaba chuan lal hnenah, "Lalpa i Pathian chuan mipuite tipungin, engzata tampawh, a leh za pawh, lalpa ka pu hian hmu hman rawh se; nimahsela lalpa ka pu chuan chu chu engah nge maw hriat a chak le?" a ti a.
4. Nimahsela lal thu chuan Joaba leh sipai hotute chu a hneh ta a. Tichuan Joaba leh sipai hotute chu Israel mite chhiar tur chuan lal hnen ata chu an chhuak ta a.
5. Tin, Jordan ral an va kai a, tin, Gad ruam lai tak ding lama khaw awm Aroer=ah chuan Jazer thlengin awm hmun an khuar a:
6. tin, Gilead ram leh Tahtim hodsi ram an thleng a; tin, Dan-Jaan an thleng a, Zidon paninan kal vel a,
7. tin, tura kulh te an va thleng a, tin, Hiv-ho leh Kanaan mite khua zawng zawng an thleng a; tin, Judai chhim lam Beer-seba-ah an kal a.
8. Chutichuan ram zawng zawng an fan chhuah hnu chuan thla kua leh ni sawmhnih ralah Jerusalem-ah an lo haw a.
9. Tin, Joaba chuan mihring zat chin chu lal a pe a: tin, Israel ramah chuan khandaih keng mi, mi huaisen nuairiat an awm a; tin, Juda mi chu nuainga an awm a.
10. Tin, mihring a han chhiar zawh chuan Davida thinlung chu a na ta hle mai a. Tin, Davida chuan LALPA hnenah, "Ka thiltihah hian nasa takin ka lo sual ta a ni. Nimahsela tunah hian aw LALPA, i chhiahhlawh khawlohna hi dah bo turin ka ngen a che; atthlak takin ka lo ti ta a ni e," a ti a.
11. Tin, zinga Davida a han thawh chuan Davida zawlnei, zawlnei Gada hnenah LALPA thu a lo thleng a,
12. "Kal la, Davida hnenah, 'LALPA'n heti hian a ti, Thil kawng thum ka ruatsak chia, i chunga ka tih turin pakhat thlang rawh,' a ti e," tiin.
13. Chutichuan Gada chu Davida hnenah a kal a, a hrilh a, a hnenah, "I ramah hian kum sarih tam i chunga lo tlak nge, i hmelma hmaa an um chhung chea thjla thum lo tlan bo rih nge, i rama ni thum hri lo len? Mi tirtu eng angin nge ka chhan dawn inngaihtuah la, min hrilh rawh khai," a ti a.
14. Tin, Davida chuan Gada hnenah, "Ka mang a ang hle mai a: LALPA kutah tlu lut phawt mai i la, a zahngaihna chu a nasa hle si a; mihring ktuah chuan min tluk luhtir lul suh ang che," a ti a.
15. Chutichuan LALPA chuan Israel mite chungah chuan zing atangin hun ruat ang thleng chuan hri alentir a; tichuan mipui zinga mi chu Dan atangin Beer-seba thlengin mi singsarih an thi a.
16. Tin, vantirhkohin tichhe tura Jerusalemlama a kut a han lek chuan LALPA'n thil tha lo tih a tum chu alamlet a, mite tiboraltu vantirhkoh hnenah chuan, "A tawk tawh e; i kut lek tawh suh," a ti a. Tin, LALPA vantirhkoh chu Jebus mi Arauna hrui bulah chuan a awm a.
17. Tin, Davida'n mi tihlumtu vantirhkoh a hmuh chuan LALPA a bia a, "Ngai teh, ka lo sual ta a, dil lo takin thil ka lo ti ta a ni: mahse, he'ng beramte hian eng thil nge an tih? I kut chuan keimah leh ka pa chhungte mi khawih zawk rawh se aw," a ti a.
18. Tin, chu mini chuan Gada chu Davida hnenah a kal a, a hnenah, "Chho la, Jebus mi Arauna hruiah LALPA tan maicham han siam rawh," a ti a.
19. Tin, Davida chu LALPA'n thu apek anga Gadan a hrilh ang chuan a chho ta a.
20. Tin, Arauna chu a han hawi a, a lam pana lal leh ahote lo kal chu a hmu a: tichuan Arauna chu a chhuak a, a hmaia lei siinlal hmaah chuana bawkkhup a.
21. Tin, Arauna chuan, "Eng vangin nge LALPA ka pu chu a chhiahhlawh hnenah a lo kal le?" a ti a. Tin, Davida chuan, "Mipuite laka hri a kian theihna tura LALPA tana maicham siamna'na i hrui lei turin," a ti a.
22. Tin, Arauna chuan Davida hnenah, "LALPA ka pu chuan awm a tih la se la hlan ang hmiang: en teh, halral thil hlan atan bawngpate chu a awm a, tin, thing atan hrui hmanruate leh sebawng thuamnate chu a awm a.
23. Aw lalpa, he'ng zawng zawng hi Arauna chuan lal a pe vek e," a ti a. Tin, Arauna chuan lal hnenah, "LALPA i Pathian chuan lo chhang che rawh se," a ti a.
24. Tin, lal chuan Arauna hnenah, "Ni lo e a, aman neiin a lei ngeiin ka leisak zawk ang che; engmah insengso loin LALPA ka Pathian hnenah halral thilhlante ka hlawn tawp lo ang," a ti a. Chutichuan Davida chuan hrui leh sebawngpate chu tangka sekel sawmngain a lei ta a.Tin, Davida'n chutah chuan LALPA tan maicham a siam a, halral thilhlante leh remna thillante a hlan a. Chutichuan LALPA chu an ram chungah chuan a lungawi a, Israel ram ata hri chu a kiang ta a.
25. Tin, Davida'n chutah chuan LALPA tan maicham a siam a, halral thilhlante leh remna thillante a hlan a. Chutichuan LALPA chu an ram chungah chuan a lungawi a, Israel ram ata hri chu a kiang ta a.
