1. Chutiang bawkin nangni nupuite u, mahni pasalte thu thuin awm rawh u; chutichuan an zinga tupawhin thu chu awih lo mah se la, thu loin an nupuite chetziaa hnehin an awm thei ang,
2. tih dek chunga in chetze thianghlim enin.
3. In incheina chu pawn lam incheina - sam phiar te, rangkachak thi awrh te, silhfena inthuam te chu - ni loin,
4. thinlunga mihring thuruk chu, rilru thuhnuairawlh leh nunnem, silhfen chhe thei loa inthuam ni zawk rawh se, chu chu Pathian mithmuhin a hlu em em a ni.
5. Chutiangin hmasanga hmeichhe thianghlim Pathian ringtute pawh kha mahni pasalte thu thua awmin an inchei si thin a;
6. Sari'n Abrahama chu "Lalpa" tiin a thu a awih ang khan. Engmah hlau loa thil tha in tih tlat chuan Sari fate in ni ang.
7. Chutiang bawkin nangni pasalte u, in nupuite nen in khawsak danah bel keh awlzawk an nih hrethiam u la, khawngaih thilpek nunna chanpuitu che u an nih hriain chawimawi rawh u, tichuan in tawngtainate dal a ni dawn lo nia.
8. A tawp berah chuan in zain rilru hmunkhatin awm u la, inlainat tawn u la, unau angin inhmangaih u la, khawngaihna nei u la, thuhnuairawlhin awm rawh u:
9. sual, sualin thungrul suh u la, hauna haunain thungrul hek suh u la, malsawm hlauh zawk rawh u. He mi avangin koh in ni si, malsawmna chang tur hian.
10. "Nunna hmangaih duh leh ni thate hmuh duh chuan sual lakah a lei veng tha se la, a hmui pawh ngamthlemna thu sawi lo turin veng tha rawh se.
11. Sual hawisanin thil tha ti rawh se; remna zawngin um zel rawh se.
12. Lalpa mit chu mi felte chungah a fu a, a beng pawh an tawngtaina lamah a chhi si thin a, amaherawhchu, Lalpa hmel chu thil tisualtute chungah a awm thin," tih a ni si a.
13. Thil tha lama thahnem in ngaih chuan in pawi khawihtu tur tu nge awm ang?
14. Nimahsela, felna avanga lo tuar ta ang in nih chuan in eng a thawl ang. An hlauhawmna chu hlau suh u la, mangang hek suh u;
15. amaherawhchu in thinlungah Krista chu Lalah ruat rawh u; nangmahnia awm in beisei chhan zawt apiang che u chhang turin inpeih sa fo ang che u, nimahsela thuhnuairawlh tak leh tih dek chungin;
16. chhia leh tha hriatna thiang tak nei u la, chutichuan an sawichhiatna che u kawngah chuan Kristaa in awm dan tha tak sawihnawmtute chu tihmualphoin an awm thei ang.
17. Thil sual tih vanga tuar ai chuan Pathian remtihzawng a nih chuan thil tha tih vanga tuar hi a tha zawk si a.
18. Pathian hnena min hruai theihna'n Krista chuan sualte avangin vawi khat a tuar bawk si a, mi fel chuan mi fel lo aiin a tuar a, tisaah chuan tihthihin a awm a, thlarauvah erawh chuan tihnunin a awm ta a;
19. chu chuan tan ina thlarau awmte hnenah chuan thu a zuk tlangaupui a,
20. chu chuan hmana Nova dam laia lawng siam chhung zawnga Pathian dawhtheihnain a nghah lai khan an awih lo a; chu lawngah chuan mi tlem te (chu chu mi pariat tihna a ni) tuia chhandamin an awm ta:
21. chu mi anpui (baptisma) chuan Isua Krista thawhlehna avangin tunah a chhandam che u a ni, (tisa bawlhhlawh tihfai mai ni loin, chhia leh tha hriatna thianga Pathian lamna hi a ni zawk e);Isua Krista chu vanah kalin Pathian ding lamah a awm a; vantirhkohho te, thuneihna te, thiltihtheihna te ama thu thua awmtirin an awm a.
22. Isua Krista chu vanah kalin Pathian ding lamah a awm a; vantirhkohho te, thuneihna te, thiltihtheihna te ama thu thua awmtirin an awm a.
