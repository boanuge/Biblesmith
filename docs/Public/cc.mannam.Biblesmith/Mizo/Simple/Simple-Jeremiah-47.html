1. Pharaoan Gaza a suasam hmaa Philistia mite chungchanga zawlnei Jeremia hnena LALPA thu lo thleng chu:
2. LALPA chuan heti hian a ti: Ngai rawh u, hmar atangin tui a lo lian e, A kuang khatin a luang liam ang; Ram chu a chim ang a, a chhunga awm zawng zawng nen, Khawpui leh a chhunga chengte pawh chu; Mite an tap ang a, rama mi zawng zawng an chiau vawng vanwg ang.
3. A sakawr chak phei khawng ri-ah, A indo tawlailir ri dur durah, A tawlailir nghawr thawmah te, Pate an zam a, an fate hawi lo lekin.
4. Philistia mi zawng zawng rawkna ni. tura leh Sidon ata tanpuitu awm chhunte sah chhumna ni avangin, LALPA chuan tuipui kam Kaphtora la awm chhun, Philistia mite chu a tichhe dawn si a.
5. Gaza mite lu a kawng ta, Askelon chu a chimit ta a, A ram phaia la cheng chhunte chu; Eng chen nge i in zai dawn?
6. Aw LALPA khandaih, i awm hle hle hma eng chen nge? I paiah in khungin chawl la, awm hle hle rawh.LALPA'n tih tur a pek si che chuan, Eng tin, nge i awm hle hle theih ang? Askelon leh tuipui kam chunga che tur in, A ruat miau si che chuan.
7. LALPA'n tih tur a pek si che chuan, Eng tin, nge i awm hle hle theih ang? Askelon leh tuipui kam chunga che tur in, A ruat miau si che chuan.
