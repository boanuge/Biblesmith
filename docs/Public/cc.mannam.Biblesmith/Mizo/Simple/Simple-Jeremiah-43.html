1. Tin, heti hi a ni a, Jeremian mipuite zawng zawng hnena LALPA an Pathian thu zawng zawng, LALPA Pathianin sawi tura an hnena a tirhna thu, he'ng thu zawng zawngte ngei hi a sawi tawpah chuan,
2. Hosaia fapa Azaria leh Karea fapa Johanana leh mi intivei zawng zawng chuan Jeremia chu an bia a, a hnenah, "Dawt i sawi a ni: LALPA kan Pathianin, 'Aigupta rama cheng turin in kal tur a ni lo,' tia sawi turin a tir hlei nem che:
3. Kaldaite kutah mantira kan awm a min thah theihna tur leh Babulon sala min hruai theihna turin Neria fapa Baruka chuan min bei turin a tir che a nih zawk hi," a ti a.
4. Chutichuan Karea fapa Johanana leh sipai pawl hotu zawng leh mipui zawng zawng chuan Judai rama cheng turin LALPA tawngka chu an awih ta lo a.
5. Tichuan Karea fapa Johanana leh sipai pawl hotu zawng zawng chuan Juda awm chhun zawng zawng, Judai rama cheng tura hnawhc darha an awmna hnamte zinga ata lo kir,
6. hmeichhia te, mipa te, naupang te, lal fanute leh mi tin, vengtu hotu Nebuzaradanan Saphana fapa, Ahikama fapa Gedalia leh a hnena an hnutchhiahte chu zawlnei Jeremia leh Neria fapa Baruka te pawh, an hruai ta vek zawk a.
7. Tichuan Aigupta ram chu an thleng ta a, LALPA tawngka chu an awih si lo a; tin, Tahpanes khua ngei chu an thleng a.
8. Tin, Tahpanes-ah chuan Jeremia hnenah LALPA thu a lo thleng a,
9. "Lung lian pui pui la la, Tahpanesa Pharaoa in luhna bula leirawhchan siamnaa hlum chawh diaknaah chuan Judate mithmuh ngeiin phum rawh.
10. Tin, an hnenah chuan, 'Sipahote LALPA, Israel Pathian chuan heti hian a ti: Ngai rawh u, ka rawngbawltu Babulon lal Nebukadnezzara chu ka chah chhuak ang a, ani chuan a lalthutthleng a hung ang; ani chuan an chungah chuan a lal puan in a zar ang.
11. Tin, a lo kal ang a, Aigupta ram chu a rawn ngahsai ang, thihna pual chu khandaihah.
12. Aigupta mite pathian inahte chuan mei a chhuah ang a, a kang ang a, salah a hruai ang; berampu, puan a inbel angin Aigupta ram chuy a inbel ang; chuta tangin ralmuangin a chhuak ang a,Aigupta rama Beth-Semes lung ban sang pui puite chu a tichim ang a, Aigupta pathian inte chu meiin a hal ang, a ti,' ti rawh," a ti a.
13. Aigupta rama Beth-Semes lung ban sang pui puite chu a tichim ang a, Aigupta pathian inte chu meiin a hal ang, a ti,' ti rawh," a ti a.
