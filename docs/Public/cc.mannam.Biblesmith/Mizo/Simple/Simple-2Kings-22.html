1. Josia chu a lal tantirhin kum riat mi a ni a; Jerusalem-ah kum sawmthum leh kum khat a lal a: a nu hming chu Jedidi, Bozkath khuaa Adaia fanu a ni.
2. Tin, LALPA mithmuha thil fel tak a ti thin a, a pu Davida kawng zawh zawng zawng chu a zawh ve a, ding lamah emaw vei lamah emaw a pensan lo a.
3. Tin, lal Josia a lal kum sawmleh kum riat kumin heti hi a ni a, lal chuan Mesullama fa Azalia fapa lehkhaziaktu Saphana chu a tir a,
4. "Puithiam lalbe Hilkia hnenah han kal la, kawngkhar vengtuten mite hnen ata an lo lak khawm LALPA ina tangka rawn dah chu chhiar se la;
5. LALPA in satu hnathawktute pe rawh se;
6. thingrem siamtu te, in satu te, in chhe siamtute huan in he lai siamna thing leh lungchher an lei theihna'n," tiin LALPA inah a tir a.
7. Amaherawhchu tangka an pekate chu an enzuipui leh lem chuang lo; rinawm taka an tih thin avangin.
8. Tin, puithiam lalber Hilkia chuan lehkhaziaktu Saphana chu lal hnenah a kal a, lal hnenah thu chu a va thlen leh a, "I mite chuan thu chu a va thlen leh a, "I mite chuan ina tangka awm chu an pe chhuak vek ta e," a ti a.
9. Tin, lehkha ziaktu Saphana chu lal hnenah a kal a, lal hnenah thu chu a va thlen leh a, "I mite chuan ina tangka awm chu an pe chhuak vek a, lalpa in satu hnathawktute an pe vek ta e," a ti a.
10. Tin, lehkhaziaktu Saphana chuan, "Puithiam Hilian lehkhabu mi lo pe a sin," tiin lal chu a hrilh a. Tin, Saphana chuan, lal hmaah a chhiar a,
11. Tin, lalin dan lehkhabu thute hu a han hriat chuan heti hi a ni a, a puante a pawt thler ta a.
12. Tin, lal chuan puithiam Hilkia te, Saphana fapa Ahikama te, Mikaia fapa Akbora te, lehkhaziaktu Saphana te, lal mi Asaia te hnena chuan,
13. "Kei leh, mipui leh, Juda-ho zawng zawng hi he lehkhabu chara thu chuang thuah hian LALPA min va rawnsak teh u: kan tih tura he mi lehkhabua ziak ang zawng zawng hi kan pi leh puten an lo zawm loh avangin kan chungah hian LALPA thinura chu a alh nasat tawh em hi," a ti a.
14. Chutichuan puithiam Hilkia te, Ahikama te, Akbora te, Saphana te, Asaia te chu silhfen enkawltu Harhasa fa Tikva fapa Salluma nupui zawlnei Huldi hnenah chuan an kal a, an va bia a. (Ani chu Jerusalem Misne-a awm ania.)
15. Tin, ani chuan an hnenah, "LALPA, Israelte Pathianin heti hian a ti: Ka hnena rawn tirtu che u hi hrilh rawh u.
16. LALPA'n heti hian a ti e: Ngai teh, he mi hmunah leh, a chhunga chenghote chunga hian thil tha lo tak ka rawn thlentir dawn a ni, Juda lalin lehkhabua a thu chiar zawng zawng ang kha:
17. mi kalsan a, pathian dange hnenah rimtui an hal a, an kut chhuaka mi tihthinrim avangin he mi hmuna hian ka thinurna a alh nasa dawn a, a mit mai dawn lo e.
18. Nimahsela LALPA rawn tura rawn tirtu che u Juda lal hnenah huan hetiang hian sawi ang che u: LALPA Israelte Pathianin heti hian a ti: I thu hriat chungchang thuah chuan -
19. hemi hmun leh, a chhunga chengte hi tihtiau leh anchhedawngin an la awm ang tih thu ka sawi kha i lo hriat a, i rilrute a nem a, LALPA hmaa i ingaihtlawm a, i puante i pawh thler a, ka hmaa i tah avangin, kei pawhin i thu chu ka lo hria e, LALPA'n a ti.Chutichuan ngai teh, i pi leh pute hnenah ka hruai ang chia, i thlanah thlamuang taka hruaiin i awm ang a, he mi hmuna thil tha lo tak ka rawn thlentir tur chu i mitin a hmu hek lo ang, a ti e,' tiin," a ti a. Tichuan thu chu lal hnenah an va thlen leh a.
20. Chutichuan ngai teh, i pi leh pute hnenah ka hruai ang chia, i thlanah thlamuang taka hruaiin i awm ang a, he mi hmuna thil tha lo tak ka rawn thlentir tur chu i mitin a hmu hek lo ang, a ti e,' tiin," a ti a. Tichuan thu chu lal hnenah an va thlen leh a.
