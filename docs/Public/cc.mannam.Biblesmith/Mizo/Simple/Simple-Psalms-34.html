1. Englai pawhin LALPA chu ka fak ang a: Amah fakna chu ka kaah a chhuak fo ang.
2. Ka nunna hian LALPA chu a chhuang ang a: Thuhnuairawlhte chuan chu chu an hria ang a,a n lawm ang.
3. Aw, LALPA chu mi fakpui u la, A hming chu i chawimawi tlang ang u.
4. LALPA chu ka zawng a, tin, ani chuan mi chhang a, Ka hlauhzawng zawng lakah chuan mi chhanhim ta a.
5. Amah an en a, tin, an lo eng ta a: An hmelte chu engtikah mah a zak lo ang.
6. He mi srethei hi a au a, tin, LALPA chuan a hria a, A manganna zawng zawngah chuan a chhan chhuak ta a.
7. Amah tihtute chu, an velah LALPA vantirhkoh chuan a awm chilh a, A chhanhim thin.
8. Aw, LALPA thatzia chu tem chhin teh reng u: Amaha rina nghat mi chu a eng a thawl e.
9. Aw, a mi thianghlimte u, LALPA chu tih rawh u: Amah tihtute tan chuan tlakchhamna reng reng a awm si lo.
10. Sakeibaknei vanglaite chu an tlachhamin an ril a tam thin a: LALPA zawngtue erawh chuan thil tha reng reng an tlachham lo ang.
11. Hawh u, naupangte u, ka thu ngaithla rawh u: LALPA tih dan ka lo zirtir ang che u.
12. Thatna a hmuh theihna'na, nunna duh a Damrei ngaina mi chu tu nge ni?
13. I lei chu sual lakah veng tha la, I hmui chu tihderna thu sawi lo turin veng tha rawh.
14. Sual kalsan la, thil tha ti rawh; Remna zawng la, a umin um rawh.
15. LALPA mit chuan mi felte lam a en thin a, A beng chuan an au hre turin a ngaithla reng thin.
16. LALPA hmel chuan thil tisualtute chu a do thin, Anmahni hriat rengna chu lei ata tiboral turin.
17. Mi felte chu an au a, tin, LALPA chuan a lo hria a, An manganna zawng zawngah chuan a chhan chhuak ta a.
18. LALPA chuan thinlung lungchhiate chu a hnaih thin a, Rilrua inchhir apiangte chu a chhandam thin.
19. Mi fel hreawmnate chu a tam a, Nimahsela LALPA'n chung zawng zawngah chuan a chhan chhuak thin a.
20. A ruh zawng zawng a vawng tha a, Pakhat mah tihtliah a ni lo.
21. Thil tha loin mi suaksualte chu a tihlum ang a: Mi fel hawtute chuan thiam loh an chang a.LALPA chuan a chhiahhlawhte nunna chu a tlan thin a. Amaha rinna nghatte chu pakhat mah thiam loh chang an awm lo ang.
22. LALPA chuan a chhiahhlawhte nunna chu a tlan thin a. Amaha rinna nghatte chu pakhat mah thiam loh chang an awm lo ang.
