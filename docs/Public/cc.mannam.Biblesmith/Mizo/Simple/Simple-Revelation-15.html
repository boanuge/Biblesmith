1. Tin, vanah chhinchhiahna dang, ropui tak leh mak tak ka hmu a, vantirhkoh pasarih hremna pasarih kengte chu; chu chu hremna hnuhnung berte a ni, chungah chuan Pathian thinurna chu a kin tawh si a.
2. Tin, darthlalang, mei nena inpawlh tuifinriat ni awm tak ka hmu a; sakawlh te, a lem te, a hming nambar te hneha lo chhuakte chu Pathian tingtang kenga, darthlalang tuifinriat kama ding ka hmu bawk a.
3. Tin, Pathian bawih Mosia hla leh Beram No hla, "Aw Lalpa Pathian, Engkimtithei, I thiltihte chu a ropuiin a mak a; nang, hnam tin Lalber, I kawngte chu a felin a dik a ni.
4. Aw Lalpa, tu nge i hming zah lo anga chawimawi lo ang? Nang chauh i thianghlim si; I thiltih felte chu tihlan a nih tawh avangin, nnam tinrengin chibai an rawn buk ang che," tih hi an sa a.
5. Chung hnuah chuan ka han en a, tin, vana hriattirna biak buk pindan chhungnung chu an hawng a,
6. tichuan, pindan chhungnung atang chuan hremna pasarih keng vantirhkoh pasarihte chu, puan thianghlim var tak sinin, an awma rangkachak kawnghrena inhrengin an lo chhuak a.
7. Tin, Thilnung pali zinga pakhat chuan, vantirhkoh pasarihte hnenah chuan, Pathian chatuana nung renga thinurnaa khat rangkachak berhbu pasarih a pe a.Tin, pindan chhungnung chu Pathian ropuina leh a thiltihtheihna khuin a khat ta a; vantirhkoh pasarihte hremna pasarih chu a reh hma loh zawng, pindan chhungnungah chuan tumah an lut thei lo.
8. Tin, pindan chhungnung chu Pathian ropuina leh a thiltihtheihna khuin a khat ta a; vantirhkoh pasarihte hremna pasarih chu a reh hma loh zawng, pindan chhungnungah chuan tumah an lut thei lo.
