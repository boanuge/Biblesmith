1. Chutichuan Anija chu a pute hnenah a muhil a, Davida khawpuiah an phum a, a aiah a fapa Asa a lal ta a: alal chhung chuan an ram chu kum sawm zet ralmuangin a aawm a.
2. Asa chuan LALPA a Pathian mithmuhah thil tha leh dik a ti thin a:
3. ram dang pathian maichamte leh hmun sangte a thehthang bo a, lungphunte a tichim a, Aseri-te a kit a:
4. Juda-hote chu LALPA an pi leh pute Pathian zawng tur leh a dan leh a thupek zawm turin thu a pe a.
5. Tin, Juda-te khua zawng zawnga hmun sangte leh mi lemte a thehthang bo vek bawk a; a hnuaiah ram chu ralmuangin a awm a.
6. Tin, Judai rama chuan kulh nei khuate a siam a; ram chu ralmuangin a awm a, LALPA'n ralmuanna a pek avangin chung kum chuan tumah do a nei lo.
7. Judate hnena chuan, "Heng khuate hii din tha ang u khai, kulhte in hung i la, in sang te, kulh kawngkhar te, akalhna te i siam ang u; LALPA kan Pathian hi kan zawn tak avangin ram chu kan huaiah a lo awm ta a; amah chu kan zawng a, ani chuan kil tin, kil tanga ralmuanga awmna min pe ta a ni," a ti a. Chutichuan an din tha a, an hmuingil ta zel a.
8. Tin, Asa chuan Juda mi, phaw leh fei keng mi sipai nuaithum anei a; tin, Benjamin mi, phaw leh thal hmang mi nuaihnih leh singriat: chung zawng anwg chu mi chak huaisen vek an ni.
9. Tin, Ethiopia mi Zera, sipai maktaduai leh tawlailir zathum nen anmahni bei turin a lo thawk a; Maresa a lo thleng a.
10. Tichuan Asa chu a bei turin a va thawk a, Maresa-ah Zephatha ruamah an va inzar a.
11. Tin, Asa chuan LALPA a Pathian a au a, "LALPA chauh lo chu mi chak tak laka chak lo tanpuitu an awm lo a; aw LALPA kan Pathian, min tanpui rawh khai; nangmah ringin i hmingin he'ng mipuiho hi kan rawn bei a ni a. Aw LALPA, kan Pathian i ni a, mihringin nang chu ngam suh se," a ti a.
12. Chutichuan LALPA'n Asa leh Judate hmaah chuan Ethiopia mite chu a bei a; tichuan Ethiopia mite chu an tlanchhe ta a.
13. Tin, Asa leh a hote chuan Gerar thlengin an um a; Ethiopia mite chu thi an tam em avangin tan an khawh thei ta lo a; LALPA leh a sipaite hmaa chuan tihtlawm an ni ta a; rallak sum tam tak an la a.
14. Gerar vela khua zawng zawngte chu an bei a, LALPA chu an hlau ta a, khua zawng zawng chu an rawk a, rallak sum a tam hle mai a.Ranpuho puan inte pawh an bei a, beram leh sanghawngsei tam tak an kawl a, Jerusalem-ah an haw leh ta a.
15. Ranpuho puan inte pawh an bei a, beram leh sanghawngsei tam tak an kawl a, Jerusalem-ah an haw leh ta a.
