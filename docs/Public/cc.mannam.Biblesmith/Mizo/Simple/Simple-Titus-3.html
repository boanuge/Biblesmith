1. Lalte leh thuneitute laka intulut tur te, thu awih tur te, thil tha tinreng tiha inpeih tur te,
2. tumah sawichhe lo tur te, kawhmawh bawl lo tur te, mi zawng zawng laka thuhnuairawlhna tinreng tilanga nunnem turtein anmahni hriat chhuahtir leh rawh.
3. Keini pawh hmanah khan a tak te, thu awih lo te, buma awm te, chakna leh lawmna tinreng rawngbawl te, sual leh itsik hmanga awm te, tenawm te, inhaw tawn te kan ni si a.
4. Nimahsela, Pathian min Chhandamtu ngilneihzia leh mihring chunga a hmangaihzia chu a lo lan lai khan,
5. Thil fel taka tih, keimahni ngeia tihte chu, chu mi avang ni loin, siamtharna silna leh Thlarau Thianghlim siamthat lehna chuanin a khawngaihna ang zel zawkin min chhandam ta.
6. Chu Thlarau Thianghlim chu Isua Krista min Chhandamtu zarah chuan nasa taka kan chunga a leih kha.
7. Chutichuan, a khawngaihnaa thiam chantira lo awmin, chatuana nun beiseina ang zela roluahtu tura siamin kan awm thei ang.
8. "Pathian ring tawhte chuan thil tha ti zel turin ngaihtuah rawh se,"tih thu hi a rinawm a ni, chung thu chanchin chu huai taka i hrilh tlat ka duh a ni. Chung thu chu mihringte tan a thain a sawt a ni;
9. nimahsela zawhna atthlak te, Dan thua insualna te chu hawisan zel rawh; chung chu thil sawt lo leh engmah lo a ni si a.
10. Rin hran nei mi chu vawi hnih khat zilh hnu chuan zilh duh tawh suh;
11. chutiang mi chu a kal pengin a sual a ni tih in hria e, amah leh amah thiam loh inchantir a ni si a.
12. Artema emaw, Tukika emaw i hnena ka rawn tirh hunah chuan Nikopoli khuaah ka hnenah lo kal hram hram ang che; chutah chuan thlasika awm ka tum si a.
13. Zina, dan hretu leh, Appollova chuan engmah an tlakchham lohna'n an zinnaah chuan thahnemngi takin puibawm ang che.
14. Kan mite chu tlakchham an neih ngai lohna'n hna tangkai thawka inhmang reng turin fuih la, an awm mai mai tur a ni lo.Ka hnena awm zawng zawngte hian chibai an buk a che. Rinnaa min hmangaihtute kha chibai min buksak ang che. In zaa hnenah khawngaihna awm rawh se.
15. Ka hnena awm zawng zawngte hian chibai an buk a che. Rinnaa min hmangaihtute kha chibai min buksak ang che. In zaa hnenah khawngaihna awm rawh se.
