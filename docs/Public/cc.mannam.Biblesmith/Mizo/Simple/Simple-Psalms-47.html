1. Aw, mi tinrengte u, in kut beng u la; Hlimna awin Pathian hnenah au rawh u.
2. LALPA Chungnungbera chu a hlauhawm si a; Lei chung zawng zawnga Lal ropui ak a ni.
3. Ani chuan mi tin, chu kan hnuaiah a tudawl ang a, Hnam tin chu kan ke hnuaiah a tudawl bawk ang.
4. Ani chuan kan roluah tur chu min thlansak ang, A hmangaih Jakoba chhuan ber ram chu. (Selah)
5. Pathian chu au thawm nen a chho a, LALPA chu tawtawrawt ri nen a chho ta e.
6. Pathian fakin zai u la, fakin zai rawh u.: Kan lalber fakin zai u la, fakin zai rawh u.
7. Pathian chu khawvel zawng zawng Lal a ni si a: Hre thiama fakin zai rawh u.
8. Pathian chu hnam tin chungah lalah a thu a: Pathian chu a lalthutphah thianghlimah chuan a thu a.Abrahama Pathian mite ni turin Mi ti zinga mi liante chu an awm khawm a: Leilung phawte chu Pathian ta ani si a; Ani chu nasa taka chawimawiin a awm.
9. Abrahama Pathian mite ni turin Mi ti zinga mi liante chu an awm khawm a: Leilung phawte chu Pathian ta ani si a; Ani chu nasa taka chawimawiin a awm.
