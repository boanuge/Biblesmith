1. Chutichuan Israel-ho zawng zawng chu Hebron-a Davida hnenah an kal khawm a, "Ngai teh, i thisen zawmpui kan ni a.,
2. Tun hmaa Saula a lal lai khan Israelte hruai chhuak leh hruai luttu i ni thin a; tin, LALPA i Pathianin i hnenah, 'Ka mi Israelte chu i chawm ang a, ka mi Israelte chungah hotuah i awm ang,' a ti chia," an ti a.
3. Chutichuan Israel-ho upa zawng zawng chu Hebron-ah lal hnenah chuan an kal a; tin, Davida'n Hebron-ah chuan LALPA hmaah an hnenah thu a thlung a, tin, LALPA'n Samuela hmanga a sawi ang khan Davida chu Israelte lal atan hriak an thih ta a.
4. Tin, Davida leh Israel-ho zawng zawng chu Jerusalem-ah an kal a (chu chu Jebus a ni);tin, chutah chuan chu mi rama mi, Jebus-hote chu an awm a.
5. Tin, Jebus-a awmhote chuan Davida hnenah, "Hetah hian i lo lut tawp lo ang," an ti a. Nimahsela Davida chuan Zion kulh chu a la ta reng reng a: chu chu Davida khua tih a ni ta a.
6. Tin, Davida'n, "Tu pawh Jebus-ho han bei hmasa apiang chu sipai hotu berah a awm ang," a ti a. Tichuan Zeruia fapa Joaba chu a han chho hmasa a, hotu berah an siam ta a.
7. Tin, Davida chu kulhah a awm ta a; chuvangin Davida khawpui an ti a ni.
8. Tin, Milo atangin khawpui vel kulh chu a siam tha a; tin, Joaban khawpui chhung a siam tha a.
9. Tichuan Davida chu a lo ropui ta deuh deuh a; sipaihote LALPA chu a hnenah a awm avangin.
10. Hengte hi Davida sipai huaisen, Israel zawng zawngte nena tanga, Israelte chungchang thua LALPA'n a sawi anga lala siam tura a ram dinpuitu huaisen tak takte chu an ni.
11. Hengte hi sipai huaisen, Davida hnena awmte an ni: Hakmon mi fapa Jasobeama, sawmthum pawl hotu, zathum chungah a fei leka tum khata thattu chu.
12. Tin, a dawttu chu Aho mi Dodova fapa Eleazara, sipai huaisen pathumte zinga pakhat kha.
13. Ani chu barli awmna chuk, Philistia mite indo tura infawk khawmna hmun Pas-dammim-ah khan Davida hnenah a awm a; tin, mipuite chu Philistia mite hmaah chuan an tlanchhe ta a.
14. Tin, anni chu chu mi hmunah chuan an ding a, an lo tang tlat a, Philistia mite chu an that a, tichuan LALPA'n ropui taka ngamsakin a chhandam a.
15. Tin, sipai sawmthum zinga huaisen pathumte chu lungpuiah Adulam pukan Davida hnenah an chhuk a; Philistia sipaiho chuan Rephaim rumah tanhmun an khuar a.
16. Chutih lai chuan Davida chu kulhbingah a awm a, tin, Philistia sipaiho chu Bethlehem-ah an awm a.
17. TiN Davida chuan, "Beth-lehem-a tuichhunchhuah tui, kulh kawngkhar kianga mi kha in tura mi pe han awm teh se la aw!" a ti a, a ngai em em a.
18. Tin, pathumte chuan Philistia sipaiho chu an tawn tlang a, kulh kawngkhar kianga mi Bethlehem-a tui chhuunchhuah tui chu an va chawi a, Davida hnenah an rawn la a; nimahsela Davida chuan a lo in duh lo a, LALPA tan a bun chhuak a,
19. Hei hi ka in loh turzia mai chu: he'ng thih huama inpehote thisen chu ka in dawn em ni? Thih huama inpein an rawn la ani si a," a ti a. Chutichuan a in duh ta lo a. Mi huaisen pathumte chuan chutiang chuan an ti a.
20. Tin, Joaba unau Abisaia chu pathumte zinga hotu ber a ni a; mi zathumte chungah a fei a lek a, anmahni chu a thah avangin pathumte zingah chuan hming that a hlawh a.
21. pathumte zingah chuan pahnihte ai chuan amah chu chawimawi tlak a ni a, an hotuah an siam a, pathum hamsate zingah erawh chuan a tel lo.
22. Tin, Kabzeel mi huai, thil nasa tak titu Jehoiada fapa Benaia chuan Moab mi Ariela fapate pahnih chu a that a; tin, a zuk chhuk a, vur tlak hun laiin khuarkhurumah sakeibaknei a zuk that bawk a.
23. Tin, Aigupta mi, mi awmkhauh pui, tawng ngaa sang a that a; chu Aigupta mi chuan fei lian pui, paun tahtu themkawl ang hi a keng a. Tin, ani chuan tiang nen chuan a zuk pan a, Aigupta mi fei chu a pawhthlaksak a, ama feiin a chhunhlum ta a.
24. Jehoiada fapa Benaia chuan chutiang chuan a ti a, sipai huaisen pathumte zingah hming that a hlawh a.
25. Ngai teh u, mi sawmthumte ai chuan chawimawi tlak a ni a, pathum hmasate zingah erawh chuan a tel lo; tin, Davida chuan a vengtu pawl hotuah a dah a.
26. Sipaite zinga mi huaisen dangte chu hengte hi an ni. Joaba unau Asahela te, Bethlehem khuaa mi Dodova fapa Elhanana te,
27. Haror mi Samotha te, Pelon mi Heleza te,
28. Teko mi Ikesa fapa Ira te, Anathot mi Abiezera te,
29. Hushat mi Sibekaia te, Aho mi Ilaia te,
30. Netophat mi Maharaia te, Netophat mi Baana fapa Heleda te,
31. Benjamina thlah Gibea khuaa mi Ribaia fapa Ithaia te, Pirathon mi Benaia te;
32. Gass luitea mi Huraia te, Arbat mi Abiela te;
33. Baharum mi Azmavetha te, Saalbon mi Eliaba te;
34. Gizon mi Hasema fapa te, Harar mi Sagea fapa Jonathana te;
35. Harar mi Sakara fapa Ahiama te, Ura fapa Eliphala te;
36. Mekerat mi Hephera te, Pelon mi Ahija te;
37. Karmel mi Hezroa te, Ezbaia fapa Naaraia te;
38. Nathana unaupa Joela te, Hagria fapa Mibaha te;
39. Amon mi Zeleka te, Zereuia fapa Joaba ralthuam kengtu Berot mi Naharaia te;
40. Ithra mi Ira te, Ithra mi Gareba te;
41. Hit mi Uria te, Ahlaia fapa Zabada e;
42. Reuben m Siza fapa Adina, Reuben mite zinga hotu, a hnena mi sawmthumte nen;
43. Maaka fapa Hanana te, Mithna mi Josaphata te;
44. Asterat mi Uzia te, Aroer mi Hothama fapa Sama leh Jeiela te;
45. Simria fapa Jediaela te, a unaupa Tiz mi Joha te;
46. Mahav mi Eliela te, Elnaama fapa Jeribaia leh Josavia te, Moab mi Ithma te,Mezoba mi Eliela te, Obeda te, Jaasiela te pawh.
47. Mezoba mi Eliela te, Obeda te, Jaasiela te pawh.
