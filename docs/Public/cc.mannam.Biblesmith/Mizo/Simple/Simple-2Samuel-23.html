1. Heng hi Davida thusawi hnuhnung ber a ni. Jesaia fapa Davida, "Sang taka chawi sana, Jakoba Pathian hriak thih a, Israelte hla mawi tak phuahtu chuan a sawi a:
2. LALPA thlarau chuan keimah mi hmangin a sawi a: A thu chu ka leiah a awm a. A thu chu ka leiah a awm a.
3. Israelte Pathian chuan a sawi a, Israelte Lungpui chuan: 'Mi chunga fel taka rorel thin a, Pathian tih chunga rorel thina chu,
4. Ni a lo chhuah a, chhum awm hauh lo Zing eng ang hi a ni ang a. Ruah sur hnua khaw lo thiang a, Leia hlobet chawr no dep ang hia ni ang.
5. Kan chhungkua chu Pathian hnena mi ani lo'm ni? Chatuan thuthlung chu ka hnenah a thlung a, Engkim fel leh tinghet tlatin; Ka chhandamna kimleh duhthusam kim, A chawr chhuahtir lawng maw?
6. Mi suaksualte erawh chu an zain a hnawla hnawlbo mi, hling ang an ni ang a, Kuta lak mi an ni si lo:
7. A khawihtu tur chuan thir leh feingul rawih zawk tur a ni a; Tichuan an hmunah halral vek an ni ang,' tiin mi bia a," a ti a.
8. Heng hi Davida sipai huaisen hmingthangte chu a ni: Takemon miJosebasehebetha chu sipai hotute zinga hotu ber a ni a; ani chu tum khata mi zariat thattu Eznet Mi Adinoa an tih bawk kha a ni.
9. Tin, a dawttu chu Aho mi fapa Dodaia fapa Eleazara a ni a; chu mi chu Philistia mite indo tura an punkhawm a, an cho a, Israel mite an kal bo vek laia Davida hnena sipai huaisen pathumte zinga pakhat kha a ni:
10. tin, ani chu a tho a,P hilistia mite chu a kut a khama a khandaih pawh thlah thei lo hial khawpin a that a: chu mini chuan LALPA'n nasa takin a ngamssak a; tin, mipuite chu a hnungah rallak thil la tur ngawtin an lo kir leh a.
11. Tin, chu mi dawttu chu Hara mi Agea fapa Sama a ni a. Tin, dailuah awmna chuk hmunah Philistia mite chu lak tumin an infawk khawm a; tin, mipuite chuan Philistia mite chu an tlan chhiatsan a.
12. Nimahsela ani chu chu mi hmunah chuan a ding a, a veng tlat a, Philistia mite chu a that a: tin, LALPA'n nasa takin a ngamsak a.
13. Tin, sipai huai huai sawmthum zinga pathumte chu an chhuk a, buh seng lai takin Davida hnenah Adulam pukah an kal a; tin, Philistia sipaiho chuan Rephaim kawnah tanhmun an khuar a.
14. Tin, chutih lai chuan Davida chu kulhbingah a awm a, chutih lai bawk chuan Philistia sipaiho chu Beth-lehem-ah an awm a.
15. Tin, Davida chuan tui a chak em em mai a, "Beth-lehem-a tuichhunchhuah tui, kulh kawngkhar kianga mi kha in tura mi pe han awm teh se la aw!" a ti a.
16. Chutichuan sipai huaisen pathumte chuan Philistia sipaiho chu an tawn tlang a, kulh kawngkhar kiang ami Beth-lehem-a tuichhunchhuah chu an va chawi a, an keng a, Davida hnenah an kalpui a: nimahsela a lo in duh lo a, LALPA tan a bun chhuak ta zawk a.
17. "Aw LALPA, hei hi ka tih loh turzia mai chu; thih huama kalte thisen chu ka in dawn em ni?" a ti a; chutichuan a in duhta lo a. Chung thilte chu sipai huaisen pathumte chuan an ti a ni.
18. Zeruia fapa Joaba unau Abisaia chu an pathum zinga hotu ber a ni a. A feiin mi zathumte chu a bei a, a that a, pathumte zingah chuan hming that a hlawh a.
19. Pathumte zingah chuan chawimawi tlak ber a ni lo'm ni? Chuvangin an hotu atan an siam a: nimahsela chungnungber pathum zinga chhiar tel a kai lo.
20. Tin, Kebzeel mi huai fapa Jehoiada fapa Benaia thil nasa tak titu chuan Moab mi Ariela fapate pahnih chu a that a: tin, a zuk chhuk a, vut tlak hun laiin khuarkhurrumah sakeibaknei a zuk that bawka:
21. tin, Aigupta mi, mi lian tak a that a: Aigupta mi chuan fei a keng a; nimahsela ani chuan tiang kengin a zuk pan a, Aigupta mi fei ken chu a chhuhsak a, ama fei chuan a chhunhlum ta a,
22. Jehoiada fapa Benaia chuan chutiang thil chu a tih avangin sipai huaisen pathumte zingah chuan hmingthat a hlawh a.
23. Mi sawmthumte ai chuan chawimawitlak a ni a; nimahsela chungnungber pathumte zinga chhiar tel a kai lo. Tin, Davida chuan amah vengtu hotu atan a dah a.
24. Joaba unau Asahela chu sawmthum zinga pakhat chu a nia; Beth-lehem khuaa mi Dadoa fapa Elhanana te;
25. Harod mi Sama te, Harod mi Elika te;
26. Pal mi Heleza te, Teko mi Ikesa fapa Ira te;
27. Anathot mi Abiezera te, Husat mi Mebunaia te; Aho mi Zalmona te, Netophat mi Maharaia te;
28. Netophat mi Bana fapa Heleba te, Benjamina thlah, Gebea khuaa mi Ribaia fapa Itaia te;
29. Pirathon mi Benaia te, Gaas-a luitea mi Hidaia te;
30. Arbat mi Abi-albona te, Barhum mi Azmavetha te;
31. Saalbon mi Eliaba te, Jesena fapate zinga Jonathana te;
32. Harar mi Sama te, Araar mi Sarara fapa Ahiama te:
33. Maakat mi Ahasbaia fapa Elipheleta te, Gilon mi Ahitophela fapa Eliama te;
34. Karmel mi Hezroa te, Arba mi Paaria te;
35. Zoba khuaa mi Nathana fapa Igala te, Gad mi Bania te;
36. Zeruia fapa Joaba ralthuam kengtu Amon mi Zeleka te, Beerot mi Naharaia te:
37. It mi Ira te, Itmi Gareba te;Hit mi Uria te an ni: an za chuan sawmthum leh pasarih an ni.
38. Hit mi Uria te an ni: an za chuan sawmthum leh pasarih an ni.
