1. Tin, tura lal Hiraman Davida hnenah mi a tir a, Sidar thingte a thawn a, lung chhertute, themthiamte a in sa turin a tir bawk a.
2. Tin, Davida chuan LALPA'n Israel mite chunga lalah amah chu a tinghetin a mi Israelte avangin a ram chu a chawimawi a ni tih a hre ta a.
3. Tin, Davida chuan Jerusalem-ah nupuite a nei belhchhah leh a, tin, Davida chuan fanu leh fapate a nei leh a.
4. Jerusalem-a a fa neihte chu hengte hi an ni: Samua te, Sobaba te, Nathana te, Solomona te;
5. Ibhara te, Elisua te, Elpeleta te;
6. Noga te, Nephega te, Japhia te;
7. Elisama te, Beeliada te, Elipheletea te.
8. Tin, Philistia miten Davida chu Israel mi zawng zawngte lal atan hriak an thih tih an hriatin, Philistia mi zawng zawng chu Davida zawng turin an chho ta a; tin, Davida chan a lo hria a, anamahn bei turin a kal ta a.
9. Tin, Philistia mite chu rephaim ruamah an inzar a.
10. Tin, Davida chuan, "Philistia mite bei turin ka han chho dawn em? Ka kuta hian anniho chu i pe dawn em?" tiin LALPA a rawn a. Tin, LALPA chuan, "Han chho rawh; anniho chu i kutah ka pe dawn a ni," a ti a.
11. Tichuan baal-perazim-ah an chho a, chuta chuan Davida'n anniho chu a hneh a, "LALPA'n ka hmelmate chu ka kut hmangin tuikhuah sah ang maiin min tihdarhsak ta," a ti a.Chutichuan chu mi hmun hmingah chuan Baal-perazim an vuah a.
12. Tin, chutah chuan an milemte an kalsan a, tin, Davida'n thu a pe a, an hal ta a.
13. Tin, Philisita-hote ch ruamah an inzar leh a.
14. Tin, Davida'n Pathian a rawn leh a; tin, Pathianin a hnenah, "Um loin kiansan la, thingtheihmukung zawnlamah han bei zawk rawh.
15. Tin, ehti hi ani ang a, thingtheihmu lera kal thawm ang ri thup thup i hriat chuan che rawh, Philsitai sipaite bei turin i hmaah Pathian a chhuak ta tihna ani ang," a ti a.
16. Tin, Pathianin thu apek angin Davida chuan a ti a; Philistia sipaite chu Gibeon atanga Gezer thalngin an bei a.Tichuan ram tinah Davida hming chu a lo thang ta a; LALPA'n hnam tin, mah a tihtir ta a.
17. Tichuan ram tinah Davida hming chu a lo thang ta a; LALPA'n hnam tin, mah a tihtir ta a.
