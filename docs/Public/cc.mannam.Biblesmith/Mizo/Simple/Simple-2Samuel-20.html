1. Tin, chutah chuan Benjamin mi, Bikria fapa a hming Seba, Beliala mi pakhat a lo awm hlau mai mai a: tawtawrawt a ham a, "Davida-ah hian chanvo kanei lo a, Jesaia fapaah hian roluah tur ka nei hek lo: aw Israel mite u, in puan in lam theuhvah haw rawh u," a ti a.
2. Chutichan Israel mi zawng zawngin Davida an zui chu an let chhohsan a, Bikria fapa Seba chu an zui ta a; Juda mite eraw chuan Jordan atangin Jerusalem thlengin an lal chu an vuan ta tlat a.
3. Chutichuan Davida chuan Jerusalem a a in chu a thleng a; tin, lal chuan hmeiche sawm a hmeite in nghak atana a dahte chu a hruai a, a khung a, an ei tur a tum a, nimahsela anhnenah a lut lo a. Tichuan an thih ni thlenga khunga awmin hmeithaiin an awm ta a.
4. Chutichuan lal chuan Amasa hnenah, "Ni thum chhungin Juda mite min koh khawmsak la, tin, nang pawh lo awm ve ang che," a ti a.
5. Chutichuan Amasa chu Juda mite ko khawm turin a kal ta a; amaherawhchu a hun ruatsak aiin a va thang rei ta a.
6. Tichuan Davida'n Aisaia hnenah chuan, "Bikria fapa Seba hian Absaloma ai mahin kan pawi a khawih dawn alawm le: i pu mite hruai la, um rawh u khai; chutilochuan khaw kulh neiahte a lut hman ang a, kan hmu phak loah kan chauh mai dah ang e," a ti a.
7. Tichuan Joaba-ho te, Keret mi te, Pelet mite leh an mi chak zawng zawngte chuan amah an zui chhuak ta a: Jerusalem atang chuan Bikria fapa Seba um tur chuan anchuak ta a.
8. Gideon-a lungui awmna an va thlen chuan Amasa chuan anmahni chu a lo hmuak a. Tin, Joaba chuan indona thuamhnaw a inbel a, tin, a chungah chuan khandaih a pai chawpin kawnghrenah a pai a, tin, a kal zawngin a dawk chhuak a.
9. Tin, Joaba chuan Amasa hnenah chuan, "Unaupa, i tha rih alawm maw?" a ti a. Tin, Joaba chuan Amasa fawh tumin a khabe hmul chu a kut ding lamin a chelh a.
10. Nimahsela Amasa chuan Joaban khandaih a ken chu a ngaihsak lo va: chutichuan apumah chuan a vit a, a rilte chu a titla nuai mai a, a vit nawn leh ta lo a; tichuan a thi ta a. Tichuan Joaba leh auaupa Abisaia chuan Bikria fapa Seba chu an um ta a.
11. Tin, a kianga Joaahote zinga tlangval pakhat chu a ding a, "Joaba lama tang leh Davida lam lama tang apiang chuan Joaba zui zel rawh se," a ti a.
12. Tin, Amasa chu lamlian lai takah a thiin a lo bual nuaih mai a. Tin, chu mi chuan mi zawng zawng ding renga a hmuh chuan Amasa ruang hu lamlian ata phul lamah sawnin puanin akhuh a, akianga lo kal apiang an ding zela a hmuh avangin.
13. Tin, lamlian ata dah sawn a nih hnu chuan Bikria fapa Seba um turin mi zawng zawgin Joaba chu an zui ta a.
14. Seba chuan Abel thleng leh Bethmaaka thlengin Israel-ho ram chu a tlawh vek a, Ber mite pawh a tlawh a; tichuan mite chu an pung khawm a, amah an zui ve ta a.
15. Beth-maaka-ah chuan an rawn hual a; tin, a khaw hrulah lei an tiang pawng a, kulh hrulah chuan an ding a; tin, Joaba hnena mi awm zawng zawngte chuan tichim turin kulh chuan chhu bur bur a.
16. Chutichuan hmeiche fing pakhat khua atang chuan a lo au a, "Khai kai; Joaba hnenah, 'Lo kal hnai rawh, i hnenah thu ka lo sawi teh ang,' a ti, han ti rawh u khai," a ti a.
17. Tin, a hnenah chuan a va kal hnai a; tin, hmeichhia chuan, "Joaba i ni em?" a ti a. "Ni e," a ti a, a chhang a. Chutichuan ahnenah, "I bawihnu thusawi hi ngaithla teh," a ti a. Tin, ani chuan a chhang a, "Ka ngaithla e," a ti a.
18. Chutichuan , "Hmanlai chuan, "Abel khuaah hian an inrawn phawt ang,' an ti thin a; chutiang chuan thu an titawp thin.
19. Kei hi Israel zinga inrem duh leh rinawm pawla mi ka ni a: nang chuan Israel khua, a khaw bulpui hi tihchhiat i tum a nih hi; LALPA ro chu engah nge tihmang i tum?" tiina sawi a.
20. Tin, Joaba chan a chang a, "Chutang ani awzawng lo v, tihmang ew, tihchhiat emaw ka tum lo reng reng e.
21. Chutiang lam thu chu a ni lo e: Ephraim tlang rama mi Bikria fapa ahming Seba chuan lal dodalin, Dvida ngei dodalina kut a lek a; ama chu min pe mai u la, khua chu ka chauhsan mai ang e," a ti a. Tin, hmeichhia chuan Joaba hnenah, "Ngai teh, a lu chu kulhah kan rawn paih liamsak ang che," a ti a.
22. zawngte zingah chuan a kal a. Tin, Bikria fapa Seba lu chu an tan a. Joaba hnen lamah chuan an thehchhuak a. Tin, tawtawrawt a ham a, mahni puan in lam theuhvah khua ata chu an darh ta a. Tin, Joaba chu Jerusalemah lal hnenah chuan a haw leh ta a.
23. Joaba chu Israel sipai zawng zawng hotu a ni a; tin, Jeoada fapa Benaia chu Keret mi leh Pelet mite hotu a ni a:
24. tin, Adorama chu hnathawka rawih luihte hotu a ni a; tin, Ahiluda fapa Jehosaphata chu chanchin ziaktu a ni a;
25. tin, Seva chu lehkha ziaktu a ni a; tin, Zadoka leh Abiathara chu Puithiam an ni a:tin, Jair mi Ira pawh chu Davida puithiam a ni a.
26. tin, Jair mi Ira pawh chu Davida puithiam a ni a.
