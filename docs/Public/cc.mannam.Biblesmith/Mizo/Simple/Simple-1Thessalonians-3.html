1. Chutichuan, kan tuar zawh tak loh chinah chuan Atheni khuaah keimahni chauh hnutchhiahin awm i la tha kan ti a,
2. Timothea, kan unaupa, Krista Chanchin Tha lama Pathian rawngbawltu hi, tinghet tur che u leh in rinna kawnga thlamuan tur che uin, kan rawn tir a ni,
3. he'ng hrehawm tuarnate avang hian tumah an nghin lohna turin, hengte hi tuar turin ruat kan ni tih nangmahni ngeiin inhre si a.
4. In hnena kan awm lai khan hrehawm tuar tur kan ni tih kan hrilh lawk ngei che u kha; chutiang ngei chuan a thleng tawh a, chu chu in hriat kha.
5. Chu mi avang chuan kan tuar zawh tak loh hnuin, thlemtu chuan a thlem thlu hlauh ang che u a, kan thawh rimna chu athlawn dah ang e, tiin in rinzia ka lo hriat theihna turin mi ka rawn tir a ni.
6. Nimahsela, Timothea chu in hnen atangin tunah kan hnen a lo thlen hian, hmuh che u kan chak ang bawkin min hmuh chak em emin khawngaih takin min hre reng fo tih thu leh in rinna leh in hmangaihna chanchin lawmawm tak min rawn hawn a;
7. chu mi avang chuan unaute u, kan manganna leh hrehawmna zawng zawngah, in rinna avangin in chungah thlamuang takin kan awm ta.
8. Tunah chuan kan nung ta, Lalpaa in din ngheh phawt hi chuan.
9. In hmel kan hmuh theihna tur leh, in rina lama in tlakchhamna kan phuhruksak theihna tur che uin
10. a chhun a zana nasa taka tawngtaiin kan Pathian hmaah in chunga kan hlimna zawng zawng avang hian nangmahni lam thu-ah Pathian hnenah eng lawmthu nge kan hrilh ve thung theih ang le aw?
11. Tichuan, kan Pathian, kan pa ngei chuan kan Lalpa Isua pawhin in hnen lamah min hruai rawh se.
12. Tin, nangmahni lama hmangaihna kan ngah angin, nangmahni lamah inhmangaih tawnna leh mi zawng zawng lamah hmangaihna chu Lalpa chuan tihpunsakin tihngahsak che u rawh se:chutichuan kan Lalpa Isua, a mi thianghlim zawng zawngte nena a lo kal hunah, kan Pathian leh kan Pa hmaah in thinlungte thianghlimna kawngah sawiselboin a tinghet thei ang che u.
13. chutichuan kan Lalpa Isua, a mi thianghlim zawng zawngte nena a lo kal hunah, kan Pathian leh kan Pa hmaah in thinlungte thianghlimna kawngah sawiselboin a tinghet thei ang che u.
