1. Nebata fapa lal Jeroboama kum sawm leh kum riat a lal kum chuan Juda hote chungah Abijama chu a lal tan a.
2. Jerusalem khuaah kum thum a lal a: a nu hming chu Kaaki, Abisaloma fanu a ni.
3. A hmaa a pain thil sual a tih zawng zawngte kha a ti ve a: a pu Davida thinlung angin a thinlung chu LALPA a Pathian lamah a tha famkim ve lo a.
4. Nimahsela Davida avangin LALPA a Pathianin a hnua a fapa la tundinna tur leh Jerusalem khua din nghet turin Jerusalem khuaah chuan khawnvar a neihtir a;
5. Hit mi Uria chungchang tih loah chuan Davida'n LALPA mithmuha thil tha a tih thin avang leh a dam chhung zawnga ti tura thu a pek tawh phawt chu a hnial ngai hauh loh avnaign.
6. Rehoboama nen an indo reng thin a.
7. Abijama chanchin dangte leh a thiltih zawng zawngte chu Juda lalte lal lai thu ziakna buah chuan a chuang a ni lo'm ni? Tin, Abijama leh Jeroboama chu an indo thin a.
8. Tin, Abijama chu apute hnenah chuan a muhil a; tichuan Davida khawpuiah an phum ta a: tin, a aiawhin a fapa Asa chu a lal ta a.
9. Israel lal Jeroboama kum sawmhnih a lal kum chuan Asa chu Juda-hote chungah a lal tan a.
10. Tin, kum sawmli leh kum khat Jerusalem-aha lal a: a nu hming chu Maaki, Abisaloma fanu a ni.
11. Tin, Asa chuan apu Davida ang bawkin LALPA mithmuha thil tha a ti thin a.
12. Tin, mipa inzuarhote chu a ram ata a hnawt chhuak a, a putena milem an lo siam zawng zawngte chu a thehthang bo vek a.
13. Tin, a nu Maaki pawh chu, milem tenawm tak tak Aseri a siamsak avangin, a lalnu nih a ban a; tin, Asa chuan a milem siam chu a sat thlu a, Kidron luiah a hal a.
14. Hmun sangte erawh chu a thehthang bo lo va: nimahsela Asa thinlung chu a dam chhung zawng LALPA lamah a tha famkim a ni.
15. Tin, a pain thil alo hlan sa tawhte leh, amah ngeiin a lo hlan tawh, tangkarua te, rangkachak te, bungbelte chu LALPA inah a la lut a.
16. Tin, Asa leh Israel leh Baasa chu an lal chhung zawng an indo reng thin a.
17. Tin, Israel lal Baasa chuan Juda-ho a han bei a, Juda lal Asa hnena lut tur leh chhuak tur danna'n Rama khua a siam a.
18. Chutichuan Asan LALPA in leh lal ina ro la awm, tangkarua leh rangkachak zawng zawngte chu a la a, a chhiahhlawhte a pe a; tin, lal Asa chuan Damasaka khuaa awm Suria lal Heziona fa tabrimona fapa Benhadada hnenah chuan,
19. "Kei leh nanga karah leh ka pa leh i pa karah thuthlung a awm a: ngai teh, lawmman atan tangkarua leh rangkachak ka han thawn a che; ti teh, Israel lal Baasa hian mi tinsanna turin a hnena i thuthlun kha that teh mai," tiin a thawn a.
20. Tin, Benhadada chuan lal Asa thu chu a lo pawm a, tichuan Israel khuate chu bei turin sipai hotute a tir a, Ijon khua te, Dan khua te, Abel-bethmaaka khua te, Kineroth bial zawng zawng, Naphtali ram zawng zawngte nen a bei a.
21. Tin, heti hi ani a, Baasan chu mi thu chu a lo hriat veleh Rama khaw siam chu a bang a, Tirza khuaah a awm ta a.
22. Chutichuan lal Asa chuan Judai ram zawng zawngah thu a than vel ta a; tumah awl reng reng an awm lo: tichuan Baasan Rama khaw siamna'na lung leh thing a hmante chu an thiar a; tin, chung hmang chuan lal Asan Mizpe khua leh Banjamin rama Geba khua a siam a.
23. Asa chanchin dang zawng zawng te, a thiltih zawng zawng te, a khaw siamte chu Juda lalte lal lai thu ziakna buah chuan a chuang a ni lo'm ni? Amaherawhchu a tar lamah a keah hri eng emaw alo vei ta a.
24. Tin, Asa chu a pute hnenah a muhil a, a pu Davida khawpuiah a pute zingah an phum ta a; tin, a fapa Jehosaphata chu a aiawhin a lal ta a.
25. Tin, Jeroboama fapa Nadaba chu Juda lal Asa lal kum hnihnaah chuan Israel- hote chungah a lal tan a, Israel hote chungah kum hnih a lal tan a, Israel hote chungah kum hnih a lal a.
26. LALPA mithmuhin thil tha lo tak a ti thin a, a pa kawng zawh chu a zawh ve a, Israel-hote thil tihsualtira a tih sualna kha a ti ve a.t
27. Tin, Isakara chhungte zinga mi Ahija fapa Baasan amah a phiar ru a, Philistia-ho chan, Gibbethon khuaah Baasa chuan a that ta a; Nadaba leh Israel-hoz awng zawngte chuan Gibbethon khua chu an hual lai a ni.
28. Juda lal Asa lal kum thumnaah a ni, Baasa chuan a thah a, a aiawha a lal ni.
29. Tin, heti hi a ni a, lal anih veleh chuan a tihchimih hma loh chuan Jeroboama chhungte zawng zawng chu a that a; nung tawh phawt chu pakhat mah Jeroboama chu a zuahsak lo; LALPA'n Silo khuaa mi a chhiahhlawh Ahija hmanga a lo sawi tawh ang khan:
30. chu chu Israel hote htil tihsualtira a tih, Jeroboama sualnate avang leh LALPA, Israelte Pathian chu a tihthinrim avang a ni.
31. Nadaba chanchin dangte leh a thiltih zawng zawngte chu Israel lalte lal lai thu ziakna buah a chuang a ni lo'm ni?
32. Tin, Asa leh Israel lal Baasa chu an lal chhung zawng an indo reng thin a.
33. Juda lal Asa lal kum thumnaah chuan Ahija fapa Baasa chu Tirza khuaan Israel-ho zawng zawng chungah a lal tan a, kum sawmhnih leh kum li a lal a.LALPA mithmuhin thil tha lo tak a ti thin a, Jeroboama kawng zawh chu a zawh ve a, Israel-hote thil tihsualtira a tih sualna kha a ti ve a.
34. LALPA mithmuhin thil tha lo tak a ti thin a, Jeroboama kawng zawh chu a zawh ve a, Israel-hote thil tihsualtira a tih sualna kha a ti ve a.
