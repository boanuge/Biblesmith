1. Tin, Solomona hmingthanzia Seba lalnuin a lo hriat chuan Solomona chu thu khirh tak taka fiah tumin hnung zuitu tam tak hruaiin sanghawngseitea englo rimtuite leh rangkachak tam tak leh lunghlute phurin Jerusalem-ah a han kal a; tin, Solomona hnen a han thlen chuan, a rilrua awm zawng zawng chu phawrh fai vekin a bi a.
2. Tin, Solomona chuan a thu zawzawng awng chu a chhang vek a; Solomona chuan a chan theih loh reng reng a awm lo a.
3. Mi inawmpawlhho leh sumdawnghote rawn lak luh tiam hauh lohin Solomona hnena kum khat chhunga rangkachak lo lut rih lam chu rangkachak talen zaruk leh sawmruk leh paruk buk a ni.
4. Tin, Arabia lal zawng zawngte lehram awptute chuan Solomona hnenah rangkachak te, tangkarua te an rawn la thin a.
5. Lal hnenah chuan, "I thiltihte leh i finzia thuthang ka rama ka lo hriat chu a dik ngei a lo nih hi le.
6. Nimahsela, keima mit ngeia ka rawn hmuh hma loh chuan an sawi mai chu ka awi thin lo a, ngai teh, i finna ropuizia zatve pawh min lo hrilh hlei nem le: i hmingthan ka lo hriat dan chu i khum daih mai a.
7. I mite chu an va nihlawhin, he'ng i chiahhlawh i bula awm foa i finna thu ngaithla fo thinte hi an va nihlawh em!
8. LALPA i Pathian, i chunga lawm, a lal atana a lalthutthlenga thuttirtu che LALPA i Pathian chu fakin awm raw se; i Pathianin Israel-ho hi kumkhuaa tihngheh atan a hmangaih a, chuvangin fel taka rorel turin an lalah a siam che a nih hi," a ti a.
9. Chutichuan lal chu rangkachak talent za leh sawmhnih le englo rimtui tam tak mai leh lunghlute a pe a, Seba lalnun lal Solomona englo rimtui a pek ang reng reng hi a awm lo.
10. Tin, Solomona chhiahhlawhte leh Hurama chhiahhlawh te, Ophir atanga rangkachak va phurte pawh chuan algum thing leh lunghlute an rawn phur a.
11. Tin, lal chuan alfum thing chu LALPA in leh lal in hungna'n leh hla satute tana tingtang leh perhkhuangah a siam a: chutiang chu Judai ramah tun hma lam chuan an la hmu ngai lo.
12. Tin, Seba lalnuin thil a pek tiam loin lal Solomona chuan a duh apiang a pe bawk a. Chutichuan a ram lama a hote nen an haw leh ta a.
13. Mi inawmpawlhho leh sumdawnghote rawn lak luhtiam hauh loin Solomona hnena kum khat chhunga rangkachak lo lut rih lam chu rangkachak talen zaruk leh sawmruk leh paruk buk a ni.
14. Tin, Arabia lal zawng zawngte lehram awptute chuan Solomona hnenah rangkachak te, tangkarua te an rawn la thin a.
15. Tin, lal Solomona chuan rangkachak den phekin phaw lian mi zahnih a siam a: phaw lian pakhatin rangkachak sekel zaruh a duh a.
16. Tin, rangkachak den phekin phaw zathum a siam a; phaw pakhatin rangkachak sekel zathum a duh a; chung chu Lebanon ngawpui inah chuan a dah a.
17. Tin, lal chuan saingho lalthutthleng ropui tak a siam bawk a, rangkachak hlir hlakin a luan a.
18. lalthutthlengah chuan a kai chhohna rahka paruk te, lalthutthleng nena zawmk, rangkachak te, nghahchhan te, thutthleng sir tuaka ban nghahna te, a ban nghahna bula ding sakeibaknei lem pahnihte a awm a.
19. Tin, rahka paruk sir tuak chungah chuan sakeibaknei sawm leh pahnih a ding a; chutiang chu khawii ramah mah an siam ngai lo.
20. Lal Solomona no zawng anwg chu rangkachak vek a ni a, tin, Lebanon ngawpui ina bungbel zawng zawngte pawh rangkachak hlir hlak vek a ni a; Solomona dam lai chuan tangkarua chu engah mah an ngai lo.
21. Lal chuan lawng rual, Hurama chhiahhlawhte Tarsis-a kal thin kawppui a nei a, kum thum dan zelah Tarsis lawng rual chuan rangkachak te, tangkarua te, saingho te, zawng te, arawn te arawn phur thin a.
22. Chutichuan lal Solomona chuan khawvel lal zawng zawngte chu hausakah leh finah a khum vek a ni.
23. Tichuan Pathianin a thinlunga a dah, a finna thu chu ngaithla turin khawvel lal zawng zawngte chuan Solomona chu an rawn pan huai huai thin a.
24. Tin, kum tin pek bi neiin tangkaraw bungbel te, rangkachak bungbel te, puan te, ralthuam te, englo rimtui te, sakawr te, sabengtungte an rawn pe theuh thin a.
25. Tin, Solomona chuan sakawr leh tawlailir dahna hmun sangli a nei a, tin, sakawr chungchuang mi singkhat leh sanghnih a nei a, chungte chu tawlailir dahna khuaah te, Jerusalema lal hnenah te a awmtir a.
26. Tin, Lui atanga Philistia ram leh Aigupta ram thlenga lal zawng zawng chungah a lal a.
27. Tin, lal chuan Jerusalem khuaah chuan a tam em avangin tangkaruate chu lung ang maia ngaih khawpin a awmtir a, sidar thingte chu phaia theipui thing ang leka ngaih khawpin a awmtir a.
28. Tin, Solomona tan sakawrte Aigupta atangte leh ram tin, atangtein an rawn la thin a.
29. Solomona chanchin dangte chu a bul atanga a tawp thlengin zawlnei Nathana ziak chanchin buah te, Silo mi Ahija hrilhlawkna buah te, Nebata fapa Jeroboama chungchang thua zawlnei Idova inlarna buahte a chuang lo em ni?
30. Tin, Solomona chu Jerusalem-ah Israel mi zawng zawng chungah kum sawmli a lal a.Tin, Solomona chu a pi leh pute hnenah a muhil a, a pa Davida khuaah an phum a, a aiah a fapa Rehoboama a lal ta a.
31. Tin, Solomona chu a pi leh pute hnenah a muhil a, a pa Davida khuaah an phum a, a aiah a fapa Rehoboama a lal ta a.
