1. Tin, Kanaan mi, Arad lal Chhim lama awmin Israel mite atarim kawngah an lo kal tih sawi thawm a hria a; tichuan Israel mite chu a bei a, salahte a man nual a.
2. Tin, Israel mite chuan LALPA hnenah thu an tiam a, "Heng mite hi kan kuta min pek chuan an khuate hi kan tiboral hlauh ang e," an ti a.
3. Tichuan LALPA'n Israel mite thu chu a pawm a, Kanaan mite chu a pe a, anmahni leh an khuate chu an tiboral hlauh a; a hmun hmingah chuan Horma an vuah a.
4. Tin, Hor tlang atang chuan Tuifinriat Sen lamah Edom ram kal hualin an kal a: kawng a kual avangin mipuite rilru chu a lungni lo hle mai a.
5. Mipuite chuan Pathian leh Mosia kalin, "Thlalera thi turin engati nge Aigupta ata min hruai chhuah? Chhang awm hlei lo, tui pawh awm hlei lo; he chhang tenawm pawh hi kan nunnain a ten tawh a ni," an ti a.
6. Chutichuan LALPA'n mipui zingah chuan rul chuk na mite a tir a, mite a chu a, Israel mite tam tak an thi a.
7. Tin, mite chuan Mosia hnenah an kal a. "LALPA leh nangmah kalhin kan lo tawng ta hi kan lo sual ta a ni e, kan zinga rulte hi tibo turin LALPA hnenah tawngtai rawh khai," an ti a; tichuan mite chu Mosia'n chuan a tawngtaisak a.
8. tin, LALPA'n Mosia hnenah "Rul chuk na mi, a lem siam la, banah khai rawh; tin, heti hi a ni ang a, rul chuk apiangin chu chu an en chuan an dam ang," a ti a.
9. Chutichuan Mosia'n dar rul a siam a, banah a khai a; tin, heti hi a ni a, rul chuk apiangin dar rul chu an enin an dam zel a.
10. Tin, Israela tlahte chu an kal ta zel a, Oboth-ah awmhmun an khuar a.
11. Tin, Oboth atang chuan an kal leh a, ni chhuahna lam Moab thlaler bul Ieyabarim-ah awmhmun an khuar a.
12. Chuta tang chuan an kal leh a, Zered ruamah awmhmun an khuar a.
13. Chuta tang chuan an kal leh a, Amor-ho ramri atanga la filawr thlalera Arnon lehlam pangah awmhmun an khuar a; Arnon chu Moab ramri, Moab leh Amor-ho inkara awm a ni a.
14. Chuvangin LALPA Indonate Buah chuan "Supha-a Vaheb, Arnon ruamte,
15. Ar awmna lama thle Moab rama innghat Ruam awih tlan pangperte chu," tia sawi a ni.
16. Chuta tang chuan Beer-ah an kal leh a: chu chu LALPA'n Mosia hnena, "Mipuite chu ko khawm la, tui ka pe ang e," a tihna tuichhunchhuah chu a ni.
17. Tin, he hla hi Israelten an sa a; Aw tuikhur, lo chik chhuak la, zaiin chawi la,
18. Lalten an laih tuikhur chu. An lal tiang leh an talhtuma, Mipuite mi lianho laih chu. Tin, thlaler atangte chuan Matanaah an kal leh a:
19. tin, Matana atangin Nahaliel-ah; Nahaliel atang chuan bamoth-ah:
20. Bamoth atang chuan Moab loa ruam leh thlaler zuk chhun en Pisga chhipah
21. Tin, Israel mite chuan amor-ho lal Sihona hnenah, "I ramah hian min kal tlangtir rawh khai; loah emaw, grep huanah emaw kan peng lo ang a; tui chhunchhuah tuite chu kan in lo ang a: i ram kan pelh hma loh chuan lal lamlianah kan kal zel ang ," tiin mi an tir a.
22. Tin, Israel mite chuan amor-ho lal Sihona hnenah, "I ramah hian min kal tlangtir rawh khai; loah emaw, grep huanah emaw kan peng lo ang a; tui chhunchhuah tuite chu kan in lo ang a: i ram kan pelh hma loh chuan lal lamlianah kan kal zel ang ," tiin mi an tir a.
23. "Nimahsela Sihona chuan Israel mite hu a rama kal tlang a phal lo va: Sihona chuan a mite zawn zawng chu a khawm vek a, Israel mite bei turin thlalerah chuan an thawk chhuak ta awk a, Jahaz an va thlen a; tin, Israel mite chu an bei a.
24. Tin, Israel mite chuan khandaihin an bei a, a ram chu Arnon atanga Jakob tlengin, Amona thlahte thleng hialin an neihsak a: Amona thlahte ram chu ven tlat a ni.chu ven tlat a ni.
25. Tin, Israel mite chuan chung khua zawng zawngte chu an la a: tichuan Israel mite chu Amor-ho khua zawng zawngahte chuan an awm a, Hesbon leh a khawper zawng zawngahte pawh chuan.
26. Hesbon khua chu Amor-ho lal Sihona khua a ni a, ani chu chu mi hmaa Moab lal lo beia a kut ata a ram zawng zawng, Arnon thlenga laksaktu a ni.
27. Chuvangin hlaa phuahtute chuan, Hesbon-ah lo kal u la, Sihona khawpui chu din leh tihngheh ni se;
28. Hesbon atangin mei a chhuak a, Sihona khawpui atangin meialh: Moab-a Ar a kang zo a, Arnon hmun sang lalhote chu
29. I chung a pik e, Moab! In boral ta, aw Kemos mi: A fapate chu tlan bo atan A fanute salah a pe, Amor-ho lal Sihona hnenah.
30. Kan han kap a, Hesbon a boral, Dibon thlengin, Nopha thlengin kan titiau, Chu chuan Medaba a thleng," a ti.
31. Chutiang chuan a ni, Amor-ho ramah Israel mite chu an awm. tin, Mosia'n jezer va thlithlai turin mi a tir a, chuta khuate chu an la a, Amor-ho awmte chu an hnawtchuak a.
32. Tin, an let a, Basan kawng lamah an chho va: tin, Bazan lal Oga chu anmahni bei turin, amah leh a mite zawng zanwg, Edrei-a lo bei turin, a thawk chhuak a.
33. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia hnenah, "Amahh chu hlau suh: amah pawh, a mite zawng zawng pawh, a ram pawh i kutah ka pe tawh a ni: Hesbon-a awm Amor-ho lal Sihona chunga i tih angin i ti tur a ni," a ti a.Chutichuan amah pawh, a fapate pawh, zuah reng reng nei loin a mite zawng zawng nen an that a: a ram chu an luahsak ta a.
34. Chutichuan amah pawh, a fapate pawh, zuah reng reng nei loin a mite zawng zawng nen an that a: a ram chu an luahsak ta a.
