1. Chung niahte chuan mipui tam tak an lo awm leh a, ei tur engmah an neih loh lai chuan ani chuan a zirtirte chu a ko a, an hnenah,
2. "Mipuiho hi tunah ni thum ka hnenah an awm tawh a, ei tur engmah an neih loh avangin ka khawngaih em em a ni;
3. chaw ngheia an ina ka hawntir chuan kawng lakah an chau dawn si a; thenkhatte phei chu an lo kalna a hla hle si a," a ti a.
4. Tin, a zirtirte chuan, "Miin heta thlalerah hian khawia chhangin nge hengho hi an tihtlai theih ang?" tiin amah an chhang a.
5. Tin, ani chuan, "Chhangper engzat nge in neih?" tiin anni chu a zawt a. Tin, anni chuan, "Per sarih," an ti a.
6. Tin, mipuiho chu leia thu turin thu a pe a; tin, chhangper pasarih chu a la a, lawm thu a sawi zawhin a phel a, an hmaa hlui turin a zirtirte hnenah a pe a; tin, mipuihote hmaah chuan an hlui ta a.
7. Nghate tlem te an nei bawk a; tin, ani chuan mal a sawmsak a, chungte pawh chu an hmaa hlui turin thu a pe a.
8. Tichuan an ei a, an puar ta hlawm a; an ei bang nawi pawh chu bawm sarih an rut khat a.
9. Anni chu sangli lai an ni a; tin, ani chuan anni chu a tintir ta a.
10. Tin, ani chu a zirtirte hnenah lawngah chuan a chuang ta nghal a, Dalmanutha ram chu a thleng ta a.
11. Tin, Pharisaite an lo chhuak a, amah fiahna'n, a lakah van ata chhinchhiahna zawngin ani chu an hnial ta a.
12. Tin, ani chu a rilruin nasa takin a rum a, "Engati nge tun lai chhuante hian chhinchhiahna an zawn?Tih tak meuhin ka hrilh a che u, tun lai chhuante hnenah hian chhinchhiahna reng reng pek a ni lo ang," a ti a.
13. Tin, anni chu a kalsan a, lawngah chuan a chuang leh a, ral lehlamah a kal ta a.
14. Tin, a zirtirte chuan chhang an ken theihnghilh a, chhang tlang khat chauh lo chu lawngah chuan chhang dang reng an nei si lo a.
15. Tin, ani chuan an hnenah, "Pharisaite dawidim leh Heroda dawidim lakah chuan inveng u la, fimkhur rawh u," a ti tlat a.
16. Tin, anni chuan, "Chhang kan nei hauh lo," tiin an inngaihtuah a.
17. Tin, Isua'n chu chu a lo hria a, an hnenah "Engati nge chhang in neih loh avanga in inngaihtuah ni? Tunah pawh in rilruin in la hre lo fo maw? a ngaihna pawh in la hre lo maw? in thinlung chu in tisak ta em ni?
18. Mit nei siin in hmu lo em ni? beng nei siin in hre lo em ni? in hre reng lo vem ni?
19. Sangnga zinga chhangper panga ka phel lai khan a nawi kha bawmrang engzat nge in ruh khah?" a ti a. Tin, anni chuan a hnenah, "Sawm leh pahnih," an ti a.
20. Tin, sangli zinga pasarih ka phel lai khan, a nawi kha bawm engzat nge in ruh khah?" a ti a. Tin anni chuan a hnenah, "Bawm sarih," an ti a.
21. Tin, ani chuan an hnenah, "Tunah pawh a ngaihna in la hre lo fo maw?" a ti a.
22. Tin, Bethsaida khua chu an lo thleng a. Tin, a hnenah mitdel an rawn hruai a, amah dek turin an ngen a.
23. Tin, ani chuan mitdel kut chu a chelh a, khua ata a kai chhuak a; tin, a mitah chil a chhak a, a chunga a kut a nghah zawhin, "Khua i hmu thei em?" tiin a zawt a.
24. Tin, a han dak chhuak a, "Mihring ka hmu a, thing kal ang maiin ka hmu si," ti a.
25. Tin, a mitah chuan a kut a nghat leh a; tin, ani chuan a en reng a, a lo dam ta mai a, engkim fiah takin a hmu thei ta a.
26. Tin, Isua chuan, "Khuaah pawh hian lut suh ang che," tiin a inah a hawntir ta a.
27. Tin, Isua leh a zirtirte chu an kal chhuak a, Philipa Kaisari khawperahte chuan an kal a; tin, kal pahin a zirtirte hnenah, "Miten tu nge a ni mi tih?" tiin a zawt a.
28. Tin, anni chuan, " 'Baptistu Johana a ni,' an ti chia; mi dangin 'Elija a ni, an ti chia; thenkhat erawh chuan, 'Zawlnei tua emaw a ni,' an ti che," tiin an hrilh a.
29. Tin, ani chuan an hnenah, "A nih leh nangni'n tu nge a ni mi tih?" tiin a zawt a, Petera chuan a hnenah "Krista chu i ni," tiin a chhang a.
30. Tin, ani chuan a chanchin tumah hrilh loh tur a ti tlat a.
31. Tin, Mihring Fapa chuan thil tam tam a tuar tur te, upate, puithiam lalte, lehkhaziaktute duh loha a awm tur te, tihhluma a awm tur te, ni thum china a thawhleh tur te chu anmahni a zirtir tan ta a.
32. Chung thu chu chiang takin a sawi a ni a. Tin, Petera chuan ani chu a hruai hrang a, a zilh ta a.
33. Ani erawh chu a lehhawi a, a zirtirte chu a en a, "Setan, ka hnungah kal rawh, Pathian thilte ngaihtuah loin, mihring thilte i ngaihtuah zawk a nih hi," tiin Petera a zilh ta a.
34. Tin, mipuiho chu a zirtirte nen a ko a, an hnenah, "Tupawhin mi zui a duh chuan, mahni hrehawm pawisa loin, a kraws puin mi zui rawh se.
35. Tupawh a nun humhim duh apiangin a chan ang a; tupawh keimah avang leh Chanchin Tha avanga nun chan apiangin a humhim ang.
36. Miin khawvel hi apumin nei se la, a nun chan si se la, a tan eng nge sawt ang?
37. Miin a nun aiah eng nge a pek theih ang?Tupawh tun lai chuan uire leh thil tihsual hmangte zinga keimah leh ka thu zahpui apiang, Mihring Fapa hian, vantirhkoh thianghlimte nena a Pa ropuinaa inthuama a lo kal hunah chuan, chu mi chu a rawn zahpui ve thung ang," a ti a.
38. Tupawh tun lai chuan uire leh thil tihsual hmangte zinga keimah leh ka thu zahpui apiang, Mihring Fapa hian, vantirhkoh thianghlimte nena a Pa ropuinaa inthuama a lo kal hunah chuan, chu mi chu a rawn zahpui ve thung ang," a ti a.
