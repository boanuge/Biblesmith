1. Tin, Jehosaphata chu a pi leh pute hnenah a muhil a, a pute zinga a pu Davida khawpuiah an phum a; tin, a fapa Jehorama a aiah a lal ta a.
2. Tin, ani chuan unau, Jehosaphata fapate a nei hlawm a, Azaria te, Jehiela te, Zekaria te, Azaria te, Mikaela te, Sephatia te an ni. Heng zawng zawng hi Israel lal Jehosaphata fapate vek an ni.
3. Tin, an pa chuan thil ropui tak tak a pe a, tangkarua te, rangkachak te, thil hlu tak tak te, Judai rama kulh nei khuate nen: nimasela ram chu Jehorama hnenah fa u ber a nih avangin a pe a.
4. Chutichuan Jehoraman a pa ram a han luah chuan a lo intichak ta a, a unaute zawng zawng chu Israelte zinga puipa tam takte nen khandaihin a tihlum vek a.
5. Jehorama chu kum sawmthum leh kum hnih a nihin a lal tan a; Jerusalem-ah kum riat a lal a.
6. Ahaba chhungte tih angin Israel lalte kawng chu a zawm ve a: Ahaba fanu nupuiah a nei a ni: tichuan LALPA mithmuhah thil tha lo tak a ti thin a.
7. Nimahsela LALPA'n Davida chhungkua chu a tiboral duh lo a, Davida hnena thuthlung avang leh amah leh a thlahte chena khawnvar neihtir reng a lo tiam tawh avangin.
8. Ama chanah chuan Edom-ho chu Judate laka an hel a, lal an insiam chawp a.
9. TichuanJehorama chu a sipai hotute leh a tawlailir zawng zawngte nen a va thawk chhuak a, zanah a va thawk a, anmahni leh tawlailir chunga hotute hualtu Edom-hote chu a va bei a.
10. Chutichuan Edom-hote chu tun thlengin Judate lakah an la hel ta reng a ni; tin, LALPA, an pi leh pute Pathian chu a hawisan avangin, chu mi lai bawk chuan Libna khua pawh a lakah an hel ve a.
11. Tin, Judai ram tlangahte hmun sang a siam a, Jerusalem mite chu a uiretir a, Judate chu a hruai bo a.
12. Tin, zawlnei Elija atangin a hnenah lehkha a lo thleng a, "LALPA, i pu Davida Pathian chuan heti hian a ti, ' I pa Jehosaphata kawngte i zawh lo a, Judate lal Asa kawngte pawh zawh bawk hek lo a,
13. Israel lalte kawng i zawh zawk a, Judate leh Jerusalem-a awmte Ahaba chhungte anga i uiretir a, i unau, i pa chhungte i aia tha zawk i thah bawk avangin,
14. ngai teh, LALPA'n i mi te, i fa te, i nupuite i rante hripuiin a hrem ang a;
15. tin, nangin ril natna nasa tak i nei ang a, ni tin, chu natna avanga i ril chu a tlak a tlak a, a tlak zawh vek thlengin," tiin.
16. Chutichuan Ethiopia mite kiang vela mi Philistia-ho leh Arabia-ho rilru chu Jehorama bei turin LALPA'n a chawk tho va:
17. tichuan Judate chu an han bei a, an ramah an lut a, lal ina thil zawng zawng chu an la vek a, a fapate, a nupuite pawh; chutichuan a fapa naupang ber Jehoahaza chauh lo chu a fa pawh an hnutchiahsak lo.
18. Tin, chu mi hnuah chuan LALPA'n tihdam theih loh ril natnain a hrem a.
19. Tin, heti hi a ni a, a kum hnihna a lo vei hnuin a natna avang chuan a ril chu a tla ta a, a natna rapthlak takin a lo thi ta a. A mite chuan a pa meipui an chhehsak ang khan an chhehsak ve lo.A lal tirhin kum sawmthum leh kum hnih a ni a, Jerusalem-ah kum riat a lal a; tuma ui hlawh loin a thi a, Davida khawpuiah an phum a, lalho thlanah erawh chuan a ni lo.
20. A lal tirhin kum sawmthum leh kum hnih a ni a, Jerusalem-ah kum riat a lal a; tuma ui hlawh loin a thi a, Davida khawpuiah an phum a, lalho thlanah erawh chuan a ni lo.
