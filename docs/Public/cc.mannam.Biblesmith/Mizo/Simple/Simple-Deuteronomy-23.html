1. Sawn chu LALPA mite pung khawm zingah an tel tur a ni lo; chhuan sawmna thlengin a thlahte chu LALPA mite pung khawm zingah an tel tur a ni lo.
2. Amon mite leh Moab mite chu LALPA mite pung khawm zingah an tel tur a ni lo. Chuan sawmna thlengin an zinga mi chu LALPA mite pung khawm zingah an tel hauh lo tur a ni;
3. Aigupta ram atanga in lo chhuah laia chhang leh tuia an lo hmuah loh che u avang leh anchhe lawh tur che ua Mesopotamia rama Pethor khuaa Beora fapa Balaama an rawih avang khan.
4. Nimahsela LALPA in Pathian chuan a hmangaih che u avangin LALPA in Pathian chuan anchhia chu in tan malsawmnaah a chantir ta zawk a.
5. In dam chung zawng anmahni nena inremna leh an hmuingilna engtikah mah in ngaihtuahpui tur a ni lo.
6. Edom mite chu in lungten tur a ni lo a, in unaute an ni si a; Aigupta mite chu in lungten tur ani lo, an ramah chuan in va mikhual tawh avangin.
7. An thlahte chu chhuan thumna atnagin LALPA mite pungkhawm zingah chuan an tel tur a ni.
8. In hmelmate beia in va riahna hmuna chuan thil tha lo tinreng lakah in inthiarfihlim tur a ni.
9. In zingah tupawh zana amaha thil chuak avanga bawlhhlawh an awm chuan, riahhmun ata a chhuak ang a, riahhmunah chuan a lut leh tur ani lo a;
10. amaherawhchu khua a lo tlai lamin a inbual ang a; tin, ni tlakah riahmunah a lut leh tur a ni.
11. Tin, riahhmun pawn lama hmun in ruat ang a, chutah chuan in kal thin tur a ni;
12. tin, in hmanraw zinga chuan suahdur in nei ang a; tin, heti hi ani ang a, chuchuan in va thutna tur chu in lai khuar ang a, in zunram thiar chu in vur leh tur a ni;
13. LALPA in Pathian chu in riahhmuna nangmahni chhanhim tur leh in hmelmate hlan tur che uin a lo tel zel si thin a; chuvangin riahhmun chu a thianghlim tur a ni; chuti lo chuan in zinga thil bawlhhlawh a hmu ang a, a kal bosan dah ang che u.
14. Chhiahhlawh, in hnena an pu rawn tlan chhuahsan chu in pe let leh tur a ni lo:
15. in awmnaa an du ber an thlan apianga chuan in zingah an awm ve mai tur a ni; in hnehchhiah turani lo
16. Israel fanute zingah nawhchizuar an awm tur ani lo a, Israel fapate zingah mipa inzuar an awm tur a ni hek lo.
17. Nawhchi zawrh man emaw, uicho man emaw , thutiamna'n LALPA in Pathian ina in keng tur ani lo; a eng ve ve pawh chu LALPA in Pathian ngaihin thil tenawm a ni.
18. A pung umin in unaute inpuktir tur a ni lo; tangka lama pung emaw, ei tur lama pung emaw, engpawh a pung uma puktir reng reng chu:
19. ram dang mite chu a pung umin in puktir thei ang; nimahsela in unaute chu a pung umin in puktir tur a ni lo; chutichuan in va luah tur rama in kutkawih zawng zawngah LALPA in Pathianin mal a sawm dawn che u nia.
20. LALPA in Pathian hnena thu in tiam in rei rial loin pek nghal tur a ni; LALPA in Pathian chuan a phut dawn ngei si che u a, in tan sual a lo ni mai ang.
21. Thutiam kawnga in insum chuan in tan sual a ni lo ang.
22. In kaa chhuak tawh chu in ti ngei tur a ni; duh thua thilhlan in kaa in tiam, LALPA in Pathian hnena in tiam ang zelin.
23. In thenawm grep huana in kalin in duh tawkin in ei thei a, amaherawhchu in emah in khung tur a ni lo.In thenawm buh ding lai hmunah in kalin a vui chu in thliak thei a; amaherawhchu in thenawm buh ding laiah chuan favah in ruai tur a ni lo.
24. In thenawm buh ding lai hmunah in kalin a vui chu in thliak thei a; amaherawhchu in thenawm buh ding laiah chuan favah in ruai tur a ni lo.
