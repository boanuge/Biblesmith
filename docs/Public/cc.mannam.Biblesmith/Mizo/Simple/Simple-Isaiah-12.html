1. Chu mi niah chuan heti hian i la sawi ang, "Aw LALPA, lawm thu ka hrilh ang che; ka chungah lo thinur ta mah la, i thinur chu a reh ta a, nangin mi thlamuan ta a nih hi.
2. Ngai teh, Pathian chu mi chhandamtu a ni a; ka ring ang a, hlauin ka awm lo vang: LALPA JEHOVA chu ka chakna leh ka hla a ni si a; mi chhandamtu a lo ni ta bawk a," tiin.
3. Chuvangin chhandamna tuikhur atate chu hlim takin tui in la chawi ang.
4. Tin, chu mi niah chuan heti hian in la sawi ang, "LALPA hnenah chuan lawmthu hril u la, a hming lam u la, a thiltihte chu mi tin zingah hriattir u la, a hming chu chawiawiin a awm tih sawi rawh u.
5. LALPA chawimawiin hla sa rawh u; ani chuan thil tha berte chu a lo ti tawh si a; chu chu leilung luah zawng zawngah hian hriatin awm rawh se.Zion-a awmte u , ring takin au u la, au tuar tuar rawh u, Israel-ho Mi thianghlim in zinga rawm chu a ropui si a," tiin.
6. Zion-a awmte u , ring takin au u la, au tuar tuar rawh u, Israel-ho Mi thianghlim in zinga rawm chu a ropui si a," tiin.
