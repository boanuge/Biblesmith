1. Tin, chhinchhiahna pasarihna chu a han phelh veleh, dar chanve lai van chu a reh thuap a.
2. Tin, Pathian hmaa vantirhkoh pasarih ding thinte chu ka hmu a; tin, an hnenah tawtawrawt pasarih an pe a.
3. Tin, vantirhkoh dang a lo kal a, rangkachak rimtui halna bawm kengin maicham kiangah chuan a ding ta a:tin, lalthutphah hmaa rangkachak maicham chunga mi thianghlim zawng zawng tawngtainate belhchhahna turin, englo rimtui tam tak a hnenah an pe a.
4. Tin, englo rimtui khu chu vantirhkoh kut ata Pathian hmaah a chho ta a, mi thianghlimho tawngtainate nen chuan.
5. Tin, vantirhkoh chuan englo rimtui halna bawm chu a la a, maichama meiin a tikhat a, leiah a paih thla ta a; tichuan khawpui ri te, aw te, kawlphe te, lirnghing te a lo awm ve ta nghal a.
6. Tin, vantirhkoh pasarih, tawtawrawt pasarih kengte chu ham turin an inbuatsaih ta a.
7. Tin, a hmasa ber chuan a han ham a; tichuan rial leh mei, thisen nena inpawlh a lo awm ta nghal a, chung chu leiah paih thlakin a awm ta; tichuan lei hmun thuma thena hmun khat chu a kangral ta a, thingte pawh hmun thuma thena hmun khat chu a kangral a, hlobet hring zawng zawngte pawh a kangral ta bawk a.
8. Tin, vantirhkoh pahnihna chuan a han ham a; tichuan tlang lian pui meia kang lai ang tuifinriatah an paih thla a; tichuan tuifinriat hmun thuma thena hmun khat chu thisenah a lo chang ta a;
9. tuifinriata thil siam, nun neite chu, hmun thuma thena hmunkhat chu an thi ta bawk a; lawngte pawh hmun thuma thena hmun khat chu tihchhiat a ni ta bawk a.
10. Tin, vantirhkoh pathumna chuan a han ham a; tichuan arsi lian pui meichher anga alh, van atangin a lo tla a, lui hmun thuma thena hmun khat leh tuikhurte chu a delh ta a;
11. chu arsi hming chu Sai a ni a; tui hmun thuma thena hmun khat chu saiah a lo chang ta; tichuan chu tui chu an in a, mi tam tak an thi a, a lo khak tak avangin.
12. Tin, vantirhkoh palina chuan a han ham a; tichuan ni hmun thuma thena hmun khat, thla pawh hmun thuma thena hmun khat, arsite pawh hmun thuma thena hmun khat chu tihchhiat a ni ta a; chungho hmun thuma thena hmun khat chu a thimna tur leh, chhun chu hmun thuma thena hmun khat a en lohna turin; zan pawh chutiang bawkin a ni.Tin, ka han en a, mupui vanlaizawla thlawkin aw ring takin, "Vantirhkoh pathum tawtawrawt la ham tur, chu mi ri lo la awm turte avang chuan leia awmhote chung a pik e, a pik e, a pik e!" a tih ka hria a.
13. Tin, ka han en a, mupui vanlaizawla thlawkin aw ring takin, "Vantirhkoh pathum tawtawrawt la ham tur, chu mi ri lo la awm turte avang chuan leia awmhote chung a pik e, a pik e, a pik e!" a tih ka hria a.
