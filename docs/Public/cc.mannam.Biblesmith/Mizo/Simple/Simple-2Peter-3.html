1. Duh takte u, tuna in hnena ka ziak hi ka lehkha thawn pahnihna a ni a, a pahnihin in rilruah ngaihtuahna thianghlim chawk tho turin he'ng thilte hi hriat nawntirna tur che uin ka ziak a ni.
2. Zawlnei thianghlimte thusawi lawk leh in tirhkohte kaah Lalpa leh Chhandamtu thupek chu in hriat rengna turin;
3. hei hi hre hmasa u la, ni hnuhnungahte chuan nuihsantute chu anmahni chakna anga awmin, nuihsan chungin an lo kal ang a,
4. "A lo kal lehna thutiam chu khawiah nge? Siam tirh atangin engkim a awm pangngai angin chi thlahtute muthlilh ni ata tawh kha a la awm zel alawm," an la ti ang.
5. Heng hi an haider lui si thin: Pathian thuin hman laiin vante a lo awm a, lei pawh tui ata leh tui zingah awm khawmin a lo awm a;
6. chungah chuan chu mi laia khawvel awm chu tuiin a chim a, a boral ta, tih hi;
7. nimahsela, tunah vante leh lei awmte hi chu mi thu avang vek chuan Pathian ngaihsak lo mite rorel leh boral ni atana dahin hal tura khek a ni.
8. Nimahsela, duh takte u, he thu kamkhat hi theihnghilh suh u, Lalpa ngaih chuan ni khat hi kum sangkhat ang a ni a, kum sangkhat pawh ni khat ang a ni, tih hi.
9. Mi thenkhatin muanga an ruat angin Lalpa chu a thutiam kawngah chuan a muang lo e; tuma boral duh lo a, mi zawng zawng lo sim duhin, nangmahni lamah a dawhthei a ni zawk e.
10. Nimahsela, Lalpa ni chu rukru angin a lo thleng ngei ang, chu mi niah chuan vante chu nasa taka riin a boral ang a, thil bulte chu meia emralin a awm tawh ang a, lei leh a chhunga hnathawh awmte hi a kangral ang.
11. Chung thilte chu chutianga boral vek tur a ni tih in hriat avangin Pathian ni lo thlen hun tur chu beisei leh thahnemngai taka duhin, awm dan thianghlim leh Pathian ngaihsak kawngah chuan mi eng ang nge in nih tur le?
12. Chu mi ni a thlen avangin vante lo kangin a boral ang a, thil bulte pawh chu meia em tuiin a awm tawh ang.
13. Nimahsela, a thuthiam ang chuan van tharte leh lei thar, a chhunga felna awmte chu kan beisei a ni.
14. Chuvangin, duh takte u, chutiang thilte chu in beisei avangin bawlhhlawh kai lo leh sawiselboin, thlamuang taka in awm a hmuh theihna'n che u, taihmak chhuah rawh u.
15. Tin, kan Lalpa dawhtheihzia chu chhandamnaah ruat rawh u; kan unaupa duh tak Paula pawhin a hnena finna pek ang zela in hnena a ziak ang khan;
16. a lehkha thawn zawng zawngah chuan chung thu chu sawiin, chutiang bawkin a ziak a ni; chungah chuan thu hriat har takte a awm a, chung chu mi fing lo leh mi nghet loten Pathian Lehkha Thu dang an ngai kawi angin an ngai kawi a, mahni leh mahni an intihboral phah thin.
17. Chutichuan, duh takte u, nangnin chung thu chu in lo hriat lawk avangin fimkhur rawh u, chutilochuan mi sualte dik lohnaa hruai bova awmin in nghet tha lai in tlu dah ang e.Amaherawhchu, kan Lalpa leh Chhandamtu Isua Krista khawngaihna leh hriatnaah chuan lo thang lian rawh u. Tunah leh chatuan thlengin ama hnenah chuan ropuina awm rawh se. Amen.
18. Amaherawhchu, kan Lalpa leh Chhandamtu Isua Krista khawngaihna leh hriatnaah chuan lo thang lian rawh u. Tunah leh chatuan thlengin ama hnenah chuan ropuina awm rawh se. Amen.
