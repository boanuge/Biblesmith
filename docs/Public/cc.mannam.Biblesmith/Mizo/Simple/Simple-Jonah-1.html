Jonah (1/4)  →
1. Amittaia fapa jona hnenah LALPA thu a lo thleng a,
2. Tho la, Ninevi khua, khawpui ropui takah chuan kalla, a chung thu tlangaupui rawh; an suahsualna chu ka hmaah a lo thleng chho ta si a, tiin.
3. Nimahsela Jona chu LALPA hma ata Tarsis-ah tlanbo turin a tho ta a; tin, Joppa khuaah a kal chhuk a, Tarsis lawng pan tur lawng a hmu a: tichuan a kal man chu a pe a, LALPA hma ata chu Tarsis pan turte nena kal turin lawngah chuan a lut thla ta a.
4. Nimahsela LALPA chuan thli na tak mai tuifinriatah chuan a tlehtir a, tuifinriatah chuan thlipui nasa tak a tleh a, tichuan, lawng chu a keh dawn ang maiin a awm ta a.
5. Tin, lawng mite chuan an hlau a, mahni pathian an au theuh a; lawng chu an tan a zanna'n lawng chhunga bungruate chu tuifinriatah chuan an paih chhuak ta a. Jona erawh chu lawng chhungril berah a lo lut tawh a ni a; a mu a, a lo muhil der a.
6. Chutichuan lawng hotu chu a hnenah a lo kal a, Aw nang muhil, eng tizia nge ni? Tho la, i Pathian ko rawh, Pathian chuan, kan boral loh nan, min ngaihtuah mahna, a ti a.
7. Tin, hawh u, he thil tha lo kan chunga lo awm hi tu vang nge ni tih kan hriat theih nan, thum i vawr ang u, tiin an in be hlawm a. Tichuan, thum an vawr a, thum vawr chu Jona chungah a tlu ta a.
8. Tichuan, a hnenah chuan, He thil tha lo kan chunga awm hi tu vang nge ni, min hrilh rawh, kan ngen a che; i hnathawh eng nge ni? Khawi atanga lo kal nge i nih? Eng hnam mi nge i nih? an ti a.
9. Ani chuan an hnenah, Hebrai mi ka ni a; tuifinriat leh khawmual siamtu LALPA, van Pathian chu ka hlau a ni, a ti a.
10. Tin, mite chuan an hlau nasa ta em em a, a hnenah chuan, Hei le, eng nge i lo tih ni? an ti a; a hrilh tawh avangin, mite chuan LALPA hma ata a tlanbo a ni tih an hre si a.
11. Tichuan, a hnenah chuan, kan tan tuifinriat hi a fawn loh nan, i chungah eng nge kan tih ang? an ti a, tuifinriat chu a fawn nasa telh telh si a.
12. Tin, a ni chuan an hnenah, Mi la kang u la, tuifinriatah hian mi theh chhuak rawh u; tichuan tuifinriat hi in tan a fawn tawh lo ang: keimah avangin in chungah he thlipui nasa tak hi a tleh a ni tih ka hre si a, a ti a.
13. Nimahsela mite chuan khawmualah kir turin an kar vak vak a; amaherawhchu an thei lo va: tuifinriat lah chu an chungah a fawn nasa telh telh si a.
14. Chuvang chuan LALPA chu an au a, Aw LALPA, he mi nunna avang hian min tiboral suh la, mithiam thisen mawh kan chunga nghat lul lo turin kan ngen a che, Aw LALPA, nangin i duh anga i tih a ni si a, an ti a.
15. Chutichuan Jona chu an la kang a, tuifinriatah chuan an theh chhuak ta a: tin, tuifinriat chu a fawn phul bulh bulh a reh ta a.
16. Tichuan, mite chuan LALPA chu nasa takin an hlau ta a; LALPA hnenah chuan inthawina an hlan a, thute an tiam ta a.Tin, LALPA chuan Jona lem turin sangha lian tak a lo ruat tawh a; chu sangha kawchhungah chuan Jona chu ni thum leh zan thum a awm a.
17. Tin, LALPA chuan Jona lem turin sangha lian tak a lo ruat tawh a; chu sangha kawchhungah chuan Jona chu ni thum leh zan thum a awm a.
