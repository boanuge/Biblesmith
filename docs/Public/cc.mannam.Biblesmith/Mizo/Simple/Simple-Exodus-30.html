1. Tin, rimtui halna tur maicham i siam tur a ni: thingri thingin i siam tur a ni.
2. A dunglam tawngkhat a vanglam tawng khat a ni tur a ni; kil li neia siam tur a ni: tin, a sanlam chu tawng hnih nisela: a kite chu maicham nan chuan in zawm vek tur a ni.
3. Tin, a chute , a pang velte leh a kite chu rangkachak hlir hlak tiin i luantur a ni; tin, a kotlang velah chuan rangkachak i dah tur a ni.
4. Tin, a kotlang hnuailamah, a sir tuakah chuan rangkachak khang pahnih i siam tur a ni; a sir pahnihah chuan i siam tur a ni, chung chu a zawn fung rawlhna tur a ni ang.
5. Tin, thingri thinga zawnfungte siamin rangkachak tiin i luantur a ni.
6. Tin, chu chu thuhriattirnachua ka rawn biak thinna tur che zahngaihna thutphah hmaah, thuhriattirna bawm kianga puanzar bulah i dah tur a ni.
7. Tin, Aronan chutah chuan englo rimtui tak hal tur rimtui a hal tur a ni: tuktin khawnvarte a chei hun apiangin a hal thin tur a ni.
8. Tin, Aronan tlai lamah khawnvarte a dah hun chuan rimtui chu a hal thin tur a ni: chu chu in thlahte thlenga LALPA hmaa rimtui a ni reng ang.
9. Chutah chuan rimtui dang emaw, halral thilhlan emaw, ei tur thilhlan emaw in hlan tur a ni loa; tin, chutah chuan in tur thilhlan pawh in leih tur a ni lo.
10. Tin, Aronan kumkhat ah vawikhat ki chauh te chuan inremna a siamtur a ni: inremna tura sual tawina thisen chuan kumtin vawikhat in thlahte thlengin inremna chu a siam thin tur a ni: chu chu LALPA tan thianghlim tak a ni,'' tiin.
11. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia'n hnenah, Israela thlahte awm zat i chhiar ang zel chuan mitinin an nunna tlanna chu LALPA an pe zel tur a ni, i chhiar hun chuan an mahni i chhiar huna an zinga hri a lo len loh nan.
12. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia'n hnenah, Israela thlahte awm zat i chhiar ang zel chuan mitinin an nunna tlanna chu LALPA an pe zel tur a ni, i chhiar hun chuan an mahni i chhiar huna an zinga hri a lo len loh nan.
13. Heihi an pek tur chu a ni: chhiar tawhte zinga tel tura kal apiangte chuan hmunhtianghlim sekel chanve an pe tur a ni (sekel chu gera sawmhnih a ni): LALPA puala atan sekel chanve.
14. Chhiar tawhte zinga tel tura kal apiang, kum sawmhnih a chin chulam zawng chuan LALPA puala thilhlan chu an pe tur a ni.
15. in nunna tlanna tura LALPA puala thil an hlan chuan sekel chanve aia tam chu mi hausa na pe tur a ni lo a, mi rethei pawhin chu mi aia tlem chu a pe tur a ni lo.
16. Tin, Israela thlahte tlanna man tangka chu lo lain, in biakna puan ina rawngbawl nan i lo ruat tur a ni; in nunna tlan nana LALPA hmaa Israela thlahte tana i hriat rengna a lo nih theina turin," tiin a sawi a.
17. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia hnenah, insilna tur dar maihun, a hun chhan dar nen i siam bawk tur a ni: chu chu inbiakna puanin leh maicham inkarah i hung tur a ni; tin, chutah chuan tui i dah tur a ni.
18. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia hnenah, insilna tur dar maihun, a hun chhan dar nen i siam bawk tur a ni: chu chu inbiakna puanin leh maicham inkarah i hung tur a ni; tin, chutah chuan tui i dah tur a ni.
19. Tin, Arona leh a fapaten chutah chuan an kete an sil thin tur a ni:
20. inbiakna puanin chhunga an luh hun in emaw, LALPA tana meia thilhlan hal tura maicham bula rawngbawl tura an lo kal hun in emaw tui chuan an in sil tur a ni; chutilochuan an thi mai ang:
21. Chutichuan an thihlonna'n an kut leh an kete chu an sil thin tur a ni: chu chu an tan kumkhua a tana thuruat ani ang, a mah leh a thlahte leh an thlahte thleng atan pawh,'' tiin a sawi a.
22. LALPA vekin Mosia hnenah, Rimtui tuiber zinga mi-murra tui sekel zanga leh, thakthing rimtui, chumi zatve sekel zahnih leh sawmnga zet- leh fu rimtui sekel zahnih leh sawmnga leh,
23. LALPA vekin Mosia hnenah, Rimtui tuiber zinga mi-murra tui sekel zanga leh, thakthing rimtui, chumi zatve sekel zahnih leh sawmnga zet- leh fu rimtui sekel zahnih leh sawmnga leh,
24. thakthing chi dang sekel zanga, hmunthianghlima sekel an pek thin ang, olive hriak tin khat nen rawn lain,
25. rimtui siamtu angin hriakthih chu hriak thianghlim a ni ang.
26. Tin, inbiakna puan in te, thuhriattirna bawm te,
27. dawhkan lehng a bungbel zawng zawng te, khawnvar dahna leh a hmanrua te, rimtui halna maicham te,
28. halral thilhlan maicham leh a hmanrua zawng zawng te, maihun leh a hun chhan te nen, chu chuan i hnawih vek tur a ni.
29. Tin, thianghlim tak an lo nih theihna'n i tithianghlim tur a ni: chung dek a piangte chu an lo thianghlim zel ang.
30. Tin, ka tan puithiam hnathawh thawka ka rawng an bawl theihna'n Arona leh a fapate chu hriak thihin i tithianghlim tur a ni.
31. Tin, Israela thlahte hnenah, 'He hriak hi i thlahte thlenga ka hriak thianghlim thih mi chu a ni ang.
32. Mihring taksa ah chuan leih tur a ni lo a, he mi siam dan anga siam, a ang tak hian in siam tur a ni hek lo: hei hi zawng a thianghlima, tichuan in tan thil thianghlim a ni tur a ni.
33. Tupawhin a anpui siam se la emaw, chu mi chu a chipuite zinga ata tihboral a ni ang,"tiin sawi ang che,'' tiin a sawi leh a.
34. Tin, LALPA'n Mosia hnenah, Rimtui tui tak takte te, onuka te, ngalbanum te - la ang che; rimtui ti tak beraw fim tak nen buk in zat vekin;
35. chu chu rimtui siamtu siam angin chi-a al, fim leh thianghlim takin rimtui i siam tur a ni:
36. a thente chu rawt dip dep tur a ni a, ka rawn biak thinna tur che inbiakna puan in chua thuhriattirna bulah chuan i dah tur a ni: chu chu in tan thianghlim tak a ni ang.
37. Tin, chu mi rimtui i siamtur, a siam dan tur anga siam chu zawng nangmahni tan in siamtur a ni lo e: LALPA tana thil thianghlimah i ngai tur a ni.Tupawhin hnim atan ngawta chutiang a siam chuan chu mi chu a chipuite zing ata tihboral a ni ang.'' a ti a.
38. Tupawhin hnim atan ngawta chutiang a siam chuan chu mi chu a chipuite zing ata tihboral a ni ang.'' a ti a.
