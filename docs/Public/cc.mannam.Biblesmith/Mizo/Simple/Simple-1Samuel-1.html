1. Ephraim tlang ram Ramathaim-zophim kuaah chuan mi pakhat a awm a, a hming chu Elkana ani a; chu mi chu Jehorama fapa ani a, chu mi Elihua fapa a ni a, chu mi chu Tohua fapa ani a, chu mi chu Ephraim m Zupha fapa a ni.
2. Tin, nupui pahnih a nei a; a pakhata hming chu Hani a ni a, a pakhata hming chu chu Penini a ni a; tin, Penini chuan fate a nei a, Hani erawh chuan fa a nei ve lo a.
3. Tin, chu mi chu an khua atangin Silo khuaah Sipai hote LALPA be tur leh inthawi turin kum tin, a han kal thin a. Chutah chuan Elia fapate pahnih, Hophnia leh Phinehasa, LALPA puithiamte an awm a.
4. Tin, Elkana inthawi ni a lo thlen chuan, a nupui Penini leh a fapate leh a fanute zawng zawng chu chanvo a siam a;
5. Hani chu hmangaih em em mah se, LALPA'n fa nei thei loa a siam avangin chan khat chauh a siamsak a.
6. Fa a neih theih loh avanga a lungngaihna tizual turin amah ertu Penini chuan a lo deuhsawh thin a.
7. Tin, chutianga kum tin, a tih avangin LALPA in a han kal apiang chuan a lungngaihna chu a lo chawhthawhsak thin a; chutichuan a tap a tap a, chaw pawh a ei lo a.
8. Tin, a pasal Elkana chuan a hnenah, "Hani, engati nge i tah? Engati nge chaw i ei loh? Enga ti nge i thin a nat? Fapa sawm ai pawin i tan ka tha zawk lo'm ni?" a ti a.
9. Chutichuan Silo khuaa chaw an eia an in zaw chuan Hani chu a tho a. Tin, Puithiam Elia chu LALPA biak in kawngka ban u la a thutnaah chuan a thu a.
10. Tin, Hani chu a rilru a mangang em m mai a, LALPA hnenah chuan a tawngtai a, lungchhe takin a tap a.
11. Tin, thu a tiam a, "Aw Sipaihote LALPA, i bawihnu tlawmzia hi min hriatsak tak meuh a, min hriat reng a, i bawihnu hi min theihnghilh loh a, i bawihnu hi fapa i pek chuan, a dam chhung zawng LALPA ka pe ve thung ang a, a lu pawh meh reng reng a ni lo ang," a ti a.
12. Tin, heti hi a ni a, LALPA hmaa a tawngtai reng lai chuan Elia chuan a hmui phun chu a lo chhinchiah a.
13. Hani chuan a rilruina sawi a, a hmui chau a phu a, a awm chu hriat a ni lo va: chuvangin Elia chuan zua rui niin a ring a.
14. Chutichuan Elia chuan a hnenah, "Eng chen nge rui renga i awm dawn le? I uain chu dah bo rawh khai," a ti a.
15. Tin, Hani chuan,"Ka rui lo a sin ka pu, hmeichhe rilru lungngai tawpkhawk ka ni a; uain pawh, zupui pawh ka in hau lo a, LALPA hmaa ka rilru ka phawrh zawk a ni.
16. I bawihnu hi Beliala fanuah puh lul suh ang che: ka rilrua khat liam ka lungnih lohna leh ka lungngaihna ka sawi a nih hi," tiin a chhang a.
17. Tin, Elia chuan, "Thlamuang takin kal rawh khai, Israelte Pathian chuan i thil dil chu pe che rawh se," tiin a chhang a.
18. Tin, ani chuan, "I chhiahhlawhnu hian i mit lo tlung rawh se," a ti a. Chutichuan hmeichhia chu a kal ta a, chaw te a ei a, lungngai hmel a pu ta lo
19. Tin, zing takah a tho a, LALPA chu an bia a, an haw a, Rama kua an in chu an thleng leh ta a: tin, Elkana chuan a nupui Hani chu a pawl a: tin, LALPA chuan ani chu a hre renga.
20. Chutichuan heti hi a ni a. Hani chuan nau a pai a, tina hun a lo thlen chuan fapa a hring a; "LALPA ka dil a ni," tiin ahmingah Samuela a sa a.
21. Tichuan Elkana le a chhungte zawng zawng chu LALPA hnena an kum tin, inthawina hlan tur leh a thutiam hlen turin an kal cho a.
22. Hani erawh chu a kal chho ve lo va: a pasal hnenah chuan, "Kei zawng naupangin hnute hnek a ban hma loh chuan ka kal chho ve lo ang e; chu mi hnuah chuan LALPA hmaa a inlan theihna'n ka hruai ang a, chutah chuan an awm reng tawh dawn nia," a ti a
23. Tin, a pasal Elkana chuan a hnenah, "Tha awm i ti apiang chuan ti rawh; a hnute hnek i bantir thlengin awm rawh; amaherawhchu LALPA chuan a thu chu tinghet ngei rawh se," a ti a. Chutichuan a hnute hnek ban hma loh chuan hmeichhia chu a awm ta, a fapa chu hnute a hnektir ta a.
24. Tin, hnute hnek a bantir chuan, chhangphut epha khat leh uain saidawium khat kengin sebawng pathum nena han hruai a, Silo khuaa LALPA inah chuan a kalpui a: naupang chu ala naupang hle a.
25. Tin, bawngpa chu an talh a, naupang chu Elia hnenah an hruai a.
26. Tin, ani chuan, "Aw ka pu, i nunna chalin ka sawi e, kei chu e ka pu, he mi hmuna i kianga dinga LALPA hnena tawngtaitu hmeichhia kha ka ni ngei a.
27. He naupang dil hian ka tawngtai a ni a; tin, LALPA chuan ka dil chu min pe ta a:chuvangin kei pawhin LALPA hnenah ka hlan a ni: a dam chhung zawng LALPA tana hlan a ni," a ti a. Tin, chutah chuan LALPA chu a bia a.
28. chuvangin kei pawhin LALPA hnenah ka hlan a ni: a dam chhung zawng LALPA tana hlan a ni," a ti a. Tin, chutah chuan LALPA chu a bia a.
