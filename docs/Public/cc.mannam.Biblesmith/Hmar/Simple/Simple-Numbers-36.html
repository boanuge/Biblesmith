1. Josefa thlaa mi, Manase naupa Makir, Makir naupa Gilead thlaa mi sungkuo luhai chu an hung a, Mosie le Israel pahnam luhai hmaa chun thu an hril a:
2. "LALPA chun Israelhai ram hluo ding chu thum vawra sem dingin kan pu thu a pek a; chun, LALPA chun kan pu chu kan unaupa Zelofehad hluo ding ram chu a naunuhai pe dingin thu a pek bok a.
3. Tuhin, Israel sungkuoa mi pahnam dang pasalah lo nei ta hai sien, an pi le pu rama an rochan chu an pasal va neihai kutah a lut ding a na. Chuong a ni chun, kan pahnam ta dinga pek ram chu a kiem ding tina a nih.
4. Israelhai ta dinga Jubili Kum a hung tlung pha leh, pasal an va neina hnamhai ramah kan ram chu kop tang a ta, kan thlatuhai ram chu lak hmang a hung ni ding a nih' tiin.
5. Chuongchun, LALPA thu angin Mosie chun Israelhai chu thu a pek a: "Josefa thlahai thu hung hril hi a'n dik a nih.
6. Hi hi Zelofehad naunuhai chungthua LALPA thupek chu a nih: an pahnam sunga mi an ni phot chun tu khom pasala an nei a thieng.
7. Israel thlahai ram chu pahnam pakhata inthokin pahnam dang kutah son ding a ni naw a, Israel pahnam tinin an pi le pua inthoka an rochung ram chu an kol seng ding a nih.
8. Israel thlahai laia rohluotu ding nuhmei taphot chun, Israel thlahaiin an pi le pu ram an hluo seng theina dingin, a pa hnampui laia mi ngei pasalah a nei ding a nih.
9. Ram hrim hrim pahnam pakhata inthokin pahnam dangah son ding a ni naw a, Israel pahnam tinin an rochung ram chu an kol ding a nih" tiin.
10. LALPAN thu a pek angin Zelofehad naunuhai chun an thaw a.
11. Zelofehad naunu Mahla hai, Tirza hai, Hogla hai, Milka hai le Noa hai chun an pa unauhai naupahai chu pasalah an nei seng a.
12. An pasalhai chu Josefa naupa Manase naupahai an na, an ram chu an pa hnama chun a um zing tah a nih.
13. Hienghai hi LALPAN Mosie hmanga Jeriko zawn, Jordan ral, Moab phaia Israelhai kuoma thupek le dan a pekhai chu an nih.
