1. Hienghai hi Israel naupahai chu an nih: Reuben, Simeon, Levi, Juda, Isakar, Zebulun,
2. Dan, Josefa, Benjamin, Naftalia, Gad le Aser.
3. Juda chun naupa panga a nei a. A nuhmei Batsua, Kanan mia inthokin pathum Er, Onan le Sela an nih. A upa tak Er chu LALPA mithmuah a suol em leiin a sukhlum a.
4. A monu Tamar-in Juda lakah nau inphir, Perez le le Zera a neipek a.
5. Perez naupahai chu Hezron le Hamul an nih.
6. Zera naupahai chu Zimri, Etan, Heman, Kalkol le Darda (Dara), an rengin panga an nih.
7. Karmi naupa chu Akar (Akan), lak thieng loa khap thil hluihlawn a lak leia Israelhai sukbuoitu kha a nih.
8. Ethan naupa chu Azaria a nih.
9. Hezron naupahai chu Jerameel, Rama le Kaleb (Kelubai) an nih.
10. Rama inthoka Jesai chena an inthla sawng dan chu: Rama'n Aminadab a hring a, Aminadab-in Juda thlahai laia thuoitu langsar Nason a hring a,
11. Nason-in Salmon (Salma), Salmon-in Boaza a hring a,
12. Boaza-in Obed, Obed-in Jesai a hring.
13. Jesai naupahai laia a nau lutir chu Eliab a na, a pahnina Abinadab, a pathumna Simea,
14. a palina Nethanel, a pangana Radai,
15. a parukna Ozem le a pasarina Davida a nih.
16. A naunuhai chu Zeruia le Abigail an nih. Zeruia chun naupa pathum Abisai, Joab le Asahel a hring a.
17. Abigail chun Amasa a hring a, Amasa pa chu Ismael mi Jether a nih.
18. Hezron naupa Kaleb chun nuhmei pahnih, Azuba le Jeriot a nei a, Azuba naupahai chu Jeser, Sobab le Ardon an nih.
19. Azuba a thi chun Kaleb-in nuhmeiin Efratha a nei a, ama chun Hura a hringpek a.
20. Hura chun Uri a hring a, Uri chu Bezalel pa a nih.
21. Chu hnunga chun Gilead pa Makir naunu chu Hezron-in kum sawmruk a ni hnungin a pawl a, nuhmeiah a nei a, Segub a hringpek a.
22. Segub chu Gilead rama khuo sawmhni le khaw thum neitu Jair pa a nih.
23. (Nisienlakhom, Gesur le Aram haiin Jair-a khuo Havot Jair le Kenat le a se vel khuo sawmruk chu an lakpek a.) Chuonghai po po chu Gilead pa Makir thlahai chu an nih.
24. Kaleb-Efratha khuoa Hezron a thi hnungin a nuhmei Abija chun Asur a hringpek a, ama chu Tekoa pa a nih.
25. Hezron nau lutir Jerameel naupahai chu: a nau lutir Rama bakah Buna, Oren, Ozem le Ahija an nih.
26. Jerameel chun nuhmei dang a hming Atara a nei a, ama chu Onam nu a nih.
27. Jerameel nau lutir Rama naupahai chu Maaz, Jamin le Eker an nih.
28. Onam naupahai chu Samai le Jada an nih. Samai naupahai chu Nadab le Abisur an nih.
29. Abisur nuhmei hming chu Abihail a na, ama chun Aban le Molid a hringpek a.
30. Nadab naupahai chu Seled le Apaim an na, Seled chu suonmongin a thi a.
31. Apaim naupa Isi chu Sesan pa a na, Sesan chu Ahlai pa a nih.
32. Samai unaupa Jada naupahai chu Jether le Jonathan an na, Jether chu suonmongin a thi a.
33. Jonathan naupahai chu Pelet le Zaza an nih. Chuonghai chu Jerameel thlahai chu an nih.
34. Sesan chun naunu chauh a nei a. Aigupta mi siehlaw, a hming Jarha a nei a.
35. Sesan chun a naunu chu Jarha a'n neitir a, naupa Atai a hringpek a.
36. Atai chu Nathan pa a na, Nathan chu Zabad pa a na,
37. Zabad chu Eflal pa a na, Eflal chu Obed pa a nih.
38. Obed chu Jehu pa a na, Jehu chu Azaria pa a nih.
39. Azaria chu Helez pa a na, Helez chu Eleasa pa a nih.
40. Eleasa chu Sismai pa a na, Sismai chu Salum pa a nih.
41. Salum chu Jekamia pa a na, Jekamia chu Elisama pa a nih.
42. Jerameel unaupa Kaleb naupahai chu: A nau lutir Mesa chun Zip a hring a, Zip chun Maresa a hring a, Maresa chun Hebron a hring a.
43. Hebron naupahai chu Kora, Tapua, Rekem le Sema an nih.
44. Sema chu Raham pa a na, Raham chu Jorkeam pa a nih. Rekem chu Samai pa a nih.
45. Samai naupa chu Moan a na, ama chu Beth Zur pa a nih.
46. Kaleb hmei Efa chun Haran, Moza le Gazez a hringpek a. Haran naupa hming khom Gazez bok a nih.
47. Jadai naupahai chu Regem, Jotham, Gesan, Pelet, Efa leh Saaf an nih.
48. Kaleb hmei dang Maaka chun naupa Seber le Tirhana a hringpek a.
49. Ama chun Saaf khom a hring bok a. A hnungah nau pahnih, Madmana khuo indintu Saaf le Makbena le Gilbea khuo indintu Seva hai chu a hringpek bok a. Kaleb naunu chu Aksa a nih.
50. Chuonghai chu Kaleb thlahai chu an nih.Efratha nau lutir Hura thlahai chu: Kiriat Jearim pa Sobal,
51. Bethlehem pa Salma le Beth Gader pa Haref an nih.
52. Kiriat Jearim-hai thlatu Sobal chun Haroe-hai, Manahat hnam zatve a thla a.
53. Kirait Jearim hnamhai chu Ithri hai, Put hai, Sumat hai, Misra hai an nih. Hienghaia inthok hin Zorat-hai le Estaol-hai an hung suok a nih.
54. Salma thlahai chu Bethlehem hai, Netofat hai, Atrot Beth Joab hai, Manahat hai zatve le Zor mi hai,
55. lekha ziektu Sofer hnam, Jabez-a um Tirat hai, Simeat hai, Sukat hai an nih. Chuonghai chu Rekab thlahai laia mi Hamat thla Ken mihai chu an nih.
