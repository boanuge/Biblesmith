1. Samson chu Timna khuoah a zu fe a, Filistin nunghak pakhat a zu hmu a.
2. In tieng a hung inlawi chun a nu le pa kuomah, "Timna khuoah Filistin nunghak pakhat ka hmu a, ka nuhmei dingin zu thuoi ro" a ta.
3. A nu le pa chun, "Ei sungkuo le ei chipuihai lai hin i lung kuoi zawng nunghak i hmu der naw am a ni, chuonga ser tan lo Filistinhai lai nuhmei ding i zu zong el ta chu?" an ta. Samson chun, "Mi thuoipek el ro, ka'n koppui thei chi ding tak a nih" a ta.
4. (Chuong laia Israelhai chunga roreltu chu Filistinhai an na, anni le an intauh thei dan remchang chu zonga chu thil chu LALPAN a thaw a nih ti chu a nu le pain an hriet si nawh.)
5. Chuongchun, Samson chu a nu le pa leh Timna khuoah an zu fe a. Timnaa grep huon an tlung tawmin sakeibaknei vanglaiin a lo inrum khum thut a.
6. Samson chunga chun LALPA Thlarau thilthawtheina a hung tlung a, hriemhrei ieng khom chawi naw sienkhom, kut lawng ringotin sakeibaknei chu kel te ang elin a pot nawi a, sienkhom a thilthaw chu a nu le pa a hril nawh.
7. Chun, an zu tlung a, nunghak chu Samsonin a'n biekpui a, a dit hle a.
8. Iemani chen hnungah nunghak chu nei dingin an zu fe nawk a, sakeibaknei ruong chu en dingin Samson chu a pet vak a, a ruonga chun khuoiin bu an lo sep a, khuoizu khom a lo um a.
9. Khuoizu chu a lak a, a fe nawk a, lawn malamin a fak pei a, a nu le pa a hnot phak chun anni khom a then a pek a, an fak a, sienkhom sakeibaknei ruonga inthoka khuoizu chu a lak a nih ti a hril tawl nawh.
10. Chun, a pa chun nunghak chu a zu hmu a, chu taka chun Samsonin ruoi a the a, chu chu moneitu tlangvalhai thaw dan hlak hrim a nih.
11. Samson an hmu charin a tawiawmtu ding mi sawmthum an hung thuoipek nghal a.
12. Chun, Samson chun an kuomah, "Tu hin thu khirh ka'n don ding cheu a nih. Ruoi the hun ni sari sunga a donna in mi hril thei chun puon zai inhmi thle sawmthum le silfen sawmthum peng ka ti cheu.
13. A donna in mi hril thei naw chun puon thle sawmthum le silfen sawmthum in mi pek ding a nih" a ta. Anni chun, "Thu khirh chu mi hang hril ta la, lo ngaithlang kan tih" an ta.
14. Ama chun," A fatua inthoka fak ding suok,Hrat taka inthoka thil thlum suok"a ta. Ni thum sungin an don thei nawh.
15. A ni linaa chun Samson nuhmei kuomah, "Hi thu khirh hi mi hrilfie dingin i pasal hi thlut rawh, chuong naw chun nang le i pahai sungkuo hi kan raw hlum el ding cheu a nih. Mi rok dingin a ni maw hi taka hin in mi fiel?" an ta.
16. Samson nuhmei chun tap pumin a zuot a, "I mi lo theida hle a ni hi tie! I mi lo hmangai tak tak naw top hih. Ka chipuihai hlak thu khirh i'n don a, kei hlak a hrilfiena khom i mi hril si nawh" a ta. Ama chun, "La to ta, ka nu le pa khom la hril loin nang ka hril ding che maw?" a ta.
17. Ruoi the hun ni sari sung chun a hmaah tapin a thlut ngat ngat leiin a ni sarina chun a hril ta a, ama chun thu khirh umzie chu a chipuihai kuomah a hril sawng a.
18. A ni sarina nisa tlak hma chun a khaw mihai chun a kuomah, "Khuoizu neka thlum lem iem a na? Sakeibaknei neka hrat lem iem a na?" an ta. Samson chun an kuomah,"Ka bawng la hi hnuoi inletah sor naw inla chu,Ka thu khirh hre suok teu naw ti niu"a tipek tawl a.
19. Chun, LALPA Thlarau chu Samson chungah thilthawtheinain a hung inlang a. Askelon-ah a fe thlak a, mi sawmthum a that a, an thuom a hlippek a, thu khirh hrilfietuhai kuoma chun silfenhai chu a pek a, lungsen erin a pa in tieng chun a kir nawk ta a.
20. Samson nuhmei chu inneinaa Samsonin thiena a hmang kuomah an lo pek lem el ta a.
