Hosea (1/14)  →
1. Juda lal Uzia hai, Jotham hai, Ahaz hai le Hezekia hai inlal lai le Israel lal Jeroboam, Jehoasha naupa inlal laia Beri naupa Hosea kuoma LALPA thu hung tlung chu:
2. LALPAN Hosea hmanga Israelhai kuoma thu a hril tan lai khan, LALPA chun Hosea kuomah, "A ramin LALPA nghatsan a, suol taka a uiretsan angin, nang khom fe la, nuhmeiah nawchizor va nei la, nawchizor le chun nau nei ro" a ta.
3. Chuongchun a fe a, Diblaim naunu Gomer chu nuhmeiah a nei a, nau a pai a, naupa a hringpek a.
4. LALPA chun Hosea kuomah, "A hmingah Jezreel sak rawh; sawt riel loin Jezreel phuba chu Jehu sungkuo chungah ka lak ding a ni si a; Israel lalram khom suktawp bok ka tih.
5. Chu nia chun Israel thalngul chu Jezreel Ruomah ul tliek ka tih" a ta.
6. Chun, Gomer chun nau a pai nawk a, naunu a hring a. LALPA chun Hosea kuomah, " Israel sungkuo chu laina zing ta loa ka ngaidam el ta naw ding a ni leiin, a hmingah 'Lo-Ruhama'b sak rawh.
7. Juda sungkuo ruok chu laina zing ka ta, thalngul amanih, kawlhnam amanih, sakor le sakor chungchuong mihai hratna hmang loin, LALPA an Pathien chun san suok ka tih" a ta.
8. Lo-Ruhama-in nene a nghei hnung chun Gomer-in nau a pai nawk a, naupa a hring a.
9. LALPA chun, "Ka mihai in ni ta naw a, in Pathien ka ni bok naw leiin a hmingah Lo-Ammic sak rawh" a ta.
10. Amiruokchu, Israel nauhai chu tiem seng lo le inkhi ruol lo, tuifinriet kama phaiphin anga tam la ning an ta, "Ka mihai in ni nawh" a tina hmuna ngei chun "Pathien hring nauhai in nih" tiin la ko an tih.
11. Juda mihai le Israel mihai chu insuikhawm nawk an ta, thuoitu pakhat nei an ta, an ram chu hluong an ta, Jezreel nid chu la ropui a tih.
