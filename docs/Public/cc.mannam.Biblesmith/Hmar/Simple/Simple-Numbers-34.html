1. LALPA chun Mosie kuomah,
2. "Israelhai chu thu pe la, hieng hin hril rawh: 'LALPAN rochana a pek cheu Kanan ram in hluo pha chu hieng ang hi a ramri ding chu ning a tih:
3. Sim tieng ramri chu Zin thlalera inthokin Edom ramri zulah feng a tih. Sak tienga a'n tanna chu Tuipui Thi sim tawpa inthokin ning a tih.
4. Chu taka inthok chun sim tieng pan a ta, Ainuvet Kawn tanin, Zin thlalerah, Kadesh Barnea chen feng a tih. Chu taka inthok chun hmar thlang tieng inherin, Hazar Addar hrawin, Azmon chen feng a ta,
5. chu taka inthok chun inher a ta, Aigupta Ruom zui a ta, Mediterranean Tuipui-ah tawp a tih
6. " 'Thlang tieng ramri chu Mediterranean Tuipui kam ning a tih. Chu chu tlak tienga in ramri ding chu a nih.
7. " 'Hmar tieng ramri chu Mediterranean Tuipuia inthokin Hor tlang chen feng a ta
8. chu taka inthok chun Hamat Kawna hrawng a ta, Zedad tieng fe pei a ta,
9. Zifron tieng pan a ta, Hazar Enan-ah tawp a tih. Chu chu hmar tienga in ramri ding chu a nih.
10. " 'Sak tieng ramri chu Hazar Enan-a inthokin Stefam chen ning a tih.
11. Chu taka inthok chun Ain sak tienga um Ribla-ah tum thlang a ta, Galili Dil suo tieng kam pang perah feng a ta,
12. Jordan vadung zui pei a ta, Tuipui Thi-ah tawp a tih. Chuonghai chu sak le thlang, sim le hmara in ramri ding chu a nih' ti rawh" a ta.
13. Mosie chun Israelhai chu thu a pek a, "Ram in rochung ding chu thum vawrin insem ro. Hnam kuo le a chenve kuoma sem dingin LALPAN thu a pek a nih,
14. Reuben pahnam, Gad pahnam le Manase pahnam chenve chun an chanvo ding chu an hluo ta si a.
15. Hieng hnam hni le a chenve hai hin ni suokna tieng Jeriko zawn Jordan ral sak tienga an ram hluo ding chu an chang der tah a nih" a ta.
16. LALPA chun Mosie kuomah,
17. "Ram hluo ding thepektu ding cheu chu thiempu Eleazar le Nun-a naupa Josua ning an tih.
18. Pahnam tin luhai chu a sempuitu dingin ruot rawh.
19. Chuonghai hming chu: Juda hnama inthokin Jefune naupa Kaleb;
20. Simeon hnama inthokin Amihud naupa Semuel;
21. Benjamin hnama inthokin Kislon naupa Elidad;
22. Dan hnama inthokin Jogli naupa Bukki;
23. Josefa naupa Manase hnama inthokin Efod naupa Haniel;
24. Josefa naupa Efraim hnama inthokin Shiftan naupa Kemuel;
25. Zebulun hnama inthokin Parnak naupa Elizafan;
26. Isakar hnama inthokin Azan naupa Paltiel;
27. Aser hnama inthokin Selomi naupa Ahihud;
28. Naftali hnama inthokin Amihud naupa Pedahel" tiin.
29. Chuonghai chu Kanan rama Israelhai ram hluo ding sem dinga LALPAN thu a pekhai chu an nih.
