←  Isaiah (4/66)  →
1. Chu nia chun nuhmei pasariin,
2. Chu ni chun LALPA-a inthoka inder suok Kau chu mawi tak le ropui takin la hung um a ta, a ram tharsuok chu Israel rama dam khawsuoka la umhai uongpui tak le an ropuina chu la ning a tih.
3. Zion-a la um zing, Jerusalema la um zinga an hming inchikhai chu mi thienghlim tia ko ning an tih.
4. LALPA thlarau chun Zion minuhai chungthu chu rel felin, an porchena chu sawp fai a ta, Jerusalema thisen kai po po chu LALPA thlarau mei chun kang fai a tih.
5. LALPA chun Zion tlang le chu taka mi cheng khawmhai chunga chun sunah sum anga inzam meikhu sieng a ta, zanah meichok var sie bok a ta, a ram pum chu a ropuinain zar khum a tih.
6. Chu chun sun lum vanglaia zar hlimtu bawkte le thlipui le ruopui laka an inhumhimna ning a tih.
