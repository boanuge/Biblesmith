1. Zingkar inhma takin Josua le Israel thlahai po po chu Sitim-a inthokin an inthok suok a, Jordan vadung an tlung a, an kan hmain chu taka chun an riek a.
2. Ni thum hnunga chun hotuhaiin riebuk chu an fang vel a,
3. "Thiempu Levi thlahaiin LALPA in Pathien Thuthlung Bawm zawna an suok in hmu pha leh in umnaa inthoka insonin anni chu in zui ding a nih.
4. Hi lampui hi a hmain in la hraw ngai naw leiin anni chun lampui kawkhmung an ti cheu. Amiruokchu, nangni le Thuthlung Bawm inkarah kilometer khat vel a um ding a nih; in va hnai taluo ding a ni nawh" tiin an hril a.
5. Josua chun mipui kuomah, "Insukthienghlim ro, zinga hin eini lai thil mak tak el LALPAN a thaw ding a nih" a ta.
6. Chun, thiempuhai kuoma chun, "Thuthlung Bawm chu zawn unla, mipui hmaah fe ro" a ti bok a. Chuongchun, bawm chu an zawn a, mipui hma an thuoi a.
7. LALPA chun Josua kuomah, "Mosie kuoma ka um hlak ang khan i kuoma khom ka um ding a nih ti an hriet theina dingin voisun hin Israel mi po po mithmua ngei ka chawimawi tan ding che a nih.
8. Thuthlung Bawm zawntu thiempuhai chu Jordan vadung kamah tui chu pala ngir dingin hril rawh" a ta.
9. Josua chun Israelhai kuomah, "Hi taka hin hung unla, LALPA in Pathien thuhai chu hung ngaithla ro.
10. Nangni lai Pathien hring chu a um zing a, in hmaah Kanan mihai, Hit mihai, Hiv mihai, Periz mihai, Girgas hai, Amor hai le Jebu hai chu matheiloin a hnot suok ding a nih ti hi thila inthok hin hre thei in tih.
11. Ngai ta u, khawvel po po LALPA Thuthlung Bawm chun in hma thuoiin hi Jordan vadung hi a kan hmasa ding a nih.
12. Tuhin, Israel hnam tina mi sawm le pahni thlang suok ro.
13. LALPA, khawvel po po LALPA thuthlung bawm zawntu thiempuhaiin Jordan vadung tui an pal tan charin Jordan vadung luong chu inchat a ta, a hnar tienga inthoka tui hung luong po po chu vum thura intiengin ngir a tih" a ta.
14. Chun, Jordan vadung kan dinga puon ina inthoka mipui an suok chun Thuthlung Bawm zawntu thiempuhai chu mipui hmaa chun an fe a.
15. Hi hun hi busik hun, Jordan vadung kuong a liem zur zur hun lai a nih. Bawm zawntuhai chun Jordan vadung chu an tlung a, bawm zawntu thiempuhai kein vakama tui a tawk charin,
16. a hnar tienga inthoka tui hung luong chu a tawp a, hla fea Zarethan sevela um khuo pakhat Adam an ti hmuna chun tumbu ang elin tui chu an tieng thur a, Tuipui Thi tienga luong thla ding chu a kangchat a, Jeriko khaw zawn taka chun mipui chun vadung chu an kai a.
17. Hnuoi chara Israel mipuiin vadung an kan vong chenin, LALPA Thuthlung Bawm zawntu thiempuhai chu Jordan vadung lailunga chun an ngir a.
