←  Isaiah (12/66)  →
1. Chu ni chun hieng hin hla sak in tih:"Inpak ka ti che aw LALPA,Ka chunga i lungsenna chu,I mi lak kiengpek ta a,Tuhin i mi thlamuong tah.
2. LALPA chu mi sandamtu a na,Ring ka ta, ti ta naw ning;Lalpa, JEHOVA ngei chu,Ka hratna le ka hla a na,A mi sandamtu a nih" tiin,
3. Sandamna tuikhura tui chu,Hlim takin chawi tang in tih.
4. Chu ni chun lawm hla remin,LALPA kuomah lawmthu hril ro,A hming chu lam rop hlak ro!A thilthaw le ropuiziehai,Hnam tin kuomah puong darin,A hming chawimawi tlak a nizie,Hnam tin kuomah hril vel ro.
5. LALPA inpakna hla remin,Thil ropui tak a thawhai chu,Khawvel pumpui inhriettir ro.
6. Zion mihai, hlima khekin,Inring takin zai hla rem ro,Nangni laia um mi ropui chu,Israelhai Mi Thienghlim a ni si.
