1. Israel thlahai po po chu an rengin Silo khuoah an intuokkhawm a, chu taka chun Intuokna Puon in an zar a.
2. A ram chu an thu hnuoiah um ta sienkhom Israel thlahai laia hnam sarihai chun an chanpuol an la chang hri nawh.
3. Josua chun Israelhai kuomah, "LALPA in pi le puhai Pathienin a pek ta cheu ram hi ieng chen am hluo loin in la nghak ding a na?
4. Hnam tina mi pathum seng hung thlang suok unla, a ram va fang vel dingin tir suok ka ta, an ram hluo ding seng chanchin hung ziek an ta, chu zoah ka kuomah hung kir nawk an tih.
5. A ram chu hmun sariah the phot in ta, Juda thlahai chu sim tienga an chanpuolah um an ta, Josefa sungkuohai chu hmar tienga an chanpuolah um an tih.
6. Ram chu hmun sariah the unla, chu chu ziekin mi hung pe unla, hi taka hin LALPA ei Pathien hmaah thum lo vawrpek ka ti cheu.
7. Levihai ruok chun nangni lai chan an nei ve naw a, LALPA thiempu nina sin hi an chanpuol a nih. Chun, Gad thlahai, Reuben thlahai le Manase hnam zatve hai ruok chu Jordan ral sak tieng LALPA siehlaw Mosien an chanpuol seng a lo pek ta kha" a ta.
8. Ram entu le a um dan ziek thlatu dinghai an inthok suok lai chun Josuan, "Fe unla, ram chu va en chieng unla, a um dan ziek thlain ka kuomah hung kir nawk unla, Silo hmuna hin LALPA hmaah thum lo vawrpek ka ti cheu" tiin thu a'n cha a.
9. Mihai chu an fe a, ram chu an va fang a, hmun sariah an the a, lekhabuah a khuo po po chu an ziek thlak a, an riebuk Silo-ah chun an hung kir nawk a.
10. Silo-a chun Josuan LALPA hmaah thum a lo vawrpek a, chu taka chun an hnam inthe dan ang peiin ram chu a sem a.
11. An sungkuo zir ang peia ram sem dinga thum an vawr chu Benjamin thlahai chungah a tla hmasa tak a, an ramri chin chu Juda thlahai le Josefa thlahai inkar a nih.
12. Hmar tieng panga an ramri chu Jordan vadungah a'n tan a, ramri chu Jeriko sir hmar tieng a fe tung a, chu taka inthok chun thlang tieng panin tlang ramah a fe tung pei a, ramri tawpna chu Bet-aven thlaler a nih.
13. Chu taka inthok chun Luz (Bethel) sim tieng pangperah a fe a, chu taka inthok chun Bet Horon tlang chuonhnuoia um Atarot Adar tlangah a fe thlak a.
14. Bet Horon sim tieng tlang bula inthokin thlang tieng pangah sim tieng panin a fe kuol a, Juda thlahai khawpui Kiriat-Baal (Kiriat-jearim) ah a tawp a, chu chu thlang tienga an ramri chu a nih.
15. Sim tieng ramri chu Kiriat Jearim thlang tieng kotsuoa inthokin a'n tan a, Neftoa tuikhurah a hung suok a.
16. Ramri chu Refaim Ruom hmar tieng, Ben Hinom ruom ep tlang thuta chun a liem thla a, Jebuhai khawpui sim tieng pangper zuiin Hinom ruomah a fe thlak a, En Rogel a zu sun a.
17. Chu taka inthok chun hmar tieng a kawi a, En Semes tieng a fe a, Adumim Kawn epa um Gelilot chen a tlung a, chu taka inthok chun Reuben naupa Bohan Lungphun a zuk sun a.
18. Bet Araba hmar tieng pangper zuiin Araba-ah a fe thlak a.
19. Chu taka inthok chun Bet Hogla hmar tieng pangper a zui thlak a, sim tienga Jordan vadung suo, Tuipui Thi hmar tieng litengsawlah a tawp a. Hi hi sim tieng ramri chu a nih.
20. Sak tieng panga ramri chu Jordan vadung a nih.Chuonghai chu an sungkuo zir ang peia Benjamin thlahai chanpuol kil tina huoltu ramri chu a nih.
21. Benjamin thlahaiin an sungkuo zir ang peia khuo an hluohai chu Jeriko, Bet-holga, Emek-keziz,
22. Bet-Araba, Zemaraim, Bethel;
23. Avim, Para, Ofra,
24. Kefar-Amoni, Ofni le Geba, khaw sawmpahni le an khawperhai;
25. Gibeon, Rama le Berot,
26. Mizpa, Kefira le Moza,
27. Rekem, Irpel le Tarala,
28. Zela, Haelep le Jebuhai khawpui (Jerusalem), Gibea le Kiriat, khawpui sawmpali le an khawperhai an nih.Chuonghai chu Benjamin sungkuo ta dinga rochan chu a nih.
