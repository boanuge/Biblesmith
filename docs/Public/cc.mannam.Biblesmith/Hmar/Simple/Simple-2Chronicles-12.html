1. Rehoboam chu a hung inlal nghet phing leh ama tienga thang Israelhai po po leh LALPA dan an nghatsan ta a.
2. LALPA chungah an ringum naw leiin lal Rehoboam inlala inthoka kum nganaa chun Aigupta lal Sisak-in Jerusalem a hung run a.
3. Sisak chun sakor-tawlailir sang le indo sakor singruk le Libya mi hai, Suki mihai le Kushd mihai, tiem seng loa tam chu Aiguptaa inthokin a hung thuoi a,
4. Judai rama kula inhuon khuohai chu a lak a, Jerusalem chen a hang tlung a.
5. Rehoboam le Juda hnama lukhaituhai, Sisak an ti leia Jerusalema pungkhawmhai kuoma chun zawlnei Semaia a hang fe a, an kuomah, "LALPA chun hieng hin a hril: 'In mi nghatsan a, chuleiin kei khomin Sisak kutah ka maksan ve ta cheu a nih' a tih" tiin a hril a.
6. Israel lal le an lukhaituhai chu inpha hnuoiin, "LALPA chu a'n dik a nih" an ta.
7. An inpha hnuoi ti LALPAN a hmu chun LALPA thu chu Semaia kuomah a hung tlung a, "An inpha hnuoi leiin anni chu sukse loin intlan suok vat tawl ka tih. Sisak hmangin Jerusalem chungah ka lungsenna bun thla ta naw ning.
8. Amiruokchu, ka rongbawl le hnuoi lalhai rongbawl indanglamzie an hriet theina dingin Sisak thu hnuoiah sie tawl ka tih" a ta.
9. Aigupta lal Sisak chun Jerusalem chu a bei a, LALPA tempul le lal ina rosum po po chu a lak fai vong a, Solomon siem rangkachak phawhai po po khom a lak bok a.
10. Lal Rehoboam chun a aiin dar phaw a siem a, lal in kot vengtu kawmandarhai chu a'n koltir a.
11. LALPA tempulah lal a fe pha leh vengtuhai chun dar phaw chu an chawi a, an hung kir pha leh vengtuhai pindana a siena hmuna chun an thak kir nawk hlak a.
12. Rehoboam inpha hnuoi lei chun LALPAN a lungsenna a lak kieng a, a suksiet vong ta naw a, Judai ram chu a tha tluong pei a.
13. Lal Rehoboam chu Jerusalemah inlal zom peiin a'n suknghet pei a. Kum sawmli le kum khat a ni kuma inlal tanin, LALPAN a hming umna dinga Israel hnam po pohai hmun laia a thlang bik khuo Jerusalema chun kum sawm le kum sari a'n lal a, a nu hming chu Naama a na, Amon mi a nih.
14. LALPA tieng lungril a pek naw leiin thil suol a thaw a.
15. Rehoboam inlal sunga thil tlunghai, a bula inthoka a tawp chen chu zawlnei Semaia ziek le zawlnei Ido zieka inthla sawng dan chanchin an suinaah a chuong. Rehoboam le Jeroboam chu an indo zing zing hlak a.
16. Chun, Rehoboam chu a pi le puhai kuomah a chawl a, Davida khuoah an phum a, a naupa Abija-in a lalna chu a hung hluo ta a.
