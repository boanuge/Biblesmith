1. A naupa Jehosafat chun a lalna chu a hung hluo a, Israelhai do thei dingin a ngirhmun a suknghet a.
2. Judai rama kul nei khuo po poah vengtu sipai a sie a, Judai ram le a pa Asa lo lak Efraim rama khuoa hai chun sipai hmunpui a sie vong bok a.
3. Jehosafat chun a hung inlal tir lai chun a thlatu Davida kalchawi dan a zui a, Baal a biek naw leiin a kuomah LALPA a um a.
4. Israel lalhai ching dan zui ve loin an pi le puhai Pathien a zong a, a thupekhai a zawm a.
5. Chuleiin, a ram chu ama enkolna hnuoiah LALPAN a suknghet a, Judai rama mi po poin thilpek an hung pek a, hausain a hung ropui ta hle a.
6. A lungril chu LALPA lampui tieng a thle a, Judai ram hmun sanga bawlhmun po po chu a thiek a, Ashera lem thinghai chu a phur thluk vong bok a.
7. A hung inlala inthoka kum thumnaa chun a thoktu Ben-Hail hai, Obadia hai, Zekaria hai, Nathanel hai le Mikaia hai chu Judai ram khuoa mihai inchuktir dingin a tir suok a;
8. chuonghai chu Levi chia mi Semaia hai, Nethania hai, Zebadia hai, Asahel hai, Semiramot hai, Jehonathan hai, Adonija hai, Tobija hai le Tob-Adonija hai bakah thiempu Elisama le Jehoram haiin an zui a.
9. LALPA Dan Bu chawiin Judai rama khaw tin an sir a, mihai an inchuktir a.
10. Judai ram vela ram dang po po chun LALPA an ti leiin tu khomin Jehosafat chu an do ngam nawh.
11. Filistin mi thenkhatin Jehosafat chu thilpek an hung inhlan a, a thenin sie dingin tangkaruo an hung chawi a; Arab mihai khomin ran ruol, beramchal sangsari le zasari le kelchal sangsari le zasari an hung pek bok a.
12. Jehosafat chu a hung ropui deu deu a, Judai rama chun kul le thil siekhawmna khuohai a bawl a,
13. Judai ram khuoa hai chun fak ding a sek khawm teu a. Jerusalemah ral do thiem mi a sie teu bok a.
14. A chi chiin mani chi hotu an nei a, Juda thlahai laia sipai sangkhat pei inrawituhai chunga kawmandarhai chu Adna a na, mi nuoithum chunga kawmandar a nih.
15. A dottu chu Jehohanan a na, sipai nuoihni le singriet chunga kawmandar a nih.
16. A pathumna chu LALPA rongbawltu dinga ama thu ngota inpe Zikri naupa Amasia a na, sipai nuoihni chunga kawmandar a nih.
17. Benjamin sungkuoa inthok chun, sipai huoisen Eliada, thal le phaw chawi mi nuoihni chunga kawmandar le
18. Jehozabad, indo dinga inralthuom sa mi nuoikhat le singriet chunga kawmandar hai an nih. Hienghai hi Judai ram sunga kul nei khaw hrang hranga sipai a siehai baka lal kuoma umhai chu an nih.
19. (A um nawh)
