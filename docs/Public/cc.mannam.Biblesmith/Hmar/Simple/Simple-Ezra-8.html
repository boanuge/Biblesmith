1. Hieng hi Artazerzia inlal laia Babulona inthoka keima ruola hung sungkuo lukhaituhai le an sungkuo inziek dan chu a nih:
2. Finehas thlaa mi Gersom; Ithamar thlaa mi Daniel; Davida thlahaia inthokin Hatus;
3. Sekania thlahai; Paros thlaa mi Zekaria le a hungpui pasal za le sawmnga;
4. Pahat-Moab thlaa mi Zerahia naupa Eliehoenai le a hungpui pasal zahni;
5. Zatu thlaa mi Jahaziel naupa Sekania le a hungpui pasal zathum;
6. Adin thlaa mi Jonathan naupa Ebed le a hungpui pasal sawmnga;
7. Elam thlaa mi Athalia naupa Jesaia le a hungpui pasal sawmsari;
8. Sefatia thlaa mi Mikael naupa Zebadia le a hungpui pasal sawmriet;
9. Joab thlaa mi Jehiel naupa Obadia le a hungpui pasal zahni le sawmpariet;
10. Bani thlaa mi Josifia naupa Selomit le a hungpui pasal za le sawmruk;
11. Bebai thlaa mi, Bebai naupa Zekaria le a hungpui pasal sawmhni le pariet;
12. Azgad thlaa mi Hakatan naupa Johanan le a hungpui pasal za le sawm;
13. Adonikam thlaa mi, a nuhnung tak Elifelet hai, Jeuel hai le Semaia hai le an hungpui pasal sawmruk;
14. Bigvai thlaa mi Uthai le Zakur le an hungpui pasal sawmsari an nih.
15. Chuonghai po chu Ahava khaw kawla tuilamah ka ko khawm a, chu hmuna chun ni thum kan riek a. Mipui le thiempu hunghai hming ziekna ka hang en chun Levi chia mi hrim hrim tu khom ka hmu naw a.
16. Chuleiin, anni laia lukhaitu Eliezar, Ariel, Semaia, Elnathan, Jarib, Elanathan, Nathan, Zekaria le Mesulam hai chu ka ko a, an mi thiem Joiarib le Elnathan hai khom ka ko sa bok a.
17. Anni chu Kasifiaa Levihai thuoitu Ido kuomah fe a, Jerusalema Pathien tempula rongbawltu ding a unauhai le an tempula thoktu laia mihai chu hung tir ve dinga hni dingin ka tir tawl a; ei Pathien ina thoktu ding mi an hung tir ve theina dinga Ido le a unauhai le Kasifia tempula thoktuhai kuoma an tong dan ding chenin ka hril vong a.
18. Ei Pathien lunginsietna kut chu kan chungah a um leiin, mi pakhat a hming Serebia le a naupahai le unauhai mi sawm le pariet an hung tir a. Ama hi pa thei tak el, Mahli thlaa mi, Israel naupa Levi chia mi a nih.
19. Chun, Hasabia le Merari thlaa mi Jesai chu a unauhai le naupahai sawmhni leh an hung tir bok a.
20. Chu bakah tempula thoktu mi zahni le sawmhni an thang sa a. Hieng tempula thoktuhai hi Levihai thangpuitu dinga Davida le a thoktuhaiin an lo ruot tahai kha an na, an renga hming ziek lut vong a nih.
21. Ahava khaw kawla tuilam kama chun kan inzin sung Pathienin keini le kan naupanghai mi thuoi a, kan thuomhnawhai mi vengpek a, him taka kan inzin theina dingin a hmaah kan inphahnuoi a, bu ngheiin kan tongtai a.
22. Lal kuomah, "Kan Pathien chun ama chawibiektu chu a hum hlak a, ama phatsantu chunga ruok chu a lungsenna a chok hlak" tia kan lo hril tah hlak si leiin, lampuia ral laka mi vengtu ding sakor chungchuong sipaihai tir dinga hang hni chu inzakum ka ti ta bok si a.
23. Chu lei chun a nih bu ngheia Pathien kuoma mi hum dinga nasa taka kan tongtai chuh; kan hnina chu a mi hung don tah hrim a.
24. Chuongchun, hotu ding thiempu mi sawm le pahnih ka ruot hrang a, chuonghai chu Serebia le Hasabia le an unauhai sawm an nih.
25. Lal le a khawnbawlhai le mi lienhai le Israel mipuihaiin Pathien Tempul bawlna dinga tangkaruo hai, rangkachak hai le bur le bel hai an hung thaw khawm po po chu ka buk a, an kutah ka'n hlan a.
26. Ka buk le an kuta ka'n hlan sawng zat po chu tangkaruo kilogram singhni le sanghni; tangkaruo bur le bel zakhat, kilogram sawmsaria rik; rangkachak kilogram sangthum le zalia rik;
27. rangkachak thlengkum sawmhni, kilogram riet le a chenve vela rik; rangkachak tluka hlu, dar bel tle mawi taka siem pahnih a nih.
28. Chun, thiempuhai kuomah, "Nangni le hieng rosumhai hi LALPA ta dinga inhlan thienghlim in ni ta a; tangkaruo le rangkachakhai hi LALPA, in pi le puhai Pathien ta dinga mihaiin mani lung puoka an hung inhlan vong a nih.
29. Jerusalema LALPA tempula hlu lut dinga thiempu lal hai, Levi chi hai le Israel upahai kuta a hlekte khom inhmang loa in inhlan hma po chu hieng rosumhai hi tha takin veng ro" tiin ka hril a.
30. Chuongchun, thiempuhai le Levi chia mihai chun chuong rosumhai chu Jerusalema Pathien Tempula chawi dingin an lak ta a.
31. Ahava tuilama inthokin Jerusalem panin thla khatna ni sawm le pahniah kan inthok suok a, Pathien lunginsietna kut chun a mi veng a, hmelmahai le lampuia suomhmanghai lakah a mi humhim a.
32. Chuong ang chun Jerusalem kan tlung a, ni thum sung chu kan chawl hadam a.
33. Kan tlunga inthoka ni linaa chun tangkaruo hai, rangkachak hai, bur le bel hai chu Pathien inah kan buk a, thiempu Uria naupa Meremot kutah kan inhlan a; chu taka chun Finehas naupa thiempu Eleazar le Levi chia mi Jesua naupa Jozabad le Binui naupa Noadia hai khom an um a.
34. Iengkim kan tiem a, an buk vong a, an buk zat chu ziekin an inchik thlap a.
35. Chu zoa chun saltang hung kir po po chun Israel Pathien hmaah pumraw thilhlan an inhlan a. Israel hnam hming kipa dingin bawngchal sawm le pahnih, beramchal sawmkuo le paruk le beramte sawmsari le pasarih an inhlan a. Suol inthawina dingin kelchal sawm le pahnih an inhlan bok a. Hienghai po po hi LALPA kuoma pumraw thilhlan vong a nih.
36. Chun, lal thusuokhai chu Eufretis vadung thlang tieng biela ram optu le a rawihai kuoma chun an inentir a, anni chun thlopin, Tempul bawlna le Pathien chawibieknaah mipuihai chu an thangpui pei a.
