1. Chun, LALPA chun Josua a biek a,
2. "Mosie hmanga ka hril ta cheu ang khan, tu khom tum lawk lei ni loa tuol that palh chu thisen phuba la tumtu laka a him theina dingin,
3. a tuol tlanna ding khawpui ruotpek dingin Israelhai chu hril rawh.
4. Chuong khawpui laia a ienga khom chun tlan a ta, khawpui lutna kotkharah va ngir a ta, chu taka khaw upahai hrieta chun a chanchin intlun a ta, anni chun khaw sungah thuoi lut an ta, anni lai a cheng ve theina dingin a umna ding ruotpek an tih.
5. Thisen phubalatu dingin a hung hnot a ni khomin a kutah an lo pek ding a ni nawh, a tuolbawm chu a hmaa theidana nei loa pangpala a that a ni leiin.
6. Mipui roreltuhai hmaa ngira a chungthu rel a ni hma le chuong laia chu khuoa thiempu chu a thi hma khat chun, chu khuoa chun a um zing ding a nih. Chu zoah a hung tlan suoksan khuoa chun ama ina ngei kir nawk thei a tih ti hril rawh" a ta.
7. Chuongchun, Galili biel tlangrama Naftali-hai khuo Kedes dam, tlang rama Efraim-hai khuo Sekem dam, tlang rama Juda-hai khuo Kiriat Arba (Hebron) dam chu an ruot a.
8. Jordan ral sak tieng chun Reuben-hai biela thlaler tlang zawla um Bezer dam, Gilead biela Gad-hai khuo Ramot dam le Basan biela Manase-hai khuo Golan dam chu an ruot bok a.
9. Israel thlahai laia mi amanih anni laia um hnam dang mi amanih tu khom tum reng lei ni loa pangpala tuol thattu chu roreltuhaiin a chungthu an rel hma khat thisen phubalatu laka a him theina dingin, chuong khuo ruota hai chun a tuol tlan thei a nih.
