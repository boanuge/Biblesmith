1. Solomon hming inthangzie le LALPA hming le an inkungkeina chanchin Seba lalnuin a lo hriet chun thu khir tak taka fie tumin a'n thok suok a.
2. Hnungzuitu tam tak le sanghawngsei tam tak thuoiin Jerusalemah a hang fe a, rimtui le rangkachak le lunghlu tam tak a phur teuh bok a. Solomon kuom a tlung chun lungrila indon nuom a nei po po chu phorin a'n don a.
3. A thu indon taphot chu lal Solomon chun a don vong a, hriet naw le hrilfie harsa ti hrim a nei nawh.
4. Seba lalnu chun Solomon varzie chu a hriet a, a in bawl ropuizie hai a hmu a;
5. Solomon dawkana fak ding an hung siehai dam, a khawnbawl thung khup el hai dam, rongbawltuhai le an inchei dan mawizie hai dam, uoiin semtuhai dam le Tempula pumraw inthawinaa thil a'n hlan tamzie hai dam Seba lalnuin a hmu chun maktiin a ngaina a hriet naw hiel a.
6. Lal kuoma chun, "I thilthawhai le i varzie hai ka rama inthoka ka lo hriet kha a lo indik khop el.
7. Keima hunga mit ngeia ka hmu hma chun ka lo awi ngai naw a, a chenve khom an mi lo hril naw lem a ni hi tie! Varna le hausaknaah an hung hril nek daiin i lo insang lem a nih.
8. I mihai hi ieng ang lawma hamtha am an na! I bula cheng, i varna thu ngaithla roptu i siehlawhai hi an va hang hamtha ngei de aw!
9. LALPA i Pathien, a lawmtu che le lalthungphaa sietu che chu inpakin um raw se. LALPAN kumkhuoin Israelhai a hmangai leiin fel taka an roreltu dingin a sie che a ni hi" a ta.
10. Chuongchun, lal kuomah thilpek rangkachak kilogram sangli le rimtui le lunghlu tam tak a pek a. Seba lalnuin rimtui Solomon a pek ang zozaia tam chu tukhomin an pek nawk ta ngai nawh.
11. (Hiram long ruol Ofira rangkachak tam tak phura fehai chun almuk thing tam tak le lunghlu an hung phur teu bok a.
12. Lal chun almuk thinghai chu LALPA Tempul le ama lal in thut do nghetna dingin a hmang bakah rimawi tumtuhai hmang ding tingtang le perkhuong siemnain a hmang bok a. Chuong ang zozaia tam almuk thing chu a hnungin an lak lut ta ngai naw a, an hmu ta ngai bok nawh.)
13. Lal Solomon chun Seba lalnu dit le hni taphot chu a thawpek bakah a lal ina rosum tam tak a pek bok a, chuongchun a rawihai leh ama ram tieng a kir nawk ta a.
14. Kum tina Solomon kuta rangkachak lut zat chu kilogram singhni le sangthum a nih.
15. Chu baka chun sumdawngtu le thil zortuhai le Arab lalhai le a ram optuhaia inthokin sie le leiman a hmu teuh bok a.
16. Lal Solomon chun rangkachak sut phekin phaw lien tak tak zahni a siem a, phaw pakhat peiah rangkachak kilogram thum le gram sawmnga vel an hmang a.
17. Chun, rangkachak sut phek bokin phaw chin lem zathum a siem bok a, phaw pakhat peiah rangkachak kilogram khat le gram sawmsari an hmang a. Chuonghai chu a lal in 'Lebanon Ngawpui Run' a chun a sie a.
18. Chun, lal chun saiha ngotin lalthungpha ropui tak a siem bok a, chu chu rangkachak tuia inluon vong a nih.
19. Lalthungpha kai tungna chu kailawn paruk a um a, lalthungpha hnung tieng chu a'n kuol pup a, thungna sir ve vea chun kut innghatna ban a um a, chu sir ve vea chun sakeibaknei lem an ngir a.
20. Kailawn paruka chun sakeibaknei lem sawmpahnih, kailawn sir tinah pakhat pei an ngir a, chuong ang chu khaw lai rama khom an la siem ngai nawh.
21. Lal Solomon no po po chu rangkachaka siem vong a na, Lebanon Ngawpui Runa a bur le belhai chu rangkachak hlir hlaka siem vong a nih. Solomon dam lai chun tangkaruo chu ieng thama ngai a ni naw leiin a bur le belhai chu tangkaruoa siem hrim hrim a um nawh.
22. Lal chun tuifinrieta Hiram long ruol le inzin hlak sumdawng long ruol a nei a, chuonghai chun kum thum dan peiah rangkachak dam, tangkaruo dam, saiha dam, zawng le ngau lien chi dam an hung phur hlak a.
23. Hausakna le varnaah Solomon chun khawvela lal dang po po a khum vong a.
24. Pathienin a lungrila varna thu a siepek chu ngaithla dingin khawvela mi po poin Solomon chu an hung pan huoi huoi hlak a.
25. Chun, kum tinin a kuoma hung taphotin thilpek tangkaruo le rangkachaka siem thil dam, silfen dam, ralthuom dam, rimtui dam, sakor dam le sabengtung dam an hung chawi hlak a.
26. Solomon chun tawlailir sangkhat le zali le sakor singkhat le sanghni a nei a, chuonghai chu tawlailir siena khuoa hai le Jerusalemah ama kuomah a sie a.
27. Lal chun Jerusalemah thil a hau em leiin tangkaruo dam chu lungte angina a tam a, sidar thing khom tlang thuta theichang bil ang hielin a tam bok.
28. Solomon sakorhai chu Musri le Kilikih rama inthoka a lak deu vong a nih. Chu taka inthok chun lal sumdawnghaiin a honin an inchawk hlak a,
29. tawlailirhai chu Aigupta rama inthokin pakhat peiah tangkaruo shekel zaruk le sakor chu tangkaruo shekel za sawmnga peiin an inchawk a. Chun, Hit mihai le Aram lalhai kuomah an zor sawng bok a.
