1. Chuong thil hnunga chun Absalomin ama hmang dingin tawlailir le a hnuktu sakorhai le a zuitu mi sawmnga a buotsai a.
2. Zing inhma takin a tho a, khawpui lutna kul kotkhar bulah a ngir a, lal kuoma thubuoi hril dinga hung taphot chu a lo ko a, "Khaw lai khuoa inthoka hung am i na?" a lo ti seng a. "I siehlaw hi Israel hnamhai laia mi ka ni ve" an ti pei a.
3. Chu zoah Absalom chun, "Ngai ta, i thu chu a'n dikin a tha ngei a, sienkhom a ngaituotu ding lalin a ruot si naw a ni hi tie!" a lo ti pei a.
4. Absalom bok chun, "Kei dam hi roreltuin a mi ruot chu ni sien, thubuoi le thupoi nei taphot chu ka kuomah hung an ta, indik takin ka lo relpek el ding a ni vei leh" a ti zie bok a.
5. Chun, tu khom a hmaah kuna hung hnai taphot chu a kutin a lo ban a, a kuo a, a lo fawp pei a.
6. Lal kuoma thu intlun dinga hung taphot chuong anga a lo thaw pei leiin Absalom chun Israel mihai lungril a hip ta hle a.
7. Chun, kum lii a hung invoi hnung chun Absalomin lal kuomah, "LALPA kuoma ka thu intiemkamna hlen dingin Hebron khuoah mi'n fetir rawh.
8. Suriaj ram Gesur-a i siehlaw a um lai khan, 'LALPAN Jerusalemah a mi thuoi kir nawk chun Hebron-ah inthawina inhlan ka tih' tiin a lo intiem ta si a" a ta.
9. Lal chun, "Thlamuong takin fe rawh" a ta, Hebrona chun a fe ta a.
10. Absalom chun, "Tawtawrawt ri in hriet charin, 'Absalom chu Hebronah lal a nih' ti ro" tia hril dingin Israel mihai po po lai a rukin mi a tir a.
11. Jerusalema inthoka mi zahni a fielhai chun Absalom chu an zui a, anni chun thil chinchang ieng khom an hriet ve nawh.
12. Absalomin inthawina a'n hlan lai chun Gilon mi Ahitofel, Davida khawnbawl chu a umna Gilo-a inthokin a'n cha a. A thil phierruk chu hung hrat peiin a zuitu chu an hung pung ta tuol tuol a.
13. Chun, Davida kuomah mi a hung a, "Israel mihai lungril chu Absalom tieng a thle vong tah" tiin a hung hril a.
14. Davida chun Jerusalema a thoktuhai po po kuomah, "Tho unla, tlan suok ei tiu, Absalomin tu khom mi hla naw nih. Inrang taka ei suok a ngai. Chuong naw chun mi hung nang hman dingin a suok nghal ring a um a, ei chungah sietna intlunin, kawlhnamin a khaw phek hin mi hung sam rawi a tih" a ta.
15. Lal thoktuhai chun a kuomah, "I siehlawhai hin lalin a ti ang ang chu thaw dingin kan inpei zing a nih" an ta.
16. Chun, lal chu a suok a, a sunghai po poin an zui a, lal in enkoltu dingin a hmeihai sawm a maksan a.
17. Lal a hang suok chun mipuiin an zui a, iemani chena hlaa chun an chawl hri phot a.
18. A mihai po po le Keret mihai le Pelet mihai chun an fe pel a, Git mi zaruk ruol, Gath-a inthoka hung zuituhai chu lal hmaa chun an fe a.
19. Lal chun Git mi Itai kuomah, "Ieng leiin am nang hi i hung ve ding? Kir nawk la, lal Absalom kuoma chun um rawh, nang chu mikhuol, ram dang mi chambang i ni hi.
20. Zani ta hung tlung chauh kha i na, voisun hin a suk a tunga mi zui dingin ka phut ve ding che am a ni? Kei ngei khomin ka fena ding chin hrim ka hriet nawh. Kir nawk la, i mihai hi thuoi rawh. Lunginsietna le ringumna chu i chungah um raw se" a ta.
21. Sienkhom, Itai chun lal kuomah, "LALPA hringna le ka lal hringna salin ka hril hi, lal, ka pu hi i umna taphotah thi le thi, dam le dam, i siehlawin um zing a tih" a ta.
22. Davida chun Itai kuomah, "An leh, hma thuoi unla, fe pei ei tiu" a ta. Chuongchun, Git mi Itai le a mihai le sungkuo an thuoihai chun hma tieng an pan pei a.
23. An liem lai chun a ram phekin inring takin an ta a. Lal khomin Kidron vadung chu a kan a, thlaler lam tieng panin mipui chun an zui a.
24. Thiempu Zadok khom anni lai a thang a, a kuoma fe ve Levihai po po chun Pathien Bawm an zawn a. Chun, Pathien Bawm chu an innghat a, thiempu Abiathar chun mipuiin khawpui an suoksan zo vong chenin inthawina a'n hlan a.
25. Lal chun Zadok kuomah, "Pathien Bawm hi khawpui sungah zawn kir nawk ro, Pathien mit ka tlung chun mi thuoi kir nawk a ta, Pathien Bawm le a umna chu mi'n hmutir nawk a tih.
26. Amiruokchu, 'Nangmaah lawmna hrim ka nei nawh' a ti ruok chun ka chungah a nuom ang anga thaw dingin ka'n pei zing a nih" a ta.
27. Chun, lalin thiempu Zadok kuoma bok chun, "Nang chu zawlnei i nih an naw maw? I naupa Ahimaaz le Abiathar naupa Jonathan hi thuoi la, thlamuong takin khawpui tieng kir nawk ta ro. Nang le Abiatharin i naupa pahnihai hi thuoi ro.
28. Kei chu chanchin in mi hung inhriettir nawk hma po chu thlalera vadung kaia hin lo um ka tih" a ta.
29. Chuongchun, Zadok le Abiathar chun Pathien Bawm chu Jerusalemah an kirpui nawk a, chu taka chun a um a.
30. Chuongchun, Davida chun tap malam pei pumin Oliv tlang chu a pan tung a, a lu a'n khum a, ke lawngin a fe a. Ama le fe mipui po po khom chun lu an inkhum a, an tap malam pei a.
31. Chun, "Absalom thu phierrukpuitu chu Ahitofel a nih" tiin Davida chu an hung hril a. Chuongchun, Davida chu a tongtai a, "Aw LALPA, Ahitofel thu phierpui chu invetthlakah inchangtir dingin ka ngen che" a ta.
32. Davidan tlang sip, Pathien an biekna hmun a hang lawn kai chun Ark mi a hming Husai, a zakuo potthler chu haka, a luah pilvut kai nung el chun a lo tuok a.
33. Davida chun a kuomah, "Ka kuomah i hung chun ka ta dingin phurrik ning i tih.
34. Jerusalemah i kir nawk ruok chun Absalom kuomah, 'Aw lalpa, i siehlaw ning ka tih. Kha hma khan i pa siehlaw ka ni hlak a, tu hin chu i siehlaw ni lem tang ka tih' ti la, Ahitofel thu phierpui po po sukhruttu ning i tih.
35. Chu taka chun i kuomah thiempu Zadok le Abiathar khom an um ve ding a na, lal ina thu suok i hriet po po chu anni kuomah i hril hlak ding annawm.
36. Ngai ta, an naupahai pahni, Zadok naupa Ahimaza le Abiathar naupa Jonathan hai khom an kuomah an um kha. I thil hriet taphot chu mi'n hriettir dingin anni chu i hung tir hlak ding a nih" a ta.
37. Chuongchun, Davida ruol tha Husai chu Absalomin Jerusalem a lut lai chun khawpuiah a hung lut ve ta a.
