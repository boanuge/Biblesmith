1. Rehoboam chu Sekem khuoah a fe a, Israel mihai po po khom ama chu lala siem dingin chu taka chun an fe ve a.
2. Nebat naupa Jeroboam, lal Solomon leia Aigupta rama tlan hmang chun chu ri chu a lo hriet a, Aigupta rama inthokin a kir nawk a.
3. Israel mi pungkhawmhai po po chun Jeroboam chu an ko a, ama rawiin Rehoboam kuomah palai an fe a,
4. "I pa khan kan phur a sukrik taluo a, tuhin inrim taka a mi sorna le ringkol rik tak a min phurtir hi mi sukzangpek la, i rong bawl kan tih" an ta.
5. Rehoboam chun, "Kir nawk ta unla, ni thum hnungah hung nawk ro" a ta, palaihai chu an suok a.
6. Lal Rehoboam chun a pa Solomon dam sung po poa a hnuoia sinthaw hlak upahai chu a rawn a, "Hieng mihai hi iengti anga don dingin am in ngai a?" a ta.
7. Anni chun, "Voisun hin hieng mihai thu hi ngilnei tak le tha taka i lo dongsawng chun kumkhuoin i siehlawah um an tih" an ta.
8. Sienkhom, upahai remruotpui chu Rehoboam chun pom loin, ama ruoltlakpui tlangval ruol, a bula khawsa hlakhai chu a rawn lem a.
9. An kuomah, "Iem in ngai ve dan chu? Mipuihaiin, 'I pa ringkol mi'n phurtir hi mi sukzangpek deu rawh' an ti hi iengtin am ei don ding a na?" a ta.
10. A ruolchamhai chun, " 'I pa ringkol min phurtir hi mi sukzangpek rawh' tituhai kuoma chun, 'Ka kutte hi ka pa kawng nekin a lien lem.
11. Ka pa khan phur rik tak a'n phurtir cheu a, kei chun in phur ka sukrik zuol ding a nih. Ka pa khan vuokna hruolin a hrik hlak cheu a, kei ruok chun khawmuol kaikuongin ka hrik ding cheu a nih' tiin hril rawh" an ta.
12. Lalin, "Ni thum hnungah hung nawk ro" a ti angin ni thum hnungah Jeroboam le Israelhai chu Rehoboam kuomah an hung nawk a.
13. Lal chun upahai thurawn a lak naw leiin inrum takin a lo biek a.
14. Tlangvalhai remruotpui angin, "Ka pa khan in phur a lo sukrik a, kei chun ka sukrik zuol ding a nih. Ka pa khan vuokna hruolin a hrik hlak cheu a, kei ruok chun khawmuol kaikuongin ka hrik ding cheu a nih" a ta.
15. Chuong ang chun mipuihai thu chu lal chun a ngaithlak nuom nawh; chu thil hung tlung chu LALPAN Silo mi Ahija hmanga Nebat naupa Jeroboam kuoma a lo hril lawk tah chu sukdikna dinga LALPA thilthaw a ni si a.
16. Lal chun an thu a ngaithlak nuom der nawh ti Israelhaiin an hrietin lal chu hieng ang hin an don a:"Davida lakah iem chanvo kan nei hrim a? Jesai naupa lakah rochan iem kan nei ve a? Aw Israelhai, in puon in tieng kir nawk ta ro.Aw Davida, i sungkuo chu nangin enkol ta rawh" tiin. Chuongchun, Israelhai chu in tieng an kir ta rip a.
17. Judai rama um Israelhai chunga chauh Rehoboam chu lal a hung ni ta a.
18. Lal Rehoboam chun sinthawa mi sor luihai inrawitu Adoniram chu Israel pungkhawmhai kuomah a tir a, anni chun lungin an lo deng hlum a. Lal Rehoboam ruok chu a tawlailirah a lut hman hram a, Jerusalem tieng a tlan sie a.
19. Chuongchun, Israelhai chu Davida sungkuo lakah tu chen hin an hel tah a nih.
