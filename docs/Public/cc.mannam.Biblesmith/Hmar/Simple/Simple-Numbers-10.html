1. Chun, LALPA chun Mosie a biek a,
2. "Mipuihai ko khawmna ding le riebuk inson ding phaa.inhriettirnaa hmang dingin tangkaruoin tawtawrawt pahni insuttir rawh.
3. Chu chu an mut kop chun mipui po po i kuomah Intuokna Puon in kot bulah an hung tlan khawm vong ding a nih.
4. Pakhat chauh an mut chun, Israel pahnam tina lukhaituhai chauh i hmaah an hung khawm ding a nih.
5. Chun, ralkhel ri anga an mut chun suo tienga um hai chu an suok ding a nih.
6. Ral khel ri anga an mut voi hninaah sim tienga umhai an suok ding a nih. Ralkhel ri chu inthok suok ding ti hrilna a nih.
7. Mipui ko khawmna dinga in mut pha ruok chu ralkhel ri angin mut naw ro.
8. "Aron le a thla thiempuhai hi tawtawrawt muttu ding chu an nih. Hi hi nangni le in thlahai chena in kumkhaw thaw dan ding chu a nih.
9. In rama in hmelma, neksawrtuhai cheu do dinga in suok ding pha leh ralkhel mutin tawtawrawt in mut ding a nih. Chuong anga in thaw chun LALPA in Pathienin hre zing a ti cheu a, hmelmahai lakah san suok a ti cheu.
10. Chun, in hlim ni, a hunbia in kut chang le thla thar lawmna in nei chang dam le pumraw thilhlan le inremna thilhlan in thaw changin in mut hlak bok ding a nih. Chu chu in Pathien hmaa hriet in ni zingna ding a nih. Kei hi LALPA, in Pathien chu ka nih.
11. Kum hnina, thla hnina, ni sawmhninaa chun sum chu Thuthlung biekbuk chunga inthokin a'n kang tung a.
12. Chuongchun, Israel thlahai chu Sinai thlalera inthokin an inthok suok a, hmun hrang hrang an fe pel hnungin, sum chu Paran thlalerah a ngir a.
13. Hi hi Mosie hmanga LALPA thupeka an inthok suok hmasakna tak a nih.
14. A hmasa takin Juda thlahai chu an hnam inchikna puonzar chawiin an suok a, anni inrawitu chu Aminadab naupa Nason a nih.
15. Isakar thlahai inrawitu chu Zuara naupa Nethanel a nih.
16. Zebulun thlahai inrawitu chu Helon naupa Eliab a nih.
17. Biekbuk chu an thiek a, a zawntu Gersonhai le Merarihai chun zawnin an inthok suok a.
18. Anni zoah Reuben thlahai chu an hnam inchikna puonzar khaiin an inthok suok a, anni inrawitu chu Sedeur naupa Elizur a nih.
19. Simeon thlahai inrawitu chu Zurisadai naupa Selumiel a na,
20. Gad thlahai inrawitu chu Deuel naupa Elisaf a nih.
21. Chu zoah Kohathaiin thil thienghlimhai chu phurin an inthok suok a. An hung tlung tawl hmaa biekbuk chu lo inngir ding a nih.
22. Anni hnungah Efraim thlahai chu an hnam inchikna puonzar khaiin an inthok suok a, anni inrawitu chu Amihud naupa Elisama a nih.
23. Manase hnam inrawitu chu Pedazur naupa Gamaliel a na,
24. Benjamin thlahai inrawitu chu Gideoni naupa Abidan a nih.
25. Dan thlahai chun an hnam inchikna puonzar khaiin mi po po hnung dalah an thang a, anni inrawitu chu Amisadai naupa Ahiezer a nih.
26. Aser thlahai inrawitu chu Okran naupa Pagiel a na,
27. Naftali thlahai inrawitu chu Enan naupa Ahira a nih.
28. Hi hi Israel thlahai an fe suok phaa indot peia an insom suok dan chu a nih.
29. Mosie tarpu Midian mi Reuel naupa Hobab kuoma chun Mosien, " 'Peng ka ti cheu' tia LALPAN a hrilna ram tieng pan chun kan fe ding a nih. Nangni khom hung ve unla, in chungah tha takin thawng kan tih, LALPA chun thil tha Israelhai chungah a tiem si a" a ta.
30. Ama chun, "Hung da el ka tih. Ka ram tieng, ka mihai kuom tieng kir nawk ka tih" a ta.
31. Mosie chun, "Mi fesan lul naw rawh. Thlalera riebuk khuorna ding i hriet lem a, keini ta dingin mit i ni ringot a nih.
32. Kan kuomah i hung ve chun LALPAN thil tha a mi pek taphot chu ei intlang ding annawm" a ta.
33. Chuong ang chun, LALPA tlanga inthok chun an suok a, ni thum an inzin a. LALPA Thuthlung bawm chun an ta dinga chawlna hmun remchang zongin ni thum an hma a thuoi a.
34. An riebuka inthoka an suok chun sunah LALPA sum chu an chungah a'n zar a.
35. Thuthlung bawm chu a suok ding zatin Mosien, "Aw LALPA, hung tho la,I hmelmahai chu hnot dar la,I ralhai chu i hmaah tlan se raw hai se"a ta.
36. A chawl ding zatin,"Aw LALPA hung kir nawk rawh,I mi Israel sang tam tak lai hin"a ti zie bok a nih.
