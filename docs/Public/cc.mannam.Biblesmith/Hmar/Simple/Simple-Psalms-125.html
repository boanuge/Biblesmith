1. LALPA-a innghattu chu,Zion tlang sukhning ruol lo,Kumkhaw dai ang chu an nih.
2. Jerusalem khawpui chu,Tlangin a'n huol vel angin,LALPA chun a mihai chu,Tuta le chatuon chenin,An huol vel zing hlak a nih.
3. Mi indikhai rama chun,Mi suol inlal zing naw nih,Mi indikhai chun thil suol,An thaw ve nawna dingin.
4. Aw LALPA, mi thahai leLungril ngilhai chunga chun,Thil tha thawpek rop hlak rawh.
5. Suol lam hrawtuhai ruok chu,Mi suolhai ruolin LALPAN,La hnot suok tawl vong a tih.Israelhai chunga chun,Inremna inleng raw se.
