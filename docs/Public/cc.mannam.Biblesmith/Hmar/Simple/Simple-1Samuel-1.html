1. Efraim tlang ram Ramathaim- zofim khuoah mi pakhat a hming Elkana a um a, ama chu Jehoram naupa a na, ama chu Elihu naupa a na, ama chu Tohu naupa a na, ama chu Efraim mi Zufa naupa a nih.
2. Elkana chun nuhmei pahni a nei a, pakhat chu Hana a na, pakhat chu Penina a nih. Penina chun nau a nei a, Hana ruok chun a nei ve nawh.
3. Iengkim LALPA chawibiek ding le a hmaa inthawina inhlan dingin Elkana chu an khuoa inthokin Silo khuoah kum tinin a hang fe zie hlak a, chu taka chun thiempu Eli naupa Hofni le Finehas hai chun thiempu sin an thaw a.
4. Inthawina an inhlan ding pha Elkana chun a nuhmei Penina le a naupa le naunu hai chan ding seng sa iemani zat a sem hlak a.
5. Hana ruok chu a hmangai a, LALPAN a sul a khar hri bok leh, chan hni a siempek bik zie hlak a.
6. LALPAN a sul a khar lei chun a khingpuinu chun a lung khei zawng ding tak thawin a suklungsen hlak a.
7. Kum tin kum tin elin LALPA ina Hana a fe zatin a khingpuinu chun elsenin a suktap a, bu a nghei pha hiel hlak a.
8. Elkana chun, "Hana, ieng dinga i tap am? Iem a ta bu i fak naw? Iem a ta i lung a hnuol? Naupa sawm nek khomin i ta dingin ka hlu lem naw maw?" tiin a thlem hlak a.
9. Tum khat chu Silo-a an fak an dawn zoin Hana chu a tho a. Chuong lai chun thiempu Eli chu LALPA tempul kotkhar ban bula thungnaah a'n thung a.
10. Beidong le lungril na taka tap hlop hlop pumin Hana chu LALPA kuomah a tongtai a.
11. LALPA kuomah, "Iengkim LALPA, i bawinu rinum tuor hi zuk thlir la, hre zing rawh. I bawinu hi theinghil loa nau pasal i pek chun, a dam sung po po LALPA ta dingin pe suok ka ta, a lu khom vaw ni ngai hrim hrim naw nih" tiin inkham meuin thu a tiem a.
12. Chuonga a tongtai mel mel laia a hmur inphu chu thiempu Eli chun a lo thlir zing a.
13. Hana chu lungril takin a tongtai a, ri nei si loin a hmur a'n phu sep sep a.
14. Eli chun zu inrui sawnin, "Ieng chen am inruiin i um zing ding a na? I uoiin sie hmang raw khai" a ta.
15. Hana chun, "K'an rui naw ie, Pu, nu lungril rimsi tawpkhawk ka ni hi. Zu amanih uoiin amanih ka dawn nawh; LALPA hmaah ka lungril ka phor suok lem a nih.
16. I siehlaw hi nuhmei rilo angah mi sie naw rawh; ka lungrila sip liem ka lungngaina le beidongna hai ka phor suok lem a nih" tiin a don a.
17. Eli chun, "Lungmuong takin fe ta rawh, LALPA, Israelhai Pathien chun i hni ang tak chu hung pe raw se che" a ta.
18. Ama chun, "I bawinu hin i mit tlung ngei raw se" a ta. Chuongchun, nuhmei chu a fe a, bu a fak a, lungngai hmel a put zing ta naw a.
19. A zinga chun inhma takin an tho a, LALPA an chawibiek a, an khuo Rama tieng an kir nawk a. Elkana chun a nuhmei a pawl a, LALPAN ama nu chu a hriet zing a.
20. Chuongchun, Hana chun nau a pai a, nau pasal a nei a, "LALPA kuoma ka hni suok a nih" a ta, a hmingah Samuel a sak a.
21. Chun, LALPA hmaa kum tina pumraw inthawina an nei hlak ang le a thu intiemkamna hlen dingin a sunghai thuoiin Elkana chu Silo-ah a hang fe a.
22. Hi tum hin Hana ruok chu a hang fe ve nawh. A pasal kuomah, "Naupang hin nene a nghei pha le chauh ama thuoiin LALPA hmaah hang inhlan ka ta, chu taka chun ama chu um zing tang a tih" a ta.
23. A pasal Elkana chun, "Tha i ti ang tak chun thaw rawh. Nene nek i'n bantir chenin hi taka hin um rawh; LALPAN i thu chu sukpuitlingpek ngei raw se che" a ta. Chuongchun, naupangin nene nek a ban hma khat chu Hana chu inah a um a, a naupa chu a enkol a.
24. Nene nek a ban chun naupang dawng tak la ni sien khom a nu Hana chun bawngchal kum thum mi keiin, beiphit kilogram sawm le uoiin savun peng khat chawi bokin Silo khuoa LALPA inah a fepui a.
25. Bawngchal chu an that a, naupang chu Eli kuomah an thuoi a.
26. Nuhmei chun, "Pu, i hringna salin ka hril, kei hi i bula hi hmuna ngira LALPA kuoma tongtai nu ngei kha ka nih.
27. Hi naupang hi hniin ka tongtai a, ka hni ang takin LALPAN a mi pek a.
28. Tuhin keikhomin LALPA kuomah naupang hi ka hung inhlan a, a dam sunga LALPA ta dinga pek suok a nih" a ta. Chu taka chun Samuel chu LALPA rongbawlin a um ta a.
