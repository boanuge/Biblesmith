1. Chawimawi ka che, ka Lal Pathien,Kumkhuon i hming inpak ka tih.
2. Ni tinin inpak ka ti che,Kumkhuon i hming chawimawi ka tih.
3. LALPA chu a ropui em a, Inpak chungchuong ding hrim a nih,A ropuizie sui phak an nawh.
4. Thang tinin i thilthawhai chu,Ruoltharhai hril sawng pei an ta,I thaw ropuizie puong an tih.
5. I mawi ropuizie hril an ta,Keikhom i thilthaw makziehai,Sutin dawnkhawl ve hlak ka tih.
6. I thaw ropui le rapumzie,Mihaiin puong dar tawl an ta,I ropuizie hril veng ka tih.
7. Thatna i hauzie chu lawmin,Hlim takin tlang lampui an ta,I'n dikzie hi khekpui an tih.
8. LALPA chu lainatna hau tak,Mi ngilnei le lung inuoi tak,Hmangaina tawp lo nei a nih.
9. Mi tin chungah LALPA a tha,A thilsiem po po hai khom hi,An rengin a lunginsiet vong.
10. Aw LALPA i thilsiemhaiin,Lawmthu hrilin, inpak an che,I mi thienghlim po po khomin,Inpakin chawimawi an ti che!
11. I lalram ropuizie hrilin,I thilthawtheizie puong an tih.
12. I thilthaw ropuiziehai chu,Mihai kuomah puong dar an ta,I ram ropuizie hril an tih.
13. I ram chu kumhlun ram a na,I lalram khomin suon tin a daih.A thutiemah LALPA a ringum,A thilsiem chungah ngil a nei.
14. Tluhai chu LALPAN a tungding,Indom kunhai chu a kei har.
15. Nangma thlirtuhai po po chu,A hun takah fak ding i pek.
16. I kut phara i hang hrai chun,Hring tinrenghai an tlai seng hlak.
17. Kong iengkimah LALPA a'n dik,A thaw taphotah ngil a nei.
18. Ama kotu taphot kuomah,LALPA chu a hnai zing hlak a,Titakzeta kotu kuomah.
19. Ama tituhai ditzawng chu,A sukhlawtlingpek ngei hlak a,An rawl hrein a san him hlak.
20. Ama hmangaituhai po chu,LALPAN a veng him tlat hlak a,Mi suolhai chu sukbo a tih.
21. LALPA inpakna thuhai chu,Ka bauin puong suok hlak a ta,Thilsiem tinin a hming thienghlim,Kumkhuoin inpak raw hai se.
