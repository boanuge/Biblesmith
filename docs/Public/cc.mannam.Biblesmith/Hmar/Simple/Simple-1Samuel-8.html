1. Samuel chu a hung upa ta a, a naupahai chu Israelhai chunga roreltuah a sie a.
2. A upa tak hming chu Joel a na, a dottu hming chu Abija a na, Beershebaa inthokin ro an rel a.
3. Nisienlakhom, a nauhai chun a um dan la ve loin sum le pai an hnot a, thamna an lak a, indik lo takin ro an rel a.
4. Chuongchun, Israel upahai po po chu an inkhawm a, Rama khuoah Samuel kuoma chun an fe a,
5. a kuomah, "Ngai ta, i hung upa ta hle a, i naupahai hlak chun i um dan an lak ve si naw a, hnam dang po pohai thaw dan angin kan chunga roreltu dingin lal mi ruotpek rawh" an ta.
6. "Roreltu dingin lal mi ruotpek rawh" an ti lei chun Samuel chu a lawm naw hle a, LALPA kuomah a tongtai a.
7. LALPA chun a kuomah, "Mipuiin i kuoma an hril po po chu ngaithla rawh. Nangma an dit naw lei che a ni naw a, an chunga roreltu dingin keima an mi dit naw lei lem a nih.
8. Aigupta rama inthoka ka thuoi suok nia inthok tah khan tu chen hin mi nghatsan le pathien dang rong mi bawlsan an ching a, chuong ang chun i chunga khom an thaw ve a ni hih.
9. Chuleiin, an thu ngaithla la, amiruokchu, an chunga lalhaiin an la hung thaw dan dinghai chu ngun takin hril lawk tawl rawh" a ta.
10. Samuel chun LALPA thu po po chu lal hnitu mipuihai chu a hril vong a.
11. An kuoma chun, "In chunga roreltu lal a la hung chet dan ding chu hieng ang hin ning a tih: A tawlailir le a sakorhai enkoltu dingin in naupahai lakpek a ti cheu a, a tawlailir hmaa chun tlan an tih.
12. A then chu mi sang chunga kawmandar dam le sawmnga chunga kawmandar damin sieng a ta, a thenin a hnuoi inletin a bu sik an ta, a then ralthuom siemtu le a tawlailir hmangruo siemtuah hmang tawl a tih.
13. In naunuhai chu rimtui siemtua dam, bu suongtua dam, bei siemtua dam hmang a tih.
14. Chun, in in le lohai, in grep huonhai le in oliv huonhai a tha tha lakpek a ti cheu a, a siehlawhai peng a tih.
15. In bu tharsuok le in uoin tharsuok sawma pakhat lang a ta, a thoktuhai kuomah peng a tih.
16. In bawipa le bawinuhai le in ran ruol laia a tha tak ngot le in sabengtunghai chu ama hmang dingin lapek a ti cheu.
17. In beram ruol laia mi sawma pakhat lang a ta, nangni ngei a siehlawah thang in tih.
18. Chu ni chu a tlung pha leh in lal thlang laka inthoka thawvengna hniin inrum in ta, chu huna chun LALPAN donsak naw ni cheu" a ta.
19. Nisienlakhom, mipuihai chun Samuel thu chu an ngaithlak nuom naw a, "Ni lo e, kan chunga lal ding kan dit a nih.
20. Chuongchun, hnam danghai ang hung ni veng ei ta, ei lal chun ro mi relpek a ta, hma thuoiin ei ralhai mi dopek a tih" an ta.
21. Samuel chun an hril po po chu a hriet a, LALPA hmaa chun a hril sawng pei a.
22. LALPA chun, "An thu ngaithla la, lal pe tawl rawh" a ta. Samuel chun Israel mihai kuomah, "Mi tin mani khuo sengah kir nawk ta ro" a ta.
