1. LALPA chun Mosie kuomah,
2. "Aron le a naupahai le Israel thlahai po po kuomah hieng hin hril rawh: 'Hi hi LALPA thupek chu a nih:
3. Israel mi tukhom bawngchal amanih, beram le kel amanih riebuk sung le a puo tieng amania that a,
4. LALPA biekbuk hmaa LALPA ta dinga thilhlan Intuokna Puon in kota hung la naw taphot chu thisen insuo leia thiem naw changa ngai ning a ta, thisen insuotu a ni leiin a mipuihai laia inthoka tuithlar a ni ngei ding a nih.
5. Chuong chu a ni leiin, tuta tuolpara Israelhaiin inthawina an inhlan hlak po po hi LALPA hmaah an hung inhlan vong ta ding a nih. Intuokna Puon in kotah thiempu kuomah, LALPA hmaah hung chawi an ta, inremna thilhlanah an hung inhlan ding a nih.
6. Thiempu chun Intuokna Puon in kot bula maicham chu ran thisen chun theng a ta, a thau chu LALPA ta dinga rimtui inhnik dingin rawng a tih.
7. An uirena hlak kel lemhai kuoma chun an inthawi ding a ni ta nawh' ti hi an thla peihai ding khoma an kumkhaw dan ding chu a nih" tiin.
8. " Chun, hieng hin hril tawl rawh: 'Israel mi amanih nangni laia hnam dang mi chenghai tu khomin pumraw inthawi an inhlan a,
9. Intuokna Puon in kot bula LALPA hmaah an hung inhlan si naw chun, chu mi chu a chipuihai laia inthoka tuithlar a ni ngei ding a nih.
10. " 'Israel mi tu khom le anni laia cheng ram dang mi tu khom thisen fatu laka chun ka hmai sukthim ka ta, a chipuihai intuithlartir ka tih.
11. Thilhring hringna chu a thisenah a um si a, maichama in ta dinga inremna siemna dinga ka pek cheu a nih; hringna ta dinga inremna siemna chu thisen a ni si a.
12. Chu lei chun a nih Israelhai kuomah, 'Tu khomin thisen in fak ding a ni nawh, nangni laia cheng ram dang mi khomin an fak ding a ni bok nawh' tia ka hril chu.
13. " 'Israel mi tu khom le nangni laia cheng le ram dang mi tu khomin fak dinga ram sa amanih vate a pel chun a thisen chu hnuoiah infar kangtir vong a ta, chu chu pilin a vur khum ding a nih,
14. thilhring tin hringna chu a thisen a ni si a. Chu lei chun annawm Israelhai kuomah, 'Thilhring taphot thisen in fak ding a ni nawh, thilhring hringna chu a thisen a ni si a; a fa taphot chu tuithlar ding a nih' ka ti chu.
15. " 'A rama pieng amanih ram dang mi tu khom, sa thi sa amanih le sahuoiin an pot chen neng tahnung sa a fak chun a puon sawpin insil a ta, zan tieng chen porche kai a ta, chu zoah hung inthieng a tih. A puon sawp loa a'n sil bok naw chun a maw chu phur a tih' tiin " a ta.
16. (A um nawh)
