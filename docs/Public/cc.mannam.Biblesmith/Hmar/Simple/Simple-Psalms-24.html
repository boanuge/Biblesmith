1. Leihnuoi le a sunga umhai,Khawvel le a sunga chenghai,A rengin Lalpa ta a nih.
2. Tuipui inthuk chungah remin,Tuipui tuongah chun a'n nghat a.
3. Lalpa tlangahr tu am lawn a,A hmun thienghlima ngir ding chu?
4. Kut fai le lungril thienghlim put,Mi lem bea kun ve ngai lo,Khela inkham ngai lo mi chu:
5. LALPA malsawmna dong a ta,A Sandamtu Pathien ngei chun,Thiem inchangtir ngei bok a tih.
6. Jakob Pathien, chuong mihai chu,I hmel dittu le zongtu che,Suon le parhai chu ning an tih.
7. Ropuina Lal lut theina din,Aw kotkharhai, inhong sangin,Kotkhar hlunhai, inhong lien ro.
8. Tu am a na, Lal ropui chu?LALPA ropui le hrat khawkheng,Hnetu LALPA ngei chu a nih.
9. Ropuina Lal lut theina din,Aw kotkharhai, inhong unla,Korkhar hlunhai, inhong sang ro.
10. Tu am a na, Lal ropui chu?Iengkim LALPAs ngei chu a na,Ama chu Lal ropui a nih.
