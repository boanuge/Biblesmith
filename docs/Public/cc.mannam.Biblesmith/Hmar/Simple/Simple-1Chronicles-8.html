1. Benjamin nau lutir chu Bela a na, a pahnina Asbel, a pathumna Ahara,
2. a palina Noha le a pangana Rafa a nih.
3. Bela naupahai chu Adar, Gera, Abihud,
4. Abisua, Naaman, Ahoa,
5. Gera, Sefufan le Huram an nih.
6. Ehud thlahai,
7. Gebaa umhai laia sungkuo tin lukhaituhai le Mahanat-a sala an thuoihai chu Naaman, Ahija, le Gera an na, Gera chu sala thuoitu a na, naupa Uza le Ahihud hai a hring a.
8. Saharaim chun Moab-a a um laiin a nuhmei Husim le Baara hai a mak a,
9. Hodes a nei a, ama le chun naupa Jobab, Zibia, Mesa, Malkam,
10. Jeuz, Sakia le Mirma, an rengin pasari a nei a.
11. Husim lakah Abitub le Elpaal a nei bok a.
12. Elpaal naupahai chu Eber, Misam, Semed an na, Semed chu Ono khuo le Lod khuo le an khawperhai indintu a nih.
13. Beria le Sema chu Aijalona an sungkuohai lukhaitu le Gath khuoa mihai hnot suoktu an nih.
14. Beria naupahai chu Ahio, Sasak, Jeremot,
15. Zebadia, Arad, Eder,
16. Mikael, Ispa le Joha an nih.
17. Elpaal naupahai chu Zebadia, Mesulam, Hizki, Heber,
18. Ismerai, Islia le Jobab an nih.
19. Simei naupahai chu Jakim, Jikri, Zabdi,
20. Elienai, Zilethai, Eliel,
21. Adaia, Beraia le Simrat an nih.
22. Sasak naupahai chu Ispan, Eber, Eliel,
23. Abdon, Zikri, Hanan,
24. Hanania, Elam, Anthothija,
25. Ifdeia le Penuel an nih.
26. Jeroham naupahai chu Samserai, Seharia, Athalia,
27. Jaaresia, Elija le Zikri an nih.
28. Hienghai hi an sungkuo chanchin inziek dana an lukhaituhai an na, Jerusalema um an nih.
29. Gibeon khuoa chun Gibeon pa Jeiel a um a, a nuhmei hming chu Maaka a na,
30. an naupa upa tak chu Abdon a na, a danghai chu Zura, Kisa, Baal, Nera, Nadab,
31. Gedor, Ahio, Zeker,
32. le Miklot an na, Miklot chu Simea pa a nih. Anni khom Jerusalema an sungkuohai laia um an nih.
33. Nera chu Kisa pa a na, ama chu Saula pa a na, ama chu Jonathan pa a na, ama chu Malki-Sua, Abinadab le Esh-Baal (Is-Boset) hai pa a nih.
34. Jonathan naupa chu Meri-Baal (Mefiboset) a na, ama chu Mika pa a nih.
35. Mika naupahai chu Pithon, Melek, Tarea le Ahaz an nih.
36. Ahaz naupa chu Jehoada a na, chu naupahai chu Alemet, Azmavet le Zimri an na, Zimri naupa chu Moza a na,
37. ama naupa chu Binea a na, ama naupa chu Rafa a na, ama naupa chu Eleasa a na, ama naupa chu Azel a nih.
38. Azel naupa parukhai chu Azrikam, Bokeru, Ismael, Searia, Obadia, le Hanan an nih. Chuonghai po po chu Azel naupahai an nih.
39. A unaupa Esek naupahai chu, a upa tak Ulam, a pahnina Jeush, a pathumna Elifelet a nih.
40. Ulam nauhai chu mi huoisen, thal kap thiem an nih. Anni chun tu le te tam tak, an rengin za le sawmnga, an nei.Chuonghai po chu Benjamin thlahai chu an nih.
