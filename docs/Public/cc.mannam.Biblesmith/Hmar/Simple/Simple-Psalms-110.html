1. LALPAN ka Lalpa kuomah,"Ka chang tieng hin hung thung rawh,I hmelmahai po po chuI ke sirsana ka siemHmakhat chun" tiin a hril.
2. LALPAN i lal hmol ropui,Zion a'nthok sukseiin,I hmelma zing khup lai chun,Nangin ro hung rel i tih.
3. I hmelmahai bei dinga,Tlang thienghlim i pan ni chun,Tlawmngaiin i tlangvalhai,Hung inpe tuk tuk an ta,Zing daidaw tla khawm angin,Thalai hung inkhawm an tih.j
4. "Melkisedek ang chi a,Chatuon Thiempu nang i nih"Ti'n LALPAN thu a tiem a,A thu chu sut ngai naw nih.
5. I chang tieng LALPA a um,A lunginsen nia chun,Lalhai chu nuoi hawp a tih.
6. Hnamhai chungthu rel a ta,Mi ruong chu deng bum a ta,Hnuoia lalhai po po chu,Hnein intlawm vong a tih.
7. Lam sira fiertui luong chu,Dawnin har thar nawk a tih.
