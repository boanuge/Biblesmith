1. Josefa thlahai chanpuol chu Jeriko zawn, Jordan vadunga inthoka thlaler hnai tieng, sak tieng Jeriko tuihmunah a suok a, Jerikoa inthok chun tlang ramah Bethel (chu chu Luz) chenin a kai tung a,
2. Bethela inthok chun Atarot-a Ark-hai ram a fe thleng a,
3. chu taka inthok chun Jaflet-hai ram tieng panin thlang tieng an her thla a, Bet Horon chuonhnuoi chen a fe a, chu zoah Gezer tieng a fe a, tuifinrietah a suok a.
4. Chu po chu Manase le Efraim, Josefa thlahai chanpuol chu a nih.
5. An sungkuo zir peia Efraim thlahai ramri chu hieng ang hi a nih: sak tienga an chanpuol ramri chu Astarot Adar-a inthokin Bet Horon chuonchungah,
6. tuifinriet chen a fe a. Chu taka inthok chun Mikmethat hmar tieng fein, Tanat Silo panin sak tieng an her a, Janoa chu a tan tlang a.
7. Janoat-a inthok chun Atarot le Naara-ah a fe thlak a, Jeriko a hraw a, Jordan vadungah a suok a.
8. Tapua-a inthokin ramri chu thlang tieng Kana vadung ruom a zui a, tuifinrietah a suok a. Chu chu an sungkuo zir ang peia Efraim thlahai chanpuol ram chu a nih.
9. Chun, Manase thlahai chanpuol sunga Efraim thlahai chanpuol dinga ruot hrang khawpuihai le an khawperhai khom chu a thang sa bok a nih.
10. Chun, Gezer-a um Kanan mihai chu an hnot suok vong naw leiin, tu chen hin Kanan mihai chu sinthawa sor lui dingin Efraim mihai lai an la um a nih.
