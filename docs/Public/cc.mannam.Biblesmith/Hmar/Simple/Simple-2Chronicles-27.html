1. Jotham a hung inlal chun kum sawmhni le kum nga mi a nih. Jerusalemah kum sawm le kum ruk lalin a um a; a nu chu Zadok naunu, a hming Jerusa a nih.
2. A pa Uzia-in a thaw ang bokin LALPA mithmuah thil tha a thaw hlak a, sienkhom a pa thaw angin LALPA Tempul sunga ruok chu a lut lui ve nawh. Mipuihai ruok chun thil tirdakum thaw an la ching zing a.
3. Chun, LALPA Tempula kotkhar chunghnung lem chu a siem tha a, Ofel muola kul banghai chu nasa takin a'n din thar nawk a.
4. Judai ram tlang pheia chun khuohai a'n din a, ramngaw hmuna hai chun kul le vengbuk insanghai a bawl bok a.
5. Jotham chun Amon lal a do a, a hne a. Chu kum chun Amonhaiin tangkaruo kilogram sangthum zali, huit metric ton sangkhat le barli metric ton sangkhat an hung pek a. Chuong zat bok chu a kum hnina le kum thumnaa khom an hung pek bok a.
6. LALPA a Pathien hmaa ringum taka kal a chawi leiin Jotham chu a hung hrat ta deu deu a.
7. Jotham inlal sunga thil tlung danghai, a ral do po po le a thilthaw danghai po po chu Israel le Juda lalhai chanchin inziekna buah a chuong.
8. Kum sawmhni le kum nga mi a niin a hung inlal a, Jerusalemah kum sawm le kum ruk a'n lal a.
9. Jotham chu a pi le puhai kuomah a chawl a, Davida khawpuia chun an phum a, a naupa Ahaz chun a lalna chu a hung hluo ta a.
