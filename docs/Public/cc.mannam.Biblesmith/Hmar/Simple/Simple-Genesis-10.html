1. Hienghai hi Nova naupa Semahai, Hama hai le Jafet hai, tui let hnunga an inthla sawng pei dan chanchin chu a nih.
2. Jafet nauhai chu Gomer hai, Magog hai, Madai hai, Javan hai, Tubal hai, Mesek hai le Tiras hai an nih.
3. Gomer nauhai chu Askenaz hai, Rifat hai le Togarma hai an nih.
4. Javan nauhai chu Elisa hai, Tarsis hai, Kitim hai le Rodanimc hai an nih.
5. Hienghai hih tuipuia fak zong mi, chi bing binga tong bing seng neia hmun hrang hranga khawsahai chu an nih.
6. Hama thlahai chu Kusa hai, Mizraim hai, Putad hai le Kanan hai an nih.
7. Kusa thlahai chu Seba hai, Havila hai, Sabta hai, Raama hai le Sapteka hai an nih. Raama thlahai chu Seba le Dedan an nih.
8. Kusa naupa chu Nimrod a na, ama chu khawvela ral hrat khawkheng a hung ni a.
9. LALPA mit tlung, ramvak hrat tak a ni leiin, "Nimrod anga ramvachalin LALPAN siem raw se che" an lo ti hlak a nih.
10. A'n lal tanna khawpuihai chu Sinar phaia um Babel khuo dam, Erek khuo dam, Akad khuo dam le Kalne khuo dam a nih.
11. Chu rama inthok chun a suok a, Asuria ramah a lut a, Ninevi khuo dam, Rehobot-Ir khuo dam, Kala khuo dam,
12. Ninevi le Kala khaw inkara um Resen khuo dam a'n din a, Resen chu khaw ropui tak a nih.
13. Chun, Mizraim thlahai chu Lud hai, Anam hai, Lehad hai, Naftu hai,
14. Pathru hai, Kaslu hai (Filistinhai hung suokna) le Kaftor hai an nih.
15. Kanan chun nau lutirah Sidon a nei a, chun Hit hai,
16. Jebu hai, Amor hai, Girgas hai
17. Hiv hai, Arki hai, Sin hai,
18. Arvad hai, Zemar hai le Hamat hai a thla bok a. Chu hnung chun Kanan hnamhai chu an indar zau pei a.
19. Kananhai ramri huop sung chu Zidon khuoa inthoka Gerar pan zawngin Gaza khuo chenin; Sodom le Gomora le Adma le Zeboiim pan zawngin Lasa khuo chen a nih.
20. Chuonghai chu Hama thlahai an na, anni chi le tong ang peia hnama insiemin ram an del a.
21. Eberhai thlatu Jafet u Sema khomin nau a nei teu a.
22. Sema nauhai chu Elam hai, Asur hai, Arpaksad hai, Lud hai le Aram hai an nih.
23. Aram naupahai chu Uz hai, Hul hai, Gether hai le Mesek (Masa) hai an nih.
24. Arpaksad chun naupa Sela a nei a, Sela chun Eber a thla a.
25. Eberin naupa pahnih a nei a. Pakhat chu a dam laiin khawvel mi an inthe leiin a hmingah Peleg an sak a, a unaupa hming chu Joktan a nih.
26. Joktan chu Almodad hai, Selep hai, Hazarmavet hai, Jera hai,
27. Hadoram hai, Uzal hai, Dikla hai,
28. Obal hai, Abimael hai, Seba hai,
29. Ofir hai, Havila hai le Jobab hai thlatu a na, chuonghai po po chu a nauhai an nih.
30. An chengna chu sak tieng tlangram, Mesaa inthoka Sefar chen a nih.
31. Chuonghai chu Sema thlahai an nih; mani chi chi, mani ram ramah a hnam hnamin an um a, mani tong seng an hmang.
32. Chuonghai chu an chihnam ang peia Nova thlahai chu an nih; hnam danghai khom tui let hnunga an laka pienga hung indar an nih.
