1. LALPA thu chu Johan kuomah a hung tlung nawk a:
2. "Tho la, khaw ropui tak Nenevi-a chun fe la, thu ka hril che hi tlang insampui rawh" a ta.
3. Chuongchun Jona chu a tho a, LALPA thu ang chun Ninevi khuoa chun a fe a. Ninevi chu khaw poimaw tak el a na, a lienzie chu kea ni thum lam a nih.
4. A ni khatnaa chun Jona chun khawpuiah, "Ni sawmli hnungah Ninevi hi sukbohmang ning a tih" tiin tlang a'n sam a.
5. Ninevi mihai chun Pathien an ring a, bu nghei an puong a, a lien taka inthoka a chin tak chenin saiip an sil a.
6. Ninevi khaw lalin chu chu a lo hriet chun a lalthungphaa inthokin a tho a, a lal thuom a hlip a, saiip a sil a, pilvut lai a'n thung a.
7. Ninevi khaw lal le a mi lienhai chun hieng ang hin thupek an insuo a: "Lal le a khawnbawlhai thupek angin, mihriem amanih, ran amanih, beram ruol le bawng ruol amanih tu khomin ieng khom fain an dawn ding a ni nawh.
8. Mihriem le ransahai khomin saiip sil hai sien, thahnemngai takin Pathien ko raw se. Mi tinin mani lampui tha naw le tharum hmanga thisen insuona chu bansan raw se.
9. Ei bohmang nawna dingin Pathien chu inlamletin, a zangaina nasa tak lei chun a lungsenna nasa taka inthok chun in kei kir naw nih ti tuin am a hriet?" tiin.
10. Pathienin an um dan chu a hmu a, an suol an sim ti a hriet chun anni chu a lunginsiet a, an chunga sietna intlun dinga a hril chu a thul ta a.
