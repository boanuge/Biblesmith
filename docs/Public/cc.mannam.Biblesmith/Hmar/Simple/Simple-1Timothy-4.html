1. Hun nuhnunga hai chun mihaiin ringna thu hi bansanin, hlemtu thlarau le ramhuoia inthoka suok zirtirna indik naw chu la zui an tih ti chieng takin Thlarauin a hril a.
2. Chuong ang zirtirna thu the dartuhai chu khela mi lemder thiem an ni a, an sie le tha hrietna khom thir linga dep angin a kang char tah.
3. Mi innei ding dam an khap a; mihriem fak dinga Pathien siem thil thenkhat, thutak hrea pomtuhaiin lawmthu hril puma fak el ding a nih ti an hriet chu, fak an khap bok a.
4. Pathien thilsiem taphot hi a tha vong a, ieng khom hnawl ding a ni nawh.
5. Pathien thu le tongtainaa inhlan thienghlim ta chu lawmthu hril puma dong el ding a nih.
6. Hieng thuhai hi unauhai kuomah hril la, Krista Isu rongbawltu tha ning i tih; ringna thu le inzirtirna tha i zawm hlakhai khan thlarau tieng inthang lientir pei bok a ti che.
7. Pathien thu le inzom lo thusim le pitarhai tienami mei mei chu nghatsan la, Pathien mi ni dingin insawizoi rawh.
8. Taksa sawizoi hi iemani chenah a tangkai ngei a, sienkhom Pathien ngaisak mi ni hi iengkimah a hlu lem a, hi dam sung nun le la hung um dinga thil tangkai a nih.
9. Hi thu hi a'n dik a, pom tlak a nih.
10. Pathien hring chu ei beisei innghatna a ni tak leiin sin ei thaw inrimin hma ei nor nasa a nih. Ama chu mi po po ta dinga sandamtu a na, a ringtuhai ta dingin a ni nawkzuol.
11. Hieng thuhai hi pein inchuktir pei rawh.
12. I naupang leiin tu khomin ngainep naw raw hai se che. Thuhril konga dam, chetziea dam, hmangainaa dam, ringnaa dam, thienghlimnaa dam ringtuhai enton tlak ni ngei rawh.
13. Ka hung hma po chu vantlang laia Pathien thu tiem tienga dam, thuhril tienga dam le zirtir tienga dam inhmang rawh.
14. Upahaiin i chungah kut an innghat tuma zawlneihaiin an puong, thlarau tienga i thilpek donghai kha ngaitha naw rawh.
15. Chuong tienga chun taima takin inhmang la, i pumin inpe la, mi tinin i hmasawnzie hmu thei an tih.
16. I khawsak le i thil inchuktirah inveng fimkhur la, chuonga i thaw fan fan chun nang le i thu hretuhai chu san himin um in tih.
