1. Chuong lai chun Jeroboam naupa Abhija chu a hung damnaw ta a.
2. Jeroboam chun a nuhmei kuomah, "Inthok la, Jeroboam nuhmei i nih ti an hriet thei nawna dingin inchei danglam la, Silo-ah fe rawh. Chu taka chun zawlnei Ahija a um a, ama chu hi mihai chunga lala ka la hung um ding thu mi hriltu kha a nih.
3. Bei tluon sawm le kek iemani zat le khuoizu bur khat chawi la, a kuomah va fe la, naupang um dan ding chu lo hril a ti che" a ta.
4. A hril angin Jeroboam nuhmei chun a thaw a, Silo-a Ahija inah a fe a. A upat leiin Ahija mit chu a'n thim ta a, khuo a hmu thei ta nawh.
5. Amiruokchu, LALPAN Ahija kuomah, "Jeroboam nuhmei chu a naupa damnaw chungthu indon ding chein a hung mek a, chu chu kha kha tiin i lo hril ding a nih. A hung pha chun mi dang ni awm takin hung insiem a tih" a ta.
6. Chun, Ahija chun kotkhara a hung pen lut lai ri a hriet charin, "Nang Jeroboam nuhmei, hung lut raw khai. Ieng dinga mi dang anga i'n siem am a na? Chanchin tha naw tak hril ding chea tir ka nih.
7. Kir nawk ta la, Jeroboam kuoma chun "LALPA, Israelhai Pathien chun hieng hin a tih: 'Mipuihai laia inthokin ka chawisang che a, ka mi Israelhai chungah hotuah ka siem che a,
8. Davida sungkuohai ram ka lakpek a, chu chu ka pek che a, sienkhom nang ruok chu ka siehlaw Davida angin i um ve nawh; ama ruok chun ka thupekhai a zawm a, ka mithmuah thil tha chauh thaw a tum a, a lungril po poin a mi zui hlak.
9. Nang ruok chun i hmaa mihai po po nekin thil suol i thaw nasa lem a, lungsen dinga mi cho phawkin, hnam dang pathienhai lem thirin i siem a, hnung tieng i mi nghatsan a.
10. Chuleiin, Jeroboam sungkuoah sietna hung intlungtir ka ta. Jeroboama inthoka nau pasal pieng taphot chu, bawia um amanih zalena um amani khom, sukram vong ka ta, miin ek tawng a raw fai vong hma khata a raw angin Jeroboama sungkuo chu raw fai vong ka tih.
11. Jeroboam sungkuoa mi, khawpuia thi taphot chu uiin fang an ta, ram hnuoia thi taphot chu chungleng vatehaiin tlan an tih. Chu chu LALPA thu a nih' tiin hril rawh.
12. "Chuongchun, tho la, i in tieng kir nawk el ta rawh. Khawpui sung i kein i sir charin naupang chu thing a tih.
13. Ama chu Israelhai po poin sun an ta, vui an tih, Jeroboam sungkuo lai ama chauh chu thlana phum hlaw ding um sun chu a nih. Jeroboam sungkuo lai hin ama chunga chauh hin thatna iemani LALPA Israelhai Pathien chun a hmu a nih.
14. "Voisun la la hin Jeroboam sungkuo sukbohmangtu ding LALPAN ama ta dingin Israelhai lal ding hung indin suok a tih. Voisun ngei hin tie! Tak tak maw? Ni e, tu ngei el hin tie!
15. LALPAN Israel chu vuong a ta, va kama phairuong ang elin inhning an tih. LALPA chu suklungsen a, pathiennu Aser lem thinghai siema an phun leiin, an puhai a lo pek tah, hi ram tha taka inthok hin Israelhai chu pot suok fai vong a ta, Eufretis Vadungpui rala chen la sukdar vong a tih.
16. Jeroboamin thil suol a thawhai lei le Israelhai khom thil suol a'n thawtir leiin, Israelhai chu nghatsan a tih" a ta.
17. Chuongchun, Jeroboam nuhmei chu a tho a, a suok a, Tirza khuo tieng a pan a, an sumphuk a tlung charin naupang chu a thi a.
18. LALPAN a siehlaw zawlnei Ahija hmanga a hril tah angin an phum a, Israelhai po poin an sun a.
19. Jeroboam inlal laia thil tlung dang hai, a ral bei thu dam le a rorel danhai chu Israel lalhai chanchin inziekna lekhabuah a chuong.
20. Kum sawmhni le kum hni sung ro a rel hnungin a thlatuhai kuomah a chawl a, a naupa Nadab chun a lalna chu a hung hluo a.
21. Solomon naupa Rehoboam chu Judai ramah lalin a um a, a hung inlal chun kum sawmli le kum khat mi a na, LALPAN a hming umna dinga Israel hnamhai khuo po po laia a thlang khawpui Jerusalema chun kum sawm le pasari a'n lal a, a nu hming chu Naami a na, Amon mi a nih.
22. Judahai chun LALPA mithmuah thil suol an thaw a, an thlatuhai po po khuma an suol lei chun a thikthu an chok tho a.
23. Hmun sang siemin, muol bawk insangna deu taphot le thingbuk inzar thatna taphotah lung ser le pathiennu Asera lem thinghai chu an phun a.
24. A ram sunga chun pasal inzorna buk dam siemin, Israel thlahai hmaa LALPAN a hnot suok tah hnamhai ang bokin thil tirdakum tinreng an thaw ve a.
25. Lal Rehoboam inlala inthoka kum nganaah Aigupta lal Sisak chun Jerusalem a hung run a.
26. LALPA tempula rosum le lal ina rosumhai chu a lak fai vong a, Solomon siem rangkachak phawhai po po khom a lak sa bok a.
27. Lal Rehoboam chun a aiin dar phaw a siem a, lal in kot vengtu kawmandarhai chu a'n koltir a.
28. LALPA tempulah lal a fe pha leh vengtuhai chun dar phaw chu an chawi a, an hung kir pha leh vengtuhai pindana a siena hmuna chun an thak kir nawk hlak a.
29. Rehoboam inlal sunga thil tlung danghai le a thilthaw po po chu Juda lalhai inlal lai chanchin ziekna lekhabuah a chuong.
30. Rehoboam le Jeroboam chu indo zing zinga khawsa an nih.
31. Chun, Rehoboam chu a thlatuhai kuoma chun a chawl a, Davida khawpuiah a thlatuhai lai an phum a. A nu hming chu Naama a na, Amon mi a nih. A naupa Abija chun a lalna chu a hung hluo a.
