1. Efraim chun thli, bu-ah a ring a,Sunnithlak khawsak thli a hnot a,iKhel le thisen suokna a sukpung.Asuria leh thuthlung a thlung a,Aigupta kuomah hriek a thon a.
2. Juda chunga khom LALPAN,Lungni nawna a nei a,Jakob chu a um dan anga hremin,A thilthaw dungzuiin rul a tih .
3. Sula an um lai khomin,A unau kekhongah a man a;A puitling hnungin Pathien a buon a.
4. Vantirko leh an inbei a,Ama chun a hneban a,Tapin ditsakna a hni a,Ama chu Bethelah a hmu a,Chu taka chun a'n biekpui a.
5. A hming chu LALPA a nih,Iengkim LALPA Pathien chu.
6. Chuleiin, i Pathien kuomah kir nawk la,Hmangaina le indikna vongin,I Pathien chu nghak zing hlak rawh.
7. Kanan mihai ang bokin,Israel mi sumdawnghai chun,Bukna indik lo an hmang a,Mi hlema fakpek an lawm.
8. Efraim chun chapo takin,"Ka hausa a, ka neinung a,Ka hausakna po po lei hin,Tu khomin thil suol thawah,Mi'n tum ngam naw nih" a tih.
9. " Kei hi LALPA in Pathien,Aigupta rama inthokaThuoi suoktu cheu chu ka nih.Thlalera kut in hmang lai ang khan,Puon inah inchengtir nawk ka ti cheu.
10. Zawlneihai ka biek hlak a,Inlarna voi tam ka'n hmutir a,Tekhinthu hmangin ka hril hlak."
11. Gilead chu a rilo hle maw?A mihai hmangna bo an nih!Gilgalah bawngchal inhlanin,Milem hmaah an inthawi maw? Leilet sira lung deng bum angin,An maichamhai chu siem ka tih.
12. Jakob chu Aramah a tlan hmang a,Israel chun a nuhmei ding hlawin,Sin a thaw a, beram a veng a.
13. Aigupta rama inthoka,Israelhai thuoi suok dingin,LALPAN zawlnei a hmang a,A zawlnei hmangin a enkol a.
14. Nisienlakhom Efraim chun,Nasa takin a suklungsen a nih.A thisen insuona maw chu,LALPAN a chungah intlaktir a ta,A sukmuolphona po po leiin,LALPAN sukmuolpho veng a tih.
