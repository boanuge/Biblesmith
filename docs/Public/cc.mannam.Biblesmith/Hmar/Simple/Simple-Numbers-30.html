1. Mosie chun Israel pahnam tin thuoituhai kuomah, "Hi hi LALPA thupek chu a nih:
2. Miin LALPA kuomah thu intiemkamna a nei chun a thu sut loin a tiem ta po po chu a hlen ngei ding a nih.
3. "A pa ina la khawsa nunghakin LALPA kuomah thu intiemkamna a nei a,
4. a pain a'n tiemkamna chu a hriet a, ieng khom a hril si naw chun, a thu intiemkamna chu a hlen ngei ding a nih.
5. A pain a hriet hnunga a phal naw a ni ruok chun a thu intiemkamnaa inthok chun fihlim a ta, a pain a phal naw leiin LALPAN ngaidam a tih.
6. "Thu intiemkamna a nei zing lai ngaituo chik loa pasal a nei a,
7. a pasalin a hriet nia ieng khom a hril naw chun, a thu intiemkamna chu a hlen ngei ding a nih.
8. Amiruokchu, a pasalin a hriet nia a phal naw a ni chun dawn sei loa thu a lo intiemkamnaa inthok chun fihlim a ta, LALPAN ngaidam a tih.
9. Hmeithai amanih, mak tuok amanih hai chun an thu intiemkamna chu an hlen ngei ding a nih.
10. A pasal le in khata um nuhmeiin thu tiem amanih intiemkamna amanih a nei a,
11. a pasalin a hriet hnunga ieng khom a hril si naw chun, a thu intiemkamna chu a hlen ngei ding a nih.
12. Amiruokchu, a pasalin a hriet nia a thiek chun a thu intiemkamna chu sut ni tang a tih, a pasalin a sutpek leiin LALPAN ngaidam a tih.
13. A thu intiemkamna hrim hrim chu a pasalin a remtipui thei a, a sutpek thei bok.
14. Amiruokchu, a pasalin a hriet a, ieng khom a hril naw chun a thu intiemkamna ang chun a thaw ngei ding a nih; a hriet nia inthoka a tosan leiin a namnghetpui tina a nih.
15. A pasalin a hriet hnunga a sutpek a ni ruok chun a pasal chun a maw chu phur a tih," a ta.
16. Chuonghai chu nuhmei pasal nei lo, pahai ina la um amanih, pasal nei laiin amanih intiemkamna an nei chungthua thaw dan ding LALPAN Mosie a pekhai chu an nih.
