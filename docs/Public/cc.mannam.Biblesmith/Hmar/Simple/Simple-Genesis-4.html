1. Adam chun a nuhmei Evi chu a pawl a, nau a pai a, "LALPA zarin nau pasal ka nei tah" a ta, a hmingah Kaina a sak a.
2. A hnungah Abel a hung nei nawk a. Abel chu beram vengtu a na, Kaina chu lo neitu a nih.
3. Iemani chen hnungah Kaina chun LALPA kuoma inhlan dingin a lo tharsuok thenkhat a hung chawi a.
4. Abel khomin a beram ruol laia pieng hmasa tak le thau tak chu a hung chawi bok a. LALPA chun Abel le a thil inhlan chu a lawm a,
5. Kaina le a thil inhlan ruok chu a lawm nawh. Chuongchun, Kaina lung chu a sen hle a, a hmel a'n tur ngul el a.
6. LALPA chun Kaina kuomah, "Ieng dinga i lung a sen am a na? Ieng dinga i hmel a'n tur am?
7. Thil indik i thaw chun, lawm i hlaw naw ding am a ni? Thil indik lo i thaw ruok chun suolin i kot bulah a'n chan zing che a nih; a dit tak chu nangma hi i ni a, sienkhom i thuhne ding a nih," a ta.
8. Chun, Kaina chun a sang Abel kuomah, "Loah feng ei tih" a ta. Loa an um lai chun Kaina chu a sang chungah a tuong a, a that el ta a.
9. LALPA chun Kaina kuomah, "Khaw la'm i sang Abel a um tah a?" a ta. Ama chun, "Ka hriet le! Ka unaupa vengtu am ka nih?" a ta.
10. LALPA chun, "Ieng thil top am i thaw an ta le? Ngai ta, i sang thisen chun hnuoia inthokin a mi hung ko a nih.
11. Tu hin i kuta inthoka far thla i sang thisen lo dongtu leihnuoia inthokin tongsephur i ni tah.
12. Hnuoi inlet la khom, i ta dingin a hangna insuopek ta naw ni che. Hnuoia hin chanhai takin vakvai tang i tih" a ta.
13. Kaina chun LALPA kuomah, "I mi hremna hi a na em a, tuor zo naw ning.
14. Ngai ta, voisun hin hi rama inthok hin nangin i mi hnot suok a, i hmu phak loah umin, hnuoia hin chanhai takin ka'n vakvai ta ding a na, chuleiin a mi hmutu chun mi that el an tih" a ta.
15. LALPA chun a kuomah, "That lo e! Kaina thattu taphot chu a let saria thungrul ning a tih" a ta. Tu khomin an hmua an that nawna dingin Kainaa chun LALPAN inchikna a siepek a.
16. Chuongchun, Kaina chu Pathien angsunga inthok chun a suok a, Eden suo tieng Nod ramah umhmun a khuor ta a.
17. Kaina chun a nuhmei a pawl a, nau a pai a, Enok a nei a. Chuong lai chun Kaina'n khuo a'n din a, a naupa hming chawiin Enok a'n buk a.
18. Enok chun Irad a thla a; Irad chun Mehujael, Mehujael chun Methusael, Methusael chun Lamek.
19. Lamek chun nuhmei pahni a nei a, pakhat hming chu Ada a na, a dang hming chu Zila a nih.
20. Ada chun Jabal a hring a, Jabal thlahai chu puon inah khawsaa ran vai hlak mi an nih.
21. Jabal unaupa hming chu Jubal a na, ama chu tingtang le theihle tumtu pawlhai thlatu a nih.
22. Zila chun nau pasal dang, a hming Tubal-Kaina a nei a, ama chu dar le thir hmangruo tinreng suttuhai thlatu a nih. Tubal-Kaina farnu chu Naama a nih.
23. Lamek chun a nuhmeihai kuomah, "Ada le Zila, mi lo ngaithla ro. Lamek nuhmeihai, ka thu lo ngaithla ro.Ami'n hliemtu tlangval chu ka that,Mi'n hliemtu tlangval ngei chu;
24. Kaina thattu a let saria rul a ni chun,Lamek thattu chu voi sawmsari pasarih" a ta.
25. Adam chun a nuhmei a pawl nawk a, nau pasal a hring a, "Kainan Abel a that hnungin, a aia chi thlatu ding Pathienin a mi pek a nih" a ta, a hmingah Set a sak a.
26. Set khomin nau pasal a nei a, a hmingah Enos a sak a. Chuong lai chun mihaiin LALPA hming an hung lam tan ta a.
