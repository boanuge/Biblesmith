1. Maichama ngir lai LALPA chun,Ka kuomah hieng hin a hril:"Tempul sutpui ler vaw rak la,Tuong nghor hning dur dur sien la,An lu chungah chim thla sien la,A del hlum mahla pohai chu,Keiman kawlhnamin that ka tih;Pakhat khom suok naw ni hai a,Pakhat khomin pumpel naw nih.
2. Mithi khuo chen kuo hreu sien khom,Ka banin pot suok tho ka tih;Van chen hang lawn hai sien khom,Hnuk thla nawk vong tawl ka tih.
3. Karmel sipah inbihai sien khom,Pel suokin man tawl ka tih;Tuipui mongah inbihai sien khom,Rulpui thu pein inchuktir ka tih.
4. Hmelman salah thuoihai sien khom,Sat hlum dingin thu peng ka tih.Anni chu sukhlum dingin,Ieng lai khom melkai ka tih.
5. Lalpa, Iengkim LALPA chun Hnuoi a them a, a'n tui zop a,A sunga chenghan an sun a. Aigupta ram chunga Nile vadungpui,A liena a kiem nawk duok angin,Ram pum chu insangin a tlum.
6. Van sangah chengna in a bawl a,Hnuoi hi a in thutah a hmang a,Tuifinrieta tuihai chu ko a,Hnuoi chungah a'n surtir hlak.A hming chu LALPA a nih.
7. LALPA chun Israelhai kuomah,"Nangni hi Ethiopia mihai leh,Ka ta din angkhat in ni naw maw?Aigupta rama inthokin Israelhai,Kaftor-a inthokin Filistin mihai,Kir-a inthokin Aram mihai,Ka thuoi suok tawl an naw maw?
8. "Lalpa PATHIEN LALPA mit chu,Ram suol chungah a fu a,Hnuoi a'nthokin sukbo ka ta,Jakob sungkuohai ruok chu,An rengin inram naw ning"Ti hi LALPA thu suok a nih.
9. "Keima ngeiin thu peng ka ta,Leidara bu an sep ang elin,Hnam danghai laia inthokin,Israelhai chu sep suok ka ta,A mal hlek khom tla dar naw nih.
10. Ka mihai laia mi suol po le,'Sietnan mi nang naw ni a,Mi nang teu naw nih' tituhai chu,Kawlhnamin bohmang an tih.
11. "Davida bawkte chim chu,Chu ni chun tungding ka ta,A'n awngnahai thol phui ka ta,A chimhai rem thang ka ta,A ngaiin indin nawk ka tih.
12. Edom rama hlaa la umhai le,Ka hming puttu hnamhai chu,Anni chun nei nawk an tih" tiinA thaw theitu LALPAN a hril.
13. LALPA chun hieng hin a tih:"Busiktuhai le lokeituhai, Grep chingtuhai le sawrtuhaiAn inphahman zat hun ding le,Tlanga inthokin uoiin thar,A hung far thla huou huou a,Muol tina a luong hun ding,Nihai chu a tlung mek ie.
14. Saltang ka mi Israelhai chu,Hung thuoi kir nawk tang ka ta;Khaw ram hnung indin tharin,Umhmun khuor thar nawk an tih.Grep huon siema, uoiin dawnin,Huon siemin a ra tlan an tih.
15. Phawi dok nawk ta lo dingin,Anni ka pek ngei rama chun,Israelhai chu phun ka tih"Ti hi LALPA Pathien thu suok a nih.
