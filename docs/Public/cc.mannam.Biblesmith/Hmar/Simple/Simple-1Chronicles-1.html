1. Adamin Seth a thla a, Seth-inEnos, Enos-in Kenan,
2. Kenanin Mahalalel, Mahalalelin Jared,
3. Jared-in Enok, Enokin Methusela, Methusela-in Lamek, Lamekin Nova a thla a,
4. Nova naupahai chu Sema, Hama le Jafet an nih.
5. Jafet naupahai chu Gomer, Magog, Madai, Javan, Tubal, Mesek le Tiras hai an nih.
6. Gomer naupahai chu Askenaz, Difat (Rifat) le Togarma an nih.
7. Javan naupahai chu Elisa, Tarsis, Kitim le Rodanim an nih.
8. Hama naupahai chu Kusa, Mizraim, Puta le Kanan an nih.
9. Kusa naupahai chu Seba, Havila, Sabta, Raama le Sabteka an nih. Raama naupahai chu Seba le Dedan an nih.
10. Kusa chu khawvela indoa hrat khawkheng Nimrod thlatu a nih.
11. Mizraim naupahai chu Luda, Anam, Lehab, Naftu,
12. Pathrus, Filistinhai thlatu Kaslu le Kaftor an nih.
13. Kanan naupahai chu, a nau lutir Sidon le Hit,
14. Jebu, Amor, Gisgas,
15. Hiv, Arka, Sina,
16. Arvad, Zemar le Hamat hai an nih.
17. Sema naupahai chu Elam, Asur, Arpaksad, Luda le Aram an nih. Aram naupahai chu Uza, Hula, Gether le Mesek an nih.
18. Arpaksad chun Sela a hring a, Sela chun Eber a hring a.
19. Eber naupa pahnihai laia pakhat chu a dam laia mihriemhai an in the dar leiin a hmingah Peleg an sak a, a sang hming chu Joktan a nih.
20. Joktan naupahai chu Almodad, Selep, Hazarmavet, Jera,
21. Hadoram, Uzal, Dikla,
22. Obal, Abimael, Seba,
23. Ofir, Havila le Jobab an nih. Chuonghai po chu Joktan naupahai vong an nih.
24. Semaa inthoka Abram (Abraham) chena an inthla sawng dan chu: Sema, Arpaksad, Sela,
25. Eber, Peleg, Reu,
26. Serug, Nahor, Tera le
27. Abram (Abraham) an nih.
28. Abraham naupahai chu Isak le Ismael an nih.
29. Ismael naupahai, hnam sawmpahni thlatuhai chu a nau lutir Nabaiot le Kedar, Abdeel, Mibsam,
30. Mishma, Duma, Masa, Hadah, Tema,
31. Jetur, Nafis le Kedema an nih.
32. Abraham hmei Ketura naupahai chu Zimran, Joksan, Medan, Midian, Isbak le Sua an nih. Joksan nau pasalhai chu Seba le Dedan an nih.
33. Midian naupahai chu Efa, Efer, Hanok, Abida le Elda a nih. Chuonghai po chu Ketura thlahai chu an nih.
34. Abraham chu Isak pa a na, Isak naupahai chu Esau le Jakob an nih.
35. Esau naupahai chu Elifaz, Reuel, Jeush, Jalam le Kora an nih.
36. Elifaz naupahai chu Teman, Omar, Zefi, Gatam le Kenaz an na; a nuhmei Timnaa inthokin Amalek a nih.
37. Reuel naupahai chu Nahat, Zera, Sama le Miza an nih.
38. Seir naupahai chu Lotan, Sobal, Zibeon, Ana, Dison, Ezer le Disan an nih.
39. Lotan naupahai chu Hori le Homam an nih. Timna chu Lotan farnu a nih.
40. Sobal naupahai chu Alvan, Manahat, Ebal, Sefi le Onam an nih. Zibeon naupahai chu Aia le Ana an nih.
41. Ana naupa chu Dison a nih. Dison naupahai chu Hemdan, Esban, Ithran le Keran an nih.
42. Ezer naupahai chu Bilhan, Zavan le Akan an nih. Disan naupahai chu Uza le Aran an nih.
43. Israela thlahai an inlal hmaa Edom rama lalhai chu hienghai hi an nih: Beor naupa Bela a na, a khawpui hming chu Dinhaba a nih.
44. Bela a thi chun Bozraa inthoka hung Zera naupa Jobab chun a lalna chu a hung hluo a.
45. Jobab a thi chun Temanhai rama inthoka hung Husam chun a lalna chu a hung hluo a.
46. Husam a thi chun Bedad naupa Hadad, Moab rama Midianhai hnetu chun a hung thlak a, a khawpui hming chu Avit a nih.
47. Hadad a thi chun Masreka mi Samla chun a hung thlak a.
48. Samla a thi chun tuipui kama Rehobot khaw mi Saul chun a hung thlak a.
49. Saul a thi chun Akbor naupa Baal-Hanan chun a lalna chu a hung hluo a.
50. Baal-Hanan a thi chun Hadad-in a hung thlak a, a khawpui hming chu Pau (Pai) a na, a nuhmei hming chu Me-Zahab naunu Matred naunu Mehetabel a nih.
51. Chun, Hadad khom chu a thi a. Edom rama hnam lukhaituhai chu Timna, Alva, Jethet,
52. Oholibama, Ela, Pinon,
53. Kenaz, Teman, Mibzar,
54. Magdiel le Iram hai an nih.
