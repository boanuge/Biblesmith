1. Israelhan Aigupta ram suoksana,Jakob suonhan mi ram an suoksan khan,
2. Judai ram chu Pathien hmun thienghlim leIsrael ram chu a lalram a hung nih.
3. Tuipuiin a hmu chun a tlansie a,Jordan vadung khom chu a luong kir a;
4. Tlanghai beramchal ang an inchawm a,Muolhai beramte angin tuol an chai.
5. Aw tuipui, ieng leia i tlan sie am?Aw Jordan, ieng leia i luong kir am?
6. Tlanghai, ieng da beram ang inchawma,Muolhai, beramte ang in tuolchai am?
7. Aw leihnuoi, LALPA hmaah inthin la,Jakob Pathien hmaa chun inthin rawh.
8. Suonglungpui chu dilah a'n changtir a,Lungthir khom tuihnarah a'n changtir bok.
