1. Saltang hung kirhaiin LALPA Israel Pathien ta ding Tempul an bawl tan nawk tah ti Judahai le Benjaminhai hmelmahaiin an lo hriet a.
2. Chuongchun, Zerubabel le an sungkuo lukhaituhai kuomah an fe a, an kuomah, "Keini khomin bawlpui veng kan ti cheu, in Pathien biek hi keini khomin kan biek ve a, hi taka mi thuoitu Asuria lal Esar-hadon inlal laia inthok ta khan keini khom a kuomah kan inthawi hlak a nih" an ta.
3. Nisienlakhom, Zerubabel le Jesua le Israel sungkuo lukhaituhai chun an kuomah, "LALPA kan Pathien ta ding Tempul kan bawlnaa hin in hung thang ve ngai naw nih. Persia lal Kura thupek angin LALPA, Israel Pathien ta dinga Tempul chu keini ngeiin kan bawlpek ding a nih" an ta.
4. Chu phing leh an kawl le kienga mihai chun Juda mihai chu suklunghnuol tumin an thang a, Tempul chu bawl lo dingin an inthi a.
5. Persia lal Kura inlal sung po po le Daria inlal hun chenin anni kal zawngin an thang ru a, anni hektu dingin hrilpuitu dam a hranin an ruoi a, an thiltumhai chu sukhlamzui an tum tlat a.
6. Chu hnung chun, Lal Artazerzia inlal tan tir laiin Judai ram le Jerusalema umhai chu an hmelmahai chun lal kuomah lekhathonin an hek a.
7. Chu hnung khom chun Persia lal Artazerzia kuomah Bislam hai, Mithredat hai, Tabeel hai le an inkoppuihaiin Judahai an hekna lekha, Aramik tonga inziek chu an thon a, chu chu Persia tongin lal chu an inletpek a.
8. Chun, ram optu Rehum le lekha ziektu Simsai khomin Jerusalem mihai ngirhmun chu Persia lal Artazerzia kuomah ripawt an thon bok a:
9. A thontuhai chu: Ram optu Rehum le a lekhaziektu Simsai le an inkoppui Erek le Babulon le Elam ram Susaa mi roreltuhai le thoktuhai dam,
10. mi ropui Asurbanipal (Osnappar)-in sala inthoka a hung insuo, Samaria khawpui le Eufretis vadung thlang tieng rama a'n chengtirhai dam chu an nih.
11. Lal Artazerzia kuomah hieng ang hin lekha an thon a: "Lal tak Artazerzia, i siehlaw Eufretis vadung thlang tienga mihaiin chibai kan buk che.
12. I kuoma inthoka Juda mi hung tunghai kha Jerusalemah an hung a, hi khawpui suol tak le hel hmang hi indin thain, a lungphumhai an phum tha ti lalin lo hre raw se.
13. Chu bakah, hi khuo hi indin tha a, a kulhai siem zo a ni pha chun sie le leiman le an thil nei man hrim hrim chawi nuom ta naw ni hai a, lalin sum a chan pha ding a nih ti lo hre bok raw se.
14. Lal chunga mawphurna latu kan ni leiin sukhmelhema lal a um hmu hi keini ta dingin a fel naw leiin hi thu hi lal kuomah kan hung intlun a nih.
15. Chuleiin, hi khuo hi hel hmang khuo a nizie dam, a ram le lal ta dinga buoina siemtu a ni hlakzie dam le tiena tlanga inthoka hel zing zing a nizie dam lalin a hriet theina dingin thil hlui siekhawmnaa a pi le puhai chanchin inziekna buhai chu indaptir raw se. Chuong thil leia hi khawpui hi suksiet a lo nih.
16. Hi khawpui hi indin that a ni a, a kulhai zo fel a ni pha chun Eufretis vadung thlang tieng hin lalin chan nei naw phang i tih ti lal kan hung inhriettir a nih" tiin.
17. Lal chun donna a hung thon a: "Ram optu Rehum, lekha ziektu Simsai le an inthloppui, Samaria ram le Eufretis vadung thlang tienga umhai po po chibai ka buk cheu.
18. In lekha hung thon kha ka hmaah fie takin an inlet a
19. Chuongchun, thu ka pek a, an zong a, hi khuoa mihai hi tiena tlanga inthokin lal chungah an hel hlakzie dam, chu taka chun buoina le helna a tlung hlakzie dam kan hmu suok ta a.
20. Jerusalema khan lal thilthawthei tak, Eufretis vadung thlang tieng po poa roreltu dam an lo um ta hlak a, an kuomah sie chawiin, thilpek hrang hrang an lo pek ta hlak a.
21. Khang mihai khan thu ka pek nawk hma khat chu khawpui siem that sin kha chawlsan phot dingin thupek lo insuo ro.
22. Hi thil hi ngaitha hrim hrim naw ro. Lal ta dinga sietna ding thil hi ieng leiin am a zuolkai pei ei phal ding a na?" tiin.
23. Rehum le lekha ziektu Simsai le an inkoppuihaiin lal Artazerzia lekhathon an tiem zo chun inhmaw takin Jerusalema Judahai kuomah an fe nghal a, thawluinain an thilthaw lai chu an inchawltir a.
24. Chuongchun, Jerusalema Pathien in indin thar sin chu Persia lal Daria hung inlal kum hnina chenin an chawlsan ta hri a.
