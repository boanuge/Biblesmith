2Thessalonians (1/3)  →
1. Paula, Silvan le Timothe hai chun Thesalonika khuoa Pa Pathien le Lalpa Isu Krista kohranhai chibai kan buk.
2. Pa Pathien le ei Lalpa Isu Kristaa inthoka lunginsietna le inremna chu in kuomah um raw se.
3. Unauhai, ringna tieng le inhmangai tuona tienga in inthanglien pei leiin Pathien kuomah lawmthu kan hril hlak a, chu chu kan thaw ding tak hrim a nih.
4. Suknomnatnahai le fienahai sel tak le ringna nghet taka in tuorhai hi Pathien kohranhai hmaah kan uongpui hle hlak.
5. Pathien ram leia tuor in ni a, chu taka lut tlak ni dinga Pathien thil rel hang indikzie sukchiengna chu in nih.
6. Pathien chu a'n dik a, nangni sukrinumtu cheu chu rinumnain thungrul veng a tih.
7. Lalpa Isu chu vana inthoka ama vantirko thilthawthei takhai leh meichoka a hung inlang huna chun, nangni rinum tuorhai hi hung inchawltir a ti cheu a, keini khom mi'n chawltir bok a tih.
8. Pathien hre lo le ei Lalpa Isu Chanchin Tha ring nuom nawhai chungah phuba hung lang a tih.
9. Kumkhaw sietnaah chun pei hawn ning an ta, Lalpa le a thuneina ropui tak hmaah khar khipin um an tih.
10. Chu ni chu a mi thienghlimhaiin an chawimawi ni ding le ama ringtuhaiin mak ropui an ti ni ding chu a nih. Nangni khom in kuoma kan thu hril in lo pom leiin ringtuhai lai chun thang veng in tih.
11. Chuong ang lei chun, Pathienin a kona cheu phua in um theina ding le that in nuomna po po le ringnaa in thilthaw tinreng chu a hratnaa a sukfamkimpek theina ding cheuin ma loin kan tongtaipek hlak cheu a nih.
12. Chuongchun, ei Pathien le Lalpa Isu Krista lunginsietna zarah ei Lalpa Isu hming chu chawimawiin um thei a ta, nangni khom ama zarah chawimawiin um thei bok in tih.
