1. Iemani chen hnungah Paulan Athens khuo a suoksan a, Korinth khuoah a fe a.
2. Chu taka chun Juda mi pakhat Akuila a hmu a, ama chu Ponto rama pieng a nih. Rom lal Klaudia-in Juda mi po po Rom khuo suoksan dinga thu a pek leia a nuhmei Prisilla leh Italy rama inthoka hung tlung hlim an nih.
3. Paula chun anni chu a va sir a, ama ang boka puon in siemtu an ni ve leiin a tlung chil a, sin a thawpui hlak a.
4. Sabat ni po leh Sunagog-ah thu a hril a, Judahai le Grikhai chu inringtir tumin thang a lak a.
5. Makedoniaa inthoka Sila le Timothe an hung tlung thlak chinah Paula chu thuhril rongbawlna tieng a'n pumpek a, Isu kha Krista chu a nizie Judahai a'n hriettir hlak a.
6. Nisienlakhom, Judahaiin ama chu nasa taka kala an biek sisaw leiin Paulan a puon a thin a, "In thisen maw chu in lu chungah tla raw se. Chu mawphurna laka inthok chun kei chu ka'n thieng. Tuta inthok chu Jentelhai tieng ngha tang ka tih" a ta.
7. Chu taka inthok chun a fe suok a, sunagog bul ela um Tita Justa, Pathien ngaisak mi ina chun a lut a.
8. Sunagog hotu Krispa le a sunghai po poin Lalpa an ring a, a thu hril ngaithlatu Korinth khuoa mi tam tak khomin an ring bok a, baptisma an chang tah a.
9. Zan khat chu Paulan inlarna a hmu a, Lalpa chun a kuomah, " Ti naw rawh, ma loin thu hril pei rawh.
10. Kei i kuomah ka um leiin, sukna ding chein i chungah tu khomin kut thlak naw ni hai, hi khuoa hin mi tam tak ka nei si a" a ta.
11. Paula chu anni lai chun Pathien thu hrilin kum khat le thla ruk a cham a.
12. Gallio chu Akaia ram optu a ni laiin Judahai inthuruolin Paula chu an bei hron a, roreltu hmaah an thuoi a.
13. "Hi pa hin kan Dan kal zawnga Pathien be dingin mi a thlem hlak a nih" an ta.
14. Paula chun hang don a tum a, sienkhom Gallio chun Judahai kuomah, "Aw Judahai, hi thil hi thil indik lo amanih le dan bawsietna thil chu ni sien, in thu ka ngaithlak ding a na.
15. Nisienlakhom, thu le hming le nangni Dan chungchanga in buoina thu el a ni chun nangnin ngaituo ro, kei chuong thila chun inrol naw ning" a ta.
16. Rorelna ina inthok chun a'n suoktir a.
17. Sienkhom mi dang po po chu sunagog hotu Sosthen chunga chun an tuong a, an man a, rorelna ina chun an vuok a. Chu khom chu Gallio chun ienga khom a uksak chuong nawh.
18. Paula chu Korinth khuoah sawt tak a cham hnungin unauhai maksanin Suria ram panin longin a suok a, Prisila le Akuila hai pahniin an zui a. Longa an chuong hma chun, a hmaa thu a lo tiem tah angin Paula chun Kenkriei-ah a lu a'n vawtir a.
19. Efesi khuo an tlung a, chu taka chun Prisila le Akuila chu a maksan a. Ama ruok chu sunagog-ah a lut a, Judahai chu Pathien thu a suipui a.
20. Anni chun la cham hri dingin an thlo a, sienkhom a remti naw a.
21. "Pathienin rem a ti chun in kuomah ka la hung nawk ding annawm"q tiin tha takin a'n thla tawl a, Efesi-a inthok chun longin a suok nawk a.
22. Kaisari a tlung a, chu zoah kohranhai chibai buk dingin Jerusalemah a hang fe a, Antiokei tieng a kir nawk a.
23. Antiokeia iemani chen a chawl hnungin a suok nawk a, Galati ram le Frugia ram a fang suok a, ringtuhai chu a suknghet pei a.
24. Chun, Juda mi pakhat, Apolo, Alexandria khuoa pieng chu Efesi khuoah a hung a. Ama chu lekha thiem, Pathien Lekha thu hre tak a na.
25. Lalpa lampui thuah inchuktirna tha tak dong tah a ni leiin Isu chanchin chu thahnem ngai tak le chieng taka hrilin mi a'n chuktir hlak a; nisienlakhom, Johan baptisma chauh a la hriet a.
26. Sunagoga khom thu huoi takin a hril a, Prisila le Akuilan a thu hril an hriet chun an inah an thuoi a, Pathien lampui chu ngun takin an hril fie a.
27. Akaia ramah a fe tum ti an hriet chun unauhaiin an thangpui a, ama an lo lawm lut theina dingin zirtirhai kuomah lekha an ziekpek a. A zuk tlung chun ringtuhai chu Pathien lunginsietna zarin nasa takin a thangpui a.
28. Isu chu Krista a nizie Pathien Lekha thu hmangin mi a'n chuktir a, vantlang hmaah Judahai chu a sel dong hlak.
