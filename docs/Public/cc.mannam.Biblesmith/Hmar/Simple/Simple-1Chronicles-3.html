1. Hienghai hi Davida naupahai,
2. pathumna chu Geshur lal Talmai naunu Maaka laka mi Absalom a na; palina chu Hagit laka mi Adonija a na;
3. pangana chu Abital laka mi Sefatia a na; parukna chu a nuhmei Egla laka mi Ithream a nih.
4. Hieng parukhai hi kum sari le thla ruk Hebron khuoa Davida inlal laia pienghai chu an nih.Kum sawmthum le kum thum Jerusalema Davida a'n lal sunga a naupa pienghai chu Samua (Simea), Sobab, Nathan le Solomon an nih.
5. Hieng palihai hi Amiel naunu Bathsheba laka pienghai chu an nih.
6. Hienghai baka hin naupa pakuo Ibar, Elisua, Elifelet,
7. Noga, Nefeg, Jafia,
8. Elisama, Eliada le Elifelet hai a nei bok.
9. Chuonghai chu a hmei nauhai tiem sa loa Davida naupahai chu an nih. An farnu chu Tamar a nih.
10. Solomon naupa chu Rehoboam a na, chu naupa chu Abija a na, chu naupa chu Asa a na, chu naupa chu Jehosafat a na,
11. chu naupa chu Jehoram (Joram) a na, chu naupa chu Ahazia a na, chu naupa chu Joash a na,
12. chu naupa chu Amazia a na, chu naupa chu Azaria a na, chu naupa chu Jotham a na,
13. chu naupa chu Ahaz a na, chu naupa chu Hezekia a na, chu naupa chu Manase a na,
14. chu naupa chu Amon a na, chu naupa chu Josia a nih.
15. Josia naupahai chu a upa tak Johanan, a dottu Jehoiakim, a pathumna Zedekia le a palina Salum an nih.
16. Jehoiakim naupahai chu Jehoiachin (Jekonia) le Zedekia an nih.
17. Babulon sala intang Jehoiachin (Jekonia) naupahai pasari chu Seatiel,
18. Malkiram, Pedaia, Senazar, Jekamia, Hosama le Nedabia an nih.
19. Pedaia naupahai chu Zerubabel le Simei an nih. Zerubabel naupahai chu Mesulam le Hanania an na, an farnu chu Selomit a nih.
20. A naupa dang pangahai chu Hasuba, Ohel, Berekia, Hasadia le Jusab-Hesed an nih.
21. Hanania naupahai chu Pelatia le Jesaia an nih. Jesaia chu Refaia pa a na, ama chu Arnan pa a na, ama chu Obadia pa a na, ama chu Sekania pa a nih.
22. Sekania naupa Semaia naupahai chu Hatus, Igal, Baria, Nearia le Safat an nih.
23. Nearia naupahai chu Elioenai, Hizkia le Azrikam an nih.
24. Elioenei naupahai chu Hodavia, Eliasib, Pelaia, Akub, Johanan, Delaia le Anani an nih.
