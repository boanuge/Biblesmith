1. Aw LALPA, ieng chen rau am,I mi thlathlam ding a na?I mi theinghil song ding maw?Ka lakah ieng chen hiel am,I hmel i thup ding a na?
2. Ieng chen top am rimsi a,Sun-zan lungril beidong a,Hmelmahai lu ling khum a,Nangin i mi sie ding chu?
3. Aw LALPA, aw ka Pathien,Mi en la, mi hung don rawh,Varin ka mit hung khaw rawh,In hlum song pal rawi ka tih;
4. "Ka tukdol der tah" tiin,Ka hmelma uong pal a ta,Ka tlu hlauh chun ka ralhai,Lawm sa chang pal rawi ka tih.
5. I hmangaina chul lo hi,Kei chu ka'n hnemna a na,I sandamna chunga hin,Ka lungril a lawm a nih.
6. LALPA chu inpak ka tih,Ka chungah a tha si a.
