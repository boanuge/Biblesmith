1. "Chuong nihai le chu huna chun,Judai ram le Jerusalem vangneina chu,Ka tungding nawk ding a nih.
2. Jehosafat Ruoma chun,Hnam tinhai ko khawm ka ta,Ka rochan, ka mi Israelhai,Hnam tin laia hnot dartu le,Ka ram sem dartu hnamhai chu,An chungthu la rel ka tih.
3. Thum vawrin ka mihai an insem a,Pasaltehai chu nawchi manah an zor a,Nuhmeitehai chu an dawn ding,Uoiin inchawkna manah an zor bok.
4. "Aw nangni Tura le Sidon le Filistin ram biel po pohai, ieng leia hienga in mi khing am a na? Ka thilthawhai kha mi thungrul in tum am a ni? Mi thungrul in tum a ni chun ka chunga in thaw ang char chu inrang takin in lu chungah bun let ka tih.
5. Ka tangkaruo hai, ka rangkachak hai le ka rohlu tam takhai chu lain in tempulah in hlu lut a.
6. Judai rama mihai le Jerusalem mihai chu an rama inthoka hla taka an inson hmang theina dingin Grik mihai kuomah in zor bok a.
7. "Ngai ro, in zorna hmuna inthok khan anni chu hung chok tho ka ta, in thilthaw chu in lu chungah hung bun let an tih.
8. In naunu le naupahai chu Juda mihai kuomah zor ka ta, anni chun kawl kila um Sabed mihai kuomah zor sawng an tih" ti hi LALPA kam suok thu chu a nih.
9. Hi thu hi hnamhai lai puong ro:Indo dingin in buotsai ro!Pasalthahai kha chok tho ro! Ral do thei chin hung hnai unla,Hma tieng norin bei ro!
10. In lo keina langkumhai kha,Kawlhnama hmang di'n sut unla,Thei kau tanna chemkuoi kha,Feia hmang dingin sut ro. Mi hrat naw khomin, "Takchapa!Ka hrat a nih" ti raw hai se.
11. Nangni a se vela hnam po pohai,Inhmaw takin hung tlan unla,Chu taka chun pung khawm ro.Aw LALPA, i pasaltha ruolhai chu,Hung tir thla tawl tah raw khai!
12. Hnamhai inralring hai sien,Jehosafat Ruom pan raw hai se;Chu taka chun inthung ka ta,Hnamhai chungthu chu rel ka tih.
13. An suolna a rapthlak em a,In kawitehai kha vilik ro,Bu sik dingin a hmin tah.Grep sawr khura sir sawr angin,A kuong sipin sir sawr ro,Uoiin bela liem zur zur khopin.
14. Mipui an pungkhawm luoi luoi,Rorelna ding ruoma chun;LALPA ni chu a tlung hnai tah,Rorelna ding ruoma chun.
15. Ni le thla inthim a ta,Arasi khom de ta naw nih.
16. LALPA chu Zion-a inthokin,Hung inrum dur dur a ta,Jerusalema inthokin,Van inrum angin inrum a ta,Hnuoi le van inthin a tih.A mihai ta ding ruok chun,LALPA chu an inhumhimna,Israel thlahai kulpui ning a tih.Pathien mihai ta dinga malsawmna
17. Chu ni a hung tlung pha chun,Kei, LALPA in Pathien ngei hi,Ka tlang thienghlim Zion-ah,Ka cheng ti hre tang in tih.Jerusalem chu thienghlim a ta,Hnam danghan run ta ngai naw nih.
18. "Chu nia chun tlanghaiin,Uoiin thar insuong an ta,Muol tina chun nene tui,Hung put thla huou huou a ta;Ruom tinah tui luong a tih.LALPA ina inthokin,Tui hnar inchik suok a ta,Sitim ruom chawm hnawng a tih.
19. Judahai chungah nunrawnga,Poi tawk lo thisen an insuo leiin,Aigupta chu ram vong a ta,Edom chu thlalerah inchang a tih.
20. Judai ram chu hluo kumkhuong an ta,Suon a'nthoka suon nawk peiin,Jerusalem chu hluo tang an tih.
21. An thisen phuba lang ka ta,Thil suol thawhai chu hla naw ning,LALPA chu Ziona cheng a ni si!
