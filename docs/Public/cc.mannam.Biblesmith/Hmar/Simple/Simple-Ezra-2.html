1. Judai rama mi Babulon lal Nebukadnezzarin Babulon tienga sala a thuoi, Jerusalem le Judai rama mani khuo senga hung kir nawkhai chu hienghai hi an nih.
2. An thuoihai chu Zerubabel, Jesua, Nehemia, Seraia, Reelaia, Mordekai, Bilsan, Mispar, Bigvai, Rehum, le Baana an nih. Israel mi hung kirhai chu:
3. Paros thlahai sanghni zakhat sawmsari pahnih;
4. Sefatia thlahai zathum sawmsari pahnih;
5. Ara thlahai zasari sawmsari panga;
6. Pahat-Moab thlahai (Jesua le Joab thlahai) sanghni zariet sawmpahnih;
7. Elam thlahai sangkhat zahni sawmnga pali;
8. Zatu thlahai zakuo sawmli panga;
9. Zakai thlahai zasari sawmruk;
10. Bani thlahai zaruk sawmli pahnih;
11. Bebai thlahai zaruk sawmhni pathum;
12. Azgad thlahai sangkhat zahni sawmhni pahnih;
13. Adonikam thlahai zaruk sawmruk paruk;
14. Bigvai thlahai sanghni sawmnga paruk;
15. Adin thlahai zali sawmnga pali;
16. Ater thlahai (Hezekia thlahai) sawmkuo pariet;
17. Bezai thlahai zathum sawmhni pathum;
18. Jora thlahai za le sawmpahnih;
19. Hasum thlahai zahni sawmhni pathum;
20. Gibar thlahai sawmkuo panga;
21. Bethlehem mihai za sawmhni pathum;
22. Netofa thlahai sawmnga paruk;
23. Anathot thlahai za sawmhni pariet;
24. Azmavet thlahai sawmli pahnih;
25. Kiriat Jearim, Kefira le Beerot khuoa mihai zasari sawmli pathum;
26. Rama le Geba khuoa mihai zaruk sawmhni pakhat;
27. Mikmas khaw mihai za sawmhni le pahnih;
28. Bethel le Ai khaw mihai zahni sawmhni pathum;
29. Nebo khaw mihai sawmnga pahnih;
30. Magbish mihai za sawmnga paruk;
31. Elam mihai sangkhat zahni sawmnga pali;
32. Harim mihai zathum sawmhni;
33. Lod, Hadid le Ono khaw mihai zasari sawmhni panga;
34. Jeriko khaw mihai zathum sawmli panga;
35. Senaa khaw mihai sangthum zaruk sawmthum.
36. Jedaia thlahai (Jesua zungzama mi) zakuo sawmsari pathum;
37. Imer thlahai sangkhat le sawmnga pahnih;
38. Pasur thlahai sangkhat zahni sawmli pasari;
39. Harim thlahai sangkhat le sawmpasarih.
40. Jesua le Kadmiel thlahai (Hodavia thlahai) sawmsari pali;
41. hla saktu Asaf thlahai za sawmhni pariet;
42. kotkhar vengtu Salum, Ater, Talmon, Akub, Hatita le Sobai thlahai za sawmthum pakuo.
43. Tempula thoktuhai thlahai chu: Ziha, Hasufa le Tabaot thlahai;
44. Keros, Siaha le Padon thlahai;
45. Lebanah, Hagaba le Akub thlahai;
46. Hagab, Salmai (Samlai) le Hanan thlahai;
47. Gidel, Gahar le Reaia thlahai;
48. Rezin, Nekoda le Gazam thlahai;
49. Uza, Pasea le Besai thlahai;
50. Asna, Meunim le Nefusim thlahai;
51. Bakbuk, Hakufa le Harhur thlahai;
52. Bazlut, Mehida le Harsha thlahai;
53. Barkos, Sisera le Tema thlahai;
54. Nezia le Hatifa thlahai an nih.
55. Lal Solomon siehlawhai thlahai chu Sotai, Soferet (Hasoferet) le Peruda thlahai;
56. Jaala, Darkon le Gidel thlahai;
57. Sefatia, Hatil, Pokeret-Hazebaim le Ami thlahai an nih.
58. Tempula thoktuhai le Solomon siehlawhai thlaa mi hung kir po po chu an rengin mi zathum sawmkuo pahnih an nih.
59. Telmela, Tel-harsa, Kerub, Adan le Imer khuohaia inthoka Jerusalema hung kir an um bok a, amiruokchu Israel thla mi le sa an ni le ni naw sui thei a ni nawh.
60. Hi pawl hi Delaia, Tobia le Nekoda thlahai an na, an rengin zaruk sawmnga pahnih an nih.
61. Chun, thiempu chihai laia mi Hobaia, Hakoz le Barzilai thlahai khom Jerusalemah an hung kir bok a. Hi Barzilai hin Gilead mi Barzilai naunu chu nuhmeiah a nei a, a nuhmei tieng hming a put ta lem a nih.
62. Hieng thiempuhai hin an sungkuo inthla sawng dan inchikna lekhabu chu an sukhmang leiin thiempua pom an ni ta naw a.
63. Urim le Thummimb hmanga hieng thila LALPA rawn theitu thiempu an nei hma lem chun hienghai hin inthawina thilhlana thiempu chanvo an fak le dawn ding takngiel khom ram optuin a phalpek ve nawh.
64. Judai rama hung kir nawk po po chu an rengin mi singli sanghni zathum sawmruk an nih.
65. Chu bakah siehlaw nuhmei le pasal mi sangsari zathum sawmthum pasari le hla saktu nuhmei le pasal mi zahni an thang sa bok a.
66. Sakor zasari sawmthum paruk, sabengtung lien chi zahni sawmli panga,
67. sanghawngsei zali sawmthum panga le sabengtung chin chi sangruk zasari sawmhni an nei bok a.
68. Jerusalema LALPA in an hang tlung chun a hmun pangngaia Pathien in chu bawl nawk dingin sungkuoa lukhaitu thenkhat chun thilpek an inhlan a.
69. An tlin ang tawka thawlawma an pek khawm zat chu rangkachak kilogram zanga, tangkaruo kilogram sanghni zariet le thiempu zakuo zahni a nih.
70. Chuongchun, thiempu hai, Levi-hai, kotkhar vengtuhai le tempula rongbawltuhai le vantlang mipuihai laia thenkhat chu Jerusalem se vela chun an um a, Israel mi danghai po po chu Judai rama anni khuo sengah an inbengbel nawk ta a.
