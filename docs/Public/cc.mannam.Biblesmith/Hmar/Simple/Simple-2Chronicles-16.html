1. Asa inlal kum sawmthum le kum ruknaah Israel lal Baasa-in a hang bei a, Juda lal Asa rama inthoka tu khom suoka an lut thei nawna dingin Rama khuo chu kulin an huontir a.
2. Chu phing leh Asa chun LALPA tempul le ama ina mi tangkaruo le rangkachak um po po chu a lak suok a, Damaskaa Aram lal Ben-Hadad kuomah a thon a,
3. "Ka pa le i pa inkara inremna thuthlung an siem ang bok khan nangma le keima inkara khom thuthlung siem veng ei tih. En ta, tangkaruo le rangkachak ka hung thon hi. Israel lal Baasa le in thuthlung lo siem tah kha sut la, ka rama inthokin a sipaihai hi hnuk dok raw se" tiin thu a thon a.
4. Ben-Hadad chun lal Asa thu chu a remtipui a, Israel rama khuohai run dingin a sipai kawmandarhai a tir a, Ijon khuo dam, Dan khuo dam, Abel Maim le Naftalia biela hmangruo sie khawmna khuo po po chu a bei a.
5. Chu thu chu Baasa-in a hriet charin Rama khuo an huon lai chu a tawpsan ta a.
6. Chuongchun, Asa chun Juda mihai po po chu a'n pun khawm a, a thuoi a, Baasa-in Rama kul siemna dinga thing le lung a lak khawmhai po po chu an lak vong a, chu chu Geba le Mizpa khuo siemna dingin an hmang ta a.
7. Chuong lai chun zawlnei Hanani chu Juda lal Asa kuomah a hung a, a kuomah, "LALPA i Pathienah innghat loa Aram lal i ring lem leiin Israel lal sipaihai chu i'n suo tah a ni kha.
8. Kush mihai le Libia mihai khan sipai le sakor-tawlailir le sakor chungchuong mi tam tak an nei a ni naw am a ni? Sienkhom, LALPA i ring leiin i kutah a pek che a ni kha.
9. Ama tienga lungril pe vongtuhai chu sukhrat dingin hnuoi chung po poa hin LALPAN mit a'n len kuol zing hlak a nih. Thil invetthlak tak i thaw leiin tuta inthok chu indopui nei zing tang i tih" a ta.
10. Asa chu zawlnei chunga chun a lungsen hle a, lung inah a khum ta hiel a. Chu taka inthok chun mi dang chunga khom rawng taka kut thlak a hung ching ta a.
11. Asa chanchin a bula inthoka a tawp chen chu Juda le Israel lalhai chanchin inziekna bu-ah a chuong.
12. Kum sawmthum le kum kuo a'n lal kumin Asa kepar chu nasa takin a hung na ta a, a na inrim hle a, sienkhom a natnaa chun LALPA rawn loin damdawi thiemhai a ruoi lem a.
13. Kum sawmli le kum khat a'n lal kumin Asa chu a thi a, a pi le puhai kuomah a chawl ve ta a.
14. Davida khawpuia ama thlan dinga a kera chun an phum a. A kuonga chun rimtui siemtuhaiin rimtui chi hrang hrang an siemhai chu an thun teu a, chu chunga chun a ruong chu an inzal a, inza inentirnain ama puolin meipui nasa tak an sep a.
