1. Yasisithi kuMozisi: Yenyukela e Nkosi ni, wena loAroni, uNadabi loAbihu, labangamatshumi ayisikhombisa kubadala bakoIsrayeli, likhonze likhatshana.
2. Njalo uMozisi yedwa uzasondela eNkosini, kodwa bona kabayikusondela; labantu kabayikwenyuka laye.
3. UMozisi wasesiza wabatshela abantu wonke amazwi e Nkosi lokwahlulela konke; bonke abantu baphendula ngalizwi linye bathi: Wonke amazwi e Nkosi ewakhulumileyo sizawenza.
4. UMozisi wasebhala wonke amazwi e Nkosi. Wavuka ekuseni kakhulu, wakha ilathi phansi kwentaba, lensika ezilitshumi lambili, ngokwezizwe ezilitshumi lambili zakoIsrayeli.
5. Wathuma amajaha abantwana bakoIsrayeli, anikela iminikelo yokutshiswa, ahlabela i Nkosi iminikelo yokuthula yamajongosi.
6. UMozisi wasethatha ingxenye eyodwa kwezimbili yegazi, wayifaka emiganwini; lengxenye eyodwa kwezimbili yegazi wayifafaza phezu kwelathi.
7. Wasethatha incwadi yesivumelwano, wayibala ezindlebeni zabantu. Basebesithi: Konke i Nkosi ekutshiloyo sizakwenza silalele.
8. UMozisi wasethatha igazi, walifafaza phezu kwabantu, wathi: Khangelani igazi lesivumelwano i Nkosi esenze lani mayelana lawo wonke lamazwi.
9. Basebesenyuka oMozisi loAroni, uNadabi loAbihu, labangamatshumi ayisikhombisa kubadala bakoIsrayeli,
10. basebebona uNkulunkulu wakoIsrayeli, langaphansi kwenyawo zakhe kwakukhona oku njengomsebenzi wamasafire* agandelweyo, lanjengamazulu uqobo ngokucwengeka.
11. Kodwa kaselulanga isandla sakhe kuzo iziphathamandla zabantwana bakoIsrayeli. Sebembonile-ke uNkulunkulu badla banatha.
12. I Nkosi yasisithi kuMozisi: Yenyukela kimi entabeni, ube khona; njalo ngizakunika izibhebhe zamatshe lomlayo lemithetho, engikubhalileyo ukubafundisa.
13. Wasukuma-ke uMozisi, lenceku yakhe uJoshuwa; uMozisi wasesenyukela entabeni kaNkulunkulu.
14. Wasesithi ebadaleni: Silindeleni lapha size sibuye kini; khangelani-ke uAroni loHuri balani; olezindaba kasondele kubo.
15. UMozisi wasesenyukela entabeni, leyezi lasibekela intaba.
16. Njalo inkazimulo ye Nkosi yahlala entabeni yeSinayi; leyezi layisibekela insuku eziyisithupha; yasimbiza uMozisi ngosuku lwesikhombisa iphakathi kweyezi.
17. Njalo ukubonakala kwenkazimulo ye Nkosi kwakunjengomlilo oqothulayo, engqongeni yentaba, emehlweni abantwana bakoIsrayeli.
18. UMozisi wasengena phakathi kweyezi, wenyukela entabeni; njalo uMozisi waba sentabeni insuku ezingamatshumi amane lobusuku obungamatshumi amane.
