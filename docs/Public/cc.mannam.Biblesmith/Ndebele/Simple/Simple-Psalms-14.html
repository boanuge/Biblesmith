1. Isithutha sithi enhliziyweni yaso: KakulaNkulunkulu. Bonakele, benza imisebenzi eyenyanyekayo; kakho owenza okuhle.
2. I Nkosi yakhangela phansi isemazulwini phezu kwabantwana babantu, ukubona ukuthi ukhona yini oqedisisayo, odinga uNkulunkulu.
3. Baphambukile bonke, bebonke bonakele; kakho owenza okuhle, kakho loyedwa.
4. Kabazi yini bonke abenza okubi, abadla abantu bami njengoba besidla isinkwa? Kabayibizi i Nkosi.
5. Lapho besaba lokwesaba, ngoba uNkulunkulu usesizukulwaneni solungileyo.
6. Liliyangisile icebo lomyanga, ngoba i Nkosi iyisiphephelo sakhe.
7. Kungathi usindiso lukaIsrayeli lungavela eZiyoni! Lapho i Nkosi ibuyisa ukuthunjwa kwabantu bayo, uJakobe uzathaba, uIsrayeli athokoze.
