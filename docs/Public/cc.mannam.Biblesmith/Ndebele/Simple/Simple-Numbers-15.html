1. I Nkosi yasikhuluma kuMozisi isithi:
2. Tshono ebantwaneni bakoIsrayeli uthi kubo: Lapho lifika elizweni lokuhlala kwenu, engilinika lona,
3. lisenza umnikelo owenzelwe i Nkosi ngomlilo, umnikelo wokutshiswa kumbe umhlatshelo, owokugcwalisa isithembiso, loba emnikelweni wesihle, loba emikhosini yenu emisiweyo, ukwenza uqhatshi olumnandi e Nkosi ni, okuvela enkomeni kumbe ezimvini,
4. lowo-ke onikelayo umnikelo wakhe e Nkosi ni uzasondeza umnikelo wokudla, ube yingxenye yetshumi lempuphu ecolekileyo ixubene lengxenye yesine yehini* yamafutha;
5. lewayini libe ngumnikelo wokunathwayo, ingxenye yesine yehini*, uzakulungisa lomnikelo wokutshiswa loba umhlatshelo, owewundlu elilodwa.
6. Kumbe owenqama lizalungisa umnikelo wokudla, ube zingxenye ezimbili etshumini zempuphu ecolekileyo ixubene lamafutha, ingxenye yesithathu yehini*;
7. lewayini libe ngumnikelo wokunathwayo, ingxenye yesithathu yehini*, uzalinikela libe liphunga elimnandi e Nkosi ni.
8. Njalo nxa lilungisa ithole eliduna lenkomo libe ngumnikelo wokutshiswa kumbe umhlatshelo, owokugcwalisa isithembiso, kumbe iminikelo yokuthula e Nkosi ni,
9. uzanikelela-ke ithole lenkomo umnikelo wokudla, ube zingxenye ezintathu etshumini lempuphu ecolekileyo ixubene lamafutha, ingxenye eyodwa kwezimbili yehini*.
10. Linikele iwayini libe ngumnikelo wokunathwayo, ingxenye eyodwa kwezimbili yehini, kube ngumnikelo owenziwe ngomlilo olephunga elimnandi e Nkosi ni.
11. Kuzakwenziwa njalo ngaleyonkunzi, loba ngaleyonqama, loba ngewundlu, kumbe ngezinyane.
12. Njengenani elizalilungisa, lizakwenza njalo kuleyo laleyo, ngokwenani lazo.
13. Wonke owokuzalwa elizweni uzazenza njalo lezizinto, ekunikeleni umnikelo owenziwe ngomlilo woqhatshi olumnandi e Nkosi ni.
14. Uba-ke owemzini ohlala njengowezizwe lani, kumbe loba ngubani ophakathi kwenu ezizukulwaneni zenu, njalo enikela umnikelo owenziwe ngomlilo woqhatshi olumnandi e Nkosi ni, njengoba lisenza uzakwenza njalo.
15. Kuzakuba lesimiso sinye kini abebandla lakowemzini ohlala njengowezizwe, isimiso esilaphakade ezizukulwaneni zenu; njengani uzakuba njalo owemzini phambi kwe Nkosi.
16. Kuzakuba lomthetho munye lesimiso sinye kini lakowemzini ohlala lani njengowezizwe.
17. I Nkosi yasikhuluma kuMozisi isithi:
18. Tshono ebantwaneni bakoIsrayeli uthi kubo: Ekungeneni kwenu elizweni engilisa kulo,
19. kuzakuthi nxa lisidla okokudla kwelizwe, linikele umnikelo wokuphakanyiswa e Nkosi ni.
20. Ngokokuqala kwenhlama yenu lizanikela iqebelengwana, kube ngumnikelo wokuphakanyiswa; njengomnikelo wokuphakanyiswa webala lokubhulela, ngokunjalo lizawuphakamisa.
21. Ngokokuqala kwenhlama yenu lizanika e Nkosi ni umnikelo wokuphakanyiswa ezizukulwaneni zenu.
22. Uba-ke liduha, lingenzanga yonke imilayo le i Nkosi eyikhulume kuMozisi,
23. konke i Nkosi elilaye khona ngesandla sikaMozisi kusukela ngosuku i Nkosi eyalaya ngalo kusiya phambili ezizukulwaneni zenu,
24. kuzakuthi-ke uba kwenziwe ngokungazi, kungekho emehlweni enhlangano, inhlangano yonke izanikela ijongosi elilodwa ithole lenkomo, libe ngumnikelo wokutshiswa, kube luqhatshi olumnandi e Nkosi ni, lomnikelo walo wokudla, lomnikelo walo wokunathwayo njengomthetho, lezinyane lembuzi elilodwa, libe ngumnikelo wesono.
25. Umpristi uzakwenzela-ke inhlangano yonke yabantwana bakoIsrayeli inhlawulo yokuthula, bakuthethelelwe, ngoba kuyikungazi, futhi bona bazaletha umnikelo wabo, umnikelo owenzelwe i Nkosi ngomlilo, lomnikelo wabo wesono phambi kwe Nkosi, ngenxa yesiphambeko sokungazi kwabo.
26. Lenhlangano yonke yabantwana bakoIsrayeli izakuthethelelwa, lowemzini ohlala njengowezizwe phakathi kwabo, ngoba kubo bonke abantu kwakusekungazini.
27. Uba-ke umuntu oyedwa esona ngokungazi, uzasondeza isibhuzikazi esilomnyaka owodwa, sibe ngumnikelo wesono.
28. Lompristi uzawenzela lowomphefumulo owone ngokungazi inhlawulo yokuthula, lapho one ngokungazi phambi kwe Nkosi, ukumenzela inhlawulo yokuthula; uzakuthethelelwa-ke.
29. Lizakuba lomlayo munye kwalowo owona ngokungazi, owokuzalwa elizweni phakathi kwabantwana bakoIsrayeli, lowemzini ohlala njengowezizwe phakathi kwabo.
30. Kodwa umuntu okwenza ngabomo, engowokuzalwa elizweni loba engowemzini, ethuka i Nkosi; njalo lowomuntu uzaqunywa asuke phakathi kwabantu bakibo.
31. Ngoba edelele ilizwi le Nkosi, wephula umlayo wayo, lowomuntu uzaqunywa lokuqunywa; ububi bakhe buzakuba phezu kwakhe.
32. Kwathi abantwana bakoIsrayeli besenkangala, bafica umuntu etheza inkuni ngosuku lwesabatha.
33. Labo abamficayo etheza inkuni bamletha kuMozisi lakuAroni lakunhlangano yonke.
34. Bamfaka esitokisini, ngoba kwakunga ka tshiwo ukuthi kuzakwenziwani kuye.
35. I Nkosi yasisithi kuMozisi: Lowomuntu kabulawe lokubulawa; inhlangano yonke izamkhanda ngamatshe ngaphandle kwenkamba.
36. Lenhlangano yonke yamkhuphela ngaphandle kwenkamba, yamkhanda ngamatshe waze wafa, njengokulaya kwe Nkosi kuMozisi.
37. I Nkosi yasikhuluma kuMozisi isithi:
38. Khuluma ebantwaneni bakoIsrayeli, ubatshele ukuthi bazenzele izintshaka emiphethweni yezembatho zabo, ezizukulwaneni zabo, lokuthi bafake entshakeni yomphetho intambo eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka.
39. Njalo kuzakuba kini yintshaka ukuze likukhangele, likhumbule yonke imilayo ye Nkosi, liyenze, lingadingi lilandele inhliziyo yenu lilandele amehlo enu, eliphinga ngokukulandela.
40. Ukuze likhumbule lenze yonke imilayo yami, libe ngcwele kuNkulunkulu wenu.
41. Ngiyi Nkosi uNkulunkulu wenu eyalikhupha elizweni leGibhithe ukuba nguNkulunkulu wenu; ngiyi Nkosi uNkulunkulu wenu.
