1. Inhlangano yonke yabantwana bakoIsrayeli yasuka enkangala yeSini, ngamalombolombo abo, ngokomlayo we Nkosi, bamisa inkamba eRefidimi; kodwa kwakungelamanzi okunatha abantu.
2. Ngakho abantu bamsola uMozisi bathi: Sinike amanzi, sinathe. UMozisi wasesithi kubo: Lingisolelani? Liyilingelani i Nkosi?
3. Abantu basebesomela amanzi lapho, abantu bangunguna kuMozisi bathi: Wasenyuselani eGibhithe ukuze usibulale thina labantwana bethu lezifuyo zethu ngokoma?
4. UMozisi wasekhala e Nkosi ni esithi: Ngizakwenzani kulababantu? Sekukancane ukuthi bangikhande ngamatshe.
5. I Nkosi yasisithi kuMozisi: Dlula phambi kwabantu, uthathe kanye lawe kubadala bakoIsrayeli, lentonga yakho owatshaya umfula ngayo, ithathe ngesandla sakho, uhambe.
6. Khangela, ngizakuma lapho phambi kwakho phezu kwedwala eHorebe; njalo uzatshaya idwala, kuphume-ke kulo amanzi ukuze abantu banathe. UMozisi wasesenza njalo phambi kwamehlo abadala bakoIsrayeli.
7. Wasebiza ibizo lendawo ngokuthi yiMasa lo kuthi yiMeriba ngenxa yokusola kwabantwana bakoIsrayeli langenxa yokuyilinga kwabo i Nkosi besithi: I Nkosi iphakathi kwethu yini loba hatshi?
8. Kwasekufika uAmaleki, walwa loIsrayeli eRefidimi.
9. UMozisi wasesithi kuJoshuwa: Sikhethele amadoda, uphume uyekulwa loAmaleki; kusasa ngizakuma engqongeni yoqaqa, lentonga kaNkulunkulu izakuba sesandleni sami.
10. UJoshuwa wasesenza njengokutsho kukaMozisi kuye, walwa loAmaleki. OMozisi, uAroni loHuri basebesenyukela engqongeni yoqaqa.
11. Kwasekusithi lapho uMozisi ephakamisa isandla sakhe, uIsrayeli waba lamandla; kodwa lapho wehlisa isandla sakhe, uAmaleki waba lamandla.
12. Kodwa izandla zikaMozisi zaba nzima, ngakho bathatha ilitshe, balibeka ngaphansi kwakhe, ukuthi ahlale kulo. OAroni loHuri basebesekela izandla zakhe, omunye engapha lomunye engale, ukuze izandla zakhe ziqine lize litshone ilanga.
13. UJoshuwa wasemchitha uAmaleki labantu bakhe ngobukhali benkemba.
14. I Nkosi yasisithi kuMozisi: Bhala lokhu egwalweni kube yisikhumbuzo, ukubeke ezindlebeni zikaJoshuwa ukuthi ngokwesula ngizakwesula ukukhunjulwa kukaAmaleki ngaphansi kwamazulu.
15. UMozisi wasesakha ilathi, wabiza ibizo lalo ngokuthi: UJehova-nisi;
16. wathi: Ngoba isandla siphezu kwesihlalo sobukhosi se Nkosi, i Nkosi izakuba lempi imelene loAmaleki kusukela kusizukulwana kusiya kusizukulwana.
